X-Rite Inc. Unveils Product

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

GRANDVILLE, Mich. -- X-Rite Inc. introduced an instrument used for measuring shades of color in the graphic arts industry. Production of the so-called X-scanning densitometer is expected during the fourth quarter of 1990, the company said.

In addition, X-Rite said it is adding 30,000 square feet of specialized production area to its existing facility to cover an expected increase in sales of instruments.
