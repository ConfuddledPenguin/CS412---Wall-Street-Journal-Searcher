Who's News: International Paper Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

INTERNATIONAL PAPER Co. (Purchase, N.Y.) -- J. Alan Day, previously vice president-quality management, environment and health, was named vice president-environment, health and safety for this paper and forest-products company. Newland A. Lesko, manager-manufacturing, coated papers and pulp, was named staff vice president and director of quality management.
