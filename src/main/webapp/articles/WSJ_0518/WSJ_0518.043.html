Who's News: Grand Metropolitan PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

GRAND METROPOLITAN PLC (London) -- Robert J. Stetson, 39 years old, chief financial officer of the Miami-based Burger King Corp. unit, was named chief operating officer of the fast-food chain, succeeding C. Ronald Petty, who earlier resigned. Mr. Petty's title of president wasn't filled, nor was a new chief financial officer immediately named.
