Amrep to Sell Utility Systems

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

NEW YORK -- Amrep Corp., a builder of communities and international distributor of magazines, said it agreed to sell water and sewer utility systems serving Rio Rancho and Santa Fe, N.M., to a subsidiary of GWC Corp., a Wilmington, Del., water utility holding company, for more than $36 million. Of the proceeds, about $20.5 million will be used to repay utility debt, and part of the remainder will be applied to reduce other debt, with the balance to be added to working capital.

The sale, subject to approval by the New Mexico Public Service Commission, includes the Rio Rancho water and sewer system and the small water utility serving Amrep's Eldorado subdivision in Santa Fe.
