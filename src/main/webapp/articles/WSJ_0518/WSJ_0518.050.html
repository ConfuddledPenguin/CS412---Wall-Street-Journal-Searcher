Who's News: Management Co. Entertainment Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

MANAGEMENT CO. ENTERTAINMENT GROUP (New York) -- Raymond H. Godfrey Jr., 45 years old, formerly a managing director and senior vice president, investment banking, at Rodman & Renshaw Capital Group Inc., was named president and chief operating officer, filling a vacancy at this company, which manages actors and actresses and finances, and produces and distributes entertainment.
