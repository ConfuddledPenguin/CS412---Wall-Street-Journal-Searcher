Marketing Brief -- Pathe Communications Corp.: Third Payment Is Made In MGM/UA Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

MGM/UA Communications Co. said Pathe Communications Corp. made the third of four $50 million security deposits toward its $1.27 billion acquisition of MGM/UA. Culver City, Calif.-based MGM/UA had granted Pathe an extra week, until yesterday, to make the payment. In addition to the $200 million in security deposits, Pathe has granted MGM/UA a security interest of $75 million in three Pathe films.

Pathe, a Los Angeles-based entertainment concern controlled by Italian financier Giancarlo Parretti, launched its $20-a-share offer for MGM/UA on March 14. It is set to expire June 7.
