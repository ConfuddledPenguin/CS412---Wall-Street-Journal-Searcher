Financing Business: Alcan Aluminum Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

ALCAN ALUMINIUM Ltd. -- This Montreal aluminum producer said it is offering in the U.S. $150 million of 9.4% debentures, due June 1, 1995. Alcan said proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes. Company officials weren't available to elaborate. Alcan said the debentures are priced at par, to yield 9.4%. First Boston Corp. and Morgan Stanley & Co. are underwriting the offering.
