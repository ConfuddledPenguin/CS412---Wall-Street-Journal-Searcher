Swiss Wholesale Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

BERN -- Switzerland's wholesale price index rose 0.2% in April from March and 1.5% from April 1989, the government said. The figures approximated the expectations of some analysts, who had expected a slowdown due to the resurgence of the Swiss franc.

The measure of wholesale import prices rose 0.1% month to month in April and declined 1.3% from April 1989, while the gauge of domestic wholesale prices rose 0.2% from March and increased 2.6% from last April.
