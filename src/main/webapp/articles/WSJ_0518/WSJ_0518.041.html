Boeing Wins Army Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

WASHINGTON -- Boeing Co. won a $33.5 million Army contract for air defense systems.

Litton Systems Inc., a unit of Litton Industries Inc., was given a $33.2 million Navy contract for aircraft navigation systems.

FMC Corp. received a $26.3 million Army contract for armored vehicle support.

National Beef Packing Co. was awarded a $10.1 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for chilled beef.
