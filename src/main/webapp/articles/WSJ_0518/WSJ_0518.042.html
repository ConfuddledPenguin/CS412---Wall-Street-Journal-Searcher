Mellon Bank, Merrill Link Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

PITTSBURGH -- Mellon Bank and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets said they established an independent corporation that will purchase receivables from Mellon's corporate customers. Three Rivers Funding Corp., as the entity will be called, will issue as much as $500 million of its commercial paper through Merrill Lynch and use the proceeds to buy trade, credit-card and other corporate receivables from Mellon customers.

By selling receivables to Three Rivers, Mellon corporate customers will be able to remove those receivables from their balance sheets and finance them at commercial paper-based rates.

Mellon Bank is a unit of Mellon Bank Corp., a bank holding company.
