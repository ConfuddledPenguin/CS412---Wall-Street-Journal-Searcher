S&P 500 Index to Drop Norton

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

NEW YORK -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it will drop Norton Co. from its S&P 500 index of stocks, replacing it with Cooper Tire & Rubber Co., effective at the close of trading next Thursday. Norton, an abrasives maker based in Worcester, Mass., is being acquired by French glass maker Compagnie de Saint Gobain. Cooper Tire is a tire maker based in Findlay, Ohio.

S&P generally announces changes in the composition of its S&P 500 index a week in advance.
