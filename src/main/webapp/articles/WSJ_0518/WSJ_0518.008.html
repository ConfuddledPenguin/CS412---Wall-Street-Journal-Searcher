Dividend News: Tiffany & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

TIFFANY & Co. said it increased its quarterly dividend 40% to seven cents a share from five cents, payable July 10 to stock of record June 20. The New York-based retailer of fine gifts and jewelry said the increase reflects its "world-wide growth strategies, strong financial position and optimistic outlook."
