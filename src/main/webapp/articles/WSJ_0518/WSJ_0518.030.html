Raytheon's Beech Aircraft Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

LEXINGTON, Mass. -- Raytheon Co. said its Beech Aircraft division has agreed to join with Pilatus Aircraft Ltd., of Switzerland, to vie for a U.S. military contract for trainer airplanes. Beech and Pilatus will propose a version of Pilatus's PC-9 turboprop trainer for selection in the Joint Primary Aircraft Training System of the Navy and Air Force. Beech will be team leader. The contract also includes simulators and training systems.

The companies said that if Beech obtains a contract, Pilatus will produce a significant portion of the planes, with final assembly in Beech plants in Kansas.
