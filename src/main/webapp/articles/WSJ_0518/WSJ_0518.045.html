Who's News: Xerox Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

XEROX Corp. (Stamford, Conn.) -- Four executives who continue in their current responsibilities were named corporate vice presidents. They are Patricia C. Barron, director, corporate-information management; John Seely Brown, a vice president of the corporate-research group in charge of advanced research at the Palo Alto Research Center; Philip D. Fishbach, vice president, finance, for the U.S. marketing group; and Charles P. Holt, vice president of the systems reprographics development unit. Xerox is a business-machines and financial-services company.
