Malaysia Industrial Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia -- Malaysia's first-quarter industrial production rose 11.1% from the same period last year, the Statistics Department said. The department added that the March index at 160.8, was 15% higher than the February index at 139.8, and 10.4% above the March 1989 index of 145.6.

The department attributed the increase in the index to significant gains in all three major divisions. The manufacturing index rose 16.3%, to 172.0 in the first quarter, from 147.8 in 1989's first quarter; while the electricity index rose 12.8%, to 153.8 from 136.4; and the mining index rose 2.4%, from 129.5 to 132.6.

Fourteen of the 16 industrial groups under the manufacturing division recorded increases in first-quarter production indices. The leader was transport equipment, followed by electrical machinery and nonmetallic mineral products.
