Dividend News: American Southwest Mortgage Investments Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

AMERICAN SOUTHWEST MORTGAGE INVESTMENTS Corp. said it boosted its monthly dividend 20% to six cents a share from five cents, payable June 15 to stock of record May 31, marking the third time since last September that the Tucson, Ariz., real estate investment trust has increased the payout.
