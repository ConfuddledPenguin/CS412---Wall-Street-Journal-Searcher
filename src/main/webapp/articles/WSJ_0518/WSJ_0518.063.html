Who's News: SCEcorp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

SCECORP. (Rosemead, Calif.) -- Harold B. Ray, vice president, nuclear engineering, safety and licensing, at this holding company's Southern California Edison Co., was named a senior vice president of the unit, retaining the same responsibilities. Charles M. McCarthy Jr., also a vice president, was elected a senior vice president of the unit. H.E. Morgan was elected a vice president of the unit and site manager at the San Onofre Nuclear Generating Station, where he is station manager. The appointments all are effective June 1. SCEcorp. has interests in utility, energy, land, electric power and financing businesses.
