Who's News: Nacco Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

NACCO INDUSTRIES Inc. (Cleveland) -- John C. Sawhill, president and chief executive officer of the Nature Conservancy, and Richard B. Tullis, chairman of NCC Funds and Waste-Quip Inc., were elected directors of this forklift-trucks producer and consumer-products and mining concern. They succeed Everett Ware Smith and Gaetano G. Pirrone, who didn't stand for re-election.
