International Brief -- Nobel Industrier Sverige AB: Accord Reached to Acquire Unit of Stora Kopparbergs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

Nobel Industrier Sverige AB agreed to buy the chemicals division of Stora Kopparbergs Bergslags AB, buttressing its position as a global leader in chemicals used by the pulp and paper industry. The acquisition is the fourth proposed by Nobel this month. If successful, the transactions valued at 5.95 billion Swedish kronor ($989.5 million) would boost the acquisitive Swedish concern's annual sales by more than 25%. Nobel said it would pay 1.93 billion kronor for Stora's chemicals unit.

Shedding that chemicals business opens an asset-disposal program by Stora, prompted by last month's takeover of West Germany's Feldmuehle Nobel AG. Industry analysts also expect Stora to put its foundering Akerlund & Rausing packaging unit on the block-in addition to recouping as much as half of the sum it paid for Feldmuehle Nobel by disposing of the West German concern's non-forestry divisions.

Nobel's Eka Nobel division and Stora's chemicals unit are both among Europe's leading producers of forestry chemicals.
