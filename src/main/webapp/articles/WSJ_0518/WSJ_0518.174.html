Business Brief -- Southmark Corp.: Company Expects to Post Narrower Quarterly Deficit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

Southmark Corp. said in Dallas that it expects to report a net loss of $59 million for the third quarter, ended March 31, compared with a net loss of $1.04 billion after massive asset write-downs in the year-earlier period. The real estate and thrift concern, which is operating under bankruptcy-law proceedings, said it has notified the Securities and Exchange Commission that it will file its Form 10-Q late. The company said the competing demands of the bankruptcy proceedings have kept it from getting its financial report prepared on time.

In the third quarter last year, Southmark reported a loss from continuing operations of $936.4 million, including more than $800 million in asset write-downs. Its loss from discontinued operations was $104.3 million.
