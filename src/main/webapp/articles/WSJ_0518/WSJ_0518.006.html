International Managers See Sales, Profit Rise, D&B Survey Reports

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

NEW YORK -- International business leaders expect sales and profits to rise this quarter, reversing a recent trend of pessimism, according to Dun & Bradstreet Corp.'s latest survey of 10,000 executives from 12 countries. The prospects were felt to be bleakest in Brazil, Australia and England; while optimism ran highest in Italy, Switzerland and West Germany.

The survey, which included the U.S. and Canada, said expectations for increases in selling prices and unemployment were unchanged from the first quarter.
