Business Brief -- Helm Resources Inc.: Concern Finishes Public Sale Of 500,000 Shares of Seitel

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

Helm Resources Inc., Greenwich, Conn., said it completed a public sale of a 500,000-share stake in Seitel Inc. for about $4.6 million, or $10.25 a share, reducing its Seitel stake to 33%. Proceeds from the sale will be used partly to buy back 24,870 shares of preferred stock. The company also said it would pay interest in cash on its debentures that are due in 1994 rather than through the issuance of common stock, which had been the practice since 1982.

Helm Resources has interests in marketing of thermoplastic resins, seismic data for oil companies, optical disk software and agricultural products. Houstonbased Seitel markets seismic data.

In American Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Helm closed at 43.75 cents a share, unchanged. Seitel closed at $12.875 a share, up 62.5 cents.
