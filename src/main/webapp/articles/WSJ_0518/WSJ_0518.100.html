Copperweld Special Meeting

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said it rescheduled its special stockholders' meeting to July 10. Shareholders of record June 8 will vote on Copperweld's agreement to be acquired by Imetal, Paris, which has agreed to acquire the 34.8% of Copperweld it doesn't already own for $17 a share.
