Who's News: Continental Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

CONTINENTAL CORP. (New York) -- Anne Wexler, chairman of a Washington, D.C., government-relations and public-affairs company, and Henry Kates, president and chief executive officer of Mutual Benefit Life Insurance Co., Kansas City, Mo., were elected directors of this insurance holding company. The elections fill one vacancy and increase the size of the board to 16 from 15.
