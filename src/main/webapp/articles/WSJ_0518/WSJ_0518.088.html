Business Brief -- May Department Stores Co.: St. Louis Concern's Profit Rose 27% in First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

May Department Stores Co. said profit rose nearly 27% in the fiscal first quarter. The St. Louis retailer reported net income of $80 million, or 58 cents a share, compared with $63 million, or 45 cents a share, a year earlier. The year-ago figure includes a loss of $1 million, or one cent a share, on the company's discontinued discount store operations.

May Chairman David C. Farrell said the results indicate that the company "is off to a good start for 1990." Sales in the quarter ended May 5 rose to $2.19 billion from $2.05 billion.
