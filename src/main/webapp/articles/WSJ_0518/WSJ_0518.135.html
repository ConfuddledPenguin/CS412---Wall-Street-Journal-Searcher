Business Brief -- Medco Research Inc.: Accord With Oppenheimer On Finding Buyer Is Ended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

Medco Research Inc. said it and Oppenheimer & Co., its investment banker, mutually agreed to end their agreement under which Oppenheimer was exploring the possibility of selling the Los Angeles company earlier than planned. The agreement was set to terminate in August, said Medco, a drug company that develops treatments and diagnoses of cardiovascular diseases. Although Oppenheimer contacted a "significant number" of pharmaceuticals concerns about acquiring the company, Medco said, none of them elected to follow through. The company retained Oppenheimer in November.

Medco said it didn't plan to hire another investment banker immediately. The company added, however, that it may later review various "strategic alternatives" available to it.
