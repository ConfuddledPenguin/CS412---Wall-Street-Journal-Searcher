Who's News: Caisse Des Depots et Consignations

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

CAISSE DES DEPOTS ET CONSIGNATIONS (Paris) -- Gordon I. Miller Jr., previously senior vice president, fixed-income risk management, at the Chase Manhattan Bank unit of Chase Manhattan Corp., was named executive vice president and treasurer of CDC Capital Inc., the New York subsidiary of this financial group, which has interests in banking, asset management, brokering, public finance and life insurance.
