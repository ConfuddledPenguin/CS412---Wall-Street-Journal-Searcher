Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 317,282 metric tons in the week ended May 12, up 11% from 286,509 tons in the preceeding week but down 0.2% from 317,283 tons a year earlier, according to Statistics Canada, a federal agency. A metric ton equals 2,204.6 pounds. The cumulative total in 1990 was 5,229,474 metric tons, down 12% from 5,915,088 tons a year earlier.
