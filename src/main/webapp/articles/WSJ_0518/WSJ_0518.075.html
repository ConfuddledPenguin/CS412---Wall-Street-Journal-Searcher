Who's News: Universal Matchbox Group Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

UNIVERSAL MATCHBOX GROUP Ltd. (Hong Kong) -- Thomas J. Kalinske, chief executive officer of this toy maker's Matchbox International Ltd., the U.S. unit based in Moonachie, N.J., was named to the additional post of president of the U.S. marketing subsidiary, Matchbox Toys USA, succeeding Andrew Gatto, who resigned to pursue other interests. David Yeh, chairman and chief executive of the parent, assumed Mr. Kalinske's duties as chief operating officer of the parent. Kenneth A. Bloom, Matchbox International chief financial officer, also was named financial chief at Matchbox USA.
