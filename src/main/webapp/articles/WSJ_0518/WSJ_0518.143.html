Who's News: Aca Joe Taps Shulman Of Bendel for Posts Of President, Director

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Aca Joe Inc. said it named retail-clothing veteran Mark Shulman as president and a director of the company, succeeding Peter Muller, who resigned. Mr. Shulman said Mr. Muller resigned to pursue other interests. However, Mr. Muller couldn't be reached for comment on the move.

The casual-apparel company said the 41-year-old Mr. Shulman, who served since 1987 as president and chief executive officer of Henri Bendel of New York, will add the chief executive position to his list of duties at Aca Joe on June 19. As chief executive, he will succeed Alice Lam, who will remain chairman and a director.

Before his tenure at Bendel, Mr. Shulman was president and chief executive of Ann Taylor. He has also served in retailing positions with Bloomingdale's in New York and I. Magnin in San Francisco.

Mr. Shulman said he plans to take the next 30 to 60 days to evaluate what needs to be done with the clothing retailer, which emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law proceedings just over a year ago.

"The first order of business is to get the stores looking the way they should," he said. Better visual display, better sales associates, and better merchandising lead the list of tasks, he said. "I think people will tend to see a change in merchandise around the holidays," he added.
