Italy Commercial Vehicle Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

MILAN -- New commercial vehicle sales in Italy rose 0.4% in April from the year before and were up 7.2% in the first four months of 1990, according to figures released by the Italian Federation of Auto Manufacturers, Anfia. Sales of the commercial vehicles, which are small buses and trucks weighing up to 3.5 tons, totaled 13,501 in April, compared with 13,448 in April 1989, Anfia said. During the first four months of the year, commercial vehicle sales totaled 59,260, compared with 55,297 in the first four months of 1988.

Domestic manufacturers controlled 49.8% of the market, while Ford Motor Co. was a leader among foreign manufacturers with a 14% market share.
