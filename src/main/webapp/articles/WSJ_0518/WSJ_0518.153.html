Who's News: Ames Names Thorner To Management Posts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

ROCKY HILL, Conn. -- Ames Department Stores Inc., which is operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, said it named Peter Thorner executive vice president and chief financial officer. Mr. Thorner, 46 years old, succeeds Duane R. Wolter, 40, who will be leaving the company in August. A spokesman for Ames declined to comment on the reasons for Mr. Wolter's planned departure and said Mr. Wolter was unavailable for comment.

Mr. Thorner was most recently a managing director, senior vice president and chief financial officer of Wheelabrator Technologies Inc., Danvers, Mass.

Ames filed for bankruptcy-law protection from its creditors last month. Earlier this week, a bankruptcy court judge approved Ames's request that it be allowed to receive $250 million in interim financing from Chemical Banking Corp.'s Chemical Bank unit.
