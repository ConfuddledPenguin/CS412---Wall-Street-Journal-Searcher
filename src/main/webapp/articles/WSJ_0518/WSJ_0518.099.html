Goodrich Unit Opens 2 Plants

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

AKRON, Ohio -- B.F. Goodrich Co.'s aerospace subsidiary said it opened two two facilities to service commercial and civil aircraft wheels, brakes and tires in Louisville, Ky., and London. The Louisville facility will support United Parcel Service under an existing contract, as well as additional customers, Goodrich said. The London plant represents an expansion into the European market, with Air Europe as the initial customer, Goodrich said.

Goodrich also produces chemicals and plastics.
