U.S. Steel Imports Fell Nearly 8% in March

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

WASHINGTON -- U.S. steel imports in March declined nearly 8%. For the first three months of the year, steel imports declined 15.6%, according to the American Iron and Steel Institute compilation of Commerce Department figures. March imports of 1,120,000 tons compared with 1,216,000 tons a year earlier. February imports of 1,111,000 were down 17% from 1,366,000 tons a year earlier.

For the three months, imports totaled 3,661,587 tons, down from 4,335,858 tons a year earlier.

Steel exports in March totaled 365,000 tons down from 606,000 tons a year earlier, but higher than the 313,000 tons in February, the association said. In the latest quarter, exports totaled 1,052,000 tons, compared with 1,102,000 tons a year ago.
