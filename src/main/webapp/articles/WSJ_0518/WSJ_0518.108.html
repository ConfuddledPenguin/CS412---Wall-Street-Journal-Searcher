Who's News: Prudential Insurance Co. of America

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

PRUDENTIAL INSURANCE CO. OF AMERICA (Newark, N.J.) -- Kenneth E. MacWilliams, chairman and chief executive officer of the Prudential Capital Corp. unit, was named president and chief operating officer of Gateway Investors, which manages the company's three main public securities investment units. Eric A. Simonson, managing director, corporate finance group, will succeed Mr. MacWilliams at the capital unit, effective June 1.
