Business Brief -- Lufthansa: Airline Says Pretax Profit Increased 7.9% Last Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

Lufthansa said that its 1989 group pretax profit climbed 7.9% to 260 million marks ($158 million) from 241 million marks in 1988. The West German national air carrier, 51%-owned by the West German government, said that group sales climbed 10% to 13.1 billion marks from 11.85 billion.

But the company said relatively flat freight business along with the stronger mark had led to earnings weakness in the first four months of 1990.
