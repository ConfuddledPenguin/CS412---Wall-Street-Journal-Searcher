Graham's Sales Outlook for '90

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/18/90

BATAVIA, N.Y. -- Officials of Graham Corp., a designer and maker of vacuum and heat-transfer equipment, told shareholders at the annual meeting that sales this year will increase about 18% to about $80 million from $67.8 million last year. The company didn't make a specific earnings forecast, but said it expects each of its units to be profitable. Executives said they expect continuing strength in the company's principal markets, including chemical and petrochemical process industries and co-generation power.

Graham said shareholders voted to double the number of the company's authorized common shares to six million.
