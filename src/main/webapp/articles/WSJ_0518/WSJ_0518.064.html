Dividend News: UDC-Universal Development L.P.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/18/90

UDC-UNIVERSAL DEVELOPMENT Limited Partnership cut its quarterly 29% to 50 cents a share from 70 cents, payable July 5 to stock of record June 15. Just three months ago, the Tempe, Ariz., builder of residential communities increased the quarterly to 70 cents a share from 60 cents, and declared a special distribution of 50 cents a share. UDC said the reduction reflects "increased uncertainty related to the economy and the timing of realization of revenues from new subdivisions, particularly those in California." UDC also declared quarterlies of 93.75 cents a share on its Series A preferred, and 90.625 cents a share on Series B preferred, both payable July 5 to stock of record June 15.
