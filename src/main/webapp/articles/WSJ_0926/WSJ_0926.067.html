Business Brief -- GTE Corp.: Some Eastern Planes to Get Airfone Unit's Seat Phones

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

GTE Corp.'s GTE Airfone unit in Oak Brook, Ill., said it agreed to equip 150 Eastern Airlines planes with seat phones. Terms weren't disclosed.

The phones, to be installed on Eastern's domestic flights, would allow passengers to place calls without leaving their seats. The installation will be completed by mid1991.

A trustee currently is running Eastern, which had been part of Continental Airlines Holdings Inc., Houston. GTE, Stamford, Conn., has interests in telecommunications, lighting-products and precision-materials.
