Marketing Brief -- TVX Broadcast Group Inc.: Holders Advised to Accept Paramount's Latest Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

Independent directors of TVX Broadcast Group Inc. recommended that shareholders accept a sweetened offer by Paramount Communications Inc. for the 17% of TVX shares in public hands. New York-based Paramount, which owns 83% of the television broadcaster, boosted its offer to $9.50 a share from $7.50 for more than seven million shares outstanding. The offer would be valued at $66 million for the remaining shares of the Virginia Beach, Va., company.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, TVX shares closed at $9, up $1.375 a share. The offer is subject to negotiation of an acquisition agreement and approval by TVX shareholders.

Paramount is an entertainment and publishing concern.
