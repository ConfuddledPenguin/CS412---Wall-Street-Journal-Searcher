Vulcan International Stock Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

CINCINNATI -- Vulcan International Corp. said its board adopted a plan to buy its stock from holders owning 99 shares or fewer. Vulcan said the price paid will be the closing price of Vulcan common shares on the date the shares are received, plus $5.

Vulcan said 470 of its 974 shareholders have 99 or fewer shares. They own 11,474 shares of its 1,494,553 shares outstanding.

The plan is in effect through Dec. 31, but could be extended, the company said.

Vulcan makes shoes, and sports and plastic products.
