Brite Voice Systems

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

WICHITA, Kan. -- Brite Voice Systems Inc. said it settled a lawsuit against West Interactive Corp., formerly 900 Services Inc., Omaha, Neb. The settlement will result in a pretax charge against Brite Voice earnings of about $463,000 in the third quarter, ending Sunday. Brite Voice is a supplier of audiotex systems.

The suit, filed in September 1989, sought collection of about $614,000 remaining due under a contract for the sale of a Gateway 5000 system. Under the settlement, Brite Voice released its claim for the balance due under the contract and the defendants released their counterclaims for damages, according to Brite Voice.

West Interactive said it was satisfied with the settlement.
