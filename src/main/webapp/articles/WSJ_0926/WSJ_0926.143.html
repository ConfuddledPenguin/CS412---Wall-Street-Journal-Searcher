Crane May Spin Off Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

NEW YORK -- Crane Co. said it is considering distributing its defense businesses to its shareholders. Combined sales for Resistoflex/Defense, Unidynamics/Phoenix, and Crane defense operating units totaled some $77.6 million in 1989, representing about 5% of Crane's total sales of $1.5 billion.

Officials at the maker of engineered products for aerospace, construction and defense declined to elaborate.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Crane shares closed at $19.25, up 50 cents.
