Business Brief -- Kerr-McGee Corp.: Refining Unit Plans to Sell Southeastern Retail Property

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

Kerr-McGee Corp. said its refining unit will sell its retail properties in the Southeast, including 52 operating service stations, and concentrate its marketing efforts on retail properties in the Midwest and Texas. The Oklahoma City energy company said the 95 properties to be sold also include 31 closed service stations and 12 undeveloped sites in Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi and Tennessee. The company said it will continue its wholesale marketing operations in the Southeast.

The sale of the operating service stations in the Southeast will reduce the number of company-operated stations by half, a Kerr-McGee spokesman said. Another 1,700 stations are operated by lessees under the company brand.
