Allied Mutual Insurance Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

DES MOINES, Iowa -- Allied Group Inc. said it approved the buy-back of up to $1 million of the insurance holding company's common stock. Based on the stock's current $12.50-a-share over-the-counter price, the buy-back could involve up to 80,000 shares, or 2% of the company's stock outstanding. The program, expected to be finished within a year, depends on market conditions.

About 62% of Allied's stock is owned by its parent, Allied Mutual Insurance Co., also of Des Moines.
