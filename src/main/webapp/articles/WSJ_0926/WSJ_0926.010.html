French Jobless Rate Steady

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

PARIS -- France's unemployment rate held steady at 8.9% in August, according to seasonally adjusted data published by the Labor Ministry. The steady rate represents the third consecutive month in which unemployment has been at 8.9% despite a 0.7% drop in the number of job seekers in August to a seasonally adjusted 2,489,300 from 2,507,600 in July.
