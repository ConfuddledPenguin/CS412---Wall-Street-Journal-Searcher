International Brief -- Jardine Matheson Holdings Ltd.: After-Tax Profit in 1st Half Rose 26% to $123.3 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

Jardine Matheson Holdings Ltd. reported that after-tax profit in the first half spurted 26% to $123.3 million from $97.9 million a year earlier. The Hong Kong conglomerate, parent company of Jardine Matheson Group, said per-share earnings rose 27% to 24.48 cents from 19.25 cents.

Jardine Matheson directors proposed raising its interim dividend 17% to 3.15 cents from 2.69 cents.

The company attributed the unexpectedly brisk rise to the strength of its operating subsidiaries and several listed associates, as well as nonrecurring items.
