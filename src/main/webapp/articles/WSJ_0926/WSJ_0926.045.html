Dividend News: ICN Biomedicals

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

ICN BIOMEDICALS Inc. declared a special one-time dividend of 3.5 cents a share. The special dividend is in addition to the quarterly dividend of 3.5 cents a share. Both are payable Nov. 1 to stock of record Oct. 16. ICN Biomedicals, a supplier to the biotechnology research and clinical diagnostic marketplaces said it declared the special dividend "to recognize the continued growth and strong performance of the company." The Costa Mesa, Calif., company has 11.3 million common shares outstanding. For the first half, the company had net income of $4.7 million, or 42 cents a share, on sales of $68.3 million, compared with net income of $2.5 million, or 23 cents a share, on sales of $27.1 million a year earlier.
