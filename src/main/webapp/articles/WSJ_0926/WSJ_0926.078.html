Who's News: Bankers Trust New York

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

BANKERS TRUST NEW YORK (New York) -- C. Edward Midgely has been hired to head Bankers Trust's Corporate Finance Group formed in June. Mr. Midgely, 53 years old, was vice chairman of Fieldcrest Mills. Previously, he had been head of the corporate finance department of Kidder, Peabody Co., where he worked from 1962 to 1988. The Corporate Finance Group at Bankers Trust was formed to work in an strategic, advisory and financing basis with corporate managements a bank spokesman said.
