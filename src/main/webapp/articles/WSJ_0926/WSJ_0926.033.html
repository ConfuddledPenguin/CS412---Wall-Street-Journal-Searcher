BSN Officer Files Amended 13D

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

DALLAS -- BSN Corp., sporting-equipment maker, said its president, Michael Blumenfeld, filed an amended 13D report with the Securities and Exchange Commission reflecting recent purchases of 70,300 shares of BSN common stock at prices from $5.125 to $6.25 a share. The purchases raise Mr. Blumenfeld's interest in BSN to approximately 18.5% from 16% previously, he said.

Mr. Blumenfeld denied the purchase is related to recent actions by a Houston investment group, Pelham Investment Associates L.P. Pelham requested a list of BSN shareholders and said recently it increased its share in BSN to 7.99%.

The purchases were part of management plan to acquire as many as 500,000 BSN shares, Mr. Blumenfeld said.
