Unimin Canadian Subsidiary

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

TORONTO -- Falconbridge Ltd. said it sold its Indusmin industrial minerals division to the Canadian subsidiary of closely held Unimin Corp., New Canaan, Conn. Terms weren't disclosed.

Indusmin, which has 410 employees, had 1989 sales of 46 million Canadian dollars (U.S.$40 million).

Falconbridge is a base-metals producer that is jointly owned by Noranda Inc. of Toronto and Trelleborg AB of Sweden.
