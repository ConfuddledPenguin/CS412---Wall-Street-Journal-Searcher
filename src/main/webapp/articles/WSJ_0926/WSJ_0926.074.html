Martin Marietta Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

WASHINGTON -- Martin Marietta Corp. was given a $59.3 million Air Force contract for spare parts for aircraft navigation equipment.

General Dynamics Corp. won $29 million in Air Force contracts for space-launch vehicle support and aircraft software.

Raytheon Co. got a $25 million Navy contract for missile-guidance kits.

Southwest Mobile Systems Corp., a unit of Emerson Electric Co., received a $23.5 million Army contract for semitrailers.

Oregon Freeze Dry got a $10.5 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for dehydrated meat.
