Business Brief -- FileNet Corp.: Third-Quarter Loss Seen; Stock Price Plummets 43%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

Shares of FileNet Corp. plunged 43% after the Costa Mesa, Calif.-based maker of document image-processing equipment said it may report a small loss for the third quarter because of "a significant number of delayed decisions" by potential customers. "These recent delays appear to be directly related to the increasingly negative economic environment," Ted Smith, president and chief executive officer, said.

FileNet shares plummeted $3.875, to close at $5.125, in national over-the-counter trading yesterday on heavy volume of slightly more than one million shares.

In the 1989 third quarter, FileNet had a loss of $1.8 million, or 17 cents a share, on revenue of $18.6 million. For the current period, analysts had forecast profit of 14 cents to 20 cents a share, FileNet said. Revenue will be about $5 million less than the $27 million to $28 million analysts were predicting, the company said.
