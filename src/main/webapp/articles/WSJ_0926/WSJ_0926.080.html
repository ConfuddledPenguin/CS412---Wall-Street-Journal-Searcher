Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

PAPERS: Ad revenue of Times Mirror Co.'s newspaper publishing group rose less than 1% in the four weeks ended Sept. 2, to $121.7 million from $120.8 million for the corresponding period a year earlier. For the first 35 weeks of the year, ad revenue fell less than 1% to $1.11 billion from $1.12 billion. The cumulative results this year included 35 Sundays vs. 36 a year ago.

WHO'S NEWS: Marshall Turner was elected chairman and Daniel Brenner was re-elected vice chairman of the board at the Corporation for Public Broadcasting. Mr. Turner is a general partner at Taylor & Turner Associates Ltd. in San Francisco. Mr. Brenner is counsel to LeBoeuf, Lamb, Lieby & MacRae in Los Angeles.
