Hughes Aircraft Joins Samsung to Pursue South Korean Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

LOS ANGELES -- Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., and Samsung Group in Seoul, South Korea, said they have teamed up to pursue a contract to introduce domestic satellite communications to South Korea. The company said the South Korean government has said it wants to establish a domestic satellite system with data, voice and video communications and direct-broadcast television capabilities. South Korea is expected to request proposals in the spring of next year and to award a contract in early 1992.

Hughes is a large manufacturer of commercial communications satellites and a supplier of defense electronics. Samsung is a conglomerate with interests in electronics, telecommunications, precision instruments and chemicals.

Hughes said the work will be done through its Space & Communications Group and the Samsung Co. international division of the Samsung Group. Hughes also said its Hughes Network Systems subsidiary has signed an agreement making Samsung its representative in South Korea for sales of ground-station equipment for satellite systems.
