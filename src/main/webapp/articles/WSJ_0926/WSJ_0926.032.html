ABB Asea Brown Boveri

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

ZURICH -- ABB Asea Brown Boveri AG, the Swedish-Swiss heavy engineering group, said it signed a joint venture agreement with the Hungarian turbine manufacturer Lang Gyepgyar. ABB will own 75% of a new company, to be called ABB Lang, while Lang Gyepgyar will own 25%.

The company, which will begin operations in October 1990 and employ about 650 persons, will manufacture small and medium-sized turbines. ABB will contribute technology for steam turbines, as well as assist in structuring the management of the new company, ABB said.
