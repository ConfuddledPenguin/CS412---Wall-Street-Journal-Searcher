Western Union Extends Offers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

UPPER SADDLE RIVER, N.J. -- Western Union Corp., a financial services and communications company, said it extended to Oct. 9 its offer to swap new notes and common shares for two issues of senior notes. The exchange offer, which is an integral part of Western Union's recapitalization plan, had been scheduled to expire last Monday, when note holders had tendered $348.1 million of the $500 million of 19.25% notes outstanding and $1.4 million of the $30 million of 16% notes outstanding.
