Who's News: An Investment Bank, Fieldstone, Is Formed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

NEW YORK -- A group of former Bankers Trust Co. employees and Japan Leasing Corp. formed an investment bank that will specialize in financing projects and leases, assisting with private placements of securities and restructuring companies. The new company, Fieldstone Private Capital Group L.P., based here, includes the Tokyo-based commercial financing and leasing company, and 33 former employees of the Bankers Trust New York Corp. subsidiary.

Five senior partners at Fieldstone formerly were managing directors at Bankers Trust: Charles J. Hill, formerly the bank's co-head of merchant banking; Sharon K. Peterson and C. Wesley Reynolds, former co-heads of the lease and project finance group; Charles A. Quattrochi, former group head of the high-yield capital markets unit; and Gaby Abdelnour, who was managing director of merchant banking.

---

Corrections & Amplifications

FIELDSTONE Co., a consulting firm in New York specializing in personal computers and sales and marketing training for executives, is not related to Fieldstone Private Capital Group L.P., an investment bank specializing in lease financings and private placements of securities. A report of Fieldstone Private Capital's formation appeared in Wednesday's editions.

(WSJ Sept. 28, 1990)
