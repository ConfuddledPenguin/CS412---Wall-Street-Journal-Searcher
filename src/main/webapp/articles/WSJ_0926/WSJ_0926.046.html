Ultra Pac Stock Listing

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Ultra Pac Inc., a developer and marketer of recyclable packaging, said its common stock will begin trading tomorrow on the Nasdaq over-the-counter list, using the symbol UPAC. For the six months ended July 31, the company reported sales of $4.9 million and earnings of $295,817, or 15 cents a share.
