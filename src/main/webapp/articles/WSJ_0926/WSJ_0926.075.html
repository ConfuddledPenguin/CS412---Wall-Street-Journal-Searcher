Eastern Shore Consolidated

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

PEQUANNOCK, N.J. -- Eastern Shore Consolidated Industries Inc. said it extended its tender offer to buy 586,000 shares of MHP Machines Inc. common stock at 50 cents a share until Oct. 9. The previous offer was to expire today. As of noon yesterday, 92,500 shares had been tendered. MHP Machines of Cheektowaga, N.Y., makes computer controlled metal-cutting machining and turning centers. Eastern Shore Consolidated Industries was formed in July to make the offer.
