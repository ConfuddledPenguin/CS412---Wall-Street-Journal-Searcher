Business Brief -- Benetton Group S.p.A.: Apparel Company's Earnings Advanced 20% in First Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

Benetton Group S.p.A. said consolidated earnings in the first half rose 20% to 65.6 billion lire ($56.3 million) from 54.7 billion lire in the 1989 first half. The Italian casual wear manufacturer said revenue rose 25% to 1.046 trillion lire from 836.3 billion lire.

Benetton added that it expects consolidated revenue for all of 1990 to exceed 2 trillion lire. Revenue totaled 1.657 trillion lire in 1989. The company didn't give an earnings forecast.

Operating earnings advanced 13% to 138.2 billion lire from 122.3 billion lire, Benetton said.

The sharp increase in sales reflected, in part, a rapid expansion in East Asian markets, particularly in South Korea and Japan. Sales in the U.S. increased 5.7%, the company added.
