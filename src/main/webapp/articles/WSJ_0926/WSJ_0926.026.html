Chicago Mercantile Seat

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange was sold for $429,500, down $36,500 from the previous sale Sept. 19. Seats currently are quoted at $380,000 bid, $430,000 asked. The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set in March 1989.
