Japan's Economic Indicator

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

TOKYO -- The Japanese economy, underpinned by strong domestic demand, will continue to expand, a set of indicators released by the Economic Planning Agency shows. The leading indicator of the economy stood at 80 in July, the latest reporting month, comfortably above the so-called boom-or-bust line of 50 and rising from 61.5 in both June and May. July was the fifth consecutive month in which the leading indicator stayed above 50.
