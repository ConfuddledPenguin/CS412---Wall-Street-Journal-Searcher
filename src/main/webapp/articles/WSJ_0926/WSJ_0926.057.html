PWA Plans to Sell Stake in Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

CALGARY, Alberta -- PWA Corp. said it plans to sell its 50% stake in GPA Jetprop Ltd., an aircraft trading and leasing concern, to Ireland's GPA Group PLC, for proceeds of about 26 million Canadian dollars (US$22.6 million). PWA, a holding company that owns Canadian Airlines International Ltd., said the sale reflects its desire to sell assets that don't fit its long-term plans. GPA Group is a closely held aviation investment concern.

The sale is subject to approval by regulatory authorities in Ireland, PWA said.
