Business Brief -- Vons Cos.: Stake in Supermarket Chain Raised to 15.9% by Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

A group that includes Acadia Partners L.P. and Robert M. Bass Group Inc. said it raised its stake in Vons Cos. to 15.9% of common shares outstanding. Vons is a supermarket chain based in Arcadia, Calif. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it holds 6,163,648 Vons shares, including 476,300 shares purchased between Aug. 6 and Sept. 21 for $18.50 to $19.55 a share.

Vons closed yesterday in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange at $19.125 a share, down 37.5 cents.

A Vons spokeswoman didn't have any comment on the filing. The investors currently hold two seats on Vons' board. Acadia's managing general partner, Peter T. Joseph, has been a Vons director since 1987, and Vons gave Acadia and Bass Group a second seat after they bought a 7.5% stake in the company from Warburg, Pincus Associates L.P. last year.
