Inspiration Resources Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

NEW YORK -- Inspiration Resources Corp., a natural resources company, said it will have a $16.5 million non-cash charge in the third quarter related to the previously announced plan to close operations of an offshore gold mining vessel in Alaska, Western Gold Exploration & Mining Co. Inspiration holds a 50% interest in the venture. Though the charge reduced Inspiration's equity investment to zero, Western Gold's net assets at Sept. 30 are expected to total about $40 million.
