Cenvest Loan-Loss Provision

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/26/90

MERIDEN, Conn. -- Bank holding company Cenvest Inc. said it will make a special $5.4 million third-quarter provision for possible loan losses, a move stemming largely from the state's weak commercial real estate market. For the year-earlier third period, Cenvest posted a net loss of $1.5 million, or 39 cents a share. Cenvest shares didn't trade yesterday on the national over-the-counter market. They last closed at $2.375.
