Dividend News: New York Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

NEW YORK BANCORP Inc. directors declared an initial quarterly dividend of 25 cents a share. The payable and record dates are to be set if regulators clear the move by the holding company for Home Federal Savings Bank. New York Bancorp, which converted to stock ownership from mutual ownership in February 1988, had nearly 3.2 million shares outstanding at June 30, 1990.
