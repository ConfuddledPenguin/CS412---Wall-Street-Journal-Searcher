Who's News: Rouse Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/26/90

ROUSE Co. (Columbia, Md.) -- Mathias J. DeVito, chairman and chief executive officer of this real estate development firm, was named to the additional post of president, succeeding Michael D. Spear, who died in a plane crash in late August. Jeremiah E. Casey, 50-year-old chief executive-U.S.A. of Allied Irish Banks PLC and chairman of its First Maryland Bancorp unit, was elected a Rouse director, filling the vacancy created by Mr. Spear's death.
