Members Service Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

HOUSTON -- Members Service Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire First American Biltmore Securities Inc. of Phoenix, Ariz., from Shillelagh Ventures for an undisclosed amount of stock and cash. Members Service, with holdings in telecommunications, energy and financial services, said First American focuses on raising capital through private placements, initial public offerings and bonds.
