OPEC Members' Oil Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

LONDON -- Average wellhead crude-oil production by the 13 member nations of the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries edged up to 23.8 million barrels a day in March from 23.7 million barrels in February, an AP-Dow Jones survey of oil market participants indicated. The survey of integrated oil companies, independent refiners and oil industry consultants also suggests that OPEC members in March produced an average of 1.7 million barrels a day of natural gas liquids, which are light liquid hydrocarbons generally found with natural gas deposits; this was unchanged from February.

Average output of crude oil and natural gas liquids from OPEC member states in March totaled 25.5 million barrels a day, up from 25.4 million barrels in February, the survey indicated.
