U.K. Retail Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

LONDON -- U.K. retail sales volume fell a provisional 1.4% in March from February and was up 0.5% from March 1989, the U.K. Department of Trade and Industry said. In the three months ended in March, retail sales volume was up 0.75% from the previous three months and up 1.5% from a year earlier.
