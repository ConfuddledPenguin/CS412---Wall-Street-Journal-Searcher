International Brief -- Trusthouse Forte PLC: Hotel, Catering Concern's Annual Pretax Profit Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

Trusthouse Forte PLC of Britain, a hotel and catering concern, said its annual pretax profit rose 12%, helped by strong growth in both of its core businesses. Pretax earnings of #260 million ($426 million) for the year ended Jan. 31 compare with #232 million in the year through Oct. 31, 1988. Trusthouse Forte changed its accounting period last year. For the latest 15-month period, the company had pretax profit of #291 million.

Profit after taxes and minority interests, or profit attributable to shareholders, increased 18% to #203 million from #172 million, and totaled #225 million for the 15-month period. Per-share earnings rose 18% to 25.9 pence from 22 pence, and totaled 28.7 pence for the 15 months.

Sales increased 21% to #2.47 billion from #2.04 billion, and for the 15 months, totaled #2.98 billion.

The board recommended a final dividend of 7.16 pence a share, lifting the payout for the latest year to 9.66 pence, up from 8.4 pence in fiscal 1988. The total dividend for the 15 months was 10.66 pence.
