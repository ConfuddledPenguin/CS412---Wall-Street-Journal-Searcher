Who's News: Barnett Banks Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

BARNETT BANKS Inc. (Jacksonville, Fla.) -- Clarence V. McKee was named to the board of this bank holding company, filling a vacancy created by the retirement of W. Ashley Verlander, chairman of American Heritage Life Insurance Co. of Jacksonville. Mr. McKee is chairman, chief executive officer and co-owner of WTVT Holdings Inc., licensee of WTVT Channel 13, the CBS affiliate in Tampa, Fla.
