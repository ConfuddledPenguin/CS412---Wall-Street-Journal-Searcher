REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Communism = Fascism

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

Many scholars have noted that the fascism of Hitler's National Socialists resembled communism more than it did any form of conservatism. Now the Czechs have linked communism with fascism as a matter of law. Tomas Sokol, the Prague public prosecutor, threatens to outlaw the Communist Party under an existing statute prohibiting fascism. Mr. Sokol says communism and Nazism are movements aimed at the "destruction of all democratic rights" through "terror and social demagoguery." East Europeans understand these ideologies, having had to live under both.
