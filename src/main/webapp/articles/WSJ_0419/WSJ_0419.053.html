Who's News: CSX Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

CSX Corp. (Richmond, Va.) -- Charles E. Rice, chairman and chief executive officer of Barnett Banks Inc., Jacksonville, Fla., was elected to the board of this transportation company. Edward J. Boling, president emeritus and a professor of the University of Tennessee; and Charles K. Cross Sr., chairman, president and chief executive of Barnett Bank of Central Florida, both retired from the CSX board. The changes leave CSX with 12 directors.
