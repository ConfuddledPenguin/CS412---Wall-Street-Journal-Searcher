Who's News: Dow Jones & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

DOW JONES & Co. (New York) -- Irvine O. Hockaday Jr., 53 years old, president and chief executive officer of Hallmark Cards Inc., was elected a director, increasing board membership to 15. Dow Jones publishes The Wall Street Journal, Barron's magazine and other periodicals, electronic business information services and community newspapers. It also owns Telerate Inc., which provides computerized financial information on global markets.
