Who's News: New Hampshire Savings Bank Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

NEW HAMPSHIRE SAVINGS BANK Corp. (Concord, N.H.) -- The board of this bank accepted the resignation of David H. Rogers as president of the bank and director of affiliated banks and companies. No replacement was immediately named. In a statement, the company also said it elected Philip B. Emma senior vice president, chief financial officer and treasurer, succeeding John I. Snow, who is also leaving the company. Mr. Emma has served as vice president, chief accounting officer and controller since 1985.
