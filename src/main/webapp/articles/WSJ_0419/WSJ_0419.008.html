Control Data to Sell Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Control Data Corp. said it agreed to sell an 80% interest in VTC Inc., its chronically troubled computer chip unit, to Seattle Silicon Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. Control Data, which wrote off losses on the operation last year, said it will continue to buy certain integrated circuits from VTC.

Seattle Silicon, based in Bellevue, Wash., is closely held.
