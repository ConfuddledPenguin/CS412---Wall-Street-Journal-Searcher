Chemical Banking Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

NEW YORK -- Chemical Banking Corp. said it is seeking buyers for the U.S. and United Kingdom portions of its institutional fund management business. The businesses, which are comparatively small money managers, invest $5 billion in funds in fixed-income and equity funds through offices in Chicago, New York and London. Chemical said is keeping its Japanese fund manager, which handles $4 billion in funds; its Frankfurt, West Germany, unit; the fund manager of its Texas Commerce Bancshares unit, and its asset manager for wealthy individuals.

Chemical said it retained Berkshire Capital Corp. to assist in the sale.
