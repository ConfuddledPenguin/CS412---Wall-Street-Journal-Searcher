Business Brief -- Pic 'N' Save Corp.: Girard Partners Is Planning Proxy Battle for Control

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

A partnership controlled by David H. Batchelder, which holds a 6.6% stake in Pic 'N' Save Corp. and plans to make a bid to acquire the company, said it intends to solicit proxies to gain a majority on the board. The plan, which was disclosed in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, appears to be tactical shift by Girard Partners Limited Partnership. Girard had previously said it planned to obtain control of the board through the written-consents process.

Last week, the La Jolla, Calif.-based partnership formally asked Pic 'N' Save to adopt a resolution giving Girard control of the board. The resolution would cut the size of the board to five members, including three Girard appointees. The board currently has seven members. Now the partnership said it plans to solicit proxies to elect its nominees at Pic 'N' Save's 1990 annual meeting.

The company hasn't set a date for the meeting, a spokesman said. He declined to comment on the filing. Last year, Pic 'N' Save's annual meeting was held on May 18. Based in Dominguez, Calif., Pic 'N' Save is a retail chain that buys manufactuers' close-out merchandise and sells it at deep discounts to customers.
