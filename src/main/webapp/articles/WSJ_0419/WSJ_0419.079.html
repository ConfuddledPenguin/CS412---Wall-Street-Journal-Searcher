Treasury Plans to Sell $10.5 Billion in Notes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $675 million in new cash with the sale Wednesday of about $10.5 billion in two-year notes to redeem $9.83 billion in maturing notes. The offering will be mature on April 30, 1992.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $5,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Wednesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
