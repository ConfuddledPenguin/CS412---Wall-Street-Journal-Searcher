Business Brief -- TSB Group PLC: Hill Samuel Unit Acquires Atlanta Capital Management

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

Hill Samuel Investment Management Group Ltd., a unit of Britain's TSB Group PLC, said it has acquired closely held Atlanta Capital Management Co. in the U.S. The purchase price wasn't disclosed. The acquisition, which includes an agreement allowing its senior executives to stay with the company, represents Hill Samuel's second fund-management operation in the U.S. and brings the unit's total funds under management to $10.2 billion.

Hill Samuel acquired a Minneapolis fund manager in 1986, a company that currently has $7.6 billion of funds under management. Atlanta Capital's managed funds total $1.6 billion currently. Hill Samuel's London operation also controls an additional $1 billion in managed funds.
