Conversion Industries Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Conversion Industries Inc., an independent power-production company, said it agreed to build a $50 million oil-recovery facility and cogeneration power plant in Kern County, Calif., in a joint venture with Energy Reserve Inc. of Phoenix, Ariz. At full production, the oil-recovery facility is expected to produce about 2,500 barrels of oil a day, Conversion said. Project construction is expected to begin next month. All electric power will be sold to San Francisco-based Pacific Gas & Electric Co.
