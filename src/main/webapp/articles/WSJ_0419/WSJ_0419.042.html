Who's News: D&N Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

D&N FINANCIAL Corp. (Hancock, Mich.) -- George J. Butvilas, 44 years old, previously executive vice president of Boulevard Bancorp, Chicago, and chairman and chief executive officer of Boulevard's Citizens National Bank of Downers Grove, Ill., was named president and chief operating officer of this thrift holding company and of D&N Savings Bank, its principal subsidiary, succeeding Filmore J. Halonen, who previously announced plans to retire on May 31 but who will remain a director.
