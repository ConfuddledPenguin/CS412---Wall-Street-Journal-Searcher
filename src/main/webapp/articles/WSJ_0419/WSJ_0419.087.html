NEW YORK -- United Telecommunications Inc.'s US Sprint Communications Co. said it will provide basic and enhanced 800 service for the Internal Revenue Service Taxpayer Assistaram. The company estimates the value of the services over the 10-year life of the contract at more than $450 million, which will result in the largest service order to date under FTS-2000, the government's new telecommunications service.
