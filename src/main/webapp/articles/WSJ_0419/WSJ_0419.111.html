Business Brief -- MCI Communications Corp.: First-Quarter Profit Rose 21% on 20% Revenue Gain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

MCI Communications Corp., Washington, reported first-quarter net income increased 21% with strong growth in revenue and long-distance telephone traffic volume. The long-distance carrier said net income grew to $163 million, or 62 cents a share, from $135 million, or 51 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter.

Revenue increased 20% to $1.81 billion from $1.5 billion.

Analysts said results were consistent with their predictions. Joel Gross of Donaldson Lufkin & Jenrette said MCI "is gaining market share and will continue to do so." Jack Grubman of PaineWebber said results were "fine," but the stock isn't going up because MCI "is getting penalized for doing the right thing." He said the company is investing for the long term in marketing, sales efforts, equipment and network improvements.

In national over-the-counter trading, MCI stock closed at $35.125, down $1.625.
