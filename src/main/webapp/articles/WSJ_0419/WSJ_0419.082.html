WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Great Western Financial Corp., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.

K-Swiss Inc., proposed initial offering of 1.45 million Class A common shares, of which 1,115,000 will be sold by the company and 335,000 will be sold by holders, via Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Orbot Systems Ltd., proposed initial offering of three million ordinary shares, via Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc., Alex. Brown & Sons Inc., and Shearson Lehman Hutton International Inc.
