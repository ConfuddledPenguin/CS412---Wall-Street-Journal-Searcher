Business Brief -- Peugeot S.A.: French Auto Maker Posts 16% Rise in Profit for 1989

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

Peugeot S.A., a French auto maker, said consolidated profit grew 16% to 10.3 billion francs ($1.83 billion) in 1989 from 8.85 billion francs a year earlier. The result slightly exceeded analysts' previous estimates of about 10 billion francs.

The earnings advance came despite a strike at two assembly plants of Automobiles Peugeot S.A. during September and October that pared about 60,000 autos from 1989 production and reduced consolidated revenue by about three billion francs, according to company estimates.

Revenue rose 10% to 152.96 billion francs last year from 138.45 billion francs in 1988.

The company said its directors proposed a net annual dividend of 16 francs a share. The proposed payout compares with the 1988 dividend of 28 francs, which was made prior to July's 2-for-1 stock split.
