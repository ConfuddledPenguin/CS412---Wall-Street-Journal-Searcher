Who's News: Action Auto Rental Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

ACTION AUTO RENTAL Inc. (Cleveland) -- Craig E. Hons, president, was named chief executive officer of this insurance replacement auto rental concern, succeeding Russell Smith, who remains chairman. Mr. Hons and James A. Terwood and James L. Schiering, executive vice presidents, have each purchased 50,000 shares of the company's common stock from Mr. Smith in private transactions at $10 a share.
