Who's News: Conagra Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

CONAGRA Inc. (Omaha, Neb.) -- James R. Tindall, 44 years old, previously senior vice president and general manager, North American Foods, at Quaker Oats Co., was named president and chief operating officer of this diversified food company's Prepared Foods Cos. unit, succeeding Philip B. Fletcher, 56, who remains ConAgra president and chief operating officer. Quaker named Verinder K. Syal, 41, previously president of its Golden Grain division, to succeed Mr. Tindall at North American Foods.
