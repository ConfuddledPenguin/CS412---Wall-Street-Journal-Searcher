Who's News: Wertheim Schroder & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

WERTHEIM SCHRODER & Co. (New York) -- Peter Young, 37 years old, formerly vice president, world-wide chemical mergers and acquisitions, at the Salomon Brothers Inc. unit of Salomon Inc., was named managing director, chemical sector, a new post at this international investment banking firm.
