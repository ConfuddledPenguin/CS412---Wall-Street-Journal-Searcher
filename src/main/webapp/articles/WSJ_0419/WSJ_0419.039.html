Who's News: Diana Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

DIANA Corp. (Milwaukee) -- Robert L. Pagel, formerly president of Fan Fair Corp., was named president of Diana, a holding company with interests in retail and food-service industries. Mr. Pagel, 53 years old, succeeds Richard Fisher, who continues as chairman. The company said that Messrs. Pagel and Fisher will share the duties of chief executive officer.
