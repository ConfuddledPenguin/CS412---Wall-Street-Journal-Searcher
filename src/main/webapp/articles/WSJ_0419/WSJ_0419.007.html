Agnico-Eagle Mines Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

TORONTO -- Agnico-Eagle Mines Ltd., a gold mining company, said it plans to raise about 40 million Canadian dollars (US$34.3 million) through an offering in Canada of common shares and share-purchase warrants, with proceeds to be used for exploration and mine development. The mix of shares and warrants hasn't been determined, Agnico said. The offering will be underwritten by a group headed by RBC Dominion Securities Inc.
