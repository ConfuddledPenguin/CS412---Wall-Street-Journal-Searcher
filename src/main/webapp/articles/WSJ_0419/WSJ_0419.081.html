International Technology Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

TORRANCE, Calif. -- A joint venture formed by International Technology Corp. and the Davy McKee Corp. unit of London-based Davy Corp. was awarded two contracts, valued at $89.9 million, by the Texas Water Commission to clean up a waste-disposal site in Crosby, Texas, the companies said. The contracts will be funded by the state and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. Terms specify that the work be completed within six years, said International Technology, which has a 77.5% interest in the venture; Davy McKee has 22.5%.
