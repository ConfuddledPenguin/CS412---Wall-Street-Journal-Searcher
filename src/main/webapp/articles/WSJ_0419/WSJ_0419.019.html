OHM to Post Quarterly Net

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

FINDLAY, Ohio -- OHM Corp., an environmental services concern, said it expects to post first-quarter net income of five to seven cents a share, compared with a loss of 13 cents a share a year earlier, on revenue of $47 million, an indicated 43% rise from $32.8 million. The company attributed the better-than-expected results to strength in its Remediation Services Group, including increased revenue from industrial clients and federal and state government contracts.
