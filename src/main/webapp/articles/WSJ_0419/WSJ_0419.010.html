Magma Gets Morgan Request

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

SAN DIEGO -- Magma Power Co., which develops and sells geothermal resources used to generate electric power, said it received a request from J.P. Morgan & Co., a New York bank holding company, to exercise a warrant convertible into one million Magma common shares. Pursuant to the request, Magma said it plans to file a statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission to register the shares. After the registration takes effect, J.P. Morgan intends to exercise the warrant, said Magma, which has about 22.1 million common shares outstanding.
