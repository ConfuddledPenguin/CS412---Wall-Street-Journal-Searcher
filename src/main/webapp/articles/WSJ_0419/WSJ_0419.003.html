Who's News: FNW Bancorp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

FNW BANCORP. (Chicago) -- Harold R. Pehlke, 50-year-old president of this bank holding company, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer, and Richard L. Joutras, 66, an outside director, was named chairman. They succeed Wilber G. Smith, 62, who retired from the two posts.
