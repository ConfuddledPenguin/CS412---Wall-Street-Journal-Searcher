Who's News: Colonial Gas Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

COLONIAL GAS Co. (Lowell, Mass.) -- Charles O. Swanson II, 58-year-old executive vice president of this gas utility, was named president, effective June 30, to succeed Eugene P. Hart, 65 years old, who will retire as president and chief operating officer but will remain a director. A successor in the operating chief's post wasn't named.
