Gambro Extends Offer for Cobe

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

LUND, Sweden -- Gambro AB said it extended the expiration date of its $37-a-share, or $253 million, tender offer for Cobe Laboratories Inc. until May 31. The offer was to have expired yesterday. Gambro, a medical equipment producer, said it needs more time to comply with Federal Trade Commission requests for information under antitrust regulations. Gambro said 1,208,719 shares of Cobe, a medical equipment company, had been tendered as of Tuesday.
