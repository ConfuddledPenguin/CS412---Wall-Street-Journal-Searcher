U.K. Industrial Production

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

LONDON -- U.K. industrial production fell 0.7% in February from January and was up 0.3% from February 1989, according to provisional data released by the U.K. Central Statistical Office. Output in the energy sector, which can vary greatly with swings in the oil market, fell 1.4% in February from January and was down 0.7% from a year earlier.
