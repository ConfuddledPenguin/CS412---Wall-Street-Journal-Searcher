Who's News: Interco Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/19/90

INTERCO Inc. (St. Louis) -- Richard B. Loynd, 61-year-old president and chief executive officer of this maker of furniture and shoes, was named to the additional post of chairman, effective June 1, succeeding Harvey Saligman, 51, who previously announced he will step down from the position but will remain a director.
