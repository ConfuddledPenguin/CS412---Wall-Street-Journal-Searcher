Business Brief -- McClatchy Newspapers Inc.: Newspaper Chain's Earnings Plunged 51% in 1st Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/19/90

SACRAMENTO, Calif. -- McClatchy Newspapers Inc. said a sharp drop in advertising linage in March helped drive down first-quarter earnings by 51% to $2.4 million, or eight cents a share. The newspaper chain earned $4.85 million, or 17 cents a share, the year earlier. Revenue grew 9% to $95.1 million from $87 million. McClatchy added that results were hurt by startup costs associated with a newsprint mill partnership, and costs associated with the recent acquisition of six newspapers in South Carolina.
