Business Brief -- Exploration Co. of Louisiana: Purchase of Further Interest In Cox Field Area Is Slated

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

Exploration Co. of Louisiana Inc., Lafayette, La., said it agreed to buy additional working interest in the Berry R. Cox Field area in south Texas for $40 million, a purchase that will make it the field's operator. The company said the area, still in the early development stage, has nine producing wells and proven reserves of approximately 200 billion cubic feet of gas. The working interests in the producing wells vary from 28% to 49% and include proven reserves of about 60 billion cubic feet.

To pay for the acquisition, Exploration said it would begin a rights issue of 600,000 units, each comprising one share of preferred stock and 10 warrants. The rights issue is expected to raise about $50 million, the balance of which will be used to reduce bank debt and provide additional working capital.

Exploration Co. is an oil and gas exploration and production company.
