Republic Waste Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

HOUSTON -- Republic Waste Industries Inc. said it agreed in principle to acquire GI Industries. The value of the transaction will be about $15 million, based on an exchange of GI Industries' stock for Republic Waste common shares. Republic Waste specializes in the collection, processing and disposal of solid waste. GI Industries is a rubbish collection company in Southern California.
