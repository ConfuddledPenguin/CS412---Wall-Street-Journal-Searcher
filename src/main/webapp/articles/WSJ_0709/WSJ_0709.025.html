Norway's Offshore Crude Oil

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

STAVANGER, Norway -- Crude oil production from Norway's offshore fields averaged 1,654,000 barrels a day in May, up from 1,630,000 in April and 1,500,000 a year earlier, the Norwegian Petroleum Directorate said. Total petroleum production in May was 8.8 million tons of oil equivalent, up from 8.5 million tons in April. May production was divided into 6.8 million tons of oil, 1.8 million tons of oil equivalent of natural gas, and 200,000 tons of oil equivalent of condensate.

The country's North Sea fields produced 44.7 million tons of oil equivalent in the first five months of this year, compared with 43.2 million tons a year earlier.
