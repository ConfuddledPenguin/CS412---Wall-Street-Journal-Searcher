Riedel Expects to Post Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Riedel Environmental Technologies Inc., citing delays in Environmental Protection Agency orders and other environmental remediation work, said it expects to report a net loss in its second quarter. In the year-earlier quarter, the company earned $563,000, or 16 cents a share, on $19.1 million in revenue. The company also announced that it received approval from Seattle to proceed in a joint venture for a 10-year, $42 million sludge management services contract. Riedel's stock closed Friday at $12.25 a share, down 12.5 cents, in American Stock Exchange composite trading.
