American Airlines' June Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- American Airlines said its June traffic dipped 0.3% from June 1989. The airline unit of AMR Corp. said it flew 6.86 billion revenue passenger miles last month, compared with 6.88 billion miles a year ago. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. Load factor, or percentage of seats filled, also fell, to 67.4% from 70.8%.

For the six months, American said it flew 36.91 billion revenue passenger miles, up 5% from 35.18 billion miles a year earlier. Load factor fell to 61.9% from 62.7%.
