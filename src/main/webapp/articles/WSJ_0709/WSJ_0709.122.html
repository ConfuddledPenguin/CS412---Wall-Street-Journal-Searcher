Business Brief -- Ingersoll-Rand Co.: Schlage Electronics Unit May Be Put Up for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

Ingersoll-Rand Co., Woodcliff Lake, N.J., is considering the possible sale of its Schlage Electronics Inc. unit, a maker of security systems. A spokesman for Ingersoll-Rand, which also makes products including equipment for road construction and well and rock drilling, was unable to provide annual sales for Schlage, Santa Clara, Calif.

The company retained Merrill Lynch Capital Markets to evaluate alternatives for Schlage. Ingersoll-Rand's spokesman couldn't say why Ingersoll-Rand took that step for the security-systems unit, which is profitable. But he did say the business is a smaller part of the locks unit of the parent's bearings, locks and tools segment. Schlage has 300 employees. The parent had 31,600 employees at the end of 1989.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Ingersoll-Rand shares closed at $58.125, up 37.5 cents.
