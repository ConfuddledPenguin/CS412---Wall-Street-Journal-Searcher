WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Acclaim Entertainment Inc., offering of two million common shares, via Salomon Brothers Inc. and Bear, Stearns & Co.

Itel Corp., offering of 16.7 million common shares of Itel, via First Boston Corp., Lazard Freres & Co., Salomon Brothers, Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd., Lazard Brothers & Co., and Salomon Brothers International Ltd.

Noble Drilling Corp., proposed offering of five million common shares, of which four million are being offered by holders, via Salomon Brothers and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Office Club Inc., offering of 1.3 million common shares, via Salomon Brothers and William Blair & Co.

Corrections & Amplifications

NOBLE DRILLING Corp. filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 10,350,000 common shares -- five million for itself and four million for shareholders. The remaining shares cover overallotment agreements with underwriters. Monday's edition misstated the size of the offering.

(WSJ July 11, 1990)
