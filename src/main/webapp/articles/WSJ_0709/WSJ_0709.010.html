Retail Sales in West Germany

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Retail sales in West Germany in May rose a real, or price-adjusted, 8.9% from a year earlier, the Federal Statistics Office said. On a nominal, or unadjusted, basis, sales rose 11%, the first double-digit rise in nominal sales volumes at West German retailers since the 13% increase in March 1988, when real sales growth was 7%.
