New Stock Listings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange and the National Market System of the over-the-counter market each announced one new stock listing.

Policy Management Systems Corp., a Columbia, S.C., supplier of standardized software systems to the insurance industry, begins Big Board trading tomorrow under the symbol PMS. It previously traded over the counter.

Wet Seal Inc., a specialty retailer of women's apparel, based in Irvine, Calif., began trading over the counter last week under WTSLA.
