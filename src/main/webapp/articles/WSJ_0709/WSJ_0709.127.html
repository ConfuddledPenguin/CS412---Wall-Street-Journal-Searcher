Switzerland's Inflation Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

BERN -- Switzerland's cost-of-living index in June inched up 0.1% from May and rose 5% from a year earlier, the government said, noting that more favorable oil and energy prices helped slow the pace of inflation. The increases represented a slowdown from May's rises of 0.5% from the preceding month and 5.1% from a year earlier.
