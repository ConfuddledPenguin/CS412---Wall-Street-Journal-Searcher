Business Brief -- B.F. Goodrich Co.: Carlisle Cos. Agrees to Buy Off-Highway Braking Line

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

B.F. Goodrich Co. said it agreed to sell its Off-Highway Braking Systems business to Carlisle Cos. Terms weren't disclosed. The business, based in Bloomington, Ind., makes brakes and hydraulic controls used on construction, mining and other off-highway vehicles. It had sales of about $47 million in 1989.

Goodrich, an Akron, Ohio, maker of plastics, specialty chemicals and aircraft parts, said the divestiture reflects its belief that the business would be a better fit "within a company specializing in groundtransportation equipment." Carlisle, of Syracuse, N.Y., makes products for automotive, industrial and construction markets.

Goodrich said the sale is subject to approval by appropriate governmental agencies.
