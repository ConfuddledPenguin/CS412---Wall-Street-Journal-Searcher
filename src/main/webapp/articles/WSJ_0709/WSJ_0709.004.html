Heinz Votes on Takeover Law

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

PITTSBURGH -- H.J. Heinz Co. said its directors voted to exclude the food company from all provisions of Pennsylvania's new anti-takeover law. The legislation, signed into law April 27, allows publicly owned concerns incorporated in the state to exclude themselves from all or part of its anti-takeover provisions within 90 days of passage.
