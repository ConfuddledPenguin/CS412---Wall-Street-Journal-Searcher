Martin Marietta Wins Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

WASHINGTON -- Martin Marietta Corp. received a $326.8 million Air Force contract for modifications to space launch facilities at Cape Canaveral, Fla.

Lockheed Corp. got an $82.4 million Air Force contract for the launch complex at Vandenberg Air Force Base, Calif.

Grumman Corp. was awarded a $54.2 million Navy contract for F-14 aircraft.

Harris Corp. received a $33.6 million Air Force contract for electronics equipment.
