New Jersey Resources Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

WALL, N.J. -- New Jersey Resources Corp., a utility, said Gerald W. Conway, 52-year-old vice chairman, president and chief operating officer, resigned, effective July 31, to return to private law practice. A spokesman wouldn't comment on plans for a successor. Mr. Conway has been a director of the company and its subsidiary, New Jersey Natural Gas Co., since 1977, and chairman of New Jersey Natural Gas since 1988. Previously, he was the unit's outside legal counsel.
