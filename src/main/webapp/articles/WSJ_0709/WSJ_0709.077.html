Business Brief -- Sun Sportswear Inc.: Second-Quarter Sales Fell Up to 12%; Weather Cited

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

Sun Sportswear Inc., Kent, Wash., one of the nation's largest screen-printers of casual sportswear sold in discount retail stores, said cool spring weather caused second-quarter sales to drop between 9% and 12% to an estimated $22 million to $23 million from $25.1 million a year earlier. Officials attributed the poor performance to slowing orders and an inventory buildup. Earnings and sales data won't be available until the end of the month, a spokeswoman said. But, reflecting the sales slump, Sun Sportswear shares plunged $1.25, to close at $8.25, in national over-the-counter trading Friday.
