Business Brief -- Xicor Inc.: Loss of $4.8 Million Posted For Fiscal Second Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

Xicor Inc., Milpitas, Calif., reported a loss of $4.8 million, or 26 cents a share, for the second quarter ending June 17. The company had net income of $2.2 million, or 12 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter. Citing failure to meet some of its orders, the company said net sales were down to $20.4 million from $22 million a year ago.

For the first half of fiscal 1990, the manufacturer of semiconductors and related devices reported a loss of $11.8 million, or 65 cents a share. In the year-earlier first half, the company had net income of $5.2 million, or 28 cents a share.

Company President Raphael Klein said the concern was unable to meet orders because of manufacturing difficulties that have plagued it for several quarters. The company has recently completed a $50 million factory renovation.
