  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

PUBLISHING: Britain's EMAP PLC is taking part in the #17.2 million ($30.7 million) acquisition of Le Chasseur Francais, a French life-style magazine. The monthly will be purchased jointly by EMAP and French publishing group Bayard Presse from rival French publisher Dido Bottin. Both will contribute #2.7 million, with the rest financed through French bank loans.
