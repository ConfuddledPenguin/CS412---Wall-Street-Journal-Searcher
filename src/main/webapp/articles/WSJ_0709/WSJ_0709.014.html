Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 280,137 metric tons in the week ended June 30, down 2.6% from 287,537 tons in the preceding week and down 0.5% from 281,421 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton equals about 2,204.6 pounds. For the year through June 30, production totaled 7,266,572 metric tons, down 8.8% from 7,971,417 tons a year earlier.
