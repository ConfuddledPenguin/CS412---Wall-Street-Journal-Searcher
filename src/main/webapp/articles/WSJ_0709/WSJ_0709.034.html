Economic Summit (A Special Report): By the Numbers --- Snapshot of the Seven: A Statistical Portrait of the Summit Countries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

BY ANY MEASURE, the seven summit countries carry enormous economic clout. Together, they account for about 55% of world gross national product, 54% of world exports and 55% of world imports. Income per capita in the seven countries ranges from $12,810 to $21,020. Here's a closer look at how the seven stack up. --- Per-Capita GNP
