Economic Summit (A Special Report): Postscript --- Loose Lips

  • Author: Philip Revzin
  • Date: 07/09/90

SO MUCH TO talk about. And so little nerve. The contentious issues seem endless. A unified Germany. A unified Europe. The end of the U.S.-British special relationship. U.S.-Japanese trade squabbles. EC trade squabbles with just about everybody. At the end of their meeting, the summit leaders will issue a communique saying everything is just splendid. But before they do, imagine some of the private conversations -- even if they're probably too candid ever to occur. (See accompanying comic strip -- WSJ July 9, 1990)
