Lufthansa Signs Planes Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Deutsche Lufthansa AG said it signed formal documents for the previously announced order of 20 medium-haul A-321 aircraft from the European aerospace consortium Airbus Industrie. The companies agreed on the order in August 1989, with Lufthansa's supervisory board approving it last September.

The 51% government-owned Lufthansa announced in March an order for engines to power these aircraft from a consortium headed by the Pratt & Whitney engines subsidiary of United Technologies Corp.
