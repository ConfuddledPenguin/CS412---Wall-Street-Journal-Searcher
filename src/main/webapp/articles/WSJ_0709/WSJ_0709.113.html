International Brief -- Bond Corp.: Firm Modifies Plan to Sell Breweries to Bell Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd. said it made further changes in an agreement to sell its Australian breweries to associate company Bell Resources Ltd. Australia-based Bond Corp. has extended a deadline for completing the sale of the Australian breweries to July 31 from June 30.

Bond Corp. said in March that it intended to sell the Australian breweries to 58%-owned Bell Resources for 1.85 billion Australian dollars (US$1.48 billion), as part of a program to reduce debt by selling assets. Bond Corp. owes Bell Resources about A$1 billion, which has been turned into a deposit for the breweries.

However, Bond Corp. said in a statement late Friday that if the sale isn't completed by July 31 it will be required to repay the deposit on Aug. 17.

Terms of the purchase price also have been altered, Bond Corp. said, to include certain brand names in Britain.

Bond Corp. also said it has altered terms of a previously announced agreement to sell some of its stake in Bond Media Ltd. to Bell Resources.
