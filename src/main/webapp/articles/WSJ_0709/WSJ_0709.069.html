Forstmann Begins Tender Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/09/90

NEW YORK -- Forstmann Little & Co. began its tender offer for all shares outstanding of General Instrument Corp. at $44.50 each, or a total of $1.42 billion. On Friday, in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, General Instrument's common closed at $45.625, up 12.5 cents. Counting options that would be converted in the purchase, the maker of cable-television equipment has more than 32 million shares outstanding.

Forstmann's bid, scheduled to expire at 12:01 a.m. EDT Aug.14, unless extended, is conditional, among other things, on at least a majority of the shares outstanding, on a fully diluted basis, being tendered.
