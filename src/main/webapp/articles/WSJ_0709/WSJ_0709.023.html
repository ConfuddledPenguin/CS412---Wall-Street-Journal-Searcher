British Gas Signs Agreement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/09/90

LONDON -- British Gas PLC said it has signed an agreement to buy natural gas from the Welland Fields. The fields' owners are Atlantic Richfield Co., the fields' operator, with 25%; Mobil Corp.'s Mobil North Sea Ltd. subsidiary, 30%; Gas Council (Exploration), 20%; British Petroleum Co., 12.5%, and Occidental Petroleum Co., 12.5%.

In accordance with a 1988 report on contract gas prices by the Monopolies and Mergers Commission, British Gas will buy only 90% of the gas produced. The remaining 10% will be sold to competitors of British Gas. The Welland Fields' first gas will flow in October.
