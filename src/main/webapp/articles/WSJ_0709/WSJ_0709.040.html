Chapter and Worse

Everything that is written

Has been written before;

There are slight variations,

Hardly anything more.

Close analysis shows

There is nothing to it --

Unless, of course,

You're trying to do it.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

Conveniently Deaf

When parents warn

Of snares and sin

The ear teen-agers

Turn is tin.

-- Bert H. Kruse.
