Hub City Inc. Recapitalization

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

PITTSBURGH -- Westinghouse Credit Corp. said it provided $31 million to recapitalize Hub City Inc., Aberdeen, S.D., a maker of power transmission parts. The refinancing includes $21 million in senior and senior subordinated term loans and a $10 million revolving line of credit. Hub City was acquired in a management-led leveraged buy-out in 1989. Westinghouse Credit is a unit of Westinghouse Electric Corp., a broadcast, financing and electrical concern.
