Geodyne Acquires Properties

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Geodyne Resources Inc. said it agreed to purchase for $30 million interests in producing oil and gas properties from Enron Oil & Gas Co. of Houston. The properties were purchased on behalf of the partnerships Geodyne sponsors through its PaineWebber/Geodyne Energy Income Programs. The acquisition includes working interests in approximately 59 wells located in Texas, New Mexico and Oklahoma. The properties' proven reserves are about 95% gas and 5% oil.
