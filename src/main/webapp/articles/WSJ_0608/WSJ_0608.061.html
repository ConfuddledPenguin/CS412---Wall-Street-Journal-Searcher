Calgon Carbon Sets Labor Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

PITTSBURGH -- Calgon Carbon Corp. and AFL-CIO Local Union 7047 reached an agreement on a three-year labor contract covering 250 members of AFL-CIO Local Union 7047 employed at Calgon's Catlettsburg facility. Calgon produces carbon for various uses, including purification and filtration.

The agreement provides wage and benefit increases over its term and will expire on June 6, 1993. Details of the agreement weren't disclosed.
