  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Bristol Research Corp., offering of 1.2 million units, each unit consisting of five common shares and five redeemable warrants to purchase five common shares, via F.N. Wolf & Co.

Burlington Resources Inc., proposed offering of $300 million of debt securities, via Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.

Healthsouth Rehabilitation Corp., offering of 2.2 million common shares, of which two million will be sold by Healthsouth and 200,000 shares by Richard M. Scrushy, Healthsouth's chairman, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Meca Software Inc., initial offering of 1.5 million common shares, of which one million will be sold by the company, and 500,000 will be sold by holders, via Needham & Co., Hambrecht & Quist Inc. and Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc.

National Rural Utilities Cooperative Finance Corp., shelf offering of up to $500 million of collateral trust bonds, via Shearson Lehman.

Tyson Foods Inc., proposed public offering of 2.6 million Class A common shares, via Stephens Inc. and A.G. Edwards & Sons Inc.
