Valid 8% Stake Sold to IBM

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Valid Logic Systems Inc., which produces software that helps engineers design electronics parts, said it completed the sale of $11.2 million in stock to International Business Machines Corp., Armonk, N.Y. IBM bought 2.7 million shares of Valid voting convertible preferred for $4.20 each, giving it an 8% stake, Valid said.
