Who's News: Structural Dynamics Research Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

STRUCTURAL DYNAMICS RESEARCH Corp. (Milford, Ohio) -- Ronald H. Hoffman, previously vice president, finance and operations, global businesses, at Baxter International Inc., was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this engineering software and engineering services concern, succeeding William P. Sarther, who resigned for personal reasons in March.
