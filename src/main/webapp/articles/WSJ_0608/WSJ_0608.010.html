Who's News: Morrison Knudsen Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

MORRISON KNUDSEN Corp. (Boise, Idaho) -- John J. Whalen, 56 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of the MK International Group unit of this engineering and construction concern. Mr. Whalen, formerly an MK International senior vice president, succeeds Walter C. Bell, 55, who retired last Friday.
