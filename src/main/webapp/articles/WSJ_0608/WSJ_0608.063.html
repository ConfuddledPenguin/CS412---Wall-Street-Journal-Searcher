Hyundai Unit Gets Libya Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- The Hyundai Construction Co. unit of the Hyundai Group said it received a $133 million contract to build a petrochemical plant for Libya's state-run Raslanuf Oil & Gas Processing Co. The plant, with a capability of producing 160,000 tons of polyethylene a year, will be built in Raslanuf, 500 miles east of Tripoli. Hyundai didn't say when the construction is scheduled to start and be completed.
