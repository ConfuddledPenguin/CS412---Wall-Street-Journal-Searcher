Financing Business: HQ Office Supplies Warehouse Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

HQ OFFICE SUPPLIES WAREHOUSE Inc. said it began an offering of 10,000 units priced at $1,000 each. Each unit entitles the holder to $1,000 principal amount of a convertible debenture, due June 1, 1995, and 200 Class C warrants. Each warrant entitles the holder to buy one Class A common share for $6. The office products retailer, based in Long Beach, Calif., said it expects net proceeds of about $8.6 million from the offering, with a portion to be used for expansion in the Southern California market.
