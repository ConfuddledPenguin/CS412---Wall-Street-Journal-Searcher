Financing Business: Burlington Resources Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

BURLINGTON RESOURCES Inc., Seattle, said it registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a public offering of $300 million principal amount of debt securities, including $150 million in notes, due 2000, and $150 million in debentures, due 2010. The energy, forest products and real estate concern said net proceeds from the offering will go toward working capital and general corporate purposes, including acquisition of gas and oil properties, repayment of short-term debt, and capital expenditures. The underwriter will be Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.
