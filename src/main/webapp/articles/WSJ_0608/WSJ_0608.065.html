Swiss Domestic Output Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

BERN -- Switzerland's real gross domestic product rose 3.2% in the first quarter from a year earlier, the government economic research agency reported. The year-to-year rate of 3.2% rose slightly from the 3% pace in the fourth quarter of last year.

On an adjusted basis, the annualized rate of GDP growth in the first quarter was 1.9%, down from 3.6% growth in the fourth quarter.

Meanwhile, a measure of inflation rose 5.1% year to year in the first quarter, up from 4.7% in the fourth quarter.
