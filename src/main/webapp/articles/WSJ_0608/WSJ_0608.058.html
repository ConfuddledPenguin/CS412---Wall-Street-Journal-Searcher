WILLIS FABER PLC of Britain will merge with New York-based Corroon & Black Corp. in a stock swap valued at $712.3 million. Tuesday's edition described the transaction as an acquisition of Corroon by Willis Faber. (See: "Faber Agrees To Buy Corroon Via Stock Swap" -- WSJ June 5, 1990)

---

TEXAS AIR Corp. will change its name to Continental Airlines Holdings Inc. effective Monday, and its stock will then trade on the American Stock Exchange under the symbol CTA. The new name was misstated in yesterday's edition.

(See: "Business Brief -- Texas Air Corp.: Company Changes Name, Shareholders Elect Directors" -- WSJ June 7, 1990)

---

THE NEW YORK STOCK EXCHANGE said customers purchased 642,299 shares in odd-lot transactions Tuesday. The Odd-Lot Trading table in yesterday's edition misstated the amount.
