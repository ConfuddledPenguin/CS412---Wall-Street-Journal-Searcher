Business Brief -- Systems Center Inc.: Australian Software Concern Acquired for $43.5 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

Systems Center Inc., Reston, Va., said it acquired Software Developments International Pty. Ltd., a developer of network-management software, in a transaction valued at about $43.5 million. Systems Center, a provider of data-base software, said it paid about $3.3 million in cash and 1,896,930 shares of its common stock. Software Developments, based in Chatswood, New South Wales, Australia, said the transaction would be accounted for as a pooling of interests.

Systems Center said the acquisition consolidates ownership and distribution of NET/MASTER, a family of network-management-software products that the Australian concern developed.
