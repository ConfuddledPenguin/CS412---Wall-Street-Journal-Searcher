Mitchell Energy Well

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

WOODLANDS, Texas -- Mitchell Energy & Development Corp. said that a discovery well in a new producing field in Lea County, N.M., flowed at a rate of 408 barrels of oil a day through a 16/64-inch opening with 250 pounds per square inch of flowing tubing pressure. Mitchell holds a 52% working interest in the Texaco Federal No. 1 well, and the operator and holder of the remaining interest is Manzano Oil Corp. of Roswell, N.M.

Mitchell is an independent energy concern and real estate developer.
