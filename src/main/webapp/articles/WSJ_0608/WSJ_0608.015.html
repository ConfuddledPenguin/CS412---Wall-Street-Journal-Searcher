Who's News: American Capital & Research Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

AMERICAN CAPITAL & RESEARCH Corp. (Fairfax, Va.) -- Samuel Belzberg, 61-year-old chairman and chief executive officer of First City Financial Corp., was named a director of this environmental services company, as part of a board restructuring in which eight management directors stepped down, reducing board membership to nine. Mr. Belzberg and his family own about 12.2% of American Capital's Class A common stock through an affiliate.
