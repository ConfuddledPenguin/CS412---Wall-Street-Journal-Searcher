Who's News: Visa International

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

VISA INTERNATIONAL (San Francisco) -- Seven bankers were appointed to the boards of this membership association and Visa U.S.A., which administers the Visa program in the U.S. Joining the Visa International board are: James L. Bailey, group executive of Citibank's U.S. Card Products Group; W. R. Fithern, senior vice president at Royal Bank of Canada; Adolfo Lagos, director of Commercial Banking at Bancomer S.N.C. of Mexico; Peter E. Madden, president and chief operating officer of State Street Bank & Trust Co., Boston; Will F. Nicholson Jr., chairman and chief executive officer of Colorado National Bankshares Inc. in Denver; and Ayoub Farid Saab, deputy chairman and chief executive officer of Federal Bank of the Middle East Ltd. in Cyprus. Mr. Bailey also joins the Visa U.S.A. board along with Thomas E. Peterson, vice chairman and head of Bank of America's consumer banking group.
