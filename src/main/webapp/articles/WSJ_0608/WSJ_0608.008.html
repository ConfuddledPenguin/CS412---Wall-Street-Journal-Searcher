Letters to the Editor: A Respected Adviser

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

Clark Clifford has given me astute and honest counsel throughout most of my 40-year tenure on Wall Street. I was therefore greatly distressed to read your May 3 page-one article on the Bank of Credit and Commerce International (for which Mr. Clifford is a lawyer).

For more than 50 years Mr. Clifford has been a respected adviser and friend to U.S. presidents, regardless of party affiliation. He has served and upheld the laws of this country without prejudice.

Your article twists facts and implicates by innuendo.

Harry A. Jacobs Jr.

New York
