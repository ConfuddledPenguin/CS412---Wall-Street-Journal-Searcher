Union Texas Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

HOUSTON -- Union Texas Petroleum Holdings Inc. said it hired Goldman, Sachs & Co. to assist it in a potential sale of the company. The oil and gas concern disclosed early last month that it was putting itself up for sale. Industry experts have estimated the company might bring as much as $2 billion, or in the lower-to-mid $20-a-share range, if sold in its entirety. Union Texas is 40%-owned Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. and 40%-owned by Allied-Signal Inc.
