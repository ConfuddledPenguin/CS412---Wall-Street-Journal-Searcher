Who's News: Family Steak Houses of Florida Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

FAMILY STEAK HOUSES OF FLORIDA Inc. (Neptune Beach, Fla.) -- James W. Osborn, formerly senior vice president, operations, USA cafes L.P., a Bonanza steakhouse franchisor, was named president and chief executive officer of this restaurant company, effective June 11. Mr. Osborn, 48 years old, succeeds Eddie L. Ervin Jr., 61, the company founder, who remains chairman.
