Business Brief -- Fluor Corp.: Firm Is Awarded Contracts With Value of $30 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

Fluor Corp., Irvine, Calif., said it was awarded six contracts with a combined value of $30 million from three concerns. The engineering, construction and technical services company said that its Fluor Daniel unit received two contracts from Atlantic Richfield Co. for $17 million to provide engineering and technical services to Atlantic Richfield's Alaska unit.

Fluor said its unit received two other contracts with a combined value of $5 million from Big Rivers Electric Corp., a power plant owner and operator based in City of Henderson, Ky., to provide maintenance services to nine coal-fired power generating units.

The unit received two contracts from the Fort Dodge Laboratories unit of American Home Products Corp. valued at $8 million. One contract is to provide engineering, procurement, construction management and other services for a veterinary products manufacturing facility in Sligo, Ireland. The other contract is to design and help construct an expansion to a veterinary products facility in Fort Dodge, Iowa, Fluor said.
