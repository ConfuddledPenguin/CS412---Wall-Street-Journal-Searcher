Business Brief -- Guardsman Products Inc.: President Is `Less Optimistic' About This Period's Results

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

Guardsman Products Inc. President Charles E. Bennett said the Grand Rapids, Mich., company is "less optimistic today than previously" about financial results in the current quarter and for the rest of 1990. For the first quarter, the maker of paints, varnishes and other chemical coatings reported sales of $47.7 million and net income of $1.7 million, or 23 cents a share. That was down from year-earlier sales of $46.9 million and net of $1.5 million, or 27 cents a share.

"Our best prediction now for the current period (ending June 30) is that our second-quarter numbers for both sales and earnings may be flat or slightly down from the first quarter," Mr. Bennett said.

Guardsman's stock stopped trading on the New York Stock Exchange late Thursday afternoon.
