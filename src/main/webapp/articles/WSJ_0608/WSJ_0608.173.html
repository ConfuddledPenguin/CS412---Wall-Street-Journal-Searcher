Who's News: Sumitomo Trust & Banking Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

SUMITOMO TRUST & BANKING Co. (Tokyo) -- Fumihiko Imamura, 44 years old, previously manager of the company's Los Angeles office, was named president and chief executive officer of the New York-based Sumitomo Trust & Banking Co. (U.S.A.) unit. He succeeds Hirokzu Mizukami, 42, who became general manager of financial institutions at the home office.
