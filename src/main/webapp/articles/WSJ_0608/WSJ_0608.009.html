Money-Fund Assets Fell During Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

NEW YORK -- Assets of the nation's 504 money-market funds fell $804.8 million, to $380.73 billion, for the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington trade association. Assets of 241 general-purpose funds rose $1.01 billion, to $130.30 billion, and assets of 102 broker-dealer funds fell $1.34 billion, to $158.28 billion. Assets of 161 institutional funds fell $475.3 million to $92.14 billion.
