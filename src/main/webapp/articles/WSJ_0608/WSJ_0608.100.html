Chili's Sets Debt Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

DALLAS -- Chili's Inc. said it has called for redemption July 6 its $35 million of 6.25% convertible subordinated debentures due 2012 at a price of $1,057.81 per $1,000 face amount, eliminating almost all of the restaurant company's debt. Chili's said the debentures are convertible until June 26 into common stock at a price of $27.67 a share, or about 36 common shares per $1,000 bond. Assuming all the debentures are converted to common stock, Chili's will issue 1.3 million new common shares.

The company currently has about 10.6 million shares and stock options outstanding. Chili's said that under an agreement with Goldman, Sachs & Co., Goldman will purchase any debentures that aren't converted and then will convert its holding to common stock.
