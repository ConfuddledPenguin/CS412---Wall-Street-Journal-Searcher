SCS/Compute Fiscal Year

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

ST. LOUIS -- SCS/Compute Inc. said it changed its fiscal year to end Jan. 31 instead of April 30. The processor of income tax returns also said it expects revenue for the three months ended April 30 to be about $17 million compared with $16.1 million a year ago. The company didn't make an earnings estimate for the quarter.
