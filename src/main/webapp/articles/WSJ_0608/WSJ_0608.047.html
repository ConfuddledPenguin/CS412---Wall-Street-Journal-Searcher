Norstan Reviews Stock Holding

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Norstan Inc., whose stock was halted in yesterday's national over-the-counter trading, said it's reviewing the value of its $5.65 million stake in closely held New Summit Inc. Norstan makes office-automation products. New Summit, Plymouth, Minn., is a maker of gears for boats and planes.

Norstan said New Summit is in default on certain of its loan covenants with a lender. Norstan said that "any potential reduction" in the value of its investment in New Summit will be recorded as a loss in the Norstan's fourth quarter ended April 30.

Norstan also said it may delay its planned earnings announcement for its fourth quarter. In the year-earlier quarter, Norstan had net income of $1.3 million, or 43 cents a share. Sales were $30.1 million.
