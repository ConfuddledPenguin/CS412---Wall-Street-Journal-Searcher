America West Airlines' Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/08/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines said its May traffic rose 53% to 910.3 million revenue passenger miles from 593.2 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's May load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, increased to 60.6% from 53.3%.

For the first five months of this year, America West's traffic rose 43% to 4.36 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.05 billion a year earlier. The load factor increased to 61.8% from 56.8%.
