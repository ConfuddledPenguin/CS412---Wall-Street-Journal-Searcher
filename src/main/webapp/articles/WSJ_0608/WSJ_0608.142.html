Business Brief -- Valmont Industries Inc.: Second-Quarter Performance Expected to Exceed First

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

Valmont Industries Inc. said its second-quarter earnings won't equal last year's second quarter, but will exceed the $4.5 million, or 38 cents a share, it earned in the first quarter. In the second quarter of 1989, the maker of irrigation systems and industrial products earned $6.1 million, or $1.05 a share, on sales of $199.6 million.

Valmont of Valley, Neb., said its second-quarter performance of 1990 is the result of sales in its irrigation markets slowing sooner than expected. The lower activity was due to an early spring planting season and abundant rainfalls in irrigation markets, as well as the slowing of industrial products sales in construction markets.

The company said that, while its second-quarter earnings will be lower than last year's, it expects earnings for 1990 will exceed the 1989 level of $20.7 million, or $1.78 a share, on sales of $789.8 million.
