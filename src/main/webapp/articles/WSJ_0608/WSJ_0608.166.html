Gantos's May Sales Rose 17%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc. said its May sales increased 17% to $21.1 million from $18.1 million a year earlier. The women's apparel retailer said comparable-store May 1990 sales rose 4% from a year earlier. Comparable-store results are for stores open at least a year. Gantos said sales for the 17 weeks ended June 2 rose 19% to $82.3 million from $69.1 million a year earlier. Comparable-store sales for the 17-week period increased 4%.
