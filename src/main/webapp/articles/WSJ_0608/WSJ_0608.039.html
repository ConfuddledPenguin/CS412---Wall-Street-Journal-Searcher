Head Count

("Approximately one in every 360 citizens of the United States is a lawyer." -- News item)

No wonder lawyers are independent

And, indeed, often show defiance --

Since each has, approximately,

Three hundred and fifty-nine clients.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.
