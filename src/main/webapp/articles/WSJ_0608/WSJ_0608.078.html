Marine Midland Bank Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/08/90

NEW YORK -- Marine Midland Bank said it agreed to acquire five Citibank branches in the Albany, N.Y., area, with $200 million in deposits. Loan portfolios aren't included in the transaction. Marine Midland, part of the HongKongBank group, said the purchase will increase to 26 the number of its branches in the New York state capital region.
