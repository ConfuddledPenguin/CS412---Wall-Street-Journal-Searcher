Bain Will Join Buy-Out Of Indevo AB of Sweden

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

STOCKHOLM -- Bain & Co. agreed to join a leveraged buy-out of Swedish management consultant Indevo AB and gained an option to take full control later this year in a transaction valued at 300 million Swedish kronor ($49.4 million). A Bain official said that, if completed, the Indevo acquisition would be the Boston-based management consultant's biggest to date. Bain currently lacks permanent representation in Scandinavia. But swallowing Indevo, which claims to be Sweden's biggest management consultant, would bring the U.S. concern a 20% share of an estimated 1.2 billion kronor market, expanding at a rate of nearly 40% a year.

Bain expects the local connection to increase its clout with Sweden's big international companies. But Indevo also has been involved in the restructuring of Sweden's state-owned railways and national postal service. Some industry analysts think urgently needed reform of the sprawling public sector will become a major source of new business for management consultants in coming years.

Peter Toernqvist, a Munich-based Bain director, said the U.S. firm has handled assignments from Swedish corporate clients for several years, but always on a one-time basis. "We've lacked staying power, a disadvantage when you don't have your own representation," he said.
