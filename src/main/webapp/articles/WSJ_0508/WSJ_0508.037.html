Singapore Output Is Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

SINGAPORE -- Singapore's gross domestic product grew by 10.1% in the first quarter, the republic's Ministry of Trade and Industry said. Official estimates are calling for a GDP growth of 6% to 8% in 1990, compared with 9.2% in 1989. The first-quarter growth compares favorably with the 9.6% rate registered in the first quarter of 1989 and the 8.9% recorded in the last quarter of 1989.
