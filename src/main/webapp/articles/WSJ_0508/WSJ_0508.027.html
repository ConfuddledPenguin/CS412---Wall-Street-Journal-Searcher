Henley Properties Sells Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

HAMPTON, N.H. -- Henley Properties Inc. said it sold a 90% interest in a 3,500-acre tract on the island of Hawaii to Nansay Hawaii Inc., a Japanese real estate concern, for $42 million. Henley, a real estate developer, said the land involved in the sale is near large resorts on the west coast of the island. The company added that it retained a 10% interest in two new limited partnerships that will develop the land.
