Marketing & Media: CBS Records Buys Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

NEW YORK -- CBS Records Inc. acquired a 50% stake in Important Record Distributors Inc., a distributor of "alternative" rock music under the Relativity, Combat and In-Effect labels. A price wasn't disclosed. CBS Records, a unit of Sony Corp., said the stake will give it access to "street-wise" and lesser-known musical acts while offering Important stronger distribution and marketing.
