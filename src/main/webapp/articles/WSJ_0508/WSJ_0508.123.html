Marketing Brief: Noted. . . .

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

WHO'S NEWS: Carolyn McCandless was named vice president, human resources and administration, of Time Warner Inc. She was vice president and director of employee benefits at Time Inc., which merged with Warner Communications in January. Alvin Washington, Time Warner's new vice president, human resources, will report to Ms. McCandless. He held the same position at Warner.
