Live Entertainment Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

LOS ANGELES -- Live Entertainment Inc. completed the acquisition of a majority interest in VCL Communications G.m.b.H., a West German home-video distribution company with annual revenue of $25 million. Terms weren't disclosed. Live, a videocassette producer and distributor, said the acquisition represented its entry into the overseas video market. VCL will be renamed VCL/Carolco Communications G.m.b.H. and become a fourth operating unit of Live.

VCL founder Datty Ruth will continue as managing director. Live President Devendra Mishra and David Mount, president of Live's domestic home-video unit, also will serve as managing directors.

Carolco Pictures Inc. owns a 49% voting stake in Live.
