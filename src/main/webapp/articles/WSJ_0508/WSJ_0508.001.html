Siliconix in Patent Talks

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

SANTA CLARA, Calif. -- Siliconix Inc. said it is in direct negotiations with International Rectifier Corp. with regard to an April 9 patent infringement ruling. A federal judge ruled in April that the semiconductor maker had infringed on certain patents related to International Rectifier's power transistor technology. Partly as a result of the adverse judgment, Siliconix subsequently filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code.

Siliconix said it has met with International Rectifier several times since the court ruling, and added that it hopes the parties can reach an agreement soon to resolve the four-year-old dispute.
