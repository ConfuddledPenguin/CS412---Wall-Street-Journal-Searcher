Who's News: Chemical Banking Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

CHEMICAL BANKING Corp. (New York) -- Chemical Bank, the main subsidiary, named nine new senior vice presidents: Mike Coughlin, Tony Nuzzo, Steve Rotella, Richard B. Aliperti, Raymond T. Downey, Marianne B. Grandin, Joseph F. Purcell, Michael A. Romano and Joseph A. Pocchia. Also named were 12 new managing directors: Glenn S. Havlicek, Joseph M. Martin, Heidi G. Miller, Francis X. Burnes III, Thomas S. Keating, Thomas J. Letarte, Darla D. Moore, James L. Stone, Peter H. Fischer, Mark S. Garvin, James A. Moore and David Reason.
