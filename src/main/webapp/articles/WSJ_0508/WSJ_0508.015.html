Journal's Gumbel, Rosett Honored at Press Dinner

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

NEW YORK -- Two Wall Street Journal staff members were honored at the annual awards dinner of the Overseas Press Club of America. Peter Gumbel, the Journal's Moscow bureau chief, received the Forbes Magazine award for the best newspaper business and/or economic news reporting from abroad. Mr. Gumbel, who was cited for a two-part series on economic troubles in the Soviet Union, received a $1,000 prize.

Claudia Rosett, editorial page editor for The Asian Wall Street Journal, received a citation for interpretation on foreign affairs, for her coverage of China.
