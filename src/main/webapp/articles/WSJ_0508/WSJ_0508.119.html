Business Brief -- OSF International Inc.: Old Spaghetti Warehouse Settles Two Suits by Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

Old Spaghetti Warehouse Inc. said it settled two lawsuits brought against it by OSF International Inc., the owner of the Old Spaghetti Factory restaurant chain, by agreeing, among other things, to change its name and pay the plaintiff $400,000. Garland, Texas-based Old Spaghetti said the payment will be financed by its insurance carriers and won't affect earnings.

Under terms of the settlement, Old Spaghetti Warehouse said it agreed to cease using the word "Old" in its signs, trade names, and service marks, and to submit to its shareholders at the next annual meeting a proposal to change its corporate name to The Spaghetti Warehouse Inc.

In addition, the company said it agreed to display on its menu and in the entrance to each store a statement disclaiming any association between its restaurants and those run by Portland, Oregon-based OSF.
