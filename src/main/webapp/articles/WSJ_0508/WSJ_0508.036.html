Southwest Airlines Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

DALLAS -- Southwest Airlines Co. said April traffic rose 12% to 820.8 million revenue passenger miles from 736.1 million revenue passenger miles a year ago. Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, was 61.8%, about flat from 61.1%.

For the year to date, the carrier said traffic rose 6.7% to 3.15 billion revenue passenger miles from 2.95 billion revenue passenger miles in the year-ago period. Load factor was 60.7%, down from 64.2%.
