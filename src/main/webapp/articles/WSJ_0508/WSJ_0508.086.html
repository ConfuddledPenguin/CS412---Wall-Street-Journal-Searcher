Financing Business: Primerica Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

PRIMERICA Corp. and its direct-marketing subsidiary, Fingerhut Cos., said Fingerhut sold an additional 345,000 shares at the public offering price of $16.50 each. The shares were sold after underwriters exercised an overallotment option. In the recent initial public offering, six million Fingerhut shares were sold. Primerica, an insurance, consumer credit and financial services company, said total proceeds to it, including the latest sale, would be about $98 million after expenses. The latest transaction leaves Primerica with ownership of about 71% of Fingerhut's common shares outstanding. In composite New York Stock Exchange trading, Fingerhut closed at $17.125, up 12.5 cents, and Primerica closed at $29.875, up 12.5 cents.
