McKesson Buys Garner Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- McKesson Corp. said it purchased the assets of Garner Wholesale Merchandisers of Greenville, N.C., for $13 million cash plus the assumption of $3 million in debt. Garner is a distributor of merchandise to supermarkets. McKesson is a supplier of bottled water and automotive products, and a processor of third-party prescription drug claims.
