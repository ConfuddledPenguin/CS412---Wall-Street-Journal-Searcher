West German Industry Orders

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

BONN -- New orders to the West German manufacturing industry rose a seasonally adjusted 2.9% in March from February and were up 3.5% from a year earlier, the Bonn Economics Ministry said in a preliminary report. The rise reflected a sharp 10.5% month-to-month increase in foreign orders, which resulted from a few large orders obtained by the nation's aerospace industry, the ministry said. Domestic orders dropped 1.5% during the month.
