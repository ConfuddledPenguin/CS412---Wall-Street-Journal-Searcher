Grace Energy Corp. Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

DALLAS -- Grace Energy Corp. said it completed the previously announced acquisition of the oil-field tool business of Land & Marine Rental Co. for $15 million. Land & Marine is a unit of Tesoro Petroleum Corp., San Antonio, Texas. Grace, a diversified energy holding company, is 83%-owned by W.R. Grace & Co., New York.
