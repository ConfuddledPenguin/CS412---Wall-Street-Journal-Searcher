Who's News: Advanced Micro Devices Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

ADVANCED MICRO DEVICES Inc. (Sunnyvale, Calif.) -- Robert M. Krueger, group engineering manager for Digital Equipment Corp.'s local area network products, was named vice president of the Network Products division of this maker of integrated circuits. Mr. Krueger, 42 years old, succeeds David Simpson, who retired several months ago at age 55.
