Who's News: Nomura Securities Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

Nomura Securities Co. (New York) -- Barry D. Kelly was named a senior vice president of this securities firm's U.S. unit, charged with the task of starting up a group to handle private placements of securities. Mr. Kelly, 35 years old, joins Nomura from Morgan Stanley Group Inc.'s private-placement group. In the newly created post at Nomura, Mr. Kelly will oversee the distribution in the U.S. of both securities products originating in the firm's Structured Finance group, as well as securities for U.S. and non-U.S. issuers.
