Cara Discloses Price for Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

TORONTO -- Cara Operations Ltd. said it will pay about 105 million Canadian dollars (US$90.3 million) to acquire Grand & Toy Ltd., a Toronto wholesale and retail distributor of office supplies. Cara, a Toronto food services concern, agreed in April to buy Grand & Toy from the Grand family of Toronto. Cara said the closing is expected June 20.
