Archive to Cut 108 Jobs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

Archive Corp., a Costa Mesa, Calif., maker of computer-tape drives, announced a reorganization and work force reductions in the wake of its acquisition last month of Cipher Data Products Corp. Archive said it expects to eliminate about 108 jobs, or about 3% of its 3,300 employees. The company also plans to realign its operations into six business units. Manufacturing operations will be consolidated in Singapore, the company said.
