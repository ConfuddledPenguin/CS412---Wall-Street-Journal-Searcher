Who's News: Reader's Digest Association Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

READER'S DIGEST ASSOCIATION Inc. (Pleasantville, N.Y.) -- Richard F. McLoughlin, president and chief operating officer since 1986, said he is retiring early so he can spend more time with his wife, who is ill. George V. Grune, chairman and chief executive officer, will assume Mr. McLoughlin's posts for an indefinite period. The changes take effect June 30. Reader's Digest publishes magazines, books and music.
