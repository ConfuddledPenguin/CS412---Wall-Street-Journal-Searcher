Halliburton Slates Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

DALLAS -- Halliburton Co. said it signed a definitive agreement to sell certain of its Brown & Root division's marine construction assets to Offshore Pipelines Inc. for $80 million. In March, Halliburton said it signed a letter of intent with Offshore Pipelines for the sale of the assets, but later said negotiations had been terminated.

The assets include 23 derrick, pipelay and other marine construction vessels and interests in certain land facilities in the Far East, Middle East and U.S.

Halliburton is a diversified oil field services concern.
