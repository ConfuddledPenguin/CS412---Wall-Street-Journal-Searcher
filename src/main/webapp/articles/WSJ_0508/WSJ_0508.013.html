CFM International's Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

EVENDALE, Ohio -- CFM International said it received an order to supply Gulf Air with CFM56-5 engines for as many as 18 Airbus Industrie A320 aircraft. The order is valued at more than $225 million. CFM is owned jointly by General Electric Co., the Fairfield, Conn.-based manufacturing, financial services and entertainment concern; and Snecma, a French jet-engine manufacturer. Gulf Air is owned by four Gulf states -- Bahrain, Oman, Qatar and Abu Dhabi.
