Who's News: Yamaichi Securities

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

YAMAICHI SECURITIES (Tokyo) -- David F. Sexton was named to the newly created post of senior executive vice president and director, and corporate secretary of this firm's Yamaichi International (America) Inc. unit, a New York securities and investment firm. Mr. Sexton was senior investment banker at CS First Boston, the parent company of the New York investment firm First Boston Corp.
