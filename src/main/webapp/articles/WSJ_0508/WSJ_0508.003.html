Keegan Management Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. - Keegan Management Co. said it signed a letter of intent to sell 31 Nutri-System Weight Loss Centers in the San Francisco Bay area to Nutri-System Inc. for $13.25 million. Keegan, a major franchiser of Nutri-System Weight Loss Centers, said that if the transaction is completed, it would have 43 remaining centers. The company said it plans to use the proceeds of the sale to make acquisitions.
