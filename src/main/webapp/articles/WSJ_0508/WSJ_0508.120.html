Business Brief -- Thomson Corp.: KKR Affiliate to Acquire Thomson Corp. Unit Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

An affiliate of New York investment bank Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. agreed to acquire the assets of Thomson Corp.'s Ward's Communications subsidiary, a provider of auto-industry data. Terms weren't disclosed.

The buyer, K-III Holdings, includes Intertec Publishing Corp., a publisher of trade magazines and technical books; Macmillan Book Clubs Inc., an operator of professional book clubs; and K-III Press Inc., a provider of information to the rail, trucking and ocean-freight industries.

Thomson, based in Toronto, has interests in newspapers, specialty publishing and travel.
