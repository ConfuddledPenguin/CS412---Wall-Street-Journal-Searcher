Who's News: Van Genderen Resigns As Patten Chief, President

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

STAMFORD, Vt. -- Patten Corp., a developer of rural properties, said Warren Van Genderen resigned as president, chief executive officer and a director. The company gave no explanation for the resignation of Mr. Van Genderen, who was named to succeed company founder Harry S. Patten as president and chief executive in November. Neither Mr. Van Genderen nor company officials were immediately available for further comment.

Patten Corp. said it established an office of the chief executive, consisting of Mr. Patten, Andre J. Melief and Bradford T. Whitmore. Mr. Patten will also reassume the title of president.
