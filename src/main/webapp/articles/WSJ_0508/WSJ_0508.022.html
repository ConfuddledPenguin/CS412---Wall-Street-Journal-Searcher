Rollins Truck Leasing Debt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Rollins Truck Leasing Corp. said it sold $50 million of Series I, 10.35% collateral trust debentures due May 15, 2000. The leasing and rental truck company said net proceeds will be used mainly to reduce outstanding variable-rate equipment debt.

The debentures were reoffered at par by Goldman, Sachs & Co.
