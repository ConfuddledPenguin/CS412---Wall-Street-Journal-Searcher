Rexene Agrees to Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

DALLAS -- Rexene Corp. said it entered into a non-binding letter of intent to purchase Poly-Pac Inc. from a unit of Rauma Repola, a Finland packaging company. Rexene, a petrochemical company, said it expects the purchase price to be about $15 million. The proposed acquisition is subject to a definitive agreement and approval of the boards of the Rauma Repola unit and Rexene.
