Business Brief -- Amertek Inc.: Firm Files Plan to Defer Payment of Its Accounts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

Amertek Inc. said it filed a proposal under Canada's Bankruptcy Act asking its creditors to allow it to defer payment of outstanding accounts totaling about 4.5 million Canadian dollars (US$3.9 million). Amertek said it is proposing to pay about 60% of that sum in early 1991 and the balance of the debt in 1992 as profits and cash flow allow.

The Woodstock, Ontario, company said it "has met with a large number of significant creditors to outline the present condition of the corporation and believes that there is adequate support for the proposal, and for the continuing operations of the corporation."

The maker of specialty trucks said the first meeting of creditors to discuss the proposal will take place May 28. The company said it is continuing discussions with potential financial partners in an effort to construct a more sound financial base for the company's operations.
