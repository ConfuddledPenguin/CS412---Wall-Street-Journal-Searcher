Who's News: Ciba-Geigy's U.S. Unit Says President Barth To Add Chairmanship

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

ARDSLEY, N.Y. -- Ciba-Geigy Corp. said Richard Barth, president and chief executive officer, will also become chairman at the end of the year. Mr. Barth, 59 years old, will succeed Otto Sturzenegger, 65, who will remain a director of Ciba-Geigy and the parent, Ciba-Geigy Ltd., the Basel, Switzerland-based pharmaceutical and chemical concern.

In other management changes, all of which a spokesman said were expected and "not an upheaval by any means," Charles O. O'Brien, 61, executive vice president and chief operating officer, and Jack Schneller, 62, senior vice president, will retire at year end.

Effective Jan. 1, the company said, it will add the following to its board: Leo Bontempo, president, agricultural division; Harold Kinne, president, additive division; Vernon Lemaster, president, dyestuff and chemical division; Dr. Rolf Meyer, vice president, finance and information systems; and Douglas Watson, president, pharmaceutical division.
