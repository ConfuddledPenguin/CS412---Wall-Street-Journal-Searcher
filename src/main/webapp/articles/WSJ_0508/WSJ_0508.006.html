Who's News: Textron Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

TEXTRON Inc. (Providence, R.I.) -- Russell G. Downing, 44 years old, was named president of the Randall division of this aerospace, financial services and commercial products concern, succeeding Richard J. Price, 64, who was named to the new position of chairman of the division. The company said Mr. Price plans to retire in November, when he reaches the age of 65. Most recently Mr. Downing was vice president, operations, for Randall, a Cincinnati maker of functional and decorative metal and plastic parts used for automobiles.
