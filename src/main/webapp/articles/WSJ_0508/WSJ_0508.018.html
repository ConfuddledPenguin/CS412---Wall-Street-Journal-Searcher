Who's News: Conagra Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/08/90

CONAGRA Inc. (Omaha, Neb.) -- Scott W. Rahn, president of the frozen-foods unit of this food company, was named to the new post of vice president, internal development, for the parent company. James R. Tindall, president and chief operating officer of ConAgra's Prepared Food Cos., will assume the additional position of president of the frozen-foods unit.
