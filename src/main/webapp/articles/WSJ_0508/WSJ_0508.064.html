Raw-Steel Production Rose 0.6% Last Week To 1,941,000 Tons

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 0.6% last week to 1,941,000 tons from 1,929,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 2.2% from the 1,984,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 86.4% of its capability last week, compared with 85.8% the previous week and 89.7% a year ago.
