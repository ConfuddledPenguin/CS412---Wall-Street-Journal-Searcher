Japanese VTR Exports

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/08/90

TOKYO -- Japan's exports of video tape recorders (VTRs) increased 3.9% in March from a year earlier, while exports of color television sets fell 8.6%, the Electronics Industry Association of Japan reported. Japanese manufacturers exported a total of 2,270,000 VTRs in March. According to the report, exports to regions outside of the U.S. and the European Community (EC) grew strongly.
