Treasury Slates Sale Of $18 Billion in Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $2.1 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $18 billion in short-term bills to redeem $15.9 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Oct. 25, 1990, and Jan. 24, 1991, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
