Vishay Intertechnology Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

MALVERN, Pa. -- Vishay Intertechnology Inc. said that the board of Crystalate Holdings PLC unanimously recommended that its shareholders accept a 90-pence-a-share ($1.62) tender offer from Vishay. Crystalate Holdings, a British electronics firm, has about 31.6 million shares outstanding, putting the total price of the offer at about $51 million.

Vishay, an electronics concern, would also assume about $24 million in debt.
