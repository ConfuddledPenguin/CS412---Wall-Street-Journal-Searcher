Commodity Funds Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

CHICAGO -- The average managed commodity fund rose 1.7% in June, according to Norwood Securities, which tracks the performance of 135 publicly offered commodity funds. Norwood said there were 91 "gainers," with an average rise of 3.1% in June.

For the first six months of 1990, the funds showed an average gain of 6.1%, Norwood said. There were 100 funds advancing, with an average gain of 9.7%.
