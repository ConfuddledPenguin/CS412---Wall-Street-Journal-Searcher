Du Pont, Kansai Set Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Du Pont Co., a chemical concern, said it agreed to form a joint venture, effective Aug. 1, with Kansai Paint Co. of Osaka, Japan, to supply coatings to Japanese and South Korean auto makers with operations in North America. Du Pont said that the venture, Du Pont-Kansai Automotive Coatings Co., will include auto parts suppliers in its market, and that it is the first in North America between U.S. and Japanese suppliers of auto finishes. Du Pont will own 60% of the venture, for which it projected first-year sales of about $40 million.

H. Nelson Furman, formerly a business manager in Du Pont's finishes business, is the venture's president, and J. Ishiwatari, formerly president of Kansai's U.S. unit, is executive vice president.
