International Brief -- Mitsubishi Electric Corp.: U.S. Revamping to Begin For Electronics, Sales Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

Japan's Mitsubishi Electric Corp. said it plans to begin reorganizing its business footholds in the U.S. next month in response to expansion as well as diversification of its local business activity. As the primary change, officials said, Mitsubishi Electric Sales America Inc. and Mitsubishi Electronics America Inc. will merge, keeping the name of the latter.

Until now, Mitsubishi Electric sales handled electric home appliances, and Mitsubishi Electronics dealt with electronics parts. However, Mitsubishi Electric noted a need to merge the companies, as products like high-definition television blur the boundaries between the two. The new company will be in Cypress, Calif.

In an attempt to better generate funds while hedging exchange risks, Mitsubishi Electric will also establish a finance company called Mitsubishi Electric Finance America Inc. in Cypress. It will be capitalized at $3.3 million.
