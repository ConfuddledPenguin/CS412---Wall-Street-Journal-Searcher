Cooper Industries Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

HOUSTON -- Cooper Industries Inc. said it retained the investment banking firm of Dillon, Read & Co. to advise it on the possible sale of its Arrow-Hart Components business. The electronic products manufacturing concern said the sale would include manufacturing operations in the U.S. and Plymouth, England, which have combined annual revenue of about $25 million. Cooper acquired the operations in 1981 with the acquisition of Crouse-Hinds Co.

Cooper didn't disclose what price it would seek for the Arrow-Hart operations.
