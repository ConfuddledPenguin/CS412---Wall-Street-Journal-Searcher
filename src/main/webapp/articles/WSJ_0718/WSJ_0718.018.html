Who's News: Fuqua Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

FUQUA INDUSTRIES Inc. (Atlanta) -- This consumer products and services company named these vice presidents to the new posts of senior vice presidents: James M. Bahin, 45 years old, operations; John F. Bartels, 45, chief financial officer; Colin W. Brown, 41, general counsel and secretary; James R. Foster, 44, finance, corporate development and treasurer.
