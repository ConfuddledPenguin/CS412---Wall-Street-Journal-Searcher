Keithley Instruments' Outlook

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

SOLON, Ohio -- Keithley Instruments Inc., which makes electronic test and measurement instruments, said that for its third quarter ended June 30, it expects to post a decline in per-share earnings from the year-earlier 17 cents, when net income was $611,000. It didn't specify earnings but said sales rose 5% to $24.2 million from $23.2 million. Keithley cited higher operating expenses and a higher tax rate for the earnings decline. The company said orders were lower than expected and slightly below those of a year earlier, principally because of a weak semiconducter market.
