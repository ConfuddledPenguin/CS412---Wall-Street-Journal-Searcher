Who's News: Arthur J. Gallagher & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

ROLLING MEADOWS, Ill. -- Arthur J. Gallagher & Co. named chief executive officer Robert E. Gallagher to the additional post of chairman. Mr. Gallagher, 67 years old, at the same time ceded the president's title to former vice president, operations, J. Patrick Gallagher, 38, who added the title of chief operating officer.

The financial services concern also named former executive vice presidentJohn P. Gallagher, 62, to the new post of vice chairman.

John P. Gallagher is the brother of Robert Gallagher and the father of J. Patrick Gallagher.
