International Brief -- Roche Holding AG: First-Half Sales Rose 6%; Higher 1990 Net Expected

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

Roche Holding AG, parent of a Swiss drug and chemical group, said in Basel that its first-half group sales rose 6% to 5.18 billion francs ($3.65 billion) from 4.89 billion francs a year earlier. The company also said that it expects to post a higher profit this year, although in this regard it also mentioned exchange rate uncertainties.

Profit in 1989 rose 33% to 852 million francs on a 21% increase in group sales to 9.81 billion francs.
