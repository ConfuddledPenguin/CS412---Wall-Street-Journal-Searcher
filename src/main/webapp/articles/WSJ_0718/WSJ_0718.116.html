GTE Earnings Rise 11%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- GTE Corp. reported second-quarter net income rose 11% on 6% higher revenue. The provider of telecommunications products and services and electrical products said earnings rose to $363.7 million, or 54 cents a share, from $328.8 million, or 49 cents a share, a year earlier. Per-share earnings reflect a 2-for-1 stock split effective May 23. Sales grew to $4.58 billion from $4.32 billion.

GTE shares fell 75 cents apiece to $28.25 in composite New York Stock Exchange trading on volume of nearly 3.6 million shares.
