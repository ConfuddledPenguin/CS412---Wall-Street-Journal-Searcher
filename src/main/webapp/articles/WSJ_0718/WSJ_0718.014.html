Swiss Trade Deficit Narrowed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

ZURICH -- Switzerland's trade deficit in June narrowed to 516 million francs ($363.9 million) from a revised May gap of 889 million francs, the government announced. In June 1989, the deficit was 974 million francs.

While exports in June, at 7.6 billion francs, were little changed from the levels in the previous month and in June 1989, imports slowed somewhat, to 8.18 billion francs in June from 8.6 billion francs in May and 8.8 billion francs the previous year.
