Who's News: Inland Steel Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

INLAND STEEL INDUSTRIES Inc. (Chicago) -- W. Gordon Kay, 53 years old, previously president and chief executive officer of this steel concern's J.M. Tull Metals Co. unit and president of the Joseph T. Ryerson & Son Inc. unit, was named Inland senior vice president, and chief operating officer of the company's distribution and processing businesses, both new posts. The Tull and Ryerson steel service center units will be reorganized into four regional distribution units and a coil processing unit, reporting to Mr. Kay.
