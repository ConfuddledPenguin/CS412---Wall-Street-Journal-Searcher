Who's News: Leggett & Platt Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

LEGGETT & PLATT Inc. (Carthage, Mo.) -- Robert A. Jefferies Jr., formerly vice president, general counsel and secretary at this furniture and bed concern, was named senior vice president, director of mergers and acquisitions and strategic planning, and Michael A. Glauber, previously vice president, finance and treasurer, was named senior vice president, finance and administration; both executives' positions are new.
