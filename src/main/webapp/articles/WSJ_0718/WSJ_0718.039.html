Commercial-Paper Rates Fall at Citicorp Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.01% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.21% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $380 million were submitted. Accepted bids were at 8.01%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 7.9647% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.16% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $381.2 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.924% to 7.98%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
