Who's News: National Foods Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

NATIONAL FOODS Inc. (New York) -- Steven I. Bachenheimer, president and chief operating officer since July 1989, left this closely held maker of kosher deli products under the Hebrew National name. A spokesman for the company said Mr. Bachenheimer decided to leave "because of irreconcilable differences about corporate growth priorities." Mr. Bachenheimer confirmed his decision to leave as a result of disagreements with National Foods Chairman Isidore Pines. He said they "haven't been able to agree on how to go about making the company grow." Harvey Potkin, previously senior vice president, succeeds Mr. Bachenheimer, who said he has no specific plans after leaving National Foods.
