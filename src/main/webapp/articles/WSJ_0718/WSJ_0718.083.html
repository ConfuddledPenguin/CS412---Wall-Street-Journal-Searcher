International Brief -- ASDA Group PLC: British Supermarket Chain's Annual Pretax Profit Falls

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

ASDA Group PLC of Britain, a major supermarket chain, reported that pretax profit for the year ended April 28 fell 27% to #180 million ($324.4 million) from #247 million a year earlier, hurt by soft consumer demand and expenses related to the purchase of 60 Gateway superstores and the setup of a new distribution system. Still, the result was at the upper end of market forecasts, which had ranged between #160 million and #185 million. Profit after taxes and minority interests but before extraordinary items also fell 27% to #122.6 million from #167.6 million. And per-share earnings dropped 27% to 10.13 pence from 13.82 pence.

The latest results included an extraordinary gain of #86.7 million related to disposals to pay for the Gateway acquisitions; the year-earlier period hadn't any special items. Profit attributable to shareholders, after taxes and extraordinary items, improved 25% to #209.3 million from #167.6 million.

Sales rose 31% to #3.55 billion from #2.71 billion.
