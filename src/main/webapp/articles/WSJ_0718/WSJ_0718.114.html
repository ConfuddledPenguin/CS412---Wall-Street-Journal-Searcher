Business Brief -- First Bank System Inc.: Fed Clears Proposal to Sell $175 Million of Common

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

First Bank System Inc., Minneapolis, said Corporate Partners and the State Board of Administration of Florida received Federal Reserve Board approval to purchase $175 million of its common equity. The banking company said the transaction is expected to close within three business days.

The company has said it would raise $175 million through a private placement of about 12.6 million shares of newly issued common at $13.875 a share to Corporate Partners, an investment partnership, and the State Board of Administration of Florida. Their investments, which are $145 million and $30 million, respectively, will result in net proceeds of about $172 million.
