Who's News: Environmental Control Elects Esher Chairman

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

PENNSAUKEN, N.J. -- Environmental Control Group Inc. said its president and chief executive officer, Brian Esher, 42 years old, assumed the additional duties of chairman, succeeding John Stoddard, who left the company for "personal reasons." Mr. Stoddard had held the position of chairman since early 1989; he relinquished duties as chief executive to Mr. Esher in September.

The asbestos abatement and insurance company also reported net income of $481,000, or seven cents a share, compared with year-earlier net of $971,000, or 14 cents a share; revenue fell to $14.7 million from $17.2 million.

The company also said it has relocated its headquarters from Maple Shade, N.J., to an existing facility in Pennsauken.
