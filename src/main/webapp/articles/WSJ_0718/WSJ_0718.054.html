International: Deutsche Bank Seeks More Of East German Venture

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 07/18/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West Germany's largest banking group, Deutsche Bank AG, said it's in "intensive talks" aimed at boosting its minority ownership of its big East German banking venture to almost 100%. Georg Krupp, Deutsche Bank's management board member responsible for Eastern Europe, said Deutsche Bank is proposing "a powerful capital increase" in Deutsche Bank-Kreditbank AG, a joint venture; East Germany's newly formed financial holding company Deutsche Kreditbank AG holds 47%.

The remaining ownership in the venture is divided among a few East German industrial groups.
