Elf Aquitaine Acquisitions

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/18/90

PARIS -- Societe Nationale Elf Aquitaine will pay about three billion francs ($540 million) for its planned acquisition of a 25% interest in Spain's Cia. Espanola de Petroleos and slightly less than one billion francs for 3.2% of Banco Central, according to a newspaper interview with a senior official of the state-controlled French energy group. Alain Guillon, president of the group's Elf France unit, said in an interview with the business daily La Tribune de l'Expansion that both purchases are aimed at reinforcing the group in southern Europe, in line with its drive to have marketing and refining operations in areas where it is present in exploration and development.

Elf announced both planned purchases Monday, but didn't provide financial details or price estimates at that time.
