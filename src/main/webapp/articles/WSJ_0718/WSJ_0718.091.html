Business Brief -- American Capital Management: Primerica Agrees to Acquire Rest of Shares in Company

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

Primerica Corp., New York, definitively agreed to acquire the 17.4% of American Capital Management & Research Inc. it doesn't already own for stock or cash, at shareholders' option. Primerica, a financial-services company, will pay either 0.32 share of its stock or $11.50 cash for each of the more than 4.2 million American Capital shares it doesn't already hold.

American Capital, Houston, provides investment advice to mutual funds and investment firms. The transaction was announced in May; the companies expect to close the purchase in late September or early October.

Separately, American Capital reported that second-quarter net income was flat at $5 million, or 21 cents a share, as revenue eased 4.8% to $24.5 million.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Primerica shares fell 50 cents to $36.875 while American Capital's shares were unchanged at $11.50.
