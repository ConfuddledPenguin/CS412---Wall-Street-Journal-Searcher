Dividend News: Lillian Vernon

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

LILLIAN VERNON Corp. declared a 3-for-2 stock split, payable Aug. 14 to shareholders of record on July 30. The Mount Vernon, N.Y., specialty-catalog retailer, which has 6.2 million common shares outstanding, said the split reflects the company's continuing growth and will improve the liquidity of its stock. After the split, it will have about 9.3 million common shares. In trading on the American Stock Exchange, Lillian Vernon closed at $24.25, up 12.5 cents.
