CMS Energy Corp. Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/18/90

CADILLAC, Mich. -- CMS Energy Corp. and Cogeneration Michigan Associates said they have formed a partnership to build and operate a 34-megawatt wood-fired independent power project in Cadillac. Each company will hold a 50% equity ownership interest in the project. The plant's electric output will be sold to Consumers Power Co. under a previously negotiated long-term contract. Construction of the $66 million project is scheduled to begin in spring 1991, with operation expected by January 1993.
