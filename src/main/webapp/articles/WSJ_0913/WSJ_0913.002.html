Matra Gets Norway Missile Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

PARIS -- Matra S.A. of France, a defense and electronics concern, said it received a contract from Norway to supply its navy with Mistral surface-to-air missiles. Matra didn't disclose the value of the agreement but said this is the largest defense materials contract ever signed by France and Norway. The company said Norway represents the 11th Mistral customer to date, while the Norwegian navy is the fourth defense force to entrust its ship defense system to Matra's product.

Matra said the accord includes an industrial cooperation agreement, effective immediately, that is expected to open the door to defense-related projects linking French and Norwegian companies.
