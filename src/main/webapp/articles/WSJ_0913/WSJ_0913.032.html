Business Brief -- Santa Fe Pacific Corp.: Debentures Are Accepted In a Debt-for-Equity Swap

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

Santa Fe Pacific Corp., Chicago, said it has accepted for exchange $215.6 million principal amount of its 16% senior subordinated debentures, due 2003, under its previously announced debt-for-equity swap. The railroad, real estate and natural resources concern said debenture holders will receive 67.25 shares of its stock plus $26.67 for each $1,000 principal amount of debentures accepted for exchange. Santa Fe said it will issue 14.5 million common shares in the exchange offer.

Santa Fe said the debentures are being purchased on a pro rata basis; the definitive pro rata factor is expected to be announced on or about next Tuesday.

The company said it is continuing with its plan to refinance and redeem the remaining $565 million of debentures outstanding on about Oct. 31 through the issuance of long-term debt at the parent company and proceeds of an offering of variable-rate exchangeable debentures at its pipeline subsidiary.
