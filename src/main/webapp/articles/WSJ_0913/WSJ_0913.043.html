Who's News: Nomura Securities Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

NOMURA SECURITIES Co. (Tokyo) -- Joseph R. Jagentenfl was named to the board of Nomura Research Institute, a New York affiliate of NRI America, the U.S. arm of this investment and securities company. Mr. Jagentenfl, 46 years old, becomes the ninth director at this computer systems applications and equity research company, where he heads the systems development division.
