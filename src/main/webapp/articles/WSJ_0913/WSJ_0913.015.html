Rockwell Gets Air Force Work

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

WASHINGTON -- Rockwell International Corp. was given $130.5 million in Air Force contracts for MX missile components.

General Electric Co. got a $30.2 million Air Force contract for satellite systems.

LTV Corp. received a $30.2 million Army contract for initial production of the Tactical Missile System.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., won a $25 million Navy contract for communications equipment.
