America West August Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines said August traffic climbed 54% to 1.13 billion revenue passenger miles from 729 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger carried one mile. Load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, rose to 67.9% from 61.9%. For the first eight months, America West said it flew 7.4 billion revenue passenger miles, a 44% increase from 5.12 billion miles a year earlier. Load factor was 62.8%, compared with 58%.
