Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

WHO'S NEWS: Times Mirror Co. named Steven L. Isenberg, 49, executive vice president, marketing, of the Los Angeles Times, effective Jan. 1. He succeeds Don Clark, 64, who will continue as an executive vice president during 1991. Mr. Isenberg was associate publisher of Newsday, another Times Mirror newspaper.
