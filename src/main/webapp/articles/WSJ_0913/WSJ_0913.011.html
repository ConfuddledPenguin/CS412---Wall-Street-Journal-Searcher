Who's News: Rospatch Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

ROSPATCH Corp. (Grand Rapids, Mich.) -- Neil L. Diver, 52 years old, an administrator of a private investment firm, was elected chairman of this maker of defense products and furniture. Kevin Coyne, 40, president of CMB Industries Corp., San Francisco, was elected a director. Messrs. Diver and Coyne succeed directors who chose not to seek re-election to the seven-member board.
