Business Brief -- Armco Inc.: Consent Solicitation, Offer For Notes Extended Again

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

Armco Inc., a steelmaker based in Parsippany, N.J., said it extended the expiration date for its consent solicitation and offer to purchase its 13 1/2% senior notes, due 1994, to 5 p.m. EDT next Wednesday. The offer initially was scheduled to expire last Thursday and had been extended once to 5 p.m. EDT yesterday.

Armco said it is seeking the consent of its note holders to amend provisions of the indenture relating to the notes that prohibit paying dividends on its common stock. Armco said it needs approval from 50.1% of the $164 million of notes outstanding to amend the indenture.

At the close of business Tuesday, consents representing about $53.1 million principal amount of the notes were deposited; of this amount, approximately $21.3 million principal amount of the notes had been tendered and deposited.

Armco said it is considering amending its pending consent solicitation and offer to purchase on or prior to next Wednesday. If any amendment is made, the solicitation and offer will be extended further.
