International Brief -- Ratners Group PLC: Suitor of Kay Jewelers Inc. Posts Drop in Pretax Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

Ratners Group PLC, the British retailer in the midst of a takeover bid for Kay Jewelers Inc. of Alexandria, Va., reported a 37% plunge in pretax profit for the first six months of the year. Britain's biggest jewelery chain said pretax profit fell to #9.30 million ($17.3 million) from #14.7 million a year earlier.

The latest results, in line with market expectations, suffer in the comparison because of a #7.4 million gain on investments and property disposals in the 1989 period. There were no such gains in the latest period.

Ratner said that despite the slowdown in U.K. consumer demand, the company is heading toward "the all-important Christmas season with confidence."

The $328 million bid for Kay, funded in large part through a 1-for-4 rights issue, is expected to be completed before the Christmas selling period, it said.
