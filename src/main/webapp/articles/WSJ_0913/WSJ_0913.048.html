Athena Gold Enters Joint Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

RENO, Nev. -- Athena Gold Corp. and its U.S. subsidiary, Athena Gold Inc., said they signed a joint venture accord with Placer Dome U.S. to develop Athena's 19-square-mile Talapoosa property. Athena said terms provide for Placer Dome U.S., a unit of Placer Dome Inc., based in Vancouver, to be the operator and to receive a 51% interest in the overall Talapoosa property. In return, Placer Dome will spend about $25.5 million to fund exploration, engineering, and project capital, along with making certain advances and other payments to Athena Gold Inc.
