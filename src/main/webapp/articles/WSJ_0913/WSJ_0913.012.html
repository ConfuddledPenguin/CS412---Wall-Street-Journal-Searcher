Martin Marietta Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

BETHESDA, Md. -- Martin Marietta Corp. said it acquired Perry Technologies, a developer of undersea work systems, from Perry Group Inc., a closely held Riviera Beach, Fla., concern. Terms weren't disclosed. The aerospace, defense and construction materials concern said the acquired unit complements its own undersea systems and anti-submarine warfare operations.
