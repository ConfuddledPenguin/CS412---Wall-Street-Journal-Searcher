Federal-Mogul Corp. Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

SOUTHFIELD, Mich. -- Federal-Mogul Corp. said it acquired a 50% interest in Glyco AG of Wiesbaden, West Germany, for an undisclosed amount. Federal-Mogul, a maker of vehicle-engine parts, said it is continuing its discussions for purchase of the balance of Glyco's shares outstanding.

Glyco is a privately held manufacturer of automotive and diesel engine bearings.
