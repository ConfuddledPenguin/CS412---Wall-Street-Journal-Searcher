SAS Plan to Buy Holding In Continental Is Cleared

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

WASHINGTON -- The Transportation Department approved Scandinavian Airlines System's proposal to purchase Frank Lorenzo's stake in Continental Airlines Holdings. SAS and Continental, which is the parent company of Continental Airlines, announced SAS's proposed acquisition of Mr. Lorenzo's stockholdings on Aug. 9. The transaction would boost SAS's equity stake to 16.8%.

The department said that, upon completion of the transaction, Continental would continue to be "fit" to operate an airline. In addition, it said the transaction complies with federal laws requiring that U.S. carriers remain under the control of U.S. citizens.

Mr. Lorenzo would receive more than $30 million for his shares.
