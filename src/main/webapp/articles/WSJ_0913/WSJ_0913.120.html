Business Brief -- Southeast Banking Corp.: Firm's Sale of Some Assets To Sears Unit Is Completed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

Southeast Banking Corp., Miami, said it completed the sale of its residential mortgage servicing portfolio and certain related assets to Sears Mortgage Corp. for $108 million. Sears Mortgage, based in Riverwoods, Ill., is a unit of Coldwell Banker Real Estate Group, which in turn is a unit of Sears, Roebuck & Co.

Southeast didn't say how much of a capital gain it will record on the sale of the $6.7 billion portfolio, but a spokeswoman said one analyst's estimate of $40 million to $50 million "is in the ballpark."

Ida Roberts, the Southeast spokeswoman, said the company will give further details of the transaction when it reports its third-quarter results, probably in the second week of October.

For Sears Mortgage, the deal gives it a deeper presence in Florida, a fast-growing market where it previously had nine loan origination offices. It said the purchase of servicing rights on the 83,000 loans increased its servicing portfolio to 190,000 loans totaling $14 billion.

Southeast's move is aimed at shoring up capital at the banking concern.
