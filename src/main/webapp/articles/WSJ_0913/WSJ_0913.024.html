Pan American August Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

NEW YORK -- Pan Am Corp. said August traffic at its Pan American World Airways unit rose 7.5% to 3.38 billion revenue passenger miles, which it said was a single-month record for the carrier, from 3.15 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline said its August load factor, or percentage of available seats occupied, jumped to 77.8% from 76%.

For the first eight months, Pan Am's traffic rose 10% to 21.4 billion revenue passenger miles from 19.46 billion. The load factor rose to 68.7% from 66.7%.
