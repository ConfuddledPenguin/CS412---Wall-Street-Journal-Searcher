Business Brief -- DeKalb Genetics Corp.: Year's Per-Share Earnings Rose 20%, Firm Estimates

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

DeKalb Genetics Corp. estimated that per-share earnings for its fiscal year ended Aug. 31 rose 20% on a 7% increase in revenue. In fiscal 1989, the company earned $12.1 million, or $2.14 a share, on revenue of $255.3 million.

The DeKalb, Ill., company said all three of its businesses -- seed, swine and poultry -- contributed to the improved performance. Its largest business, hybrid seed corn, will report increased sales in the U.S., driven by both higher planted corn acreage and increased market share for its corn hybrids.

DeKalb also said it estimates that its return on equity exceeded 1989's 12.2%, but that it didn't reach its near-term goal of 15%.
