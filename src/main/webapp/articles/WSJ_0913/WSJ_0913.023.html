Who's News: Securities Industry Association

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

WASHINGTON -- Gedale B. Horowitz, senior executive director at Salomon Inc.'s Salomon Brothers Inc. subsidiary, was named chairman of the Securities Industry Association for 1991. The 58-year-old Mr. Horowitz will lead the securities industry's main trade group after his formal election at the association's annual meeting in Boca Raton, Fla., in November.

Mr. Horowitz was unavailable for comment. He succeeds Robert Downey, 54, a general partner at Goldman, Sachs & Co.
