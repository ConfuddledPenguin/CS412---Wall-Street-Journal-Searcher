Lionel Corp. Sells Trademarks

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

NEW YORK -- Lionel Corp. said it sold to Lionel Trains Inc., the unaffiliated manufacturer of Lionel trains, certain trademarks and other rights under their license agreement covering the manufacture and sale of model trains and other toys. The toy retailer said it received $10 million and the license agreement was terminated. Proceeds from the sale will be used to pay down Lionel's bank-credit agreements.

The company will continue to use "Lionel" in connection with its corporate name and trademarks used in its toy retailing operations.

Based in Mount Clements, Mich., Lionel Trains is a privately held manufacturer of toy trains and miniature cars.
