Who's News: International Paper Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

INTERNATIONAL PAPER Co. (Purchase, N.Y.) -- The paper and forest products giant named three executives to new posts. Newland A. Lesko, previously staff vice president and director of quality management, was named vice president for coated papers. Mr. Lesko is succeeded by David A. Bailey, previously manager at the Androscoggin mill in Jay, Maine. Paul T. Stecko, previously manufacturing manager at the Hammerhill division, was named vice president of manufacturing for the Hammerhill and pulp divisions.
