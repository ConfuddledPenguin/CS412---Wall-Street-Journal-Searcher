Dividend News: America First Participating Preferred Equity Mortgage Fund

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

AMERICA FIRST PARTICIPATING PREFERRED EQUITY MORTGAGE FUND, Omaha, Neb., said units of record Sept. 30 will receive a special distribution of $2 a unit, payable Oct. 31. The special payout represents "approximately two-thirds of the HUD {Department of Housing and Urban Development} insurance proceeds" from the assignment of the mortgage on Casa Sandoval, a "senior living center" in Hayward, Calif. Because the distribution will reduce the partners' capital, America First Participating said, the fund's annual cash distribution rate will be adjusted to $1.15 a unit, beginning with the distribution to units of record Oct. 31, and payable Nov. 30.
