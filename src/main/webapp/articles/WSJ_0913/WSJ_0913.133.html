Who's News: General Development Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/13/90

GENERAL DEVELOPMENT Corp. (Miami) -- Charles M. Andolsek, formerly senior vice president of this real estate developer, was named executive vice president and remains chief financial officer. Margaret A. Graff, formerly vice president and treasurer, was named senior vice president, corporate planning, a new post. Joseph P. Zdon, formerly vice president and controller, was named senior vice president and controller.
