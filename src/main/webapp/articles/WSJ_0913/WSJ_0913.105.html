Who's News: U.S. Shoe Expands Board to 14 Members

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

CINCINNATI -- U.S. Shoe Corp. said it expanded its board to 14, from 11 members. The directors named were Charles S. Mechem Jr., retired chairman of the Great American Broadcasting Co.; Phyllis S. Sewell, retired senior vice president of Federated Department Stores; and Thomas Laco, retired vice chairman of Procter & Gamble Co.

U.S. Shoe reported a 29% drop in profit for the second quarter because of a slowdown in sales at its LensCrafters Optical stores, previously the company's fastest growing unit.

In the second quarter, the company earned $1.9 million, or four cents a share, compared with $2.6 million, or six cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Sales were $671.4 million, compared with $583.2 million.
