Dividend News: MBIA Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

MBIA Inc. said directors boosted the quarterly dividend 40% to 14 cents a share from 10 cents, payable Oct. 15 to stock of record Sept. 27. The New York-based insurer of municipal bonds, which has nearly 38.5 million shares outstanding, said the payout move reflects its "favorable long-term outlook."
