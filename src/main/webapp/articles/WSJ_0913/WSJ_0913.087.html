Sturm, Ruger & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

SOUTHPORT, Conn. -- Sturm, Ruger & Co. said Investors U.S. Growth Fund Ltd., a Canadian mutual fund, acquired 5% of the firearms manufacturer's 6.7 million shares outstanding. According to a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Investors U.S. Growth Fund made the acquisition only for investment purposes, Sturm Ruger said.
