Pier 1 Sales Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Pier 1 Imports Inc. said sales for the second quarter ended Sept.2 increased 11%, to $149.6 million from $134.9 million. Sales at stores open more than a year increased 4.1%. Pier One is a specialty retailer of home furnishings. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

PIER 1 IMPORTS Inc.'s sales for the second quarter ended Sept. 2 at stores open at least one year declined 4.1%. The figure was given as an increase in yesterday's edition.

(WSJ Sept. 14, 1990)
