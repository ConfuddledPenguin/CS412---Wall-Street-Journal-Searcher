Southdown to Post a Charge

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

HOUSTON -- Southdown Inc. said an arbitrator's ruling calling for the company to pay Martin Marietta Corp. $10 million, plus interest in settlement of a contract dispute, will result in a third-quarter charge. The amount of the charge wasn't disclosed and a spokeswoman for the company wasn't available for comment. In the year-ago third quarter ended Sept. 30, Southdown had net income of $13.4 million, or 70 cents a share, on revenue of $162 million. Results for the period included $3.7 million from discontinued operations.

The dispute centered on the lease of a cement plant in Utah. Southdown said it had previously placed $10 million in escrow, pending the outcome of the arbitration proceeding.
