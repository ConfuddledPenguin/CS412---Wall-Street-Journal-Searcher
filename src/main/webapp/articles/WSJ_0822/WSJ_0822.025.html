Kelly Services Sets Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

TROY, Mich. -- Kelly Services Inc. said it will buy back as many as 1.5 million Class A or Class B common shares to use for general corporate purposes. There are 29 million shares of Class A common stock and three million shares of Class B common stock outstanding, the temporary-help company said. The purchases will be made from time to time in the national over-the-counter market.
