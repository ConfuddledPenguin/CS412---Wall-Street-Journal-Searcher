Business Brief -- Circle K Corp.: Firm Will Close About 4% Of Stores by End of Month

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

Circle K Corp., which is operating under bankruptcy law protection, said it expects to close 201 stores, or about 4% of its 4,631 stores, by the end of the month. A spokesman said in Phoenix, Ariz., that cost savings figures related to the closures weren't available but would be "substantial." More store closings may come later, but "this appears to be the brunt of it."

Circle K, one of the nation's largest convenience-store operators, ran into trouble earlier this year after expanding too quickly and taking on more than $1 billion in below-investment-grade debt to buy and open additional stores.

The stores being closed are in 21 of the 32 states where Circle K operates. Fortythree of the stores are in Texas and 29 in California. Most of the 1,600 employees affected by the closures will be moved to other stores, the spokesman said.
