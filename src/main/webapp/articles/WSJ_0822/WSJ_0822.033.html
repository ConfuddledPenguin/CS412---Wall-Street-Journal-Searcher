Y&A Group Withdraws Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

ST. LOUIS -- Y&A Group Inc., citing current market conditions, said its board has withdrawn the company's recent registration statement relating to a proposed offering of 1.6 million common shares. The provider of engineering services to the electric utility industry said increased bank credit facilities, working capital and a possible private placement of its securities will provide it with sufficient resources to maintain its current business objectives.
