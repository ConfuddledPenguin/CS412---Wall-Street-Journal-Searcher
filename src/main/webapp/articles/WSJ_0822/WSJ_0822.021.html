Dividend News: Ohio Edison

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

OHIO EDISON, Akron, said it cut its dividend after the Public Utilities Commission of Ohio cut its rate increase request of $218 million to $142 million. The rate increase was intended to recover costs associated with its share of Beaver Valley 2, a generating unit. The commission's decision requires that the utility take immediate action to preserve its financial health and protect the long-term interests of all stockholders by setting the dividend at a sustainable level.
