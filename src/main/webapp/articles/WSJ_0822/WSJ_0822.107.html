International Brief -- Canon Inc.: Unconsolidated Pretax Profit For First Half Jumped 67%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

Canon Inc. of Japan reported that unconsolidated pretax profit for the first half jumped 67% to 37.65 billion yen ($275.2 million) from 22.53 billion yen a year earlier. Net income increased 57% to 20.15 billion yen, or 27.17 yen a share, from 12.8 billion yen, or 20.41 yen a share.

Supported by strong demand for color copiers, laser printers and high-quality cameras, sales climbed 22% to 455.49 billion yen from 374.5 billion yen.

Exports, which accounted for 73% of total sales, increased 24% from a year earlier, as shipments to all destinations showed healthy growth, a Canon official said. Domestic sales also did well, rising 16%. Mainline copier sales increased 20% to 147.92 billion yen, the official said. Computer-related equipment sales grew 27% to 125.79 billion yen.
