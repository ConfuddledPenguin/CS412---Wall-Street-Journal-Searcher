Carolco Withdraws Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

LOS ANGELES -- Carolco Pictures Inc. said it is withdrawing its $100 million convertible subordinated debenture offering, which was filed just last week. "While we believe that as structured, the convertible debenture offering could be sold successfully in the marketplace," said Carolco's president and chief executive officer, Peter Hoffman, "the company isn't prepared to sell stock at these current levels."

A spokesman emphasized that the withdrawal wouldn't have any effect on the entertainment company's operations or production plans.

Carolco's stock closed yesterday in New York Stock Exchange composite trading at $8.375 a share, up 12.5 cents.
