PacifiCare Health Systems

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

CYPRESS, Calif. -- PacifiCare Health Systems Inc. said it may purchase on the open market as many as 500,000 shares, or about 4%, of its 11.5 million common shares outstanding. "With the recent significant decline in the market . . . and PacifiCare's stock price in particular, there may be attractive opportunities . . . to acquire shares," said Terry Hartshorn, president and chief executive officer of the health maintenance organization. The purchases will be made when market and business conditions are favorable, he said.
