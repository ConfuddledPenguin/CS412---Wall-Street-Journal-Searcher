Who's News: American Precision Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

AMERICAN PRECISION INDUSTRIES Inc. (Buffalo, N.Y.) -- Thomas R. Horton was elected a director. Mr. Horton, 64 years old, is chairman and chief executive officer of American Management Association, a management information organization in New York. He will take the seat vacated by William A. Donovan, 63, the company's former general counsel, who retired in April. American Precision Industries makes heat transfer equipment and electronic products.
