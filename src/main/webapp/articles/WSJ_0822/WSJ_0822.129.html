Business Brief -- Oglebay Norton Co.: Investor Mactier Lifts Stake To 5.1% in Latest Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

Private investor James A. Mactier increased his stake in Oglebay Norton Co. to 5.1% of the Cleveland-based raw materials and shipping concern. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Mactier said recent purchases of 57,600 shares, added to his previous holding of 73,200 shares, increase his total holding to 130,800 shares. The investor said the purchases were for investment reasons. He declined comment through his lawyer, John C. Hewitt.

A spokesman for Oglebay Norton said the company has not spoken with Mr. Mactier, but it is not concerned about any takeover possibilities "at this point."

The spokesman also said that John D. Weil, a private St. Louis investor, had recently increased his stake to 5.3%. He noted that there is no known connection between the two investors.
