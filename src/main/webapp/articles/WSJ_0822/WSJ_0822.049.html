Allegheny Ludlum Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

PITTSBURGH -- Allegheny Ludlum Corp. said it completed its previously announced acquisition of the assets of Thinsheet Metals Co., Waterbury, Conn. Terms weren't disclosed, but a spokesman for the specialty steel concern said the company expects the acquisition to produce annual revenue of $40 million. Thinsheet Metals makes precision rolled steel for high-technology applications.
