Business Brief -- American Express Co.: Acquisition of Epsilon Data Management Is Completed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

American Express Co. said it completed the acquisition of Epsilon Data Management Inc., a database management company, in a stock swap valued at $53 million. Epsilon, of Burlington, Mass., provides software and direct-marketing services for such companies as the Quaker Oats Co., General Electric Co., Apple Computer Inc. and MCI Communications Corp., according to American Express spokeswoman Maureen Bailey.

The Travel Related Services subsidiary of New York-based American Express will use Epsilon software, provide it to the businesses that accept its charge cards, and market it to others, said Ms. Bailey.

Epsilon shareholders, who will receive 0.655 share of newly issued American Express common stock for each Epsilon share, approved the transaction at a special meeting Monday.
