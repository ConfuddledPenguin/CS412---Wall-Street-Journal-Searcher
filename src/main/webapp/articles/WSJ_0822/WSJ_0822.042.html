T&N's $187 Million Offer Set

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

NEW YORK -- T&N PLC of Britain said it completed its $187 million, or $17.30-a-share tender offer for J.P. Industries Inc. The offer also includes the assumption of $185 million of J.P. Industries' debt. The companies said about 10.8 million shares, or 99.5%, of J.P. Industries common stock outstanding were accepted Monday for payment. The merger is expected to be completed this month.

The tender offer was the first step in the previously announced agreement to merge T&N's subsidiary, T&N Automotive Components, with J.P. Industries, an Ann Arbor, Mich.-based automotive engine parts maker. T&N is an equipment manufacturer based in Manchester, England.
