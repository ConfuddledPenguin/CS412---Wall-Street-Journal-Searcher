International: Siemens Gets Indonesian Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

BONN, West Germany -- Siemens AG, an electrical and electronics giant, said it obtained a contract valued at several hundred million marks to deliver electro-technical equipment for the Gresik gas and steam turbine energy plant in Indonesia, as part of a 1.1 billion-mark ($702.5 million) order granted by Perusahaan Umum Listrik Negara, the Indonesian state-owned energy works, to a consortium led by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd. of Japan. Siemens said the contract calls for delivery of 12 generators, high-voltage switch gear and 18 transformers. Siemens plants in Muelheim, Berlin and Nuremberg will complete the order.
