Business Brief -- MGM/UA Communications Co.: Firm Received $50 Million In Payments From Pathe

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

MGM/UA Communications Co. said it received checks totaling $50 million from Pathe Communications Corp., the latest in a series of payments toward Pathe's $1.3 billion acquisition of MGM/UA. After MGM/UA receives payment on the checks, Pathe will have paid $303 million cash toward the merger and will have given MGM/UA a $75 million security interest in three films slated by Pathe. Pathe must make another $50 million payment Sept. 20, according to terms of the merger agreement.

The agreement calls for MGM/UA shareholders to receive a total of $21.50 a share, including a $4-a-share payment distributed on July 20. Payment of the remaining $17.50 a share and closing of the merger is set to occur on or before Oct. 23.
