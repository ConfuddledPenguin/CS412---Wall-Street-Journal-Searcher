First American Plans Staff Cuts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

NASHVILLE, Tenn. -- First American Corp. said it plans to reduce its 3,800-employee work force by 8% to 10%, by the end of next year. Struggling with problem loans and falling earnings in recent quarters, the bank said that a significant portion of the reduction would be acheived through normal employee turnover. A spokeswoman said some jobs would be eliminated, but said she couldn't say how many.
