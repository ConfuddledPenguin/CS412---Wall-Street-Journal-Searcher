Who's News: CF Income Partners Limited Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

CF INCOME PARTNERS LIMITED PARTNERSHIP (Los Angeles) -- Jeffrey H. Lynford, 42 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of CF Management Corp., the general partner of CF Income. He succeeds R. Craig Overturf, 55, who resigned. Mr. Lynford was named vice chairman, chief financial officer and co-chief executive in June. He continues as chief financial officer until a successor is named; the vice chairman title will be dropped, a spokeswoman said. Mr. Lynford is chairman of Wellsford Group Inc., an affiliate of New York-based Wellsford Capital Partners Limited Partnership, which acquired 20% of CF Management in December. CF Partners is a real estate investment concern.
