Western Union Extends Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

UPPER SADDLE RIVER, N.J. -- Western Union Corp. said it extended to midnight Tuesday from midnight Monday its offer to exchange common stock for secured notes. Under the offer, the company wants to issue new notes and common shares in exchange for up to $500 million in outstanding 19 1/4% senior secured notes and $30 million of 16% notes. The company has said it is seeking at least 95% of the $500 million and 80% of the $30 million as part of its recapitalization plan.

As of midnight Monday, $305.2 million of the 19 1/4% notes and $1.2 million of 16% notes had been tendered, the telecommunications company said.
