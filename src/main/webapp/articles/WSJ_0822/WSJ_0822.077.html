International: Daiei to Raise Cattle in Kansas

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

TOKYO -- Daiei Inc. will link up with Excel Corp., the livestock division of Cargill Inc. of the U.S., to raise cattle in Kansas, an official of the Japanese supermarket chain operator said. Daiei, which imports about 13,000 metric tons of beef annually, predicts growing demand for beef when Japan's beef import regulations are liberalized in April.

The link with Excel will help Daiei secure a supply of low-cost U.S. beef. Daiei is planning to import 3,000 head of cattle in the first year and 5,000 in the second.
