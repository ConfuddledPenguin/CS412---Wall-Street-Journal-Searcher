Business Brief -- Charter Medical Corp.: SEC to Review Buy-Out Led by Managers in 1988

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

Charter Medical Corp., Macon, Ga., said it was advised by the Securities and Exchange Commission that the agency plans to review Charter's 1988 management-led leveraged buy-out. No Charter executives have been interviewed by the SEC and no documents have been requested, according to J. Rodney Laughlin, an executive vice president of the psychiatric and general hospital concern. "It's so preliminary, I don't know what they'll be reviewing," he said.

SEC officials couldn't be reached.

In recent months, Charter has been struggling under $1.6 billion in debt from the buy-out. The company has been cutting costs, by reducing personnel, among other measures.
