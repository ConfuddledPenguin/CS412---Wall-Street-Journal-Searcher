Sharon Steel Resolves Dispute

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

FARRELL, Pa. -- Sharon Steel Corp. said that it resolved a dispute with the U.S. government about cleanup of hazardous materials, and that the agreement potentially "cleared a major hurdle" in the reorganization of the nation's 18th-largest steel company, which is in Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law proceedings. The settlement regarding two sites fixes Sharon Steel's maximum liability and provides payment options of either a $22 million lump sum or an unsecured claim of $70 million and cash payments of $12 million over six years. The agreement requires bankruptcy court approval.
