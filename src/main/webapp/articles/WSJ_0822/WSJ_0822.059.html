Japan's Wholesale Prices Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

TOKYO -- Japan's wholesale prices rose 0.2% in the first 10 days of August from the previous 10-day period, and increased 0.9% from the previous year, the Bank of Japan reported. The wholesale price index stood at 90.5 at the end of the ten days. The index's base of 1985 prices equals 100.

Domestic prices climbed 0.1% from the previous ten days and rose 0.4% from a year earlier.

In July, wholesale prices rose 0.4% from the previous month and 0.5% from a year earlier, the central bank reported last week.
