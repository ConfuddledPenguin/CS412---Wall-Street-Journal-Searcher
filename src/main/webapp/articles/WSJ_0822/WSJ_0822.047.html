International: Unilever Acquisitions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

ROTTERDAM, the Netherlands -- The Anglo-Dutch food and consumer products manufacturer Unilever N.V. said it would seek to acquire a 49% stake in a group made up of two frozen-food businesses, Mora B.V. of the Netherlands and Saltos N.V. of Belgium. Under the terms of the agreement currently being discussed, Unilever would also be granted an option to acquire the remaining 51% of the Mora/Saltos group over a period of five years. Financial details of the transaction weren't disclosed.
