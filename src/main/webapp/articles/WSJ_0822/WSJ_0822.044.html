Dividend News: San Juan Basin Royalty Trust

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

SAN JUAN BASIN ROYALTY TRUST, Fort Worth, Texas, said it won't make a monthly cash distribution to units of record Aug. 31. The oil and gas trust didn't make a distribution to units of record July 31. It last paid a distribution of 23 cents a unit on July 16.
