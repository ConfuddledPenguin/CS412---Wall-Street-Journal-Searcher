Susquehanna Accord For Acquisition Ended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

LITITZ, Pa. -- Susquehanna Bancshares Inc. said an agreement to purchase Elmwood Federal Savings Bank for about $24 million in stock was ended because of "recent changes in market conditions." The decision to call off the purchase was made by mutual consent, the banks said. In May, Susquehanna lowered its previous $27 million offer for Elmwood, based in Media, Pa., because of weak conditions in the banking market.

The companies said that "the door was left open" for future discussions.
