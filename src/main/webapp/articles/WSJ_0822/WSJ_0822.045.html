Who's News: Coopers & Lybrand

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

COOPERS & LYBRAND (New York) -- Stephen W. McKessy was named deputy chairman for operations, succeeding William J. Holland, who is retiring. Succeeding Mr. McKessy, who was previously managing partner of this accounting and consulting firm's New York office, is Harvey J. Bazaar, previously chairman of its financial services unit. Both changes are effective Sept. 1. Mr. McKessy, who also serves on the firm's executive committee, will maintain his role as vice chairman of the firm's New York metro region.
