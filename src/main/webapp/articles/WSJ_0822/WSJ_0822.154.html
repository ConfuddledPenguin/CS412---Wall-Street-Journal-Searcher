Credit Ratings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

LOCKHEED Corp. (Calabasas, Calif.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it downgraded about $800 million of this aerospace concern's senior long-term debt to Baa-1 from single-A-3. The agency cited the "less favorable defense-business outlook and the likelihood that the company's cash-flow protection of debt will decline, while its borrowings will increase." Lockheed's Prime-2 commercial-paper rating wasn't changed.
