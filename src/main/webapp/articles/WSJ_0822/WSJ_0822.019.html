Dionex Stock Repurchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Dionex Corp. said its board approved the repurchase of as many as one million common shares in the open market. The repurchase program is in additon to a one-million-share buy-back plan approved last year. Under that plan, Dionex, a maker of chemical analysis equipment, has purchased 913,500 shares. Dionex has 8.9 million shares outstanding.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, Dionex closed at $18.875, down 12.5 cents.
