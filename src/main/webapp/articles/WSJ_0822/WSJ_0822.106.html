Business Brief -- Boeing Co.: De Havilland Unit Receives Commuter Aircraft Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

Boeing Co. said its de Havilland unit received an order for four Dash 8 series 300 commuter airplanes from Worldwide Resources Ltd., a unit of Canopus Group. The value of the order, which includes spare parts, wasn't disclosed.

The twin-engine, turboprop planes will be assigned to Sea/Air/Ground Leasing Corp., another Canopus unit, to be marketed to the regional-airline industry, Seattle-based Boeing said.

A Boeing spokesman said the company continues to discuss the possible sale of the unprofitable de Havilland unit with Aeritalia of Italy and Aerospatiale of France.
