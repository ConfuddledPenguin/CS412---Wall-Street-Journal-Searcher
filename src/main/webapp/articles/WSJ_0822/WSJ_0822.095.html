TV: Telemundo Group Inc. completed the purchase of 85% of the stock of KDVA/Channel 60, San Antonio, Texas. The Spanish language TV network bought the stock from certain holders for an undisclosed amount. Telemundo is 74%-owned by Reliance Capital Group L.P., an affiliate of Reliance Group Holdings Inc.
