Who's News: Numerica Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

NUMERICA FINANCIAL Corp. (Manchester, N.H.) -- David A. Heaberlin, 41 years old, was named executive vice president and chief financial officer of this savings and loan holding company. Mr. Heaberlin, most recently chief financial officer of Financial Corp. of Santa Barbara, Santa Barbara, Calif., succeeds Richard Ducharme, 46, who resigned in April for personal reasons.
