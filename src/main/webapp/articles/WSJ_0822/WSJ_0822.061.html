Who's News: Scor S.A.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

SCOR S.A. -- William K. Lowry Jr., 38 years old, previously chief financial officer at Phoenix Re Corp., a reinsurer, was named chief financial officer and senior vice president at Scor U.S. Corp., filling a vacancy at the New York-based unit of this Paris-based insurance and reinsurance holding company.
