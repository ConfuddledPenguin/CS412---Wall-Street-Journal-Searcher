Who's News: Student Loan Marketing Association

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

STUDENT LOAN MARKETING ASSOCIATION (Washington) -- Robert A. Estrada, chairman of Estrada Securities Inc., Dallas, and a former special assistant to President Bush, was appointed a director of this financial intermediary, known as Sallie Mae. He succeeds Irby Clifford Simpkins Jr., who was a board member for nine years.
