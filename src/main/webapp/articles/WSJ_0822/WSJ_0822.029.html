Canadian Bankruptcies Increase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian bankruptcies in July rose 60% to 4,116 from 2,573 a year earlier, the Canadian Consumer and Corporate Affairs Department said. Individual bankruptcies in July totaled 3,301, and business bankruptcies 815.

In the first seven months of this year, Canadian bankruptcies rose 34% to 27,836 from 20,747 a year earlier.
