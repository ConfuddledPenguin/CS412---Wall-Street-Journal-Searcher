Canada's June Retail Sales Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian retail sales rose 0.8% in June from May, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. In May, retail sales fell 1%.

The June gain was the largest monthly increase since January, when retail sales rose 1.4%. It was partly attributed to a rise in sales of automobiles and other vehicles.

Retail sales fell 1.7% in the second quarter despite the June rise, Statistics Canada said.
