DS Bancor Chairman's Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

DERBY, Conn. -- DS Bancor Inc., the holding company for Derby Savings Bank, said Chairman Eugene D. Micci boosted his stake in the company to 8% of the outstanding common stock. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Micci said he may increase his holdings to 9.9% and will continue with management and the board to explore "all viable options for maximizing shareholder value" including the possible sale of the company.

A spokeswoman for the bank noted that Mr. Micci has been increasing his holdings over the past few years. At June 30, Derby Savings Bank had assets of $627 million.
