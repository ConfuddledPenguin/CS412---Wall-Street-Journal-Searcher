REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Anchor Wars

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

The Persian Gulf anchorman war now faces the threat of a mind-boggling escalation. Under orders from Iraqi authorities ABC's Ted Koppel and CBS's Dan Rather have left Iraq. Now comes word that Jesse Jackson is heading for Baghdad, in his role as host for "The Jesse Jackson Show," soon to be syndicated by Time-Warner Inc. "It would not be appropriate to go there as a diplomat," said Mr. Jackson, a former free-lance negotiator. No word yet on whether Larry King, Geraldo, Donahue, Oprah or Regis will be flying in.
