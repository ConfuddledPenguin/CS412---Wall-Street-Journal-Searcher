Canadian Bond Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government reported an average yield of 10.865% at an auction of 1.2 billion Canadian dollars (US$1.05 billion) of two-year bonds maturing Sept. 5, 1992. Proceeds of the sales will be used to redeem C$650 million of government bonds maturing Sept. 5, 1990, and for general government purposes.
