Commercial Paper Rates Rise at Citicorp Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.0099% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 7.861% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $415.6 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.005% to 8.01%.

Citicorp also said the average rate rose to 8.039% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.764% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $380 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.03% to 8.04%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
