Who's News: Neiman Marcus Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

NEIMAN MARCUS GROUP Inc. (Chestnut Hill, Mass.) -- Michael F. Panutich, previously assistant controller, was named vice president of accounting, and Craig B. Sawain, previously director of financial planning, budgeting and analysis, was named vice president of planning and analysis. The two were promoted to new positions of the specialty retailer.
