Adjectivitis

May I ask, in this

Innocent letter,

Why do things have to

Get worse before

They get better?

If one's interrupted,

Even though shrewdly,

Why must it always

Be "rudely"?

And why are some books,

Though overrated,

Referred to as "Long-awaited"?

What baffles this bard

Is why must the look

Be "long and hard"?

Finally, I've always

Been mystified

That something that's cut

Must also be dried.

-- Dow Richardson.

Wait Control

"Patience is a virtue"

And I admire the aforesaid --

In the driver behind me,

Not in the one ahead.

-- Gail Cooke.
