Parkvale Financial Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/22/90

MONROEVILLE, Pa. -- Parkvale Financial Corp. said its board authorized the buy-back of as many as 85,300 shares, or 5% of the company's common shares outstanding. The repurchases will be made over the next six months in the open market. Robert J. McCarthy Jr., president and chief executive officer, said the current stock price "does not adequately reflect the company's long-term business and earnings prospects."
