American Brands Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/22/90

OLD GREENWICH, Conn. -- American Brands Inc. said it completed the acquisition of Moen Group Inc. for $975 million. American Brands' wholly owned subsidiary, MasterBrand Industries Inc., bought the Elyria, Ohio-based manufacturer of kitchen and bathroom faucets from Forstmann, Little & Co. The acquisition was first announced on July 20.

Analysts said the purchase is part of American Brands' attempt to sharpen its hardware and home-improvement business. That is one of the areas where the company is trying to diversify in order to reduce its dependence on tobacco, which still generates a major portion of its sales and operating profit.
