Business Brief -- Loral Corp.: Firm Posts Flat Earnings For Fiscal Fourth Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

Loral Corp. reported flat net income for its fiscal fourth quarter, but fourth-quarter earnings from continuing operations jumped 49% from a year ago. The New York-based defense electronics concern said it earned $25.1 million, or $1 a share, in the latest three-month period ended March 31, the same as in its year-earlier fourth quarter.

All of this year's fourth-quarter net income was from continuing operations. In the year-ago period, however, the company earned $16.8 million, or 67 cents a share on continuing operations, and $8.3 million, or 33 cents a share, on discontinued operations.

Sales rose 36% to $444.7 million, from $326.8 million in the quarter.

For the full fiscal year, Loral earned $78.2 million, or $3.11 a share, down 11% from $87.6 million, or $3.50 a share. On continuing operations, earnings for the fiscal 12 months rose 28% to $77.5 million, or $3.08 a share, from $60.3 million, or $2.41 a share.

Results for the latest full year include a $10.5 million pretax charge covering costs related to the company's guilty plea to charges arising from a Justice Department investigation into Pentagon contracting abuses.

Sales for the 12 months climbed 6.7% to $1.27 billion from $1.19 billion.
