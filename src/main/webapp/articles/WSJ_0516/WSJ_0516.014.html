Cambridge BioScience Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

WORCESTER, Mass. -- Cambridge BioScience Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement covering its plan to acquire Biotech Research Laboratories Inc., Rockville, Md., for six million shares of stock. The two makers of AIDS screening tests are merging to combine technologies and gain market clout. The agreement remains subject to shareholder approval at special meetings.
