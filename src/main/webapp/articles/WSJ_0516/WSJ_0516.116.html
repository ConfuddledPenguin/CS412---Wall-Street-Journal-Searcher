International Brief -- Hanson PLC: Interim Pretax Profit Rises To $958 Million, a Record

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

Hanson PLC, Britain's largest industrial conglomerate, said interim pretax profit rose 28% to #570 million ($958 million), a record high, for the six months to March 31, with a record contribution from U.S. operations. Although Hanson couldn't be reached for comment on extraordinary gains, industry analysts said they most likely related to disposals during the past six months of assets obtained from the #3.5 billion acquisition of Consolidated Goldfields PLC in August 1989. So far, the disposal program has generated #1.1 billion, Hanson said.

Goldfields' U.S. operations, which realized a 21% profit jump to #180 million from #149 million, made their first contribution to Hanson's half-year results. Last November Hanson announced the sale of four Arc America companies, Goldfields assets, for $650 million.
