Who's News: Metropolitan Savings & Loan Association

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

METROPOLITAN SAVINGS & LOAN ASSOCIATION (Seattle) -- Ferris L. Dracobly, senior vice president of this financial institution, was named interim chief executive officer, succeeding Judson S. Shorett, who resigned as president, chief executive and a director to pursue other interests. The company didn't state a timetable for filling the posts.
