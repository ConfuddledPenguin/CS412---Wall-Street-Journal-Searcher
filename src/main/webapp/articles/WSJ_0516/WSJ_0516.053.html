French Current Account Data

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

PARIS -- France's current account balance of payments surplus widened in March to 1.33 billion francs ($241 million) from a revised surplus of 826 million francs in February, according to seasonally adjusted figures released by the Economics Ministry. February's figure was revised downward from a previously estimated surplus of 1.9 billion francs, and brought the accumulated surplus for the first three months of this year to 5.47 billion francs, down from a surplus of 9.93 billion francs in the same 1989 period.

On an unadjusted basis, the French current account showed a surplus of 883 million francs in March, compared with a deficit of 4.12 billion francs in February.
