Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

NEWSPAPERS: The Forward, a national English-language weekly Jewish newspaper, is expected to be launched on May 25. Being published by Forward Publishing Co., the paper initially will be distributed in the New York metropolitan area and will begin national distribution on Sept. 14. Seth Lipsky, a former Wall Street Journal editor, will be president and chief executive officer of Forward Publishing Co. and editor of the Forward.

WHO'S NEWS: Atlantic Records, a unit of Time Warner Inc., named Senior Vice President Melvyn R. Lewinter to the additional post of chief financial officer.
