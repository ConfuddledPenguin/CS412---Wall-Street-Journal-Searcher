Who's News: Parsons Elects Pieroni Chairman as Leonhard Retires From Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Leonard J. Pieroni became chief executive officer and was elected chairman of Parsons Corp., succeeding William E. Leonhard, who retired after 15 years at the helm. Mr. Pieroni, 51 years old, was elected chairman at a board meeting, when he also assumed the chief executive title. He had been named to that post in April.

Mr. Pieroni has held various positions at Parsons since joining one of its subsidiaries more than 17 years ago. Last year, he was named executive vice president in charge of corporate planning. Those duties will be divided among other executives, Parsons said.

Mr. Leonhard, 75, joined Parsons in 1966, was named chief executive in 1975 and chairman in 1978. The engineering and construction company was acquired by its employees in 1985.
