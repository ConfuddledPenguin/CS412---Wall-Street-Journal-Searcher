Business Brief -- Northern Telecom Ltd.: Concern Expected to Build Research Facility in Ottawa

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

Northern Telecom Ltd., New York, is expected to announce today a $100 million project to build a new research and development facility to house its research and development operations in the Ottawa area, according to individuals familiar with the company's plans. Northern, which has been seeking ways to cut overhead, declined to comment on its plans. But the telecommunications equipment manufacturer currently maintains satellite research and development operations in three leased sites in the Ottawa area. These work with its main Bell Northern Research Center in Nepean, Ont., in a building that is owned by Northern.

The new facility will be built next to the existing BNR lab in Nepean, and will add 600,000 square feet to the operation, bringing the total space to 1.3 million square feet, the individuals said. Northern currently employs 4,500 workers in its Ottawa research and development operations.

They said the consolidation into the new site won't lead to a work-force reduction.
