Memotec Data Sees Flat Net

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

MONTREAL -- Memotec Data Inc., a telecommunications, computer systems and communications products concern, expects 1990 net income will be "about flat" with the year-earlier 20.1 million Canadian dollars (US$17 million), or 52 Canadian cents (44 U.S. cents) a share, said Morrie Glick, vice president, finance. Mr. Glick said after the annual meeting that earnings each quarter are expected to increase from year-earlier levels for the remainder of this year. Memotec had a first-quarter net loss, after special charges, of C$4.1 million, or 10 Canadian cents a share, on revenue of C$89.5 million.
