Business Brief -- Cincinnati Milacron Inc.: Firm to Supply Equipment For Toyota Plant in U.S.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

Cincinnati Milacron Inc. said it received a contract for more than $6 million to supply plastics molding equipment to Toyota Motor Manufacturing USA, a unit of Toyota Motor Corp. of Japan. Both Milacron and Toyota said the contract is a breakthrough because it marks the beginning of a long-term supplier relationship between Milacron and Toyota's plant in Georgetown, Ky. The plastics machinery currently in operation at Georgetown was purchased in Japan.

Three large injection molding machines and related equipment will be supplied by the Cincinnati company. The machines will be used to make large interior plastic parts for Toyota Camry sedans and a new station wagon that Toyota will introduce in 1992.

The machinery is to be installed at the Georgetown facility in May 1991.
