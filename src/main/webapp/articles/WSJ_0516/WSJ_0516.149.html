Who's News: Black & Decker Picks Hammes of Chrysler To Lead Largest Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

TOWSON, Md. -- Black & Decker Corp., as expected, named Michael N. Hammes, Chrysler Corp.'s vice president of international operations, to the post of executive vice president. Mr. Hammes, 48 years old, will head Black & Decker's largest unit, the $2 billion Power Tools and Home Improvement Group. Mr. Hammes, who joined Chrysler in 1986 after almost 30 years with Ford Motor Co., has extensive experience in international operations and joint-venture activities.

Black & Decker has been searching for an executive with global experience to fill the job since February when George M. Sherman left the company to become president of Danaher Corp.
