Who's News: BankAmerica Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

BANKAMERICA Corp. (San Francisco) -- Richard V. Fulp was named executive vice president of Bank of America, this holding company's main unit. Mr. Fulp, 47 years old, previously was senior vice president. He will continue to oversee the bank's recently formed custody and trust services division. He also is in charge of the bank's trust and investment services division.
