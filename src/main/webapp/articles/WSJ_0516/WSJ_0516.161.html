Dravo Pleads Guilty To Mail Fraud Count

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

PITTSBURGH -- Dravo Corp. has agreed to enter a guilty plea to one count of mail fraud in connection with a criminal filing prepared by the U.S. Attorney for South Carolina. The government's filing, which Dravo revealed in a report to the Securities and Exchange Commission, stems from improper charges for steel pipe provided in 1985 by a former Dravo division to the U.S. Department of Energy's Savannah River, S.C., plant.

In agreeing to enter a guilty plea, Dravo said it would reimburse the Department of Energy for $165,000, the amount improperly billed.

Dravo noted that while a fine may be imposed as part of the sentence, the company does not believe that either the fine, or restitution, will have a material effect on the company's financial performance.
