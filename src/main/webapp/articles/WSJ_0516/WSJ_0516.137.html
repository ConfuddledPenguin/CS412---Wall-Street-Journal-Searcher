International Brief -- Bass PLC: Sale of Crest Hotel Chain To Trusthouse Is Completed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

Bass PLC, a major British brewing company, said it completed the sale of its Crest Hotel chain to Trusthouse Forte PLC, a U.K. hotels group. Bass said Trusthouse Forte will pay #300 million ($504.5 million) in cash, lifting the total proceeds from Bass's hotel sales thus far this year to #400 million.

Bass said the sale is the "most significant step" so far in its continuing program of disposing hotel properties that no longer fit into its long-term strategy of focusing on the development of its Holiday Inn brand world-wide.

Last August, Bass bought the Holiday Inn hoteliers in the U.S. for $1.98 billion. It has also held a number of Holiday Inn franchises in the U.S. since acquiring Holiday Corp.'s operations outside North America in 1988.

The transaction with Trusthouse Forte will involve the sale of 43 hotels, while Bass will retain four hotels in the U.K. and four in continental Europe.

Trusthouse Forte's chief executive, Rocco Forte, said the deal was an "excellent purchase," which is expected to make a positive contribution to profits in the current financial year. "The Crest Hotels we are buying are in very good condition and are well managed," he added.
