Business Brief -- International Monetary Exchange: Singapore to Offer Trading In Euroyen Futures Options

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

The Singapore International Monetary Exchange said it will launch a Euroyen futures options contract June 19. The new contract will provide hedging and trading opportunities for market players currently trading the Euroyen cash and futures contracts, said Simex.

The exchange also trades three-month Eurodollar deposit rate futures, currency, gold, the Nikkei stock index futures and fuel oil futures.

Simex said First Continental Trading Inc. will be a market-maker for the Euroyen options contract.
