Business Brief -- Outokumpu Group of Finland: Company Agrees to Acquire Division of MMR Holding

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

Outokumpu Engineering Enterprises Inc., a U.S. holding company for the Outokumpu Group of Finland, said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire H-R International, an engineering construction company based in Edison, N.J., and Houston. H-R International, which serves the chemical and industrial process industries, is the engineering division of MMR Holding Corp., Baton Rouge, La., which is operating under bankruptcy court protection from creditors. H-R International wasn't included in the Chapter 11 Bankruptcy Code filing.

The sale is subject to federal court approval. Terms weren't disclosed. Outokumpu Engineering Enterprises, is based in Owings Mills, Md.

The Outokumpu Group is in the copper and base metals business. It is a major stainless steel producer, with 10 companies in the U.S.
