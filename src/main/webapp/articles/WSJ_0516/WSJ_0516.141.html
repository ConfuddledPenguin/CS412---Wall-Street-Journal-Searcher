Business Brief -- Atrix Laboratories Inc.: Company Files for Exchange Of Common-Stock Warrants

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

Atrix Laboratories Inc. said it has filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an exchange offer wherein 646,875 common-stock purchase warrants will be offered by the company to holders of currently outstanding common-stock purchase warrants. The company, based in Fort Collins, Colo., develops and produces therapeutic drugs for periodontal disease.

In the exchange, one new warrant will be swapped for each outstanding warrant. Current warrant holders aren't required to accept the offer.

Each new warrant to be issued will entitle the holder to purchase one share of common stock at the price of $2 until Aug. 15, 1990, the company said.

In over the counter trading yesterday, Atrix closed at $3.438, bid.
