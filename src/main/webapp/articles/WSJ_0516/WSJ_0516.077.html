Canada Savings Bonds Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government raised the interest rate on all Canada Savings Bonds to 11.5% a year from 10.5%. The new rate will be in effect for the five months beginning June 1 and ending Oct. 31. Canada Savings Bonds can be redeemed at any time at face value. They can be purchased only by Canadian residents and are one of the government's main instruments for raising money.

Canadians have been redeeming Canada Savings Bonds heavily in recent months to take advantage of competing instruments with higher interest rates.
