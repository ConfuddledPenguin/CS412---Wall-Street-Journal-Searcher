Business Brief -- Boulevard Bancorp Inc.: Work Force Cut to Result In a Second-Quarter Charge

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

Boulevard Bancorp Inc. said it will take a $650,000 charge against its second-quarter earnings as a result of a 10% reduction in its work force. The Chicago-based bank holding company, which has assets of $1.7 billion, has about 865 employees.

Boulevard said the reduction is one of several actions it is taking to improve long-term profitability and shareholder returns. The company said it expects the measures to result in annual savings of between $2 million to $2.5 million.

In the year-earlier quarter, Boulevard earned $2.5 million, or 39 cents a share.
