Business Brief -- Borden Inc.: Firm Buys Pasta Business From Campbell Soup Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

Borden Inc. said it acquired a Canadian pasta and pasta sauce business from Campbell Soup Co. and a South Carolina producer of foils and papers for laminates. The Gattuso pasta business has estimated annual sales of $14 million; it will become part of Borden's Catelli unit in Montreal.

New York-based Borden also acquired Letron Inc., a maker of foils, with estimated 1990 sales of $20 million. That business complements Borden's Orchard Decorative Products unit, which supplies decorative printed overlays for wallcoverings, furniture, floor tiles and other applications.

Borden, with annual sales of $7.6 billion in 1989, produces foods, non-food consumer products, packaging and industrial products.
