International Brief -- Daimler-Benz AG: Company Predicts Growth In Sales to Continue in 1990

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

Daimler-Benz AG predicted further sales growth for 1990 as it continues its strategy of widespread diversification. The Stuttgart, West Germany-based auto, aerospace and electronics group said first-quarter sales rose to 19.5 billion Deutsche marks ($11.9 billion) from 17 billion marks in the year-earlier period, reflecting to a major extent the acquisition last year of aerospace concern Messerschmitt-Boelkow-Blohm GmbH (MBB). Group sales are expected to rise to 85 billion marks this year from 76.39 billion marks a year earlier.
