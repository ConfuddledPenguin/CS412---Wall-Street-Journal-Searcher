Who's News: McGraw-Hill Ryerson Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

MCGRAW-HILL RYERSON Ltd. (Toronto) -- Michael G. Richardson was named president and chief executive officer, succeeding Robert M. Freeman, who left the company. The publishing company said Mr. Richardson will remain an executive vice president with the parent company, McGraw-Hill Inc., which holds about 70% of the Canadian concern. Mr. Richardson was president of the Canadian subsidiary in 1986 and 1987. He was then promoted to the position of president of International Book Group and replaced as president of the Canadian unit by Mr. Freeman.
