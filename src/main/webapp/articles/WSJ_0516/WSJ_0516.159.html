Who's News: Pitney Bowes Realigns Officers in New Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Pitney Bowes Inc. named four officers to head new units in what the business equipment, supplies and financial services company called a "strategic realignment." Hiro R. Hiranandani, 52 years old, previously president, business systems, international unit, was appointed president, mailing systems, a new unit combining U.S. and international operations. Mr. Hiranandani succeeds James L. Bast, 54, formerly president, U.S. business systems, who left the company.

Carole F. St. Mark, 47, formerly president, business supplies and services, was named president, logistics systems and business services.

Marc C. Breslawsky, 47, formerly vice president and chief executive officer, Dictaphone Corp. and facsimile and copier systems, was appointed president, office systems.

Douglas A. Riggs, 45, formerly vice president, corporate communications and external affairs, was named vice president, corporate planning, communications and external affairs.
