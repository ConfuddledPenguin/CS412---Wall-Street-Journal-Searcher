Business Brief -- Castle & Cooke Inc.: Spinoff of Dole Food Unit Gets Delayed Indefinitely

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

Castle & Cooke Inc., Los Angeles, said that its planned spinoff of its Dole Food Co. unit has been delayed indefinitely because of financing concerns and uncertainty over the tax treatment of the transaction. The spinoff to shareholders is intended to split Castle & Cooke into two separate companies, one containing the Dole operations and the other, Castle & Cooke's smaller real-estate development operations.

When Castle & Cooke proposed the spinoff in February, it said the transaction would be completed by the end of next month, subject to various approvals, including an Internal Revenue Service ruling that the transaction would be tax-free to the company and to its shareholders.

"The revenue ruling has not yet been received and very recently there has been a substantial tightening of credit available for companies in the real-estate industry," Castle & Cooke said.

The company said its board decided against proceeding with the transaction as long as "acceptable bank financing" isn't available to the independent real-estate development company that would be created.
