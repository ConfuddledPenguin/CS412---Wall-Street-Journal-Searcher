Average Interest Rate Falls At Citicorp Weekly Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.1478% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.27% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $431 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.129% to 8.148%.

Citicorp also said the average rate fell to 8.1466% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.2968% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $391 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.105% to 8.15%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
