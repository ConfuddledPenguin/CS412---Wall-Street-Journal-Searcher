Tab Products Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Tab Products Co. said it retained Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. Inc. to help the company evaluate alternatives to improve stockholder value. A spokesman for the company, which manufactures and markets office filing and furniture systems, said the evaluation probably will take three to six months.

For the nine months ended Feb. 28, the company had net income of $3.8 million, or 58 cents a share, on revenue of $104 million.
