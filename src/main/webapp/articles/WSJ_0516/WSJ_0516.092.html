Who's News: Atlantic Richfield Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

ATLANTIC RICHFIELD Co. (Los Angeles) -- Marlan W. Downey was appointed to the new post of senior vice president of exploration with the company's Arco International Oil & Gas Co. division, based in Plano, Texas. Atlantic Richfield is a diversified oil, gas and minerals company. Mr. Downey was previously chief executive officer of Roxanna Oil Co. of Houston.
