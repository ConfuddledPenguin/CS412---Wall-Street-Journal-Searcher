WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Colonial Gas Co., offering of 600,000 shares, via A.G. Edwards & Sons, Inc. and Edward D. Jones & Co.

General Motors Acceptance Corp., a unit of General Motors Corp., shelf offering of up to $4.3 billion of medium-term notes, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Photronics Inc., offering of 1,450,000 shares, 1.2 million of which will be sold by the company and 250,000 shares by holders, via Prudential-Bache Capital Funding and Tucker Anthony Inc.

Western Massachusetts Electric Co., a unit of Northeast Utilities, shelf offering of up to $175 million of first mortgage bonds.
