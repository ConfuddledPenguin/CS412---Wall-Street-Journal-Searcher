GE Wins Jet Engine Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

FAIRFIELD, Conn. -- General Electric Co. said its aircraft engines subsidiary received a $100 million order for CF34-3A1 jet engines from a partner of Lufthansa Airlines. The contract covers engines for 13 aircraft, and an option for 12 more.

Deutsche Luftverkehrgesellschaft, West Germany, is ordering the jets, called Canadair regional jets, from Canadair Ltd. The jets, which are expected to begin service in 1992, seat 50 passengers.
