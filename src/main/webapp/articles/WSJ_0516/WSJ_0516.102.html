Financing Business: Lidak Pharmaceuticals

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

LIDAK PHARMACEUTICALS said it completed its initial public offering of one million units at $5 each. Each unit consists of five common shares and five redeemable Class A warrants. Each Class A warrant is exercisable to buy one common share and one redeemable Class B warrant at $1.50 until May 8, 1995. Each Class B warrant holder may buy one common share at $2.25 until May 8, 1995. D.H. Blair & Co. managed the underwriters, the La Jolla, Calif., company said. Proceeds will be used to fund human clinical trials of a therapeutic product to treat herpes and certain inflammatory skin disorders and for research and development of other pharmaceutical products.
