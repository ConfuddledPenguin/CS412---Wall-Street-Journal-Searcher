VeloBind Inc. Settlement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

FREMONT, Calif. -- VeloBind Inc. settled its patent infringement action brought against Southwest Plastic Binding Co. last year in the U.S. District Court for Missouri's Eastern District, the two companies said. As part of the settlement, Southwest, based in St. Louis, Mo., admitted that VeloBind's binding strip patent is valid and that Southwest's binding strips infringed on VeloBind's patent, the companies said.

All other terms of the settlement are confidential, the companies said.
