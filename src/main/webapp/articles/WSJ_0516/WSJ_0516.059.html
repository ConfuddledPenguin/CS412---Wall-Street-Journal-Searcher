Financing Business: New Line Cinema Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

NEW LINE CINEMA Corp., (New York) -- This distributor of films agreed on a $75 million three-year revolving-credit facility from a bank group led by Chemical Banking Corp.'s Chemical Bank. New Line will use the credit, which replaces a $35 million facility the company had with Chemical Bank, for general purposes, including movie production.
