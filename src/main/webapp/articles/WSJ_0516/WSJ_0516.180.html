Financing Business: Fleet/Norstar Financial Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

FLEET/NORSTAR FINANCIAL GROUP Inc. sold $150 million of 9.95% notes at par. The notes will mature on May 15, 1993. The Providence, R.I.-based financial-services company said it will use the proceeds for general corporate purposes. The issue was co-managed by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Salomon Brothers Inc. The notes were issued as a portion of a $1 billion shelf registration made with the Securities and Exchange Commission in 1988. Following the current offering, about $234 million of debt securities will remain in registration, Fleet said.
