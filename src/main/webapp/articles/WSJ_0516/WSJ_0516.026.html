United Technologies Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

WASHINGTON -- United Technologies Corp. won a $49.2 million Army contract for 36 UH-60L helicopters.

Norfolk Shipbuilding & Drydock Corp. got a $25.3 million Navy contract for destroyer overhaul.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. was given a $18.6 million Air Force pact for aircraft electronics repair kits.
