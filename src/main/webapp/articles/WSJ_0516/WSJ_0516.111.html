PAUL C. MARSH, an analyst at Bateman Eichler, Hill Richards Inc., places the value of Hanna-Barbera Productions Inc. at about $300 million, and the rest of Great American Communications Co.'s entertainment assets at about $200 million. Yesterday's edition misstated the latter figure. (See: "Media: MCA Views Top Creator of Cartoons" -- WSJ May 15, 1990)

---

NINTENDO Co., based in Kyoto, Japan, reported pretax profit at the parent company of 55.246 billion yen in the seven-month fiscal period ended March 31. In yesterday's edition, the profit figure and the city where Nintendo is based were misstated.

(See: "International Brief -- Nintendo Co.: Tokyo Firm's Profit Surges In Period Ended March 31" -- WSJ May 15, 1990)
