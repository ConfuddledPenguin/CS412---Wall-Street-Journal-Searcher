Office-Products Firm Sees Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- Office Products of America Inc. said it expects to report a net operating loss for 1990, its first full year of operation, despite substantial operating revenue generated from its three stores. The office-products warehouse concern said the loss will result from start-up and administrative expenses associated with its already-opened stores and stores it intends to open this year. In the first quarter, Office Products reported a net loss of $798,079, or nine cents a share, on revenue of about $1 million. The company went public in August 1989.
