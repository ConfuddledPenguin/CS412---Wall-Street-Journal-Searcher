Divi Hotels Debt Payment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

ARUBA -- Divi Hotels N.V. said it didn't plan to make the interest payment due yesterday on its $60 million of 14 1/2% senior debentures. The company said it had previously announced that it was entering into a bond exchange process with the holders of its two issues of debt securities.

Divi said that if the exchange offers are completed, this would reduce the company's cash debt service requirement for three years, allowing the company time to implement its strategy of selling or recapitalizing its ownership interests in its hotel and resort properties. The offers will expire May 24, unless extended, the company said.
