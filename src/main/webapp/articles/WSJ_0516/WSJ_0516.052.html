South Africa Gold Output Fell

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa -- South African gold output fell 3.5% to 1,557,000 ounces in April from 1,613,000 ounces a year earlier, according to the Chamber of Mines, which represents the country's mining houses. April's output represents a 2.9% decrease from March's 1,603,000 ounces.

Total output for the first four months of the year totaled 4,766,000 ounces, down 26% from 6,440,000 ounces in the same period of 1989.

---

Corrections & Amplifications

SOUTH AFRICA'S gold output for the first four months of the year totaled 6,322,000 ounces, down 1.8% from 6,440,000 ounces in the year-earlier period. The figures were misstated in yesterday's edition.

(WSJ May 17,1990)
