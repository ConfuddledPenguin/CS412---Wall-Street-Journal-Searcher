Who's News: Foote, Cone & Belding Communications Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

FOOTE, CONE & BELDING COMMUNICATIONS Inc. (Chicago) -- Gregory W. Blaine, director of corporate resources and development, and Laurel Cutler, vice chairman and director of marketing planning, were named directors of this advertising concern, filling one vacancy and expanding the board to 15.
