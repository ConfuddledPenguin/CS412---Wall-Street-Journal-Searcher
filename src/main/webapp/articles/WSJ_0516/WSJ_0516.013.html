ICN Biomedicals Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

COSTA MESA, Calif. -- ICN Biomedicals Inc. said it adjusted the price it paid to buy the biomedical business of GRC International Inc. to $37.6 million from $41.5 million. ICN and GRC International, formerly Flow General Inc., signed a definitive agreement for ICN to buy the biomedical business last October; the accord was subject to an audit of its inventories. An ICN spokesman said a drop in the level of those inventories between Flow's July 31 fiscal year end, which was used to formulate the original purchase price, and Dec. 31 prompted the adjustment.
