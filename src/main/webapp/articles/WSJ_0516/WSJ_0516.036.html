Who's News: BICC Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/16/90

BICC GROUP (London) -- This cable and construction company said Michael C. Eisner was named chief financial officer, senior vice president and a director of New York-based subsidiary Cablec Corp. Mr. Eisner, 50-year-old former chief financial officer at Sony Corp. of America, succeeds Charles Killeen, who becomes executive vice president of the industrial electronics group.
