Dividend News: Leisure Technology Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

LEISURE TECHNOLOGY Inc. said it won't make the June 1 dividend payment on its $2.25 cumulative convertible exchangeable preferred stock. "It's not a well kept secret that the housing business is in very difficult circumstances," said Michael L. Tenzer, chairman, president and chief executive officer of the Los Angeles real estate developer. He said the company's housing results are poor in the northeastern U.S. and in Florida and have moderated in California. He declined to indicate if the previous 56-cent-a-share quarterly dividend on the preferred would be resumed in subsequent quarters. For the third quarter ended Dec. 31, the company reported net income of $231,000 on revenue of $55.5 million.
