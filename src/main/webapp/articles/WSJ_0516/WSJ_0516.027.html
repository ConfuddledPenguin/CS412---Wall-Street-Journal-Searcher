Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/16/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- James Li, 43 years old, previously president for Asia, Pacific and Australia, based in Hong Kong, at the Travel Related Services unit of this travel and financial services company, was named executive vice president, chief quality and credit officer, of the unit, succeeding Ray Larkin, 64, who is to retire early next year.
