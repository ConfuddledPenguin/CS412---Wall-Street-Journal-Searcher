Marketing Brief -- Carolco Pictures Inc.: De Laurentiis Entertainment Reorganization Plan Cleared

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

A bankruptcy court confirmed the previously announced revised reorganization plan for De Laurentiis Entertainment Group Inc., a movie studio. The Los Angeles-based De Laurentiis concern revised the plan in March as part of an agreement by Carolco Pictures Inc. to acquire the company. De Laurentiis filed for creditor protection under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code in September 1988 after a string of box-office bombs.

Under the plan, Carolco will acquire 49.9% of De Laurentiis's common shares, and creditors will receive the remainder along with cash and certain rights and warrants. Because of certain preferred stock to be issued to Carolco as part of the reorganization, Carolco will own from 60% to 70% of shareholders equity in the new company.

Current shareholders of De Laurentiis will receive no distribution under the plan.
