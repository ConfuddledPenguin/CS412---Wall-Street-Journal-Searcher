Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc., a unit of this business information provider, named Daniel N. Heimowitz executive vice president and director of the public finance department. Mr. Heimowitz, 36, succeeds Freda Johnson, who resigned in January to start a consulting company within the industry. After Ms. Johnson resigned, her duties were split between Mr. Heimowitz, who then was vice president and managing director of ratings and Joseph Rosenblum, vice president and managing director, ratings administration. Mr. Heimowitz joined Moody's in 1977. Moody's public finance area grades the credit-worthiness of municipalities and their securities.
