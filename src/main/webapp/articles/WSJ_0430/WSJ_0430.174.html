Fidelity National Financial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fidelity National Financial Inc. said 210,542 shares were tendered in a Dutch auction for up to 250,000 shares of its stock. The shares were tendered at, or below, the $10-a-share purchase price the company said it preliminarily expects to pay for the shares. Determination of the final purchase price for the shares is subject to final confirmation of the number of shares tendered and delivery of the shares, Fidelity National said.

Fidelity National issues title insurance and performs other title-related services in 31 states and the District of Columbia.
