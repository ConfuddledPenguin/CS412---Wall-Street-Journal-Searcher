Business Brief -- Lincoln NC Realty Fund Inc.: Silverstone Partners' Offer Of $19 Million Is Rejected

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

Lincoln NC Realty Fund Inc., Palo Alto, Calif., said it rejected an offer from Silverstone Partners, a closely held Los Angeles partnership, because it viewed the proposal as unfair. In a letter, Silverstone Partners offered to buy all of Lincoln's common stock and warrants outstanding at $9.21875 a share and 3.1 cents a warrant in a transaction valued at about $19 million.

In composite trading Friday on the American Stock Exchange, Lincoln fell 12.5 cents to $8.375.

Lincoln previously rebuffed Silverstone. In March, the investment trust terminated a letter of agreement to be acquired by Silverstone because Lincoln's banker couldn't confirm the financial fairness of the deal.

Silverstone, which couldn't be reached to comment, holds 8.6% of Lincoln.
