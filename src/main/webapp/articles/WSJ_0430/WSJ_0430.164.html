Letters to the Editor: Troubled Mortgages

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

In your March 23 Heard on the Street ("Real Estate Woes May Hurt Big Insurers"), you quote selectively from a report by Fox-Pitt Kelton, identifying mortgage exposure as an investment concern. The report found that "the level of troubled mortgages was actually down vs. year-end 1988," although many commercial banks and savings and loans had significant increases in their troubled mortgages during the year. The report also said: "We do not view the {Lincoln National} mortgage portfolio or its potential for deterioration from its present condition as an investment risk of any significance."

Robert W. Crispin

Fort Wayne, Ind.
