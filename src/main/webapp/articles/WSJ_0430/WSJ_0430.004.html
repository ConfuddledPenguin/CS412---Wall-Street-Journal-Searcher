Dividend News: Kentucky Medical Insurance Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

KENTUCKY MEDICAL INSURANCE Co., Louisville, declared a 4-for-3 stock split of its Class A and Class B stock. The split will increase the number of shares outstanding to about 1.4 million from one million. The insurance company said the stock split will be paid May 24 to stock of record May 10.
