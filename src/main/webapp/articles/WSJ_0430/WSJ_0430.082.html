SciMed Life Systems Is Sued

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- SciMed Life Systems Inc. said competitor C.R. Bard Inc. filed a suit in federal court here alleging that SciMed is infringing on a C.R. Bard heart-catheter patent. In a statement, Dale Spencer, president of SciMed, said the company is reviewing the matter with counsel. He said, "SciMed's policy is not to violate the patent rights of others. SciMed intends to vigorously defend against this suit."

SciMed, a medical products maker, has run into other legal troubles. Two suits by an Eli Lilly & Co. unit, which allege that SciMed infringed on Lilly heart-catheter patents, are still pending, according to Mr. Spencer, who was reached at home.

Officials of C.R. Bard, based in Murray Hill, N.J., couldn't be reached.
