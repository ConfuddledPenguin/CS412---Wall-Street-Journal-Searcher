CMS Venture Sets Power Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

DEARBORN, Mich. -- CMS Energy Corp. said the Midland Cogeneration Venture will sell 200 megawatts of power to Ontario Hydro, Canada's largest electric utility, from May through September. A CMS spokesman described the transaction as the first major sale for the Midland Cogeneration Venture, but he wouldn't provide a dollar value for the deal. CMS is a 49%-owner of the Midland Cogeneration Venture.

The Midland Cogeneration Venture was designed originally as a nuclear power plant but was abandoned before completion in 1984. The plant was reopened on March 16 as a gas-fired plant.
