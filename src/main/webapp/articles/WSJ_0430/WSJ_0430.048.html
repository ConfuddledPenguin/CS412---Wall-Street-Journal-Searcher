OECD Jobless Rate Steady

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

PARIS -- The average unemployment rate in 16 Western industrialized countries in February was 6%, unchanged from the previous two months, the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development said. Jobless rates in North America and Japan were unchanged, while West Germany, Italy and Britain experienced a mild easing, and Australian unemployment drifted higher. The OECD said the standardized unemployment rate fell in five of the six countries for which March data are available, while unemployment in the sixth country, Belgium, was unchanged.

The unemployment rate in the European Community was steady in February at 8.6%.
