Who's News: Alpharel Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

ALPHAREL Inc. (Camarillo, Calif.) -- This financially troubled maker of computerized data storage systems said its president and chief executive officer, John A. Fasching, resigned. Alpharel said Mr. Fasching also resigned from the company's board. Alpharel's chairman, Robert T. Bruce, will assume the additional duties of president and chief executive, the company said. Mr. Fasching couldn't be reached for comment.
