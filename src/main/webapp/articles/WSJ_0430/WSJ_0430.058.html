Letters to the Editor: Eat Your Heart Out

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

Spare me from the federal government giving a seal of approval to healthy foods ("AHA to Drop Seal Endorsing Low Fat Foods," April 2). If I can't trust the Heart Association to evaluate foods in good faith, I certainly don't trust the FDA to do it. Why do people expect the government to do everything for them? I suspect what some are really looking for is someone or some seal to say junk food is good for you after all. Guess maybe we'll have to read labels and exercise a little common sense after all. Darn it.

Melissa Gaskill

Austin, Texas
