Business Brief -- Pinnacle West Capital Corp.: Malapai Mining Unit's Sale To Firm in France Is Set

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

Pinnacle West Capital Corp., Phoenix, Ariz., said it agreed to sell its Malapai Resources Co. uranium mining unit to an affiliate of Electricite de France of Paris. The cash price wasn't disclosed. Pinnacle West, parent of Arizona Public Service Co., the state's largest electric utility, said in January it had put Malapai up for sale. Proceeds will be used to cut Pinnacle West debt of about $1.1 billion.

Malapai had total equity and liabilities of $86.4 million at Dec. 31, Pinnacle West said, and expects a $36.6 million pretax loss for the 18 months ending June 30.

Pinnacle West said Electricite de France is the only electric utility company in France, where about 75% of all electric power is nuclear-generated. Malapai, which mines uranium for nuclear plants, would be operated by Total Minerals Corp., a unit of Total Cie. Francaise des Petroles, under a separate pact between Total and the Electricite de France affiliate, which is in formation. The sale is subject to a definitive agreement and regulatory approvals. Pinnacle West said it hopes the deal can be completed by the end of June.
