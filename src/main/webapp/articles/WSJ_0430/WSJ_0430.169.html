Who's News: Dofasco Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

DOFASCO Inc. (Hamilton, Ontario) -- Paul J. Phoenix was named chairman in addition to his current position of chief executive officer. Mr. Phoenix will be succeeded as president by Willam L. Wallace, who remains chief operating officer. Frank H. Sherman, 74 years old, will retire as chairman of the steel-making concern, becoming honorary chairman.
