FHP INTERNATIONAL Corp.'s recently reported fiscal third quarter earnings of 35 cents a share were about in line with analysts' average estimate of 34 cents a share. Because the estimate was incorrect in Thursday's Quarterly Earnings Surprises table, due to a database error at Zacks Investment Research, the company's results shouldn't have been included among negative surprises. (See: "Quarterly Earnings Surprises" -- WSJ April 26, 1990)
