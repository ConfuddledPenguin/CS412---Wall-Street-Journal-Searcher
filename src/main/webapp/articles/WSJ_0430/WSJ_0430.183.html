New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange announced five new stock listings and the National Market System of the over-the-counter market six.

Joining the Big Board Friday under the symbol EF was Europe Fund Inc., a diversified closed-end management investment company specializing in European equities.

Fingerhut Cos., a Minnetonka, Minn., direct-mail marketer, was listed Wednesday under FHT.

Safeway Inc., an Oakland, Calif., food retailer, started trading Friday under SWY.

Vista Resources Inc., a leather-products company here, begins trading today under VS.

Wahlco Environmental Systems Inc., a San Diego provider of air pollution-control systems for utilities, joined the New York exchange last Wednesday, under the symbol WAL. Europe Fund, Fingerhut, Safeway and Wahlco were listed after their initial public offerings. Vista joins the Big Board from the over-the-counter market.

Joining the OTC market is BE Avionics Inc., a Santa Ana, Calif., maker of passenger control units for airline audio and video entertainment and service systems, which began trading under BEAV.

Granite Construction Inc., a Watsonville, Calif., heavy-construction company, is trading under the symbol GCCO.

Home Federal Savings Bank of Missouri, St. Louis, trades under HFMO.

Orbital Sciences Corp., a Fairfax, Va., space-technology company, goes national OTC under ORBI.

Telebit Corp., a Sunnyvale, Calif., manufacturer of data-transmission products, is trading under TBIT.

Unitrin Inc., a Chicago insurer spun off from Teledyne Inc., is listed under UNIT.

The American Stock Exchange opened trading last Monday on put and call options on the stock of Collagen Corp., a Palo Alto, Calif., manufacturer of products used to care for aging skin and bones.
