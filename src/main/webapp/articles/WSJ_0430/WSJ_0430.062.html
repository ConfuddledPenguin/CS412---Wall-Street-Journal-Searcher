Who's News: Equimark Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

EQUIMARK Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Nicholas J. Zennario resigned as president of the Equibank unit of this bank holding company. Mr. Zennario, who said he will pursue other business interests, will be succeeded by James H. McLaughlin, who was Mr. Zennario's predecessor as president. Mr. McLaughlin left that position in July to work on Equimark's proposed acquisition of National Bancshares Corp. of Texas. That deal fell through Friday.
