Sony's Columbia Calls Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

NEW YORK -- Columbia Pictures Entertainment Inc. said it called for redemption of about $279 million of senior debt, to be repaid from funds provided by its parent Sony Corp. of Japan. The entertainment company said it will redeem $250 million of 10.625% senior subordinated debentures due 2018 at 108.717% of face value. It also will redeem $28.8 million of 14.5% senior subordinated debentures due 2000 at 107.25% of face value. Columbia also will pay accrued interest to the date of redemption on both issues. The securities will be redeemed starting June 1, 1990.
