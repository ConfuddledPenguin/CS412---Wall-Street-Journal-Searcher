NEW YORK -- Macmillan Inc. said its computer unit acquired RightSoft Inc., a Sarasota, Fla., software publisher in a $5 million transaction. RightSoft's major product is RightWriter, a personal-computer software program that checks style and grammar.

Macmillan is a unit of Maxwell Communication Corp., London.
