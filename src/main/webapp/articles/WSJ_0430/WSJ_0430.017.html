Corona to Lift Stake In Breakwater to 27.6% In Exchange of Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

TORONTO -- Corona Corp. said it agreed to acquire an additional 15.8% of Breakwater Resources Ltd., Vancouver, British Columbia, in exchange for about 3.3 million Corona Class A shares with an indicated market value of US$22.1 million. The gold mining company said acquisition of the 10 million Breakwater shares would boost its stake to about 17.4 million shares, or 27.6%, of Breakwater, a mining company. Corona already holds about 7.4 million Breakwater shares, including 500,000 that it said it bought in a private transaction last Thursday.

In American Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Corona Class A shares closed at $6.625, down 12.5 cents. In national over-the-counter trading, Breakwater shares closed at $1.8125, unchanged.

Corona said the 10 million Breakwater shares will be acquired in two transactions, of nine million and one million shares, respectively, which are subject to regulatory approval. It didn't identify the current holders of the shares.

A Breakwater spokeswoman said the company doesn't know the identity of the holders, indicating that the holding of nine million shares had been accumulated recently. The company declined to comment further.

Corona, which has long had a major stake in Breakwater, said it intends to increase its holding further, for investment purposes.
