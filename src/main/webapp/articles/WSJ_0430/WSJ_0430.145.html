Who's News: Colgate-Palmolive Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

COLGATE-PALMOLIVE Co. (New York) -- William A. Sander, 50, was appointed vice president, U.S. operations for this consumer products company. Previously, Mr. Sander was vice president, manufacturing and distribution at Sterling Drug Co.'s Lehn & Fink unit. He succeeds Robert Swinney, who is retiring.
