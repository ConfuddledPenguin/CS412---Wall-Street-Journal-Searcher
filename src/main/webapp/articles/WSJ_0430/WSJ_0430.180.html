Who's News: Herman Miller's Ruch Adds President's Post

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

ZEELAND, Mich. -- Herman Miller Inc., an office furniture maker, said Richard H. Ruch, 60-year-old chief executive officer, was elected to the additional post of president, succeeding Edward R. Simon, 52. Mr. Simon said earlier this month that he was resigning to "provide {Mr. Ruch} with an opportunity for further alignment of the organization."

Philip J. Mercorella, 45, president of the Milcare Inc. unit, was named senior vice president, global sales, marketing and dealer distribution, succeeding David Armstrong, 54, who is taking early retirement as senior vice president, sales and marketing. The company said Mr. Mercorella's post involves broader responsibilities than Mr. Armstrong had. Mr. Mercorella will remain Milcare president until a successor is named.
