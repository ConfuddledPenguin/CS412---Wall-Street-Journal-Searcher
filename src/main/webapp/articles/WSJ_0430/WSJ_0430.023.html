Grumman Corp. Wins Contract From Navy For $142.9 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

WASHINGTON -- Grumman Corp. received a $142.9 million Navy contract for 4E-2C aircraft and A-6E aircraft improvements.

General Motors Corp. won a $132.3 million Army contract for tank transmissions.

Honeywell Inc. was awarded a $123.9 million Army contract for bomb parts.

Rockwell International Corp. got a $76.6 million Air Force contract for missile guidance units.

Raytheon Co. received a $45.9 million Army contract for Hawk missile parts.

FMC Corp. was awarded a $37.9 million Army contract for tank tracks.

General Electric Co. won $29.9 million in contracts for Navy aircraft guidance systems and Army helicopter electronics.

Allied-Signal Corp. got $25.1 million in contracts for Navy aircraft power units and Army gyroscopes.

General Dynamics Corp. received a $16.2 million Navy contract for Sparrow missile parts.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. was awarded a $14 million Army contract for drug surveillance systems.

General Instrument Corp. won a $12.9 million Navy contract for acoustic equipment.
