Canadian Wheat Stocks Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian wheat stocks totaled 15,098,000 metric tons at March 31, up 58% from 9,546,000 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. The stocks included 9,055,000 metric tons on farms, up from 5,335,000 tons a year earlier, and 6,043,000 tons at terminals and other commercial locations, up from 4,211,000 tons. Statistics Canada said stocks of wheat remain at comparatively low levels. A ton of wheat is equal to about 36.74 bushels.

Stocks of barley totaled 5,564,000 metric tons, down 11% from 6,287,000 tons a year earlier. Canola, or rapeseed, totaled 1,805,000 tons, down 27% from 2,474,000 tons.
