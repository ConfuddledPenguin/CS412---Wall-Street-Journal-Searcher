Who's News: Huntington Bancshares Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

HUNTINGTON BANCSHARES Inc. (Columbus, Ohio) -- William J. Lhota, executive vice president, operations for American Electric Power Service Corp.; Gerald E. Mayo, president and chief executive officer of Midland Mutual Life Insurance Co., and Melvin L. Schottenstein, executive vice president and treasurer of M/I Schottenstein Homes Inc., were named directors of this regional bank. Huntington said board membership is increased to 11 from nine, with one of the new members filling a vacancy.
