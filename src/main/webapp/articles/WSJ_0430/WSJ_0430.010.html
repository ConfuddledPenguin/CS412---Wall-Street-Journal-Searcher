Spain Names Labor Minister

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

MADRID -- Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez named Luis Martinez Noval, a 41-year-old Socialist deputy and economist, as his new Minister of Labor. Mr. Martinez Noval succeeds Manuel Chaves, who was designated the Socialist party's candidate to head the regional government of Andalucia in elections set for June 23.

The cabinet change is the only one Mr. Gonzalez is expected to make following his party's victory in last October's general elections.
