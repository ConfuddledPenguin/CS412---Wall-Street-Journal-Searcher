The Prince and the Piper

The boss's fair-haired boy

Doesn't get there overnight;

It takes considerable work,

With things done exactly right.

One must be on constant call,

Showing diligence and flair,

And in time the fair-haired boy

Will most likely have gray hair.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.
