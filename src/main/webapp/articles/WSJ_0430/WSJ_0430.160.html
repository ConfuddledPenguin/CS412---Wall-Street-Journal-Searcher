Who's News: John H. Harland Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

JOHN H. HARLAND Co. (Atlanta) -- Robert R. Woodson was named chief executive officer of this check printer. Mr. Woodson, 57 years old, is currently president and retains that position. He succeeds J. William Robinson, 70, who continues as chairman of the board and chairman of its executive committee.
