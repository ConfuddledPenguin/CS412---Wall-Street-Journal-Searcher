Letters to the Editor: We'll All Be in the Red

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

George Melloan's April 2 Global View "Who Will Pay the Bill for Russia's Decline?" was truly outstanding. Mr. Melloan should be commended for clearly and concisely portraying the capital demands that exist today. As a financial consultant with a global view of investing, I keep asking how all the capital needs that these new-found democracies require will be met. So far, the only answer is probably higher interest rates and greater market uncertainty. To answer the question of who will pay the bill: We all will.

William J. Sharpe

Vice President

Partnership Council Member

Prudential-Bache Securities

St. Petersburg, Fla.
