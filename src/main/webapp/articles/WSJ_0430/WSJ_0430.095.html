Who's News: Pacific Scientific Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

PACIFIC SCIENTIFIC Co. (Newport Beach, Calif.) -- Edward S. Brower, 59-year-old president and chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of chairman, succeeding Harry R. Goff who retired. Mr. Goff, 74, was named director emeritus. John C. Dieckman was elected a director, filling Mr. Goff's position on the board. Mr. Dieckman is a trustee for the McAllister Trusts, which owns beneficially 247,392 shares or 4.4% of Pacific Scientific's stock. Pacific Scientific makes and markets technology based proprietary products for niche markets.
