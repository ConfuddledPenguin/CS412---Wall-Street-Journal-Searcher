Premark Begins Offer for Sikes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

DEERFIELD, Ill. -- Premark International Inc. said it began its tender offer for all the Class A common shares outstanding of Sikes Corp. at $16.40 a share. The consumer and restaurant products concern has agreed to pay about $201 million for Sikes, a Lakeland, Fla., maker of ceramic tile. Premark will also pay $26.78 a share for Sikes's 861,136 Class B common shares.

Premark said the offer is conditioned upon the tender of a majority of the Class A shares. The company said the offer is scheduled to expire May 24.
