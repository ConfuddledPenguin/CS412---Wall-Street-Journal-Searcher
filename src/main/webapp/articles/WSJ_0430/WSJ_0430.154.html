Letters to the Editor: Dress Rehearsal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

Regarding your April 5 page-one story on school dress codes: If you tell those inner-city kids that when they play for the New York Yankees they will have to wear a uniform, maybe they can understand a dress code in high school isn't so bad. If you also tell them that when they become executives at General Motors they will have to wear a uniform, maybe they'll get the picture.

Everyone wears a uniform -- even Santa Claus.

Alan Hirsch

New York
