Letters to the Editor: Revenge of the Turtles

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

It's true that parents don't "get it" when it comes to Teen-age Mutant Ninja Turtles ("Ninja Turtle Juggernaut Demolishes Box Office Record," April 3). I get it -- in spades. The Turtles are my parents' revenge for the Three Stooges. Their antics consumed my every waking moment 31 years ago the way my four-year-old now lives and breathes Cowabungas. Jack Jackson

Needham, Mass.
