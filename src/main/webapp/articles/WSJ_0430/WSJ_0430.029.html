Tons of Toys Inc. Files A Reorganization Plan At Bankruptcy Court

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

WAREHAM, Mass. -- Tons of Toys Inc. said it filed a reorganization plan with the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Boston. The plan calls for Tons of Toys to be merged with T.H.E. Corp., a closely held toy retailer in California, the company said. The plan also calls for Tons of Toys' unsecured creditors to get about $2.8 million, which the company said will represent at least 20% of their claims approved by the court. T.H.E. stockholders will own about 65% of Tons of Toys' stock after the reorganization.

Tons of Toys' remaining stock will be divided between holders of the company's $5 million of subordinated debt, an unidentified investor and current shareholders, the company said.

The plan is subject to various conditions, including receipt of $4 million in equity financing by certain investors, the company said.

Certain Tons of Toys' creditors last December filed a petition to place the company in involuntary bankruptcy. A motion by creditors now is pending before the bankruptcy court to convert the bankruptcy proceeding to a liquidation under Chapter 7 of the Bankruptcy Code, Tons of Toys said. The motion is scheduled for a hearing next Monday.
