Who's News: Dartmouth Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

DARTMOUTH BANCORP (Hookset, N.H.) -- Daniel Edgar resigned as president and chief executive officer of this bank holding company and its Dartmouth Bank affiliate. Raymond Closson, chairman, will become acting chief executive of the holding company. Robert Keller, senior executive vice president and chief operating officer of Dartmouth Bank, will become acting chief executive of the bank.
