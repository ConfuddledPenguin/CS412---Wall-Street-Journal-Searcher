Who's News: LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton S.A.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

LVMH MOET HENNESSY LOUIS VUITTON S.A. (Paris) -- Bernard Arnault, chairman of this French luxury-goods group, said a successor has been found for Henry Racamier, who resigned Thursday as chairman of Louis Vuitton S.A. Mr. Arnault said Jean Dromer, currently president of Societe Financiere Agache, will take the reins at the luggage and leather-goods unit. Mr. Racamier resigned following the Paris Court of Appeal's announcement Thursday of its decision not to cancel a bond issue that helped consolidate power within LVMH in the hands of Mr. Arnault. Mr. Racamier launched several court cases challenging the bonds.
