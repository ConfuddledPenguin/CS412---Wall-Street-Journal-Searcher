Business Brief -- Pic `N' Save Corp.: California Retail Chain Sets Annual Meeting for June 20

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

Pic `N' Save Corp., the target of a potential takeover by a partnership led by David H. Batchelder, set its annual meeting for June 20 in Los Angeles. The Dominguez, Calif., company is expected to face a proxy challenge from Girard Partners L.P. for control of the board. The La Jolla, Calif., partnership holds a 6.6% stake in Pic `N' Save.

Girard has said it intends to make a bid to acquire the company and to solicit shareholder votes for its board nominees at the meeting.

Pic `N' Save is a retail chain that buys manufacturers' close-out merchandise and sells it at deep discounts.
