International Brief -- Degussa AG: First-Half Earnings Dropped Despite 3% Increase in Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

Degussa AG said earnings declined in the six months ended March 31 because of weaker profit at its chemical division. The West German metals and chemicals concern said six-month group sales advanced 3% to 7.1 billion marks ($4.22 billion) from 6.86 billion marks a year earlier.

Degussa didn't give any profit figures. At the annual shareholder meeting, Chairman Gert Becker said negative foreign exchange movements and rising competition caused profit margins in the chemical sector to shrink.

But Degussa's metals and pharmaceuticals division posted higher earnings, and Mr. Becker said he is still confident for fiscal 1990, which ends Sept. 30.
