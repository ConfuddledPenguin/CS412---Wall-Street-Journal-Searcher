Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Connecticut Light & Power Co., a unit of Northeast Utilities, shelf offering of up to $300 million of first and refunding mortgage bonds.

Gensia Pharmaceuticals Inc., proposed initial offering of 2.5 million shares of common stock, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc., Montgomery Securities and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Mycogen Corp., proposed offering of 1.7 million common shares, via Morgan Stanley & Co. and Montgomery Securities.

United Telecommunications Inc., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.
