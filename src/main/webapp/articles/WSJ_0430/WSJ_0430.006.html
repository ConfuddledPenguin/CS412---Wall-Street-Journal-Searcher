Dividend News: Mobil Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

MOBIL Corp., a New York-based oil company, said directors boosted the quarterly dividend 12% to 72.5 cents a share from 65 cents, payable June 11 to stock of record May 11. Allen E. Murray, chairman, said the increase, the third in three years, reflects increased optimism about Mobil's growth opportunities and industry fundamentals.
