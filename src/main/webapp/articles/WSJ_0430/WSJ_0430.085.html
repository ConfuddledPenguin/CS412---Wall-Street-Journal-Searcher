Who's News: First Wachovia Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

FIRST WACHOVIA Corp. (Winston-Salem, N.C.) -- A new chief financial officer and two new board members were named by this banking company. Anthony L. Furr was named chief financial officer. Mr. Furr, 46 years old, is currently Piedmont Triad Region executive for Wachovia Bank & Trust Co. He succeeds H. Jack Runnion Jr., 60, who will retire June 30. The two new directors are Thomas K. Hearn Jr. and F. Kenneth Iverson. Mr. Hearn, 52, is president of Wake Forest University. Mr. Iverson is chairman and chief executive officer of Nucor Corp.
