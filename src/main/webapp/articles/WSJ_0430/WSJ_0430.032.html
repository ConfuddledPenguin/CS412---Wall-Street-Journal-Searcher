Scott & Stringfellow Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- Scott & Stringfellow Financial Inc., a securities brokerage and financial services concern, said it authorized the purchase of an additional 100,000 of its 2,014,907 common shares outstanding, in open-market transactions from time to time, increasing to 300,000 from 200,000 the number of shares authorized for buyback.
