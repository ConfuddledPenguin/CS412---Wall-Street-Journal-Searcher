Who's News: Constellation Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

CONSTELLATION BANCORP (Elizabeth, N.J.) -- Robert W. Kean Jr. was named to the new post of chairman of the nine-member board of this holding company for banks, which is also a leasing company and a mortgage originator. The 67-year-old Mr. Kean had been president of Elizabethtown Water Co. John J. Connolly, 51, remains president and chief executive officer at Constellation.
