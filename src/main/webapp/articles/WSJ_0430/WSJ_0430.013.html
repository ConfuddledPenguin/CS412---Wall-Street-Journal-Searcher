Georgia Gulf Revamping

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

ATLANTA -- Georgia Gulf Corp. said it completed its recapitalization plan and expects to begin payments to shareholders today. Under the recapitalization, each share of common will be exchanged for $30 in cash, $8.50 principal amount of 15% senior subordinated notes maturing in the year 2000 and one share of a new issue of common.

Georgia Gulf, a chemical company, unveiled its plan to recapitalize in January and amended the plan in March. Shareholders approved the recapitalization at a special meeting April 20.
