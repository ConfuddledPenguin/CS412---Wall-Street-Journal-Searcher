Glassmakers Form Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

PARIS -- The French glass and building materials group Cie. de Saint-Gobain S.A. said Friday that its West German unit Vegla Vereinigte Glaswerke G.m.b.H. reached a joint venture agreement with the East German glass monopoly VEB Flachglas Kombinat Torgau. Saint-Gobain said in a communique that each group will hold 50% of a new company to be created for the study of possible joint ventures in the field of float glass, auto glass and windows with a special focus on meeting demand that is likely to arise from expansion by the West German auto makers Volkswagenwerke AG in Zwickau, Adam Opel AG in Eisenach and Daimler-Benz AG in Ludwigsfelde.

It said that all other areas of glass production and marketing will be studied by the new joint venture to identify opportunities for further cooperation.

Financial details of the new joint venture weren't disclosed.
