U.S. Treasury to Sell $10 Billion in Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $950 million in new cash Thursday by selling about $10 billion in 52-week bills and redeeming $9.06 billion in maturing bills. The bills will be dated May 10 and will mature on May 9, 1991. They will be available in minimum denominations of $10,000.

Tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Thursday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
