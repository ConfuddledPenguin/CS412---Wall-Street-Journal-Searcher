Who's News: Champion Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

CHAMPION ENTERPRISES Inc. (Dryden, Mich.) -- Walter R. Young Jr. was named president and chief executive officer of this manufacturer of motor homes and medium-duty buses, succeeding Joseph J. Morris, 61 years old, who is retiring. Mr. Young, 45, was previously president of Wheelabrator Corp. He has also held various positions with Budd Co., which makes and distributes parts for the automotive industry.
