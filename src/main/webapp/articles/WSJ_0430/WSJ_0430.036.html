Archive Completes Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

COSTA MESA, Calif. -- Archive Corp. said it completed its previously announced acquisition of San Diego-based Cipher Data Products Corp. for $8.25 a share, or about $120 million. Both companies make computer-tape drives, the backup data-storage devices for computers. Archive also said it borrowed $140.2 million under its credit line with Barclays Bank. The funds will repay loans made by Barclays to purchase Cipher shares, will go toward the costs and fees related to the transaction, and will be used for additional working capital, the company said.
