Who's News: Framingham Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

FRAMINGHAM SAVINGS BANK (Framingham, Mass.) -- The board elected Ted S. Valpey Jr. and Allen M. Glick to the new posts of chairman and vice chairman, respectively. The company also said Allen Model, a Philadelphia lawyer, was elected to the board, succeeding Charles Patterson, who retired. The company said it is "implementing a plan of succession" to select a successor to Donald Hughes, president and chief executive officer. A spokesman said Mr. Hughes will be 64 years old in September but declined to say when he might retire.
