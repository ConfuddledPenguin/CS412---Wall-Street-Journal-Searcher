Langenscheidt Publishers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/30/90

MASPETH, N.Y. -- Langenscheidt Publishers Inc., said it acquired Arrow Publishing Co., a Boston publisher of street and road maps. Terms weren't disclosed. Arrow will continue to operate as Arrow Map Co. and retain its current management. Langenscheidt, also a map publisher, said the acquisition will expand its coverage of the East Coast.
