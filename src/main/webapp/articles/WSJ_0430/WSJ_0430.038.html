Telebit Corp. Offers Shares to the Public

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Telebit Corp. said it offered 2,090,000 common shares for $20.9 million, or $10 a share, in an initial public offering. The maker of data-transmission products said that of the shares being offered, 1,928,820 are being sold by Telebit and 161,180 by certain shareholders. The shares will be traded on the Nasdaq National Market System under the symbol TBIT.

Telebit said the stock is being offered through an underwriting syndicate managed by Cowen & Co. and Needham & Co. Telebit has granted the underwriters an option to purchase up to an additional 313,500 shares of common stock to cover any over-allotments.

The company said it plans to use proceeds from the offering for capital expenditures and for general corporate purposes. It added that some of the proceeds may also be used to fund acquisitions of data-communications businesses, products or technologies, although no such acquisitions are currently identified.
