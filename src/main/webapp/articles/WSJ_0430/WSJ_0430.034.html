Voplex Discusses Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/30/90

TROY, Mich. -- Voplex Corp. said it was discussing a joint venture with another company that would combine the automotive plastics groups of the two companies. Voplex didn't identify the other company.

The automotive plastics groups at the two companies had combined 1989 sales of about $150 million, according to Voplex. Automotive plastics accounted for about 80% of Voplex's 1989 sales of $71.3 million, a company spokesman said.

"The proposed joint venture would be another major milestone in our strategy to enhance our position as a tier one supplier for automotive plastics," said Joseph W. Bauer, president and chief executive officer of Voplex.
