Who's News: UNC Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

UNC Inc. (Annapolis, Md.) -- John J. Bonasia, former president of UNC's Airwork Corp. unit, was elected to the new post of group vice president of engine overhaul operations. Mr. Bonasia will oversee the operations of the company's Airwork and Pacific Airmotive Corp. units. UNC makes and repairs aircraft engines.
