CMS Energy's Debt Securities

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

DEARBORN, Mich. -- CMS Energy Corp. said the Michigan Supreme Court has restricted the company from using its debt securities in the Midland Cogeneration Venture. However, the court released CMS from any restrictions on its equity interest in the venture, including about $87 million of cash. The utility holding company said it used the cash yesterday to reduce bank debt.

The court continued to allow CMS to use the current earnings on the approximately $1.2 billion principal amount of notes it holds.
