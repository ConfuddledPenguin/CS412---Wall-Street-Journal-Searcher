Who's News: Bristol-Myers Squibb Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

BRISTOL-MYERS SQUIBB Co. (New York) -- Jose M. de Lasa was named vice president, legal affairs, in the pharmaceutical and nutritional group of this pharmaceutical company, with the appointment to take effect Saturday. Mr. de Lasa, 48, will continue to serve as an associate general counsel of the company.
