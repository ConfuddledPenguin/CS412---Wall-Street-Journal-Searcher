Who's News: U S West Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

U S WEST Inc. (Denver) -- Robert P. Cochran, 40 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of the Financial Security Assurance unit of this regional Bell operating company, succeeding Walter J. Lopp 2d, who died earlier this month. Mr. Cochran, a member of the policy-making management committee at Financial Security, which insures corporate and municipal bond issues, formerly was managing director of the company's Financial Guaranty group.
