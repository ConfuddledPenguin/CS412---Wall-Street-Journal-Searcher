Business Brief -- VLSI Technology Inc.: Concern Expects to Report Loss for Fiscal 3rd Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

VLSI Technology Inc., San Jose, Calif., said it expects to report a loss and lower revenue for the third quarter. The semiconductor maker said the loss will mainly be the result of a decision to withdraw from a segment of the memory-chip business. It said the withdrawal from the static random-access memory business will result in a one-time charge to operations of $12 million to $15 million against third-quarter results.

VLSI also said it will lay off about 100 of its 2,000 employees between now and the end of the quarter.

Revenue is running below expectations because of declines in demand for older products, VLSI said.
