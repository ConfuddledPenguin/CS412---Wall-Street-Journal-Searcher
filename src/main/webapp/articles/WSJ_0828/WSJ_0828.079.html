Alpine Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

HACKENSACK, N.J. -- Alpine Group Inc. said its agreement to sell its General Plasma Inc. subsidiary to closely held Ontario Corp., Muncie, Ind., was terminated. Alpine said Ontario Corp. couldn't arrange financing for the purchase, which the companies definitively agreed upon late last month. Alpine plans to pursue other expressions of interest it has received regarding General Plasma, which operates thermal spray coating systems used to coat industrial parts.

Alpine also has interests in information displays. Ontario Corp. officials couldn't be reached for comment.
