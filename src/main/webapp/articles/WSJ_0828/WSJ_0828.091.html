Business Brief -- Nynex Corp.: Unit Acquires 32% Stake In GMO of West Germany

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

Nynex Corp.'s Information Solutions Group, White Plains, N.Y., said that it paid $20 million to acquire 32% of GMO AG, a West German software and systems company, and that it signed a long-term agreement under which Nynex will acquire a majority stake. The New York-based regional Bell operating company said the agreement provides for GMO to work with London-based BIS Group, a unit of Nynex Information Solutions Group. The acquisition is part of the company's strategy to expand into European software and information technology markets.

Nynex said GMO had 1989 revenue of about $60 million. GMO is West Germany's largest supplier of International Business Machines Corp. midrange systems and provides software, consulting and training.
