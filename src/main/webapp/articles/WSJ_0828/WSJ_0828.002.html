Who's News: Roosevelt Financial Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

ROOSEVELT FINANCIAL GROUP Inc. (St. Louis) -- Clarence M. Turley Jr., 61-year-old vice chairman of Turley Martin Co. and a Roosevelt director, was named chairman of this bank holding company and its Roosevelt Bank subsidiary, succeeding Raymond T. Gusnard, 63, who is retiring as chairman, president and chief executive officer of the company and unit. The company formed an office of the president, made up of Charles M. Stout, 42, previously senior vice president and manager, real estate and retail banking group, who was named president and chief executive; Stanley J. Bradshaw, 33, who remains executive vice president and chief operating officer; and Loyd W. Garrison, 39, chief financial officer, who was named executive vice president.
