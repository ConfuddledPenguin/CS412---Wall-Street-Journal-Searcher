Manufacturers National Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

DETROIT -- Manufacturers National Corp. said it will consolidate its banks in Ann Arbor, Grand Rapids and Lansing, Mich., into Manufacturers National Bank of Detroit. The company said the consolidation will allow it to "offer all of its products throughout Michigan on a more uniform basis."

The consolidation is expected to take 12 to 18 months to complete.
