W.R. Grace Unit's Emissions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

NEW YORK -- W.R. Grace & Co., a diversified chemical concern, said its Grace Specialty Chemicals Co. unit plans to reduce pollution emissions 50% over the next five years, primarily by substituting materials and modifying certain manufacturing processes to minimize hazardous waste. Grace Specialty Chemicals had 1989 sales of $3.3 billion. Separately, W.R. Grace said its European Darex division purchased an additional 22.5% of Neue Transvac Maschinen AG, based in Kriens, Switzerland, raising its stake to 47.5%. Grace said it also raised to 39.2% its stake in Canonie Environmental Services Corp., based in Porter, Ind.
