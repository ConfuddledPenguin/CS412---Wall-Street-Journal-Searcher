CML Completes Sale of Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

ACTON, Mass. -- CML Group Inc., a direct marketer of consumer products, said it completed the sale of its Carroll Reed unit to an affiliate of Swire Pacific Ltd., Hong Kong. Terms of the cash transaction weren't disclosed. CML said proceeds were used to reduce long-term bank debt. It said the sale completes a corporate restructuring, announced last year, in which the company sold several businesses.
