WestAir Holding Airline Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- WestAir Holding Inc. said it plans to merge its two regional airline units into a single company with two operating divisions. The company said NPA Inc., which operates in the Pacific Northwest as United Express, will be merged with WestAir Airline, which operates as United Express in four Western and 13 Eastern states.

The company said it expects to complete the merger by year end, resulting in the layoff of as many as 75 employees.
