Steve's Homemade Buys Stake in Rival, American Glace Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

LINDENHURST, N.Y. -- Steve's Homemade Ice Cream Inc. acquired a majority scoop of American Glace Inc., a closely held New York marketer of dessert products that have no fat or cholesterol. Steve's Homemade declined to disclose the exact size of the holding it bought or the purchase price.

American Glace's products complement the Steve's Homemade lines of premium ice cream, marketed under the Steve's Super Premium, Steve's Gourmet Light, and Swensen's brands, and Heidi's frozen yogurt, said Gary P. Stevens, president of Steve's Homemade. Steve's Homemade also franchises restaurants and ice cream shops.

Steve's Homemade recently reported a second-quarter net loss of $86,000, or one cent a share, compared with net income of $531,000, or four cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Revenue fell 24% to $6.8 million from $8.9 million. Officials have said, however, that they expect third-quarter results to turn around.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, Steve's Homemade shares were quoted at $1.0625, unchanged.
