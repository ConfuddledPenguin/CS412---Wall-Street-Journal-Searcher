Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

WHO'S NEWS: Nancy Woodhull was named to the new position of executive vice president and editor-in-chief of Southern Progress Corp., a subsidiary of Time Warner Inc. She will oversee the editorial direction of Southern Living, Progressive Farmer, Southern Accents, Travel South and Cooking Light magazines. She was president of Gannett News Services.
