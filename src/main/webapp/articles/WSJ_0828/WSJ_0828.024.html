Cubic Buys Back 100,000 Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

SAN DIEGO -- Cubic Corp. said it has purchased about 100,000 of its common shares since the end of April, when its board authorized the buy-back of as many as 500,000 shares. The technology concern said it is continuing to buy its shares on the open market at management's discretion. As of June 30, Cubic had about seven million shares outstanding.

In American Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Cubic shares rose $1.375 to $16.625.
