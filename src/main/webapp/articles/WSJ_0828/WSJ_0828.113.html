Business Brief -- Hotel Investors Trust: Group Seeks to Replace Firm's Current Management

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

An investor group including Leonard M. Ross, Merri Jean Ross, and RRH Capital Management Inc. said it intends to nominate slates for election of trustees and directors of Hotel Investors Trust and Hotel Investors Corp. Hotel Investors Trust is a real estate investment trust based in Los Angeles whose shares are paired for trading with Hotel Investors Corp. in San Diego. The companies are in the process of selling most of their assets.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the investors said they believe that current managers have failed to maximize shareholder value.

"We disagree with the allegations and will file complete proxy materials to respond more fully," said a spokesman for the two companies. The companies' annual meeting is set for Sept. 24.

The investors said they also intend to propose the repeal of the recent amendments to the by-laws and regulations of the companies which they said are designed to restrict shareholders' ability to make proposals and nominate opposition candidates.

Mr. Ross and his wife own a 7.6% stake in the two companies. RRH Capital Management, an investment concern, owns 6.3%.
