Business Brief -- Trefoil Capital Investors L.P.: Concern Reaches Agreement To Buy Child World Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

Trefoil Capital Investors L.P. said it signed a definitive agreement with CNC Holding Corp. for Trefoil's previously announced purchase of CNC's 82% stake in Child World Inc. Terms of the non-cash transaction haven't been disclosed. Earlier this month, doubts were raised about the transaction when Trefoil announced that the planned signing of a definitive agreement with CNC had been postponed.

Trefoil, based in Burbank, Calif., was formed by the executives of Shamrock Holdings Inc., a holding company controlled by Roy E. Disney. CNC is a Cleveland-based holding company. Child World is an Avon, Mass., toy retailer.

Trefoil said it also signed an agreement for its plan to purchase the 18% of Child World now in public hands for $14 a share, or $28 million.

Trefoil said both transactions have been approved by Child World's board. The transaction with CNC is expected to be completed on or about Nov. 19, and the Child World acquisition is expected to be completed about 90 days later, Trefoil said.
