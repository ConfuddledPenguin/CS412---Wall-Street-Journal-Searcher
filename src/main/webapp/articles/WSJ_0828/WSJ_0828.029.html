Albertson's Net Climbs 16%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Albertson's Inc. reported net income increased 16% to $52.9 million, or 40 cents a share, for its second quarter ended Aug. 2. Sales rose 14% to $2.07 billion, although year-earlier results were slightly depressed by a strike by workers at 34 of its stores in the Seattle, Wash., area. The company estimated that sales would have increased by only 7.7% if the strike had not occurred.

In the year-earlier period, the company earned $45.6 million, or 34 cents a share, on sales of $1.8 billion.

Albertson's operates 523 grocery stores in 16 states.
