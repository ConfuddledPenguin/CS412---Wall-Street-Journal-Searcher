French Jobless Rate Was Steady

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

PARIS -- France's unemployment rate was unchanged in July at 8.9%, the Labor Ministry said. The number of job-seekers stood at 2,507,600 on a seasonally adjusted basis at the end of last month, down 0.2% from a month before and down 1.2% from July 1989.

On an unadjusted basis, the number of job-seekers in July rose 2.4% from June to 2,409,600, but was still 1.2% below the July 1989 level.
