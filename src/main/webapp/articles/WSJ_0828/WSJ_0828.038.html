Who's News: Reebok International Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

REEBOK INTERNATIONAL Ltd. (Stoughton, Mass.) -- David G. Ropes was named to the newly created position of vice president, marketing services, at the company's Reebok division. Mr. Ropes will coordinate marketing support services, including advertising, merchandising, celebrity endorsements and retail promotions, the footwear and apparel retailer said. Mr. Ropes previously was vice president of advertising at Pepsi-Cola Co., a unit of PepsiCo Inc., Purchase, N.Y.
