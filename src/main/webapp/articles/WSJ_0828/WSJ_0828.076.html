International Brief -- Bayer AG: Group Pretax Profit Fell 9.5% in First Six Months

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

Bayer AG of West Germany, a chemical and pharmaceutical company, said its group pretax profit for the first half fell 9.5% to 2.01 billion marks ($1.29 billion) from 2.22 billion marks a year earlier. Net income declined 5.1% to 1.02 billion marks from 1.07 billion marks.

Sales slipped 3.7% to 21.79 billion marks from 22.62 billion marks. Domestic sales declined to 9.14 billion marks from 9.46 billion marks, and foreign sales fell to 12.65 billion marks from 13.16 billion marks.

The parent's pretax profit eased to 1.14 billion marks from 1.27 billion marks, and its sales fell to 9.94 billion marks from 10.09 billion marks.
