Alcoa-Royal Dutch Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

PITTSBURGH -- Aluminum Co. of America said a subsidiary of its South American unit will construct a bauxite mine in Brazil with an affiliate of the Royal Dutch/Shell Group. Initial investment will be $150 million. Alcoa's South American unit, Alcoa Aluminio S.A., owns 60% of the subsidiary, Alcoa Mineracao. The Royal Dutch/Shell Group affiliate, Billiton Metais, owns 40%.

The mine's initial annual capacity will be 2.25 million metric tons of bauxite, the primary ore in aluminum. Operations will begin in the second half of 1993. One metric ton equals 2,204.6 pounds.
