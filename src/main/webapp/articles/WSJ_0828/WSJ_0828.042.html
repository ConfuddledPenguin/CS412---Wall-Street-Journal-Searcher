Raw-Steel Production Rose 3.5% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 3.5% last week to 1,937,000 tons from 1,927,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 14% from the 1,701,000 tons produced a year earlier. The industry used 86.8% of its capability last week, compared with 86.4% the previous week and 76.5% a year earlier.
