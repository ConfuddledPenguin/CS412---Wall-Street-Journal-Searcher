First Chattanooga, BMR End Agreement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

CHATTANOOGA, Tenn. -- First Chattanooga Financial Corp. and BMR Financial Group Inc. said they agreed to terminate BMR's planned $76 million acquisition of First Chattanooga. In February, BMR, a bank holding company based in Atlanta, agreed to acquire First Chattanooga for cash and stock valued at $27.25 a share. Yesterday, however, the two companies said that instability in financial markets had made the transaction less promising and that both companies had agreed to end their agreement.

In national over-the-counter trading Monday, First Chattanooga closed at $14.25 a share, down 25 cents. BMR closed at $6 a share, unchanged.

Separately, First Chattanooga, a thrift holding company, said its board authorized an increase in the company's stock repurchase program to $4 million from $1.5 million. First Chattanooga has about 2.8 million shares outstanding.
