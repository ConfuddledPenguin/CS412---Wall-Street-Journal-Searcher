Service Fracturing Sells Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/28/90

PAMPA, Texas -- Service Fracturing Co., an oil services concern, said that it sold 1.5 million common shares at about $6.88 each through a private placement, and that net proceeds of nearly $9.5 million will be used primarily for increasing exploration, refurbishing and replacing equipment, and purchasing new equipment.
