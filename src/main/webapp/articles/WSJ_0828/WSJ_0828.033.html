EC's Shipbuilding Subsidies

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

THE HAGUE -- The Dutch economic affairs minister, J.E. Andriessen, and the Danish industry minister, A. Lundholt, said they will propose that the European Community phase out all forms of shipbuilding subsidies. The initiative is timed to coincide with discussions in Brussels on the seventh directive on aid to the shipbuilding industry. The current directive expires next Jan. 1.

Mr. Andriessen said the firm market in shipbuilding and an expected long-term upturn in the industry offer a good opportunity to phase out the subsidies.
