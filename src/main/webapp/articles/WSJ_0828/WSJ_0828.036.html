Amwest Insurance to Post Gain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/28/90

WOODLAND HILLS, Calif. -- Amwest Insurance Group Inc. said it will post a fiscal third-quarter gain of $797,000, or 34 cents a share, in the wake of a state measure signed into law Sunday. The law exempts certain types of insurance, including surety bonds, from rate rollbacks and other major provisions of Proposition 103, a controversial initiative that passed in 1988. Amwest is an insurance holding company that specializes in surety bonds.

Amwest had established a $1.4 million pretax reserve for potential premium rebates in its second quarter, ended June 30. The $797,000 gain reflects a reversal of the reserve in the current quarter, ending Sept. 30.
