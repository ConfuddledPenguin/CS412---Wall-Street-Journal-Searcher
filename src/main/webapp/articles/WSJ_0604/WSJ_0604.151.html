Victor Venture With Philips

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

TOKYO -- Victor Co. of Japan announced Friday that it will establish a joint venture in Malaysia with N.V. Philips of the Netherlands for the production of video cassette recorders. The venture will be created in Shah Alam in the state of Selangor in the fall of this year; it will be owned equally by the two partners.

Employing about 1,000 people, it will start production in February 1991.

The company will have an annual production capacity of 700,000 VHS cassette recorders initially. Eventually, it will be equipped with facilities capable of making two million recorders, according to officials.

The company plans to export its products mainly to the U.S. in addition to the Far East and Central and South America.
