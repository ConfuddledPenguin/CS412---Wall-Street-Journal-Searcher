Sudden Interest

Folks long ignoring the dollar

With its up-and-down gyrations,

Now start praying for a strong one --

In time for their vacations.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

Ticklish Ivories

Does there exist someone who took

Piano as a pup

Who now responds to all who ask,

He's glad he gave it up?

-- Dick Emmons.
