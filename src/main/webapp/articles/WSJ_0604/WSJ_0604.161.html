China Light-Exxon Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

HONG KONG -- Kowloon Electricity Supply Co., a joint venture between China Light & Power Co. and Exxon Corp. of the U.S., said it placed an order valued at 600 million Hong Kong dollars (US$77.1 million) with John Brown Engineering Ltd., a Scottish power supplier, to provide three 100,000-kilowatt combustion turbines for a power station on the colony's Lantau Island. The facilities are to be made by General Electric Co. of the U.S. The plant is to go into operation in early 1992.
