Who's News: News Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

NEWS Corp. (Sydney, Australia) -- Walter Senior, 46 years old, was named president of international marketing and distribution of the Los Angeles-based Twentieth Century Fox Film unit. Mr. Senior succeeds Jean-Louis Rubin, 50, who joined Largo Entertainment, a Hollywood entertainment concern formed last year by producer Larry Gordon. Mr. Senior was chairman and chief executive officer of Famous Players Inc., a Canadian theater chain.
