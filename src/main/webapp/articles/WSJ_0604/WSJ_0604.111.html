Genzyme-Amoco Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

BOSTON -- Genzyme Corp., a biotechnology and pharmaceutical concern, and the Amoco Biotechnology Co. unit of Amoco Corp., Chicago, said the previously announced agreement to sell their interests in their Gene-Trak Systems joint venture to Hoechst Group AG of West Germany was terminated without reaching terms. The companies said they now are free to negotiate with "other interested parties"; none was identified. Gene-Trak is developing tests for food-borne pathogens and infectious diseases, using nucleic acid probe technology, the companies said.
