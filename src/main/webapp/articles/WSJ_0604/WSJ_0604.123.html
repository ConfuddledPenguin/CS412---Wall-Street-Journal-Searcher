Business Brief -- Televideo Systems Inc.: Firm Sets Up Joint Venture To Sell Systems in Moscow

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Televideo Systems Inc. said it has signed two joint-venture agreements to market the company's computer systems in the Soviet Union. The agreement, which also involves West Germany's ABM Computer Systems, will provide computers to the city of Moscow and the Ministry of Construction. The companies will offer computers and computer parts for resale through a computer store chain in Moscow that will be set up as part of the venture. Terms weren't disclosed.

Televideo said it doesn't expect the venture to be profitable for about two years. Initially, it expects to provide 1,000 to 2,000 systems a month.

As part of the agreement, Moscow is providing land near the Kremlin that the joint-venture parties will develop into a hotel and office complex for western visitors and a source of hard currency earnings.
