Marketing Brief -- A/S/M Communications Inc.: Affiliated Publications Inc. To Acquire an 80% Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

Affiliated Publications Inc., publisher of the Boston Globe and a stable of trade magazines, said it has agreed to acquire 80% of A/S/M Communications Inc., publisher of Adweek magazine. The purchase price was not disclosed, but industry executives familiar with the transaction estimated the Boston-based Affiliated paid about $17 million for the stake to a group of private holders. Kenneth Fadner, president of A/S/M, is the largest holder; he will continue in his post and retain a substantial portion of the remaining 20%.

Adweek, started in 1978, has been battered by a decline in advertising by media companies. A/S/M, attempting to ease financial difficulties, had quietly folded seven publications in recent months, and had been seeking an outside investor to provide cash.

Affiliated is acquiring Adweek, Adweek's Marketing Week, and Photo District News. All of the A/S/M titles will become part of BPI Communications Inc, Affiliated's specialty publishing subsidiary.

Adweek will be a strategic addition to BPI Communications's growing group of media and entertainment-related magazines. BPI also publishes The Hollywood Reporter, Billboard, Back Stage, American Film, Photo Design and Interiors.
