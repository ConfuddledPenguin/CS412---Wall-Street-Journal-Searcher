Peru's May Inflation Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

LIMA, Peru -- Peru's monthly inflation rate hit 32.8% in May, bringing the accumulated rate over the past 12 months to 1,968%, according to official figures. The National Statistics Institute's figures showed a decline from April's inflation rate of 37.3%. Prices have risen 309.9% so far this year.

Peru's inflation reached 2,775% in 1989, the highest rate in the nation's history.
