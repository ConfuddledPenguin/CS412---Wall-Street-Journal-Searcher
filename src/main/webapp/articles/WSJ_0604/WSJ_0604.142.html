Bank of New England Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

BOSTON -- Bank of New England Corp. said it completed the sale of its BancNewEngland Leasing Group operations to Bank of Tokyo Ltd. The sale, approved by the Federal Reserve May 21, includes about $500 million in assets, the bank said. Bank of Tokyo will assume the group's 250 employees in a new subsidiary, BOT Financial Corp., based in Boston.
