Marketing & Media: MGM/UA Video Unit's Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

LOS ANGELES -- MGM/UA Home Video, a unit of MGM/UA Communications Co., said it reached a sponsorship agreement with Procter & Gamble Co.'s Downy fabric softener on a $21 million marketing campaign for the videocassette release of the animated film "All Dogs Go to Heaven." The promotion is similar to last year's $8.5 million campaign for the videocassette release of "The Wizard of Oz," MGM/UA said. As with "Oz," Procter & Gamble will offer a $5 rebate on the $24.98 purchase price. The television portion of the campaign will begin Aug. 29 and run through Christmas.
