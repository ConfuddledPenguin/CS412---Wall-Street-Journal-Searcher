GM's Cadillac Rebate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Corp. said its Cadillac division has extended the incentives on certain models through June 30. The rebates were originally scheduled to end May 31. Cadillac is offering rebates of $1,500 on the De Ville, Fleetwood and Brougham, and rebates of $2,000 on the Eldorado and Seville.
