Morrison Knudson Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. and a joint-venture associate said they have received a $40 million contract to build a 57-mile underwater natural gas pipeline in British Columbia. Morrison Knudsen's Vancouver-based unit, Northern Construction Co., and Stena Offshore Ltd., of Aberdeen, Scotland, will build the pipeline, which will run from the British Columbia mainland to Vancouver Island. Work will start immediately and be completed by December.
