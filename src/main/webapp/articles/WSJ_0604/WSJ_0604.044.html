Who's News: Votrax Inc. Dismisses Finance Chief, 6 Others

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

FARMINGTON HILLS, Mich. -- Votrax Inc. said it dismissed its vice president and chief financial officer, Karen Greenfield, and six other employees as part of a cost-cutting effort. Since March, 58 employees have either quit or been dismissed, the company said. Votrax, which has 22 employees, said it does not anticipate further staff reductions.

"These releases reflect the final step in the company's efforts to refocus on its core business," said Larry W. Jernigan, president and chief executive officer.

Votrax designs and makes interactive telephone response systems, such as those that allow a customer to call a bank and perform transactions using a touch-tone phone.
