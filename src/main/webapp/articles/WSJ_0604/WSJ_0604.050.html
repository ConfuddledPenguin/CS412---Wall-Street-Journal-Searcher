SIS Corp. Withdraws Reorganization Plan Over Lack of Financing

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

ROCKY RIVER, Ohio -- SIS Corp. said it withdrew its plan of reorganization from U.S. bankruptcy court because the financing needed to fund the plan couldn't be obtained. SIS said its next plan will propose the sale of the company's assets and won't provide any payments to company shareholders. The court has instructed the company to file a new plan within 45 days.

Until the sale of assets occurs, the company said it will continue to operate its chain of Sisters Chicken & Biscuits restaurants.

The company has been under bankruptcy-law protection since February 1989.
