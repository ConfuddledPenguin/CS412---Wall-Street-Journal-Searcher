REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Sierra Loves Gerrymander

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

The Sierra Club is now unabashedly in the muck of partisan politics, trying to defeat two California initiatives tomorrow that would end the gerrymandering that ensures Democratic advantage in drawing election districts. A TV ad shows a hand pulling an oil-sodden otter from the water. Then actor James Garner (sic transit "Maverick") says the initiatives "could let big business exploit our oceans, trees and few remaining wilderness areas. The Sierra Club says no on 118, no on 119."
