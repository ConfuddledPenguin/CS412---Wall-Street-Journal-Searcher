EC Sends Soviet Refugees Aid

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

BRUSSELS -- The European Community Commission last Friday sent 500,000 European currency units ($607,150) in emergency aid to refugees in the Soviet republics of Armenia and Azerbaijan. The aid was to be handled by the Belgian branch of a French medical charity, Doctors Without Borders. The money will provide medical aid to about 30,000 Armenians who fled to Armenia from Azerbaijan following civil unrest there last January, and to 15,000 Azerbaijanis who left Armenia for Azerbaijan.
