Business Brief -- Mediagenic: Company Agrees on Paying $6.6 Million in Damages

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

MENLO PARK, Calif. -- Mediagenic said it agreed to a long-term payment of the $6.6 million in patent infringement court damages that, as previously reported, a federal court awarded earlier this year to Philips Consumer Electronics Company, a unit of North American Philips Corp. Under the agreement, the entertainment software company will pay Philips monthly installments of $150,000 from July 1990 to Dec. 1993, with a balloon payment at Dec. 31, 1993.

The suit was originally brought by the Magnavox Company (now Philips) against Activision (now Mediagenic). The patent at issue expired in 1989.
