Borden to Realign Japan Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

NEW YORK -- Borden Inc. said it will restructure its food businesses in Japan over the next 18 months, moving toward full ownership and control of its product lines. The producer of food, packaging and consumer and industrial products said it makes and distributes Borden brand ice cream, cheese and margarine in Japan. The company until now has licensed Borden's margarine and ice cream to Meiji Milk Products, a Japanese company. Borden also had a joint venture manufacturing operation for cheese, which was distributed by Meiji.
