McDonnell Douglas Gets Air Force Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. received a $47 million Air Force contract for C-17 aircraft support equipment.

United Technologies Corp. was awarded a $29 million Navy contract for helicopter and aircraft parts.

Chamberlain Manufacturing Corp. got a $25.1 million Army contract for projectile parts.

Nichols Research Corp. received an $18.2 million Army contract for threat simulator reports.

TRW Inc. was awarded an $11.4 million Air Force job for management information systems.
