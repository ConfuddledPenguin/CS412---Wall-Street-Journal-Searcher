Wainoco to Buy Tredegar Sites

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

HOUSTON -- Wainoco Oil Corp., an oil and gas exploration and production company, said it agreed to buy all the U.S. oil and gas properties of Tredegar Industries Inc. of Richmond, Va., a diversified maker of plastics and aluminum, for $16.5 million in a transaction to be completed by July. Tredegar said it expects to incur an after-tax loss of about $4 million, or 35 cents a share, on the sale. The properties are in Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi and Arkansas, and Wainoco said they hold net proved reserves of about 1.6 million barrels of oil and 6.9 billion cubic feet of natural gas, 97% of which are developed. Wainoco said the purchase will increase its U.S. oil production by 93% and its U.S. gas production by 46%.
