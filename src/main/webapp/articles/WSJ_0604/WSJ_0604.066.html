Glaverbel Stake in AFG

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

BRUSSELS -- Belgian glass maker Glaverbel said it raised its stake in U.S. flat-glass producer AFG Industries Inc. to 26.6% from 19.7%. Glaverbel owns all the preferred shares of AFG, which it acquired as part of a management buy-out of the U.S. concern in 1988. It has said it wants to acquire a controlling stake.

Glaverbel is 72%-owned by Asahi Glass Co. of Japan.

AFG, the second largest flat-glass producer in the U.S., has 4,465 employees. It had sales of $648 million in 1989.
