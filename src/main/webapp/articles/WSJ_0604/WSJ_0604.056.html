Independent Holdings, And Group Raise Stake In Landsing Pacific

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

WASHINGTON -- A group that includes Independent Holdings Inc. of San Francisco said it raised its stake in Landsing Pacific Fund to 6.25% of the outstanding common stock. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it bought 71,000 shares, from March 28 to May 29, for $7.75 to $8.06 each, raising its holdings to 380,700 shares.

In March, the group said it held a 5.1% stake in Landsing Pacific as an investment. In the filing, the group said it may seek to discuss the company's direction with management.
