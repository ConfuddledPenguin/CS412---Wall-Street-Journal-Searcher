Marketing Brief -- Time Warner Inc.: Firm Completes Acquisition Of Lane for $225 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

Time Warner Inc. said it completed the acquisition of Lane Publishing Co. for $225 million. Lane Publishing, Menlo Park, Calif., publishes Sunset, the Magazine of Western Living, and Sunset Books, and produces Sunset Films. The acquisition makes media and entertainment giant Time Warner the nation's largest publisher of regional magazines.

Time Warner said that Sunset Magazine and Sunset Books will become part of Time Publishing Ventures, a unit of Time Inc. Magazine Co., which includes all of the company's regional and special interest businesses, and its world-wide development activity.
