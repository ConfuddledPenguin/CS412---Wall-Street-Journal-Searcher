Who's News: Hotel Investors Picks Morrissey, a Director, As Its New President

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

LOS ANGELES -- Hotel Investors Trust elected director John D. Morrissey, 53 years old, president and chief executive officer, filling the posts vacated by John F. Rothman, 55, who resigned in March. Shares of Hotel Investors Trust, a real estate investment trust, and Hotel Investors Corp., a hotel-management concern, are paired for trading. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Hotel Investors shares closed at $3.50, unchanged.

When Mr. Rothman resigned, the companies had said they didn't plan to elect a replacement, since they are selling most of their assets in order to pursue other business interests. But a spokeswoman said Friday that the companies will still retain about a third of their assets, and that "the board therefore thought it was necessary to have a president and CEO." Hotel Investors has been in negotiations for the asset sales in recent months.
