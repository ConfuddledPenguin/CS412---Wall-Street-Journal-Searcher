New York City Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

NEW YORK -- Loews Corp. President Preston Robert Tisch is expected to be elected chairman of the New York City Partnership and the New York Chamber of Commerce, succeeding American Express Co. Chairman James D. Robinson III. Mr. Tisch served as U.S. postmaster general from 1986 to 1988.

The Partnership, founded in 1979, is a civic organization consisting of private-sector leaders working on solutions to the city's education, housing and economic-development problems.
