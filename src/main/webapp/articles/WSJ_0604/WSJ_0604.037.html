Airbus Gets Firm A-321 Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

PARIS -- Airbus Industrie said Iberia Air Lines of Spain placed a firm order for eight of the European aircraft manufacturing consortium's A-321 passenger jets, following through on a memorandum of understanding announced in July. Financial details weren't disclosed, though people in the industry estimate the price of an A-321 is slightly less than $50 million. The planes will be delivered beginning in mid-1994. The A-321 is a stretched version of the A-320, a short-haul to medium-haul aircraft that is scheduled to enter service in 1994. Airbus said Iberia took an undetermined amount of new options on additional aircraft.

The Airbus consortium, based in Toulouse, France, is made up of Aerospatiale of France, Messerschmitt-Boelkow-Blohm G.m.b.H. of West Germany, British Aerospace PLC and Construcciones Aeronauticas S.A. of Spain.
