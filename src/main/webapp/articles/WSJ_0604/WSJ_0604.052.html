Aerovox Inc. Stake Of 6.7% Is Acquired By Texas Art Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

WASHINGTON -- A group including Houston-based Texas Art Supply Co. said it acquired a 6.7% stake in Aerovox Inc., New Bedford, Mass., for investment purposes. The group, in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, said it holds a total of 340,234 Aerovox common shares, all but 134 shares of which were bought in the open market between April 12 and June 1 at $3.50 to $4.50 a share.

The group also includes members of the Adler family and the Adler Foundation of Houston. Louis K. Adler is president of Texas Art Supply.

The group said it acquired its Aerovox stock as an investment and doesn't currently plan to seek control of the company or to acquire a substantial amount of shares. Mr. Adler, however, may seek board representation, the filing said.
