Business Brief -- GenCorp Inc.: Sale of Auto-Vinyls Plant To Its Employees Is Set

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

GenCorp Inc., a Fairlawn, Ohio, aerospace and automotive parts company, said it agreed to sell its Toledo automotive vinyls plant to employees. The sale was financed in part by the employee stock ownership plan. Terms weren't disclosed. The new employee-owned company, Textileather Corp., expects to have annual sales of $43 million. Workers voted to take a pay cut averaging 70 cents an hour in exchange for stock in the new company. The plant has 280 hourly and salaried employees.
