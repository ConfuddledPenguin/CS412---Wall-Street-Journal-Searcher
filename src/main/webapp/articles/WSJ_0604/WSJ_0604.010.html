SUBARU OF AMERICA Inc. (Cherry Hill, N.J.) -- Masaharu Masumitsu, previously executive vice president, chief financial officer and secretary of Subaru-Isuzu Automotive Inc., a joint venture between Fuji Heavy Industries Ltd. and Isuzu Motors Ltd. of Japan, was named vice chairman of this auto marketing company, succeeding Sohei Matsukuma, who became president of Fuji USA.
