Who's News: Heldor Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

HELDOR INDUSTRIES Inc. (Morristown, N.J.) -- Charles J. Patterson, a private investor, was elected to the board of this maker of swimming-pool equipment, boosting membership to six. Mr. Patterson was chairman and chief executive officer and principal stockholder of Arneson Products Inc. until it was sold in 1988 to Hayward Pool Products of Elizabeth, N.J.
