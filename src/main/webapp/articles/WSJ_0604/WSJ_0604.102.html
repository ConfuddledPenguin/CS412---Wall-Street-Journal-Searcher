France's Quarterly GDP Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

PARIS -- France's gross domestic product in the first quarter rose an inflation-adjusted 0.7% from the 1989 fourth quarter, the National Statistics Institute said. The state agency said the data were in line with results for the previous three quarters, which showed GDP increases of 0.7% in the 1989 second quarter, 0.5% in the third quarter and 0.9% in the fourth quarter; the fourth-period figure was revised downward slightly from the previously reported 1% rise. The institute doesn't provide year-to-year growth data for GDP.

Paris market economists said the first-quarter GDP figure was in line with their expectations and tended to confirm their previous forecasts for a slight slowdown in 1990 from the 4% rate that was measured on a similar merchandise basis for 1989.
