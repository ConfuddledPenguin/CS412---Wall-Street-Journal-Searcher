Business Brief -- Lawson Mardon Group Ltd.: Toronto Company Acquires German Carton Operations

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

Lawson Mardon Group Ltd., Toronto, said its European folding carton division acquired two West German carton manufacturing operations in a transaction valued at about 16.5 million Canadian dollars (US$14.1 million). Both companies, W. Cordes and Hanse Druck, produce cartons for the tobacco, confectionary and cosmetics industry in Europe, said Lawson Mardon, a packaging and printing concern.

Lawrence Tapp, president and chief executive officer, said the acquisition will enhance Lawson Mardon's presence in the niche markets that it targets.

The company already had four carton plants in Britain and two in Germany.
