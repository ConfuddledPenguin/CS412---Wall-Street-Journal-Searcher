Letters to the Editor: Baptists Torn Asunder

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

R. Gustav Niebuhr's April 25 page-one article "House Divided" is a fine example of factual, unbiased journalism. It is evident that Mr. Niebuhr has keen insight and a thorough knowledge of his subject. Southern Baptists, since inception, have held tenaciously to the rights to think, to question and to speak. Not so slowly, but surely, these rights are being snuffed out within the denomination. A great religious body is being torn asunder and destroyed from within.

Mary Lou R. McKay

North Augusta, S.C.
