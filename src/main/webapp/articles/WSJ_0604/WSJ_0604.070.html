Burlington Resources Unit Completes Tests Of Texas Wild Cat Well

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

HOUSTON -- Meridian Oil Inc., a unit of Burlington Resources Inc., said it completed tests of a large wildcat discovery well in the Austin Chalk Trend in south central Texas. Meridian said the well flowed at average daily rates of 12,254 barrels of oil, 4.9 million cubic feet of gas and 3,032 barrels of water through two 2 1/16-inch chokes at a flowing tubing pressure of 300 pounds per square inch during a four-hour test period. The well was drilled as a medium radius, extended reach horizontal drainhole to a measured depth of 11,368 feet.

Meridian noted that although the results were favorable, the Austin Chalk Trend has historically had a significant production decline in certain areas. Meridian said it will continue to evaluate the potential of its well.
