Financing Business: Centocor Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

CENTOCOR Inc., Malvern, Pa., said it is offering 1.3 million common shares at $43.25 each through Hambrecht & Quist Inc., PaineWebber Inc. and Robertson Stephens & Co. The developer of health-care products has 11,983,355 shares outstanding. Proceeds from the offering will be used to fund expansion, working-capital requirements, and possible acquisitions.
