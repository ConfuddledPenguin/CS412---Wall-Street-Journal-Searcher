Financing Business: Caldor Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

CALDOR Inc., Norwalk, Conn., said it completed $300 million in new permanent financing arrangements to help retire a $460 million bridge loan obtained in connection with its management buy-out in November 1989. It also arranged up to $235 million in new working capital from Citibank, Chemical Bank and Security Pacific Business Inc.
