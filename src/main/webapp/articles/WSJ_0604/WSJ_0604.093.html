Alberta Telephones Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

EDMONTON, Alberta -- The government of Alberta said it intends to privatize the Alberta Government Telephones Commission after passage of legislation that would restructure AGT financially and legally. The Alberta Treasury said all AGT debentures outstanding, payable in Canadian and U.S. funds, will continue to be unconditionally guaranteed as to principal and interest by the provincial government.

Under the restructuring, all AGT assets, including investments in nonregulated entities, will be owned by wholly owned subsidiaries of a new holding company. A public offering of common shares in the holding company would follow.
