Financing Business: Management Co. Entertainment Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

MANAGEMENT CO. ENTERTAINMENT GROUP, New York, said General Electric Co.'s General Electric Capital Corp. unit has assumed all the rights and obligations of Kidder Peabody Group Inc. related to $72.5 million of loans Management received from Kidder. Management manages actors and actresses and produces, finances and distributes entertainment. Kidder is a brokerage and investment subsidiary of GE, Fairfield, Conn.
