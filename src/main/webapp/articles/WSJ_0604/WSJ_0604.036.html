  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

MILPITAS, Calif. -- LSI Logic Corp. said its Headland Technology affiliate has signed an agreement valued at as much as $20 million to supply Hewlett-Packard (Canada) Ltd. with advanced video graphics boards for use in Hewlett-Packard's Vectra personal computers. LSI Logic Corp of Canada Inc. will manufacture the boards in Canada. The high-performance graphic cards reduce wait times to "virtually zero," the company said.
