Rexworks Inc. Stake Reported

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

WASHINGTON -- William J. Nasgovitz, president of Heartland Advisors Inc., a Milwaukee investment advisory firm, said he holds a 5.2% stake in Rexworks Inc.'s common stock for investment purposes. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Nasgovitz said he holds 93,552 shares, including 39,000 shares purchased between May 4 and May 21 for $3.50 to $3.73 a share.

Rexworks, based in Milwaukee, makes concrete mixer equipment.
