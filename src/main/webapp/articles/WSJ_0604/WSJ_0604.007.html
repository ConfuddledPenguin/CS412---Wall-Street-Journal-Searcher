Who's News: Carrington Laboratories Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

CARRINGTON LABORATORIES INC. (Irving, Texas) -- Selvi Vescovi, former president of Upjohn International, was elected chairman of this pharmaceutical company. Clinton Howard, president and chief operating officer, was elected to the additional post of chief executive, succeeding Thomas J. Marquez, who remains on the board. The position of chief operating officer was eliminated. Additionally, George DeMott, former group vice president of American Home Products Corp., Richard P. Storm, president of Rorer International Pharmaceuticals, and Karl H. Meister, former president of Schering-Plough Animal Health, were elected to the board, succeeding three members whose terms expired.
