Financing Business: Culbro Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

CULBRO Corp., New York, said it closed a three and a half year, $90 million revolving credit agreement. Proceeds will be used to replace short-term debt and for working capital and general corporate purposes. The banking group was led by Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. Culbro businesses include cigars, tobacco and consumer product distribution, and industrial products.
