Smurfit to Buy Line in France

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

LONDON -- The packaging and paper company Jefferson Smurfit Group PLC, Ireland's largest industrial concern, said it will purchase the corrugated case operations of Cie. Generale de Papiers S.A. of France for about 235 million francs ($41 million). Smurfit said the planned acquisition, its first in France, "represents an important step in Smurfit's stated aim of expansion in Europe." The deal requires approval by the French company's shareholders.
