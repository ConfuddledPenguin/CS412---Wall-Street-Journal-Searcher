Ex-Im Bank Backs Israeli Loan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

WASHINGTON -- The U.S. Export-Import Bank said it will guarantee a $20.4 million loan to Israel Aircraft Industries Ltd. for the purchase of equipment from U.S. suppliers. The Israeli government-owned aerospace company plans to buy aircraft engines, test equipment and other machinery for manufacturing, modifying and repairing commercial aerospace products. The Ex-Im Bank said the Garrett Engine Division of Allied-Signal Aerospace Co., a Phoenix, Ariz., subsidiary of Allied-Signal Inc., will be the primary supplier of the equipment, which is valued at about $24 million.
