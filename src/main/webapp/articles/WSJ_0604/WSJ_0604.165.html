Who's News: ABB Asea Brown Boveri Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/04/90

ABB ASEA BROWN BOVERI Ltd. (Zurich, Switzerland) -- Donald H. Rumsfeld was named to the board of this electrical-engineering company. Mr. Rumsfeld, a senior advisor to William Blair & Co., of Chicago, becomes the eighth member and the only American on this company's board. He was a U.S. ambassador to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization during 1973 and 1974 and secretary of defense in the Ford administration from 1975 to 1977.
