Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/04/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

BJ Services Co., a unit of Baker Hughes Inc., initial offering of nine million primary shares of common stock, via First Boston Corp.

Citicorp, shelf offering of up to $500 million of preferred stock.

Crop Genetics International Corp., offering of two million shares of convertible exchangeable preferred stock, via Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. and Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc.

Du Pont Co., offering of $300 million of zero coupon convertible subordinated notes, via First Boston.

IKOS Systems Inc., proposed initial offering of 3.1 million common shares, of which 2.5 million will be sold be the company, 544,103 by holders, and 55,897 by underwriters, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Montgomery Securities.

Premark International Inc., shelf offering of up to $200 million of debt securities.

Repligen Corp., proposed offering of two million common shares, of which 775,000 will be sold by the company, and 1,225,000 shares will be offered by Centocor Inc., via First Boston, Shearson Lehman and Hambrecht & Quist Inc.

Reynolds Metals Co., shelf offering of up to $200 million of debt securities.

Valhi Inc., offering of $100 million of convertible subordinated debentures, via Bear, Stearns & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc.

Wisconsin Pharmacal Co., initial offering of one million common shares, via Raffensperger, Hughes & Co.
