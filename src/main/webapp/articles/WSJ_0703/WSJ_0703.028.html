Semco Pipeline Authorization

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

PORT HURON, Mich. -- Southeastern Michigan Gas Enterprises Inc. said Semco Pipeline Co. received authorization to construct an extension and operate its Eaton Rapids pipeline. The pipeline will allow Semco to transport gas from the Eaton Rapids Gas Storage System to storage customers.

The Michigan Public Service Commission has authorized the issuance of as much as $24 million in long-term debt by the Eaton Rapids Gas Storage System. Proceeds of the debt will be used to refinance short-term debt incurred for the acquisition of property and construction of facilities for the storage system.
