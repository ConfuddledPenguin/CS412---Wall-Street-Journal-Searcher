Air Products & Chemicals Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

ALLENTOWN, Pa. -- Air Products & Chemicals Inc. said it completed the sale of its polyvinyl-chloride business to Pacific Western Extruded Plastics Co. Terms weren't disclosed. The sale involves plants in Calvert City, Ky., and Pensacola, Fla. Pacific Western Extruded Plastics, based in Eugene, Ore., is a privately held maker of PVC pipe.
