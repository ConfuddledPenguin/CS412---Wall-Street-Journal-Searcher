Schering-Plough Sells Line

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

MADISON, N.J. -- Schering-Plough Corp. said it completed the sale of its Maybelline cosmetics business for $300 million to Maybelline Holdings Inc., a newly created affiliate of Wasserstein Perella Partners L.P. The Wasserstein group agreed to the cash purchase a month ago, after a similar accord with another group collapsed. The first group was led by Playtex Family Products Corp. and Playtex and Maybelline management. Schering put the unit up for sale in December, saying it wanted to concentrate on pharmaceuticals.

Maybelline is a leading maker and marketer of color cosmetics in the U.S., Canada, Puerto Rico and other world markets.
