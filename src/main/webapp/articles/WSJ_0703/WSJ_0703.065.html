Hallwood Group Unit Forms Partnership, Hallwood Realty

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

DALLAS -- Hallwood Realty Corp. said it has created a new partnership, Hallwood Realty Partners L.P., which will exchange limited partner interests for outstanding limited partner interests in 11 partnerships now sponsored by Equitec Financial Group Inc. The exchange, said company officials, will be contingent on the approval of a majority of the interests held by investors in each partnership. The newly issued interests are to be listed on the American Stock Exchange under the symbol HRY.

Equitec, said Hallwood officials, which recently defaulted on significant debt obligations, believes the exchange is in the best interests of its investors. Equitec will withdraw as general partner once the exchange is completed, leaving Hallwood Realty Partners as indirect owner of 27 properties appraised at $534.7 million.

Dean Witter Reynolds Inc. is acting as dealer-manager of the exchange, which is scheduled to expire at 5 p.m. Aug. 24.

Hallwood Realty Corp. is a subsidiary of The Hallwood Group Inc., which engages in a range of merchant-banking services and specializes in corporate rescue financing. It is an investor in real estate, oil and gas, specialty restaurants, retailing and textiles.
