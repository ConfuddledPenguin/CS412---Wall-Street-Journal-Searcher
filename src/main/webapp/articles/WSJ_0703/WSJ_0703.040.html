Chock Full O' Nuts Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

NEW YORK -- Chock Full O' Nuts Corp. said it completed the acquisition of Everfresh Products Inc., a privately held processor and packer of peanuts. Terms weren't disclosed. Dr. Leon Pordy, chairman and chief executive officer of the coffee and food service company, said the new acquisition was "profitable," with sales of over five million pounds of peanuts in fiscal 1989.
