Marketing Brief -- King World Productions Inc.: Net Income Increases 23% For Fiscal Third Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

King World Productions Inc., New York, said fiscal third-quarter net income climbed 23%, as it continued to take in higher license fees from three television shows it distributes -- "The Oprah Winfrey Show," "Wheel of Fortune" and "Jeopardy]" King World also said it benefited from its TV news show "Inside Edition," which it produces and distributes.

For the period ended May 31, net rose to $14.2 million, or 54 cents a share, from $11.6 million, or 46 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue grew 12% to $81.2 million from $72.4 million. The latest results reflect a charge of six cents a share for amortization and depreciation from the December 1988 acquisition of WIVB-TV of Buffalo, N.Y.

The company recently announced a 3-for-2 stock split, payable July 23 to stock of record July 9. The per-share net King reported doesn't reflect that split.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, King World shares fell 37.5 cents to close at $37.125.
