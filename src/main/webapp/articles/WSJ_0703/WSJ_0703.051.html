Who's News: NCNB Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

NCNB Corp. (Charlotte, N.C.) -- Robert B. Lane, 52 years old, previously corporate executive vice president, general banking, at NCNB Texas, was named president of the banking unit, succeeding Kenneth D. Lewis, who last week was named president, general banking, at the parent company, a bank holding company.
