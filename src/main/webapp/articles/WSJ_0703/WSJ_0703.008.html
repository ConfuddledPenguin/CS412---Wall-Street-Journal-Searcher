Motor Vehicle Sales in Japan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

TOKYO -- June sales of cars, trucks and buses in Japan increased 5.6% from a year earlier to 536,731 units, a record for the month, the Japan Automobile Dealers' Association said. Vehicle sales in Japan have posted monthly records for 16 months in a row, supported by strong domestic demand as the nation's economy continues to expand. First-half sales totaled a record 3.06 million units, a 13% increase from a year earlier.
