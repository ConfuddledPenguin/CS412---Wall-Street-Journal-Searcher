Who's News: Bristol-Myers Squibb Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

BRISTOL-MYERS SQUIBB CO. (New York) -- Frederick S. Schiff was named vice president and controller at this pharmaceutical and consumer products company. Mr. Schiff, 42 years old, succeeds Anthony W. Ruggiero, 49, who joined Reader's Digest Association Inc. as vice president and chief financial officer.
