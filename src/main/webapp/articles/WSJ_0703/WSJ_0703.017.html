General Signal Interest in Borri

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- General Signal Corp. said it acquired a majority interest in Borri S.p.A. Elettronica Industriale, an Italian maker of uninterruptible power supplies. General Signal said that Borri, which had $16 million in sales in 1989, will operate under its present management as an international division of General's Sola Electric subsidiary. Terms weren't disclosed.
