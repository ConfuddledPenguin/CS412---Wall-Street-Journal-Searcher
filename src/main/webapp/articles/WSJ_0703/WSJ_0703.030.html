  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

DALLAS -- Comstock Resources Inc. said it has signed a letter of intent to enter into a gas purchase contract with Maple Gas Corp. The agreement will require Maple to spend at least $20 million for pipeline gathering facilities, and a gas processing plant capable of accepting all casing head gas production from Comstock's leases in four Texas counties. All casing head gas from the Comstock properties will be dedicated to Maple for 15 years.
