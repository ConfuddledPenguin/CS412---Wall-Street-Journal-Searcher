Sales of New Cars in Europe

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

LONDON -- Sales of new cars in Europe in May rose 3.8% from a year earlier and 1.8% from April, but analysts said the performance doesn't alter their view of underlying sluggishness or their forecasts for continuing flat sales this year. The April sales were down 4.3% from a year earlier. The data are from industries and governments in the 12 countries that normally account for 94% of noncommunist Europe's passenger car sales.

A booming West German economy and buoyant French market headed growth in the latest month, countered by declines in Britain and Spain, where consumers are subject to tight monetary regimes.
