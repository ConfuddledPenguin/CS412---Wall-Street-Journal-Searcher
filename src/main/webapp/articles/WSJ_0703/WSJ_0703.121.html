International Brief -- Scottish & Newcastle Breweries: Pretax Profit for Fiscal '90 Rose 33% to $320.6 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

Scottish & Newcastle Breweries PLC of Britain said pretax profit for the year ended April 29 rose 33% to #183.3 million ($320.6 million) from #138.2 million a year earlier, helped by strong gains in brewing and the company's new leisure division. Profit after taxes, less minority interests and allocations to profit-sharing and before an extraordinary gain of #424.4 million from sales of assets, rose 27% to #116.1 million from #91.3 million a year earlier, when the company had an extraordinary loss of #4.8 million in connection with successfully defending a hostile takeover bid. Earnings per share increased 23% to 28.7 pence from 23.4 pence. Profit attributable shareholders surged to #535.5 million from #81.2 million. Operating profit rose 32% to #210.9 million from #159.8 million.

Sales climbed 20% to #1.24 billion from #1.03 billion.

The company is recommending a final dividend of 8.58 pence a share, making a total payout of 13 pence, an increase of 20% from 10.83 pence a year earlier.
