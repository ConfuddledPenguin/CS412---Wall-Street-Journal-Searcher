REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: School Reform

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

The Los Angeles school board has relented to public pressure and voted 6-1 to expel kids who bring guns to high schools or junior highs. Knives and BB-guns are still not an automatic cause for dismissal in the 610,000-student district. Board member Rita Walters, from a central-city area worst affected by campus violence, was a holdout to the end, saying that firearm-toters should instead be taught to "write and read and compute . . . so they have a sense of belonging." Her constituents seem to have a better sense of where those characters belong and don't belong.
