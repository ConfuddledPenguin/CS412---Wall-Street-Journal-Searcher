Who's News: May Department Stores Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

MAY DEPARTMENT STORES Co. (St. Louis) -- William D. Edkins, 38 years old, was named senior vice president of strategic planning at this retailer. He is currently senior engagement manager at McKinsey & Co., a Pittsburgh-based management consulting firm. Mr. Edkins will succeed Ken C. Hicks, who was named senior vice president and general merchandise manager of home furnishings at May Merchandising Corp., a unit of the retailer. Mr. Hicks, 37, will succeed Thomas A. Larkin, 67, who is retiring.
