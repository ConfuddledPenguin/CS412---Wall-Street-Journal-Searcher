Technology Brief -- Energy Conversion Devices Inc.: Alliance Is Set With Canon To Build Solar Cells in U.S.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

Energy Conversion Devices Inc., Troy Mich., said it has agreed to form a joint venture with Canon Inc. of Japan to manufacture solar cells in the U.S. Canon is making an initial $30 million capital contribution to the joint venture, including a credit of $8 million for canceling a term loan to Energy Conversion Devices. Energy Conversion Devices is contributing its solar manufacturing plant and a technology license.

Eliichi Kondo, chief executive of Canon's chemical products operations, will be president and chief executive officer of the joint venture.
