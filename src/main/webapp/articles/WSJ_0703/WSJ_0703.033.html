Jack Henry & Associates

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

MONETT, Mo. -- Jack Henry & Associates Inc. said it and Allied Capital Corp II ended talks to merge Henry and Peerless Systems Inc., an Allied affiliate. No reason was given, and officials couldn't be reached for comment. Henry said it does not intend to pursue a sale, merger or business combination with other parties. This past February, the data-processing systems concern had said it would evaluate unsolicited acquisition inquiries.
