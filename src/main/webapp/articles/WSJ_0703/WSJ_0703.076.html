P. Leiner Says Earnings Fell

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

TORRANCE, Calif. -- P. Leiner Nutritional Products Corp. said it expects to report that net income fell "substantially" in the fiscal first quarter ended Saturday. A year earlier the nutritional products concern reported net income of $929,000, or 18 cents a share. Revenue declined about 15% to about $27 million from $32.2 million a year earlier. Leiner attributed the decline to the recall of L-tryptophan amino acid products, overall softness in vitamin sales, and a delay in introduction of new products and programs. The company previously said it expected lower first-half results, and believes that sales should improve during the second half of fiscal 1990.
