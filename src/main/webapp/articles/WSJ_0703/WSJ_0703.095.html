Business Brief -- Perry Drug Stores Inc.: Revco Purchases Reinforce Chain's Michigan Stronghold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

Perry Drug Stores, Inc., Pontiac Mich., said it has signed an agreement to acquire for an undisclosed price 24 additional former Revco drugstores throughout Michigan. Separately, Perry said it has completed the acquisition of 16 former Revco drugstores in the Detroit area, previously announced on May 19.

The sales are part of a larger transaction in which Revco sold 221 stores through Chapter 11 proceedings to Reliable Drug Stores Inc. of Indianapolis.

The acquisitions tie in with Perry's strategy of filling in existing markets and of concentrating on Michigan, said Jack A. Robinson, Perry's chairman and chief executive officer. With these two acquisitions, Perry will operate 210 drugstores throughout Michigan, reinforcing its position as Michigan's No. 1 drugstore chain. Perry also operates 23 stores in the Chicago area.
