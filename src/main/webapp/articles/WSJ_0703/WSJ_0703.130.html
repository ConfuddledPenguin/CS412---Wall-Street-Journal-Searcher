International Brief -- Rothmans International PLC: Firm Will Buy Dutch Unit Of American Brands Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

Rothmans International PLC, London, said Monday that it has agreed to buy the Dutch tobacco unit of American Brands Inc. for 245 million guilders ($131 million). The purchase of Theodorus Niemeyer B.V., a unit of American Brands's Gallaher Ltd. subsidiary, is aimed at strengthening Rothmans's position in the pipe and fine-cut tobacco markets in continental Europe, the company said.

Gallaher will continue to market the Dutch company's Saamson, Can and Holland House tobaccos in the U.K. and Ireland.

Niemeyer is expected to show net assets of 172 million guilders as of June 30. For the nine months ended June 30, pretax profit is estimated to have been 34 million guilders.

American Brands is based in Old Greenwich, Conn.
