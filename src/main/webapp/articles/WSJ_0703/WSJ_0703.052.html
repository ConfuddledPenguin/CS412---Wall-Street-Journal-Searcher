NUI Early Retirement Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

BEDMINSTER, N.J. -- NUI Corp. said it has offered an early retirement incentive to about 6.5% of its work force in an effort to pare operating expenses. NUI, the parent of natural gas distributors Elizabethtown (N.J.) Gas Co. and City Gas Co. of Florida, said it could incur a pretax cost of about $2 million if all eligible employees accepted the offer. But it said it hopes to reduce costs by $5.5 million. It said about 50 of the 770 NUI and Elizabethtown employees would be eligible; City Gas employees have a separate pension plan.

Under the offer, all employees covered under the pension plan and born before Jan. 1, 1932, would be eligible. The company's incentives include crediting eligible employees with three more years credited service for purposes of calculating their pension benefits. NUI said eligible employees have until Sept. 30 to choose.
