U.S. Tobacco Prices Rise 4.3%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- U.S. Tobacco Sales & Marketing Co. announced a price increase of 4.3% on all its moist smokeless tobacco, effectively immediately. The subsidiary of U.S. Tobacco Co. raised its prices 7.5% late last year. U.S. Tobacco Sales & Marketing's primary brands in the smokeless-tobacco market are Copenhagen, Skoal, Skoal Long Cut and Skoal Bandits. The concern also markets pipes, imported cigars and other tobacco-related products.
