Financing Business: TW Holdings Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

TW HOLDINGS Inc., Spartanburg, S.C., said it entered a note purchase agreement with a group led by Morgan Stanley & Co. for $160 million of mortgage financing for 240 Denny's restaurants, which TW owns and operates.The company said the mortgage notes will bear interest at 11% for a 10-year term. Financing of the notes, which were placed in the Japanese market, is expected to close within two weeks. The net proceeds of about $120 million will be used to reduce senior bank debt. The company said it signed a letter of agreement separately under which Financial Security Assurance will guarantee $225 million of mortgage notes for 398 Hardee's and Quincy's Family Steakhouse restaurants that TW owns and operates.
