Who's News: Metalclad Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

METALCLAD Corp. (Anaheim, Calif.) -- Mark Tomberlin was named acting chief executive officer of this industrial insulation contracting company. He succeeds Leland E. Sweetser, 62 years old, who retains his post as chairman. Mr. Tomberlin, 38, had served as chief financial officer. Bruce Haglund, a board member, said Mr. Tomberlin had been named acting chief executive to give him time to help formulate a long-term plan for the company and gain some operating experience. Glenn W. Mayer, 39, was elected president of Metalclad's principal unit, Metalclad Insulation. He succeeds Russell Saunders, 70, who is retiring.
