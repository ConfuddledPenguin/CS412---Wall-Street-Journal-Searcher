Midlantic Sells Asset

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

EDISON, N.J. -- Midlantic Corp. said it completed the sale of its credit card business to Manufacturers Hanover Corp. for $60 million. In addition, Midlantic said it sold $250 million of securities backed by home equity loans originated by its lead bank, Midlantic National Bank.

Both sales are part of Midlantic's efforts to reduce its asset size and shore up its troubled commercial real estate loan portfolio. Midlantic has indicated that it expects to post a "modest" loss for the second quarter.
