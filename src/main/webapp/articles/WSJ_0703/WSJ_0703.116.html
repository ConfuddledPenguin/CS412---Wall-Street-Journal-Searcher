Burlington Coat Factory

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

BURLINGTON, N.J. -- Burlington Coat Factory Warehouse Corp. said it plans to buy back as much as $10 million of its common stock on the open market, or privately over the next two years. Based on the current selling price of $13.25 a share on the New York Stock Exchange yesterday, the company could repurchase as much as 755,000 shares. Currently 17,797,000 shares are outstanding.

Burlington, which operates a chain of discount apparel stores, said it plans to use the shares for stock option plans and for other purposes.
