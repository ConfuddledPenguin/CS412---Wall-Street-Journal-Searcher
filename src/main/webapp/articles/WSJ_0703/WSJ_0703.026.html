Martin Lawrence Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

VAN NUYS, Calif. -- Martin Lawrence Limited Editions Inc. said it bought back 500,000 common shares between April 30 and Friday at $4.38 to $7.25 a share. The board also authorized the repurchase of as many as one million common shares on the open market and through private transactions over the next 12 months. The publisher and seller of artwork said the buy-back would be funded by internally generated cash. As of Friday, Martin Lawrence had 7,799,648 common shares outstanding.

Allen A. Baron, chief financial officer, said the company expanded the buy-back program because it believes the stock is a good investment at its current trading levels. The stock closed yesterday at $4.375 a share, unchanged, in national over-the-counter trading.
