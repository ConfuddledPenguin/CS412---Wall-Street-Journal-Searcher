Dividend News: Jackpot Enterprises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

JACKPOT ENTERPRISES Inc. raised the quarterly to eight cents from seven cents and declared a 5% stock dividend, both payable July 31 to stock of record July 17. The Las Vegas, Nev., operator of slot machines said the shares distributed as a stock dividend will not receive the cash dividend. The stock dividend will reduce the conversion price of Jackpot's $34.5 million of 8.75% convertible subordinated debentures due May 1, 2014, to $14.74 a share from $15.14 effective July 17. Separately, Bristol Holdings Inc., also of Las Vegas, authorized a special dividend of one Jackpot common share for every 200 shares of Bristol common stock, payable July 31 to stock of record July 18. The dividend is expected to involve about 17,000 Jackpot shares, which will reduce Bristol's stake in Jackpot to about 4.5%.
