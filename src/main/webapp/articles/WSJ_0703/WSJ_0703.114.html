Ogden Corp. Unit, Universal Services Form Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

NEW YORK -- Ogden Corp. said its Ogden Allied Services Corp. unit and Universal Services, a Seattle-based remote services concern, have formed a joint venture, Universal Ogden Services. Ogden said the company, to be based in Seattle, will be one of the largest remote services companies in the U.S., providing catering, housekeeping and other services to hundreds of offshore and other secluded locations throughout the U.S., Canada and Europe.

Universal Ogden, with 3,000 employees, will serve roughly 50,000 meals daily; it is expected to have revenue of $135 million for 1991, an Ogden spokesman said.

Universal Services was founded in 1946 to house and feed the large work force rebuilding defense bases in Alaska following World War II. Ogden, in a statement, said a division of Ogden Allied Services pioneered the remote site catering business to the oil and and gas drilling industries in the Gulf of Mexico.
