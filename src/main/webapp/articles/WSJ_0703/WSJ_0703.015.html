Who's News: Equitable Life Assurance Society

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

EQUITABLE LIFE ASSURANCE SOCIETY (New York) -- Raymond L. Colotti was named executive vice president, a new post at this life insurance and financial services company. Mr. Colotti, 44 years old, was previously senior vice president of finance. He is responsible for treasury, planning and finance functions in Equitable's office of the chief financial officer.
