Credit Ratings: First City Bancorp., Unit, Credit Ratings Placed Under Review

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

HOUSTON -- The credit ratings of First City Bancorp. of Texas Inc., a bank holding company, and its lead bank, First City, Texas-Houston, have been placed under review for possible downgrade by Moody's Investors Service Inc. The review could affect about $641 million in securities, including First City Bancorp. senior long-term debt, now Ba-1, and its subordinated debt, now Ba-2; and First City National long-term deposit obligations, now Baa-2, and short-term deposit obligations, now Prime-3.

Moody's cited First City Bancorp.'s announcement of an increase in nonperforming loans, which was expected to be $200 million by the end of the second quarter, up from $140 million at the end of 1989. But a First City Bancorp. spokesman said the company "looks forward to the opportunity to discuss its loan portfolio" and believes that a thorough review will confirm that current ratings should be maintained.
