Who's News: Tokai Bank Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

TOKAI BANK Ltd. (Nagoya, Japan) -- Eiichi Kimata was named a director. Mr. Kimata, 50 years old, fills a vacancy on Tokai's board of 30 members, and is currently general manager of the company's New York branch, which holds $15 billion of the bank's $229 billion total assets.
