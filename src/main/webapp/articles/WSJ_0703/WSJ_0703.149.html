Business Brief -- Federal Express Corp.: Mexican Documents Carrier, Aeroenvios S.A., Purchased

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

Federal Express Corp. said it purchased Aeroenvios S.A. de C.V., a closely owned carrier of documents and small packages based in Mexico City. Terms weren't disclosed. Federal Express said Aeroenvios, which has annual sales of $8 million, was formed in 1988 as a Federal Express licensee that handled Federal Express pickups and deliveries in Mexico.

Federal Express, a Memphis, Tenn., express transportation concern, said its managing director for Mexico, Puerto Rico and the Caribbean, Fernando Duenas, was named to the additional post of managing director of the new company, which will be called Federal Express, Mexico. Alejandro Espejo, who was general director of Aeroenvios at the time of the purchase, will serve as a consultant to Federal Express, it said.
