Business Brief -- Foremost Corp. of America: Home Insurance Firm Halts 5-Month Search for a Buyer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

Foremost Corp. of America, Grand Rapids, Mich., said it had ended a five-month search for a buyer for the company. "We have proceeded diligently over the past five months in identifying and contacting potential purchasers," said Richard L. Antonini, Foremost's president and chief executive officer. "Our efforts, however, have been unsuccessful and, accordingly, the board has determined that it is not in the best interests of the company and its stockholders to continue to explore a possible sale."

Foremost, which provides insurance for manufactured homes and recreational vehicles, lost $20.8 million in 1989. For the quarter ended March 31, Foremost posted earnings of $5.2 million, down from $11.9 million a year earlier.
