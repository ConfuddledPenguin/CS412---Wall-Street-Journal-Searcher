Who's News: Integra Says Corcoran Quits 2 Senior Posts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

DALLAS -- Integra-A Hotel & Restaurant Co., said that Thomas J. Corcoran Jr. resigned as president and chief executive officer but will remain on the board. He has been succeeded by David R.A. Steadman, an Integra director for the past year and president of Atlantic Management Associates Inc. Mr. Corcoran's resignation came a day after Integra said an agreement providing $10 million in new capital for the company was terminated. The failed agreement also would have allowed Hallwood Group Inc. to sell its 13% stake in Integra.

Company officials were unavailable to say whether Mr. Corcoran's resignation was related to the failed agreement.
