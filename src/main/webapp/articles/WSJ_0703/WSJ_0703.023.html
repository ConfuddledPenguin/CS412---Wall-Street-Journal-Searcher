McDonnell Douglas Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. received an $85.4 million Navy contract for F-18 fighters and T-45 trainers.

General Dynamics Corp. got a $63.5 million Army contract for M-1 tank support.

Cubic Defense Systems was issued a $34.6 million Army contract for training-center instruments.

Grumman Corp. was awarded a $16.7 million Navy contract for F-14 aircraft parts.

Burle Industries Inc. received a $10.3 million Air Force contract for television cameras.
