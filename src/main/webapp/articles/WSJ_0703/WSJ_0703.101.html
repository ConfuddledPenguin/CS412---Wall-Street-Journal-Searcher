Photographic Sciences Patent Suit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

WEBSTER, N.Y. -- Photographic Sciences Corp. said it sued Symbol Technologies Inc., alleging infringement on two PSC patents relating to hand-held bar code scanners. L. Michael Hone, PSC's president and chief executive officer, said the company is seeking a court order to prevent permanently Symbol and its officers from infringing on its patents.

Damages weren't specified, Mr. Hone said, but the suit seeks punitive damages of not less than three times the court judgment for actual damages.

Symbol, based in Bohemia, N.Y., is a competitor of Photographic Sciences.
