Raw-Steel Output Fell 1.8% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 1.8% last week to 1,858,000 tons from 1,893,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output was slightly higher than the 1,850,000 tons produced a year earlier.

he industry used 82.7% of its capability last week, compared with 84.2% the previous week and 83.6% a year ago.
