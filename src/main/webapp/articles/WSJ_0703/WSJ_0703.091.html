Business Brief -- GNI Group Inc.: Firm Expects New Plant To Boost Results Sharply

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

GNI Group Inc. said the start-up and commercial operation of a new chemical resource processing plant will result in a nearly 40% increase in both revenue and net income for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1991. For the year ended June 30, 1989, the Houston company reported a loss of $75,000, or two cents a share, on revenue of about $12.5 million. Results for the period included a $550,000 charge related to the write-down of assets. The company expects to release earnings for fiscal 1990 in early August.

Located at GNI's Deer Park, Texas, complex, the plant provides waste reclamation and custom processing services for the chemical and petrochemical industries.
