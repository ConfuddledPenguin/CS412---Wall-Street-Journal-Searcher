REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Coffee, Tea and Liberty

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

"Continental Lands in Managua," blared the 24-point headline. We did a double-take. The reactivation of a new Contra force? No, just the most hopeful sign yet that perhaps things really are getting better in Central America. It was a Continental Airlines' newspaper ad inaugurating a direct flight to Managua from Houston. "We make getting down to business in Nicaragua easy, and reliable," the ad explained. We'd say that's exactly what the voters had in mind when they voted the Sandinistas out of office last February.
