Coastal, Arkla Units Set Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

HOUSTON -- The Coastal Gas Marketing Co. unit of Coastal Corp., an energy holding company, said it signed a 12-year contract with the Arkla Energy Marketing Co. unit of Arkla Inc., a natural gas concern, to purchase as much as 7.3 billion cubic feet of natural gas a year. Terms weren't disclosed. Coastal said the gas will be sold to a cogeneration plant jointly held by it and the Public Service Electric & Gas unit of Public Service Enterprise Group Inc. of Newark, N.J. The plant is at Coastal's Eagle Point refinery in New Jersey.
