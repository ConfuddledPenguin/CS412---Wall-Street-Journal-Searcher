Business Brief -- Sterling Software Inc.: NASA/Ames Research Pact Is Signed 2 Years After Bid

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

Two years after initially bidding to provide software to the NASA/Ames Research Center in California, Sterling Software Inc., Dallas, said it has signed a three-year contract with a two-year renewal option. The contract has been valued by NASA at $210 million, according to company officials. Sterling has had a similar contract with Ames since 1970, but in 1988 lost its renewal bid to Computer Sciences Corp. of El Segundo, Calif. Sterling appealed, on the basis that Computer Sciences hadn't submitted the low bid, but lost. It appealed again and won, with a panel of the Board of Contract Appeals of the General Services Administration concluding that Computer Sciences had misrepresented its bid.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Sterling closed at $10.625, unchanged.
