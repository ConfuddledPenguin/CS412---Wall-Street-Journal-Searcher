Range Rover Raises Prices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

LANHAM, Mass. -- Range Rover of North America, which imports luxury four-wheel-drive vehicles, said it had raised the base price of the Range Rover by 2.6%, effective yesterday. The lowest-priced Range Rover will cost $39,025, a $1,000 increase. Range Rover, the U.S. sales arm of the Rover Group PLC of Great Britain, is also tacking a $1,000 price increase on its Country model, raising its price to $41,125.
