Cimflex Sells Robot Operations

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

PITTSBURGH -- Cimflex Teknowledge Corp. said it sold its Gantry and Merlin robot operations, in Detroit, to AEG Westinghouse Industrial Automation Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. Cimflex, a supplier of software products and systems for factory automation, said the divestiture is in keeping with its strategy to focus on its core software and systems businesses.

AEG Westinghouse is a joint venture of Westinghouse Electric Corp., Pittsburgh, and AEG, the West German electrical products concern and unit of Daimler-Benz AG.
