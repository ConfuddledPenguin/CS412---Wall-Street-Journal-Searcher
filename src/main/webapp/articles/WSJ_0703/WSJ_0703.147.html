Business Brief -- Portland General Corp.: Agency Proposes to Narrow Revenue Rise Utility Seeks

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/03/90

Portland General Electric Co. said the staff of the Oregon Public Utility Commission recommended increasing the Portland General Corp. unit's 1991 revenue by $6 million, sharply less than the $89 million increase the Portland, Ore., company had requested. In March, the utility filed a request for the 11.4% increase in estimated 1991 revenue and a return on equity of 13.5%. The electricity price increase proposal will be negotiated during the next several weeks, and public hearings are scheduled to begin Sept. 5.

A spokesman for Portland General Electric, which serves 570,000 customers, said the company has requested a final order to be issued in time for it to implement the new rate structure on Jan. 1.
