Business Brief -- Campbell Soup Co.: ABA Holding Inc. Acquires Juice Bowl Products Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/03/90

Campbell Soup Co., Camden, N.J., said it sold its Juice Bowl Products Inc. subsidiary to ABA Holding Inc. Terms weren't disclosed. Campbell, which is trying to sell several businesses to boost profit, disclosed in January that it had put Juice Bowl on the auction block. The company said the unit, which had sales in fiscal 1989 of about $60 million, no longer fits with plans to focus on Campbell's two main beverages, tomato and V8 juices.

The sale involves a plant in Lakeland, Fla., which employs 220 people. Campbell said ABA Holding will retain the work force, the plant and the Juice Bowl name.

ABA Holding, based in Cranbury, N.J., is the parent of a Belgium fruit juice company, Looza N.V.
