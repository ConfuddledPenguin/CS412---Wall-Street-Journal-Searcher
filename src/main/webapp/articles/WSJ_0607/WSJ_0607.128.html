Business Brief -- Tambrands Inc.: Relocation of Headquarters Set in Plan to Cut Costs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

Tambrands Inc., a women's care products maker based in Lake Success, N.Y., plans to move its headquarters to Westchester County, north of New York City, to cut costs by choosing a smaller site. The company said it will sell its Long Island offices and expects to complete the move by the end of 1991. The new head office site, which hasn't been determined, will be closer to Tambrands' Technical Center in Palmer, Mass., and to its U.S. plants, all in New York state or New England, which have a total of 1,300 employees. Of the 200 employees in Lake Success, 50 will be offered posts at the Technical Center, which has 210 employees, and 150 will move to Westchester. Tambrands has 4,100 employees world-wide.

In the past year, the company has determined to focus on feminine care, with its Tampax tampons and Maxithins sanitary pads. Tambrands has been praised by analysts for its strong restructuring moves, including payroll cuts and stock buy-backs.
