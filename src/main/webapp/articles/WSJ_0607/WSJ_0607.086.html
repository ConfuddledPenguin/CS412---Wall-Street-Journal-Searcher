United Gaming Inc. Stock Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- United Gaming Inc. said it completed the sale of 1.2 million new shares to its chairman, Alfred Wilms, for $10 million, bringing his stake in the company to about 54%. The operator of gambling machine routes and seller of gambling equipment said it received an opinion from an independent investment banking firm as to the fairness of the $8.32-a-share price, which was 90% of the average trading price over a 20-day period.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, United Gaming closed at $9.25, down 12.5 cents.
