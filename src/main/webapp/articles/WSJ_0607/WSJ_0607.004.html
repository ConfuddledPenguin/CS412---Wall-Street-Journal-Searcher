Who's News: Primerica Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

PRIMERICA Corp. (Greenwich, Conn.) -- This financial-services concern named Daniel J. McAthie to the new post of vice president and treasurer of Fingerhut Cos., its 70%-owned direct-mail marketing unit in Minneapolis. Mr. McAthie had been vice president and treasurer of CVN Cos., a direct-marketing company in Minneapolis acquired last fall by QVC Network Inc., a West Chester, Pa., television retail merchandiser.
