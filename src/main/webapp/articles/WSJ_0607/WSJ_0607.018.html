Who's News: Control Data Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

CONTROL DATA Corp. (Minneapolis) -- William J. Miller was named executive vice president and chief financial officer of this computer systems and services concern. Mr. Miller, acting president of Control Data's Micrognosis unit, succeeds John K. Buckner, who resigned as chief financial officer in August 1989. Since that time, the company's president and chief executive officer, Lawrence Perlman, has served as acting chief financial officer. Mr. Miller was formerly president and chief executive officer of Imprimis Technology Inc., a former Control Data unit that was sold to Seagate Technology Inc. Control Data said John J. Driver, vice president, marketing, for Micrognosis, will succeed Mr. Miller as president of the business.
