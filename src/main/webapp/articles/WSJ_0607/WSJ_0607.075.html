Northwest Air's Traffic Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- Northwest Airlines, a unit of NWA Inc., said its May traffic rose 11% to 4.17 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.76 billion miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline said its May load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, dropped to 64.3% from 66.1%.

For the first five months of this year, Northwest's traffic rose 19% to 19.82 billion revenue passenger miles from 16.73 billion miles a year earlier. The load factor rose to 63.8% from 62.7%.
