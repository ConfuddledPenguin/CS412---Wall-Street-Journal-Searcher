Financing Business: SCEcorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

SCEcorp., Rosemead, Calif., said its Southern California Edison unit sold $200 million of 31-year first and refunding mortgage bonds to a group of underwriters led by Salomon Brothers Inc. for about $191 million. The 9.25% bonds were purchased by Salomon for 95.44% of the principal amount. Salomon will reoffer the bonds, due 2021, at 95.92%. The company will use proceeds from the financing for redeeming, repaying or retiring debt, financing construction, or other general corporate purposes.
