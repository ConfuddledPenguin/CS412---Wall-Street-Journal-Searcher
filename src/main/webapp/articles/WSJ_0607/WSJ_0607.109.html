International Brief -- GPA Group Ltd.: Irish Plane Lessor's Profit Jumped 59% in Fiscal 1990

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

GPA Group Ltd., an aircraft lessor based in Shannon, Ireland, said net income for the year ended March 31 rose 59% to $242 million from $152 million a year earlier. Revenue surged 88% to $1.96 billion from $1.04 billion.

Fiscal fourth-quarter net jumped 49% to $62.6 million from a year earlier, on revenue that soared 125% to $714.5 million.

GPA said it gained 20 new aircraft lease customers in fiscal 1990. At fiscal year end, its portfolio stood at 240 airplanes on lease, with 68 airlines in 41 countries.
