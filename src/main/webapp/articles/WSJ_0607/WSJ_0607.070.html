Upjohn Set to Sell Africa Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

KALAMAZOO, Mich. -- Upjohn Co. said it signed a letter of intent to sell its Asgrow S.A. seed operations in South Africa to the unit's management. Terms weren't disclosed. Completion of the deal is subject to U.S. and South African regulatory approval. Upjohn will continue to operate its Upjohn Ltd. human-pharmaceutical and animal-health business in South Africa.
