Financing Business: Trans Mountain Pipeline Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

TRANS MOUNTAIN PIPE LINE Co., Vancouver, British Columbia, said it plans to issue 35 million Canadian dollars (US$29.8 million) of 11.50% Series C debentures, maturing June 20, 2010. Trans Mountain, an oil pipeline concern 67.1%-owned by B.C. Gas Inc., British Columbia's natural-gas utility, said net proceeds of C$34.6 million will be used to reduce short-term debt. It said the issue, which follows a prior C$30 million debenture offering, completes financings linked to a C$61 million pipeline expansion program that is now completed. The debentures, which are redeemable at the company's option, will be purchased and subsequently offered privately by Gordon Capital Corp., a Toronto-based securities dealer, the company said. The issue is expected to close June 20.
