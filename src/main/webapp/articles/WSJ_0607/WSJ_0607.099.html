Business Brief -- Ishikawajima-Harima: Tokyo Company to Develop New Boeing Jetliner Engine

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

Ishikawajima-Harima Heavy Industries Co. said that it agreed with Rolls-Royce PLC of Britain to develop an engine for Boeing Co.'s new 300-seat jetliner. Ishikawajima-Harima of Tokyo said the company will shoulder 5% of the cost for developing the Trent 800 engine for the Boeing 777 jet, estimated at about #400 million ($674.6 million).

Rolls-Royce is reported urging another Japanese company, Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd., to join the development project, which will begin this summer.
