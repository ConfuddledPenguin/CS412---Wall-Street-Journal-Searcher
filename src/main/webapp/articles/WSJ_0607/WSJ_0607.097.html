Japan's Imported Vehicle Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

TOKYO -- Sales of imported cars, trucks and buses in Japan increased 21.4% in May from a year earlier to 16,325 units, a record for the month, the Japan Automobile Importers' Association said. May marked the 77th consecutive month of year-to-year sales growth. The latest rise was led by sales of midsized cars from West Germany and the U.S. Sales of imported passenger cars totaled 16,152 units in May, up 21.3% from a year earlier, and accounting for 99% of overall import sales.

In the first five months of this year, sales of imported vehicles grew 43.9% to 91,369 units from a year earlier. Sales of passenger cars totaled 90,485 units, also up 43.9%.
