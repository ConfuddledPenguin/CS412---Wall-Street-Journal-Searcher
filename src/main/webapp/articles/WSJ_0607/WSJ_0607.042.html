Gottschalks' May Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- Gottschalks Inc. said May sales increased 23% to $18.4 million from $14.9 million in May 1989. Same-store sales -- sales at stores open more than a year -- were up 12%, the department-store company said. Apparel sales led the increases and home sales also grew, a Gottschalks official said. For the 17 weeks ended June 2, sales were up 22% to $71 million from $58.3 million a year before. Same store sales were up 12% in the period, Gottschalks said.
