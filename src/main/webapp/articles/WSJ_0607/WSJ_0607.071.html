Mitel Unit Sets Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

KANATA, Ontario -- Mitel Inc., a U.S. unit of Mitel Corp., agreed to pay as much as $10 million to acquire Coradian Corp., a U.S. distributor of telecommunications equipment. Mitel, a maker of telecommunications equipment, said the price could be reduced to a minimum of $7.5 million, depending on Coradian's financial results. Coradian, based in Latham, N.Y., had 1989 sales of more than $27 million. It reported a loss of $247,000 for the 1990 first quarter.
