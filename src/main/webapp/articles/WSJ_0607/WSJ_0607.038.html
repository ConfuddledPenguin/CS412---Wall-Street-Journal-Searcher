Financing Business: Staples Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

STAPLES Inc., a Newton, Mass., discount office-supplies retailer, said it is making a public offering of $70 million of 7% convertible subordinated debentures, due June 1, 2015, priced at par and convertible into common stock at $30 a share. Net proceeds will be used for financing new store openings and additional distribution facilities, increasing working capital, and other general corporate purposes. Goldman, Sachs & Co. is lead manager and Montgomery Securities and PaineWebber Inc. are co-managers of the offering.
