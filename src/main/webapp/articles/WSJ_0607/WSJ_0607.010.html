Who's News: Carter Hawley Hale Stores Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

CARTER HAWLEY HALE STORES Inc. (Los Angeles) -- Thomas E. Dokter, formerly president and chief executive of this retailer's Broadway-Southwest unit, was named president of its Broadway-Southern California operation, effective July 15. In his new post, Mr. Dokter, 51, will succeed Richard Clayton, 47, who was named to the new post of vice chairman. Carol Greer, 47, was named to succeed Mr. Dokter as president and chief executive of the Broadway-Southwest. Ms. Greer is currently senior vice president and general merchandise manager at Federated Department Stores' Atlanta operation, Rich's.
