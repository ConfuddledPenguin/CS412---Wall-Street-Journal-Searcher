International Brief -- Bass PLC: Firm to Add 50,000 Rooms To Its Holiday Inn Chain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

Bass PLC said it plans to add more than 50,000 rooms to its Holiday Inn hotel chain over the next three to five years, boosting total capacity by 16%. The British food, drinks and leisure concern's 1,576 hotels world-wide will have about 365,000 rooms, up from around 315,000 currently. About 30,000 of the rooms will be added in North America.

Bass also said cost-saving plans at its Holiday Inn business in North America are expected to save $17 million a year. The company said the move will result in a $20 million charge against earnings.

The U.K. group purchased the North American assets of Holiday Corp. in August for $1.98 billion after paying $475 million for the company's international hotels businesses in 1988.

Bass also said it plans to sell four of its Crest hotels to Buckingham International PLC, a Holiday Inn franchisee. Terms weren't disclosed.
