Midwest Energy Says FTC Seeks More Data On Proposed Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

SIOUX CITY, Iowa -- Midwest Energy Co. said the Federal Trade Commission has requested additional information about the company's proposed merger with Iowa Resources Inc. of Des Moines. The company said the request was made under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1975. Midwest said the FTC's action extends the waiting period, which would have expired last Sunday, to 20 days from the date the FTC receives the new information.

Shareholders of both companies have approved the proposed merger, which is termed a "merger of equals" by the two utilities. The merger would create a new holding company called Midwest Resources Inc., which would have an indicated market value of about $923 million.

Midwest said it will respond as promptly as possible to the request.
