Business Brief -- Placer Dome Inc.: Gold Producer Considering Withdrawing Bid for Stikine

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

Placer Dome Inc. said it is considering withdrawing its 229.5 million Canadian dollar (US$196.2 million) offer to acquire Stikine Resources Ltd., after a group that owns 42% of Stikine agreed Monday to tender its stock to a rival bid. Placer Dome, a big, cash-rich gold producer in Vancouver, British Columbia, said it won't decide whether to pull its C$67.50-a-share offer until it obtains further details of the pact between the Stikine shareholder group and Toronto-based Corona Corp. Stikine plans to issue an information circular today that is expected to include details of the "lock-up" agreement.

On Monday, Corona announced a sweetened offer to acquire Stikine and Prime Resources Group Inc., joint owners of a big gold deposit in northwestern British Columbia. As part of Corona's offer, holders of Stikine's 3.4 million shares would receive 6.5 Corona shares and two shares of Corona Gold, a new unit, for each Stikine share. Stikine holders would also be eligible to subsequently receive a special stock dividend.
