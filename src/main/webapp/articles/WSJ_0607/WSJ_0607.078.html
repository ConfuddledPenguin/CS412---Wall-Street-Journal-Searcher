Polish Bank's New York Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

NEW YORK -- Bank Polska Kasa Opieki S.C., Poland's largest commercial bank, said it will open a branch office in New York later this year. Theodore M. Sysol, an attorney for the bank, said the Park Avenue branch will be the first full branch operation of any Eastern bloc banking concern. A few other Eastern European banks have representative offices in the U.S.

The Bank Polska branch will provide commercial banking services including taking deposits in excess of $100,000, making loans, arranging wire transfers and providing letters of credit.
