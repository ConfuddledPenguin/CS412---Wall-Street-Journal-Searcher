Highland Superstores' Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

PLYMOUTH, Mich. -- Highland Superstores Inc., a consumer electronics retailer, said its May sales rose 1% to $63.9 million from $63.1 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year fell 1%. The data exclude sales from the Shreveport, La., and Milwaukee markets, which the company recently left.
