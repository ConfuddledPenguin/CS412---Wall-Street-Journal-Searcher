Technology: Motorola Inc. Says Japan Picks Its Voice Technology

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

TOKYO -- Motorola Inc. said the Japanese government had, as expected, officially chosen its digital voice technology for part of Japan's emerging digital mobile-phone standard. As reported, Motorola's coding technology last month performed best in tests conducted by a division of the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications. Seven other companies, including Nippon Telegraph & Telephone Co., participated in the contest.

Motorola and government officials emphasized that the company's technology would be used to draft part of the standard, and that other aspects of the total standard would be drafted by other companies as well.

An official news conference announcing the decision is scheduled for today.
