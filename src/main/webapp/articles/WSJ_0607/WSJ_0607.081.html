USAir's Traffic Fell in May

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

ARLINGTON, Va. -- USAir, a unit of USAir Group Inc., said its May traffic declined less than 1% to 2.97 billion revenue passenger miles from 2.98 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's May load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 58.9% from 62.8%.

For the first five months of this year, USAir's traffic rose 5.8% to 14.23 billion revenue passenger miles from 13.45 billion a year earlier. The load factor fell to 58.7% from 59.1%.
