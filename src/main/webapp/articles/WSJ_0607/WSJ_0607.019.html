Who's News: Roy F. Weston Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

ROY F. WESTON Inc. (West Chester, Pa.) -- Henry Diamond, a partner in the Washington, D.C.-based environmental law firm Beveridge & Diamond, and Marvin O. Schlanger, senior vice president and chief financial officer of Arco Chemical Co., were elected directors of this environmental engineering, design and consulting company, expanding board membership to 13.
