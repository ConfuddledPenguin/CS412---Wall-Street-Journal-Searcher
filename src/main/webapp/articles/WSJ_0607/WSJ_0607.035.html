Tyco Toys Says Talks Ended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

MOUNT LAUREL, N.J. -- Tyco Toys Inc. said that talks between it and a party seeking to acquire the company were terminated without an agreement being reached. The party's identity wasn't disclosed. The toy maker said last week that it received an unsolicited approach from the potential buyer. Tyco's stock, which rose on heavy volume following that announcement, closed yesterday at $22.75 a share in national over-the-counter trading, down $4.75.
