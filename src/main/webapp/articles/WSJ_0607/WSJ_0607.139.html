Who's News: Power Spectra Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

POWER SPECTRA Inc. (Fremont, Calif.) -- John W. Pauly, 67 years old, was appointed a director of this electronics company, increasing board membership to eight. Mr. Pauly, a retired U.S. Air Force general, is chairman and chief executive officer of Systems Control Technology Inc., Palo Alto, Calif., and is a member of the Army Science Board.
