Spain's Producer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

MADRID -- Spain's producer price index in April was unchanged from March, but climbed 2.2% from a year earlier, the Statistics Institute said. The April index registered a 0.6% drop in energy prices from March. Wholesale energy prices, however, jumped 4.9%.

The April index of wholesale prices showed a 0.3% increase in capital goods costs from March; capital goods prices surged 4.7%.

Consumer goods prices in April rose 0.1% from March and 3.2% from a year earlier.
