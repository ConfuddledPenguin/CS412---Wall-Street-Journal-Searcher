Who's News: 3Com Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

3COM Corp. (SANTA CLARA, Calif.) -- Eric Benhamou, president and chief operating officer of this network-systems company, was appointed a director. Mr. Benhamou, 34 years old, succeeds Robert Metcalfe, who retired Tuesday from the board and his position as marketing vice president. Mr. Metcalfe is now a private consultant to 3Com.
