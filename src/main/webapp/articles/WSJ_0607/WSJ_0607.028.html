Manufacturers Hanover Trust

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

NEW YORK -- Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. said it agreed to purchase credit-card accounts and receivables from Midlantic Corp., Edison, N.J. At April 30, the receivables totaled about $252 million; Manufacturers Hanover said it is paying about a $60 million premium. The bank said it purchased the business to expand its national credit-card operations. The purchase will boost its credit-card receivables to more than $3.3 billion.

Midlantic Corp., a bank-holding company, said the sale will help restructure its balance sheet. The company has been hurt by the deteriorating New Jersey real-estate market.

Midlantic said it will enter into an agreement to continue providing customers with credit cards under the Midlantic name.
