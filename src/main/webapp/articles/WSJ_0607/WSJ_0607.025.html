Swedish Bank Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's third largest commercial bank, PKbanken, and one of the country's most profitable banks, Nordbanken, completed their merger. PKbanken's successful bid for Nordbanken, presented last December, marked the end of a fierce battle for the provincial bank between PKbanken and Sweden's largest commercial bank, S-E-Banken.
