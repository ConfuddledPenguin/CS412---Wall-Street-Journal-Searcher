International Brief -- Reed International PLC: Annual Pretax Profit Rose 11%, Lifted by U.S. Results

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

Reed International PLC, a British publishing company, said pretax profit for the year ended March 31 rose 11% to #302 million ($509.3 million) from #271 million a year earlier, at the top of market expectations and boosted by strong results in the enlarged U.S. business publishing unit. Sales edged up 1.5% to #1.58 billion from #1.55 billion.

Increases of 11% also were posted in profit after taxes and minority interests to #211.3 million from #189.7 million, and earnings per share to 38.2 pence from 34.5 pence. Net profit attributable to shareholders fell 55% to #211 million from #498 million; the year-earlier figure was boosted by an extraordinary gain of #308.4 million largely from the sale of a business.

In the U.S., where, among other acquisitions last year, Reed in November paid $303.7 million to buy the legal publisher Martindale-Hubbell Inc., operating profit on business publishing soared 76% to #80.2 million, and sales increased 45% to #464 million.
