Financing Business: Ryka Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/07/90

RYKA Inc., a Weymouth, Mass., maker of athletic footwear for women, said it signed a letter of intent to raise $4 million through a public stock offering, subject to approval by the Securities and Exchange Commission. Ryka intends to sell units consisting of one share of common stock and one warrant, exercisable over a five-year period.
