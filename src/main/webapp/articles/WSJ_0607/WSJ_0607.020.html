Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

BizMart Inc., offering of 2,750,000 common shares, of which two million will be offered by the company and 750,000 by holders, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Robertson, Stephens & Co.

CNA Financial Corp., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.

Hologic Inc., secondary offering of 700,000 common shares, to be sold by holders, via Hambrecht & Quist Inc., Needham & Co. Inc. and Adams, Harkness & Hill Inc.

Modtech Inc., initial offering of 1.2 million common shares, of which one million shares will be sold by Modtech and 200,000 by a holder, via Seidler Amdec Securities Inc.

Research Frontiers Inc., offering of 786,500 common shares.
