Who's News: Star Technologies Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/07/90

STAR TECHNOLOGIES Inc. (Sterling, Va.) -- Robert C. Compton, 43 years old, president and chief executive officer of this maker of high-performance computer products, assumed the additional post of chairman. He succeeds Herbert S. Shaw, 57, who will remain a director. The board also accepted the resignations of Ralph R. Shaw and J. Robert Newton, reducing board membership to four. No successors were appointed.
