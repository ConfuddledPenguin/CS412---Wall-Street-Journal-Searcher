TII's SEC Filing to Be Delayed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

COPIAGUE, N.Y. -- TII Industries Inc., a maker of surge protection and specialized electronic devices, said filing of its results for the year ended June 29 with the Securities and Exchange Commission will be delayed, because of the "imminent completion" of negotiations with bank lenders to restructure loan agreements. TII said release of the fiscal 1990 data to shareholders is expected to be delayed as long as 15 days.
