I Have to Ask]

This I address to minds

Superior:

Isn't there somewhere

A motive that isn't

"Ulterior"?

And why must wisdom,

However translated,

Be forever referred to

As "accumulated"?

Something else that

Makes me glowerful:

Need the Ways and Means

Committee always be

"Powerful"?

-- Dow Richardson.

A Stitch in Time

There is one issue

On which there's no doubt:

A pressing problem

Should be ironed out.

If the irritant's not

Cleared up in a twinkle,

It may very well

Add a new wrinkle.

-- Mimi Kay.
