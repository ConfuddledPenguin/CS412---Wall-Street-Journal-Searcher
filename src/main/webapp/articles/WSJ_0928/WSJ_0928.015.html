Jefferies Group Elects Chairman

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

LOS ANGELES -- Frank E. Baxter, the 53-year-old president and chief executive officer of Jefferies Group Inc., was elected to the additional post of chairman. The post had been vacant since March 1987 when founder Boyd L. Jefferies resigned before pleading guilty to two federal counts of violating federal securities laws. He later received a suspended sentence and was fined $250,000. Jefferies is a securities firm primarily engaged in securities trading.
