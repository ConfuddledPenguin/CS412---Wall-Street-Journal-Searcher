Elf Aquitaine Plans Stock Split

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

PARIS -- Societe Nationale Elf Aquitaine said it plans a 2-for-1 stock split to help improve liquidity in the secondary market for its stock. The French state-controlled oil group said the split will take effect Nov. 23 and will cut the nominal value of its stock to five French francs (95 cents) a share from 10 francs. Elf said the split won't have any impact on its share-purchase warrants, as they expire Oct. 19.
