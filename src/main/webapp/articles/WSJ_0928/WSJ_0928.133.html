Business Brief -- Van Dorn Co.: Pact Reached With Chief To Settle Holder Claims

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

Van Dorn Co., Cleveland, said it reached agreement with its chairman and chief executive officer, Lawrence C. Jones, to settle claims against him in a shareholder derivative action. Van Dorn said the agreement provides for the company to petition the court to dismiss all claims against Mr. Jones. In exchange, Mr. Jones agreed to make undisclosed payments in settlement of disputed claims, dating back to the 1960s.

The agreement was approved by the company's board following a recommendation by its special litigation committee.

Company officials weren't available for further comment.

The lawsuit was filed last year by Vincent Coleman, former president of Van Dorn Heat Treating Co. The suit alleges that Mr. Jones engaged for decades in "fraudulent and deceptive acts" including the use of Van Dorn credit and funds for personal business such as landscaping and maintaining personal real estate.

Van Dorn makes containers and plastic molding machinery.
