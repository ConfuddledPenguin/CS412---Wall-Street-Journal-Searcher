Letters to the Editor: It's Always Too Late

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

Your Aug. 28 page-one article on the U.S. military's ill-preparedness for conflict in the Middle East reminded me of a quote from "Wolf Willow," a book by Wallace Stegner: "{M}ost education trains us for the past, as most preparation for war readies us for the war just over." Arlie R. Nogay

Pittsburgh
