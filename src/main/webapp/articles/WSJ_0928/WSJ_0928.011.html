Who's News: Novatny Resigns Posts At Laclede Gas Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

ST. LOUIS -- Laclede Gas Co. said that its president and chief operating officer Donald A. Novatny, 55 years old, resigned from the company, effective Monday, for personal reasons. Succeeding Mr. Novatny in both positions at the natural gas distributor is Robert C. Jaudes, 56, executive vice president-operations and marketing.

Lee Liberman, chairman and chief executive officer, said in a written statement that Mr. Novatny's resignation was unexpected.

"I had intended to recommend his promotion to chief executive officer of the company at our annual meeting next January. However, he told me he had decided after much thought that he simply did not want the responsibilities and pressures" associated with that job, Mr. Liberman said.

A Laclede spokesman said that Mr. Novatny was unavailable to comment on his resignation.
