Who's News: Sony Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

SONY Corp. (Tokyo) -- Abbott L. Brown was named executive vice president and chief financial officer of Columbia Pictures Entertainment Inc., a Los Angeles-based subsidiary of this international electronics concern. Mr. Brown, who also will be named to the Columbia board, succeeds Larry Ruisi, who continues as an executive vice president. Mr. Brown was previously the Southern California group managing partner at Price Waterhouse, the accounting firm. Price Waterhouse separately announced that L. Dale Crandall succeeds Mr. Brown. Previously, he was managing partner of the firm's San Francisco office.
