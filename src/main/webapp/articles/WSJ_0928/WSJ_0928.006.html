Metro Bancshares Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

JERICHO, N.Y. -- Metro Bancshares Inc. directors approved the buy-back of as much as 5%, or about 118,000 shares of the savings bank holding company, subject to regulatory approval. The company, which owns Bayside Federal Savings Bank, has 2.4 million shares outstanding.
