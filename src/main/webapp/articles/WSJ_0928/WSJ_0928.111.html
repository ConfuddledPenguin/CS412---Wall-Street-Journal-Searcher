Who's News: Toys `R' Us Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

TOYS "R" US Inc. (Paramus, N.J.) -- Rick Markee, previously vice president of the Target Stores unit of Dayton Hudson Corp., was named vice president, general merchandise manager, at this toy concern's clothing unit, succeeding Lorelei Yudice, who retired.
