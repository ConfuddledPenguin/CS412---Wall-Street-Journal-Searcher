Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

PAPERS: A National Labor Relations Board administrative law judge ordered Tribune Co.'s Chicago Tribune newspaper to sign a union contract reached with Chicago Mailers Local 2 on July 27, 1987, and make its terms retroactive to that date. Union officials said signing the contract under those terms could cost the paper $10 million or more in back pay. The company said the ruling will be appealed and declined comment on the cost estimate.
