Hees Bid for Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

TORONTO -- Canadian Express Ltd. said Hees International Bancorp, a merchant banking concern controlled by Toronto's Bronfman family, and the Toronto Stock Exchange agreed to extend to Oct. 18 the deadline on Hees's bid to acquire as many as 40 million Canadian Express shares for as much as 26 million Canadian dollars (U.S.$22.5 million). The offer of 65 Canadian cents (56 U.S. cents) a share was to be made next Wednesday. The bid would raise Hees's voting stake in the Toronto banking company to about 56% from 40%. Canadian Express said it asked for the extension to allow time for the completion of a fairness opinion.
