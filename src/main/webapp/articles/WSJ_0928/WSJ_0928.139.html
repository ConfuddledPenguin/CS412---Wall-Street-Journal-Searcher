Japanese Motor Vehicle Exports

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

TOKYO -- Japan's exports of cars, trucks and buses fell 0.5% in August from a year earlier to 444,545 units, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers' Association said. Motor vehicle exports have been zigzagging for some time, though they showed an increase of 2.9% in July and 4.1% in June.
