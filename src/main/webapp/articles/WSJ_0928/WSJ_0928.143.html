Business Brief -- RCN B.V.: Buehrmann-Tetterode Raises Stake in Supplier to 100%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

Buehrmann-Tetterode N.V., the Dutch paper distributor, office supply and packaging concern based in Amsterdam, said that it raised its stake in RCN B.V. to 100%, from 51%. Financial details of the transaction weren't disclosed.

With a work force of 70, Netherlands-based RCN, supplies office automation equipment within that country.

Buehrmann-Tetterode said the acquisition of the remainder of RCN's shares will strengthen Buehrmann-Tetterode's existing position in office supply in the Netherlands, Spain and West Germany. Buehrmann-Tetterode initially obtained its stake in RCN in August 1989.
