Ex-First Boston Banker, Gookin, to Work in Dallas

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

DALLAS -- Clifford Gookin, an investment banker laid off by First Boston Corp. in December, is moving to Texas to work for Rauscher Pierce Refsnes Inc., a unit of Inter-Regional Financial Group. Mr. Gookin, who was the subject of a recent front-page profile in The Wall Street Journal, was hired by First Boston in Wall Street's 1980s boom. He was let go during an industrywide retrenchment.

Mr. Gookin, 33 years old, was hired by Rauscher Pierce Refsnes, a Dallas-based regional brokerage firm, to work in corporate finance, the company said. Mr. Gookin said he was "thrilled" to find a job in investment banking.
