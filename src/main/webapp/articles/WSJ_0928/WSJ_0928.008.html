Letters to the Editor: All Dazzle, No Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

Regarding your Aug. 27 page-one article on Hartmarx and men's clothing, I wonder how your writer could take such a skewed approach. According to him, soon I shall be called upon by men robed in mustard-colored, Italian-cut $1,000 suits. No doubt I'll be so disarmed and distracted that I'll fail to concentrate on their salesmanship. Enter the dowdy fellow in an American-made Hartmarx or Brooks Brothers -- the poor guy; his appearance is so ordinary that he actually closes the deal with me. Betsey Lynch

Cincinnati
