Business Brief -- Chambers Development Co.: Firm to Buy Four Concerns That Collect Solid Waste

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

Chambers Development Co., Pittsburgh, said it will buy four Georgia companies engaged in the collection and hauling of commercial and residential solid waste. Terms weren't disclosed. The acquisition of the four companies is expected to generate annual revenue of $1.7 million. Global Environmental Inc., Southern Waste Systems Inc. and Chadwick Enterprises will be consolidated with Chambers' Atlanta operations, and Dependable Garbage Service will be consolidated with Chambers' Cedartown operations.

Chambers is an environmental concern offering services in collection, hauling, disposal, recycling and landfill development. Separately, Chambers said it received a permit from the Pennsylvania Department of Environmental Resources for its Fulkroad landfill in Dauphin County near Harrisburg. Chambers plans to construct and operate about 15 acres of landfill space at the facility.
