Who's News: Manufacturers Hanover Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

MANUFACTURERS HANOVER Corp. (New York) -- Magna L. Dodge was named officer-in-charge of the loan syndications and sales group at Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co., a subsidiary of this bank-holding company. Ms. Dodge succeeds Michael P. Zarrilli, who was named managing director of corporate finance for the holding company. Ms. Dodge, also an executive vice president, headed the bank's media group unit. She is succeeded by Joan Fitzgibbon, a senior vice president, and Cecily Stone, who was named senior vice president. They will jointly head the media group unit.
