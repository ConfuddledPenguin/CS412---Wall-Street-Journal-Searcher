Citizens Utilities Co. And Louisiana General Amend Merger Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Citizens Utilities Co. and Louisiana General Services Inc. said they have amended their pending merger agreement. The amended agreement would change the share exchange ratio in the stock swap merger so that Louisiana General shareholders would receive from 0.6719 to 0.8 share of Citizens Utilities common Series B for each Louisiana General common share held.

The exchange was originally set at 0.6719 share. The actual figure will depend on the average share price of Citizens shares during a period prior to the merger.

The amendment to the merger agreement is subject to approval by the shareholders of both companies. Citizens is a public utility, providing telecommunications, electric, gas and water services in 12 states. Louisiana General is primarily involved in residential and commercial gas operations and industrial gas operations in Louisiana.
