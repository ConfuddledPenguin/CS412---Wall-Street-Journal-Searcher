Corrections & Amplifications

A LIST of the world's 25 largest securities firms compiled by Wright Investors Service should have given Nomura Securities' capital as $12.8 billion, not $10.9 billion. The list represents figures as of each company's 1989 fiscal year's end. In addition, Principal Mutual Life is based in the U.S., not the U.K. as indicated in the list of the world's 50 largest insurers. Both lists appeared in The Wall Street Journal Report on world business.

(WSJ Sept. 28, 1990)
