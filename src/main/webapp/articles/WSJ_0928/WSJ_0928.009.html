Michael Baker Sets a Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

BEAVER, Pa. -- Michael Baker Corp. and Gehrmann Consult G.m.b.H. of West Germany, both architectural and engineering concerns, said they agreed to establish a company to provide engineering services in Germany and Eastern Europe. The company, which hasn't been named, will provide environmental management and facilities planning and design services, and will operate separately from the Michael Baker G.m.b.H. subsidiary, which was formed Aug. 28.
