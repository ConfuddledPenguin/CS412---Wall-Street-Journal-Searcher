VF Corp. Expects Disappointing Results For 3rd, 4th Quarters

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

WYOMISSING, Pa. -- VF Corp., hurt by substantially lower orders and shipments in its Lee jeans division, said financial results for the third and fourth quarters will be "well below recent estimates." The apparel maker expressed "serious concerns about general economic and industry conditions." The company said current sluggishness appears to be linked to a decline in sales for jeans during the back-to-school season. Business at other VF divisions "appears to be meeting expectations," the company said.

The company also indicated that fourth-quarter results would suffer from the slump even more than third-quarter performance. VF didn't say what its recent estimates for the third and fourth periods had been, and wouldn't specify its revised estimates.

In the year-ago third quarter the company earned $52 million, or 80 cents a share, on sales of $695.4 million.

VF said several alternatives are being explored to respond to the unexpected shortfall. The company didn't elaborate.
