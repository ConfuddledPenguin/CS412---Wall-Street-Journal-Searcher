Business Brief -- Saskatchewan Oil & Gas Corp.: Firm's Largest Shareholder Plans to Cut Stake to 10%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

Saskatchewan Oil & Gas Corp., or Saskoil, said its largest shareholder plans to reduce its stake to 10% from 28%, by selling 145 million Canadian dollars (US$125.6 million) of Saskoil shares to several securities dealers. Saskoil said the sale by Saskatchewan government-owned CIC Industrial Interests Inc. of 10 million Saskoil shares at C$14.50 each is expected to close next month, pending regulatory approval.

A spokeswoman for Saskoil, an oil and gas producer that went public in 1986, said a group of seven securities dealers purchased the shares for redistribution. Under provincial law, no single investor can acquire more than 4% of Saskoil's shares.

In Toronto Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Saskoil's shares closed at C$14.375 each, down C$1.125 on volume of 203,000 shares.
