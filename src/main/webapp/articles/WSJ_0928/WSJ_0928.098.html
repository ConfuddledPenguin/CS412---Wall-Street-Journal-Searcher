Who's News: Interstate/Johnson Lane Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

INTERSTATE/JOHNSON LANE Inc. (Charlotte, N.C.) -- James H. Morgan was elected president and chief operating officer of this brokerage company. Mr. Morgan, 43, had originally joined Interstate/Johnson Lane in 1986, becoming senior vice president and director of investment policy. He resigned in July 1989 to become president of Morgan Investments Inc., Greenville, S.C. Mr. Morgan succeeds Elmon L. Vernier Jr., who will continue as a consultant to the company to pursue formation of an investment-management joint venture in which Interstate/Johnson Lane may be a participant.
