Action Auto Rental Inc. Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

CLEVELAND -- Action Auto Rental Inc. said it will buy back as many as three million of its common shares. The auto rental company had about 10.3 million shares outstanding in August, about 33% of which were owned by management. The company said share purchases will be made in the open market or otherwise, subject to the stock's price and other market conditions.
