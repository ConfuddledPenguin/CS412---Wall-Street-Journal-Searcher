Howtek, Adobe Systems in Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

HUDSON, N.H. -- Howtek Inc. said it signed an agreement with Adobe Systems Inc. to combine Howtek's Personal Color scanner with Adobe's Photoshop Limited Edition, a special version of Adobe's Photoshop software. Howtek said the combination of the two products will allow personal computers to use color for such applications as business graphics, presentations and color desktop publishing.

The product will be available for shipment by the end of October and will retail for about $2,595.

Howtek makes computer products for the color graphics market. Adobe is a software concern based in Palo Alto, Calif.
