Amerada Hess Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

NEW YORK -- Amerada Hess Corp. said its Canadian subsidiary completed the acquisition of oil and gas producing properties owned by a subsidiary of Placer Dome Inc. Amerada Minerals Corp. of Canada acquired 80% of the producing properties and 250,000 exploration acres from Placer Cego Petroleum Holdings Ltd. for about $215 million. The cost was 17% lower than Amerada stated last July; a spokesman said the earlier cost of $260 million dropped because of adjustments.

Amerada Minerals said it expects the properties, which are principally in Alberta and British Columbia, to produce 14,000 barrels per day of crude oil and natural gas liquids by 1992.
