Claims for Jobless Pay Rose in Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits rose to a seasonally adjusted 403,000 in the week ended Sept. 15 from 382,000 the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended Sept. 8 was a seasonally adjusted 2,602,000, or 2.5% of those covered by unemployment insurance, up from 2,471,000 the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate was 2.4%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended Sept. 15 rose to 2,307,400 from 2,137,500 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
