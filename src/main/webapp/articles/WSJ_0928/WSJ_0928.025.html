Union Pacific Unit Sets Deal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- The Union Pacific Resources Co. unit of Union Pacific Corp., Bethlehem, Pa., said it agreed with Oryx Energy Co. of Dallas to purchase producing properties in two fields for about $109 million. In one property, the Carthage Field in east Texas, the unit will acquire leases covering 31,500 acres. The other property, about 4,500 acres, is the Echo Springs Field in southern Wyoming. The two fields combined will increase Union Pacific Resources' net production of natural gas by more than 20 million cubic feet daily.
