International Brief -- Trusthouse Forte PLC: Pretax Profit for First Half Was Flat at $213.2 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

Trusthouse Forte PLC of Britain, a hotel and catering concern, said its first-half pretax profit was flat at #114 million ($213.2 million), despite growth in its three major divisions. Profit after taxes and minority interests, or net profit attributable to shareholders in the absence of any extraordinary items, rose 1.2% to #85 million from #84 million a year earlier. Per-share earnings rose 0.9% to 10.8 pence from 10.7 pence. The pretax profit figures included a #2 million net surplus on property and other disposals in the latest period, and an #11 million net surplus on disposals a year earlier. Trading profit, or profit after interest but before property disposals, increased 9% to #108 million from #99 million.

Sales grew 11% to #1.32 billion from #1.19 billion.

Trusthouse Forte said it is raising the interim dividend 10% to 2.75 pence from 2.5 pence.
