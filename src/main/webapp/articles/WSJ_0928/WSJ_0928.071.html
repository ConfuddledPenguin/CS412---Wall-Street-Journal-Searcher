Money-Fund Assets Rose in Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 524 money-market funds rose $361.6 million, to $409.12 billion, in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 256 general-purpose funds rose $681.3 million, to $145.53 billion, and assets of 105 broker-dealer funds fell $505.5 million, to $165.1 billion. Assets of 163 institutional funds rose $185.8 million, to $98.48 billion.

The total assets for the money-market mutual funds were revised for Sept. 19 to $408.76 billion. The increase reported for Sept. 19 was revised to $1.72 billion.
