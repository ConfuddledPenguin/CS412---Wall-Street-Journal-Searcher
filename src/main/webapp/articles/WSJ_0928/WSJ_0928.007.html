Canadian Steel Production

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 162,642 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, up 2.1% from 159,248 tons in the preceding week but down 42% from 278,888 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production fell 16% to 9.6 million metric tons from 11.4 million tons a year earlier.
