Data I/O Corp.'s Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

REDMOND, Wash. -- Data I/O Corp. said it expects to report lower-than-anticipated third-quarter sales. The electronics company said it expects sales in the quarter ended Sept. 28 to be $12.5 million to $13.5 million, about $1.5 million to $2 million less than analysts expected. Net income in the quarter will about break even, Data I/O said. The company blamed the sales shortfall on longer-than-expected production times of certain products.
