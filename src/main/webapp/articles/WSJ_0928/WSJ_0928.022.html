Norway's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

OSLO -- Norway's unemployment rate fell to 4% in late September from 4.8% at the end of August, largely because of government work programs, but the rate was up from 3.6% a year earlier, the state Labor Directorate said. Unemployment has more than doubled since 1987, when the annual unemployment figure was 2%.

The directorate said 86,800 people in Norway were without work in September, 17,600 fewer than in August but up 7,300 from September 1989.

Provisional figures showed that the total of people employed in government work and training programs in September was about 43,300, up 15,400 from August. The total was 57,300 in September 1989.
