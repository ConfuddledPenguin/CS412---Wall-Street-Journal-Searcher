Business Brief -- Degussa AG: ASTA Unit Buys Temmler, A Pharmaceutical Business

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/28/90

Degussa AG, a West German chemical and metal concern, said its ASTA Pharma AG unit bought a family-owned pharmaceutical business, Temmler-Group G.m.b.H. Terms weren't disclosed. Temmler, which expects 1990 sales of 25 million marks ($15.9 million), makes medicines indicated for colds, asthma, headaches, bronchitis and digestive disorders. With the acquisition, Frankfurt-based ASTA said it expects to solidify its national and global market strength by marketing an additional line of products.
