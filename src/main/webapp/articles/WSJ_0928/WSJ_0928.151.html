Central Illinois Public Service

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

SPRINGFIELD, Ill. -- The Securities and Exchange Commission approved formation of a holding company for Central Illinois Public Service Co., the company said. Holders of the gas and electric utility's common will become owners of the common of the holding concern, to be called Cipsco Inc., the utility said. The reorganization, scheduled for Monday, has been approved by the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, the Illinois Commerce Commission and the utility's holders.
