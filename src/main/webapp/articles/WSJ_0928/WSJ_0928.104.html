Apple Computer Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/28/90

CUPERTINO, Calif. -- Apple Computer Inc. said its board approved the buy-back of as many as 10 million of its common shares in the open market. Apple, with 120 million shares outstanding on June 29, said that current market prices for its shares were attractive and that it would make the purchases "from time to time." Apple said it has bought back about 30 million shares since 1986.

Apple stock fell $1.50 a share yesterday to close at $28.25 in national over-the-counter trading.
