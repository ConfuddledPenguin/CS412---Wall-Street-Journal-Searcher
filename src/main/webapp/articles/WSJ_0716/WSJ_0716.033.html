Who's News: Republic New York Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

REPUBLIC NEW YORK Corp. (New York) -- David Rubin was named chairman and Saul Langer senior executive vice president at Republic Factors Corp., a subsidiary of this bank holding company. Mr. Rubin, 64 years old, retains his post as president and chief exeuctive officer; Mr. Langer, 52, continues as head of the New York office of the factoring company.
