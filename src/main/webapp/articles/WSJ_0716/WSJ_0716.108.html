Who's News: United Education & Software Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

UNITED EDUCATION & SOFTWARE Inc. (Los Angeles) -- This troubled company said that Aaron Cohen resigned as president and chief executive officer. The company, whose principal unit filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection from creditors in December, didn't say why Mr. Cohen resigned and he couldn't be reached for comment. United Education said that its 67-year-old chairman, Stanley R. Schoen, will immediately assume Mr. Cohen's titles. Mr. Cohen, 54, will remain a director and will become a consultant to the company's ailing correspondence-school unit. The subsidiary operates about 12 schools, down from a peak of about 35 schools in late 1988.
