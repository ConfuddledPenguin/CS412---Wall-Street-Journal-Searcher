South Korean Auto Exports

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- Automobile exports more than doubled in June, but were still below last year's levels, the Trade and Industry Ministry reported. South Korean auto makers sold 27,240 cars overseas in June, compared with 12,365 cars in May. The June total was down 2.2% from a year ago, the ministry said.

June sales growth, although helped by Hyundai's introduction of a new subcompact, Scoupe, was based on recovering demand in the U.S. market, officials said.
