Allcom Trading Co. Formed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

LONDON -- Hungary's state mineral trading company, Mineralimpex, and international energy trader Phibro Energy AG have formed a joint venture international mineral trading company, a Mineralimpex official said. The company, called Allcom Trading Co., began operations July 1, trading "nonferrous ores, aluminum and oil products," the Hungarian official said.

The official said Hungarian law prohibited the new company from trading certain minerals, including iron ore and domestically produced crude oil.

The Mineralimpex official didn't specify the breakdown of shares in the partnership, but Hungarian joint ventures usually have been split 50-50 between Hungarian and foreign partners in the past.

The Mineralimpex official didn't give any further details of the venture, and Phibro officials weren't available to comment.
