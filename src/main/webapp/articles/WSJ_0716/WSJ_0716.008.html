Buiness Brief -- Pic `N' Save Corp.: Standstill Pact Is Extended By Retailer, Girard Partners

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

Pic `N' Save Corp. said it extended its standstill agreement with Girard Partners L.P. until after July 29 from July 15, a move that gives other potential bidders for the Domingus, Calif., discount retailer additional time to develop proposals. Under the agreement, both companies agreed not to solicit proxies or consents for control of the company. Pic `N' Save added over the weekend that it had extended the date of its annual meeting to Aug. 29 from Aug. 15. Holders of record July 29 will be able to vote at the meeting. Girard holds a 6.7% stake in Pic `N' Save.

In April, Girard Partners, a partnership controlled by David H. Batchelder, said it planned to make a bid for Pic `N' Save and indicated that if it succeeded it would sell the company. Pic `N' Save then said its board authorized its investment bankers to seek bids for the company.
