Mid Am Inc. to Acquire Thrift

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

BOWLING GREEN, Ohio -- Mid Am Inc. said it agreed to acquire Citizens Federal Savings & Loan Association, Bellfontaine, Ohio, in a transaction that doesn't require the bank holding company to pay cash or issue stock. Citizens Federal doesn't meet new capital requirements, so Mid Am said it agreed to inject capital into the thrift. Citizens Federal is a mutual institution, which means it doesn't have any shareholders and is technically owned by depositors. Mid Am has $930 million in assets; Citizens Federal, $80 million.
