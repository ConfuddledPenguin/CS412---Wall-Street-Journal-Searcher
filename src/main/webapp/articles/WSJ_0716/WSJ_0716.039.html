THOMSON CSF, a French manufacturer of military equipment, was selected by the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service to provide the technological system for producing a new generation of "green cards" for resident immigrants in the U.S. The cards themselves will continue to be made in Arlington, Texas. Thursday's edition incorrectly said the cards would be made in France. (See: "International: World Wire" -- WSJ July 12, 1990)

RODERICK A. YOUNG was named president and chief operating officer of Diasonics Inc. Mr. Young's title was misstated in Friday's edition.

(See: "Who's News: Diasonics Inc." -- WSJ July 13, 1990)
