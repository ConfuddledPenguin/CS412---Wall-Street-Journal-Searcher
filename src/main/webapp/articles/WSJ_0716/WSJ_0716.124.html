International Brief -- Moulinex S.A.: French Appliance Concern's Revenue Rose 17% in Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

Moulinex S.A. of France, an appliance concern, said its consolidated revenue for the 1990 first half climbed 17% to 2.51 billion francs ($457.2 million) from 2.15 billion francs a year earlier. The company said that the revenue rise was helped by the launch of a new line of household air conditioners in the Spanish market and that the increase was only 4% after adjustment for changes in the company's structure.

Moulinex didn't provide first-half earnings figures. For all of 1989, consolidated profit after payments to minority interests was 153 million francs on revenue of 5.13 billion francs.
