Japan's Corporate Bankruptcies

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

TOKYO -- Japanese corporate bankruptcies totaled 515 cases in June, down 19% from a year earlier, Teikoku Data Bank, a private credit research concern, said. The value of liabilities left behind by the failed companies totaled 88.19 billion yen ($597.9 million), up 7.4% from the year-earlier month.

June was the third consecutive month in which the total amount of liabilities grew on a year-to-year basis, indicating the increasing number of large-sized bankruptcies.
