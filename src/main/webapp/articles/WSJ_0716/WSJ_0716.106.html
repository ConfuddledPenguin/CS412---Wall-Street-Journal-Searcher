Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

WHO'S NEWS: Gilbert L. Wachsman resigned as chief executive officer of Lieberman Enterprises Inc., Live Entertainment's largest subsidiary, to pursue other business activities. Devendra Mishra, Live's president, assumed the additional duties of acting chief executive of Lieberman, a video, music and computer software mass-merchandising concern. . . . The New York Daily News, owned by the Tribune Co., named Robert B. Holzkamp senior vice president and director of advertising and marketing. He was vice president and director of advertising for the Chicago Tribune, a sister paper. He succeeds James Mason, who resigned as vice president of advertising and marketing.
