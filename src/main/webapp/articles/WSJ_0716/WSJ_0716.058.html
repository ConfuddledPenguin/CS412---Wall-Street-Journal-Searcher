Who's News: Scott Paper Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

SCOTT PAPER Co. (Philadelphia) -- Peter Harf was named a director of this paper-products concern, enlarging board membership to 14. Mr. Harf, 44 years old, is chairman and chief executive officer of Banckiser Group and its flagship company, Joh. A. Benckiser G.m.b.H.
