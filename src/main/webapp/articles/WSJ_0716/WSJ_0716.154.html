Bancorp Hawaii Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

HONOLULU -- Bancorp Hawaii Inc. said the Office of Thrift Supervision approved Bancorp's previously reported plan to acquire FirstFed America Inc. and its subsidiaries. The acquisition still must be approved by the Federal Reserve Board, Bancorp said.

Bancorp is a bank holding company with operations in Asia and the South and West Pacific. FirstFed has operations in Hawaii, Guam and Saipan.
