Hubbell Inc. Acquires Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

ORANGE, Conn. -- Hubbell Inc., a maker of electrical and electronic products for commercial, industrial, utility and telecommunciations markets, said it acquired the Marion, N.C., assets of the Brand-Rex Telecommunications unit of Brintec Systems Corp., Willimantic, Conn. It declined to disclose the amount of the cash transaction. Hubbell said the acquired assets produced sales of about $17 million last year, and make a broad range of parts used in telecommunications applications. It said the acquisition "represents a major addition to Hubbell's expanding participation in the premises-wiring market for industrial and commercial applications."
