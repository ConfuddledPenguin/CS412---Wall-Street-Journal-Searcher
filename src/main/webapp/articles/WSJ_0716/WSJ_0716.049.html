REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Sweet Revenge

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

This takes the cake. House Democrats Friday, in the name of fiscal responsibility, voted to freeze the office expenses appropriated for former Presidents. Rep. Andy Jacobs was bellowing something about "millionaires." Tension between the executive and Congress is natural, but it seems more than a little gratuitous for these Members to be reaching back to whack four American Presidents.
