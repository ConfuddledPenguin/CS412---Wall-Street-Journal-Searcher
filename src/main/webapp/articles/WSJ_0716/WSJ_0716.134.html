Business Brief -- Lincoln N.C. Realty Fund Inc.: REIT Resumes Negotiations With Silverstone Partners

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

Lincoln N.C. Realty Fund Inc. said in Palo Alto, Calif., that it resumed negotiations with Silverstone Partners over a possible tender offer by Silverstone to acquire Lincoln. The announcement is the latest in an on-again, off-again series of negotiations between the companies. Lincoln rejected an offer by Silverstone, a closely held Los Angeles partnership, in April. Lincoln in February signed a letter of intent to be acquired by Silverstone but Lincoln terminated the letter in March because Lincoln's banker couldn't confirm the financial fairness of the transaction.

Lincoln, a real estate investment trust, said the companies would negotiate the exact terms of a tender offer, in which Silverstone would purchase as much as all of Lincoln's common shares and warrants outstanding.
