Consolidated Enfield Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

TORONTO -- Consolidated Enfield Corp., formerly Enfield Corp., said it acquired 1,224,489 common shares of Consumers Packaging Inc. from Federal Pioneer Ltd. for 17.5 million Canadian dollars (US$15.1 million). Consolidated Enfield, a holding company indirectly controlled by Toronto financiers Peter and Edward Bronfman, said the shares were purchased to fulfill its obligation to Group Schneider S.A. of France, as part of Schneider's C$300 million acquisition of Federal Pioneer earlier this year.

Consolidated Enfield said that as a result of the share purchase, it owns 50% of the 20.8 million Consumers Packaging common shares outstanding.
