Business Brief -- Willamette Industries Inc.: Second-Quarter Profit Fell; Weak Markets Are Cited

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

Willamette Industries Inc., a forest products company based in Portland, Ore., said second-quarter earnings fell 7.4% to $43.6 million, or $1.71 a share, from $47.1 million, or $1.85 a share, a year earlier. Sales of $491.6 million were little changed from the year-earlier $491.5 million.

The company said that strong prices for its building materials helped earnings but that markets were weak for its pulp and converted-paper products. Operating rates for all facilities were strong in the quarter, Willamette said.

In the first half, profit eased less than 1% to $86.1 million, or $3.38 a share, from $86.9 million, or $3.42 a share, a year earlier. Sales rose 3.5% to $961.5 million from $929.3 million.
