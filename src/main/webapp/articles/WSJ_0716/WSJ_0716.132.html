Allegheny Ludlum Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

PITTSBURGH -- Allegheny Ludlum Corp. said its directors voted to exclude the steelmaker from certain provisions of Pennsylvania's new anti-takeover law. The legislation, signed into law April 27, allows publicly owned concerns to exclude themselves from all or part of its anti-takeover provisions within 90 days of passage.
