Business Brief -- Lynch Corp.: Second-Quarter Net Income Estimated at About $650,000

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

Lynch Corp. estimated second-quarter net income at about $650,000, or 50 cents a share, up at least 25% from a year earlier. Lynch also said full-year net should be at least $1.75 a share, or $2.2 million, up at least 33%. For the second quarter of 1989, the Greenwich, Conn., company reported net of $516,000, or 40 cents a share, on revenue of $23.2 milllion. In 1989 as a whole, the company had net of $1.7 million, or $1.31 a share, on revenue of $88.1 million.

The maker of glass-forming machines that diversified into transportation and telecommunications attributed the anticipated increase in 1990 earnings primarily to the telephone operations of Western New Mexico Telephone Co., acquired in the 1989 fourth quarter.
