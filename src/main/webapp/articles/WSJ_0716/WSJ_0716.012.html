United Dominion Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

TORONTO -- United Dominion Industries Ltd., an industrial holding company with interests in steelmaking, said it plans to redeem on Sept. 1 all of its 9.25% cumulative redeemable retractable Series 3 preferred shares outstanding for 25 Canadian dollars (US$21.59) a share, plus accrued dividends. About 2.2 million of the shares were outstanding at last report. United Dominion is 55.4%-held by Montreal-based Canadian Pacific Ltd., a resource and transportation concern.
