Commercial Vehicles in Italy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

MILAN, Italy -- Sales of new commercial vehicles in Italy fell 2.5% in June to 13,738 from 14,093 a year earlier, Anfia, the national association of car manufacturers, said. Commercial vehicles are small trucks or buses with a weight of as much as 3.5 tons. In the first six months of this year, commercial vehicle sales rose 6% to 87,730 units from 82,783 a year earlier.

Ford Motor Co. led among foreign brands with a 15.38% market share in June and 14.35% in the first half.
