Who's News: Xtra Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

XTRA Corp. (Boston) -- Lewis Rubin, president of this truck-leasing concern, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer, succeeding Charles F. Kaye, who resigned last March. The company also named as directors Martin L. Solomon and Francis J. Palamara, expanding the board to nine. Mr. Solomon, a managing director and general partner of Value Equity Management I L.P., owns about 14% of Xtra. Mr. Palamara is a self-employed business consultant.
