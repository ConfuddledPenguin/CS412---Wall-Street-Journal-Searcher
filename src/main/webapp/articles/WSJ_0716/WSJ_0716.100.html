Business Brief -- IntelliCorp Inc.: Firm Expects to Post Loss For Quarter; Stock Plunges

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

IntelliCorp Inc. said it expects to post a net loss for the fiscal fourth quarter because of lower-than-expected revenue. The announcement pushed the company's stock down 31% Friday.

The Mountainview, Calif., concern said it expected to report that revenue for the quarter ended June 30 fell "substantially short" of the year-earlier quarter's. IntelliCorp.'s net income in the year-earlier quarter was $394,000, or five cents a share, on revenue of $6 million. The maker of artificial-intelligence software cited an unexpected slowdown in sales of its old product line. The company said it is in the process of introducing a new product line.

IntelliCorp.'s stock closed at $4.75 a share, down $2.125, in national over-the-counter trading Friday.
