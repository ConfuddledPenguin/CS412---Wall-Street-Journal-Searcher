Who's News: Consolidated Freightways Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

CONSOLIDATED FREIGHTWAYS INC. (Menlo Park, Calif.) -- Donald E. Moffitt, 58 years old, was named to the new post of executive vice president, finance, of this transportation concern. Mr. Moffitt, who retired as vice chairman of Consolidated in 1988, has been managing financial restructurings of several transportation companies.
