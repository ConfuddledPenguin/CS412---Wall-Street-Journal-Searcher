Business Brief -- Kaman Corp.: Board Clears Buying Back Additional 500,000 Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

Kaman Corp., Bloomfield, Conn., said its board authorized purchasing as many as an additional 500,000, or 2.7%, of its 18.2 million Class A shares outstanding. Kaman has diverse businesses, including defense contracting; distributing bearings, power-transmission products and electronic parts; and manufacturing and distributing musical instruments, including guitars under the Ovation name.

The company recently completed a buy-back of 300,000 shares. In the latest buy-back, it will use the purchased shares for incentive and employee stock-purchase plans.

In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Kaman shares rose 25 cents to close at $6.875.
