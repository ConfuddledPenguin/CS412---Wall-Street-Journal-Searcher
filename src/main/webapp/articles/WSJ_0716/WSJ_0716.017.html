Who's News: Channel Home Centers Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

CHANNEL HOME CENTERS Inc. (Whippany, N.J.) -- Leon Berger resigned as chairman and president of this home-improvement store chain. Mr. Berger, 67 years old, is succeeded as president by Joseph Nusim, 54, who also was named chief executive officer, succeeding Malcolm L. Sherman. Mr. Sherman, 59, was named chairman. Mr. Nusim had been president, chief executive officer and a director of Makro Inc., a warehouse club with nine stores based in Cincinnati.
