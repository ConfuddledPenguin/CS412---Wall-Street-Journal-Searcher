Who's News: Merck & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

MERCK & Co. (Rahway, N.J.) -- Ronald A. Ahrens was named senior vice president for Europe, Canada, Latin America and Africa, a new post expanding the over-the-counter pharmaceutical business at the consumer-health group of the health-products giant. The 50-year-old Mr. Ahrens had been consumer-group president for North America at Bristol-Myers Squibb Co.
