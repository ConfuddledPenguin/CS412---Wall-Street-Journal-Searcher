Timken Files Debt Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/16/90

CANTON, Ohio -- Timken Co. said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed public offering of as much as $250 million in debt securities. The industrial manufacturer said it expects to issue from time to time these debt securities as medium-term notes, with terms to be determined at the time of each sale.

Morgan Stanley & Co. and J.P. Morgan Securities Inc. are expected to be the principal underwriters.

Timken said proceeds from the sale would be used for its capital expenditure program, additions to working capital, possible acquisitions and reducing existing indebtedness, including short-term debt.
