Australia's Shorn Wool Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

CANBERRA, Australia -- Australia's shorn wool production is forecast to fall to 1.01 million metric tons in the year ending June 30, 1991, from a record 1.03 million tons estimated for fiscal 1990, the Bureau of Statistics said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. The bureau estimated that 216.2 million sheep will be shorn in fiscal 1991, up 1.3% from a record 213.3 million a year earlier. But the amount of wool cut from each sheep will fall to about 10.3 pounds from 10.6 pounds.

The estimate for shorn wool production in fiscal 1991 is down from a forecast of 1.02 million tons of wool by the government's Bureau of Agricultural and Resource Economics in its report for the June quarter.
