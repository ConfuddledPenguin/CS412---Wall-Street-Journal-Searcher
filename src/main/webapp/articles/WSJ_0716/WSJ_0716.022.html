Kollmorgen Multiwire Division

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

SIMSBURY, Conn. -- Kollmorgen Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to sell the assets and business of its Multiwire division to Winthrop Investment Corp., a New York financial holding company. Terms weren't disclosed. But Kollmorgen said the sale, taken together with the recently reported sale of the discrete wiring part of its PCK Technology division, wouldn't significantly affect results.

A maker of motors and electro-optical systems, Kollmorgen earlier had said it would close the Multiwire division if it failed to find a buyer.
