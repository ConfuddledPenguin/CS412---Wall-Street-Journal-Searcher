Spanish Car Sales Slump 21%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/16/90

MADRID -- Spanish car sales plummeted 21% in June to 91,818 cars from 115,618 cars a year earlier, the Spanish Association of Car Makers said. The result brought sales in the year's first six months to 542,184 cars, down 8.7% from 593,801 cars a year earlier.

The drop in June sales exceeded even the most pessimistic projections, which anticipated a 16% decline.

The association said Spain's tight credit restrictions, coupled with a value-added tax of 33%, has hit auto sales hard.
