Who's News: National Cooperative Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

NATIONAL COOPERATIVE BANK (Washington) -- Grace Huebscher was named president and chief executive officer of the NCB Mortgage Corp. subsidiary. The parent provides financial services to cooperative enterprises. Ms. Huebscher switched positions with Bradford T. Nordholm, who was named president and chief executive of the NCB Business Credit Corp. subsidiary.
