ONEOK Settles Suits on Pacts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

TULSA, Okla. -- ONEOK Inc. said it settled three federal court "take-or-pay" lawsuits over natural gas supply contracts. Terms weren't disclosed. Two of the suits involved wells in Pittsburg County, Okla., and one involved wells and contracts in Carter and Love counties.
