Perini Investment Properties

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- Perini Investment Properties Inc. said it hired Lazard Freres & Co. as its financial adviser to help the company explore a possible restructuring, merger or sale. A Perini spokesman declined to elaborate. Earlier this year the real estate investment trust suspended its dividend, noting a cash flow decline.

For the quarter ended Sept. 30, 1990, Perini reported a loss of $956,000 in net cash flow, or 24 cents a share, a reverse of the year-ago quarter's net cash flow of $925,000, or 24 cents a share.

The company's shares closed yesterday at $6.125, up 75 cents, in American Stock Exchange composite trading.
