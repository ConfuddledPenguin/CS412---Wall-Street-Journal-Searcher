Angeion Acquires XMED

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Angeion Corp., a maker of cardiovascular products, said it acquired XMED, a research concern developing products for the automatic implantable defibrillator market. Angeion announced the acquisition plan in November. Angeion acquired all outstanding stock of XMED, a closely held Minneapolis company, which becomes a subsidiary of Angeion named AngeMed Inc.
