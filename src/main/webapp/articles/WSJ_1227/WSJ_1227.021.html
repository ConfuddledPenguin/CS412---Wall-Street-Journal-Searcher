Two Pesos Stock Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

HOUSTON -- Two Pesos Inc. said its president and chief executive officer, Ghulam M. Bombaywala, exercised his option to purchase 250,000 shares of the company's common stock from an affiliate of Marno M. McDermott Jr., chairman, for $375,000. The option was the first installment of an agreement that permits Mr. Bombaywala to exercise options on up to one million shares of Two Pesos common held by Mr. McDermott in 250,000-share increments every six months. Mr. Bombaywala now owns 2,250,000 shares, or 27.8%, of Two Pesos common, all purchased from Mr. McDermott. He also controls another 1,732,679 shares of the Mexican-style restaurant chain's stock through a voting agreement with Mr. McDermott.
