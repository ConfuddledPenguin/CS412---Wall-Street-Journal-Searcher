Business Brief -- Pansophic Systems Inc.: Two Investors Announce They Have a 5.7% Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

George Soros and Purnendu Chatterjee said they hold a 5.7% stake in Pansophic Systems Inc.'s common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the investors said they hold 1,026,800 Pansophic shares, including 806,800 shares bought from Oct. 29 to Dec. 20 at $7 to $8.25 each.

According to the filing, the shares were bought for investment reasons.

In Lisle, Ill., a Pansophic official said the software concern had no immediate comment.
