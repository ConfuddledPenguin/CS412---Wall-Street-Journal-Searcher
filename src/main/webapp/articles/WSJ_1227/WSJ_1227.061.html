RADIO: Minnesota Public Radio has agreed to purchase Twin Cities station WLOL 99.5 FM from Emmis Broadcasting Co. for $12 million. MPR, a nonprofit, noncommercial public broadcasting organization, said it will move its KSJN 91.1 classical music format to 99.5 -- which offers a clearer, higher quality signal. MPR's news and information format, currently broadcast on KNOW 1330 AM, will then move to 91.1, where it will broadcast under increased wattage. Emmis is a closely held Indianapolis broadcasting concern.

WHO'S NEWS: Jeff Weiner was named director of corporate development, a new post, at the Ziff-Davis Publishing unit of Ziff Communications Co. Mr. Weiner, previously a marketing consultant with over 20 years experience in the publishing industry, was a former publisher of PC Magazine, a Ziff-Davis publication.
