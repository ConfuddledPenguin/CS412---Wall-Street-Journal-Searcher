Ameritrust Has Building Delay

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

CLEVELAND -- Ameritrust Corp. said construction on its 60-story downtown headquarters will be delayed. The developer, Jacobs, Visconsi & Jacobs Co., said it asked the bank holding company for the delay due to uncertain economic conditions. The office tower, which will include a 484-room Hyatt Regency Hotel, was expected to open in 1994. Revised projections call for construction to begin in 1993, and it's to be completed in 1996.
