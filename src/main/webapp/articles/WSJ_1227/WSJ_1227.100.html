Business Brief -- Society For Savings Bancorp Inc.: Bank Firm Is Asked to Add Non-Managers to the Board

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

A group including Heine Securities Corp. of Short Hills, N.J., and Michael F. Price said it asked Society For Savings Bancorp Inc. to add individuals who aren't members of management to the board. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the group said it reserves the right to take action to obtain board representation if it is not otherwise offered.

The filing said the group met with the new chairman of Society For Savings on Dec. 19 to reiterate its view that the company could enhance shareholder values by spinning off its consumer finance subsidiary, Fidelity Acceptance Corp.

Society for Savings, Hartford, Conn., couldn't be reached for comment.
