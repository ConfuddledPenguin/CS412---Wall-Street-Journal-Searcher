Fed Approves Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

NEW YORK -- The Federal Reserve Board approved an alliance between Swiss Bank Corp. and the big Chicago options firm, O'Connor Partners. The alliance between Triple-A rated Swiss Bank and O'Connor marks another entry into the highly profitable business of creating customized futures and options on stocks, bonds, commodities, and currencies for investors.

Swiss Bank will tap O'Connor's trading talent and technology, while O'Connor, a secretive proprietary trading firm, will gain exposure to new clients through Swiss Bank's franchise.

The new unit will be called SBC/OC Services L.P., and will be jointly owned by Swiss Bank and O'Connor Partners.

The Federal Reserve Board announced the approval late last week.
