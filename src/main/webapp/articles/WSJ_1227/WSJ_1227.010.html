McDonnell Douglas Gets Navy Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. was given a $248.2 million Navy contract for 24 AV-8B aircraft.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. won a $32.7 million Air Force contract for F-16 aircraft radar.

Rockwell International Corp. received a $20.5 million Navy contract for submarine navigation systems.

United Technologies Corp. was issued $17.6 million of contracts for Navy aircraft computers and jet engine parts.
