Who's News: Sunrise Technologies Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

SUNRISE TECHNOLOGIES Inc. (Fremont, Calif.) -- Ira J. Fertik, 50 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this medical laser equipment concern. Mr. Fertik, a former Cooper Medical Group of Cos. executive, will assume the post effective Jan. 2, 1991. He succeeds Joseph Shaffer, 43, a co-founder of the company, who will become a vice president and devote his time to the research and development of new products.
