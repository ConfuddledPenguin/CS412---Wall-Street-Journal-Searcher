H&R Block to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- H&R Block Inc. said it signed an agreement to sell Path Management Industries Inc., its business skills training subsidiary, to the American Management Association, New York. The tax-preparation company said the sale price of its subsidiary was $20 million in cash and notes. "We concluded that Path Management Industries no longer fits in the strategic plans of H&R Block," said Henry W. Bloch, chairman and chief executive officer of H&R Block.
