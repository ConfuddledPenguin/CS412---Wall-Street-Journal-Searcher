Business Brief -- Knowledge Data Systems Inc.: Firm Posts $104,000 Loss For Its Fiscal 4th Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

Knowledge Data Systems Inc. reported a loss of $104,000, or 0.4 cent a share, for its fiscal fourth quarter ended Sept. 30, compared with net of $110,000, or 0.4 cent a share, in the year-earlier quarter. Sales for the quarter fell nearly 30% to $2.8 million from $4 million a year ago.

For the year, the Salt Lake City-based company said net income, on the strength of an agreement with a hospital chain in Northern California, rose to $377,000, or one cent a share, compared with a loss of $1.7 million, or seven cents a share, a year ago.

The company, which provides data processing systems and computer software equipment for the health-care industry, said annual revenue increased 43% to $12.8 million from $8.9 million.

A spokesman said the increased yearly sales represent a data system and software package accepted for use at 14 Kaiser Permanente Medical Centers in Northern California. The medical centers are units of Kaiser Foundation Hospital.
