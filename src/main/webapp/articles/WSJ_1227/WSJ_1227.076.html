Business Brief -- Archer-Daniels-Midland Co.: FTC Approves the Purchase Of White Lily by Windmill

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

Windmill Corp. said the Federal Trade Commission approved its pending acquisition of White Lily Foods Co., a unit of Archer-Daniels-Midland Co. Based in San Francisco, Windmill, a privately held specialty miller of consumer baking products sold under a variety of labels, said the acquisition of White Lily will "fit well" with the company's current product line. And the purchase fits into the company's strategy to broaden its reach as a national supplier of consumer baking products. Windmill reported annual sales of $300 million a year ago. Terms weren't disclosed.

White Lily Foods, which is based in Knoxville, Tenn., supplies consumer flour, cornmeal and other baking products throughout the southeastern U.S. Windmill said the transaction should be completed sometime in January.
