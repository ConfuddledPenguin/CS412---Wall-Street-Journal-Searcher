Business Brief -- Citizens Banking Corp: Trucking Concern Is Cleared To Buy Up to 24.9% Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

Federal regulators cleared CenTra Inc., a closely held Warren, Mich., trucking and transportation concern, to acquire as much as 24.9% of Citizens Banking Corp. Citizens said the ruling allows CenTra to be a passive investor in the bank holding company but restricts the amount of influence the trucking company can wield.

CenTra agreed, among other things, "not to exercise, or attempt to exercise, a controlling influence over the management or policies of Citizens or its bank subsidiaries," the Flint, Mich., company said.

CenTra, a longtime investor in Citizens, said it is interested in the bank strictly as an investment. CenTra owns nearly 10%, or 664,000, of Citizens' 6.6 million shares outstanding. Citizens shares closed unchanged at $18.625 in national over-the-counter trading yesterday.
