W.R. Grace Unit's Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

NEW YORK -- Grace Specialty Chemicals Co., a unit of W.R. Grace & Co., acquired Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.'s Premier product line, used by the printing and newspaper industries. Grace said it will absorb the Premier business into its Lexington, Mass., Polyfibron Division. The company will produce the Premier products, including liquid photopolymers for commercial printing, at an expanded facility in Morristown, Tenn., where it makes other graphic arts supplies. Terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
