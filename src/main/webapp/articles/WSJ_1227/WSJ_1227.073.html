Business Brief -- Reading & Bates Corp.: A Special Holder Meeting Is Postponed Until Jan. 23

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

Reading & Bates Corp. postponed to Jan. 23 a special stockholder meeting scheduled for tomorrow, to allow more time to complete and distribute proxy materials. The meeting of the Houston offshore drilling and engineering concern will vote on a pending recapitalization plan.

The plan would eliminate some $280 million of senior debt and lease obligations and cancel contingent debt of $50 million in exchange for about 192 million shares of common. As a group, senior creditors currently hold nearly 60% of common equity, and the recapitalization would boost that to nearly 90%.

The company said that all but one of its senior creditors involved in an earlier restructuring have signed commitment letters relating to the latest recapitalization. It said the remaining senior creditor has agreed to defer until Jan. 31 an interest payment due next Monday.
