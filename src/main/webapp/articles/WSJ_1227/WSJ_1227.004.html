Spanish Agricultural Revenue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

MADRID -- Spanish agricultural revenue grew 4.5% to more than 2 billion pesetas ($20.3 million) in 1990, said Spain's agricultural minister, Carlos Romero. Olive production surged 38% to 551 metric tons in 1990 compared with the year before, earning the sector 148 billion pesetas, the minister said.

Wine production leaped 40% from 1989 figures to 42 million hectoliters, valued at 168 million pesetas, he added.
