Business Brief -- Concurrent Computer Corp.: Agreement on Senior Debt Reached With Most Banks

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

After months of negotiations, Concurrent Computer Corp. said it reached an agreement with the majority of its bank lenders toward a restructuring of senior debt. The Tinton Falls, N.J., company said it is also in negotiations with bondholders to restructure its subordinated debt.

The provider of high-performance computer systems said it will reduce its principal owed by $1.9 million, part of a payment due last September. In turn, the banks agreed not to take any action to enforce payment owed before Feb. 1. The company said its banks have committed to working closely with it to achieve a debt restructuring agreement before the Feb. 1 deadline.

Company officials couldn't be reached for comment.

Concurrent has been involved in negotiations with its bank group after it missed two payments due Sept. 15 and Sept. 28.
