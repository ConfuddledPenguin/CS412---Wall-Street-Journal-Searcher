Business Brief -- Builders Transport Inc.: Ex-Director of the Concern Was Denied Stake Request

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

Lee A. Gay, an ex-director of Builders Transport Inc., said he was advised on Nov. 30 that the board rejected his request for advance approval to buy 15% or more of Builders Transport stock. Builders, located in Camden, S.C., is a trucking-services holding company. Mr. Gay holds a 5.2% stake in the company.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Gay said that in the Nov. 30 letter the board said that it may wish to reconsider the issue if Mr. Gay's holdings draw near the 15% threshold.

Mr. Gay responded with a Dec. 21 letter to David C. Walentas, the chairman of Builders Transport, asking that the board reconsider his request. Mr. Gay states that "as a concerned stockholder of BTI, I am of course concerned that the board act in the best interest of all stockholders."
