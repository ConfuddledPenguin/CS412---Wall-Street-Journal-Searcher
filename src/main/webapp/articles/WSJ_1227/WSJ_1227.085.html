French Unemployment Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

PARIS -- The French unemployment rate rose to 9.0% of the work force in November from 8.9% in October, according to seasonally adjusted data from the Labor Ministry. The number of French job-seekers grew 0.5% to 2,535,500 in November from 2,522,400 in October, the Ministry said. The figure was also up 0.5% from November 1989.

On an unadjusted basis, the number of job-seekers fell 0.2% to 2,582,500 in November from October, while the unadjusted figure was up 0.2% from a year ago.

The Ministry said the rise in job-seekers reflected reduced hirings as economic growth slowed. The increase in job-seekers also comes despite a 0.4% decline in the rate of new entries in the job market and a 7.2% drop in the pace of individuals returning to the work force after a prolonged absence.
