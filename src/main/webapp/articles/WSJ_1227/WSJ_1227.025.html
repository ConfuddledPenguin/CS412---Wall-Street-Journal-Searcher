Eastern Air Gets U.S. Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/27/90

MIAMI -- Eastern Airlines said it received a federal contract for 1991 government business travel on more than 345 domestic and international routes. Eastern estimated the contract's value between $82 million and $100 million. The carrier's 1990 contract for travel on 220 routes had an estimated value of about $52 million. Eastern is being operated by a trustee of the federal bankruptcy court.
