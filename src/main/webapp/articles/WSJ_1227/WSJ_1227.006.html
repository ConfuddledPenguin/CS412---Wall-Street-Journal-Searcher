Furon Co. Sale to NTN Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/27/90

LAGUNA NIGUEL, Calif. -- Furon Co. said it agreed to dissolve its Japanese joint venture, NTN-Rulon, selling its 45% interest to partner NTN Corp. for about $16.5 million. The companies plan to continue mutual sales of products and materials, and exchange information on new products and technologies. The transaction is expected to close in late January.

Furon, a maker of polymer products, said it will use the proceeds to pay down long-term debt.
