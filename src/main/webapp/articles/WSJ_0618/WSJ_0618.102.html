Business Brief -- Nissan Motor Co.: Japanese Auto Maker Forms Finance Subsidiary in U.S.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

Nissan Motor Co. said it has established a U.S. corporate finance subsidiary to consolidate finance activities for its U.S. companies. The Japanese auto maker said the new unit, Nissan Capital of America Inc., will take over the capital markets activities of Nissan Motor Acceptance Corp., the company's consumer finance unit. Nissan Capital will begin issuing by the end of the month $3 billion in U.S. commercial paper that will be guaranteed by the parent company, Nissan said.

Standard & Poor's Corp. and Moody's Investors Service Inc. assigned Nissan Capital's commercial paper the same rating currently assigned to Nissan Motor Acceptance's commercial paper, the company said. S&P's rating is A-1; Moody's rating is P-1.
