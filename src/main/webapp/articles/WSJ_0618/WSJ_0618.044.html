Who's News: Marks & Spencer PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

MARKS & SPENCER PLC (London) -- Nigel L. Colne and William V. Roberti were named directors of Brooks Brothers Japan, expanding the board to five members. The venture is owned 51% by Marks & Spencer, a retailing concern, and 49% by Daido, a Tokyo-based industrial products company. Mr. Colne is executive director of international operations for Marks & Spencer. Mr. Roberti is president and chief executive of Brooks Brothers, a New York-based unit of Marks & Spencer.
