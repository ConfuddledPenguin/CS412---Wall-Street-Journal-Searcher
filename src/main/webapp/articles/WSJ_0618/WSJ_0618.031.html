Corroon & Black Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

NEW YORK -- Corroon & Black Corp. said it acquired Collier Cobb & Associates Inc. in a stock transaction. Terms weren't disclosed. Collier Cobb, a Charlotte, N.C., insurance broker serving the construction industry, posted revenue of $25.5 million for 1989 and employs 245 people.

Fitzgerald S. Hudson, chairman and chief executive officer of Collier Cobb, will serve as chairman of Corroon & Black's construction industry division, succeeding John R. Lamberson, president and chief operating officer of Corroon & Black.
