Michigan National Enters Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

FARMINGTON HILLS, Mich. -- Michigan National Corp. said it made a definitive agreement to acquire all the common outstanding of Lockwood Banc Group for $17.3 million. Michigan National, a bank holding company, said the acquisition is expected to be completed this fall.

Lockwood Banc Group is the holding company for Lockwood National Bank, which has two offices in Houston and total assets of about $181 million.
