Gordon Capital Ban Postponed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

TORONTO -- Gordon Capital Corp. said the Ontario Securities Commission postponed a 10-day ban on principal trading by Gordon, pending the brokerage concern's appeal of the ban. The ban was imposed because of a regulatory violation that Gordon says was inadvertent. Principal trading involves trading shares for a securities dealer's own account.

The ban was to have taken effect July 18. Gordon said no hearing date has been set for its appeal.
