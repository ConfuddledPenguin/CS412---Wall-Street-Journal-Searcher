Business Brief -- American Healthcare Management: First Boston Corp. Retained To Find Restructuring Steps

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

American Healthcare Management Inc., which emerged from bankruptcy-law proceedings less than six months ago, said it retained First Boston Corp. to evaluate additional capital restructuring alternatives. Chief Executive Steven L. Volla, in a news release, said the Dallas health-care firm's reorganization plan was based on overly optimistic assumptions. The release noted that further restructuring is needed to reduce the financial risk of the current "highly leveraged capital structure."

Company officials didn't return phone calls seeking further explanation.

American Healthcare, which owns or leases 17 hospitals in 10 states, sought protection from creditors in August 1987. It completed its reorganization at the end of 1989 by issuing 15.2 million new shares of common stock to creditors and shareholders and by issuing new debt.
