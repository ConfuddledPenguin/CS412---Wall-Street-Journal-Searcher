Hallmark Cards Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- Hallmark Cards Inc. said it agreed in principle to acquire Willitts Designs, a Petaluma, Calif., maker of collectibles and gift items. Terms weren't disclosed. The closely held maker of greeting cards and gifts said the purchase is expected to be completed in July. Willitts has 100 employees and serves 12,000 retail accounts in the U.S. and Canada. Its products include music boxes, snow globes, porcelain figurines, kaleidoscopes, ceramic mugs and plates.
