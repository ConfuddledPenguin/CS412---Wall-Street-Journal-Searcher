Letters to the Editor: Origin of the Species

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

In reference to a May 29 letter to the editor from David B. Charnay, of Tarzana, Calif., on chic pets: Please, the name of the noble breed to which he refers is spelled Dalmatian, after the region of its supposed origin, Dalmatia. My two Dals, ages 12 and 5, would like you to note that. This misspelling is a common error.

John A. Newpher

Lyndon Center, Vt.
