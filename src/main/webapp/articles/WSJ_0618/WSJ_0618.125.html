Who's News: Action Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

ACTION INDUSTRIES Inc. (Pittsburgh) -- Ernest S. Berez, 65 years old, will retire as chairman of this promotional merchandise marketer, effective July 31, but remain a director. Mr. Berez previously said he would retire as chief executive officer, to be succeeded by R. Craig Kirsch, Action's 43-year-old president. The board is expected to select a new chairman at its July meeting.
