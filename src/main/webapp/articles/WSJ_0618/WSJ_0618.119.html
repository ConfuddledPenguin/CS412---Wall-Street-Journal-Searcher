Business Brief -- Allegheny International Inc.: Sunbeam Unit Plans to End Manufacturing in Canada

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

Allegheny International Inc. said in Pittsburgh that its Sunbeam Corp. (Canada) Ltd. unit will end manufacturing in Canada, eliminating 200 jobs, some of them seasonal. Sunbeam will close its Solaray facility at Brantford, Ontario, by mid-December, ending production of electric and non-electric blankets, heating pads and humidification products. Sales, marketing and other administrative functions currently performed at Brantford will be consolidated with Sunbeam's Toronto operation.

In addition, Sunbeam will end production of gas barbecue grills and lawnmowers at its Toronto facility, effective immediately.

Sunbeam said it will continue to sell a full line of products in Canada, most of them manufactured in the company's U.S. plants. Sunbeam Canada will employ about 160 people after the cutbacks.

Allegheny is operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code.
