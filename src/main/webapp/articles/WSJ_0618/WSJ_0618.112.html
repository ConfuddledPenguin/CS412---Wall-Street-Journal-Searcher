Business Brief -- Philips Industries Inc.: Tomkins PLC Will Acquire Ohio Firm for $550 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

Philips Industries Inc. and Tomkins PLC signed a definitive agreement for Tomkins to acquire Philips for $550 million, or $18.50 a share. The agreement is subject to approval by Tomkins shareholders at a meeting set for July 9 and by Philips holders at a meeting in late August.

Tomkins said it entered into an underwriting agreement with Barclays de Zoete Wedd Ltd., London, covering a rights offering to Tomkins shareholders in the U.K. that will provide most of the funds for the acquisition. Barclays agreed to purchase any shares not purchased by Tomkins holders under the rights offering, Tomkins said.

Philips is a Dayton, Ohio, maker of windows, bathtubs, air-moving equipment and certain other items for residential, commercial and industrial buildings, mobile homes and recreational vehicles.
