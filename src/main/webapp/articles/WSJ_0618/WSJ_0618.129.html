American Capital Payment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

MIAMI -- American Capital Corp. said it missed a $3.3 million interest payment on its 8.4% subordinated notes that it was supposed to make Friday. The savings and loan holding company said it has until July 16 to make the payment before it is in default. It said it is discussing a proposed transaction with noteholders that would involve the exchange of the notes for other securities not requiring cash interest payments.

If those talks fail, the company said it will decide by July 16 whether to make the interest payment.
