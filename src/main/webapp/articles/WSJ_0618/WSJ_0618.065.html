Cumberland Federal Bancorp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Cumberland Federal Bancorp. said it holds four first-mortgage loans from Hunnington Group Inc., which filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code Wednesday. Cumberland, a thrift holding company, said $17.4 million is due on the loans. The company said it believes collateral for the loans, a group of retail, commercial and residental development projects in eastern Jefferson County, Ky., "is adequate to protect its investment."
