THE RESOLUTION TRUST CORP.'S inventory of real estate increased 23% between September and March. A story in Thursday's edition misstated the percentage increase. (See: "RTC Has Swollen Real Estate Inventory From Sick S&Ls Despite Recent Big Sales" -- WSJ June 14, 1990)

ARMSTRONG WORLD INDUSTRIES Inc. completed the second phase of its sale of woodlands in Georgia for about $40 million. In Friday's edition, the sale price was misidentified as a pretax gain.

(See: "Business Brief -- Armstrong World Industries Inc.: Second Phase of Land Sale Results in $40 Million Gain" -- WSJ June 15, 1990)
