Who's News: Martin Lawrence Limited Editions Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

MARTIN LAWRENCE LIMITED EDITIONS Inc. (Van Nuys, Calif.) -- Barry R. Levine, 45 years old, was named president of this publisher and marketer of lithographs, sculptures and other works of art. He succeeds Martin S. Blinder, 43, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer. The two men, along with Allen A. Baron, 49, chief financial officer, are members of a new office of the chairman.
