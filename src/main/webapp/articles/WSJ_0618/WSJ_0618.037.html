Sweden's Jobless Rate Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's unemployment rate in May rose to 1.3% of the work force from 1.1% in April and 1.1% in May 1989, Statistics Sweden said. The national statistics agency said 50,000 people were without work last month, lower than the preceding month's 51,000 and unchanged from the May 1989 figure.

The agency said unemployment among youth rose to 2.9% in May from 2.4% in April and from 2.2% in May 1989.
