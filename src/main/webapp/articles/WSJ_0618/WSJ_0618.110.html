Business Brief -- Wellcome PLC: Group Supports Wider Use Of AIDS-Treatment Drug

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

British pharmaceuticals group Wellcome PLC said the Committee for Proprietary Medicinal Products of the European Community recommended that Retrovir, Wellcome's acquired immune deficiency syndrome drug, be approved for wider use. The committee has sanctioned the use of Retrovir in patients other than those suffering from severe human immunodeficiency virus infections, which include AIDS and AIDS-related complex, Wellcome said.

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration gave its approval to wider use of the drug this year.
