Who's News: Cineplex Odeon Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

CINEPLEX ODEON Corp. (Toronto) -- Allen Karp, 49-year-old president and chief operating officer of this movie theater chain, was appointed to the additional position of chief executive officer. The post had been vacant since December 1989, when Garth Drabinsky resigned as chairman, president and chief executive.
