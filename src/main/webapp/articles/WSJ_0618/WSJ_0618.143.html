International Brief -- Mitsubishi Motors Corp.: Pretax Profit Climbed 2.2% In Year Ended March 31

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

Mitsubishi Motors Corp. said its consolidated pretax profit edged up 2.2% in the year ended March 31 to 50.23 billion yen ($324 million) from 49.13 billion yen in the previous fiscal year. Net income climbed 11% to 21.13 billion yen from 19.03 billion.

Sales soared to 2.361 trillion yen, an 8% increase from 2.186 trillion a year earlier.

Mitsubishi's U.S. joint venture with Chrysler Corp., Diamond/Star Motors Corp., was a drag on consolidated earnings in the year, a spokesman said. However, he said his company expects to redeem losses at the venture, which isn't producing at capacity, in the current fiscal year. Diamond Star started operations in September 1988 and began shipments in January 1989.
