Merry-Go-Round Enterprises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

JOPPA, Md. -- Merry-Go-Round Enterprises Inc. said it and certain non-employee stockholders are offering 2,150,000 common shares at $26.625 each, or a total of $57.2 million. The company, an apparel clothing chain with 62 stores in 37 states, said it is selling 850,000 of the shares and the non-employee stockholders are selling the rest. The offering is being managed by Alex. Brown & Sons Inc., Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and PaineWebber Inc.
