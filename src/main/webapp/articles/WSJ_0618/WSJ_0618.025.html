Swiss Wholesale Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

BERN, Switzerland -- The Swiss wholesale price index declined 0.1% in May from April and rose 1% on a year-to-year basis, the government reported. The year-to-year rise was the smallest since January 1988.

The 1% rise compares with a 1.5% year-to-year rate in April. In May 1989, however, wholesale prices were up 5.4% from the same month in 1988.
