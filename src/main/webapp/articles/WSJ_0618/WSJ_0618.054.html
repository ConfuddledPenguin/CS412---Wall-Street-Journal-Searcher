Dividend News: Synoptics Communications Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

SYNOPTICS COMMUNICATIONS Inc. said its board approved a 2-for-1 stock split, payable around July 20 to stock of record June 26. After the distribution, the Mountain View, Calif., maker of computer networking products will have about 18.4 million shares outstanding. The company said it's making the distribution to "increase the appeal of SynOptics stock to a broader range of investors."
