WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

In Home Health Inc., proposed offering of $2.5 million of units consisting of common and warrants, via Lew Lieberbaum & Co., Inc.

Lattice Semiconductor Corp., proposed offering of 2.5 million common shares, of which 1,494,000 are to be sold by the company and 1,006,000 by holders, via Dillon, Read & Co., Hambrecht & Quist Inc. and Cowen & Co.

LTX Corp., exchange offering for a new issue of 13 1/2% convertible subordinated debentures, in return for outstanding 7 1/4% convertible subordinated debentures, via Kidder Peabody & Co. Inc.

Selas Corp. of America, offering of 900,000 common shares, via Wheat First Butcher & Singer Capital Markets.

Unisys Corp., shelf offering of $800 million of debt securities.

Vie de France Corp., offering of three million common shares.
