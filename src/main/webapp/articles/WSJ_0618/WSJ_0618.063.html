Dutch Consumer Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

AMSTERDAM -- The Dutch consumer price index was unchanged in the month ending in mid-May, but up 2.2% from the year-earlier period, the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics said. It was the fourth month in a row that inflation showed a rise of 2.2% over the year-earlier month. In January, the price rise was 2.1% from a year earlier.

When making its forecast for the Dutch economy earlier in the year, the Dutch Central Planning Bureau estimated that inflation for 1990 would amount to 2.1%.
