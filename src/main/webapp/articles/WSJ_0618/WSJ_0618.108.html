Georgia Gulf Stakes Disclosed by Director, Investment Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

WASHINGTON -- Holdings in Georgia Gulf Corp. were disclosed by an investment group and a director of the Atlanta-based chemical company. A group including EGS Associates Limited Partnership and EGS Partners L.P. of New York, fund managers, said it holds the equivalent of a 5.2% stake in Georgia Gulf for investment purposes.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it controls 1,746,300 Georgia Gulf common shares, including publicly traded call options on 573,000 shares.

Recent common-stock transactions include 1,206,021 shares bought from April 17 to June 12 for $5.82 to $9.23 a share. The group said it may buy more common or sell shares at any time.

Separately, Holcombe T. Green Jr., a Georgia Gulf director, said he holds a 6.3% stake in the company's common as a result of Georgia Gulf's recent recapitalization.

In an SEC filing, Mr. Green said he holds 2,131,143 Georgia Gulf reclassified common shares. The stake includes 438,000 shares acquired in the open market from May 10 to June 1 for $6.25 to $9.125 each. Mr. Green said these shares were acquired for investment purposes.

Georgia Gulf declined to comment on the holdings.
