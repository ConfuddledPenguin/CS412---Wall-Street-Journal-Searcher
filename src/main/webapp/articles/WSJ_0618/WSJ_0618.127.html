Letters to the Editor: Watchdog Needs Teeth

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

Your May 10 page-one article "Getting Tough: SEC Under Breeden Takes a Harder Line on Securities Crime" sounds like the right man is in the right place at the right time. As one of thousands of grain and livestock producers who despise the Commodity Futures Trading Commission, we'd welcome any ideas as to how we could get a clone of Richard Breeden to take over as chairman of the despicably weak and exchange-biased CFTC.

Your Aug. 2, 1984, headline said it best: "Futures Imperfect: Puppylike Watchdog Irks Some but Pleases Commodities Industry."

Doug Wildin

Doug Wildin & Associates

Ranch Brokers

Hutchinson, Kan.
