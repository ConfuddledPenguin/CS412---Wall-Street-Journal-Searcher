Immunex to Redeem Debt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

SEATTLE -- Immunex Corp. said it called for redemption July 6 all $40 million of its 7 1/2% convertible subordinated debt due 2011. Each $1000 principal amount of debt is redeemable for $1085, including accrued interest. Until close of business July 6, each $1,000 of debt is convertible in common shares at a price of $18.90 a share.

In over-the-counter trading Friday, Immunex closed at $26.875, down 12.5 cents.
