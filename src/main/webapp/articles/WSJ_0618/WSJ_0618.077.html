Business Brief -- Dravo Corp.: Pittsburgh Concern Resumes Shell Dredging in Louisiana

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/18/90

Dravo Corp., Pittsburgh, said it has resumed its shell dredging in southern Louisiana following a consent judgment ending a lengthy dispute over the legality of Dravo's leases. Dredging had been suspended last May 25. Dravo said the dispute began in 1983 over procedures in which the company obtained leases to dredge for shells, which it uses as an aggregate in road construction. The agreement deletes from the leases three specific areas that previously had been included in the dredging tracts, Dravo said, but impact on shell recovery "will be minimal."

Dravo produces construction aggregates in different areas of the country.
