Business Brief -- Scott Paper Co.: Concern to Form Venture With Germany's Feldmuhle

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/18/90

Scott Paper Co. said it will form a joint venture with Feldmuhle AG, a West German company, to own and operate Feldmuhle's sanitary-tissue business. Scott, a Philadelphia-based maker of paper products, said it plans to pay $125 million for a 51% stake in the joint venture. At Feldmuhle's option or after three years, Scott would have the right to acquire the remaining 49% of the joint venture and would have to pay Feldmuhle an equivalent price for that stake.

The joint venture is subject to approval by the German Federal Cartel Office and both company's boards. Scott said it expects the joint venture to be formed in the fourth quarter.

Feldmuhle markets sanitary-tissue products in Europe and manufactures tissue products in Germany and in the Netherlands. The company had gross sales of about $300 million in 1989; Scott had European sales of about $1.2 billion in 1989.
