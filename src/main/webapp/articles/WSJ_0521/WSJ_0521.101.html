Marketing Brief -- Media General Inc.: Virginia Broadcast Company Names a Chief Executive

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

Media General Inc., a Richmond, Va.-based broadcast, cable-TV and publishing company, said J. Stewart Bryan III was named chairman, president and chief executive officer, effective July 1. Mr. Bryan, 52 years old, is currently chief operating officer of the company and publisher of its Richmond newspapers. He will succeed his 83-year-old father, D. Tennant Bryan, as chairman, and James S. Evans, 68, as president and chief executive.

The elder Mr. Bryan and Mr. Evans will remain directors of the company and serve as vice chairman and chairman of the executive committee, respectively.
