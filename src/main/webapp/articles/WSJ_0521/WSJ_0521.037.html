Carlson Cos. Buys 51% Stake in Forum, An Australian Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

MELBOURNE, Australia -- Carlson Cos., a U.S. marketing group, said it has bought 51% of Forum Organization Pty. Ltd., an Australian marketing firm. The price of the acquisition wasn't disclosed, but the companies said in a statement that Forum has annual revenue of around 20 million Australian dollars.

E.C. Gage, chief executive of Carlson, said the purchase allows the company to "rapidly expand its global network of full service marketing facilities to its many international clients and throughout SouthEast Asia and Australia." Forum has offices in Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane and Perth.
