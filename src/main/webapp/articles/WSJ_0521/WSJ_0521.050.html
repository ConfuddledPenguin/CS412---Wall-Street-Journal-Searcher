Who's News: Society Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

SOCIETY Corp. (Cleveland) -- James W. Wert, 43 years old, formerly executive vice president of this bank holding company, was named a vice chairman, a new post, and a director, expanding board membership to 25. Henry L. Meyer III, 40, formerly vice chairman of Society's lead bank, was named the bank's president and chief operating officer, succeeding Robert W. Gillespie, 45, who remains chairman and chief executive officer of both the parent and the bank; Mr. Meyer also remains executive vice president of the parent. Messrs. Wert, Meyer and Gillespie and Roger Noall, 55, a vice chairman, form the senior executive management team.
