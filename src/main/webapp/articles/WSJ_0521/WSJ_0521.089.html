French Industrial Output Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

PARIS -- France's industrial-production index rose 0.7% in March from February and was up 2.4% from a year earlier, according to seasonally adjusted figures published Friday by the National Statistics Institute. The March result was slightly weaker than forecasters had anticipated. Many economists had expected the index to make up most of the 1.8% drop recorded in February, when energy output was held down by unusually warm weather.

The March result put the index at 112.7, up from 111.9 in February and 110.1 in March 1989. The index uses 1980 levels as a base of 100.
