Who's News: Rospatch Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

ROSPATCH Corp. (Grand Rapids, Mich.) -- Edwin Wachtel, president of G.W. Investors Corp. and a general partner of Alizac Partners, the second-largest holder of this furniture maker with a 10.6% stake, was elected a director, filling a vacancy on the six-member board. Kevin Coyne, son of Michael F. Coyne, whose family owns about 9% of Rospatch, and Neil Diver, an investment adviser to the Williametta K. Day Trust, which owns about 7.5% of Rospatch's common, were invited to consult with Rospatch's board and attend board and committee meetings.
