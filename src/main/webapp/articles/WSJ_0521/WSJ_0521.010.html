Ford to Idle Atlanta Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

DEARBORN, Mich. -- Ford Motor Co. said it will temporarily idle its Atlanta assembly plant next week for one week because of slow dealer orders for the Ford Taurus and Mercury Sable cars built there. The move will affect about 2,500 hourly workers on the plant's two shifts, and it will cut Taurus and Sable production by about 5,000 cars. Ford's decision to idle the Atlanta plant follows the one-week idling this week of its Chicago plant, which also builds the midsized Taurus and Sable. Ford also blamed the Chicago plant move on a shortage of dealer orders.
