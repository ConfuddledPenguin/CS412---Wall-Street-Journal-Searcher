Dividend News: Superior Industries International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

SUPERIOR INDUSTRIES INTERNATIONAL Inc. said it will distribute a 10% common stock dividend, one share for each 10 shares owned, on June 29 to stock of record June 15. The Van Nuys, Calif., maker of aluminum wheels for autos and other auto products also said it will maintain its quarterly cash dividend at a rate of seven cents a share, payable July 27 to stock of record July 6.
