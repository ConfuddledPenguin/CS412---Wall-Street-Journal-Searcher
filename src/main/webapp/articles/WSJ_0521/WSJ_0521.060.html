Dividend News: Mortgage Investments Plus Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

MORTGAGE INVESTMENTS PLUS Inc. reduced its quarterly dividend 50% to 10 cents a share from 20 cents, payable July 2 to stock of record June 1. The Newport Beach, Calif., real estate trust, with assets concentrated in California commercial real estate, said it cut the dividend because Mortgage Investments' "environment has changed considerably over the past several months." Funds resulting from the lower dividend and from a cost-cutting program will be used to reduce borrowings and strengthen the balance sheet, the company said.
