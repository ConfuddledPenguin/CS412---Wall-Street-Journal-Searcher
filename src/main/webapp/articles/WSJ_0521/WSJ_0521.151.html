Business Brief -- Furman Selz Mager Dietz: New York Investment Firm Raises Stake in Company

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

Furman Selz Mager Dietz & Birney Inc., a New York investment firm, said it raised its stake in Coast Distribution System Inc. to 8.98% of common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Furman Selz said it holds 373,300 Coast Distribution shares, including 214,200 shares purchased between Dec. 22 and May 15 for $5.50 to $6.250 a share.

The filing did not give a reason for the increase in stake.

Coast Distribution System is based in San Jose, Calif.
