Mellon Bank Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

PITTSBURGH -- Mellon Bank Corp. said it completed the purchase of 54 branch offices in the Philadelphia area from Meritor Savings Bank for about $335 million. Under the agreement, Mellon assumed deposits of about $5.2 billion and acquired assets of approximately $4.9 billion.

Mellon, a bank holding company, said it will finance the transaction by using the proceeds from its recent Series H preferred stock issuance and by applying proceeds from the sale of its Mellon Financial Services, a Chicago-based consumer finance business. That sale is expected to close in June 1990.
