Letters to the Editor: Wanderlust

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

As a native Iowan, I think I've read about enough gassy remembrances from Michael (I know everybody) Gartner ("Keep 'Em in Iowa After They've Seen Moscow," op-ed page, April 26). Mr. Gartner may have started the trend toward leaving years ago when he enrolled in a college in Minnesota.

Eric M. Davis

Prior Lake, Minn.
