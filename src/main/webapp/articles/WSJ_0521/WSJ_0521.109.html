Dividend News: San Juan Basin Royalty Trust

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

SAN JUAN BASIN ROYALTY TRUST said it won't pay a cash distribution to stock of record May 31, because expenses exceeded revenue by about $180,000. The Fort Worth, Texas-based trust said it has been notified that royalty income may be negative or negligible for the next several months because of the cost for anticipated drilling of coal seam natural gas wells, low seasonal demand for natural gas and seasonally depressed natural gas prices.
