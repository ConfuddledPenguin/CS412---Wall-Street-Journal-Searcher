Business Brief -- Interlake Corp.: Firm Sells Arwood Assets To Unit of Wyman-Gordon

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

Interlake Corp. said it completed the sale of its Arwood investment casting unit's assets to a Wyman-Gordon Co. unit, for about $54 million. Interlake, an Oak Brook, Ill.-maker of engineered materials and handling and packaging systems, said proceeds from the sale were used to prepay bank debt.

Arwood had 1989 sales of about $89 million; Interlake's overall sales were $912.9 million. Investment casting is a process which produces a highly finished product that requires little additional processing.

Interlake last year borrowed heavily in a recapitalization, including a special dividend to shareholders that is generally thought to have deflected any interest from what was then seen as a potential suitor.
