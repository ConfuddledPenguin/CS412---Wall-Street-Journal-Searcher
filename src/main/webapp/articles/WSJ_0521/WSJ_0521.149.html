Rogers Communications Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

TORONTO -- Edward S. Rogers, president and chief executive officer of Rogers Communications Inc., said he increased his holding of the company's nonvoting Class B shares by 2,074,485 shares to 13,808,623 shares, or 19.3% of the Class B stock outstanding. Mr. Rogers and companies and trusts controlled by him also own 89.4% of the telecommunication company's Class A voting shares, he said.
