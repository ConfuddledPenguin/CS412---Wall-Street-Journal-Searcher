Who's News: Abiomed's Singh Takes Indefinite Leave of Absence

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

DANVERS, Mass. -- Abiomed Inc. said Param I. Singh has decided to go on indefinite leave of absence and resign from his position as executive vice president effective Aug. 1. Dr. Singh, who was also general manager of the company's Abiodent dental division, said in a news release that the decision "was based entirely on health reasons." He will continue to serve as an Abiomed director.

Abiomed develops medical and dental devices and cardiac support systems.
