Columbia Gas System Offers Unit for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Columbia Gas System Inc. said it is offering for sale its Columbia Gas of New York Inc. distribution unit. The unit, with annual sales of $65 million, delivers an average of 15 billion cubic feet of gas a year and serves 67,500 customers. The company indicated that, although the unit has been profitable, resources could "be better devoted to distribution properties in states where we have a greater market share."

The investment banking firm of Morgan Stanley & Co. has been retained to solicit bids, which won't affect any of the five other Columbia Gas distribution units.
