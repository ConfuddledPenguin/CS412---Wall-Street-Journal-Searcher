Business Brief -- Guilford Mills Inc.: Posner of Graniteville Co. Increases Holding to 10.3%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

Investor Victor Posner said Guilford Mills Inc. is seeking talks in connection with a possible business combination between Guilford, Greensboro, S.C., and closely held Graniteville Co., Graniteville, S.C. Mr. Posner is chairman of Graniteville. Charles Hayes, Guilford chairman and chief executive officer, said his company is interested in acquiring Graniteville for either cash or stock. He said he contacted Mr. Posner about two weeks ago.

Mr. Hayes said the two companies would make a good combination, as both specialize in manufacturing clothes for the career apparel market. In fiscal 1989, Guilford earned $26 million, or $2.67 a share, on revenue of about $620 million.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Posner said he raised his stake in Guilford to 10.3% after buying 20,000 shares on May 3 for $22 a share and 88,100 shares for $20.625 a share.
