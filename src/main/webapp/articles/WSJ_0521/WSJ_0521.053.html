Who's News: ITT Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

ITT Corp. (New York) -- Ramani Ayer, 42 years old, previously president of a unit of the company's Hartford Insurance Group, was named executive vice president, North American property-casualty operations, a new post at the subsidiary, one of ITT's nine operating units. Donald R. Frahm, Hartford's 56-year-old chairman and chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of president, property-casualty operations. Both moves are related to the recent appointment of Dale R. Comey, formerly president and chief operating officer, property-casualty operations, as executive vice president of ITT.
