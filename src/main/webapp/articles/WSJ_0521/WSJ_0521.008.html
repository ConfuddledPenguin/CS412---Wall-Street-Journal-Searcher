Hitachi Venture Tender Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

LOS ANGELES -- A joint venture owned by affiliates of Hitachi Corp. said it acquired 14.8 million shares, or 94%, of Dataproducts Corp.'s shares outstanding under its tender offer. The Tokyo electronics group's two affiliates, Hitachi Koki Co. and Nissei Sangyo Co., offered to buy all shares of Dataproducts, a Woodland Hills, Calif., computer printer maker, for $10 a share. The joint venture is named HND Corp. The tender offer expired last Thursday.
