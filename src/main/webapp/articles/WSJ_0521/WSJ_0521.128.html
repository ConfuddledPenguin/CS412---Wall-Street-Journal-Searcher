Business Brief -- Monitek Technologies Inc.: R&W Ventures Holds Stake Of 5% in High-Tech Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

R&W Ventures II said it holds the equivalent of a 5% stake in Monitek Technologies Inc.'s common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, R&W, a Menlo Park, Calif., venture capital concern controlled by investor Roy L. Rogers, said it controls 190,900 Monitek common shares, assuming the conversion of Class A stock and warrants it holds.

R&W's stake includes 77,100 common shares bought from March 26 to May 16 for $1.31 to $1.44 apiece.

R&W said it acquired its Monitek securities for investment purposes only, indicating that it considers high-technology companies to be undervalued. It said it would consider its alternatives regarding Monitek, including buying more securities or selling some or all of its holdings.

Monitek is based in Hayward, Calif.
