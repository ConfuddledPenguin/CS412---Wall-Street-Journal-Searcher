WPPSS to Sell Bonds in June

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

RICHLAND, Wash. -- Washington Public Power Supply System said it plans to sell $600 million of refunding revenue bonds in the first week of June. WPPSS defaulted in 1983 on $2.25 billion of bonds, the largest default in municipal bond history. This will be its second bond sale this year. The new securities, which will range in maturity from two years to 27 years, involve WPPSS units 2 and 3, which didn't default. First Boston Corp. is senior manager.
