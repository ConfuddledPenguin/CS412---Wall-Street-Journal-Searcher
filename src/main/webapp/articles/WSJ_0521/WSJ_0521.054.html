Carter Holt to Buy Brierley's 50% Stake In Jointly Owned Firm

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 05/21/90

AUCKLAND, New Zealand -- Carter Holt Harvey Ltd. said it is buying Brierley Investments Ltd.'s 50% stake in their joint-venture packaging concern Printpac-UEB for 110 million New Zealand dollars (US$63.1 million). The New Zealand timber, building materials and packaging concern said the purchase price will include NZ$30.5 million and an issue of 30 million Carter Holt shares valued at NZ$2.65 each.

The purchase, which would be retroactive to April 1, is subject to approval by New Zealand's antitrust regulators, Carter Holt said.

Carter Holt and Brierley formed Printpac-UEB in 1989 as a vehicle to buy UEB Packaging Ltd.

Richard Carter, executive chairman of Carter Holt, said that following the purchase of UEB Packaging last year, the joint venture "is now a large, finely focussed and mature" organization. "It is, therefore, now more appropriately designed to operate under a single ownership structure," Mr. Carter said.

Bruce Hancox, chairman of Brierley Investments, said his company is satisfied with its investment in Carter Holt through the share issue.
