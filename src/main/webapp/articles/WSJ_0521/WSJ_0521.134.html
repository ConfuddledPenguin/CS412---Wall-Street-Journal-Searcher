Marketing Brief -- News Corp.: London Unit Sets Venture For Printing in Germany

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

News International PLC, London, which is part of the Australian-based News Corp. Ltd., said that it is setting up a joint venture with Burda Verwaltungs G.m.b.H., of West Germany, for printing units in both East and West Germany. No financial details are being released, said a News International spokeswoman. A "great deal of discussion on many factors" still has to take place, she said.

The agreement, so far, includes the current Burda plant in Darmstadt being spun off into a new joint venture company called Burda News Druck G.m.b.H. That company will print some of the five million Sunday magazines, a supplement accompanying News International's British Sunday newspaper the News Of The World. There are no other details on how the plant will be used, said the spokeswoman.

The two companies also agreed in principle to set up a joint venture to print works in East Germany, said the spokeswoman. No sites or financing proposals have been decided, she said.
