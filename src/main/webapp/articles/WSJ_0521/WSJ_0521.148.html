Who's News: DH Technology Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

DH TECHNOLOGY Inc. (San Diego) -- William J. Bowers was elected a director of this independent supplier of high technology impact printer products. He succeeds Helmut Falk, who retired. Mr. Bowers was chairman of MSI Data Corp. until 1988 when Symbol Technologies Inc. acquired the firm.
