Gander Mountain Sells Its Animal Care Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

WILMOT, Wisc. -- Gander Mountain Inc. said it sold its Master Animal Care operations to closely held Humboldt Industries Inc. The mail-order retailer of outdoor recreation products didn't disclose terms of the sale of its line.

Master Animal Care sells pet supplies and accessories; an official said the unit represented about 5% of Gander's overall sales.

Gander said the unit sale marks the final divestiture under a previously announced company program to shed its subsidiaries and focus on its "core" hunting, camping and fishing supply sales.
