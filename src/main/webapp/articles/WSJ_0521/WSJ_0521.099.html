Technology Brief -- Compaq Computer Corp.: Company Plans to Unveil A New Line of Computers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

Compaq Computer Corp. said it will introduce today a new line of personal computers that have built-in networking features. Dubbed the Deskpro 286N and Deskpro 386N, the new PCs are about half the height of Compaq's current desk-top units and come with extensive hardware and software security features. Suggested resale prices for the Deskpro 286N start at $1,699 and for the 386N at $2,299.

Price competition is a major reason for the Compaq introduction: many customers for Compaq's top-of-the-line file servers -- powerful PCs that can process information simultaneously for dozens of terminals -- were buying rivals' desk-top units, according to Michael Swavely, president of the Houston-based company's North American operations. At the same time, he added, networked systems are becoming the major PC growth area of the 1990s.
