Comstock Begins Public Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

DALLAS -- Comstock Resources Inc. said it commenced a public offering of 2.5 million of common shares for $5.75 each. The company said proceeds will be used to repay part of its $9.8 million in bank debt, continue development of 95 oil and gas wells near Amarillo, Texas, and acquire additional oil and gas properties.

Eppler, Guerin & Turner Inc., Dallas, is managing the underwriting.
