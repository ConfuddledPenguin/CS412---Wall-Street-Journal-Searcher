Who's News: Chrysler Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

CHRYSLER Corp. -- The Highland Park, Mich., auto maker said it reassigned three executives in its marketing operations. The company named Lawrence W. Baker as general manager of its Jeep/Eagle division. Previously, he was general manager of Chrysler's Dodge car and truck division. He will be succeeded at Dodge by Martin R. Levine who was the Jeep/Eagle general manager. The company also named Ralph A. Sarotte as director of multibrand marketing programs. He had been director of consumer marketing programs in the company's consumer affairs office. Before that, Mr. Sarotte was Eagle general marketing manager.
