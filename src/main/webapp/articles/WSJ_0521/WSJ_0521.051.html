Who's News: Maxwell Communication Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

MAXWELL COMMUNICATION Corp. (London) -- Craig E. Burr was named president and chief operating officer of Standard Rate & Data Service, a division of this publishing and communications company's New York-based Macmillan Inc. unit. Mr. Burr will manage Standard Rate's 15 directories, which are advertising references for the planning and buying of media. Mr. Burr was vice president of marketing at Standard Rate & Data.
