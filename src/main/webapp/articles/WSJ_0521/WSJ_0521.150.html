Coffee, Sugar, Cocoa Exchange

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

NEW YORK -- Two memberships on the Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange were sold Friday. The first was sold for $96,000 and the second for $95,000. These compare with the last sale on April 24 at $88,000 and one a year ago, on May 2, 1989, at $95,000. Currently, a seat is bid at $82,000 and offered at $100,000, according to an exchange spokeswoman.
