CALIFORNIA FEDERAL BANK FSB -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. cut the ratings on senior debt and commercial paper of the unit of CalFed Inc., based in Los Angeles. It also lowered the rating for subordinated debt of CalFed. The actions affect $875 million in long-term debt. Moody's lowered the rating of CalFed's subordinated debt to Ba-3 from Ba-2, and reduced CalFed's rating of senior debt to Ba-3 from Ba-2 and commercial paper to Prime-3 from Prime-2. In a statement, CalFed expressed disappointment over the action, "especially in light of the improvements we have achieved in the fundamentals of our banking business." ---

GREAT WESTERN FINANCIAL Corp. (Los Angeles) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. reduced its rating on Great Western Financial's senior debt to single-A-minus from single-A. The action affects about $1 billion in debt, S&P said. In a statement, Great Western expressed disappointment over the rating cut but said it was "pleased" that S&P reaffirmed ratings on the senior debt and commercial paper of a subsidiary, Great Western Bank.
