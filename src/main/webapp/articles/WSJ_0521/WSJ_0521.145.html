Who's News: Manufacturers National Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/21/90

MANUFACTURERS NATIONAL Corp. (Detroit) -- Paul H. Martzowka, 51 years old, treasurer and chief financial officer of this bank holding company, was elected a director, filling a vacancy, and to the board of the principal subsidiary, Manufacturers National Bank of Detroit, succeeding Kenneth Aird, 64, the company's vice chairman, who is retiring in July.
