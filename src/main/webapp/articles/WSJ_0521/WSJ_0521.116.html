Business Brief -- EnviroSource Inc.: Firm Decides Not to Start Talks on Envirosafe Swap

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/21/90

EnviroSource Inc. said it has decided not to initiate negotiations in connection with a tax-free stock-swap transaction involving Envirosafe Services Inc. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, EnviroSource, which holds a 62.5% stake in Envirosafe, said it made the decision due to "market conditions and other factors."

EnviroSource had planned to seek such a transaction last January.

Envirosafe is based in King of Prussia, Pa., and EnviroSource is based in Stamford, Conn.
