Who's News: Maxxam Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

MAXXAM Inc. (Los Angeles) -- James V. Iaco, 45-year-old chief financial officer, will resign from his position Dec. 31 because of the company's previously announced decision to relocate its headquarters to Houston, the company said. No successor was named. Maxxam is a holding company for aluminum production, forest product and real estate operations. The relocation is scheduled to be completed this fall.
