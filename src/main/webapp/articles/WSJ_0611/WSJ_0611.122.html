Canada's Jobless Rate Rose to 7.6% in May

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's unemployment rate rose to 7.6% of the work force in May from 7.2% in April, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The rise was attributed to a weakening trend in the Canadian economy.

Manufacturing employment fell sharply in May for the third consecutive month.

Statistics Canada also said its help-wanted index, an early indicator of labor demand, fell 5.3% in May. The index monitors changes in help-wanted advertisements in major Canadian cities.
