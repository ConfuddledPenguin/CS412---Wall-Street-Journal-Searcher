Salick Buys Back Debentures

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Salick Health Care Inc. said it bought back $2,573,000 principal amount of its 7.25% convertible subordinated debentures, due 2001, for about $1.61 million. The company, which provides cancer and dialysis treatment, said it purchased the bonds in a private transaction, because it believes the market undervalues the company.
