Great Western Bank Debt Issue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Great Western Bank announced a $150 million issue of 10-year, fixed-rate subordinated debt yielding 10.356%. The company said the issue will raise its risk-adjusted capital ratio -- the ratio of capital to calculation of its assets adjusted to reflect risks -- to 8.75% from 8.22%. The lead underwriter for the offering is Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.
