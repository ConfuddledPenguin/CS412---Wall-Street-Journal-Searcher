Bytex Installs Software

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

SOUTHBOROUGH, Mass. -- Bytex Corp. said it installed its Unity network switching systems and management system software into five facilities of Comdisco Disaster Recovery Services Inc., of Rosemont, Ill., in a transaction valued at about $1 million. The installations are part of a three-year contract signed by the companies in January, which is valued at between $3 million and $4 million.

Under the contract, Bytex will install about 17 more systems in Comdisco's North American facilities during the next three years, Comdisco said.

Bytex is a data communications switches concern. Comdisco is a provider of backup computing and network capabilities during emergencies.
