Seabrook's Operator Selects Its New Chief

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

SEABROOK, N.H. -- New Hampshire Yankee, operator of the Seabrook Station nuclear-power plant, said Ted C. Feigenbaum, 40 years old, senior vice president and chief operating officer, will be named president and chief executive officer Oct. 1. Edward A. Brown, current president and chief executive, will retire Oct. 1.

Mr. Brown, 60, said he completed his "mission" at New Hampshire Yankee -- to get the Seabrook Station built and on line -- and will now spend more time with his family.

New Hampshire Yankee was created by Seabrook Station's joint-owner utility companies to complete the plant's construction and licensing and to oversee operation of the facility.
