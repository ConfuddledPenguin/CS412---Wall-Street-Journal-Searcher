Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- Donnelley Directory, a unit of this business and information services company, announced three senior appointments: Edward Lederer, senior vice president and general manager of Donnelley headquarters in Purchase, N.Y.; Leonard Fabrizio, senior vice president and Northeast region general manager; and William Young, who becomes senior vice president and general manager of the company's Independent Telco Operations. Mr. Lederer succeeds Mr. Young, and Mr. Fabrizio succeeds Mr. Lederer.
