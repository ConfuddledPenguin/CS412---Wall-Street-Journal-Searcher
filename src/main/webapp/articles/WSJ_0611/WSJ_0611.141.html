Chicago Milwaukee Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

CHICAGO -- Chicago Milwaukee Corp. said it will spin off its Heartland Partners L.P. real estate business in a distribution of one limited-partner unit for each common share of Chicago Milwaukee. The holding company said it plans to pay the distribution June 30 to stock of record June 18. The transaction is subject to approval by the Securities and Exchange Commission.
