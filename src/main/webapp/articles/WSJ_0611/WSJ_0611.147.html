First of America Bank Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

KALAMAZOO, Mich. -- First of America Bank Corp. said it has signed a definitive agreement to acquire Trustcorp Bank-Columbus N.A. The amount wasn't disclosed. First of America said it will acquire Trustcorp from Society Corp. of Cleveland. The transaction will be a cash purchase and is expected to be complete within six months.
