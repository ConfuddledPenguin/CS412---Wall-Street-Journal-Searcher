General Sekiyu Posts Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

TOKYO -- General Sekiyu KK, a Japanese oil refiner, reported that consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 fell 69% to 5.34 billion yen ($34.9 million) from 17.04 billion yen a year earlier, but net income rose 41% to 11.59 billion yen, or 57.14 yen a share, from 8.25 billion yen, or 50.4 yen a share. The company sold 11.25 billion yen in fixed assets in fiscal 1990, a rise of 10.89 billion yen from the year-earlier level. Sales rose 13% to 472.03 billion yen from 417.79 billion yen. Cost of sales, which includes oil acquisition costs, rose to 413.14 billion yen from 349.11 billion yen.
