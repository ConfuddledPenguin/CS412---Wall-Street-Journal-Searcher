Sallie Mae Will Price Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

WASHINGTON -- The Student Loan Marketing Association, known as Sallie Mae, said it will price today $600 million of short-term floating-rate notes, due Dec. 13. Notes will be offered simultaneously in the U.S. domestic and the Eurodollar markets. The agency said trading is expected to begin at 8:20 a.m. EDT tomorrow and the notes will settle on Thursday. The notes will be priced at a spread to the bond-equivalent yield of the 91-day Treasury bill. The interest rate will be reset on the day after each weekly Treasury bill auction.
