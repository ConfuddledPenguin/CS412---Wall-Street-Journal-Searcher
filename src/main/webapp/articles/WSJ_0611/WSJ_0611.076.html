Consolidated Capital Plans to Sell Interests In Some Partnerships

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

DALLAS -- Consolidated Capital Equities Corp. said it will sell its general partner and management interests in 16 publicly held real estate partnerships, representing most of its assets, for $6.3 million. The buyer, ConCap Equities Inc., includes R&B Enterprises of Los Angeles; Hayman Co. in Southfield, Mich.; HornBarlow Cos. of Dallas and Metroplex Inc. of Chicago, all real estate development and property management companies.

The sale is subject to approval by limited partners. Consolidated Capital Equities, which sought protection under bankruptcy laws in late 1988, said it expects to mail proxies to limited partners by mid-July at the latest.

Proceeds from the sale will be used to settle the company's debts, which are estimated at between $17 million and $42 million.
