Premark Completes Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

DEERFIELD, Ill. -- Premark International Inc. said it completed its $201 million, or $16.40-a-share, tender offer for Sikes Corp. The consumer and restaurant products company agreed in April to acquire Sikes, a Lakeland, Fla., maker of ceramic tile.

Premark said it purchased 10.3 million Class A shares, or 96% of those outstanding.
