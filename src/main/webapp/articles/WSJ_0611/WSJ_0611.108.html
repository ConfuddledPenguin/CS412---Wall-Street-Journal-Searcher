International Brief -- Christies International PLC: ADT Ltd. Increases Stake In Auction Group to 21.27%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

ADT Ltd., a Bermuda-based holding company, continued its monthslong accumulation of Christies International PLC stock by increasing its stake in the auction group to 21.27% from 19.6%. Christies said in London that it had been informed that ADT, through its retirement fund unit, recently increased its holding to 32.9 million ordinary shares from 30.3 million. However, ADT's holding is diluted to 19.1% by closely held Christies A shares. David Tyler, Christies controller, said ADT hasn't yet informed the company of its intentions. ADT officials couldn't be reached for comment.
