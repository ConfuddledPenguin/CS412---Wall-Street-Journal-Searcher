Marketing Brief -- Capital Cities/ABC Inc.: Seven Magazines Are Sold To VS&A's PJS Subsidiary

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

ABC Consumer Magazines, a subsidiary of New York-based Capital Cities/ABC Inc., said it sold McCall's Needlework & Crafts and six other special-interest magazines to PJS Publications Inc., Peoria, Ill., a wholly owned unit of VS&A Communications Partners Limited Partnership, New York. Terms weren't disclosed. McCall's Needlework & Crafts, which features how-to articles on knitting and other crafts, will become part of the PJS Creative Women's Network, which also includes Crafts, Sew News and a related magazine for the crafts trade, Profitable Craft Merchandising. PJS Publications also publishes Shooting Times and HQ (Handgun Quarterly).

VS&A Communications Partners is an investment affiliate of Veronis, Suhler & Associates Inc., a New York-based investment banking firm.
