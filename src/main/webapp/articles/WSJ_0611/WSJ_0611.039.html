ANA Enterprises Plans Hotels

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

SINGAPORE -- Japan's ANA Enterprises Ltd. plans to open two hotels in China, and one each in the U.S., Austria and Australia in the next four years, a company official said. Guido Jonas, general manager of ANA Hotel Singapore, a subsidiary of All Nippon Airways Co. of Japan, said the company would open new hotels in Beijing, China, and Miami Beach, Fla., next year.

Mr. Jones also disclosed plans to open another hotel, the Chang-An International, in China, the ANA Hotel Sydney in Australia in 1992 and a hotel in Vienna, Austria, in 1994.
