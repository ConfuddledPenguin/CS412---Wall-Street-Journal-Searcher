Who's News: Galatic Resources Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

GALACTIC RESOURCES Ltd. (Vancouver, British Columbia) -- Peter J. Guest, 52 years old, a consultant and formerly a Fluor Corp. executive, was named president and chief executive officer of this gold mining concern, succeeding Robert Friedland, 39, who was named vice chairman, a new post. Mr. Friedland also was succeeded as chairman by James H. Foreman, 58, a Galactic director and previously chairman, president and chief executive of Pegasus Gold Inc.
