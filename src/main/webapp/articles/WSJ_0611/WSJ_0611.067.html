Who's News: Pan Am Corp. Creates Office of Chairman, Realigns Management

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

NEW YORK -- Pan Am Corp. said it formed an office of the chairman in a move that will allow two of its senior executives to join in the decision-making process for the company. The airline holding company said the office will consist of its 45-year-old chairman, president and chief executive officer, Thomas G. Plaskett, and two new executive vice presidents: Richard H. Francis, 58, formerly senior vice president and chief financial officer for Pan Am and its principal unit, Pan American World Airways; and Peter T. McHugh, 43, formerly senior vice president, marketing, for Pan American World Airways.

Mr. Francis was also named executive vice president, finance and chief financial officer, and Mr. McHugh, executive vice president, marketing and planning, both for Pan American World Airways.

The company also named Roger A. Cooke, formerly corporate secretary, as senior vice president and general counsel for both the parent and Pan American World Airways, succeeding John M. Lindsay. Gregory W. Buhler, formerly deputy general counsel, was named to the new post of vice president, legal, for Pan American World Airways.
