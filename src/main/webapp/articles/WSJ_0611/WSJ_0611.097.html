Business Brief -- Towle Manufacturing Co.: Certain Assets to Be Sold To Fieldston Investors L.P.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

Towle Manufacturing Co., Newburyport, Mass., currently operating under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code, said it signed a definitive agreement with Fieldston Investors Limited Partnership, a private New York investment concern, for the sale of certain assets of its silversmiths, retail, Canada and Hong Kong businesses. Terms weren't disclosed.

The agreement, already approved by Towle's board, requires the approval of the U.S. bankruptcy court. With approval, Towle said it expects to close the transaction within about 60 days.

Fieldston said it intends to continue operating the businesses after the sale.

Towle is a maker of silver and giftware products.
