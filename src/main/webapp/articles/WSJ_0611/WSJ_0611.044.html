Who's News: New Hampshire Yankee's Brown to Retire on Oct. 1

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

SEABROOK, N.H. -- Edward A. Brown announced he will retire on Oct. 1 from his position as president and chief executive officer of New Hampshire Yankee, operator of Seabrook Station. Mr. Brown, 60 years, joined the company in 1984. New Hampshire Yankee, a division of the Public Service Co. of New Hampshire, was created to complete Seabrook Station's construction and licensing, and to oversee management and operation of the electricity plant.

It is expected that Ted C. Feigenbaum, 40, senior vice president and chief operating officer of New Hampshire Yankee, will assume Mr. Brown's duties at Seabrook Station.
