Microcom Expects First-Quarter Loss; Overstocking Is Cited

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

NORWOOD, Mass. -- Microcom Inc., citing overstocking by distributors of its software products, said it expects to report a loss of 15 cents to 20 cents a share for the first quarter, ending June 30, on revenue of $14 million. In the year-ago period, the workstation connectivity company had net income of $2.5 million, or 29 cents a share, on revenue of $17 million.

James M. Dow, president and chief executive officer, said it is "unlikely" that Microcom's distributors will "require significant additional product shipments . . . prior to June 30."
