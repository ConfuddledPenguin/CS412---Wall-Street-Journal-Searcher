Business Brief -- Wang Laboratories Inc.: Wang Financial Unit Sold For Around $16 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

Wang Laboratories Inc., Lowell, Mass., said that, as part of a continuing effort to reduce debt, it completed the sale of Wang Financial Information Services Corp. to Shark Information Services Corp., a unit of New York-based Infotechnology Inc., for about $16 million. The acquired Wang unit, to be called Shark, provides quotes, financial news and analytical information. Wang, a computer maker, previously said it will take a book loss of less than $10 million in connection with the sale.

Infotechnology owns United Press International and 47% of Financial News Network.
