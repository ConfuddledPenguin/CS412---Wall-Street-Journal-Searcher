International: Banks Clear Bond Media Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

SYDNEY, Australia -- A bank syndicate approved plans by Australian publisher and investor Kerry Packer to take control of Bond Media Ltd. Earlier last week, Australia's corporate regulators cleared the plan.

Bond Media directors recently agreed to restructure the debt-burdened company's capital and convert $200 million Australian (US$155.4 million) of preference shares held by Mr. Packer into common shares. Eventually, the plan calls for Mr. Packer's closely held Consolidated Press Holdings Ltd. to control almost 60% of the radio and television-station operator.

Bond Media also said that a National Australia Bank Ltd.-led syndicate owed A$367 million extended the credit until March 29, 1991.
