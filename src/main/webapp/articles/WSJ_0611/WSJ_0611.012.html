Siemens Gets Systems Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

MUNICH, West Germany -- Siemens AG said it received a contract to supply up to 55,000 personal computer systems to members of the Amadeus airline reservations network over a five-year period. The electronics concern said the order could be worth up to 680 million Deutsche marks ($400.6 million) if all the computer systems available under the contract are acquired.

Siemens said it received the supply order from Amadeus Development Co. of Nice, France. Amadeus is a business cooperation in the area of shared reservation systems. The venture includes Air France, West Germany's Lufthansa, Spain's Iberia and SAS Scandinavian Airlines System.
