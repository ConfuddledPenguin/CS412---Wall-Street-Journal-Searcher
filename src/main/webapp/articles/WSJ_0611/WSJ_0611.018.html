Nomura Securities in Berlin

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

TOKYO -- Nomura Securities Co. announced Friday that it will establish a representative office in West Berlin to collect information on the rapid changes expected in the eventual unification of East and West Germany. Nomura becomes the second Japanese financial institution to open an office in Berlin, a Nomura official said. The Bank of Tokyo Ltd. started doing business in East Berlin June 1. Nomura's new office will start operations Monday.

With the new office, the number of Nomura's subsidiaries and representative offices overseas comes to 54 in 23 countries.
