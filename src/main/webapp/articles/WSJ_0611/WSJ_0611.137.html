Business Brief -- Fisher Group Inc.: TriMas Acquires Draw-Tite For More Than $50 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

TriMas Corp., Ann Arbor, Mich., said it acquired the Draw-Tite divisions of the Fisher Group Inc. for more than $50 million. The transaction includes real estate and other related assets. TriMas manufactures products for recreational vehicle and marine markets. Draw-Tite manufactures and markets trailer hitches and other towing-system products throughout the U.S. and Canada.
