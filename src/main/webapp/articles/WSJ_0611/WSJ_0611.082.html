Business Brief -- American Southwest Mortgage: Home Owners Savings Bank Sells Its Stake in Company

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

Home Owners Savings Bank said it sold its stake in American Southwest Mortgage Investments Corp., a real-estate investment trust in Tucson, Ariz. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Home Owners Savings said it sold 954,800 American Southwest common shares in the open market from April 17 to June 4 for $3.18 to $4.05 each.

Home Owners Savings, formerly Home Owners Federal Savings & Loan Association, gave no reason in the filing for selling its holding.

Joseph C. Chan, American Southwest's chief operating officer, said the stock sale is "positive for us, because it removes the uncertainty as to that block of stock," which amounted to less than a 7% stake in the company.
