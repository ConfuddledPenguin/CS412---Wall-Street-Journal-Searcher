SAY Packaging Files For Chapter 11 Status

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

LEOMINSTER, Mass. -- S.A.Y. Packaging Inc. said it filed for relief under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, after its largest creditor, the Bank of New England, terminated its credit facilities. S.A.Y. said it will continue to seek alternative sources of financing and to formulate a plan of reorganization that will be acceptable to creditors and shareholders.

The maker of plastic packaging for motor oil and other consumer products added that it will operate under bankruptcy court protection.

S.A.Y. said it has assets of about $10.5 million and liabilities of about $10.3 million.
