Business Brief -- SCEcorp: California Energy, Partners Are Named in Complaint

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

California Energy Co. said SCEcorp's Mission Power Engineering Co. unit filed a complaint in state court in Inyo County against California Energy and its partners. In the complaint, Mission Power claims additional compensation of between $70 million and $80 million for construction work on a series of power plants, California Energy said. California Energy said it is discussing the claims with Mission Power. San Francisco-based California Energy, which produces energy from geothermal resources, said it "disputes the amounts claimed by Mission Power Engineering."

Officials at SCEcorp, a Rosemead, Calif., holding company, couldn't be reached for comment.
