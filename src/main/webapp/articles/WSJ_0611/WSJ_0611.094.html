Business Brief -- Modine Manufacturing Co.: Rise of 5% to 10% Expected For Fiscal Year Sales, Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

Modine Manufacturing Co., Racine, Wis., said its sales and earnings for the fiscal year ending March 31, 1991, are expected to rise 5%-to-10% over the previous year. For the year ended March 31, 1990, Modine earned $25.2 million, or $1.68 a share, on sales of $436.3 million.

The maker of automotive parts and heating and air-conditioning equipment said its outlook for the new year indicates a stronger automotive aftermarket. Modine also said it expects more growth in its heavy-duty and industrial businesses, as well as continued strength in its heating and ventilating business and its international activities.
