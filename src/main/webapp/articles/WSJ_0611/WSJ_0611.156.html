Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Greenery Rehabiliation Group Inc., offering of two million common shares, of which 500,000 shares are being sold by the company and 1.5 million shares by Gerard M. Martin, chairman and chief executive, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. and Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.

Merry-Go-Round Enterprises Inc., proposed offering of 2.15 million common shares, of which 850,000 will be sold by the company and 1.3 million by holders, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc., Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and PaineWebber Inc.

Morgan Stanley Group Inc., offering of up to $1.018 billion of debt securities, including medium-term notes and warrants to purchase debt securities.

Software Toolworks Inc., offering of 3.85 million common shares, of which two million shares will be sold by the company and 1.85 million by holders, via Montgomery Securities and PaineWebber.

Vintage Petroleum Inc., initial offering of six million common shares, of which 3.4 million shares will be sold by the company and 2.6 million by holders, via Dillon, Read & Co. and Dean Witter Reynolds Inc.
