Martin Marietta Wins $145.7 Million Job For Gear From Navy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

WASHINGTON -- Martin Marietta Corp. was awarded a $145.7 million Navy contract for submarine sonar equipment.

Earth Technology Corp. received a $47 million Air Force contract for missile engineering services.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. won a $23.5 million Air Force contract for radar equipment.

Loral Corp. was issued a $20.2 million Air Force contract for radar warning receivers.

Norden Systems, a unit of United Technologies Corp., received a $12.2 million Army contract for missile equipment.

Federal Electric International Inc. got a $10.4 million Air Force contract for engineering services.
