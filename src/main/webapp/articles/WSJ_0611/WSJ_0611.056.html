Grove Bank for Savings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

BRIGHTON, Mass. -- Grove Bank for Savings said it voted at its annual meeting yesterday to form a Delaware-chartered holding company, Grove Bankcorp Inc. Grove Bank also said it expanded its board to 17, naming James Rosenwald III, a principal in a California investment advisory firm, and Christopher Sullivan, a partner in a Boston law firm, directors.
