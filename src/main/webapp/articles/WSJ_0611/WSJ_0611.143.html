Atlas Copco AB Stock Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/11/90

STOCKHOLM -- Swedish engineering and rock-drilling concern Atlas Copco AB said Friday it has acquired all of the stock of Rotoflow Corp. of the U.S. Atlas Copco, which declined to disclose the price of the transaction, said Rotoflow will operate as an independent company within its Applied Compressor Technique division. That division focuses its operations on specially built air-gas compressors, expansion turbines and cryogenic pumps.

Based in Los Angeles, Rotoflow, a leading company in turbo-expander technology, is profiting from the growing demand for natural gas; the company expects sales to reach $30 million this year, Atlas Copco said.
