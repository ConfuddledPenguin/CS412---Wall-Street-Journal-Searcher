Business Brief -- Pathe Communications Corp.: Company Gives MGM/UA Its Final Down Payment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/11/90

MGM/UA Communications Co. said in Culver City, Calif., that it received the fourth and final $50 million down payment from Pathe Communications Corp. toward Pathe's $1.2 billion tender offer for the movie studio. Pathe has now put down a security deposit of $200 million in cash. Time Warner Inc. has agreed to provide a $650 million loan as part of the purchase price, and is in discussions with Pathe and its chairman, Italian financier Giancarlo Parretti, about Pathe's ability to come up with the rest of the financing.

The tender offer is scheduled to expire Friday, but can be extended until June 23, and if all parties are agreeable, even further.
