Potlatch Corp. Debentures

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Potlatch Corp. said it decided to redeem for cash its $75 million face amount outstanding of 5.75% convertible subordinated debentures. The forest-products concern said the redemption price will be $1,040.25 a debenture plus accrued interest to June 26, the redemption date. Prior to the close of business on June 26, holders can convert their debentures into Potlatch common based on a conversion price of $45.3125 a share, the company said.
