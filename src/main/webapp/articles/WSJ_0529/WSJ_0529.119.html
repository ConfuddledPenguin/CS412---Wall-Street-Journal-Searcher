Who's News: CSX Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

CSX Corp. (Richmond, Va.) -- B.C. Eason, 41 years old, was named vice president, customer service and systems, for CSX Transportation Inc., the rail-transport and distribution-services arm of this holding company. William H. Cosgrove will succeed Mr. Eason as vice president, car management.
