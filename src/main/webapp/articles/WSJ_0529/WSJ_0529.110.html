Who's News: Regal International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

REGAL INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Houston) -- Vijay Dandapani was elected a director of this energy industry products maker. Mr. Dandapani is president of Zurich Corp., an investment concern and major investor in Regal. He succeeds George Moore, who resigned from the board last Tuesday for unspecified reasons.
