REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Models

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

There has been quite a bit of talk recently about how individuals need role models, mostly, it seems, in law school. Country singer Reba McEntire had an interesting insight into all this while addressing, not a college, but a Tennessee grade-school's commencement. She told the kids to be careful not to mislead their younger school colleagues, noting that her own idols were actually kids a few years older than herself. Her advice: "Kids in the first grade will be looking up to you, so watch what you say or do."
