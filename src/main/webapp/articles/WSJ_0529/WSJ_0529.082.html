International Brief -- Mitsubishi Motors Corp.: Unconsolidated Pretax Profit For Fiscal Year Rose 23%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

Mitsubishi Motors Corp., Japan's third-largest auto maker, reported that its unconsolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 increased 23% to 41.42 billion yen ($274.4 million) from 33.72 billion yen a year earlier. Operating profit rose 11% to 48.77 billion yen from 44.07 billion yen, contributing to a 58% rise in net income to 20.24 billion yen from 12.84 billion yen. Per-share earnings rose to 23.71 yen from 16.59 yen.

Sales climbed 6.7% to 2.026 trillion yen from 1.899 trillion yen, boosted by an increase in domestic sales and a surge in exported parts for overseas production; revenue from the latter more than doubled to 161.6 billion yen from 69.8 billion yen. Interest and dividends income rose 28% to 20.55 billion yen.

The company decided to extend a dividend of 5.5 yen a common share, up 10% from five yen a year earlier.

Mitsubishi predicted sales of 2.2 trillion yen in fiscal 1991, up 8.6%; net income is seen at 22 billion yen, up 8.7%, and pretax profit at 46 billion yen, up 11%.
