Hong Kong Maintains Forecast

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

HONG KONG -- Despite the Hong Kong economy's stagnation in the first quarter, the government is maintaining its forecast that gross domestic product will grow 3% in 1990, up from 1989's 2.5% expansion. Government economist Tang Kwong-yiu said that while Hong Kong has registered three consecutive quarters of "negligible" growth, the economy's underlying strength is indicated by the persistently high level of capacity at which local factories operate and the large number of job vacancies.
