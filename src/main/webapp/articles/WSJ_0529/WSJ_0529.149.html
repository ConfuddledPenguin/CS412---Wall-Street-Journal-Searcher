International Brief -- Nippon Telegraph & Telephone: Pretax Earnings Rose 17% For Year Ended March 31

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

Nippon Telegraph & Telephone Corp. said its consolidated pretax earnings in the fiscal year ended March 31 climbed 17% to 510.55 billion yen ($3.38 billion) from 438.06 billion yen in the previous fiscal year. Net profit earned by the parent company and its one consolidated subsidiary, NTT Data Communications System Corp., rose more, going up 23% to 273.75 billion yen from 222.96 billion.

Operating revenue came to 6.022 trillion yen, rising 3.1% from 5.841 trillion yen and exceeding six trillion yen for the first time.

NTT plans to pay a dividend for the year of 6,000 yen, including a special 1,000-yen dividend to commemorate the 100th anniversary of telephone service in Japan. Last fiscal year, the company paid a dividend of 5,000 yen.
