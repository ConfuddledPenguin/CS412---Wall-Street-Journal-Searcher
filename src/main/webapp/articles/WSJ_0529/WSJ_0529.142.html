France's Unemployment Data

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

PARIS -- The number of people in France seeking jobs in April fell an adjusted 0.9% from March to 2,481,300, representing a decrease of 20,900, and declined 2.3% from a year earlier, the French Labor Ministry said. France's unemployment rate decreased to 9.3% in April from 9.4% in March. The rate had been 9.4% since December 1989.
