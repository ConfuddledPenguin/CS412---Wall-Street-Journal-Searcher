Aceto Corp. Drops Planned Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

NEW YORK -- Aceto Corp. said it agreed to drop the planned sale of its Pfaltz & Bauer Inc. research chemicals business to a U.S. subsidiary of MTM PLC of the U.K. The agreement was mutual, Aceto said. Aceto said it plans to keep the business and strengthen its position as a supplier of research and development chemicals.
