Saving Graces

In our throwaway society,

Replete with disposable planners,

Perhaps we should try to preserve a few things --

Like ethics and morals and manners.

-- George O. Ludcke.

Barren Soil

The growth stock I bought

May someday bear fruit,

But so far, for years,

It has yet to take root.

-- Mimi Kay.
