Business Brief -- Acme-Cleveland Corp.: Sunderland Group Reduces Its Stake in Firm to 3.9%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

A group including Sunderland Holdings Corp., a Washington, D.C.-based investment concern, said it lowered its stake in Acme-Cleveland Corp. to 3.9% of the common stock outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it sold 67,500 Acme-Cleveland common shares from Feb. 16 to May 8 for $8.875 to $10 each, lowering its position to 247,100 shares.

Last December, the group said it acquired a 5% stake in the Cleveland-based maker of machine tools and other products. The group also includes Sunderland's chairman, Richard L. Kramer, and its president, William L. Remley.
