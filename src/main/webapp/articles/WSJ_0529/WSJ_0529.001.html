NTT's Foreign Purchases Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

TOKYO -- Nippon Telegraph & Telephone Corp. said its procurement from foreign suppliers rose 22% in fiscal 1990 to 50.4 billion yen ($334 million), exceeding the company's construction investment. In the year ended March 31, the telecommunications company substantially raised overseas purchases of such items as digital equipment, optical fiber cables, digital transmission and information processing equipment.

Foreign procurement of pocket pagers and mobile telephone equipment rose.

The U.S. accounted for 90% of the total increased procurement.
