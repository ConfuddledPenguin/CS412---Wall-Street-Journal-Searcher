Who's News: Bank of New England Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

BANK OF NEW ENGLAND Corp. (Boston) -- James F. McNally, chairman of this bank holding company's Connecticut Bank & Trust Co. unit, has informed the company that he intends to retire this summer. Mr. McNally, 57 years old, has been with Connecticut Bank & Trust for 31 years and has been chairman of the unit since 1988. Bank of New England said it hasn't yet named a successor to Mr. McNally.
