International Brief -- NEC Corp.: Pretax Earnings for Year Rose 31% to Record Level

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

NEC Corp., a leading Japanese maker of computers and other information equipment, said its consolidated pretax earnings for the year ended March 31 climbed 31% to a record 173.74 billion yen ($1.15 billion) from 132.19 billion yen a year earlier. Net income rose 32% to 85.22 billion yen, or 56.08 yen a share, from 64.48 billion yen, or 43.18 yen a share. Sales rose 12% to 3.444 trillion yen from 3.082 trillion yen.

NEC's unconsolidated results were equally strong. Pretax earnings rose 30% to 133.24 billion yen from 102.14 billion yen. Unconsolidated net jumped 35% to 74.89 billion yen, or 49.29 yen a share, from 55.34 billion yen, or 37.05 yen a share. Sales rose 8.6% to 2.76 trillion yen from 2.542 trillion yen. All of the results were records.

Sales of computers and other electronic products -- NEC's largest line, representing 44% of the total -- rose 13% to 1.506 trillion yen; communications equipment rose 9% to 875.02 billion yen; and electronic devices, including semiconductors, rose 7.1% to 605.95 billion yen.
