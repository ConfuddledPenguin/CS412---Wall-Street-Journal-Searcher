Burlington Resources Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

SEATTLE -- Burlington Resources Inc. said its Meridian Oil Inc. subsidiary agreed to buy the producing properties of Unicon Producing Co. for $399 million. The acquisition covers about 500 billion cubic feet of proved natural gas or equivalent reserves, nearly all of which are in the San Juan Basin of northwest New Mexico, where Meridian has a significant interest. Burlington, an energy company, said the transaction is its largest energy acquisition since 1985 and would boost Meridian's total reserves about 11%. The transaction is subject to several conditions, including Federal Trade Commission approval. Burlington said it plans to complete the deal next month.

Unicon is a partnership of Union Texas Petroleum Holdings Inc., Houston, and Kiewit Continental Inc., Norwalk, Conn. Union Texas recently put itself up for sale.
