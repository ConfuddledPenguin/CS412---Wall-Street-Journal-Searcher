Who's News: Allied Research Adds Chief to Carter's Posts, Posts 3rd-Period Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

BALTIMORE -- Allied Research Corp. named Reinald W. Carter to the added post of chief executive officer, succeeding M.B. Ruffin. Mr. Carter, 58 years old, has been president and chief operating officer. Mr. Ruffin, 72 years old, plans to remain chairman until December, and then become a consultant to the company.

Separately, the company reported a $2.1 million loss for the third quarter on revenue of $8.4 million, and said it may not return to profitability as it had predicted. The company cited revisions to a contract at its Belgian subsidiary, Mecar S.A., and a spokesman said the margins on the $9 million contract with an unnamed country haven't been as large as the company anticipated.

The company said Allied Research, which designs and develops high-technology products and weapons systems, "was facing a variety of financial issues," but expected to resolve them through more cost cutting and refinancing.
