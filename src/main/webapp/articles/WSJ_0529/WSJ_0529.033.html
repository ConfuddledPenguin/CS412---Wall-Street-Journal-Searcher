Dividend News: Shearson Payout Is Omitted

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

NEW YORK -- Shearson Lehman Hutton Holdings Inc. directors omitted the quarterly dividend of 18.75 cents a share last paid April 2. American Express Co., which owns about 60% of Shearson, is acquiring the common shares it doesn't already own of the brokerage and investment banking company, and the dividend omission is among terms of the pending merger agreement.
