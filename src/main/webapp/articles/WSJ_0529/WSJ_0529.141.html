RMI Titanium Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

NILES, Ohio -- RMI Titanium Co. said it plans to enter a joint venture arrangement with a unit of Australia's Minproc Holdings Ltd. to build a $35 million plant to make titanium sponge. RMI said the plant, at Ashtabula, Ohio, will be constructed and operated by a venture with Minproc Resources Inc. Terms of the venture and financing haven't been completed.

Construction is expected to be completed by the end of 1991. RMI, which went public in April, makes titanium products.
