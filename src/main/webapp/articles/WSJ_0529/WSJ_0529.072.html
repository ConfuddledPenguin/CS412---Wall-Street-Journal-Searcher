Who's News: John Wiley's Ellis Is Appointed Chief

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

NEW YORK -- John Wiley & Sons Inc., an independent book publishing company, said it named Charles R. Ellis president and chief executive officer, succeeding Ruth R. McMullin, who resigned, effective next Thursday. Mr. Ellis, 54 years old, is an executive vice president of Wiley and president of its Wiley Publishing Group unit. The company specializes in scientific, technical and business titles.

The company said Mrs. McMullin, 47, is leaving voluntarily to pursue personal interests. Before being named chief executive last year, she was the company's chief operating officer for two years.
