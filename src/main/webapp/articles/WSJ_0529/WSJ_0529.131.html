French Bankruptcies Rise 4.3%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

PARIS -- The number of corporate bankruptcies, liquidations and legal settlements in France rose 4.3% in April to a seasonally adjusted 3,780 from 3,623 in March and 3,165 in February, the National Statistics Institute said. The monthly average rate for corporate failures in 1989 was 3,337.
