Business Brief -- Interface Systems Inc.: Firm Acquires 25% Stake In Sintec Peripherals Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

Interface Systems Inc. said it has acquired 25% of Sintec Peripherals Ltd., a London-based distributor of computer peripherals to users of IBM mainframe and midrange systems. Terms weren't disclosed.

As part of the agreement, Carl L. Bixby, the president and board chairman of Interface Systems, Ann Arbor, Mich., has joined the board of directors of Sintec Peripherals. Interface Systems said it also received an option to purchase in 1992 the remaining 75% of Sintec Peripherals, along with all of its wholly owned subsidiary, Sintec Supplies Ltd., which sells printer supplies in Europe.

Interface said it is making the acquisition to expand its share of the growing European market. Sintec Peripherals has handled European sales and distribution of Interface Systems' IBM-compatible printers since 1987.
