Canadian Steel Output Drops

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 296,125 metric tons in the week ended May 19, a 6.7% decline from 317,282 tons the previous week and a 4.5% decline from 309,983 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. From Jan. 1 through May 19, production totaled 5,525,599 tons, an 11% drop from 6,225,071 tons a year earlier.
