Business Brief -- Horizon Industries Inc.: Investor Buys More Shares, Boosting Interest to 7.58%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

Investor M. Edward Ralston said he bought 240,000 shares of Horizon Industries Inc. common, raising his individual position in the carpet company to 7.58%. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Ralston said he bought the 240,000 shares on May 17 for $7 each.

Mr. Ralston is part of a larger shareholder group, Beaulieu of America Inc., a Dalton, Ga., yarn and carpet manufacturer, which has said it may seek control of Horizon.

The additional shares bought by Mr. Ralston bring the group's combined ownership to about 17.7%.

Mr. Ralston is president of D&W Carpet & Rug Co., a Chatsworth, Ga., carpet maker.

Horizon, based in Calhoun, Ga., declined to comment.
