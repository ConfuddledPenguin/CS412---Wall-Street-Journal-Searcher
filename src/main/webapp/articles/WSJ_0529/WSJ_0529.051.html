Who's News: CMS Energy Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

CMS ENERGY Corp. -- The Consumers Power Co. unit elected Carl L. English to the new post of vice president, gas distribution and customer services. The move takes effect Friday. Mr. English, 43 years old, was previously executive manager of customer services. Since joining Consumers Power in 1969, he has held several customer and general service positions, including staff assistant to the chairman.
