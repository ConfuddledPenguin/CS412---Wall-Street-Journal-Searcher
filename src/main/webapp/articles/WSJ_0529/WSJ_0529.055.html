Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Alias Research Inc., initial offering of two million common shares, one million will be sold by the company, one million by holders, via Robertson, Stephens & Co. and Wessels, Arnold & Henderson.

Avco Financial Services Inc., a unit of Textron Inc., shelf offering of $500 million amount of debt securities and warrants.

Colorado Interstate Gas Co., a unit of Coastal Corp of Houston, offering $180 million of senior debentures, due June 2005, via Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.

Cincinnati Milacron Inc., offering of three million common shares, 2.4 million of which will be sold in the U.S. and 600,000 to be offered internationally, via First Boston Corp. and Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd.

Environmental Elements Corp., initial offering of 2.3 million common shares, 1.6 million of which will be sold by the company and 700,000 by holders, via Kidder, Peabody & Co. and Legg Mason Wood Walker Inc.

Swift Transportation Co. Inc., of Delaware, a unit of Swift Transportation Co. Inc., of Phoenix, Ariz., initial offering of 1,650,000 common shares, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc and Dean Witter Reynolds Inc.

Toro Co., offering of 1.5 million common shares, via Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Dain Bosworth Inc.
