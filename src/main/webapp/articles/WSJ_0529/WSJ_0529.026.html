Dividend News: Albertson's Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

ALBERTSON'S Inc., Boise, Idaho, said its board approved a 2-for-1 stock split in the form of a 100% stock dividend, payable June 29 to stock of record June 8. The food and drug chain also declared a regular quarterly dividend on the post-split shares equal to 12 cents a share, payable Aug. 25 to stock of record Aug. 10.
