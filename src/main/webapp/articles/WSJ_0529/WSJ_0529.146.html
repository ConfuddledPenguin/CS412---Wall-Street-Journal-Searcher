OECD Jobless Rate For 11 Nations Falls

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 05/29/90

PARIS -- The average unemployment rate among the 11 leading industrialized countries eased to 5.9% in March from 6% in February, the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development said. The organization said jobless rates declined in nine of the countries, with the most significant drops in Canada, the Netherlands and Australia.

The OECD average unemployment rate in this year's first quarter was 6%, down from 5.8% a year earlier. But the jobless rate has remained roughly unchanged since mid-1989. European unemployment rates, however, have eased about 0.5% on average, compared with a year earlier, with the greatest declines in West Germany, Italy, the Netherlands and Britain.

The organization said April unemployment rose slightly in four of the six countries for which data are available.
