GE Capital Unit To Buy Subsidiary Of Travelers Corp.

  • Author: Beatrice E. Garcia
  • Date: 05/29/90

A GE Capital Corp. unit will move into a new area of the mortgage finance business with its agreement to purchase Travelers Corp.'s mortgage services subsidiary. Terms weren't disclosed. The acquisition will allow GE Mortgage Capital Corp., Raleigh, N.C., to enter the mortgage servicing business. Until now, GE Mortgage's main activities were selling mortgage insurance and packaging bundles of mortgage loans for the secondary market. GE Mortgage was formed in 1983.

"In today's marketplace, many of our customers are trying to sell valuable servicing rights to generate capital. Due to the current restructuring of the industry, there's a shortage of buyers," said Greg Barmore, GE Mortgage Capital's president.

Travelers Mortgage Services Inc., headquartered in Cherry Hill, N.J., will be transferring to GE Mortgage a $12 billion portfolio of residential mortgage loans that it currently services. The acquisition includes Travelers' mortgage servicing business, its relocation services and its mortgage wholesale operation, which buys mortgages originated by other companies as well as thrifts and banks.

But GE Mortgage's purchase won't include Travelers' retail mortgage origination business. GE Mortgage doesn't want to be in the business of making mortgage loans because it doesn't want to compete with its customers, which are thrifts, banks and other mortgage finance companies.

Before it finalizes its deal with GE Mortgage, Travelers hopes to find another buyer for the mortgage origination business. The Hartford, Conn., insurer originally planned to sell the entire mortgage services unit to management and investor group nearly a year ago. But the transaction fell through in last December and the mortgage unit remained since then.

Travelers is selling the mortgage unit as part of its restructuring to return to its core insurance business. The insurer purchased the unit, formerly known as Brokers Mortgage Services, in 1985.
