Who's News: New York Mercantile Exchange

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

NEW YORK MERCANTILE EXCHANGE (New York) -- Stephen C. Daffron was named vice president of the newly created strategic planning department at this commodity exchange and energy market trading company, beginning June 1. Mr. Daffron, an assistant professor at the U.S. Military Academy at West Point, N.Y., has been serving as a consultant to the exchange on the development of electronic trading and the effects of eliminating dual trading.
