PrimeEnergy Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- K.R.M. Petroleum Corp. stockholders approved a name change for the company. The new name, effective immediately, is PrimeEnergy Corp. The company's stock is traded over the counter; its trading symbol changes to PNRG, from KRMC. The company is the managing general partner for 54 public and privately held drilling and income fund limited partnerships.
