Business Brief -- British Petroleum Co.: BP America Affiliate to Sell Coal Operations to Zeigler

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/29/90

Cleveland-based BP America Inc., the U.S. affiliate of British Petroleum Co., said it agreed to sell most of its remaining U.S. coal operations to closely held Zeigler Coal Holding Co., Fairview Heights, Ill., for $115 million. The agreement, which is subject to government approval, includes the sale of BP's Franklin Coal and Old Ben Coal companies. The coal operations consist of BP's four underground steam coal mines in Illinois, two surface steam coal mines in Indiana, and steam and metallurgical operations in West Virginia. Only a few small inactive reserves will remain in BP's coal operations.

Last year, the oil and gas concern sold a tract of low-sulfur coal reserves to Ashland Coal Inc. for $137 million.
