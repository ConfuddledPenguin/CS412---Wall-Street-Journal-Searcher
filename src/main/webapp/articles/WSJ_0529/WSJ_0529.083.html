Who's News: Boeing Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/29/90

BOEING Co. (Seattle) -- John E. Schmick, formerly vice president of this aerospace concern's Washington, D.C., office, was named vice president of the Electronic Systems division of the Boeing Defense & Space Group. Mr. Schmick, 54 years old, succeeds John Sheridan, who was recently named executive vice president of the Aerospace & Electronics division.
