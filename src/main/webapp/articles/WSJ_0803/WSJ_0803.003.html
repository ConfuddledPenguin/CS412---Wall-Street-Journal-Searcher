Who's News: Western Digital Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

WESTERN DIGITAL Corp. (Irvine, Calif.) -- A.J. Moyer, senior vice president for finance and chief financial officer, resigned from this computer products concern. Mr. Moyer, 46 years old, said he plans "to take a break and retire." H. Richard Byrne, corporate controller and chief accounting officer, was named as acting chief financial officer.
