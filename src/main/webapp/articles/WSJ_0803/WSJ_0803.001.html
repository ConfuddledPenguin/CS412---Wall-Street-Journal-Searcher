Midway Air's July Traffic Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

CHICAGO -- Midway Airlines said its July traffic rose 47% to 441 million revenue passenger miles from 299.5 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier said its July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, rose to 60.7% from 57.5%.

In the first seven months, traffic rose 48% to 2.94 billion revenue passenger miles from 1.99 billion a year earlier. The load factor rose to 59.6% from 56.6%.
