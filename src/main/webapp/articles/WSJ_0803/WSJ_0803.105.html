Who's News: Morrison Knudsen Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

MORRISON KNUDSEN Corp. (Boise, Idaho) -- Irene Carswell Peden, a professor of engineering at the University of Washington, and Gerard K. Roche, chairman of Heidrick & Struggles Inc., were named to the board, bringing the number of directors to 11. Separately, William J. Agee, chairman and chief executive officer, was given the added title of president, succeeding Frank M. Adams, who died May 27.
