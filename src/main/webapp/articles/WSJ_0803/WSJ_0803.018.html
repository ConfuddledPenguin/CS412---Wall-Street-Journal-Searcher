Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Dennis J. Beckingham was named senior vice president and controller of American Express Travel Related Services Co., a unit of this travel and financial services company. He succeeds Salvatore Vasto, recently named senior vice president and chief financial officer of the unit. Mr. Beckingham previously was senior vice president and chief financial officer of the direct-marketing and Travelers Cheque group.
