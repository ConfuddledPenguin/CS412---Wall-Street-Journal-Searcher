Who's News: Advanced Polymer Systems Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

ADVANCED POLYMER SYSTEMS Inc. (Redwood City, Calif.) -- Alan H. Bushell, 43 years old, was named chief executive officer. He succeeds John H. Williford, who remains chairman. Mr. Bushell also serves as president and chief operating officer, posts he previously held. The company develops pharmaceutical, cosmetic and food products for other companies.
