Who's News: MLX Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

MLX Corp. (Troy, Mich.) -- William C. Houser, 47 years old, was named to the board of this refrigeration and air conditioning distributor, expanding the board to nine members. Mr. Houser also was named president and chief executive officer of MLX's Refrigeration & Air Conditioning Group. Formerly, Mr. Houser was president of the Mid-Central and Western Operations of that group. In his new role, Mr. Houser assumes some of the former responsibilities of William P. Panny, MLX chairman. MLX said it created the new position for Mr. Houser to allow the company to focus better on its refrigeration and air conditioning business, which accounts for more than 85% of the company's sales.
