Coca-Cola Enterprises Files Shelf Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

ATLANTA -- Coca-Cola Enterprises Inc. said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer up to $1 billion in debt securities and warrants. The big Coke bottler, 49%-owned by Coca-Cola Co., said it planned to offer senior debt securities and warrants to buy debt securities, among other issues. The offerings, it said, would be made "from time to time."

In November, Coca-Cola Enterprises had made a similar $1 billion shelf registration. A spokesman said that there have been several note issues since then, including a $250 million offering in March, and that most of the previous shelf filing authorization has been exhausted.
