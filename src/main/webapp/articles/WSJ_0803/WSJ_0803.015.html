Who's News: Carter Hawley Hale Stores Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

CARTER HAWLEY HALE STORES Inc. (Los Angeles) -- Robert A. Dourian, formerly executive vice president, personnel, at this department store chain's Broadway-Southern California division, was named executive vice president, human relations, at the corporate parent. Mr. Dourian, 58 years old, succeeds in his new post Executive Vice President Loyd E. Ellis, 67; Mr. Ellis will continue to head Carter Hawley's information services operation.
