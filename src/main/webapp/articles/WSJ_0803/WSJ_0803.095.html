Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 254,057 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, down 3.2% from 262,440 the preceding week and 12% from 287,931 a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through Saturday, output totaled 8,261,581 metric tons, down 9.9% from 9,169,147 a year earlier.
