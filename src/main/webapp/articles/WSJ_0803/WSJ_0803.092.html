Who's News: Seagate Technology

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

SEAGATE TECHNOLOGY (Scotts Valley, Calif.) -- Doug Mahon resigned from the board of this computer disk-drive maker. Mr. Mahon also resigned as chief technical officer. Seagate named Brendan Hegarty to succeed Mr. Mahon as chief technical officer. The company didn't say whether it planned to select someone for the board spot left vacant by Mr. Mahon.
