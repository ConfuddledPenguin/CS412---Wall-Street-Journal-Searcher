Business Brief -- USX Corp.: U.K. Pipeline Investment Set at About $200 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

A USX Corp. spokesman said the company will invest about $200 million in a pipeline in the North Sea to transport gas to the United Kingdom. Pittsburgh-based USX had said it would become 20% owner of the 148-mile pipeline, which will transport gas from the company's Brae fields. Those fields have an estimated 2.3 trillion cubic feet of gas, of which USX owns about 900 billion cubic feet.

Separately, USX said it is offering $800 million principal amount at maturity ($251.1 million of gross proceeds) of zero coupon convertible senior debentures due 2005.

First Boston Corp. is managing the underwriting syndicate.

Each debenture is offered at $313.93 per $1,000 principal amount at maturity and is convertible at the option of the holder -- at any time on or prior to maturity -- into, at the company's option, 8.207 shares of USX common stock or cash. The conversion rate is based upon an initial premium of 12.1% above USX's closing stock price of $34.125 on Wednesday.
