Business Brief -- Procter & Gamble Co.: Bar Soap Plant Expansion To Create Up to 50 Jobs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

Procter & Gamble Co. said it will expand and modernize its Ivorydale bar soap plant in St. Bernard, Ohio. The project includes a 65,000-square-foot addition to the existing bar soap plant. About 45 to 50 new jobs will be added to the soap plant, which already employs about 750 people.

The Cincinnati consumer-products company said there could be further improvements at Ivorydale. Earlier this week, P&G won a tax abatement for up to $200 million in improvements at that site. P&G won't give details on this expansion, but a company representative said it will cost less than $100 million.

P&G's Ivory brand soap has been made at this site since it opened in 1886.
