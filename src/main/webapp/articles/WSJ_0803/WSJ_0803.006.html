Hong Kong's Retail Sales Data

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

HONG KONG -- The value of Hong Kong's total retail sales in May rose 5% from a year earlier, and the volume of sales slipped 1%, the government said. May retail sales were up 6% from April measured by value and up 5% by volume.

Sales in supermarkets surged 19% in value from May 1989, and department store sales climbed 11%. Sales of consumer durables rose 11% and car sales advanced 7%.

For this year's first five months, sales were up 2% in value but off 3% in volume.
