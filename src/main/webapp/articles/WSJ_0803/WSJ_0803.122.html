Marketing Brief -- Reed International PLC: British Publisher to Acquire Estates Gazette Magazine

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

Reed International PLC, a British publishing concern, will pay #72 million ($133.6 million) for Estates Gazette Ltd., a family-owned commercial property magazine. The London company said it may pay an additional five million pounds depending on the Gazette's advertising revenue in the three years to December 1993. The sale is expected to be completed Aug. 31, Reed said.

The agreement was struck on condition the deal isn't referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission for investigation on competitive grounds, and could lapse if it is referred.

Peter Davies, Reed International chairman, said the acquisition fits in with Reed's other trade publications, which include Computer Weekly, Flight International and Farmers Weekly.

Peter Wilson, owner of the Estates Gazette, said the price paid for the business was "fair" and in the best interests of the company.

Reed shares shed five pence to 428 pence ($7.94) in line with the broadly lower market on the London Stock Exchange.
