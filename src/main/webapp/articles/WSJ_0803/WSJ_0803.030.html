Untroubled Waters

("Companies who have been accused of being environmentally insensitive are funding municipal aquariums." -- Fortune)

Some companies who've had bad press

(More so than they would wish),

In looking to improve PR

Are reeling in the fish.

They're pouring money into plans

To house aquatic creatures,

Hoping generosity

Will dampen other features.

They hope to drown their critics out,

To silence or to bury 'em;

They look forward to the dawning of

The new Age of Aquarium.

-- Harriet Cooper.

Mature Judgment

Everything's relative, they say,

And that goes for the passage of time:

Old age is always fifteen years

Older than what I'm.

-- George O. Ludcke.
