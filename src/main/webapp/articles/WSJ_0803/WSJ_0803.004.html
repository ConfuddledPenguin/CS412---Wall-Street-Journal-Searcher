US Sprint Ad Campaign

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

NEW YORK -- US Sprint Communications Co., stung by a loss of market share to its rivals in the long-distance telephone industry, began a new advertising campaign aimed at reversing its slide. The television ads tout numerous business and consumer services offered by the United Telecommunications Inc. unit and invite customers to call a toll-free number to have their service switched to Sprint from another carrier, such as American Telephone & Telegraph Co.
