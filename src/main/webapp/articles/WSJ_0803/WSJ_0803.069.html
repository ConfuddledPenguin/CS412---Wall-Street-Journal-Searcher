Citicorp Is Initiating Notes, Warrants Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

WASHINGTON -- Citicorp said it is initiating a shelf offering of as much as $2 billion in principal amounts of notes and warrants to purchase notes. The New York-based bank holding company said in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing that notes will be offered as a series on terms and prices to be determined at the time of sale.

Proceeds will be used for corporate purposes, such as investments, and credit extensions to its subsidiaries, the company said in the filing. Underwriters weren't identified.
