Moog Unit Wins Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

EAST AURORA, N.Y. -- Moog Inc. said its engine controls division won a two-year, $9 million contract from Allied-Signal Inc.'s Bendix unit, South Bend, Ind. The order is for fuel control valves for the General Electric CF6 engine and could reach $30 million through 1997.

Moog makes control parts and systems for defense, aerospace and industrial applications. Allied-Signal is an automotive, aerospace and engineered materials concern.
