Ketema Authorizes Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

BENSALEM, Pa. -- Ketema Inc. said its board has authorized the purchase of as many as 250,000 of its shares outstanding. The purchases will be made on the open market or in negotiated transactions until Dec. 31. Ketema currently has about 4.3 million shares outstanding.

In composite trading on the American Stock Exchange yesterday, Ketema closed at $11.75, down 12.5 cents.
