Tyco Completes Assets Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

EXETER, N.H. -- Tyco Laboratories Inc., a maker of fire protection systems, packaging materials and electrical parts, said it completed the acquisition of the operating assets of Wormald International Ltd., a fire protection and distribution company based in Australia. The transaction, which included $350 million in cash and stock, had a total indicated value of more than $600 million.

As a result of the acquisition, Tyco said it expects to have sales of about $3.4 billion in its current fiscal year, being adjusted to end June 30 to coordinate financial reporting between Tyco and Wormald. In the year ended May 31, Tyco had sales of $2.1 billion.
