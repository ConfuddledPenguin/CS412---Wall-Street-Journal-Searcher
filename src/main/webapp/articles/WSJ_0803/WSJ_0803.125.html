Business Brief -- Havas S.A.: French Company's Revenue Climbed 28% in First Half

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

French media and tourism group Havas S.A. said consolidated revenue grew 28% in the first half to 11.76 billion francs ($2.21 billion) from 9.15 billion francs in the year-earlier period. On a comparable structural and exchange-rate basis, Havas said first-half revenue rose 18%. The company noted that sales of SCAC Voyages S.A., acquired from Bollore Technologies S.A. as part of an asset swap, have been consolidated into the balance sheet starting April 1, 1990.

A breakdown of Havas's revenue in the January-through-June period showed tourism revenue up 44%, outdoor advertising revenue up 20% and revenue from publishing and print media also up 20%.

Havas said sales outside of France accounted for 20% of total sales in the half, up from 14% in the like period of 1989.
