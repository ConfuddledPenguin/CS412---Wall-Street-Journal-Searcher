Banc One Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Banc One Corp. said it completed the acquisition of Meuse, Rinker, Chapman, Endres & Brooks, an investment banking firm based here. Terms weren't disclosed. David R. Meuse, 45 years old, who founded the firm in 1961, will continue as chairman and chief executive officer. The business, which also has offices in Cleveland and Dallas, was renamed Banc One Capital Corp.

Banc One is a bank holding company with assets of about $27.9 billion.
