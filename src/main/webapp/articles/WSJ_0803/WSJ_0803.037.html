Initial Jobless Claims Fell To 355,000 in Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits fell to a seasonally adjusted 355,000 for the week ended July 21, from 358,000 the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended July 14 was a seasonally adjusted 2,517,000, or 2.4% of those covered by unemployment insurance, compared with 2,490,000 the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate was also 2.4%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended July 14 rose to 2,508,400 from 2,475,300 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
