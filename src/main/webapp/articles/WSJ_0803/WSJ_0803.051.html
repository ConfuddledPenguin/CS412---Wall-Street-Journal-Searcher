McDonnell Gets Pact For Army Helicopters

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. received a $303.4 million Army contract for 66 AH-64 attack helicopters.

Avondale Industries Inc. got a $63.6 million Navy contract for a mine-warfare ship.

General Motors Corp. was awarded a $31.2 million Army contract for turbine engines.
