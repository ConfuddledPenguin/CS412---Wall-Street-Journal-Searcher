Fleetwood Enterprises Results

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

RIVERSIDE, Calif. -- Fleetwood Enterprises Inc. said preliminary sales for the first quarter ended July 29, slipped 1.5% to $389 million from $394.9 million a year ago. The company said a strong sales performance by its manufactured-housing group was more than offset by a 6% decline in recreational-vehicle revenue. Lower recreational-vehicle sales are "a result of a continuing slowdown in consumer spending, caused by concerns about a weakening economy and the threat of a recession," said Glenn Kummer, Fleetwood president.
