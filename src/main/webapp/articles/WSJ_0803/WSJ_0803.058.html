Who's News: Corroon & Black Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

CORROON & BLACK Corp. (New York) -- Robert J. Jennings, 42 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this insurance brokerage concern's Corroon & Black Administrative Services Inc. unit, newly formed from the merger of Corroon & Black Management Inc. and Corroon & Black Benefits Inc. Mr. Jennings previously was president and chief executive of Corroon & Black Management and president and chief operating officer of Corroon & Black Benefits.
