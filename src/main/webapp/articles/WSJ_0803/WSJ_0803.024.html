American Express's Bank Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

NEW YORK -- American Express Bank Ltd., the international banking arm of American Express Co., said it was granted a limited branch license by the Polish government. The Warsaw branch, American Express (Poland) Ltd., is expected to open by year end and will specialize in financial, travel and tourism-related services. American Express hasn't had a physical presence in Poland since World War II, though its travel cards and traveler's checks are accepted there.
