Who's News: Flight International Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

FLIGHT INTERNATIONAL GROUP Inc. (Newport News, Va.) -- Arthur R. Mann, 59 years old, was named chief operating officer, executive vice president and a director of this aviation services company. Mr. Mann succeeds Michael J. Garrihy, 52, formerly president, chief operating officer and a director of Flight, who has assumed the post of chairman and chief executive officer of Jet Express Inc., a regional air passenger carrier based in Newport News. Flight has an option to purchase Jet before June 27, 1991. As part of the restructuring, J. Nelson Happy, 46, chairman and chief executive officer of Flight, was named president of Flight.
