  • Date: 08/03/90

EQUITABLE LIFE ASSURANCE SOCIETY OF THE U.S. (New York) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it lowered its rating on this insurer's financial strength to single-A-1 from double-A-3. The rating concern also said it downgraded the Euronote issue of the Equitable Life Financing Corp. subsidiary to single-A-1 from double-A- 3. Moody's confirmed its prime-1 rating on Equitable commercial paper. It cited concerns about Equitable's "above average" exposure to highly leveraged transactions and to the weakened commercial real estate markets. ---

TEXAS-NEW MEXICO POWER CO. -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it lowered the ratings on about $190 million of this Fort Worth, Texas, utility's first mortgage bonds, to Baa-1 from single-A-3. The company is a unit of TNP Enterprises Inc. Moody's said it believes the utility will be subject to, among other things, continuing regulatory and capital market risks and soaring costs, including construction costs, for an extended period under which it will also be under financial pressure. No official of the company could be reached for comment.
