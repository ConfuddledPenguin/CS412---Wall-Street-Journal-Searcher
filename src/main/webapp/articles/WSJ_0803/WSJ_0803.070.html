Banner Industries Stock Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

CLEVELAND -- Banner Industries Inc. said it completed the initial public offering of common stock of its Banner Aerospace Inc. unit. The company said it sold 9.5 million shares, or about 53% of Banner Aerospace, for $14 a share. It will retain a 47% interest.

Banner Aerospace distributes new and overhauled aviation replacement parts, including fasteners, avionics, bearings and electrical and airframe parts.
