Chiyoda to Build Saudi Plants

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

TOKYO -- Chiyoda Corp., a plant-engineering company, said it secured an order for two fertilizer plants, valued at about 50 billion yen ($339.8 million), from Saudi Arabian Fertilizer Co., known as Safco. The Japanese concern, formerly called Chiyoda Chemical Engineering & Construction Co., will build the plants on the Al-Jubail industrial site; one will turn out 500,000 tons of ammonia and the other 600,000 tons of urea a year. Construction, expected to take 29 months, will begin after Chiyoda receives formal notification of the order, the company said.
