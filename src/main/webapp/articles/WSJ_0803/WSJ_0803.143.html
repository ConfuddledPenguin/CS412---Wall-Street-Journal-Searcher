International Brief -- TI Group PLC: Pretax Profit Increased 29% In First Six Months of 1990

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

TI Group PLC, a British industrial-engineering concern, said pretax profit jumped 29% in the six months ended June 30, as growth in most of its core activities continued despite difficult conditions in some of its key markets. Pretax profit rose to #63.3 million ($117.5 million) from #49.2 million a year earlier, hitting the top end of the range of market expectations.

Profit after taxes and minorities firmed 28% to #42.5 million from #33.1 million, while per-share earnings were up 22% at 27.0 pence from 22.2 pence.

Net profit attributable to shareholders gained 29% to #41.7 million pounds from #32.3 million after an #800,000 net extraordinary loss on the closure and disposal of businesses. The extraordinary loss was equal to that reported a year earlier.
