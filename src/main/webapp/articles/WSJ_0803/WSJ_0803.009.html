McDonald & Co. Investments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/03/90

CLEVELAND -- McDonald & Co. Investments Inc. said its board approved the continuation of its open-market share-repurchase program. The investment company had an average of 5,660,000 shares outstanding in the quarter ended June 29.

The current extension, which authorizes the buy-back of up to 300,000 shares, expires July 31, 1991. The program, which was originally announced in July 1987, was extended in 1988 and 1989.
