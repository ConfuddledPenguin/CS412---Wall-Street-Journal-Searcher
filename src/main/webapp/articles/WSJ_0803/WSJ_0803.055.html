Campeau Land-Sale Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/03/90

KIRKLAND, Quebec -- Grilli Property Group Inc. said it and Richcraft Homes Ltd. agreed to purchase jointly about 104 million square feet of vacant land in the Ottawa area from Toronto-based Campeau Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. Grilli said the land is near Ottawa International Airport. Grilli, a property development concern based here, said it also agreed to acquire an interest in an 11 million-square-foot block of vacant land owned by Richcraft, an Ottawa developer, near the land to be acquired from Campeau.
