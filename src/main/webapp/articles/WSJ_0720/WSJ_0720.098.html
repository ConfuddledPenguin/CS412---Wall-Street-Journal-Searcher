Financing Business: Hilton Hotels Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

HILTON HOTELS Corp. said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed offering of notes, due in 2005, with a principal amount of $650 million. The Beverly Hills, Calif., owner and operator of hotels and casinos said the notes are zero coupon, so the offering is expected to result in proceeds to Hilton of only $270 million. The notes will have a principal amount when they mature of $650 million, representing a 6% yield to maturity. The notes also are subordinated and convertible into Hilton common. Hilton said it will use the proceeds for general cororate purposes, possibly including repayment of debt.
