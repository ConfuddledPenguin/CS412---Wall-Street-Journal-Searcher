Providence Energy's New Chief

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

PROVIDENCE, R.I. -- Providence Energy Corp. named James H. Dodge its president and chief executive officer after a yearlong search. Mr. Dodge, 50 years old, has been president and chief executive officer of Vermont Gas Systems. He succeeds Louis R. Hampton, who remains chairman of Providence Energy, a natural-gas service concern. Mr. Hampton had held the chief executive officer post since the resignation of William S. LaLonde in April 1989.
