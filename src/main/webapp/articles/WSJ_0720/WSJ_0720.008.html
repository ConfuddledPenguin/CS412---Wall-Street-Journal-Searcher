Town & Country Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

CHELSEA, Mass. -- Town & Country Corp. said it signed a letter of intent with an investment banker to underwrite the sale of 30% of its Thailand unit, Essex International Co. The underwriting, which is contingent on regulatory approval, will generate net proceeds of $20 million, Town & Country said.

Town & Country, a jewelry concern, said the stock of Essex International will be traded on the Stock Exchange of Thailand once approval is gained.
