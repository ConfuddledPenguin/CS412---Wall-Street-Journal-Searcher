Phone Antics

I do not mind when I am told

They'll have to put my call on "hold";

It gives me time to think and jot

Down notes, flesh out a novel's plot,

Make changes in next week's routine,

Dust off my phone and fax machine,

Then savor the true power that's mine --

To hang up when they're on the line]

-- Bern Sharfman.

Tress Management

"Trim it up," I told the barber back when

I had no bald spots to hide,

But as the years passed, instructions changed

To "Just take some off the side."

-- George O. Ludcke.
