International Brief -- Ferranti International PLC: Fiscal 1990 Pretax Loss Of $293.6 Million Posted

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

Ferranti International PLC, a British electronics and engineering concern, posted a pretax loss for the year ended March 31 of #161.7 million ($293.6 million), compared with pretax profit of #13.1 million a year earlier, citing its "most difficult year" in the wake of fraud discovered in September 1989. Revenue fell 14% to #749.9 million from #927.5 million.

The fiscal 1990 loss after taxes and minority interests was #157.5 million, compared with profit of #3.9 million a year earlier. The loss per share was 21 pence, compared with year-ago earnings of 0.88 pence a share. Net profit attributable to shareholders, after taxes and special items, was #43.2 million, compared with a net loss of #800,000 a year earlier. The latest results had an extraordinary profit of #200.7 million from shedding assets, the year-earlier period an extraordinary loss of #7.5 million. A final dividend isn't proposed, resulting in no annual payout.
