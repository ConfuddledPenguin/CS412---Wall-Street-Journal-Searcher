Business Brief -- Commonwealth Edison Co.: Utility Reports Net Loss, Spurred by Court Charges

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

Commonwealth Edison Co., Chicago, hurt by special charges associated with two recent adverse court rulings, reported a second-quarter net loss of $378.4 million, or $1.88 a share. In the year-ago period, the utility had net income of $152.6 million, or 61 cents a share. Revenues, adjusted to reflect court-ordered rate cuts, fell to $846.4 million from $1.31 billion.

A rate rollback and refund, ordered by the Illinois Supreme Court in May, generated an after-tax provision of $253 million, or $1.19 a share.

Disallowances and a refund ordered in June by the Illinois Appellate Court, concerning Commonwealth's Byron 1 nuclear plant, required a charge of $208 million, or 98 cents a share, in the latest quarter.
