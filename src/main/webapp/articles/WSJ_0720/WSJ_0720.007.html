Mentor Graphics 2nd Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

BEAVERTON, Ore. -- Mentor Graphics Corp., as expected, reported a sharp drop in earnings and a dip in revenue for its second quarter. On July 3, Mentor, which makes software used by engineers, said it expected to post a disappointing quarter because of a slowdown in orders caused, in part, by competitors' fierce pricing.

Net income for the quarter was $3.6 million, down from $12.3 million in the year-ago quarter. Revenue was $101 million, down from $105.8 million.
