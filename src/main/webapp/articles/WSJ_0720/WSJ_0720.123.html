Business Brief -- Onex Corp.: Beatrice Foods Inc. Agrees To Acquire Palm Dairies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

Onex Corp. said its Beatrice Foods Inc. unit agreed to acquire Palm Dairies Ltd., a Calgary, Alberta, dairy, baked goods and cheese-making concern. Terms weren't disclosed, but Toronto-based Onex said the operations of Palm Dairies will add about 130 million Canadian dollars (US$112.6 million) annually to Beatrice's total sales. In 1989, Beatrice reported revenue of C$672.1 million.

Last year, Beatrice paid C$40 million to acquire Eplett Dairies Ltd. Gerry Schwartz, Onex's chairman and chief executive officer, said this year that the company was planning another dairy acquisition of similar size. He said Onex had about C$110 million available in cash to finance acquisitions.

Beatrice plans to acquire Palm Dairies Ltd. from closely held Pocklington Financial Corp., Onex said.

The acquisition "strengthens our presence in Western Canada and represents an important opportunity for Beatrice to broaden its distribution and processing base," said Don McCarthy, chairman of Beatrice.

Palm Dairies has operations in Alberta, Saskatchewan and Northern Ontario.
