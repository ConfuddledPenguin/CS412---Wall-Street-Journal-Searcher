Recent SEC Filings

  • Date: 07/20/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Business Insurance Corp., initial offering of 1.8 million common shares, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. and Alex. Brown & Sons Inc.

Dialogue Inc., formerly Bluestone Capital Corp., offering of $4,025,000 in units consisting of one common share and one common stock purchase warrant.

Keystone Consolidated Industries Inc., offering of 1.5 million common shares, via Oppenheimer & Co., Inc. and Balis & Zorn Inc.

Queensland Treasury Corp., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.

Salomon Inc., shelf offering of $250 million of series A notes, via Salomon Brothers Inc. and J.W. Korth & Co.
