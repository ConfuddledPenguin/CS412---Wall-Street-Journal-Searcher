Initial State Jobless Claims Rose in Week Ended July 7

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits rose to a seasonally adjusted 361,000 in the week ended July 7 from 350,000 in the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended June 30 fell to a seasonally adjusted 2,429,000, or 2.3% of those covered by unemployment insurance, from 2,440,000 in the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate also was 2.3%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended June 30 fell to 2,135,600 from 2,233,400 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
