Dividend News: SANDWHICH CO-OPERATIVE BANK

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

SANDWICH CO-OPERATIVE BANK, Sandwich, Mass., reduced its quarterly dividend to five cents a share from 15 cents. The new dividend is payable Aug. 14 to stock of record July 31. Sandwich said that "given the current real estate market and economic forecast, it is prudent to conserve capital." The bank said that its capital-to-assets ratio is a strong 13.4%.
