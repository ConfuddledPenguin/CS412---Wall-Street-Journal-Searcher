Caesars World Tender Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

LOS ANGELES -- Caesars World Inc. said it will make a tender offer for the 13.4% stake it doesn't already hold in Caesars New Jersey Inc. for $22 a share, or $48 million. Caesars World owns and operates hotels and casinos. Caesars New Jersey owns and operates Caesars Atlantic City, a hotel and casino.

Caesars World said it will make an offer next week for about 2.2 million shares. It said it received a positive fairness opinion for its financial adviser Wasserstein Perella & Co.

A spokesman said Caesars New Jersey was set up by Caesars World in 1978 as a unit. Later that year the company offered units and warrants in Caesars New Jersey to shareholders, retaining about 80%. In 1981, the company bought back enough shares to increase its stake to its current level of 86.6%.
