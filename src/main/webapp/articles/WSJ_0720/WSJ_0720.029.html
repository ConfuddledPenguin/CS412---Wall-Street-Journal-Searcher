Vencor Acquires Denver Hospital

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Vencor Inc. said it has signed an agreement to lease Doctors Hospital in Denver. Terms weren't disclosed. The acquisition marks the tenth facility in Vencor's network of hospitals and expands the company's operations into a seventh state. Vencor provides long-term hospital care for patients with catastrophic illnesses.
