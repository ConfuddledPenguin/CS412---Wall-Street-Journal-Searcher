CBOT Plans New Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

CHICAGO -- The Chicago Board of Trade said it plans to develop reinsurance futures contracts based on homeowners physical damage and commercial property damage policies. The planned contracts are in addition to previously proposed auto and health reinsurance futures contracts.

The exchange said the proposed reinsurance futures contracts are designed to allow insurers to hedge against losses on policies they write in these areas.
