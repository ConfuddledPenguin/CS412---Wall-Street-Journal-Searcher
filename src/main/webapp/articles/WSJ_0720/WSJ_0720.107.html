Canadian Steel Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 251,936 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, a 12% increase from 224,171 tons the previous week but a 17% decline from 302,523 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. From Jan. 1 through last Saturday, production totaled 7,745,084 tons, a 9.8% decline from 8,581,850 tons a year earlier.
