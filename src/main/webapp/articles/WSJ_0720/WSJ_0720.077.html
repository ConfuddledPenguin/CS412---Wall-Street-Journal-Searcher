Big U.S. Auto Makers Planned Output Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

The ten major U.S. auto makers planned to build 91,409 cars this week, up 10.1% from 83,027 manufactured a year earlier. Ford Motor Co. said it has scheduled daily overtime at its car assembly plants in Wayne and Wixom, Mich. The No. 2 auto maker has also planned overtime at all but two of its seven U.S. truck plants.
