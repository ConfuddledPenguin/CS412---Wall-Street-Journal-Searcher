Business Brief -- First Federal Bancorp: Court Round Lost in Fight Against U.S. Move on Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

PONTIAC, Mich. -- A federal district court denied First Federal Bancorp's request for a preliminary injunction that would prevent federal regulators from appointing a conservator or receiver for a First Federal subsidiary. However, the court continued the temporary restraining order in effect against the federal Office of Thrift Supervision until July 27, to give the First Federal officials time to appeal.

The bank said it intends to file such an appeal.

In May, federal authorities placed strict limits on the lending operations of two First Federal units, Citizens Federal Savings Bank, of Jacksonville, Fla., and First Federal Savings Bank & Trust Co.
