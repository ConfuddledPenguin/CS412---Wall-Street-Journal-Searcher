Lewis Galoob Copyright Suit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

SOUTH SAN FRANCISCO -- Lewis Galoob Toys Inc. said a federal appeals court granted its motion for an expedited appeal of a preliminary injunction that two weeks ago halted sales of its Game Genie videogame toy. Nintendo of America Inc., a unit of Japan's Nintendo Co., sued Galoob in June, complaining that the genie game infringes on a Nintendo copyright and that it makes Nintendo games too easy to play. Nintendo, which recently has sued other companies for alleged copyright infringements, worries that its customers will get bored with its games if they are too easy to play.
