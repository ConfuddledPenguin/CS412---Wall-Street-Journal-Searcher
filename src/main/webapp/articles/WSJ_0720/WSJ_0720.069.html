Who's News: Clorox Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

CLOROX Co. (Oakland, Calif.) -- Robert A. Bolingbroke, 52 years old, was elected president, chief operating officer and a director of this household-products concern. Mr. Bolingbroke, who has worked with Clorox for 28 years, succeeds J.W. Collins, who is retiring.
