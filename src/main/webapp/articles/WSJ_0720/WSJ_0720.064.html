Airbus Gets Order for 3 Jets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

PARIS -- Airbus Industrie, the European aircraft consortium, said it received firm orders from the Romanian flag carrier Transporturile Aeriene Romane, or Tarom, for three A-310-300 jets, with deliveries scheduled from March 1992 to February 1993. Terms weren't disclosed but the price of one of the airliners is estimated at $60 million. Each plane will be equipped to seat 209 passengers in a two-class layout, and will be powered by PW4156A engines made by the Pratt & Whitney unit of United Technologies Corp., Hartford, Conn.
