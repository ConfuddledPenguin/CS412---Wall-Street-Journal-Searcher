Dutch Industrial Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

AMSTERDAM -- Dutch industrial production in May declined 1% from April, the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics said. But in the first five months of the year, output grew 5% from a year earlier, when the year-to-year increase was 4%. The latest five-month pace was led by the electrical engineering, rubber and synthetics, and metals industries.
