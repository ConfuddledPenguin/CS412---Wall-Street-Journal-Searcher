Who's News: Maybelline Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

MAYBELLINE Inc. (Memphis, Tenn.) -- Bob Pierce, formerly a senior vice president with Noxell Corp., was named to the newly created post of senior vice president and chief financial officer. Mr. Pierce will be responsible for overseeing all information services, treasury, tax, and financial planning functions at Maybelline. Maybelline, the nation's No. 2 maker of mass-market cosmetics, was sold July 1 by Schering-Plough Corp. to Wasserstein Perella Partners L.P.
