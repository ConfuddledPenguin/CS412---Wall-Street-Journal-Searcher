International Brief -- Volkswagen AG: First-Half Profit Exceeded Year-Earlier Record Level

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

Volkswagen AG Chairman Carl Hahn said the West German auto maker's first-half earnings slightly exceeded the record year-earlier profit of 403 million marks ($244.5 million), but he didn't provide specifics. At the annual meeting, Mr. Hahn also said sales of the Volkswagen, Audi and SEAT brands in the six months all surpassed levels in the 1989 first half.

Volkswagen's first-quarter profit rose 5.5% to 191 million marks. Net income for 1989 climbed 33% to 1.04 billion marks.

Mr. Hahn said that world-wide sales in the 1990 first half rose 1.5% to 1.55 million vehicles, and that the order backlog is "unusually large."
