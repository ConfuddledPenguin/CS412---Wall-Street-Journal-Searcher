H.F. Ahmanson Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

LOS ANGELES -- H.F. Ahmanson & Co. said it sold its downtown headquarters here for a pre-tax gain of $12 million. Ahmanson, which owns Home Savings of America, sold the 24-story building to a partnership made up of Kawasaki Enterprises Inc. of Japan and Takenaka & Co. of Los Angeles.
