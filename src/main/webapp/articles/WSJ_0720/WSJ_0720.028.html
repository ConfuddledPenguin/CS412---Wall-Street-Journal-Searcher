Who's News: Central & South West Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

CENTRAL & SOUTH WEST Corp. (Dallas) -- Harry D. Mattison was promoted to the new post of executive vice president and chief operating officer, effective Sept. 1. Mr. Mattison, 53 years old, is currently president and chief executive officer of Southwestern Electric Power Co., a Central & South West operating unit. Richard H. Bremer, 42, vice president of operations for Southwestern Electric Power, will succeed Mr. Mattison. Glenn D. Rosilier, 42, vice president of finance for the parent, will be promoted to senior vice president and chief financial officer, effective Sept. 1. The post has been vacant for three years. Ferd. C. Meyer Jr., 50, vice president and general counsel, was promoted to senior vice president and general counsel. Thomas V. Shockley III, 45, senior vice president at the parent, will be promoted to executive vice president.
