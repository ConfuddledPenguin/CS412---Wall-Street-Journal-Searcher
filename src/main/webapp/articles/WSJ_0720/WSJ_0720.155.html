Business Brief -- Occidental Petroleum Corp.: Profit Increased 11% as Sales Edged Up in 2nd Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

Despite a small operating loss in its oil and gas division, Occidental Petroleum Corp.'s second-quarter net income rose 11% to $89 million, or 30 cents a share. In the year earlier, Los Angeles-based Occidental had net of $80 million, or 29 cents a share. Sales edged up to $5.12 billion from $5.05 billion.

Occidental's income before extraordinary items fell 18% to $69 million. The extraordinary items included a $20 million gain from tax-loss carry-forwards. Profit was bolstered further by a $27 million benefit from the reversal of certain tax reserves. The reversals resulted from an Internal Revenue Service audit of Occidental and certain subsidiaries that determined the reserves are no longer necessary.
