Business Brief -- Tandem Computers Inc.: Stock Price Drops on News Of June Quarter's Results

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

Tandem Computers Inc.'s stock skidded on disappointing results for its third quarter, ended June 30. The computer maker's stock fell $1.875 to close at $19.625 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading. Tandem, based in Cupertino, Calif., reported $472 million in revenue, an increase of 12% from the year-earlier period but below analysts' expectations. Net income also was below expectations, totaling $32.4 million, or 30 cents a share, up 5.5% from $30.8 million, or 31 cents a share, the year before. There were more shares outstanding in this year's quarter.

John B. Jones, an analyst with Montgomery Securities Inc., said Tandem's sales were hurt by soft business in the United Kingdom and relatively weak demand for its highly reliable mainframe and minicomputers among the securities industry and government, two traditional big sectors for the company.
