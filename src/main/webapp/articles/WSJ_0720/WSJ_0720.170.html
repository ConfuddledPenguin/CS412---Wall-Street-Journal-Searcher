Business Brief -- Chrysler Corp.: Concern Clarifies Comments Made by Official Wednesday

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

Chrysler Corp., Highland Park, Mich., clarifying comments made Wednesday by a top executive, said that it expects to post second-quarter results in line with analysts' expectations, and that it expects "a substantial loss in the third quarter." In comments at a press luncheon, Robert A. Lutz, president of the company's Chrysler Motors unit, had said analysts expecting the company to report earnings of about 78 cents a share for the second quarter would be "pleasantly surprised." Chrysler expects to release its second-quarter results by the end of this month.

Mr. Lutz also said the third quarter won't be "a terrible bath" for the No. 3 U.S. auto maker. Chrysler will lose production of its profitable minivans in the third quarter because plants building the vehicles will close temporarily to retool for the redesigned 1991 models.
