American President's Earnings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

OAKLAND, Calif. -- American President Cos. said it earned $1.5 million in the second quarter, a 74% decline from $5.7 million the year earlier. The company, which provides container distribution and transport services, said it lost one cent a share for the quarter, after reflecting the preferred stock dividend. In the year-earlier period, the company earned 14 cents a share.

Revenue for the quarter fell slightly to $511 million from $530 million, in part because of a decline in the volume of the company's international business.
