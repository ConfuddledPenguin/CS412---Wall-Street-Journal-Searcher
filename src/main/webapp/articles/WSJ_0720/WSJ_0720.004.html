Britain's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

LONDON -- Unemployment in Britain rose to a provisional, seasonally adjusted 5.7% of the work force in June, unchanged from May, the Department of Employment estimated. Without seasonal adjustment, unemployment was 5.5%, unchanged from May but down from 6.1% in June 1989. The number of jobless people on this basis was 1,555,610 in June, compared with 1,578,527 in May and 1,743,141 in June 1989.
