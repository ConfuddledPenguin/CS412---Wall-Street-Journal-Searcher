Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

LONG ISLAND LIGHTING Co. (Hicksville, N.Y.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it raised its ratings on $4.3 billion of securities of this utility. The rating concern cited a rate settlement, a financial restructuring and the removal of potentially substantial liabilities related to charges filed against Lilco under anti-racketeering statutes. Moody's upgraded its ratings on Lilco's first mortgage and general and refunding bonds to Baa-3 from Ba-1; debentures and unsecured pollution-control bonds to Ba-1 from Ba-2; and preferred stock to Ba-2 from Ba-3.
