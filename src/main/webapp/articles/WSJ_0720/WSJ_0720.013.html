Corning to Post Gain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

CORNING, N.Y. -- Corning Inc. said it would post a one-time, $18 million after-tax gain in the third quarter on the sale of most of its 33% interest in a Japanese glass company. Corning earned $75.9 million in the year-ago quarter.

Corning said the sale of its investment in Iwaki Glass Co. of Japan to Asahi Glass Co. would release funds for new investments.

Corning is a telecommunications, specialty glass, ceramic products and laboratory services concern.
