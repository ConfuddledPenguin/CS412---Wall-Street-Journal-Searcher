Business Brief -- Glenfed Inc.: Company Reports 16% Drop In Net for 4th Fiscal Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

Glenfed Inc., the Glendale, Calif.-based parent of Glendale Federal Bank, reported a 16% drop in net income for its fiscal fourth quarter ended June 30. In the latest quarter, net was $24.4 million, or 77 cents a share, compared with year-earlier results of $29.2 million, or 98 cents per share.

Loan volume for the quarter was $1.57 billion, slightly below the year-ago period. The ratio of nonperforming loans to total assets improved slightly, declining to 1.76% percent from 1.84% at the end of March. But restructured loans rose to 0.37% of total assets, from 0.35% at the end of March.

For the fiscal year, net rose marginally, rising to $117.3 million, compared with $116 million the year before. However, per-share earnings for the year were lower, at $3.79 in the latest year compared with $3.92 the year before.
