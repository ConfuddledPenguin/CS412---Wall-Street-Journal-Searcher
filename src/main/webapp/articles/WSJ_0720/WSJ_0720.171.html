Business Brief -- Societe Bic S.A.: French Firm Says Revenue For First Half Climbed 3%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

Societe Bic S.A. of France, a maker of disposable pens and lighters, said its first-half consolidated revenue climbed 3% to 2.9 billion francs ($524.5 million) from 2.82 billion francs a year earlier. Sales by Bic's main operating branch would have been up 7%, after adjustment for changes in exchange rates and group structure, rather than 3%, suggesting that the firm tone of the franc against the dollar held back sales growth during the period.

Bic didn't announce first-half earnings data. In 1989, it earned 403 million francs after payments to minority interests, on revenue of 5.35 billion francs.
