Business Brief -- First Financial Management Corp.: Agreement Reached to Buy Dun & Bradstreet's Zytron

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

First Financial Management Corp., Atlanta, said it signed a definitive agreement to buy Zytron Corp. from Dun & Bradstreet Corp. for $82.5 million. The financial-services company said Zytron, a Menlo Park, Calif., company that provides data-imaging products and services, had 1989 revenue of about $75 million. Among other things, Zytron offers laser printing services and specialized printing and mail services.

In April, Dun & Bradstreet, a New York-based information-services company, said it wanted to sell Zytron, among other companies, to sharpen its focus on certain information markets.
