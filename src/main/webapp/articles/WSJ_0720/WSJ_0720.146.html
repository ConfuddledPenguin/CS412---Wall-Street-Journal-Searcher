Business Brief -- Bamberger Polymers Inc.: Restraining Order Prevents Ex-Employees' Competition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

Bamberger Polymers Inc. said it secured a temporary restraining order against 11 former sales and marketing employees, who, it said, are starting a competing plastics business. The New Hyde Park, N.Y., plastics merchant has said loss of the employees would have a "material adverse effect" on its sales and earnings from the Northeast and West Coast operations in the third quarter.

The order enjoins the former employees from soliciting Bamberger employees, contacting its customers, or using confidential information obtained from Bamberger.

Walter Craig, legal counsel for Helm Resources Inc., a holding company that controls about half of Bamberger, said the defecting workers are "engaged in basically the same business they were involved in, the trading and sale of thermoplastic resins."

The company also is seeking damages against the former employees. A request for a preliminary injunction will be considered Tuesday.

None of the defendants could be reached for comment.
