Humana Refund to Lift Net

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Humana Inc. said a tax refund will boost net income for its fourth quarter ending Aug. 31 by $19 million, or 18 cents a share. The health-care company had year-earlier profit of $66.7 million, or 66 cents a share. Humana said it received about $68 million in settlement of all contested issues with the Internal Revenue Service through 1986. The payment consisted of a refund of about $31 million in federal income tax and $37 million in interest.
