Grumman Gets Navy Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

WASHINGTON -- Grumman Corp. was given a $115 million Navy contract for EA6B aircraft electronics equipment.

Cubic Defense Systems, a unit of Cubic Corp., received a $35.3 million Army contract for training-range instruments.

Pan Am World Services Inc., a unit of Pan Am Corp., got a $29.5 million Army contract for signal support services.
