Japan's Wholesale Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

TOKYO -- Japan's wholesale prices in the first 10 days of July edged up 0.1% from the previous 10 days and rose 1% from a year earlier, the Bank of Japan said. The wholesale price index, in which the year 1985 is equal to 100, stood at 90.6. The price gains in the latest period followed a drop in June of 0.1% from May and a 0.9% increase from a year earlier.
