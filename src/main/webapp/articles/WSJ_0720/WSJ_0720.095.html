Who's News: Old Stone Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

OLD STONE Corp. (Providence, R.I.) -- Edward J. Braks was named chief financial officer and an executive vice president of this thrift holding company. Mr. Braks is currently a partner in the accounting firm Deloitte & Touche. Mr. Braks succeeds Thomas F. Hogg, who will remain as treasurer and an executive vice president.
