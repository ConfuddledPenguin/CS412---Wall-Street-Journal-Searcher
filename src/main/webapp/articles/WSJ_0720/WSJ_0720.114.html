Business Brief -- Legg Mason Inc.: Net Rose 4% in 1st Period On 1.2% Revenue Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

Legg Mason Inc., propped by gains in its investment advisory business, reported a 4% rise in fiscal first-quarter profit. The regional brokerage firm, based in Baltimore, said net income for the quarter ended June 30 rose to $3.2 million, or 37 cents a share, up from $3.1 million, or 36 cents a share, a year earlier.

Revenue for the period rose 1.2% to $60.1 million from $59.4 million. Investment advisory revenue rose 19% as assets under management rose to more than $8.5 billion, offsetting a drop in commission revenue.
