Who's News: Philip Morris Cos.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

PHILIP MORRIS Cos. (New York) -- Charles R. Wall was named vice president and associate general counsel, a new post, at this tobacco, food, brewing and financial-services company. Mr. Wall, 45 years old, was previously a senior partner in the law firm of Shook, Hardy and Bacon, based in Kansas City, Mo.
