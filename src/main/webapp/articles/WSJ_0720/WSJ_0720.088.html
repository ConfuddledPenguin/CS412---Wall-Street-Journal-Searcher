CXR French Unit's Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- CXR Corp. said its French subsidiary, CXR S.A., completed its previously reported acquisition of the LEA business unit of Minnesota Mining & Mfg. Co.'s French unit. Terms of the deal weren't disclosed.

CXR makes data-communications equipment. LEA markets telecommunications test equipment and has annual revenue of about $7 million.
