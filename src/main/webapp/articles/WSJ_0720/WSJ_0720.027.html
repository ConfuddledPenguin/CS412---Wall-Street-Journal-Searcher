Who's News: Fleet/Norstar Financial Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/20/90

FLEET/NORSTAR FINANCIAL Group (Providence, R.I.) -- Robert J. Higgins, 44 years old, was named chairman of this financial concern's Fleet Bank unit, succeeding Terrence Murray, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer of Fleet/Norstar. Mr. Higgins remains chief executive officer of the bank. Thomas J. Skala, 46 years old, was named president of Fleet Bank, succeeding Mr. Higgins. Mr. Skala was previously senior executive vice president of the bank.
