Morrison Knudsen Rail Bid

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/20/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it formally notified a Texas state agency that it plans to compete for a contract to build a proposed $2.2 billion high-speed rail link to carry passengers between Houston and Dallas in just one hour and 45 minutes. Morrison Knudsen is heading a consortium hoping to win the job of building the rail link, which ultimately could be expanded to include other Texas cities. As reported, the company's proposal faces many hurdles, including an expected competing application from a West German group.
