Treasury Plans to Sell $16.4 Billion of Bills

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to pay down about $9.25 billion on the federal debt with the sale Monday of about $16.4 billion in short-term bills. Maturing bills outstanding total $25.64 billion. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills, which will be dated April 26 and will mature on July 26 and Oct. 25, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
