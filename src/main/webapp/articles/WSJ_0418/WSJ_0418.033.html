Louisiana-Pacific Profit Falls

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Louisiana-Pacific Corp. said a construction slowdown and poor weather in its Southern operating regions drove down first-quarter profit about 10% to $35.8 million, or 95 cents a share. The forest-products concern earned $39.9 million, or $1.05 a share, in the year-earlier period. Revenue grew about 2% to $474.8 million from $467 million. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Louisiana-Pacific closed at $42.875, down 75 cents.
