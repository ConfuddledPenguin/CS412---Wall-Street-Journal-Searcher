Who's News: RCM Technologies Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

RCM TECHNOLOGIES Inc. (Los Angeles) -- Sylvan E. Leinwand, a consultant, was appointed a director of this provider of engineering and technical services. He succeeds William Acton, who resigned from the board in January. Mr. Acton had also resigned as president, chief operating officer and chief financial officer.
