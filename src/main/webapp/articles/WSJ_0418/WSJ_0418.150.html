Business Brief -- Pirelli Tyre Holding N.V.: Dutch Tire Maker Reports 7.8% Increase in 1989 Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

Pirelli Tyre Holding N.V., a Dutch tire maker, said its 1989 profit rose 7.8% to 206 million guilders ($108.9 million) from 191 million guilders a year earlier. Sales rose 14% to 6.7 billion guilders from 5.9 billion guilders.

Pirelli said it expects a more modest growth rate in sales this year, foreseeing "marginal growth of the global tire market and sustained growth in the premium car and all-steel truck tire segments. Given {Pirelli's} strong and increasing presence in those segments, the company is confident that a sales growth of well over 5% can be achieved."

Pirelli said that because of increased price competition, it expects its 1990 profit to be about the same as that for 1989.

The company will propose a cash dividend of two guilders a share, or a total payout of 93 million guilders, leaving Pirelli with 113.4 million guilders in profit to be retained.
