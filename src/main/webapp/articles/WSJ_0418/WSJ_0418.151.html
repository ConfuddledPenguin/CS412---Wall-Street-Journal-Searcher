Marketing Brief -- MCA Inc.: Geffen Records Is Acquired With $500 Million in Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

MCA Inc., Universal City, Calif., completed the acquisition of Geffen Records for stock valued at nearly $500 million. The entertainment concern issued to music entrepreneur David Geffen about 921,900 new Series B convertible preferred shares, which will pay dividends equal to 10 times the rate of the common dividend, but will carry only one vote a share.

Mr. Geffen also has signed a long-term employment agreement with MCA to provide for his exclusive services in the music industry.
