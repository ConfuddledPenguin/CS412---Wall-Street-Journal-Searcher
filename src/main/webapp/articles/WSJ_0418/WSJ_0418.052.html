Pic `N' Save Sales Rise 62%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

DOMINGUEZ, Calif. -- Pic `N' Save Corp., the target of a potential takeover, said its sales for the two weeks before Easter rose 62%, to $24 million, boosting year-to-date sales to $102.9 million. Last year, in the 11 weeks ending April 15, the company had sales of $84 million. Pic `N' Save buys manufacturers' close-out merchandise and sells it at deep discounts through 189 retail stores.

A partnership controlled by David H. Batchelder, a former strategist for T. Boone Pickens and an adviser to Marvin Davis, disclosed Thursday that it holds a 6.6% stake in the company and plans to make a bid to acquire it.
