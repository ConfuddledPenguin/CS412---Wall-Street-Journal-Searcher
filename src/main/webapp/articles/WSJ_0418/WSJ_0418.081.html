Who's News: UJB Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

UJB FINANCIAL Corp. (Princeton, N.J.) -- Fred M. Moses was named president and chief executive officer of United Jersey Bank, the largest member bank of this $12 billion financial services company. He succeeds Clifford H. Coyman, 62 years old, who retired after 25 years with the bank. Mr. Moses, 55, previously was a senior executive vice president for the bank.
