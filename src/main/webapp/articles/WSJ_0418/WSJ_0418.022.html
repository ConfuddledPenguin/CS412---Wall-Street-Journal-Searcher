Polaris 1990 Earnings Estimate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Polaris Industries Limited Partnership said it expects to generate 1990 net income of $31 million, compared with $26.2 million for 1989. The snowmobile maker didn't estimate per-share net income. Net income for 1989 was equal to $3.24 a share.

Polaris said it expects to generate sales for 1990 of $275 million, compared with $242.6 million for 1989.

The company said snowmobile inventory is at very low levels.
