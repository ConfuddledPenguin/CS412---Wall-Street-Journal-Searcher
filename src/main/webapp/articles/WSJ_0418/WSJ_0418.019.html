National Intergroup Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

PITTSBURGH -- National Intergroup Inc. said it has withdrawn its proposal to buy the units outstanding of Permian Partners Limited Partnership it doesn't already own. National Intergroup said independent directors of the partnership advised the company that they couldn't recommend the offer. The company had offered $4 a unit. A subsidiary of National Intergroup is the general partner of Permian Partners.

National Intergroup owns a 44.7% interest in Permian Partners, a limited partnership involved in oil and gas gathering and marketing.
