Turner Corp. Wins Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

NEW YORK -- Turner Corp. said it was awarded a contract for Two International Place, a $95 million construction project in Boston for Chiofaro Co., a Boston developer. Construction began last month; completion is scheduled for early 1992. When the 35-story complex is finished, it will be one of Boston's largest office projects, at 1.8 million square feet, Turner Corp. said.
