Who's News: American Stock Exchange

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

AMERICAN STOCK EXCHANGE (New York) -- Joel L. Lovett, president and director of Jacee Securities Inc., was elected to a three-year term as vice chairman of the exchange's board of governors. Mr. Lovett, 51 years old, succeeds Jeffrey B. Lane, 47, president of Primerica Holdings Inc.
