International Brief -- Thomson-CSF S.A.: Company's Consolidated Net Was Down 13% Last Year

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

Thomson-CSF S.A. said its consolidated net profit after payments to minority interests eased 13% to 2.63 billion francs ($467 million) in 1989 from 2.97 billion francs a year earlier. The French state-controlled defense and electronics group said that the widely expected drop reflected weakness at its Altus Finance unit, formerly Thomson-CSF Finance. This unit, traditionally one of the group's strong points, saw its net profit fall to 1.21 billion francs in 1989 from 1.76 billion a year earlier. The group didn't elaborate on the reasons for the weaker financial results.

Thomson-CSF noted, however, that lower profit from financial activities was offset to a large extent by its major industrial units.
