Cabot Buys AO Safety

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Cabot Corp. said it completed its previously announced purchase of AO Safety, a safety products division of American Optical Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. AO Safety and American Optical are in Southbridge, Mass.

Cabot, a Waltham specialty chemicals concern, said it plans to merge AO Safety with its E-A-R noise control and hearing protection business in a subsidiary, Cabot Safety Corp. Cabot said it expects annual revenue from the combined businesses to approach $200 million.
