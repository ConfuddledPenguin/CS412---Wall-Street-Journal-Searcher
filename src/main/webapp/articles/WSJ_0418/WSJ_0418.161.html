Tenneco Inc. Unit Sells Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

HOUSTON -- Tenneco Inc. said its JI Case unit sold its 50% interest in Intertractor, a West German maker of undercarriage parts for construction equipment, to an affiliate of Deutsche Bank AG. Tenneco, whose interests include farm equipment, shipbuilding and a natural gas pipeline, wouldn't disclose the terms.

Tenneco said Intertractor had 1989 sales of about $210 million.
