Meredith to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

DES MOINES, Iowa -- Meredith Corp. said that it agreed in principle to sell its MMT Sales Inc. unit to a management group. The publishing and broadcasting concern said terms are being negotiated.

MMT is a New York-based advertising sales concern that represents 30 television stations, including Meredith-owned stations.
