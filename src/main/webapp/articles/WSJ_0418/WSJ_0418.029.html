Who's News: Northern Trust Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

NORTHERN TRUST Corp. (Chicago) -- David W. Fox, president and chief executive officer, was also elected chairman of this bank holding company. Mr. Fox, 58 years old, succeeds Weston Christopherson, 64, who had announced in December his intention to retire. His retirement decreases the number of directors by one, to 14. The company also said that Robert F. Reusche, the 62-year-old vice chairman and head of trust and financial services, will retire June 30. Those operations will report directly to the chairman.
