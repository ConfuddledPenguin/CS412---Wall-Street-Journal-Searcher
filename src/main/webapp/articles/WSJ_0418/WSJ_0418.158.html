Business Brief -- AST Research Inc.: Earnings Climbed to Record In the Fiscal Third Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

Brisk sales and improved profit margins helped AST Research Inc. to fiscal third-quarter profit of $9.6 million, or 78 cents a share. It was a record high for any quarter. The Irvine, Calif., personal computer maker posted a loss of $1.3 million, or 11 cents a share, in the year-earlier period.

Sales for the quarter ended March 30 surged 21% to a record $137.2 million from $113.8 million.

For the nine-month period, AST reported profit of $19.9 million, or $1.65 a share, compared with a loss of $9.9 million, or 86 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales grew 15% to $382 million from $333.1 million.

AST said its results were aided by gross profit margins that surged to 33% in the quarter. The company said the improved margins were the result of brisk sales of its high-end computers powered by Intel Corp.'s 386 microprocessor, and by a continued drop in the cost of parts for those systems.
