Dividend Brief -- Charter Federal Savings Bank: Firm Increases Its Dividend 20% to 15 Cents a Share

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

Charter Federal Savings Bank, citing confidence in future earnings, increased its quarterly dividend 20% to 15 cents a share from 12.5 cents. The Randolph, N.J., bank also reported that earnings for the second quarter ended March 31 rose 11% to $855,581, or 62 cents a share, from $770,484, or 55 cents a share, a year earlier. A bank spokesman cited "improved core earnings," its large number of adjustable-rate mortgages, and few nonperforming loans.

In the same period, Charter Federal reported an increase of 4% in assets and 3.9% in deposits. As of March 31, Charter Federal had assets of $286 million.
