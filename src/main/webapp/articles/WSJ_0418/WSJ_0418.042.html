Newport Pharmaceuticals Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

LAGUNA HILLS, Calif. -- Newport Pharmaceuticals International Inc. said it completed an exchange offer that will result in a noncash charge of about $1.3 million in the second quarter. Newport said about $6.5 million face amount of its 10% senior secured convertible notes due 2002 were tendered in response to the offer, representing more than 72% of the notes outstanding.
