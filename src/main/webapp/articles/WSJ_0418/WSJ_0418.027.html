Who's News: B.F. Goodrich Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

B.F. GOODRICH Co. (Akron, Ohio) -- David L. Burner, president of BFGoodrich Aerospace, will assume the additional post of senior vice president of this plastics, specialty chemical and aerospace products concern. William L. Wallace, executive vice president and chief operating officer of Dofasco Inc., was elected a director, which keeps the membership at 13.
