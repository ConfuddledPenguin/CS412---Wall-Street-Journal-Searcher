Peerless Oil Gets Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

WASHINGTON -- Peerless Oil & Chemicals Inc. was given a $60 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

General Dynamics Corp. received a $27.1 million Air Force contract for F-16 aircraft engineering services.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. got a $22.6 million Army contract for helicopter support.

Olin Defense Systems Group, a unit of Olin Corp., received a $13.4 million Army contract for operation of an ammunition plant.
