Who's News: L.B. Foster President Plans to Resign May 8

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

PITTSBURGH -- L.B. Foster Co., a maker and distributor of construction, rail and tubular products, said Joseph H. Dugan will resign as president and chief executive officer following the annual meeting, scheduled for May 8. Mr. Dugan, who won't stand for re-election as a director at the meeting, said L.B. Foster "is in a strong position in its core business, and I can return to my consulting practice."

The company said a successor won't be named until after the annual meeting.

Mr. Dugan joined L.B. Foster in 1986 as executive vice president and chief operating officer, and was elected to his current posts in October 1987.
