Philippines Is Failing on Targets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

MANILA -- The Philippines is failing to meet fiscal targets prescribed by the International Monetary Fund covering the government sector in exchange for new loans, Finance Secretary Jesus Estanislao said. The official said the national government deficit for 1990 is heading toward 24 billion pesos, ($1.08 billion) wider than the target of 17 billion pesos.

Consolidated government sector deficit, originally set at 33.4 billion pesos, is moving toward 55.2 billion pesos, while government sector borrowing has exceeded the ceiling of 28.4 billion pesos and is approaching 43.4 billion pesos.
