Business Brief -- American Home Products Corp.: Net Income Increased 12% In Quarter; Sales Up 3.8%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

American Home Products Corp. said net income rose 12% to $314.5 million in the first quarter, or $2.01 a share, from year-earlier net of $280.1 million, or $1.81 a share. Sales rose 3.8% to $1.81 billion from $1.75 billion. The New York drug company said sales at its Health Care Products unit increased 3% in the quarter, while its Food & Household Products unit posted an 8% rise in sales from a year earlier.

Net was also helped by a lower tax rate resulting from the company's merger in 1989 with A.H. Robins Co. and use of its tax-loss carry-forwards related to the funding of the Dalkon Shield Trusts. In the quarter, federal and foreign taxes fell 20% to $104 million.

In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, American Home rose 62.5 cents to close at $104 a share.
