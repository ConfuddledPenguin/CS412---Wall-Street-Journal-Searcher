Members Service Corp.'s Plans

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

HOUSTON -- Members Service Corp., which has interests in telecommunications and entered the energy business last year, said it plans to buy $250 million of oil and gas properties in the next three years. A spokesman said Members Service wants to acquire only producing oil and gas wells.

Members Service also said it began talks with two companies that hold 45% of the shares outstanding of Kleer-Vu Industries Inc., a Brownsville, Tenn., consumer products concern. In January, Members Service said it discontinued merger talks with Kleer-Vu.
