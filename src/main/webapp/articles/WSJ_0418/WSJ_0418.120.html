Business Brief -- Warner-Lambert Co.: First-Quarter Net Up 19% And Sales Increased 7.9%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

Warner-Lambert Co. said first-quarter net grew 19% to $120.3 million, or $1.79 a share, from $101.1 million, or $1.49 a share. Sales rose 7.9% to $1.1 billion from $1.02 billion. Joseph D. Williams, chairman and chief executive officer of the Morris Plains, N.J.-based drug company, said all the company's business segments contributed to the quarter's growth.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Warner-Lambert closed at $112.75 a share, up $3.125.
