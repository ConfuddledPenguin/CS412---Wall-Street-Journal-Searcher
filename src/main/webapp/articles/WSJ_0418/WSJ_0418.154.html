Raytheon Unit Gets U.S. Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

LEXINGTON, Mass. -- Raytheon Co. said a subsidiary has been selected by the Department of Energy to provide support services at the Nevada nuclear weapons test site and a Pacific operations site. Raytheon and the DOE will negotiate a contract for support services valued at up to $625 million over five years, with the first-year increment estimated at about $115 million, the company said.
