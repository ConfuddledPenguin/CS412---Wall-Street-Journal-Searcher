Japanese Economic Expansion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

TOKYO -- The Japanese economy continues to expand on strong corporate fixed investment and private consumption expenditures, despite the recent instability in the markets, the Bank of Japan said. In its monthly Review of Monetary and Economic Trends, the central bank said fixed investment by both manufacturers and non-manufacturers continues to be strong while private consumption, boosted by rising disposable income, remains firm.

The report comes amid fears that the roughly 27% fall of the Tokyo stock market since January, together with the yen's weakness, higher oil prices and higher interest rates, will have a large, negative impact on the Japanese economy.
