Who's News: N.V. Philips

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

N.V. PHILIPS -- William A. Enser was appointed to the newly created post of president of Philips Electronic Instruments Co., a Mahwah, N.J., unit of this Dutch electronics giant. Prior to Mr. Enser's appointment, the operations of Philips Electronic were conducted by an executive management committee, of which Mr. Enser, 45 years old, was chairman.
