Business Brief -- Saztec International: Firm Will Post a Deficit; Problem at Client Is Cited

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

Saztec International said it will post a third-quarter loss of about $450,000 because of a delay in revenue resulting from a work stoppage by one of the company's major clients. The Kansas City, Mo., information conversion concern also cited the continued investment in its information conversion express network and the elimination of its hardware division, which includes a write-off of inventory of $100,000 and a write-off of receivables of $70,000.

In the year-earlier quarter, ended March 31, Saztec earned $201,657, or six cents a share, on revenue of $5 million.

Despite the third-quarter loss, Saztec said it expects to show a profit for the fourth quarter. The company said it expects multiple new contracts to provide more than $10 million in revenue over the next 18 to 24 months and strong revenue from its utility division.
