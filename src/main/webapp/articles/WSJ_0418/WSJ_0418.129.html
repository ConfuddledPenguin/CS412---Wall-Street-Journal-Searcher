PAY MOVIE CHANNEL: TVN Entertainment Corp. launched a pay movie channel aimed at three million owners of home satellite dishes. Starting this summer, satellite owners will be able to see hit movies 24 hours a day by subscribing for $19.95 a month plus $4 per movie viewed. TVN developed the channel in a joint venture with American Telephone & Telegraph Corp.'s satellite communications unit and the U.S. unit of Japan's Uniden Corp.
