Dividend News: Sundstrand Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

SUNDSTRAND Corp. said shareholders at the annual meeting approved tripling to 150 million shares the Rockford, Ill., company's authorized common stock, which clears the way for a previously announced 2-for-1 stock split. The aerospace parts maker's directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of 27.5 cents a post-split share. The split and the dividend are both payable June 20 to stock of record June 6.
