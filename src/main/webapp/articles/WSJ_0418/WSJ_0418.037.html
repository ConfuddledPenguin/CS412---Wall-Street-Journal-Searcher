U.K. Retail Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

LONDON -- U.K. retail sales volume during March dropped 0.8% from February and rose 1.3% from a year earlier, 11 economists surveyed by AP-Dow Jones forecast on average. In February, sales volume grew 2.2% from January and 2.5% from a year earlier, based on final government data. The March figures are to be released today.
