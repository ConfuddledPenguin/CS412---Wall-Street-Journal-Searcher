Teledyne Spinoff Set for Friday

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

LOS ANGELES -- Teledyne Inc. said the spinoff of its insurance and finance subsidiaries into a new public company named Unitrin Inc. will be completed Friday. Teledyne said its shareholders will receive one share of Unitrin for each share of Teledyne owned.

Teledyne, a manufacturing concern, said it was advised by the New York Stock Exchange that its stock will trade ex-distribution on Monday. Beginning Monday, Unitrin shares will be quoted on the Nasdaq national market system using the symbol UNIT, Teledyne said.
