Manufacturers National Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

DETROIT -- Manufacturers National Corp. said it has taken a pretax charge of $6 million in the first quarter of 1990 for estimated expenses associated with the consolidation of its Illinois banks. The bank-holding company said it expects first-quarter earnings will fall compared with a year ago as a result of the charge, but the company didn't provide further details. For the first quarter of 1989, Manufacturers National reported net income of $24.8 million, or $1.64 a share. The company said it will announce on Friday earnings for the quarter ended March 31.

The Illinois banks that are to be consolidated are subsidiaries of Affiliated Banc Group, Inc., which Manufacturers National acquired in 1987.
