AmeriTrust Earnings Release

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

CLEVELAND -- AmeriTrust Corp. said it has delayed the release of its first-quarter earnings while federal bank examiners review its loan portfolio. The bank, which had planned to release earnings tomorrow, said it wanted to wait until regulators have completed their review in case loan-loss provisions must be increased. AmeriTrust said examiners haven't indicated that the firm's loan-loss reserves will need to be increased.

Amid mounting concerns over asset quality, examiners from the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency have been applying tougher lending criteria to banks throughout the country, forcing some banks to increase loan-loss provisions.
