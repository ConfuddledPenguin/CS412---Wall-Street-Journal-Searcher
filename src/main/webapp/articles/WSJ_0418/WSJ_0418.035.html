Telxon Buys Debentures

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

AKRON, Ohio -- Telxon Corp. said it completed the purchase of $10 million of its 7.5% convertible subordinated debentures, due 2012. The purchase represents about 22% of the issue outstanding, leaving about $36 million outstanding.

Telxon makes hand-held computers and computer systems.
