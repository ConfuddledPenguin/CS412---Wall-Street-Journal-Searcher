Hong Kong's Jobless Rate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

HONG KONG -- Hong Kong's jobless rate for the three months through February edged up to 1.5% from 1.2% a year earlier and 1.3% in the preceding three months. The number of unemployed people was estimated at 17,500, up slightly from 17,200 a year ago, but down from 22,000 in the three months through November.

The underemployment rate in the latest period was 0.6%, unchanged from a year earlier and off from 0.8% in the preceding three months. Underemployed people are those who work fewer than 35 hours a week and are available for more work.

The provisional unemployment rate for the first quarter was 1.6%, and the provisional underemployment level was 0.7%.
