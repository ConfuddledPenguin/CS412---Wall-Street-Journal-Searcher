M/A-Com's MAC Unit Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

BURLINGTON, Mass. -- M/A-Com Inc. said it reached an agreement to sell its MAC division to Microwave Radio Corp. of Lowell, Mass., as part of a previously announced restructuring program. Terms of the cash transaction weren't disclosed.

The maker of semiconductors and parts used in telecommunications, radar and electronic warfare said the division, which makes radio communications systems, had fiscal 1989 sales of about $19 million.

The company said it expects to complete the sale by the end of the month.

Microwave Radio makes portable microwave radios for electronic news gathering.
