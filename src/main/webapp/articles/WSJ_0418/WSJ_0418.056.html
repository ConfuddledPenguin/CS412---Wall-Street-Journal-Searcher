Average Interest Rate Rises At Citicorp Paper Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.227% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.213% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $446 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.144% to 8.235%.

Citicorp also said the average rate rose to 8.19% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.16% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $425 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.18% to 8.194%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
