Marketing Brief -- MGM/UA Communications Co.: Kerkorian Hasn't Decided On the Future of His Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

Kirk Kerkorian, who holds an 82% stake in MGM/UA Communications Co. through Tracinda Corp., said in a filing that he hasn't decided whether to retain an equity interest in MGM/UA if it is acquired by Pathe Communications Corp. The filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission followed published reports that Mr. Kerkorian intends to retain a 15% interest in MGM/UA.

"That's not true, but it's an option he has," said a spokesman for Mr. Kerkorian. MGM/UA is a Beverly Hills, Calif.-based entertainment concern.

Pathe Communications, a Los Angeles-based entertainment concern controlled by Italian financier Giancarlo Parretti, began a $20-a-share tender offer for MGM/UA last month. Pathe has said it expects the $1.25 billion offer to be completed by April 30.
