Key Centurion Sets Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

CHARLESTON, W.Va. -- Key Centurion Bancshares Inc. said it agreed to acquire Southern Bankshares Inc., Beckley, W.Va, for $60.06 a share, or $36 million. Southern Bankshares, with $300 million in assets, operates Beckley National Bank and Merchants & Miners National Bank. Key Centurion Bancshares is West Virginia's largest bank holding company with $2.4 billion in assets.

The acquisition is subject to approval by Southern Bankshares holders and regulatory agencies.
