Del Monte Agrees to Sell European Unit to Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

LONDON -- Del Monte Corp. agreed to sell its European processed foods division to an investor group consisting of the unit's management and affiliates of Charterhouse PLC, a British merchant bank, for $375 million in cash and assumed debt. The unit is part of the Del Monte foods business acquired last Jan. 9 for $1.48 billion by Del Monte management and an investor group led by Merrill Lynch & Co.

In recent months, the investor group has been selling off parts of Del Monte, partly to pay down the debt incurred in the acquisition. Hawaiian Punch was sold to Procter & Gamble Co. for $150 million, and Del Monte's Far East business, excluding the Philippines, was sold to Kikkoman Corp. for $110 million.

In the fiscal year ended last Nov. 30, the European unit earned $48 million on sales of $351 million.
