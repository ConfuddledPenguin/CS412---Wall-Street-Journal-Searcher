NEC's U.S. Unit Cuts Staff

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

BOXBOROUGH, Mass. -- NEC Technologies Inc., a subsidiary of Tokyo-based NEC Corp., said it dismissed 250 employees here and in Wood Dale, Ill., or about 12% of the work force, reflecting economies achieved in the wake of the merger last November of two NEC units to form NEC Technologies. The company markets laptop and desktop computers, printers, monitors, video games, and audio-visual electronic products.
