Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/18/90

CIRCLE K Corp. (Phoenix, Ariz.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it downgraded to Ca from Caa its rating on about $449 million of subordinated debt issued by this convenience-store chain. The rating concern said it took the action after Circle K missed an interest payment earlier this week on $125 million of senior subordinated debt. Moody's also said it expects that Circle K interest payments due in May and June on other subordinated debt issues won't be paid. In addition, Circle K's pending offer to exchange all of its subordinated debt for securities that will be significantly below the par value of the issues outstanding would result in a permanent impairment of the bonds' value, Moody's said.
