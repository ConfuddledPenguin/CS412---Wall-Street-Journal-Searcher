Who's News: Suntrust Banks Inc.'s Williams Named Chief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

ATLANTA -- SunTrust Banks Inc. said James B. Williams, 56-year-old vice chairman of the regional banking company, was elected president and chief executive officer, and Joel R. Wells Jr., 61, president, was elected chairman. The actions, taken yesterday at the company's annual meeting, were first recommended in February, when Robert Strickland, chairman and chief executive, announced his plans to retire in 1992. Mr. Strickland, 62, remains the board's executive committee chairman.

The company also said L. Phillip Humann, 44, previously executive vice president, was named senior executive vice president, banking, a new post.
