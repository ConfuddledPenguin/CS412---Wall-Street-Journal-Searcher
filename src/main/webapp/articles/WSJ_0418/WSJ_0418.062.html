Citizens Banking Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/18/90

FLINT, Mich. -- Citizens Banking Corp. said it approved a stock-repurchase program that permits management to acquire as many as 200,000 shares of the company's common stock on the open market. The company said it has 6.7 million shares outstanding. This program is similar to one approved in November 1987, under which Citizens purchased 191,700 shares at an average price of $19 a share.
