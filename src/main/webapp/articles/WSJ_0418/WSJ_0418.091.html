Crosswords

The clue was "Johnson,"

Three spaces to fill.

No problem, I thought --

That is, until

I was reminded

That time marches on:

I'd filled in "Van";

The answer was "Don."

-- Annie Komorny.

---

Varied Viewpoints

On the subject of hard-driving bosses

Quite different opinions are given,

As high marks come from business pundits,

And lower ones from those being driven.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

From the Podium

Our speaker tonight has risen to every occasion; but at his age, just not as often.

-- Robert Orben.
