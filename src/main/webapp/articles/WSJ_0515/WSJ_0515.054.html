Big O Tires Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

ENGLEWOOD, Colo. -- Big O Tires Inc. said its directors authorized it to borrow as much as $5 million so that its employee stock ownership plan can purchase more of the company's shares. The company said the approval doubles the board's previous authorization of $2.5 million; however, the company hasn't yet obtained a loan for the ESOP.

The owner and franchiser of tire stores said the loan may be used to buy stock on the open market, or from the company. The ESOP already owns 826,843 Big O shares, or about 4.7% of the company's stock.
