Copperweld Opens Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said it started up its $20 million structural-tubing-production facility in Birmingham, Ala. Copperweld said it expects the facility, which began shipping May 1, to generate about $30 million in revenue in its initial year of operation.

In addition to tubing, Copperweld produces wire, cable and rods.
