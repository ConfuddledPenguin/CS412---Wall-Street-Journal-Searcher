Pacific Telesis Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Pacific Telesis Group's Pacific Bell unit called for the redemption, effective June 22, of all the $100 million outstanding of seven-year 11 5/8% Eurobond debentures due May 8, 1992. The company said the move is a "continuing effort to reduce costs." The redemption price of the debentures will be 101% of their principal amount.
