Who's News: Airgas Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

AIRGAS Inc. (Wilmington, Del.) -- Michael T. Kennedy, chief financial officer of this natural gas distribution company, resigned, effective June 1. Mr. Kennedy, 35 years old, is leaving to form his own investment company. Britton H. Murdoch, 33-year-old vice president of corporate development, will act as interim chief financial officer while the company searches for a permanent replacement.
