REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: They're Everywhere

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

We're not much for sounding alarms about Japan buying America, but the latest incident simply can't be ignored. A recent issue of Michigan Law School's alumni publication hails alumnus and noted Japan critic Rep. Richard Gephardt (J.D. '65) for his election as House Majority Leader. The same issue reports that Nippon Life Insurance Company has given the school $1.2 million to endow a professorship in Japanese law. None dare call it conspiracy.
