Raw-Steel Production Rose 1% Last Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 1% last week to 1,960,000 tons from 1,941,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 0.1% from the 1,958,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 87.2% of its capability last week, compared with 86.4% the previous week and 88.51% a year ago.
