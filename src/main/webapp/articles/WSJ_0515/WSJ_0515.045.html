Compaq Sets Bond Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

HOUSTON -- Compaq Computer Corp. said it will redeem its $200 million outstanding of 6 1/2% convertible subordinated debentures due May 1, 2013, on June 13, for $1,059.58 per $1,000 face amount, including $7.58 in accrued interest. The computer concern said that holders can convert the debentures into stock at a price of $65 a share, or 15.38 shares per $1,000 face amount until June 13. If all the debentures are converted, Compaq would issue approximately 3.1 million new common shares.

The company had about 39.3 million common shares outstanding at Dec. 31.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Compaq closed at $112.375, up $2.
