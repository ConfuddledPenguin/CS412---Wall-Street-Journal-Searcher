Who's News: General Signal Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

GENERAL SIGNAL Corp. (Stamford, Conn.) -- Terry J. Mortimer, 45 years old, was named vice president, controller of this manufacturer of semiconductors and electrical equipment for transportation industries. Mr. Mortimer, previously director of finance and chief accountant for Apple Computer Inc., in Cupertino, Calif., succeeds J. Robert Hipps, 50, who resigned.
