International Brief -- Swiss Bank Corp.: Firm Acquires 49% Stake In Switzerland's Unigestion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

Swiss Bank Corp., Switzerland's second-largest bank, said it has a 49% stake in the financial company Unigestion SA of Geneva with the acquisition of a nonlisted stake of registered shares. Unigestion, in turn, has a 49.9% voting stake, through a subsidiary, in the medium-sized Swiss bank Banca della Svizzera Italiana.

No other details were given, pending a news conference set for today in Zurich.

The transaction continues a consolidation in the Swiss banking industry.

Analysts suggested that Unigestion had problems in absorbing the costs of obtaining its indirect controlling stake in Banca della Svizzera Italiana.

Analysts speculated that the 49% stake in Unigestion may give the big Swiss bank enough control to influence Unigestion's management.
