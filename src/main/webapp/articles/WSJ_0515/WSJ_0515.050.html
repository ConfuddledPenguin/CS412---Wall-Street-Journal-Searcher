Valhi Acquires Rest of Sybra

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

DALLAS -- Valhi Inc., a holding company controlled by the investor Harold Simmons, said it completed the purchase of the 3% of Sybra Inc. that it didn't already own in a stock swap. Sybra, which is Arby's spelled backwards, is an Atlanta-based Arby's fast-food franchise. Valhi said holders of 79,292 Sybra common shares received 1.6 Valhi shares for each Sybra share owned. Holders of options to buy 87,820 Sybra shares will receive 1.6 Valhi common shares when those options are exercised, Valhi said.
