Fab Industries' Poison Pill

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

NEW YORK -- Fab Industries Inc., a manufacturer of knitted textile fabrics and other products, adopted a poison pill -- a measure designed to make hostile takeovers prohibitively expensive. The company will issue rights to shareholders of record June 6. Under certain takeover circumstances, the rights would entitle the holders to buy shares of Fab or its buyer at half the market price.

The move isn't a response to any specific takeover effort, Fab said. At March 22, Fab had 3.3 million shares outstanding.
