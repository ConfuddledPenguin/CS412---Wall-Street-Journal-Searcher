Who's News: Caisse des Depots & Consignations

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

CAISSE DES DEPOTS & CONSIGNATIONS (Paris) -- This financial institution's New York subsidiary, CDC Capital Inc., named Gordon I. Miller Jr. executive vice president and treasurer, a new position. Mr. Miller, 43 years old, was formerly vice president, fixed-income risk management, at Chase Manhattan Bank in New York.
