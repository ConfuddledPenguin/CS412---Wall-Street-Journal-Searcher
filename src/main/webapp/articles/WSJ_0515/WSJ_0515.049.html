National Semiconductor Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

SANTA CLARA, Calif. -- National Semiconductor Corp. said it invested in Wafer-Scale Integration, a memory-chip supplier in Fremont, Calif. National also said it agreed to a manufacturing and technology exchange with Wafer-Scale for five years. As part of the deal, National, one of the largest semiconductor suppliers in the U.S., will manufacture and market some of Wafer-Scale's chips.

National didn't disclose the size of its investment in Wafer-Scale.
