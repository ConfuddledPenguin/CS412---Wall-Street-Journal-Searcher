Business Brief -- Sudbury Inc.: British Firm Boosts Interest To 9.2% of Common Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

Suter PLC of England said it raised its stake in Sudbury Inc., of Cleveland, to 9.2% of the common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Suter said it holds 1,180,000 Sudbury shares through its Longform Ltd. unit, including 160,000 shares bought from April 10 to last Thursday for $3.25 to $3.75 each.

Officials at Sudbury, which makes metal, plastic and rubber industrial parts, lubricants and equipment, couldn't immediately be reached.

Last August, Suter disclosed it held a 5.9% stake in Sudbury and may make an offer to acquire Sudbury through a tender offer, merger or "otherwise."

In the current SEC filing, Suter said that since December it has had discussions with Sudbury shareholders, including its founder. Suter is a holding company for a group of industrial and distribution businesses and property interests.
