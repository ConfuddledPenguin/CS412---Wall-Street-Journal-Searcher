Business Brief -- Hurco Cos.: Quarterly Profit Rose 42% On 14% Increase in Revenue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

Hurco Cos., citing market-share gains both in domestic and overseas markets, said net income climbed 42% in the second quarter, ended April 30, on a 14% increase in revenue. The Indianapolis maker of computer-controlled machine tool systems said net income was $2.5 million, or 45 cents a share, up from the year-earlier $1.8 million, or 40 cents a share. Per-share figures reflect an increase in average shares outstanding to 5.7 million from 4.5 million.

Revenue was $24.8 million in the latest period, up from $21.9 million.

Orders were $22.6 million in the latest period, up 13%.

For the six months, Hurco's net income rose 54% to $4.7 million, or 83 cents a share, from the year-earlier $3.1 million, or 69 cents a share on fewer shares outstanding. Six-month revenue was $47.6 million, up 18% from the year-earlier $40.3 million.
