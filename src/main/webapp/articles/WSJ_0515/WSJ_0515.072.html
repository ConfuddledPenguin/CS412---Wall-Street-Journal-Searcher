Printronix Buys Back Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Printronix Inc. said it bought back 927,142 shares of its common stock, or about 20%, between June 9, 1989 and Feb. 1, 1990, and that its board has authorized the buy-back of an additional 600,000 shares, or 16%, on the open market. The company has about 3.7 million common shares outstanding. Printronix designs, makes and markets computer printers.
