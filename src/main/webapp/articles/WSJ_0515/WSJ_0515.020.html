Scientific-Atlanta Gets Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

ATLANTA -- Scientific-Atlanta Inc. said it was selected by K Prime Partners as the system supplier for K Prime's direct-to-home satellite-television service. The initial order for Scientific-Atlanta's B-MAC satellite-transmission system exceeds $25 million, the telecommunications company said.

K Prime is a joint venture of 10 cable-TV and communications companies.
