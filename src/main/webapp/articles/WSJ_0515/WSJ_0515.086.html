Who's News: Ultimate Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

ULTIMATE Corp. (East Hanover, N.J.) -- The computer-systems marketer named Thomas F. Perry president of the newly created Systems division. Reporting to Michael J. O'Donnell, chairman and president, the 46-year-old Mr. Perry will oversee relations with Ultimate's dealers and end users of its systems. He formerly was Ultimate's senior vice president of world-wide sales operations.
