INTERACTIVE NETWORK Inc., Mountain View, Calif., has been conducting inhome tests of its interactive television system for six weeks. In Thursday's edition, the time period was misstated. AIR CANADA'S first-quarter results included 6 million Canadian dollars (US$5.1 million) net proceeds from the sale of shares in an investment and an undisclosed amount from the sale of an aircraft. Yesterday's edition incorrectly described these sales as extraordinary gains and misstated the total amount.

(See: "Air Canada Says Loss for 1st Quarter Was Flat at $11.1 Million" -- WSJ May 14, 1990)
