Who's News: Xerox Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

XEROX Corp. (Stamford, Conn.) -- Robert J. Vairo was named chairman of Crum & Forster Inc., the Basking Ridge, N.J., insurance-holding-company unit of this business-machines and financial-services concern. Mr. Vairo, 59 years old, is also president and chief executive officer of the insurance holding company. He succeeds Melvin Howard, 65, chairman of Xerox Financial Services, who retired.
