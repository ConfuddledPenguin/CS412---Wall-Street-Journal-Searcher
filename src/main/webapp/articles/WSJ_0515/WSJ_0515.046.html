News Corp. Sells TV Station

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

LOS ANGELES -- Fox Television Stations Inc. said it completed the sale of Boston TV station WFXT to a limited partnership of the Boston Celtics basketball team. Terms weren't disclosed. The sale was required under a divestiture order by the Federal Communications Commission, Fox Television said. FCC rules prohibit a company from owning a television station and newspaper in the same market. Rupert Murdoch's Sydney, Australia-based News Corp., owns Fox Television and the Boston Herald newspaper.
