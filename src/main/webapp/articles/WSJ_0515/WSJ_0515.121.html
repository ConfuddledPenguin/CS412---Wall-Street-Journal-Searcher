Business Brief -- Pic `N' Save Corp.: Partnership Says Big Holder Is Supporting Its Proxy Bid

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

A partnership controlled by David H. Batchelder that is trying to oust the board of Pic `N' Save Corp. said a large shareholder agreed to support the partnership's slate. Bill M. Thomas, who owns about 1.4 million Pic `N' Save shares, or 4% of the 35.4 million shares outstanding, also has agreed to be one of the nominees of Girard Partners L.P., according to a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Girard holds 6.6% of Pic `N' Save.

Mr. Thomas, who is in his mid-50s, was the company's executive vice president and chief financial officer for about eight years until April 1987. The partnership has nominated four other men to the company's seven-member board.

A spokesman for the Dominguez, Calif.-based discount retailer declined to comment.

Girard has been soliciting consents from holders in support of its slate and has said it is seeking access to the company's nonpublic financial information to make a bid to acquire the company. Girard also has threatened a proxy contest at the June 20 annual meeting.
