Who's News: Fabri-Centers of America Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

FABRI-CENTERS OF AMERICA Inc. (Cleveland) -- James Monro Jr., executive vice president, was named to the new post of chief operating officer of this chain of 621 fabric stores. Mr. Monro, 40 years old, will be responsible for store operations, in addition to his existing duties for merchandising, advertising and merchandise allocation. Robert Norton, 43, executive vice president and chief financial officer, was named to the new post of chief administrative officer. Mr. Norton's responsibilities now include physical distribution and human resources along with his existing responsibilities of management information systems, accounting, internal audit and financial services, the company said.
