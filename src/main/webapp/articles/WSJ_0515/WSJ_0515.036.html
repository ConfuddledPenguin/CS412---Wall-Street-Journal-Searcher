Who's News: Morgan Stanley Loses Kipnis, Who Is Hired By County NatWest

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

NEW YORK -- Gregory M. Kipnis, who helped run Morgan Stanley & Co.'s high-tech trading unit, resigned from the firm yesterday to join County NatWest Ltd. Mr. Kipnis, 49 years old, who was a principal at Morgan Stanley, will join County NatWest as an executive director in charge of the British broker-dealer's world-wide proprietary trading and risk management. He will be based in London. County NatWest has been aggressively building up its equity operations this year; the dealer has hired several former Drexel Burnham Lambert Group Inc. employees.

Lately, Mr. Kipnis was responsible for distributing some of the technology developed by Morgan's Analytical Proprietary Trading unit to the other trading departments around the firm. Mr. Kipnis's departure follows the resignation of Nunzio Tartaglia, the former director of the Analytical Proprietary Trading unit, eight months ago.
