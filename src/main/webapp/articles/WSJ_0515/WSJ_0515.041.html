EC Unemployment Declines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

BRUSSELS -- Seasonally adjusted unemployment of the 12 European Community members was an average 8.5% in March, down from 8.6% in February and 9.4% a year earlier. Seasonally adjusted March unemployment was highest in Ireland at 16.3%, followed by Spain at 16.1% and Italy at 10.8%.

Unemployment was lowest in Luxembourg at 1.6%, followed by Portugal at 5.1% and West Germany at 5.2%.
