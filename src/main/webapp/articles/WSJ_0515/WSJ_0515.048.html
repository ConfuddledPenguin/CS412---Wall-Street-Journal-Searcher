Edison Bros. to Buy Sega Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

ST. LOUIS, Mo. -- Edison Brothers Stores Inc. said it agreed to acquire the Time-Out unit of Japan's Sega Enterprises Ltd. The retailer didn't disclose terms of the pact, which it said will expand its Edison Brothers Entertainment division.

Time-Out, a U.S. division of Sega, operates 88 game rooms and family entertainment centers in shopping malls. Time-Out's 1989 sales were about $33 million, Edison Brothers said.
