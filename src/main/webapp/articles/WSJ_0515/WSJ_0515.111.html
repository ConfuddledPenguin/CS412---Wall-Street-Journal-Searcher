Business Brief -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc.: Company Wins 2 Contracts With Value of $79 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said it received two contracts valued at $79 million from Ciba-Geigy Corp. to design and construct a dyestuff manufacturing facility and a chemical plant in St. Gabriel, La. Jacobs, based in Pasadena, Calif., said it plans to complete construction of the $67 million dyestuff plant by June 1991. It plans to complete construction of a $12 million chemical plant by this December.

Ciba-Geigy Corp. is the U.S. unit of Swiss-based Ciba-Geigy Ltd., a maker of agricultural chemicals, dyestuffs, and other products. In addition to construction and engineering services, Jacobs provides environmental and hazardous-waste services.
