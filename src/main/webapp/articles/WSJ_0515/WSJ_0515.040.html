Financial Center Bancorp Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Financial Center Bancorp's sole subsidiary acquired deposit liabilities and selected assets of three branches of Cabrillo Federal Savings Bank from the Resolution Trust Corp., a federal agency acting as receiver of Cabrillo, a thrift. Cabrillo's branches, located in northern California, carry a total of $38 million in deposits.
