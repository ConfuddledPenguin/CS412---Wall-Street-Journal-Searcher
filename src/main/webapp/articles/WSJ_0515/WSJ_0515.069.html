Credit Ratings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/15/90

MASCO Corp. -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. lowered Masco Corp.'s senior debt rating to single-A-3 from single-A-2 and its subordinated debt rating to Baa-1 from single-A-3. Moody's said it downgraded Masco's rating because of high debt levels and modest interest coverage, in part because of the debt of its Masco Industries affiliate. Also, Masco has spent about $2 billion on the acquisition and integration of its home-furnishing operations since 1986, Moody's said. The rating service said the company's expansion has kept debt levels high both at Masco Corp. and 49%-owned Masco Industries. Debt-protection measurements have weakened because of reduced earnings, Moody's said. Masco's earnings in the first quarter were $69.8 million, or 45 cents a share, down 7.4% from $75.4 million, or 48 cents a share, a year ago. Masco, a Taylor, Mich., maker of building and home furnishings, declined to comment.
