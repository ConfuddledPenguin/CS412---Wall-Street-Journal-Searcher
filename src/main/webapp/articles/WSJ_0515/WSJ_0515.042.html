British Producer Prices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/15/90

LONDON -- British producer output prices, or prices of manufactured goods, rose 1% in April from March, while producer input prices, or prices of raw materials and fuels purchased by manufacturing industry, fell an unadjusted 0.3%, Britain's Central Statistical Office reported. The statistical office also said output prices in April were up 6.1% from a year earlier, the highest year-to-year rise since a jump of 6.3% in December 1982.
