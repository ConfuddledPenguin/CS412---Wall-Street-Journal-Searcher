Pansophic Systems Sets Deal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

LISLE, Ill. -- Pansophic Systems Inc., a software concern, said it signed a letter of intent to sell its graphics division to closely held Autographix Inc., Waltham, Mass., and expects the deal to be completed by July 31. Terms weren't disclosed. Pansophic said earlier this week that it planned to dispose of the graphics business and it established a $27.1 million estimated loss on the disposal. It said that, based on the "upfront cash and expected future payments" from the transaction, the reserves are "adequate."
