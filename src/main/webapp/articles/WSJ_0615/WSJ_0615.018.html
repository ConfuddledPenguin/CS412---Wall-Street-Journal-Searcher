Darn That Democracy]

The recent dramatic world changes

Have left many people annoyed;

Suddenly, out of nowhere,

They feel a tremendous void.

It isn't that these onlookers

Are unhappy or do not care,

But all at once they have to find

A channel for anger elsewhere.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

---

Blackout

I can rid my mind of thoughts,

Leaving a clean slate.

Plans, problems, notions --

All tend to dissipate.

It's a recurring talent

That always reaches peak

When I'm before a group

And just about to speak.

-- Joshua Adams.

---

Balancing Act?

In our low-budget driving vacation plan,

The theory has some holes,

As what's saved on lodging and dining

Gets spent on gas and tolls.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
