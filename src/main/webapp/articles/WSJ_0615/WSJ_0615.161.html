Chicago Board of Trade Seat

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Board of Trade was sold for $360,000, down $500 from the previous sale June 5. Seats currently are quoted at $350,000 bid, $375,000 asked.

The record price, set Aug.31, 1987, for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000.
