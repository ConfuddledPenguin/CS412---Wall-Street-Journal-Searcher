TV: "House Party," a daytime syndicated show that was the first joint venture between General Electric Co.'s NBC Productions and Westinghouse Electric Corp.'s Group W Productions, was canceled after only six months. The one-hour show, which was on 33 stations, had minuscule ratings. The venture has two other projects in the development stage.
