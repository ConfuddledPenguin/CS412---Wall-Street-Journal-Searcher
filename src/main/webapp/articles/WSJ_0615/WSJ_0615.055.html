Italy's Producer Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

ROME -- Italy's industrial producer price index in April rose 0.1% from March and was up 4% from a year earlier, Istat, the state statistical institute, said. The April increase followed a monthly increase of 0.1% in March, when the yearto-year increase stood at 4.4%.

The wholesale price index rose 0.2% in April, compared with March, and increased 4.4% from a year earlier. This followed the March index that was flat with February's, though the year-to-year rate in March was up 5.3%.

Both the producer price index and the wholesale price index confirm a downward trend in inflation in recent months. Consumer price inflation fell to 5.7% in May from 5.8% in April.
