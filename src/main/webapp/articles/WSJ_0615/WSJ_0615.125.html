Hamilton Oil Corp. Discovery

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

DENVER -- Hamilton Oil Corp. said it discovered oil and natural gas in the British sector of the North Sea. The well had a flow at a rate of 4,850 barrels of oil and 2.5 million cubic feet of natural gas a day through a 40/64ths-inch opening.

BP Exploration is the operator with a 62.82% interest. Its partners are Hamilton Oil, with 12.44%; Ultramar Exploration Ltd., 7.77%; Ocean Exploration, 7.5%; Fina, 5%; Murphy Petroleum Ltd., 3.95%; and Monument Oil & Gas PLC, 0.52%.
