Business Brief -- Contel Corp.: Firm Offers 900 Employees An Early-Retirement Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

Contel Corp. said in Atlanta that it will offer about 900 employees an early-retirement package as part of a previously reported reorganization. The company also said it expects the total pretax cost of the reorganization to be $15 million to $25 million, which will be recorded in the second half of 1990. The telecommunications concern said it will offer about 900 employees in its telephone operations sector a three-year age and service credit as a retirement incentive. Contel said about 14,000 employees are covered by the division's pension plan.

The total reorganization plan calls for a total work force cut of about 4%, largely through attrition and voluntary departures. The amount of the second-half charge depends on the actual size of the work force reduction, the company said.
