  • Date: 06/15/90

Rockford Institute President Allan Carlson, writing in the May issue of Chronicles:

{I}t turns out that emotional bonds are not enough to preserve the family as an autonomous social unit. Love is insufficient when arrayed against a state apparatus that has appropriated the functions of early child development, basic education, economic support, recreation, civil religion and old age security. Simply put, the comprehensive welfare state holds all the "loyalty" trump cards in its hand.
