Dividend News: Network General Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

NETWORK GENERAL Corp. said its board approved a 100% stock dividend. The Menlo Park, Calif., maker of diagnostic equipment for computer networks said the dividend is contingent on shareholder approval of a proposal to increase authorized common shares to 50 million from 10 million. Holders will vote on the proposal at the annual meeting Aug. 2. If that measure passes, the dividend will be distributed around Aug. 20 to stock of record Aug. 3. The company said that after the dividend is distributed, it will have about 14.2 million shares outstanding.
