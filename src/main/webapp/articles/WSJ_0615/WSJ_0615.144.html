Business Brief -- Jack Eckerd Corp.: Company Plans to Sell 120 Of Revco Stores It's Buying

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

Jack Eckerd Corp., Clearwater, Fla., said it plans to liquidate or sell about 120 of the 220 drug stores it agreed to acquire from Revco D.S. Inc. Separately, the drug-store operator said it obtained commitments from investors to purchase not less than $100 million of senior secured notes, due 1997. The company said it plans to use proceeds from the notes for working capital and to reduce other indebtedness.

As reported, an affiliate of Eckerd agreed on June 2 to acquire 220 Revco drug stores located in Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi, Oklahoma and Texas. The agreement is subject to approval by the U.S. Bankruptcy Court where certain proceedings regarding Revco are pending.

Eckerd said it will operate the remaining 100 Revco stores it agreed to acquire as Eckerd Drug stores.
