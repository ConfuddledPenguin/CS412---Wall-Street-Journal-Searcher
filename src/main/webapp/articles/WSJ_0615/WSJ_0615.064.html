Krug International Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Krug International Corp. said it sold its British waste-handling equipment subsidiary, Shelvoke Dempster Ltd., to the unit's management, completing the parent's divestiture of its industrial businesses. Krug declined to disclose the sale price, but said Lloyds Bank provided the financing. The company has two remaining businesses, a biotechnological research and test equipment line, and a consumer products operation that makes housewares and garden products, and distributes marine equipment. Krug previously sold a U.S. waste-handling equipment business and a power and control business.
