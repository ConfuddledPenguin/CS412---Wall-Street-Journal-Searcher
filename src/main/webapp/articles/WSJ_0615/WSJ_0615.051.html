Moscom, Teleco Reach Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

EAST ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Moscom Corp. said its Control Key division reached an agreement with Teleco Inc., Greenville, S.C., calling for Teleco to market the telecommunications management system maker's PL Series and M3000 family of call-accounting products. The PL Series is a system used for accounting for telephone calls in businesses and hotels. The M3000 product is call-accounting software for personal computers. The products are compatible with telephone systems sold by Teleco, an independent marketer of business telephone systems, Moscom said.
