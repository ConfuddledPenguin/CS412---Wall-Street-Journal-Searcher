Who's News: Pacificorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

PACIFICORP (Portland, Ore.) -- William J. Glasgow, 43 years old, president and chief executive officer of Pacificorp Financial Services Inc., was named to the additional post of chairman, filling a vacancy at the unit of this electric utility concern, which also has interests in telecommunications and natural resources. He also was named to PacifiCorp's Corporate Policy Group, which consists of the senior executives of its various business units.
