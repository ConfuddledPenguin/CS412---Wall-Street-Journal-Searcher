Canadian Steel Production

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 294,294 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, a 2.1% increase from 288,305 tons in the previous week but a 3.4% decline from 304,533 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production totaled 6,406,381 metric tons, a 10% decline from 7,139,164 tons a year earlier.
