Italy's Electricity Consumption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

MILAN, Italy -- Electricity consumption in Italy in May rose 3.7% from a year earlier to 17.02 billion kilowatt-hours, ENEL, the state electricity utility, said. A kilowatt-hour is the amount of electrical energy consumed when 1,000 watts are used for one hour. ENEL also said demand for electricity in the first five months of this year rose 3.4% to more than 99 billion kwh, compared with the year-earlier period.
