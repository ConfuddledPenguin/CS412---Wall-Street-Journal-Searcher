Auto Makers Plan To Boost Car Output By 2.4% This Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

DETROIT -- The 10 major U.S. auto makers plan to build 146,054 cars this week, a 2.4% increase from 142,566 a year ago. Auto makers said all production facilities are scheduled to operate with several plants working overtime.

General Motors Corp. said it has scheduled overtime at assembly plants in Lansing and Hamtramck, Mich., and Janesville, Wis.

Ford Motor Co. said it has planned daily and weekend overtime at plants in Wayne and Wixom, Mich., and St. Louis.

Chrysler Corp. has slated overtime at assembly plants in Belvidere, Ill., Newark, Del., Sterling Heights, Mich., and St. Louis.
