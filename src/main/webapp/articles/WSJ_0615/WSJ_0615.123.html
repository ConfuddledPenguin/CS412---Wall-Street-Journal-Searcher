C-TEC Corp. Unit Rates

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

WILKES-BARRE, Pa. -- Commonwealth Long Distance, a newly formed unit of C-TEC Corp. and an affiliate of Commonwealth Telephone Co., said it will offer long-distance service at a 10% discount off AT&T's long-distance rates this summer. The service and low rates will become available July 1 to about 40,000 customers in 20 counties in eastern Pennsylvania.

The company said low rates will continue indefinitely.
