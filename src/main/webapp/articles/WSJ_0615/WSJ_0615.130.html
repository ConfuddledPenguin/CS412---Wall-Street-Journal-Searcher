Money Fund Assets Rose $988.6 Million In Weekly Measure

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

NEW YORK -- Assets of the nation's 504 money-market funds rose $988.6 million to $382.14 billion, in the week ended Wednesday, June 13, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 239 general purpose funds rose $1.01 billion to $131.32 billion and assets of 105 broker-dealer funds rose $432.5 million to $159.23 billion. Assets of 160 institutional funds fell $455.0 million to $91.59 billion.
