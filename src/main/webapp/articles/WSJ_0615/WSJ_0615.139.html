Business Brief -- Consolidated Natural Gas Co.: Investment of $48 Million In Storage Sites Is Planned

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

Consolidated Natural Gas Co., Pittsburgh, said it plans to invest $48 million in underground natural-gas storage facilities, following approval of the plan by the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission. Consolidated, a large producer, transporter and distributor of natural gas, said it will equip depleted gas wells to store sufficient gas for six retail gas distributors to provide 38 million cubic feet of gas daily during peak use periods beginning in 1991. Additional storage is to be provided four of the companies in 1992, adding capacity for 41 million cubic feet of gas daily. The retail distributors would own the gas.

The project also will provide 20 million cubic feet daily of storage service for Texas Eastern Transmission Corp. in 1992, the company said.
