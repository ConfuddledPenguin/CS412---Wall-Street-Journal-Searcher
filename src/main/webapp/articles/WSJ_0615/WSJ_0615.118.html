French Current-Account Gap

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

PARIS -- France's seasonally adjusted current account swung to a preliminary deficit of 4.19 billion francs ($738.9 million) in April from a revised surplus of 1.64 billion francs in March, the Economics Ministry said. Prior to the revision, the seasonally adjusted March surplus had been reported at 1.334 billion francs.

On an unadjusted basis, the April current account was in deficit by 2.760 billion francs, swinging from a March surplus of 1.445 billion francs. The unadjusted March surplus was also revised upward, from the previous total of 883 million francs.
