Pay 'N Pak Executive Resigns

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

KENT, Wash. -- PNP Prime Corp.'s Pay 'N Pak Stores Inc. unit said its chief financial officer, Douglas G. Southern, resigned. Pay 'N Pak didn't say why Mr. Southern, who had been with the company for more than four years, is leaving. The home-improvement products retailer said Mr. Southern will continue as a consultant to the company.

Fred Brotherton, currently vice president of administration and human resources at Pay 'N Pak, will assume the position of chief financial officer until a permanent replacement for Mr. Southern is hired, the company said.
