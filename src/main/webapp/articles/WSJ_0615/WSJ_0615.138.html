Who's News: Shiseido Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

SHISEIDO Co. (Tokyo) -- This cosmetics company named Robert M. Viola senior vice president and chief financial officer, a new post, at Zotos International Inc., its hair-care products unit based in Darien, Conn. Mr. Viola, 43 years old, was previously corporate controller of Faberge USA/Elizabeth Arden USA, New York-based makers of cosmetics and toiletries, and units of Unilever N.V., based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.
