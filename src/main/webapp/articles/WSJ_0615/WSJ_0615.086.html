Who's News: National Media Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

NATIONAL MEDIA Corp. (Philadelphia) -- Donald J. McDonald Jr., 37 years old, was elected president of this direct marketing services concern. He succeeds John Sgarlat, who resigned in April 1989. No one has been named to fill Mr. McDonald's former post as executive vice president. Mr. McDonald retains his position as chief operating officer. The company said it added six new board members, expanding the panel to nine members. The new directors are Mr. McDonald, Anthony Pironti, Nancy M. Langston, Ewin Ducat, David Baxter and Brian McAdams.
