Business Brief -- Rubbermaid Inc.: Company Acquires for Cash Floor Care Products Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

Rubbermaid Inc. said it purchased EWU AG, a Swiss producer of floor care supplies and equipment, for cash, but declined to disclose the price. Privately held EWU expects sales of about $15 million for 1990, Rubbermaid said.

Rubbermaid said it made the acquisition primarily to strengthen its European distribution system for floor care products. The Swiss concern also produces some items that Rubbermaid doesn't currently manufacture, which will be added to Rubbermaid's U.S. product line, the company said.

Rubbermaid is a Wooster, Ohio, producer of rubber and plastic products ranging from housewares to toys; it also has a unit that produces commercial products for food service, sanitary maintenance, health care and other applications.
