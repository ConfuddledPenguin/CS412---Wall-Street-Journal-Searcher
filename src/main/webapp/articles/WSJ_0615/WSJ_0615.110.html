Balfour Maclaine Unit Fined

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

CHICAGO -- The Chicago Board of Trade said it cited Balfour, Maclaine Futures Inc., a member of the exchange, for violating exchange rules; the board levied fines on the firm totaling $15,000. Balfour, a unit of New York-based Balfour, Maclaine Corp., was fined $10,000 for order, margin and accounting irregularities involving customer options accounts. The firm also must pay $5,000 for failing to keep accurate books and records.

Michael Grant, a senior vice-president of Balfour, Maclaine Futures, said the matter arose from "a routine annual audit by the CBOT." Mr. Grant said that "no customer funds were affected."
