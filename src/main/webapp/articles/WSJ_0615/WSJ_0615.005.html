Fred Meyer Inc. Credit Line

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Fred Meyer Inc. said it reached a new financing agreement with 10 major banks that more than doubles the retailing chain's credit line to $335 million. The company said Continental Illinois Corp.'s main unit heads the bank group.

Fred Meyer said it expanded its credit line to help finance expected growth and to help pay for upgrading its computer systems.

The company was hurt in 1989 by writeoffs on computer hardware, higher sales promotion costs and other unusual costs. It had a loss for the year of $6.8 million on sales of $2.28 billion.
