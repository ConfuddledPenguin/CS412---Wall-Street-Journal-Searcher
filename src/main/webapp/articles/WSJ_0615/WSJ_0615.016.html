Italy's Small Truck, Bus Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

MILAN, Italy -- Sales of new commercial vehicles in Italy rose 9.7% in May to 14,697 from 13,397 a year earlier, Anfia, the national association of car manufacturers, said. These vehicles are small trucks and buses, weighing as much as 3.5 tons. In the first five months of this year, commercial vehicle sales rose 7.7% to 73,977 units from 68,692 a year earlier.
