James River's $65 Million Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- James River Corp. said it will contruct a $65 million plant to recycle waste paper at its existing tissue mill in Halsey, Ore. The company, a paper goods manufacturer, said the plant will clean and process industrial and office-waste papers from the western United States. The facility is expected to produce up to 300 tons a day of high-grade fiber for several of the company's Northwest mills.

The company said the plant will employ 50 people when it is completed in mid-1992.
