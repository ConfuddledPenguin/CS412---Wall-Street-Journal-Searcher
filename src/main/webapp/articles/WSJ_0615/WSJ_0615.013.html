French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/15/90

PARIS -- French consumer prices in May rose 0.2% from April and 3% from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute said in a preliminary report. The May result represents a slowdown in price growth from April's monthly rise of 0.4% and trims year-to-year inflation from the April rate of 3.2%. Both of the May figures are in line with previous estimates by economists, who had based their outlook on signs that service-sector inflation would slow from the unusually high rate of 0.8% in April.

A full breakdown of May price growth by product category won't be available until the government publishes its definitive May index on June 28.
