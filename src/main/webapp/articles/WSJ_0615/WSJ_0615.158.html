Business Brief -- Koninklijke Ahold N.V.: Dutch Food Retailer's Net For First Quarter Rose 43%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/15/90

Citing higher margins and lower interest charges, the Dutch food retailer Koninklijke Ahold N.V. said net income for its first quarter ended April 22 rose 43% to 67.8 million guilders ($35.7 million) from 47.4 million guilders a year earlier. The company also said that although earnings growth in subsequent quarters this year isn't likely to match the fiscal first-period rise, "we maintain our expectations that net earnings for the full year will be considerably higher than in 1989, barring unforeseen sharp fluctuations of the exchange rate of the dollar." In fiscal 1989, Ahold earned 194.6 million guilders. Ahold declined to specify its expectations for fiscal 1990 earnings growth.
