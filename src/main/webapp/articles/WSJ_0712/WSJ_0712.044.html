Who's News: Tokai Bank Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

TOKAI BANK Ltd. (Nagoya, Japan) -- Joseph P. Geraghty was named senior vice president for corporate finance at the New York branch, one of Tokai's six subsidiaries in North America. Mr. Geraghty, 36 years old, fills a vacancy. He had been first vice president and head of the syndication division at Swiss Bank Corp.'s New York branch.
