Business Brief -- Taylor Devices Inc.: Sharp Rise in Net Income To Be Posted for Fiscal '90

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

Taylor Devices Inc., North Tonawanda, N.Y., said that for the year ended May 31, it expects to post net income of about $320,000, or 13 cents a share, up sharply from fiscal 1989 profit of $42,400, or two cents a share. Taylor, which makes shock absorbers and other products, said sales totaled $4.8 million, off an indicated 7.7% from $5.2 million a year earlier. Officials said earnings increased, despite the drop in sales, because of higher profit margins on its shock absorber business and higher engineering research fees, among other things.
