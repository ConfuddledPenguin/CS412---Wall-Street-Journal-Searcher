Business Brief -- Time Warner Inc.: Former Warner Shareholder Sues Over Appraisal Rights

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

A former Warner Communications Inc. shareholder filed a lawsuit in Delaware Chancery Court against Time Warner Inc. alleging the shareholders were deprived of appraisal rights when Time Inc. acquired Warner earlier this year. The lawsuit, filed July 9, alleges that Time "misled shareholders into believing they didn't have appraisal rights," according to David Brower, an attorney representing the shareholder, Howard S. Klotz.

Mr. Klotz, however, asserts that Warner shareholders indeed have such rights and, Mr. Brower said, "if appraisal rights were to take place, Warner shareholders would get more money."

Mr. Brower said Mr. Klotz was "acting on behalf of all Warner shareholders," and that he would seek class-action status for the suit.

A spokeswoman for Time Warner said the company had not seen the suit and couldn't comment.
