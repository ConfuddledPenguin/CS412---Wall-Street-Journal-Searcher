Business Brief -- Allen Group Inc.: Second-Quarter Share Net Estimated to Have Surged

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

Allen Group Inc., which makes automobile-testing equipment and truck parts, expects to report second-quarter per-share earnings more than doubled on strength in two divisions. The Melville, N.Y., company told analysts that per-share earnings reached about 55 cents to 60 cents, compared with 27 cents the year earlier. The estimates translate to net income of $5.5 million to $6 million versus $3.2 million.

In a news release, the company didn't estimate revenue. For the year-ago period, Allen Group's revenue was $94 million.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Allen Group shares rose 37.5 cents to $19.

The company's chairman and chief executive officer, Philip W. Coburn, cited Allen Testproducts, which makes automotive diagnostic and emission-test equipment, and Antenna Specialists, which sells equipment for cellular-telephone systems, as particularly strong-performing divisions.
