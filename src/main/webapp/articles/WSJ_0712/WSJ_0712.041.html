Fleet/Norstar Files With the SEC to Sell $700 Million of Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

PROVIDENCE, R.I. -- Fleet/Norstar Financial Group Inc. filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a shelf registration covering $700 million of senior or subordinated debt securities and warrants to purchase senior or subordinated debt securities. The securities may be offered from time to time directly by the bank holding company or through underwriters including Salomon Brothers Inc., Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and others, at amounts, prices, interest rates and other terms to be determined at the time of offering.

Proceeds of the proposed sale of the securities will be used for general corporate purposes, mainly to extend credit to, or fund investments in the company's subsidiaries.

A spokesman said the concern last filed a similar shelf registration of $1 billion in June, 1988, and that amount has subsequently been issued.
