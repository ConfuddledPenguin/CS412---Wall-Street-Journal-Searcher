West German Wholesale Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Wholesale prices in West Germany in June eased 0.3% from May and were off 0.2% from June 1989, the Federal Statistics Office said. The declines continued a trend toward modest inflationary tendencies this year. In May, wholesale prices rose 0.3% from April and were up 0.2% from May 1989.
