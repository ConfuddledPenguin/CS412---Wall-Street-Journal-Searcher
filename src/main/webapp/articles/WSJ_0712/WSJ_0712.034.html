Ford Matches Unocal Offer Of $700 for Gas Guzzlers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

LOS ANGELES -- Ford Motor Co. said it is offering owners of old gas guzzlers $700 a vehicle to scrap them. Ford said it will buy 1,000 of the most heavily polluting cars here. The car company is piggy-backing on a program launched by Unocal Corp. in April. Unocal offered to buy 7,000 cars made before 1971 at a price of $700 a vehicle. More than 10,000 owners applied, leaving the company with a waiting list.

A Unocal spokesman says Ford approached Unocal about participating in the program. So far Unocal has scrapped about 2,500 cars and plans to buy as many as 4,700 more, for a total of 7,200, slightly more than the original plan.

Ford is combining its environmental gesture with a sales pitch. Ford said it is offering sellers an additional $700 toward the purchase or lease of a new, low-emission Ford vehicle, such as a Ford Festiva, Ford Escort or Mercury Tracer. Ford said it is also making that offer to those who sell their cars to Unocal.
