Business Brief -- Shorewood Packaging Corp.: Buy-Back of Up to 7.6% Of Common Is Authorized

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

Shorewood Packaging Corp. directors authorized the New York-based maker of paperboard packaging to buy as many as one million, or 7.6%, of its 13.2 million common shares outstanding, in open-market transactions. The authorization will be 1.5 million shares after a 3-for-2 stock split, payable about Aug. 6. After the split, Shorewood will have 19.8 million shares outstanding.

Chairman Paul B. Shore said the company has completed a capital spending program. "Absent a major acquisition, which we have not identified at this time, we believe that the optimum use of the free cash flow currently being generated by Shorewood is to repurchase our shares," he said.

The company's packaging is used in diverse industries, including music, home video, tobacco, film and cosmetics.
