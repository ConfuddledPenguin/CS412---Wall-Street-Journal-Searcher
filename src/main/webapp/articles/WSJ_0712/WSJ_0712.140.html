Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

WHO'S NEWS: Jo Ann Emmerich, senior vice president of daytime programming and one of the highest ranking women at Capital Cities/ABC Inc.'s TV network, is leaving the position. ABC said it hopes to continue a "working relationship" with her. She couldn't be reached for comment. She will be succeeded by Mary Alice Dwyer-Dobbin, ABC's vice president of daytime programming, East Coast, for two years, but Ms. Dwyer-Dobbin won't become senior vice president. . . .Reader's Digest Association Inc. named Ronald L. Cole to the new post of vice president of the magazine publishing group. He will be succeeded as publisher of U.S. Reader's Digest by John J. Donoghue, who was director of U.S. ad sales. . . .Walt Disney Co. said Bobbi Blair was named to the new post of senior vice president, media, for Buena Vista Pictures Marketing, a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios.
