Transcisco Purchase Dropped

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Transcisco Industries Inc. said it won't acquire Piper Aircraft Corp.'s aircraft-parts business as previously agreed. Transcisco agreed early last month to acquire the Piper business, but said yesterday that it allowed the letter of intent to expire June 30. The companies decided that "it was not in their best interests" to carry through with the transaction because of "unresolvable business issues," Transcisco said.
