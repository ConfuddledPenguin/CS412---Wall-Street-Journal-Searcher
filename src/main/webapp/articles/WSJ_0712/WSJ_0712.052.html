Marketing & Media: E.W. Scripps Unit Sets Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

CINCINNATI -- E.W. Scripps Co., a media company, said its Scripps Howard Productions unit agreed to develop the producer Jay Wolpert's game shows for networks, cable and syndication. Terms weren't disclosed. Scripps said Mr. Wolpert was the first producer of "The New Price is Right" game show and has been involved in creating shows such as "Match Game" and "Family Feud."
