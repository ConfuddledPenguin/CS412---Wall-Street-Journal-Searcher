Worthington's Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Worthington Industries Inc. said it formed a joint venture with Thyssen Stahl of West Germany to build a steel processing facility in the U.S. Terms weren't disclosed.

The location of the new plant won't be announced until later. The joint venture will begin with a Detroit pilot production and development plant, which will open later this year.

The new plant will use laser welding to convert flat sheets of various types of steel into larger pieces needed in the automotive and appliance industries.

Worthington Industries is a maker of metal and plastic products. Thyssen Stahl, based in Duisburg, West Germany, is a steel producer.
