  • Date: 07/12/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Robin Renn, 36-year-old vice president, strategic planning, at American Express Travel Related Services Co., was named senior vice president, strategic planning, at the travel business subsidiary of this travel, financial and information services company, succeeding John I. Williams Jr., 36, who was named head of marketing for the unit's platinum card.
