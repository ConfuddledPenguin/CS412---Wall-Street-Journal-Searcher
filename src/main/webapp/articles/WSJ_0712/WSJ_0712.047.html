Financing Business: Canadian Utilities Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

CANADIAN UTILITIES Ltd. said it plans to issue 125 million Canadian dollars (US$108.1 million) of 11.40%, 1990 Series debentures maturing Aug. 15, 2010. Proceeds of the proposed issue will be used to finance the company's capital spending program and for general purposes, it said. The issue will be underwritten by three Toronto-based securities dealers. Canadian Utilities, Edmonton, Alberta, has interests in natural gas and electric utilities, and oil and gas exploration and production.
