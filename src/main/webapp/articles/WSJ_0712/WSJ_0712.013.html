Who's News: Kewaunee Scientific Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

KEWAUNEE SCIENTIFIC Corp. (Wilmette, Ill.) -- Eli Manchester Jr., 59 years old, formerly president and chief executive officer of BIW Cable Systems Inc., was named president and chief executive of this maker of scientific laboratory furniture, succeeding William M. Bartlett, 57, who resigned to pursue other business interests.
