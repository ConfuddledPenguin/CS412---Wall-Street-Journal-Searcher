Who's News: Swedish Match B.V.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

SWEDISH MATCH B.V. (Nyon, Switzerland) -- Fred E. Hiller was named president and chief executive officer of Wilkinson Sword N.A., the Atlanta-based unit of this consumer products company. Mr. Hiller, 50 years old, had been vice president of finance at Wilkinson, a maker of shaving products, since 1986. He succeeds Norman R. Proulx, 43, who resigned. No reason was given for his departure.
