Business Brief -- Bain & Co.: Management Concern Forms Joint Venture With Soviets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

Bain & Co., a Boston-based, international management consulting firm, said it formed a joint venture in the Soviet Union with the Soviet-based consulting firm Link Inc. As part of the joint venture, Bain will open an office in Moscow to assist Bain's Western clients in evaluating and pursuing business opportunities in the Soviet Union.

Bain will initially purchase a minority interest in the venture, to be called BainLink, with an option to purchase a majority of the venture's Western-owned shares at a later date. Terms weren't disclosed.

Bain said Link currently serves a number of Western companies.
