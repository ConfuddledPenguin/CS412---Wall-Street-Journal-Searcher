Business Brief -- Promus Cos.: Embassy Suites Unit Sells 95% Stake in Partnership

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

The Embassy Suites Inc. unit of Promus Cos., a hotel and gambling company based in Memphis, Tenn., said it sold a 95% interest in EPT Atlanta Buckhead Limited Partnership, Atlanta, to U.S. subsidiaries of offshore investors for $38.8 million. The partnership owns a 328-room Embassy Suites hotel in Atlanta. The Embassy unit said it will continue to manage and license the hotel and will retain a 5% general partnership interest valued at $2 million. It said proceeds from the sale will be used for paying off the hotel's mortgage debt and general corporate purposes.
