Business Brief -- Charles Schwab Corp.: Company Extends Its Offer For 700,000 Warrants

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

Charles Schwab Corp. of San Francisco said it will extend until Wednesday a tender offer for 700,000 warrants issued to certain former shareholders. Under the terms of the offer, the company could withdraw or amend the offering if its stock dropped below $14.25 on or before the original July 11 expiration date. The stock closed at $13.50 Tuesday, but the company didn't withdraw the offer.

Company officials said the tender offer has been fully subscribed, with 826,612 warrants tendered as of July 10 at a purchase price of $13 a warrant. Each warrant requires the holder to purchase one share of the company's common stock at $1.42 a share. There are 2.1 million shares outstanding.

Through its subsidiary, Charles Schwab & Co., the company is the largest discount brokerage house in the U.S.
