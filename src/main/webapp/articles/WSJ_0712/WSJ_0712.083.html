Gottschalks Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- Gottschalks Inc., a regional department-store chain in California's Central Valley, said its June sales rose 29% from a year earlier. Dresses and improved sportswear apparel accounted for the increase, company officials said. Gottschalks reported June sales of $24 million, up from $18.6 million the year before. For the year ended in June, the company reported a 24% increase to $95 million from $76.9 million.

Gottschalks Inc. has 21 department and 32 specialty apparel outlets in the Central Valley and central coast areas of the state. Its stock closed at $12.875, up 62.5 cents, in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange.
