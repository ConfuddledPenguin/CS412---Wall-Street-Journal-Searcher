Who's News: Action Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

ACTION INDUSTRIES Inc. (Pittsburgh) -- Sholom D. Comay, vice chairman, was named chairman of this marketer of housewares and giftware. Mr. Comay, 52 years old, succeeds Ernest S. Berez, 64, who retires at the end of July. The vice chairman post won't be filled.
