Perry Projects Fiscal '91 Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

PONTIAC, Mich. -- Perry Drug Stores Inc. expects its fiscal 1991 sales to range from $720 million to $730 million as a result of an agreement to buy 40 former Revco stores, Jack A. Robinson, chairman, said. Perry's 1990 fiscal year ends Oct. 31. The company's fiscal 1989 sales of $670 million included revenue from the A.L. Price division, which Perry sold May 30. Mr. Robinson, speaking to analysts and portfolio managers, also said he expects Perry's rate of profitability to exceed the percentage increase in sales for fiscal 1991.
