Marketing & Media: American Banker Editor, Fred R. Bleakley, Resigns

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

NEW YORK -- Fred R. Bleakley resigned as editor of American Banker, a daily newspaper owned by Thomson Publishing Corp. Mr. Bleakley, a former deputy business editor of the New York Times, said he resigned because of a disagreement with George W. Landgrebe, president of American Banker, who had hired Mr. Bleakley. "George and I couldn't reach agreement on my proposal to report directly to him," Mr. Bleakley said. "I wasn't comfortable with other executives getting involved."

Mr. Bleakley had been editor since April 1989, when he was hired to thoroughly redesign the paper.

A successor to Mr. Bleakley wasn't named and executives of Thomson Publishing couldn't be reached for comment.
