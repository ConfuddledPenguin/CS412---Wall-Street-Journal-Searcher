Who's News: Dow Chemical Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

DOW CHEMICAL Co. (Midland, Mich.) -- Enrique J. Sosa and William S. Stavropoulos were elected to the board of this world-wide chemical company, increasing board membership to 19. Messrs. Sosa and Stavropoulos are group vice presidents at Dow U.S.A, the parent's U.S. entity.
