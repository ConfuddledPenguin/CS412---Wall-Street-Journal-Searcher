Business Brief -- Alcan Aluminium Ltd.: Four Can-Recycling Firms Are Acquired by Company

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

Alcan Aluminium Ltd. said it acquired four aluminum beverage can collection and processing concerns. Alcan said it acquired Reliable Recycling Inc. and the assets of UBC Recycling Inc., both based in Brampton, a Toronto suburb. Alcan said it acquired the assets of R. Johnson Management Services Ltd. and Camco Recycling Services Ltd., both based in Lachine, a Montreal suburb. Terms weren't disclosed.

Alcan, based in Montreal, said the four acquisitions, combined with a Vancouver, British Columbia, recycling concern acquired earlier this year, give it an annual collection and processing capacity of two billion aluminum beverage cans a year.
