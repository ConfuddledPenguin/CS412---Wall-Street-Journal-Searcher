Reckitt & Colman Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

WAYNE, N.J. -- Reckitt & Colman Inc. said it plans to sell three of its household carpet and upholstery cleaners, marketed under the Glamorene brand name. Reckitt, a subsidiary of Reckitt & Colman PLC, London, said it entered into a consent order with the Federal Trade Commission to sell its Spray 'n Vac, Spray 'n Brush and Apply 'n Vac brands of carpet and upholstery cleaners.

Reckitt recently acquired the Boyle-Midway household products division of American Home Products Corp., for about $1.25 billion; the business includes the Woolite brand of carpet cleaning products.

TM Capital Corp., a New York investment banking firm, was retained by Reckitt & Colman to assist in the planned divestiture.
