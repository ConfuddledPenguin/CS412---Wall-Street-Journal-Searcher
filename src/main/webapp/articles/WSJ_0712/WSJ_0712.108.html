Who's News: Coastal Names Hesse, Ex-Energy Regulator, To Gas-Pipeline Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

HOUSTON -- Coastal Corp. said it named former federal energy regulator Martha O. Hesse to the board of its natural-gas pipeline unit. Ms. Hesse, chairman of the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission from October 1986 until November 1989, is now a senior vice president of First Chicago Corp., a banking concern. During her stint at FERC, the agency overhauled the naturalgas and electric industries in an attempt to lessen regulation.

Before that assignment, she had held posts with the U.S. Energy and Commerce departments and with the U.S. Office of Management and Budget. In Chicago, she was a co-founder and chief operating officer of SEI Information Technology, a dataprocessing consulting concern.

She was named to the board of Coastal's American Natural Resources Co. unit, which includes the ANR Pipeline Co. Her appointment increases the unit's board to ten members.
