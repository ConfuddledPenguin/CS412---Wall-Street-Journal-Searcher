Marketing & Media: News Corp. Unit's Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

SYDNEY, Australia -- Rupert Murdoch's News Corp. said yesterday that its Harper-Collins Publishers Inc. unit has agreed to buy British-based book publisher Unwin Hyman Ltd. News Corp. didn't reveal the price of the transaction in a statement to the Australian Stock Exchange, but it said Unwin Hyman's group annual sales exceeded #15 million ($27.3 million) in 1989, and is split evenly between the U.K. and overseas.

Unwin Hyman's Australian and New Zealand units aren't included in the transaction, but the U.S. unit, Unwin Hyman Inc. of Boston, will become part of Harper-Collins North America.

In the U.K., Unwin Hyman's books include trade, education and academic topics.
