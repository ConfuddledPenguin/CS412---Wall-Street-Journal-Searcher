Who's News: Banc One Appoints Buckeye's Chairman As President of Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Michael J. McMennamin was named president and chief operating officer of Banc One Corp.'s Columbus bank, effective Aug. 1. Mr. McMennamin, 45 years old, has been chairman and chief executive officer of Buckeye Financial Corp., Columbus. At Bank One, Columbus, he succeeds William M. Bennett, 52, who remains chief executive and becomes chairman of Bank One, Columbus, effective Aug. 1.

At Buckeye, Mr. McMennamin is succeeded by a Buckeye director, Michael H. Thomas, 40, executive vice president of JMAC Inc., a Worthington, Ohio, investment firm.

Robert W. Strouse, 41, Buckeye's chief legal counsel, was named president and managing officer of its principal subsidiary, Buckeye Federal Savings and Loan Association.

Mr. Strouse, succeeds W. Ford Kieft III as president of the thrift. Mr. Kieft, 49, remains president and chief operating officer of the holding company.
