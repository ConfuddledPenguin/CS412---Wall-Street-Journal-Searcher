Conner Peripherals Makes Public Offering Of 6.4 Million Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Conner Peripherals Inc. said it made a public offering to sell 6.4 million shares of common stock, at $26.75 a share. Prior to the stock offer, Conner had 41.8 million common shares outstanding. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Conner closed at $27.75, up $1.

Conner designs, makes and sells computer disk drives.

Of the shares offered, six million were sold by the company and certain shareholders sold 400,000. The company said 5.1 million shares were sold in the U.S. and 1.3 million in Europe.

Proceeds will be used principally to expand and improve manufacturing capacity world-wide and to provide working capital for general corporate purposes.
