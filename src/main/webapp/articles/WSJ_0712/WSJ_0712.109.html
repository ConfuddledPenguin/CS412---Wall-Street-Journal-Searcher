Who's News: American Republic Appoints Oberholzer Bank Unit President

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

TORRANCE, Calif. -- American Republic Bancorp. said it named William B. Oberholzer, 44 years old, as president of its Republic Bank unit. Mr. Oberholzer was executive vice president of the Republic Bank unit. In his new post, he assumes one of the titles held by John B. O'Donnell before he resigned in late May as president and chief executive officer of the parent and unit.

At the time, Mr. O'Donnell said in a letter to the boards that he was resigning because he "may have inadvertently engaged in activities which appear inconsistent with company policy." The company didn't specify the policy, but said it didn't suffer any losses as a result of the activities.

Mr. O'Donnell couldn't be reached for comment yesterday.
