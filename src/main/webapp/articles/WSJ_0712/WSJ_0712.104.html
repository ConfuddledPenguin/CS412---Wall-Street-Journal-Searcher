Raytheon Unit Gets $61.5 Million Navy Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

WASHINGTON -- Beech Aerospace Services Inc., a subsidiary of Raytheon Co., was given a $61.5 million Navy contract for logistics support of trainer aircraft.

Grumman Corp. won a $46 million Navy contract for E-2C aircraft for Japan.

Electro Design Manufacturing Inc. got a $27.2 million Army contract for TOW-2 missile equipment.

Keco Industries Inc. received a $15.2 million Army contract for air conditioners.

Frank E. Basil Inc. was awarded a $14.2 million Navy contract for base-operating services at the naval air station at Barbers Point, Hawaii.

Martin Marietta Corp. received a $12.5 million Air Force pact for Centaur space launch vehicles.
