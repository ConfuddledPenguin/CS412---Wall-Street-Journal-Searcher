Business Brief -- Regal International Inc.: Polystar to Get 45% Stake In Firm for Canceling Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

Regal International Inc. said a debt holder would receive a 45% stake in the company in exchange for cash and the cancellation of debt. The Houston oil field services concern said that under a recapitalization program, Polystar Corp. will exercise its options for the purchase of 12 million shares of common stock and in exchange will cancel about $2.7 million of Regal's debt and pay the company $570,000 in cash. Polystar, a unit of Zurich Corp. of Greenville, N.C., will hold about 45% of Regal's 26.5 million shares outstanding upon completion of the deal.

Regal said the transaction will more than double shareholder equity to about $5.6 million, up from $2.3 million at March 31.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Regal shares closed unchanged at 31.25 cents.
