Sulcus Takeover Talks Halted

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

GREENSBURG, Pa. -- Sulcus Computer Group said its negotiations on the acquisition of Cam Data Systems Inc., Fountain Valley, Calif., have been suspended for three to six months. The company didn't elaborate on why the talks were suspended. In June, the two companies announced the signing of a letter of intent. Terms at the time called for Sulcus to exchange each share of Cam Data for one share of Sulcus, plus one warrant of Sulcus for every two shares of Cam Data.
