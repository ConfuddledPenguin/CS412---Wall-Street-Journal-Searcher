Chiles Offshore Leases Rigs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

HOUSTON -- Chiles Offshore Corp., a drilling contractor, said it signed three-year charter agreements to lease three mobile offshore drilling rigs, with options to acquire each of them. The agreements cover the Johnnie Hoffman, Penrod 96 and Penrod 97 jackup drilling units, each capable of operating in water as deep as 250 feet. The leases call for profit-sharing arrangements of 75% to Chiles and 25% to the owners, all affiliated with a Norwegian investor, after Chiles has recouped its operating costs.

Chiles's exclusive option to purchase the rigs expires Nov. 30, after which it has the right to match any purchase offers.
