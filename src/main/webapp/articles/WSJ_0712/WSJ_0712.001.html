Business Brief -- Fluor Corp.: Daniel Unit Wins Contract To Design Plant in China

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

Fluor Corp. said its Fluor Daniel subsidiary was awarded a $24 million contract by Jilin Chemical Industries in China to provide engineering design and other services for the construction of an ethylene oxide plant. Fluor, based in Irvine, Calif., said it expects to complete the contract by late 1993. Fluor said the plant will be an addition to Jilin Chemical's petrochemical facility in northeast China.

Fluor said the subsidiary also was awarded a contract by National Technical Import & Export Co. in China for an undisclosed amount. The subsidiary will provide consulting services to help the Chinese company build two plants that meet U.S. Food and Drug Administration standards. The consulting services will continue for three years.

Fluor is an international engineering, construction, maintenance and technical services company.
