Business Brief -- Lewis Galoob Toys Inc.: Significant Losses Expected For Second Quarter, Year

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

Lewis Galoob Toys Inc., San Francisco, said it expected to report "significant losses" for the second quarter and for the year. The toy company blamed the projected losses on soft retail sales and lower-than-expected sales of two big product lines. Galoob said the losses also come because the company won't begin significant shipments of new products until the third quarter.

In the year-earlier second quarter, Galoob had net income of $4.7 million, or 49 cents a share, on revenue of $56.1 million. Galoob's stock closed at $4.75, up 25 cents, in trading on the New York Stock Exchange yesterday.
