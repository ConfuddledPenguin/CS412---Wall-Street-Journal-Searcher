Copperweld Resets Meeting

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said it rescheduled a special stockholders meeting to July 31. Shareholders of record June 8 will vote on Copperweld's agreement to be acquired by Imetal, a Paris building materials maker. Imetal has agreed to buy the 34.8% of Copperweld it doesn't already own for $17 a share. Copperweld makes tubing and rods.

The meeting had been scheduled for this past Tuesday.
