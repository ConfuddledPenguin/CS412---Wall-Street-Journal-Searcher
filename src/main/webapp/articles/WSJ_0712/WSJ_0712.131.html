Air Wis Units' Traffic Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

APPLETON, Wis. -- Air Wis Services Inc. said June traffic at its Air Wisconsin and Aspen Airways units rose 11% to 45.2 million revenue passenger miles from 40.8 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier, which operates as United Express, said its June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 50.1% from 51.5%.
