Financing Business: Foundation Health Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

FOUNDATION HEALTH Corp., a health maintenance organization based in Sacramento, Calif., said it made an initial public offering of 3.3 million common shares at $12.50 each. Proceeds will be used for debt repayment and general corporate purposes. The offering is being made through Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and PaineWebber Inc., and the stock will begin trading today on the American Stock Exchange under the symbol FH. Foundation administers heath-care services to more than 1.1 million people through its HMO, one of the largest in California; the HMO has about 280,000 enrollees in Northern and Central California.
