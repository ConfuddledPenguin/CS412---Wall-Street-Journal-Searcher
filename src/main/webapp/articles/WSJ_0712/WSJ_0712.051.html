Who's News: Pacific Stock Exchange Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

PACIFIC STOCK EXCHANGE Inc. (San Francisco) -- Robert M. Greber was named executive vice president for marketing and strategic planning, a new post, effective Monday at this regional stock exchange. Mr. Greber, 52 years old, was previously a partner in Leon A. Farley Associates, an executive search firm based in San Francisco.
