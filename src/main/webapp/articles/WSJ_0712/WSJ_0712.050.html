Who's News: CBOE Names John Shad, Formerly of SEC, to Board

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

CHICAGO -- The Chicago Board Options Exchange named former Securities and Exchange Commission Chairman John Shad a public director of the exchange. The CBOE has four such directors on its 21-member board. Each public director serves a two-year term.

In 1989, Mr. Shad became chairman of Drexel Burnham Lambert Group Inc.'s board and oversight committee, which is responsible for assuring the firm complies with its consent agreements with the SEC and the Justice Department.
