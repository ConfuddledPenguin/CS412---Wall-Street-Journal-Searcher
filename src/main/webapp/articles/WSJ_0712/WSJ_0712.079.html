Sandoz Unit Name Change

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/12/90

SACRAMENTO, Calif. -- McLaren Environmental Engineering Inc., a unit of Sandoz International Ltd.'s Sandoz Corp. affiliate, said it changed its name to McLaren/ Hart after acquiring Hart Environmental Management Corp., New York. Terms of the acquisition weren't disclosed. McLaren said the transaction created one of the largest firms in the U.S. for environmental engineering and management.
