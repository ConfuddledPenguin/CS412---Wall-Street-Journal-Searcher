Spain's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/12/90

MADRID -- Spain's unemployment rate slipped to 15.3% in June from 15.5% in May, the Spanish Labor Ministry said. The number of jobless people fell to about 2,290,000 in June from 2,330,000 in May, marking the first time since November 1983 that the total fell below 2.3 million, the ministry said.
