AN INVESTMENT STAKE in Miller, Anderson & Sherrerd by Long-Term Credit Bank of Japan is estimated at $50 million by Ulmer Brothers Inc. Data supplied by Ulmer Brothers for a table in Thursday's edition incorrectly stated the estimate. (See: "Simon to Re-Enter Buy-Out Arena in Fund With Tokio Marine" -- WSJ July 19, 1990)

SYNOPTICS COMMUNICATIONS Inc. reported second-quarter earnings of 34 cents a share, which was 19% greater than the average estimate of analysts as compiled by Zacks Investment Research. Because of a database error, SynOptics' earnings were mistakenly listed as a negative surprise in the Quarterly Earnings Surprises table in Friday's edition.

(See: "Quarterly Earnings Surprises" -- WSJ July 20, 1990)
