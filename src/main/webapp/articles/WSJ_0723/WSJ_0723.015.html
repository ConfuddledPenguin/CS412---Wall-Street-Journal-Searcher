Swiss Wholesale Prices Drop

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

BERN -- Switzerland's wholesale price index declined 0.3% in June from May but was up 1.1% from a year earlier, the government statistics office said. In May, the index declined 0.1% from April and rose 1.0% from a year earlier. In June of last year, the index declined 0.4% from the previous month and rose 4.6% from a year earlier.
