Business Brief -- Hotel Investors Corp.: California Investor Proposes To Buy Certain Properties

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

Beverly Hills, Calif., investor Leonard M. Ross said he made a proposal to acquire certain hotel and gaming properties of Hotel Investors Trust and Hotel Investors Corp. for $46 million. In a July 18 letter attached to a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Mr. Ross indicated these assets aren't among the hotel properties that the company already entered into an agreement to sell.

Hotel Investors Trust is a real estate investment trust based in Los Angeles whose shares are paired for trading with Hotel Investors Corp., based in San Diego. The companies are in the process of selling most of their assets following a falling stock price in the past few years. Hotel Investors executives didn't return phone calls, nor did Mr. Ross.

Hotel Investors shares closed 12.5 cents lower at $3.625 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday.
