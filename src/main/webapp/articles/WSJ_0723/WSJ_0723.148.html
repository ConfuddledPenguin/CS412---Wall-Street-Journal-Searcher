Who's News: Langer Biomechanics Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

LANGER BIOMECHANICS GROUP Inc. (Deer Park, N.Y.) -- Paul S. Langer, 34 years old, chief operating officer of this maker of medical and health products, was named to the additional post of president. He succeeds his father, Sheldon Langer, 66-year-old founder and a podiatrist, who remains chairman and chief executive officer. Paul Langer's former post of executive vice president, operations, won't be filled; he also was president of the orthotics division, a post that was eliminated. Separately, Jason M. Kraus, 33, previously vice president, marketing, was named senior vice president, marketing.
