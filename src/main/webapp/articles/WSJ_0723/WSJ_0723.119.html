LORAL CORP. (New York) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it placed this defense contractor's triple B-plus senior debt and triple B subordinated debt ratings on its CreditWatch with negative implications. About $410 million in debt is affected. S&P's action followed Loral's announcement that is a finalist in the bidding for the aerospace operations of Ford Motor Co. The acquisition could be well in excess of $500 million, S&P said, and Loral's credit quality could be hurt if it acquired the operations largely with debt. Officials of Loral couldn't be reached for comment Friday. ---

Metropolitan Life Insurance Co. -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it affirmed the triple-A rating on the company's claims-paying ability and the A-1-plus rating on its commercial paper. S&P also affirmed the triple-A rating on the senior debt of the insurance company's MetLife Funding Inc. affiliate and the A-1-plus commercial paper rating for MetLife Funding.
