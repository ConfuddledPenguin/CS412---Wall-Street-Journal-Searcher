Business Brief -- Time Warner Inc.: Firm's Lorimar Unit Sues LJM Toys for $4 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

Lorimar Telepictures Inc., a unit of Time Warner Inc., filed a lawsuit in Los Angeles Superior Court seeking $4 million in damages from LJN Toys Ltd. for allegedly breaching a pair of toy licensing agreements. MCA Inc. agreed to sell LJN, its financially troubled toy making unit, to Oyster Bay, N.Y.-based Acclaim Entertainment last March.

The complaint alleges that LJN agreed to pay Lorimar a $3 million advance for use of characters from its TV show "Thundercats" by last Aug. 31, and that the payment was not made. Lorimar also alleges that $1 million of a "royalty guarantee" for use of the characters from "The Comic Strip" was not paid by Dec. 31. LJN executives couldn't be reached for comment.
