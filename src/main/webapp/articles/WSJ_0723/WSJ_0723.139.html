Business Brief -- Caesars World Inc.: Holders File Suits Over Bid For Caesars New Jersey

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

Two shareholder lawsuits seeking class action status have been filed against Caesars World Inc. in the wake of its announced intention to make a tender offer for the 13.4% stake it doesn't already hold in Caesars New Jersey Inc. In a suit filed in U.S. District Court in Los Angeles, an investor alleges that the company's $22-a-share, or $48 million, offer is inadequate and that it didn't seek competing acquisition proposals. Another suit filed in state court by four investors names 10 officers and directors of the company as defendants and seeks an injunction blocking the offer. Caesars World, based in Los Angeles, owns and operates hotels and casinos.
