Citizens Banking Rights Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

FLINT, Mich. -- Citizens Banking Corp. said its board has adopted a shareholder rights plan under which shareholders will receive rights to purchase shares of preferred stock. Under the plan, shareholders may purchase one 1/100 of a share of preferred stock for each share outstanding of its common stock.

According to the terms of the plan, if anyone acquires or makes a tender offer for 15% or more of Citizens' common stock, all rights holders except the acquiring person may purchase Citizens' common stock at 50% of the market price. If Citizens is acquired, all rights holders may purchase common stock from the acquiring person at a similar discount.
