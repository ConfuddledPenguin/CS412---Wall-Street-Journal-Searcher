Business Brief -- Sun Savings & Loan Association: RTC Shuts Colorado Thrift, After Effort to Sell It Fails

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

The Resolution Trust Corp. said Sun Savings & Loan Association, Parker, Colo., was closed by the federal government on Friday, after the federal agency failed to sell the thrift. The RTC, which was named receiver, said that it solicited bids for Sun Savings but that it decided to pay off insured despositors instead, because it didn't receive any "acceptable bids."

Sun Savings had about $240.7 million in deposits, with "minimal" uninsured deposits, the RTC said. The thrift had assets of $208.4 million and liabilities of $298.7 million.
