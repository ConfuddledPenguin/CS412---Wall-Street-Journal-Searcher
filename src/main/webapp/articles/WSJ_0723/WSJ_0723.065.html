WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Alcan Aluminium Ltd., shelf offering for $400 million of debt securities.

American Airlines, a unit of AMR Corp., offering of zero coupon, convertible subordinated notes, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Banc One Corp., shelf offering for $300 million of unsecured debt securities.

Chrysler Auto Receivables Co., a unit of Chrysler Corp., offering of $1,001,435,874 of class A asset backed certificates, via Merrill Lynch.

Intelligent Electronics Inc., proposed offering of 2,141,667 common shares, of which two million are being sold by the company and 141,667 by venture capital firms, via Merrill Lynch and Janney Montgomery Scott Inc.

NRM Steelastic, proposed initial offering of 3.35 million common shares, of which 2.8 million are being offered by the company and 550,000 by holders, via Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.

Selas Corp. of America, offering of 900,000 common shares, via Wheat First, Butcher & Singer Capital Markets.

Willamette Industries Inc., shelf offering of up to $200 million of senior debt securities.
