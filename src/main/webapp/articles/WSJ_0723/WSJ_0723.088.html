Equitable Resources Vote

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

PITTSBURGH -- Equitable Resources Inc. said its directors voted to exclude the utility holding company from some of the provisions of Pennsylvania's new anti-takeover law. The legislation, signed into law April 27, allows publicly owned concerns incorporated in the state to exclude themselves from all or part of its anti-takeover provisions within 90 days of passage.
