Business Brief -- Rio Algom Ltd.: Closing of 2 Mines to Begin As Early as September

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

Rio Algom Ltd. said it plans to close two of its uranium mines at the beginning of September, as much as nine months sooner than the Toronto mining company had indicated. Rio Algom, which is 52%-owned by RTZ Corp. of Britain, said the closures of the Quirke and Panel mines in Elliot Lake, Ontario, will eliminate about 1,700 of the company's work force of 5,000. A third mine in the area will continue to produce uranium under a long-term contract with provincially owned Ontario Hydro.

Rio Algom had said in January it planned to shut the two mines in the first half of 1991 because of low ore grades and depressed uranium market prices. A Rio Algom spokesman said the closure date was advanced after agreements were reached with customers to reduce the amount of uranium contracted.
