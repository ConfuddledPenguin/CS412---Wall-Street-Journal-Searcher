Colorado Agency Sues OEA

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

DENVER -- OEA Inc. said the Colorado Department of Health filed a suit in state court claiming the automotive and aerospace maker violated the Colorado Hazardous Waste Act. The suit asks for an injunction prohibiting OEA from generating any hazardous waste until the state agency believes that OEA is in compliance with the department's regulations.

OEA said it asked the court for a stay of any injunction and has temporarily discontinued production at its Denver facility until the injunction request is heard and decided.
