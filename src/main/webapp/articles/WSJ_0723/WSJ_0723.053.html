Dividend News: MGM/UA Communications Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

MGM/UA COMMUNICATIONS CO. said it distributed $4 a share to its shareholders from a partial payment made by Pathe Communications Corp. in its $1.3 billion agreement to buy the movie studio. The payment gives majority shareholder Kirk Kerkorian a gain of $180 million. Mr. Kerkorian, who has worked to sell the beleaguered studio for two years, owns about 35 million of MGM/UA's 51 million common shares and 10 million preferred.
