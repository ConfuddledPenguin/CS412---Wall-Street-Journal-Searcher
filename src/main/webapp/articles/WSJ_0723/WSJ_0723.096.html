Technology: Advanced Micro Posts Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Advanced Micro Devices Inc.'s stock skidded Friday on a report that the chip maker posted a net loss of $5.9 million, or 10 cents a share, for its second quarter ended July 1. Advanced Micro was expected to break even or turn a small profit of as much as five cents a share. The company's loss would have been even larger had the quarter not included a $6.1 million gain from the sale of its stake in another chip maker. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Advanced Micro's shares closed at $7.25, down $1.75.

Analysts blamed the poor results on a shortfall of roughly $17 million in revenue, which overall declined 2.6% to $267.7 million from $274.9 million. The decline was caused by a 29% drop in the price of the company's 286 microprocessor. A lack of availability of certain memory and logic chips also hurt sales.

In the year-earlier period, Advanced Micro earned $12.1 million, or 12 cents a share.
