Financing Business: Novell Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

NOVELL Inc., a Provo, Utah, maker of software products for computer networks, said it plans to call for redemption on Aug. 24 all of its 7 1/4% convertible subordinated debentures, due 2013, with an aggregate principal amount of $52 million outstanding. Prior to the close of business on Aug. 24, holders may convert the debentures into Novell common at a price of $36 a share. In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Novell closed at $50.75, down $3.50.
