Japan Trade Surplus Narrows

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

TOKYO -- Japan's trade surplus narrowed to $873.3 million in the first 10 days of July, from $1.93 billion in the year-earlier period, the Ministry of Finance said. Exports during the first 10 days of July dropped 3.1% to $6.83 billion from $7.05 billion a year ago, while imports rose 16% to $5.95 billion from $5.12 billion.

The Finance Ministry said the average exchange rate of 154.27 yen to the dollar was used in measuring goods during the 10-day reporting period, compared with 143.28 yen the year before.
