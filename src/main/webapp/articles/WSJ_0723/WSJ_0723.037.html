Petrocel Gets $26 Million Loan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

WASHINGTON -- The International Finance Corp., a World Bank affiliate, approved a $26 million loan to Petrocel S.A., a Mexican producer and exporter of intermediate chemicals used in producing polyester fibers and plastic products. The agency said the Mexican company, which is embarking on a three-year, $101 million expansion program, is jointly owned by Grupo Industrial Alfa S.A. of Mexico and Hercules Inc. of the U.S.

Other financing for the program will be provided by a group of banks in Mexico and other countries and by Petrocel.
