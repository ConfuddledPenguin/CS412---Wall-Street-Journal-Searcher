International Brief -- Lonrho PLC: British Overseas Trader Buys 10% Stake in German Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

Lonrho PLC, a British overseas trader, said it purchased a 10% stake in Asko Deutsche Kaufhaus AG, a German wholesale and retail store operator, for 94 million pounds ($170.7 million). Tiny Rowland, Lonrho's chairman, will join Asko's supervisory board. The Asko stake "will enable Lonrho to make further advances in the expanding opportunities which will occur" in Germany as well as Eastern Europe, the company said.

Lonrho is buying the holding from German retailer Massa AG. The stake will be paid for by a mixture of 8.5 million Lonrho shares and the balance of 71 million pounds in cash.
