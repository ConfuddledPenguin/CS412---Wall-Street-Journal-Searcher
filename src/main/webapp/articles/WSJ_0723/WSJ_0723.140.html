Business Brief -- Wells Fargo & Co.: Firm Continues Expansion And Buys Citizens Holdings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

Wells Fargo & Co. continued to expand its operations in Southern California, reaching a definitive agreement to purchase Citizens Holdings, parent of El Camino Bank of Anaheim and Citizens Bank of Costa Mesa. Terms of the deal weren't disclosed. The two banks have a total of six branches in Orange County, one of the state's wealthiest areas, but one where Wells Fargo has only a small presence now.

This month, the San Francisco banking and financial services company agreed to acquire the California branches of Great America Bank for $492 million in a move that gives it the leading retail banking and thrift share of the lucrative San Diego area.
