Who's News: Wood Gundy Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

WOOD GUNDY Inc. (Toronto) -- John Hunkin was appointed deputy chairman and chief executive officer of this Torontobased securities dealer, and Richard Venn was named president. Mr. Hunkin, 45 years old, was president and chief operating officer of Wood Gundy. He succeeds G. Edmund King as chief executive. Mr. King, 56, remains chairman of the brokerage firm. Mr. Venn, 39, was previously president of CIBC Wood Gundy Capital. Wood Gundy is about 62%-owned by Toronto-based Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce. The remaining shares are held by Wood Gundy employees.
