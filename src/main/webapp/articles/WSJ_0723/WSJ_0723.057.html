Watts Industries to Buy Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

NORTH ANDOVER, Mass. -- Watts Industries Inc. said it agreed to acquire Brunswick Corp.'s Circle Seal Controls unit. Terms weren't disclosed. Watts, a water-valve maker, said that the acquisition of Circle Seal, a maker of aviation and commercial valves, is subject to review by government regulators.

Circle Seal had revenue of $32.9 million last year. Watts had sales in the nine months ended March 31 of $217.5 million and net income of $22 million, or $1.73 a share.

Brunswick makes boats, recreation equipment and industrial products.
