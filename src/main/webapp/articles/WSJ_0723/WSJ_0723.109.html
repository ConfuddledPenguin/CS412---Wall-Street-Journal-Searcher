International Brief -- Sanyo Electric Co.: Pretax Profit Gained 1.2% In the Fiscal First Half

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

Sanyo Electric Co. said consolidated pretax profit in the first half ended May 31 rose 1.2% to 19.02 billion yen ($128.6 million) from 18.8 billion yen a year earlier. Net income edged up 2.8% to 5.71 billion yen, or 3.02 yen a share, from 5.56 billion yen, or 2.95 yen a share.

Citing Japan's strong consumer spending, the company reported sales of 676.17 billion yen, a 4.3% gain from 648.17 billion yen. Domestic sales rose 3% to 412.01 billion yen, while overseas sales gained 6.4% to 264.15 billion yen.

Sales of television sets and other visual products gained 14% to 122.35 billion yen.

However, audio products sales declined 10% to 62.47 billion yen. Sales of household electric appliances rose 7.7% to 163.15 billion yen.

For the year ending in November, the company predicted consolidated sales would rise to 1.45 trillion yen from 1.37 trillion yen a year ago. Pretax profit is seen at 47.5 billion yen, rising from 46.57 billion yen, with net income standing at 17.5 billion yen, up from 16.84 billion yen.
