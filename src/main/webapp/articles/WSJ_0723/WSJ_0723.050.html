Southern New England Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

NEW HAVEN, Conn. -- Southern New England Telephone, a unit of Southern New England Telecommunications Corp., said it filed for a rate increase of $86.4 million, or 8.7%. If the state Department of Public Utility Control approves the move following hearings this fall, the increase would take effect in January. The company said it hasn't requested a rate increase in eight years.
