Who's News: Stratford American Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

STRATFORD AMERICAN Corp. (Phoenix, Ariz.) -- Jon P. Held, 36 years old, a former senior audit manager for Price Waterhouse, was named vice president and chief financial officer of this real estate concern that also has interests in oil and gas wells, succeeding Joseph F. Ladrigan, 46, who remains president of the Stratford American Properties subsidiary. Mr. Held also will assume the duties of David E. Hall, who resigned as executive vice president and chief operating officer.
