Oak Industries Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Oak Industries Inc. said it sold its Diamond H. Controls Ltd. unit to Yale & Valor PLC. Terms weren't disclosed, but Oak said the price is "substantially in excess of book value." Oak, a maker of electrical and electronics parts and controls said that it decided to sell the British unit because it prefers to invest proceeds in the U.S. where it can take advantage of corporate tax-loss carryforwards. Diamond H. makes appliance controls.
