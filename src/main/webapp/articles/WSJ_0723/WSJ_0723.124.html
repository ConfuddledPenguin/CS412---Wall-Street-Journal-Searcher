Who's News: Medical Dynamics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

MEDICAL DYNAMICS Inc. (Englewood, Colo.) -- Van A. Horsley, formerly chief financial officer of this maker of medical video equipment, was named president and chief executive officer and was elected a director, increasing board membership to four. As president, he succeeds Jo Brehm, who became vice president, sales and administration, and as chief executive, he succeeds Dr. Edwin L. Adair, who remains chairman.
