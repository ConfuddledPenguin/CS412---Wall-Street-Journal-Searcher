Firm Extends Note Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

CINCINNATI -- Great American Communications Co. said it has extended the expiration date of its offer to purchase all the 13 1/4% senior extended reset notes issued by its GACC Holding unit to July 25. The original expiration date was July 3. About $37 million principal amount of reset notes have been tendered to date.
