Mazda to Recall Some Cars

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Mazda Motor Corp. said it is recalling 102,548 of its 1986 and 1987 Mazda 626 models because of a possible problem with the ignition switch. The Japanese auto maker said the ignition switch could wear out after prolonged use. If this happens, the windshield wipers, windshield washer, engine-cooling fan, heater blower and air-conditioner compressor will not operate.

Mazda said owners should return the cars to dealers for inspection.
