  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

WHO'S NEWS: News Corp.'s Fox Broadcasting Co. named Anita Klever senior vice president of Fox Network News. The venture, scheduled to begin within the next year, will provide news to Fox's affiliated stations nationwide. Ms. Klever, who was vice president of news and program services at Conus Communications, will design and organize the news service.
