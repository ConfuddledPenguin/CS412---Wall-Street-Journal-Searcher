Italy Producer Prices Are Flat

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/23/90

MILAN -- Italy's industrial producer price index was unchanged in May from April and was up 3.6% from a year earlier, the state statistical institute Istat said. The May increase followed a monthly increase of 0.1% in April, when the year-toyear increase stood at 4.0%.

Istat also reported that the wholesale price index fell 0.4% in May from April but rose 3.9% from a year earlier.

The May increase in wholesale prices followed a monthly increase of 0.2% in April, when the index was up 4.4% from a year earlier.
