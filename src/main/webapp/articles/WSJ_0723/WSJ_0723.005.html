HEI Offer to Columbia Hospital

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Columbia Hospital Corp. said shareholders of HEI Corp. have tendered about 4.8 million shares, or about 98% of HEI shares outstanding, in connection with Columbia's $4.50-a-share, or $22 million, offer for HEI. Both companies own and operate hospitals. Columbia also makes medical equipment.
