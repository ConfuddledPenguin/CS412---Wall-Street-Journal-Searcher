Rocky Mountain Helicopters

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

PROVO, Utah -- Rocky Mountain Helicopters Inc. said it filed an initial public offering with the Securities and Exchange Commission to sell 1.5 million shares of stock at prices ranging from $8.50 to $9.50 a share. The offering will be underwritten by Blunt Ellis & Leowi Inc., Milwaukee, and 300,000 shares will be sold by shareholders. Proceeds from the sale of the remaining shares will be used to repay certain indebtedness.

Rocky Mountain provides air transport services to hospitals and ambulance operations in its region.
