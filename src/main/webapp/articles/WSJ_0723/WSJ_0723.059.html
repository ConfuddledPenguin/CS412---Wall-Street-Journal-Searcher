A&P to Buy Stores in Canada

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

MONTVALE, N.J. -- Great Atlantic & Pacific Tea Co. said it agreed to acquire 69 supermarkets in Ontario from Canadian food retailer Steinberg Inc. Terms weren't disclosed. The supermarkets to be purchased include 58 Miracle Marts and 11 Ultra Mart Food and Drug stores, most of which are located in the Toronto area. A&P, which is majority owned by Tengelmann Group of West Germany, operates 1,218 supermarkets in the U.S. and Canada.
