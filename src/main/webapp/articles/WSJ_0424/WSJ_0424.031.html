Lone Star Industries Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Lone Star Industries Inc. said it sold 49% of its interest in a joint cement-manufacturing venture to its partner, Centex Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. The manufacturer of cement and other construction materials said it sold its interest in Mountain Cement Co., of Laramie, Wyo., as part of a continuing restructuring program. Lone Star also sold a cement-distribution terminal and related facilities at Salt Lake City to Centex, a Dallas-based home builder and general contractor.
