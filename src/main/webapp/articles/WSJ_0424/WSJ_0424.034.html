Elf to Buy BP's French Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

PARIS -- France's state-controlled oil group Societe Nationale Elf Aquitaine said one of its subsidiaries agreed to acquire all of British Petroleum Co.'s exploration and production assets in France. Terms weren't disclosed. The assets include 28 exploration permits and nine production licenses, Elf said. The oil companies will complete the transaction July 1.

Elf added that the acquisition is subject to approval by French authorities.
