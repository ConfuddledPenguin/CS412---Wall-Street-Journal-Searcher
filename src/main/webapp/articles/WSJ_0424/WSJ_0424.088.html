AMES DEPARTMENT STORES Inc. (Rocky Hill, Conn.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its rating on $355 million of this discount department store company's subordinated debt to triple-C-plus from single-B-plus. The rating concern cited "growing uncertainty" that Ames can correct violations of its bank credit agreement, as well as "dimmed" prospects for a turnaround of the company's operating problems. S&P said Ames's subordinated debt remains on its CreditWatch list with negative implications.
