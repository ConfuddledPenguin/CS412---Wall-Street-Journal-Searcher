Business Brief -- Varian Associates Inc.: High-Technology Firm's Net Fell 35% in Second Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

Varian Associates Inc., Palo Alto, Calif., said net income declined 35% in the fiscal second quarter to $7.8 million, or 40 cents a share, from $12 million, or 58 cents a share, a year earlier. The high-technology concern said revenue in the period ended March 30 dropped 7.6% to $331.5 million from $358.6 million.

The company said the earnings decline primarily reflects lower results from semiconductor equipment operations, where weakened demand and lower profit margins on key new products combined to hurt profit. The company said it is attempting to reposition a number of its strategies and business, but said the efforts are too premature to discuss in detail.

In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, Varian closed at $23.375, down 50 cents.

In the first half, net fell 28% to $15.4 million, or 79 cents a share, from $21.4 million, or $1.02 a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose to $649.2 million from $646 million.
