International Brief -- Ratners Group PLC: Pretax Profit for Fiscal '90 Rose 41% to $198.4 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

Ratners Group PLC of Britain reported that for the year ended Feb. 3, pretax profit surged 41% to #121 million ($198.4 million) from #86 million a year earlier, bolstered by strong sales at most of the jewelry company's units, and exceeding some market forecasts. Profit attributable to shareholders -- after taxes and extraordinary items -- increased 46% to #79.1 million from #54.2 million. The latest results included an extraordinary gain of #94,000 from an office sale, less costs related to termination of a business. The year-earlier period didn't have any special items. Earnings per share rose 14% to 30.2 pence from 26.5 pence.

Sales increased 41% to #898 million from #635 million.

Ratners recommended a final dividend of 7.50 pence a share, increasing the total payout for the year 27% to 9.50 pence from 7.50 pence.

Overall operating profit rose 40% to #129 million. In Britain and Ireland, operating profit increased 37% to #91.1 million, on a 39% sales jump to #649 million. In the U.S., operating profit rose 48% to #38.4 million, on a 47% sales increase to #249 million.
