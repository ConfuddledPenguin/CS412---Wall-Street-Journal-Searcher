Rohr Industries Suit Is Settled

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

CHULA VISTA, Calif. -- Rohr Industries Inc. said the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority agreed to pay it $11 million to settle an eight-year-old lawsuit. Rohr said the payment will reimburse the aerospace supplier for interest it paid to finance the manufacture of 300 subway cars for the transit authority under a 1972 contract.

Rohr later sued the transit authority over defects in the specifications for the subway cars, charging they cost the company $54.3 million. Its claims for expenses, other than for interest costs, were resolved earlier for $12.3 million.
