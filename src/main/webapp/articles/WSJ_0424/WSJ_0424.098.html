Business Brief -- Ashland Oil Inc.: Oil Refiner Reports Profit Of $913,000 for 2nd Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

Ashland Oil Inc., squeezed by weak refinery margins, reported profit of $913,000, or two cents a share, for the second quarter ended March 31. That compares with a year-ago loss of $13.8 million, or 25 cents a share, which included a $9 million charge related to an arbitration settlement between Ashland's Riley Stoker Corp. unit and a customer. Sales rose 11% to $1.97 billion from $1.78 billion.

The Ashland, Ky., oil refiner said refinery margins -- the difference between crude-oil prices and petroleum products -- were strong in January but deteriorated badly in February, resulting in a $2.3 million operating loss at its Ashland Petroleum unit.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Ashland stock closed at $35.50 a share, down 50 cents.
