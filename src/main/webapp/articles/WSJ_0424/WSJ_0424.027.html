Braniff Transfer Is Approved

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

ORLANDO, Fla. -- A federal bankruptcy judge approved the transfer of defunct Braniff Inc.'s operating certificate and trade name to BN Air Inc. for $313,000. BN Air is a newly formed company whose chairman and chief executive officer is Jeffrey Chodorow, formerly a director of Braniff and chairman of the airline's parent, BIA-COR Holdings Inc. A Braniff spokeswoman said BN plans to rehire former Braniff employees and begin scheduled and charter flights in late summer, subject to regulatory approvals.
