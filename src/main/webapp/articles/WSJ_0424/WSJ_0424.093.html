Raw-Steel Output Fell 3.2% Last Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 3.2% last week to 1,954,000 tons from 2,019,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 4.9% from the 2,054,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 86.9% of its capability last week, compared with 89.8% the previous week and 92.9% a year ago.

The American Iron and Steel Institute reported:

The capability utilization rate is a calculation designed to indicate at what percent of its production capability the industry is operating in a given week.
