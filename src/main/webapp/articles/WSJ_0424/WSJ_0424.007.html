Asea Brown Unit Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Asea Brown Boveri Inc. said a subsidiary signed a $115 million turnkey contract to build a wastewater treatment plant in Egypt. The award, by the U.S. Agency for International Development, is the last major piece of the Greater Cairo Wastewater System. Asea Brown is a U.S. unit of ABB Group, Zurich, the $25 billion electrical engineering company.
