Addington Resources Results

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

ASHLAND, Ky. -- Addington Resources Inc. said it expects first-quarter profit from continuing operations to be between 15 cents and 20 cents a share, which it said is below analysts' expectations. In the year-ago period, per-share net was 15 cents. Addington, a coal mining, water transportation and construction company, attributed the disappointing first-quarter results to difficult mining conditions in West Virginia and Eastern Kentucky, higher fuel costs and unusually heavy rain in Western Kentucky.
