Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

El Paso Natural Gas Co., a unit of Burlington Resources Inc., shelf offering of up to $300 million of debt securities.

John Deere Capital Corp., a unit of Deere & Co., shelf offering of up to $400 million of medium-term notes, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Goldman, Sachs & Co.

Whirlpool Corp., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities and warrants to purchase debt securities, via Goldman Sachs.
