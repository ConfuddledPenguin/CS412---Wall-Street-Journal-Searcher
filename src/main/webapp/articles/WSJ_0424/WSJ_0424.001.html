Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- George M.C. Fisher, chairman and chief executive officer of Motorola Inc., Beverly Sills Greenough, president of the New York City Opera, and Frank P. Popoff, president and chief executive officer of Dow Chemical Co. were elected directors of this financial-services company.
