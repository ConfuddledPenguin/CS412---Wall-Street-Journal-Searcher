Dividend News: Poughkeepsie Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

POUGHKEEPSIE SAVINGS BANK said it would omit its second-quarter dividend. The Poughkeepsie thrift, citing a troubled real estate investment portfolio, also reported a first-quarter net loss of $7.8 million, or $2.21 a share. Poughkeepsie Savings, which operates 11 branch offices in New York's Hudson Valley, said net interest income for the first quarter was $7.7 million, compared with $9.6 million a year earlier. But latest-quarter results reflected an $8 million increase in loan-loss reserves and a $2.9 million stock-transaction loss. A year earlier, Poughkeepsie had a profit of $1.9 million, or 53 cents a share. Poughkeepsie said that the recently declared dividend of 15 cents a share will be payable May 1. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Poughkeepsie stock fell 12.5 cents to $6.125.
