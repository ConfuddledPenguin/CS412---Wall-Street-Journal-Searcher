AMI Sells Four Swiss Hospitals

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- American Medical International Inc. said it sold its four Swiss hospitals for about 250 million Swiss francs (US$168.4 million) to Klinik Hirslanden AG of Zurich. The U.S. hospital concern has been selling assets to reduce debt it assumed in connection with its recently completed acquisition by IMA Holdings Corp., an investor group. The sale is subject to the receipt of certain waivers from Swiss authorities, American Medical said.
