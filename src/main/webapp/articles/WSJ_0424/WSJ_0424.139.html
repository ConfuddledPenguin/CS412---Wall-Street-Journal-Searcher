International Brief -- Aquascutum Group PLC: Japan's Renown Is to Buy Retailer for $121 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

Aquascutum Group PLC, a Regent-Street retailing institution in London, will be controlled by Japan's Renown Inc. under a #73.8 million ($121 million) acquisition plan. The British retailer, honored this year with the Queen's Award for Export Achievement, is also known as the preferred supplier of British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher's suits.

The friendly bid includes an offer of 200 pence ($3.28) a share for each ordinary "A" share and of 550 pence for each of the closely held regular ordinary shares.

Gerald Abrahams, chairman and chief executive officer of Aquascutum, said he welcomes "these offers which the board believes are in the best interest of the company and shareholders."
