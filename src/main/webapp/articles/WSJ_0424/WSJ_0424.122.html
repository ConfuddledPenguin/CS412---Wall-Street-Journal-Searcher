Business Brief -- ATI Medical Inc.: Roxbury Partners, 2 Others Raise Their Stake to 10.59%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

Roxbury Partners and its partners, Jonathan Bowers and Christopher Middendorf of Washington, D.C., said they raised their ownership in ATI Medical Inc. to about 10.59% from 9.34%. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Roxbury said it holds 564,800 common shares, including net purchases between March 6 and April 19 for $9.125 to $10.375 a share.

In Las Vegas, a spokesman for ATI wasn't concerned about the increased stake. "They've been good shareholders," he said.

ATI rents medical equipment to 3,200 hospitals across the U.S. It has about 5.3 million common shares outstanding.

In American Stock Exchange composite trading, ATI was unchanged at $10.375.
