Seagull Natural Gas Discovery

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

HOUSTON -- Seagull Energy Corp. said it tested a natural gas discovery well in the Gulf of Mexico about 25 miles southeast of Freeport, Texas. The well, SGO No. 1 OCS G-8131, tested at a daily rate of 6.4 million cubic feet of gas from depths of 6,598 feet to 6,627 feet. The well tested at a daily rate of 6.5 million cubic feet at depths of 6,854 feet to 6,877 feet.

Seagull's exploration and production subsidiary operates the property and has a 50% working interest. Ashland Exploration Inc. and Columbus Mills Inc. each hold 25%.
