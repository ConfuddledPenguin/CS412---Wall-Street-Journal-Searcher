South Africa's Inflation Rate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa -- South Africa's inflation rate as measured by the consumer price index rose 14.9% in March from a year earlier, the government said. The index rose to 196.4 points from 170.9 in March 1989; it was up 1.6% from 193.3 in February. The February year-to-year increase was 14.9%.

The March food index was 210.2 points, up 15.1% from 182.6 in March 1989; the February year-to-year increase was 15.7%.
