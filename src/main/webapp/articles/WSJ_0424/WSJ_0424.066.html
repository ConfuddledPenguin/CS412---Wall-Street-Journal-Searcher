Who's News: MAF Bancorp Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

MAF BANCORP Inc. (Clarendon Hills, Ill.) -- Joe F. Hanauer, president of Combined Investments L.P., an investment-management business, was elected a director of this bank holding company and its subsidiary, MidAmerica Federal Savings Bank. Mr. Hanauer, 52 years old, fills a vacancy on the board.
