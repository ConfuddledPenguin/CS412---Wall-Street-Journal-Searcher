  • Date: 04/24/90

NEW ORLEANS -- Louisiana Land & Exploration Co. said it discovered natural gas in the western Gulf of Mexico. The well, OCS-G 11318 Galveston Block 331 No. 1, tested at a daily rate of 11.1 million cubic feet of natural gas with 263 barrels of condensate from depths between 10,400 and 10,456.

Louisiana Land operates the well and has a 50% working interest in it. Other partners are a CMS Energy Corp. unit, with a 25% working interest; an Equitable Resources Inc. unit, with 12.5%; and a PacifiCorp unit, with 12.5%.
