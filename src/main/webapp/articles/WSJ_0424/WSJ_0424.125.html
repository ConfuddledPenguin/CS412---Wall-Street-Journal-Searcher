Business Brief -- Armstrong World Industries Inc.: First-Period Net Rose 74%; One-Time Gains Are Factor

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

Armstrong World Industries Inc. reported first-quarter earnings of $65.2 million, or $1.45 a share, a 74% increase from year-ago net of $37.4 million, or 81 cents a share. The 1990 quarter's earnings include one-time gains totaling $24.5 million, or 59 cents a share, from an accounting change and the sale of woodlands. In the 1989 quarter, net included a gain of $2.7 million, or six cents a share, from settlement of asbestos litigation, and profits of $3.4 million, or eight cents a share, from discontinued operations.

The Lancaster, Pa., building products concern reported sales of $640.4 million for the quarter. In the year-ago quarter, Armstrong had sales from continuing operations of $618.7 million.

Armstrong attributed the strong performance to better-than-expected profit margins in manufacturing. The company said that sales and profit increased in four of its major divisions, including the flagship flooring unit.

Armstrong shares closed yesterday at $33.625, up $1, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
