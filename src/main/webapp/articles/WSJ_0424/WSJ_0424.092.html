Dividend News: Baybanks Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

BAYBANKS Inc., which earlier said it would consider the declaration of its quarterly dividend at the company's annual meeting on Thursday, declared a quarterly dividend of 45 cents a share, payable June 1 to stock of record May 14. The Boston bank holding company said its decision "was reached after careful analysis and is based on our current expectations for the future. Decisions on future dividends will be made based on the circumstances prevailing at the time." A spokesman said the company said it is still expecting a final report from a regulatory examination "in due course." He said the on-site review of the company was concluded in March. Federal and state bank examiners have been studying the company's loan portfolio.
