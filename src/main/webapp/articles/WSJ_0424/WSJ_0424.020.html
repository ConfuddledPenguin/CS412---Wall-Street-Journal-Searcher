Excalibur Technologies Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

ALBUQUERQUE, N.M. -- Excalibur Technologies Inc. said it agreed to sell 1.2 million shares of its common stock to New York City-based investment firm Allen & Company Inc. for $10 a share, or $12 million. Proceeds from the sale will be used to expand several parts of Excalibur's sales, marketing and support efforts, the company said. Excalibur, a computer software concern, said it has 6,774,979 shares outstanding.

A spokesman for Excalibur added that Allen & Co. already has purchased half the 1.2 million shares and is expected to buy the remaining shares within three weeks. With the purchase of the Excalibur shares, Allen & Co. will increase its stake in Excalibur to 56% from 47%, the spokesman said.
