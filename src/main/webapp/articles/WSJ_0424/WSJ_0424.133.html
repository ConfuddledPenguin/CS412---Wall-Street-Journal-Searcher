Who's News: Guinness Names Kettle President and Chief Of Schenley Industries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Guinness PLC named Graham Kettle president and chief executive officer of Schenley Industries Inc., a Dallas distilling and distributing subsidiary of the United Distillers unit of Guinness. The 42-year-old Mr. Kettle currently is managing director of Arthur Bell Distillers, which oversees sales and marketing for United Distillers in the United Kingdom.

In his new posts, he succeeds James Espey, who has been acting president and chief executive officer since Alexander Berk, 40, resigned in March to pursue other business interests.

Mr. Espey, 47, becomes chairman of Schenley, while continuing as regional managing director of United's North American operations here. Joseph Heid, 43, was named vice chairman of Schenley; he will continue as chief financial officer of United Distillers America.

United, like its parent Guinness, is based in London. Guinness has brewing and distilling interests.
