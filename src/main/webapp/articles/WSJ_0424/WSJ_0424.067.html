PPG Acquires French Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

PITTSBURGH -- PPG Industries Inc., a paints, coatings and adhesives maker, said it acquired Establissements Robert Ouvrie S.A. of France, a producer of surfactants, which are surface-active agents, and paper specialty chemicals. Terms weren't disclosed. PPG said the French company has annual sales exceeding $11 million.
