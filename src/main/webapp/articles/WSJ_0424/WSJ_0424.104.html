A STORY in the April 5 edition reported that W.R. Grace & Co. had been found negligent in a chemical-dumping case and had then settled out of court. The settlement, however, was reached only after the judge had vacated the negligence verdict and ordered a new trial. (See: "Law -- Legal Beat: Arbitration Award Is Made Under RICO" -- WSJ April 5, 1990)

KEMPER CORP. expects to report that its securities operations had a "substantially smaller" loss in the first quarter of 1990 than the operations incurred in the fourth quarter of 1989. In yesterday's edition, the comparison was misstated.

(See: "Kemper's Reserve of $19.7 Million Is Weather Protection" -- WSJ April 23, 1990)
