Oneok Unit Plans Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Oklahoma Natural Gas Co. said it signed a letter of understanding to buy Oklahoma transmission and distribution properties from Lone Star Gas Co. for $34 million. Oklahoma Natural Gas is a unit of Oneok Inc. Lone Star, a unit of Enserch Corp., Dallas, expects a pretax gain of about $9 million on the sale.

The proposed transaction is subject to execution of a definitive agreement and approval of both companies' boards and of federal and state regulators.
