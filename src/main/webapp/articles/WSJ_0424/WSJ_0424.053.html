Hallwood Energy's Swap Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

DALLAS -- Hallwood Energy Partners L.P. said it extended its exchange offer for 18 non-traded oil and gas partnerships to 5 p.m. Monday, Dallas time. The company is offering one unit of Hallwood Energy Partners for each $15 of exchange value of the non-traded partnerships. Hallwood said the partnerships have reserves valued at a total of $66 million.
