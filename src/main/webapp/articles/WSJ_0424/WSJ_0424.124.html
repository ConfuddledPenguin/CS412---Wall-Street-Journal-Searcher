Who's News: Fletcher Challenge Unit Makes Appointments

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

VANCOUVER, British Columbia -- Fletcher Challenge Canada Ltd. said Douglas Whitehead, 44-year-old vice president, packaging, at the forest products concern, was designated to be senior vice president and chief operating officer, succeeding George Pearson, 62. The appointment is to take effect in July, when Mr. Pearson, executive vice president, operating chief and senior vice president, pulp and paper, plans to retire.

Fletcher Challenge Canada, 72%-owned by Fletcher Challenge Ltd. of New Zealand, also appointed Keith Winrow, 49, to succeed Blair Beaton, 62, as senior vice president, finance, in May, when Mr. Beaton will become senior vice president, special projects. Mr. Winrow is vice president and controller of the Canadian Pacific Forest Products Ltd. unit of Montreal-based Canadian Pacific Ltd., an industrial holding concern.
