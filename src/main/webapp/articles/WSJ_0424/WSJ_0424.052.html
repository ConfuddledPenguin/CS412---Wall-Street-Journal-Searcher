Japanese Auto Output Falls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

TOKYO -- Japan's production of cars, trucks and buses in March declined 2.8% from a year earlier to 1,234,000 units, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers Association said. Despite the fall in March, the overall output of vehicles produced in Japan during the year ended March 31 reached 12,954,000 units, up 1.0% from a year earlier to a record level. The previous record of 12,819,000 units was set in the prior fiscal year.

In March alone, sluggish truck and bus output hindered overall production growth, while car production posted a steady 9.4% year-on-year rise.
