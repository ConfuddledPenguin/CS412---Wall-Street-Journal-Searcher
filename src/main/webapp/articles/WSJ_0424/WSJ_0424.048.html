Vons Cos. First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/24/90

EL MONTE, Calif. -- Vons Cos. posted first-quarter net income of $15.2 million, or 39 cents a share, compared with a net loss of $13.4 million, or 35 cents a share, a year earlier. The retail supermarket chain said the latest net included an extraordinary gain of $7 million, or 18 cents a share, from a tax-loss carry-forward. Sales rose 3%, to $1.2 billion from $1.1 billion.
