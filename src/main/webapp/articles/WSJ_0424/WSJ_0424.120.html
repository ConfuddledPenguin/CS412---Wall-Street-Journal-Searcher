Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/24/90

MAGAZINES: CMP Publications Inc. will cease publication of Long Island Monthly; a search for a suitable buyer for the upscale consumer magazine proved fruitless. CMP, which publishes 15 major business-to-business trade publications, decided to stick with its core business.

CABLE: Intercontinental Television Group Inc. plans to launch a 24-hour multilanguage cable network in July. International Channel will feature Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Cambodian and other Asian-language programming, as well as French, Italian, Farsi and Hebrew programs.

TV: Ventura Entertainment Group Ltd. and Tokyo's Marubeni Corp. announced a joint venture to produce 14 hours of rock 'n' roll TV programming for world-wide distribution. Terms weren't disclosed.
