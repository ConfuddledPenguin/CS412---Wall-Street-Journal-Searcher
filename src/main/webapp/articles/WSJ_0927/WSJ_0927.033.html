United Illuminating Buy-Backs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

NEW HAVEN, Conn. -- United Illuminating Co., a utility, said it purchased $97.9 million of its 12% debentures, due 2017, as part of a program to reduce debt. The company also purchased $80.4 million principal amount of its 10.5% debentures, due 1995, and $11.8 million principal amount of its 10.25% debentures, due 2000, in open-market transactions. It estimated that these transactions will produce pretax savings of nearly $6 million.

United Illuminating provides service in the New Haven and Bridgeport areas.
