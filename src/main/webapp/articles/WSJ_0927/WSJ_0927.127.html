Business Brief -- Gradco Systems Inc. Printer Products Business Spinoff Will Be Explored

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

Gradco Systems Inc. said it will explore the possibility of spinning off its printer products business to shareholders. The Irvine, Calif., company said the plan would divide its more profitable copier products business, based in Japan, and printer products operations into two publicly traded companies. Printer products contributed about 25% of Gradco's $90 million in revenue last year, a spokesman said. For the year ended March 31, Gradco had a loss of $28.1 million, including a $23 million charge for restructuring and consolidation of operations.

Gradco said that the businesses' different development cycles and risk profiles have confused investors, and that separating them would "focus market attention" on the potential of each and maximize share value. Gradco said it is reviewing the feasibility of the plan, including any possible tax consequences. It said it won't adopt or implement any definitive plan before the annual meeting, adjourned earlier this month, reconvenes Oct. 12.
