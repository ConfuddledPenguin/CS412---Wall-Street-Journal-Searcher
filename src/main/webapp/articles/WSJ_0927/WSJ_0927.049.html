Who's News: Maxxam Names Seidl President to Succeed Leone on Retirement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

HOUSTON -- Maxxam Inc. said its board named John M. Seidl, 51-year-old chairman and chief executive officer of its Kaiser Aluminum & Chemical Corp. subsidiary, president of Maxxam. He succeeds William C. Leone, 66, who announced in March that he would retire this month. Mr. Leone continues as Maxxam's vice chairman, a director and member of its executive committee. Mr. Seidl remains in the top posts at Kaiser Aluminum.

Maxxam also named another Kaiser Aluminum executive, John T. La Duc, 47, senior vice president and chief financial officer of Maxxam. He succeeds James V. Iaco Jr., 46, who announced in June that he would resign because of the company's relocation to Houston from Los Angeles, recently completed. Mr. La Duc continues as Kaiser Aluminum's vice president and chief financial officer.

Maxxam is engaged in aluminum, forest products and real estate operations.
