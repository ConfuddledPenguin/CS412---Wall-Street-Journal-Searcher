Witco Puts Richardson on Block

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

NEW YORK -- Witco Corp., a specialty chemicals and petroleum company, said that it plans to sell its Richardson Co. subsidiary, a maker of injection molded plastic and rubber battery containers and accessories, and that it engaged Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. to assist in the sale. In 1989, Richardson had sales of $66.3 million. Witco had sales of $798 million for the six months ended June 30. A Witco spokesman said the Richardson business, part of an acquisition made in the early 1980s, "doesn't fit our strategic core businesses" and might be more appropriate for a battery maker or injection molding company.
