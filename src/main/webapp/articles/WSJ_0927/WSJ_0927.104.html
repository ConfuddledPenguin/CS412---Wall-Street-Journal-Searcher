Two Newsprint Firms Follow Price Boosts

  • Author: A Wall Street Journal News Roundup
  • Date: 09/27/90

Two big Canadian newsprint producers matched price increases announced Monday by Stone Container Corp. Canadian Pacific Forest Products Ltd., Montreal, and MacMillan Bloedel Ltd., Vancouver, British Columbia, said they will raise newsprint prices about 5% effective Jan. 1. As reported previously, Chicago-based Stone Container said it will raise its benchmark Eastern U.S. list price for newsprint by $35 a metric ton, or 5.1%, to $720 a metric ton effective Jan. 1. Stone Container raised prices by similar amounts in other markets. A metric ton equals 2,204.6 pounds.

Analysts and industry executives expect other companies to match the increases. Most major North American newsprint concerns raised prices about 5% in June.
