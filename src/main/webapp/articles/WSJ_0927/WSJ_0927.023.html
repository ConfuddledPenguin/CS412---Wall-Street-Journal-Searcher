Lockheed Unit's EPA Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

CALABASAS, Calif. -- Lockheed Corp. said a unit received two contracts from the Environmental Protection Agency with a combined value of about $200 million over five years. Lockheed said the contracts are for environmental monitoring and remote sensing services at the agency's Environmental Monitoring Systems Laboratory in Las Vegas. Lockheed said the work would be performed by its Houston-based Lockheed Engineering & Science unit.
