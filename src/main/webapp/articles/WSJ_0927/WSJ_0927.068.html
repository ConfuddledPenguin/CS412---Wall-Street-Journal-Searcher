Who's News: Standard Commercial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

STANDARD COMMERCIAL Corp. (Wilson, N.C.) -- J. Alec G. Murray, the 54-year-old chairman of the company's wool division, was named Standard Commercial's vice chairman and chief executive officer, succeeding Ery W. Kehaya as chief executive. Mr. Kehaya will remain chairman of the leaf tobacco and wool concern.
