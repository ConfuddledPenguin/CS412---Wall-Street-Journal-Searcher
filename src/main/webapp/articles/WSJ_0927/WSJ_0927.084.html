Who's News: Moore Medical Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

MOORE MEDICAL Corp. (New Britain, Conn.) -- Mark E. Karp, 43-year-old president and chief operating officer of this distributor of pharmaceutical products and medical and surgical supplies, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer, effective Jan. 1, to succeed Jerald K. Rome, 55, who will remain chairman.
