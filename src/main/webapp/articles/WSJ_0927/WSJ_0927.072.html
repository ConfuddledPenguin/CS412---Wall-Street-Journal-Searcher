Spain's Merchandise Trade Gap

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

MADRID -- Spain's merchandise trade deficit on a customs basis widened 1.8% to 2.244 trillion pesetas ($22.78 billion) in the first eight months from 2.205 trillion pesetas a year earlier, the Customs Directorate said. In August, the trade gap narrowed 25% to 216.6 billion pesetas from 290.1 billion pesetas a year earlier. The directorate said an 11% drop in imports in August to 523.9 billion pesetas from 589.6 billion pesetas a year earlier helped shrink the monthly deficit.
