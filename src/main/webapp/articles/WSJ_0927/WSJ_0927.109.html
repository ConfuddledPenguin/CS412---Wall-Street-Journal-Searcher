Vallen Corp. Sets Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

HOUSTON -- Vallen Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to purchase the business and assets of Instrument Services Inc., a Houston concern specializing in sales and service of gas-detection and environmental-monitoring equipment. Terms weren't disclosed. Vallen, an industrial health and safety products concern, said Instrument Services had sales of $2.6 million in 1989.
