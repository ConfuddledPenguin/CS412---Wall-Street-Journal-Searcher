Who's News: United Biscuits Holdings PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

UNITED BISCUITS (HOLDINGS) PLC (Middlesex, England) -- John Warren, 37 years old, was appointed to the board of this British food company, effective in October. Mr. Warren will succeed Jim Blyth, 60, as group finance director of the company in January 1991. Mr. Blyth retires Dec. 31. Mr. Warren is currently group finance director designate.
