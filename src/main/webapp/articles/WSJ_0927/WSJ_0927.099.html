Who's News: Interstate/Johnson Names Dalton Chief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- Interstate/Johnson Lane Inc. named Parks H. Dalton chairman and chief executive officer. Mr. Dalton, 60 years old, had been chairman emeritus of the company. He succeeds J. Craighill Redwine, 49, who resigned in late August.

Interstate/Johnson, one of the largest brokerage firms in the Southeast, has had several quarters of declining revenue and widening losses. In the quarter ended June 30, the company had a loss of $1.4 million, or 20 cents a share, on revenue of $31.9 million. The loss included a $750,000 charge related to litigation.

At the time of Mr. Redwine's resignation, the company said it was reviewing its businesses and options to develop a "strategic direction."

Mr. Dalton had been chief executive officer of Interstate Securities from 1968 to 1986 and chairman through January 1988 before the company's merger with Johnson Lane. In addition to his position as chairman emeritus at Interstate/Johnson Lane, Mr. Dalton has served recently as chairman of the executive committee of the company's board.
