Zapata Corp. to Redeem Bonds

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

HOUSTON -- Zapata Corp. said it would redeem on Nov. 1 about $27.6 million face amount of its 8 5/8% U.S. government guaranteed ship financing bonds, due June 15, 1996. The energy company said it will pay 101.815% of the principal, plus interest accrued from June 15, or about $1,050.73 for each of the $1,000 bonds.
