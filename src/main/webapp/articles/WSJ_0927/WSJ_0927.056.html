Dividend News: Intermark Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

INTERMARK Inc. said it won't pay its quarterly dividend due in October on its common or its preferred shares. The La Jolla, Calif., holding company was scheduled to pay a three cent-a-share dividend on its common and a 2.75 cent a share dividend on its preferred. Intermark said it took the action because it hasn't yet been able to "fully evaluate" its dividend policy in light of its merger with Triton Group Ltd. on Aug. 30. The company said it also needs to evaluate its debt and the expected restructuring of its balance sheet.
