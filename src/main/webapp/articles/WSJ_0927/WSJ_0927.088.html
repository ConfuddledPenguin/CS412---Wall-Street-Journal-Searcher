Who's News: Investors Savings Corp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

INVESTORS SAVINGS Corp. (Minneapolis) -- James M. Burkholder was named president and chief executive officer, succeeding Thomas R. Fritz, who resigned from the thrift holding company in August. Mr. Burkholder, 47 years old and a cofounder of the company, was formerly chief operating officer, chief financial officer and an executive vice president. The chairman's title, formerly held by Mr. Fritz, hasn't been filled, Mr. Burkholder said, adding that the board doesn't plan to name a chairman in the near future. Also, Investors named senior vice president and controller Lynn Bueltel, 40, as chief financial officer.
