Who's News: Brown & Sharpe Manufacturing Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

BROWN & SHARPE MANUFACTURING Co. (North Kingstown, R.I.) -- Fred Stuber, 52 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this tool maker, effective Jan. 1, 1991. He will succeed Donald A. Roach, who announced earlier that he planned to retire at the end of the current year. Mr. Stuber is currently vice president and managing director of Tesa S.A., a subsidiary in Renens, Switzerland. In addition, Mr. Stuber will become a director of the company, expanding the board membership to eight. Mr. Roach will continue as a director.
