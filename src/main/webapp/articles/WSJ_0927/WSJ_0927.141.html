Business Brief -- Pier 1 Imports Inc.: Stake in Sunbelt Nursery Bought for $22.7 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

After buying the Sunbelt Nursery Group Inc. from Tandy Corp. in 1975 and spinning it off 10 years later, Pier 1 Imports Inc. bought back a controlling stake for $22.7 million. Pier 1 purchased 1,891,896 shares, or 50.4%, of Sunbelt common stock outstanding at $12 a share from Intermark Inc. of La Jolla, Calif., which is also Pier 1's largest shareholder. Pier 1 will begin a tender offer no later than Oct. 2 for the approximately 1,860,000 remaining shares of Sunbelt at $12 a share.

Pier 1, a home-furnishing retailer and importer, will operate Sunbelt as a subsidiary and combine certain aspects of both businesses, said Pier 1 Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Clark A. Johnson. Both companies are based in Fort Worth.

Separately, Intermark said it will not pay a quarterly cash dividend for October on its common or participating preferred stock. Intermark paid a three-cent dividend last quarter.
