Who's News: Reuters Holdings PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

REUTERS HOLDINGS PLC (London) -- Rob Rowley, 41 years old, was named an executive director, succeeding Nigel Judah, 59, as finance director of this British news and financial information company. Mr. Rowley was previously deputy finance director and joint secretary. Mr. Judah resigned as finance director to reduce his workload after a bout of ill health, but he remains company secretary. Mark Woods, 38, editor in chief, also was named an executive director.
