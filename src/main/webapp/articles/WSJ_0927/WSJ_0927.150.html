Who's News: LVI Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

LVI GROUP Inc. (New York) -- R. Gene Kemp Jr. was named executive vice president and chief financial officer, a new post, at this interior construction and asbestos abatement company. Mr. Kemp, 46 years old, was previously chief financial officer at George A. Fuller Co., a New York-based building contractor and construction management company.
