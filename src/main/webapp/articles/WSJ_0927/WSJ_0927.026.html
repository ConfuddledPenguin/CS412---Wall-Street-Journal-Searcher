Who's News: Cadbury Schweppes PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

CADBURY SCHWEPPES PLC (London) -- David Jinks, 55 years old, will be named group finance director, and member of the main board of this British soft drink and confectionery group, effective Oct. 27. Mr. Jinks is currently director of finance, operations and control, within group finance, and succeeds Neville Bain, 50, who will leave the company to take up the position of chief executive of Coats Viyella PLC, a British textile group.
