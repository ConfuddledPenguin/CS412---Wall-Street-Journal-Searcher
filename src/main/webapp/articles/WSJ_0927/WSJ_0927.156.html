Business Brief -- J2 Communications: Entertainment Firm Revises Offer for National Lampoon

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

J2 Communications, an entertainment concern, said it modified slightly its $4.7 million offer to acquire National Lampoon Inc. Under terms of the original and the revised offers, shareholders of the New York-based humor magazine would receive two-thirds of a share of J2 common stock plus a warrant for each share of Lampoon currently held. The revised offer lowered the exercise price of the warrant to $3.25 a share from $3.75 a share.

The modification reflects a minor change in the relative value of the two companies' stocks since the agreement was reached in March, J2 said. National Lampoon's shares have fallen to $1.75 bid from $3.125 bid in over-the-counter trading. J2's shares have dropped to $1.875 bid from $3 bid.

Shareholders of both companies will vote on the transaction at meetings set for Oct. 24 in Los Angeles.
