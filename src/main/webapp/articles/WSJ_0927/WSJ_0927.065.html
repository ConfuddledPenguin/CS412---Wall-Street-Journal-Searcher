Japan's Retail Sales Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

TOKYO -- Sales at Japan's department stores and other major retail stores in August rose 7.9% to 1.525 trillion yen ($11.15 billion) from a year earlier, the government said. The August growth followed a 9% year-to-year increase posted in July. Though the pace of growth slowed, compared with the second quarter, in which retail sales grew between 10% and 16%, "Retail sales are on a steady upward trend," an official said. The pace of growth usually slows in August because of seasonal factors.
