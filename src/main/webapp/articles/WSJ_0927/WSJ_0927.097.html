Who's News: Hanson PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

HANSON PLC (London) -- Anthony R. Cotten, 47 years old, deputy chief operating officer U.K., and Peter J. Harper, 55, chairman of Hanson Industrial Services, have been named executive directors of this British industrial conglomerate. Ronald S. Fulford, 56, chairman of Imperial Tobacco, a subsidiary of this group, is appointed to the new position of senior associate director.
