Technology Brief -- Kinetic Concepts Inc.: About 10% of Employees To Be Laid Off by Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

Kinetic Concepts Inc., in restructuring its hospital equipment rental operations, will lay off about 10% of its 1,500 workers and take a third-quarter charge of $6 million to $7 million, or seven cents to eight cents a share, Dr. James R. Leininger, chairman, said. Kinetic Concepts expects net income of $1 million to $1.5 million, or two cents to three cents a share, in the third quarter, compared with $6 million, or 12 cents a share, a year earlier.

Revenue is expected to rise 32% to $59 million to $60 million from $44.6 million, the company said, largely as a result of the acquisition July 2 of Medirec Inc., a Utah medical rental concern with $50 million in annual sales.

The layoffs address excess capacity in the national network of 150 service centers and at headquarters in San Antonio. The retrenchment will account for $1 million to $2 million of the charges.

For the nine months, earnings will be about $13 million, or 25 cents to 26 cents a share, compared with $18.9 million, or 38 cents a share, a year earlier, the company said. Revenue will rise to about $155 million from $130 million, it added.
