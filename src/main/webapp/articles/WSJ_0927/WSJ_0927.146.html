International Brief -- Cie. Generale d'Electricite: French Concern Says Profit For Six Months Fell 24%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

Generale d'Electricite said profit after payments to minority interests was 1.98 billion French francs ($376.3 million) for the first half of the year, down 24% from 2.59 billion francs in the year-earlier period. The French electricity and telecommunications group noted that profit in the year-ago period reflected heavy exceptional gains related to the creation of GECAlsthom S.A., a joint venture with General Electric PLC. General Electric PLC is not related to General Electric Co. of the U.S.

Revenue rose 2% to 68.55 billion francs from 67.17 billion francs.

The company said it expects full-year net profit on a comparable basis to continue to grow at the same pace as in 1989, despite economic uncertainties linked to higher oil prices.
