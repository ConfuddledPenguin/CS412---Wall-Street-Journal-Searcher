International: Enimont Must Stay Intact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

ROME -- Enimont S.p.A. must remain intact and Italian, according to conditions set by the government's Interministerial Committee on Industrial Policy. After a meeting here, the policy committee decided that Enimont's operating units can't be stripped and sold off in parcels if private partner Montedison S.p.A., which holds 40% of the venture, acquires complete control of the public-private chemical partnership.

The state partner, energy agency Ente Nazionale Idrocarburi, or ENI, plans to sell its 40% stake.
