Litton Will Redeem Entire $440 Million Of Floating-Rate Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Litton Industries Inc. said it will redeem all $440 million of its floating-rate subordinated notes due 2000 for their face amount plus interest accrued to Nov. 15. Litton said last month it planned to redeem the notes when it announced a $1 billion offering of zero-coupon bonds. The redemption will be funded with the $258 million net proceeds from that offering and from internal cash, Litton said.

Litton, a diversified technology concern, said the notes will be redeemed at company headquarters here or at the Midwest Securities Exchange Clearing Corp. in New York. Interest on the notes will cease to accrue after the redemption date, Litton said.

"The redemption will strengthen the company's balance sheet and improve our positive cash flow," Litton said. A notice of redemption will be mailed to noteholders within the next few days, the company said.
