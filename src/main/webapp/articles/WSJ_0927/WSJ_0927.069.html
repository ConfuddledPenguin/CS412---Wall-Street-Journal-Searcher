Who's News: Boc Group PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

BOC GROUP PLC (Surrey, London) -- Debajyoti Chatterji, 46 years old, joins the board of this U.K. industrial concern in October. Mr. Chatterji becomes managing director for technology with world-wide responsibility for research and development. Mr. Chatterji, who is currently chief executive for technical activities, will also be responsible for environmental issues. Mr. Chatterji succeeds David J. Craig, 64, currently managing director for engineering and technology, who is retiring.
