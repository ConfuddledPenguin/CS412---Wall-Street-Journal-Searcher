Teledyne Unit Wins Air Force Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

WASHINGTON -- Teledyne Continental Motors Corp., a unit of Teledyne Inc., won a $67.8 million Air Force contract for ground power generators.

LTV Corp. was awarded a $57.2 million Army contract for multiple-launch rocket systems.

Loral Corp. got a $51.5 million Navy contract for helicopter training systems.

United Technologies Corp. was issued a $36.9 million contract for Navy CH-53E helicopters and Army aircraft weapons racks.

Litton Systems Inc., a unit of Litton Industries Inc., got a $31.6 million Navy contract for aircraft instrument repairs.

Rockwell International Corp. received a $30 million Air Force contract for the AC130U gunship program.

BMY Corp., a unit of Harsco Corp., got a $25.9 million Army contract for five-ton trucks.

Target Sportswear Inc. received a $22.3 million Defense Logistics Agency pact for lightweight jackets.

Battelle Memorial Institute got a $20 million Air Force contract for translating foreign-language documents.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. was issued a $19.1 million Air Force contract for C-17 aircraft parts.

Honeywell Inc. received a $15.4 million Army contract for communications equipment.
