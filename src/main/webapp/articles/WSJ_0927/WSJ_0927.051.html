Par Pharmaceutical's Quad Unit Is Barred From Working for U.S.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

SPRING VALLEY, N.Y. -- Quad Pharmaceuticals Inc. was barred by the Defense Logistics Agency from contracting with the federal government until Aug. 31, 1992. The move affects Quad, based in Indianapolis, but not its parent, Par Pharmaceutical Inc.

Par and Quad were fined earlier this year for illegal payments to a Food and Drug Administration official. Both have recalled products amid charges that they changed manufacturing processes without following FDA requirements. The action against Quad follows a two-year investigation of the company.

"While Quad is of course disappointed in this decision, we look forward to the time when we can demonstrate that we are responsible to do business with the federal government," said Kenneth I. Sawyer, president of Par.
