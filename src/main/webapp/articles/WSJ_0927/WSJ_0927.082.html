Who's News: BGS Systems Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/27/90

BGS SYSTEMS Inc. (Waltham, Mass.) -- James S. McGuire, 46 years old, was named to the new position of chief operating officer of this computer software concern. Previously he was vice president of sales and marketing. He will report to Harold S. Schwenk Jr., president and chief executive officer.
