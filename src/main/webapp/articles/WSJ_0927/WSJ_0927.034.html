Kollmorgen Pact Is Terminated

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/27/90

DANBURY, Conn. -- Kollmorgen Corp. said a $20 million contract its Electro-Optical Division received last April to supply periscopes was terminated by the customer. The company wouldn't disclose the name of the customer. It said the customer chose to terminate the contract under a clause in the agreement.

Kollmorgen said that because it has spent "very little" money on the contract, the termination won't have any adverse financial impact on the company. Kollmorgen makes periscopes for the U.S. Navy and for military customers in other countries.
