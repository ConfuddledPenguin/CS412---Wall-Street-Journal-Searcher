Who's News: Newport News Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

NEWPORT NEWS SAVINGS BANK (Newport News, Va.) -- Robert N. Springer, 35 years old, was appointed president and chief operating officer, subject to approval by the Office of Thrift Supervision. He succeeds Theodore W. Potter, 53, who resigned last month as executive vice president and chief operating officer. Mr. Springer will retain his current position as chief financial officer.
