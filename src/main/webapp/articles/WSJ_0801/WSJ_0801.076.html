Japanese VCR Exports Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

TOKYO -- Japanese manufacturers exported a total of 11.4 million videocassette recorders in the first half of 1990, up 5.3% from a year earlier, the Electronics Industry Association of Japan said. Shipments of color televisions during the term, however, fell 6.6% to 1.6 million units.

For June, about 2.2 million VCRs were exported, up 13% from a year earlier.

VCR exports to the U.S. posted sharp year-to-year declines both in June and in the first half, an association official said. Despite the decline, the U.S. remained by far the biggest importer of Japanese VCRs.

The U.S. imported 657,849 VCRs in June, down 28% from the year before, while imports for the January-June period declined 26% to 3.7 million.
