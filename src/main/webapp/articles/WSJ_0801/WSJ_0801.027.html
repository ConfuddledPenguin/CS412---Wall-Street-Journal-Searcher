Letters to the Editor: Allenisms Were a Hoot

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

I'm surprised that Frederick C. Klein, when writing about baseball announcer Mel Allen (Leisure & Arts, June 29), chose to recall "Ballantine blast" from the immediate post-World War II period instead of a more quintessential Allenism, such as, "Foul, by the width of a White Owl cigar]" L. Brooks Hughes

Marshall, Mich.
