Technology Brief -- Siliconix Inc.: Daimler-Benz Unit Agrees To Invest Funds in Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

Siliconix Inc. said AEG Aktiengesellschaft, a subsidiary of Daimier-Benz AG, agreed to invest $13 million in it and cancel a $2 million secured note. In April, Siliconix filed for reorganization under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. The Santa Clara, Calif., company, which makes semiconductors, said the accord with the unit of Daimier-Benz must be approved by the bankruptcy court.

The agreement would give the Daimier-Benz unit an 80% share of Siliconix, leaving remaining shareholders with 20%.
