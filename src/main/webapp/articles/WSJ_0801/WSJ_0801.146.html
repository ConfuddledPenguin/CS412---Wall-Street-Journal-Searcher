Average Interest Rate Falls At Citicorp Weekly Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 7.8192% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 7.944% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $410 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.80% to 7.828%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 7.728% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.8495% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $380 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.715% to 7.74%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
