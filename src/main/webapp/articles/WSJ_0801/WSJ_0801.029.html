Marriott Sale of Fairfield Inns

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

WASHINGTON -- Marriott Corp. said it completed the sale of 50 Fairfield Inns to the Fairfield Inn by Marriott Limited Partnership for $235.5 million. Marriott, which will continue as manager of the inns and will be the general partner, said the inns are aimed at the "economy segment" of the lodging industry. The company said it anticipates operating more than 300 Fairfield Inns by the mid-1990s.

It currently operates 59 Fairfield Inns.
