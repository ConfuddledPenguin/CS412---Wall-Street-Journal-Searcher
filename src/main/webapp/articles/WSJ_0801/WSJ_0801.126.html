International Brief -- Sumitomo Chemical Co.: Pretax Profit Climbs 8.7%, Net Profit Rises to High

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

Sumitomo Chemical Co.'s unconsolidated pretax profit for the first half of 1990 rose 8.7% to 27.96 billion yen ($189 million) from 25.72 billion yen a year earlier, the Japanese company reported. Net profit rose 5.9% to 12.31 billion yen, a record for a half-year period, from 11.63 billion the year before. Per-share net rose to 7.59 yen from 7.17.

Net sales climbed 16% to 347.25 billion yen from 299.90 billion during the year-earlier period.
