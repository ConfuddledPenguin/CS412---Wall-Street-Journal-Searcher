Who's News: Mitsubishi Bank Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

MITSUBISHI BANK Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Yutaka Hasegawa, 50 years old, was named senior executive officer of this bank's North American headquarters as well as director and general manager of its New York branch. Mr. Hasegawa previously was general manager of the North American administration division. He succeeds Takeshi Tange, who has been named director and general manager of the international planning division in the Tokyo headquarters.
