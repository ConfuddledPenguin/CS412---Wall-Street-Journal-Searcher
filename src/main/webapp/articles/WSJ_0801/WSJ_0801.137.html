Healthcare International Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

AUSTIN, Texas -- Healthcare International Inc. said it is selling a hospital and office building in Tustin, Calif., to Concept Health Group. Under terms, Concept, a Seattle hospital management firm, will lease the facility until the sale is closed. The company said the sale is expected to close in about nine months.

Healthcare, a hospital operator, said it will use an estimated $33 million in sales proceeds to reduce a $60 million mortgage held on the property by HealthVest, a real estate investment trust.
