Who's News: National Securities & Research Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

NATIONAL SECURITIES & RESEARCH Corp. (Greenwich, Conn.) -- Robert J. Adler was named president of NSR Distributors Inc., the mutual fund broker/dealer subsidiary of this fund management company. Mr. Adler, 34 years old, was senior vice president and director of mutual fund product development at Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.
