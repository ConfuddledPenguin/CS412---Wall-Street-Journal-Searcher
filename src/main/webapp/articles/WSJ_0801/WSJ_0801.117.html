Business Brief -- Energy Service Co.: Texas Drilling Firm to Sell Two Rigs for $95 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

Energy Service Co., Houston, said it agreed to sell two semi-submersible rigs and related equipment from its Penrod Drilling Corp. affiliate's fleet of offshore and onshore rigs to Wilrig AS of Norway for $95 million. Energy Service, an oil and gas drilling concern, said the sale is expected to help finance a recapitalization of Penrod, of which Energy Service, as partner in an investment group, holds 40.2%.

The sale is conditional upon completion of the purchase of the remaining 31% of Penrod's senior debt and equity securities by the investor group and another group holding 28.5%. Energy Service said the two groups expect to complete that purchase by Oct. 31.

Energy Service said the two rigs are currently operating in the Gulf of Mexico, and are expected to be moved to the North Sea by Wilrig. As part of the pact, Wilrig has options to purchase three additional rigs.
