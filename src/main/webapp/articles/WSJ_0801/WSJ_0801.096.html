Business Brief -- MCI Communications Corp.: Purchase of Telecom USA Is Approved by the FCC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

The Federal Communications Commission approved MCI Communications Corp.'s planned acquisition of Telecom USA Inc., Atlanta. The agency's Common Carrier Bureau found that the combination "would continue to be subject to vigorous competition" from American Telephone & Telegraph Co., United Telecommunications' Sprint Communications Corp. unit and other long-distance carriers. MCI is the U.S.'s second largest long-distance company, and Telecom USA is the fourth largest.

An MCI spokeswoman said the company expects to complete the $1.25 billion merger this month after receiving approval from various state regulators.
