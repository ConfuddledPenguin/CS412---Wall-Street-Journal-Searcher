Letters to the Editor: Are Any Cute Guys Interviewing for CEO?

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

Your exceptional coverage of Donald Trump's financial imbroglio has fascinated me. However, as a professional woman and newly minted Harvard MBA, I found "Trump Girl: Donald's Latest Venture Is a Brunette" by Joanne Kaufman (Leisure and Arts, June 26) about women auditioning as Trump Girl hostesses on the forthcoming "Trump Card" game show blatantly sexist. The portrayal of women as blonde and brunette girls who seem to symbolize sexual triumphs for Mr. Trump degrades all women. Moreover, I wonder if Mr. Trump and The Wall Street Journal would dare describe any man in the Trump organization as a boy?

Victoria E. Watkins

Oakland, Calif.

---

Ms. Kaufman's piece about auditions for the "Trump Girl" is one of the cutest and slickest bits of writing I've seen for quite a while. Let's have more from this talented writer.

Kenneth E. Van Horn

DeKalb, Ill.
