Business Brief -- UDC-Universal Development: Partnership Hires Banker To Help Review Strategy

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

UDC-Universal Development Limited Partnership, Tempe, Ariz., said it retained an investment banker to help review strategic and financial alternatives for the company to "increase shareholder value." A spokesman said "we haven't limited our alternatives" when asked whether the options might include a sale of the partnership. The review wasn't prompted by any expressions of interest from potential acquirers, he said.

UDC-Universal, a home builder with operations in California, Arizona, Florida, Georgia and the Carolinas, said it believes its stock is undervalued because investors are focusing on its current financial results rather than its long-term value.

UDC-Universal shares, which traded as high as $27.25 earlier this year, rose $1.375 to $13.625 yesterday in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.

Last week, UDC-Universal reported that second-quarter net income fell 59% to $4.9 million, or 16 cents a share, from $12 million, or $1.11 a share, a year earlier.
