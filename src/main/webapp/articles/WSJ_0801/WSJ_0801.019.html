Financing Business: Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

CANADIAN IMPERIAL BANK OF COMMERCE, Toronto, said it plans to issue 150 million Canadian dollars ($130.2 million) of debentures to mature in August, 2010. It said the debentures would pay 11.85% interest for the first five years, with the rate adjusted through an auction process for each subsequent five-year period. The issue will be underwritten by a group headed by Wood Gundy Inc. The bank said proceeds would be added to its capital base.
