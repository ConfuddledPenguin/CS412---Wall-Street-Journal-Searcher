Who's News: Capital Associates Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

CAPITAL ASSOCIATES Inc. (Lakewood, Colo.) -- Richard Kazan, chairman and chief executive officer of this financial services company, has added the position of president, succeeding Jack Durliat, who resigned to pursue other interests. The company said Mr. Durliat will continue to serve on the board until the annual meeting in November, but won't stand for re-election.
