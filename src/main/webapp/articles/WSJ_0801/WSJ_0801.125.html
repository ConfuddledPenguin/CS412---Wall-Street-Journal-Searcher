International Brief -- PowerGen PLC: Electricity Generating Firm Has Profit of $431.6 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

PowerGen PLC reported pretax profit of #233.6 million ($431.6 million) for the year ended March 31. The company is one of two U.K. electricity generating companies scheduled to be privatized in February. The results, made public for the first time, also showed a net loss of #79.9 million, after #185.8 million of extraordinary provisions against the cost of layoffs and a restructuring.

Comparative figures for the year earlier weren't provided.

Robert Malpas, PowerGen chairman, wouldn't comment on a possible bid for the company by Hanson PLC, a British industrial conglomerate, which the government has said might pre-empt a public share sale.

Mr. Malpas also wouldn't comment on speculation that PowerGen management might launch its own bid for control of the company.
