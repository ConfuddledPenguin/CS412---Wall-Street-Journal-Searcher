Pace Mocker

Some retirees serve on boards;

Some pursue active hobbies;

Some retirees do volunteer work;

Some join political lobbies.

Some retirees attend reunions

At companies where they were hired;

(The frantic pace of some retirees

Simply makes me tired])

-- George O. Ludcke.

The Annual Meeting

The dollar was high against the yen;

Parts were delivered late;

There was pressure on our profits again,

But everything is great.

-- Richard F. Barrett.
