Hercules Inc. Expansion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Hercules Inc. said it plans to start construction next year on a $23.7 million research center in Barneveld, the Netherlands. The chemicals company said the research will support its businesses in paper chemicals and flavors and fragrances. The company expects the facility to be completed by late 1992 and initially to employ about 90 scientists.
