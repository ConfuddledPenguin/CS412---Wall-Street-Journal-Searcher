Edisto's Note Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

DALLAS -- Edisto Resources Corp. said it agreed to purchase $10 million face amount of 13 1/4% convertible secured notes issued by Hall-Houston Offshore for $17 million in cash, plus accrued interest, from certain Prudential-Bache Energy Growth Fund Partnerships. Edisto, an oil and gas exploration and production company, said the notes are convertible into a general partner interest representing 10.4% of Hall-Houston Offshore, a joint venture formed in 1988 by Edisto and Hall-Houston Oil Co., a closely held company, to develop and explore offshore Gulf of Mexico reserves.
