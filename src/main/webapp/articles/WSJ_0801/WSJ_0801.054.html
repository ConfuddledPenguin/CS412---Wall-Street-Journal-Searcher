REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Yankee Soap Opera

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

We don't imagine there's too much weeping in the land for the summary justice meted out by Baseball Commissioner Fay Vincent to Yankees owner George Steinbrenner. However, the support for Mr. Vincent's decision to deprive Mr. Steinbrenner of his majority ownership in the team seems to have little connection to the issue at hand -- the Yankee owner's dealings with a reputed gambler. Instead, the fans appear to feel that George is being "sentenced" with exile for the crime of turning the team into a 17-year-long soap opera. If the world of politics was as closed and private as baseball, Mr. Vincent would now go to Capitol Hill to investigate and ban from public life the perpetrators of the S&L mess.
