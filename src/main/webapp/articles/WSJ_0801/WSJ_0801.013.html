Copperweld Holders Clear Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said shareholders at a special meeting here approved the previously announced agreement for Copperweld to be acquired by Imetal, a Paris building materials maker. The transaction is expected to be completed by the close of business today.

As previously reported, Imetal agreed to acquire the 34.8% of Copperweld it didn't already own for $17 a share.

Copperweld produces wire, cable, rods and tubing.
