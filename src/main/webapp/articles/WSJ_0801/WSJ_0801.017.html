Who's News: Cimflex Teknowledge Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

CIMFLEX TEKNOWLEDGE Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Sushil K. Trikha, president and chief executive officer of this software products concern, resigned for personal reasons, the company said. Daniel Robusto, formerly vice president of finance and administration, was named president. The company formed a committee to consider candidates, including Mr. Robusto, for the chief executive position.
