Who's News: Aluminum Co. of America

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

ALUMINUM CO. OF AMERICA (Pittsburgh) -- George E. Bergeron, 48 years old, was named vice president and general manager of Alcoa's Rigid Packaging division, the company's largest unit. Mr. Bergeron succeeds Thomas J. Taylor, 59, who will assume a newly created position for sales, trading and recycling in Alcoa's metals and chemicals group. Mr. Bergeron had been president of Alcoa's closure systems unit since 1982.
