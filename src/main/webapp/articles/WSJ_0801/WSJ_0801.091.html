Who's News: IMO Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

IMO INDUSTRIES Inc. (Lawrenceville, N.J.) -- Robert R. Womack, 52 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this instruments, controls and power products concern. In this newly created position, he will report to the company's chief executive officer, William J. Holcombe.
