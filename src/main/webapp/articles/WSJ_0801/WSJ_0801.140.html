International Brief -- Allianz AG: West German Insurer Puts 1989 Profit at $698 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

Allianz AG said in Munich that consolidated net group profit totaled 1.16 billion marks ($698 million) in 1989. This is the first time the West German insurance company has published consolidated group earnings, so year-earlier figures weren't available.

Allianz said that, of the 1.16 billion marks, 460 million marks of profit came from its foreign operations. Based on available data, Allianz's 1989 domestic group net profit thus climbed 32% to 700 million marks from 529 million a year earlier.

World-wide group premium income jumped 8.9% to 31.8 billion marks from 29.2 billion, Allianz said. It added that it expects 1990 premium income to rise further, to about 36 billion marks.

Allianz said it expects earnings to be "satisfactory" in 1990. Current earnings levels are running according to plans, the company said.
