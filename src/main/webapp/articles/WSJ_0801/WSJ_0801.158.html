Treasury Will Sell 44-Day Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise new cash Thursday by selling about $4 billion in 44-day cash-management bills. The bills will be dated Aug. 7 and will mature Sept. 20.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum denominations of $1,000,000, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Thursday at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
