Letters to the Editor: Spelling Beez Would Never Be the Same

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

Amity Shlaes's (Shlaziz) ardikl about the French chrying 2 rerite ther langwidge n 2 spelling wurdz egzakli lik tha soun seemz lik a gud idea (editorial page, Joon 27). Jus think, awl children hoo lurn 2 reed an spel fonetikli kud pronouns an spel nyeerli inny wurd tha c without evn noing hwot it meenz. Awlso, ther wud b fyew argyumints or gisadreemints (I meen disagreemintz) kunserning how a wurd iz pronounsd or speld. Anuthr idea iz 2 develup a kod that nonfonik tip peepl kud not komprehin. That iz, revurs leterz or silabls from wun wurd to anuthr n yur sintens. For egzampl, this paragraf's furst sintens wud reed: Inuthr adea iz 2 kevelup a dod that fon-nonic pip teepl nud kot pomkrehin. B kairfl tho bkawz u mit kreat sum awf kulr fraziz an sounz. I don't no if enuf peepl wud do this but it kan kreat sum inchresting sounz and wurdz (or wounz and surdz).

No madr hwot itz stil sorda fun and thanx for a thawt-provoking pees of wurk (or wees of purk).

Troy Mohon

Birmingham, Ala.
