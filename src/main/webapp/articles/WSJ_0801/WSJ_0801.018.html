Canada's Domestic Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's gross domestic product fell 0.2% in May from April on a decline in construction, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The May drop followed a 0.1% decline in April and a 0.2% rise in March. Gross domestic product is the total value of a nation's output of goods and services.

Production of goods fell 0.3% in May mainly because of a 1.6% drop in construction activity. Service-industry output fell 0.2% in May.
