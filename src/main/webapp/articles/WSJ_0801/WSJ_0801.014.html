Who's News: Unisys Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

UNISYS Corp. (Blue Bell, Pa.) -- This computer company named four executives to the additional post of corporate vice president: Edward A. Blechschmidt, president, Pacific Asia Americas division; Frank G. Brandenberg, vice president, consumer systems group; John W. Perry, vice president, Unisys U.K.; and Harry B. Traylor, president, government Systems division.
