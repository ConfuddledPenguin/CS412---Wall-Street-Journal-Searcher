CML Group's Sale of Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

ACTON, Mass. -- CML Group Inc. said it signed an agreement to sell certain assets and liabilities of its Carroll Reed subsidiary to affiliates of Swire Pacific Ltd. of Hong Kong. Terms of the cash sale were not disclosed. CML, a marketer of consumer products under the trade names NordicTrack, the Nature Company and Britches of Georgetowne, said the transaction would be completed in 60 days. The sale completes a corporate restructuring announced last year, the company said.
