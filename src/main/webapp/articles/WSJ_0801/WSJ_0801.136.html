International Brief -- Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd.: Plan to Sell Brewing Assets To an Affiliate Is Backed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

Shareholders in Alan Bond's financially troubled Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd. agreed to a proposal to sell the company's Australian brewing assets to independently managed affiliate Bell Resources Ltd. But a banking syndicate led by National Australia Bank Ltd. that is owed 880 million Australian dollars (US$697 million) by Bond unit Bond Brewing Holdings Ltd. requires the asset sale to be completed immediately.

And the A$1.85 billion sale of the company's brewing assets needs further approval from a meeting of bondholders in London Aug. 9 and from shareholders in Bell Resources Aug. 15 before it can proceed.

Under a settlement reached in May, if the sale didn't proceed by yesterday, Bond Brewing would have until Sept. 30 to repay the banks. If the sale went ahead, Bond Brewing would have until July 1991 to repay the A$880 million.

However, the banks were expected to agree to extend the deadline to await the outcome of the meetings of bondholders and Bell Resources.
