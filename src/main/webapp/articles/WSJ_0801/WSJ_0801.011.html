Who's News: D&N Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/01/90

D&N FINANCIAL Corp. (Hancock, Mich.) -- Joseph C. Bromley was appointed to the board of this thrift, filling a vacancy created by the death of Richard E. Hinks. Mr. Bromley, 61 years old, has been a director of D&N Savings Bank, the company's principal subsidiary, since 1980. He previously served as a director of D&N Financial from 1988 through 1989.
