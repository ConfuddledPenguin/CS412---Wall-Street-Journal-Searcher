GE Gets $101 Million In Defense Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/01/90

WASHINGTON -- General Electric Co. received $101 million in defense contracts for shipboard weapons and computer equipment for the Navy and jet engine parts for the Air Force.

General Dynamics Corp. got a $63.8 million Navy contract for shipboard weapon systems.

Contel Federal Systems Inc., a unit of Contel Corp., was awarded a $33.2 million Air Force contract for data-processing systems.

Unisys Corp. got a $16.6 million Army contract for computer systems.

United Technologies Corp. received a $14.2 million Army contract for four UH-60 helicopters.

Litton Systems, a unit of Litton Industries Inc., won a $13.9 million Navy contract for missile guidance systems.

AM General Corp. was issued an $11.9 million Army contract for trucks.

Texas Instruments Inc. received a $10.8 million contract for Army electronics and Air Force laser-guided bombs.

NI Industries, a unit of Masco Corp., got a $10 million Army contract for ammunition.
