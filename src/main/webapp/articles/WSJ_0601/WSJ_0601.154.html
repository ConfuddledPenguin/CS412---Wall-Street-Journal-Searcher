Wainoco Oil Discovery

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

HOUSTON -- Wainoco Oil Corp. announced an oil discovery in La Salle County, Texas, that flowed at a rate of 1,170 barrels a day. Wainoco, one of several investors in the well, said that the Austin Chalk oil discovery was made with a horizontally drilled well and that it also flowed 470,000 cubic feet of gas a day. Pinnacle Royalty, the operating company, conducted a 24-hour test through a 20/64-inch opening.

The company put the vertical depth of the well at some 7,400 feet and said it was drilled horizontally 3,088 feet.
