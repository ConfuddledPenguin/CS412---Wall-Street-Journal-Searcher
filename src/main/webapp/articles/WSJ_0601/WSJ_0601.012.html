BMW's 5-Month Sales Rose 3%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, an auto maker, said its world-wide group sales in this year's first five months totaled 11.6 billion marks ($6.89 billion), up 3% from a year earlier. Chairman Eberhard V. Kuenheim, in remarks prepared for BMW's annual meeting, said the company produced 237,000 autos in the first five months, but didn't provide a year-earlier comparison. He said new registrations of BMWs world-wide at 231,000 units were little changed from a year earlier.
