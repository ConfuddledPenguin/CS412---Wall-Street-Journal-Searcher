Niagara Mohawk Power Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

SYRACUSE, N.Y. -- Niagara Mohawk Power Corp. said that on June 30 it will redeem 640,000 of its 960,000 shares outstanding of 10.75% preferred stock at $25 each, plus accrued dividends. The utility said stock of record May 18 will be redeemed, as part of a program to reduce average cost of capital.
