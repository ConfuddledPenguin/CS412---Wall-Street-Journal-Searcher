REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Schoolyard Arms Control

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

Protests are taking a new turn at the high-school level, notably one last Friday at Eagle Rock High School in Los Angeles. This walkout was in favor of tougher discipline policies. "No more weapons]" the students chanted. After a classmate was stabbed last week, they demanded that the school board expel those who bring weapons to school. Later Friday afternoon, another student was stabbed just off the campus, now beset with gang violence after years of relative calm. Alas, the city school board earlier this month rejected, 6 to 1, a proposal to expel automatically anyone caught with a weapon at school. The majority called the measure too unwieldy and punitive. In Los Angeles as in many parts of the nation, it may take some form of voucher or choice mechanism to get insensate school officials to recognize the real world.
