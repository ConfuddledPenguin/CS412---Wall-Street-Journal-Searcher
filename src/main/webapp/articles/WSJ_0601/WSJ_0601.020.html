Who's News: Norsk Hydro AS

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

NORSK HYDRO AS (Stockholm) -- Egil Myklebust, a 48-year-old lawyer who heads a Norwegian business and industry confederation, was designated chief executive officer of this state-controlled petroleum, fertilizer and metals concern, to succeed Torvild Aakvaag, 63, who is to retire next year. Mr. Myklebust, a former veteran executive at Norsk Hydro, will rejoin the company in the fall and is expected to serve as deputy to Mr. Aakvaag before succeeding him sometime next year.
