Who's News: Price Waterhouse

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

PRICE WATERHOUSE (New York) -- Thomas H. Chamberlain was appointed chief financial officer, a new post at the accounting firm. Mr. Chamberlain, previously vice chairman in charge of the New York Metro Region, is succeeded by James J. Schiro, previously national managing partner of mergers and acquisitions. Mr. Schiro also was named to the firm's management committee. Mr. Chamberlain joined Price Waterhouse in 1960 and was named a partner in 1971; Mr. Schiro joined the firm in 1967 and became a partner in 1979. Both executives now are part of the management committee, a 15-member group that manages the firm's policies and objectives.
