Business Brief -- Asset Investors Corp.: Bank Loan Pact Renewed With Credit Line Reduced

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

Asset Investors Corp., a Denver-based real estate investment trust, said it renewed its bank loan agreement with First Bank of Minneapolis, reducing its credit line to a maximum of $30 million from $60 million. The unit of First Bank System Inc. of Minneapolis said that it will be allowed to borrow as much as $30 million but this amount will decline by $5 million each quarter, and that the agreement will mature next May 31. The previous agreement expired yesterday.

Asset Investors, which invests in collateralized mortgage obligations and other mortgage-related assets, said it has $16.7 million outstanding on its bank credit line and $24.8 million in short-term borrowings through repurchase agreements with investment banking firms.
