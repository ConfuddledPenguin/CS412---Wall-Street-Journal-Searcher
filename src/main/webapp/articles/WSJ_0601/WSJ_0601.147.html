Fluor Corp. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp. said it completed the purchase for $125 million of a 42.5% interest in Doe Run Co. from Homestake Mining Co. Doe Run, a major lead producer, is now a wholly owned Fluor subsidiary. Fluor is mainly an engineering and construction concern. Homestake, based in San Francisco, is a gold-mining company with interests in the U.S., Australia, Chile and Canada.
