Norway's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

OSLO -- Norway's unemployment rate was 3.9% at the end of May, down from 4.2% at the end of April but up from 3.5% a year earlier, Arbeidsdirektoratet, the government's labor agency, said. The jobless rate peaked at 4.7% in January, when 101,900 people were out of work. The May total was 84,800 -- 52,200 men and 32,600 women -- down 6,700 from one month earlier but up 8,500 from a year earlier. The rate data excluded 45,600 people employed in extraordinary state work programs, down 4,800 from last month and up 9,300 from 12 months earlier.
