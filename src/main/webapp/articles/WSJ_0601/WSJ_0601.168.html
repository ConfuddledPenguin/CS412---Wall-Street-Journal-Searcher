Landmark Land Pact Is Ended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

CARMEL, Calif. -- Landmark Land Co. said its $60 million contract to sell resort assets at Carmel Valley Ranch to American International Golf Resorts Inc. has been terminated. The sale would have yielded Landmark a $20 million profit. The assets included the Carmel Valley Ranch resort, golf course and tennis club.
