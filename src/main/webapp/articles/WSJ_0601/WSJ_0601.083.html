U.S. Auto Firms Plan Output This Week Of 121,895 Vehicles

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

DETROIT -- The 10 major U.S. auto makers planned to build 121,895 cars this week, nearly unchanged from 121,995 a year ago. All car assembly plants for the major domestic auto makers are scheduled to operate next week, with several working overtime.

General Motors Corp. said it has scheduled Saturday overtime at plants in Hamtramck, Lansing and Orion, Mich., and Lordstown, Ohio.

Ford Motor Co. plans overtime at its Kansas City, Mo., Louisville, Ky., and Twin Cities truck plants.

Chrysler Corp. said it has scheduled overtime at its Belvidere, Ill., Sterling Heights, Mich., and St. Louis II assembly plants.
