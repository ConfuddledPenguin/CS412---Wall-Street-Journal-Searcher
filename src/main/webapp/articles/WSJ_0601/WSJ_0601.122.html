First Commercial Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

LITTLE ROCK, Ark. -- First Commercial Corp. said it completed the purchase of ABT Bancshares Corp. for about one million common shares, valued at about $18.9 million. First Commercial, a bank holding company with about 7.8 million shares outstanding before the transaction, said the acquisition will be accounted for as a pooling of interests. Closely held ABT, based in Hot Springs, is the parent of Arkansas Bank & Trust Co.

As part of the transaction, First Commercial said Cecil W. Cupp Jr., chairman of ABT, will become a member of First Commercial's executive committee and a director of First Commercial, increasing its board size to 21 members.
