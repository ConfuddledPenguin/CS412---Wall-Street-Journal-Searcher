Business Brief -- ECI Environmental Inc.: Asbestos Cleanup Company Says Third-Period Net Fell

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

ECI Environmental Inc., a San Leandro, Calif., asbestos cleanup firm, said that cost overruns and a decline in new projects probably depressed fiscal third quarter net income. The company estimated net at about $180,000, or four cents a share, for the quarter ended yesterday, down from $672,000, or 19 cents a share, the year earlier.

ECI estimated that third-period revenue ranged between $5.6 million and $6 million, compared with $5.5 million the year earlier.

The company said that a drop in new projects from San Francisco commercial high-rise owners and high development costs in its Southern California operations probably also will hurt fourth-quarter net, causing it to trail the $1.9 million of a year earlier.

Yesterday, in composite trading on the American Stock Exchange, ECI common plunged $2.125 a share to close at $4.75 each on volume of 33,600 shares.
