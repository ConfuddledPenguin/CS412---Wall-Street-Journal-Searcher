Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 298,183 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, up 0.7% from 296,125 tons in the preceding week but down 2.4% from 305,438 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, output totaled 5,823,782 metric tons, down 11% from 6,530,509 tons a year earlier.
