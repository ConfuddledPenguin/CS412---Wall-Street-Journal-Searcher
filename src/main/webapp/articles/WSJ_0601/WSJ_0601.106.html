Financing Business: Urcarco Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

URCARCO Inc. said it completed an offering of five million common shares at $19.875 each, more than 2.5 times the company's initial stock offering price last November. The Fort Worth, Texas, high-flying operator of used-car lots said it will use the proceeds to buy cars for inventory, to expand its business, reduce debt and for general corporate purposes. Urcarco had about 25.6 million shares outstanding before the offering. It went public at $11 a share late last year and has since paid a 3-for-2 stock split. Yesterday, in national over-the-counter trading, the stock was the second most active issue on volume of 3.3 million shares; it closed at $20.625, up 75 cents. Merrill Lynch Capital Markets Inc. led the underwriting group.
