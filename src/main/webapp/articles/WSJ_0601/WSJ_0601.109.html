Who's News: Advest Group Inc. Taps Allen Weintraub For Chief Executive

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

HARTFORD, Conn. -- Advest Group Inc. named Allen Weintraub, 55 years old, chief executive officer, effective Oct. 1. Mr. Weintraub, currently president, will succeed Anthony A. LaCroix, 61, who has announced plans to retire at that time. Mr. LaCroix will continue as chairman of the financial services company.

Mr. Weintraub is also president of Advest Inc., a broker/dealer subsidiary. Mr. Weintraub will become chief executive of Advest Inc.

Arnold E. Amstutz, 53, will become president of Advest Group, succeeding Mr. Weintraub. Mr. Amstutz is currently senior executive vice president. Grant W. Kurtz, 48, will succeed Mr. Weintraub as president of Advest Inc. He is currently senior executive vice president and national sales manager of Advest Group.

The parent company also said Eli Broad, chairman and chief executive of Broad Inc., resigned as director because of "extensive commitments." Jay S. Wintrob, senior vice president of Broad Inc., was named to succeed Mr. Broad as a director. Broad Inc. holds a 8.3% stake in Advest.
