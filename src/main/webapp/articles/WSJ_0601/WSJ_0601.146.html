Technology Brief -- Adobe Systems Inc.: Firm Sues EFI on Patent For Color Printing Systems

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

Adobe Systems Inc., Mountain View, Calif., filed suit against Electronics for Imaging Inc. in federal court Wednesday, seeking a court declaration that it hasn't infringed on an EFI patent for color printing systems. In January, EFI accused Adobe of infringing on the patent and the two software companies began negotiating the dispute.

In response to the suit, EFI, a San Bruno, Calif.-based firm that develops color printing for desktop publishing, said it will file a counterclaim, charging Adobe with infringement. EFI holds the license to the color-printing patent from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

EFI contends that Adobe is using the patented process in its PostScript Level 2 software with color extension. An Adobe spokesman called EFI's allegations "completely without merit."
