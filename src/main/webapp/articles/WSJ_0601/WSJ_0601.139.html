Who's News: McDonnell Helicopter Names Gunn President

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

ST. LOUIS -- Thomas M. Gunn was named president of McDonnell Douglas Helicopter Co. He succeeds William P. Brown, who resigned last month in the wake of the company's poor financial performance. Mr. Gunn, 46 years old, was most recently vice president of program development at McDonnell Aircraft Co. The helicopter company, based in Mesa, Ariz., and the aircraft company, based in St. Louis, are units of McDonnell Douglas Corp.

Mr. Gunn has held various marketing, strategic planning and government-affairs posts with other McDonnell Douglas affiliates since joining the company in 1975. He also holds a law degree. Mr. Brown, 57, has been a consultant to the parent company since he resigned.

Last year, the helicopter company had a $137 million write-off mainly related to the Apache program, which accounted for about 80% of the company's revenue.
