Tyco Laboratories Inc. Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

EXETER, N.H. -- Tyco Laboratories Inc. said its Simplex Wire & Cable Co. unit received a contract valued at $42 million from American Telephone & Telegraph Co.'s Federal Systems unit to make undersea fiberoptic cables for the Navy. Under terms, Simplex, in Newington, N.H., will make cables that will be used by the Navy for oceanographic research, Tyco said.

Tyco makes fire protection/flow control systems, electrical and electronic parts and packaging materials.
