Titan to Buy M/A-Com Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

SAN DIEGO -- Titan Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire M/A-Com Government Systems Inc. from M/A-Com Inc. of Burlington, Mass. Terms of the cash acquisition weren't disclosed. The M/A-Com unit is a supplier of earth-based satellite communications equipment to the U.S. government. Titan makes computer products and provides services for defense programs. Titan said that completion of the acquisition, expected at the end of June, is subject to the signing of a definitive loan agreement and regulatory approvals.
