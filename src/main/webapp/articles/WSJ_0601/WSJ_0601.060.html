Money Market Assets Gain $128.3 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

NEW YORK -- Assets of the U.S.'s 502 money market funds rose $128.3 million to $381.54 billion, in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington trade association. Assets of 239 general purpose funds fell $231.2 million to $129.25 billion, and assets of 102 broker-dealer funds rose $481.4 million to $159.62 billion. Assets of 161 institutional funds fell $121.9 million to $92.68 billion.

The number of funds was revised for the week ending May 23 to exclude one general purpose fund that closed.
