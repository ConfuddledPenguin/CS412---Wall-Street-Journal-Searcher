Who's News: Hanson Industries

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

HANSON INDUSTRIES (New York) -- This U.S. arm of Hanson PLC, the British industrial company, named John H. Wimberly vice president for its building products group. Mr. Wimberly succeeds William D. Henderson, who becomes president and chief operating officer of Smith Corona Corp., effective July 1. Smith Corona is 47.9%-owned by Hanson.
