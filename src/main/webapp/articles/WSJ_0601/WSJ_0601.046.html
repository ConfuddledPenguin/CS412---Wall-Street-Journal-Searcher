DOW JONES INDUSTRY GROUP indexes for Tuesday, May 29, were affected by a correction from the Pacific Stock Exchange on the price of Philip Morris Cos. stock, to $43.625 a share from the originally reported $42.50. This change caused the following index revisions: the household products industry group to 509.81 from 503.74 originally; the non-cyclical consumer sector to 507.06 from 505.98, and the Dow Jones Equity Market Index to 335.56 from 335.42. ---

HUDSON'S BAY Co.'s real estate subsidiary, Markborough Properties Inc., estimates the market value of its properties at 2.9 billion Canadian dollars (US$2.47 billion). An earlier edition misstated the figure.

(See: "Hudson's Bay Co. Plans to Spin Off Real Estate Unit" -- WSJ May 25, 1990)
