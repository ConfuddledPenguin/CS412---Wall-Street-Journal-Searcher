Business Brief -- Armstrong World Industries Inc.: Company Buys About 75% Of Stake Sold by Belzbergs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

Armstrong World Industries Inc. disclosed that it bought about three-fourths of the 11.7% Armstrong stake sold Wednesday by the Belzberg family of Canada. The Belzbergs, who owned 4.8 million shares in Armstrong through various affiliates, sold their entire block of shares to Goldman, Sachs & Co. late Wednesday at $35.75 a share, about $1.625 a share below the prevailing market price.

Lancaster, Pa.-based Armstrong said it purchased about 3.7 million shares through Goldman. The company added that Goldman indicated "a broad cross-section of buyers" were interested in purchasing the other 1.1 million shares.
