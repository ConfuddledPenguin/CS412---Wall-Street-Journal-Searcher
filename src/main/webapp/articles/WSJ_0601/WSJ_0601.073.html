Humana Inc. Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Humana Inc. said it has agreed to buy Prime Health Management Services Inc., a health-maintenance organization. Terms weren't disclosed. The sale is subject to a definitive agreement and regulatory approvals.

Prime Health has about 80,000 members in Missouri and Kansas. Humana operates hospitals and health benefit plans.
