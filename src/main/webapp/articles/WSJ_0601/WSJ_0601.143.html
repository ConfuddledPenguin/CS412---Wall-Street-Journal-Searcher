GR Foods Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

WOODBRIDGE, N.J. -- International Proteins Corp., a food company and owner of the Ground Round restaurant chain, changed its name to GR Foods Inc., effective today. GR Foods, previously traded under American Stock Exchange symbol PRO, now trades under GRX. The company said there is no need to exchange existing stock certificates since they remain valid.
