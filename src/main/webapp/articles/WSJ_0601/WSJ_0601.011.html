Benton Oil Completes a Well

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

VENTURA, Calif. -- Benton Oil & Gas Co. said it completed the Steidlmayer 6-2A well in Colusa County, Calif., which tested at a rate of 4.9 million cubic feet of natural gas a day without water. Calculated absolute flow was determined to be 9.6 million cubic feet a day. The well was tested using a 16/64ths-inch opening with flowing tubing pressure of 3,083 pounds per square inch. The well was drilled to 7,750 feet and output was found between 7,588 feet and 7,624 feet.
