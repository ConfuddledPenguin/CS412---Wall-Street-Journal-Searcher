Italy's Inflation Rate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

ROME -- Italy's consumer price inflation rate eased to 5.7% in May, from 5.8% in April, the Bank of Italy's governor Carlo A. Ciampi said in his annual address to shareholders. The inflation was slightly below expectations.

Italy's consumer price index rose 5.7% in May from a year earlier; it was up 0.3% in May from April, the state statistical institute Istat reported in Milan.

Istat said the index registered 105.1 in May, compared with 104.8 in April and with 104.4 in March.

The index (1989 equals 100) isn't seasonally adjusted.
