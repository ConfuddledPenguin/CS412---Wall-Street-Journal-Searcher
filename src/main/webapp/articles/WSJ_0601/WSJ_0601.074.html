GM's Hughes Unit Wins Navy Contract For $209.8 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

WASHINGTON -- Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., received a $209.8 million Navy contract for two communications satellites.

Olin Corp. was given a $69.4 million Army contract for 120mm tank ammunition.

Martin Marietta Corp. got a $60.4 million Navy contract for shipboard missile-launch equipment.

CAE-Link Corp. was issued a $42.6 million Navy pact for aircraft trainers.

ITT Corp. won a combined $41.4 million Air Force and Army contract for aircraft electronics.

GTE Government Systems Corp., a unit of GTE Corp., got a $19.6 million Army contract for communications equipment training.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. received a $12.6 million Air Force pact for long-range search radar.

Vitro Corp. got a $10.9 million Navy contract for British naval missile-engineering services.

United Technologies Corp. received a $10 million Army contract for helicopters.

Grumman Corp. was awarded a $10 million Navy contract for E-2C aircraft.
