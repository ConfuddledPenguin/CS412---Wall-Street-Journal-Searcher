The Good Guys] Will Expand

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- The Good Guys], an electronics products retailer in Northern California and Nevada, said it is expanding into Southern California, opening 10 to 12 stores in the area within 18 months. The company said its first store, which will open in Los Angeles on July 1, will be the first major consumer electronics store in the U.S. to operate 24 hours a day.
