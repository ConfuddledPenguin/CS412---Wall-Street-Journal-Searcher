Chips & Technologies Lawsuit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Chips & Technologies Inc. said it settled its trade secrets suit, filed in January, against Elite Microelectronics Inc. and several former Chips employees. The settlement involves no monetary payment, which Chips had originally sought. Instead, the agreement stipulates that an independent inspector make periodic visits to Elite's facilities to be certain the company isn't using Chips' trade secrets.

The lawsuit had charged that former Chips engineers and design managers, who went to work for Elite, were misusing privileged information about their former employer's VLSI semiconductors and chip sets.
