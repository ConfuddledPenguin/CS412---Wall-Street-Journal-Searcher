Presidio Oil Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

DENVER -- Presidio Oil Co. said it is considering selling its West Virginia oil- and gas-producing properties and gas-gathering systems or forming a joint venture with a partner capable of helping fund drilling in the area. Presidio, an oil and gas concern, said that it has about 76 billion cubic feet of gas in West Virginia, or about 15% of its oil and gas reserves. It said proceeds from any sale of the properties would be used to reduce its $145 million in bank debt.

The company said it hired Petrie Parkman & Co. of Houston to advise it about a possible transaction.
