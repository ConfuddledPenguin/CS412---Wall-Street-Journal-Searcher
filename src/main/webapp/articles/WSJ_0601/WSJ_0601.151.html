SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Laserscope said it has received expanded clearance from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration to market a surgical laser system for all laparoscopic general surgery. The FDA clearance permits use of the company's KTP/532 Surgical Laser Systems for a broad range of laparoscopic procedures, including gall-bladder removal, appendectomies, liver biopsies and hernias.

In 1988, Laserscope received FDA approval to market this same laser system for endoscopic and open surgical procedures.
