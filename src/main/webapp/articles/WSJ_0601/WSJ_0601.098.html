Humana to Redeem Debt Issue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Humana Inc., which owns hospitals and operates health-benefit plans, said it will redeem on July 2 all of its 8.5% convertible subordinated debentures, due Aug. 15, 2009, at a price of $1,042.50 each, plus accrued interest. Slightly more than $199.9 million of the debentures are outstanding. The redemption will save the company about $17 million a year in interest costs, Humana said. Assuming full conversion, Humana said its per-share earnings won't be affected, because the company has included the effect of equivalent shares in its results since the debentures were issued in 1984.
