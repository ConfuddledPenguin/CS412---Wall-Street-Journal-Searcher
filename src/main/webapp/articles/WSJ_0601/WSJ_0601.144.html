Technology: Dell Computer Cuts Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

AUSTIN, Texas -- Dell Computer Corp., citing competitive pressures from the computer marketplace, said it cut the prices on its computers by $50 to $1,100, effective Monday. The direct-seller of computer products said the price cuts affect its laptop computers, and its computers built around the Intel Corp. 286, 386 and 486 microprocessors. The actual price changes, however, depend on the computer, the amount of memory in it and the memory capability of the machine's hard-disk drive.
