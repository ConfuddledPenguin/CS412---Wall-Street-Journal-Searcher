Bull & Bear Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

NEW YORK -- Bull & Bear Group Inc., a manager of mutual funds, said it terminated efforts to seek a buyer or an equity partner in a joint venture arrangement for its fund management business. Bassett S. Winmill, chairman of Bull & Bear, said, "While we received numerous expressions of interest, acceptable terms could not be negotiated which would be in the best long-term interests of both our employees and our shareholders."

The company manages about $345 million in fund assets.
