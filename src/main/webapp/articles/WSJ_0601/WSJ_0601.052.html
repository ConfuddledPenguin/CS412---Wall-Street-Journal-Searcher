Hamilton Oil Corp. Discovery

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

DENVER -- Hamilton Oil Corp. said it discovered natural gas and condensate in the U.K. sector of the North Sea. The company said the well flowed at a rate of 11 million cubic feet of gas and 3,000 barrels of condensate a day through a 40/64 inch opening.

Hamilton Oil is the operator with a 27.3% interest. The company said its partners are Sun Oil Britain Ltd., with a 30.3% interest; Elf Oil & Gas Ltd., 23.3%; Ultramar Exploration Ltd., 8.3%; Monument Resources Ltd., 1.7%, and Enterprise Oil PLC, 9.1%.
