International Brief -- Nissan Motor Co.: Annual Pretax Profit Rose 66% on Record Sales Level

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/01/90

Nissan Motor Co. said consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 rose 66% to 333.11 billion yen ($2.2 billion) from 200.82 billion yen a year earlier, and sales surged 17% to a record 5.645 trillion yen from 4.812 trillion yen. Net income also set a record, 116.01 billion yen, up 1.2% from 114.63 billion yen a year earlier. Per-share net was 46.25 yen, down from 46.27 yen because of an increase in shares outstanding.

Nissan also posted record unconsolidated pretax profit of 184.27 billion yen, up 19% from 154.77 billion yen. Net rose 34% to 85.377 billion yen from 63.61 billion yen, and per-share earnings increased to 34.03 yen from 25.68 yen. Unconsolidated sales increased 12% to a record 4.006 trillion yen from 3.58 trillion yen.

Nissan vehicle sales rose 5.7% to 2,372,000 from 1,978,000. Demand exceeded production capacity, an official said, but conceded that the U.S. operations' profit was "worse than mediocre," because of weakness in that market.
