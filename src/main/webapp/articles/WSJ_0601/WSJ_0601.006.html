Mr. Coffee Stock Issue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

NEW YORK -- Mr. Coffee Inc.'s initial public offering reached the market at a premium to its $10-a-share offering price, and ended the day at $11.125 a share on national over-the-counter volume of 4.2 million shares. Four million shares were brought to market; the Bedford Heights, Ohio, coffeemaker company, which was bought in 1987 for $72 million, will pay off two-thirds of the debt that was used to acquire it. Three million shares were offered by the company; one million by existing shareholders.

The offering was underwritten by a syndicate led by Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. The stock's trading symbol is JAVA.
