Lafarge Buys Stake in Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/01/90

RESTON, Va. -- Lafarge Corp., a construction materials maker, said it purchased 52% of a new aggregate and asphalt company, Parker Lafarge Inc., formed from assets previously owned by Houston-based Parker Brothers & Co., which will retain a 48% interest. Terms weren't disclosed. The new company has annual sales of about $40 million. Lafarge, which has many operations in Canada, said the acquisition is part of its expansion in the U.S.
