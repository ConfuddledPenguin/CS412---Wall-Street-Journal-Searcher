Notable & Quotable

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

Mitchell E. Daniels Jr., president of the Hudson Institute, in a commencement speech May 19 at the University of Indianapolis:

Now, every commencement should include at least one piece of gratuitous advice, and here is mine: Make babies] Have children -- lots of them. . . . They are your wager on the future, your gift to society, and the most useful, fulfilling graduate course you will ever take.
