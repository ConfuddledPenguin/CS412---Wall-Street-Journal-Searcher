SHERWOOD CAPITAL Inc. agreed to indemnify J.T. Moran Financial Corp. for problems arising after Moran's May 1988 purchase of several Sherwood retail offices, but the indemnification was limited to activities of brokers during their employment at Sherwood. An article in the April 26 edition said incorrectly that the indemnity covered "difficulties resulting from brokers' practices after the purchase." (See: "Floating Party: Boiler-Room Brokers Just Keep Resurfacing, J.T. Moran Case Shows --- Former First Jersey Salesmen Appeared at Moran, Using Hype on Cold-Call Clients --- Trading-Floor Animal House" -- WSJ April 26, 1990)

SEASONAL FUTURES TRADES recommended by Pacific Research Center, listed in a table accompanying the commodities story in yesterday's edition, should have said to buy September Eurodollar futures on June 25 and sell them on July 20. The table misstated this trade recommendation.

(See: "Commodities: Stotler Unit Seeks Seasonal Trade Bargains With Profit Potential; 12 Recommendations Made" -- WSJ June 4, 1990)
