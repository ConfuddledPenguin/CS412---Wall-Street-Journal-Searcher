Marketing Brief -- Carolco Pictures Inc.: Firm, Live Entertainment Create Videocassette Label

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

Carolco Pictures Inc. and Live Entertainment Inc. said they created a new videocassette label called Carolco Home Video. Films under the new label will be distributed exclusively by Live's International Video Entertainment Inc. unit. Carolco, which holds a 48% stake in Live, has had an exclusive home video distribution agreement with International Video since 1986.

The first title to be released under the new Carolco Home Video label will be "Music Box," on June 21. Other titles will include "Mountains of the Moon," "Total Recall," "Repossessed" and "Air America."

Carolco is a Los Angeles-based entertainment company. Live is a Van Nuys, Calif., entertainment software supplier, distributor and retailer.
