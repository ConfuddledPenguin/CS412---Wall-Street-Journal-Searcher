BP Petroleum to Sell Funk

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

LONDON -- BP Nutrition Inc., a unit of British Petroleum Co., said it is offering its Edward J. Funk & Sons Inc. unit for sale. Edward J. Funk & Sons produces and markets corn and soybean seed.

Robin Gourlay, chief executive of BP Nutrition, said the company's strategy "is to pursue opportunities in our traditional businesses while shifting the balance of our portfolio towards value-added products and the final consumer."
