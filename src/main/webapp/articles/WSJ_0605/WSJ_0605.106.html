Business Brief -- Isomedix Inc.: Investment Group Increases Stake in Company to 7.9%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

A group led by New York money manager Julian H. Robertson Jr. lifted its stake in Isomedix Inc. to 7.9%, but the company said the increase was only an investment. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the group disclosed that it holds 510,000 shares of the Whippany, N.J., provider of sterilization services. The holding includes 95,000 shares purchased April 12 to May 17 for $10.75 to $11.87 apiece.

John Masefield, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Isomedix, said that the company speaks with Mr. Robertson "routinely." The investors led by Mr. Robertson "take three-{year} to five-year positions in companies they like," Mr. Masefield said.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Isomedix shares rose 25 cents to $12.375.

For 1990's first quarter, Isomedix reported earnings rose 35% to $968,997, or 15 cents a share, from $714,361 or 14 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Average shares outstanding rose 29% to 6.7 million. Sales advanced 21% to $5.9 million from $4.9 million.
