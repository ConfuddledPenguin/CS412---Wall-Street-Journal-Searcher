Who's News: Glasky Picked to Lead Private Placement Unit At Chase Manhattan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

NEW YORK -- Chase Manhattan Corp. named Joel H. Glasky managing director and division executive in charge of the private placements unit of Chase Securities Inc., the bank holding company's securities subsidiary. The 41-year-old Mr. Glasky comes to Chase after five years in a similar post at Shearson Lehman Hutton Holdings Inc., a unit of American Express Co., where he supervised or participated in over 200 private placement transactions totaling more than $11 billion.

Private placements are the sale of securities to small groups of institutions rather than to the public at large. Because the buyers of such securities are assumed to be sophisticated investors, the issuance of private-placement securities don't need the lengthy and complex filings with regulatory authorities that are required of issues sold to the public.
