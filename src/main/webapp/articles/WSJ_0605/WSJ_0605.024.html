MEI Diversified Completes Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- MEI Diversified Inc. said it completed the sale of its candy and snack group to Ezaki Glico Co. of Osaka, Japan, for $58 million. MEI previously said the sale of the business, which had 1989 sales of $107 million, marks the company's planned departure from the candy and snack business. The sale won't result in a gain because the purchase price approximates the book value of the assets sold.
