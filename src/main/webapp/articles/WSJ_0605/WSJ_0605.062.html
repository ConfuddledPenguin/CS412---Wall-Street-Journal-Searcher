Dillard Completes Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

LITTLE ROCK, Ark. -- Dillard Department Stores said it completed the purchase of J.B. Ivey & Co. from a unit of B.A.T Industries PLC for about $109 million. B.A.T decided to sell Ivey, a chain of 23 department stores based in Charlotte, N.C., along with other, larger department-store units as part of a restructuring to fend off hostile overtures from investor Sir James Goldsmith.

Dillard owns about 162 department stores in the Central and Southwest U.S. The purchase of Ivey marks its entrance into the North and South Carolina markets and expands its presence in Florida.
