Business Brief -- Regional Bancorp Inc.: Report of Loss Is Expected For Fiscal Second Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

Regional Bancorp Inc. said it expects to report a loss for the second quarter ending June 30. In the year-earlier period, the bank holding company had net income of $665,000, or 24 cents a share. The Medford, Mass., company also said it will declare a 15 cent dividend, payable July 13 to stock of record June 15.

"We expect second-quarter results to be adversely affected by the costs that we were forced to incur in connection with the recent proxy contest initiated by a shareholder group and by a special provision of approximately $1.2 million to the bank's loan-loss reserve," said Thomas F. O'Connor, president.

The provision related to nonperforming commercial real estate loans to a single borrower, said Mr. O'Connor. He added that the bank holding company has a capital-to-assets ratio exceeding 13%.
