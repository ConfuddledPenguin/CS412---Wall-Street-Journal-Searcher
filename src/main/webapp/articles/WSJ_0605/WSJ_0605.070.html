Kansas Gas & Electric Ruling

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

WICHITA, Kansas -- Kansas Gas & Electric Co. said a ruling by the Kansas Court of Appeals means the company won't have to refund $8.7 million plus interest to its customers as previously ordered. The utility said that the court ruled that a $29 million annual rate increase granted by the Kansas Corporation Commission effective January 1989 should have been permanent. Earlier this year, the state commission rejected a staff recommendation and ruled that part of the rate increase was subject to refund. The commission at that time also ordered a second $6.9 million refund that the company is still appealing.
