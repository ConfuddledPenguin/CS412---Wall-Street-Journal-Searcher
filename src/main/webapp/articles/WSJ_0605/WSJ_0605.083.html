Business Brief -- Ford Motor Co.: Explorer Utility Vehicles, Truck, Bus Chassis Recalled

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

Ford Motor Co., Dearborn, Mich., said it is recalling 2,700 1991-model, four-door Ford Explorer sport utility vehicles to replace faulty sunroof hinges. The auto maker said the hinges may misalign when owners reinstall the removable sunroof glass. As a result, Ford said, the sunroof may come off while the vehicles are being driven. The company said it has received four reports of sunroofs detaching, but has no reports of injuries.

Ford also said it is recalling 365 1990-model F-series and B-series truck and bus chassis equipped with Hydro-Max power brake systems to replace master brake cylinders.
