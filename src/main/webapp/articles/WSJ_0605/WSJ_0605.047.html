National Income Realty Trust

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

DALLAS -- National Income Realty Trust said it sold the Hawaii Business Park in Pearl City, Hawaii, for $11.4 million. The real estate investment trust said it expects to record a second-quarter gain of more than $5 million from the sale. The trust had a net loss of $1.3 million, or 36 cents a share, on revenue of $7.1 million in last year's second quarter.

National Income said it plans to use proceeds from the transaction to improve other properties and for investments.
