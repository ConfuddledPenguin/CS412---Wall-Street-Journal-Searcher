Who's News: Grossman's Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

GROSSMAN'S Inc. (Braintree, Mass.) -- Thomas R. Schwarz, president of Dunkin' Donuts Inc., was named president and chief operating officer of this home improvement retailer, effective June 25. Mike Grossman, chairman, president and chief executive, said he will step aside to become chairman emeritus and a consultant on Oct. 1, at which time Mr. Schwarz will become chairman and chief executive. Mr. Grossman said he is retiring because he has attained the goals he set for himself, including renovation and modernization of stores and reduction of long-term debt.
