Business Brief -- Doskocil Cos.: Pork Plant Line in Missouri To Be Closed Temporarily

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

Doskocil Cos. of Hutchinson, Kan., a maker of pork products and pizza toppings, said it will close its Marshall, Mo., slaughtering operations, starting next Monday, until pork industry conditions improve. The company, which is operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, said that high hog prices and reduced demand for pork products have hurt slaughtering margins, and that it expects this imbalance to continue through the summer. The temporary closing is expected to affect about 500 employees, but won't include other operations at the plant. Doskocil said it expects to reopen the Missouri facility as soon "as the environment becomes more favorable," probably within six months.
