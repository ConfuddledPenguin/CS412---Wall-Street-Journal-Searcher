Thermo Electron Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Thermo Electron Corp. said it completed the acquisition of E. et M. Lamort S.A., a closely held French maker of equipment for recycling paper, for 1.5 million shares of newly issued common stock. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Thermo Electron closed at $34.125, up 12.5 cents.

Thermo Electron and Lamort are longtime joint-venture partners in Sweden and North America. Lamort will become part of Thermo Electron Web Systems, a division of Thermo Electron which sells accessory products for paper makers.
