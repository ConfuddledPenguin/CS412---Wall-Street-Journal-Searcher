Wolohan Lumber's Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

SAGINAW, Mich. -- Wolohan Lumber Co. said its May sales increased 9.6% to $31.8 million from $29 million from a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year rose 2.7%. For the five months through May, sales rose 9.4% to $111.1 million from $101.5 million year earlier. Same-store sales increased 4.6%.
