UPS to Build Facility in Texas

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- United Parcel Service of America Inc., a world-wide package delivery company, said it plans to build a $21 million, 250,000-square-foot package handling facility at the Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport. Scheduled to open in mid-1994, the facility will have about 600 employees, with a planned expansion to 1,000 by 1997. UPS's national air hub is in Louisville, Ky.; the company has regional air facilities in Philadelphia and Ontario.
