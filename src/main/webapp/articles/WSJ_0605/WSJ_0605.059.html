Foster Wheeler Unit Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

CLINTON, N.J. -- Foster Wheeler Corp. said a French unit received a contract valued at $115 million to design and build a gasoline isomerization plant in France. The engineering and construction concern said Foster Wheeler Conception Etudes Entretien will build the plant for BP France, a unit of British Petroleum PLC. The plant is scheduled to begin operating in 1992, it said.

Isomerization is a process that can increase the octane ratings of unleaded gasoline.
