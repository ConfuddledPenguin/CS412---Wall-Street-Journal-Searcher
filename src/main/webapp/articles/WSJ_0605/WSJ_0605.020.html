Who's News: Federal-Mogul Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

FEDERAL-MOGUL Corp.(Southfield, Mich.) -- Philip Tomkinson was named vice president of global business development, a new post at this maker of automotive and aerospace parts. Mr. Tomkinson, 55 years old, joined the company in 1963 and most recently served as the president of World Trade, a Federal-Mogul subsidiary.
