Who's News: American Brands Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

AMERICAN BRANDS Inc. (Old Greenwich, Conn.) -- This maker of tobacco products and distilled spirits named three vice presidents to new positions in its American Tobacco Co., based in Stamford, Conn. William J. Moore becomes executive vice president of marketing. Mr. Moore was previously senior vice president of marketing. Donald S. Johnston becomes executive vice president of sales; previously he was senior vice president of sales. John W. Tucker becomes vice president of quality and manufacturing services; previously he was director of manufacturing quality and planning.
