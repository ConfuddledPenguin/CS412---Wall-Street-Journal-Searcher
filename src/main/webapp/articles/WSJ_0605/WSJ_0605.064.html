Amdura Signs Pact to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

DENVER -- Amdura Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement to sell its Coast to Coast Stores Inc. unit to Servistar for an estimated $75 million. Amdura, a hardware distributor that is operating under federal bankruptcy-law proceedings, said the transaction is subject to various conditions and bankruptcy court approval. It said the purchase price will depend on the value of the assets at closing.

Coast to Coast, Amdura's largest operating unit, has about $400 million in revenue. Servistar is a Butler, Pa., hardware cooperative with about 3,800 members.
