International: Anglo American's Profit Increased 18% for Year

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 06/05/90

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa -- Anglo American Corp. of South Africa Ltd. said net income rose 18% in the year ended in March, to 3.13 billion rand ($1.18 billion) from 2.65 billion rand a year earlier. Per-share earnings increased to 651 South African cents from 545 cents. The mining house and gold producer reported a final dividend of 240 cents a share, up from 200 cents, making the year's total dividend 325 cents a share, up 20% from 270 cents a share.
