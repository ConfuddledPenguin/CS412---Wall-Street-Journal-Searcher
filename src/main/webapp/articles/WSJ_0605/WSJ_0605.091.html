Who's News: Comarco Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

COMARCO Inc. (Anaheim, Calif.) -- Harrison Young II, 46 years old, said he resigned as president and chief executive officer of this computer products and services company to rejoin his family in Virginia. Mr. Young worked full-time at Comarco's offices here since assuming the president post in the wake of the company's financial problems nearly three years ago. Mr. Young oversaw Comarco's return to profitability and debt reduction program. Comarco said it has begun a nationwide search for a new chief executive. Don M. Bailey, 44, senior vice president, will assume Mr. Young's duties until a permanent successor is named.
