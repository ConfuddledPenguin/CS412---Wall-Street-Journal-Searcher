Recruit Co. Unit Businesses

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

JERSEY CITY, N.J. -- Recruit U.S.A. Inc. separated its businesses into three subsidiaries to help customers better distinguish the businesses' respective services. The company is a subsidiary of Japan's Recruit Co., an information concern.

The three units operating within Recruit U.S.A. are Information Technology Alliance Corp., providing information services; Newport Office Properties Corp., a real estate division; and International Career Information Inc., with publishing interests.
