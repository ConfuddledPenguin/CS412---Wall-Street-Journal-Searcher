Who's News: Corestates Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

CORESTATES FINANCIAL Corp. (Philadelphia) -- David N. Deutsch, 31 years old, previously a vice president, corporate finance, at Bear, Stearns & Co., was named vice president and managing director, investment banking, new posts at Congress Financial Corp., the principal domestic non-banking unit of this bank holding company.
