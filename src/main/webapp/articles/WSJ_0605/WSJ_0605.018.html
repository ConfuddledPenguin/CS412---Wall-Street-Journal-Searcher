Giant Group Sets Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Giant Group Ltd. called for redemption on July 5 of $10 million in principal amount of its $29 million outstanding of 14 1/2% subordinated notes due April 15, 1995. The notes will be redeemed at par plus accrued interest from April 16 to the redemption date of $32.22 per $1,000 principal amount of notes. Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. will serve as paying agent in connection with the redemption.

Giant Group owns Keystone Cement Co. and Giant Cement Co., which is involved in waste material burning. Giant also owns 44% of Rally's Inc., a fast-food chain.
