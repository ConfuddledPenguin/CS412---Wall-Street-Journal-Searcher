Medco Research Holder Suit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

LOS ANGELES -- Medco Research Inc. said a shareholder lawsuit filed against the company and its former director of research and medical affairs was dismissed without prejudice by the plaintiffs. The complaint had alleged that the former executive, Dr. William C. Govier, had violated securities law when he sold stock in the company in March, prior to the public announcement in April that several prospective buyers weren't interested in the company. Last month, Medco fired Dr. Govier, but wouldn't say whether his dismissal was related to the suit.

Medco develops treatments and diagnoses of cardiovascular diseases.
