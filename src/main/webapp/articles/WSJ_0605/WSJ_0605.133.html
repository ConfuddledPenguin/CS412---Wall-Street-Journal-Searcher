Business Brief -- NFC PLC: Freight Hauling Firm Posts 12% Rise in Pretax Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

NFC PLC of Britain reported a 12% rise in pretax profit for the 25 weeks ended March 24 to #36.4 million ($61.2 million) from #32.6 million in the 24-week period a year earlier. But the diversified freight hauling company reduced its full-year forecast, citing a slowdown in its transport and property divisions. NFC said its "best view" is that pretax profit will total #97 million, up 7.5% from #90.2 million a year earlier, but down from its earlier forecast of #105 million.

Profit after taxes and minority interests for the fiscal first half rose 17% to #27 million from #23 million a year earlier. Earnings per share rose 8.8% to 4.9 pence from 4.5 pence. Profit attributable to shareholders also totaled #27 million, up 41% from #19.2 million a year earlier, after a #3.8 million extraordinary loss. The latest period didn't include any special items. Sales rose 13% to #729 million from #648 million.

NFC said it agreed to acquire S.A. Distribuidora EMA, Spain's third-largest transport and distribution company, for one billion pesetas ($9.5 million).
