Marketing & Media: Noted. . . .

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

WHO'S NEWS: MCA Inc.'s Universal Studios unit said Linda Berkeley was named to the newly created post of vice president of business development. She will report directly to MCA President Sidney Sheinberg. She worked for eight months through October 1988 for Walt Disney Co., a major MCA rival, as director of marketing resources and planning. . . . Times Mirror Co. said Francis P. Pandolfi, president and chief executive officer of Times Mirror Magazines, assumed the additional new post of chairman of Sporting News Publishing Co., whose publications include the Sporting News and the Sporting Goods Dealer. . . . Ziff Communications Inc. said its Ziff-Davis Publishing Co. unit named Eric Hippeau executive vice president in charge of foreign and domestic magazine operations. He was publisher of PC Magazine. Ziff-Davis also said it will launch a magazine, PC Sources, for mail-order microcomputer products. Last week, it said it would create a book division, Ziff-Davis Press, to publish computer books.
