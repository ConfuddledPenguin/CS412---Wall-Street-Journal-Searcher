Business Brief -- Sprouse-Reitz Stores Inc.: Retail Chain Plans to Hold Talks With Potential Suitors

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

Sprouse-Reitz Stores Inc. said it will begin discussions with parties interested in acquiring all or part of the company, sparking a surge in its stock. The Portland, Ore.-based company said it isn't certain that such discussions will lead to a sale. The general merchandise retail chain also said it would continue to evaluate other methods of enhancing shareholder value.

Sprouse last month said it received an unsolicited buy-out proposal of $17 a share, or about $24.4 million, from real-estate property manager and investor Robert Bisno, chairman of TransAction Financial Corp. of Oakland, Calif.

Sprouse's stock rose $2.25 to $14.50 in national over-the-counter trading.
