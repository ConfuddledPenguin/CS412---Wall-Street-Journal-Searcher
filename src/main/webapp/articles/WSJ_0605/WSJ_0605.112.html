Nynex Unit to Establish New Area Code for Bronx

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

NEW YORK -- New York Telephone, a unit of Nynex Corp., said it plans to establish a separate telephone area code for the Bronx to meet the rapidly growing demand for cellular phones and pagers. The new area code, 917, would go into effect in September 1992 under a plan filed with the New York State Public Service Commission. The new area code will also serve all cellular phones and pagers currently in the 212 area code in New York City.

Currently, Manhattan and the Bronx share the 212 area code. Growth in information-intensive businesses could exhaust telephone numbers available in the 212 area code by 1993. Queens, Brooklyn, and Staten Island, which all share the 718 area code, woudn't be affected.
