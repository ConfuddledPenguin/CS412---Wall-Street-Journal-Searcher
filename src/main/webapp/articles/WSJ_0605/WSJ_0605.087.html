Business Brief -- Manpower PLC: Six Units Sold to a Group Led by Harvest Ventures

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

Manpower PLC said it sold six subsidiaries to an investor group led by Harvest Ventures Inc. for $44.9 million and preferred stock valued at $6 million. The Milwaukee employment services concern said the six temporary-staffing concerns sold were: Potomac Employment Services Inc. and Temporaries Inc., Washington, D.C.; Career Employment Services Inc., New York; Flexi-Force, Milwaukee; Professional Office Personnel Inc., Winston-Salem, N.C.; and Tempo Services Inc., Long Island, N.Y.

Harvest Ventures is an investment and merchant banking firm based in New York.
