Who's News: National Lumber & Supply Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

NATIONAL LUMBER & SUPPLY Inc., (Fountain Valley, Calif.) -- Marty Schaeffer, 43, was named to the new post of executive vice president and chief operating officer. Mr. Schaeffer will manage all but the financial operations of the company, which operates low-price home center stores. National Lumber, which last April filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection from creditors, said Mr. Schaeffer, formerly with Chicago specialty retailer Handy Andy Home Centers, would be "instrumental" in its reorganization.
