Banister Unit Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

EDMONTON, Alberta -- Banister Inc. said its pipeline unit received a 55 million Canadian dollar (US$46.8 million) contract by Pacific Coast Energy Corp., to build part of its new natural gas pipeline to Vancouver Island. Banister, formerly Banister Continental Ltd., said construction is scheduled to begin this month, with completion set for late summer 1991. Banister said the pact calls for it to build the Vancouver Island section of the pipeline, which will extend from from British Columbia's southern mainland.

Pacific Coast Energy is a joint venture of Westcoast Energy Inc. of Vancouver, British Columbia, and Alberta Energy Co. of Calgary, Alberta.
