BEI Electronics Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- BEI Electronics Inc. said it agreed to pay $22 million for four divisions of Systron Donner Corp., a Concord-based unit of Britain's Thorn EMI PLC. The Systron divisions will provide technology to bolster BEI's business of selling sensors for applications in such industries as aerospace, automobiles and medical equipment, BEI said. The company said it expects the transaction to close in early July.
