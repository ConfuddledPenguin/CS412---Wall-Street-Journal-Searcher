NBD Bancorp Acquires Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

DETROIT -- NBD Bancorp said it assumed the deposits and acquired some assets of First Venice Savings & Loan Association of Venice, Fla., from Resolution Trust Corp. NBD said it will form NBD Bank, Federal Savings Bank, to complete the deal. Resolution Trust is a federal agency created to sell off failed thrifts seized by the government.

First Venice has $57 million of deposits and NBD will acquire $46 million of assets, which are primarily residential mortgage loans. NBD Chairman Charles T. Fisher III said the acquisition will help NBD deliver custom banking services to NBD trust customers in Florida. "We do not intend to become a major player in the savings and loan business," Mr. Fisher said.
