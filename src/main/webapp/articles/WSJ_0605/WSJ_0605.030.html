Who's News: Columbia Gas System Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

COLUMBIA GAS SYSTEM Inc. (Wilmington, Del.) -- Lawrence J. Macdonald, former executive vice president of Voyager Energy Inc., was elected president of this gas transmission company's Columbia Gas Development of Canada Ltd. unit, effective June 18. Mr. Macdonald, 43 years old, succeeds Spencer S. Chambers, who retired earlier this year.
