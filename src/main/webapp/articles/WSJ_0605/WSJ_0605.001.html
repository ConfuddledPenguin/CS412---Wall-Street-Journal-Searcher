MCN Corp. to Buy Concern

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

DETROIT -- MCN Corp., a natural gas utility holding company with data processing, natural gas technology and energy services units, said it agreed to buy the Genix Enterprises Inc. data processing subsidiary of National Intergroup Inc., a Pittsburgh-based drug distributor and steel company, for $20 million in cash and notes, subject to change through the closing, expected by June 30. National Intergroup said the sale is expected to result in a one-time, pretax gain of $13 million. The deal is subject to approval by MCN's board later this month. National Intergroup founded Genix in 1984 as an information services unit that provides mainframe computer operations services.
