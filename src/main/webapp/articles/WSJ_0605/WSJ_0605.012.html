Hillhaven Agrees on Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

TACOMA, Wash. -- Hillhaven Corp. said it will buy five nursing centers from National Medical Enterprises Inc., Santa Monica, Calif., for $30 million in cash and assumption of debt. The company said it expects to make the acquisitions, which are subject to government approval, before Aug. 31. The acquisition price includes assumption of about $5 million in debt, the company said. Hillhaven now leases the five centers, which are in New Hampshire, Vermont, Tennessee and Missouri. Hillhaven runs nursing centers pharmacies and retirement centers in 38 states.
