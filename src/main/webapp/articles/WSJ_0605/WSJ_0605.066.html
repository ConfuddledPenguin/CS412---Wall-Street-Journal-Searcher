Potlatch Corp. to Buy Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Potlatch Corp. said it agreed in principle to buy Blandin Paper Co.'s board plant in Grand Rapids, Minn., for an amount that wasn't disclosed. The company expects the transaction to be final by late this month.

A spokesman for Potlatch, a maker of wood products, said the company bought Blandin's board plant because it is in the process of doubling its mill capacity in the Minnesota area and Blandin's plant fits into that expansion.
