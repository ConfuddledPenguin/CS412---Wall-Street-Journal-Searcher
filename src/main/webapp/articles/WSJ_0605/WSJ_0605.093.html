International: Czech Venture for Siemens

  • Author: A Wall Street Journal News Roundup
  • Date: 06/05/90

Siemens AG of West Germany and Czechoslovakia's Tesla Prague agreed in principle to form a joint-venture telecommunications company, the official news agency CTK said. The new Czechoslovak-managed company, with a capital of 50 million to 80 million marks ($29.4 million to $47 million), will produce the Siemens EWSD digital telecommunication system for sale in Czechoslovakia and for export, CTK said. Apart from contributing to the company's capital, Siemens will invest 20 million marks in the plant, CTK said. The final agreement is due to be signed within three months.
