Osborn Communications Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

NEW YORK -- Osborn Communications Corp. said it expects to have a second-quarter pre-tax gain of about $1 million, or 14 cents a share, from repurchase of debt. The radio, television and cable TV operator said it repurchased $4 million of 13.875% debentures for about $2.8 million, to strengthen its balance sheet and reduce interest costs.
