Raster Devices Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

EDEN PRAIRIE, Minn. -- Raster Devices Corp. said it acquired "The Company," a closely held Boston-based maker of computerized typefaces for use in desktop publishing. Raster, a maker of hardware and software for laser printers, didn't disclose terms.

The new acquisition will operate under the name Digital Typeface Corp., as Raster's third operating unit.
