U.S. Energy Uranium Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

RIVERTON, Wyo. -- U.S. Energy Corp. and its Crested Corp. unit said they completed the formation of a joint venture with Kennecott Corp. to produce uranium in south-central Wyoming. The two companies said that Kennecott paid them $9 million last Friday and $6 million previously for a 50% interest in the mineral and other properties owned by U.S. Energy and its affiliates.

Kennecott, based in Salt Lake City, Utah, has agreed to fund the first $50 million in expenses on the project and to pay additional operating costs for U.S. Energy and Crested based on the price it receives for uranium oxide.
