Business Brief -- Shelby Williams Industries Inc.: Company Expects Earnings To Fall in Second Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

Shelby Williams Industries Inc. said it expects earnings in its second quarter ending June 30 to be lower than the year earlier's record $1.9 million, or 20 cents a share. Manfred Steinfeld, chairman and chief executive officer, said order rates for the Chicago-based company's seats used in restaurants and other businesses have slowed sharply in recent weeks.

Mr. Steinfeld said earnings may be closer to this year's first-quarter results than the year-ago second-quarter record. In the first quarter, net income was $910,000, or 10 cents a share, on sales of $40,961,000.
