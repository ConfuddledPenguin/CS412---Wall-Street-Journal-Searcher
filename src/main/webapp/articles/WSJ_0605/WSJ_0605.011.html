Mozambique Gets IMF Loan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

WASHINGTON -- The International Monetary Fund approved a $112.8 million loan to Mozambique to support structural economic adjustments in the African country through the end of 1992. The IMF said the loan will be disbursed in installments. The first semiannual installment of $12.1 million will be available for disbursement June 15. The loan, from a special pool of resources called the "enhanced structural adjustment facility," is for 10 years and carries an annual interest rate of 0.5%.
