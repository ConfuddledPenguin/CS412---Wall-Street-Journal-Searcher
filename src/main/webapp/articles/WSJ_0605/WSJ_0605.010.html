Technology: Du Pont to Market Biobit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/05/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Du Pont Co. said it was granted U.S. marketing rights for Novo Nordisk Bioindustrials Inc.'s biological insecticide known as Biobit. The product, made naturally through fermentation, is designed to control a variety of insects in fruit, vegetables, and cotton in addition to soybeans and alfalfa. Du Pont, a chemicals and energy concern, said the agreement marks its entry into the bioinsecticide market.
