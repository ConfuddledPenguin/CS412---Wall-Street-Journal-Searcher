Cadence Design Forms Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Cadence Design Systems Inc. said it is forming a European joint-venture company with European Silicon Structures S.A. that will develop software that will aid in the design of electronic systems. The joint-venture company, European CAD Developments Ltd., will be based in the United Kingdom and operate as an independent entity. It will focus its development on the companies' specialties, Cadence's electronic design automation software and European Silicon's designs.
