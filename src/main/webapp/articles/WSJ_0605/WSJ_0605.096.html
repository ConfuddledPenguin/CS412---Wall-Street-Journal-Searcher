Business Brief -- Santa Anita Realty Enterprises: Japanese Firm's Unit Buys California Office Building

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

Santa Anita Realty Enterprises Inc. said it sold for $33 million a Pasadena, Calif., office building. The Los Angeles-based real estate investment trust, which owned a 47% interest in the building, said its partner, Halferty Development Co. of Pasadena, Calif., also sold its 53% interest. The purchaser is Tohshin International Corp., a Hawaii-based unit of Tohshin Corp. of Japan.

Santa Anita said it expects to post about $2 million of income related to the sale in the second quarter. In the year-earlier quarter, the company earned $2.7 million, or 25 cents a share.
