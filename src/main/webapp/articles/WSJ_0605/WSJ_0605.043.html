S&P Adds Morrison Knudsen

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/05/90

NEW YORK -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said Morrison Knudsen Corp., a Boise, Idaho, engineering and construction company, will be added to its 500-stock composite index Thursday. It replaces Genentech Inc., a health-care and biotechnology company based in South San Francisco, Calif., 60% of which is being acquired by Roche Holding Ltd., a Swiss pharmaceutical company.
