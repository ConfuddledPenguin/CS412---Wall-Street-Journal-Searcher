Business Brief -- Trans Financial Bancorp Inc.: Bank Holding Firm Seeks Approval for Purchase Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/16/90

Trans Financial Bancorp Inc. said it is seeking approval of the Office of Thrift Supervision to make a bid for First Financial of Russellville Inc., the parent of First Federal Savings & Loan Association of Russellville, Ky. No formal offer has been made, but the Bowling Green, Ky., bank holding company indicated that it would offer about $23 a share.

First Financial has 363,630 shares outstanding, giving the transaction an indicated value of about $8.4 million. Trans Financial said there is no guarantee that either the federal thrift office or First Financial's board will approve the proposal.
