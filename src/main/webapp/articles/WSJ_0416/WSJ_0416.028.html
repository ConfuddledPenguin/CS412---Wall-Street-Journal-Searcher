International: Japan's Wholesale Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/16/90

TOKYO -- Japan's wholesale prices in March jumped 0.7% from the previous month and 3.9% from a year earlier, the Bank of Japan said. The month-to-month increase was the largest since a 0.7% rise in June 1989.

A Bank of Japan official attributed the steep increase to the weak yen.
