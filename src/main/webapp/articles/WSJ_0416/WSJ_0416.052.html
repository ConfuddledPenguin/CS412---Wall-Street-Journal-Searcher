Genetics Institute Says Loss Grew in Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/16/90

CAMBRIDGE, Mass. -- Genetics Institute Inc. said its first-quarter loss widened and revenue fell. The biotechnology concern said the loss widened to $7 million, or 57 cents a share, from $3.9 million, or 28 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue fell 44% to $8.6 million from $12.4 million.

A spokeswoman said first-quarter results were hampered by lower shipments of the company's anemia drug, erythropoeitin, or EPO. She also said that the results didn't include any revenue from the manufacture or sale of an immunity-building protein sold a year ago.
