Las Vegas Shopping Center

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/16/90

TOKYO -- Kabutodecom Inc. and a U.S. partner will build a shopping center in Las Vegas, Nev., an official of the Japanese real estate developer said. Kabutodecom's subsidiary, Kabuto International Nevada, and Johnson Development of New Mexico will jointly build the project. Kabuto International will manage the completed shopping center.

The project is expected to cost the equivalent of 10 billion yen ($63 million). The official wouldn't specify what funding each company would contribute.

The target date for opening the shopping center is May 1991.
