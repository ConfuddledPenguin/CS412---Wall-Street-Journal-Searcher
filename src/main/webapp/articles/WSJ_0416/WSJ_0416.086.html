Air Force Gives IBM $238.7 Million Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/16/90

WASHINGTON -- International Business Machines Corp. received a $238.7 million Air Force contract for aircraft electronics.

Ten companies received $50 million contracts each from the Air Force for hazardous-waste research. They were: Roy F. Weston Inc., Earth Technology Corp., Engineering-Science, ICF Technology Inc., Jacobs Engineering Group Inc., Law Environmental Inc., NUS Corp., O'Brien & Gere Engineers Inc., Radian Corp. and Tetra Tech Inc.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. got a $25.5 million Air Force contract for F-15E aircraft.

Raytheon Co. received a $21.7 million Army contract for Stinger missiles.

Martin Marietta Corp. was awarded a $14.9 million Army contract for mortar ammunition.
