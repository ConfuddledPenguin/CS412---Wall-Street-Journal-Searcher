Midway Airlines Slates Increases in Service

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/16/90

CHICAGO -- Midway Airlines said it plans to boost service to Philadelphia, Los Angeles and Cleveland from its hub here. The carrier said that on May 1 it will add two round-trip flights to Philadelphia International Airport, where it late last year started a second hub operation. The increase brings to eight the number of Midway daily round trips between Philadelphia and Chicago.

Effective June 1, the airline will also add a daily round-trip flight to Los Angeles International Airport, giving it five daily nonstop round trips to that city. It will additionally add a sixth daily round-trip flight between Chicago and Cleveland.
