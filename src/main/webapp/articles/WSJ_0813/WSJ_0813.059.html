Dutch Trade Balance Fell in May

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

THE HAGUE -- The Dutch balance of trade slid into a deficit of 900 million guilders (US$500 million) in May but registered a surplus of 2.2 billion guilders for the first five months of this year, the Netherlands Economics Ministry said. The May deficit resulted from a strong 9% rise in the value of imports at a time when exports were falling 2%. Imports in May were valued at 20.6 billion guilders compared with 19.6 billion guilders of exports.
