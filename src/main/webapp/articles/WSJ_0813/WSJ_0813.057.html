Eastern Airlines Fare Increase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

MIAMI -- Eastern Airlines said it will increase its domestic fares by 5.3%, effective Aug. 15, because of higher fuel costs. The carrier cited Iraq's invasion of Kuwait for the fare rise. Several other airlines have boosted fares, or tacked on surcharges, since the Middle East crisis sent crude oil skyward.

Eastern said the increase will not apply to passengers holding special weekend fare reservations for travel between now and Aug. 28.
