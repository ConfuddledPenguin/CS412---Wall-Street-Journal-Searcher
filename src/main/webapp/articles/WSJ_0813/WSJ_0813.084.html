Italy's Industrial Production

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

ROME -- Italy's unadjusted index of industrial production fell 3.6% in June from a year earlier, the State Statistical Institute Istat said. Istat said the index (1985 equals 100) registered 125.2 in June, compared with 129.9 in June 1989.

The drop was partly due to fewer working days in June of this year. The index was based on 21 working days; while June 1989 counted 22 working days.

The drop, which followed a year-on-year rise of 0.7% in May, confirms a downard trend in economic activity in recent months. Taking into account the difference in number of working days, the average daily industrial production edged up 0.1%, Istat said.
