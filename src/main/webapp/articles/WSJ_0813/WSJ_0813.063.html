Santa Fe Pacific Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

CHICAGO -- Santa Fe Pacific Corp. said it will make a scheduled Sept. 1 interest payment of $62.5 million on its 16% subordinated debentures, due 2003. The payment will be made in cash to holders of record Aug. 15.

The railroad, real estate and natural-resources company has the option of paying in cash or in additional debentures until 1994.
