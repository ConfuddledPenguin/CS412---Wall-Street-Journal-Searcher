Mark IV Buys Rolls-Royce Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

AMHERST, N.Y. -- Mark IV Industries Inc. said it acquired Ferranti-Packard Electronics from NEI Canada Limited, a subsidiary of Rolls-Royce, for 20.9 million Canadian dollars (US$18.2 million). Ferranti-Packard is a Canadian designer, manufacturer and marketer of electromagnetic display devices. It expects 1990 revenue of C$24 million. Mark IV is a maker of industrial products.
