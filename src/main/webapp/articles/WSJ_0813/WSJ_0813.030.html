Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

American General Finance Corp., a unit of Zurich-American Insurance Group, which in turn is a subsidiary of Zurich Insurance Co. of Switzerland, shelf offering of $1 billion of debt securities.

American Waste Services, Inc., offering of six million class A common shares, via Goldman, Sachs & Co., PaineWebber Inc. and First Analysis Securities Corp.

Carolco Pictures Inc., offering of $100 million of aggregate convertible subordinated debentures, via Bear, Stearns & Co. Inc. and Salomon Brothers Inc.

Input/Output Inc., a subsidiary of Triton Energy Corp., offering of 4.9 million common shares, 3.2 million to be sold by Triton, 1.7 million to be sold by Input/Output, via PaineWebber.

Scholastic Corp., proposed initial offering of 4.2 million common shares, of which 3,270,000 will be sold by the company and 930,000 by holders, via Goldman Sachs, Alex. Brown & Sons Inc., and Goldman Sachs International Ltd.

Vetco Gray Inc., initial offering of nine million common shares, via Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc. and Salomon Brothers.

With Design In Mind International Inc., shelf offering of 670,000 units, each consisting of one share of common stock and one common stock purchase warrant, via Paulson Investment Co. Inc.

---

Corrections & Amplifications

AMERICAN GENERAL FINANCE Corp. is a unit of American General Corp., Houston; its affiliation was incorrect in Monday's edition.
