The Mideast Conflict: North Carolina Sues Exxon Over Run-Up in Gas Prices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

RALEIGH, N.C. -- Following public outcry about the huge run-up in gasoline prices, the state attorney general filed suit against Exxon Corp., alleging deceptive trade practices. While a number of states have said they would pursue investigations into allegations of price-gouging and profiteering by major oil companies after the Iraqi invasion of Kuwait, it is believed this is the first suit following the big gasoline price increases.

North Carolina charged that Exxon raised its wholesale gasoline prices to distributors by as much as six cents a gallon after contracting to sell that gasoline at a lower price. In some cases, the state alleges, distributors had made deliveries to service stations before learning of the increase in prices.

An Exxon spokesman in Houston declined to comment on the suit.
