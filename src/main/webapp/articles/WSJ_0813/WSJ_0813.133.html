Business Brief -- McCaw Cellular Communications: Loss Narrowed in Quarter As Revenue Jumped 102%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

McCaw Cellular Communications Inc. reported a narrowed net loss of $60.7 million, or 34 cents a share, in the second quarter compared with $87.5 million, or 65 cents a share, a year earlier. The Kirkland, Wash., company said revenue and cash flow were at record levels. For the quarter, revenue increased 102% to $242.2 million from $120.1 million a year earlier. Cash flow rose to $63.4 million, compared with $11.6 million. The company cited the strength of its operations, further penetration into the market and the consolidation of its financial results with LIN Broadcasting Corp., which it acquired control of in March.

For the first six months, the company reported net income of $791.1 million compared to a loss of $185.2 million a year earlier. The latest six months includes the gain from the sale of its Southeast cellular systems to Contel Cellular Inc.
