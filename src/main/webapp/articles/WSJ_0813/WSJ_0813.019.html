Business Brief -- Trans World Airlines Inc.: Airline Swung to a Profit Of $103.4 Million in Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

Trans World Airlines Inc. posted net income of $103.4 million for the second quarter, compared with a net loss of $4.4 million a year earlier. Results included gains of $160.2 million from the sale of Chicago airport facilities, aircraft and part of TWA's stake in a passenger reservation system. Closely held TWA also had an extraordinary gain of $19.8 million from the repurchase of $213.1 million of debt.

Revenue rose 2.5% to $1.23 billion from $1.20 billion a year earlier.

Operating profit before interest expense and certain other costs fell almost by half to $43.9 million from $80.5 million a year earlier.

For the half, TWA had a net loss of $39.6 million on revenue of $2.24 billion, compared with a year-earlier net loss of $87.4 million on revenue of $2.19 billion.
