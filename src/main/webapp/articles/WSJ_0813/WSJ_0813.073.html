Who's News: Amsouth Bancorp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

AMSOUTH BANCORP. (Birmingham, Ala.) -- Dan L. Hendley will resign as vice chairman of this Southeast banking company and will step down as president and chief operating officer of its subsidiary, Amsouth Bank N.A. Mr. Hendley, 52 years old, said he was resigning to pursue other business interests. No specific date was given for the resignation, and no successor was named.
