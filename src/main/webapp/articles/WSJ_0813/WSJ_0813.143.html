Dividend News: La Jolla Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

LA JOLLA BANCORP, La Jolla, Calif., declared a special one-time cash dividend of 21 cents a share, payable Aug. 23 to stock of record Aug. 20. The bank holding company also declared a one-time 2% stock dividend, payable Aug. 23 to stock of record Aug. 21.
