Chemical Waste Management

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- Chemical Waste Management Inc. offered $500 million of subordinated convertible notes. The debt, due in 2010, was priced at $306.56 for each $1,000 note, to yield 6% to maturity. The notes are convertible into shares of the hazardous-waste disposal and cleanup company's common stock at an initial rate of 11.676 shares per note.

The company expects the offering to yield $150 million in proceeds. The funds will be used to retire debt of Waste Management Inc., which owns 77% of Chemical Waste Management.
