Business Brief -- Evans Inc.: Retailer Gets $33.5 Million Revolving Credit Agreement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

Evans Inc. said it received a $33.5 million revolving credit arrangement from a bank and an insurance company that gives the specialty retailer more financial flexibility. Chicago-based Evans wouldn't name the lenders. The working capital loan agreement, secured by all Evans's assets, also stretches out some principal payments on a note payable.

Evans had a loss of $3.2 million, or 64 cents a share, in the quarter ended June 2, on sales of $20.3 million. David B. Meltzer, chairman, said the new financing means the company "can now focus on the execution of our business plan."
