Spain's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

MADRID -- Spain's consumer price index rose 1.3% in July from June, the country's National Statistics Institute said Friday. The institute said that the increase brings Spain's year-on-year inflation rate to 6.2%, down from 6.5% in June.

Prices in July excluding volatile food prices edged up 0.4% from June.
