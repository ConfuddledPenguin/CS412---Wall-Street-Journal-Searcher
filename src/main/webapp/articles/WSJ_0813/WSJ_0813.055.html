REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Khomeini's Ghost

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

Saddam Hussein seems to be warming to his new role as the world's most demonic character. What else is one to make of the phantasmagoria of images and religious incantations that he released in a statement Friday? "O Arabs, O Moslems, O believers in God wherever you are. This is your day to rise and spread quickly in order to defend Mecca, which is captive of the spears of the Americans and the Zionist," said Saddam, who seems to have undergone a conversion on the road to Riyadh. Saddam, heretofore thought to be running a homicidal secular Islamic state, has suddenly got religion. "Arabs, Moslems, believers in God wherever you are. This is your day to jump up and defend Mecca, hostage of the Americans." The Iraqi theologian is offering a fairly hot crusade: "Burn the soil under their feet. Burn the soil under the feet of the aggressors and invaders." We suspect believers in God everywhere understand the dangers of being caught on the wrong side of a wind-blown, albeit holy, chemical attack.
