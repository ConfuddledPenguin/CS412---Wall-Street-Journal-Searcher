PRIME MOTOR INNS Inc. (Fairfield, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its ratings on about $230 million of debt of this hotel and real estate company, and removed the company from its CreditWatch list of issues under review. Subordinated debt was lowered to single-B-minus from double-B-plus. S&P noted that Prime has been affected by the rapid weakening in the hotel industry, which has reduced Prime's financial flexibility and will depress profitability and cash flow.
