International Brief -- Nestle S.A.: Swiss Food Firm Says Sales Were Flat in the First Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

Nestle S.A. posted sales of 22.7 billion francs ($16.9 billion) for the first half of 1990, compared with 22.8 billion francs a year earlier. The Vevey, Switzerland, food company cited the weakness of several currencies against the Swiss franc as the main reason for the flat sales. The results matched analysts' expectations.

Analysts expect Nestle's group net income for the full year to rise as much as 10%, depending on currency rates, from the year-earlier 2.41 billion francs.
