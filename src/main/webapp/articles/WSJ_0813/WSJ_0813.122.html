Business Brief -- WestAir Holding Inc.: Revenue Passenger Miles Increased 37% Last Month

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

WestAir Holding Inc., Fresno, Calif., said July revenue passenger miles increased 37% to 57.3 million from 41.8 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The load factor, or percentage of seats filled, for the month was 45.5%, compared with 48% a year earlier. WestAir attributed the drop to last year's "kids fly free" promotion, which boosted load factors.

The company also announced that it will raise fares 5% Wednesday to offset higher fuel prices. WestAir operates as United Express through a marketing agreement with United Air Lines. It operates more than 850 daily flights to 60 U.S. cities.

For the year through July 31, WestAir said revenue passenger miles rose 36% to 364.6 million from 267.7 million a year earlier.
