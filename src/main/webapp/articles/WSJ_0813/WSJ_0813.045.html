Washington National Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

EVANSTON, Ill. -- Washington National Corp. said it approved the additional buy-back of 750,000 shares of common stock. The company said it approved the purchases because its board believes its stock is undervalued at recent price levels. In New York Stock Exchange trading Friday, the stock closed at 18.875, up 50 cents.

About 300,000 shares remain under a prior buy-back program started in 1988.
