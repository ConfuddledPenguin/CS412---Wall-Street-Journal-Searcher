SmithKline Beecham Suit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

PHILADELPHIA -- SmithKline Beecham Corp. said a federal judge here gave preliminary approval to a proposed $22 million settlement of a class-action suit brought by shareholders in 1988. The company, which denied any liability or law violation, said the proposed settlement approved by Judge Robert Gawthrop III is subject to final court approval at an Oct. 3 hearing. SmithKline said it was settling to "eliminate the burden and expense of litigation." The shareholders alleged that SmithKline officials delayed disclosure of pharmaceutical sales trends that would have hurt the company's stock price.
