Money Rates

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

Friday, August 10, 1990

The key U.S. and foreign annual interest rates below are a guide to general levels but don't always represent actual transactions.

PRIME RATE: 10%. The base rate on corporate loans at large U.S. money center commercial banks.

FEDERAL FUNDS: 8 1/16% high, 7 15/16% low, 8% near closing bid, 8 1/16% offered. Reserves traded among commercial banks for overnight use in amounts of $1 million or more. Source: Babcock Fulton Prebon (U.S.A.) Inc.

DISCOUNT RATE: 7%. The charge on loans to depository institutions by the New York Federal Reserve Bank.

CALL MONEY: 9 1/4%. The charge on loans to brokers on stock exchange collateral.

COMMERCIAL PAPER placed directly by General Motors Acceptance Corp.: 7.85% 30 to 44 days; 7.75% 45 to 59 days; 7.65% 60 to 89 days; 7.575% 90 to 119 days; 7.50% 120 to 179 days; 7.40% 180 to 270 days.

COMMERCIAL PAPER: High-grade unsecured notes sold through dealers by major corporations in multiples of $1,000: 7.97% 30 days; 7.87% 60 days; 7.80% 90 days.

CERTIFICATES OF DEPOSIT: 7.65% one month; 7.66% two months; 7.70% three months; 7.92% six months; 8.05% one year. Average of top rates paid by major New York banks on primary new issues of negotiable C.D.s, usually on amounts of $1 million and more. The minimum unit is $100,000. Typical rates in the secondary market: 7.93% one month; 7.93% three months; 7.93% six months.

BANKERS ACCEPTANCES: 7.84% 30 days; 7.76% 60 days; 7.67% 90 days; 7.61% 120 days; 7.57% 150 days; 7.54% 180 days. Negotiable, bank-backed business credit instruments typically financing an import order.

LONDON LATE EURODOLLARS: 8 1/16% - 7 15/16% one month; 8 1/16% - 7 15/16% two months; 8% - 7 7/8% three months; 8% - 7 7/8% four months; 8% - 7 7/8% five months; 8% - 7 7/8% six months.

LONDON INTERBANK OFFERED RATES (LIBOR): 8 1/16% one month; 8% three months; 8% six months; 8 1/8% one year. The average of interbank offered rates for dollar deposits in the London market based on quotations at five major banks.

FOREIGN PRIME RATES: Canada 14.75%; Germany 10.50%; Japan 7.13%; Switzerland 10.88; Britain 15%. These rate indications aren't directly comparable; lending practices vary widely by location.

TREASURY BILLS: Results of the Monday, August 6, 1990, auction of short-term U.S. government bills, sold at a discount from face value in units of $10,000 to $1 million: 7.23% 13 weeks; 7.19% 26 weeks.

FEDERAL HOME LOAN MORTGAGE CORP. (Freddie Mac): Posted yields on 30-year mortgage commitments for delivery within 30 days. 10.04%, standard conventional fixed-rate mortgages; 8.125%, 2% rate capped one-year adjustable rate mortgages. Source: Telerate Systems Inc.

FEDERAL NATIONAL MORTGAGE ASSOCIATION (Fannie Mae): Posted yields on 30-year mortgage commitments for delivery within 30 days (priced at par). 9.99%, standard conventional fixed-rate mortgages; 8.35%, 6/2 rate capped one-year adjustable rate mortgages. Source: Telerate Systems Inc.

MERRILL LYNCH READY ASSETS TRUST: 7.69%. Annualized average rate of return after expenses for the past 30 days; not a forecast of future returns.
