JLG to Cut Work Force

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

MCCONNELLSBURG, Pa. -- JLG Industries Inc. said it will cut its McConnellsburg plant work force by 150, as a result of soft residential and commercial construction markets. There has already been a reduction of 135 workers over the past several months at the company's domestic and foreign facilities.

The maker of hydraulic machinery reported that its work force after the reduction will total about 1,400, with 1,150 in the U.S. and 250 overseas.
