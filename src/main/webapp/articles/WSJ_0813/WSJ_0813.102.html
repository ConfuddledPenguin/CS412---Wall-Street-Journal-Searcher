Sonat to Offer Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

BIRMINGHAM, Ala. -- Sonat Inc. said it registered an issue of zero-coupon, subordinated notes having a face value of $575 million at maturity and convertible into Sonat common shares. The energy company said proceeds from the offering, expected to total about $200 million, will be used for general corporate purposes, including debt reduction.

The notes, registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, are LYONs, or Liquid Yield Option Notes, a trademark of Merrill Lynch & Co. Merrill Lynch Capital Markets will serve as the underwriter for the offering.
