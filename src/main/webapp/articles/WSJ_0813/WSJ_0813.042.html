Who's News: MCA Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

MCA Inc. (Universal City, Calif.) -- Richard Palmese, 42 years old, was appointed president of this entertainment company's MCA Records division. Mr. Palmese, who joined the company in 1983 and was most recently executive vice president and general manager of the record division, succeeds Al Teller, 45, who last September became chairman of MCA's music entertainment group.
