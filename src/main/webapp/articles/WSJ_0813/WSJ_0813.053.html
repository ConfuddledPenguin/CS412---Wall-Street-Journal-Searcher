Dutch Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

THE HAGUE, the Netherlands -- Dutch consumer prices rose 0.3% in the month to mid-July and were up 2.3% from a year earlier, the Netherlands Economics Ministry reported. The upturn in July reflected mainly higher rent increases that are imposed annually in that month, the provisional data disclosed.

The consumer price index rose to 103.5 in the month to mid-July from 103.2 in the previous month and 101.2 in mid-July 1989.
