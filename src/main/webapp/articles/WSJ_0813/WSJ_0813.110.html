Who's News: Hogan Systems Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

HOGAN SYSTEMS Inc. (Dallas) -- Patric J. Jerge was named president and chief executive officer, and William O. Hunt was named chairman, succeeding Gary W. Fiedler, who resigned from all three positions to resume a career in banking. Mr. Jerge previously was executive vice president of the company's Integrated Banking Applications group. Mr. Hunt is and will remain chairman and chief executive of Alliance Telecommunications Corp. Mr. Fiedler will remain a member of the board of this software maker.
