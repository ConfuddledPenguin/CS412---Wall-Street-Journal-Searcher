Dividend News: Lumex Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

LUMEX Inc. directors omitted the two-cent quarterly dividend. As of June 30 the company had an average of 4.2 million shares outstanding. The Bay Shore, N.Y., manufacturer of products for the fitness, health-care and physical-therapy markets cited "depressed first-half results and the uncertain economic climate." For the first six months, Lumex reported a loss of $1.1 million, or 25 cents a share, against net income of $811,000, or 19 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. The company said, however, that it expects to be profitable in the second half. In American Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Lumex fell 62.5 cents to $5.75.
