Addington Environmental Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

ASHLAND, Ky. -- Addington Environmental Inc. said it signed an option to acquire an existing landfill in South Carolina. The option allows Addington Environmental, a subsidiary of the coal mining concern Addington Resources Inc., to purchase the assets of Appleton Sanitary Landfill in Allendale County, S.C., for an undisclosed amount.
