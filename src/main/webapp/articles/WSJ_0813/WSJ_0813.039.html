Norway's Inflation Held at 3.6%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

OSLO -- Norway's inflation rate was 3.6% in July, unchanged from June and down sharply from the July 1989 level of 4.8%, according to official figures. The Norwegian Central Bureau of Statistics announced Friday that July's Consumer Price Index was 231.2, a fractional change from the 231 index reported in June. The July 1989 figure was 223.2.
