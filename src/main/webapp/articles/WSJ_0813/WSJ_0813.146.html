Morrison Knudsen Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. said its joint venture with John Laing PLC of London was selected to manage construction of an $800 million research campus to be built 30 miles north of London. Galaxo Group Ltd., a London pharmaceuticals maker, chose the joint venture to build a 75-acre campus in Stevenage, Hertfordshire. The campus will include chemistry, pharmacology, molecular and cellular laboratories and support facilities.
