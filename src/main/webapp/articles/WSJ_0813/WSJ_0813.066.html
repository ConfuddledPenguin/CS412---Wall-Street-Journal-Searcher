Hillhaven Corp. to Buy Six Nursing Centers It Currently Is Operating

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

TACOMA, Wash. -- Hillhaven Corp. said it will purchase for $20.8 million six nursing centers in Kentucky that it currently operates under leasing agreements. The transaction will be financed through an offering of asset-backed commercial paper, a company spokesman said. The transaction is subject to governmental and other approvals and is expected to close before Oct. 31. The nursing centers to be acquired have 800 beds, and the company reports a 98% occupancy rate.

Hillhaven owns about one-third and leases the remainder of 342 long-term care facilities in 38 states. Hillhaven provides care for 42,000 people and operates 24 retirement communities as well as 116 retail and institutional pharmacies in the U.S.
