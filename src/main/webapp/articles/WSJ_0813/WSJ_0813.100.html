Canada Housing Starts Fall 11%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian housing starts declined 11% in July to a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 173,000 units from a June level of 195,000 units, Canadian Mortgage and Housing Corp., a government agency, said. The corporation said the July decline provided further evidence that weakness in house-building activity is becoming more widespread across the country.

Separately, Statistics Canada, a government agency, reported that Canada's seasonally adjusted unemployment rate rose to 7.8% in July from 7.5% in June.
