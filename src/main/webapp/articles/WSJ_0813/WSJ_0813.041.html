First Federal Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

PONTIAC, Mich. -- First Federal Bancorp said a temporary restraining order in effect against the federal Office of Thrift Supervision was indefinitely extended by the Cincinnatti Court of Appeals. The restraining order was to have ended Friday. It will give First Federal officials time to appeal a federal district judge's denial for a preliminary injunction that will prevent federal regulators from appointing a conservator or receiver for a First Federal subsidiary.
