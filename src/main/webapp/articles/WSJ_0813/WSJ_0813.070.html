Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 175,432 metric tons in the week ended Aug. 4, down 31% from 254,057 tons in the preceding week and down 43% from 305,149 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton equals 2,204.6 pounds. Cumulative output this year is 8,437,013 metric tons, down 11% from 9,474,296 metric tons a year earlier.
