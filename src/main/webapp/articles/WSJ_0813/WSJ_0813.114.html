Who's News: Storehouse PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

STOREHOUSE PLC (London) -- Martin A. Burke was named executive vice president and chief operating officer, a new post, at Conran's Habitat USA, a New York division of Habitat Group, a London subsidiary of this retailing company. The 37-year-old Mr. Burke had been vice president of R.H. Macy & Co., the New York retailer.
