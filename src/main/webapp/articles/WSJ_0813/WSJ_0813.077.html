Technology: Phoenix Laser Systems Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Phoenix Laser Systems Inc. said that human clinical trials for its ophthalmic workstation have begun at the Barraquer Institute in Bogota, Colombia. The workstation is a self-contained unit that combines on-line diagnostic measurement, advanced laser beam positioning, high-speed tissue tracking and therapeutic devices for the precise control of laser surgery.

The tests are under the guidance of a team of doctors from the institute, the Horatio Ferrer Institute in Miami and the Pacific Presbyterian Medical Center in San Francisco.
