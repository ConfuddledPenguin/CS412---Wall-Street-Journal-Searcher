Exabyte Corp. 3rd-Quarter Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

BOULDER, Colo. -- Exabyte Corp. said it expects third-quarter sales to "substantially exceed" the sales of $23.9 million for the same period a year ago. But the company said sales and net income for the third quarter will fall below second-quarter results. For the second quarter, ended June 30, Exabyte reported profit of $8 million, or 38 cents a share, on sales of $46.3 million.

The maker of cartridge-tape systems said third-quarter sales will be affected by excess inventory at certain customers, seasonal slowness in the European market and a general softening in the computer industry.
