Monsanto Co. Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

ST. LOUIS -- Monsanto Co. said it signed an agreement in principle to form a joint venture in Southeast Asia with Golden Hope Plantations Berhad, located in Malaysia. The venture will focus on the manufacture of thermoplastic elastomers, which are vulcanized rubber products with applications in the automotive, medical, food and electrical industries.
