Who's News: Morrison Knudsen

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

MORRISON KNUDSEN Corp. (Boise, Idaho) -- Joseph G. Fearon was named chairman of this construction, transportation and engineering concern's Rail Systems Group. Thomas J. Smith was named president and chief executive officer of the group. The company said it made the adjustment to meet the needs of its rapidly growing mass transportation manufacturing group. Mr. Fearon was formerly the president of the group. Mr. Smith was formerly the senior vice president of manufacturing operations for Fokker Aircraft BV in Amsterdam.
