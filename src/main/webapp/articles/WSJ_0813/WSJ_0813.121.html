Who's News: Safety-Kleen Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/13/90

SAFETY-KLEEN Corp. (Elgin, Ill.) -- Joseph F. Knott, 54 years old and formerly executive vice president of this automotive and industrial cleaning concern, was named president and chief operating officer. He was also named a director, expanding the board to nine members. Donald W. Brinckman, 59, and formerly president, was named chairman and remains chief executive officer. The former chairman, Russell A. Gwillen, 68, was named chairman emeritus and remains a director.
