Who's News: News Corp. Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/13/90

NEWS CORP. Ltd. (Sydney, Australia) -- Tom Sherak was named executive vice president of Twentieth Century Fox, the film unit of News Corp.'s Fox Inc. Mr. Sherak, 45 years old, has been president of distribution and marketing for the studio since he joined the company in 1983. He will continue to oversee distribution and marketing, the company said, but his responsibilities will be expanded for him to work more closely with Twentieth Century Fox Chairman Joe Roth on production activities.
