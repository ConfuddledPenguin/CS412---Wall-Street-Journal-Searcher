Treasury Will Offer $9.75 Billion of Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $675 million in new cash Thursday by selling about $9.75 billion in 52-week bills and redeeming $9.08 billion in maturing bills. The bills will be dated April 12 and will mature on April 11, 1991. They will be available in minimum denominations of $10,000.

Tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Thursday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
