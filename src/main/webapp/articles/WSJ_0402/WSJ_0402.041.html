Weaver Arms Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

CALABASAS, Calif. -- Weaver Arms Inc., which is being reorganized under Chapter 11 federal bankruptcy protection, said it has entered into an agreement to acquire Video USA, a New York-based chain of video rental stores. Video USA, which consists of 68 stores, is owned by a group of limited partnerships. Daniel Lezak, president of Weaver Arms, said the 300 limited partners of Video USA will receive 20 million units of Weaver, or 60% of the company, in exchange for Video USA. Mr. Lezak said the units will consist of common stock and warrants to purchase common stock.
