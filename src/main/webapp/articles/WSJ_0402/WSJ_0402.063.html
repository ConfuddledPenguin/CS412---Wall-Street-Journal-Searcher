Astra AB Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

STOCKHOLM -- Astra AB said it's buying Spanish pharmaceutical company Laboratorio Ifesa S.A. The Swedish pharmaceutical group said it bought the total stock of Ifesa, which produces and markets drugs used against respiratory diseases.

Astra, which declined to disclose the price of the transaction, said Ifesa's total sales are expected to be 120 million kronor ($19.6 million) this year.

The group said the purchase was part of its aim to increase its influence over the marketing of its products in Spain.
