Who's News: National City Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

NATIONAL CITY Corp. (Cleveland) -- David C. Fynn, vice president of sales administration, was named vice president and corporate compliance officer of this bank holding company, succeeding Richard Dorow, who retired in February. Mary H. Griffith, senior vice president of public relations at First National Bank of Louisville, was named vice president and director of corporate communications, a new post at National City. First National Bank is a member bank of National City.
