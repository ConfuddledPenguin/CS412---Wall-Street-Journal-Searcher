Chase Manhattan Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

NEW YORK -- Chase Manhattan Corp. said that it has agreed to buy nine upstate New York branches of Citicorp. Terms weren't disclosed. The nine branches, with a total of $380 million in deposits, are located in Syracuse, Camillus, Cicero, Dewitt, Liverpool, Manilius, Skaneateles, Utica and New Hartford.

The sale signals a push by Chase's Rochester-based bank, Chase Lincoln First Bank, into the state's center.
