Business Brief -- Domtar Inc.: Canadian, Some U.S. Assets Of Salt Division Are Sold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

Domtar Inc. said it sold all Canadian and some U.S. assets of its Sifto Salt division to North American Salt Co., Mission, Kan. Terms weren't disclosed. The sale largely completes the disposal of Domtar's chemical and energy interests, which the Montreal forest and building-products concern began last year. A small specialty chemicals concern, Clough Chemicals Inc., remains to be sold.

Domtar said the salt assets sold include a rock salt mine in Goderich, Ontario, evaporated salt plants in Goderich, Amherst, Nova Scotia and Unity, Saskatchewan, Nickell Salt Co. in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, and related distribution facilities.
