Coast Savings Financial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

LOS ANGELES -- Coast Savings Financial Inc. said it withdrew its registration statement for a contemplated offering of $150 million of convertible preferred stock, citing poor market conditions and the excessive dilution that would result from such an offering. Coast Savings Financial is the holding company of Coast Savings & Loan, one of the nation's largest savings and loan associations.
