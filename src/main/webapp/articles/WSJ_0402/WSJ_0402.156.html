Business Brief -- NCR Corp.: Teradata Stake Purchased In Computer-Venture Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

NCR Corp. and Teradata Corp., completing terms of a venture for jointly developing their parallel-processing computer technologies, said NCR bought 1,400,995 newly issued Teradata shares for $26.26 each, or $36.8 million. The purchase gives Dayton, Ohio-based NCR, an information-processing systems company, a 9.4% stake in Teradata, a Los Angeles-based computer systems concern. The price was a 15% premium over the average closing price of Teradata shares in the 30 days before the venture was first announced. In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Teradata shares closed at $31.50, down 75 cents. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, NCR shares closed at $68.625, down $1.875.

Under the agreement, NCR and Teradata will contribute personnel, technology and funding to the project, with NCR providing most of the funding initially. The two companies will develop general business computer systems during the three-year project.
