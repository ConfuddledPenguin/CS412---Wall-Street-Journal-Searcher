Battle Mountain Holding

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

HOUSTON -- Battle Mountain Gold Co. said it will pay $15.8 million and 150,000 common shares to increase its stake in Empresa Minera Inti Raymi S.A. of Bolivia to 51% from 33.3%. The mining concern said it will exercise its option to buy half the shares, or an 8.8% Inti Raymi stake, from Westworld Resources Inc. for $7.9 million and 75,000 shares of Battle Mountain common. It said it will buy the other half, for the same price, from Zeland Mines S.A., a Panamanian company.

After the transaction is closed, Battle Mountain said it will have paid a total of $38.8 million and 550,000 common shares for its share of the Bolivian concern. The transaction will reduce Westworld's stake in Inti Raymi to 24.5%, Westworld said.
