REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Maximum Hypocrisy II

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

We recently noted that while millions of small businesses have to endure the paper work of the revised minimum-wage law, Rep. Frank Annunzio of Illinois, chairman of the House Administration Committee, wants to kill an extension of the law to cover the House's own 10,000 employees. Rep. Annunzio, a liberal Democrat, says that, after all, the Senate exempts itself from the law and the record-keeping would tie up the House in knots. He told ABC News recently, "We talk about eliminating bureaucracies. We're building another bureaucracy that is not necessary, not needed; it's too expensive, and we ought to say so, and we ought to cut it out." Hmmm. He better not repeat that too often. Voters might think he is referring to Congress itself.
