Taiwan Will Cut Auto Tariffs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

TAIPEI -- The Taiwanese Finance Ministry said it will lower the tariff on small passager cars to 30% from 40%. During the annual tariff reduction meeting, ministry officials said the cut for imported autos is in line with the six-year Auto Industry Development Plan that expires in 1991. The plan is aimed at gradually opening the local market to foreign cars.
