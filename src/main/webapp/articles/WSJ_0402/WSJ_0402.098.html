Technology: General Computer Posts Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

TWINSBURG, Ohio -- General Computer Corp., which develops computer systems for pharmacies and medical offices, posted a deficit for its fiscal third quarter and said it will receive $775,000 in interim financing from its chairman, Sidney Dworkin, to meet capital requirements to fund continuing operations. The company said its loss narrowed to $376,000, or 24 cents a share, for the quarter ended Feb. 28, from $875,000, or 55 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue fell 15% to $3 million from $3.6 million.

In the first nine months, the loss narrowed to $1.8 million, or $1.13 a share, from $2 million, or $1.27 a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose 3.5% to $8.9 million from $8.6 million.
