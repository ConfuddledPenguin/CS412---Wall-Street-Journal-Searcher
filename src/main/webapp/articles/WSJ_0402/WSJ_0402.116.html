Marketing & Media: CBS Sports Isn't Renewing Brent Musburger's Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

NEW YORK -- CBS Sports said it isn't renewing the contract of its lead anchor, Brent Musburger, whose four-year contract, worth $2 million annually, expires in June. Mr. Musburger, who has been with the CBS Inc. sports unit for 15 years, will have his final CBS broadcast tonight with the National Collegiate Athletic Association basketball championship.

A CBS official said Mr. Musburger and the network couldn't come to terms on a new agreement. The Associated Press reported that Ted Shaker, executive producer of CBS Sports, said network officials believed Mr. Musburger's workload was too heavy, but the broadcaster resisted attempts by CBS in contract talks to have him cut down.

Mr. Musburger, in a statement, said he was "surprised" by CBS's announcement, but it has been "a great run." He said he plans to take an extended vacation.
