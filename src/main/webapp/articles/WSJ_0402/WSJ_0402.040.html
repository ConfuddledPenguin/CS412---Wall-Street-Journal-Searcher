GM, Chrysler Rebate Programs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Corp.'s Cadillac division will offer rebates on certain 1990 models, and Chrysler Corp. extended its guaranteed rebate program. GM is offering rebates of $1,500 on the DeVille, Fleetwood and Brougham models, and $2,000 on Eldorado and Seville models through May 31.

Chrysler's rebate program, which had been scheduled to end last Saturday, was extended through May 31 for 1990-model cars and trucks, except minivans and LeBaron convertibles. Chrysler guarantees that if it offers a higher rebate through Sept. 30, it will make up the difference to a buyer who got a lower rebate. It also increased the rebate on its 1989-model LeBaron coupe to $2,000 from $1,000.
