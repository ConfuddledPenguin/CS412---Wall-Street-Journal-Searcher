Business Brief -- Curtice-Burns Foods Inc.: Rochester, N.Y., Firm Says Third-Period Net Fell 50%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

Curtice-Burns Foods Inc. said it expects third-quarter earnings to drop about 50% from year-earlier period on an 8% sales rise. The third quarter ended Friday.

A year ago, the Rochester, N.Y., company had net income of $3.2 million, or 39 cents a share, on sales of $204.1 million. The per-share figure reflects a 3-for-2 stock split paid in December.

David J. McDonald, Curtice-Burns president and chief executive officer, said the earnings decline reflects a poor crop year in the Northeast and high ingredient costs in the meat snack business. Sales of the company's oat bran products have also declined and prices on certain canned and frozen vegetables have dropped.
