South Korean Current Account

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

SEOUL -- South Korea's current-account deficit widened to $329 million in February, the second consecutive monthly setback, because of a slowdown in exports, central bank officials said. Tallies by the Bank of Korea listed the two-month deficit as $646 million, compared with a surplus of $888 million in the year-earlier period.

Bank officials said the deficit in February alone was the largest since February 1986, when an economic boom began.
