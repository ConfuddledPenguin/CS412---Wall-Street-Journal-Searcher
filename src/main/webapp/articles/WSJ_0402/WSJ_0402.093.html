Copperweld to Plan Meeting

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said it delayed preparation of its annual report and proxy statement until the company selects a date for a special shareholders meeting to vote on a merger offer. The company previously announced that a special committee of directors is evaluating a merger proposal from Imetal S.A., a French metals company that is Copperweld's largest shareholder.

Copperweld, a producer of wire, cable, rods and tubing, said it expects to mail its proxy statement to shareholders by May 15.
