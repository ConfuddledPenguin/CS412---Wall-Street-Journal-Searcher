First World Cheese Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

MAPLEWOOD, N.J. -- First World Cheese Inc. said it completed a $12 million, two-year revolving credit agreement with Bank of Tokyo Trust Co. The financing, which is secured by the company's available assets, replaces the company's prior short-term credit arrangements with another bank, National State Bank of Elizabeth, N.J. As of last Thursday, First World's borrowings under the prior arrangements were about $5.3 million.

The credit agreement won't include any potential equity interest for Bank of Tokyo Trust, First World said.
