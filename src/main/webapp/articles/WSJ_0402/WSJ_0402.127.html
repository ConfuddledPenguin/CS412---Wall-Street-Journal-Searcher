Who's News: Booz Allen Names Stasior President, Operations Chief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

NEW YORK -- Booz, Allen & Hamilton said William F. Stasior was named president and chief operating officer. The management and technology consulting firm also said Cyrus F. Freidheim Jr., managing director, U.S. management consulting, was appointed to the additional position of vice chairman. In the new post, Mr. Freidheim, 54 years old, is responsible for leading the firm's efforts toward globalization of management and technology services.

As president, Mr. Stasior, 49, formerly president of Booz Allen's Information Technology Center, will be responsible for the firm's day-to-day operations. A Booz Allen spokesman said the president's post hasn't been filled since 1984, when R. Michael McCullough, 51, took over as chairman.

John Rhodes has retired from Booz Allen as vice chairman, but the spokesman said Mr. Freidheim isn't succeeding Mr. Rhodes.
