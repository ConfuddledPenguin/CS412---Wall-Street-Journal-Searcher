Who's News: Angio-Medical Names Hymes to Added Posts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

NEW YORK -- Angio-Medical Corp. said W. Frederick Hymes, president and chief operating officer, was elected to the additional posts of chairman and chief executive officer. Dr. Hymes, 51 years old, succeeds Robert S. Sinn, 59, the company's founder, who resigned as chairman, chief executive officer and a director to pursue other business interests. John D. Douros Jr., a consultant to pharmaceutical companies, was elected to Mr. Sinn's board seat.

Angio-Medical, which develops and markets pharmaceutical and cosmetic products, has reported losses recently. The company has initiated a series of cost-saving measures and has raised financing to fund working capital.
