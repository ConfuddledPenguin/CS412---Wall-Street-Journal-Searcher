Financing Business: Storage Technology Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

STORAGE TECHNOLOGY Corp., Louisville, Colo., said it will redeem on May 14 $34.1 million principal amount of its 13.5% senior debentures, due 1996, as part of a plan to use the proceeds of a recent $119 million equity offering to retire debt. The maker of data storage equipment said the redemption price is $106.78 for each bond, including accrued and unpaid interest and a 2% premium. April 9 is the record date for selection by lot of the bonds to be redeemed. Storage Technology has $285 million in senior debentures outstanding.
