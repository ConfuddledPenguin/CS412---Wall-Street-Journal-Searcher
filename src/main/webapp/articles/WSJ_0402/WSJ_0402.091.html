Dividend News: Finevest Foods Inc. Suspends Dividend

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Finevest Foods Inc. said it suspended its quarterly dividend of three cents a common share because of a deficit resulting from its 1989 operating loss. Finevest, a dairy processor and wholesale distributor of frozen foods and dairy products in the Southeast, said it will save about $1.3 million annually from the payout suspension. The company's 1989 net loss was $19 million on sales of $700.8 million. The loss included a third-quarter charge of $10.7 million from restructuring the company's dairy operations.

With improved operating efficiencies from the restructuring and with declining raw-milk costs this spring, Finevest said it expects its dairy operation to return to profitability this year.

Finevest shares fell 50 cents, to close at $7.50, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday.
