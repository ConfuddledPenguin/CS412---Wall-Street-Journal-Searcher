Business Brief -- J.P. Industries Inc.: T&N PLC of Britain Begins $190 Million Tender Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

T&N PLC of Britain said it began its $190 million, or $17.30-a-share, tender offer for J.P. Industries Inc. The offer also includes the assumption of $185 million of J.P. Industries's debt. The offer expires midnight April 26 unless extended; it is conditioned on T&N's ability to attract at least a majority of J.P. Industries's stock.

T&N, based in Manchester, England, said it plans to merge its U.S. auto parts operations into J.P. Industries and make the Ann Arbor, Mich.-based car, truck and locomotive engine-parts manufacturer a T&N subsidiary.

Under terms of the definitive merger agreement reached last week, T&N will receive $12 million if J.P. Industries is acquired by another party.
