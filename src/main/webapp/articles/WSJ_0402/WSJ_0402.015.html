Who's News: Ricoh Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

RICOH CO. (Tokyo) -- Hisashi Kubo, 58 years old, executive vice president of this maker of copiers, fax machines and office automation products, returned to the U.S. to serve as chairman of Ricoh Corp., the U.S. unit. He succeeds Keiji Endoh, 54, who returned to the parent as managing director in charge of commercial affairs. Mr. Kubo led Ricoh's U.S. operation as president and later chairman from 1979 to 1986.
