Business Brief -- Gillette Co.: Company Arranges to Sell Some European Operations

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

Gillette Co. said it agreed to sell its European skin care and hair care businesses to Nobel Consumer AB, a division of Nobel Industries of Sweden, for $107 million. Gillette also will retain some $30 million in receivables. Gillette said in Boston that it expects the sale to be completed within 60 days.

The European brands being sold to Nobel include Silkience hair care products, Antica Erboristeria herbal toiletries in Italy and La Toja toiletry products in Spain.

Gillette isn't selling its European deodorant, anti-perspirant and shaving preparations lines, which it will continue to make and market.

Gillette had said last December that it planned to sell certain European toiletries businesses as part of a realignment of its North Atlantic shaving and personal care products into one operating unit.
