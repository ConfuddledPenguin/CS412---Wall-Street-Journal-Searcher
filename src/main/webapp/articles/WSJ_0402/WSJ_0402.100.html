Financing Business: Molson Cos.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

MOLSON Cos., a Toronto-based brewing, retailing and consumer products concern, said it plans to redeem June 26 its 18.25% debentures, due Nov. 1, 1991, and April 16, 1995. Redemption prices will be 100% of the principal amount plus accrued interest for the 1991 debentures, and 101.45% of the principal amount plus accrued interest for the 1995 debentures. Molson said the total principal amount of the debentures to be redeemed is 36.7 million Canadian dollars (US$31.4 million).
