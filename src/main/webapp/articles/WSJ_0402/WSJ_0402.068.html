Hoechst Unit Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- The West German chemical group Hoechst AG said it agreed to merge its latex paints business with that of Mitsubishi Kasei Corp. of Japan. Financial terms weren't disclosed. Hoechst said the companies' respective paint operations will be placed into an existing 50-50 joint venture company, Kasei Hoechst Co. Ltd., which for the past 23 years has produced and marketed reactive dyes.

The agreement for the new business cooperation still needs the approval of "relevant authorities," Hoechst said, adding that the merger is planned to take effect July 1.
