Business Brief -- Pernod-Ricard S.A.: Beverage Concern Posts Rise Of 95% in 1989 Earnings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

Pernod-Ricard S.A. said its consolidated net profit after payments to minority interests rose to 1.45 billion francs ($254 million) in 1989, up 95% from the year-earlier total of 745 million francs ($130 million). The French beverage concern told a news conference that the jump includes a nonrecurring gain of one billion francs from the sale of its soft-drink unit, Societe Parisienne de Boissons Gazeuses, to Coca-Cola Co. after a legal dispute over French distribution rights for the U.S. company's products.

Consolidated revenue climbed to 16.7 billion francs from 13.5 billion in 1988.
