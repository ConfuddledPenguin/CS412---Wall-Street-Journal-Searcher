WHO'S NEWS: Newsweek Inc., a unit of the Washington Post Co., named Walter Buchleitner, 47 years old, a vice president, associate publisher and advertising director of the magazine's U.S. edition. He was vice president and director of ad sales for U.S. News & World Report. He succeeds Arthur Karlan, who recently retired.
