Great Lakes Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

ANN ARBOR, Mich. -- Great Lakes Bancorp said it received final approval for its acquisition of Dollar Federal Savings Bank of Hamilton, Ohio, from the Office of Thrift Supervision. Dollar Federal's shareholders will receive $37.50 a share, making the total purchase price $12.4 million. Dollar Federal has $152 million in assets.
