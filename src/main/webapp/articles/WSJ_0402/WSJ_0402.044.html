Who's News: Becton, Dickinson & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

BECTON DICKINSON & Co. (Franklin Lakes, N.J.) -- Raymond P. Ohlmuller, 50 years old, assistant general counsel of this supplier of medical devices and diagnostic systems, was elected to the additional posts of vice president and secretary. He succeeds Robert G. Butler, 59, who retired.
