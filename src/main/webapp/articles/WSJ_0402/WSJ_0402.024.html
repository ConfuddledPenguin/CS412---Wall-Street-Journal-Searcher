GE Wins Contract For $402.9 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

WASHINGTON -- General Electric Co. received a $402.9 million Air Force contract for fighter engines.

Lockheed Corp. was awarded a $352.2 million Navy contract for Trident missile services.

Other contracts announced by the Pentagon went to:

-- Unisys Corp., $85.8 million from the Navy for fire-control systems and missile engineering services.

-- Raytheon Co., $73.4 million from the Air Force for radar and satellite equipment.

-- General Dynamics Corp., $68.5 million from the Army for Stinger missile parts.

-- International Business Machines Corp., $35.2 million from the Air Force for aircraft radar.

-- United Technologies Corp., $31.1 million from the Navy for helicopters.

-- Ford Aerospace Corp., a unit of Ford Motor Co.,$26.7 million from the Air Force for Sidewinder missile parts.

-- Honeywell Inc., $26.5 million from the Army for munitions.

-- Martin Marietta Corp., $20.9 million from the Army for night-vision equipment.

-- Diamond Shamrock Refining & Marketing Co., $20.8 million from the Defense Logistics Agency for jet fuel.

-- Jaycor, $19.7 million from the Army for nuclear test experiments.

-- Loral Corp., $17.5 million from the Army for radar warning receivers and intelligence training.

-- Grumman Corp., $16.3 million from the Navy for E-2C aircraft.

-- General Instrument Corp., $13 million from the Air Force for radar warning receivers.
