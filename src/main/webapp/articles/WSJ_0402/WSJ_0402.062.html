Varco International Buys Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

ORANGE, Calif. -- Varco International Inc., petroleum industry tools and equipment maker, said it agreed to acquire for $30 million all the assets of the MartinDecker unit of Cooper Industries Inc., a Houston-based maker of electrical, automotive, petroleum and industrial products. Varco had 1989 sales of about $87 million. Martin-Decker, with sales of about $25 million and 200 employees, makes instruments and equipment for oil-related and other industrial applications.
