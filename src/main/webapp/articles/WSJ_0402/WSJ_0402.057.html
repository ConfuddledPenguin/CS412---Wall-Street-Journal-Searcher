Chrysler Idles Assembly Plants

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

HIGHLAND PARK, Mich. -- Chrysler Corp. said it is temporarily halting production at four production facilities this week because of sluggish sales. The No. 3 auto maker is idling assembly plants in Belvidere, Ill., Newark, Del., Toledo, Ohio, and Sterling Heights, Mich. The facilities are scheduled to resume production April 9. A total of about 12,700 workers will be affected.
