Who's News: General Development Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

GENERAL DEVELOPMENT Corp. (Miami) -- James I. Wyer, 66 years old, was named a director of this community developer, increasing board membership to nine. Mr. Wyer, former vice president and general counsel of American Cyanamid Co., is an attorney with Robinson, Wayne & LaSala in Newark, N.J.
