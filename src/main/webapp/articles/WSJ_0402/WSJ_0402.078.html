VIAG's Kloeckner Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

BONN -- The West German trading concern Kloeckner & Co., a unit of the conglomerate VIAG AG, said it sold its building materials trading division to London-based Meyer International PLC. Terms weren't disclosed. Kloeckner said the division has annual sales of about 300 million marks ($176.8 million) and was a "peripheral part" of its operations. Shedding the unit is in line with Kloeckner's strategy of concentrating more on international activities. The construction materials trading units only operate within West Germany.
