Marketing Brief -- Walt Disney Co.: First `Mickey's Kitchen' Set To Open at Store in April

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/02/90

Walt Disney Co. said it would open its first "Mickey's Kitchen" restaurant adjacent to a Disney retail store later this month. A Disney spokesman confirmed a United Press International report that the restaurant was under construction in Montclair, Calif., saying it was a "prototype" and that no others were being built. "We'll study it," he said, before rolling out more.

Disney, based in Burbank, Calif., has been operating restaurants in its theme parks and hotels for years, but this would be the first tied to its fast-expanding retail-store business. The company has about 45 stores open nationwide selling Disney paraphernalia. Currently, it is opening about three a month, with a goal of more than 100 by 1992.
