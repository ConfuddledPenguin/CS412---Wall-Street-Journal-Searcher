United Gaming Casino

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/02/90

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- United Gaming Inc., a gambling machine operator, said it received approval from state gambling authorities to take over the operations of the Plantation Casino in Sparks, Nev. United Gaming purchased the casino for $9.5 million six months ago, and had been awaiting approval from the Nevada Gaming Control Board and the Nevada Gaming Commission to operate it.

United Gaming also said it has agreed to sell the Trolley Stop, a downtown Las Vegas slot machine parlor, which is being constructed. The company will retain a participation in the Trolley Stop's revenue, it said. It declined to provide details on the price or to identify the purchaser.
