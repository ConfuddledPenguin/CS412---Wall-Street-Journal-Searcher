Output of Raw Steel Rose 2.2% Last Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 2.2% last week to 1,821,000 tons from 1,781,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 1.0% from the 1,840,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 81.6% of its capability last week, compared with 79.8% the previous week and 82.8% a year ago.
