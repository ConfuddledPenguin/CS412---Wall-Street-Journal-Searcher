Business Brief -- NVR L.P.: Partnership Expects to Get Extension of Credit Facilities

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

NVR L.P., McLean, Va., said its working capital and mortgage warehouse credit facilities expired yesterday but that it expects to have an extension at current levels in place by next Monday. NVR also said land-development partnerships in which its NVR Development subsidiary is general partner failed to make several interest payments due yesterday and won't make any other loan payments on that debt until the debt is restructured. Currently, NVR Development isin default on a $10 million unsecured working capital line.

In addition, NVR said its home-building operations are cutting prices in response to a weak market and that it is scaling back certain operations in Florida and in the Midwest. In connection with the moves, the company said it expects to take a charge against earnings that will result in a second-quarter loss. In the 1989 quarter, NVR had net income of $9 million, or 34 cents a share.

NVR said it expects the credit lines for the parent company to be extended to at least Aug. 31.
