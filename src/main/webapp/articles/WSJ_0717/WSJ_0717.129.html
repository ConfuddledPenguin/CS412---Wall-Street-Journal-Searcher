Financing Business: Cardinal Distribution Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

CARDINAL DISTRIBUTION Inc., Dublin, Ohio, said it will offer $70 million of 7 1/4% convertible subordinated debentures due 2015. Cardinal said the debentures are convertible into common shares of Cardinal at any time at or before maturity at a conversion price of $34.20 a share. Cardinal is a wholesale drug distributor serving independent and chain drugstores and hospital customers located primarily in the Midwest and Northeast.
