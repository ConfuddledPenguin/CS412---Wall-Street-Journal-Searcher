Litton Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Litton Industries Inc. said it received a contract it valued at about $25 million from Ford Aerospace Corp. to produce more than 200 laser subsystems for McDonnell Douglas Corp.'s F/A-18 attack fighter jet. The subsystems are part of the jets' target systems, made by Ford Aerospace, a unit of Ford Motor Co. The Litton contract includes options for more than 300 additional subsystems. Litton, a technology concern, said the subsystems would be made at the company's Laser Systems division in Apopka, Fla.
