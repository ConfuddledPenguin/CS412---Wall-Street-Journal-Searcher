Business Brief -- U.S. Shoe Corp.: Firm to Sell Certain Assets Of Division to Liz Claiborne

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

U.S. Shoe Corp., Cincinnati, said it agreed to sell certain assets of its Liz Claiborne shoe division to Liz Claiborne Inc. Terms weren't disclosed.

Liz Claiborne also agreed to assume responsibility for U.S. Shoe's Liz Claiborne division.

U.S. Shoe has operated the division since 1981 under a license from Liz Claiborne.

U.S. Shoe said it will act as Liz Claiborne's footwear sourcing representative and provide distribution support services for an extended period.

U.S. Shoe officials said the agreement follows Liz Claiborne's strategy toward "more complete control of its name."

The agreement is effective Aug. 3.
