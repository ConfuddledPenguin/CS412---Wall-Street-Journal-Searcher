Canadian Government Bond Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government said it will offer 1.6 billion Canadian dollars (US$1.38 billion) of bonds in four issues this week. Its sale will involve 11.75% bonds due Oct. 15, 1993; 10.75% bonds due Dec. 15, 1995; 10.50% bonds due July 1, 2000; and 10.25% bonds due March 15, 2014. Offering terms are to be announced today.

The Bank of Canada has agreed to purchase at least C$100 million of the new bonds. The remainder will be available to public investors.

Proceeds will be used to redeem C$1.53 billion of bonds maturing Aug. 1 and for general government purposes.
