BASF Corp. Expansion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

PARSIPPANY, N.J. -- BASF Corp. said it plans to build a $29 million acrylic copolymer dispersion plant at its Monaca, Pa., site. Construction is to begin next month, with completion set for the second quarter of 1992.

The material is used for paper coatings, textile binders, paints, cement modifications and adhesives and other uses.

BASF Corp., the North American member of the BASF Group of West Germany, produces a variety of chemicals as well as pharmaceuticals and other products.
