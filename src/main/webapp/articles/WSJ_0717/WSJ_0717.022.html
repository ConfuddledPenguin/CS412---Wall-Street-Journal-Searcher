Britain's Retail Sales Volume

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

LONDON -- Retail sales volume in June rose a provisional 0.9% from a year earlier but fell 2.8% from May, Britain's Central Statistical Office said. Market forecasts for June were for a 1% fall for the month and a 2.7% year-to-year rise. The index -- January 1985 equals 100 -- was 122.2 in June, compared with 125.7 in May and 121.1 in June 1989. In the three months through June, retail sales volume rose 0.6% from the previous three months and 1.6% from a year earlier.

The value of retail sales in June was up about 7% from a year earlier.
