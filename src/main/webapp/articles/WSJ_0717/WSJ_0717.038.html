Crown Cork Makes Acquisitions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Crown Cork & Seal Co. said it completed the acquisition of two metal-can divisions from Continental Can Co. for $336 million and the assumption of certain liabilities. The metal-container maker said the two acquired divisions operate more than 30 plants in the U.S. and had 1989 revenue of $1.3 billion.

Continental Can is a unit of Peter Kiewit Sons' Inc., Omaha, Neb.
