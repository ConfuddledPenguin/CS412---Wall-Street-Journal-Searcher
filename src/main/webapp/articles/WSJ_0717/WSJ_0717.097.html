Business Brief -- Lam Research Corp.: After-Tax Charge Incurred For Fiscal Fourth Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

Lam Research Corp., Fremont, Calif., said it will incur a $14 million to $18 million after-tax charge for its fourth quarter, ended June 30, to cover phasing out production of its Gemini line of silicon-wafer fabrication systems. The semiconductor-industry equipment supplier said the decision will result in a net loss for the fourth quarter and the year. Lam said the Gemini line, which accounted for about 17% of revenue, had been unprofitable.

In national over-the-counter trading, Lam closed at $5.75, down 50 cents.

Lam reported profit of $2.6 million, or 24 cents a share, on sales of $34.2 million in the year-earlier fourth quarter and net income of $9.3 million, or 84 cents, on sales of $126 million for the last fiscal year.
