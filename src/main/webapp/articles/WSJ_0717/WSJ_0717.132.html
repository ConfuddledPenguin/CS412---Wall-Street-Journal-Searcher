Divi, Accor Discuss Hotel Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

ARUBA, Netherlands Antilles -- Divi Hotels N.V. said it is negotiating with Accor S.A., the French hotel company, to sell Divi's resort hotel business in the Caribbean. Divi said the transaction would include the purchase or management of 11 hotel properties. The resort company said that any transaction won't involve its time-share or casino businesses.
