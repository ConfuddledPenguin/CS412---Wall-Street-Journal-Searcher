International Brief -- Tomkins PLC: Pretax Profit for Fiscal '90 Rose 18% to $138.8 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

Tomkins PLC of Britain said pretax profit for the year ended April 28 rose 18% to #77 million ($138.8 million) from #65.1 million a year earlier, helped by the industrial management concern's low exposure to the depressed British economy. The results were at the top end of market expectations, which were for a rise of between #75 million and #77.5 million, and were in line with the company's forecast of "no less than" #76 million.

Profit attributable to shareholders, after taxes, rose 22% to #53.6 million from #43.9 million. Per-share earnings rose 15% to 28.2 pence from 24.5 pence.

Sales rose 30% to #720 million from #550 million.

Tomkins said it is proposing to pay a final dividend of 6.55 pence a share, which, together with its interim payment of 2.70 pence, would make the full-year payout 9.25 pence, up 37% from 6.75 pence last year.
