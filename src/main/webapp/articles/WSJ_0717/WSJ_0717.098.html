Business Brief -- Stone Container Corp.: Recycling Venture Is Set With Waste Management

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

Stone Container Corp. and Waste Management Inc. definitively agreed to form a paper-recycling joint venture, which the two companies said they expect to become "the world's largest marketing enterprise for recycled paper." The new company will sell paper collected by Oak Brook, Ill.-based Waste Management, the nation's largest waste-handling concern, and by third parties. Waste Management is collecting increasing amounts of recyclable paper, aluminum and plastic through its curbside collections, under its Recycle America program.

Chicago-based Stone, which is already a big manufacturer of recycled paper and has under way a $200 million program to boost its capacity, will be "the major purchaser" of the old corrugated boxes and newspapers the venture collects.

A major impediment to paper-recycling efforts has been the absence of solid long-term markets for collected waste.
