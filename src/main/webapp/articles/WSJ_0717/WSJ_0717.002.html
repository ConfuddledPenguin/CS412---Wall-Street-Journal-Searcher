Who's News: Takeda Chemical Industries Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

TAKEDA CHEMICAL INDUSTRIES Ltd. (Osaka, Japan) -- John A. Henriksen, 62 years old, previously executive vice president of Takeda Chemical Products USA Inc., was named president of the Wilmington, N.C.-based subsidiary of this pharmaceutical and nutritional products company, succeeding Hirohiko Ichikawa, who returns to Tokyo as a director and president of the vitamin and food division.
