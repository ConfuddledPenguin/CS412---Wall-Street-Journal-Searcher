International Brief -- Sandoz AG: Chemical and Drug Group Says 1st-Half Sales Rose 3%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

Sandoz AG, a major Swiss chemical and pharmaceutical group, said first-half sales rose 3% to 6.77 billion francs ($4.83 billion) from 6.56 billion francs a year earlier. Its chemical, construction and environment, and nutrition divisions showed slight declines in sales, while its pharmaceutical and agro-chemical units posted mild gains.

The seed division sales rose 34%, largely because of the influence of an acquisition, the company said.

The company said that based on first-half figures, group profit for 1990 should at least match last year's 958 million francs and return on sales rate of 7.7%, despite currency influences and a sluggish U.S. economy. The company wasn't more specific. In keeping with Swiss business practices, the company didn't provide any firsthalf profit figures.
