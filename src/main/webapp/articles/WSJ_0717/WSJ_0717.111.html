Business Brief -- ISS International Service System: Board Accepts Takeover Bid By Danish Majority Owner

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

ISS International Service System Inc.'s directors accepted a $12-a-share bid from ISS International Service System AS, Copenhagen. ISS AS seeks to acquire the remaining 1.3 million shares, or 36%, it doesn't already own of the New York-based building-maintenance services company. An ISS International Service System official said that the company hopes to have a special stockholder meeting in September to act on the merger agreement.

In June a special committee of the ISS board rejected the original offer made by ISS AS to acquire the shares at $10 each.

In composite trading yesterday on the American Stock Exchange, ISS International shares closed at $11.625, up 25 cents.
