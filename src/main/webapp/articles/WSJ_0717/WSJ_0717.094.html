Who's News: Frank B. Hall & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

FRANK B. HALL & Co. (Briarcliff Manor, N.Y.) -- Joseph L. Lombardo was named executive vice president, a new post, at this insurer's U.S. brokerage operation. Mr. Lombardo, 51 years old, was previously senior vice president and marketing director at Alexander & Alexander Services Inc., a New York insurance broker.
