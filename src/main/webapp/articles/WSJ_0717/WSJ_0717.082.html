Who's News: Fleer Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

FLEER Corp. (Philadelphia) -- Robert Cieri was named a director of this maker of sports cards and confectionary products, increasing the number of directors to 13. Mr. Cieri, 66 years old, is a consultant and former vice president and sales manager of Nestle S.A.'s chocolate division.
