Who's News: FPL Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

FPL GROUP Inc. (North Palm Beach, Fla.) -- H. Jesse Arnelle and Stephen E. Frank were elected directors of this utility, energy and agricultural holding company. Mr. Arnelle, 56 years old, is a senior partner in the California-based law firm of Arnelle & Hastie. Mr. Frank, 48, is president of FPL's Florida Power & Light Co. He succeeds Robert E. Tallon, the former president of Florida P&L, who resigned from the board in June. Mr. Arnelle is an addition to the board and increases its size to 14 members.
