Italy's Gross Domestic Product

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

MILAN -- Italy's gross domestic product in the first quarter rose a seasonally adjusted 0.9% from the 1989 fourth quarter and was up 3.1% from a year earlier, Istat, the national statistics agency, said. The first-quarter growth rates compare with a quarterly increase of 0.5% and a year-to-year increase of 2.8% in the 1989 fourth quarter.
