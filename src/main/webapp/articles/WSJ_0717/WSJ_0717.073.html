IMEX MEDICAL SYSTEMS Inc. (Golden, Colo.) -- This maker of medical monitoring gear said it's reassigning its executive officers to better align its corporate goals with its executives' skills and resources. Jerry Donahue, president, chief executive officer and a director, will become chairman. Dennis Newman, founder and chairman, will become president and chief executive.
