LAC's Bond International Gold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

TORONTO -- Bond International Gold Inc., a unit of LAC Minerals Ltd., a gold mining concern, said it has a commitment from a syndicate of banks led by N.M. Rothschild & Sons Ltd. for a 420,000-ounce gold loan, to be repaid in quarterly payments beginning next January and maturing in July 1995. Bond said the loan is subject to completion of documentation, which is expected later this month, and is guaranteed by LAC. It said that the new facility replaces gold loans totaling about 470,000 ounces, and that the difference of 50,000 ounces will be repaid from cash reserves.
