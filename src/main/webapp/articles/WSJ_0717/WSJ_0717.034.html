Bayer Unit to Buy Acid Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

PARIS -- Rhone-Poulenc S.A. agreed to sell a citric acid plant in the U.K. to Haarman & Reimer, a unit of Bayer AG of West Germany, for #56 million ($100.8 million). The French state-owned chemical and pharmaceutical group said the agreement is subject to the approval of the authorities.

Rhone-Poulenc acquired the Selby facility when it took over the chemical activities of RTZ PLC in 1989. The facility was inaugurated at the beginning of this year and has a capacity of 23,000 tons.
