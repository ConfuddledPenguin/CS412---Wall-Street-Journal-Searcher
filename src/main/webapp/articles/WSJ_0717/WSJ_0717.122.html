Dividend News: Jacor Communications Reaches Pact on Debt; A Payout Is Deferred

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

CINCINNATI -- Jacor Communications Inc. said it reached a new agreement with its banks on refinancing debts, which will allow it to defer indefinitely certain payments on subordinated debt, warrants or capital stock. The action includes deferring a July 16 dividend payment on the company's 7% cumulative preferred stock.

Jacor said its banks have extended until Aug. 20 the interest payment dates on substantially all advances. The banks also extended to the same date their requirement that Jacor raise $15 million in equity.

Jacor, which owns radio stations and a cable television system, said it has hired Salomon Brothers Inc. to help refinance its debts.
