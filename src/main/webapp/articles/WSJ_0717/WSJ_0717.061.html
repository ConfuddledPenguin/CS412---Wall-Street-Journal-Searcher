Business Brief -- MCEG Inc.: GE Subsidiary Negotiating 30-Day Standstill Agreement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

Management Company Entertainment Group Inc. said it is negotiating a 30-day standstill agreement with G.E. Capital, a unit of General Electric Co., in connection with $72.5 million in loans that matured Saturday. MCEG, a talent management and movie production company based in Los Angeles, said it is continuing to negotiate refinancing arrangements with GE and other creditors. The company said it is no longer pursuing a previously reported agreement to restructure the loans into two series of notes maturing in 1992 and 1995.

MCEG borrowed the $72.5 million last July to acquire Virgin Vision Ltd., an independent movie, television and video distributor, from Virgin Group, a London-based entertainment and airline concern.
