Marketing & Media: Live Entertainment Sets Oct. 4 for `Turtles' Release

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

HOLLYWOOD -- Live Entertainment Inc. set Oct. 4 as the date for its video release of "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: The Movie." It will be supported by a $20 million marketing campaign as well as a cross-promotion with PepsiCo Inc.'s Pizza Hut restaurants. The motion picture has grossed more than $130 million at the box office so far, and a series of animated TV versions released earlier in video by Live have sold more than 13 million units.

Purchasers of the "Ninja Turtles" cassette will receive coupons redeemable for more than $20 in free food and Pepsi from Pizza Hut's 6,500 restaurants. Pizza Hut will co-sponsor the television, print and radio advertising campaign.

Despite the $24.99 suggested price, Live will provide cooperative advertising to retailers who price the tapes as low as $18.88. But some mass merchants are likely to price the videocassettes -- which sell for about $16 wholesale -- even lower.

Separately, Live, whose voting stock is 48%-owned by Carolco Pictures Inc., also said it will change the name of its home video unit from International Video Entertainment Inc., or IVE, to Live Home Video Inc.
