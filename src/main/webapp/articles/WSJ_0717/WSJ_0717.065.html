Redemption Notices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended July 13, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

ADAMS COUNTY, COLORADO, will call for partial redemption, on Aug. 1, 1990, $2,350,000 of its single-family mortgage revenue bonds, 1980 Series A, due Aug. 1, 1991-1999 and 2011. First Interstate Bank of Denver, and Citibank, New York, are paying agents.

AUGUSTA, KAN., will redeem $35,000 principal amount of 8.50% hospital revenue bonds, Series A, Augusta Medical Complex issue, due Feb. 1, 1999, on Aug. 1, 1990. Ameritrust Texas, Dallas, is redemption agent.

CHARLOTTE, N.C., will call on Aug. 15, 1990, all outstanding airport bonds, series 1980 A, due March 1, 19912005. First Union National Bank of North Carolina, Charlotte, and Bankers Trust Co., New York, are paying agents.

DENVER, COLO., will call $495,000 of single-family mortgage revenue bonds 1978 series A, due Aug. 1, 1991-1999 and Aug. 1, 2010, on Aug. 1, 1990. United Bank of Denver, Denver, Colo., and Bankers Trust Co., New York, are paying agents.

CORPORATES

MITEL CORP. will redeem its 7% convertible subordinated debentures due Dec. 1, 1997, on Aug. 15, 1990. Chase Manhattan Bank, New York, is trustee.

TALMAN FINANCE CORP. F on Aug. 27, 1990, will call 100 shares of market auction preferred stock. Bankers Trust Co., New York, is paying agent.
