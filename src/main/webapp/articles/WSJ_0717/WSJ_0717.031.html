Northwest Air's Japan Service

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- NWA Inc. said its Northwest Airlines unit will begin nonstop flights three times weekly between New York and Osaka, Japan, on Oct. 29, adding that this is the first nonstop service between the two cities. The closely held carrier also said it will begin daily nonstop service between New York and Seoul, South Korea, in September.

Both routes will be served by Boeing Co. 747-400 long-range aircraft.
