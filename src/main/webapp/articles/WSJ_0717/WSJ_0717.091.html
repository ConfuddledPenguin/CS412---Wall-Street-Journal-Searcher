Who's News: Ryder System Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

RYDER SYSTEM Inc. (Miami) -- R. Frank Leftwich was appointed president of the Aviation Leasing & Services division of this transportation services concern. Mr. Leftwich, 54 years old, had been group vice president of airline engine and distribution services at Aviall, a Ryder subsidiary. He succeeds Robert G. Lambert, who is now Ryder System's senior executive vice president for aviation.
