French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

PARIS -- French consumer prices in June rose a preliminary 0.2% from May and 3% from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute said. The June result matches May's monthly rise of 0.2% and leaves the year-to-year inflation rate unchanged at 3%.

The latest monthly rise was in line with estimates by economists, who had expected price growth to remain broadly under control in June, with the growth rates for most main price categories showing little change from May. But the stable year-to-year rate was slightly below previous forecasts, which had generally called for a rise of 3.1%.
