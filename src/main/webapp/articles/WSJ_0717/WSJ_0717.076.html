Business Brief -- Bamberger Polymers Inc.: Some Sales Staffers Quit; 3rd-Period Net to Be Hurt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

Bamberger Polymers Inc., New Hyde Park, N.Y., said 11 members of its sales force in the Northeast and West Coast divisions quit, which will have an adverse effect on third-quarter sales and earnings. In the third quarter a year earlier, the plastics company earned $159,000, or five cents a share, on sales of $41 million.

Company officials said they would take "all legal action available" if they determine that the sales employees "have acted in concert to interfere" with its business relationships.

In composite trading on the American Stock Exchange, Bamberger shares closed at $2.25 a share, down 25 cents a share.

Officials couldn't be reached to elaborate on the announcement.
