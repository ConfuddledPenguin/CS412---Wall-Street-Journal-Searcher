Business Brief -- Sequoia Systems Inc.: Firm Joins Samsung in Pact To Sell Computers in Asia

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

Sequoia Systems Inc., Marlborough, Mass., said it formed a strategic alliance with Samsung Electronics Co. under which the Korean electronics concern will market Sequoia computers in Asia. Terms weren't disclosed. But Sequoia said some shipments under the pact already have commenced.

In a statement, the companies said Samsung will act as exclusive Korean marketer for Sequoia, a maker of fault-tolerant computers based on the Unix software operating system. The companies also said they will jointly develop a low-end computer based on Sequoia's patented multiprocessor computer design. It should be ready in the 1991 first quarter, the statement said.
