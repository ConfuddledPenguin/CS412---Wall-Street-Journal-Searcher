Who's News: Bank of New England Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/17/90

BANK OF NEW ENGLAND Corp. (Boston) -- William E. Moeller, 48-year-old executive vice president of the Southeast Bank unit of Southeast Banking Corp., Miami, was named president and chief operating officer of this holding company's Connecticut Bank & Trust Co. unit, effective this Thursday, succeeding James F. McNally, 57, who announced his retirement in May, pending the naming of a successor.
