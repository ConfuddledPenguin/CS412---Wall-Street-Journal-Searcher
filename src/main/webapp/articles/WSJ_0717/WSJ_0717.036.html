Commonwealth Mortgage Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/17/90

HOUSTON -- Commonwealth Mortgage of America L.P. said it completed the sale of its nationwide loan-production operations to United Savings Association of the Southwest FSB for about $240 million. The sale, which included the purchase by United Southwest of more than $200 million of Commonwealth's mortgage loans and other assets, is part of a plan to liquidate and dissolve Commonwealth.
