Westmark Extends Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

SEATTLE, Wash. -- Westmark International Inc. said it extended until 8:00 p.m. EDT April 20 its offer of 97 cents a share for all the shares and one cent a share for all publicly held warrants of First Medical Devices Corp., of Kirkland, Wash. Westmark said 2.6 million shares have been tendered so far. Along with shares already held by Westmark, and shares that officials of First Medical have agreed to tender, Westmark said it holds 90.7% of the company. First Medical makes heart defibrillators, used by emergency personnel. Westmark makes diagnostic imaging systems and patient-monitoring systems.
