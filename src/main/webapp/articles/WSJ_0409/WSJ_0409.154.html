Business Brief -- Weyerhaeuser Co.: Firm Plans to Sell Venture With Portland General

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

Weyerhaeuser Co., Seattle, and Portland General Corp. said they plan to sell Cornerstone Columbia Development Co., a commercial development and hotel management concern they have jointly owned since 1985. A price wasn't disclosed. Portland General said its decision to sell follows a plan announced earlier to get out of the real estate business and concentrate on the energy business. Weyerhaeuser said Cornerstone's activities overlap building and development activities of its unit, Weyerhaeuser Real Estate Co.

The companies said they may retain a minor interest. They said Seattle-based Cornerstone will continue to develop real estate in the Northwest and is expected to develop projects valued at more than $100 million this year.
