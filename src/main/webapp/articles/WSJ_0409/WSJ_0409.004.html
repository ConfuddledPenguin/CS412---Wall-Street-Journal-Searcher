Big Board Seats Are Sold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

NEW YORK -- A New York Stock Exchange seat sold for $395,000 Friday, up $5,000 from two previous sales earlier in the day. The last prior seat sale before Friday was for $360,000 on March 13. Currently, the bid price for Big Board seats is $360,000, while the asking price is $450,000.
