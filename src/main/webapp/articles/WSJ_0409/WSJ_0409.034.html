MLX Says Distributor Of Air Conditioning Will Revamp Credit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

TROY, Mich. -- MLX Corp. said that its Refrigeration & Air Conditioning Group has agreed with its senior lenders on a plan to restructure its credit facilities. Under the agreement, the senior lenders will exchange $33.5 million of the group's $163.5 million term loan for MLX junior subordinated notes and shares of MLX common stock. The senior lenders will also extend the final maturity of the remaining term loan and reduce the amortization of that debt. The pact also calls for the senior lenders to reduce the interest rates on the remaining term loan.

The agreement, approved by MLX's board, is subject to approval by senior lenders and is contingent on other closing conditions.

MLX said the agreement will allow senior management to return the focus to running this distributor of commercial refrigeration and air conditioning equipment.
