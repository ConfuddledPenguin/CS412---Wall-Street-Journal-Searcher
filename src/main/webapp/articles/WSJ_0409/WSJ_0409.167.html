Morrison Knudsen Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it received a contract from the city of New York to manage construction of a $200 million prison infirmary and mental health center. The center will be built at New York City's Riker's Island correctional complex on the East River, Morrison said.

Construction is planned to begin in early 1992, with completion expected for summer 1994, the company added.
