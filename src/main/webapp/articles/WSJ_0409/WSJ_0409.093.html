Financing Business: Figgie International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

FIGGIE INTERNATIONAL Inc., of Willoughby, Ohio, said its board authorized the purchase of as many as 275,133 of its Class B common shares. The company said it is taking the action to restore a ratio between Class A and Class B shares that existed before a tender offer for both classes of stock in September. At that time, shareholders tendered three million shares of Class A stock, the number the company said it would buy, but only 834,867 shares of Class B stock, which was less than the 1.1 million shares the company sought. Shares are adjusted for a 3-for-1 stock split in January. Figgie's businesses include fire protection and other security products and services, electronics, sporting goods and industrial equipment.
