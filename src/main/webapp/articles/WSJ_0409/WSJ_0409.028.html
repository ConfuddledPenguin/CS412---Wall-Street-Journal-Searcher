Business Brief -- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.: Marketing Unit Revamped, Staff Cutback May Follow

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co. reorganized its North American tire marketing organization and named two new vice presidents. By midyear, the Akron, Ohio-based company will have plans for the exact structure of the new organization, which may involve a reduction in employment, Goodyear said. "Changing competitive conditions demand a more flexible and customer-responsive organization," the company said.

The company established separate units to handle sales of passenger car tires to consumers through independent dealers and for sales to commercial accounts, such as truck and auto fleets. The latter organization also will handle sales of truck, farm and off-road tires through dealers.

Donald E. Salyers, formerly general manager sales, was appointed vice president of the consumer division. H.J. Wilson, formerly vice president Asia-Africa, was named vice president commercial division.

And R.M. Morrison, who was general manager of the company-owned retail operations, was promoted to vice president.

All three will report to Louis DiPasqua, vice president tire sales and marketing, Goodyear said.
