Business Brief -- Cambior Inc.: Quebec Gold Mining Firm Says It Seeks Acquisitions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

Cambior Inc. is "very actively" seeking acquisitions, Louis Gignac, president and chief executive officer, said after a presentation to analysts in Montreal. Mr. Gignac said the Val D'Or, Quebec, gold mining concern would prefer to make one major acquisition rather than a number of smaller purchases and is particularly interested in buying a U.S. gold property. He said that with more than 100 million Canadian dollars (US$86 million) in cash Cambior can consider an acquisition of around C$200 million.

Mr. Gignac said Cambior's objective is to roughly double its gold production to about 500,000 ounces annually by the end of 1994 from a forecast 220,000 ounces this year.
