New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

NEW YORK -- The National Market System of the over-the-counter market announced four new listings.

Pharmacy Management Services Inc., a Tampa, Fla., provider of medical cost containment services and products, began trading under the symbol PMSV.

Pinkerton's Inc., a Van Nuys, Calif., security guard services company, began trading under the symbol PKTN.

Tetra Technologies Inc. began trading under the symbol TTRA. The Woodlands, Texas, company provides recycling and treatment services for environmentally sensitive byproducts and waste streams.

Vicor Corp. started trading with the symbol VICR. The Andover, Mass., company makes modular power converters, power system parts and power systems.
