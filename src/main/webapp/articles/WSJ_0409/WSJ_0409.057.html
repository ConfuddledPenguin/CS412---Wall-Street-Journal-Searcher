Ashland Oil Sells 58% Stake in Unit to ESOP

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

ASHLAND, Ky. -- Ashland Oil Inc. said it sold 58% of the stock of Ashland Technology Corp. to a newly formed employee stock ownership plan. The company said there will be no impact on earnings. Ashland said it will receive cash, stock and subordinated debt of the new company. Total value received for engineering businesses was $97 million, Ashland said, but that sum includes proceeds from the sale last February of Williams Brothers Engineering Co., a subsidiary of Ashland Technology, to Fluor Corp., Irvine, Calif.

Ashland Technology, which will be renamed AECOM Technology Corp., performs architectural, engineering and construction management services, mainly for buildings. Williams Brothers is involved in engineering and construction of pipelines and oil processing facilities. Ashland Oil is a diversified oil refining and marketing concern.
