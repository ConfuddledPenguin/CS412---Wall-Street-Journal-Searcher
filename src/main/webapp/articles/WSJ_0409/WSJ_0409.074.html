Japanese GNP Forecast Is Cut

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

TOKYO -- The Nikko Research Center said it lowered slightly, to 3.9%, its estimate for Japanese gross national product growth in the year ending March 1991. The center predicted domestic demand will fall off during the second half of the fiscal year, as stock market instability and higher interest rates cause private capital investment and housing outlays to ease.

Nikko Research predicts growth of private capital investment will decline to 7.5% from an estimated 15.8% the year before. The original prediction was for 8.0% growth.
