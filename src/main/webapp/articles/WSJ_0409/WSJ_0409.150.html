Who's News: Bizmart Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

BIZMART Inc. (Arlington, Texas) -- Harold Toppel, founder of Pueblo International Inc., was elected to the board of this retailer of discount office products. Mr. Toppel succeeds Patrick A. Hopf, one of BizMart's early venture-capital investors, who resigned to make the seat available.
