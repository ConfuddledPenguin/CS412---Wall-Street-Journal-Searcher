New York Cotton Exchange

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

NEW YORK -- A membership on the New York Cotton Exchange sold for $44,000 Friday, up from the $41,000 paid at the last prior sale on March 6. A year ago, on April 3, 1989, a seat sold for $40,000. Currently, the bid is at $40,000 and the offer is at $50,000, according to the exchange.
