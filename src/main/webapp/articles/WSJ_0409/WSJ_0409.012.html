Allegheny International Is Sued

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

PITTSBURGH -- Japonica Partners said it filed suit against Allegheny International Inc. in federal bankruptcy court in Pittsburgh seeking to disallow all votes cast in favor of a reorganization plan proposed by Allegheny and to deny confirmation of the plan. Allegheny is seeking an arrangement with creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. Japonica, a New York investment and merchant-banking firm, is pressing its own plan for reorganizing Allegheny, a maker of appliances and other products.

Earlier last week, Japonica prevented Allegheny from obtaining the necessary two-thirds approval of all classes of creditors for its plan, and Allegheny said it would sue to disallow the votes of Japonica.
