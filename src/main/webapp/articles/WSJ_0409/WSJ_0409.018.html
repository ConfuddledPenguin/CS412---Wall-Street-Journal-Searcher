A Hard Day's Bite

At the end of the day

When we've been away

Stalking the corporate beast,

We return to the nest

Hoping to rest

And share in a hard-earned feast.

We're too tired to cook

So we hungrily look

Through the house with primeval anxiety.

We eat what we find

And again, we're aligned

With a hunting-and-gathering society.

-- Pat D'Amico.

Channel Confusion

Our TV viewing habits

Should make us quite uneasy,

As we honor the serious shows,

But watch those that are sleazy.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
