Who's News: American Stock Exchange Gives Posts to Steele, Finn

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

NEW YORK -- The American Stock Exchange named Philip Steele senior vice president and Christopher Finn executive vice president, both new positions. Mr. Steele will be in charge of strategic planning and special projects. Most recently he was a senior management consultant specializing in strategic planning and new venture analysis for Seidman & Seidman.

Mr. Finn will be be responsible for marketing, research, communications and government relations. Previously, at Air & Water Technologies Capital Corp., he was senior vice president for strategic planning, mergers and acquisitions.
