Service Corp. Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

HOUSTON -- Service Corp. International said it intends to buy back as many as four million shares of its own common from time to time in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions. The funeral service and cemetery company said that purchases would be in such amounts and on terms and at times as management considers appropriate. The company has 48.5 million shares outstanding.

R.L. Waltrip, chairman, said the board believes the company's common shares are a good value and represent an attractive investment opportunity.
