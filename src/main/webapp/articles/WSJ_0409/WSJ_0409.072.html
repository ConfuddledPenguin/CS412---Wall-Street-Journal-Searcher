Who's News: Eli Lilly & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

ELI LILLY & Co. (Indianapolis) -- Mitchell E. Daniels Jr., president and chief executive officer of the Hudson Institute, was named to the new position of vice president, corporate affairs, at this pharmaceutical, medical and agricultural products company. In his new post, effective this autumn, Mr. Daniels, 41 years old, will be responsible for government relations, corporate communications and community relations. He will resign his Hudson posts.
