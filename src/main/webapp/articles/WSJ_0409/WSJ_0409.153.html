Business Brief -- Galveston-Houston Co.: Chief Executive to Resign; Company Will Hire Adviser

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

Galveston-Houston Co. said in Houston that its chairman, president and chief executive officer intends to resign, and the company plans to retain an investment banking firm to help form a strategic plan in view of his departure. Nathan M. Avery has told Galveston-Houston he expects to be nominated to serve as commercial minister to the European Community and senior adviser to the secretary of Commerce, the company said. If the nomination is approved, Mr. Avery told the company he expects to assume his position in October.

Galveston-Houston makes valve actuators and custom steel castings for the energy and other markets.
