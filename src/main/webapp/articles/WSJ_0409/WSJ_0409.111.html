McKesson Unit Extends Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- McKesson Corp. said its unit, MCK Acquisition Corp., extended until this Friday its previously announced $20-a-share cash offer for the shares of PCS Inc. that it doesn't already own. The tender offer was set to expire today. In December, McKesson, which already held 86.2% of PCS's 14.5 million shares, offered to buy the rest of the company. The transaction is valued at about $40 million. McKesson, a drug-distribution concern, said that as of last week, 346,061 shares had been tendered. PCS processes thirdparty prescription drug claims for insurers.
