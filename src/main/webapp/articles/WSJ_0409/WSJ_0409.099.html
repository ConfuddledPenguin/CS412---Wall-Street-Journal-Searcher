Who's News: Merck & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

MERCK & Co. (Rahway, N.J.) -- Michael M. Tarnow was named president of this pharmaceuticals and health-care company's Merck Frosst Canada Inc. division. He succeeds Robert Ingram, 46 years old, who recently left to join Glaxo PLC. Mr. Tarnow, 48, most recently was executive director, corporate development, for the parent and was responsible for initiating, implementing and coordinating the corporatewide development program.
