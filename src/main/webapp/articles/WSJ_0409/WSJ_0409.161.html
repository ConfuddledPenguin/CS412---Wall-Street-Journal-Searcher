Possible Norwest Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Norwest Corp. said it is continuing discussions with Wyoming National Bancorp over a possible Norwest acquisition of the Wyoming bank holding company. Last month, Norwest signed a letter of intent to acquire Wyoming National for $20 a share, or $43 million.

The two companies said then that they would determine no later than Friday whether to proceed with the transaction.
