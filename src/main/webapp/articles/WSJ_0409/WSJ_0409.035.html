Business Brief -- Commercial Intertech Corp.: Ohio Producer Repurchases Stock From Bear Stearns

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

Commercial Intertech Corp. said it repurchased 573,100 shares of its common stock at $28.625 a share from Bear Stearns & Co., New York. The Youngstown, Ohio, producer of hydraulic equipment and fluid purification products said previously that an unknown investor was accumulating the company's shares. It now appears that the investor was Bear Stearns, Commercial Intertech said.

At Bear Stearns, there was no one available who could say whether the stock had been accumulated by Bear Stearns or if Bear Stearns sold the stock for another investor.

The repurchase was made under a board authorization last Feb. 15 to repurchase up to 2.5 million shares. The company said it has repurchased a total of 1,463,210 shares under that authorization.

The company had 12.4 million common shares outstanding before beginning the repurchases. However, including convertible preferred stock, the total number of shares outstanding on a fully diluted basis remains above 13 million, a spokesman said.
