Who's News: Major Realty Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

MAJOR REALTY Corp. (Orlando, Fla.) -- James A. Adams III, the 45-year-old chairman and chief executive officer of this real-estate developer, assumed the additional post of president. He takes over the duties of Robert J. Pliska, who resigned, a company spokeswoman said, for personal reasons related to relocating from his home in Michigan to Florida. The spokeswoman said she couldn't elaborate, and Mr. Pliska, who joined Major Realty last December, couldn't be reached.
