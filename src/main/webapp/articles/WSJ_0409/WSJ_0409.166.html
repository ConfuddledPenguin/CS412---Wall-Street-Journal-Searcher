Copperweld Calls Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said it is calling for redemption its 9.92% convertible subordinated debentures due 2008, effective April 23. The debenture call is part of an agreement under which Imetal, a French steel concern, is to buy for $17 a share the 35% of Copperweld shares it doesn't already own. Copperweld makes specialty metal products.
