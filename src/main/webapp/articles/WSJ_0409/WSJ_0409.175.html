International Brief -- Daimler-Benz AG: Service Unit Is Hoping To Double Its Business

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

Daimler-Benz AG's new service subsidiary said it hopes to double its business in the next five years from an estimated 3.8 billion marks ($2.2 billion) in 1990. Daimler-Benz Interservices AG, based in West Berlin, started operating at the beginning of the year. The unit includes software and computer systems, financial services, insurance, trading and marketing services activities.
