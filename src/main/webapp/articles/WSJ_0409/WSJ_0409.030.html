National Intergroup Symbol

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

PITTSBURGH -- National Intergroup Inc. said it is changing the trading symbol for its Granite City Steel convertible subordinated debentures to NII94, from GRC94. The change is being made with the New York Stock Exchange, effective April 9, the company said, to avoid confusion with another firm's trading symbol on the American Stock Exchange.
