Who's News: El Paso Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

EL PASO ELECTRIC Co. (El Paso, Texas) -- Jack A. Cardwell and John C. Schweitzer were appointed directors of this utility, succeeding Tad R. Smith and Hal M. Daugherty Jr., who resigned. Mr. Cardwell is president and principal shareholder of Crinco Investments Inc., an El Paso holding and investment company. Mr. Schweitzer is managing partner of Continental Properties Co., an Austin, Texas, real estate and investment partnership.
