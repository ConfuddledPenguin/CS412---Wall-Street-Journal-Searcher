Financing Business: Sun Microsystems Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

SUN MICROSYSTEMS Inc. said it called for redemption of all its outstanding 5 1/4% convertible subordinated debentures due Feb. 15, 2012. The debentures may be redeemed for cash or conversion to common stock on May 7, 1990. In addition, the Mountain View, Calif., supplier of computing systems, workstations and software said it filed an amended registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to approximately 5.1 million common shares, the maximum number of shares issuable in connection with the conversion of the debentures.
