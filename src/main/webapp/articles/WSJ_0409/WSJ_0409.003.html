Dresdner Bank Dividend

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Dresdner Bank AG said it has decided to boost its annual cash dividend for 1989 to 12 marks (US$7) a share from 10 marks paid for the previous year. The second-largest West German banking group last raised it dividend in 1985, when the annual payout rose from 7.5 marks a share.
