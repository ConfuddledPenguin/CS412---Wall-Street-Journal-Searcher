Japanese Output Estimate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

TOKYO -- Nikko Research Center lowered by 0.1% its estimate for Japanese gross national product growth in fiscal 1990 to 3.9%, the company announced. The company predicted that domestic demand will fall off during the second half of the fiscal year, as stock market instability and higher interest rates trim private capital investment and housing outlays.

Nikko Research predicted growth of private capital investment in fiscal 1990 will decline to 7.5% from an estimated 15.8% the year before, a fall from its original prediction of 8% growth.
