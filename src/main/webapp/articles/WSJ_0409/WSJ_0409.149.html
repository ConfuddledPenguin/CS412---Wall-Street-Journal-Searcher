Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

TV: Maria Shriver is leaving as co-anchor of NBC's "Sunday Today" and anchor on the Saturday edition of "NBC Nightly News." Upon her return from maternity leave, Ms. Shriver yesterday told viewers of "Sunday Today" that her weekly commute from her home in California to the East Coast to do the "Nightly News" and "Sunday Today" had grown too difficult following the birth of her first child in December. Ms. Shriver, who is married to actor Arnold Schwarzenegger, will work on a series of NBC prime-time news specials and segments for the week-night news program. No successor to Ms. Shriver in either job was announced.

RADIO: Westwood One Inc. is launching its fourth major radio network, the Westwood One News and Entertainment Network. It initially will provide 55 hours a week of live programming, including evening news and the Larry King Show. In exchange, affiliated stations will allow Westwood to sell some ads during the programs. Westwood operates NBC Radio Networks, the Mutual Broadcasting System and Westwood One Radio Networks.
