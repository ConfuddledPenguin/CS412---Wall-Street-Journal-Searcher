Comstock Resources Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

DALLAS -- Comstock Resources Inc. said it registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 2.5 million common shares. Proceeds will be used to repay the company's $9.8 million in bank debt, to complete 95 oil and gas wells and to acquire additional oil and gas properties.

Eppler, Guerin & Turner Inc. of Dallas is managing the underwriting.
