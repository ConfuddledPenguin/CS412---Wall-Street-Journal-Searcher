Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

U S WEST Inc. (Denver) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its senior debt ratings of three U S West units -- BetaWest Properties, U S West Capital Funding Inc. and U S West Financial Services -- from A-plus to double-A-minus. S&P also downgraded the senior debt ratings of U S West Communications Group to double-A-minus from double-A. S&P affirmed U S West Capital Funding's commercial paper at A1-plus and U S West Communications Group's commercial paper at A1-plus. S&P said the change reflects 1989 consolidated debt leverage of 51.8% of total capitalization, of which about 53% consists of U S West Communications debt obligations. Moreover, S&P said U S West has been diversifying into higher-risk ventures, such as financial services and cellular telephone. S&P also attributed the downgrade to increasing business risk in the company's telephone service areas, which the rating firm said hadn't been offset by improved financial support for credit.
