Who's News: Prospect Park Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

PROSPECT PARK FINANCIAL Corp. (West Paterson, N.J.) -- Edward J. Gurak was named president and chief operating officer of this bank holding company and its principal subsidiary, Prospect Park Savings Bank. As president, he succeeds Russell L. Frignoca, 54 years old, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer of both the holding company and the bank. The position of operating chief is new. Mr. Gurak, 41, most recently was executive vice president, treasurer and chief financial officer of the company and the bank.
