Raytheon's Air Force Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

WASHINGTON -- Raytheon Co. was awarded a $58.6 million Air Force contract for phased array radars.

General Motors Corp. won a $40.5 million Air Force contract for C-130 aircraft engines.

Saft America Inc. received a $13.1 million Army contract for batteries.
