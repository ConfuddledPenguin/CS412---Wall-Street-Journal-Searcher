Air Canada Sets Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

MONTREAL -- Air Canada said it agreed in principle to acquire the Air Toronto division of Soundair Corp. The price wasn't disclosed. The transaction is subject to board and regulatory approvals.

Air Toronto serves as one of Air Canada's six connector airlines and serves eight U.S. destinations from Toronto. Soundair is a Toronto-based charter and cargo airline concern.
