Comair Passenger Boardings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

CINCINNATI -- Comair Holdings Inc. said passenger boardings at its Comair Inc. airline rose 30% in March to 164,893 passengers, from 126,735 a year earlier. Revenue passenger miles jumped 40% to 32.9 million, from 23.5 million in March 1989, Comair said. The carrier filled 44.5% of the available seats, down from 45.6% in the year earlier month.

For the fiscal year ended March 31, passenger boardings increased 28% to 1.6 million, and revenue passenger miles climbed 32% to 305.6 million. Comair filled 44.7% of available seats during the latest year, up from 42.3% in fiscal 1989.

A revenue passenger mile is a paying passenger transported one mile.
