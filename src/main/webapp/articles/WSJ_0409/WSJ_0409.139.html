GENUS Inc. (Mountain View, Calif.) -- Kenneth Schroeder, formerly president and chief executive officer of Photon Dynamics, of San Jose, Calif., was named president and chief operating officer of this maker of semiconductor-fabrication equipment. He succeeds William W.R. Elder, who remains chairman and chief executive officer.
