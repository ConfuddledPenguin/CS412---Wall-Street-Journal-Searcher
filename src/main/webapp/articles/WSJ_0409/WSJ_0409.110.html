Business Brief -- General Motors Corp.: Canadian Unit Cuts Prices Of Camaro, Firebird Models

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

General Motors Corp.'s Canadian unit said it reduced prices on most 1991 models of its sporty Chevrolet Camaro and Pontiac Firebird, which went on sale in Canada last week. The new Firebird price policy differs from that in the U.S., where the parent company last month raised the 1991 price of a standard, six-cylinder Firebird by 6% from the 1990 model, to $11,985.

General Motors of Canada Ltd., based in Oshawa, Ontario, said the base price of a 1991, six-cylinder Pontiac Firebird with automatic transmission was reduced to 16,898 Canadian dollars (UA$14,460) from C$17,805 for the 1990 model. The price of a similarly equipped Camaro RS was reduced to C$16,398 Canadian dollars from C$17,805.
