Business Brief -- Baruch-Foster Corp.: Company to Hire Adviser On Possible Sale of Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

Baruch-Foster Corp. said in Dallas that its directors have decided to hire an investment banking firm to advise it about the possible sale of the company. The company didn't say whether an investment banker had been chosen and a spokesman couldn't be reached for comment.

Last month, the oil and gas concern said it had received several unsolicited inquiries from other oil and gas concerns about a possible transaction.

Based on its 2.7 million shares outstanding and its closing stock price Friday of $6 in American Stock Exchange composite trading, the company has an estimated value of $16.2 million.

In 1989, Baruch-Foster had a net loss of $272,326, or 10 cents a share, on revenue of $8.9 million.
