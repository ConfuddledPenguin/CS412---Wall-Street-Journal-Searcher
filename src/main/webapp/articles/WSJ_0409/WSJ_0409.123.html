Iran to Supply Gas to Soviets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

MANAMA, Bahrain -- Iran signed a 15-year agreement to supply the Soviet Union with natural gas, the official Islamic Republic News Service reported. The accord calls for Iran to supply three billion cubic meters of gas annually through its pipeline link to the Soviet Union. Iranian Oil Minister Gholamreza Aqazadeh said annual revenue from the gas will amount to about $300 million, the news agency said. Gas deliveries are set to begin today and may be increased to 10 billion cubic meters annually at a later date, IRNA said.

Mr. Aqazadeh said Iran and the Soviet Union also reached agreement on the transfer of Iranian natural gas to Eastern and Western Europe via the Soviet pipeline system.
