Who's News: Waterford Wedgwood PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

WATERFORD WEDGWOOD PLC (London) -- Jim Colleran resigned as chief executive officer for the Americas of this troubled Anglo-Irish glass and dinnerware maker. Mr. Colleran, who joined the company in 1983 as executive vice president of Waterford Crystal Inc., left "to pursue other career interests," the company said. He will be succeeded temporarily by director of overseas sales and marketing, Redmond O'Donoghue. Waterford Wedgwood, which reported a 1989 pretax loss of 20.6 million punts ($32.5 million), last month announced a capital restructuring built around a 29.9% stake jointly held by Fitzwilton Holdings PLC and Morgan Stanley & Co.
