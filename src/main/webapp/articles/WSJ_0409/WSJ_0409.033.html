International: South Korean Auto Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

SEOUL -- South Korean auto makers' domestic sales in March jumped 62.2% from a year earlier to 86,300 vehicles while their exports plummeted 50.4% to 22,100 vehicles, according to their preliminary statistics. The five makers' domestic sales in the first quarter totaled 223,200 vehicles, up 37.5% from the year-earlier period. The number of Korean motorists has increased, reflecting rising incomes.

Exports for the first quarter declined 39.3% to 62,350 vehicles.
