American Home Products

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

NEW YORK -- American Home Products Corp. definitively agreed to sell its household-products business for $1.25 billion to Reckitt & Colman PLC, a London consumer-products company. Closing of the previously announced transaction still is subject to antitrust clearance and other regulatory filings and approvals. American Home said the definitive agreement targets May 31 as the closing date for at least the U.S. operations involved. If sales of other lines are cleared on that date, they will be completed as well.

American Home is selling its Boyle Midway subsidiary but is keeping its Pam and Butter Buds products. The company is focusing its efforts on its pharmaceutical lines.
