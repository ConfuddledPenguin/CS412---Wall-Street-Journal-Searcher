Business Brief -- Lincoln N.C. Realty Fund Inc.: Partnership Sets Condition For Proceeding With Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

Silverstone Partners, a closely held Los Angeles partnership, said it is willing to proceed with an offer for Lincoln N.C. Realty Fund Inc., if Lincoln's board waives poison pill provisions. As previously reported, Lincoln, of Palo Alto, Calif., last month said a letter of agreement to be acquired by Silverstone was terminated because Lincoln's investment banker couldn't confirm the financial fairness of the deal. Lincoln had signed a letter of intent to be acquired by Silverstone for $9.54 a share and 3.1 cents a warrant in a deal valued at about $19 million. Silverstone holds 8.6% of Lincoln.

Lincoln officials couldn't be reached for comment.
