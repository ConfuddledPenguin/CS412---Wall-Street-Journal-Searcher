Malaysian Action on Copyrights

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia -- The Malaysian government said it will amend its Copyright Act to comply with the Berne Convention for the protection of literary and artistic works. News reports published here last week had stated that Malaysia, along with 16 other countries, had been placed on a "watch list" by the U.S. for not providing adequate protection of intellectual property rights.
