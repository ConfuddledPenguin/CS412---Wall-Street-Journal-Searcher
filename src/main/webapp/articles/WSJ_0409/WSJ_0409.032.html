McDermott Says 98% Of Stock Warrants Have Been Exercised

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

NEW ORLEANS -- McDermott International Inc. said 98% of the six million warrants to purchase shares of its common were exercised on or before the expiration date last Monday. The company received $122.4 million in principal amount of incorporated 10% subordinated debentures due 2003 and $24.9 million in cash as payment of the exercise price of the warrants. About 6,335,000 additional common shares have been issued in connection with the exercise of the warrants, bringing the total number of shares outstanding to 43,860,225. Approximately $27.6 million of the debentures remain outstanding. Neither the company nor its McDermott Inc. unit has any current intention to purchase the remaining debt, the company said.

McDermott International is a world-wide energy services company.
