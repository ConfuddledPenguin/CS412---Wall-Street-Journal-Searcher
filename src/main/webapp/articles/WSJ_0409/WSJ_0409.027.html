Business Brief -- Storage Equities Inc.: Group Lifts Stake in Trust To 7.2% of Common Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/09/90

A group including Storage Equities Inc.'s chairman and president said it raised its stake in Storage Equities to 7.2% of the common stock outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group said it holds 787,953 Storage Equities common shares, including 125,018 shares purchased from March 2 to March 30 for $7.50 to $8 each.

Storage Equities' chairman is Kenneth Q. Volk Jr. and its president is B. Wayne Hughes.

According to the filing, the group purchased additional shares for investment purposes, believing that the stock is undervalued. It doesn't intend to seek control of the real estate investment trust.

Other group members include PS Insurance Co., Public Storage Inc., Public Storage Inc. Profit Sharing Plan & Trust, and other members of the Hughes family.

In Glendale, Calif., officers of Storage Equities couldn't be reached for comment on the SEC filing.
