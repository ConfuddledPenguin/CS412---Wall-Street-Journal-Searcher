Weatherford Share Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

HOUSTON -- Weatherford International Inc. said it proposed to offer to the public three million common shares. A price for the issue, scheduled for this month or next, hasn't been set, the company said. Proceeds from the offering will be used for repaying debt and for general working capital. A registration for the securities has been filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission but isn't yet effective.

Weatherford is a diversified energy service and manufacturing company.
