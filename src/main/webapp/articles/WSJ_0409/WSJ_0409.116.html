International Brief -- Inco Ltd.: Sale Expected to Yield Gain Of $125 Million After Tax

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

Inco Ltd. said it expects net proceeds of $250 million from a public offering of 20% of P.T. International Nickel Indonesia, resulting in an after-tax gain of about $125 million. The Toronto nickel producer said proceeds will be used to pay debt and for general corporate purposes. Inco had $925 million in long-term debt at Dec. 31.

Inco said the offering, which will reduce its stake in P.T. Indonesia to 58% from 78%, is part of an agreement signed in 1968 with the Indonesian government. The shares are expected to begin trading on Indonesia's stock exchanges next month, the company said. Inco said in November that it was considering an offering.

P.T. Indonesia, which produced 64 million pounds of semifinished nickel last year, is 20%-owned by Sumitomo Metal Mining Co. of Japan and 2% by a group of other Japanese companies.
