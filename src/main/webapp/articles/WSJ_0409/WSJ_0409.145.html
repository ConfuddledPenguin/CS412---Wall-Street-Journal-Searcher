Who's News: Terminal Data Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/09/90

TERMINAL DATA Corp. (Moorpark, Calif.) -- Richard M. Drysdale, 58 years old, was elected chairman of this maker of document image capture devices. He succeeds Stanley R. Schoen, 67, who resigned for "personal reasons," the company said. He continues as a director. Mr. Drysdale, a company director since 1986, is president of Westwood Management Co., a Los Angeles venture capital concern.
