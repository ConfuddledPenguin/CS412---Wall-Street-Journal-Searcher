Pacific Southwest Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

NORTHRIDGE, Calif. -- Pacific Southwest Corp. expects net income for its fiscal year ended July 31 of between $800,000 and $950,000, or between 25 cents and 30 cents a share, on $5.5 million of revenue. The financial services and real estate concern cited the strong performance of its investment portfolio and the receipt of certain notes payable in its fourth quarter. The company posted a 1989 net loss of $71,000, or four cents a share, on $2.8 million of revenue. Pacific Southwest shares began trading yesterday in the national over-the-counter market.
