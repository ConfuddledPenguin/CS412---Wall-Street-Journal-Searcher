Gendex Completes Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

MILWAUKEE, Wis. -- Gendex Corp. said it has completed the previously announced acquisition of Kestrel Dental Corp. for $41.3 million. Gendex, which makes dental and medical X-ray equipment, said the purchase price includes the refinancing of $9.7 million in existing Kestrel long-term debt, and is subject to certain post-closing adjustments.

In 1989, Kestrel, a dental-equipment concern, had sales of $41.6 million. For fiscal 1990, ended March 31, Gendex had sales of $29.4 million.
