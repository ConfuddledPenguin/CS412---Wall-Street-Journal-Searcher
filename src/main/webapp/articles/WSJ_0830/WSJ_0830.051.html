  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Ecology & Environment Inc. said it received a contract from the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency that could generate $111.3 million in revenue over the next four years. The two-year contract, with a two-year extension option, involves routine prevention program services and emergency response assistance for oil or hazardous-material spills, the company said.

The company performs environmental services for industry and governments world-wide.
