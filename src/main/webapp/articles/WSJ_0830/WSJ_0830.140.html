Business Brief -- Sierra Tucson Cos.: Drug Rehabilitation Concern Expects 2% Drop in Profit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

Sierra Tucson Cos. said it expects a 2% decline in third-quarter earnings because its new addiction-treatment center in Germany won't break even this year. Net for the quarter ending Sept. 30 will be about $980,000, or 14 cents a share, the company said. In the third quarter a year ago, net was $1 million, or 20 cents a share, on revenue of $5 million.

Sierra Tucson said, however, that it still anticipates a 42% increase in full-year net, to $5.1 million, or 73 cents a share. Net for 1989 was $3.6 million, or 62 cents a share, on revenue of $18.4 million.

The company declined to forecast its revenue for the latest third quarter and year. It opened the treatment center in Garmisch, West Germany, in December 1989.
