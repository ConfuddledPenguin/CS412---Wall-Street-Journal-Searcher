Business Brief -- Falcon Products Inc.: Earnings for Oct. 31 Year Expected to Be About Flat

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

Falcon Products Inc. said it expects net income for the fiscal year ending Oct. 31 to be between $2.1 million, or 62 cents a share, and $2.3 million, or 66 cents a share. Sales are expected to rise 15% to about $40 million. In the prior year, Falcon earned $2.2 million, or 65 cents a share, on sales of $34.6 million.

Falcon said two new products are being introduced, and the full impact on sales is "yet to be felt." The company said its core products -- particularly tables, table bases, chairs and booths -- "are doing exceptionally well."
