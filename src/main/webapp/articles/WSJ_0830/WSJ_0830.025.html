Image Bank to Buy Back Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

NEW YORK -- Image Bank Inc., which licenses photos and film, said its board authorized it to buy back as many as 30,000, or 0.8%, of its nearly 3.7 million shares outstanding. Image Bank would buy the shares on the open market or privately over the next year and use them for an employee stock purchase plan. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, the shares closed unchanged at $2.75.
