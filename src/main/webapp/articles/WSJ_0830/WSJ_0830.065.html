Mead Stock Buy-Back Approved

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Mead Corp. directors authorized the repurchase of an additional five million common shares. The forest products and electronic publishing concern said it repurchased 1,625,000 shares under a previous authorization to buy as many as two million shares. As of July 1, the company had about 62 million shares outstanding.

Repurchases under the new authorization will be made in the open market using internally generated funds, Mead said.
