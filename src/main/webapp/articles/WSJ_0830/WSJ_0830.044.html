Technology: Liposome Technology Patent

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

MENLO PARK, Calif. -- Liposome Technology Inc. said it received a patent for the use of a drug to treat certain bacterial infections. The patent claims that liposomal gentamicin, an antibiotic, is useful in treating patients with infectious levels of mycobacterium avium.

Liposome is a pharmaceutical company.
