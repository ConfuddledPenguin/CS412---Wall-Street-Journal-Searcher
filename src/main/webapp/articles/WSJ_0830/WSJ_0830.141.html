Business Brief -- Riggs National Corp.: Some $655 Million in Assets To Be Sold Back to FDIC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

Riggs National Corp., Washington, D.C., said it intends to execute an option to sell back at least $655 million in assets from the National Bank of Washington to the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp., the receiver for the failed bank. A Riggs unit bought the loans earlier this month when federal regulators declared the National Bank of Washington insolvent and closed it. The National Bank of Washington was a unit of Washington Bancorp., which filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection from creditors Aug. 1.

Riggs, a bank holding company, said it may opt to sell back to the FDIC up to $95 million more in acquired loans.
