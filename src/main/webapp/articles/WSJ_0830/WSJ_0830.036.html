Shell Oil Wins Jet Fuel Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

WASHINGTON -- The Defense Logistics Agency awarded jet fuel contracts to Shell Oil Co., unit of Royal Dutch/Shell Group, for $135.3 million; Fina Oil & Chemical Co. for $41.9 million; Wyoming Refining Co. for $25.1 million; and Sinclair Oil Corp. for $10.1 million.

Allied-Signal Inc. was issued a $20.7 million Air Force contract for aircraft parts.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. received a $16.4 million Air Force contract for aircraft radar.

Texas Instruments Inc. got a $10.3 million Navy contract for missile test equipment.
