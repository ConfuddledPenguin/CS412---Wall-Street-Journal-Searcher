Action Auto Stores Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

FLINT, Mich. -- Action Auto Stores Inc. said it will auction pieces of its property next month in an effort to reduce its debt. The automotive-parts retailer said it hopes to raise about $8 million from the sale of more than 50 property sites throughout Michigan.

"This strategic move is in keeping with the consolidation of our company," said C. James Sabo, chairman.

The company in June filed a petition for reorganization under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code.
