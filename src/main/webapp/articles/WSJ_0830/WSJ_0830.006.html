Commercial Intertech Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

YOUNGSTOWN, Ohio -- Commercial Intertech Corp. said directors authorized repurchase of up to 500,000 shares of common stock in the open market or through privately negotiated transactions. The producer of hydraulic parts, fluid purification products and metal products last month completed repurchase of a previously authorized 2.5 million shares.

"The current depressed market price of Commercial's shares represents an undervaluation," said Paul Powers, chairman, president and chief executive officer. The stock closed yesterday at $14.75, about half the 52-week high of $28.625 a share. There are about 9.9 million shares outstanding.
