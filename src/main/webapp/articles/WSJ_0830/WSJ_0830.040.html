Computer Data Systems Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

ROCKVILLE, Md. -- Computer Data Systems Inc. said its board authorized the repurchase of as many as 200,000 shares of its common stock at par value. Internally generated money will fund the repurchase. The board believes the company's stock is undervalued and the repurchase represents a good investment opportunity. In national over-the-counter trading Computer Data's stock closed at $8.625, up $1.375.
