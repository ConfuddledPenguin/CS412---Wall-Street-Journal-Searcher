Dividend News: Philip Morris Lifts Quarterly Payout 25%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

NEW YORK -- Philip Morris Cos. directors boosted the quarterly dividend 25%, reflecting what a spokesman called continuing strong operations. The board of the food, tobacco and brewing giant declared a payout of 43 cents a share, up from 34.375 cents, payable Oct. 10 to stock of record Sept. 14. Philip Morris had nearly 925 million shares outstanding on July 31.

For the first six months of this year, the company's net income advanced 29% to $1.72 billion, or $1.86 a share, from $1.34 billion, or $1.44 a share, a year earlier. Sales advanced 9.5% to $24.13 billion from $22.03 billion.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Philip Morris shares rose 50 cents to close at $45.50 on volume of more than 2.5 million shares, making it the third most active issue on the Big Board.
