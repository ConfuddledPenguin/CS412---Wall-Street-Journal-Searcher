National Lumber Bankruptcy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

FOUNTAIN VALLEY, Calif. -- National Lumber & Supply Inc. said the company's board approved the conversion of its Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing to a Chapter 7 liquidation of assets. The company, which filed for Chapter 11 protection from creditors in April, said its financial condition has deteriorated and its main secured lender stopped funding its operations.

The home-center retailer has begun liquidating its inventories and is in discussions with potential buyers of several outlets. Mel Jaffe, National Lumber's president, is among the potential bidders. Such transactions are subject to approval by the bankruptcy court and the Chapter 7 liquidation trustee.
