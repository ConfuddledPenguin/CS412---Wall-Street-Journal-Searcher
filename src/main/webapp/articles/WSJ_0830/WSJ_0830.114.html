WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Health Images Inc., offering of 1.5 million common shares, via Robertson, Stephens & Co. and Stephens Inc.

J.C. Penney Co., shelf offering of $500 million in senior debt securities and warrants.

Scott Paper Co., shelf offering of $500 million in debt securities, which includes a current filing of $350 million plus $150 million of debt securities left over from a previous shelf filing.

Stoneridge Resources Inc., offering of $30 million in convertible senior subordinated debentures, via Jefferies & Co.
