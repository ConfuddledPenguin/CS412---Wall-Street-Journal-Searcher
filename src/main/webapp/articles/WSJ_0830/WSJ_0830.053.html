Michaels Stores' Sales Rise 19.4%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

IRVING, Texas -- Michaels Stores Inc. said sales in the month ended August 26 rose 19.4% to $22.1 million from $18.5 million last year. Sales in stores open in August of both years rose 4.6% to $18.8 million from $18 million. Michaels owns and operates a chain of 133 specialty retail stores.
