Business Brief -- Perkin-Elmer Corp.: Fiscal 4th-Period Net Rose 3% on Sales Gain of 7.4%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

Perkin-Elmer Corp. reported a 3% increase in net income for the fourth quarter ending July 31 as sales rose 7.4%, reflecting successful restructuring efforts completed during fiscal 1990. Perkin-Elmer, which manufactures analytical instruments used in material-science laboratories, reported quarterly net income rose to $13.6 million, or 38 cents a share, from $13.2 million, or 30 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Per-share earnings reflect the company's buy-back of 10.1 million of its common shares.

In addition to the share buy-back, the restructuring included the sale of seven business units by the Norwalk, Conn., company.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Perkin-Elmer shares closed at $22.875, up $1.375.
