Chevron Relocates Trading Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Chevron Corp. is decentralizing its commodity-trading activities by moving its Chevron International Oil Co. unit to Houston from San Francisco. Robert F. Walsh will remain president of the unit, which trades crude-oil and gasoline futures contracts and buys and sells those commodities.

Peter McCrea was named to the new position of senior vice president of supply and trading and will report to Mr. Walsh.
