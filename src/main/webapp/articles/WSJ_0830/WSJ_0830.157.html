Business Brief -- AT&T: Avis, Caterpillar, Textron And ITT Award Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

AT&T said it signed four contracts to provide voice and data networks for Avis Rent A Car System Inc., Caterpillar Inc., ITT Corp. and Textron Inc. The contracts total more than $100 million. Garden City, N.Y.-based Avis signed a five-year, $35 million contract for a voice and data network that links its domestic car rental locations throughout the U.S.

Caterpillar, a Peoria, Ill.-based manufacturer of construction equipment and engines, awarded the company a five-year, $25 million contract to link more than 100 domestic and international Caterpillar locations.

ITT, based in Farmington, Conn., signed for a contract valued at a minimum of $37.7 million over three years. AT&T will provide ITT with interstate and international calling between 850 company locations world-wide.

Textron, a company with operations in aerospace technology, commercial products and financial services, awarded AT&T a three-year contract to provide inbound and outbound voice and data communications to more than 500 domestic locations. Textron wouldn't release the contract's value, but said it would save Textron about $2.5 million in annual communications costs.
