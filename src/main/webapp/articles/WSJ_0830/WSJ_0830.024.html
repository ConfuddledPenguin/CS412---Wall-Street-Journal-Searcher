International Brief -- ABB Asea Brown Boveri AG: Engineering Concern Posts Higher Pretax Profit, Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

ABB Asea Brown Boveri AG, the big Swiss-based electrical-engineering company, said pretax profit in the first half rose 28% to $500 million, from $391 million in the same period last year. Sales in the first half rose 35% to $12.79 billion, from $9.44 billion the previous year.

The pretax profit figure came in just under some analysts' expectations, while sales growth surpassed these forecasts somewhat.
