International Brief -- Cadbury Schweppes PLC: Pretax Profit Increased 12% In 1st Half at British Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

Cadbury Schweppes PLC, a British beverages and sweets manufacturer, reported a 12% rise in pretax profit on the back of strong earnings in Europe which were partially offset by a sharp rise in interest charges. Pretax profit for the six months to June 16 rose to 105 million pounds ($204 million) from 93.9 million pounds.

Cadbury said U.K and overseas properties acquired in the last year are making "satisfactory progress" and contributed to the gain in earnings.

Group sales jumped 22% to 1.41 billion pounds from 1.15 billion pounds, including record sales of 640 million pounds in the U.K., up from 506 million pounds last year.
