Who's News: Horsham Board Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

TORONTO -- Horsham Corp. said it increased the size of its board to 12 members, from eight, and elected a fifth new member to succeed a retiring director. Horsham said David Beck and James Tory, both Toronto lawyers; David Gilmour, vice chairman of New York-based Arlington Investments Ltd.; Robert Helman, a Chicago lawyer; and Duke Ligon of Bankers Trust Co., New York, were elected to its board.

The company said Samuel Zell, chairman of Chicago-based Equity Financial & Management Co., retired from the board.

Horsham, an investment and oil and gas concern, said it had been operating with "a relatively small board," and decided to increase the number of directors to strengthen the board.
