Horn & Hardart to Sell Bojangles

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

NEW YORK -- Horn & Hardart Co. agreed to sell its Bojangles chain of chicken restaurants to a unit of Sienna Holdings, a closely held Los Angeles investment firm. In return for Bojangles, which is profitable, Horn & Hardart will receive a $20 million, 14% senior subordinated debt that Sienna holds. Horn & Hardart is selling the restaurant chain as part of a plan to focus on its direct-marketing operations.
