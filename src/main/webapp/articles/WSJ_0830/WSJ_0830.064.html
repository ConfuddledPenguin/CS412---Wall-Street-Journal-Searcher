Dividend News: Long Island Lighting Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

LONG ISLAND LIGHTING Co. directors boosted the quarterly dividend 50%, to 37.5 cents from 25 cents, payable Oct. 1 to stock of record Sept. 10. The action is in line with the Hicksville, N.Y., utility's 1989 agreement with New York state, under which Lilco abandoned its Shoreham nuclear power plant in return for rate increases and other items. Lilco has 111 million shares outstanding. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Lilco's shares rose 12.5 cents to $19.25.
