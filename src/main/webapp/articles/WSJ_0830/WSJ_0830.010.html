Bethlehem Steel Revamping

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

BETHLEHEM, Pa. -- Bethlehem Steel Corp. said it will reorganize certain customer service and marketing functions, eliminating 56 customer service positions. When fully implemented in about two years, the changes will save about $3 million a year, as well as provide better service to customers, the company said.

Customer service work currently performed at field sales offices will be consolidated at plants in Burns Harbor, Ind., and Sparrows Point, Md., and at the Structural Products division in Bethlehem, the company said.

Also, separate marketing offices will be established for hot and cold rolled products and coated sheet products, the company said.
