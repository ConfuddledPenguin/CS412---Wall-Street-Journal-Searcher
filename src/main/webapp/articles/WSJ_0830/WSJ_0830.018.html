Financing Business: Toronto-Dominion Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

TORONTO-DOMINION BANK, Toronto, said it plans to offer 125 million Canadian dollars (US$109.4 million) of 11.65% debentures due September 2000. It said the offer will be underwritten by a group led by Toronto Dominion Securities Inc., a subsidiary of the bank. The bank said proceeds will be added to its capital base.
