France's Gross Domestic Product

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

PARIS -- France's gross domestic product grew 0.1% in the second quarter from the first quarter, slowing from a revised 0.5% pace in the first quarter, the National Statistics Institute said. The first-quarter growth rate was originally reported as 0.7%.

The agency also said that GDP growth in the fourth quarter of 1989 was revised to 0.9% from 1%, indicating that economic expansion has been slowing steadily since that period. The fourth-quarter 1989 figure compared favorably with a 0.6% gain in the previous quarter.
