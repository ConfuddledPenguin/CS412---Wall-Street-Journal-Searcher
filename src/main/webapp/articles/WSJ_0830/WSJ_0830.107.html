Who's News: Perpetual Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

PERPETUAL FINANCIAL Corp. (Vienna, Va.) -- John Morton III, formerly executive vice president of Baltimore-based Maryland National Bank, was named president, chief executive officer and a director of this thrift holding company. Mr. Morton, 46 years old, succeeds Thomas J. Owen, 55, as chief executive officer. Mr. Owen remains chairman. Ross C. Towne, formerly president and chief operating officer, 45, resigned in July to pursue other business interests.
