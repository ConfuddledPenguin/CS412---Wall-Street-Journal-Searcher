Financing Business: Amax Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

AMAX Inc. and its 87%-owned Amax Gold Inc. said they filed a registration statement for a secondary offering of at least 9.5 million shares of Amax Gold. At yesterday's New York Stock Exchange closing price for Amax Gold common of $16.875, the sale would raise about $160 million for the parent company. Thomas McKeever, Amax executive vice president, said the New York-based energy and mining company would use the proceeds for general corporate purposes. Under the offering, Amax would sell 9.5 million units consisting of one share and a warrant to buy another share of Amax Gold. If all the shares are sold and the warrants exercised, Amax's stake in Amax Gold would be reduced to 55%. If the warrants aren't exercised, the parent would continue to hold about 71% of Denver-based Amax Gold. Merrill Lynch Capital Markets is the lead underwriter.
