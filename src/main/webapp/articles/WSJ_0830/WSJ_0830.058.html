Fleetwood Enterprises Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

RIVERSIDE, Calif. -- Fleetwood Enterprises Inc., which produces recreational vehicles and manufactured housing, said its board authorized the purchase of an additional two million, or 9%, of its 22 million common shares outstanding. A previously authorized buy-back of two million shares has been nearly completed at a cost of $34.6 million. Fleetwood also said net income for its first quarter ended July 29 fell 31% to $10.8 million, or 48 cents a share, from $15.6 million, or 68 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter, which ended July 30. Sales slipped 1% to $389.2 million from $394.9 million.

The company blamed the earnings decline on lower recreational-vehicle sales and a shift in buyers' preference for lowerpriced products with narrower profit margins. Strong sales of manufactured housing were more than offset by the decline in recreational-vehicle sales, Fleetwood said.
