Japan's Steel Exports Fall

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

TOKYO -- Japan's steel exports dropped 15% in July from a year ago, continuing a five-year trend of year-on-year declines, the Japan Iron and Steel Federation reported. Steel exports were particularly lower to China, Taiwan, South Korea, the Soviet Union and the U.S., a federation spokesman said, although domestic demand continued to buoy business for Japanese producers.

Japan's steel exports in July totaled 1.441 million metric tons, up 5.3% from June.
