Who's News: Autodesk Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

AUTODESK Inc. (San Francisco) -- Malcolm Davies and Jim Warren were named directors, expanding the board. Mr. Davies, 43 years old, is senior vice president of marketing and sales for Autodesk, which makes computer software. Mr. Warren, 54, has been an executive with various computer publishing concerns as well as the founding host of Public Broadcasting System's "Computer Chronicles" television show.
