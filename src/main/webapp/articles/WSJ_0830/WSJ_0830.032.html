Home Federal Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

SEYMOUR, Ind. -- Home Federal Savings Bank said it will ask shareholders at the annual meeting, scheduled for Oct. 30, to approve the formation of a holding company, to be named Home Federal Bancorp. The company said a holding-company structure would provide it with "greater operating flexibility, as well as increased ability to take advantage of possible future acquisition opportunities."
