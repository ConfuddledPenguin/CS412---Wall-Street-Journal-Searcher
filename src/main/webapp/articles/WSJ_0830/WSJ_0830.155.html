Business Brief -- Brunswick Corp.: Sale of Businesses Expected To Lead to One-Time Gain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/30/90

Brunswick Corp., Skokie, Ill., said it expects to report a one-time gain of $45 to $50 million from dispositions of non-defense, Technical businesses in 1990. Such a gain would represent about 51 cents a share to 57 cents a share, the maker of marine and recreation products said.

"We are concluding key transactions and have sufficient information to approximate the gain," the company said. The dispositions are part of Brunswick's previously announced plans to sell its Technical segment's Industrial Products and Technetics divisions.

Net proceeds from dispositions, which could range from $120 million to $150 million "depending on which transactions are closed," will be used to pay down debt, Brunswick said.

For the first six months, Brunswick had net income of $34.2 million, or 39 cents a share, on sales of $1.36 billion.
