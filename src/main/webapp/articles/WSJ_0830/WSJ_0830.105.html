Who's News: Lodgistix Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/30/90

LODGISTIX Inc. (Wichita, Kan.) -- Paul E. Hammar, president, chief executive officer and a director of this technical systems designer, was named to the added post of chairman, succeeding George A. Zugmier. Mr. Zugmier remains a director, and board membership remains at six.
