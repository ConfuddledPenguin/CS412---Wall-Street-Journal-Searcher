Who's News: Quantum Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

QUANTUM Corp. (New York) -- Dennis J. Spina, former vice president, Eastern region, of Federal Express Corp., was named president of the Suburban Propane division and corporate vice president, effective Sept. 1. Mr. Spina, 46 years old, succeeds Mark J. Anton, 64, who continues as vice chairman of the division.
