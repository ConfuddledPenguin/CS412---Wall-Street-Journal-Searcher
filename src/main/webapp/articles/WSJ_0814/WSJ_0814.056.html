Who's News: American Family Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

AMERICAN FAMILY Corp. (Columbus, Ga.) -- Daniel P. Amos, 39 years old, has been named vice chairman and chief executive officer of this insurance and broadcasting concern, following the death of his uncle, John B. Amos, 66, the company's founder, chairman and chief executive. Daniel Amos was also named chief executive of the company's principal subsidiary, American Family Life Assurance Corp., again succeeding John Amos. Daniel Amos had been deputy chief executive officer of the company and its unit. Paul Amos, 64, a co-founder and former vice chairman of American Family Corp., has been named the company's chairman.
