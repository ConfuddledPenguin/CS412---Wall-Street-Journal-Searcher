Cray Research Agrees To Settle '89 Lawsuit With Shareholders

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Cray Research Inc. said it agreed in principle to settle a 1989 shareholder lawsuit for $700,000. Cray said three separate suits, which claimed the company had failed to make public certain financial information and details about future business prospects in 1989, were consolidated into a single action in Minneapolis federal court last fall.

In a letter to employees yesterday, Cray contended that the suit is without merit. The company said it agreed to settle the matter to save time and expense. Cray added that the agreement is subject to certain contingencies, including court approval.
