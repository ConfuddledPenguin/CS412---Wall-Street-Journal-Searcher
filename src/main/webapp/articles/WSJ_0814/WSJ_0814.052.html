MinVen's Canadian Mines

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

DENVER -- MinVen Gold Corp. said it will stop its gold and silver mining operations at the Blackdome Mine in British Columbia, Canada, late in the fourth quarter. The mining company wrote off its investment in Blackdome in the first quarter due to a significant decline in the grade of ore at the mine. An additional vein of higher grade ore was discovered since then, but that will also be mined out by November 1990, the company said.

MinVen has interests in four other operating mines in the U.S. and Canada.
