Business Brief -- Fluor Corp.: Concern Awarded Contracts Valued at $22 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp., an engineering, construction, and technical services concern, said that it was awarded two contracts valued at $22 million. Fluor said that its Fluor Daniel unit was awarded a contract by Vancouver-based Westcoast Energy Inc. for $10 million to provide process design, detailed engineering and procurement for expansion of a gas processing facility near Fort St. John, in northeast British Columbia. Project completion is scheduled for late 1991.

The unit was also awarded a contract by Mobil Oil Corp. for $12 million to provide engineering, procurement and construction management services for a sour water stripper tower to remove pollutants from the water stream at Mobil's Chalmette, La. refinery. Project completion is scheduled for mid-1991.
