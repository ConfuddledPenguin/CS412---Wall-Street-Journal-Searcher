International Brief -- N.V. Nationale Borg-Maatschappi: Firms Has Takeover Talks On Dutch Insurer's Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

The Dutch insurance group Nationale-Nederlanden NV said that it was holding negotiations with the insurance group NV Nationale Borg-Maatschappij on acquiring NBM's priority and ordinary shares outstanding for 270 guilders a share. On Friday, NBM's shares closed at 178.50 guilders a share on the secondary, parallel stock market.

Nationale-Nederlanden expressed confidence that its bid for NBM would be accepted.

In 1989, NBM had premium income of 63.7 million guilders and net profit of 7.5 million guilders.

Nationale-Nederlanden, based in The Hague, one of the big three Dutch insurers, had premium income of 23.1 billion guilders in 1989 and net profit of 973 million guilders.
