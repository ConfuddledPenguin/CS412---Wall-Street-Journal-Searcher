Avnet Board Clears Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

GREAT NECK, N.Y. -- Avnet Inc. directors authorized repurchase of as many as one million of its common shares. Avnet, a distributor of computer products and electronic parts, has 36 million common shares outstanding. It last repurchased shares in 1987.

Avnet said, effective immediately, it will buy shares in the open market or in directly negotiated purchases. In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, Avnet shares closed at $23.50 a share, up 12.5 cents.
