Who's News: BankAmerica Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

BANKAMERICA Corp. (San Francisco) -- Eric R. Durant, a bank stock analyst at Fox-Pitt Kelton Inc., was named senior vice president and director of investor relations for this bank holding company. Mr. Durant, 43 years old, previously worked in institutional equity sales at First Manhattan Co. From 1979 to 1986, he worked at Wells Fargo & Co., where he rose to senior vice president and director of investor relations.
