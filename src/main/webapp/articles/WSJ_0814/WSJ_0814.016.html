Kaman Wins GE Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

BLOOMFIELD, Conn. -- Kaman Corp. said its Kaman Aerospace Corp. unit received a multiyear contract from GE Aircraft Engines, a unit of General Electric Co., to manufacture fan reverser fixed structures for GE's new CF6-80E commercial jet engines. The contract, with an initial value of $85 million, covers production through the late 1990s, with options for additional output.

The total value of the contract with options could exceed $150 million, the defense contractor said.
