Business Brief -- New Brunswick Scientific Co.: Lab Equipment Maker Had Wider Loss in 2nd Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

New Brunswick Scientific Co. reported a wider loss in the second quarter, citing a softer market for laboratory equipment among its university and government agency customers. The Edison, N.J., company reported a loss of $1.1 million, or 32 cents a share, compared with a loss of $121,000, or three cents a share, a year earlier. Sales were $7.7 million, down from $8.1 million.

The company said it completed a workforce reduction of 25%, to about 340 employees, in order to offset a downturn in orders. It said its Eastern Europe export business was down considerably.
