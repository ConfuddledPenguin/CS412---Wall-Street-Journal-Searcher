Who's News: Rexene Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

REXENE Corp. (Dallas) -- Kevin W. McAleer was named executive vice president, a new position, and chief financial officer of this petrochemical products maker. Formerly he was chief financial officer and a director of Varo Inc., a unit of IMO Industries Inc. Herman Rosenman, who was chief financial officer and vice chairman of Rexene, resigned in April.
