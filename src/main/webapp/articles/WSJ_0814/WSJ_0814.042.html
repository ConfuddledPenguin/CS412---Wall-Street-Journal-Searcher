Weyerhaeuser Stock Halted

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

Tacoma, Wash. -- Weyerhaeuser Co. said trading in its stock was halted after it notified shareholders and stock exchanges of its program to swap $2.625 convertible exchangeable preference shares for a new issue of $200 million principal amount of 5 1/4% convertible debentures due 2017. Effective Sept. 15, preference shareholders of record Aug. 10 are to receive $1,000 pricipal amount of the debentures in exchange for 20 preference shares held. Shareholders will receive an amount equal to the dividend of 65.625 cents a share accruing to Sept. 15, 1990, the company added. Alternatively, the preference shares may be converted into common at a rate of 1.0416 common shares a preference share.
