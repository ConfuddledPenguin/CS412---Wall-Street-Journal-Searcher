U.S. Steel Output Rose 2.3% in Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 2.3% last week to 1,901,000 tons from 1,858,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 4.6% from the 1,818,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 85.2% of its capability last week, compared with 83.3% the previous week and 81.8% a year ago.
