Business Brief -- Federal Paper Board: Firm Agrees to Acquire Unit of Peter Kiewit Sons

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

Federal Paper Board Co. said it agreed to acquire Continental Bondware Inc., a paper cup maker, from Peter Kiewit Sons Inc. Terms of the deal were not disclosed. Kiewit is based in Omaha, Neb.

Federal Paper Board, Montvale, N.J., which manufactures a wide variety of paper and paper goods, said that the acquisition will complement its purchase last year of Imperial Cup Corp.

Continental Bondware, which operates two plants in Illinois, has annual sales of about $180 million. Federal Paper Board had sales of $1.2 billion last year.

Federal also announced that it agreed to share and exchange technical information with Tokan Kogyo Co., Japan's largest paper cup manufacturer.
