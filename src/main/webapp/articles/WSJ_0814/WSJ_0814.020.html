Ecogen Gets Investment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

LANGHORN, Pa. -- Ecogen Inc. said it closed an $8.5 million investment by David Blech, a private investor who controls about 40% of the company's common stock. Ecogen has received $7.6 million of the proceeds and the balance is anticipated following stockholder approval of the issuance of 450,000 shares of common stock next month. The company said the investment will allow Ecogen to continue operating as an independent company and to promote its three new biopesticides.
