INTERNATIONAL PAPER Corp. (Purchase, N.Y.) -- Kenneth Defren was named staff vice president and director of corporate communications and state corporate affairs at this maker of paper and forest products. Mr. Defren, 43 years old, succeeds William Greener III, now vice president for public affairs of Wheelabrator Technologies Inc., in Hampton, N.H. Mr. Defren was previously executive vice president for New York-based Porter/Novelli, a public relations agency.
