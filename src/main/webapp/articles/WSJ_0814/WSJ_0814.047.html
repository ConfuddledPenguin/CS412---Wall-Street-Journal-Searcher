Night Shift

There are times in my life

When there's need for a change,

Positions I've taken

That I must rearange;

Some attitudes assumed

That I cannot abide,

And that's when, with effort,

I turn onto my side.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

Kitchen Kvetcher

Some mount a soapbox to orate;

I'm no unruly agitator.

My views, for all to love or hate,

Are taped to my refrigerator.

-- Mary Mobilia.
