Financing Business: American Barrick Resources Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

AMERICAN BARRICK RESOURCES Corp. said it plans to sell five million common shares for total proceeds of 122.5 million Canadian dollars (U.S.$106.8 million). The Toronto gold mining concern said an underwriting group led by Gordon Capital Corp. agreed to purchase the shares for C$24.50 each for later resale to the public in Canada and in Europe. American Barrick said it will use the proceeds for development work on existing properties, potential acquisitions and the repayment of long-term debt.
