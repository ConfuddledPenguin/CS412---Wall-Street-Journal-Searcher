Who's News: Mutual Life Insurance Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

MUTUAL LIFE INSURANCE Co. (New York) -- Tom H. Barrett and Claude N. Ballard, Jr., were named trustees, expanding the board to a total of 18 at this insurance, pension and investment management firm. Mr. Barrett, 59 years old, is chairman, president and chief executive officer of Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co., in Akron, Ohio. Mr. Ballard, 60, is a partner at Goldman, Sachs & Co., in New York.
