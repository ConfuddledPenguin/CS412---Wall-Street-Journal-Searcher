W. Bell Expects to Get Bid

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

ROCKVILLE, Md. -- W. Bell & Co. said it received a potential offer valued at $8.6 million to $10.5 million from Hong Kong-based Venture Link Co. Ltd. The company said Venture Link expects to submit a bid for all of W. Bell's 3.8 million shares outstanding based on the estimated book value at closing. W. Bell said Venture Link estimates book value will range from $2.25 to $2.75 a share by the Oct. 31 closing date. The catalog showroom retailer said the offer is subject to due diligence by Venture Link, acceptance by W. Bell shareholders and financing. W. Bell said its board expects to respond shortly to the potential offer. W. Bell also said it expects to report a "significant" operating loss for the second quarter ended Aug. 4, following a first-quarter loss of $1.1 million, or 30 cents a share. In the 1989 second quarter, W. Bell posted a loss of $946,000, or 31 cents a share.
