Triton Energy Discovery

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

DALLAS -- Triton Energy Corp. said an oil discovery in the Boyne lease in Santa Barbara County, Calif., tested at a rate of 250 barrels of oil a day. Triton, an independent exploration and production company, said a second well tested at a rate of 398 barrels of oil a day. Triton holds a 40% interest in the wells, and Oryx Energy Co. is the operator with a 60% interest.
