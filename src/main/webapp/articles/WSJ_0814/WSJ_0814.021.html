Lawson Mardon Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

TORONTO -- Lawson Mardon Group Ltd. said it plans to acquire certain paper-label manufacturing assets of closely held H.S. Crocker Inc. of San Francisco. Lawson Mardon, a packaging and printing concern based in Toronto, said the assets to be acquired include two facilities producing labels for the food and beverage industry. The plants, with 245 employees, are located in Sparks, Nev., and Baltimore.

Lawson Mardon also will acquire 10 of Crocker's sales offices in the U.S.

Terms weren't disclosed. The deal is expected to close next month.
