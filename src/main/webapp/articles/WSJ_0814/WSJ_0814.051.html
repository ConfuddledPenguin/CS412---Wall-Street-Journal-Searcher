Letters to the Editor: Artificial Intelligence Has Real Potential

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

Your July 5 article "Bright Outlook for Artificial Intelligence Yields to Slow Growth and Big Cutbacks" presented a myopic view of artificial-intelligence (AI) technology and its commercial potential. You presented the opinions of self-appointed high-tech industry analysts who focused on American companies and some of AI's earliest technologies in the U.S. domestic market. You did not consider the rapid growth of a new generation of companies and products during the past two to three years in the AI field. The commercial market impact of these new companies and their products had not yet been fully realized.

The article also failed to mention the active investments that foreign venture capitalists and major corporations, especially Japanese-based, have made in U.S. artificial-intelligence companies.

Tai Sugimoto

President and CEO

International Chip Corp.

Columbia, S.C.
