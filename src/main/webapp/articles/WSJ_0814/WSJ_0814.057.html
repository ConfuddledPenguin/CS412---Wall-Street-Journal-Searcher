French Steel Output Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

PARIS -- French companies produced 1,683,000 metric tons of crude steel in July, up 8.9% from a year earlier, according to industry data. The National Steel Manufacturers' Association said the July production brings French output for the first seven months of the year to 11,629,000 tons, down 2% from the like 1989 period.
