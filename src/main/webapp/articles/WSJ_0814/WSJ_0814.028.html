Who's News: Teradata Names Officials

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

LOS ANGELES -- Teradata Corp. named Donald C. Cantor, 45 years old, to the newly created post of chief operating officer. He remains executive vice president. The computer systems concern also named Michael F.G. Ashby, 41, formerly vice president, finance and administration, to the new post of senior vice president, finance and administration.
