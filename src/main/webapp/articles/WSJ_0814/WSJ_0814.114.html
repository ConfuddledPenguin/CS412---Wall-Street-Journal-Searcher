Business Brief -- Columbia Gas System: Utility Agrees to Sell Unit For $39.2 Million Plus Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

Columbia Gas System Inc. said it agreed to sell its New York subsidiary to New York State Electric & Gas Corp. for $39.2 million plus debt outstanding. Binghamton-based New York State Electric has agreed to pay $20 to $25 million to purchase all of Columbia Gas of New York's debt outstanding at the closing date at face value. The transaction is subject to regulatory approval and is expected to close by Dec. 31. Columbia Gas of New York, also based in Binghamton, serves about 68,000 customers. Wilmington, Del.-based Columbia Gas produces, stores and distributes natural gas in 14 states and the District of Columbia.
