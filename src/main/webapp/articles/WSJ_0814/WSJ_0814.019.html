Technology: Cray Research Exxon Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Cray Research Inc. said Exxon Corp. ordered a Cray Y-MP8 computer system valued at about $15 million. The system, to replace two existing Cray supercomputers, is to be installed in the fourth quarter at the New York-based oil company's technical computing center in Houston. It will be the first Cray Y-MP8 supercomputer used in the petroleum industry, said Cray, which makes large-scale, high-speed computers.
