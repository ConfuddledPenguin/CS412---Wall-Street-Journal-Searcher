Thomson-CSF Radar Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

PARIS -- French defense and electronics group Thomson-CSF announced that it has received an order to supply an unspecified number of radar systems to the French navy. The state-controlled group said in a communique that the medium-range air surveillance radar systems will be designed for use in limited-risk areas. Delivery will start toward the middle of 1991.

Financial details of the contract weren't disclosed.
