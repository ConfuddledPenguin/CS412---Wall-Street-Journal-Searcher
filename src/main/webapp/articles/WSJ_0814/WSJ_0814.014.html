British Retail Sales Volume Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

LONDON -- Retail sales volume in Britain rose a provisional 1.0% in July from June and was up 2.3% from July 1989, the U.K. Central Statistical Office said. Market forecasts for July were for a rise of 0.4% on the month.

Retail sales volume was up 0.1% in the three months ended in July, compared with the previous three months but was up 1.6% from a year earlier, the statistical office said.

The value of retail sales was up about 9% in July from July 1989.
