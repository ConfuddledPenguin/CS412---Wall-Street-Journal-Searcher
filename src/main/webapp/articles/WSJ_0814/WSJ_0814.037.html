Dow Jones Index Changes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

NEW YORK -- Several changes are being made in the industry groups of the Dow Jones Equity Market index, all effective today. General Instrument Corp. is being removed from the communication technology group because it is being acquired, and is being replaced by Scientific-Atlanta Inc.

Honeywell Inc. is being moved to the electronic components industry group from the diversified technology group, and Acuson Corp. is being moved to the medical and biotechnology industry group from the medical supplies group. Both changes were made to better reflect the two companies' businesses.

Motel 6 L.P. is being removed from the lodging industry group because it is being acquired.
