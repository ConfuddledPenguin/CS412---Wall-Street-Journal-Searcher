Baldor Electric Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

FORT SMITH, Ark. -- Baldor Electric Co. said it plans to purchase as many as 300,000 of its common shares on the open market and in negotiated transactions for its stock option plan and other employee benefit plans. The stock will be purchased between now and June 30, 1991. The maker of electric motors has about 9,656,000 shares outstanding.
