Philippine Trade Deficit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/14/90

MANILA, the Philippines -- The Philippine trade deficit widened 63% to $1.87 billion in the first half from $1.15 billion a year earlier, the National Statistics Office said. Exports rose 3.7% to $3.9 billion from $3.76 billion in the first six months of 1989, while imports grew a hefty 18% to $5.77 billion from $4.91 billion.
