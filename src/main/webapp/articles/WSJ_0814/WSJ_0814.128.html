Sun Microsystems Inc. Files Stock Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/14/90

Mountainview, Calif. -- Sun Microsystems Inc. said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering a proposed offering of 5,938,834 new shares of common stock together with 1,061,166 shares to be offered by certain selling shareholders. Sun said 5.6 million of the shares will be sold in the United States by Goldman, Sachs & Co., Salomon Brothers Inc. and Dean Witter Reynolds Inc.

Another 1.4 million shares will be sold outside the U.S. by an international syndicate managed by Goldman Sachs International Ltd., Salomon Brothers International Ltd. and Dean Witter Capital Markets International Ltd.
