Kemper's Holder Rights Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

LONG GROVE, Ill. -- Kemper Corp. said its board approved a stockholder rights plan, designed to deter unfair takeover practices and protect its "long-range strategic plans" against disruption. The insurance and investment services concern said it is not aware of any takeover activity directed toward it.

Under the plan, shareholders will, on July 30, be issued rights which, under certain takeover circumstances, allow the holder to buy stock in the company at half price.
