Who's News: Legent Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

LEGENT Corp. (Vienna, Va.) -- Joe M. Henson, chairman and chief executive of this systems software concern, will assume the duties of chief operating officer. Mr. Henson, 57 years old, takes on the responsibilities of Peter J. Barris, 38, who resigned as president and chief operating officer. A Legent spokesman said that Mr. Barris left to allow Mr. Henson more direct involvement in the daily management of the firm.
