Sanwa Will Merge Two Units

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

TOKYO -- Sanwa Bank Ltd. announced that it obtained Bank of England approval to merge its British subsidiary, Sanwa International Ltd., with the Associated Japanese Bank International Ltd., which became a Sanwa unit March 30. The merger, to be completed in August, will be called Sanwa International PLC.

The Associated Japanese Bank International was launched in London in 1970 by Sanwa, the Nomura Securities Co., Dai Ichi Kangyo Bank Ltd. and Mitsui Bank Ltd.

In addition to the current businesses of the two banks, which include the underwriting and sale of bonds, the merger will also offer general banking services.

Sanwa, a leading Japanese city bank, also has offices in Switzerland and West Germany.
