Business Brief -- CyCare Systems Inc.: Arizona Firm Reports Loss Of $14.2 Million in Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

CyCare Systems Inc., Phoenix, Ariz., posted a second-quarter loss of $14.2 million, or $2.55 a share, including $21 million in write-offs and provisions related to a plan to discontinue certain product lines. In the year-ago period, CyCare had net income of $791,000, or 14 cents a share. The company had operating income in the latest quarter of $250,000, or four cents a share. Revenue fell 12%, to $19.6 million, from $22.4 million.

CyCare, a data processing concern, plans to "go back to working with physician-controlled medical groups and hospitals," said a spokesman. In recent years, CyCare had begun providing systems to certain kinds of health maintenance organizations and other non-physician controlled health-care concerns.

The "strategic redirection" reflects a six-month review of company operations initiated by Raymond R. Maturi, who became president and chief operating officer in January, the spokesman said. In the next 18 months, the company plans to close, or significantly reduce in size, four of its 15 major offices.
