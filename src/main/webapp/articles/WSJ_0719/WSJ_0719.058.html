Who's News: Knogo Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

KNOGO Corp. (Hauppauge, N.Y.) -- W.C. Nix Jr. was named senior vice president of the North America division, a new post, at this supplier of electronic detector systems. Mr. Nix, 46 years old, was previously president of United Security Supply Co., a division of ADT Ltd., a London maker of alarm equipment.
