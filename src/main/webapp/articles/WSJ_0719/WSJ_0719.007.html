Who's News: Sonoco Products Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

SONOCO PRODUCTS Co. (Hartsville, S.C.) -- Russell C. King Jr., 55-year-old senior vice president of this packaging products maker, was named president and chief operating officer; the latter post is new. As president, he succeeds Charles W. Coker, 57, who remains chief executive officer and was named chairman, a post that had been vacant since the mid-1970s.
