Who's News: Xyvision Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

XYVISION Inc. (Wakefield, Mass.) -- Terry D. Fagin, senior vice president, was named to the vacant post of president and chief operating officer. Mr. Fagin, 46 years old, joined Xyvision in December from Bolt, Beranek & Newman Inc. where he was president of the BBN Communications Corp. unit. Xyvision makes computerized publishing systems.
