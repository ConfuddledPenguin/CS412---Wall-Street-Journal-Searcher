Business Brief -- MarCor Resorts Inc.: Firm to Increase Its Stake In Hotel, Casino Operator

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

MarCor Resorts Inc., Las Vegas, Nev., said that as a result of its recent tender offer, it will increase its stake in MarCor Resorts L.P.V., a limited partnership, to 95%, from 34.9%, in exchange for $36 million worth of its stock. When the transaction is completed, the number of MarCor Resorts Inc.'s common shares will almost double to 14 million shares, from 7.5 million.

MarCor Resorts L.P.V. is a limited partnership that owns and operates the Rio Suite Hotel & Casino in Las Vegas. The partnership also owns 125 acres in Henderson, Nev. MarCor Resorts Inc. owns a portfolio of real estate and other gaming properties.
