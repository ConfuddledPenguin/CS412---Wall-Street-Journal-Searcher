Pier 1's June Sales Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Pier 1 Imports said that sales rose almost 12% in June but that sales for those at stores open more than a year fell 2.6%. The company attributed the slip in sales at older stores to a decline in furniture sales. "The consumer has lost a little confidence in the economy and is buying less furniture," said Robert G. Herndon, chief financial officer for the home-furnishing retailer. Mr. Herndon said the company's sales of soft goods remain strong.

In June, Pier 1 had sales of $56.9 million, up from $50.9 million a year ago.
