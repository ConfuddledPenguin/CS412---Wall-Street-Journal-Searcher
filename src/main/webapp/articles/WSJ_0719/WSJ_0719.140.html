GE Capital Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- GE Capital, the financial services arm of General Electric Co., said it acquired a credit card operation from Burton Group of London for $329 million. The acquisition marks GE Capital's first venture into the European credit card business. It already manages 37 million credit card accounts for more than 200 retailers in the U.S. and Canada.

GE Capital is acquiring Burton Group Financial Services PLC, which manages three million accounts for Burton Group retail stores as well as private-label credit cards.
