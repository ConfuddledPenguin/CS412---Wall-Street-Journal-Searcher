Business Brief -- Modine Manufacturing Co.: Agreement to Acquire Unit Of Sundstrand Is Signed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

Modine Manufacturing Co. said it signed an agreement in principle to purchase the heat transfer operations of Rockford, Ill.-based Sundstrand Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. The Racine, Wis., company said the sale is subject to the approval of a definitive agreement, approval of the directors of the companies, and to other customary conditions.

Earl E. Richter, chairman and chief executive officer, said the acquisition will "increase annual sales by 15% to 20%." Modine earned $25.2 million, or $1.68 a share, on sales of $436.3 million in the fiscal year ended March 31, 1990.

The maker of automotive parts and heating and air-conditioning equipment said that through the acquisition it will enter into markets it doesn't now serve.
