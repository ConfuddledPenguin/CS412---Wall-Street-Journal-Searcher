Morgan Stanley Group ESOP

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

NEW YORK -- Morgan Stanley Group Inc. directors approved the establishment of a leveraged employee stock ownership plan valued at $140 million. The investment banking firm said it will fund the ESOP through a loan to an independently managed trust, which will purchase a new issue of convertible preferred stock from the company. The ESOP is being established to broaden internal ownership in the firm and to provide benefits to its employees in a cost-effective manner, the company said.
