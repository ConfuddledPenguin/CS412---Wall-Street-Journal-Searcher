Cray Gets Computer Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Cray Research Inc. said Kia Motor Corp. of Korea ordered a Cray Y-MP4/116 supercomputer. Terms of the sale weren't disclosed. Cray said Kia, an automobile manufacturer, will use the system for automotive design. The supercomputer system is to be installed in Seoul in the fourth quarter.
