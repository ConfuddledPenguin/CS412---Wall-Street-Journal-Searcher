Convenient Food Mart Settles

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

ROSEMONT, Ill. -- Convenient Food Mart Inc. said it has settled lawsuits out of court with four of its regional franchisers: Convenient Industries of America Inc. in Louisville, Ky., CFM Enterprises of Albany, N.Y., C.F. Marts of California, and Ohio Valley CFM. The settlement involves present and deferred payments to CFMI and allows the four regional franchisers to rename their stores and have no other payment obligations to the national franchiser, CFMI said. CFMI, which through its subsidiaries and independent regional franchisers has about 500 stores operating under the Convenient Food Mart name, is currently in Chapter 11 reorganization.
