Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

WHO'S NEWS: Aaron Asher was named a vice president of News Corp.'s HarperCollins Publishers; he will publish about 20 fiction and nonfiction books a year under his own imprint, Aaron Asher Books. He was executive editor of Harper & Row before it was merged with Collins Publishers. Most recently, he was publisher of Grove Weidenfeld. . . . Also, HarperCollins Publishers named Eddie Bell to the new post of publisher and deputy chief executive of U.K. Publishing as of Sept. 1. He will be succeeded as senior vice president and publisher of Harper Paperbacks by Ed Breslin, vice president of Harper Paperbacks. . . . John Berentson was named vice chairman and chief operating officer of United Press International. He succeeds Joseph K. Taussig, who UPI said is leaving to pursue other interests. Mr. Berentson continues as senior executive vice president and chief operating officer of UPI's parent, Infotechnology Inc.
