WELLS FARGO & Co.'s second-quarter return on assets was 1.29%. The return was incorrectly stated in yesterday's edition. (See: "Regional Banks Report Profits Rose to Records --- But Second-Period Earnings Fell at Money Centers as Loan Woes Persisted" -- WSJ July 18, 1990)

THE SENATE BANKING COMMITTEE approved a bill that would renew the Export Administration Act for two years. Yesterday's edition incorrectly identified the committee.

SUZUKI MOTOR Co. will introduce this fall a budget-priced, four-door version of its Sidekick model with a base price of $11,999. The base price was misstated in yesterday's edition.

(See: "Autos: Rivalry Accelerates for Sports Trucks, Dubbed the `Family Sedans' of the '90s" -- WSJ July 18, 1990)
