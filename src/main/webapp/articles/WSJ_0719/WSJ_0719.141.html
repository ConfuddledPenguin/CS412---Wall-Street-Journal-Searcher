Canadian Bankruptcies Jump

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian bankruptcies rose to 4,109 in June, up 32.3% from 3,105 a year earlier, the Canadian Consumer and Corporate Affairs Department said. Individual bankruptcies totaled 3,286 and business bankruptcies totaled 823 in June.

In the first half, bankruptcies rose 30.4% to 23,651 from 18,138 a year earlier.
