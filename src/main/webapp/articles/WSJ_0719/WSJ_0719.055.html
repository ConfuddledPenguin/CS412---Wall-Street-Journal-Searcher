Who's News: Pacific Gas & Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

PACIFIC GAS & ELECTRIC Co. (San Francisco) -- Benjamin F. Montoya, manager of the company's Sacramento division, was elected vice president, Sacramento Valley Region. He succeeds John Keyser, who resumes his former position as vice president, marketing and customer services, at the company's headquarters. Pacific Gas is engaged mainly in supplying electric and natural gas service in northern and central California.
