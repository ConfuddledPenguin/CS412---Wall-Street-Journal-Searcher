Chicago Mercantile Seat

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange was sold for $465,000, down $30,000 from the previous sale July 5. Seats currently are quoted at $440,000 bid, $490,000 asked.

The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set March 9, 1989.
