International Brief -- Daimler-Benz AG: Workers at Mercedes Unit Agree to Overtime Shifts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

Mercedes-Benz AG said the company's workers' council agreed to special Saturday overtime shifts to help the company cope with rising demand. The auto-making arm of Daimler-Benz AG said that special shifts will be added on "several Saturdays" beginning September. The overtime shifts will affect the company's plants in Stuttgart-Untertuerkheim, Sindelfingen and Bremen.

Mercedes-Benz had tried to introduce the overtime shifts at the start of the year, but the company couldn't reach an agreement with the workers' council

In a statement, Mercedes-Benz said the extra shifts will be added "so that the company can react flexibly to the heightened demand." It said that "clear" growth rates in Europe and in other foreign markets for Mercedes-Benz cars in general, along with lively demand for certain models and for equipment, had prompted the need for extra shifts.
