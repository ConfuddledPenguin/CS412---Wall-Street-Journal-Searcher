Who's News: Bank of Bermuda Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

BANK OF BERMUDA Ltd. (Hamilton, Bermuda) -- Donald J. Danilek was named senior vice president, a new post, at this bank's New York branch. Mr. Danilek, 53 years old, will be responsible for the private banking and personal trust division. He was previously managing director of Bank of Lichtenstein Trust Co.
