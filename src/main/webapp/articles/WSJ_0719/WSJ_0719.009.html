France's Industrial Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

PARIS -- France's industrial production index rose a seasonally adjusted 0.3% in May from April and 1.5% from a year earlier, according to preliminary figures of the National Statistics Institute. The 0.3% rise represents a slowdown from April's exceptionally high growth rate of 1.4%.

A breakdown showed that the May advance was spread over most sectors of the economy except energy.

Automobiles and other consumer durable goods led the way, with a rise of 4.8% from April and 1.6% from a year earlier.

The May index stood at 112.5, up from April's revised 112.2 and the year-earlier level of 110.8. The index covers output in all sectors of the economy except building and is based on 1985 equaling 100.
