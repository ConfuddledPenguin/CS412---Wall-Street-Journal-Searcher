Bell Atlantic Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Bell Atlantic Corp. said it completed its all-stock purchase of Simborg Systems Corp., but didn't disclose the terms. The purchase represents Bell Atlantic's first subsidiary in the hospital information industry. The new venture was renamed Bell Atlantic Healthcare Systems Inc.

Closely held Simborg Systems, based in Greenbrae, Calif., develops hospital information management systems.

Bell Atlantic is a telecommunications company.
