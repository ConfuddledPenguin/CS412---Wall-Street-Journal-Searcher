Who's News: Century Telephone Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

CENTURY TELEPHONE ENTERPRISES Inc. (Monroe, La.,) -- Clarke M. Williams, chairman and company founder, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer of this telecommunications concern, effective immediately. The 68-year-old Mr. Williams succeeds his son, Clarke M. Williams Jr., who was also vice chairman and president. Mr. Williams Jr., 40, took a disability retirement because of the lingering effects of a stroke he suffered last September. Glen F. Post, chief operating officer and formerly vice president, was given the additional title of president. Harvey P. Perry, senior vice president and general counsel and secretary, was appointed to fill the board seat vacated by Mr. Williams Jr.
