Business Brief -- Syntex Corp.: Company Seeks to Overturn Verdict in Employee's Suit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

Syntex Corp., Palo Alto, Calif., said it will ask a trial judge to overturn a jury's verdict against the company in a lawsuit. The suit involved charges that the drug manufacturer terminated an employee because he objected to alleged efforts to conceal evidence of harmful side effects from an experimental drug. A jury sitting in Superior Court in San Jose, Calif., last week held that Syntex wrongfully terminated one of its researchers, awarding the former employee $2.5 million in compensatory damages and $15 million in punitive damages.

Syntex said it disputes the charges and "strongly disagrees" with the jury's verdict. It said it plans to ask the trial court judge to render a judgment in its favor.
