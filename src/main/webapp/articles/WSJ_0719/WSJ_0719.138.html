Dividend News: Standard Pacific L.P.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

STANDARD PACIFIC L.P. declared a quarterly distribution of 35 cents a unit, down 22% from 45 cents previously, payable Aug. 29 to units of record Aug. 15. The Costa Mesa, Calif., home builder said its general distribution policy is to pay about 60% of per-unit earnings in the form of distributions. Standard Pacific said it will review its distribution rate each quarter. The partnership reported net income of $16.9 million, or 62 cents a unit.
