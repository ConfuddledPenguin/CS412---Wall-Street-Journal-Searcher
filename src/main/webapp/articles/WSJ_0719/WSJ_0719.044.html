Treasury Sets the Sale Of Two-Year Notes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise about $2 billion in new cash with the sale Wednesday of a record $11.5 billion in two-year notes to redeem $9.49 billion in maturing notes. The notes will be dated July 31 and will mature July 31, 1992.

Tenders for the notes, available in minimum $5,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Wednesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
