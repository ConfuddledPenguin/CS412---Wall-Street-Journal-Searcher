Business Brief -- AmeriTrust Corp.: Banking Concern Reports Net Fell 39% in 2nd Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

AmeriTrust Corp., reeling from bad loans, said second-quarter net plunged 39%, and the Cleveland-based bank holding company indicated that the problems could increase further during the third quarter. AmeriTrust attributed its second-quarter decline to higher provisions for loan losses and an increase in other expenses. The bank earned $18.4 million, or 45 cents a share, compared with year-earlier results of $30.3 million, or 83 cents a share.

AmeriTrust also announced a plan to exercise greater care in making loans. The move is a response to bank regulators' assessment of the company and to concerns of management and board members.
