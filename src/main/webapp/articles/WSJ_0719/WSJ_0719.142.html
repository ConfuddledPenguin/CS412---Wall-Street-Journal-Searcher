Canam Manac Inc. Group Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

MONTREAL -- A group led by Canam Manac Inc. said almost all of the approximately 27% of Noverco Inc.'s shares it didn't already own were tendered under the group's offer of 15.25 Canadian dollars (US$13.22) a share. The offer by Canam Manac and two Quebec government agencies was valued at about C$220 million. The offer expired Monday. The group said it would exercise its right to acquire the remaining Noverco shares.

Noverco is a Montreal energy concern that controls Quebec's dominant natural gas distributor. Canam Manac is a St. Georges, Quebec, steel products concern.
