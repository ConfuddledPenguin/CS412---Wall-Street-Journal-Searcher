Who's News: Pacific Stock Exchange Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/19/90

PACIFIC STOCK EXCHANGE Inc. (San Francisco) -- John C. Katovich, 36 years old, previously vice president and corporate secretary at this regional stock exchange, was named senior vice president, director of legal affairs, a new post, and remains general counsel. Carin Townsend, 31, executive assistant to the chairman, was named to the additional, newly created post of vice president, administrative officer.
