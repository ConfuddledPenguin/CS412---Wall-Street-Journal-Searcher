Ethyl Corp. Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/19/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- Ethyl Corp. said it completed the purchase of a Quantum Chemical Corp. plant in Houston for about $48 million. The plant makes poly alpha olefins, which are used in motor oils as well as in other applications. Quantum, based in New York, will operate the plant for Ethyl. Both companies are chemicals concerns.
