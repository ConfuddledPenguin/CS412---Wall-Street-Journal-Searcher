Bally Extends Exchange Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

CHICAGO -- Bally Manufacturing Corp. said it is extending its offer to exchange common shares for its 13 5/8% subordinated debentures due Jan. 2, 2001, until 5 p.m. EDT July 11. The offer by the gambling and health-club concern originally was slated to expire yesterday, at which time holders had tendered only about $20 million of the four-year-old $135 million issue of junk bonds.

Bally is trying to retire its junk bond issue at a discount to the original price. Each holder of debentures accepting the offer would receive 57 shares of Bally common stock for each $1,000 principal amount of debenture.
