Merrill Lynch Affiliate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

NEW YORK -- A Merrill Lynch & Co. affiliate closed the acquisition of Londontown Holdings Corp., maker of London Fog apparel and other rainwear, from General Electric Co.'s General Electric Capital Corp. unit. Merrill Lynch Capital Partners paid about $100 million for the equity in Londontown and assumed $180 million of debt.

Merrill Lynch is the securities and investment banking firm here. GE, Fairfield, Conn., has interests in aerospace, financial services and broadcasting.
