Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- Richard H. deMoll, 40 years old, was named executive vice president of the business credit services division of this business information company. Mr. deMoll, previously senior vice president, finance, at D&B's business information group, will be responsible for Receivable Recovery Systems, a commercial collections services unit, and American Credit Indemnity, a credit-insurance unit.
