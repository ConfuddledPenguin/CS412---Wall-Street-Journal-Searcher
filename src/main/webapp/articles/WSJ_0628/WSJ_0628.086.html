Michaels Posts Higher Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

IRVING, Texas -- Michaels Stores Inc. said its June sales for the period ended Sunday rose 23.8% to $21 million from $17 million. Sales of the specialty crafts stores open for more than a year rose 8.6% to $18.2 million from $16.8 million, Michaels said.
