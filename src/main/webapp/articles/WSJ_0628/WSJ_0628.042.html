Dividend News: Neiman Marcus Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

NEIMAN MARCUS GROUP INC. said its board approved a plan allowing shareholders to reinvest their dividends to purchase more stock. General Cinema Corp., which has a controlling interest in Neiman Marcus and annually receives $22.5 million in preferred dividends and $3.3 million in common dividends, said it will reinvest its dividends in the retailer. General Cinema, based in Chestnut Hill, Mass., has interests in specialty retailing and also owns movie theaters. General Cinema currently owns 50% of Neiman Marcus's common stock and all of its preferred stock, which is convertible to an additional 9.9% of common stock. Neiman Marcus will use the cash from the dividend reinvestment plan to shore up its capital position, renovate stores and clear debt.
