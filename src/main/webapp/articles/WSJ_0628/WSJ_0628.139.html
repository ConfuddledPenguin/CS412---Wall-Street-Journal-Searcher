International Brief -- Groupe Bruxelles Lambert: Societe Generale to Buy Part of Wagons-Lits Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

Groupe Bruxelles Lambert S.A. said it agreed to sell a 19.5% stake in Cie. Internationale des Wagons-Lits & du Tourisme S.A. to Societe Generale de Belgique S.A. GBL declined to specify the exact price but said it was appreciably above Tuesday's closing price of 9,450 Belgian francs ($277) for shares in Wagons-Lits, a Franco-Belgian travel company. At Tuesday's market price, the stake's value would total about 10 billion francs ($293 million).

The sale increases the chances that the French hotel giant Accor S.A. will gain control of Wagons-Lits' chain of about 160 hotels, analysts said. Accor, in which Generale holds a 12.6% stake, has long expressed an interest in those hotels.

Generale won't have a free hand at Wagons-Lits. France's state-owned Caisse des Depots & Consignations is the largest shareholder in Wagons-Lits, with about 28%. Sodexho, a French catering company, owns 18.6%, and Rolaco Holding S.A., an Arab-owned investment firm, has 5.8%.

But Generale also acquired from GBL an option to acquire a further 7.25% stake between now and July 1, 1991.
