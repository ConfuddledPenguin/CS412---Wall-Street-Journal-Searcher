Business Brief -- BEI Electronics Inc.: U.S. Investigates a Unit's Data on Federal Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

BEI Electronics Inc., San Francisco, said a subsidiary is under investigation by the Justice Department for allegedly submitting inaccurate cost or pricing data on two government contracts. The company, which makes electronics systems used in military, commercial, industrial and aerospace applications, also said it is conducting its own internal investigation of the unit, BEI Defense Systems of Fort Worth, Texas, which has stopped making the product in question.

BEI Electronics said that it believes the allegations are without merit, but that it intends to cooperate fully with the government's investigation.
