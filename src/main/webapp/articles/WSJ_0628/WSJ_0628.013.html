Who's News: Burlington Resources Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

BURLINGTON RESOURCES Inc. -- El Paso Natural Gas Co., El Paso, Texas, the interstate natural gas transmission unit of Burlington Resources, named Joel Richards named vice president, human resources. Mr. Richards was formerly vice president, finance and human resources, of Burlington Resources' Meridian Minerals Co. unit. Burlington Resources, Seattle, is an energy and natural resources concern.
