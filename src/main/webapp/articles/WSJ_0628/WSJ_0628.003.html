France's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

PARIS -- France's unemployment rate in May was a seasonally adjusted 9.3%, unchanged from April, the Labor Ministry said. The number of job-seekers in May declined 1,100 from April to 2,480,200. In the 12 months through May, the number of job seekers declined 49,400, or 2%.

On an unadjusted basis, the number of job seekers in May declined 2.6% from April to 2,366,600, and fell 1.9% from May 1989.
