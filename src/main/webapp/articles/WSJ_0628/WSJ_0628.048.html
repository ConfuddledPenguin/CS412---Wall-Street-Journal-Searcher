Ashland Oil Unit's Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

ASHLAND, Ky. -- Ashland Oil Inc. said its exploration unit has completed the $107 million purchase of natural gas properties from a unit of Occidental Petroleum Corp., New York. Ashland said the acquisition includes about 550,000 acres of natural gas producing and undeveloped properties in West Virginia and Eastern Kentucky. Nearly 700 producing wells on the properties average about 18 million cubic feet of natural gas per day, the company said, adding that total proved reserves could total 142 billion cubic feet.

The acquisition will more than double Ashland's existing Appalachian acreage and increase the company's total natural gas production by over 30%, the company said.
