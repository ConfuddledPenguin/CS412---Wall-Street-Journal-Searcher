Business Brief -- Carl Karcher Enterprises Inc.: Arizona Lines' Franchising, Disposal Effort Completed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

Carl Karcher Enterprises Inc., Anaheim, Calif., said Frank J. Karcher bought the franchise rights for the company's fast-food restaurants in Tucson, Ariz., as the last step in the company's effort to franchise or dispose of its Arizona operations. Terms weren't disclosed. The agreement calls for Frank Karcher, brother of the company founder Carl Karcher, to own 12 Carl's Jr. restaurants in Tucson, as well as rights to develop the chain in the area. Until recently, Frank Karcher was vice president, franchise operations, at Carl Karcher Enterprises. A permanent successor hasn't been named.

Previously, the company took a $14.5 million charge against fourth-quarter results for the planned sale or franchising of its Arizona restaurants, which posted losses of about $5 million last year. The company owns, operates, franchises or licenses more than 550 Carl's Jr. restaurants in California, Arizona, Nevada, Oregon and Japan.
