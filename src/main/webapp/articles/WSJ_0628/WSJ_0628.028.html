BHA Hires Adviser on Capital

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- BHA Group Inc., a maker and supplier of parts for air-pollution control equipment, said it hired First Analysis Securities Corp., a Chicago-based investment banking firm, to find ways to raise capital to finance its continued growth. The company said it will consider alternatives such as a joint venture, a private investment by a strategic partner, or a public offering of its common stock. BHA said the actions will help it take advantage of international opportunities, including those in Eastern Europe, and provide a financing source for certain projects.
