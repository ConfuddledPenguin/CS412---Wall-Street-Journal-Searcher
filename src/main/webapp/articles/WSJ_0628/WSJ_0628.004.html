Who's News: Tucson Electric Power Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

TUCSON ELECTRIC POWER Co. (Tucson, Ariz.) -- Charles E. Bayless, senior vice president and chief financial officer, was elected a director of this utility, filling a vacancy created by the resignation of Peter D. Herder. Earlier this week the board elected Mr. Bayless president and chief executive officer, effective July 1.
