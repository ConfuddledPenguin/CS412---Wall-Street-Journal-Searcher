International Brief -- Mecca Leisure Group PLC: Firm, in Reversal, Accepts Rank Organisation's Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

Mecca Leisure Group PLC, in an about-face from its initial protest, said yesterday it will accept a #512 million ($890.6 million) bid for the company by the Rank Organisation PLC. Rank is a British communications and entertainment group.

Mecca's board originally argued that the bid undervalued the British bingo parlor, restaurants and resorts group.

However, in its statement yesterday, the company said it recognized it would be unable to reduce its debt significantly in the near term enough to make its recent heavy investments pay off.

"Mecca would benefit from being part of a larger group," the company's statement said.

It also noted that its acceptance of the Rank offer stemmed in part from the temporary disability of its chairman and chief executive, Michael Guthrie. Mr. Guthrie, the architect of Mecca's acquisition-led expansion, is recovering from recent heart surgery.
