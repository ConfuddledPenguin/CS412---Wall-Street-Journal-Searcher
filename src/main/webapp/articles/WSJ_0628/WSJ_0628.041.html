Contract to Evans & Sutherland

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

SALT LAKE CITY -- Evans & Sutherland Computer Corp. said it was selected to design and build visual systems for the Air Force Tanker/Transport Training System. The contract, valued at more than $20 million, covers 11 visual systems for flight-training simulators. Evans & Sutherland makes interactive computer systems for engineering and scientific applications.
