Who's News: Pfizer Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

PFIZER Inc. (New York) -- Robert Neimeth, 55 years old, was named president of this health care and pharmaceuticals company's Pfizer International subsidiaries. Mr. Neimeth, previously executive vice president of Pfizer International, succeeds Barry MacTaggart, 58, Pfizer International's current chairman and president, who plans to retire early in 1991.
