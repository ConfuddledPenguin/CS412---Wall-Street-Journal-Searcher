McDonnell Douglas Jet Orders

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

LONG BEACH, Calif. -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. said Atlanta-based Delta Air Lines converted three options on MD-88 twin-engine jets to firm orders, bringing the carrier's order backlog to 30 of the planes from the St. Louis-based aircraft maker. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed, but industry specialists said the average MD-80 series aircraft sells for about $30 million. Delta, the largest operator of MD-88s, has taken delivery of 62 of the planes to date.
