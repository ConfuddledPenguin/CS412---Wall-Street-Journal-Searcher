Who's News: World Container Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

WORLD CONTAINER Corp. (Burnsville, Minn.) -- Jerome E. Laitala, 51-year-old chairman of this maker of reusable plastic shipping containers, was named to the additional posts of president and chief executive officer, filling vacancies. Floyd A. Hart, 50, formerly vice president, administration, and principal financial officer, was named executive vice president and chief operating officer, new posts.
