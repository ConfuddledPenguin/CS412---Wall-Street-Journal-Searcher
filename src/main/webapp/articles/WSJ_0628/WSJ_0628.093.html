Collins Industries Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

HUTCHINSON, Kan. -- Collins Industries Inc. said it received a one-year contract to provide ambulances valued at about $12 million to the U.S. General Services Administration. Collins, a maker of specialty vehicles, said the contract gives the GSA the option of placing two additional one-year orders. The company said that if the agency exercises those options, Collins would provide a total of about 1,200 ambulances with a value of about $60 million.
