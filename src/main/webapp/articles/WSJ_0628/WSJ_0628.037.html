Timken to Build Virginia Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

CANTON, Ohio -- Timken Co. said it will build a $23 million plant in Altavista, Va., to make tapered roller bearings, as part of the previously announced $1 billion capital investment program to strengthen the company's position in the global bearing business. Timken also makes seamless mechanical alloy tubing. The plant, which will make bearings for wheels and drive trains on trucks and cars, will initially have 50 employees but could ultimately have 200.
