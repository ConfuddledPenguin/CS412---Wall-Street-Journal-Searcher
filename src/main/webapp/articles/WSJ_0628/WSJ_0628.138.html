Who's News: Kleinworth Benson Group PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

KLEINWORT BENSON GROUP PLC (London) -- Keith M. Jecks, 33 years old, was named to the new post of president of Kleinwort Benson International Investment Ltd., the U.S.-registered investment subsidiary of this merchant bank. Mr. Jecks previously was a member of the executive board of Kleinwort Benson Investment Management Ltd., responsible for developing the firm's institutional business worldwide. Mr. Jecks, who will be based in New York, will oversee the development of Kleinwort Benson International's services to clients in the U.S. and Canada. Kleinwort Benson International has $434 million in assets under management.
