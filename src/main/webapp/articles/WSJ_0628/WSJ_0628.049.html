Mr. Coffee Files for Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

CLEVELAND -- Mr. Coffee Inc., which produces coffee makers and related equipment, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of 762,577 common shares by certain holders. Of the total, 671,140 shares will be issuable upon the exercise of various warrants by those holders selling the securities. The company said it was obligated to file the registration statement after its initial public offering of common stock on May 31.
