Technology: Summit Technology Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

WATERTOWN, Mass. -- Summit Technology Inc. said the first patients have been treated with its excimer laser to remove coronary artery blockage while undergoing coronary artery bypass grafts, at the University of Rochester, N.Y. Summit said the arteries were successfully unblocked. The technology of removing artery blockage with excimer lasers has been tested using other lasers at several different U.S. medical centers.

Summit, a maker of medical excimer laser systems, said clinical trials in angioplasty are another medical application for its lasers. Summit lasers are also used for the treatment of vision disorders.
