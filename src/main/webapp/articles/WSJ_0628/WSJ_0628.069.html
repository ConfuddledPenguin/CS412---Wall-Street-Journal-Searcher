Financing Business: MDS Health Group Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

MDS HEALTH GROUP Ltd., Toronto, said it plans to offer as many as 4.3 million Class B non-voting shares for private placement at 13.75 Canadian dollars ($11.74) a share. The diagnostic-testing concern said proceeds would be used to repay C$30 million of floating-rate debentures and for working capital. It said a group headed by Wood Gundy Inc. will be agents for the offer. MDS has about 9.2 million Class B nonvoting shares and 8 million Class A voting shares outstanding. In trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday, the Class B shares closed at C$13.75, up 25 Canadian cents. The Class A shares closed at C$13.50, up 37.5 Canadian cents.
