Business Brief -- Newmark & Lewis Inc.: Retailer Expects a Profit For Year Ending Jan. 31

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

Newmark & Lewis Inc. expects a profit on a 27% sales rise for its year ending Jan. 31, said Warren G. Hyman, co-chairman and co-chief executive. In the previous year, the discount retailer of electronics and appliances had net income of $117,000, or two cents a share, including the effect of an accounting change. Sales totaled $245.5 million.

Mr. Hyman declined to estimate profit. But he said he expected sales of between $310 million and $315 million. He said the current year will include results for nine months of the retailing assets of Brick Church Appliance Inc., acquired in May.

The Hicksville, N.Y.-based company also plans to try to reduce its sales and general and administrative expenses, which totaled $66.9 million last year, in part by controlling expenses more tightly and by achieving some efficiencies of scale with the acquisition of Brick Church.
