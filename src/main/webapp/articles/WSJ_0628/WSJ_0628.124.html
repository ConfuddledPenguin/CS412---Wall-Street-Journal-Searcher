Business Brief -- Homestead Holding Corp.: CCNB to Acquire Company For $17.8 Million in Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

Homestead Holding Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to be acquired by CCNB Corp., a New Cumberland, Pa., bank and thrift holding company, in a stock transaction valued at about $17.8 million. Terms of the proposed transaction call for holders of Homestead common to receive CCNB stock valued at about $15.48 per Homestead share. Homestead, a savings bank holding company based in Middletown, Pa., has about 1.2 million common shares outstanding.

Gary L. Holman, president of Homestead, said the agreement is subject to a number of conditions, including approval by regulators and Homestead shareholders.

Homestead has assets of about $259 million and equity capital of about $18 million, Mr. Holman said. CCNB has assets of about $1.2 billion, he said.
