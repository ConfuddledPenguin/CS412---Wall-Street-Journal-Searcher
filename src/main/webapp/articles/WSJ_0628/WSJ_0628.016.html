Ford to Modernize Glass Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

DEARBORN, Mich. -- Ford Motor Co. said it plans to invest $95 million to modernize its Dearborn Glass Plant. A main element of the plan is replacement of two of the automotive windshield making operations with highly automated in-line processes, Ford said. Plans call for first line to be fully operational in the fall of 1991 and the second line about a year later. The company also plans a major rebuilding of the plant's Float Furnace facility, beginning in October 1991, and a 77,000-square-foot building expansion.
