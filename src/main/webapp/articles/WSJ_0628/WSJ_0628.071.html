Technology: Motorola, Hitachi Dispute

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

SCHAUMBURG, Ill. -- Motorola Inc. said a federal appeals court in Washington, D.C., issued a stay pending appeal of a lower court's order enjoining sale of certain microchips. The chips at stake are microprocessors or computers-on-a-chip, made by Motorola and its rival Hitachi Ltd. of Japan, which have been embroiled in an 18-month patent infringement dispute.

The two companies earlier this week said they had agreed on a framework for negotiating an out-of-court settlement. Motorola wouldn't comment on the talks, but Hitachi has said it is hopeful an agreement can be reached in two or three months.
