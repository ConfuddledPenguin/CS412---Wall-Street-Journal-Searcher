Business Brief -- American Healthcare Management: Dallas Company Will Move Base to Philadelphia Area

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

American Healthcare Management Inc. said it will move its corporate headquarters from Dallas to a western suburb of Philadelphia by Oct. 1. Steven Volla, president of the operator of hospitals, said the move will allow the company to take on a new management team in the wake of its emergence late last year from reorganization under federal bankruptcy laws.

Mr. Volla said about 15 members of American Healthcare's current management team will relocate to the Philadelphia area, and about 35 people working in the Dallas office will be laid off.
