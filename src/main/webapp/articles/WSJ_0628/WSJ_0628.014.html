Telesphere Communications

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

TERRACE, Ill. -- Telesphere Communications Inc. said negotiations are on schedule to arrange financing of its definitive pact to acquire National Telephone Services Inc. of Rockville, Md., for $21 million in cash, $14 million notes and three million Telesphere common shares. Telesphere said proposal letters have been signed with two major financial institutions. The company didn't identify the lenders. The planned closing date for the acquisition remains August.
