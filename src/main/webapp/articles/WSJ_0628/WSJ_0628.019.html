Bird Plans a Private Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

WESTWOOD, Mass. -- Bird Corp., a new holding company for Bird Inc., a building materials distributor, said it plans to raise capital with a private offering of a 40% interest in its housing group unit. The offering is part of a five-year growth plan for Bird to fuel the company's core building materials business and expand into the environmental business.
