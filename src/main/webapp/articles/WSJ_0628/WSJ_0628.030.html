Credit Ratings: Bank of New York's Ratings From Moody's Are Under Review

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

NEW YORK -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it placed the long-term debt and commercial paper ratings of Bank of New York Co. under review for a possible downgrade. Moody's cited the bank's exposure to highly leveraged borrowers and troubled real estate, as well as its proposed takeover of Northeast Bancorp in Stamford, Conn., as potential problems. Moody's and other rating firms already have reduced their opinion of other money center banks' debt for many of the same reasons.

A spokesman for the bank said that while disappointed by Moody's announcement, "we believe our basic businesses are well diversified and our earnings power remains sound."
