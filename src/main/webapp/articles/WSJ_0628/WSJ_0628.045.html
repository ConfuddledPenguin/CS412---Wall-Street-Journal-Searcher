MCorp Agrees to Sell One Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

DALLAS -- MCorp said it agreed to sell its interest in its Brownsville, Texas, bank for about $17 million to a private investor group led by the bank's management.

MCorp, which had all but five of its banks seized by federal regulators last year, has been attempting to liquidate its assets to pay creditors. MCorp said the final price of the Brownsville bank, which has $445 million in assets, may vary depending on the bank's net worth at the time of sale.

MCorp said it expects to complete the sale, subject to approval by a federal bankruptcy-court judge and by regulators, by Dec. 31.
