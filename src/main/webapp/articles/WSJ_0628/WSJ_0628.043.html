BMA Holders Clear Unit's Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- BMA Corp. said its shareholders at the annual meeting approved the sale of its insurance business to Italy's Generali-Assicurazioni Generali S.p.A. The pact calls for Generali to pay $157 million in cash for BMA's Business Men's Assurance Co. of America subsidiary. The deal also calls for the unit to transfer assets to BMA that have a book value of $128 million, BMA said. The transaction is expected to close in late July.

BMA, a holding company, also said it expects to pay the previously announced special dividend of $3.70 a share in early September.
