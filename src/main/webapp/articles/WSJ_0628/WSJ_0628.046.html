Dividend News: King World Productions Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

KING WORLD PRODUCTIONS Inc. directors declared a 3-for-2 stock split, payable July 23 to stock of record July 9. After the split, the New York-based television program producer and distributor will have slightly more than 38 million shares outstanding. The split will boost shares outstanding and lower the price. "We think {the stock is} an attractive security for individual investors," a spokeswoman said. King World's net income for the six months ended Feb. 28 grew 7.5% to $51.4 million, or $1.98 a share, as revenue climbed 17% to $270.7 million. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, King World shares rose 62.5 cents to $36.50.
