Who's News: TLC Beatrice Names Simeus to Two Posts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

NEW YORK -- TLC Beatrice International Holdings Inc. said it appointed 50-year-old Dumas M. Simeus president and chief operating officer. Mr. Simeus succeeds William Mowry, who left the company a couple of months ago, a spokesman said.

TLC Beatrice consists of Beatrice Cos.' former international food operations. Those operations were taken private in December 1987 by the investor, Reginald F. Lewis, 47 years old, who is TLC Beatrice's chairman, chief executive officer and controlling stockholder.

TLC Beatrice brought back Mr. Simeus from the company's former Latin American operations, where Mr. Simeus had been running the lines when the company sold them for more than $100 million in June 1988.
