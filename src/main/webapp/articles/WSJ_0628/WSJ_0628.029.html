Rochester Community Savings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Rochester Community Savings Bank said it expects earnings for its second half ending Nov. 30 to exceed fiscal first-half profit of $9 million, or 65 cents a share, noting that it expects "significant long-term earnings growth" from the April purchase of a 19-branch mutual thrift in central New Jersey. The savings bank also said it is benefiting from continued growth in bank fees and service charges, stable mortgage banking income, and the issuance of $125 million of auto loan-backed securities.
