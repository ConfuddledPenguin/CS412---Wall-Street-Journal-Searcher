Who's News: Warner-Lambert Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

WARNER-LAMBERT Co. (Morris Plains, N.J.) -- P. Vincent Lovio was appointed vice president, government affairs, legislative counsel of this health care and consumer products company. He will fill the vacancy left by W. Bradford Gary, who left the company for personal reasons. Mr. Lovio was formerly an attorney with the Washington law firm of Akin, Gump, Strauss, Hauer & Feld.
