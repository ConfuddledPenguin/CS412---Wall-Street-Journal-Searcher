Who's News: Berisford International PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

BERISFORD INTERNATIONAL PLC (London) -- Harry P. Wiltshire has resigned from the board of this U.K. commodities, financial services and property concern. Mr. Wiltshire remains a director of Berisford's J.H. Rayner (Mincing Lane) Ltd. subsidiary.
