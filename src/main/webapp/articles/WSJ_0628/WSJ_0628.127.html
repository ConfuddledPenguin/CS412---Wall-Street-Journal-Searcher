Business Brief -- Mellon Bank Corp.: Consolidation of Charters Sought for Banking Units

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

Mellon Bank Corp., Pittsburgh, said it filed applications with the comptroller of the currency to consolidate the charters of its five Pennsylvania banks. The bank holding company said the move would simplify its corporate financial and legal structure, and enable customers to cash checks and do all banking at any of the 353 Mellon Bank branch offices statewide. The company said the move wouldn't affect the management structure, boards or employees of the banks.

Mellon said these banks would be consolidated into a single bank called Mellon Bank N.A.: Commonwealth National Bank, Harrisburg; Mellon Bank (Central), State College; Mellon Bank (East), Philadelphia; Mellon Bank, Pittsburgh; and Mellon Bank (North), Oil City. Mellon also said it plans to change Commonwealth National Bank's name to Mellon Bank. The company expects the consolidation to be completed by early next year.
