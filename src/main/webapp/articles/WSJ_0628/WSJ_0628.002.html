Who's News: Raytheon Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

RAYTHEON Co. (Lexington, Mass.) -- William H. Swanson, 41 years old, assistant general manager of the missile systems division, was named senior vice president and will become the division's general manager, succeeding John P. Shanley, senior vice president, when he retires in August. James W. Carter, 44, manager of the missile systems laboratories, was named vice president.
