Who's News: Northern States Tabs Theisen as President, Head of Operations

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Northern States Power Co. named Edwin Theisen president and chief operating officer, filling positions that had been vacant for a number of years. James Howard, 55 years old, remains chairman and chief executive of the utility holding company.

Mr. Theisen, 59, formerly president and chief executive of the company's NSP Wisconsin subsidiary, was also named a director, increasing board membership to 14.

E.J. McIntyre, 39, formerly a vice president of the company's gas utility, was named to succeed Mr. Theisen as head of the NSP Wisconsin operation.

Northern States also created a new unit, NSP Enterprises, that will encompass the operations of the company's non-regulated activities. Roland Jensen, 60, was named president of the new unit. He had been senior vice president of Northern's States' power supply unit.

In addition, James Doudiet was named executive vice president -- finance, Hazel O'Leary, was named executive vice president -- corporate affairs, and Craig Blair was named executive vice president -- power supply. All three had been senior vice presidents.
