Business Brief -- Air Products & Chemicals: Firm Acquires 49% Interest In Italy's Gas Concern Sapio

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

Air Products & Chemicals Inc., Allentown, Pa., said it acquired a 49% interest in Sapio Industrie, an Italian producer of industrial gases with annual revenue of about $100 million. Terms weren't disclosed.

The acquisition of the Sapio stake follows a string of other European investments made by Air Products since the mid-1980s that total more than $500 million. In April, for example, Air Products increased its stake in Carburos Metalicos, the largest industrial gas supplier in Spain and Portugal, to about 30%. The expansion effort in Europe has effectively doubled the company's asset base there.

The company said four Air Products executives, including two European vice presidents and the general manager of the company's own Milan-based operation, will become members of Sapio's nine-member board.
