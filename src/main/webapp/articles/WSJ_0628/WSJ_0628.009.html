Who's News: U S West Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

U S WEST Inc. (Denver) -- John E. DeFeo, chairman of U S West New Vector Group, a cellular-telephone services provider, was named president of U S West Spectrum Enterprises, the recently formed radio communications division of this regional telecommunications company. The division will own and manage U S West's 81% interest in New Vector, and various radio-communications projects overseas, including ventures in Hungary and Czechoslovakia.
