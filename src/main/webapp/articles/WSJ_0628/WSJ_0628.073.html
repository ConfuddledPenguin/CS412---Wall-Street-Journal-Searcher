Who's News: General Instrument Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

GENERAL INSTRUMENT Corp. (New York) -- Donald P. Aiken was named president of Worldwide Wagering Group, a Maryland-based division of this cable-TV electronics, coaxial cable and satellite systems company, which makes equipment for on- and off-track wagering and lotteries. Mr. Aiken, 46 years old, was previously vice president of the information technology group at Texas Instruments Corp., the Dallas-based electronics company.
