Business Brief -- UAL Corp.: United Air Unit to Expand Service in Several Cities

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/28/90

United Airlines, a unit of Chicago-based UAL Corp., said it will expand its flight schedules in Washington, D.C., Orlando, Fla., Chicago and New Orleans in September. Systemwide, United said, its daily departures will increase 10% to 1,971 from 1,794 in September 1989. United is taking delivery, on average, of one new aircraft each week this year.

At Dulles International Airport in Washington, the carrier will operate 118 departures beginning Sept. 5, an increase of 16 flights from 102 operated last fall. At O'Hare International Airport in Chicago, United's largest hub, the airline will operate 410 daily departures, up 25 from 385 last September. In Orlando, United will double departures to 22. In New Orleans, it will increase daily flights to 15 from 11.
