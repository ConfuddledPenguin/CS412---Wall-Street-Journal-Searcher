Vencor Inc. 2nd-Quarter Net

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/28/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Vencor Inc. said it expects second-quarter net income to be about 10 cents a share. Year-earlier net was two cents a share. Vencor said the increase reflects improved results at two hospitals recently acquired by the company. The company, which specializes in providing long-term hospital care for patients who suffer from catastrophic illness, expects to release results in late July.
