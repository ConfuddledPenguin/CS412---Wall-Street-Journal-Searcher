VENTURE: United Press International and Financial News Network Inc. formed a joint venture to provide business and financial news and data. Infotechnology Inc., which owns all of UPI and 47% of FNN, named Jonathan Fram president of the venture, UPI Financial Information Services. Mr. Fram is president of Infotechnology's Institutional Research Network.

WHO'S NEWS: Jeffrey Korchek was promoted to vice president, business affairs, for MCA Inc.'s Universal Pictures unit. He was a business affairs executive in Universal's legal department. . . .Jack L. Farnsworth became president of the higher education division of Simon & Schuster, a unit of Paramount Communications Co. He succeeds Patrick Donaghy, who remains executive vice president. He was senior vice president of North American operations for Maxwell Communications Corp.'s Maxwell Macmillan International.
