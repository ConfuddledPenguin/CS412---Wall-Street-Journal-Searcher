Litton Gear Picked by Carriers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Litton Industries Inc. said two French airlines, UTA and Air France, have selected its new laser gyro inertial navigation system for their future fleets of Airbus Industrie A-340 aircraft. Financial terms weren't disclosed. The airlines are scheduled to take delivery of the aircraft beginning in October 1992, and each of these jetliners built by the European aircraft consortium will be equipped with three of the navigation systems, known as Flagship, and associated equipment.
