Business Brief -- GF Corp.: Most Furniture Unit Assets Are Sold to Tang Affiliate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

GF Corp., Youngstown, Ohio, said it sold most of the assets of its GF Furniture Systems Inc. unit to C.T. Ten, an affiliate of Tang Industries Inc., a Chicago-based steel concern. The price wasn't disclosed. GF Corp. and GF Furniture filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankrutcy Code last April, after failing in earlier efforts to sell the furniture business. At the time of the filing, GF said it had agreed in principle to sell most of its assets to a Tang affiliate.

The new company will operate under the name GF Office Furniture Ltd. It operates plants in Gallatin, Tenn., and Chicago. General management, sales and marketing operations are in Youngstown, where GF Furniture recently closed its original manufacturing plant.
