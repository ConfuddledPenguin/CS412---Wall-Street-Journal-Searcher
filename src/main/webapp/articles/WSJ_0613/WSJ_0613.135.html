International Brief -- Dai Nippon Printing Co.: Consolidated Pretax Profit For Fiscal Year Rose 12%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

Dai Nippon Printing Co., Japan's largest printing company, posted a 12% rise in consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 to 93.8 billion yen ($606.6 million) from 83.52 billion yen a year earlier. Operating profit rose 8.5% to 84.11 billion yen from 77.52 billion yen. Net income jumped 17% to 40.99 billion yen, or 53.57 yen a share, from 35.13 billion yen, or 47.80 yen a share.

Sales rose 6.3% to 1.075 trillion yen from 1.011 trillion yen. Increased sales, materials and administrative costs for the 37 consolidated subsidiaries were more than covered by the surge in sales. Sales from printing services rose 6.1% to 962.02 billion yen, and other sales rose 8.3% to 112.49 billion yen.
