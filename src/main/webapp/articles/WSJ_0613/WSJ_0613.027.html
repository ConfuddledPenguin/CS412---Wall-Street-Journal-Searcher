Commercial-Paper Rates Rise at Citicorp Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.177% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.1695% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $406 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.10% to 8.19%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate rose to 8.175% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.1047% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $396 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.15% to 8.189%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
