Business Brief -- Digital Equipment Corp.: Talks With Ingres on Stake In the Software Firm Fail

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

Digital Equipment Corp., Maynard, Mass., and Ingres Corp., Alameda, Calif., said they failed to reach an agreement on a previously announced plan for Digital to invest in the software maker. The companies disclosed their discussions in May, though they didn't give details. Digital rarely invests in other companies, but such investments have become increasingly common in the computer industry, as large computer hardware makers seek technology and encourage small companies to perform research and development related to the larger companies' computer hardware. Digital was said to be interested in Ingres's data base technology.

Ingres President Paul E. Newton said the two companies "were unable to agree on a strengthened alliance that was consistent with our respective strategies. We both view this as a missed opportunity." He said the two companies will continue to work together under existing relationships.
