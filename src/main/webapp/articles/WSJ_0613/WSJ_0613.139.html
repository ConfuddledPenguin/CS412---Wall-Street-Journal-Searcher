Digital Equipment Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

MAYNARD, Mass. -- Digital Equipment Corp. said it signed a five-year agreement with the State University of New York system to provide $30 million to $50 million of computers and related products. The computer and data processing equipment concern said the university plans to use the computers for administrative, academic and research purposes on 17 of its 64 campuses.
