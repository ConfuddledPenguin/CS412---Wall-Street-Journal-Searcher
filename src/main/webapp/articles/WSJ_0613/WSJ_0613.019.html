Who's News: Survival Technology Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

SURVIVAL TECHNOLOGY Inc. (Bethesda, Md.) -- Robert Herzstein was elected chairman, succeeding Dr. Stanley J. Sarnoff, the company's late founder, chairman and chief executive officer. Mr. Herzstein, a 59-year-old Washington lawyer, has been a director of the company since 1969. He won't be involved in the company's day-to-day operations, the company said. The company manufactures and distributes medical-emergency equipment.
