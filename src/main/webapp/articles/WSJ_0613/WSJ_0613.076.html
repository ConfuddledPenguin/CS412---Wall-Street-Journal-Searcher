Who's News: First Interstate Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

FIRST INTERSTATE BANCORP (Los Angeles) -- John Uithoven, formerly a senior vice president at this banking concern's Nevada unit, was named senior vice president in charge of credit-risk assessment at the parent company. He succeeds Owen Peters, a vice president, who joins the staff of the parent company's chief credit officer. Mr. Uithoven is 41.
