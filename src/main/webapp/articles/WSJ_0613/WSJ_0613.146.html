Business Brief -- CRI Inc.: REIT That Firm Manages To Seek Partnership Control

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

A real estate investment trust managed by CRI Inc. said it plans to try to acquire control of four real estate limited partnerships sponsored by Integrated Resources Inc. The acquisition plan by CRI Insured Mortgage Association Inc. was disclosed in a registration statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission. CRI Insured Mortgage is a real estate investment trust traded on the New York Stock Exchange that specializes in insured multifamily mortgage investments. CRI Inc., based in Rockville, Md., is a real estate syndicator that manages a portfolio of more than $4 billion.

The preliminary plan to acquire control of Integrated's American Insured Mortgage Investors series of public partnerships, which CRI Insured said hold a total of $642 million of federally insured mortgages, falls outside an auction of the funds that is being overseen by the judge in Integrated's bankruptcy proceedings in New York.
