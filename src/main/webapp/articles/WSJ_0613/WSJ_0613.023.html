Dividend News: HRE Properties

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

HRE PROPERTIES said trustees of the New York-based real estate investment trust, as expected, restored the quarterly dividend to its regular level of 45 cents a share, after a one-time cut to 25 cents in the previous quarter because of costs of HRE's defense against a takeover attempt by Kimco-Orentreich Group. The latest dividend is payable July 20 to stock of record June 29. Separately, HRE reported that net income for its second quarter ended April 30 fell 12% to $1.1 million, or 20 cents a share, from $1.2 million, or 21 cents a share, a year earlier. The results included gains of $162,000 in the latest quarter and $121,000 in the year-earlier period, both from sales of properties. At April 30, the average number of shares outstanding fell to 5.4 million from about six million.
