Seagull Energy to Buy Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

HOUSTON -- Seagull Energy Corp., a natural gas pipeline concern, said it agreed to acquire the Wacker Oil Inc. unit of Costain Group PLC of London for $77 million, adjusted for revenue and less direct operating and capital expenses incurred since the beginning of the year. As of Jan. 1, Seagull said, Wacker had 43.2 million cubic feet of proved natural gas reserves and 2.6 million barrels of proved oil reserves from its interests in 114 producing gas and oil wells.
