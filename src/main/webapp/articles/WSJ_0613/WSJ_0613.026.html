Exxon Natural Gas Discovery

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

NEW YORK -- Exxon Corp. said a natural gas discovery in Latimer County, Okla., about 13 miles southwest of Wilburton tested at rates of about 40 million cubic feet of gas a day through a one-inch choke with a flowing tubing pressure of 1,470 pounds a square inch. The oil and energy company holds a 66% working interest in the well. Chicago-based oil and energy concern Amoco Corp. is the other major partner, with 33%.
