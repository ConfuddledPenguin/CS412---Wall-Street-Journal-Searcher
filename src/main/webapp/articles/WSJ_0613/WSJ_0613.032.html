Who's News: Lone Star Technologies

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

LONE STAR TECHNOLOGIES Inc. (Dallas) -- David E. Blackwell was named to the new position of chief operating officer of the company's Lone Star Steel Co. unit. Mr. Blackwell, 51 years old, previously was president and chief executive officer of Rouge Steel Co., Dearborn, Mich. Mr. Blackwell also was named vice chairman of the steel unit's board, increasing board membership to eight. Lone Star is an oil-field and financial services company.
