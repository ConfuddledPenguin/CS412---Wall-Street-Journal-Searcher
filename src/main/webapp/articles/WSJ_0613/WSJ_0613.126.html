Ethyl to Buy Quantum Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- Ethyl Corp. said it agreed to buy Quantum Chemical Corp.'s poly alpha olefin production plant in Houston for about $48 million. Terms of the agreement call for Quantum to operate the plant for Ethyl. The unit, which has annual capacity of about 30 million gallons, will be Ethyl's first poly alpha olefin plant in the U.S.

Poly alpha olefins are widely used in premium motor oils and lubricants for gears, transmissions and compressors.

Completion of the purchase is subject to regulatory review, Ethyl said.
