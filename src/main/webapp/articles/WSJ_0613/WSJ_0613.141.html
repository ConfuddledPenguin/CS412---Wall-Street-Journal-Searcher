International Brief -- Stora AB: Pretax Profit Declined 22% In First 4 Months of Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

Sweden's Stora AB said its pretax profit in the first four months of the year tumbled 22% to 1.12 billion kronor ($183 million) from 1.43 billion kronor a year earlier, dragged down by increased competition and unfavorable cost trends. The forestry giant said group sales dropped 1.7% to 13.61 billion kronor from 13.84 billion kronor. Excluding the effects of corporate acquisitions and divestitures, sales increased 366 million kronor, or 3%, it said.

Stora said in a forecast that the relative profit decline is expected to remain for all of 1990. It said, however, that it expects its profit to pick up once the effects of its recent acquisition of West German chemicals and engineering concern Feldmuehle Nobel AG become noticeable.
