General Dynamics Gets Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

WASHINGTON -- General Dynamics Corp. received a $91.5 million Air Force contract for improvements to the F-16 aircraft.

Ford Aerospace Corp., a unit of Ford Motor Co., got a $55.2 million Air Force contract for operating the satellite-control network.

Rockwell International Corp. received a $49.3 million Air Force contract for MX missile rocket motors.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., got a $29.6 million Navy contract for electronics equipment.

Raytheon Co. was awarded a $24.7 million Navy contract for electronics equipment.

TRW Inc. got a $16.1 million Air Force contract for satellite components.
