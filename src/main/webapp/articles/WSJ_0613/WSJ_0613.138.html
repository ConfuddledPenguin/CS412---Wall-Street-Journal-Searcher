BCE Mobile to Sell Special Stock Warrants To 4 Securities Firms

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

MONTREAL -- BCE Mobile Communications Inc. said it will sell three million special warrants at 25.25 Canadian dollars (US$21.56) each to Nesbitt Thomson Deacon Inc., RBC Dominion Securities Inc., Scotia-McLeod Inc. and Trilon Securities Corp., all securities firms. BCE Mobile said each special warrant entitles the holder to purchase a unit consisting of one BCE Mobile common share and the right to purchase by April 30, 1991, another common share at C$27.25.

The cellular-telephone and communications concern said net proceeds, excluding proceeds from the exercise of the share purchase warrants, will be about C$72.8 million.

BCE Mobile said the investment firms have an option until June 14 to increase the size of the issue to 3.5 million special warrants. It said proceeds will be used mainly to fund planned capital spending of its Bell Cellular Inc. unit.

BCE Mobile is currently 73% owned by BCE Inc., a Montreal-based telecommunications, energy, and real estate concern. However, BCE Mobile said that on completion of the special-warrant sale, BCE Inc.'s interest in BCE Mobile will decline to 69.7%. Assuming full exercise of the share purchase warrants, BCE's ownership would decrease to 66.6%.

On the Toronto Stock Exchange, BCE Mobile closed at C$23.75, down 75 Canadian cents.
