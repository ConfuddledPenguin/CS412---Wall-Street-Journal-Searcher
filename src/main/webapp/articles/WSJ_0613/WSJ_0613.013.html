Who's News: Bingo King Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

BINGO KING Co. (Littleton, Colo.) -- Brian E. Baker, the third-largest stockholder of this bingo equipment maker and vice president of Norbaker Industries Ltd. of Toronto, was elected a director, succeeding Edward G. Bradley, who resigned to pursue other business interests. Leonard A. Stuart, chairman and chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of president, also succeeding Mr. Bradley.
