USX Sells West German Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

PITTSBURGH -- USX Corp., an oil, gas and steel company, said it sold a West German auto-parts subsidiary, FHS Stahlverformung G.m.b.H., to Trustor Automotive of Holland, an auto parts concern that also has operations in Sweden. The price wasn't disclosed. FHS produces gear-shift levers and assemblies, suspension parts and other auto parts. It had 1989 sales of about $80 million.
