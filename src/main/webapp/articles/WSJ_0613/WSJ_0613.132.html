GM Unit Prices Geo Model

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

WARREN, Mich. -- General Motors Corp.'s Chevrolet division said the base price for the 1990 Geo Metro LSi convertible is $9,740. Initial production of the model, newest in the Geo line, will be limited to 3,000 cars distributed in California, Florida, Hawaii, Illinois, Michigan, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania and Texas. The car will be available in greater volume beginning with the 1991 model.
