Telerate Canada Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

TORONTO -- CMQ/Telerate Inc., a provider of real-time financial information, said it changed its name to Telerate Canada Inc. Jim Davies, chief operating officer of Telerate Canada, said the company changed its name "to emphasize the close relationship we have with our parent company."

Telerate Canada was acquired in 1987 by Telerate Inc., a unit of Dow Jones & Co.

Toronto-based Telerate Canada provides data on stocks, bonds, foreign currencies and other markets to Canadian subscribers. It said it has more than 10,000 terminals installed in Canada.
