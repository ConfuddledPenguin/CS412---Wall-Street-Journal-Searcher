Dividend News: New Line Cinema Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

NEW LINE CINEMA Corp., a New York-based independent producer and distributor of films, declared a 25% stock dividend, in the form of one new share for each four common shares owned, payable July 18 to stock of record June 27. The company as of Monday had more than 6.8 million shares outstanding. It last declared a stock dividend, of 10%, in November 1988.
