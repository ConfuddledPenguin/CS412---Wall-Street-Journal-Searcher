Swiss Cost-of-Living Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

BERN -- Switzerland's cost-of-living index rose 0.5% in May from April and 5.1% from a year earlier, the Federal Statistics Office said. The rise was smaller than many economists and analysts had expected. The monthly rise had been widely forecast at 0.7% to 0.9% and the year-to-year increase at 5.3% to 5.5%.

A month earlier, the year-to-year increase in the index was 4.6%. For the year ending in May 1989, it was 3.0%.

The latest jump in the year-to-year rate was due largely to recent rent increases, which in turn followed rises in Swiss mortgage rates, economists said.
