Credit Ratings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

LIONEL Corp. -- Standard & Poor's Corp. cut the rating on the New York-based toy retailer's $59 million of subordinated debt to triple-C-plus from single-B-plus. The credit-rating company removed the issue from its CreditWatch list, where it had been placed Feb. 20. Lionel declined comment on the rating action. The downgrade stems from a weakening operating and financial position at the toy retailer, plus "diminishing prospects for a significant recovery," S&P said.
