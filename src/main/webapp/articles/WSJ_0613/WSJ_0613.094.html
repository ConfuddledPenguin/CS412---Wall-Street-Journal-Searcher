Business Brief -- Maxwell Communication Corp.: Macmillan Unit Buys 51% Of Israeli Publisher Keter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

Macmillan Inc., a New York-based unit of Maxwell Communication Corp. in London said it acquired 51% of an Israeli publisher and bookseller, Keter Publishing House Ltd. Terms weren't disclosed, but a spokesman for Macmillan said Keter Publishing has annual sales of between $12 million and $15 million. A spokesman said Keter, a major Israeli publisher, also owns about 34 bookstores in Israel. It is 75%-owned by Clal Ltd. of Israel, which will maintain a 24% interest in Keter. Macmillan said that most of Keter's books are published in Hebrew, but more will now be translated into English and distributed by Macmillan in the U.S. The spokesman said Maxwell is negotiating to buy additional publishing properties in Israel.
