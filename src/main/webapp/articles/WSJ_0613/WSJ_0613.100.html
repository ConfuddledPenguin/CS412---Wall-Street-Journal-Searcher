Business Brief -- Tokos Medical Corp.: Acquisition of a Rival Firm For Common Stock Is Set

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

Tokos Medical Corp., a provider of specialized home health care for pregnant women, said it agreed to acquire rival Physiologic Diagnostic Service Inc. for 2.7 million shares of Tokos common stock. Tokos, based in Santa Ana, Calif., went public in March. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, its stock jumped $4.25 to $15 on turnover of 447,500 shares, giving the transaction an indicated value of $40.5 million.

Craig T. Davenport, Tokos president and chief operating officer, said the two companies plan to combine their clinical-research data, potentially quickening and expanding regulatory approval for their home uterine-activity monitors.

Physiologic Diagnostic, a privately owned company in Atlanta, expects revenue of about $3.5 million this year, Mr. Davenport said. Tokos, which is forecasting 1990 revenue of about $75 million, expects to complete the acquisition by mid-August.
