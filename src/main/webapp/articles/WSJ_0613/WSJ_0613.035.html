Who's News: Great Atlantic & Pacific Tea Company

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

GREAT ATLANTIC & PACIFIC TEA Co. (Montvale, N.J.) -- R. Donald O'Leary becomes corporate vice president of marketing, succeeding John D. Ryer, who left to pursue other interests, the food and grocery company said. Mr. O'Leary was previously a partner in Noll & Co., a retail marketing and media consulting firm in Red Bank, N.J. He also is a consultant through his own firm, O'Leary & Co.
