REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Ryan's Hope

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

Most of American culture today is in turmoil or in court. The battle lines are drawn again over the American flag. Pop music is in jail in Florida, which doesn't seem to think it has to endure a rap group's obscenity. Is there no respite? Turns out there is, in the form of 43-year-old pitcher Nolan Ryan's no-hitter Monday over the World Champion Oakland A's, his sixth. No danger, we assume, that a court will try to overturn it.
