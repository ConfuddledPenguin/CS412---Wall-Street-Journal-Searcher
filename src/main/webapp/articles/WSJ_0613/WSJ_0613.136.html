Who's News: PNC Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

PNC FINANCIAL Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Bruce E. Robbins, 46 years old, previously executive vice president, credit policy, at this financial holding company, was named president of the Central Bancorp. bank holding company unit and vice chairman, a new post, of that unit's Central Trust Co., both Cincinnati. At Central Bancorp., Mr. Robbins succeeded Gary N. Kocher, 43, who was named chairman, a new position, and remains chief executive officer.
