Who's News: Andrew W. Mellon Foundation

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

ANDREW W. MELLON FOUNDATION (New York) -- T. Dennis Sullivan was named financial vice president and chief financial officer, a new post, effective Sept. 1. Mr. Sullivan is also president of Princeton University Investment Co. The Mellon Foundation is a private foundation with assets of about $1.8 billion.
