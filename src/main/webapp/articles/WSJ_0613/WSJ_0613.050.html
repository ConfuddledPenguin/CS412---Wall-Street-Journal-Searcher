Who's News: Campeau Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/13/90

CAMPEAU Corp. (Toronto) -- Conrad Black, a Toronto financier and Hollinger Inc. chairman and chief executive officer, resigned as a director of this real estate and retailing concern, citing demands on his time, reducing board membership to 10. Campeau said two new independent directors will be named in due course. Mr. Black was appointed a director last September, when Campeau named a new board and said it would attempt to restructure its U.S. retailing units, Federated Department Stores Inc. and Allied Stores Corp., which last January sought protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code.
