Financing Business: OHM Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

OHM Corp. said it completed the initial public offering of 1.5 million common shares of its asbestos abatement subsidiary, NSC Corp., at $13.50 a share. OHM continues to own 73% of the 5,510,000 shares outstanding of NSC, before exercise of an underwriters' over-allotment option. Proceeds from the offering will be used to repay certain bank debt of NSC, OHM said. Oppenheimer & Co. and Raymond James & Associates Inc. managed the underwriting. NSC is a regional provider of asbestos abatement services, primarily for commercial, industrial and institutional properties in the northeastern part of the country. OHM of Findlay, Ohio, provides environmental analysis and remediation services in the U.S. and Canada.
