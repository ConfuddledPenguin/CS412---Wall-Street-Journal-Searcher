GE Power Generation Pacts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/13/90

FAIRFIELD, Conn. -- General Electric Co. said its power generation business received three contracts totaling $246.5 million for power plant equipment. The contracts include sale of a 927-megawatt power plant for more than $200 million to Korea Electric Power Co.; a $35.5 million contract for gas turbines to Indiana Municipal Power Agency; and an $11 million contract for a gas and steam turbine unit to Trigen Energy Corp., for a plant in Uniondale, N.Y.
