FirstFed America Inc. Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

HONOLULU -- FirstFed America Inc. said shareholders approved its plan to be acquired by Bancorp Hawaii Inc. for $140.1 million. The acquisition still must be approved by federal thrift regulators. FirstFed has operations in Hawaii, Guam and Saipan; Bancorp Hawaii is a bank holding company with operations in Asia and the South and West Pacific.
