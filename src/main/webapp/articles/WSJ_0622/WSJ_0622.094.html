Who's News: Zycad Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

ZYCAD Corp. (Menlo Park, Calif.) -- Phillips W. Smith was named president, chief executive officer and a director of this maker of automated design and simulation devices for the engineering industry. Mr. Smith succeeds Mark Parsinen, who resigned. Mr. Smith, an industry consultant, previously served as chairman and chief executive of Edgcore Technology Inc., a computer concern.
