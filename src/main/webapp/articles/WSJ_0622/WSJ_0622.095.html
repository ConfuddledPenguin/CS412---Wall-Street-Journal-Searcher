Dividend News: Home Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

HOME SAVINGS BANK directors halved the quarterly dividend to 18.75 cents a share, payable July 19 to stock of record July 6. The move stems from "continued pressure on earnings," which in turn has resulted from weak real estate markets, the New York-based savings bank said. Home has reported that first-quarter earnings fell 32% to $4.1 million, or 34 cents a share. The payout cut on Home's more than 12 million shares outstanding is unrelated to, and should not affect, the bank's plan to be acquired by H.F. Ahmanson & Co. of Los Angeles for $300 million of stock, Home said. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Home Savings shares fell 50 cents to $19.50.
