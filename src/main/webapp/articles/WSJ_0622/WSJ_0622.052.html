General Dynamics Gets Award

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

WASHINGTON -- General Dynamics Corp. won a $19.8 million Army contract for support for the Egyptian M-1 tank co-production program.

M&M Services Inc. received a $17.2 million Navy contract for operation and maintenance of transportation equipment.

Teledyne Inc. was issued a $15.4 million Air Force pact for cargo aircraft loaders.

Cadillac Gage, a unit of Textron Inc., was awarded a $15.3 million Army contract for development of the light armored vehicle.

Southwest Mobile Systems Corp. got a $13.2 million Air Force contract for cargo aircraft loaders.
