Money Fund Assets Rose in Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

NEW YORK -- Assets of the nation's 512 money market funds rose $1.29 billion to $385.40 billion in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 246 general purpose funds rose $567.9 million to $133.85 billion and assets of 105 broker-dealer funds rose $296.3 million to $159.53 billion. Assets of 161 institutional funds rose $425.9 million to $92.02 billion.
