Who's News: Stride Rite Corp. Picks Shames for President

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

CAMBRIDGE, Mass. -- Stride Rite Corp. named Ervin Shames, 49 years old, president and chief executive officer, succeeding Ronald J. Jackson, who resigned in May. Arnold Hiatt continues as chairman of the children's shoe concern. Mr. Hiatt was acting president and chief executive officer since Mr. Jackson's resignation.

Mr. Shames was most recently president and chief executive officer of Kendall Co., a closely held health-care products concern in Boston.

Mr. Jackson resigned last May, after holding the posts for six months, to become president, chief executive officer and chairman of Quaker Oats Co.'s Fisher-Price unit.

Earlier this year, Quaker Oats said it planned to spin off the Fisher-Price unit in 1991.
