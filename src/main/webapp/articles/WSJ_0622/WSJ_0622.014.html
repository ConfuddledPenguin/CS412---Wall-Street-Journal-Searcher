Dividend News: Accel International Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

ACCEL INTERNATIONAL Corp., Dublin, Ohio, said its board declared a quarterly dividend of five cents a common share and set a policy of paying at least 20 cents annually, payable on a quarterly basis. In addition, R. Max Williamson, president and chief executive officer of the specialty insurance concern, was named to the additional post of chairman. He succeeds Brian L. Howell, who retires as chairman and chief financial officer on July 31. The dividend, the first paid since a special cash dividend in 1983, is payable Aug. 1 to shares of record July 9. Accel said its board believed the dividend was warranted because of the company's consistent earnings growth over the past several years.
