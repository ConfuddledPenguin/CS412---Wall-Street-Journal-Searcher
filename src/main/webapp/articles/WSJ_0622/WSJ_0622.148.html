International Brief -- Isuzu Motors Ltd.: Consolidated Pretax Profit Dropped 7.9% in First Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

Isuzu Motors Ltd., Tokyo, posted a 7.9% loss in consolidated pretax profit for the first half of fiscal 1990, ended April 30. Pretax profit fell to 10.82 billion yen ($70.3 million) from 11.75 billion yen in the year-earlier period.

Although sales rose 8%, to 589.86 billion yen from 546 billion yen, significantly higher costs of sales, materials and administration drowned out the gains.

Operating profit fell 33%, to 6.07 billion yen from 9.03 billion yen.

Net profit fell 15% to 6.05 billion yen, or 6 yen a share, from 7.1 billion yen, or 7.7 yen a share. The company recommended the dividend stay at 2.5 yen a share.

Under the aegis of General Motors Corp., Isuzu is a medium-scale auto maker.
