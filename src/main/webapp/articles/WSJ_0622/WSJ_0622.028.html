Nintendo U.S. Sales Estimate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

REDMOND, Wash. -- Nintendo of America Inc. said it expects sales of its videogame systems to surpass more than nine million units this year, 1.5 million more than it previously forecast. Nintendo said the increase is based on the pace of sales to date. The increase comes despite introduction of new video-game systems by its industry rivals. The company, a unit of Nintendo Co. of Japan, estimates it controls about 80% of the video-game systems market.
