South African Inflation

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

JOHANNESBURG -- South Africa's inflation rate as measured by the consumer price index rose 13.9% in May from a year earlier, the government's Central Statistical Services said. May's index rose to 200.4 points from 175.9 points a year earlier and was up 1.1% from April's 198.3 points.

April's year-on-year increase was 14.6%.
