Business Brief -- Mitek Systems Inc.: Wider Loss for 3rd Period, Rise in Annual Net Seen

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

Mitek Systems Inc., a San Diego-based computer concern, said that it expects to report a wider loss for its third quarter ending June 30, but that profit for the year ending Sept. 30 should still be "significantly higher" than that a year earlier. In the fiscal 1989 third quarter, Mitek had a net loss of $423,000, on revenue of $3.3 million. For fiscal 1989, it earned $403,000, or six cents a share, on revenue of $18.1 million.

Mitek said earnings and revenue in the current quarter have been hurt by stretch-outs in its government contracts, a slowdown in spending, and costs related to its acquisition in March of Tempest Products Organization, a unit of New York-based Sequa Corp. But Mitek said it is experiencing a high level of bidding activity that could be reflected in improved fiscal fourth-quarter performance.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, Mitek shares were quoted at $1.625, bid, up 6.25 cents.
