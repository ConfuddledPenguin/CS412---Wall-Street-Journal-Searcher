Letters to the Editor: Peppery Cartoonist Has Salty Comments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

Julie Salamon, in her June 14 review of the movie "Dick Tracy," makes a casual reference to "comic-free papers like this one or the New York Times." Well, what am I . . . chopped liver? I've been contributing to the Journal's "Pepper . . . and Salt" cartoon panel for more than 30 years, along with a multitude of other free-lance gag cartoonists. I understand it's one of the most popular features in the paper. Ms. Salamon can find it a couple of pages beyond the Leisure & Arts section where her reviews are printed, just below the "Letters to the Editor."

Eli Stein

Massapequa Park, N.Y.
