Canadian Retail Sales Fall

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian retail sales fell 1% in April from March, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The agency said retail sales have fluctuated "markedly" in the first four months of the year, registering "marginal overall growth." In March, retail sales rose 0.3%.

The April decline was attributed partly to a drop in sales of autos and other vehicles.
