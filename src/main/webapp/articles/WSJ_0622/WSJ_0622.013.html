Storage Technology Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

LOUISVILLE, Colo. -- Storage Technology Corp., a maker of data storage equipment, said it increased its committed line of credit from its bank group by $25 million, to $75 million, while lowering the interest rate to the prime rate from prime plus one percentage point. The banks participating in the credit line are United Bank of Denver, Security Pacific National Bank, First Interstate Bank of Denver, Harris Trust & Savings and First City Bancorp of Texas-Houston.
