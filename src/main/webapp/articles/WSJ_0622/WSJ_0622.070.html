Boeing Gets Thai Airways Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

SEATTLE -- Boeing Co.'s Commercial Airplane Group said it received an order from Thai Airways International, the national carrier of Thailand, for five additional 747-400 widebody jets valued at between $625 million and $735 million. The jets will be powered by General Electric Co.'s CF6-80C2 engines, valued at about $175 million. Thai Airways has now ordered a total of eight 747-400s, all powered by CF6-80C2 engines.
