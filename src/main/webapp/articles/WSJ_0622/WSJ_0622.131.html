Business Brief -- Cincinnati Milacron Inc.: Machine Tool Maker Begins Offer of 3 Million Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

Cincinnati Milacron Inc. said it is offering three million of its common shares at $18 a share. The offer consists of 2.4 million shares offered in the U.S. by a syndicate lead-managed by First Boston Corp. and 600,000 shares offered internationally by a group of underwriters lead-managed by Credit Suisse First Boston.

The Cincinnati machine tool concern said it granted underwriters an option to purchase as many as 450,000 additional shares at $18 each to cover overallotments.

The company currently has 24.4 million common shares outstanding.
