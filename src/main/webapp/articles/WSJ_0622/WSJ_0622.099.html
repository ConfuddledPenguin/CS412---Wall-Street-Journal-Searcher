Chiquita to Redeem 5.5% Convertible Issue

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

CINCINNATI -- Chiquita Brands International Inc. said it called for redemption on July 26 all of its outstanding 5.5% convertible subordinated debentures due Feb. 1, 1994. Approximately $34.7 million principal amount of debentures is currently outstanding.

The company will pay $1,002.50 per $1,000 principal amount plus accrued interest to the date of redemption (which amounts to $26.736 per $1,000 principal amount). Interest on the debentures won't accrue after July 26.

The debentures are covertible at the rate of $18.333 a share or about 54.5 shares per $1,000 principal amount of debentures.

In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, Chiquita closed at $26.75, up 37.5 cents.

Based on the current market price of the company's capital stock (which currently exceeds the conversion price by nearly 46%), the company said it expects debenture holders to convert their debentures into capital stock prior to the redemption date. The right to convert expires at 5 p.m. EDT, July 26. If all of the debentures are converted, an additional 1.9 million common shares will be issued. The company said issuance of these additional shares wouldn't affect the computation of fully diluted earnings per share because the debentures have already been treated as converted for purposes of this computation.
