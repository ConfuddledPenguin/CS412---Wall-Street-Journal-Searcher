Business Brief -- Pier 1 Imports Inc.: Retailer Set Record Sales, Earnings in First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

Pier 1 Imports Inc. said it achieved record sales and earnings in its first quarter ended June 2. The Fort Worth, Texas, specialty retailer recorded net income of $7.7 million, up 15% from the $6.7 million reported in the period ended May 27, 1989. Per-share net, after payment of preferred dividends, rose to 22 cents from 19 cents a year earlier. Sales increased 22% to $138.4 million from $113.9 million.

Clark A. Johnson, chairman and chief executive officer, said that sales were hurt by bad weather in many consumer areas and by a softness in demand for furniture and home-related items. But the company's financial results were helped by "strong expense control and a successful inventory-balancing program."

Pier 1 said it opened 16 new stores during the quarter, while closing two. The company expects to open 68 more stores by year end.
