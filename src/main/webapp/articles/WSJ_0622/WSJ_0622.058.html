Tandy Brands Inc. To Spin Off a Unit Into New Public Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Tandy Brands Inc. said it will spin off one of its units early next year, creating a new, publicly traded company under the name Tandy Brands Accessories Inc. The specialty retailer said stockholders will receive one share of Tandy Brands Accessories for each five shares of Tandy Brands held, to be distributed "as soon as practicable" after Dec. 31, 1990. The company said it has received a ruling from the Internal Revenue Service that the new shares will not constitute taxable income.

Carson R. Thompson, Tandy Brands chairman and chief executive, said the spinoff will provide more focused management for two distinct businesses, as well as provide shareholders with more clearly defined investment vehicles. Tandy Brands Accessories, for example, makes and sells men's and boys' leather accessories and neckwear, whereas Tandy Brands Inc. will continue operating Bombay Co., a chain of 270 stores selling specialty furniture and home accessories.

The Tandy Brands Accessories unit is expected to finish the current fiscal year June 30 with about $25 million in revenue, down from last year's $26 million, according to Mr. Thompson. The Bombay division, however, is projected to have revenue of $112 million, compared with $83 million in the last fiscal year.

After the spinoff, shareholders of Tandy Brands Inc. will be asked to approve a name change to The Bombay Company Inc. The company will then include a new unit, Alex & Ivy, which is scheduled to begin operations in September as a retailer of European country-style home furnishings. Mr. Thompson will continue as chairman and chief executive; the Tandy Brands board also has named Robert E.M. Nourse president and chief operating officer and has elected him to the board, expanding the board to nine directors.

The board also named J.S.B. Jenkins president and chief executive of Tandy Brands Accessories, and Stanley T. Ninemire vice president and chief financial officer.
