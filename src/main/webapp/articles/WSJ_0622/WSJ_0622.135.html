Business Brief -- Diagnostek Inc.: Drug Firm Reaches Accord To Buy Epic Health Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

Diagnostek Inc. executed a definitive agreement to acquire Epic Health Group Inc. for $13.5 million, the companies said. They said the price works out to $1.04 a common share and $7.70 a preferred share. To start the acquisition, a Diagnostic subsidiary said it would soon begin a tender offer for Epic shares.

However, Epic said the prices are subject to reduction under certain circumstances, on the basis of its performance during a 30-day period of the tender offer.

Diagnostek, based in Albuquerque, N. M., provides drug cost-control programs, including programs to sell drugs by mail, and pharmacy-management services. Epic, based in Elmsford, N.Y., provides mail-order prescription-drug programs.

Epic closed at 81.25 cents, up 18.75 cents, in over-the-counter trading. Diagnostek closed at $16.25, up 37.5 cents, in national over-the-counter trading.
