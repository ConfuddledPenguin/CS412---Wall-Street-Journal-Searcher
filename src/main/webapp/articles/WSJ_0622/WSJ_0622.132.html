Technology Brief -- Structural Dynamics Research: Software Job of $700,000 Received From Lockheed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

Structural Dynamics Research Corp., Milford, Ohio, said it received a software order valued at more than $700,000 from the Sunnyvale, Calif.-based Lockheed Missiles & Space Co. unit Lockheed Corp., Calabasas, Calif. The supplier of mechanical computer-aided engineering software said its I-DEAS software will be used by Lockheed's Space Systems division for design of satellite systems.

In addition, Structural Dynamics said Lockheed's Computer Integrated Engineering & Manufacturing Group picked the software as an element of its companywide mechanical design automation system.
