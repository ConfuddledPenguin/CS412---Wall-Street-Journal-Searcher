French Industrial Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

PARIS -- France's seasonally adjusted industrial production index rose 1.4% in April from March but showed a 0.3% drop from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute said. The figure was much better than previous forecasts by economists, who were calling for a flat result. But the latest data were heavily influenced by one-time factors that boosted energy production by 15.2% during the month, while all other sectors showed flat or lower output.
