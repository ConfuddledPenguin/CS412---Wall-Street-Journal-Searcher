Italian Wholesale Sales Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/22/90

MILAN -- Italy's industrial wholesale sales index in March rose 5.7% from a year earlier, the state statistical institute Istat said. In February, the index rose 5.3% from a year earlier. Domestic wholesale sales in March rose 5.3%, and sales to foreign destinations grew 6.7%.

For the first three months of this year, wholesale sales rose 5.9% from the year-earlier period, owing to increases of 5.2% in domestic sales and 8% in foreign sales.

Sales of capital products to both foreign and domestic destinations in the first three months grew 10.4% from a year earlier, and wholesale sales of consumer products rose 8.3%. Sales of intermediate goods rose 0.9%.
