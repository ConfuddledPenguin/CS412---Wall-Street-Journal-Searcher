  • Date: 06/22/90

Sony Chairman Akio Morita at Wharton's commencement:

I am not a believer in the idea of a "post-industrial" society. For me the very essence of business, in fact the very foundation of a nation's economy is manufacturing. Why? Because manufacturing creates. It takes resources and materials, and by applying knowledge to them creates products.
