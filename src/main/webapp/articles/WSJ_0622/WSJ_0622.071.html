FLEMING Cos. (Oklahoma City) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it assigned a Prime-2 rating to the commercial paper programs of this food wholesale distributor, affecting $400 million in a program administered by Merrill Lynch Money Markets Inc. and $200 million in a program administered by First Boston Corp. The rating concern also affirmed the Baa-2 rating on the company's unsecured senior securities, citing its leadership position in the industry, its adequate debt-service measurements and the expectation that its improving operating performance over the past few quarters will continue through the next year.
