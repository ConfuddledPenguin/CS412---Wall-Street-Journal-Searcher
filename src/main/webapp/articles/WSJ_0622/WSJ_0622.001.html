Japanese Household Spending

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/22/90

TOKYO -- Japan's household spending rose an inflation-adjusted 3.7% in April from the year-earlier period, helped by fast-paced growth of spending by wage earners, the Statistics Bureau of the prime minister's office said. An official at the statistics bureau said the year-on-year growth reflects a rebound from the low level of spending in April 1989 because of the introduction of a 3% consumption tax at the beginning of that month.
