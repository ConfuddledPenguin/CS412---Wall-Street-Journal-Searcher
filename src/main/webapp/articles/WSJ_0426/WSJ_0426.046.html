Waltham Unit to Buy Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Waltham Corp. said its Waltham Savings Bank subsidiary agreed to buy certain assets and deposit liabilities of two branch offices of First Mutual Bank for Savings. Terms weren't disclosed. The offices, in Waltham and Wayland, hold deposits totaling about $72 million. Following the transaction, which is subject to regulatory approvals, Waltham Savings will have deposits of about $400 million and total assets of about $600 million. The transaction is expected to be completed during the third quarter, Waltham said.
