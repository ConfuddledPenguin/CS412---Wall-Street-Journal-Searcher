NORTHERN STATES POWER Co. (Minneapolis) -- Richard M. Kovacevich, president and chief operating officer, was named a director of this utility, filling a vacancy on the board. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

RICHARD M. KOVACEVICH, president and chief operating officer of Norwest Corp., was named a director of Northern States Power Co. In Thursday's edition, he was incorrectly identified as a Northern States official.

(WSJ April 30, 1990)
