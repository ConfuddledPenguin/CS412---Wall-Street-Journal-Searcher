Dividend News: Clear Channel Communications Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

CLEAR CHANNEL COMMUNICATIONS Inc. said its board declared a 3-for-2 stock split, payable May 31 to stock of record May 15. The San Antonio, Texas, company, which operates radio and television stations, has 3,820,000 common shares outstanding and doesn't pay a regular cash dividend.
