Who's News: American Family Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

AMERICAN FAMILY Corp. (Columbus, Ga.) -- Four new directors were elected for this holding company. M. Delmar Edwards, 63 years old, is a surgeon in Columbus. Elizabeth J. Hudson, 40, is senior vice president, corporate communications, of National Broadcasting Co. Charles B. Knapp, 43, is president and chief executive officer of University of Georgia. Glenn Vaughn Jr., 60, is chairman of the Columbus Ledger-Enquirer.
