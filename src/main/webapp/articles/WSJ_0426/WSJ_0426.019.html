Panhandle Eastern Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

HOUSTON -- Panhandle Eastern Corp. shareholders approved a proposal increasing the company's authorized common and preferred shares. At the company's annual meeting, holders boosted the authorized common shares to 300 million from 200 million, and the authorized preferred to three million from two million. A spokeswoman for Panhandle said the natural gas pipeline concern will use the additional shares for general corporate purposes.
