Who's News: National Mercantile Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

NATIONAL MERCANTILE BANCORP (Los Angeles) -- William T. Hughes, 38 years old, previously senior vice president at the Security Pacific National Bank unit of Security Pacific Corp., was elected president, chief operating officer and a director of this bank holding concern, succeeding Terry T. Tomioch, who resigned earlier this month.
