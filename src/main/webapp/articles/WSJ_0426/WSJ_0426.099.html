International Brief -- Michelin: Consolidated Net Earnings Rose 3.4% to $430.1 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

Cie. Generale des Etablissements Michelin said its consolidated net profit after payments to minority interests climbed 3.4% to 2.45 billion francs ($430.1 million) in 1989 from 2.37 billion francs a year earlier. The French tire and rubber group also said it plans to boost the net annual dividend for its publicly traded Class B stock to 2.40 francs a share from 2.25 francs.

The group's 3% earnings gain in 1989 was roughly in line with previous forecasts by analysts, who had worried that weakening tire demand and the firm tone of the franc against other currencies could limit earnings growth.

Michelin said consolidated revenue rose 7% to 55.26 billion francs. Operating profit eased 3% to 4.7 billion francs.
