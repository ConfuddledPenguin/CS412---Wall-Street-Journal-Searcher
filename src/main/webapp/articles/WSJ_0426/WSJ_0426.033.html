Dividend News: Westinghouse Electric

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

WESTINGHOUSE ELECTRIC Corp. increased its quarterly 16.7% and split its stock 2-for-1. Pittsburgh-based Westinghouse, which has interests in broadcasting, defense electronics and community development, increased its quarterly to 70 cents a share from 60 cents, payable June 1 to stock of record May 5. The split is payable May 21 to stock of record May 5. The company increased the number of authorized common shares to 480 million from 240 million. Westinghouse had 145,744,576 shares outstanding as of March 31.
