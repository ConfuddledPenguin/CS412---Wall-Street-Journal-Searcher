Noranda Forest to End Line

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

TORONTO -- Noranda Forest Inc. said it will discontinue the production of coated label papers at its mill in Thorold, Ontario. A company spokesman said there's no indication that the move, which includes the loss of 95 jobs representing about 16% of the plant's work force, will result in a charge against earnings. He said the production of label papers, used for labeling cans and other containers, isn't profitable because of competition from imports. The company said the future of the Thorold mill lies in de-inked recycled papers and wallpaper.
