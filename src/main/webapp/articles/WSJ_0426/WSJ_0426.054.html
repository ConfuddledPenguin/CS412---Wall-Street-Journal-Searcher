Crystal Brands' Net Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

SOUTHPORT, Conn. -- Crystal Brands Inc. said first-quarter net income rose 9% to $6.6 million, or 73 cents a share, aided by higher profit margins and lower expenses and interest costs. The apparel maker's year-earlier profit was $6.1 million, or 67 cents a share. First-quarter sales fell 2% to $218.8 million from $223.3 million.
