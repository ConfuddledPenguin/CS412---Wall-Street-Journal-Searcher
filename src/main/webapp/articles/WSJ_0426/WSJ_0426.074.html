Who's News: First Empire State Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

FIRST EMPIRE STATE Corp. (Buffalo, N.Y.) -- William A. Buckingham, 47-year-old executive vice president at Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co., was named executive vice president, retail banking, at this bank holding company, and Robert E. Sadler Jr., 44, executive vice president at First Empire's M&T bank unit, was named executive vice president, commercial banking.
