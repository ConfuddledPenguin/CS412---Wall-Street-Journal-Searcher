Boeing's Canada Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

TORONTO -- Boeing Co.'s Canada unit said it received an order from America West Airlines Inc. for an additional 10 Dash 8 Series 100 regional aircraft valued at $90 million. The order included options for 15 more of the planes. America West plans to increase its Dash 8 fleet to 37 aircraft by 1995, Boeing said. America West, based in Phoenix, Ariz., had previously ordered 12 of the planes and already has nine in service.
