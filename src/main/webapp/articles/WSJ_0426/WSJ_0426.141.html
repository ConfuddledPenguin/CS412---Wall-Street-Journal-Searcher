Credit Ratings: Moody's Is Reviewing Ratings of Securities Backed by Fuji Bank

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 04/26/90

NEW YORK -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it placed under review for possible downgrade the credit ratings of one recreational vehicle receivable-backed security ($143 million), FCC 1989-A Grantor Trust, and one residential mortgage pass-through security ($247 million), RFMSI, Series 1990-1, supported by Fuji Bank Ltd. letters of credit. Moody's also said it placed under review all issues that are fully supported by Fuji Bank letters of credit and guarantees, totaling $2.1 billion.

Approximately $2.5 billion of structured securities are affected, the rating agency said.

Moody's said, "This rating action follows Moody's placing under review for possible downgrade the senior debt ratings of Fuji Bank, Ltd. (Triple-A rating), Fuji International Finance (HK) Ltd. (Triple-A rating), and Fuji International Finance (Australia) Ltd. (Triple-A rating)."
