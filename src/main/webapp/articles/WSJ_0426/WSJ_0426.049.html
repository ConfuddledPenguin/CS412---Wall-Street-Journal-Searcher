Who's News: Anheuser-Busch Cos.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

ANHEUSER-BUSCH Cos. (St. Louis) -- Lewis T. Preston, 63-year-old chairman of the executive committee of J.P. Morgan & Co., and Andrew B. Craig III, 58, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Boatmen's Bancshares Inc., were elected directors of this brewing concern, reducing board membership to 14 with the retirements of two directors, Armand C. Stalnaker, professor at Washington University, and Fred. W. Wenzel, chairman of Kellwood Co.; and the death of August A. Busch Jr.
