Who's News: Mine Safety Appliances Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

MINE SAFETY APPLIANCES Co. (Pittsburgh) -- Lee N. Short Jr., 63-year-old chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of chairman of this maker of plant safety equipment, succeeding John T. Ryan Jr., 78, who retired but remains a director. As president, Mr. Short was succeeded by John T. Ryan III, 46, formerly executive vice president.
