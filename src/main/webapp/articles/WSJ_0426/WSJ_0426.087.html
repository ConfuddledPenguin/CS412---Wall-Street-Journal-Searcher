MCCAW CELLULAR COMMUNICATIONS Inc. said John E. McCaw Jr., executive vice president of acquisitions, has 12,238,249 shares of the company's Class B common stock. His Class B holding was misstated in yesterday's edition because of an error by Invest/Net, a company that tracks filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The incorrect figure was confirmed by a McCaw spokesman prior to publication, and Mr. McCaw had declined to comment. (See: "Inside Track: Novell Executives Sold Stock in Month Before Lotus Offer" -- WSJ April 25, 1990)

COOPER INDUSTRIES Inc.'s 5-for-1 stock split on preferred shares is effective May 25 to stock of record May 4. In yesterday's edition the dates were transposed.

(See: "Dividend News: Cooper Industries Inc." -- WSJ April 25, 1990)
