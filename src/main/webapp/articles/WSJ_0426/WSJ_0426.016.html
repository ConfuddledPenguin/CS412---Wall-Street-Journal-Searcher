Central Illinois Public Service

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

SPRINGFIELD, Ill. -- Central Illinois Public Service Co. said shareholders at the annual meeting approved the reorganization of the utility into a holding company structure, named Cipsco Inc. Under the plan, Central Illinois holders will receive Cipsco stock on a 1-for-1 basis. The company said the exchange is expected to be take place by July 1.
