Ameritrust Corp. Posts Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

CLEVELAND -- Ameritrust Corp. had a loss of $51.6 million in the first quarter, as the bank holding company took a huge provision to comply with tougher regulatory standards. In the year-ago first quarter, Ameritrust earned $27.8 million, or 76 cents a share. In the quarter just ended, Ameritrust increased its loan-loss provision by $120 million after federal examiners reviewed the company's loan portfolio. Ameritrust said examiners expressed concern over the company's commercial real-estate loans and highly leveraged transactions. The bank's nonperforming assets increased to $240.8 million from $214.3 million at the end of 1989.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Ameritrust closed at $18.125, unchanged.
