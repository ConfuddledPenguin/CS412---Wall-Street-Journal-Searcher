Business Brief -- Apple Bancorp Inc.: Investor With 30.6% Stake Extends Tender for Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

Stanley Stahl, an investor controlling 30.6% of Apple Bancorp Inc. extended his $38-a-share tender offer for the rest of the bank to May 25 from April 25. As of March 20, Apple had 4.6 million shares outstanding. As of 10 a.m. EDT April 25, holders had tendered 1,157 shares under the offer, which is valued at $121.6 million. When the offer was announced March 28, Apple shares leaped past the offer price, suggesting that investors expect a higher offer for the bank.

Yesterday, Apple's shares fell 12.5 cents to $42.25 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.

A spokesman for Mr. Stahl declined to comment on whether the investor might boost his bid. Apple, based in New York, has rejected Mr. Stahl's offer as not in the best interests of the bank or its shareholders. The bank declined on Mr. Stahl's extension of his offer.
