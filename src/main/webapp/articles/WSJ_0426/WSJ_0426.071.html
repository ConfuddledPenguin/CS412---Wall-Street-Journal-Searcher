Who's News: NCNB Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

NCNB Corp. (Charlotte, N.C.) -- R. Eugene Taylor was elected president and a director of NCNB National Bank of Florida, a unit of this banking company. Mr. Taylor, 42 years old, has been executive vice president of the Florida operation since August 1988. He succeeds Robert L. Kirby, 59, who will retire June 30. Mr. Taylor will assume his new position on July 1.
