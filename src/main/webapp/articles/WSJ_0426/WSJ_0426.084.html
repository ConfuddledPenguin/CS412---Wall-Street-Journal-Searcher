ENGELHARD Corp. (Edison, N.J.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it downgraded its ratings on $100 million in long-term debt this company, which has interests in specialty chemical products, engineered materials and precious-metal management services. The rating concern lowered its rating on Englehard's notes to single-A-3 from single-A-2 and on commercial paper to prime-2 from prime-1. Conditions in some of Engelhard's businesses, "such as petroleum refining catalysts, may remain difficult," Moody's said, adding that these problems "are offset somewhat by the company's strong positions in its core businesses."
