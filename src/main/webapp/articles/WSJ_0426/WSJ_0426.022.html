Bindley Western Industries Net

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Bindley Western Industries Inc. expects to report that first-quarter net income rose to between 22 cents a share and 24 cents a share, a record for any first quarter, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer William E. Bindley said. Mr. Bindley also said the drug wholesaler next week will announce a new account with a "major retailer."

He said net exceeded $1.5 million, compared with $901,000, or 15 cents a share, a year earlier. He said the company expects to report that sales rose 20% to $433 million from $362.2 million.
