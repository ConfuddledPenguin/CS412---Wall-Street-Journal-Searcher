Shipshape

Here's the problem about a boat

That's not in sea-chanty songs:

There's no place to put anything

Except where it belongs.

-- Ruth Boorstin.

Exit Lines

As a rule of thumb for house guests,

Try to look at it this way:

The time for leaving is best

While they're still urging you to stay.

-- Pat D'Amico.
