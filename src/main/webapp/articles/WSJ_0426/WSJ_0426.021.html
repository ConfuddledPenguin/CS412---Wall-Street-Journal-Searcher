Asarco Buy-Back Is Approved

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

NEW YORK -- Asarco Inc.'s board approved a $25 million buy-back of the company's stock in the open market over the next two years. The authorization brings to $120 million the total value of authorized stock-repurchase programs for the nonferrous-metals and specialty-chemicals company in the past two years. As of March 31, the company had purchased 1.4 million common shares and 2.9 million common-stock warrants for a total of $79.7 million.

In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange yesterday, Asarco closed at $25.75, down 12.5 cents.
