Business Brief -- Maxicare Health Plans Inc.: HMO Is Unable to Reach Pact on Bid by PacifiCare

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

Maxicare Health Plans Inc., a Los Angeles-based health maintenance organization, said it was unable to reach an accord with officials of Cypress, Calif.-based PacifiCare Health Systems Inc., a rival health-care concern that made an unsolicited acquisition bid for Maxicare last Friday. Terms of that bid weren't detailed. Maxicare has been operating under bankruptcy-law protection for 13 months. Peter Ratican, Maxicare's chairman, president and chief executive officer, said the parties met yesterday to discuss PacifiCare's interest. No further talks are planned, he said. PacifiCare officials said they were reviewing their options.

Maxicare said it plans to pursue its reorganization plan, and a hearing is scheduled for May 7 on the plan's disclosure statement. A confirmation hearing is scheduled for June 25.

About 40% of Maxicare's 320,000 members and about 70% of PacifiCare's 636,000 members are in California.
