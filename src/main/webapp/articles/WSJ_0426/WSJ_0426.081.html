Who's News: Barnett Banks Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

BARNETT BANKS Inc. (Jacksonville, Fla.) -- Clarence V. McKee Jr., 47-year-old chairman, chief executive officer and co-owner of WTVT Holdings Inc., was named a director of this banking company, succeeding W. Ashley Verlander, who reached the mandatory retirement age of 70.
