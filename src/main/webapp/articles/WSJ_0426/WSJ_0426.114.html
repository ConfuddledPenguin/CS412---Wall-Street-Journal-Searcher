Business Brief -- Hicks, Muse & Co.: New Venture Will Acquire Debt-Strapped Companies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

Hicks, Muse & Co. said it formed a joint venture to purchase industrial companies. The investment firm said the venture will target companies that have been purchased over the past two years with too much debt. "Our targets will be companies that require a strong capital base and a new management direction," said Thomas O. Hicks, chairman. "These companies will be forced to restructure their balance sheets to survive."

The new venture, HMD Partners, will include Ray C. Davis, a former group vice president at Colt Industries Inc. Equity financing for acquisitions will be provided by Hicks, Muse Equity Fund, which participated last month in the purchase of seven I.C.H. Corp. life insurance companies for $530 million.

HMD plans to purchase companies in the next five years with a total of more than $1 billion in annual revenue.
