Scientific Imaging Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

TRUMBULL, Conn. -- Scientific Imaging Instruments Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire closely held Xcel Corp. for $13 million. Scientific Imaging is developing video-based instruments for use in medical laboratory analysis. XCEL, based in Pasadena, Calif., makes electronic switches, video monitors and peripherals.

The two companies plan to operate as Xcel Corp.
