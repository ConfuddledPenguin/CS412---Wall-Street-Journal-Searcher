Who's News: Deltona Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

DELTONA Corp. (Miami) -- William R. Avella resigned as president, chief executive officer and director of this real-estate developer. Mr. Avella, 54 years old, said he is stepping down for health reasons. Maurice A. Halperin, 69, chairman, will succeed Mr. Avella as chief executive. Earle D. Cortright Jr., 49, executive vice president, will become president.
