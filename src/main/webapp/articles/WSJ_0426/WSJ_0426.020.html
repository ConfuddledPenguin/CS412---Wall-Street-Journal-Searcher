Financing Business: Colonial Gas Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

COLONIAL GAS Co., Lowell, Mass., said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a public offering of 600,000 common shares, all of which will be offered by the company. Underwriters for the proposed offering are A.G. Edwards & Sons Inc. and Edward D. Jones & Co.
