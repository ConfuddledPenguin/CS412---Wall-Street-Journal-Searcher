Business Brief -- American Express Co.: Ottawa Clears U.S. Firm To Set Up Bank in Canada

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

The Canadian government authorized New York-based American Express Co., a travel and financial services company, to establish a bank in Canada, despite strong opposition by Canadian banks. The new Amex Bank of Canada will give American Express customers access to automated teller machines and check-writing access to credit lines.

Canadian banks have complained that the planned bank would be granted broader powers than those available to banks in Canada. These include the power to promote goods and services, including insurance. Canada's Finance Department has repeatedly rejected the objections raised by Canadian banks, arguing that the American Express proposal is consistent with Canadian banking regulations.

The government is expected to introduce in Parliament later this year changes in bank laws that would allow Canadian banks to engage in some of the same activities as American Express does.
