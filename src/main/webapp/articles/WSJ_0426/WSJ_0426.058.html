Lomas Financial Corp. Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

DALLAS -- Lomas Financial Corp. said it received approval from the U.S. Bankruptcy Court to sell its commercial leasing unit to General Electric Co.'s General Electric Capital Corp. for about $161 million in cash, plus debt and stock. General Electric Capital will also assume $1 billion of debt and $30.6 million of preferred stock. The investment company said completion of the sale is subject to normal closing conditions.
