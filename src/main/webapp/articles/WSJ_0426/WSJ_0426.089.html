Who's News: Chesapeake Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

CHESAPEAKE Corp. (Richmond, Va.) -- John Hoyt Stookey, 60-year-old chairman, president and chief executive officer of Quantum Chemical Corp., was elected a director of forest products concern, succeeding Overton Dennis Jr., 71, who retired. Dr. Frank S. Royal, 50, a Richmond physician, was elected to a new seat on the board, increasing membership to 12.
