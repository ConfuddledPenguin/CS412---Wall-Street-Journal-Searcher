Business Brief -- ICM Property Investors Inc.: Investor Peter Bedford Lifts Stake in Company to 20.2%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

Peter B. Bedford, a Lafayette, Calif., investor, said he raised his stake in ICM Property Investors Inc. to the equivalent of 20.2% from 19.2% of the common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Bedford said he bought 67,600 ICM common shares from April 18 to April 23 for $6.875 to $7.125 each. The purchases bring Bedford's stake to 1,309,350 shares, including one million shares that he may acquire upon the exercise of an option.

Mr. Bedford, a real estate developer, acquired his stake in ICM, a New York-based real estate investment trust, two months ago. At the time, Mr. Bedford said he acquired the stake to expand his real estate holdings and make his real estate portfolio more liquid.

ICM, which considers Mr. Bedford's interest friendly, encouraged the initial Bedford investment.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, ICM shares closed at $7.25, up 37.5 cents.
