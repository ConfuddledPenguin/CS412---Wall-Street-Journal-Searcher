CBOT Seat Sold for $325,000

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Board of Trade was sold for $325,000, unchanged from the previous sale last Monday. Seats currently are quoted at $310,000, bid, and $375,000, asked. The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set Aug. 31, 1987.
