Technology: Sun Microsystems

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

MOUNTAINVIEW, Calif. -- Sun Microsystems Inc. said it made major performance enhancements to SparcStation 1, its flagship desktop computer. The maker of computer workstations said the new SparcStation 1-Plus, with no change in price for an entry-level system, delivers more than 20% more processing power and 25% faster graphics than the SparcStation 1. In addition, Sun said it reduced prices of mass storage and memory options for its entire product line by 30% to 50%.
