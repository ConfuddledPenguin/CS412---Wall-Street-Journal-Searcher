Who's News: Two Pesos Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

TWO PESOS Inc. (Houston) -- Ghulam Bombaywala, a local restaurant chain owner, was named president and chief operating officer of this Mexican-style restaurant chain, succeeding Marno M. McDermott, who remains chief executive officer and a director. Mr. Bombaywala also was elected a director. Mr. Bombaywala, who bought 600,000 common shares of Two Pesos for $1.25 each, or $750,000, will become chief executive if he uses his option to buy an additional 1.4 million shares within 180 days, the company said.
