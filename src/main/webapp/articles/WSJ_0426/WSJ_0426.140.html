International Brief -- Blue Circle Industries PLC: Building-Materials Firm Says '89 Pretax Profit Rose 14%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/26/90

Blue Circle Industries PLC, a British building-materials concern, said pretax profit for 1989 rose 14%, with its United Kingdom cement operation reporting significant earnings growth despite a general downturn in the British construction industry. Blue Circle said pretax profit increased to #231.8 million ($378.8 million) from #203.1 million. Sales rose 14% to #1.28 billion from #1.12 billion. Profit after tax less minority interests rose 6.3% to #163.0 million from #153.3 million.

The company reported an extraordinary gain of #23.3 million, resulting in a 30% rise in net profit attributable to shareholders, to #186.3 million from #143.7 million. In the previous year, Blue Circle reported an extraordinary loss of #9.6 million.

The company said its British cement division increased operating profit by 48%, while its property division and U.S. operations reported a drop in earnings.
