Business Brief -- Pitney Bowes Inc.: Quarterly Net After Gain Fell 50%; Operations Strong

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/26/90

Pitney Bowes Inc., a business equipment and services marketer, posted a 50% drop in first-quarter net income because of a one-time year-earlier gain, without which profit on an operating basis rose 12% on strong sales in the Stamford, Conn., company's mailing and shipping businesses. Profit declined to $59.7 million, or 75 cents a share, from a restated $119.5 million, or $1.51 a share, a year earlier, when results included a $66 million gain from an accounting change. The company is selling its Wheeler Group office-supply subsidiary, and restated its results to reflect this as a discontinued operation.

Sales rose 15% to $755.7 million from $658.2 million.
