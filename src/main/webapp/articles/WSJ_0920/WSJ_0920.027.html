Inland Steel Sells Mines

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

PITTSBURGH -- Inland Steel Co., a subsidiary of Chicago-based Inland Steel Industries Inc., said it sold some closed mines to Pfizer Specialty Minerals Inc., a subsidiary of Pfizer Inc. Terms weren't disclosed. The mines are limestone and dolomite reserves of Inland's Inland Lime & Stone Co. division in Gulliver, Mich.

Pfizer said it may start up the Gulliver mines in 1992. Inland plans to buy stone from the operation when production resumes.
