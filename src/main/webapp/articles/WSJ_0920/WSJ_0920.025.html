Kinark Drops Rights Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Kinark Corp. said that its board terminated the company's shareholder rights plan. The board also ordered the redemption of all outstanding common stock-purchase rights issued pursuant to the plan and held by shareholders of record at the close of business Oct. 15, at one cent per right, payable in cash Nov. 1.

The rights were issued in December 1989 to fend off a takeover attempt.

Kinark is a chemical and steel galvanizing concern.
