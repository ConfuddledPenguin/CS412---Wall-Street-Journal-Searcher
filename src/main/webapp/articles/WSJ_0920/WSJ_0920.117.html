Business Brief -- Hycor Biomedical Inc.: Allen & Co. Is Retained As Firm's Financial Adviser

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

Hycor Biomedical Inc. said it retained Allen & Co to act as its financial adviser in various investment banking efforts. The biotechnology company, based in Garden Grove, Calif., said Allen & Co. will provide assistance in debt or equity offerings, acquisitions, capital restructuring and joint ventures.

Richard D. Hamill, chairman, president and chief executive officer, said the company is "exploring a number of opportunities including acquisitions" but he declined to be specific.

Mr. Hamill said that there is a consolidation in biotechnology companies and he said the company wants to be a player. "We want to be one of the acquirers instead of one of the acquirees," said Mr. Hamill.
