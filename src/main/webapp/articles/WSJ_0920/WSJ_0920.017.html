Waste Management Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- Waste Management Inc. said its board authorized the repurchase of up to 25 million, or 5.3%, of its common shares outstanding over the next two years. The waste-handling giant said that, including the approximately 14.5 million shares it recently issued as part of an earlier pact to boost its stake in Wheelabrator Technologies Inc. to 55% from 22%, Waste Management currently has about 486 million common shares outstanding.
