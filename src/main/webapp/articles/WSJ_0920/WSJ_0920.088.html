REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Iraqi R&R

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

Latvia says it sighted two Iraqi missile ships training with their Soviet teachers in the waters just off Bolderaja, a suburb of Riga. Latvia's independence-minded government immediately passed a resolution condemning the event and demanded that all Iraqi soldiers "leave the territory of the republic of Latvia." Moscow itself has said it would continue to honor some old "contracts" with Iraq. We suppose that in the larger geopolitical picture, the impact of a Baltic republic's criticism of landlocked Iraq's navy doesn't loom large. But as the West sorts out the question of who its new friends are, it's worth noting that the Latvians had little trouble deciding whose side they're on.
