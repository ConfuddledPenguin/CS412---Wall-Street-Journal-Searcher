Lubrizol Upheld in Court Case

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

CLEVELAND -- Lubrizol Corp. said a Canadian federal court ruled that Exxon Corp.'s Canadian affiliate infringed on a Lubrizol patent for certain lubricants and additives. The court permanently enjoined the Exxon unit from making, using and selling about 20 additives and the finished lubricants containing them. A temporary injunction has been in effect since January 1989.

The court also ruled that Lubrizol, a specialty chemical company, is entitled to court costs and damages for the infringement. The amount will be decided by a court-appointed referee at a date to be determined later.
