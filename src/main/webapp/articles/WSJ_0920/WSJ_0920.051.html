Who's News: Pacific Gas & Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

PACIFIC GAS & ELECTRIC Co. (San Francisco) -- Barry Lawson Williams, president of Williams Pacific Ventures Inc., was elected a director, increasing board membership to 17. Separately, Ronald G. Domer, manager of hydro engineering and construction, was elected vice president, engineering. He succeeds William H. Wallace, who is taking early retirement. PG&E is engaged principally in supplying electric and natural gas service throughout northern and central California.
