East German Producer Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

FRANKFURT -- Producer prices in East Germany were down 50% in July from the average for all of 1989, the East German state news agency ADN reported. Citing data from the East German Statistics Office, ADN said this is the first time East Germany has published producer price data. Because of the lack of reliable monthly data for 1989, the year-earlier figures used for comparison are averages for the entire year.

As the two Germanys speed toward a merger on Oct. 3, the East Germans have been scrambling to produce accurate data that conform as closely as possible to Western economic reporting standards.

ADN said the Statistics Office surveyed 1,900 companies for its price report.
