Nike Stock Split Is Cleared

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

BEAVERTON, Ore. -- Nike Inc. said its shareholders approved a threefold increase in authorized Class A and B common shares, clearing the way for the company's 2-for-1 stock split. In June, Nike's board approved the split, to be distributed Oct 5.

Nike makes athletic footwear and apparel.
