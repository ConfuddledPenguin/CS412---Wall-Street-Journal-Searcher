Nynex Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

NEW YORK -- Nynex Corp. said it completed its previously announced acquisition of Stockholder Systems Inc., Norcross, Ga., for stock valued at about $65.3 million. Terms of the agreement call for Stockholder Systems holders to receive Nynex common shares valued at $15.18 for each of the 4.3 million Stockholder Systems shares outstanding.

Stockholder Systems, a marketer of financial-application software, has 200 employees and annual sales of about $26 million.
