Business Brief -- Home Savings Bank: Holders Vote to Approve Acquisition by Ahmanson

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

Home Savings Bank shareholders cleared the acquisition of the company by H.F. Ahmanson & Co., the Los Angeles parent of Home Savings of America. The companies definitively agreed on the transaction in April; completion, which is expected by year end, is subject to regulatory clearances. Home Savings Bank is based in Queens, N.Y.

Under terms, Home Savings Bank holders are to receive about $25 of Ahmanson stock for each of their shares, or about $300 million.

The offer price is subject to adjustment based on Ahmanson's stock price in the 10 trading days prior to closing: If the figure is $24.64 or more, Home Savings Bank holders get $28 a share; if the figure is $16.92 or less, Home Savings Bank can decide not to complete the transaction.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Ahmanson shares fell 12.5 cents to close at $15.875. In national over-the-counter trading, Home Savings Bank shares closed unchanged at $17.875.
