Who's News: CSP Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

CSP Inc. (Billerica, Mass.) -- Samuel Ochlis, president and chief executive officer, will retire by Sept. 1, 1991. Mr. Ochlis will be a consultant to the company after retiring from the two posts. CSP directors have formed a search committee to find a successor to Mr. Ochlis, the company said. CSP makes array processors for computers.
