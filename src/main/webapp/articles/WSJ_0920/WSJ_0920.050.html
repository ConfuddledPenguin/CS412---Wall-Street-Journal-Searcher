Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Bryan L. Knapp was named executive vice president of human resources, a new post, at the Shearson Lehman Brothers division serving retail and asset-management clients. Shearson is a subsidiary of this travel, financial and information-services company. Mr. Knapp, 46 years old, was previously first vice president responsible for human resources support to the business and staff division at Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc.
