Technology: Lotus Releases New Software

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

CAMBRIDGE, Mass. -- Lotus Development Corp. said it released a new version of its 1-2-3 Release 2.2 spreadsheet software tailored for use in Eastern Europe. The new software has added characters to form words in Czech, Slovak, German and other languages used in the region. Lotus also said it named Vupek of Prague as a Lotus authorized dealer.
