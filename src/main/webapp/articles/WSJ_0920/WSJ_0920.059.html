WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Baxter International Inc., shelf offering of up to $550 million in senior unsecured debt securities.

PepsiCo Inc., shelf offering for as much as $1.5 billion in debt securities, consisting of notes, debentures or unsecured evidences of indebtedness and warrants to purchase debt securities.

REN Corporation-USA, offering of 2,850,000 shares of common stock, of which two million will be sold by the company and 850,000 by holders, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. and Equitable Securities Corp.
