Del Webb 9.7% Stake Is Bought

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

PHOENIX -- Del Webb Corp. said that D.S. Kennedy & Co., a San Francisco money manager, acquired a 9.7% stake in Del Webb stock from Industrial Equity (Pacific) Ltd., a Hong Kong investment firm. D.S. Kennedy bought 910,000 shares late Monday at $6.375 apiece, said Del Webb, a developer of adult communities.
