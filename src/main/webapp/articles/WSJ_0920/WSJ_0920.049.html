Zurn Workers Ratify Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

ERIE, Pa. -- Zurn Industries Inc. said steelworkers at its steam generating equipment plant here ratified a new, four-year contract. The contract, approved by members of United Steelworkers of America Local 3199, ends a walkout that began Sept. 1.

The company said that as a result of the walkout, fiscal second-quarter earnings will be lower than last year's $7.1 million, or 57 cents a share.
