Who's News: Security Pacific Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

SECURITY PACIFIC Corp. (Los Angeles) -- Ronald Azzolina, president of the Security Pacific Bancorp. Southwest and Security Pacific Bank Arizona units of this bank holding company, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer. Mr. Azzolina, 47 years old, succeeds Robert L. Matthews, 52, who continues as chairman of the units. Also, James P. Albo, 41, formerly executive vice president of the Arizona bank unit, was named vice chairman and chief operating officer of the two units. Mr. Azzolina was formerly chief operating officer, and the post of vice chairman is a new one. Security Pacific Bancorp. Southwest is the holding company for Security Pacific banks in Arizona and Nevada.
