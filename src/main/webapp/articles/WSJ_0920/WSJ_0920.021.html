International: Credit Lyonnais Buys Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

MADRID -- Credit Lyonnais S.A. of France agreed to buy 40% of Iberagentes, one of Spain's leading brokerage firms, as part of a strategy to extend its world-wide brokerage network. The acquisition, for an undisclosed price, will be effected through a capital increase, Iberagentes said. Credit Lyonnais has an option to raise its holding to 50%.

Iberagentes, which had capital plus reserves of 1.44 billion pesetas ($14.7 million) as of June 30, has 130 employees and 12 offices in Spain.
