Who's News: Modine Names Savage To Added Top Post

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

RACINE, Wis.-- Modine Manufacturing Co. said Richard T. Savage, president and chief operating officer, will be named to the additional post of chief executive officer at the end of December. Mr. Savage, 52 years old, succeeds Earl E. Richter, who will retire from the post.

Mr. Richter, 67, will continue as chairman of the board. He joined Modine in 1948 and has held a variety of positions with the maker of automotive parts and heating and air conditioning equipment.

Mr. Savage joined Modine in 1970 and has held his current posts since February 1989.
