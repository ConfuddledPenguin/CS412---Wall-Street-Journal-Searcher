Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- Richard B. Williams, 45 years old, vice president, U.S. marketing, at Unisys Corp., was named senior vice president, corporate strategy, a new post at this business information, publishing and marketing services company, effective Oct. 1.
