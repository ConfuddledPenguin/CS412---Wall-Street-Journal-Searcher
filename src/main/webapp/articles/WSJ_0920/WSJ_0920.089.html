CPC International Taps Shoemate as Chairman

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

ENGLEWOOD CLIFFS, N.J. -- CPC International Inc. named Charles R. Shoemate to the additional post of chairman, succeeding James R. Eiszner, who died Sept. 11. Mr. Shoemate, president of CPC since late 1988, had been elected chief executive officer last month. At the time, he said he intended to "keep CPC following the strategy" set by Mr. Eiszner's leadership of the $5.1 billion consumer food and corn refining company.

Mr. Shoemate, 50 years old, joined CPC in 1962 and has held a number of manufacturing, finance and business management positions.

CPC's best-known brands include Hellmann's and Best Foods mayonnaise, Skippy peanut butter, Mazola corn oil, Thomas's English muffins and Knorr soups.
