CRSS Unit's Acquisition Bid

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

HOUSTON -- NaTec Resources Inc. said it offered to acquire Advance Ross Corp., Chicago, through a stock swap. Although an exchange ratio hasn't been determined, a spokesman for NaTec said the rate would be constructed so that Advance Ross shareholders would receive a premium of more than 20% over recent trading levels. Advance Ross, primarily an investment concern, holds about 13.7% of NaTec, or about 3.4 million shares, and is the company's second largest shareholder. NaTec, a unit of CRSS Inc. of Houston, has about 25 million shares outstanding.
