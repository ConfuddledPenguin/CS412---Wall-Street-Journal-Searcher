Who's News: Louisiana Land & Exploration Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

LOUISIANA LAND & EXPLORATION Co. (New Orleans) -- Retired Adm. Carlisle A. H. Trost, 60 years old, was named to the board of this oil and natural gas exploration concern. The appointment of the former chief of naval operations, who retired in July, expands the board to 12 members.
