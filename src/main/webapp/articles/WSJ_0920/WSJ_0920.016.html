Who's News: Quadrax Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

QUADRAX Corp. (Portsmouth, R.I.) -- Stephen W. Tsai has been elected to the board of directors of this maker of composite materials, expanding the board to four members from three. Mr. Tsai, a scientist in the field of advanced composites, is a professor of aeronautics and astronautics at Stanford University.
