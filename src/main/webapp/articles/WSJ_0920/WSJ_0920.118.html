Business Brief -- Tyco Toys Inc.: Toy Maker Agrees to Buy Nasta for Sweetened Price

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

Tyco Toys Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire Nasta International Inc. for a sweetened price of $1.25 per share, up from $1 a share, and extended the offer until Oct. 3. Tyco Toys of Mount Laurel, N.J., said the companies also reached an agreement, subject to certain conditions, to settle three lawsuits brought in response to Tyco's original tender offer. The settlement reflects the new offer price and includes an agreement to pay certain attorney fees and expenses.

As of Sept. 17, 607,965 shares were tendered under the original offer, which would increase Tyco's stake in New York-based Nasta to 86%, from its current 74% stake. The offer is not conditioned on any minimum number of shares being tendered and the new price applies to shares already tendered at the previous price.
