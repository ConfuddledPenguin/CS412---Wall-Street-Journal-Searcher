GE Finance Unit Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- GE Capital Corp., the financial services unit of General Electric Co., said it completed the acquisition of Pegasus Broadcasting Inc., Northbrook, Ill. Terms weren't disclosed. Pegasus, which has annual sales of about $65 million, consists of three independent television stations in San Juan, Puerto Rico; Augusta, Ga.; and Sacramento, Calif. Two radio stations in Helena, Mont., are being retained by the previous shareholders.

GE Capital said Christopher J. Brennan, president and chief executive officer of Pegasus, resigned, but the stations' managements are expected to continue in their current capacities.
