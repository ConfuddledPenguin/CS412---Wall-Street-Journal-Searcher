Federal Paper Closes Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

MONTVALE, N.J. -- Federal Paper Board Co. completed the purchase of Continental Bondware Inc., a producer of paper cups, from closely held Peter Kiewit Sons Inc., Omaha. Terms of the transaction, which was announced last month, weren't disclosed. Continental Bondware's annual sales are about $180 million.

Federal, with 1989 sales of $1.2 billion, manufactures paperboard, pulp, cartons, lumber, paper cups and paper. Peter Kiewit Sons has interests in construction, mining, timberlands and packaging.
