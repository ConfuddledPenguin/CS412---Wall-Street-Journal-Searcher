Treasury Will Offer $19.75 Billion of Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $2.62 billion in new cash with the sale of about $11.5 billion in two-year notes Tuesday and $8.25 billion in four-year notes Wednesday to redeem $17.13 billion in maturing notes. The notes mature on Sept. 30, 1992, and Sept. 30, 1994, respectively.

Tenders for the two-year notes are available Tuesday in minimum denominations of $5,000 and must be received by 1 p.m. EDT.

Tenders for the four-year notes are available Wednesday in minimum denominations of $1,000 and must be received by 1 p.m. EDT.
