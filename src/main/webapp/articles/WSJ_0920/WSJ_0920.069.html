Who's News: Bayly's Warren Adds Post of Chief Executive

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

DENVER -- Bayly Corp. said its president, Everett G. Warren, had assumed the additional post of chief executive officer. The position was previously held by George K. Hansen, who remains chairman, the company said. "The board of directors chose to make a change," David Houseman, Bayly chief financial officer, said of the action. Mr. Hansen could not be reached for comment.

In a prepared release, the apparel manufacturer said the change was part of a realignment of executive responsibilities begun Feb. 1 when Mr. Warren was brought into Bayly as president and chief operating officer.

Last week, the company announced a widened net loss of $4.2 million, or $1.97 per share, on revenue of $12 million for the quarter ended July. 31. In the year-earlier period, Bayly reported a net loss of $974,000, or 42 cents a share, on revenue of $20.3 million.
