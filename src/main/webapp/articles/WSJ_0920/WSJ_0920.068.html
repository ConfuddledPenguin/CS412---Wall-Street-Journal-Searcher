Dividend News: American Southwest Mortgage Investments Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

AMERICAN SOUTHWEST MORTGAGE INVESTMENTS Corp. declared a quarterly dividend of 22 cents a share, payable Oct. 15 to stock of record Sept. 28. The dividend is equal to a 23% increase from the total of 17 cents a share the real-estate investment trust made in three monthly payments in its second quarter. American Southwest, based in Tucson, Ariz., said it changed to a quarterly dividend policy to reduce administrative costs and to make the dividend comparable to other trusts.
