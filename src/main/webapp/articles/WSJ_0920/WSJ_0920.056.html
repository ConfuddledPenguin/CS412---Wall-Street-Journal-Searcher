Who's News: Fourth Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

FOURTH FINANCIAL Corp. (Wichita, Kan.) -- K. Gordon Greer, president of Bank IV Wichita, was named to the added post of president of this bank holding company, succeeding Thomas R. Clevenger, who resigned but remains as a consultant and board member. Additionally, Mr. Greer was elected to the board, increasing membership to 10.
