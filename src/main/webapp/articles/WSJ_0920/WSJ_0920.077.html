National Convenience Stores

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

HOUSTON -- National Convenience Stores Inc. said it had signed a letter of intent to sell 28 convenience stores in El Paso, Texas, to a unit of El Paso Refinery, L.P. Terms weren't disclosed. The convenience store operator said the transaction is subject to the negotiation and execution of a definitive asset purchase agreement with the refining concern.
