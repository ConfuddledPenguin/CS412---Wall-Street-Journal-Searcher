Business Brief -- Vitalink Communications Corp.: Firm Lays Off 32 Workers, Trims Number of Offices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

Facing fewer orders and declining profits, Vitalink Communications Corp. said it laid off 10% of its work force, or 32 employees, and cut back the number of its sales offices. In the last six months, the Fremont, Calif., company has weathered a series of setbacks: First, it had to reduce prices on some hardware products by 15%, then its chief executive officer resigned, and net income in the last quarter plunged 50% from the previous year.

Vitalink's current chief executive, Donald J. Herman, said that with the savings from firing employees, the company would invest more heavily in developing new products.

Vitalink makes systems used to connect large computer networks.
