Morgan's Foods Begins Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

CLEVELAND -- Morgan's Foods Inc., which operates 73 Kentucky Fried Chicken franchises, began its rights offering to shareholders. Under the offering, each stockholder may buy one share of common stock at $1 for every two shares owned. The offer applies to stock of record Sept. 18. The rights offering will remain open until 5 p.m. EST on Oct. 10.

From Oct. 11 through Oct. 18, directors of the company may buy any remaining unsubscribed shares. Beginning Oct. 19, any unpurchased rights shares plus up to 600,000 common shares will be offered through Whale Securities Co. The offering will close Nov. 21 at 5 p.m.
