Amcast $16.5 Million Provision

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Amcast Industrial Corp., citing higher health costs and the cost of closing iron foundries, said it will take a special fourth-quarter provision estimated at $16.5 million, or $2.43 a share. The company, which serves the defense, construction and manufacturing sectors, plans a reorganization. Leo Ladehoff, Amcast's chairman and chief executive officer, will also serve as president, effective today. He succeeds Raymond J. Birkholz, who is leaving the company.
