Who's News: Paramount Communications Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

PARAMOUNT COMMUNICATIONS Co. (New York) -- Peter J. Boni was named president of software and information services and L. William Black was named president of business information and publishing, two new divisions of Simon & Schuster, Paramount's book publishing subsidiary. Mr. Boni, 44 years old, was previously president and chief operating officer for On Line Software International Inc., Fort Lee, N.J. Mr. Black, 44, previously was managing director for the U.S. division of WEKA Publishing Inc., a privately held German publisher of business reference products.
