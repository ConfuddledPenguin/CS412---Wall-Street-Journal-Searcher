FORD MOTOR Co. (Dearborn, Mich.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it has placed about $5.2 billion in securities issued by this auto maker's finance arm under review for a possible ratings downgrade. The move follows Tuesday's announcement that Moody's is reviewing the ratings of $40 billion in Ford debt for possible downgrade in the wake of weak U.S. car sales. A Ford spokesman said yesterday that the company believes both actions are unwarranted.
