Business Brief -- Inspiration Resources Corp.: Western Gold Will Close Alaskan Mining Operation

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

Inspiration Resources Corp., New York, said Western Gold Exploration & Mining Co., a limited partnership of Luxembourg-based Minorco S.A. and Inspiration, plans to close its offshore gold mining operation in Alaska. As a result, Inspiration, a diversified agricultural and mining concern, said it may take a charge, which hasn't been determined, by the end of the year. Last year, the company earned $25.2 million, or 38 cents a share, on revenue of $1.42 billion.

Western Gold said it "reluctantly decided to end operations, due to low gold prices and persistent losses from the operation." Since the mining began off the coast of Nome, Alaska, in 1986, the offshore vessel has recovered about 130,000 ounces of gold. Last year, the operation recovered about 70,000 ounces.
