Gleason Agrees to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Gleason Corp. said a signed agreement to sell its tool and mold unit to Gen Cap America Inc., Nashville, Tenn., will boost its 1990 earnings. Last year, the gear production concern earned $2.69 a share. The unit, Alliance Carolina Tool & Mold Division, manufactures tools, dies, molds and plastic components in the southeastern United States. Terms of the sale will not be disclosed.
