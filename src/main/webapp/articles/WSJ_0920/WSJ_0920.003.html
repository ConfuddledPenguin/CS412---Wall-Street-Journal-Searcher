Timken Co. Sets Outlays

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

CANTON, Ohio -- Timken Co. said it will invest $40 million at its Latrobe Steel Co. unit in Latrobe, Pa., as part of its previously announced $1 billion, five-year capital investment program. Timken said it will build a 53,000-square-foot plant to produce billet and bar products.

Latrobe Steel supplies specialty steel products to the aerospace, high-performance bearing and other critical application markets. Timken makes antifriction bearings for the agricultural and automotive markets.
