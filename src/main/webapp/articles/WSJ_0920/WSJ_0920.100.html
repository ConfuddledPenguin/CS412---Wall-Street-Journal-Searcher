Business Brief -- Allergan Inc.: Firm to Close a Lens Plant And Transfer the Operations

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

Allergan Inc. said it will close its contact lens manufacturing facility in Woodbury, N.Y., over the next year and lay off 70 employees. The Irvine, Calif., maker of eye and skin-care products said it will transfer the operations to Allergan facilities in Irvine and in Waco, Texas. The company consolidated its manufacturing operations in Europe into one plant in Britain last year.

Allergan said the move is part of its effort to streamline operations. Last January, the company announced a restructuring plan under which it would reduce its work force, consolidate certain manufacturing operations and curtail businesses that were marginally profitable.
