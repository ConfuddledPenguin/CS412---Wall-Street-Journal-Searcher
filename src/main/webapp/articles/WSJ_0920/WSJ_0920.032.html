Technology: GM Unit Computer Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

DALLAS -- Electronic Data Systems Corp. and Australian-based Mincom said they formed an alliance to jointly market computer services and software to the mining, utility and energy industries. EDS, a subsidiary of General Motors Corp., said it will contribute its data processing and communications capabilities to the relationship, while Mincom will contribute software designed specifically for mining, utility and oil operations.
