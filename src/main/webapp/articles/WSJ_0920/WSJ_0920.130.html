Business Brief -- SFE Technologies: President Quits Board, Cites Plan for Sale of a Division

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

Michael Rosenberg, president of SFE Technologies, resigned from the board and said he is approaching several investor groups with a plan to buy the company's San Fernando Electric division. Mr. Rosenberg, 59 years old, said he resigned from the board to avoid a conflict of interest. However, he said he will continue as president and chief operating officer. A spokesman said the company doesn't plan to name a successor to the board.

William Kuehn, chairman, said the San Fernando, Calif., maker of electronic parts will continue its efforts to restructure by raising capital, restructuring its debt and selling certain operations.
