Hess Oil Virgin Islands Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/20/90

WASHINGTON -- Hess Oil Virgin Islands Corp. received a $29.7 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

FMC Corp. got a $21 million Army contract for tank components.

General Electric Co. was issued a $20.9 million Navy contract for tank driver trainers.

Pentastar Electronics Inc. received a $19.7 million Army contract for tank electrical test sets.

Aerojet Corp. got a $19.1 million Army contract for Hawk missile motors.

Thiokol Corp. received an $11.8 million Army contract for mortar rounds.
