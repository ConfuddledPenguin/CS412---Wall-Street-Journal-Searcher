Technology Brief -- Mitsubishi Electric Corp.: Firm Plans Spending Boost To Raise Chip Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/20/90

Mitsubishi Electric Corp. plans to nearly double its investment in semiconductor capacity in the current fiscal year to increase production of popular four-megabit dynamic random access memory chips. A Mitsubishi spokesman said the Tokyo concern will spend about 69 billion yen ($500.4 million) to increase production, a rise of 47% from the 47 billion yen budgeted at the beginning of the year, which began April 1. Almost all of the increase will be spent to expand capacity for four-megabit DRAMs, the spokesman said.

Mitsubishi aims to increase capacity to 500,000 memory chips a month by the end of the fiscal year from its current level of 300,000 a month.
