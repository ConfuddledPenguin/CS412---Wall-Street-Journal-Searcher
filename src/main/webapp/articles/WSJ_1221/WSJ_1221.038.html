Yamanouchi to Buy Drug Line

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

TOKYO -- Yamanouchi Pharmaceutical Co., as part of its strategy to widen further its international operations and expand its new product base, said it agreed to buy the pharmaceutical division of Royal Gist-brocades N.V. of the Netherlands. The price wasn't disclosed. A Yamanouchi spokesman said the purchase of the division, which has operations in Western Europe and the U.S., is expected to be completed by the end of February. Established in 1870, Royal Gist-brocades had 1989 sales of about 1.9 million guilders ($1.1 million), of which its pharmaceutical division accounted for 13%, or about 240 million guilders, Yamanouchi said.
