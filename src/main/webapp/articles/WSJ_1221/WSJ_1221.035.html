Samsung Electronics Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- Samsung Electronics Co. said it received a $150 million order to build a compressor plant for Calex Narodni Bodnik Co. of Czechoslovakia. Samsung will install an automatic production line capable of turning out 1.5 million compressors a year for refrigerators, the company said. It said the plant will be built in Zlape in the Slovakian Republic by the end of 1991.

Samsung also said it expects to supply Calex with parts for compressors when the plant goes into operation. Calex is Czechoslovakia's sole state-run refrigerator maker. Samsung Electronics is a subsidiary of Samsung Group, a leading South Korean industrial concern.
