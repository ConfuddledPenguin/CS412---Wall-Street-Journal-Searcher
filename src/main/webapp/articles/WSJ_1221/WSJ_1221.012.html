Canada Steel Output Rises 12%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 217,218 metric tons in the week ended Dec. 15, up 12% from 194,333 tons the previous week but a 14% decline from 253,443 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to 2,204.62 pounds. From Jan. 1 through Dec. 15, production totaled 11,639,297 tons, a 21% decline from 14,799,778 tons a year earlier. The production decline resulted from steel-plant strikes and Canada's economic slowdown.
