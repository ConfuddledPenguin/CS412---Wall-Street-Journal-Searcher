Who's News: HBO & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

HBO & Co. (Atlanta) -- Charles W. McCall was elected president and chief executive officer of this provider of hospital information systems. Mr. McCall, 46 years old, fills a position that has been open since the end of last year, when Walter Huff, the company's founder, retired. Mr. McCall is currently president and chief executive of Compuserve, a subsidiary of H&R Block Inc.
