Dividend News: UJB Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

UJB FINANCIAL Corp.'s directors sharply reduced the Princeton, N.J., bank holding company's quarterly dividend because of concerns about the weak real estate market and the economy. UJB also said it expects to break even or report a modest loss for all of 1990. For 1990's first nine months, UJB reported profit plunged 88% to $11.3 million, or 22 cents a share. The quarterly payout was cut to 15 cents a common share from the 29 cents that had been paid for the previous five quarters. The dividend is payable Feb. 1 to stock of record Jan. 8. UJB Financial, with assets exceeding $13 billion, has more than 270 offices in New Jersey and eastern Pennsylvania. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, UJB's shares fell 37.5 cents to $7.625.
