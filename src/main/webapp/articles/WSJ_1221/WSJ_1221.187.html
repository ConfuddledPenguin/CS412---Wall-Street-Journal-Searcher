Adelaide Steamship Associate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

SYDNEY, Australia -- Petersville Sleigh Ltd., a food, resources and shipping concern that is an associate of Adelaide Steamship Co., said it sold its entire stake in Howard Smith Ltd., another associate, for 167 million Australian dollars (US$128.9 million). The shares in Howard Smith, a shipping and engineering concern, were sold to local and overseas institutions for about A$3.95 a share.

Adelaide Steamship announced in November a plan to cut debt by A$3 billion and restructure. It had been criticized by analysts for its complex holdings among associate companies and said it would eliminate the cross-ownership.

Bob Cumberlidge, managing director of Petersville Sleigh, said the sale price reflects current market value and gives the company A$1.5 million in net proceeds that will be used to reduce debt.
