P.H. Glatfelter Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

SPRING GROVE, Pa. -- P.H. Glatfelter Co., a maker of printing, writing, and specialty papers, said its directors authorized the repurchase of an additional one million common shares in market or private transactions. At present, authorization still exists under previous decisions to repurchase 335,863 shares. The company has 22.6 million shares outstanding.
