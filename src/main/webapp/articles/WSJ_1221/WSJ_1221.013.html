Who's News: ABB Asea Brown Boveri Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

ABB ASEA BROWN BOVERI Ltd. (Zurich) -- Donald E. Bogle was named president and chief executive of ABB Process Automation Inc., a unit of Asea Brown Boveri Inc., which in turn is a Stamford, Conn., subsidiary of this electrical engineering company. Mr. Bogle, 45 years old, succeeds Herbert Kahn, who continues as an executive vice president. Mr. Bogle had been a vice president at Honeywell Inc., Minneapolis.
