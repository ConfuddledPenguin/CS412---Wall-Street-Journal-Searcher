Accel International Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

DUBLIN, Ohio -- Accel International Corp. said it purchased 486,202 of its common shares at $9 each from one of its directors, Eli Zborowski. The insurance concern noted that the purchase price was less than the company's $11.63 a share book value as of Sept. 30, and that Mr. Zborowski resigned as a director following the transaction. Accel has 4,424,260 common shares outstanding, following the completion of the recent purchase.
