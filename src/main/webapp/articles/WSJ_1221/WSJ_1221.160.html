Dividend News: Russ Togs Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

RUSS TOGS Inc. said it omitted its common stock dividend for the current quarter. The New York-based apparel company paid a dividend of five cents a share in the previous quarter. Ken Sitomer, president and chief executive officer, said, "Faced with a volatile retail environment and a difficult fourth quarter, the company decided that these funds would be used to support our new divisions, as well as a hedge against the future."
