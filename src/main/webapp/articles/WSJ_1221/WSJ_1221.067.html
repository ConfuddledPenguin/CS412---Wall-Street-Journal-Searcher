Letters to the Editor: How Would U.N. Have Treated Hitler?

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

Is hindsight really 20/20? Had there been a United Nations ready to assemble when Hitler invaded Austria, would the resolution then have read: "Noting that despite all efforts by the United Nations, Hitler refuses to comply with 11 separate resolutions, the Security Council therefore:

(1) demands that Hitler comply with the resolutions;

(2) allows Hitler one final opportunity as a pause of good will;

(3) authorizes all necessary means to implement the United Nations resolutions unless Hitler stops by Jan. 15;

(4) requests that states concerned keep the council regularly informed of progress."

What really has our vision of history taught us?

Lawrence R. Gordon

Los Angeles
