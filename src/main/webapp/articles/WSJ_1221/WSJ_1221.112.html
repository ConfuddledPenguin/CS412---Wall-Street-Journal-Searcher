Who's News: Coca-Cola Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

COCA-COLA Co. (Atlanta) -- Peter S. Sealey, who was recently named director, global marketing, at this soft-drink company, was elected a corporate senior vice president. Ronald W. Allen, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Delta Air Lines, was nominated to stand for election as a Coca-Cola director. Shareholders will vote on the nomination at the annual meeting in April.
