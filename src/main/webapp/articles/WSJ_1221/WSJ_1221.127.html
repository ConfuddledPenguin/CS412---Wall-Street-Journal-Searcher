Sunshine Mining Unit's Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

DALLAS -- Sunshine Mining Co. said a subsidiary formed a joint venture with a subsidiary of Mallon Resources Corp. and Red Rock Ventures Inc., to explore for gold in the Guanacaste Province of Costa Rica. Pending the analysis of results from a 36-hole drilling program, Sunshine Mining has the option to acquire a 50% interest in and become manager of the venture. If Sunshine chooses to proceed, Mallon will retain a 35% interest in the project and Red Rock Ventures will retain a 15% stake.
