Sir Run Run Calls Plan for Macy Stake Only an Investment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

HONG KONG -- Sir Run Run Shaw views his plan to buy a stake in the R.H. Macy & Co. as only an investment, and he doesn't intend to participate in running the closely held New York retailer, a spokesman for the movie investor said. He is mulling, however, "other forms of participation," such as board representation, said the spokesman.

The spokesman acknowledged Macy's problems, including relatively heavy debt, but he said Sir Run Run regards these as short-term difficulties. "The company is well-managed. It's got fine franchises," he said. "We're confident in its longer-term prospects."

Macy said this week that it had attracted as much as US$187.6 million in new equity capital to be used to buy back some of its depressed high-yield junk bonds. Sir Run Run has agreed to buy $25 million of Macy's Series III preferred stock, with an option to invest $25 million more by March 31. He would become Macy's fourth-largest shareholder if the option is executed.

Macy also said that its nonmanagement investors have agreed to purchase $119 million of Series III preferred stock and that it has sold $18.6 million of Series II preferred stock to several shopping-center developers.

Sir Run Run, 83 years old, is among Hong Kong's wealthiest individuals. His fortune is founded on the entertainment business, but he also invests heavily in real estate. Macy is his first major investment in the retail business.

The Macy stake, like much of his wealth, is held privately. His principal listed vehicle, Shaw Brothers (Hong Kong) Ltd., owns movie theaters, produces movies and licenses film releases. His about 67% stake in the company, at current market prices, is valued at about HK$1 billion, or US$128.2 million).

Through Shaw Brothers, Sir Run Run also controls Television Broadcasts Ltd., which dominates Hong Kong's television-broadcasting industry.

The spokesman says Sir Run Run has no plans to boost his Macy stake beyond what has been provided for in the option.
