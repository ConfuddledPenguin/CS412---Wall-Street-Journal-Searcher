Who's News: May Department Stores Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

MAY DEPARTMENT STORES Co. (St. Louis) -- Roy R. Paulson was named chairman of the recently acquired Thalhimers department store division, succeeding William B. Thalhimer Jr., who is retiring. Mr. Paulson was succeeded as chairman of the L.S Ayres department store division by Clarence O. Reynolds, previously senior vice president, operations, at the Foley's division.
