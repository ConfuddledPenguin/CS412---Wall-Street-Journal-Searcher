Who's News: Ameritrust Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

AMERITRUST Corp. (Cleveland) -- Larry D. Peterson, president of the Southern Ohio Region of the Ameritrust Co. unit, was named chief executive officer of three units of the bank holding company, Ameritrust Indiana Corp., Ameritrust National Banks in Elkhart, Ind., and Ameritrust National Banks in Indianapolis. Thomas L. Dusthimer will remain chairman of these units.
