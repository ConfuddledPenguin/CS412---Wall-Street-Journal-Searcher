Business Brief -- Quick & Reilly Group Inc.: Fiscal 3rd-Quarter Net Fell 25%; Revenue Dropped 22%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

Quick & Reilly Group Inc., New York, citing negative fallout from the Persian Gulf crisis, posted a 25% decline in fiscal third-quarter net income on a 22% drop in revenue. Net income in the quarter, ended Nov. 30, was $2.1 million, or 22 cents a share, down from $2.8 million, or 29 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Revenue fell to $22.8 million from $29.3 million.

For the nine months, net income fell 1% to $8.8 million, or 93 cents a share, from $8.9 million, or 94 cents a share. Revenue rose 4% to $83.1 million from $79.9 million.

The brokerage company blamed the decline on lower commission and interest income, because of investor caution after Iraq's invasion of Kuwait in August.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Quick & Reilly closed at $11, up 50 cents.
