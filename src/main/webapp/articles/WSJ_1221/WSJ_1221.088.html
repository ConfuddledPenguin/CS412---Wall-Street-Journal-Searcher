Who's News: U S West

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

U S WEST (Englewood, Colo.) -- Roger Taylor was named managing director of marketing and new product development, a new position, at Financial Security Assurance, a New York financial service subsidiary of this telecommunications holding company. Mr. Taylor, previously an adviser to the company on municipal bond insurance, is founder and former executive vice president of Financial Guaranty Insurance Co., a subsidiary of FGIC Corp., a municipal-bond insurance unit of General Electric Co.
