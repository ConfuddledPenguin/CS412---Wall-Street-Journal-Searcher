TransNet Clears Big Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

UNION, N.J. -- TransNet Corp., which markets and leases personal computers, said directors authorized the purchase of as many as two million, or an indicated 40%, of its approximately five million common shares outstanding, in open-market and private buy-backs that will be funded from operations. Steven Wilk, president and chief executive officer, said the company is completing a reorganization. Despite consecutive quarterly losses since September 1988, Mr. Wilk said, he expects good revenue growth.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, TransNet shares closed at 93.75 cents, up 18.75 cents.
