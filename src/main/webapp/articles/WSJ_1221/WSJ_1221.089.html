Intital State Jobless Claims Fell in Week Ended Dec. 8

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for state unemployment insurance fell to a seasonally adjusted 455,000 in the week ended Dec. 8 from 471,000 in the previous week, the Labor Department said. In the week ended Dec. 1, the number of people receiving regular state unemployment benefits totaled 2,953,000, or 2.8% of those covered by unemployment insurance, down from 3,004,000 in the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate was 2.9%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended Dec. 1 fell to 2,989,000 from 3,094,000 in the previous week. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
