Storage Technology Credit Line

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

LOUISVILLE, Colo. -- Storage Technology Corp., a maker of data storage devices, said it expanded its line of credit to $100 million from $75 million. Terms of the new, three-year agreement weren't disclosed, but the company said the expanded line will lower its borrowing cost. Security Pacific National Bank is agent bank for the lending group, which also includes Continental Bank, First National Bank of Boston, Harris Trust & Savings, NCNB Texas National Bank, National Bank of Detroit and First Interstate Bank of Denver.
