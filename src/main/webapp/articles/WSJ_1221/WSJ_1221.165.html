Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

NEWSPAPERS: Ad revenue of Times Mirror Co.'s Newspaper Publishing Group for the four weeks ended Dec. 2 fell 1.9% to $138.2 million from $140.9 million in the corresponding period a year earlier. For the 48 weeks ended Dec. 2, ad revenue dropped 1.6% to $1.53 million vs. $1.56 million for the period ended Dec. 3, 1989. The more recent period includes 48 Sundays compared with 49 Sundays in the year-earlier period.

MAGAZINES: CV, a two-year-old magazine for college students, has ceased publication. It was owned by CareerVision Inc., a unit of Millicom Inc. Millicom said the magazine was being closed as part of a company plan to shut down unprofitable businesses. It was unclear whether other Millicom businesses would be affected.
