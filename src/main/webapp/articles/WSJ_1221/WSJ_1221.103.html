Who's News: Rockwell International Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

ROCKWELL INTERNATIONAL Corp. (El Segundo, Calif.) -- John B. Peller, vice president and associate center director of the Strategic Defense Center, was appointed president and center director of the Center. He will also serve as a member of the corporation's senior management council. Mr. Peller, 51 years old, succeeds George W. Jeffs, 65, who continues as a senior vice president and an adviser until his retirement on March 31. The Strategic Defense Center is the corporate technology center that utilizes the resources of Rockwell's business units. Rockwell is a multi-industry company that applies advanced technology to a wide range of products in its aerospace, electronics, automotive and graphics businesses.
