Who's News: First Western Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

FIRST WESTERN BANCORP (New Castle, Pa.) -- This bank holding company named four new directors, increasing its board to 18 members. They are James M. Campbell, 47 years old, president of Shenango Steel Buildings Inc., a steel fabrication company in West Middlesex, Pa.; James L. Gory, 57, currently president and owner, G&C Drainage Supplies Inc., a building supply company in Akron, Ohio; Richard C. McGill, 53, managing partner, McGill, Power, Bell & Co., an accounting firm; and Donald H. Mong, 67, chairman, First Federal of Western Pennsylvania. First Western recently acquired First Federal.
