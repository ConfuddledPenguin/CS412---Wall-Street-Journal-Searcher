Beneficial Unit Buys a Portfolio

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Beneficial Corp. said its Beneficial Homeowner Service Corp. unit in Brewster, N.Y., purchased a home-equity portfolio of loans totaling $44.8 million. The seller was First Tennessee Capital Assets Corp., a subsidiary of First Tennessee Bank of Memphis, in turn a unit of First Tennessee National Corp.

Terms weren't disclosed. It marks the third loan portfolio purchased by a Beneficial subsidiary since November.
