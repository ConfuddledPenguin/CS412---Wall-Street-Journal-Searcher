Bath Iron Works Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

WASHINGTON -- Bath Iron Works Corp. was issued a $72.5 million Navy contract for destroyer shipyard services.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. got a $52.5 million in contracts for Air Force aircraft testing and modification and for Army helicopter improvements.

Metro Machine Corp. received a $46.9 million Navy contract for ship-maintenance services.

Ingalls Shipbuilding Inc., a unit of Litton Industries Inc., was awarded a $28.7 million Navy contract for destroyer overhaul.

Hercules Engines Inc. got a $13.5 million Army contract for multifuel engines.

General Electric Co. was awarded a $12.7 million Army contract for transmissions.
