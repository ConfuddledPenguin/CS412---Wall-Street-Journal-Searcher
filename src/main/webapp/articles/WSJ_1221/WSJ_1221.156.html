Who's News: Thyssen Steel Detroit Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

THYSSEN STEEL DETROIT Co. (Oak Park, Mich.) -- Kenneth J. Graham was named president of the Thyssen Steel Group and related activities of the group in the U.S. and Mexico of this processor and distributor of Thyssen Stahl products to the U.S. automotive industry. Mr. Graham will continue as president and chief executive officer of Thyssen Steel Detroit, a unit of Thyssen AG, a German steel, manufacturing and trading company.
