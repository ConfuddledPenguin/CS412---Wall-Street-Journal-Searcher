Technology: Manville Licensing Agreement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

DENVER -- Manville Corp. said it signed long-term licensing agreements with Nippon Electric Glass of Japan, covering fiberglass reinforcement technology. Terms weren't disclosed.

The building and forest products company said the agreements provide Manville with all of NEG's fiberglass reinforcement technologies and gives the company the right to produce products based on those technologies in Europe and North America. Reinforced plastics are used primarily for transportation, construction, corrosion and marine applications.
