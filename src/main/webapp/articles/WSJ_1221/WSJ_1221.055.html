Zurn Industries Gets Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

ERIE, Pa. -- Zurn Industries Inc. said it was awarded a $27 million contract to construct water-treatment facilities. The maker of waste-control systems said its Advanco Constructors was awarded a contract by the Orange County (Calif.) Sanitation District. The project is scheduled to begin next month and be completed in 1993.
