ITT Bows to Non-Smokers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

BOSTON -- ITT Sheraton said it plans to increase the number of nonsmoking rooms at its hotels in the U.S. and Canada, in response to increased consumer requests. The company, a subsidiary of ITT Corp., New York, said it will boost the number of nonsmoking rooms to a minimum of 25%, up from 10%.
