United Television Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

LOS ANGELES -- United Television Inc. said its board boosted the TV-station owner's authorization to buy back its stock to almost 5% of its 10.7 million shares outstanding. The board authorized the company to buy an additional 500,000 shares, bringing its total authorization to 530,346 shares. United said it would buy the shares periodically, depending on market conditions.

United Television shares rose 25 cents to $28.50 in national over-the-counter trading yesterday.
