RTC Sells $883 Million In Mortgage-Backed Issues

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

WASHINGTON -- The Resolution Trust Corp. sold $883 million in mortgage-backed securities yesterday, the savings-and-loan cleanup agency said, in its largest single block sale. The agency holds about $12.7 billion in mortgage-backed securities, or about 9% of its assets, comprised from failed thrifts.

Yesterday's sale included Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. 9% securities, with original face value of $341 million, and Government National Mortgage Association 8.5% securities, with an original face value of $542 million.
