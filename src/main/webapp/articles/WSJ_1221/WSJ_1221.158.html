Southwest Airlines Credit Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

DALLAS -- Southwest Airlines said it finalized a new $215 million revolving credit agreement with Texas Commerce Bank as the lead lender. "The money is there for us to use, at our discretion, to fund company needs ranging from operational costs to aircraft acquisitions," said Herb Kelleher, Southwest's chairman. He added that the company doesn't have any current plans to use the credit.
