Who's News: Met-Pro Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

MET-PRO Corp. (Harleysville, Pa.) -- Edwin A. Wilcox was named president and chief operating officer of this maker of pollution-control and fluid handling systems. Mr. Wilcox, 52 years old, has previously been president of UGI Corp.'s AmeriGas Industrial Gas unit. He had been in his most recent position, which the company declined to identify, for two months. Mr. Wilcox succeeds Walter A. Everett as president. Mr. Everett, 68, will continue as chief executive officer and will become chairman; in the latter post, he succeeds E. J. Wofsey, 73, who was named general counsel and vice chairman.
