Who's News: CPC International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

CPC INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Englewood Cliffs, N.J.) -- Alain Labergere was named vice president of the parent and president of the Europe consumer foods division. Effective Jan. 1, Mr. Labergere succeeds Ronald K. Moss, who will retire from this international food company. Mr. Labergere, 56 years old, had been vice president of regional operations at the Europe consumer foods division.
