Business Brief -- Boeing Co.: Delta Air Orders 26 Jets For Total of $1.8 Billion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

Boeing Co. said Atlanta-based Delta Air Lines ordered 26 jetliners in a transaction with a total value of about $1.8 billion. The Seattle-based aerospace company said six of the orders for 767-300 twin-engine jets are new orders. Boeing also confirmed orders for three extended-range 767-300s, one 767-300, nine 757-200s and seven 737-300s that hadn't been previously announced.

Boeing said the Delta order brings its total orders for the year to $47.7 billion, setting a new one-year record. The previous record, set in 1989, was $46.62 billion. Boeing has announced orders for 543 jetliners this year.
