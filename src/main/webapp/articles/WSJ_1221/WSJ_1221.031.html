Sweden's AGA Buys Operations

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

STOCKHOLM -- AGA Gas Inc., U.S. subsidiary of the Swedish specialty gas company AGA AB, acquired two gas distribution operations near Detroit from Linde Gases of the Great Lakes Inc. Terms weren't disclosed. Linde is a Cleveland subsidiary of Union Carbide Industrial Gases Inc., which in turn is a subsidiary of Union Carbide Corp. Both the Industrial Gases unit and the parent chemicals company are based in Danbury, Conn.

AGA Gas Inc. has made nine acquisitions in the U.S. this year, accounting for total revenue of $35 million.

AGA AB also said its AGA-Cryo AB subsidiary, a producer of industrial and medical gas, has received a 23 million krona ($4.1 million) order to build two storage tanks for nitrogen and oxygen for the Swiss company Carbagas. The tanks, to be installed in an air-separation plant, are expected to be ready by late 1991.
