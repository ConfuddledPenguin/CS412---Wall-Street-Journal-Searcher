Money-Fund Assets Rose $856.2 Million During Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 532 money-market funds rose $856.2 million to $428.86 billion in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 267 general-purpose funds fell $14.2 million to $150.64 billion, and assets of 103 broker-dealer funds fell $19.1 million to $166.12 billion. Assets of 162 institutional funds rose $889.5 million to $112.10 billion.

Assets of institutional money-market funds continued to rise briskly, reflecting the "desire by institutional investors to (take advantage of) the relatively attractive money-market yields," said Jacob Dreyer, vice president and chief economist at the institute.
