Who's News: Medstone International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

MEDSTONE INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Irvine, Calif.) -- Richard Ferrari said he resigned as president and chief operating officer of this maker of renal lithotripsy systems. Mr. Ferrari, 36 years old, said he wanted to pursue "personal growth opportunities" in the wake of the company's recent decision to abandon efforts to obtain regulatory approval to market its lithotripsy systems for gallstone treatment. Medstone said a successor to Mr. Ferrari will be selected next month from among about four senior executives.
