Who's News: Pepsico Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

PEPSICO Inc. (Purchase, N.Y.) -- Randall C. Barnes, senior vice president, strategic planning, at this soft-drink, snack food and restaurant company, was named president, Pizza Hut International, a new post to oversee the overseas operations of the Pizza Hut Inc. unit, Wichita, Kan.
