Western Co. Gets Loan Facility

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

HOUSTON -- Western Co. of North America said it entered into loan agreements for $55 million with Chase Manhattan Bank and Den Norske Bank. The oil-field service company said it will use the proceeds to retire its remaining Series A notes totaling $46.4 million and for working capital. The financing is at floating interest rates with a $25 million revolving-credit facility and a $30 million three-year term facility.
