AT&T and PepsiCo Sign Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/21/90

BASKING RIDGE, N.J. -- American Telephone & Telegraph Co. said it signed an agreement to provide long-distance service for pay telephones at PepsiCo Inc. fast-food franchises. The three-year contract could be valued at about $32 million, depending on restaurant participation, AT&T said. The service will be offered at more than 13,700 Kentucky Fried Chicken, Taco Bell and Pizza Hut corporate and franchised locations.
