Who's News: Mine Safety Appliances Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/21/90

MINE SAFETY APPLIANCES CO. (Pittsburgh) -- Helen Lee Henderson was named to complete the term of director Benson G. Henderson, who retired effective Dec. 31. The term continues until the company's 1992 annual meeting. Mr. Henderson, who is Ms. Henderson's father, was named director emeritus of the producer of respiratory protective devices. The board has eight members.
