General Instrument Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

NEW YORK -- General Instrument Corp. said it plans to sell its transportation electronics division as part of a restructuring and debt-reduction plan. In the year ended Feb. 28, the division had sales of about $40 million. It makes automotive electronic displays. General Instrument was recently acquired by an investor group led by Forstmann, Little & Co.
