Who's News: Transcisco Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

TRANSCISCO INDUSTRIES Inc. (San Francisco) -- Ottokarl Finsterwalder, 54 years old, was named a director of this transportation concern. The appointment of Mr. Finsterwalder, managing director of the bank Creditanstalt-Bankverein of Vienna, increases board membership to six.
