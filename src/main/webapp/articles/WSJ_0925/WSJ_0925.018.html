Who's News: Coats Viyella PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

COATS VIYELLA PLC (London) -- This U.K. textiles concern said it appointed Neville Bain chief executive officer, effective Oct. 29. Mr. Bain is currently finance director of Cadbury Schweppes PLC, a British confections and drinks group. Mr. Bain is succeeding James McAdam, who is leaving the post of chief operating officer but will remain as deputy chairman of Coats Viyella.
