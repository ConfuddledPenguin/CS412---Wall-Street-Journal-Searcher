Aztec Manufacturing Co. Talks

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Aztec Manufacturing Co. said it is negotiating to purchase Calvert Co., Jackson, Miss. Proposed terms weren't announced. Aztec said completion is subject to an accord on an asset-purchase plan. Aztec sells oilfield gear and steel products. Calvert manufactures and sells bus ducts, which convey electrical current and are marketed primarily to the power-generation industry.
