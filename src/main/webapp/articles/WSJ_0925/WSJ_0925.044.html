Who's News: Votrax Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

VOTRAX Inc. (Farmington Hills, Mich.) -- Larry W. Jernigan, chief executive officer and president, resigned from the business-communications-systems maker, effective Oct. 12. Mr. Jernigan, 46 years old, has been with Votrax since September 1988. Votrax said he resigned to pursue other interests and indicated that the departure was amicable. The company is searching for a successor.
