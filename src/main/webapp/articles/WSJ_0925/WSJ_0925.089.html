Lyonnaise des Eaux, Dumez

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

PARIS -- Societe Lyonnaise des Eaux, a water treatment concern, and Dumez S.A., a building company, said shareholders approved their plan, unveiled in July, to merge and create a broad-based group capable of offering a full range of services, essentially to local and national governments, from water treatment to road building and urbanism. Under the plan, Lyonnaise des Eaux will absorb Dumez through a 4-for-3 share swap.
