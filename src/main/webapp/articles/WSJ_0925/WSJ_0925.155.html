AT&T Price of Calls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

NEW YORK -- American Telephone & Telegraph Co. said it will lower the price of calls to Canada from the U.S. under its "Reach Out World" plan by 10%, beginning Oct. 5. The company said the reduction is pending regulatory approval. Each call will cost 18 cents per minute for the first 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, the price will be reduced an additional 5%. The plan applies to calls made between 5 p.m. and 8 a.m. each weekday and all weekend.
