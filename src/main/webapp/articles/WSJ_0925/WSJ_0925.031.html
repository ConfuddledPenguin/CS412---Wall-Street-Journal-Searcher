Mannesmann Discusses Alliance

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Mannesmann AG, an engineering and machinery company, confirmed that it is holding talks with a number of companies about taking on a partner in "an alliance or cooperation" for its computer-related operations. The weekly news magazine Der Spiegel reported that Mannesmann is negotiating the sale of all or a majority of the shares in the computer operations in its Mannesmann Kienzle G.m.b.H. subsidiary.
