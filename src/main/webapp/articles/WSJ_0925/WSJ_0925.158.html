HILTON HOTELS Corp. (Beverly Hills, Calif.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it is reviewing for possible downgrade ratings on about $175 million of Hilton Hotels debt and the commercial paper rating of Hilton's Hotel Waldorf Astoria Corp. subsidiary. Moody's said it is concerned that softness in Hilton's hotel and casino businesses could result in reduced debt protection measurements over the intermediate term. Those concerns are deepened by the company's plan to buy back up to six million shares of its common stock, the rating agency said.
