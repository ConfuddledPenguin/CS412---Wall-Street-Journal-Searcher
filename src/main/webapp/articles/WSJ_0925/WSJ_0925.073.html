McDonnell Douglas Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. was given a $69.2 million increase to an Air Force contract for logistics support for KC-10 aircraft. The company also received a $37 million increase to a Navy contract for T-435 trainers.

Bell Helicopter-Textron Inc., a unit of Textron Inc., got a $62.3 million Army contract for 36 OH-58D production airframes.

Consolidated Electronic Countermeasures received a $21.7 million increase to a Navy contract for airborne self-protection jammers.

General Dynamics Land Systems, a division of General Dynamics Corp., was issued a $21.4 million modification to an Army contract for material for 315 Abrams tanks.
