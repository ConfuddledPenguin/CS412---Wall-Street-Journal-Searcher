Canadian Bankruptcies Increase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian bankruptcies rose 52% to 4,935 in August from 3,240 a year earlier, the Canadian Consumer and Corporate Affairs Department said. Individual bankruptcies totaled 3,943 and business bankruptcies 992 in the latest month.

In the first eight months of this year, Canadian bankruptcies rose 37% to 32,812 from 24,027 a year earlier.
