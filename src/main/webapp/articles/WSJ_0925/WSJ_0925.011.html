Who's News: Farah Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

FARAH Inc. (El Paso, Texas) -- This apparel maker appointed James C. Swaim senior vice president and chief financial officer. Mr. Swaim joined Farah in 1984 as vice president, finance, and was named treasurer in 1987. Christopher L. Carameros, the company's previous chief financial officer, will pursue other interests and continue to serve on the board.
