Who's News: Consolidated Freightways Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

CONSOLIDATED FREIGHTWAYS Inc. (Menlo Park, Calif.) -- G. Robert Evans and Robert D. Rogers were named board directors of this transportation concern. Mr. Evans, 59 years old, is president and chief executive officer of Corporate Finance Associates Illinois Inc. in St. Charles, Ill. Mr. Rogers, 63 years old, is president and chief executive officer of Texas Industries Inc. in Dallas. The two fill vacancies on the 13-member board.
