Sobering Thought

Prices paid for amusing little wines

Strike me as quite mysterious,

Since if paying that much for a bottle,

I'd want one that was serious.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

Bride and Grim

Modern marital mores

Make the old vows out of date;

The new promise at the altar is

"To love, honor and arbitrate."

-- George O. Ludcke.
