Who's News: Dataimage Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

DATAIMAGE INC. (Glastonbury, Conn.) -- William R. Funk Jr. resigned from the board, bringing membership to four, at this maker of parts for image-processing systems. In about two weeks, Dataimage expects to fill an existing vacancy and to name a successor to Mr. Funk, who left to pursue other interests.
