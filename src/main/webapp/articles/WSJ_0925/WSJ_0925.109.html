  • Date: 09/25/90

MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif. -- ASK Computer Systems Inc. said it agreed to allow EDS Corp. to market ASK products to EDS' manufacturing customers. Under the agreement, EDS will offer ASK's management-information systems, ASK said, while ASK customers will gain access to EDS' services in consulting, systems planning and systems integration. The pact comes a week after ASK announced it agreed to sell a 20% stake to EDS, a unit of General Motors Corp., and a 10% stake to Hewlett-Packard Co. in order to finance its acquisition of software maker Ingress Corp.
