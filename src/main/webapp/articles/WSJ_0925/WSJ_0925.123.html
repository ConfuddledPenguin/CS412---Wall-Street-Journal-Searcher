Amex Postpones Trading In Options on Japan Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

NEW YORK -- The American Stock Exchange delayed today's scheduled start of options trading on its Japan Index of 210 stocks. "We have regulatory clearance. We are just smoothing out some last-minute details," a spokeswoman for the Amex said, declining to be specific. The Amex said it will announce another launch date today. The trading is designed to allow investors to bet on the direction of Japan's stock market.

Meanwhile, the two big Chicago commodity exchanges this week are launching vehicles of their own for betting on the Japanese market.

The Chicago Mercantile Exchange today is to introduce futures and options on the Nikkei Index of 225 Tokyo stocks. And the Chicago Board of Trade plans to trade in futures and options on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Index of more than 1,200 stocks, starting Thursday night and continuing thereafter in day and night trading.
