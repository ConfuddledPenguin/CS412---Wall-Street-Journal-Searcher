Technology: Ball Corp. Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

MUNCIE, Ind. -- Ball Corp. said its aerospace systems unit was awarded a $24 million contract to build a thermally insulated container to cool an instrument on the Advanced X-ray Astrophysics Facility, which will be launched into space in 1997. The contract was let by the National Aeronautics and Space Administration's Goddard Space Flight Center.

Ball makes metal and glass containers, as well as aerospace and industrial products.
