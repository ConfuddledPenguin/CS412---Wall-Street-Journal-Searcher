Who's News: Rowland Worldwide

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

Rowland Worldwide (New York) -- Mitchell Kozikowski, president and chief executive officer of the public-relations company's U.S. operations, resigned, citing "philosophical business differences" over the growth of the company in the U.S. Herbert Rowland, chief executive of Rowland Worldwide, said the parting was amicable. The company will form an office of the president, consisting of the company's four executive vice presidents, to manage the New York office. Rowland, with offices in five continents, said it has annual billings of more than $50 million.
