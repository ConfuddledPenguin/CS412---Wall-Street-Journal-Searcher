Who's News: Kalinske Resigns Post At Universal Matchbox

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

NEW YORK -- Universal Matchbox Group Ltd., shedding a layer of management, said Thomas J. Kalinske, 46-year-old president and chief executive officer of its Matchbox International Ltd. unit, has resigned to pursue other interests. His resignation is effective Monday. As a result, managers of its international toy businesses, which were under Mr. Kalinske's direction, will report directly to David C.W. Yeh, chairman and chief executive officer of the parent, based in Hong Kong.

The company said the resignation and realignment of management are part of Matchbox's continuing efforts to streamline its operations.

The toy maker, known for its die-cast replica cars, also said Kenneth Bloom, chief financial officer of Matchbox International, was named executive vice president and general manager of Matchbox Toys Ltd., in Moonachie, N.J.
