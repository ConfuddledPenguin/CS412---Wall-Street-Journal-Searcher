Who's News: Young & Rubicam

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

YOUNG & RUBICAM Inc. (New York) -- Three senior executives were named to new posts at the Burson-Marsteller division. Andrew Cooper was named executive vice president for operations at the consulting and communications concern. Mr. Cooper, 43 years old, was previously general manager of Burson-Marsteller's New York unit. James E. Murphy, 54, was named chairman and chief executive officer of the New York unit. Mr. Murphy was previously vice chairman, corporate practice, for world-wide operations. William F. Noonan was named president and chief operating officer of the New York unit. Mr. Noonan, 58, was previously president of the international unit.
