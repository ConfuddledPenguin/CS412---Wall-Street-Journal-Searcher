Business Brief -- General Binding Corp.: Company to Buy VeloBind In $50 Million Transaction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

General Binding Corp., Northbrook, Ill., intends to acquire VeloBind Inc. for $50 million, or $9.77 a share, the companies said. VeloBind's stock crept up recently before the Fremont, Calif., maker of office binding systems acknowledged it was in merger talks. The stock price jumped $1.125 a share in over-the-counter trading to $7.50 on Friday, Sept. 14. The following Monday, when VeloBind disclosed it was in merger discussions with an unidentified company, the stock advanced another $1.125, to $8.625. It closed yesterday at $8.75, off 25 cents.

The proposed merger, expected to be completed by Dec. 28, needs the approval of VeloBind shareholders and government regulators. Proposed terms call for General Binding, a maker and marketer of business machines and related supplies, to buy all of VeloBind's shares outstanding and options to purchase shares.
