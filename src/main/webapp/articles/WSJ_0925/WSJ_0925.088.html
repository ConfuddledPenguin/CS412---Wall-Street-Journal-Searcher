Business Brief -- Watkins-Johnson Co.: California Firm Dismisses 150, or 5% of Work Force

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

Watkins-Johnson Co. said it dismissed 5% of its work force to compensate for lower growth in the company's defense-electronics markets. The Palo Alto, Calif., concern said it sent layoff notices yesterday to 150 employees in plants in California, North Carolina and Maryland. The company said it will absorb the cost of the move in its operating costs for the quarter ending Friday.

Watkins has businesses in defense electronics, semiconductor equipment and environmental consulting. Its stock closed at $13, down 37.5 cents, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
