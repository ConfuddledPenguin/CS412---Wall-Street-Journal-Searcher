REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Newly Sensitized

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

The American Library Association is upset that Upper Pittsgrove Township, N.J., objects to Webster's definition of intercourse and that Laytonville, Calif., doesn't like the way Dr. Seuss depicts loggers. Well, in a world in which we've all been taught to be sensitive to any slight or offense, no one should be surprised that the "other side" would join the game.
