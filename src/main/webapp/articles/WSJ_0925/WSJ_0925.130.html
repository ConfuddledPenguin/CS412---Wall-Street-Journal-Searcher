Business Brief -- Maxwell Communication Corp.: Berlitz Plans to Sell Stake Of 20% in Japanese Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

Berlitz International Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to sell a 20% stake in its profitable Japanese subsidiary to Fukutake Publishing Co., a large educational publishing company in Japan for $25.7 million. The companies also agreed to make a public offering in Japan of the subsidiary "as soon as practicable."

Berlitz International said proceeds from the sale will be used to reduce debt and fund an expansion throughout Asia. Berlitz officials couldn't be reached for further comment.

Berlitz International is a language instruction company with 276 schools around the world, 52 of which are in Japan. The company is 56%-owned by Macmillan Inc., a subsidiary of Britain's Maxwell Communication Corp.
