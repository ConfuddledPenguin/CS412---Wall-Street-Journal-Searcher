Business Brief -- Consolidated Capital Equities: ConCap Equities to Succeed Firm as Managing Partner

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

Consolidated Capital Equities Corp., Dallas, said ConCap Equities Inc. has been elected to succeed it as managing general partner of seven public real estate limited partnerships. ConCap Equities Inc. is affiliated with four regional property management companies: R&B Enterprises, Hayman Co., Metroplex Inc. and Horn-Barlow Cos.

The four companies will collectively manage over 150 residential and commercial properties for ConCap's partnerships. The seven limited partnerships electing ConCap are: Consolidated Capital Properties and Consolidated Capital Properties II through VII.
