Marketing & Media: Fuji Photo Film U.S.A. Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

PITTSBURGH -- Fuji Photo Film U.S.A. Inc. said it will soon package its film in paper canisters, replacing the plastic containers now in use. Fuji also said most of its film will be made from recycled paper. Fuji, which is the U.S. marketing unit of Fuji Photo Film Co. Ltd. of Tokyo, said the new packaging will apply only to its 35mm film sold in the U.S. The company said the new canisters, to be made of "moisture-proof" paper developed by Fuji, are more environmentally sound than plastic. Environmentalists say plastics create waste-disposal problems.

Fuji said the new packaging will be introduced by year end.
