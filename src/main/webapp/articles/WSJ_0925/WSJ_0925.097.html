Kansas City P&L Signs Information Accord With Kansas G&E

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- Kansas City Power & Light Co. said it signed a confidentiality agreement with Kansas Gas & Electric Co, under which Kansas City P&L will be allowed to review nonpublic information about the Wichita utility. Kansas City P&L has launched a $27-a-share, or $857 million offer for Kansas Gas & Electric. Kansas G&E has called the offer inadequate and legally questionable, and urged shareholders not to tender their shares.

Kansas G&E also announced in mid-September that it is in talks over a possible acquisition by a third party, which it won't identify. At that time, it said that it would provide similar nonpublic information to Kansas City P&L, subject to a confidentiality pact.

Yesterday's announcement marks the completion of that arrangement. Kansas City P&L said the agreement is a "desirable first step which we hope will lead to broader negotiations."
