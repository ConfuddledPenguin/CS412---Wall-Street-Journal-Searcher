Who's News: Eastman Kodak Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

EASTMAN KODAK Co. (Rochester, N.Y.) -- Gordon A. Smith, assistant to the general manager of government systems for Kodak, was named president of Datatape Inc., a subsidiary of this photographic, pharmaceuticals and information systems company. Mr. Smith succeeds Russell Frame, who is retiring.
