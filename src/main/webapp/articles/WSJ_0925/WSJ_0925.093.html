Offshore Pipelines Yearly Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/25/90

HOUSTON -- Offshore Pipelines Inc. said it expects to report a net loss of $2.5 million to $3 million, or 60 cents to 72 cents a share, for the year ended July 31. Revenue was about $125 million, according to the marine construction services concern, which went public in July 1989. It had net of about $3.9 million, or $1.02 a share, on revenue of about $103.9 million in fiscal 1989.

The company attributed the expected loss to the rescheduling of a significant project to the first quarter of fiscal 1991 as well as increased interest costs, nonrecurring relocation expenses and cost overruns on certain projects.
