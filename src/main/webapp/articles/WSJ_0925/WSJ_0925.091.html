P&G, Fater Merge Lines Abroad

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

CINCINNATI -- Procter & Gamble Co., a consumer products company, and the privately held Fater group of companies, a maker of diapers and sanitary napkins that is based in Pescara, Italy, said they merged their paper products businesses in Italy, Spain, Portugal and Britain. Terms weren't disclosed.
