Technology: Convex Computer Signs Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/25/90

RICHARDSON, Texas -- Convex Computer Corp. announced a three-year, $20 million contract with E-Systems Inc. of Dallas. Convex will supply supercomputer equipment and services to defense contractor E-Systems for a computer data storage system called the E-Systems modular automated storage system, or Emass. Convex C2 supercomputers will act as the main controllers for Emass.

Emass is composed of a Convex supercomputer with magnetic disks, a robotically controlled magnetic tape storage subsystem and an operations console. Under the terms of the agreement, Convex will provide hardware and system software and will develop special interfaces that will be used with the storage device.
