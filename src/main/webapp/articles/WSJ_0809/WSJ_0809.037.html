Who's News: Republic Gypsum Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

REPUBLIC GYPSUM Co. (Dallas) -- Robert F. Sexton and Bert A. Nelson were named directors of this maker of wallboard, paperboard and corrugated containers, increasing the board's size to eight. Mr. Sexton is president of Bakery Associates Inc. Mr. Nelson is engaged in various Southwestern business activities.
