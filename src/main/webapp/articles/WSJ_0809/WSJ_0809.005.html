Financing Business: National Intergroup Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

NATIONAL INTERGROUP Inc., a Pittsburgh-based wholesale drug distributor, said it extended to Aug. 27 from tomorrow its cash tender offer for all cumulative convertible preference units outstanding that represent limited partnership interests of Permian Partners L.P., a limited partnership involved in oil and natural gas gathering and marketing. National Intergroup began the offer of $3.85 a preference unit in May.
