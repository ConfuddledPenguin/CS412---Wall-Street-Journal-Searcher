USAir's July Traffic Increased

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

ARLINGTON, Va. -- USAir, a unit of USAir Group Inc., said July traffic rose 4.3% to 3.2 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.07 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 62% from 64.8%. USAir said statistics for 1989 periods prior to its merger with Piedmont Airlines Aug. 5, 1989, were restated to reflect the combined airline operations of both carriers on a pro forma basis.

In the first seven months, traffic rose 4.6% to 20.5 billion revenue passenger miles from 19.6 billion a year earlier. The load factor declined to 59.8% from 61.2%.
