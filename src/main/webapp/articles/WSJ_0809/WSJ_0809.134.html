Business Brief -- Avon Products Inc.: Firm's Major Stockholders Purchase Additional Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

Oil heir Gordon Getty and the Fisher real-estate family have acquired an additional 1,223,508 shares of Avon Products Inc., New York, the giant cosmetics and gifts company, for $45.9 million, or $37.50 per share. Mr. Getty and the Fisher family of New York, acting as Malakand Associates L.P., bought the Avon holdings as part of an agreement previously reached with L/J Partnership on July 5. L/J Partnership is controlled by Minneapolis investor Irwin Jacobs and Amway Corp., a direct-selling company in Ada, Mich. It is unclear whether L/J Partnership continues to own any Avon shares.

Mr. Getty and the Fisher family, together with Argonaut Partners 1, are partners of Chartwell Associates L.P., Avon's largest shareholder. Argonaut, whose general partners include Richard Rogers, Mary Kay Corp. chairman; and John Rochon, Mary Kay's vice chairman, declined to exercise its option to acquire the 1,233,508 shares of Avon from L/J Partnership.

Chartwell now controls 19.8% of Avon's common and preferred shares, slightly below the 20% level at which Avon's share rights plan is activated. In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange yesterday, Avon's shares closed at $33.25 up 87.5 cents.
