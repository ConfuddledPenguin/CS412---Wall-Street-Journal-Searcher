Southwest Air July Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

DALLAS -- Southwest Airlines Co. said it flew 922.2 million revenue passenger miles in July, a 7.5% increase from 857.5 million in July 1989. Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, was 64.2%, compared with 67.1%. In the first seven months, the carrier said traffic rose 6.1% to 5.81 billion revenue passenger miles from 5.57 billion. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The load factor was 61.8%, compared with 64.9%.
