Midwest Communications Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

EDGEWOOD, Ky. -- Midwest Communications Corp. said it expects to report a net loss of about $1.8 million for its year ended June 30. The company said the loss is due to extra costs for its Honolulu airport access control project and the write-off of software costs, among other things.

As a result of the loss, the supplier of video, audio and transmission equipment said it won't be in compliance with some negative covenants under its bank credit agreement.
