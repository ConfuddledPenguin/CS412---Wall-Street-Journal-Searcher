Who's News: Coca-Cola Bottling Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

COCA-COLA BOTTLING Co. Consolidated (Charlotte, N.C.) -- David L. Kennedy, vice president for business development at Coca-Cola Co., was named to the board of this Coca-Cola bottler, succeeding M. Douglas Ivester, who is leaving the board after being named president of the Coca-Cola USA unit of Coca-Cola Co.
