Altera Cancels Share Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

SAN JOSE, Calif -- Altera Corp. said it decided to scrap a proposed offering of 2.5 million common shares because of unfavorable market conditions. Last month, the semiconductor concern filed the offering with the Securities and Exchange Commission. At the time, its stock was trading at $13.75 a share. Yesterday, in national over-the-counter trading, the stock closed at $11.50, up 75 cents. Altera has roughly 20 million shares outstanding.
