Who's News: Oshkosh Truck Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

OSHKOSH TRUCK Corp. (Oshkosh, Wis.) -- John P. Carroll, 50 years old, resigned as president, effective Sept. 30. R. Eugene Goodson, 55, hired as the company's chairman and chief executive officer last April, will assume Mr. Carroll's duties.
