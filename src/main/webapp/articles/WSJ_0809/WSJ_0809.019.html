Tyco Toys Bids for Rest of Nasta

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

MOUNT LAUREL, N.J. -- Tyco Toys Inc. said it will offer $1 a share for the approximately 1.3 million shares outstanding of Nasta International Inc. that Tyco doesn't already own. Tyco said it would commence next week a tender offer to shareholders of Nasta, a New York-based maker of lower-priced toys. Tyco, which recently increased its Nasta ownership to 74% from 63%, said it has withdrawn a previously announced merger proposal.

The toy maker also said it intends to offer to exchange a new issue of Tyco 10-year 8% subordinated debentures for Nasta's $7.5 million principal amount of 12% convertible subordinated debentures outstanding. Agreement in principle has been reached for the exchange of more than $3 million of the Nasta debentures.
