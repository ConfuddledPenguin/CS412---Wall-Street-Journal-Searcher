Jacobs Engineering Gets Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said a unit received a $51 million contract to build part of a new prison in Delano, Calif. Jacobs said the contract was awarded to its Robert E. McKee Inc. unit by the California Department of Corrections. Jacobs said the contract covers construction of the prison reception center and 430,000 square feet of cells and dormitories.
