Who's News: Polly Peck International PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

POLLY PECK INTERNATIONAL PLC (London) -- David Fawcus, 58 years old, was named a deputy chief executive of this food, electronics and leisure concern. Mr. Fawcus was previously group finance director. He will be succeeded in that post by Reg Mogg, 44, formerly finance director of Maxwell Communications Corp.
