Who's News: Kevlin Microwave Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

KEVLIN MICROWAVE Corp. (Wilmington, Mass.) -- James J. Connolly, chief operating officer of Technical Communications Corp., and Carmine Vittoria, a professor of electrical and computer engineering at Northeastern University, were named directors of this maker of microwave rotary couplers used in communications systems, filling board vacancies.
