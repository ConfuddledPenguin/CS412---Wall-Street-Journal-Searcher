Business Brief -- North Fork Bancorp Inc.: Firm Says It Won't Acquire Eastchester at Agreed Price

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

North Fork Bancorp Inc. said it "would not be prepared" to acquire Eastchester Financial Corp. at the agreed price of $20 a share because of Eastchester's current financial condition. North Fork, of Mattituck, N.Y., said in a statement that it told Eastchester it would be willing to complete the deal on "significantly different terms" than those it reached in April, when it agreed to pay $79.5 million for Eastchester, whose main unit is Eastchester Savings Bank. North Fork said it doesn't know whether Eastchester will consider revised terms.

Eastchester reported a second-quarter loss of $1.2 million, or 31 cents a share, including a loan-loss provision of $2.6 million.

Officials of Eastchester couldn't be reached for comment.
