Torstar Halts Sale of Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

TORONTO -- Torstar Corp. said it gave up a plan to sell its Miles Kimball unit because it was unable to find a satisfactory offer. The newspaper and book publishing concern said last year that it planned to sell the direct-mail marketing unit, which is based in Oshkosh, Wis.

Miles Kimball had 1989 revenue of C$68.8 million (US$59.8 million), accounting for about 7% of Torstar's revenue.
