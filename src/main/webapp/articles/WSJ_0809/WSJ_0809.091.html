West German Retail Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

FRANKFURT -- West German retail sales were up a real, or price-adjusted, 2.7% in June from a year earlier, and rose 3.8% on a nominal, or unadjusted, basis, the government's statistics office said. The data marked a modest slowing from the rates in May, when retail sales climbed a real 8.9% and a nominal 10.9% from a year earlier. May's nominal rise was the first double-digit increase in that category since March 1988, when sales climbed 13% in nominal terms and 7% in real terms.
