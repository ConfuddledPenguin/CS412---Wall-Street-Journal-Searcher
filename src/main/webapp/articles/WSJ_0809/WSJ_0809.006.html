Who's News: Waste Management Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

WASTE MANAGEMENT Inc. (Oak Brook, Ill.) -- James T. Banks, formerly director of government affairs at the Chemical Waste Management Inc. subsidiary of this waste-management concern, was named vice president and general counsel at the unit, succeeding Joan Z. Bernstein, who was previously named vice president, environmental policy and ethical standards, at the parent.
