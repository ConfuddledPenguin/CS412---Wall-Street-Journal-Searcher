Who's News: Sun Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

SUN Co. (Radnor, Pa.) -- John P. Neafsey, executive vice president and a director, will leave Sept. 1 to join Greenwich Capital Markets as president and chief executive officer. A successor to Mr. Neafsey, 51, hasn't yet been named by Sun, an oil refiner and gasoline retailer. Greenwich Capital Markets is a diversified investment banking firm based in Greenwich, Conn.
