Who's News: Eurotunnel PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

EUROTUNNEL PLC (London and Paris) -- Peter Dyke, 46, was named director of operations for the Channel Tunnel transportation system between the British and French terminals of the tunnel being built by this Anglo-French consortium. Mr. Dyke had been with BAA PLC, where his most recent position was general manager at Heathrow Airport's Terminal 3.
