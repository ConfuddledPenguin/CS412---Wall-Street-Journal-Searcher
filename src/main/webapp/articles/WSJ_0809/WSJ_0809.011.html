Pan Am July Traffic Rose 5.5%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

NEW YORK -- Pan Am Corp. said July traffic at its Pan American World Airways unit rose 5.5% to 3.3 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.1 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The airline's July load factor, or percentage of available seats occupied, rose to 75.5% from 74.1%. For the first seven months of the year, Pan Am's traffic rose 10.5% to 18 billion revenue passenger miles from 16.3 billion, while the load factor rose to 65% from 62.9%.
