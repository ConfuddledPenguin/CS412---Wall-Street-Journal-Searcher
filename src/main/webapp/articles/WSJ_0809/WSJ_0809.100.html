Business Brief -- Sharper Image Corp.: Firm Plans Layoff of 10% Of Workers to Cut Costs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

Sharper Image Corp., responding to dull sales and earnings, said it would reduce its work force by 10% as part of broader cost-cutting to improve its financial performance. The San Francisco-based specialty retailer has about 1,100 employees. Besides the layoffs, the company said "every area of the business is under review" for possible expense-cutting. It said it had already trimmed travel, entertainment and supply budgets and had imposed a hiring freeze.

Sharper Image previously said it expected to report a loss of 15 cents to 20 cents a share for the second quarter ended July 31, compared with year-earlier profit of one cent a share. Yesterday's announcement was made after markets closed.
