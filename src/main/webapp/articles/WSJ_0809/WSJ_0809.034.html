Who's News: Roadway Services Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

ROADWAY SERVICES Inc. (Akron, Ohio) -- Daniel J. Sullivan, vice president and group executive of this freight carrier, was elected to its board, bringing board membership to 11. Sullivan fills the vacancy left by William W. Blodgett, former group vice president of the company, who retired in June.
