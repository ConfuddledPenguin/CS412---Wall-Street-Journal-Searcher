Business Brief -- Borneo International Furniture: Firm Will Open Showrooms In New York, New Jersey

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

Borneo International Furniture Inc., Asia's largest furniture manufacturer and retailer, said it will open a retail showroom in New York, marking its entry into the East Coast market. The Korean company, commonly known as BiF Korea, said it purchased a 55,000-square-foot building near the Empire State building for $11 million. Two floors of the 11-story building will open to the public this fall; the rest will open after the completion of a $3 million renovation. The company also said it has leased a 182,000-square-foot warehouse and distribution facility in Bergen County, N.J., where it will eventually operate a second showroom.

BiF Korea, which sells contemporary, Italian-style home and office furnishings, entered the U.S. market in 1981, and now operates 12 stores in Southern California. Last year, the company's sales were $169 million, including $32 million in exports. BiF Korea said it forecasts 1990 sales of $190 million.
