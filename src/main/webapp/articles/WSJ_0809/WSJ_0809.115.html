Highland Superstores July Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/09/90

PLYMOUTH, Mich. -- Highland Superstores Inc. reported July sales of $64.3 million, down 2% from July 1989 sales of $65.7 million. Comparable-store sales were down 6%. In the six months ended July 31, the operator of consumer electronics and appliance stores had sales of $381.8 milllion, down 3% from $395 million. Comparable-store sales declined 7%.
