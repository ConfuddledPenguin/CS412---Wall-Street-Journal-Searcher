Dealer to Offer Shares Of Galactic Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

TORONTO -- Yorkton Continental Securities Inc., an investment dealer based here, said it plans to offer as many as 2.2 million shares of Galactic Resources Ltd. through the Toronto Stock Exchange. Yorkton said two prospectuses are being prepared, one for about 1.6 million of the shares in the Vancouver-based gold mining concern being offered for sale on behalf of various clients, and the other for the remainder of the shares, to be sold by a Yorkton unit. It didn't say when the prospectuses will be available, but noted they will be distributed three trading days before the stock is available for sale.

At last report, Galactic, which has mines in South Carolina and Colorado, had about 41.1 million shares outstanding.

In American Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Galactic shares closed at $2.9375, down 6.25 cents.
