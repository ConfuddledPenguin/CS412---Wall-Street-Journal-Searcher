Mesaba Aviation's Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- AirTran Corp. said July traffic at its Mesaba Aviation unit rose 24% to 20.6 million revenue passenger miles from 16.6 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, dropped to 57.8% from 60.5%.
