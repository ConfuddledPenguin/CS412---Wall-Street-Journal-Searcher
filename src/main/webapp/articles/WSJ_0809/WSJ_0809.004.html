Vintage Petroleum Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Vintage Petroleum Inc. said it completed the purchase of some oil and gas properties in Louisiana from Chevron Corp., San Francisco, for about $14 million. Vintage, an independent energy firm, said daily production from the properties averages about 655 barrels of oil and 1,860 million cubic feet of natural gas.
