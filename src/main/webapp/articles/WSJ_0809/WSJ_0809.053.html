TV SALE: Scripps Howard Broadcasting Co., an 80%-owned subsidiary of E.W. Scripps Co., signed a definitive agreement to buy WMAR, a Baltimore TV station, from Gillett Group Inc. for $154.7 million.

MAGAZINES: Robert Kraft, chief executive of Rand Whitney Group, invested $25,000 in Urban Profile magazine to finance a promotional campaign. The magazine for blacks said Mr. Kraft has become a partner and has an option to increase his stake. The magazine is published by Urban Profile Communications Inc.
