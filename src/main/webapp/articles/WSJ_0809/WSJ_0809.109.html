Marketing Brief -- Thorn EMI PLC: U.K. Music Concern Agrees To Buy Publisher Filmtrax

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/09/90

Thorn EMI PLC, a British music, home-appliance rental and lighting group based in London, said it has reached terms to acquire Filmtrax PLC and its U.S. subsidiary Filmtrax Copyright Holdings PLC, for as much as $115 million. The acquisition of the music publisher will be in cash, loan stock or new Thorn shares, it said. Details depend on a review of Filmtrax's financial status. Thorn said the sale should be completed in three months.

Filmtrax will be incorporated into Thorn's New York-based music publishing subsidiary, EMI Music Publishing Worldwide.

The final purchase price could change slightly depending on the outcome of due diligence investigations of Filmtrax's accounts and catalogs, he said.

Following the announcement Thorn's shares dropped 11 pence to 683 pence.
