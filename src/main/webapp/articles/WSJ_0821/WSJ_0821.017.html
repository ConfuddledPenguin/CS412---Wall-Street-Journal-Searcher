Ford Motor Offers Incentive Financing On Escorts, Tracers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

DETROIT -- Ford Motor Co. is offering cut-rate financing in an effort to boost disappointing sales of its new twin subcompact models, the Ford Escort and the Mercury Tracer. Escort sales are running more than 20% behind last year's pace, and are the big reason behind Ford's drop in market share so far this year.

The incentive financing program, which began Aug. 9 and runs until Sept. 3, will allow Escort and Tracer buyers to obtain 4.8% financing on loans up to 48 months. In 10 northeastern states, buyers will have the option of taking $500 cash instead of the budget financing.

The moves come on top of an earlier Ford program that gives $500 to Escort and Tracer buyers who are purchasing their first car. Fred Ricart, a Ford dealer in Columbus, Ohio, said his Escort sales have dropped about 50% this year, but he added that the new programs have already begun reviving sales.

Ford's share of the U.S. car market fell to 21% in the first seven months of this year from 22.8% last year. Early this year, Ford officials predicted the company would gain market share in 1990, but now that goal is looking difficult to achieve. If Ford's share of new-car sales does drop, it will be the first such decline since 1986.

Boosting sales of the fuel-efficient Escort and Tracer is important for another reason as well: It helps Ford to keep selling big cars with far lower gas mileage but far higher profit margins. This year, Ford expects its cars to average less than 26.5 miles per gallon, well under the federal standard of 27.5 miles per gallon. Ford won't pay a penalty because of credits earned from previous years, but the credits will run out in a year or so.

Ford invested $1.9 billion to develop the new Escort, which is based on a similar model from its Japanese affiliate, Mazda Motor Corp. When the 1991-model Escort first appeared in April, it had trouble competing with the 1990 Escort, which had a lower base price and carried a $1,000 rebate as well.
