Martin Lawrence Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

VAN NUYS, Calif. -- Martin Lawrence Limited Editions Inc. said it bought back 500,000 common shares between July 2 and last Friday at $3.43 to $4.63 a share. The publisher and seller of artwork said that the latest purchases bring to one million the number of shares it has bought since April 30, when it started its stock-purchase program.

The company said it will buy back as many as 500,000 additional shares, which were authorized in July, when the current program was expanded. The company said that as of Friday it had 7,349,648 shares outstanding.
