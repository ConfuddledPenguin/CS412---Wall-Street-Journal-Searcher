Redemption Notices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended Aug. 17, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

FLORIDA HOUSING FINANCE AGENCY will call for redemption $23,640,000 of its 1983 series H, FirstSouth Federal Savings & Loan Association, multifamily housing revenue bonds on Sept. 1, 1990. Barnett Banks Trust Co., Miami, Fla., is trustee.

MISSOURI HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMMISSION will redeem $60,000 principal amount of its 9.625% housing development bonds, Federally Insured Mortgage Loans, series Sept. 1, 1980, due Sept. 1, 2006, on Sept. 1, 1990. Mercantile Bank of St. Louis is trustee.

CORPORATES

BAKER HUGHES INC. will call for redemption on Aug. 31, 1990, its $3.50 convertible exchangeable preferred stock. First Chicago Trust Co., New York, is the paying agent.

TRANSCONTINENTAL GAS PIPE LINE CORP. will redeem its $2.50 cumulative preferred stock on Sept. 14, 1990. First Chicago Trust Co., New York, is redemption agent.
