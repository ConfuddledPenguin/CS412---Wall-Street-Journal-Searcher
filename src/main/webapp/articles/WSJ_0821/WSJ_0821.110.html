Who's News: Hexcel Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

HEXCEL Corp. (Dublin, Calif.) -- D. Thomas Divird, vice president and chief financial officer, resigned, effective Sept. 30. The company said Mr. Divird, who joined the aerospace and defense products maker in 1970, resigned to "pursue other career opportunities." It didn't elaborate, and Mr. Divird couldn't be reached to comment. Hexcel said it has begun a search for Mr. Divird's successor.
