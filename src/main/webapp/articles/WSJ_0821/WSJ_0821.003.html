Wang Labs Completes Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

LOWELL, Mass. -- Wang Laboratories Inc. said it completed the previously announced sale of its Intecom Inc. unit to Matra Communications, a unit of Matra S.A. of Paris. Terms weren't disclosed.

Wang, which manufactures and services computer systems, has been selling units in an effort to cut bank debt. The company said it will use proceeds from the sale to further reduce debt.
