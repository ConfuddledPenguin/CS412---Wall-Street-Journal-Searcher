Who's News: Reliance Group Holdings Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

RELIANCE GROUP HOLDINGS Inc. (New York) -- Terry L. Kendall was named a director, increasing board membership to 12 at this insurance, real estate development and investment firm. Mr. Kendall is president and chief executive officer of United Pacific Life Insurance Co., a Philadelphia-based subsidiary of Reliance Group Holdings.
