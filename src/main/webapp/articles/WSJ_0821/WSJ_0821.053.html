British Petroleum Co. Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

LONDON (AP) -- British Petroleum Co. said it will auction off its gas exploration and production holding company in the Netherlands. BP refused to disclose the estimated value of BP Exploratie B.V., a wholly owned subsidiary of British Petroleum Nederland.

The unit holds stakes in 23 offshore blocks and two onshore, and analysts said the offer would fetch between $60 million and $70 million.

The fields produce 14 million standard cubic feet of natural gas each day, said a British Petroleum spokesman, who spoke on condition of anonymity.
