Allstate Financial Credit Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

ARLINGTON, Va. -- Allstate Financial Corp. said it has accepted an increase in its credit facilities from National Westminster Bank N.A. The company said its line of credit will be doubled to $15 million from $7.5 million and its letter of credit facility will be increased to $5 million from $1.5 million.
