Business Brief -- K mart Corp.: Discount Retailer's Earnings Rose 2.1% in 2nd Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

K mart Corp., citing increased sales and "careful monitoring" of expenses, said its second-quarter earnings rose 2.1% to $148 million, or 74 cents a share, from $145 million, or 72 cents a share. Sales for the quarter ended Aug. 1 rose 11% to $7.78 billion from $7.01 billion a year earlier.

The discount retailer said that despite a relatively weak retail environment, its earnings improved as increased sales and expense monitoring offset higher interest expense and the reduction in gross margins from the lowering of prices. K mart, which is based in Troy, Mich., said it was particularly encouraged by the strength in apparel sales as well as strong sales of seasonal merchandise.

In the first six months, K mart earned $248.1 million, or $1.24 a share, compared with $239.1 million, or $1.19 a share, a year earlier. Sales rose 13% to $14.83 billion from $13.07 billion.
