Raw-Steel Production Increased Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 1.4% last week to 1,927,000 tons from 1,901,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 8.3% from the 1,779,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 86.4% of its capability last week, compared with 85.2% the previous week and 79.9% a year earlier.
