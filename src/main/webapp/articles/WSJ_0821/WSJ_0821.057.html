Letters to the Editor: Jeep at Any Price

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

In your July 18 article "Rivalry Accelerates for Sports Trucks, Dubbed Family Sedans of the '90s," you are right when you say that several years ago Jeep Cherokee virtually owned the market. I had a pickup for years but needed four doors once I had kids. In late 1987 the only four doors were Jeep and the Isuzu Trooper. I made a big mistake and bought the Trooper. It has been a maintenance nightmare. I've spent almost $3,000 on repairs and it has been in the shop more than 65 days in the past two years (I once had to wait 12 days for a thermostat housing gasket). If Joe Isuzu is going after the upscale Range Rover market, all I can say is Good Luck]

David R. Mettauer

San Antonio, Texas
