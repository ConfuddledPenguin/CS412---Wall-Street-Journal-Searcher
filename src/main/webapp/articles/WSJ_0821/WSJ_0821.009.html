Chrysler Unit's Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

WASHINGTON -- Chrysler Technologies Airborne Systems Inc., a unit of Chrysler Corp., won an $80.2 million Air Force contract for C-27A aircraft.

General Electric Co. received a $47.8 million Navy contract for engineering services in support of the Aegis cruiser/destroyer program.

Computer Sciences Corp. got an $18.7 million Defense Mapping Agency contract for an agency-distribution management system.
