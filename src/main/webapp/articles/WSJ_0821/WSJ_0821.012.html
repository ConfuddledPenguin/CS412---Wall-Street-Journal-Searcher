Marketing & Media: Newbridge Communications Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

NEW YORK -- Newbridge Communications Inc., a special interest book club, said it agreed to acquire closely held Reader's Garden Inc., which operates two book clubs, for an undisclosed price. Reader's Garden operates The Reader's Subscription for readers of serious fiction, nonfiction and poetry, and The Garden Book Club for serious gardeners.

Newbridge was previously known as Macmillan Book Clubs until it was acquired by K-III Holdings L.P., an affiliate of leveraged buy-out specialists Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co., in mid-1989. Newbridge had sales in excess of $100 million in 1989.
