Business Brief -- Holnam Inc.: Holding Company to Raise Stake to 89.3% From 87.2%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

Holnam Inc.'s holding company Holderman Inc. agreed to increase its stake in the cement manufacturer to 89.3% from 87.2% by acquiring 22.2 million newly issued common shares for $99.9 million. Separately, Dundee, Mich.-based Holnam said it expects a loss of 10 cents to 15 cents a share in 1990 because of cost increases and declining sales. The company earlier predicted it would finish the year with net income of five to 10 cents a share.

Holderman, a subsidiary of Holderbank Financiere Glaris Ltd. in Zurich, Switzerland, agreed to purchase the shares at $4.50 each. Proceeds from the sale will be used for the retirement of existing debt and other general corporate purposes, Holnam said.
