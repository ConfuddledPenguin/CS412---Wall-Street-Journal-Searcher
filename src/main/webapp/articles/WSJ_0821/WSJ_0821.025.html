Cyclops Plans Closing in Detroit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

PITTSBURGH -- Cyclops Industries Inc. said it will close its Detroit strip steel operation. The steel company said efforts to make the division profitable had failed. The closing, which will occur in the fourth quarter, will affect 100 workers. Cyclops had a second-quarter loss of $11.3 million, or $1.62 a share, after a $13.1 million restructuring charge.
