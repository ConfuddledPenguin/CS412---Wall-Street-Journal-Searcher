Business Brief -- Mail Boxes Etc.: Mail Boxes Etc. Recovers Part of Settlement Payment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

Mail Boxes Etc., San Diego, said it recovered from an insurance company $400,000 of a $2.8 million payment recently made to settle certain litigation. The insurance recovery will result in a gain of about $240,000 in the second quarter ending Oct. 31 because the entire settlement amount has already been written off, the company said.

The litigation involved charges by Nancy Tash, daughter of the company's late founder, Gerald Aul, that the company and its president, Anthony DeSio, fraudulently induced her to sell her 23% stake in 1983. Mail Boxes is a franchiser of postal-business and communication service centers.
