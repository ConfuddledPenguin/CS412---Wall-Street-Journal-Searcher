Stage II Apparel Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

NEW YORK -- Citing favorable prospects, Stage II Apparel Corp. directors authorized buying as many as 100,000, or 2.4%, of the apparel company's 4.2 million common shares outstanding, in open-market transactions from time to time. Since late 1987, the company has bought back 106,000 shares.
