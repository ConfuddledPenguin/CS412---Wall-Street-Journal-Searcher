Integrated Waste Acquisitions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Integrated Waste Services Inc. said it acquired Southwestern Environmental and Wizard Method Inc., both of Niagara Falls, N.Y. Terms weren't disclosed. The waste-management company said it would merge the two acquisitions into a wholly owned subsidiary. Southwestern Environmental is a demolition and asbestos abatement contractor, and Wizard Method is an industrial cleaning and asbestos abatement concern.
