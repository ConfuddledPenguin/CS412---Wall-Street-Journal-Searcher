Who's News: MCI Communications Taps Three Executives For Divisional Posts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

WASHINGTON -- MCI Communications Corp. appointed three new division presidents: Jonathan C. Crane, president of the Northeast division; Gregory A. LeVert, Midwest division; and Douglas L. Maine, Southeast division. Mr. Crane, who had been Southeast division president, succeeds Nathan Kantor, who announced his resignation to start a business telecommunications service company. Ronald E. Spears, who has been president of the Midwest division since 1985, also announced his resignation; the company said he decided against a new job within MCI that would have required a move from Chicago. Messrs. Kantor and Spears will continue with MCI until the end of the year to ensure a smooth transition.

Mr. LeVert has been vice president for large accounts at MCI's Washington headquarters, and Mr. Maine was MCI's senior vice president for finance. Nate Davis, also an MCI vice president, succeeds Mr. Maine in the finance post, and Barent S. Wagar, director of national accounts, succeeds Mr. LeVert as vice president of large accounts.

MCI also said that its international division will report to Eugene Eidenberg, executive vice president, and that its corporate development department will report to O. Gene Gabbard, executive vice president and chief financial officer.
