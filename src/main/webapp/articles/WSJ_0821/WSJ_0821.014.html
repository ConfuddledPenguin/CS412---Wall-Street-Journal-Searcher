Dividend News: Second National Federal Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

SECOND NATIONAL FEDERAL SAVINGS BANK, Salisbury, Md., said it cut its quarterly 50% in a move to conserve capital and increase its general loan-loss reserves. The company will be reducing its payout to 4.5 cents a share from nine cents, beginning with its next dividend payment, due to be considered at its Sept. 21 board meeting. Henry A. Berliner, president and chief executive officer, said the company's nonperforming commercial real estate loans doubled in the second quarter, to $55 million from around $27 million.
