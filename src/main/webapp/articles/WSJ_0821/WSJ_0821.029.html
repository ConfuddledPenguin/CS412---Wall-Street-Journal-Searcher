LDI to Post Rise in Share Net

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

CLEVELAND -- LDI Corp., which leases and sells data-processing, telecommunications and other high-technology equipment, said it expects net income for its second quarter ended July 31 to be in line with analysts' expectations of 38 cents a share, up from 35 cents a year earlier. The company also said it expects to post a fiscal second-quarter revenue increase from the year-earlier $87.2 million. LDI cited diversification measures and a conservative financial strategy.
