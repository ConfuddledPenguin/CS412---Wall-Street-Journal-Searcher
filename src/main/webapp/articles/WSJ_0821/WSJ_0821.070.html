Interdependent Deals

Before we can buy our dream house

We must sell the home we now own,

And the party who hopes to buy it

Is facing the same syndrome.

The person he wants to sell to

Must have a contingency deed

Based on whether he can get

The financing he will need.

So all concerned are on needles and pins

And our nerves are nearly wrecked

As we learn the hard way what is meant

By the "domino effect."

-- George O. Ludcke.

Beginning to End

It starts with waiting

To be old enough

To cross the street alone;

To select what you choose

To wear --

To walk to school

On your own.

Then it happens --

You're an adult

Able to set pace

And tone --

Then the last thing

You want is to be

Left alone]

-- Beatrice Kess.
