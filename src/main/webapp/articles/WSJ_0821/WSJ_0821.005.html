Seats Sold at Stock Exchanges

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange said a seat was sold for $310,000, down $10,000 from the previous sale last Wednesday. Seats are currently quoted at $301,000, bid, and $330,000, offered. Separately, the American Stock Exchange said a seat was sold for $107,500, the lowest price since January 1980 and down $10,000 from the last sale, on Aug. 3. Seats are being quoted at $105,000, bid, and $115,000, offered.
