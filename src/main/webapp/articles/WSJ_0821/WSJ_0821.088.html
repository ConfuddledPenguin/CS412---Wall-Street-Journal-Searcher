Business Brief -- Questar Corp.: Planned Capital Spending For '90 Lifted to a Record

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/21/90

Questar Corp., an oil and gas concern based in Salt Lake City, said it increased its expected capital expenditures for this year to a record $160 million from its initial estimate of about $140 million. The previous high is $146 million in 1982. A spokesman said the spending increase, which will mostly go for petroleum reserve acquisitions and pipeline construction, was in the works before Iraq's invasion of Kuwait triggered higher oil prices. But he acknowledged that turmoil in the Middle East "certainly re-enforced our strategy of increasing oil and gas reserves."

Questar said it will finance the spending mainly through internal sources of funds. It said the expenditures won't have much effect on earnings in the short term.
