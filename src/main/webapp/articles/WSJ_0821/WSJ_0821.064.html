Pechiney Posts Revenue Decline

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

PARIS -- Pechiney S.A. said unfavorable exchange rate conditions and weak metals prices contributed to a 10.3% drop in consolidated first-half revenue. The aluminum and packaging company said group revenue fell to 40.11 billion French francs ($7.63 billion) from 44.70 billion francs in the first half of 1989.
