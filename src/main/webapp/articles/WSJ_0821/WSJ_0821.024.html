United Artists Entertainment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

DENVER -- United Artists Entertainment Co. said it refinanced $2.8 billion in debt to attain greater financial flexibility, lengthen the payback period and reduce principal payments for the next three years. The operator of cable systems and movie theaters, which is 56%-owned by Tele-Communications Inc., said the new pact also will give it "funding availability to pursue future opportunities." The lead banks in the financing include Chemical Bank, Bank of New York, Toronto Dominion and Societe General.
