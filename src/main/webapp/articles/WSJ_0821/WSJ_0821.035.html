International: Nicaragua Devalues Currency

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

MANAGUA, Nicaragua -- The Nicaraguan cordoba was devalued 8.5% yesterday, the 32nd devaluation since President Violeta Barrios de Chamorro took office April 25. The official rate slid to 820,000 cordobas to the U.S. dollar from 750,000, where it had been since Thursday when it was last devalued.

On the black market, the cordoba traded at 850,000 to the dollar yesterday, down from 800,000 Sunday.
