Melville Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/21/90

RYE, N.Y. -- Melville Corp. completed the acquisition of the operating assets of Circus World Toy Stores Inc., a subsidiary of Greeman Bros. Inc., Farmingdale, N.Y., for $95 million. Circus World operates 330 retail toy stores in 32 states with 1989 sales of $177.6 million. The stores have become part of Melville's Kay-Bee division, which had sales of $724 million last year.

Melville, a $7.5 billion retailer, operates 6,900 toy, apparel, prescription drug and footwear stores.
