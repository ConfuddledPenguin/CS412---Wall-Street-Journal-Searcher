Who's News: Angelica Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

ANGELICA Corp. (St. Louis) -- Lawrence J. Young, president and chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of chairman, effective Nov. 1. Mr. Young, 46 years old, will succeed Leslie F. Loewe, 69, who will become chairman emeritus. Mr. Young was named president of the maker of uniforms and provider of laundry services in February 1988. In November 1989, Mr. Young succeeded Mr. Loewe as chief executive officer.
