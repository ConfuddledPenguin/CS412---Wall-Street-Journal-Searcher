Business Brief -- Wearne Brothers Ltd.: Singapore Company Offers To Buy U.S.-Based Qume

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/24/90

Wearne Brothers Ltd., a Singapore-based multinational, said it plans to acquire all shares outstanding of Qume Corp., a Santa Clara, Calif.-based computer-peripherals company. Wearne said an offer for the Qume shares at $7.25 a share has been made by New Qume Corp., a company started by a recently formed partnership between Wearne, Qume's chairman and chief executive, David Lee, and other unnamed investors.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Qume shares closed at $6.625, unchanged.

As the proposed acquisition is subject to the partners obtaining sufficient financing, Wearne Brothers will provide a $70 million loan to the partnership.

In a separate announcement, Wearne Brothers said group pretax profit rose 45% to 28.4 million Singapore dollars ($15.3 million) for the financial half year ended March 31, compared with S$19.6 million for the year-earlier period.
