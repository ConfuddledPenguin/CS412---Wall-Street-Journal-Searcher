Marketing Brief -- Sherwin-Williams Co.: New National Ad Campaign Aimed at Do-It-Yourselfers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/24/90

Sherwin-Williams Co. will launch a $10.4 million nationwide ad campaign aimed at the do-it-yourself market. The home-improvement company will begin soon television and radio commercials in addition to 48 ads in home decorating magazines.

Part of the thrust of the campaign is to convince consumers that Sherwin-Williams is a home-decorating consultant as well as a retailer, said Karl Schmitt, director of advertising. The company will be offering color-planning guides at its 1,900 stores to help do-it-yourselfers develop color schemes.
