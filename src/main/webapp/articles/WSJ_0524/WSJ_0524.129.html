Business Brief -- Del Webb Corp.: Holders Appear to Approve Takeover-Rule Resolution

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

Del Webb Corp., Phoenix, Ariz., said a preliminary report of inspectors of election for its May 16 annual meeting indicated that a nonbinding shareholder resolution to remove the company's anti-takeover provisions was approved by shareholders. The proposal by Los Angeles-based Webbcott Holdings, which owns a 9.7% stake in Webb and is controlled by director James L. Cotter, requested that the board redeem shareholder rights issued under a "poison pill" plan adopted last year. Del Webb said 50.4% of the common stock outstanding voted for the resolution.

A Del Webb spokesman said a special committee of the real estate development concern's board will consider all options and will make a report to the board regarding the resolution.
