Japan's Vehicle Output Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

TOKYO -- Japan's production of cars, trucks and buses in April increased 3.2% from a year earlier to 1,127,000 units, a record for the month, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers Association said. In March, production dropped 2.8% from the year earlier.

The association said motor vehicle sales in recent months have been supported by continuing strong domestic demand.

The association said domestic sales of motor vehicles in April rose 7.7% year to year, and predicted export figures, to be released later in May, will show a roughly 18% decline from a year earlier.
