Stelco Pipe-Making Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

HAMILTON, Ontario -- Stelco Inc. said it plans to build a pipe-making plant in Welland, Ontario, at a cost of 60 million Canadian dollars ($50.6 million). The steel maker said construction will begin immediately, for completion in early 1992. The plant will have annual capacity of 140,000 metric tons, the company said.
