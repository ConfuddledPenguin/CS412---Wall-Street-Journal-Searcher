Oracle, Digital Equipment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/24/90

REDWOOD SHORES, Calif. -- Oracle Corp., a unit of Oracle Systems Corp., said it reached a joint marketing agreement with Digital Equipment Corp., Maynard, Mass., to promote Oracle accounting software with Digital computers. Oracle declined to estimate the potential financial effect of the agreement. The companies hope to benefit from a trend of users increasingly performing accounting tasks on personal computers and networks, as opposed to large mainframe machines.
