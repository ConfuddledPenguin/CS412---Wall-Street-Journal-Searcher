South African Prices Climb

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

JOHANNESBURG -- South Africa's inflation rate as measured by the consumer price index rose 14.6% in April from a year earlier, the government's Central Statistical Services said. April's index rose to 198.3 points from 173.1 a year earlier and was up 1% from March's 196.4 points.

March's year-on-year increase was 14.9%.
