Business Brief -- Costco Wholesale Corp.: Firm to Close Four Outlets, Sees Charge of $3.7 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

Costco Wholesale Corp. said in Kirkland, Wash., that it expects to take a $3.7 million charge in the third quarter ended May 13 because it plans to close four of its warehouse stores in Florida. Costco said that three of the stores have been unprofitable and that a fourth was being closed in conjunction with the opening of a new facility. It said it will continue operating at nine locations in Florida.

In the year-earlier quarter, net income was $4.6 million, or 18 cents a share, on revenue of $645.9 million. The cash-and-carry membership warehouse operator, which said it expects to finish the fiscal year with 62 stores, plans to add 10 to 12 locations next year.
