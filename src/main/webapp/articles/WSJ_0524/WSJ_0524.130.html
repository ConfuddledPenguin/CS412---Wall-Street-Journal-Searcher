Business Brief -- Richton International Corp.: Jewelry Firm Says It Had Loss for Fiscal 4th Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

Richton International Corp., a jewelry concern, said it expects to report a loss for its fiscal fourth quarter and a profit for the year. For the year-earlier quarter, ended April 30, Richton posted a loss of $3.2 million, or $1.16 a share, including a $2.9 million one-time charge related to the downsizing of its New York office. Net sales were $5.4 million. For fiscal 1989, it posted a loss of almost $3 million, or $1.07 a share, on net sales of $30.5 million.

For the first three quarters of fiscal 1990, Richton posted a profit of $528,000, or 19 cents a share, on net sales of $18.2 million. The company said in New York that it experienced a "significant weakening" in the period just ended, but continues to take steps to reduce costs and improve sales.
