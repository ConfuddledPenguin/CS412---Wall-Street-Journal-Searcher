Who's News: Federal-Mogul Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

FEDERAL-MOGUL Corp. (Southfield, Mich.) -- Dennis J. Gormley, 50 years old, was elected chairman of the manufacturer of vehicle engine parts. Mr. Gormley also is the company's president and chief executive officer. He succeeds Thomas F. Russell, 66, who has retired. Mr. Russell will remain a member of the board.
