Technology: Rolm Sets Phone Link With Digital's VAXes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

NEW ORLEANS -- Rolm Co. said it will enable its telephone switches to communicate with Digital Equipment Corp.'s VAX computers, strengthening a recent trend toward cooperation between switch and computer makers. Rolm, a Norwalk, Conn., joint venture of International Business Machines Corp. of Armonk, N.Y., and Siemens AG of West Germany, made the announcement at a trade conference here. Earlier this month, IBM got the cooperation trend going when it said it would let its computers "talk" to telephone switches made by Rolm, Siemens, American Telephone & Telegraph Co., and the Northern Telecom Inc. unit of BCE Inc.

Links between switches and computers allow callers' files to appear on a computer screen as soon as a call is made, enabling better customer service and opening up new marketing opportunities.
