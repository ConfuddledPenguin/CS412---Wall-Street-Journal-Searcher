Business Brief -- Service Resources Corp.: Unit of Household Finance Offers Debt Commitment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/24/90

Service Resources Corp. said it received a commitment from a unit of Household Finance Corp., Prospect Heights, Ill., to refinance its existing debt obligations. The company said the $40 million facility committed by Household Commercial Financial Services Inc. includes a $15 million revolving credit loan and a $25 million term loan. Service Resources didn't specify additional terms.

The company said it will use part of the proceeds of the refinancing to repay all of the principal and accrued and unpaid interest on its 9% and 11.5% debentures, which have been in default since November 1988.

Service Resources said it isn't any longer pursuing the sale of its sole operating subsidiary, the former CPY Management Services Inc., which has formally changed its name to Ameriscribe Inc. Ameriscribe provides mailroom and other back-office support to users such as law firms.
