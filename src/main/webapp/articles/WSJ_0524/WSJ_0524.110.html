Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

PUBLISHING: Ziff Communications, a large publisher of computer magazines, formed a new division, Ziff-Davis Press, to publish computer-related books. It will publish its first titles in the first quarter of 1991 in conjunction with PC Magazine.

OLYMPICS: Westwood One Inc.'s NBC Radio Networks unit received exclusive rights to broadcast the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona, Spain, to U.S. radio audiences in English. Westwood also will provide promotional opportunities for the Olympics on its other outlets.

WHO'S NEWS: MCA Inc. said Jon Gumpert was named senior vice president of legal and business affairs for its Universal Pictures unit. He was executive vice president of Odyssey Entertainment Ltd.
