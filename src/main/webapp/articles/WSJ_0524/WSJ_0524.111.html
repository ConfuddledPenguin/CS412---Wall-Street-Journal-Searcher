Business Brief -- Baker Hughes Inc.: Definitive Pact to Acquire Instrument Concern Is Set

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

Baker Hughes Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement to buy the instruments group of Tracor Holdings Inc. for approximately $95 million. "The Tracor Instruments Group, its products and its employees will provide us with a strong addition to the instrumentation segment of Baker Hughes Process Equipment," said James D. Woods, chairman and chief executive officer of Baker Hughes.

Tracor Instruments Group, which makes analytical instrumentation, had revenue of $103 million last year. It is a division of privately held Tracor Holdings of Austin, Texas.

Houston-based Baker Hughes provides products and services for the oil, gas and process industries.
