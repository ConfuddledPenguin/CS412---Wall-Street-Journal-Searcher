West German Auto Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

FRANKFURT -- West Germany's production of automobiles slid in April, signaling a flattening of the robust growth in car demand of the past couple years. Output by West German auto manufacturers was down 8% in the latest reporting month to 378,000 units from 412,926 a year earlier, the German Auto Industry Association said. April's decline in output occurred as exports of German autos slackened.

Auto analysts had been expecting the European car market to be soft this year, although West Germany had been relatively resilient in the first quarter. The association did note, however, that there was one less day of production last month than in April 1989.
