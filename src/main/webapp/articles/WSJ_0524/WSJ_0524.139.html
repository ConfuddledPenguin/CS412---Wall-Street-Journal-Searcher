Business Brief -- Bowne & Co.: Purchase of Some Assets From Sorg Is Completed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

Bowne & Co. said it completed the purchase of some Sorg Inc. assets for $5.7 million, including the Sorg name and trademark and some printing equipment. Both financial printers are based in New York. Sorg has been operating with protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code since August. The asset sale received bankruptcy court approval Friday.

Bowne said it will employ a dozen members of Sorg's New York management and sales staff, and is considering applications from four more Sorg employees. It also said it transferred all of Sorg's work in progress to its own facility. The agreement excludes Sorg's real estate, Chicago office and various other assets, and one printing press Sorg had agreed to sell but turned out not to own, Bowne said.
