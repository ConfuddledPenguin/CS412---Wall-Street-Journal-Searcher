Who's News: First Fidelity Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

FIRST FIDELITY BANCORP (Lawrenceville, N.J.) -- Thomas H. O'Brien Jr., 47 years old, and Harold A. McCleery, 42, were named senior vice presidents, credit administration, at this bank holding company. Previously, Mr. O'Brien was executive vice president and chief credit policy officer at the Chemical New Jersey Holdings Inc. unit of Chemical Banking Corp., and Mr. McCleery was senior vice president, credit policy, at Chemical Banking.
