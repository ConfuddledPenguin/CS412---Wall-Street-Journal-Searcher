Sharper Image's Loss Widens

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Sharper Image Corp. reported a slightly wider fiscal first-quarter loss on essentially flat revenue. For the quarter ended April 30, the loss widened to $1.4 million, or 17 cents a share, from $1.2 million, or 14 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue fell 1.1% to $34.7 million from $35.1 million, the company said.

The specialty retailer cited a "sluggish national retailing environment" and higher expenses from increased catalogue circulation and from the opening of four more stores.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Sharper Image shares plunged 62.5 cents to close at $5.125.
