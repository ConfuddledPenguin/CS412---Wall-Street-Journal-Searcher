COMPAQ COMPUTER Corp. (Houston) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it upgraded the convertible subordinated debt rating to Baa-3 from Ba-2 on the computer company's $200 million of debt, which has been called for redemption and is expected to convert to equity in its entirety. The rating service cited Compaq's "cash flow generation and its strong balance sheet," as well as its asset-management capability and the broadening of its global presence.
