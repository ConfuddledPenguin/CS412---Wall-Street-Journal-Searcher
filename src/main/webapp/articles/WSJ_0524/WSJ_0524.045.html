WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Campania de Telefonos de Chile S.A., offering of American depositary shares representing 100 million of its series A common shares, via Salomon Brothers Inc.

Carnival Cruise Lines Inc., offering of zero coupon convertible subordinated notes, via Bear, Stearns & Co. and Salomon Brothers.

GTE South Inc., a unit of GTE Corp., shelf offering of up to $200 million of first mortgage bonds.

Offshore Pipelines Inc., initial public offering of 3.6 million common shares, via Prudential-Bache Capital Funding and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. Inc.

Transco Energy Co., shelf offering of up to $400 million of debt securities.
