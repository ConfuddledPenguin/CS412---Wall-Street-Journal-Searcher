Who's News: Seagram Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

SEAGRAM Co. (Montreal) -- Jeananne K. Hauswald, 46 years old, previously treasurer, was appointed vice president, human resources, at this maker of spirits, wines and soft drinks, succeeding Ronald P. Carzoli, 59, who is retiring. Succeeding Ms. Hauswald is John R. Preston, 43, formerly executive vice president, finance and administration, at the Seagram Beverage Co. unit of Joseph E. Seagram & Sons Inc., the U.S. subsidiary based in New York.
