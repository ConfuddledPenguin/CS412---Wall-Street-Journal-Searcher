International Brief -- Matsushita Electric Industrial Co.: Annual Pretax Profit Rose 7.9% on Record Sales Level

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/24/90

Matsushita Electric Industrial Co. of Japan, one of the world's largest consumer electronics makers, posted a 7.9% rise in consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 to 571.73 billion yen ($3.78 billion) from 529.45 billion yen in fiscal 1989. Operating profit rose 8.5% to 452.72 billion yen from 417.19 billion yen, and net income rose 10% to 235.56 billion yen, or 108.34 yen a share, from 213.46 billion yen, or 99.94 yen a share. The consolidated results cover 163 Matsushita Electric Industrial companies as the nucleus of the Matsushita group, exporting products under the brand names National, Panasonic, Technics and Quasar.

Sales rose 9% to 6.002 trillion yen, a record, from 5.504 trillion yen. The company cited increased Japanese and overseas demand for its broadening lines of electric and electronics products.

The company predicts 6.6% increases in both sales to 6.4 trillion yen and net income to 251 billion yen in fiscal 1991, and a 4.1% rise in pretax profit to 595 billion yen.
