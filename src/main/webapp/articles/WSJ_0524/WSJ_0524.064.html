Canada Sells Bonds Totaling $841.5 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government auctioned one billion Canadian dollars (US$841.5 million) of bonds maturing June 6, 1992. The average accepted yield bid was 12.419% for a price equivalent of 99.709.

The coupon rate on the new bonds was set at 12.25%.

Proceeds will be used to redeem C$550 million of government bonds maturing June 6 and for general government purposes.
