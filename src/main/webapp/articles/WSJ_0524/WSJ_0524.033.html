Who's News: Sears, Roebuck & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

SEARS, ROEBUCK & Co. (Chicago) -- Russell S. Davis, formerly chief financial officer of Campeau Corp., was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this retail concern's Sears Merchandise Group unit. Mr. Davis, 54 years old, succeeds Jack Wirth, 60, who is retiring.
