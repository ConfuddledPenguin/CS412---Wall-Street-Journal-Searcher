Who's News: Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

FEDERAL HOME LOAN MORTGAGE Corp. (Reston, Va.) -- Thomas Ludlow Ashley, president of the Association of Bank Holding Companies and a former member of Congress; Woodward Kingman, an investment management consultant and former president of Government National Mortgage Association, or Ginnie Mae; and George L. Argyros, president and chief executive officer of Arnel Development Co., Costa Mesa, Calif., were named by President Bush to the board of this government-sponsored, publicly traded company, which operates a secondary market in home loans.
