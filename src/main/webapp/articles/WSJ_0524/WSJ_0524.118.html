Business Brief -- CPC International Inc.: Firm Forms Joint Venture With East German Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/24/90

CPC International Inc., Englewood Cliffs, N.J., said it formed a joint venture with an East German food concern to make and distribute consumer foods under CPC's Knorr trademark. CPC will have a majority interest in the new joint venture with VEB Suppina Auerbach. James R. Eiszner, CPC's chairman and chief executive officer, said CPC has been "exporting products to several Eastern European countries for some time and has had licensing agreements with Yugoslavia since 1968."

Knorr product sales in Europe approach $1 billion annually, CPC said.
