Business Brief -- DeKalb Energy Co.: Company Buys an Interest In 36 Oil and Gas Fields

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/24/90

DeKalb Energy Co. said it purchased an interest in 36 onshore oil and gas fields that it believes will increase its proven U.S. reserves by more than 70%. The $130.2 million acquisition from Pittsburgh-based Royal Producing Corp. Texas involves approximately 250 producing wells, most of them located on the Texas Gulf Coast, and continues DeKalb's announced strategy of concentrating its activity in that region. The Denver-based oil and gas producer acquired interests in 17 offshore lease blocks earlier this year and has opened a Houston exploration office.

Bruce P. Bickner, DeKalb's chairman and chief executive, said proven reserves from the properties are approximately 5.3 million barrels of oil and condensate and 50.4 billion cubic feet of natural gas.
