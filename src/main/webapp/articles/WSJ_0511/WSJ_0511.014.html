GEC Alsthom Turbine Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

PARIS -- The Franco-British heavy engineering group GEC Alsthom announced yesterday that its Ruston Gas Turbines Co. unit has won about 400 million francs ($72.7 million) of new orders from offshore oil sites in the North Sea. GEC Alsthom said that the orders cover three gas-powered turbines of 33 megawatts each for the Scott platform operated by New York-based Amerada Hess Corp. as well as three turbine-powered compressor groups for the East Brae platform operated by Marathon Oil UK Ltd. a unit of Pittsburgh-based USX Corp.

All of the equipment is scheduled for delivery in 1991.
