Business Brief -- Procter & Gamble Co.: Three Health, Beauty Units Are Consolidated Into Two

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

Procter & Gamble Co. consolidated three health and beauty care divisions into two units, in a further move to digest and reorganize acquired companies. Effective July 1, Cincinnati-based P&G said its existing beauty care; health and personal care; and Richardson-Vicks divisions will be restructured into two, one with responsibility for beauty care and the other for health care.

The new beauty care division will group together existing activities in beauty care, along with the Noxell division. In addition, certain businesses from Richardson-Vicks as well as personal cleansing-products operations from the bar soap and household cleaning products division will now come under beauty care.

The new health care division will encompass existing activities in health and personal care as well as remaining businesses from Richardson-Vicks and the Norwich division.
