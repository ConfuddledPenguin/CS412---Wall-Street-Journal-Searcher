Norway's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

OSLO -- Norway's consumer prices remained stable during the month ended April 15, but were up 4% from a year earlier, the Central Bureau of Statistics announced. The index for both March and April was 229.8. On April 15, 1989, the index was at 221, with the 1979 index of 100 as the base.
