Technology: New Matsushita Video Camera

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

TOKYO -- Matsushita Electric Industrial Co. unveiled a hand-held video camera-recorder equipped with an electronic image stabilizer. The camera, which the Japanese company says is the world's first to offer the feature, corrects involuntary camera movement. It will go on sale next month at a price of 165,000 yen ($1,055).
