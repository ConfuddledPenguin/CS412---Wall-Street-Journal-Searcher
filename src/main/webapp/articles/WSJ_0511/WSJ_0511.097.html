Drug Distributors in Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

DUBLIN, Ohio -- Cardinal Distribution Inc. said it agreed to acquire Ohio Valley Clarksburg Inc. for $27.1 million. Cardinal, a wholesale drug distributor based here, said the acquisition would add about $190 million to its current revenue base of more than $900 million.

Ohio Valley, based in Wheeling, W.Va., is a wholesale drug distributor serving customers in a five-state region. The agreement calls for a continuance of Ohio Valley's existing management and for Ohio Valley to continue operating under its existing name.
