Comfed's Sale to Barclays Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

LOWELL, Mass. -- Comfed Bancorp Inc. said it agreed to sell $1 billion of residential loan servicing rights to a Charlotte, N.C.-based unit of Barclays Bank PLC of London for about $28 million. The bank holding company said it will record a pretax gain of $5 million from the sale in its fiscal first quarter ending June 30. In the year-earlier first quarter, Comfed had a loss of $3.7 million.

The company said the servicing rights being sold to Barclays's American/Mortgage Corp. represent about 20% of Comfed's total residential portfolio.
