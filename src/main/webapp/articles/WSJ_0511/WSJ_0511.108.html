Enfield Slates Rights Issue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

TORONTO -- Enfield Corp. said its board approved a rights issue to raise as much as 27.8 million Canadian dollars (US$23.7 million). The holding company said holders will receive one right for each share held as of May 24. Four rights and 2.5 Canadian dollars will be required to purchase one new share.

Enfield currently has about 44.5 million shares outstanding. In trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday, Enfield closed at C$3, up five Canadian cents.

The company said the rights expire June 18. It said proceeds will be used to reduce debt.
