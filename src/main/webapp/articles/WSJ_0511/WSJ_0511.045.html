Dour Decorator

Home decor should show one's personality,

So from rugs and walls to couch,

My family's suggesting we do our place

In a style called

Early Grouch.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

Verse Luck

I'm strict on scanning,

And rhythm and rhyme;

I follow the rules

Each and every time.

I rigidly avoid

Any writing offense --

Except that, at times,

The poem makes no sense.

-- Joshua Adams.
