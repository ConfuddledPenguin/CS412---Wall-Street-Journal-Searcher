Who's News: NWA's Owners Set Several Appointments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- NWA Inc.'s new owners moved to sharpen their imprint on Northwest Airlines by placing their designees in several key management positions. Alfred A. Checchi, who heads the investor group that acquired NWA last September, named Bruce Rismiller, formerly senior vice president of the Chicago retailer Carson Pirie Scott & Co. to the new position of executive vice president, human resources.

Herbert D. Ihle, former controller of Pillsbury Co., was named senior vice president, finance, a new position.

Richard B. Hirst, former general counsel at Texas Air Corp.'s Continental Airlines unit, was named Northwest's general counsel. He succeeds Timothy R. Thorton, who was named to the new position of executive vice president, labor relations.

Two other current Northwest executives were promoted. Robert L. Fornaro, vice president, marketing planning, was named senior vice president in the same area. R. Terrence Rendleman, vice president for maintenance and engineering, was named senior vice president, technical operations.
