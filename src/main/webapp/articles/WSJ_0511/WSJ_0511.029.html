Gantos Sales Up 18%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc. said sales for the four-week period ended last Saturday totaled $21.5 million, an 18% increase from sales of $18.2 million in April 1989. Comparable-store sales rose 3%. For the latest 13 weeks, sales rose 20% to $61.2 million from $51 million; comparable-store sales increased 3%.

Gantos operates 145 women's apparel stores in 23 states.
