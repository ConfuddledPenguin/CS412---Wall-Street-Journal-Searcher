Who's News: Merrill Lynch & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

MERRILL LYNCH & Co. (New York) -- Merrill Lynch Capital Markets, a unit, named John F. Lyness managing director, debt trading and sales in London. Mr. Lyness formerly was head of mortgage trading and finance at Morgan Stanley & Co. Mr. Lyness succeeds Donald A. Wright, who was named president of Merrill Lynch Canada in Toronto. Merrill's former head of Canadian operations, Michael Sanderson, resigned to become president and chief executive officer of Instinet Corp., the equities trading unit of Reuters Hold ings PLC, London.
