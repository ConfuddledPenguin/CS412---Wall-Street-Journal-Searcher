Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Dayton Hudson Corp., shelf offering of up to $1 billion of debt securities and warrants.

Georgia-Pacific Corp., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.

Horsehead Resource Development Co., a unit of Horsehead Industries Inc., initial offering of 3,250,000 common shares, via Kidder, Peabody & Co. and Alex. Brown & Sons Inc.

Lifetime Corp., offering of 2,377,353 common shares, of which 960,688 shares will be sold by the company and 1,416,665 by holders.

Medical Imaging Centers offering of 2,174,000 of common shares, of which 1,510,429 will be sold by the company and 663,571 by holders, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co., and Volpe, Welty & Co.

Morton International Inc., offering of $200 million credit sensitive debentures, via Goldman, Sachs & Co., Salomon Brothers Inc. and Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc.

Oesterreichische Kontrollbank Aktiengesellschaft, shelf offering of up to $200 million of debt securities.

---

Corrections & Amplifications

LIFETIME Corp. filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering that includes 960,688 shares owned by company management. In Friday's edition, the ownership of the shares was listed incorrectly.

(WSJ May 14, 1990)
