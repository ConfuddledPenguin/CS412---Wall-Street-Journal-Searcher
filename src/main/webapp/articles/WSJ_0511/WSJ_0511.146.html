Business Brief -- U S West Inc.: Comcast Venture Awarded London Cable-TV Franchise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

U S West Inc., Denver, said its Cable London joint venture with Comcast Corp. was awarded a franchise to provide cable-television and telephone services to two boroughs of London. Construction of the $75 million system, to serve Islington and Hackney boroughs, is expected to begin in the fall. Service is supposed to be available by the end of this year. The franchise will serve about 152,000 homes and 12,000 businesses, U S West said.

Industry officials predicted that the move will expand business opportunities in the North London market, helping to justify the expense of developing locally generated television programming.

U S West, a diversified telecommunications concern, has won eight other cable franchises in the U.K. And the company is developing a cable venture in Hong Kong. Philadelphia-based Comcast, one of the largest cable operators in the U.S., also has interests in cellular communications.
