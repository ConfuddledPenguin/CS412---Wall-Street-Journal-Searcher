Imports in the Philippines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

MANILA, the Philippines -- Total imports in the Philippines reached $2.44 billion from Jan. 1 to April 7 of this year, up 11.9%, from $2.18 billion a year ago, the Central Bank of the Philippines said. Oil imports amounted to $400.3 million, or 20.1% higher than $333.2 million a year earlier. Oil receipts in the three-month period are equivalent to 16.4% of total imports.

The country imported $2.04 billion of non-oil merchandise, which is 10.2% more than $1.85 billion in the same period last year.
