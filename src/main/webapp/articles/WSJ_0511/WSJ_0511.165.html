Business Brief -- Action Auto Stores Inc.: Company to Close 11 Stores And Post 3rd-Quarter Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

Action Auto Stores Inc. said yesterday it will close 11 of its 68 retail stores and report a loss for the fiscal third quarter. The Flint, Mich., company, which is expected to report its third-quarter earnings next week, earned $295,038, or 10 cents a share, in the January-to-March period a year ago. The company didn't indicate how big the third quarter loss would be, but company Chairman and Chief Executive Officer James Sabo said, "Our plans and projections indicate improvement" in the coming quarters.

After the 11 stores are shut, Action Auto will operate 57 stores in Michigan.
