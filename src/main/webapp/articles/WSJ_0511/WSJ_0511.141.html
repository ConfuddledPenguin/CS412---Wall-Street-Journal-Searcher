Who's News: Portland General Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

PORTLAND GENERAL Corp. (Portland, Ore.) -- John W. Creighton Jr. was named a director of the utility holding company, expanding the board to 12 members. Mr. Creighton, 57, is president and a director of Weyerhaeuser Co., a forest products firm based in Tacoma, Wash.
