Elf to Upgrade Facilities

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

PARIS -- Societe Nationale Elf Aquitaine has budgeted 3 billion francs ($545 million) over the next three years to improve its distribution network in France and increase capacity for the production of high-octane gasoline, the state-controlled oil group said. At the retail level, Elf plans to build 600 new service stations and modernize another 900 outlets. In addition, some 2,700 stations, mostly in rural areas, will be improved.
