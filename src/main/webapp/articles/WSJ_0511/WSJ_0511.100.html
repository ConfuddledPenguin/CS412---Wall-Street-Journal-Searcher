Letters to the Editor: Watch What You Say]

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

I do not wish to sound too critical, but the headline (and the last paragraph) of Herbert Stein's April 12 editorial-page article -- "As They Said in Tokyo, `Kutubare GNP]'" -- attracted my attention. We never have said and never will say that in Tokyo. We say "Kutabare GNP]" and it certainly means "To hell with GNP." I suggest next time when you transcribe a foreign language, at least please check with somebody who knows the language.

Seiko Ikeda

San Francisco
