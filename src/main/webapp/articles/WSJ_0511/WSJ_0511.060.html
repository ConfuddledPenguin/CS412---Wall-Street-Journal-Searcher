Pall Corp. Agrees to Settlement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

YOUNGSTOWN, Ohio -- Commercial Intertech Corp. said its Cuno affiliate reached a $12 million settlement in a patent infringement suit against Pall Corp. The suit, filed in 1986, charged that Pall, of Glen Cove, N.Y., had infringed on certain of Cuno's nylon membrane patents. As part of the settlement, Pall agreed to pay Cuno $12 million for licensing of the patents.
