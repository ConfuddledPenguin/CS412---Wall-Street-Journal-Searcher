Who's News: General Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

GENERAL ELECTRIC Co. (Fairfield, Conn.) -- John E. Breen, 45 years old, formerly general manager in the lighting-products engineering department, was named vice president of the lighting-technology division at GE Lighting. Thomas A. Corcoran, 45, formerly general manager of the government communications-system department of GE Aerospace, was named vice president and general manager of the government electronic-systems division at the unit.
