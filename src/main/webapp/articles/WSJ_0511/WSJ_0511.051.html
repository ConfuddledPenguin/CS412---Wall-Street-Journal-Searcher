Who's News: Bear Stearns Cos.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

BEAR STEARNS Cos. (New York) -- The Bear, Stearns & Co. subsidiary named Donald C. Weeden, 47 years old, a managing director and to the new post of senior coverage officer in its capital markets area. Previously, Mr. Weeden was managing director and head of capital markets coverage with Morgan Stanley & Co., the investment bank and brokerage firm here.
