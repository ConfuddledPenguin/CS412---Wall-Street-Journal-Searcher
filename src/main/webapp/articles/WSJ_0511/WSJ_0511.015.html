Rochester Telephone Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Rochester Telephone Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire in an exchange of stock Orion Telephone Exchange Association, Orion, Ill. Terms weren't disclosed. Orion serves about 1,700 access lines.

The purchase is subject to approval of the board of Rochester Telephone and certain regulatory agencies, Rochester Telephone said.
