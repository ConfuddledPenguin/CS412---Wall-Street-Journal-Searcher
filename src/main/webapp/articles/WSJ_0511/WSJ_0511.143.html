Hallwood Energy Acquisitions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

DALLAS -- Hallwood Energy Partners L.P. said it completed its previously announced acquisitions of Energy Development Partners Ltd.; of the assets of Hutton/Apache 1982 Energy Income Fund Ltd. and Hutton/Apache Energy Income Fund II; and of interests that were tendered in 16 other oil and gas limited partnerships. The oil and gas concern also said it completed a one-for-20 reverse split of its units, leaving it with 8,541,906 publicly traded units outstanding.
