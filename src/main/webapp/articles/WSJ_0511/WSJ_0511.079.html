Business Brief -- Pirelli S.p.A.: Italian Concern Posts Rise Of 13% in Profit for Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

Pirelli S.p.A. posted a 13% increase in net profit to 320 billion lire ($266 million) for the fiscal year ended Dec. 31, up from 282 billion lire ($234.7 million) a year earlier. Because of a capital increase last year, per-share earnings rose by only 5.6% to 282 lire a share in 1989 from 267 lire.

The Italian tire and cable group struck the increase in profit on a 13% rise in revenue to 10.342 trillion lire last year from 9.120 trillion lire in 1988. Revenue adjusted for acquisitions made in 1989 was up 10.5%, Pirelli said in a statement.
