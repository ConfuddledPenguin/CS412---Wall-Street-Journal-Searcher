Who's News: Summagraphics Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

SUMMAGRAPHICS Corp. (Seymour, Conn.) -- The computer peripheral products concern named three executives to head its newly created domestic operating divisions. Doyle K. Cavin, 54 years old, formerly president of the Houston Instrument division, recently acquired from Ametek Inc., was named president, Houston Instrument division, based in Austin, Texas. Alan W. Morton, 52, former senior vice president, chief operations officer, was named president, digitizer division. James H. Whitney, 45, former senior vice president, sales, was named president, sales and service division.
