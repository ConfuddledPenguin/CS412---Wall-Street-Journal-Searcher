Business Brief -- Harrisons & Crosfield PLC: Firm to Acquire Crossley From Bowater Industries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

Harrisons & Crosfield PLC, a U.K. chemicals, building supplies and trading company, said it will acquire building materials supplier Crossley Builders' Merchants Ltd. for about #113 million ($189.8 million) from Bowater Industries PLC. Harrisons & Crosfield said it plans to finance the acquisition through a 1-for-5 rights issue meant to raise #147 million. The company will be issuing a total of 113,919,796 new ordinary shares for 132 pence ($2.22) a share.

Additionally, the company forecast a 17% increase in its full-year dividend for fiscal 1989. It said it intends to propose a final payout of nine pence a share, compared with the 7.7 pence in fiscal 1988.

Harrisons & Crossfield said the pending purchase fits into an acquisition pattern recently established to enhance growth in its core businesses.
