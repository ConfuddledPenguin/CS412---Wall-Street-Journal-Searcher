Canadian Steel Output Off

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 286,509 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, a 0.5% decline from 288,032 tons the previous week and a 7.2% drop from 308,572 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. From Jan. 1 through last Saturday, production totaled 4,891,163 tons, a 13% decline from 5,593,395 tons a year earlier.
