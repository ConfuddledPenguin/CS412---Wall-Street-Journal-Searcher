Business Brief -- British Columbia Telephone Co.: GTE Unit Plans to Offer $42.9 Million of Debentures

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

British Columbia Telephone Co. said it will offer in Canada 50 million Canadian dollars (US$42.9 million) of 12%, Series 1 debentures, maturing May 31, 2010. Vancouver-based British Columbia Telephone, a unit of Stamford, Conn.-based GTE Corp., said proceeds would be used to reduce short-term debt. The offering will be made through a group of securities dealers headed by RBC Dominion Securities Inc., Toronto.

The debentures will be priced at C$99.75 per C$100 face amount, and yield 12.03%. The issue is subject to regulatory approvals.
