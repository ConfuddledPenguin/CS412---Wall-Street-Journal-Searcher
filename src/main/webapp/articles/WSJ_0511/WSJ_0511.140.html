Air Wisconsin Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

APPLETON, Wis. -- Air Wis Services Inc. said April traffic at its Air Wisconsin unit increased 6.1%, to 37.3 million revenue passenger miles, from 35.2 million. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The regional carrier's load factor, or percentage of seats filled, slipped to 44.3% in the latest month, from the year-earlier 48.7%.

For the year to date, Air Wisconsin's traffic was 136.9 million revenue passenger miles, up 4%, from the year-ago $131.6 million. Load factor declined to 41.4%, from 45.9%.
