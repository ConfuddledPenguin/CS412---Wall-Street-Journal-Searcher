Technology: Sunrise Technologies Product

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/11/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Sunrise Technologies Inc. said it received permission from the Food and Drug Administration to market its dental laser device dLase 300 for soft tissue surgery in the mouth. According to the American Dental Association, about 1.5 million people in the U.S. have such surgery for gum disease, Sunrise said.

The company is also developing lasers for other dental and eye applications.
