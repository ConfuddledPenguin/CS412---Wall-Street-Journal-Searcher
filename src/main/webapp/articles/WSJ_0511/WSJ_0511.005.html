Dividend News: Winnebago Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

WINNEBAGO INDUSTRIES Inc., Forest City, Iowa, said it decided not to declare a semiannual cash dividend. The company has paid such a dividend since July 1985, the most recent being 10 cents a share paid Jan. 8. Chairman John K. Hanson said the decision resulted from the current downturn in the company's motorhome market.
