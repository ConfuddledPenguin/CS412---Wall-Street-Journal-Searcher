Business Brief -- Torchmark Corp.: Service Corp. International Division Will Be Acquired

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

Torchmark Corp. said its Liberty National Life Insurance Co. unit will acquire the life insurance division of Service Corp. International for $165.1 million. Torchmark, Birmingham, Ala., said the purchase of Family Service Life Insurance Co., which sells life insurance and annuities to fund prearranged funeral plans, is expected to enhance Liberty National's successful, but small line of burial insurance.

Service Corp., Houston, said it is disposing of the insurance operations as part of a restructuring that will allow it to focus on its core, funeral-related businesses.

A Service spokesman said the unit was a "big user of cash" and -- while profitable -- was considerably less so than its funeral operations. The unit specialized in marketing $1,000 to $5,000 insurance policies.

Torchmark said closing of the deal is expected within the next several months.
