Who's News: Food Lion Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

FOOD LION Inc. (Salisbury, N.C.) -- Tom E. Smith was elected to the additional post of chairman of this grocery store chain. He succeeds co-founder Ralph W. Ketner, who will remain on the board. Mr. Smith, who began his career with the company in 1958 as a bagger, will retain his posts as president and chief executive officer of the company.
