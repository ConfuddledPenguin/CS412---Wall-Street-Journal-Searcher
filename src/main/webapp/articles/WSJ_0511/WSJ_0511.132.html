Car Sales in Italy Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/11/90

MILAN, Italy -- Car sales in Italy edged up 0.7% in April to 220,163 cars from 218,632 a year earlier, according to the National Association of Car Manufacturers. The April figure brought sales in the first four months of 1990 to 933,134, up 4.4% from 893,566 cars sold in the comparable year-earlier period.
