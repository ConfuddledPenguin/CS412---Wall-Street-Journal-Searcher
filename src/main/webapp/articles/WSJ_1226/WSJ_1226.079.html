Who's News: EJV Partners L.P.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

EJV PARTNERS L.P. (New York) -- Three people joined as senior executives at this joint venture, which was formed to distribute bond prices and other market information via desktop terminals. Randall C. Hoyle, 53 years old, who joins in a senior operations role, previously was an information resources executive with Bankers Trust Corp. George R. Reis, 52, who will take a senior financial and administrative position, was previously a senior manager at County NatWest Inc., a unit of National Westminster Bank PLC. Gerald D. Mintz, 38, who will assume a senior sales and marketing position, leaves as vice president and general manager of Telerate Canada, which is part of Telerate Systems Inc., a wholly owned unit of Dow Jones & Co. EJV Partners is jointly owned by Citicorp, First Boston Corp., Goldman Sachs & Co., Morgan Stanley & Co., Salomon Brothers Inc., and Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc.
