Financing Business: American Capital & Research Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

AMERICAN CAPITAL & RESEARCH CORP., Fairfax, Va., said it completed a private placement of $25 million in senior preferred stock with units of Ifint S.A., an international investment firm affiliated with Italy's Agnelli group of companies. American Capital, a provider of environmental and engineering services, said it initially intends to use the proceeds to reduce debt. It issued 250 shares of 9.75% senior preferred sold with detachable warrants to purchase 2.2 million Class B shares at $12 a share. The transaction gives Ifint an option to purchase an additional 250 shares of senior preferred for $25 million in the next one to two years.
