McDonald & Co. Investments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

CLEVELAND -- McDonald & Co. Investments said it expects to purchase $6.1 million principal amount of its 8% convertible subordinated debentures, due March 1, 2011, tendered at or below $70 for each $100 principal amount of debentures under its Dutch auction tender offer, which expired Friday. In a Dutch auction, the price is gradually lowered until it meets a responsive bid and is sold. Each tendering holder whose debentures are to be acquired will receive a cash payment of $70 for each $100 principal amount of debentures tendered.

McDonald said the offer was oversubscribed by about $250,000 principal amount of debentures and will require pro rata acceptance. It said it expects that about 96% of principal amount of the debentures tendered will be accepted.
