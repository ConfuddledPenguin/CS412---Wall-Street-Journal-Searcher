Louisville Gas & Electric

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

LOUISVILLE -- The Louisville Gas & Electric Co. has been granted a rate adjustment that will increase annual gas and electric revenue by $6.1 million. The company had annual revenue in the year ended June 1990 of $679.3 million. Louisville Gas & Electric had requested an annual increase of $34.9 million from the Kentucky Public Service Commission. The average monthly residential electric bill will rise to $72.52 from $71.75 in the summer and will increase to $44.46 from $43.91 in the winter.
