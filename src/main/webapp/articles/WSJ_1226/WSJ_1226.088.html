Hillsborough-Curragh Project

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

BRAMPTON, Ontario -- Hillsborough Resources Ltd., an engineering and contracting services concern serving the Canadian mining industry, said it and Curragh Resources Inc., a Toronto-based lead and zinc mining concern, obtained 55 million Canadian dollars (US$47.4 million) in financing for their Mount Hundere lead-zinc project. The venture, of which Curragh owns 80% and Hillsborough 20%, was formed to develop a lead-zinc deposit in Canada's Yukon Territory. Curragh is the project operator. Hillsborough said the financing was obtained from a Canadian bank. Commercial production is expected to begin next summer. Curragh officials couldn't be reached for comment.
