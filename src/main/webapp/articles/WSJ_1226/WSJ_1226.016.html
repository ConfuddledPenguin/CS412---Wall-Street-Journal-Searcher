Hallador Petroleum Co. Meeting

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

DENVER -- Hallador Petroleum Co. said a special meeting of shareholders that had been scheduled for last Friday to vote on a 1-for-10 reverse stock split was delayed, owing to a one-day delay in mailing notice of the meeting. The effective date for the split is expected to be next Monday, the day for which the meeting was rescheduled.
