  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Frank Masi was named head of international equity sales and trading, a new post, effective Jan. 1, in the world-wide equities unit of Lehman Brothers, the investment banking and trading division of Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc., a subsidiary of this travel, financial and information services firm. Mr. Masi was previously a managing director in charge of international equities and head of over-the-counter trading at First Boston Corp., a unit of CS First Boston Inc.
