Dividend News: All American Television Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

ALL AMERICAN TELEVISION Inc., a New York-based distributor of television programming and seller of commercial time to advertisers, said it revised its record date for payment of a cash dividend in connection with its proposed merger with closely held Scotti Brothers Entertainment Industries Inc., a Los Angeles-based entertainment company. Holders of All American common are to receive a $2.50-a-share cash payout when the merger is completed. This will be payable to shares on the transaction's closing date, rather than on Jan. 2. The record date for voting at the annual meeting remains Jan. 2. Thus, All American shares won't trade ex-dividend until the first business day following the date the dividend is payable, which hasn't been determined. Completion of the merger, which is scheduled for late January or early February, is subject to conditions such as approval by All American holders of its recapitalization.
