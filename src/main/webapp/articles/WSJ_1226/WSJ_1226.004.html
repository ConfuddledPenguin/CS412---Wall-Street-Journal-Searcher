Mark IV Acquires Vapor Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

AMHERST, N.Y. -- Mark IV Industries Inc. said it acquired Vapor Corp., a unit of Brunswick Corp., for $39.5 million. Vapor, with annual sales of $66 million, is the largest manufacturer of door systems for rail, rail transit and bus vehicles in the U.S. and Canada. Mark IV is a diversified manufacturer.
