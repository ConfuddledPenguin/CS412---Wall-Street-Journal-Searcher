Business Brief -- Oregon Steel Mills Inc. Company Planning to Offer 2.5 Million Shares to Public

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

Oregon Steel Mills Inc. said it plans a public offering of 2.5 million shares. Proceeds will be used to repay short-term bank debt, fund capital expenditures and for other general corporate purposes, which may include working capital and acquisitions. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the Portland, Ore., company said the offering will be managed by Merrill Lynch & Co. and PaineWebber Inc., and includes an option that allows the underwriters to purchase as many as 375,000 additional shares for covering any over allotments, if any.

In composite trading Monday on the New York Stock Exchange, Oregon Steel's stock closed at $22.25 a share, down $1.75.

Oregon Steel produces steel plate and large-diameter steel pipe and has facilities in Portland, Ore., and Fontana and Napa, Calif.
