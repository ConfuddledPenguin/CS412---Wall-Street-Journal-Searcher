Who's News: Bertolli USA Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

BERTOLLI USA Inc. (Secaucus, N.J.) -- William C. Monroe, was named chief executive officer, a new post, at this olive oil marketing company. Mr. Monroe remains president and chief operating officer at Bertolli, a division of S.M.E. S.p.A., a food manufacturing and retail distribution company based in Italy.
