Redemption Notices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended Dec. 21, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

CHICAGO HEIGHTS, ILL., will redeem, on Feb. 1, 1991, its industrial development first mortgage revenue bonds, series 1975. (The B.F. Goodrich Co.-lessee.) Harris Trust & Savings Bank, Chicago, is paying agent.

MARION COUNTY HIGH SCHOOL BUILDING CORP., IND., will call for redemption on Jan. 1, 1991 its 5.3% bonds due Jan. 1, 1992 and 1993, and its 5.25% bonds due Jan. 1, 1994. The bonds are dated Dec. 1, 1971. INB National Bank, trustee.

SOUTH RIPLEY HIGH SCHOOL BUILDING CORP., IND., will redeem on Jan. 1, 1991, $105,000 principal amount of its first mortgage bonds dated Dec. 1, 1964 and due Jan. 1, 1992-1994. Friendship State Bank, Ind., is trustee.

CORPORATES

NORTHEAST SAVINGS, F.A. will call its U.S. $150,000,000 collateralized floating rate notes, due 1996, on Feb. 13, 1991. Bankers Trust New York is trustee.
