First Federal Savings & Loan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/26/90

MORGANTOWN, W.Va. -- First Federal Savings & Loan Association of Morgantown said it agreed to convert from a mutual to a stock form of organization and, simultaneously, be acquired by a unit of Eagle Bancorp Inc., Charleston. The merger/conversion will be accomplished through the offering of additional shares of Eagle Bancorp's common stock, but terms weren't disclosed. First Federal will merge with First Empire Federal Savings & Loan Association, a unit of Eagle Bancorp.
