International: Israel Land Development Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/26/90

TEL AVIV -- Israel Land Development Co. said it submitted an independent proposal to purchase a controlling interest in United Mizrahi Bank, the fourth-largest bank in Israel. Terms weren't disclosed. Israel Land said its proposal was submitted to Nechasim M.I. Ltd., a government-owned corporation established for the privatization of Israeli banks. It said it proposes to acquire a controlling interest in the bank by acquiring at least 26% of the bank's shares outstanding.
