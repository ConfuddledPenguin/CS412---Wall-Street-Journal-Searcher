Marketing Brief -- Heritage Media Corp.: Company Agrees to Acquire Rest of POP Radio Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

Heritage Media Corp. said it entered into a definitive agreement to acquire the 43% of New York-based POP Radio Corp. that it doesn't already own. The Dallas owner of television and radio stations said the agreement calls for holders of each common share outstanding of POP Radio to receive $20.50. If the transaction is completed after July 31, POP Radio shareholders also will receive an additional amount equal to interest accruing at the rate of 10% a year on $20.50 a share from Aug. 1 to the date of completion.

Although a special committee of POP Radio board members has approved the transaction, the merger is subject to approval by POP Radio shareholders, the receipt of financing and certain regulatory approvals and third party consents, Heritage said. POP Radio provides customized radio programs for broadcast in retail outlets.
