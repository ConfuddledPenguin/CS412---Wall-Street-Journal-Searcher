Nashua Corp. Sells Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

NASHUA, N.H. -- Nashua Corp. said it completed the sale of the international portion of its office systems and supplies group to Gestetner Holdings PLC for $151 million. Gestetner, a United Kingdom office-equipment supplier, also assumes about $51 million of Nashua debt in the transaction.

Nashua reiterated that "it intends to use a significant portion of the net proceeds from this sale to repurchase Nashua's common stock." The company didn't indicate the amount of stock it intends to buy back.
