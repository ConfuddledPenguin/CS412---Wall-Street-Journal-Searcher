Tosco Unit Slates Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

SANTA MONICA, Calif. -- Tosco Corp., in a move to reduce its debt, said that its Seminole Fertilizer Corp. unit signed a letter of intent to sell its 50% stake in Fort Meade Chemical Products to U.S. AgriChemicals Corp., a unit of Sinochem American Holdings Inc. A spokesman for Tosco, an independent refining concern, said terms of the sale weren't disclosed. Sinochem American Holdings, which is owned by the Chinese government, already owns 50% of Fort Meade Chemical.
