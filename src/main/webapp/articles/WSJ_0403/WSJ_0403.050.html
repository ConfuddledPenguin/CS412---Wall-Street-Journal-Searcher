Black & Decker Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

TOWSON, Md. -- Black & Decker Corp. said it concluded the sale of its footwear-materials business to United Machinery Group Ltd. of Britain for about $125 million. The divestiture is part of the power-tool company's consolidation of Emhart Corp., acquired last May for $2.8 billion. Proceeds from the sale will be used to pay down acquisition debt, the company said.
