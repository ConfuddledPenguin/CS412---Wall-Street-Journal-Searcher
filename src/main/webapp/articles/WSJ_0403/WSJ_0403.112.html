Business Brief -- Atlantic Richfield Co.: Los Angeles Firm to Open 100 Mini Markets in Canada

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

Atlantic Richfield Co. said it signed a licensing agreement with the Fas Gas Oil unit of Parkland Industries Ltd., an Alberta oil and gas concern, to open about 100 am/pm mini markets in Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Eastern British Columbia. Terms weren't disclosed. The agreement is an expansion of Atlantic Richfield's mini markets abroad. The Los Angeles oil, gas and minerals concern, currently has more than 700 am/pm outlets in the U.S. franchised by Atlantic Richfield. The company said it will seek to sign licensing agreements in other parts of Canada.

In February, it signed a licensing and joint-venture agreement with Indalsa, based in Madrid, to open more than 600 am/pm mini markets in Spain and Portugal. Atlantic Richfield entered into a licensing agreement with Kyodo Oil of Tokyo in 1988 calling for 500 am/pm mini markets to be built in Japan. Atlantic Richfield also entered into a licensing agreement with Wei-Chuan Foods Corp. in Taiwan where 500 am/pm mini markets are planned.
