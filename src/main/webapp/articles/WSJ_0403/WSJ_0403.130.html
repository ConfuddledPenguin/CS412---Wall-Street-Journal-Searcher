International Brief -- London & Edinburgh Trust PLC: Sweden's SPP Boosts Stake To 50.02% of Firm's Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

Sweden's Forsakringsbolaget SPP, Omsesidigt (SPP) said it bought an additional 37.4 million ordinary shares in London & Edinburgh Trust PLC (LET), boosting its control of the British property group to 50.02% of ordinary shares. SPP said its purchase of a further 20.1% of LET shares was made both at the cash offer price of 220 pence ($3.62) a share and the 223.5 pence a share its offer is worth inclusive of a special 3.5 pence interim dividend. Before launching its #500 million ($824 million) bid, SPP owned about 1.2% of LET shares.

Together with the 28.8% of LET already irrevocably pledged to accepting the offer, SPP now has direct ownership or irrevocable acceptances of 50.02% of the U.K. concern.
