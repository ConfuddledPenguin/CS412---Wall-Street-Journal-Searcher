Business Brief -- Omnicare Inc.: Beeber Pharmacies, Serving Nursing Homes, Is Acquired

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

Omnicare Inc., Cincinnati, said it purchased Beeber Pharmacies Inc. of Dayton, Ohio, which provides pharmacy services to nursing homes. The price wasn't disclosed. Omnicare said Beeber had revenue of about $4 million in the fiscal year ended last June 30. Omnicare had 1989 revenue of $95 million, mainly in medical and dental supplies for private practitioners and respiratory equipment for home care.

Nursing home pharmacy service is an attractive business, Omnicare said, because the institutions are 95% occupied, and an aging population indicates this market will increase. Omnicare entered the business in December 1988, with the purchase of Home Care Pharmacy Inc., Cincinnati, which had revenue last year of about $10 million.
