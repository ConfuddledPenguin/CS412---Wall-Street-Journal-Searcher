Business Brief -- Suffield Financial Corp.: Banking Concern to Post $3 Million Quarterly Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

Suffield Financial Corp. said it expects to report a $3 million loss for the first quarter. The Suffield, Conn., bank holding company added that it expects to increase its loan loss reserves by $3.5 million to $4 million to a total of about $12 million. Citing "the continued general deterioration of the New England real estate market," the company said the level of nonperforming loans at both its Coastal Bancorp, in Portland, Maine, and at Suffield Bank has increased since Dec. 31.

Suffield Financial earned $516,000, or nine cents a share, in the first quarter of 1989.
