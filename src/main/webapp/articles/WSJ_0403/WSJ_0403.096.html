Systems Center Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

RESTON, Va. -- Systems Center Inc. said it completed its purchase of Cincom Systems Inc.'s Net-Master distribution business in a transaction valued at about $43.5 million. The software concern also said it intends to complete the acquisition of Software Developments Pty. of Australia in May. Software Developments is the owner and developer of the Net-Master family of network and systems management products.
