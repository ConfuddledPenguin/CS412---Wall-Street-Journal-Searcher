Marketing & Media: Bond Media Plans Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

SYDNEY -- Bond Media Ltd. of Australia said Monday it will sell its radio stations and other assets to focus on its core television network and SKY Channel satellite broadcasting operations, as part of a restructuring designed to reduce its debt. Warren Jones, executive chairman of Bond Media, said in a statement that the company will be renamed Nine Network Ltd., reflecting the core television operations.

Bond Media is seeking new equity participants to inject cash into the company, in part to help fend off Australian financier Kerry Packer.
