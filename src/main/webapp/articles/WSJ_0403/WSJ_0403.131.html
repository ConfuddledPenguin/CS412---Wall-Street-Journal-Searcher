Business Brief -- Norton Co.: Payment to Morgan Stanley In Takeover Fight Disclosed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

Morgan Stanley & Co. will be paid an initial fee of $1.5 million for aiding Norton Co.'s effort to fend off a hostile offer by BTR PLC, plus another $5.1 million if BTR drops the offer. Norton, a Worcester, Mass., abrasives and chemical maker, disclosed the fees in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Morgan Stanley also would receive the $5.1 million added payment if Norton is acquired by a third party, if Norton undertakes a recapitalization or if it spins off assets, among other things, to fend off the bid.

In addition, Norton said it is paying another investment bank, Lodestar Group, an initial fee of $250,000 plus another $250,000 on April 19, the filing said. If BTR drops its offer or if Norton implements an alternative transaction, Lodestar will receive another $500,000 to $1.5 million, the filing said.
