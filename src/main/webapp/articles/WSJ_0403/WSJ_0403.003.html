Who's News: U.S. Shoe Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

U.S. SHOE Corp. (Cincinnati) -- K. Brent Somers, 41 years old, formerly chief financial officer at this footwear and specialty retailer's Lenscrafters unit, was named the unit's chief financial officer and vice president, finance, succeeding Edward Klee, who was named footwear division vice president and group controller, new posts.
