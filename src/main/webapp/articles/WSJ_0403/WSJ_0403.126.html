Business Brief -- International Paper Co.: Concern Buys Dixon Paper, Closely Held Denver Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

International Paper Co. said it purchased Dixon Paper Co., a closely held fine paper, graphic art supply and industrial paper company based in Denver. Terms weren't disclosed. Dixon, which has been owned by a management group since 1972, had 1989 sales of about $250 million. Dixon operates 12 distribution centers, six retail stores and two sales offices.

International Paper, with 1989 sales of $11.4 billion, said in Purchase, N.Y., that the acquisition, completed Sunday, complements its wholesale and retail distribution businesses. International Paper said the addition of Dixon would give it a total of 183 wholesale and retail locations nationwide.
