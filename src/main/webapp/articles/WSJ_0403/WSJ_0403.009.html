Harsco Completes Sale of Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

HARRISBURG, Pa. -- Harsco Corp. said it completed the sale of its Kinnear unit to Wayne-Dalton Corp. for $27 million, and the diversified industrial company said it will record "a modest gain" on the sale. Kinnear, based in Columbus, Ohio, makes doors and security grills. Wayne-Dalton, based in Mount Hope, Ohio, makes metal and wood-sectional doors.
