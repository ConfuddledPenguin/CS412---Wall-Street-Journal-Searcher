Universal Foods Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

MILWAUKEE -- Universal Foods Corp. said it agreed to acquire the international flavors business of Felton Worldwide, a unit of Harrisons & Crosfield PLC of Britain. Terms weren't disclosed. Last year Felton's international flavors revenue was $34 million, Universal said. Universal, a food flavors and ingredients concern, said it expects to complete the acquisition this month.
