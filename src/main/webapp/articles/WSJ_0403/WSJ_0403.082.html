Who's News: Advanced Micro Devices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

ADVANCED MICRO DEVICES Inc. (Sunnyvale, Calif.) -- William T. Siegle, 51 years old, was named vice president of the Integrated Technology Division at this chip company. Before joining Advanced Micro, Mr. Siegle was director of International Business Machines Corp.'s Advanced Semiconductor Technology Center in East Fishkill, N.Y. Mr. Siegle fills a position at Advanced Micro that had been vacant.
