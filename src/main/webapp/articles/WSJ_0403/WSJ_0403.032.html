USX Unit Exploration Rights

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

HOUSTON -- Marathon Petroleum Australia Ltd., a subsidiary of Marathon Oil Co., said it was awarded rights to explore Block WA-217-P in northwestern Australia. The block is about 280 miles west of Darwin and the permit covers about 1.4 million acres, said Marathon, which has a 35% interest in the block.

The exploration permit has a six-year initial term and renewal options. Marathon said drilling is to begin next year. Marathon is a unit of USX Corp., Pittsburgh.

Marathon's other partners are Kuppec Australia Pty., also with a 35% interest during exploration, and Husky Oil International Inc., with a 30% interest.
