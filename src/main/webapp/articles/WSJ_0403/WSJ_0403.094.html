South Korean Consumer Prices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- South Korea's consumer prices surged 1.3% in March from the previous month, pushing up the nation's inflation rate for the January-March period to 3.2%, according to a government report Monday. The Economic Planning Board noted that March's 1.3% rise followed increases of 1% in January and 0.9% in February.

Wholesale prices rose 0.4% in March from February, it said.
