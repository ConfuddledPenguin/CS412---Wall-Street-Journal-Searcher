Oppenheimer Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- Oppenheimer Industries Inc. said its board approved terms of a sale of controlling interest in the real estate concern to two California entities. Under a preliminary agreement, control would be transferred to a group composed of U.S. Real Estate Advisors Inc. and Wespac Investors Trust III. The transaction calls for the sale of 825,000 Oppenheimer shares to Wespac for $825,000 and for additional shares to be issued to the new owners after they obtain certain financing, including a $10 million credit line for Oppenheimer.

The proposed transaction would require approval by Oppenheimer holders.
