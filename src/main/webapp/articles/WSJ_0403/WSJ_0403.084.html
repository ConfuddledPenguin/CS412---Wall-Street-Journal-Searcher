With changes in their points of view,

That asbestos is good for you.

Deliverance

Unanswered letters, unpaid bills

No longer bring guilt-feeling chills;

I face complaints calmly, not nervous,

Like most, I blame the Postal Service.

-- Bern Sharfman.
