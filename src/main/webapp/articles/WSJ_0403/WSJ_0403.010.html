General Dynamics Wins Air Force Pact For $758.9 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

WASHINGTON -- General Dynamics Corp. won a $758.9 million Air Force contract for F-16 aircraft and a $9.2 million Army contract for Stinger missile services.

United Technologies Corp. was given a $610.6 million Air Force contract for jet fighter engines.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. received a $90 million Navy contract for F/A-18 aircraft.

Contel Federal Systems Inc., a unit of Contel Corp., got a $46.3 million Air Force contract for an automated weather system.

Rockwell International Corp. received a $42 million Air Force contract for an information distribution system.

American Telephone & Telegraph Co. was issued a $32.1 million Navy contract for oceanographic equipment.

Litton Systems, a unit of Litton Industries Inc., got a $20.4 million Air Force pact for radar-warning receivers.

CAE-Link Corp. got a $15.6 million Navy contract for flight simulators.
