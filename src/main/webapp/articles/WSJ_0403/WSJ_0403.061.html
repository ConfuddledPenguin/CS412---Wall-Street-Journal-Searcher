Hong Kong Retail Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

HONG KONG -- The value of Hong Kong's total retail sales fell 1% to 10.60 billion Hong Kong dollars (US$1.36 billion) in January from HK$10.67 billion (US$1.37 billion) a year earlier, the government reported. The decline was caused by a slowing in both local and tourist spending, the government said.

January sales grew 6% from December's HK$10.04 billion.
