GF Sets Pact With Investor

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

YOUNGSTOWN, Ohio -- GF Corp. said it agreed in principle with an investor willing to provide funds for the financially troubled office furniture producer, but declined to disclose the amount of the proposed financing, its form and the identity of the prospective investor until a final agreement is negotiated. GF said it will obtain equity or debt funding or both within seven days, subject to negotiation of the final pact and approval by GF's lenders. The company operates two plants; it closed its initial plant in Youngstown recently, as part of a restructuring intended to reduce costs.
