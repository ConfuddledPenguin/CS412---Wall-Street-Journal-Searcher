First Financial Bancorp Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

MONROE, Ohio -- First Financial Bancorp, a bank holding company, said it definitively agreed to acquire Fidelity Federal Savings Bank, a federally chartered savings bank in Marion, Ind., for about $12 million of First Financial common stock. Under the agreement, each share of Fidelity Federal will be exchanged for about $20 market value of First Financial stock. Fidelity Federal shareholders will vote on the transaction in July, the company said. First Financial has assets of $1.2 billion, and Fidelity Federal, $85 million.
