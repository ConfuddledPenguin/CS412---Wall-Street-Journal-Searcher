Universal Corp. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- Universal Corp.'s Universal Leaf Tobacco Co. unit said it will acquire Gebrueder Kulenkampff AG, a Bremen, West Germany-based leaf tobacco company. Terms weren't disclosed. Gebrueder Kulenkampff and affiliates are an international leaf-tobacco trading group with more than $200 million in annual revenue. Universal Leaf has revenue of about $1 billion. The purchase is expected to close June 30.

Kulenkampff will continue to operate under present management as a subsidiary of Universal Leaf.
