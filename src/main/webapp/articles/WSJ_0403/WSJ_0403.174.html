Business Brief -- Salomon Inc.: Investment Banking Unit To Open East Berlin Office

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

Salomon Brothers Inc. will open an office in East Berlin, in hopes of winning a slice of the expected wave of corporate finance business that will come with German reunification. Initially, the East Berlin outpost will have four or five professionals from a staff of about 60 working for the Salomon Inc. unit in Frankfurt, West Germany.

Company officials emphasized that the move makes Salomon the first non-German investment bank to push into East Germany. Salomon also explored setting up offices in Prague, Czechoslovakia, and Vienna. James Massey, chief executive officer of Salomon Brothers International in London, said the choice of East Berlin "will enable us to bring to bear our skills and resources in the important and exciting process of German reunification."
