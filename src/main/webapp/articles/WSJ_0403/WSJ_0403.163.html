Dividend News: Chiquita Brands Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

CHIQUITA BRANDS Inc. said directors doubled the quarterly dividend on common shares to 10 cents a share and announced plans for a public stock offering. The Cincinnati company, with interests in bananas, specialty meats and other foods, will pay the increased dividend on May 21 to stock of record May 7. Chiquita said it plans to file a registration statement covering public sale of 6.1 million common shares, including 5.2 million shares owned by Great American Communications Co. and 925,000 shares to be sold by Chiquita. Both Chiquita and Great American, a broadcasting concern, are controlled by American Financial Corp., a closely held financial services company that has major investments in a number of other companies. The public offering would eliminate Great American's Chiquita holdings. After the offering, American Financial and its affiliates will own 65.5% of Chiquita's shares outstanding, down from the current 81.5%.
