SofTech Inc. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- SofTech Inc., a developer of software products and services, said it signed a letter of intent to acquire Information Decisions Inc., of Grand Rapids, Mich. Terms weren't disclosed. The acquisition of Information Decisions, a private value-added reseller of local area network products, is part of SofTech's strategy to expand into the commercial systems integration market, the company said.

Information Decisions, which employs more than 80 people, had revenue of $11 million in 1989. It will operate as a wholly-owned unit of SofTech, and Stephen G. Deckrow will remain as president.
