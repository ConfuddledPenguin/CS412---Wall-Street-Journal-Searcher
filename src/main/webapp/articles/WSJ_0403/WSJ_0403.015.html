Lockheed Contract Is Extended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

WASHINGTON -- The National Aeronautics and Space Administration said it extended Lockheed Corp.'s engineering-support contract at Johnson Space Center through May 1995. The extension is valued at $444.6 million. Lockheed performs engineering services for the space shuttle, space station and other programs. With the extension, the contract totals $884.8 million, NASA said.
