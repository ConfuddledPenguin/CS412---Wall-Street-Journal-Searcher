Alltel Buys Rest of Franchise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

HUDSON, Ohio -- Alltel Corp. said it completed the purchase of the 55% interest it doesn't already own in a cellular franchise that covers the Aiken, S.C., and Augusta, Ga., region. Terms weren't disclosed. Alltel, a telephone service and equipment concern based here, acquired the remaining interests from Pond Branch Telephone Co. based in Gilbert, S.C.
