Advanced Medical Buys a Line

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Advanced Medical Technologies Inc., which specializes in drug delivery systems, said it completed the previously announced acquisition of 93% of the Imed division of Henley Group Inc., a Hampton, N.H.-based aerospace and automotive concern, for $106 million. Advanced Medical formed a new unit to make the acquisition, in which Henley retains a 7% stake. Imed makes intravenous infusion pumps and had sales of $115 million last year.
