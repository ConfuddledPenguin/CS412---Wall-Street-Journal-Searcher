Business Brief -- Smith Laboratories Inc.: Posner Says He Has Stake Of 5% in San Diego Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

Miami financier Victor Posner said he holds a 5% stake in Smith Laboratories Inc., San Diego, for investment purposes. Mr. Posner said in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that he holds 674,735 Smith common shares, for which he paid $1,545,291. On March 21, Mr. Posner bought 66,000 Smith shares in the open market for $3.125 each.

Mr. Posner said he acquired his Smith stock for investment purposes. He will review periodically his investment in the company and may at any time decide to increase or decrease it, he said, depending on market conditions and other factors. He said he does not have any present plans or proposals with respect to the company.

Last December, Smith entered into a merger agreement with Columbia Hospital Corp., a closely held hospital concern controlled by former Bass family financial adviser Richard Rainwater and Richard Scott of Fort Worth, Texas.

Timothy Wollaeger, president and chief executive officer of Smith, a maker of medical equipment, said he believed Mr. Posner had boosted his stake in the company for investment purposes only. "I view it neither favorably nor unfavorably," Mr. Wollaeger said.
