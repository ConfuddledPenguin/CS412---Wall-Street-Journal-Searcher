Business Brief -- Zurn Industries Inc.: Power Equipment Company Wins $66 Million in Pacts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

Zurn Industries Inc. said it was awarded two contracts totaling $66 million to design and build combined cycle cogeneration power plants. The Erie, Pa., power equipment maker said one is from a member of Sithe/Energies Group of New York to build a 56-megawatt plant in Sherill, N.Y. The plant is scheduled to begin operations by October 1991. Electricity from the plant is to be sold to Niagara Mohawk Power Corp. Steam from the plant will be used by Oneida Silverware Co., Zurn said.

The second order is for a 50-megawatt plant for Coastal Power Product Co. at Fulton, N.Y. Coastal is a unit of Coastal Corp., a Houston energy concern. The plant, which is set to begin commercial operation in May 1991, will provide electricity to Niagara Mohawk Power Corp. and will sell steam to Nestle Foods Corp., Zurn said.
