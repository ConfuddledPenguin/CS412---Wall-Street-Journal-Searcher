Sorg's San Francisco Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

NEW YORK -- Sorg Inc., the financial printer, said its Sorg Printing Co. of California unit will close its San Francisco operations unless it can sell them soon. Customer jobs that are in-house will be fully completed, Sorg said. Sorg, which also publishes a legal manual and provides support services for lawyers, has withdrawn from Los Angeles and is closing its office in Washington.

Sorg, which is operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, said it is discussing with several parties the sale of part or all of its business.
