U.S. Steel Production Fell 1.7% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by U.S. mills decreased 1.7% last week to 1,904,000 tons from 1,936,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 6.7% from the 2,040,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 85% of its capability last week, compared with 86.4% the previous week and 91.3% a year ago.
