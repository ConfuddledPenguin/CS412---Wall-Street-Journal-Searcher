Italy's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

MILAN -- Italy's consumer price index rose 0.4% in March from February and was up 6.1% from a year earlier, Istat, the state statistical institute, said. Istat said the index registered 104.4 in March, compared with 104.0 in February and with 103.3 in January.

Calculation of the index has been revised as of January 1990 on the basis 1989 equals 100. Formerly the index was calculated on a base of 1985 equals 100. The index isn't seasonally adjusted.

The March increase followed a 0.8% monthly rise in February, when the year-to-year increase was 6.3%.
