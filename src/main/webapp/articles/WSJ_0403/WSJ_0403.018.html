MCI Expands PrimeTime Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

WASHINGTON -- MCI Communications Corp. said it expanded its PrimeTime calling plan to start at 5 p.m., rather than 7 p.m. weekdays. The change follows adjustments in American Telephone & Telegraph Co.'s Reach Out America plan that include a 15% discount off standard evening rates between 5 p.m. and 10 p.m.

MCI's PrimeTime plan, introduced last May, offers customers an hour of out-of-state calling within the U.S. for $8 a month, provided that the calls are made between 5 p.m. and 8 a.m. weekdays, and on Saturdays and Sundays. Additional calls during those hours are billed at the rate of $6.50 an hour.
