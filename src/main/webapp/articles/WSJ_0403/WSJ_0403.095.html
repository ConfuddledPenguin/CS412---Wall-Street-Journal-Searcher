Good Guys 2nd-Quarter Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Good Guys reported sales for the second quarter ended Saturday rose 48%, to $62.5 million from $42.2 million a year earlier. In the first half, sales rose 48% to $149.8 million from $101 million a year earlier. Comparable store sales rose 24% in the quarter and 24% in the first half.
