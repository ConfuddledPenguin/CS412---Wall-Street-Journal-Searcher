Business Brief -- Communications Transmission: Waivers, Given by Lenders On Some Accords, Expired

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

Communications Transmission Inc., Austin, Texas, announced the expiration of waivers it had been granted by senior and subordinated lenders on certain financial and other covenants under its $156.7 million credit agreement and its $103 million subordinated debt agreement. The microwave and fiber-optic communications operator said that it is current on interest payments due to its senior lenders, but that it hadn't made the quarterly and semiannual interest payments that were due yesterday to its subordinated lender.

Communications Transmission said that it submitted a plan to lenders for reorganizing the company and its subsidiary, ALC Communications Corp. of Birmingham, Ala., but that it can't offer any assurance it will be able to complete a restructuring on terms acceptable to its lenders.
