Greek Consumer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/03/90

ATHENS, Greece -- Greece's consumer price index for February rose 0.1% from the previous month and 17% from February 1989, the National Economy Ministry said. The ministry attributed February's 0.1% rise mainly to boosts in drugs and foodstuffs and increases in medical fees and rents. These were offset by a 4.6% decrease in clothing and footwear prices because of winter sales.

The index registered 342.2 on the basis of 1982 equals 100. The index isn't seasonally adjusted.
