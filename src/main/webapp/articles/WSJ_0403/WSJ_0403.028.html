West German Output Off

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

BONN -- West Germany's industrial production dropped a seasonally adjusted 0.8% in February from January as energy output plunged 10% because of warm winter weather, the Bonn Economics Ministry said. Though the unusually mild winter depressed output in the utility sector, the construction industry benefited and boosted production 5% during the month. Mining output increased 1.5%, while production in the manufacturing industry as a whole was off 0.5%.

January production data were revised upward slightly to show a 2.1% month-to-month rise, up from a preliminary increase of 1.7% recorded earlier.

February's industrial production was 4.2% higher than a year earlier, the ministry said.
