Wells Fargo Acquisitions

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/03/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Wells Fargo & Co. said it completed two purchases. The bank holding concern said it completed the purchase of Torrey Pines Group, of Solana Beach, Calif., for about $74.5 million. The concern operates seven branches in San Diego County. In addition, Wells Fargo completed the $160 million purchase of Central Pacific Corp., a Bakersfield, Calif., concern with 28 branches.

The moves are part of Wells Fargo's strategy of aquiring smaller banks with strong market shares in desirable consumer markets.
