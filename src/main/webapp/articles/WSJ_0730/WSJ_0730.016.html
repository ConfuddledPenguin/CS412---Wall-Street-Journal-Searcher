Hunter Environmental To Acquire Remainder Of Hunter Industrial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

SOUTHPORT, Conn. -- Hunter Environmental Services Inc. said it reached an agreement with investor J. Anthony Forstmann to acquire the remainder of Hunter Industrial Facilities Inc., which is developing an underground waste-processing plant in Texas. Currently, Hunter Environmental owns 63% and Mr. Forstmann 26% of Hunter Industrial. The rest is in public hands.

Hunter Environmental's principal assets are its Hunter Industrial holdings and an interest in an Illinois utility holding company. The merger with Hunter Industrial, a spokesman said, will make "a more efficient company."

Hunter Environmental will issue one common share for each 2.75 shares of Hunter Industrial's common and preferred that it doesn't already own. To acquire all of Hunter Industrial, the company will issue a total of 4.3 million shares, reflecting conversion of certain debt securities to common stock.

In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Hunter Environmental Service's stock closed at $4, unchanged. Hunter Industrial trades over the counter on the pink sheets.
