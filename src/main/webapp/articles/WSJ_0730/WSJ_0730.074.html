Marketing Brief -- Live Entertainment Inc.: Revolving Credit Agreement With Two Banks Is Signed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

Live Entertainment Inc., an entertainment software supplier, distributor and retailer based in Van Nuys, Calif., said it signed a $100 million revolving credit agreement with Credit Lyonnais Bank Nederland and Chemical Bank. The agreement, which runs through Jan. 2, 1992, is an amendment to the company's $50 million credit facility with Credit Lyonnais. Under the increased credit line, Credit Lyonnais and Chemical will provide $60 million, and $40 million is expected to be provided by a group of U.S. and overseas banks.

The credit line will be used for working capital by Live Entertainment and its Live Home Video Inc. subsidiary.
