Business Brief -- Sharon Steel Corp.: Accord Is Set on Payment With Posner and His Firms

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

Sharon Steel Corp. said it reached agreement in principle with Victor Posner, a Miami financier, and companies he controls calling for those companies to pay Sharon $7.5 million. Mr. Posner formerly controlled Sharon Steel, which now is operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. The agreement is subject to approval by the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for the western district of Pennsylvania.

The agreements will settle all of the disputes and claims between Sharon Steel, based in Sharon, Pa., and DWG Corp., NVF Co., Pennsylvania Engineering Corp. and Mr. Posner, said F.E. Agnew III, trustee of Sharon Steel. Mr. Posner, who controls DWG, NVF and Pennsylvania Engineering, couldn't be reached for comment.

A trustee appointed to manage Sharon in January 1988 and his successor, Mr. Agnew, filed a number of suits and claims against Mr. Posner and companies he controls involving alleged excess compensation paid to Mr. Posner by Sharon, alleged use of Sharon's funds for personal use and other matters.
