United Medicorp Inc. Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

DALLAS -- United Medicorp Inc. said it sold two million common shares -- 1,850,000 held by the medical insurance claims processing company and 150,000 by stockholders -- for $5 each in a public offering. Proceeds will be used to repay short-term debt, provide funding for advance funding services, buy certain shares from a holder and provide working capital.

United Medicorp initially filed on June 4 to sell 2.5 million shares but later amended this filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Eppler, Guerin & Turner Inc. was the underwriter.
