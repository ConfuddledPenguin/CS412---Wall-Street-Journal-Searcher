Chyron Corp. Shipments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

MELVILLE, N.Y. -- Chyron Corp., a maker of electronic graphics equipment, said its fourth-quarter shipments totaled about $10 million, compared with a projected $14 million. The company blamed the shortfall, in part, on production problems due to "working capital pressures," as well as soft market conditions.

The company also said it is continuing to discuss its financial situation with European American Bank and Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. to arrange a restructuring of bank loans. The company has been in technical default on certain loans since mid-1989.
