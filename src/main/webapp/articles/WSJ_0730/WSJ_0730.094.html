Business Brief -- Michelin Group: Uniroyal Goodrich to Raise Private Brand Tire Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

Uniroyal Goodrich Tire Co. said it will increase prices on private brand tires by 2% to 4.5% effective Sept. 4. Uniroyal is a unit of Michelin Group of France. The move by the Akron, Ohio, company is part of a general industry increase in list prices of replacement tires. Tire makers previously announced increases of 2% to 4% on replacement tires sold under the companies' own brands. They're following up with an increase in the price of tires sold to major wholesalers, mass merchandisers and other concerns that contract with tire makers to produce tires for them under private brands.

Tire prices have been discounted more than usual this year, mainly because a slowdown in auto and truck production forced some tire makers to sell in the replacement market tires they produced for Detroit. Analysts doubt that tire producers will collect the full amount of their recently announced increases, but they do expect some increase in realized prices.
