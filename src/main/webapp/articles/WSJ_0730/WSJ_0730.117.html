Letters to the Editor: MAD in the Mideast

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

Thank you for the interview with Saddam Hussein, president of Iraq ("Dictator Speaks," page one, June 28). It provided Arab insights into the Middle East situation that are usually not carried by other major American newspapers. You are to be commended for providing readers with the other side of the story. President Hussein's suggestion that the U.S. lend atomic weapons to Iraq is of interest. Mutually assured destruction may be the only effective way to prevent aggression in that part of the world.

George W. Polhemus

Pembroke, N.C.
