Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Japan Emerging Equity Fund Inc., initial offering of five million common shares, via Daiwa Securities America Inc. and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

LDDS Communications Inc., offering of $50 million of convertible subordinated debentures and 1.4 million common shares, of which 98,728 shares are being sold by the company and 1,301,272 shares by a holder.

Matrix Service Co., proposed initial offering of 3.25 million common shares, of which 2,750,000 are being offered by the company and 500,000 by holders, via PaineWebber Inc., Robertson, Stephens & Co. and First Analysis Securities Corp.

Sports-Tech International Inc., offering of one million units, each consisting of one common share and one four-year warrant to acquire one-half common share of the company.
