French Jobless Rate Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

PARIS -- France's unemployment rate edged up to a seasonally adjusted 9.4% of the work force in June from 9.3% in May but was down from the year-earlier level of 9.5%, the Labor Ministry announced. The figures come at a time of growing concern about the reliability of the ministry's labor statistics. The National Statistics Institute recently suggested that they substantially overestimate the jobless rate. Labor Minister Jean-Pierre Soisson said he plans to launch a study of the problem to make the data more indicative of underlying economic trends.
