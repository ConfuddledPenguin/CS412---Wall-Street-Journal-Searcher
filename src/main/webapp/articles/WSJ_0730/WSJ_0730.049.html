Thermo Process Gets City Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Thermo Process Systems Inc. said its TPS Technologies Inc. unit was awarded a contract by Richmond, Va., to clean up petroleum-contaminated sites in the city. The value of the contract isn't known, because the amount of soil to be cleaned hasn't been determined. Under the contract, the unit will treat soil from the city's public works, police and fire departments and other city-owned sites for as long as five years, Thermo Process said. "This is probably our biggest cleanup contract to date," a spokesman said. "It's the first time we've gotten a contract to clean up a whole city."
