Who's News: Maidenform Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

MAIDENFORM Inc. (New York) -- This closely held lingerie maker named Elizabeth J. Coleman chairman, succeeding her recently deceased mother, Beatrice Coleman. Elizabeth Coleman is a partner in the Atlanta-based law firm of Stroup & Coleman. She was previously Maidenform's vice chairman. Robert A. Brawer, president, assumes the additional post of chief executive officer previously held by Beatrice Coleman. David C. Masket, executive vice president, was named to the additional new post of chief operating officer.
