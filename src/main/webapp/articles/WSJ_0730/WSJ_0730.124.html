Eastern Enterprises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

WESTON, Mass. -- Eastern Enterprises said the Massachusetts attorney general proposed a settlement with the company's Boston Gas Co. unit granting the unit about two-thirds of a requested rate increase. The proposed settlement, submitted to the state's Department of Public Utilities, includes an annual revenue increase of $19.3 million, Eastern said. The Eastern unit requested a rate increase of $29.6 million a year in a filing on March 15.

If approved by the state, it would take effect Oct. 1, the company said. Eastern operates barges, owns a natural gas utility and sells water system components.

Separately, Eastern said it agreed in principle to buy E&H Utility Sales Inc., Carol Stream, Ill. Terms weren't disclosed. E&H, which sells parts for municipal water systems, has annual sales of about $17 million, Eastern said.
