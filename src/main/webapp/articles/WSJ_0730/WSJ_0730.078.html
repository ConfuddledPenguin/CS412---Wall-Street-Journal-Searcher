Business Brief -- Coast Savings Financial Inc.: California Investor Acquires 6.4% of Stock Outstanding

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

Theodore H. Kruttschnitt, a Burlingame, Calif., investor, said he acquired a 6.4% stake in Coast Savings Financial Inc.'s common stock outstanding. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Mr. Kruttschnitt said he holds 976,400 Coast Savings common shares, all bought May 23 to July 20 for $5.38 to $7.38 apiece.

Mr. Kruttschnitt said he acquired the stock as an investment, indicating that he may purchase more shares as an investment.

Coast Savings, Los Angeles, is one of several large West Coast thrifts that has had big losses lately from deteriorating real estate markets. The company had a loss of $28.7 million, or $1.89 a share, in the second quarter.

Neither Coast Savings nor Mr. Kruttschnitt could be reached to comment.
