Flagship Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Flagship Financial Corp. said it added $1.5 million to its loan-loss provision for the second quarter. As of March 31, the thrift's provision had a balance of $2.1 million. Management said it anticipates the provision will result in a second-quarter loss of about $260,000.

The thrift, which operates units First American Savings and Brandywine Savings Bank, underwent a recent review by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp.
