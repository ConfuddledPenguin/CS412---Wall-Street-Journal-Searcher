Who's News: Warner-Lambert Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

WARNER-LAMBERT Co. (Morris Plains, N.J.) -- Rhonda L. Brooks was named vice president, marketing and sales, a new post in the environmental polymers division of this maker of health-care and consumer products. She will assume responsibility for market development of Novon, a naturally decomposing material. She was previously general manager of medical systems at General Electric Co., the financial-services, broadcasting, and aerospace giant in Fairfield, Conn.
