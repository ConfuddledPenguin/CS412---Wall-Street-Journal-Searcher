Letters to the Editor: The Pleasure Principle

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

In reply to Prof. Donald A. Coffin's interesting mathematical dissertation (Letters, June 22) on the sale of Renoir's "Au Moulin de la Galette" (Leisure & Arts article, May 24), no consideration was given to the value of the pleasure the owner enjoyed for the many years of ownership of this great painting. While this cannot be expressed monetarily, it is obvious to many collectors that the sheer joy of living with this great work would be a reward in itself.

Herbert Gussman

Tulsa, Okla.
