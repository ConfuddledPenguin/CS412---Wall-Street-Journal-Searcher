West German Wholesale Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Sales of West German wholesalers in June declined a real, or price-adjusted, 4% from a year earlier and 5% in nominal or unadjusted terms, the Federal Statistics Office said. June wholesale sales were valued at 73 billion marks ($44.9 billion). The agency didn't supply comparable year-earlier figures. The monthly decline was viewed as surprising because wholesale sales were expected to be buoyed by the July 1 monetary and economic union of the two Germanys.

In the first half, wholesale sales rose 2% in both nominal and adjusted terms to 437 billion marks, the statistics office said.
