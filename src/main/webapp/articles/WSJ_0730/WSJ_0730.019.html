Texas Instruments Inc. Wins Navy Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

WASHINGTON -- Texas Instruments Inc. received a $58.8 million Navy contract for anti-radiation missiles.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. won a $42.4 million Navy contract for Harpoon missile equipment.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., was awarded a $15.4 million Army contract for radar equipment.

Freightliner Corp., a unit of Daimler Benz of North America Holding Co., received a $14.4 million Army contract for truck tractors.

United Technologies Corp. got a $14.2 million Army contract for UH-60 helicopters.

Brown & Root Services Corp., a unit of Halliburton Co., received a $10.2 million Air Force contract for space-launch services.
