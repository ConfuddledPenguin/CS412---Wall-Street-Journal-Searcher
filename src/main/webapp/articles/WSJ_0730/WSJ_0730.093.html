International: Peru's President Removes Heads of Navy, Air Force

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 07/30/90

LIMA, Peru -- President Alberto Fujimori confirmed yesterday that he had removed the heads of the navy and air force his first day in office but said he owed no explanation for the action. He also said he would close banks today and tomorrow to help prepare emergency economic measures but guaranteed Peruvians' savings accounts wouldn't be touched. In addition, the president said he wanted to resume payments on the nation's foreign debt.

Mr. Fujimori, who took office Saturday, said in his first news conference that economic measures would be announced Thursday or Friday.

Turning to the high-level military firings, Mr. Fujimori said, "I must deny that there exists any discontent in the armed forces."

He removed Gen. German Vucetich as head of the air force and replaced him with Gen. Arnaldo Velarde Ramirez. He also appointed Adm. Luis Montes Lecaros to head the navy after removing Admiral Alfonso Panizo.
