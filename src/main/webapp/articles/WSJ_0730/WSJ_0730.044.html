Who's News: SmithKline Beecham PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

SMITHKLINE BEECHAM PLC (Philadelphia) -- Jan Leschly, chairman of SmithKline Beecham's world-wide pharmaceutical business, was elected a director, increasing board membership to 20. Mr. Leschly, 49 years old, joined SmithKline Beecham in his current position in June 1990. Prior to that he served from 1988 to 1989 as president and chief operating officer of Squibb Corp., Lawrenceville, N.J.
