Letters to the Editor: Take 20,000 tsp flour...

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

In your June 28 article you note that Austin, Texas, holds the Guinness World Record for the largest cake ever created (90,000 pounds). Yes, Texas did hold the record. But that record was broken on Oct. 18, 1989, in a small town in northeast Alabama. Fort Payne, Ala. (population 15,000) is the current record-holder, with a cake weighing 128,238 pounds. The record was certified by Guinness in March. We're proud of our accomplishment, and our small-town "can-do" spirit.

Gary Gengozian

Fort Payne, Ala.
