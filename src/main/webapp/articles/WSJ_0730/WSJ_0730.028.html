Who's News: Bertelsmann AG

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

BERTELSMANN AG -- Arlene Friedman was named vice president and editorial director for the Literary Guild and the Doubleday Book Club, at Doubleday Book & Music Clubs Inc. Based in Garden City, N.Y., Doubleday Book & Music Clubs is a unit of Bertelsmann, a Guetersloh, West Germany, media company. Effective Aug. 13, Ms. Friedman, previously senior editor at Crown Publishers Inc., New York, succeeds Maureen Mahon Egen, who resigned to take another post.
