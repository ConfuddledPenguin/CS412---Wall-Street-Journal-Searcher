Chambers Development Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

PITTSBURGH -- Chambers Development Co. said it received a permit from the Pennsylvania Department of Environmental Resources to expand the Arden Landfill in Washington County, Pa., by 82 acres. With the permit, the landfill can accept an average of 2,400 tons of solid waste a day, the company said.

Chambers is a waste collection, hauling and disposal concern.
