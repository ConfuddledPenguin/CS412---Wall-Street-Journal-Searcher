Valero Completes Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- Valero Energy Corp., an energy concern, said it completed an offering of 4,750,000 common shares at $15.25 each and that net proceeds of about $69 million will be used to help finance capital projects. Managing underwriters for the offering, which included one million shares in Europe, were Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. and Salomon Brothers Inc.
