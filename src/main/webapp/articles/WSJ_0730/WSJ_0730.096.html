Marketing Brief -- Qintex Entertainment Inc.: Firm's Officers, Directors, Insurers, Holders Settle Suit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

Qintex Entertainment Inc. said it reached a $4 million settlement involving the film company's officers and directors, their insurer and shareholders in a class-action suit brought following Qintex's Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law filing last October. Under terms of the settlement, the insurance company will deposit $4 million for payment of claims, and plaintiffs will release all claims against Qintex and its officers and directors. The Beverly Hills, Calif., company, which said its officers and directors deny any liability or wrongdoing, said they reached the agreement to avoid the expense and time of protracted legal proceedings.

The settlement agreement is contingent upon court approval. Under Chapter 11 bankruptcy, a company is protected by the court from its creditors while it works out a plan to pay off its debt.
