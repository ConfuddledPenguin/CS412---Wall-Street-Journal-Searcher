Comair Sets Saab Plane Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

CINCINNATI -- Comair Holdings Inc. said it signed a purchase option to buy 20 Saab 2000 aircraft valued at more than $230 million from Saab-Scania AB, a Swedish aircraft producer, for use by the Comair regional airline subsidiary. The company said it expects to sign a formal purchase agreement in late 1991. The Saab turboprop aircraft, which each seat 50 passengers, will be delivered to Comair between 1993 and 1997. The carrier already operates Saab-Scania's 33-passenger Saab 340 aircraft.
