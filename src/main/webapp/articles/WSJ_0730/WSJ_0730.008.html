Harleysville to Buy Two Insurance Units

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

HARLEYSVILLE, Pa. -- Harleysville Group Inc., a regional holding company for four property and casualty insurers, said it agreed to acquire two property and casualty insurance units of Phoenix Mutual Life Insurance Co., Hartford, Conn., but didn't disclose the value of the cash transaction. Harleysville Group said it will purchase all two million shares outstanding of Phoenix General Insurance Co., Hartford, and all 1,539 shares outstanding of New York Casualty Insurance Co., Watertown, N.Y., subject to approval by regulators and the boards of both parent companies. Last year, property and casualty insurance premiums totaled $8.8 million at Phoenix General and $23.1 million at New York Casualty.

Harleysville Group had net written premiums of $300.8 million in 1989.
