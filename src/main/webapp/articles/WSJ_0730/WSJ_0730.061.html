Who's News: McDonnell Douglas Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/30/90

MCDONNELL DOUGLAS Corp. (St. Louis) -- Jim Dorrenbacher, 62 years old, formerly president of the aerospace company's McDonnell Douglas Space Systems Co. unit, was named senior vice president, group executive responsible for McDonnell Douglas' non-aerospace operations. He succeeds James T. McMillan, 65, who will retire Aug. 31. Kenneth A. Francis, 56, formerly executive vice president of McDonnell Douglas Space Systems, was named president of that unit. The appointments are effective Sept. 1.
