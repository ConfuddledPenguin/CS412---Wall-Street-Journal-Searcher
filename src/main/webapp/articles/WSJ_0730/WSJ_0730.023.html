Who's News: R.R. Donnelley & Sons Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/30/90

R.R. DONNELLEY & SONS Co. (Chicago) -- James R. Donnelley, 55 year-old former president of the company's corporate development group, was named vice chairman of this printing concern, succeeding Charles C. Haffner III, 62, who is retiring. Mr. Donnelley is the great-grandson of company founder Richard Robert Donnelley.
