International Brief -- Nippon Meat Packers Inc.: Consolidated Pretax Profit For Fiscal 1990 Fell 18%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

Nippon Meat Packers Inc. of Japan, citing rising costs, said its consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 fell 18% to 25.09 billion yen ($162.3 million) from 30.67 billion yen a year earlier. Net income fell 23% to 8.82 billion yen, or 40.99 yen a share, from 11.52 billion yen, or 54.43 yen a share.

Sales increased 11% to 658.134 billion yen from 592.623 billion yen. Sales costs and other merchandising and administrative costs grew at a faster pace, however, pinching profit. Ham sausage sales rose 6.6% to 144.04 billion yen, and processed-food sales increased 12% to 97.19 billion yen.

Nippon Meat has said its unconsolidated pretax profit rose 2.1% to 21.52 billion yen. Net income at the parent company rose 7% to 10.04 billion yen, and sales rose 10% to 488.06 billion yen.

The company said it expects fiscal 1991 sales of 740 billion yen, consolidated pretax profit of 30 billion yen and net income of 10.5 billion yen.
