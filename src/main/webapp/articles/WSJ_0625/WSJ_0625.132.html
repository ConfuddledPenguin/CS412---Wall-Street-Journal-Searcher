Bennetton Family Sells 62% of Financial Firm

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 06/25/90

MILAN, Italy -- The Benetton family of Italy sold a 62% stake in its financial-services group, In Capital S.p.A., to Sviluppo Finanziaria, the investment bank of Franceso Micheli's Finarte S.p.A. Terms weren't disclosed. The Benetton family holding, Edizione Holding S.p.A., will retain a 26% stake in In Capital, and management will keep a 12% interest, Svilippo said.

Sviluppo said the new group after the acquisition will control about 40 trillion lire ($32.54 billion) in financial-services activities.
