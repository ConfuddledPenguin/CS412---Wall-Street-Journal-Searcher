Business Brief -- Pamour Group: Giant Yellowknife Chairman To Gain Additional Titles

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

Giant Yellowknife Mines Ltd., Toronto, said Chairman Robert Needham will assume additional positions of president and chief executive officer, effective July 1. The gold mining company chairman succeeds Adrian Fleming, who will remain as a director. Mr. Needham also will serve in similar positions at other companies in the Pamour Group, the parent company of Giant Yellowknife.

The move follows the recent sale by Australia's Giant Resources Ltd. of its interests in the Pamour Group to Royal Oak Resources Ltd. of Calgary, Alberta.
