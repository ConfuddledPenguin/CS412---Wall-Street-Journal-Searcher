International Brief -- Polly Peck International PLC: Interest in Sansui Electric, Now 51%, to Rise to 72%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

Polly Peck International PLC of Britain and Sansui Electric Co. completed an agreement that will raise Polly Peck's holding in the Japanese company to 72% from 51%. Sansui is to issue 84.9 million new shares at 940 yen ($6.08) each in a private placement to investors to finance its purchase of two Polly Peck divisions, Capetronic Group and Imperial Group. Polly Peck, which announced the deal in May, said it will take up about 91% of the new issue, with the remainder going to third-party investors. The conglomerate said it expects the sale to occur on July 10.

The move is part of larger restructuring aimed at developing Polly Peck's electronics businesses around Sansui, in which it acquired a controlling interest last year.
