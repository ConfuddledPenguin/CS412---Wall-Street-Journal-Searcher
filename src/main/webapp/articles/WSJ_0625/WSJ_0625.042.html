Coda Energy, Snyder in Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

DALLAS -- Coda Energy Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement with Snyder Oil Corp. of Fort Worth to acquire a 25% interest in Snyder's Pawnee Project. The Pawnee Project encompasses about 145,000 acres in Colorado and Wyoming and is about eight miles south of a successful horizontal well in the Niobrara formation. Doug Miller, Coda chairman, said the Pawnee Project will drill at least two horizontal test wells into the same formation, at a cost to Coda of between $2 million and $2.5 million.
