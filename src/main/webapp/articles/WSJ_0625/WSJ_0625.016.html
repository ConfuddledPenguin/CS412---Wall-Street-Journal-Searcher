Proler to Repurchase Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

HOUSTON -- Proler International Corp. said it will repurchase up to 200,000 common shares, from time to time, on the open market. The environmental recovery and recycling concern said the shares may be used in connection with its employee benefits plan or for other general corporate purposes. Proler has about 4.7 million shares outstanding.
