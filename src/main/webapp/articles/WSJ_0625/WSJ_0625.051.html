MMI Medical's Profit Fell 70% in 4th Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

POMONA, Calif. -- MMI Medical Inc. reported a 70% drop in fiscal fourth-quarter net income and said it expects to report "only nominal" profits for the fiscal 1991 first quarter. MMI Medical, which provides diagnostic imaging services, said net for the quarter ended April 27 fell to $237,000, or nine cents a share, from $778,000, or 28 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue was flat at $9.7 million.

Results for the latest quarter include an after-tax charge of $87,000, or three cents a share, for adjustments to first-quarter results, which apparently were overstated. MMI Medical reported net of $576,000, or 21 cents a share, on revenue of $9.3 million for the first quarter of fiscal 1990.

Dudley A. Rauch, chairman and chief executive officer, declined to disclose details of the restatement.

Mr. Rauch said MMI Medical has suffered from industrywide pressures in the diagnostic imaging market, where rates for equipment rentals drop swiftly with the age of equipment. In addition, the tax rate for the latest quarter jumped to 35% from 22% a year earlier, as availability of investment tax credits declined. On the plus side, the company's maintenance business, which represents more than 70% of revenue, has shown "continued growth and profitability," he said.

For the fiscal year, net fell 50% to $1.4 million, or 51 cents a share, from $2.8 million, or $1.02 a share, a year earlier. Revenue was $38.1 million, up from $38 million.
