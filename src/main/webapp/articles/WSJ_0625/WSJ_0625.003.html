International: GB-Inno-BM Store in Portugal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

BRUSSELS -- GB-Inno-BM S.A. said it opened its first do-it-yourself store in Portugal. Bricogal, based on the outskirts of Lisbon, was created by the Belgian retailer last year in conjunction with a Portuguese partner, Grupo Fnac, which has a 45% stake in the venture.

Financial details weren't disclosed.
