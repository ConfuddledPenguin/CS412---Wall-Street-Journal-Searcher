Marketing Brief -- Cox Enterprises Inc.: Two Newspapers in Texas Are Being Offered for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

Atlanta-based Cox Enterprises Inc. said it put up for sale two of its Texas newspapers, the Port Arthur News and its sister publication, the Orange Leader. The closely held newspaper and cable television company didn't disclose the price it is seeking for the properties, but said it retained Lee Dirks & Associates, a Birmingham, Mich.-based newspaper merger and acquisition company, to assist in the process. Cox said proceeds from the sale would be used to reduce debt incurred by the 1985 merger of Cox Communications into Cox Enterprises.

The Port Arthur News is a morning daily newspaper with a circulation of 23,200 on weekdays and 24,900 on Sundays; the Orange Leader sells 10,100 copies on weekday evenings and Saturday mornings, and has a Sunday circulation of 12,100.
