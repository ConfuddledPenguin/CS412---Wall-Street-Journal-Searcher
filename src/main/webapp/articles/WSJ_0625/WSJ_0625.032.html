International: L'Oreal in Talks With Vittel

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

PARIS -- L'Oreal S.A. said it is negotiating a licensing agreement that will give it world-wide rights to manufacture and distribute a range of cosmetic products developed by Societe Generale des Eaux Minerales de Vittel, a French company whose main activity is bottling and marketing mineral water. Under the proposed accord, the products, which are currently sold in high-volume retail outlets, would be distributed through L'Oreal's sales network.

Financial details weren't disclosed.
