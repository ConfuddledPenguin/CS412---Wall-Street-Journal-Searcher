Who's News: Wesbanco Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

WESBANCO Inc. (Wheeling, W.Va.) -- James C. Gardill, formerly vice chairman of this bank holding company, was named chairman. Mr. Gardill, 43 years old, succeeds Roland L. Hobbs, 57, who resigned earlier this month, but continues on the board and is a member of the executive committee. Thomas G. Dove, 63, continues as chief executive officer and president.
