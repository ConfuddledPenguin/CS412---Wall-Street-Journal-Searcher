Business Brief -- Schafer Value Trust Inc.: Big Board to Suspend Trade Of Fund's Common Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

The New York Stock Exchange said it will suspend trading on the common stock of Schafer Value Trust Inc. before the opening July 5. The closed-end investment fund declared a liquidating distribution of $10.25 a share, payable July 5, to stock of record Wednesday. As a result of the dividend, the market value of the shares will be less than $5 million, the exchange said.

The exchange said it would apply to the Securities and Exchange Commission to delist Schafer, a routine action when the market value of publicly held shares falls below $5 million.

In Big Board trading Friday, Schafer closed unchanged at $10.50.
