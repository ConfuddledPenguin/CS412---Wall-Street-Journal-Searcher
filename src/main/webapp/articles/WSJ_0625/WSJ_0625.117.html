Business Brief -- Great Northern Nekoosa Corp.: Shareholders Back Merger With Georgia-Pacific Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

Georgia-Pacific Corp. said shareholders of Great Northern Nekoosa Corp. approved the merger of the Norwalk, Conn.-based company with NM Acquisition Corp., a subsidiary of Atlanta-based Georgia-Pacific. Georgia-Pacific, a forest products concern, said it expects the merger to take effect this week upon the filing of merger documents in Maine and Delaware, where Nekoosa and NM Acquisition, respectively, are incorporated.

After the merger, Nekoosa, a wood products concern, will become a wholly owned subsidiary of Georgia-Pacific. Georgia-Pacific said Nekoosa shareholders will then be sent documents telling them how to surrender their shares for a cash payment of $65.75 a share.
