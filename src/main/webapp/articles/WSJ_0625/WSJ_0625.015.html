American Century Realignment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- American Century Corp. said a federal bankruptcy court has entered an order confirming its reorganization plan, effective Friday. Under the plan, substantially all of the company's assets will be acquired by Tescorp Inc., a Houston-based maker of oilfield products, and American Century's debenture holders will receive Tescorp common stock, convertible preferred stock and warrants.
