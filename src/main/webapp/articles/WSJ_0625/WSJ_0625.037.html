International: NatWest Appoints Unit Chief

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

LONDON -- National Westminster Bank PLC, Britain's second-largest commercial bank, named Rafael Martinez Cortina chairman of its Spanish subsidiary Banco NatWest SA. Mr. Martinez Cortina, 53 years old, was vice chairman and managing director of government-controlled Banco Exterior de Espana SA and vice chairman of Arab Banking Corp.'s Banco Atlantico S.A. unit. He succeeds Alfredo Lafita, who resigned after NatWest acquired the remaining 10% stake held in Banco NatWest by Spanish financial conglomerate March Group, a NatWest spokesman said.
