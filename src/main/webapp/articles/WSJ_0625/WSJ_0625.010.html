Who's News: Chancellor Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

CHANCELLOR Corp. (Boston) -- W. Barry Tanner resigned as president and a director of this equipment-leasing firm. Stephen C. Morison, vice chairman and chief executive officer, will assume the duties of president. William C. Sprong, senior vice president and treasurer, was named to succeed Mr. Tanner as a director.
