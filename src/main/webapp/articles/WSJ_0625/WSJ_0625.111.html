Business Brief -- Petroleum Helicopters Inc.: Industrial Equity (Pacific) Increases Interest in Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

Industrial Equity (Pacific) Ltd., a Hong Kong investment firm, said it exercised an option to purchase 473,638 shares of Petroleum Helicopters Inc. common. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Industrial Equity (Pacific) said it bought the additional shares for $7,920,000.

Its stake consists of 669,838 shares, or 17.12%.

In a previous filing, Industrial Equity (Pacific) said it was seeking federal antitrust clearance to raise its stake in the company to as much as 25%. It also said it would seek board representation.

Petroleum Helicopters, based in New Orleans, operates helicopters servicing offshore oil and natural gas locations.
