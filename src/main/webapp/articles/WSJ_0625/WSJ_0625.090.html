Credit Ratings: Midlantic Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

MIDLANTIC Corp. (Edison, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its ratings on debt of this bank holding company and certain units, including about $426 million of long-term debt outstanding. "The downgrades reflect the serious erosion in asset quality in recent months, due largely to the downturn in the New Jersey real estate market," the rating concern said. S&P's rating on senior debt of the parent and its Midlantic Banks Inc. affiliate was lowered to triple-B-minus from triple-B-plus. The rating on Midlantic Corp.'s subordinated debt was downgraded to double-B-plus from triple-B. The Midlantic Funding Corp. unit's commercial paper rating was reduced to single-A-3 from single-A-2.
