Who's News: Monsanto Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

MONSANTO Co. (St. Louis) -- Grant W. Denison Jr., 41 years old, was elected vice president, corporate planning, effective July 1. He succeeds S. Allen Heininger, who is retiring. Mr. Denison joined Searle, a Monsanto unit, in 1986 as corporate vice president, strategic marketing and planning. In 1988, he became corporate vice president and president, U.S. operations for Searle. In September 1989, he was named senior director of corporate planning at Monsanto, a chemicals concern.
