International: Britain's Wages and Salaries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

LONDON -- Wages and salaries per unit of output in the whole economy in the fourth quarter rose a seasonally adjusted 10.1% from a year earlier, a revision from the previously reported 9.3%, Britain's Central Statistical Office said. The figures were revised as the result of fresh data compiled for the first-quarter gross domestic product data. As a result of the revision, the index of wages and salaries for the whole economy in the fourth quarter was 133.3, up from 130.3 in the third quarter and 121.0 in the 1988 fourth quarter. The previously reported 1988 fourth-quarter index was 132.1.
