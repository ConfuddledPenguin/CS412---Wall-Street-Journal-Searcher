International Brief -- Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd.: Consolidated Pretax Profit For Fiscal Year Rose 20%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

Kawasaki Heavy Industries Ltd. of Japan reported that its consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 climbed 20% to 21.27 billion yen ($141.4 million) from 17.72 billion yen a year earlier. Net income rose 7.5% to 12.54 billion yen, or 9.42 yen a share, from 11.66 billion yen, or 8.76 yen a share. Growth in net was held back by foreign-exchange losses, which totaled 1.43 billion yen, compared with year-earlier foreign-exchange gains of 2.24 billion yen.

Sales rose 5.1% to 920.75 billion yen from 875.59 billion yen.

The company previously reported pretax profit at the parent company increased 25% to 17.17 billion yen. Unconsolidated net income rose 55% to 12.35 billion yen, and unconsolidated sales grew 5.9% to 812.81 billion yen.
