Easel Corp. Files For Initial Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

WOBURN, Mass. -- Easel Corp., a maker of software applications that support computer graphics, said it filed a registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of 1,850,000 common shares. The company said it expects to price the shares between $9 and $11 apiece.

One million shares will be sold by the company and 850,000 will be offered by certain stockholders. Net proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, working capital and possible acquisitions.

The offering will be managed by Robertson, Stephens & Co. and Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.
