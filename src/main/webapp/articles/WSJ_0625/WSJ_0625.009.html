Who's News: Gleason Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

GLEASON CORP. (Rochester, N.Y.) -- James S. Gleason, chairman and president of this maker of gear production equipment, was also named president of the Gleason Works Division. He succeeds Richard J. Bonnie, who resigned. Also, William J. Flaherty, president of the company's Component's Group, was named vice president, operations, of the division. Gleason is selling its Components Group. Gleason said the moves, including moving the corporate headquarters to the Gleason Works in Rochester, are aimed at refocusing on its core business.
