Group Gripe

Over geese in a gaggle, I will not haggle,

Or lions in prides and soldiers in squads;

Even litters of puppies and schools of guppies --

But, come on, Webster, whales in pods?

-- George O. Ludcke.

Crock Pot

A respected scientific group

Says we came from primordial soup.

It follows then -- no ifs, ands or buts --

We surely have gone from soup to nuts.

-- Pat D'Amico.
