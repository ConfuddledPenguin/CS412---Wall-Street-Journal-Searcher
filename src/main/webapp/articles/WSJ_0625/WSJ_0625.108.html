Business Brief -- Commonwealth Mortgage: Houston Firm Backs Plan Of Liquidation, Dissolution

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

Commonwealth Mortgage of America LP, Houston, said it approved a complete plan of liquidation and dissolution. The plan was adopted by written consent of Commonwealth Mortgage Corp. of America, the general partner of Commonwealth and holder of 59.1% of its Class A units and 100% of its Class B units; and of Commonwealth Federal Savings Association, the parent of CMCA and holder of 100% of the Series I preferred units of limited partner interests in Commonwealth.

Because of the size of Commonwealth's debt and the preference in liquidation of the Series I units, Commonwealth said it was unlikely that holders of Class A units will receive any distribution.
