Americana Hotels & Realty

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

BOSTON -- Americana Hotels & Realty Corp. said it plans to distribute about $3.50 a share to holders under its previously announced plan of liquidation. The real estate investment trust said the distribution would be made upon its collection of a $15.5 million note receivable on a hotel or upon the sale of another "major" asset. Americana made a previous liquidating distribution of $8 a share last August.
