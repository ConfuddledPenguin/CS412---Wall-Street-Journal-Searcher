Dayton Hudson Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Dayton Hudson Corp. said it has completed the $1.05 billion purchase of Marshall Field & Co. from B.A.T Industries PLC. The acquisition of the Chicago-based department-store chain was delayed because of a request for more information from the Federal Trade Commission, which examines proposed transactions to see if they violate antitrust laws. The retailer said the FTC notified the company that the waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Premerger Notification Act has terminated.
