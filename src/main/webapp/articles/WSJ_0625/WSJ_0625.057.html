Zurn Wins Contracts For 2 Water Projects In Southern California

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

ERIE, Pa. -- Zurn Industries Inc. said it was awarded two contracts totaling $65 million from the Metropolitan Water District of Southern California. Next month, Zurn said, its Advanco Construction unit will begin constructing the Etiwanda Pipeline Feeder, one of the two projects.

It involves installing more than six miles of 12-foot-diameter pipe in Rancho Cucamonga and Fontana, Calif. Included in the project are five tunnels under roadways. The project is expected to be completed by March 1992, Zurn said.

Advanco also will build the Etiwanda Control Facility. The 450-foot water reservoir and control facility will intercept and regulate water flow from the Etiwanda feeder and distribute it to various water treatment and supply locations.

Zurn said Advanco will construct its powerhouse building, pressure control structure and outlet structure. That project is expected to be completed in April 1992.
