Business Brief -- Cooper Cos.: Firm Sells Anacomp Notes, Says It Holds 5.25% Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

Cooper Cos. said it sold $3 million principal amount of Anacomp Inc.'s 12.25% senior subordinated notes due 1995 and $9 million principal amount of the company's senior subordinated, increasing-rate notes due 1991. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Cooper said it has not recently sold any of its common stock holdings in Anacomp. Its stake currently consists of 1,955,600 shares, or 5.25%.

In the filing, Cooper said that no decision has yet been reached on its motion to dismiss a lawsuit filed last February against it by Anacomp. The lawsuit alleges violations of civil racketeering law.

Cooper said it continues to evaluate whether to seek a change in control of Cooper, maintain its investment, or dispose of its holdings.

Officials at Anacomp, a computer-services concern, hadn't any comment on the filing.
