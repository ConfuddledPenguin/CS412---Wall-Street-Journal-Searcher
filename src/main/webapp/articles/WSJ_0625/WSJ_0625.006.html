International: Hitachi Unit Lays Off 1,000

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

KUALA LUMPUR -- Hitachi Corp.'s Malaysian unit, Hitachi Consumer Products (Malaysia) Sdn. Bhd., dismissed about 1,000 striking workers at its electronic parts plant. The workers at Hitachi's factory in Bangi, 40 miles south of Kuala Lumpur, had been on strike last week in an action the Malaysian government has termed illegal. The labor protest followed the rejection of efforts by the workers -- who are mostly women -- to seek representation under a national trade union, the Electrical Industry Workers Union.

Under Malaysian labor law, workers in factories that make electronic parts are entitled only to form in-house unions, separate from national organizations.
