Deloitte Elects New Partners

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

WILTON, Conn. -- Deloitte & Touche said it elected its first new group of partners and principals since the merger of Deloitte Haskins & Sells and Touche Ross & Co. last December. The company, the third largest U.S. accounting firm, said the size of the class, 100 members, is about the same as the combined number admitted to the predecessor firms in recent years. The firm has about 1,700 members.
