Bytex Gets $1.8 Million Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

SOUTHBOROUGH, Mass. -- Bytex Corp. said it received a $1.8 million contract from Securities Industry Automation Corp. to expand its computer network switching systems. Securities Industry Automation is co-owned by the New York Stock Exchange, American Stock Exchange and National Securities Clearing Corp., Bytex said. The expansion of its network will help support stock-exchange transactions, the company added.

Bytex makes data communications products.
