Kline Elko's Stake in Raritan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

Kline, Elko & Co. III L.P. said it lifted its stake in Raritan Bancorp Inc. to 10% of the common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Kline Elko said it holds 115,000 Raritan Bancorp shares, including 27,500 shares purchased between April 23 and June 20 for $12.25 to $13.125 a share.

Raritan Bancorp is in Raritan, N.J.
