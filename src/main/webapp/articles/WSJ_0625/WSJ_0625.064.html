Treasury Slates Sale Of $10.25 Billion Bills

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/25/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury said it plans to raise $1.23 billion in new cash Thursday by selling about $10.25 billion in 52-week bills and redeeming $9.03 billion in maturing bills. The bills will be dated July 5 and will mature July 5, 1991.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum denominations of $10,000, must be received by 1 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time Thursday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
