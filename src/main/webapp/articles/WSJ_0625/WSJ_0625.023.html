Ladd Sells Kittinger Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

HIGH POINT, N.C. -- Ladd Furniture Inc. said it sold Kittinger Co. to U.S.C. Industries for an undisclosed amount. The furniture maker acquired the Buffalo, N.Y., company in July 1989 as part of its purchase of the former Maytag Furniture Group. U.S.C. is a company formed by businessman Michael Carlow of Pittsburgh to acquire Kittinger, Ladd said.
