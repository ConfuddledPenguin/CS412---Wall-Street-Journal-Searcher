Heldor Industries Inc. Has $1.7 Million Loss For Fiscal 2nd Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/25/90

MORRISTOWN, N.J. -- Heldor Industries Inc. reported a net loss of $1.7 million, or 47 cents a share, for the second quarter ended April 30, as sales declined 30%. For the year-earlier period, the maker and distributor of swimming-pool equipment, spas, jetted tubs and related chemicals and supplies earned $420,000, or 12 cents a share.

Sales fell to $20.2 million from $29 million.

President George S. Zabrycki said sales declined as the water-recreation industry remained "in the second year of a down cycle." In addition, he said, Heldor has curtailed sales of certain low-margin products.

On the positive side, cost-cutting efforts during the fiscal first half should trim expenses at a rate of $3.8 million a year, Mr. Zabrycki said.
