Alltel Purchase of Systematics

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

HUDSON, Ohio -- Alltel Corp. said shareholders have approved its previously announced acquisition of Systematics Inc. Under terms of the agreement, each Systematics share will be exchanged for 1.325 shares of Alltel common.

Alltel is a telephone holding company. Systematics is a maker of software for financial institutions.
