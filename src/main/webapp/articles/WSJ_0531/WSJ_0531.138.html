Who's News: General Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

GENERAL ELECTRIC Co. (Fairfield, Conn.) -- Douglas J. Woods, 45 years old, was named vice president of GE Supply, an electrical products distributor. Mr. Woods, previously general manager of the apparatus service department at GE Power Generation, succeeds J. Richard Stonesifer, 54, who becomes vice president, marketing and product management, at GE Power Generation. GE Supply is based in Bridgeport, Conn., and GE Power Generation is based in Schenectady, N.Y. The parent company's businesses include financial services, broadcasting, lighting, industrial products, aircraft engines and appliances.
