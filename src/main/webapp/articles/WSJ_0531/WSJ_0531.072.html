Tyco Toys Is Approached By Party Seeking Takeover

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

MOUNT LAUREL, N.J. -- Tyco Toys Inc. said it received an unsolicited approach from an undisclosed party seeking to acquire the company. In a statement after the stock market closed, the toy manufacturer said that discussions have been held but no agreement has yet been reached. Company officials couldn't be reached for further comment.

Earlier in the day, Tyco announced an offer to acquire the 36.7% of Nasta International Inc. that it doesn't already own for $1.75 a share in cash. Nasta has a total of about 5.1 million shares outstanding.

Nasta said it appointed a committee of disinterested directors to review and recommend action on the offer.
