Spain's Producer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

MADRID -- Spain's producer price index rose 4.6% in March from a year earlier, the Statistics Institute said. The index registered a 3.1% increase in the first three months of 1990, compared with the year-earlier period.

The index of wholesale prices showed a 2.6% jump in energy prices. This compares with a 1.5% drop in wholesale energy prices in March 1989 from the year-earlier period.

Wholesale prices of capital goods, namely machinery and transportation equipment, climbed 6.6% in March, compared with a 4% increase in March of 1989.

Consumer goods showed a 6.4% increase, compared with a 5.7% increase in March 1989 from a year earlier.
