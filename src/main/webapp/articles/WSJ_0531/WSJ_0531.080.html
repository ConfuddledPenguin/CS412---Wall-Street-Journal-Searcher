PPG to Buy Rest of Silenka

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

PITTSBURGH -- PPG Industries Inc. said it agreed to acquire the two-thirds of Silenka B.V., a Netherlands-based fiber glass producer, that the glass, chemical and glass concern doesn't already own. Terms weren't disclosed. Silenka had 1989 sales of about $120 million. PPG will buy the remaining two-thirds of Silenka from two other owners, Akzo N.V., a chemical company, and a government investment and development company. The sale is subject to government and board approval.
