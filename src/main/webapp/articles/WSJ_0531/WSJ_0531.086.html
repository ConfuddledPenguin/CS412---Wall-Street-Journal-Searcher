Durakon Agrees to Sell Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

LAPEER, Mich. -- Durakon Industries Inc. said it agreed to sell its DFM Corp. bug and gravel-deflector shields subsidiary to a private investor company that Durakon wouldn't identify. The stock purchase agreement calls for the payment of $12.5 million in cash at the closing and the receipt by Durakon of a warrant to buy stock of the parent company of the purchaser.

The sale's closing is required to occur by the end of June.

The agreement is subject to several events, including the ability of the prospective purchaser to acquire financing, Durakon said.
