Michaels Stores May Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

IRVING, Texas -- Michaels Stores Inc. said its May sales increased about 32.5% to $22.7 million from $17.1 million in the year-ago period. The specialty retailer said that sales at stores open in May of both years increased about 15.9% to $19.6 million from $16.9 million last year.
