Who's News: General Motors Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

GENERAL MOTORS Co. (Detroit) -- Ted G. Westerman was elected corporate vice president for human resources and administration at the company's Hughes Aircraft Co. unit, succeeding J. Allen Hollingsworth, who retires July 1. Mr. Westerman previously was staff vice president, human resources, with the Los Angeles electronics company, which specializes in high-technology products for military, scientific and commercial use.
