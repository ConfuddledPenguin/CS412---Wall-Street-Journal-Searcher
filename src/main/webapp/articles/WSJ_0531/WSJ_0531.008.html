Letters to the Editor: Services to Alleviate Burden of Alzheimer's

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

I want to express sincere appreciation for Brent Bower's May 2 page-one article, "Slow Death: Life With Alzheimer's Is Often the Hardest on Families of the Sick." It was an exceptionally fine job of presenting the devastating effects of Alzheimer's disease and the enormous emotional and financial burdens carried by the family members who care for the patient at home.

We would like your readers to know that the COPSA Institute for Alzheimer's Disease and Related Disorders is part of the Community Mental Health Center at Piscataway, a health-care facility of the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey. For information about Alzheimer's disease and available services, people may call 1-800-424-2494 (New Jersey residents only) or 1-201-463-4430.

B. Lee Joslin

Director

COPSA Institute for Alzheimer's Disease

and Related Disorders

University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey

Piscataway, N.J.
