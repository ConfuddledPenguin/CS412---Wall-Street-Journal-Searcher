Business Brief -- Barclays Bank PLC: Global Private Banking Line Of Hongkong Unit Bought

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

Barclays Bank PLC of Britain acquired the international private banking operations of Marine Midland Bank, the banks said. The price wasn't disclosed. The operations sold by Marine Midland, a subsidiary of Hongkong & Shanghai Banking Corp., a Hong Kong-based financial services holding company, have a staff of 40 employees, and offices in New York City and Miami, involved in private banking largely for clients in North America, Central America and South America.

Andrew Etherington, head of the Barclays international private banking operations, said the Marine Midland line is a "nice fit" with Barclays, which opened a private banking unit to service the same territory three years ago. The Barclays unit also operates offices in New York and Miami.
