Who's News: Alitalia S.p.A.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

ALITALIA S.p.A. (Milan, Italy) -- Directors of Istituto per la Ricostruzione Industriale named Michele Principe, 66 years old, a Christian Democrat and chairman of the state telecommunications holding company STET S.p.A., chairman of Italy's flagship airline, succeeding Carlo Verri, who was killed last July in an auto accident. The nominations had got bogged down in political infighting and were postponed until after Italy's administrative elections in early May.
