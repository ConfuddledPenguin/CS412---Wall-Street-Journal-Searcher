Uranium Resources Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

DALLAS -- Uranium Resources Inc. said it plans to seek shareholder and regulatory approval for a 1-for-4 reverse stock split of its approximately 26 million shares outstanding. The uranium mining company said it believes the reverse split will make its stock more liquid and help it become eligible for trading on the national market system of the over-the-counter market. The company said officers and directors owning about 60% of Uranium Resources' stock already have approved the transaction.
