Business Brief -- St. Ives Laboratories Corp.: California Couple Increase Stake in Common to 15.67%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

Stephen J. Redding, president of Stephen Redding & Associates Inc., Santa Barbara, Calif., said he and his wife, Angelina, raised their stake in St. Ives Laboratories Corp., a Chatsworth, Calif., maker of beauty and personal-care products, to 15.67% of the common shares outstanding. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the Reddings said they hold 1,086,000 St. Ives shares, including 107,500 bought from March 22 to May 18 for $7.875 to $8.625 apiece. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, St. Ives shares closed at $7.50, up 12.5 cents. The Reddings said in the filing that they acquired the St. Ives stock as an investment and that they may purchase more shares. St. Ives declined to comment on the filing.
