Partners Oil's Acquisitions

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

HOUSTON -- Partners Oil Co. said it acquired a 75% working interest in certain gas-producing properties of Atlantic Richfield Co. for $5.2 million. The properties are located in Blocks 956 and 967, North Padre Island, offshore of Texas. In addition, Partners said it acquired the full working interest in Atlantic Richfield's as-yet undeveloped Block 947 of North Padre Island. Partners did not disclose the purchase price for that block.

Partners said it will operate the properties, whose daily production from the acquired blocks is approximately 14 million cubic feet of gas. The properties are located approximately 35 miles east-southeast of Corpus Christi, Texas, in water 125 feet deep.
