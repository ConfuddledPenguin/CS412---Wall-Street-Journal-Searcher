International Aero Gets Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

EAST HARTFORD, Conn. -- International Aero Engines, an aircraft engine consortium, said it received an order valued at as much as $330 million from Mexicana Airlines. The engines will power 16 firm and 14 option orders for Airbus A-320 aircraft, for use on routes to North and Central America and within Mexico.

Shareholders in IAE are United Technologies Corp. (parent company of Pratt & Whitney) of the U.S.; Rolls-Royce Ltd. of Britain; Japanese Aero Engines Corp.; MTU of West Germany; and FiatAvio of Italy.
