Business Brief -- Pic `N' Save Corp.: Net Rose 9.6%, Sales 26% In the Fiscal First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

Pic `N' Save Corp. posted a 9.6% increase in net income for the first quarter ended April 29, to $5.9 million, or 17 cents a share, from $5.4 million, or 15 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales of the Dominguez, Calif., close-out retailer rose 26% to $119 million from $94.1 million, with sales in comparable stores up 5.8% from the year-earlier period. "While virtually all of our markets contributed to the increased sales performance of the company, sales from our newest stores, those in the Southeast, were particularly strong during the quarter," said Lewis B. Merrifield III, chairman and chief executive officer.

Mr. Merrifield also said the company plans to lower its capital expenditures for new store openings by about $9 million this year compared with last.

Pic `N' Save is seeking to fend off an attempt by Girard Partners L.P. to seize control of the board.
