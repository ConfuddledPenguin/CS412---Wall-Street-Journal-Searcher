Who's News: Genicom Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

GENICOM Corp. (Waynesboro, Va.) -- Paul T. Winn, 43 years old, formerly a vice president, graphic systems, at a division of International Business Machines Corp., was named president and chief executive officer of this maker of computer printers, succeeding Don E. Ackerman, 55, chairman.
