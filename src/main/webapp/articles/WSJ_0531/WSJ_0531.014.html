Financing Business: Nashua Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

NASHUA CORP. said that based on a preliminary count for its previously announced Dutch auction, self-tender offer, it plans to buy about 2.79 million shares of its common stock at $40.50 a share in cash. The shares represent about 32% of the Nashua, N.H., coated-paper products and office-supplies concern's outstanding shares. The company said the offer was oversubscribed.
