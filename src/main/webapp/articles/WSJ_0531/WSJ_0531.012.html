Who's News: Survival Technology Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

SURVIVAL TECHNOLOGY Inc. (Bethesda, Md.) -- James H. Miller, president of this medical-technology company, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer. Mr. Miller, 51 years old, fills one of the positions vacated by the May 23 death of Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Stanley J. Sarnoff.
