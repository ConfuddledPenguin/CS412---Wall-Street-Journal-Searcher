Business Brief -- TCBY Enterprises Inc.: Rise in Per-Share Earnings Expected for This Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

TCBY Enterprises Inc., Little Rock, Ark., said it expects to report earnings of 35 cents to 40 cents a share for the fiscal second quarter, ending today, up from 33 cents a share in the year-ago period. However, the frozen-yogurt retailer, whose stock has been something of a highflier on Wall Street, said it expects sales at stores open more than a year will be down for the quarter because of poor weather during April and May.

The company's stock, which had climbed as high as $23 two weeks ago, dropped 75 cents to close at $18 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, on unusually heavy volume of more than 1.5 million shares.

TCBY had net income of $8.9 million on sales of $41.7 million for the second quarter last year.
