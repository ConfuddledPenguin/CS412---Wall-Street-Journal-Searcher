Business Brief -- Digital Equipment Corp.: Accounting Firms, Makers Of Software Are New Allies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

Digital Equipment Corp. outlined strategic alliances with software makers, consultants and two big accounting firms in a push into the emerging markets for electronic-forms processing, automated billing and ordering, and document management. The Maynard, Mass., computer maker said its new products and alliances would simplify and speed the flow of business information between and within organizations. Companies would use the products and services to develop paperless ordering systems, for example, or to replace mountains of paper documents with computer-stored images.

Price Waterhouse, Coopers & Lybrand and a handful of software and telecommunications companies announced partnerships with Digital to provide electronic data interchange, document-management consulting and related services.

The new products include electronic data interchange software that Digital says has an edge over competitors because it may be used across a variety of data networks and is based on a popular electronicmail standard.
