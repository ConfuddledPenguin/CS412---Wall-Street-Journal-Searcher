Business Brief -- LTV Corp.: Official Says Usinor, Firm Haven't Talked Since March

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

LTV Corp. said it hasn't had any further talks with Usinor Sacilor about a transaction involving the Dallas company's steel unit. An LTV spokesman said that David Hoag, president of LTV Steel Co., said at the American Iron and Steel Institute meeting that discussions hadn't been held with Usinor, a French steelmaker, since the two said they were talking in late March.

At the time, LTV said that Usinor had indicated it was interested in buying part of LTV's steel operations and said that the two were working on confidentiality agreements.

The LTV spokesman said he couldn't elaborate on Mr. Hoag's remarks.

LTV, the U.S.'s third-largest steelmaker, is operating under federal bankruptcy-law protection from creditors.
