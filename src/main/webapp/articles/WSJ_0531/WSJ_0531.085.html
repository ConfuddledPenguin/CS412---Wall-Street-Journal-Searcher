REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Temper, Temper

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

We hope laid-back George Bush can calm Mr. Gorbachev's frazzled nerves. He seemed a tad testy in Canada. On Lithuania: "The Americans ask me what will you do with Lithuania? I say we will do it our way so that it will be in the spirit, the style, of perestroika. And that does not exclude the most strict measures." On German reunification: "The West hasn't done any thinking but has tried to dictate, and that doesn't suit us." Over to you, Mr. President.
