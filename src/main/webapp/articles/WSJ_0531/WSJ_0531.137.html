Business Brief -- Susquehanna Bancshares Inc.: Value of Elmwood Offer Reduced to $22.8 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

Susquehanna Bancshares Inc. reduced the value of its offer to acquire Elmwood Federal Savings Bank by about $5 million, to a revised price of about $22.8 million in stock. By mutual agreement, the two bank companies said that Elmwood shareholders would receive 0.88 Susquehanna share for each Elmwood share outstanding. Based on Susquehanna's closing price of $17.75 a share, down 75 cents, in national over-the-counter trading yesterday, the transaction has a value of $15.62 an Elmwood share.

Elmwood, based in Media, Pa., has about 1.46 million shares outstanding. Susquehanna, based in Lititz, Pa., has about 7.96 million shares outstanding.

In November, the two institutions agreed to a stock-swap combination valued at about $19 for each Elmwood share, or a total of about $27.7 million at then-current market prices.

Susquehanna said the price was reduced due to "recent changes in the market for bank stocks." Because the exchange ratio for the proposed merger has been fixed, any further change in Susquehanna's share price would alter the value of the transaction, an Elmwood spokesman said.
