Financing Business: Cincinnati Gas & Electric

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

CINCINNATI GAS & ELECTRIC Co., Cincinnati, said its Union Light, Heat & Power Co. subsidiary will sell $15 million of 30-year first mortgage bonds with an interest rate of 10 1/4%. The company said it accepted the offer of Morgan Stanley & Co. and Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. for the bonds at a price of 98.66%, resulting in an annual cost to the company of 10.4%. Proceeds will be used to repay debt at the utility.
