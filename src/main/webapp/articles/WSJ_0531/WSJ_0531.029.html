Business Brief -- Central Bancorp.: Board Rejects Takeover Bid, Sets Stock Buy-Back Instead

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

Central Bancorp., Wenatchee, Wash., said its board rejected an unsolicited cash offer of $12.30 a share from another financial institution and will go ahead instead with a repurchase stock plan. A company spokesman declined to name the rejected bidder. Central Bancorp., the parent company of Central Washington Bank with $105 million in assets, said it will purchase up to 150,000 shares of company stock at $10 a share. The plan was recently approved by federal bank regulatory authorities. Central Bancorp. expects to start purchasing the shares within a few weeks.
