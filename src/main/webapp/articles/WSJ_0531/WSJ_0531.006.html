McDonnell Douglas Wins Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. was given a $269 million Navy contract for F/A-18 aircraft.

Lockheed Corp. was issued a $56 million contract for Air Force satellite-launch services and Navy aircraft modifications.

Grumman Corp. got a $36.5 million Navy contract for E-2C aircraft spare parts.

Accudyne Corp. received a $10.2 million Army contract for mine parts.
