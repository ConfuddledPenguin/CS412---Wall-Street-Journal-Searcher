Business Brief -- Royal Bank of Canada: U.K. Stock Brokerage Unit, Kitkat & Aitkin, Is Closed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/31/90

Kitkat & Aitkin, the British stock brokerage division of Royal Bank of Canada, was closed permanently at the end of trading Tuesday, the bank said. "The chronic overcapacity in the securities industry will probably persist for a number of years, and therefore, it is unlikely that an acceptable rate of return from Kitkat & Aitkin can be achieved," John R. Sanders, chairman of RBC Dominion Securities International, said. He described Kitkat & Aitkin as a low-cost producer and consistently at the top of second tier of British brokerage houses.
