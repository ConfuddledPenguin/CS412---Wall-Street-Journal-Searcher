French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/31/90

PARIS -- French consumer prices rose 0.4% in April from March and were up 3.2% from a year earlier, according to definitive results announced yesterday by the National Statistics Institute. The definitive results confirm previously released preliminary data. They follow a monthly rise of 0.3% for March, when the year-on-year growth rate was 3.4%.

Unlike the preliminary data, the definitive result included a breakdown of price growth by product category. This showed that service prices led the monthly gain with a rise of 0.8% from March. This advance had been widely anticipated because the index for the first month of every calendar quarter includes housing and rent increases that generally add about 0.1% to the monthly growth rate for the overall index.
