Business Brief -- U.S. Surgical Corp.: Firm Signs 2-Year Contract To Supply Hospital Services

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

U.S. Surgical Corp. signed a two-year, sole-source contract to supply Hospital Services Corp. of America with its full line of surgical staplers. Hospital Services, a Bridgeton, Mo., group-purchasing organization for 950 acute-care hospitals, said it will buy more than $40 million in surgical stapling and laparoscopic products from U.S. Surgical during the term of the contract.

Surgical staples are an alternative to stitches. Laparoscopy involves surgical viewing of the pelvic organs.

The agreement also provides that U.S. Surgical, which is based in Norwalk, Conn., will be Hospital Services' sole supplier of suture products, which U.S. Surgical will introduce by the first quarter of 1991.
