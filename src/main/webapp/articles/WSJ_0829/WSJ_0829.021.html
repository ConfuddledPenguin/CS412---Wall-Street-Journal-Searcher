InBancshares-Comerica Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

CITY OF INDUSTRY, Calif. -- InBancshares Inc., the holding company for Bank of Industry with three branches in Los Angeles and one in San Francisco, said shareholders approved its merger with Comerica Inc., a Detroit-based multibank holding company, for $40 million. InBanshares holders will receive $20.25 for each common share. The merger, which also has regulatory approval, is scheduled to close early next year.
