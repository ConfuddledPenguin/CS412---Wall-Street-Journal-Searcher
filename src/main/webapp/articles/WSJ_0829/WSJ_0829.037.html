Who's News: Sony Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

SONY Corp. (Tokyo) -- Masayuki Nozoe was named president of Sony Consumer Video Products Co., a unit of Sony Corp. of America, Park Ridge, N.J. Mr. Nozoe, who is filling a vacancy at Sony's consumer video products unit, was previously senior vice president of Sony's personal video division.
