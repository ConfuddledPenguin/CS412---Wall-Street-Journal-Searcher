Canada's Pig Herd Declines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's pig herd declined 2.1% to 10,532,000 at July 1 from 10,763,000 a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. Cattle and calves totaled 12,286,800 head at July 1, a 0.7% increase from 12,199,100 a year earlier. Beef cows totaled 3,590,900, a 2.9% increase from 3,488,500 a year earlier.
