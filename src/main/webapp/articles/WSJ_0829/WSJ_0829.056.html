H.J. Heinz Bond Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

PITTSBURGH -- H.J. Heinz Co. said it will redeem $10 million principal amount of the 11.8% industrial revenue bonds due April 1, 1992, issued by the Puerto Rico Industrial, Medical and Environmental Pollution Control Facilities Financing Authority and guaranteed by Heinz. The bonds will be redeemed at par on Oct. 1, Heinz said, and will be selected by lot from the $34 million principal amount of the bonds outstanding.
