Who's News: Met-Coil Systems Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

MET-COIL SYSTEMS Corp. (Cedar Rapids, Iowa) -- H. Arthur Link, 43 years old, formerly president of the Standard Products Group, was named president and to the new post of chief operating officer of this maker of metal-working machinery and automated equipment. As president and a director, he succeeds Martin J. Huebschman, who resigned last June.
