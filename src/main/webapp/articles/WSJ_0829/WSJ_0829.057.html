Mellon's Corporate Trust Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

PITTSBURGH -- Mellon Bank Corp. said it is considering the sale of its corporate trust business, but will continue to serve as trustee for municipal debt issues. Mellon said a number of prospective buyers have indicated an interest in the corporate trust business, which has annual revenue of about $5 million. The bank holding company said it expects to complete a bidding process and reach an agreement on the sale in this year's fourth quarter.

The unit acts as administrator, record keeper and paying agent for corporate debt issues.
