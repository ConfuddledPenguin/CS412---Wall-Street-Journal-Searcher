Letters to the Editor: Listen to Your Grandmother

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

Hooray for Jewish grandmothers] How lucky Pittsburgh is to have one in Mayor Sophie Masloff (page one, July 25). Critics fearing she has no vision for the future need only listen to her campaign slogan, "Grandmothers have no ambitions for themselves, only aspirations for their children and grandchildren." What better vision is there? Carol Sager

President

Sager Educational Enterprises

Chicago
