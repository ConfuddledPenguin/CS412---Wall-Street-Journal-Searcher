Financing Business: URS Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

URS Corp. said it began an offering to qualified shareholders of non-transferable rights to purchase a total of about 800,000 common shares at $4.774 a share. San Francisco-based URS, an engineering, architectural and planning firm, said it agreed to make the rights offering in connection with a capital restructuring of the company that was otherwise completed on Feb. 21. It said proceeds from the sale will be used toward payment of certain company debts. URS said the rights will be exercisable until Sept. 27.
