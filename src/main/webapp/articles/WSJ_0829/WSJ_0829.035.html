Who's News: Midway Airlines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

MIDWAY AIRLINES (Chicago) -- Alfred S. Altschul, 50 years old, was named senior vice president, finance, and chief financial officer. David B. Armstrong, 50, was named senior vice president, corporate affairs, general counsel and secretary. John P. Tague, 28, was named senior vice president, marketing and planning. Lois A. Gallo, 41, was named senior vice president, customer services. They were previously vice presidents.
