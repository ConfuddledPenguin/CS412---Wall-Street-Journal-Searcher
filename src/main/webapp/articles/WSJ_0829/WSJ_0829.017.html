Who's News: Citizens & Southern Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

CITIZENS & SOUTHERN Corp. (Atlanta) -- Robert V. Royall Jr., 55 years old, will retire as a vice chairman of this banking company, effective Dec. 31. He also is head of the C&S Community & Corporate Banking Group, and chairman of C&S National Bank of South Carolina. No successor has been named for any of the positions that Mr. Royall holds.
