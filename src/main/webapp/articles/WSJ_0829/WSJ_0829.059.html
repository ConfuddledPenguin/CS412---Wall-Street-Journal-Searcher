Computer Sciences Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Computer Sciences Corp. said it received a $22.3 million contract to modernize the computer systems of the Financial Management Service, a bureau of the U.S. Treasury Department. The one-year contract includes options for nine annual renewals, bringing the 10-year value of the contract to about $48.8 million, Computer Sciences said.
