Business Brief -- TCBY Enterprises Inc.: Firm Expects to Post Fall In Third-Quarter Earnings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

TCBY Enterprises Inc. said it expects to report that earnings for the third quarter ending Aug. 31 fell to between 25 cents and 30 cents a share from 43 cents in the year-ago period. TCBY, a Little Rock, Ark., franchiser of retail stores that sell frozen yogurt, blamed the earnings decline on same-store sales drops of about 15% in June and July. The sales decline came because of increased competition, a slowing economy and reduced franchise fee income, TCBY said. The company said it has established new marketing and sales programs designed to increase sales.

For the six months ended May 31, TCBY had earnings of about $12.9 million, or 48 cents a share, compared with $11.8 million, or 44 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Revenue rose 19% to $80.6 million from $67.8 million.
