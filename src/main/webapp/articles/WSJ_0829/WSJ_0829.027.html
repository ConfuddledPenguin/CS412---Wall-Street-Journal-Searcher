GTI to Sell Shares to Telemetrix

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

SAN DIEGO -- GTI Corp. said shareholders approved the sale of 2.5 million new shares of GTI common stock for $7.5 million to London-based Telemetrix PLC, its largest holder. GTI, a maker of electronic circuit boards, will use the sale proceeds to help finance its purchase of about 90% of Valor Electronics Co. for about $8.5 million in cash and debt. Valor is a San Diego maker of electronic parts.
