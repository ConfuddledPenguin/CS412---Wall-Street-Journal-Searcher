Who's News: Tultex Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/29/90

TULTEX Corp. (Martinsville, Va.) -- John M. Franck, 37-year-old president of this apparel maker, was elected chairman and chief executive officer, effective Jan. 1, succeeding H. Richard Hunnicut, 51, who is to retire. Charles W. Davies Jr., 42, executive vice president, will succeed Mr. Franck as president, also Jan. 1.
