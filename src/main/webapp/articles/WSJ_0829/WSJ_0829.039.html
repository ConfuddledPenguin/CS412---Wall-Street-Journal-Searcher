Holnam Buys United Cement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

DUNDEE, Mich. -- Holnam Inc. said it completed the purchase of United Cement Co., Artesia, Miss., for $61 million. Holnam, a maker of cement, said the transaction included seven distribution terminals in Tennessee, South Carolina, Mississippi, Alabama and Georgia.

The acquisition was first announced by Holnam in June following a preliminary agreement.
