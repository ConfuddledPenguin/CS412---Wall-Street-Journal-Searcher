Who's News: American Travellers Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/29/90

AMERICAN TRAVELLERS Corp. (Warrington, Pa.) -- John T. Riordan, formerly a senior manager with Arthur Andersen & Co., was named president and chief operating officer of this insurance concern. Mr. Riordan, 45 years old, takes on some of the responsibilities of John A. Powell, who remains chairman and chief executive officer.
