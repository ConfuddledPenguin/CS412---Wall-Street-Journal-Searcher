Continental Medical Systems

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

MECHANICSBURG, Pa. -- Continental Medical Systems Inc. said it agreed to acquire 80% of the stock of three privately held rehabilitation-services companies based in Boone, N.C. The companies, which have common owners, are Communi Care of America Inc., Pro-Rehab Inc. and Alta Petens Inc.

Continental, a provider of rehabilitation services, said the purchase will be made with cash and stock, but the company didn't specify terms.
