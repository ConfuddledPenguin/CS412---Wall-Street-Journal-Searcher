Asahi Diamond Buys Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

TOKYO -- Asahi Diamond Industrial Co., Japan's largest diamond-tipped tools manufacturer, announced Thursday that it purchased all remaining shares of Triefus PLC, a U.K. diamond-tipped tools concern. The purchase, which took place Monday, totaled about 4 billion yen ($20.7 million), an Asahi Diamond official said.

The move reflects Asahi's plans to obtain a better foothold in Europe.
