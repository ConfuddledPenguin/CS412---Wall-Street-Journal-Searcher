Business Brief -- Banner Industries Inc.: Cleveland Company to Offer 52.8% Interest in Subsidiary

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

Banner Industries Inc., Cleveland, said it plans to sell a 52.8% interest in its aircraft-parts subsidiary for $133 million through a public stock offering. Banner is offering 9.5 million shares of common stock of Banner Aerospace Inc. at $14 a share. The company said the offering is being managed by Salomon Brothers Inc., Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Bear, Stearns & Co. Expected to be completed Aug. 2, the offering will produce a pretax profit of $60 million.

The company said it intends to use proceeds from the sale, together with current cash of $90 million, for repurchasing debt, acquisitions and other corporate purposes.

Banner Industries said Banner Aerospace, a distributor of new and overhauled replacement aircraft parts, had pro forma net income of $12.7 million, or 71 cents a share, on sales of $172.4 million, for the nine months ended March 31.
