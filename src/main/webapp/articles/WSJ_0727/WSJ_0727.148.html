Business Brief -- First National Pennsylvania Corp.: Unit in Erie, Pa., Increases Provisions for Loan Losses

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

First National Bank of Pennsylvania, Erie, Pa., said it added $8.4 million to its provisions for loan losses for the quarter ended June 30. The special provision increases the quarter's total reserve for loan losses to $14.1 million. The parent company, First National Pennsylvania Corp., said it will report a loss for the first six months and anticipates a loss for the year.

The bank, which is expected to release second-quarter results next week, said the special provision was prompted by a regular review by the comptroller of the currency.
