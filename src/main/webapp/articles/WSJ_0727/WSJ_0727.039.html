Who's News: General Motors Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

GENERAL MOTORS Corp. (Detroit) -- Richard J.S. Clout was named vice president in charge of international operations of GMAC Financial Services, the financial subsidiary of GM. Jerome B. Van Orman was named vice president in charge of world-wide borrowings for the unit, Mr. Clout's previous position. Mr. Van Orman was formerly director of U.S. borrowings.
