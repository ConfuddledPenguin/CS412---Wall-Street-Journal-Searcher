Canadian Firms' Outlays

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian spending on plant and equipment is now expected to rise 6.4% this year, somewhat more slowly than previously estimated, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The agency, which surveys spending intentions of businesses and government agencies, had estimated in March that capital spending this year would rise 7.4%.

The latest spending estimate was bigger than expected by some economic analysts who had predicted that many projects would be shelved because of a drop in corporate profits in the past year and because of a decline in business confidence in Canada's economic outlook.
