Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Household International Inc., shelf offering of up to $250 million of debt securities and warrants to purchase debt securities.

Moorco International Inc., initial offering of six million common shares, of which four million shares will be sold by the company and two million by holders, via PaineWebber Inc. and Wertheim Schroder & Co. Inc.

TakeCare Inc., initial offering of two million common shares, of which 1.4 million will be sold by the company and 600,000 by holders, via First Boston Corp., Kidder, Peabody & Co. Inc., and Robertson, Stephens & Co.

Zilog Inc., proposed offering of two million initial common shares, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. Inc.
