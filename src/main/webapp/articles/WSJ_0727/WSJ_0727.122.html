Who's News: Yamaichi Securities Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

YAMAICHI SECURITIES Co. (Tokyo) -- Yamaichi International (America) Inc. named Genji Sugiyama chief executive officer. Mr. Sugiyama, currently president of this U.S. subsidiary of Yamaichi Securities, a financial services firm, succeeds Shoji Hattori, who returned to Tokyo as senior managing director of Taiheiyo Securities Co. Scott E. Pardee, previously co-chairman of the U.S. arm, will serve as sole chairman.
