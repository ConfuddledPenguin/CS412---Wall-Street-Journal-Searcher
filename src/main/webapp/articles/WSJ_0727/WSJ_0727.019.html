Who's News: Sonat Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

SONAT Inc. (Birmingham, Ala.) -- William O. Bourke, 63 years old, chairman and chief executive officer of Reynolds Metals Co., and John J. Phelan Jr., 59, chairman and chief executive of the New York Stock Exchange, were elected directors of this oil and natural gas exploration and production concern, increasing board membership to 15.
