Who's News: Trinova Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

TRINOVA Corp. (Maumee, Ohio) -- Purdy Crawford and Steven S. Reinemund were elected to the board of this maker of auto parts. Mr. Crawford is chairman, president and chief executive officer of Imasco Ltd. Mr. Reinemund has been president and chief executive officer of Pizza Hut Inc. since 1984.
