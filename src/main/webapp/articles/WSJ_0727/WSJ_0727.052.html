Joint Venture Is Set in Italy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

LONDON -- Rolls-Royce PLC, Aeritalia and a unit of Alfa Romeo S.p.A. have agreed to jointly manufacture castings for gas turbine engines in Italy, the companies said. The three will hold equal shares in the joint venture, to be known as Europea Microfusioni Spaziali, which is to build a #25 million ($45.2 million) factory in southern Italy.

Financial details of the venture weren't disclosed. The Italian government is partially funding construction of the plant, the companies said.

The castings will be used in the Rolls-Royce Tay engine program. The venture will eventually make castings for the Rolls-Royce engine that equips the Tornado jet fighter, built by a European consortium that includes Aeritalia and British Aerospace PLC, and the engine proposed for the European Fighter Aircraft, planned by another pan-European consortium.
