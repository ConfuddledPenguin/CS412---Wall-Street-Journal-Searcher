Who's News: Liggett Group Inc.'s Name Change Cleared; New Directors Elected

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

DURHAM, N.C. -- Liggett Group Inc. said that its stockholders approved changing the tobacco company's name to Brooke Group Ltd., and that four directors were elected. The new board members are Richard S. Ressler, Michael Rosenbaum, Jean E. Sharpe and Steven J. Green.

Mr. Ressler, 31 years old, is a vice president of the company and executive vice president of Brooke Management Inc.; Mr. Rosenbaum, 52, is a vice president of the company and senior vice president of Brooke Management; Ms. Sharpe, 43, is a vice president and secretary of the company, and vice president and general counsel of Brooke Management; and Mr. Green, 44, is chief executive officer of Samsonite Corp. Their election expands board membership to six.

In connection with the name change, the company's New York Stock Exchange ticker symbol will be BGL, as of next Monday.

Last month, the company said it restructured its organization by separating its business into two separate subsidiary corporations, Liggett & Myers Tobacco Co. and Impel Marketing Inc. It said Liggett & Myers Tobacco will be renamed Liggett Group Inc., effective today.
