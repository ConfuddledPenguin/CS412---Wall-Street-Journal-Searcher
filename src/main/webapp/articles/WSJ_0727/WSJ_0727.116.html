Who's News: Universal Matchbox Group Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

UNIVERSAL MATCHBOX GROUP Ltd. (New York) -- Robert B. Hoffman was named to the board of this toy company, succeeding Paul L. Hertenstein, who retired at age 69. The board stands at 10 members. Mr. Hoffman is currently a business consultant and was previously executive vice president and chief financial officer of Staley Continental Inc., a food processing company.
