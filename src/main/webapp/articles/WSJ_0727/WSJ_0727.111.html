Money-Fund Assets Rose $1.93 Billion In the Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 513 money-market funds rose $1.93 billion to $391.84 billion, in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute. Assets of 251 general-purpose funds rose $1.21 billion to $135.91 billion; 102 brokerdealer funds rose $356.6 million to $160.75 billion; and 160 institutional funds rose $366 million to $95.18 billion.

Dr. Jacob Dreyer, vice president and chief economist at the Washington trade association, said that money funds posted the third consecutive large increase in assets, despite lagging yields resulting from declines in yields on direct money-market instruments.
