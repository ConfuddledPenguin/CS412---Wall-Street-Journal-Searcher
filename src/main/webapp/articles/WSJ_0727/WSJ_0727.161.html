Business Brief -- Anchor Savings Bank: Asset Sales Help Boost Net To $40.9 Million in Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

Anchor Savings Bank reported fourth-quarter net income of $40.9 million, compared with a year-earlier net loss, reflecting gains from asset sales plus improved operations. The earnings for the quarter equal $2.15 a share, compared with a loss of $26.9 million, or $1.75 a share, in the year-earlier period.

The Hewlett, N.Y., savings bank said that excluding $45.7 million of pretax gains on asset sales and a $3.8 million addition to its provision for credit losses, it reported profit for the quarter of $1.9 million. The comparable figure for the year-earlier period was a $3 million net loss.

The asset sales are part of Anchor's plan to bring it into full compliance with federal capital requirements.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Anchor shares closed at $1.6875, up 31.25 cents.
