Who's News: Geodynamics Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

GEODYNAMICS Corp. (Santa Barbara, Calif.) -- Donald L. Haas was appointed a director of this supplier of systems engineering, systems integration and software development to the U.S. government and prime contractors, succeeding Morris Bennett, who retired. Mr. Haas was vice president and general manager of the government systems sector of Perkin-Elmer Corp. and was Air Force deputy undersecretary for space systems from 1979 to 1982.
