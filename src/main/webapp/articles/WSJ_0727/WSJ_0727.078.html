FirstFederal Financial Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

WOOSTER, Ohio -- FirstFederal Financial Services Corp. said it agreed to acquire First Federal of Mount Vernon, Ohio, a mutual savings association with $77.4 million in assets but a negative net worth. First Federal of Mount Vernon will be merged into First Federal Savings & Loan Assn. of Wooster, the principal unit of FirstFederal Financial Services. FirstFederal Financial Services will inject capital into First Federal of Mount Vernon if it is acquired, but isn't paying anything at the outset, an official said.

FirstFederal Financial Services said it has assets of $321 million, shareholder equity of $25.7 million, and a ratio of capital to assets of 8%.
