Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- American Express Bank Ltd. named Suzan T. Picariello a senior vice president and region head for consumer financial services in Latin America and the Caribbean. The position is a new one at American Express Bank, a subsidiary of the travel, financial and information services company. Ms. Picariello, 41 years old, was previously assistant general counsel, American Express Travel Related Services Co., another unit.
