Japanese Vehicle Exports Fall

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

TOKYO -- Japan's exports of cars, trucks and buses declined 5.2% in the first half of 1990, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers' Association said. A total of 2.9 million units were exported in the latest six months, compared with 3.06 million units a year earlier. However, the latest period's result was up 2.7% from the 1989 second half.

The association also said that in June alone, 11 Japanese auto makers exported a total of 490,506 units, a 4.1% annual rise.
