Business Brief -- Mellon Bank Corp.: Branch Banks to Be Opened In 21 Stores of Giant Eagle

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

Mellon Bank Corp., a Pittsburgh-based bank holding company, said it will open full-service branch banks in 21 Giant Eagle Inc. supermarkets in western Pennsylvania. The first of the new branches will open in October, and all are to be in operation by mid-1992, Mellon said. The branches will operate during evening hours and weekends. Giant Eagle, also based in Pittsburgh, is a supermarket chain that owns 53 stores and franchises 70.

Mellon already has automated teller machines in 65 of the 78 Giant Eagle stores in Pennsylvania. These ATMs will continue to operate, Mellon said.

Mellon has assets totaling $31.7 billion.
