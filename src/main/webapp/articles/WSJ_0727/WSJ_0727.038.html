Canadian Steel Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 262,440 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, a 4.2% increase from 251,936 tons the previous week but a 12% decline from 299,366 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. From Jan. 1 through last Saturday, production totaled 8,007,524 tons, a 9.8% decline from 8,881,216 tons a year earlier.
