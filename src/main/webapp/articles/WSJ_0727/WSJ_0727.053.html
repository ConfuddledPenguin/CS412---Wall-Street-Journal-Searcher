Medical Care Debentures

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

DALLAS -- Medical Care International Inc. completed an offering of $60 million principal amount of convertible subordinated debentures, due Aug. 1, 2015. The operator of outpatient surgical centers said proceeds will be used for long-term expansion and general corporate purposes.

Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. was the lead underwriter.
