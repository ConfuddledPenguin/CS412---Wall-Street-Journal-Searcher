Who's News: MCI Communications Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

MCI COMMUNICATIONS Corp. (Washington) -- O. Gene Gabbard was named chief financial officer and an executive vice president of this telecommunications concern. Mr. Gabbard will assume the new post after the completion of the pending merger between MCI and Telecom USA Inc., an Atlanta-based telecommunications concern of which he is chairman and chief executive officer. In his new posts at MCI, he will succeed Daniel F. Akerson, who in May was named executive vice president and group executive.
