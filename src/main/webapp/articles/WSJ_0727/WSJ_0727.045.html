First Bank System Agreement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- First Bank System Inc. said it reached an agreement with JCB International Co. of Japan to process credit card transactions for JCB in the northern region of the U.S. Terms weren't disclosed. The bank holding company said it will process and settle all transactions for JCB, Japan's largest credit card issuer, with 17 million cardholders there.

First Bank also said it will provide customer service support for the card members and merchants and solicit merchants for JCB card acceptance.

As of May 1990, 250,000 U.S. merchants accepted the JCB card.
