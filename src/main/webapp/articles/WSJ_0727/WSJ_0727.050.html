United Technologies Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

EAST HARTFORD, Conn. -- United Technologies Corp.'s Pratt & Whitney division said it received a $70 million order for eight PW4156A jet engines from the Romanian national airline, Transporturile Aeriene Romane, or Tarom. The engines, including two spares, will power three new Airbus Industrie A310-300 airliners, Pratt & Whitney said.

United Technologies, in nearby Hartford, also has businesses in building and automotive systems.
