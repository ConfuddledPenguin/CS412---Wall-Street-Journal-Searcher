Business Brief -- Revco D.S. Inc.: Affiliated Food Stores Buys 13 Drug Stores in Arkansas

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

Revco D.S. Inc., Twinsburg, Ohio, said it agreed to sell 13 drug stores in Arkansas to Affiliated Food Stores, Little Rock, Ark., but didn't disclose the price. The drugstore chain operator said it has disposed of 538 of the 712 stores it plans to shed as part of a restructuring disclosed in January. Revco is operating with protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of federal bankruptcy statutes.

The company said it will reduce its store base to 1,100 outlets in 10 Eastern states to improve efficiency and increase its return on assets. Revco is trying to sell stores in Arizona, New Mexico, Colorado and Texas as well as two remaining outlets in Arkansas.
