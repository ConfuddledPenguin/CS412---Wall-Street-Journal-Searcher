THE SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION will allow until Aug. 22 for comment before granting final approval of SuperShares, a product designed to give investors protection from market drops. An article in yesterday's editions didn't fully explain the agency's action. (See: "SEC Approves Rule Curtailing Program Trades" -- WSJ July 26, 1990)
