GACC Notes Are Tendered

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

CINCINNATI -- Great American Communications Co. said $46 million of its 13 1/4% senior extendable reset notes were tendered in response to an offer by the company that recently expired. Combined with previously reported private purchases, GACC now has repurchased about $135 million of the notes, or 78% of the total issued, the company said.

Great American is a radio and television broadcasting concern that also is involved in movie and video production.

American Financial Corp., a Cincinnati insurance holding company, owns 68% of the stock of Great American.
