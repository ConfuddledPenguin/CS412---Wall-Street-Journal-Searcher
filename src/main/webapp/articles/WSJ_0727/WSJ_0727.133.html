International Brief -- Norsk Hydro AS: Pretax Profit Declined 19% In First Half of the Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

Norsk Hydro AS, a Norwegian energy, agricultural products and metals concern, said pretax profit fell 19% in the first six months of the year as earnings at its aluminum and magnesium operations fell sharply. Pretax profit for the period dropped to 2.57 billion kroner ($410 million) from 3.19 billion kroner a year earlier. After a particularly soft first quarter, pretax profit in the second quarter declined a slight 1.4% to 948 million kroner from 961 million kroner.

Profit after taxes and minority interests, or net income attributable to shareholders, fell 25% to 1.27 billion kroner from 1.70 billion kroner. Earnings per share dropped to 6.20 kroner from 8.30 kroner.

Sales totaled 30.4 billion kroner in the six months, down about 10% from 33.6 billion kroner a year earlier. The steepest declines were registered by the company's agriculture, oil and gas and metals businesses, whose sales suffered in both the first and second quarters.
