Amoco to Expand Texas Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

CHICAGO -- Amoco Corp. said it will spend more than $200 million over the next four years to expand the capacity of its Alvin, Texas, olefins plant. The energy concern said the expansion will increase annual ethylene output at the facility by about 600 million pounds from over two billion pounds presently, and will increase propylene production capacity by 200 pounds, from current annual levels of about 800 million pounds.
