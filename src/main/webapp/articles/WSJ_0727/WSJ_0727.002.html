Who's News: Stanhome Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

STANHOME Inc. (Westfield, Mass.) -- Alejandro Diaz, 45-year-old president of this direct marketer of personal-care and home products, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer, effective next Wednesday, succeeding H.L. Tower, 58, who will remain chairman. Mr. Diaz's other current title of chief operating will be dropped.
