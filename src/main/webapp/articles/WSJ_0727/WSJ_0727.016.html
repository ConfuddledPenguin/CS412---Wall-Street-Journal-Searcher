Whittaker Sells Technibilt Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

LOS ANGELES -- Whittaker Corp. said it sold its Technibilt division in Newton, N.C., to Cari-All Co. of Montreal. Terms weren't disclosed. Technibilt makes shopping carts and other equipment for the grocery industry in the U.S. Cari-All makes similar products. Whittaker is a high-technology equipment maker.
