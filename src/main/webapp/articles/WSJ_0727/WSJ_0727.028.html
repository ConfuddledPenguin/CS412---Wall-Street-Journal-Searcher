Initial Jobless Claims Fell in Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits fell to a seasonally adjusted 358,000 the week ended July 14 from 361,000 the previous week, the Labor Department said. In the week ended July 7, the number of people receiving regular state benefits rose to a seasonally adjusted 2,490,000 from 2,429,000 the previous week. Based on that data, the insured unemployment rate rose to 2.4% from 2.3% a week earlier.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, without seasonal adjustment, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended July 7 rose to 2,475,300 from 2,134,600 a week earlier.
