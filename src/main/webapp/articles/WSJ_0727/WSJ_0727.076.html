Comerica Unit Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

DETROIT -- Comerica Inc.'s Comerica Bank-Texas subsidiary said it has acquired the deposits and some of the loans of Continental Bank of Dallas in a transaction assisted by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation. Continental, which has deposits of about $124 million, operates two branches. With the Continental acquisition, Comerica Bank-Texas now has assets of over $1 billion, the company said.
