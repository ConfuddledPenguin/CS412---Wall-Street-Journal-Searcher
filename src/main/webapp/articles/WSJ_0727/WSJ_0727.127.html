Knogo Plans Stock Repurchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

HAUPPAUGE, N.Y. -- Knogo Corp. said it plans to repurchase as much as $1 million of its common shares in open market transactions. At May 31, the maker of retail anti-theft systems, had 5.3 million shares outstanding. The company said the current stock price doesn't reflect its "prospects and represents an excellent long-term investment."
