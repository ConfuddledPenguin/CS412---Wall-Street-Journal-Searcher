International Brief -- Endesa: Consolidated Earnings Rise For First 6 Months of 1990

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

Endesa, a Spanish electric utility, said it had consolidated earnings for the first half ended June 30 of 149.44 pesetas a share, or about $1.46 per American Depositary Receipt (ADR). For the first six months last year, Endesa reported consolidated earnings of 135.80 pesetas a share, or about $1.32 an ADR.

Endesa said net income for the 1990 six months was 38.86 billion pesetas, or about $379.3 million, compared with 35.31 billion pesetas, or about $344.7 million, in 1989.

Consolidated revenue for the first half was 282.87 billion pesetas, or $2.76 billion, up from 268.25 billion pesetas, or $2.62 billion.

Endesa said it reduced debt during the period by about 2.2% of total outstanding debt. Capital expenditures were about 27.27 billion pesetas, or $266.18 million, down from 27.91 billion pesetas, or about $272.45 million in the 1989 first half.

The company said pesetas were converted to dollars at a rate of $1 equals 102.45 pesetas.
