GM Gets Navy Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

WASHINGTON -- General Motors Corp. got a $65.8 million Navy contract for aircraft turbine engines.

FKW Inc. was given a $23 million Navy contract to operate surveillance field stations.

Litton Systems Inc., a unit of Litton Industries Inc., won a $20.9 million Navy contract for cruise-missile components.

Unisys Corp. got a $15 million Navy contract for Trident missile components.

General Electric Co. was issued a $14.6 million Air Force contract for aircraft jet engines.
