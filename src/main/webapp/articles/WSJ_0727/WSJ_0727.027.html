Antidote

"Jogging is very boring,"

I've heard many people say,

But in all my years of jogging

I've never found it that way.

In fact, that little comment

Really goes against the grain --

How can it be boring

While it's causing so much pain?

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

Once Is Enough

The prospect of a second childhood

I can face with acquiescence,

Provided it doesn't lead to

A second adolescence.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
