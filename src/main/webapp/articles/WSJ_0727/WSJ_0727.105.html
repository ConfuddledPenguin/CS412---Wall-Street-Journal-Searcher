Business Brief -- H.H. Robertson Co.: Company to Change Name To Robertson-Ceco Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

H.H. Robertson Co. said it will change its name to Robertson-Ceco Corp. following the proposed acquisition of Ceco Industries Inc., Oakbrook Terrace, Ill. Headquarters will remain in Pittsburgh, Robertson said.

Robertson has agreed to pay $15 million plus 3.4 million to 4.1 million of its common shares for Ceco, and to assume $85 million of Ceco debt.

Robertson is a building-products concern. Ceco manufactures doors, metal buildings and roofing systems, and it provides concrete-forming services.
