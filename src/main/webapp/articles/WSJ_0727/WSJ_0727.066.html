Who's News: Panhandle Eastern Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

PANHANDLE EASTERN Corp. (Houston) -- George E. Rupp was elected to the board of this interstate gas pipeline concern. Mr. Rupp is president of Rice University, Houston. He succeeds former U.S. Sen. James B. Pearson, who retired from the board at age 70.
