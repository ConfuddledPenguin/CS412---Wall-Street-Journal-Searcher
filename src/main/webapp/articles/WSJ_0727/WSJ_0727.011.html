Japanese Retail Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

TOKYO -- Sales at Japan's department stores and other major retail stores went up 12% in June from a year earlier to 1.622 trillion yen ($10.81 billion), the Ministry of International Trade and Industry announced. Ministry officials said unseasonally warm weather during the month led to brisk sales of summer clothing and such products as air conditioners.
