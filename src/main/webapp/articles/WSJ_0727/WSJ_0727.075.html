Coastal Corp. Unit Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

HOUSTON -- Coastal Corp. said its ANR Production Co. unit is purchasing additional interests in 374 producing oil and gas wells and a natural gas processing facility in Utah. Coastal declined to disclose the name of the seller or the purchase price. The pipeline concern said its portion of production will increase to 3,600 barrels of oil and 10.8 million cubic feet of natural gas a day from 2,400 barrels of oil and 7.2 million cubic feet of gas. The wells are in the Altamont/Bluebell field in Utah.

ANR is operator of about 154 of the wells and of the Altamont plant, which has the capacity to process 40 million cubic feet of gas a day. Propane, butane and natural gas are produced at that plant.
