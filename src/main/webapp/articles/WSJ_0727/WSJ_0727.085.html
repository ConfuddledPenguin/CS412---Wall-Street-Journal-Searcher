Who's News: Egghead Discount Software

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

EGGHEAD DISCOUNT SOFTWARE (Issaquah, Wash.) -- Matthew J. Griffin was named president and chief operating officer. Mr. Griffin, formerly executive vice president of operations, succeeds Stuart Sloan as president. The post of chief operating officer is newly created. Mr. Sloan, 46 years old, remains chairman and chief executive officer. Mr. Griffin is 38 years old. Egghead, a software retailer, has been struggling to overcome the effects of its overly rapid expansion in years past. For the year ended March 31, it had a net loss of $7.7 million, or 47 cents a share, on sales of $456.3 million.
