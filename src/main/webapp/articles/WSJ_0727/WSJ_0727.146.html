Business Brief -- Eldon Industries Inc.: Office-Products Company Looks at Business Options

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

Eldon Industries Inc., an office-products maker, said it is evaluating a number of strategic alternatives, including a sale or business combination. The Inglewood, Calif., company said it has had preliminary discussions with several companies, but that it has reached no definitive agreements and can give no assurance any sale or transaction will occur. Eldon said it has hired a financial adviser to assist in the evaluation. It did not identify the adviser.

Eldon posted net income of $2.1 million, or 23 cents a share, for the first quarter ended March 24 compared with year-earlier profit of $1.9 million, or 21 cents a share. Sales were $26.3 million compared with $25.1 million a year earlier.

For the 53-week period ended Dec. 30, Eldon had net income of $8.6 million, or 91 cents a share, compared with year-ago profit of $7.6 million, or 81 cents a share. Sales were $112.6 million compared with $101.4 million a year earlier.

Eldon closed yesterday at $17.875, up 62.5 cents, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
