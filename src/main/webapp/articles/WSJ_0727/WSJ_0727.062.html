Mediq Unit Completes Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

PENNSAUKEN, N.J. -- NutraMax Products Inc. and Aid-Pack Inc., a unit of Mediq Inc., said they have completed a previously announced merger, valued at about $18 million. Mediq said it owns 81.7% of the merged companies, which will operate and trade shares over-the-counter as NutraMax Products Inc. Mediq said it received about 120 million shares of NutraMax as a result of the merger. NutraMax shares have been trading in the over-the-counter market.

Aid-Pack, a Gloucester, Mass.-based maker and distributor of feminine personal-care products, had sales of about $13.1 million last year.

NutraMax, formerly based in Ft. Lee, N.J., and now in Gloucester, distributes and markets health and beauty products in the U.S. and Japan. The company had sales of about $347,000 last year.

Mediq, based in Pennsauken, N.J., sells health-care products and services. The company had sales of about $350 million last year.
