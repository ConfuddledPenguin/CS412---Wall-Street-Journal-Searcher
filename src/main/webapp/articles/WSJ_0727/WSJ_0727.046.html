Who's News: Eastern Enterprises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

EASTERN ENTERPRISES (Weston, Mass.) -- Thomas W. Jones was named a board member. Mr. Jones is currently executive vice president, finance and planning, for Teachers' Insurance Annuity Association/College Retirement Equities Fund of New York. He is an addition to the board and brings board membership to 12. Eastern is a utility, marine transport and water-purification products concern.
