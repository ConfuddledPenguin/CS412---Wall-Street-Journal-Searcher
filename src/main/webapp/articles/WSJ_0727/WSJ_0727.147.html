Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/27/90

MAGAZINES: Capital Cities Media Inc., a Capital Cities/ABC Inc. unit, agreed to sell Travel Agent magazine to a partnership led by Richard Friese, a travel-industry publisher. Terms weren't disclosed.

ESQUIRE: Hearst Corp. named Lee Eisenberg editor in chief of a British edition of Esquire, to be launched in March. His successor as editor of the U.S. Esquire hasn't been named. Adam Moss, former editor of the defunct 7 Days and now a consultant with Time Warner Inc., said he discussed the job with Hearst executives. A Hearst insider said Mr. Moss was among those being considered, as was Gregory Curtis, editor of Texas Monthly.

WHO'S NEWS: Donald De Line was named senior vice president of production for Walt Disney Co.'s Touchstone Pictures. He was president of production for Snowback Productions. David Vogel, vice president of production for Walt Disney Pictures, was promoted to senior vice president of production for the unit.
