Business Brief -- Atlantic Richfield Co.: Unit Sets a Joint Venture To Open Stores in Thailand

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

The am/pm International subsidiary of Atlantic Richfield Co. of Los Angeles said it signed a joint venture and licensing agreement with a Thai investor group to open more than 500 am/pm convenience stores in Thailand over the next 10 years. The new joint venture company, am/pm Thailand Ltd., will be 80% owned by the investor group and 20% by am/pm International. The investor group includes Bangkok Metropolitan Bank, BanChang Ban Pala Group, and Richard Ellis real estate of Thailand. Several Thai managers will be trained by am/pm International in Los Angeles. The Thai stores will feature Western-style fast food, as well as local fast food such as noodles flavored with chicken, pork and fishballs.

Previously, am/pm International signed similar agreements for convenience stores in Canada, Spain, Portugal, Japan and Taiwan. There are more than 720 am/pm mini-markets in the U.S.
