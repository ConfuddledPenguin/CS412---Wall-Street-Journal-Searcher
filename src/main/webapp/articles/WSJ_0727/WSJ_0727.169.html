Business Brief -- Octel Communications Corp.: California Concern Says 4th Period Net Rose 50%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

Octel Communications Corp., continuing a string of strong performances, said net income in its fourth quarter ended June 30 rose 50% to $4.9 million, or 29 cents a share. In the year-earlier period, the maker of voice processing systems earned $3.3 million, or 21 cents a share. Revenue surged 40% to $36.2 million from $25.9 million.

For the full year, profit climbed 50% to $17.7 million, or $1.04 a share, from $11.8 million, or 78 cents a share, in fiscal 1989. Revenue rose 47% to $127.8 million from $87.2 million.
