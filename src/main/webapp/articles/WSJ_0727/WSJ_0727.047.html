Mead Corp. Unit Services

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/27/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Mead Corp. said its Mead Data Central Inc. unit will triple the number of brokerage house reports available through its LEXIS and NEXIS electronic publishing services. Mead Data said it will add company and industry research reports from the Investext database on Sept. 1. Investext, a service of Thomson Financial Networks Inc., Boston, includes research reports by leading U.S. and foreign investment concerns.
