  • Date: 05/30/90

ATLANTA -- First Financial Management Corp. said it agreed to acquire an Atlanta-based debt-collection company for about $65 million. First Financial said it will purchase Nationwide Credit for 1.3 million shares of First Financial common stock and $31.8 million in cash.

Nationwide Credit provides services to financial institutions, retailers, government agencies anbd health-care providers and had more than $30 million in revenue in 1989. First Financial is an information-services company.
