Business Brief -- Hanson PLC: Marvin Electric and Others Sold for Over $62 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

Hanson Industries, the U.S. arm of Hanson PLC, the British industrial company, said it sold a group of companies including, Los Angeles-based Marvin Electric Mfg. Co., for more than $62 million. Marvin, a maker of incandescent and high intensity discharge lighting, had operating profit of $392,000 and sales of $13.9 million in the six months ended March 31. Marvin, acquired in 1987 as part of the Kidde Inc. acquisition, was sold for $18.5 million.

Hanson said it also sold a number of other businesses for more than $31.8 million in cash and $11.7 million in notes; the businesses in the past fiscal year had sales of $90.5 million and pretax losses of $86,000.
