General Dynamics Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

WASHINGTON -- General Dynamics Corp. was given a $30.6 million Army contract for an armored-vehicle facility for Pakistan.

Grumman Corp. said it won a $16.4 million Army contract to design an experiment for the Strategic Defense Initiative.

Martin Marietta Corp. received a $15.1 million Air Force contract for rocket motor testing.
