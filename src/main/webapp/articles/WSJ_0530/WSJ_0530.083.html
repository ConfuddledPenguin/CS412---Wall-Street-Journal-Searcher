Treasury Plans to Sell $17.2 Billion of Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.15 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of a record $17.2 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.06 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on Sept. 6 and Dec. 6, respectively. They will be available in minimum denominations of $10,000.

Tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
