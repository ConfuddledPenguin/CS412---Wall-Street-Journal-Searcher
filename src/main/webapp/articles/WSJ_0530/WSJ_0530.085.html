Financing Business: Vidmark Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

VIDMARK Inc., a Santa Monica, Calif.-based motion picture distributor, said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of 1.5 million common shares, of which 1.3 million shares will be offered by the company and the rest by selling shareholders. Sutro & Co. and Raymond James & Associates are managing the offering.
