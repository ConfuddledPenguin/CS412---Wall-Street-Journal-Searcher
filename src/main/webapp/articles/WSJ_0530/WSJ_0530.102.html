Duramed Pharmaceuticals Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

CINCINNATI -- Duramed Pharmaceuticals Inc. said it signed a joint-venture agreement with Atral/Cipan, giving Duramed the U.S. marketing rights to the Lisbon, Portugal-based antibiotics maker's drugs that have been approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration, including Amoxicillin, Cephalexin and Tetracycline. Terms weren't disclosed.
