Business Brief -- Pic 'N' Save Corp.: Firm Alleges Holders Group Has Broken Securities Laws

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

Pic 'N' Save Corp. charged Girard Partners L.P. with numerous violations of federal and state securities laws in connection with Girard's attempt to gain control of Pic 'N' Save's board. In a lawsuit filed yesterday in U.S. District Court in Los Angeles, Pic 'N' Save accused Girard of making "false and misleading statements" in certain regulatory filings, among other violations.

Pic 'N' Save, based in Dominguez, Calif., seeks to block Girard's solicitation of shareholder consents and proxies and to prohibit Girard from purchasing additional Pic 'N' Save shares until the allegedly false statements are corrected. The suit also asked the court to declare that all consents and proxies obtained so far are void.

In La Jolla, Calif., David H. Batchelder, Girard's controlling partner, denied the allegations. "We believe the suit is just a tactical maneuver to try and impede our solicitations."

Girard, which holds a 6.7% stake in Pic 'N' Save, is trying to install five nominees on Pic 'N' Save's seven-member board. Pic `N' Save, a discount retailer of close-out merchandise, recently put itself up for sale in an apparent attempt to convince investors that the incumbent board is more dedicated than Girard in promoting shareholder value.
