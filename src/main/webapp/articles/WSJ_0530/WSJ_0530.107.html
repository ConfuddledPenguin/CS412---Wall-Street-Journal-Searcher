Who's News: Scat Hovercraft Board Dismisses R.R. Molina From Three Top Posts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

MIAMI -- Scat Hovercraft Inc. said its board voted to remove Ronald R. Molina as president, chairman and chief executive officer and as a director. The maker of recreational hovercraft vehicles gave no reason for the action. Neither company officials nor Mr. Molina could be reached for comment.

The board elected Jordan R. Finn, secretary and a director, as interim president and interim chief executive officer until a new president is chosen.

Scat said Mr. Molina had resigned from his positions, had terminated his employment agreement and had resigned as a board member. Last Friday, however, during a special meeting of the board, Mr. Molina attempted to withdraw his resignation, Scat said.

At that point, the board voted to remove Mr. Molina from his positions and to terminate his employment agreement "in the event {Mr.} Molina's prior resignation and termination were ineffective," the company said in a news release.
