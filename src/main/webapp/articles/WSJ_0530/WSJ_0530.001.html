Republic Waste Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

HOUSTON -- Republic Waste Industries Inc., a waste-management concern, said it agreed to acquire certain assets of Boyas Excavating Inc. of Cleveland. Terms weren't disclosed. Republic said the assets to be acquired include those used in Boyas's excavation, demolition and materials handling business. Boyas had 1989 revenue of about $29.3 million.
