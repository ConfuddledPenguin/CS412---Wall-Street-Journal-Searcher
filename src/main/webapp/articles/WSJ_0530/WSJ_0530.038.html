Italy's Wholesale Sales Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

MILAN, Italy -- Italy's industrial wholesale sales index in February rose 5.3% from a year earlier, Istat, the state statistical institute, said. Domestic wholesale sales rose 4.3%, and sales to foreign destinations grew 8.6%.

For the first two months of the year, wholesale sales rose 6% from a year earlier, owing to increases of 5.2% in domestic sales and 8.7% in foreign sales. Sales of capital products to both foreign and domestic destinations grew 12%, and wholesale sales of consumer products rose 8.3%. Sales of intermediate goods rose 0.8%.
