Financing Business: Great Lakes Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

GREAT LAKES BANCORP, Ann Arbor, Mich., said it filed a registration statement for an offering of as many as 1.5 million preferred units, each consisting of 1/10th share of noncumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series A, and one warrant to purchase one-fourth of a Great Lakes common share. The underwriters also have been granted a 30-day option to buy as many as 225,000 additional preferred units. The price will be $10 a unit; the minimum purchase will be 100 units. Quarterly dividends will be paid, if declared, at an annual rate of $10 a preferred share through June 30, 1995. Subsequently, the dividend rate will adjust quarterly to 250 basis points over the one-year Treasury bill rate, multiplied by the $100 stated value of the preferred stock; a basis point is equal to 1/100th of one percentage point. But the dividend rate for any quarterly period won't amount to less than $10 a year or greater than $15 a year. The offering will be underwritten by Roney & Co., Detroit, and Ohio Co., Columbus.
