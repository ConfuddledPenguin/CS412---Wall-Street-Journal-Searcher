Sony Has Lighter Camcorder

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

TOKYO -- Sony Corp. introduced what it claims is the lightest and smallest camcorder on the market. Sony said its new TR-45 model weighs 690 grams, or about 1 1/2 pounds. The company said it trimmed 100 grams from the weight of its popular TR-55, introduced last summer, by developing a smaller image sensor and lens, and by reducing the weight of other parts.

The fully automatic TR-45 goes on sale in Japan on July 1 for 130,000 yen ($868), Sony officials said. It will be introduced in the U.S. later in the summer and in Europe in the fall, they said.

Introduction of the compact TR-55 last year greatly boosted world-wide demand for 8mm camcorders, and Sony officials said they expect to sell more than three million 8mm machines this year.
