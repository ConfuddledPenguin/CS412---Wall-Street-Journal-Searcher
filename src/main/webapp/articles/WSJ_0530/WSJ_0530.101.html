Business Brief -- ATI Medical Inc.: Roxbury Partners Lift Stake To 12.04% of Firm's Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

Roxbury Partners and its partners, Jonathan Bowers and Christopher Middendorf, raised their ownership in ATI Medical Inc. to 12.04% from 10.59%, according to a Securities and Exchange Commission filing. In March, Roxbury said it and Messrs. Bowers and Middendorf held talks with certain investment bankers about ATI on how to maximize its stockholder value. The company rents medical equipment to 3,200 hospitals across the U.S.

In Las Vegas, an ATI spokesman said that Roxbury Partners has been a "supportive" shareholder.

ATI has about 5.3 million common shares outstanding. In American Stock Exchange composite trading, ATI closed at $11.75 a share, up 12.5 cents.
