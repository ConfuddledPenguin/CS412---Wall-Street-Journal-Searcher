West German Wholesale Drop

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

WIESBADEN, West Germany -- West German wholesale sales fell 2% after inflation in April from April 1989, the result of a steep decline in sales of raw materials and semifinished goods that weighed against steady sales of finished goods, the Federal Statistics Office here said. A total of 71 billion Deutsche marks ($42.2 billion) of wholesale sales were recorded in April.

The statistics office said that virtually all sectors of raw material and semifinished goods experienced declines in wholesale sales in April.
