StatesWest Airlines Orders Jets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- StatesWest Airlines said it ordered four Boeing Dash 8-100 regional prop jets plus spare engines and parts for $45 million from Boeing Canada Ltd., a unit of Seattle-based Boeing Co. StatesWest, a regional airline that flies to destinations in Arizona and California, said it also acquired options for an additional four Dash 8-100 prop jets. StatesWest said it expects the first four planes to be delivered over the next nine months.

The Boeing Dash 8-100 carries 37 passengers.
