Italy Public Sector Deficit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

MILAN, Italy -- Italy's public-sector deficit narrowed to a provisional 40.449 trillion lire ($32.75 billion) during the first four months of the year from a revised 42.943 trillion lira deficit in the first four months of 1989, according to the treasury. The government deficit for the first four months of 1989 was revised upward from an initial estimate of 42.811 trillion lire.

Tax revenue amounted to 95.746 trillion lire. Public-sector spending totaled 136.195 trillion lire, while treasury operations resulted in a loss of 2.522 trillion lire.

The deficit was covered with the issuing of medium- and long-term government securities totaling 14.580 trillion lire, debt issues placed abroad totaling 3.841 trillion lire and other kinds of government debt totaling 24.550 trillion lire.
