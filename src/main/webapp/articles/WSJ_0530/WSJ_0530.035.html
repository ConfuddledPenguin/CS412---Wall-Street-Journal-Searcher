Who's News: Citicorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

CITICORP (New York) -- Henry B.W. de Vismes was named head of international investments for the investment division at Citibank Private Bank. The position is newly created. The private bank unit of Citicorp provides portfolio-management services and funds to individuals. Mr. de Vismes was previously managing director of Kleinwort Benson International Investment and president of Kleinwort's Investment Strategies unit, both subsidiaries of Kleinwort Benson Group, the London-based merchant bank.
