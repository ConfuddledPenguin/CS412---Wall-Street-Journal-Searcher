Citicorp Paper Sales See Rates Rise From Last Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.233% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.1545% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $410 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.215% to 8.24%.

Citicorp also said the average rate rose to 8.262% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.1801% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $425 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.239% to 8.275%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity Tuesday.
