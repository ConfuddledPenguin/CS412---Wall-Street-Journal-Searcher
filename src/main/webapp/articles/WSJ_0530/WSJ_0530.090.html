Who's News: Aetna Life & Casualty Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

AETNA LIFE & CASUALTY Co. (Hartford, Conn.) -- Zoe Baird was named vice president and general counsel of this multi-line insurance company, effective July 16. Ms. Baird, 37 years old, succeeds Stephen B. Middlebrook, who has been appointed senior vice president and executive counsel to the office of Aetna's chairman.
