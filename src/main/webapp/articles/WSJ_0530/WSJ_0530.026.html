Sweden Producer-Price Data

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's April producer prices were unchanged from March, but were up 3.5% from a year earlier, according to Statistics Sweden. The national statistics agency said export prices also remained unchanged in April from March, but were up 2.1% from the previous year.

Meanwhile, import prices slipped by 0.5% in April from March and were down 0.4% from the year before. The drop in import prices was mainly a result of falling crude-oil prices, Statistics Sweden said.
