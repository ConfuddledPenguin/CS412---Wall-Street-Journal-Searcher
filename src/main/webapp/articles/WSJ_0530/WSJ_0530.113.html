Business Brief -- Unocal Corp.: Energy Firm Completes Sale Of Its Norwegian Subsidiary

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

Unocal Corp. said it completed the previously announced sale of its Norwegian oil and natural gas subsidiary, Unocal Norge A/S, to a subsidiary of Paris-based Total Cie. Francaise des Petroles S.A. for $322 million. Unocal, a diversified energy concern that is based in Los Angeles, said it expects to report an after-tax gain of about $130 million from the sale in the second quarter. A year earlier Unocal earned $120 million, or $1.02 a share, on revenue of $3.07 billion.

The sale has been approved by the Norwegian Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Petroleum and Energy. The Total Cie. Francaise des Petroles unit is Total Marine Norsk A/S.
