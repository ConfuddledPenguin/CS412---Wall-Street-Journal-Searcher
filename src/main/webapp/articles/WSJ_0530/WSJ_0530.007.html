Who's News: Cleveland-Cliffs Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

CLEVELAND-CLIFFS Inc. (Cleveland) -- Gordon N. Carlson, vice president, operations, was named senior vice president, operations, at this iron-ore producer. Mr. Carlson succeeds Henry Whaley, who retired in March. George N. Chandler, senior vice president, coal and international sales, at Cleveland-Cliffs' Pickands Mather unit, was named vice president, a new post at the parent company.
