International Brief -- ABB Asea Brown Boveri AG: Earnings Before Taxes Rose 47% in the First Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

ABB Asea Brown Boveri AG, the big Swiss and Swedish electrical engineering company, reported that its earnings before taxes in the first quarter rose 47% to $239 million from $163 million a year earlier. The company said operating earnings after depreciation rose 45% to $333 million from $229 million a year earlier. Earnings after financial items rose 25% to $215 million.

First-quarter sales rose 39% to $6.11 billion from $4.40 billion a year earlier. When acquisitions, divestitures and exchange-rate changes are excluded, sales rose 9% in the period, the company said.

The company cited good earnings growth in the power transmission, power distribution and financial services segments.
