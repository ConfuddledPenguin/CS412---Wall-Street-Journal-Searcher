Integra Financial Buys Branches

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

PITTSBURGH -- Integra Financial Corp. said its Union National Bank subsidiary acquired 16 branch offices of Horizon Financial. The 16 offices represent about $550 million in deposits.

The Horizon offices had been operated by the Resolution Trust Corp., the federal agency overseeing the sale of collapsed thrifts. Resolution Trust said Union National has agreed to pay $12 million for the branches.

Horizon, which was based in Southampton, Pa., had assets of $1.76 billion and liabilities of $2.1 billion. Resolution Trust said it will receive about $29.5 million for Horizon, including proceeds from the sale of these and other branches.
