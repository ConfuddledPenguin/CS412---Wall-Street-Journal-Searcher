Financing Business: Morgan's Foods Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

MORGAN'S FOODS Inc. said it will begin a rights offering to shareholders as soon as it is approved at its annual meeting next month. The company's board last week approved a rights offering that would give each holder the right to purchase for $1 a share one common share for each two already held. Morgan's said shareholders must vote to increase the number of authorized common shares to 25 million from 15 million. Proceeds of the rights offering would be used to reduce bank debt and other general purposes, the company said. Morgan's Foods, which is based in Cleveland, is a national franchisee of Kentucky Fried Chicken restaurants. The company has about 11.7 million shares outstanding.
