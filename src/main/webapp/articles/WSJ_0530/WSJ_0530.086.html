Who's News: Advanced Micro Devices Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

ADVANCED MICRO DEVICES Inc. (Sunnyvale, Calif.) -- Richard Previte was named president and chief operating officer of this semiconductor maker, the company said. Mr. Previte, 55 years old, was previously executive vice president and chief operating officer. Elected at the company's annual shareholder meeting, Mr. Previte is succeeding Anthony B. Holbrook, who was named vice chairman of the company's board and continues as chief technical officer. Mr. Holbrook, 50, and Mr. Previte are longtime executives at Advanced Micro. With W.J. Sanders II, 53-year-old chairman and chief executive officer, the triumvirate make up the office of the chief executive, which was created in May 1989. The changes in title, the company said, will have no effect on reporting structures or responsibilities within the company.
