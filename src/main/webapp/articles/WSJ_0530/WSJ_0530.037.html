Who's News: Ogden Projects Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

OGDEN PROJECTS Inc. (Fairfield, N.J.) -- Scott G. Mackin and Bruce M. Stone, first executive vice presidents, joined the board at this subsidiary of New York-based Ogden Corp., a building-maintenance and environmental-services concern. Ogden Projects is a developer of waste-to-energy projects. Mr. Mackin, managing director, general counsel and secretary, and Mr. Stone, managing director of project implementation, fill vacancies created by the resignations of R. Richard Ablon, president and chief executive officer of Ogden Corp., and Maria P. Monet, president and chief executive of Ogden Financial Services, its unit.
