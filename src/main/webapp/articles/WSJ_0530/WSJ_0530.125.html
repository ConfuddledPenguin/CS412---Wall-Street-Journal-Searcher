Financing Business: Capital Holding Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/30/90

CAPITAL HOLDING Corp. said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for $300 million of debt securities. The Louisville, Ky., insurance company said this registration, along with its existing shelf registration, will allow Capital to issue as much as $509 million in debt securities. Proceeds are to be used for general corporate purposes.
