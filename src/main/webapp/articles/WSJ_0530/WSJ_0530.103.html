Who's News: Southern California Water Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA WATER Co. (San Dimas, Calif.) -- William V. Caveney, 64-year-old president and chief executive officer, was named chairman and chief executive, succeeding William W. Franklin, 75, who retired. Floyd E. Wicks, vice president of operations, 46, succeeds Mr. Caveney as president of the utility.
