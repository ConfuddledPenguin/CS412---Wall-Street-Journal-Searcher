BROOKLYN UNION GAS Co. (Brooklyn, N.Y.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it reduced its ratings on this utility's senior secured and senior unsecured debt and preferred stock to single-A from single-A-plus. The rating concern also downgraded Brooklyn Union Gas's commercial paper rating to single-A-1 from single-A-1- plus. S&P said, "The downgrades reflect the company's weak financial profile relative to the former ratings, in large part due to less than supportive regulatory decisions by the New York State Public Service Commission."
