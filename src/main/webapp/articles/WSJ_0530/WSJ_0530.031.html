Italy's Foreign Trade Account

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

MILAN -- Italy's foreign trade account deficit narrowed to a provisional, seasonally unadjusted 877 billion lire ($710.1 million) in April from gaps of 1.686 trillion lire a year earlier and 3.25 trillion lire in the previous month, Istat, the state statistical office, said. For this year's first four months, the trade balance showed a deficit of 10.014 trillion lire, narrowed from a 11.324 trillion-lira gap a year earlier. These data also are preliminary and aren't seasonally adjusted.
