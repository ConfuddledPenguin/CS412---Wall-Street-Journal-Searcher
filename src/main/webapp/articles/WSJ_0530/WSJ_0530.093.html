Business Brief -- Chicago West Pullman Corp.: Firm Won't Pursue Options For American Steel & Wire

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/30/90

Chicago West Pullman Corp. said it has stopped exploring its options in connection with a possible new bid to acquire American Steel & Wire Corp. in light of the company's plans to pay a special dividend of $4.25 a share. In December, Chicago West said it was reviewing its options after the rejection of a sweetened $9.50-a-share bid for American Steel.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Chicago West noted, however, that it reserves the right to buy more shares, or sell shares in the open market or in private transactions, with third parties. Chicago West did not disclose its current American Steel stake in the filing.

American West is based in Cuyahoga Heights, Ohio.
