Lloyd's Panel Cuts Premium

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

LONDON -- The Lloyd's of London War Risks Rating Committee said it has reduced premiums for war risks insurance on cargoes being transported to or from the Persian Gulf port of Dammam and other Saudi Arabian ports or places north of it. The cost of insuring cargoes in this category against war risks has been lowered, to 0.375% of the insured value, from 0.5%.

A spokesman for the Institute of London Underwriters said Friday that there had so far been no reports of incidents in the area. "I assume the underwriters feel that maybe the initial rate was too high," he said.
