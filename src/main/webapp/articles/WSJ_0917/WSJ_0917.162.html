Fifth Third Bancorp. Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

CINCINNATI -- Fifth Third Bancorp. said its Fifth Third Bank unit completed the acquisition of Barney Rapp Travel. Terms weren't disclosed. Fifth Third, a bank holding company, said the combination of Barney Rapp Travel with its own Fifth Third Travel Inc. will have sales in excess of $52 million annually and will be one of the largest travel agencies in greater Cincinnati.
