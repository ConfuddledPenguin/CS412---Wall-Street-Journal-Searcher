Pikeville National Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

PIKEVILLE, Ky. -- Pikeville National Corp. said it agreed in principle to acquire First Federal Savings Bank, of Campbellsville, Ky. Terms weren't disclosed. In connection with the acquisition, Pikeville National said it intends to issue and sell about 120,000 shares of its common stock. Proceeds of the sale, expected to be approximately $3 million, will be contributed to the capital of First Federal, Pikeville said.

The conversion and the merger will be subject to approvals of various regulatory agencies, Pikeville said.
