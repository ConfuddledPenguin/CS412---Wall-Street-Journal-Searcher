Vencor to Run Atlanta Hospital

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Vencor Inc. said it agreed to lease and operate the 72-bed Atlanta Hospital in Atlanta. Terms weren't disclosed. Vencor, which operates 11 hospitals, provides long-term care for patients who suffer from catastrophic illness.

The latest acquisition brings to 720 the number of licensed beds in its network, a 71% increase since the beginning of the year, Vencor said.
