Who's News: Cushman & Wakefield Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

CUSHMAN & WAKEFIELD Inc. (New York) -- John E. Oden was appointed senior vice president and director of national institutional accounts for the financial services group of this commercial real estate concern. Mr. Oden, 44 years old, will help arrange debt and equity financing for the company's clients. Mr. Oden, who was executive vice president of Drexel Burnham Realty until its parent company, Drexel Burnham Lambert Group Inc., filed for bankruptcy-law protection, said he will focus on establishing relationships with the nation's largest insurance companies.
