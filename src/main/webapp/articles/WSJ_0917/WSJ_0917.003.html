Who's News: Katy Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

KATY INDUSTRIES Inc. (Elgin, Ill.) -- Wallace E. Carroll, 90 years old, resigned as vice chairman and director of this consumer and industrial products concern, citing health reasons. Philip E. Johnson, a Denver, Colo., lawyer and son-in-law of Mr. Carroll, was named to succeed him as a director of the company.
