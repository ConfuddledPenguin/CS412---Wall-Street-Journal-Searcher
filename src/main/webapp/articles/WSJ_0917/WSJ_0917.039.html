Concurrent Computer Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

TINTON FALLS, N.J. -- Concurrent Computer Corp., a computer systems supplier, said it won't make an interest payment of about $4.7 million due Sept. 15 to holders of its senior subordinated notes, due 1998. The company has retained the investment banking firm Goldman, Sachs & Co. to help with a restructuring. Concurrent has $55 million in senior bank debt and about $110 million in subordinated debt outstanding. On Sept. 28, an additional $15 million of principal and $1.7 million of interest on the bank debt are due.

For its fourth quarter ended June 30, Concurrent had a net loss of $38 million, or $2.05 a share, including a $24.2 million restructuring provision. Last week, the company named Denis R. Brown vice chairman, president and chief executive officer to lead the restructuring effort.
