Who's News: Witco Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

WITCO Corp. (New York) -- The specialty chemicals, petroleum products and engineered materials company named Michael D. Fullwood group vice president, finance and administration. Mr. Fullwood, 43 years old, had been treasurer; he is succeeded by James M. Rutledge, who also was named a corporate vice president. Mr. Rutledge, 38, currently is assistant controller. The moves are effective Oct. 1.
