Hurco to Buy Back Common

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Hurco Cos. said its board authorized the purchase of as many as 650,000 shares of its common stock from time to time in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions. The maker of computer-controlled machine-tool systems, which has 5.5 million common shares outstanding, said the shares will be held in its treasury.
