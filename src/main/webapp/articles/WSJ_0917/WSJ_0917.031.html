Who's News: First American Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

FIRST AMERICAN Corp. (Nashville, Tenn.) -- Dana C. McLendon Jr., president of the specialized banking group of First American National Bank, resigned, effective in November. The company has been searching for a new chairman, and Mr. McLendon said his stepping aside will give that individual "the flexibility to hand pick his management team." Like many Tennessee banks, First American has been suffering from a deteriorating real-estate loan portfolio. In May, the company dismissed Kenneth L. Roberts, its chairman, president and chief executive, who was succeeded in the posts by James F. Smith, formerly vice chairman, until a successor is found. Mr. McLendon, 47 years old, joined the company in 1988.
