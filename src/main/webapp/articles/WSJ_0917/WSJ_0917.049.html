  • Date: 09/17/90

ELIZABETHTOWN Corp. (Westfield, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its ratings on $152.3 million of this water utility holding company's debt. The rating concern downgraded subordinated debt to single-A-minus from single-A, and the Elizabethtown Water Co. unit's senior unsecured debt to single-A from single-A-plus. The rating cuts reflect potentially weaker financial health stemming from an expanded construction program, S&P said.
