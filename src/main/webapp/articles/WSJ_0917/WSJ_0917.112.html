Business Brief -- Bank of Boston Corp.: Unit Sued for $125 Million Over Lending Restrictions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

A subsidiary of Bank of Boston Corp. was sued for $125 million over lending restrictions by Diversified Foods Inc., in U.S. district court. Diversified, a closely held wholesaler in Portland, Maine, had previously filed suit against Bank of Boston's Casco Northern unit in Maine state courts. That suit seeks $150 million in damages for bank actions that allegedly led to Diversified's liquidation last year.

In its latest suit Diversified said that lending restrictions kept it from prepaying a Casco Northern loan and finding new lenders.

Bank of Boston said the suit doesn't have any merit.
