International: West German Producer Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West Germany's August producer prices jumped 0.6% from July and were up 1.9% from a year earlier, the Federal Statistics Office said. Excluding oil prices, producer prices climbed a more modest 0.1% from July and rose 1.5% from August 1989. The data were in line with expectations.

In July, producer prices fell 0.1% from June and increased 1.4% from July 1989.
