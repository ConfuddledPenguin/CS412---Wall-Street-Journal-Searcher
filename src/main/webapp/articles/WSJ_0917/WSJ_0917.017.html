Who's News: Long Island City Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

LONG ISLAND CITY FINANCIAL Corp. (New York) -- Adrian Bradford was named a director of the holding company for Long Island City Savings & Loan Association. Mr. Bradford, 53 years old, succeeds Mario P. Tribuno Jr., 75, who resigned from the seven-member board. Mr. Bradford is currently managing director of Bradford Group, an investment and real estate company here.
