French Steel Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

PARIS -- French companies produced 1,161,000 metric tons of crude steel in August, down 12.5% from a year earlier, according to industry data released Friday. The National Steel Manufacturers' Association said the August result brings French output for the first eight months of the year to 12,792,000 tons, down 3.1% from the like 1989 period.

It didn't elaborate on the reasons behind the latest decline or the market's overall weakness so far this year.
