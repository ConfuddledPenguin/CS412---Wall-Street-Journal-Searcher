CF&I Steel Pension Payment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

PUEBLO, Colo. -- CF&I Steel Corp., a diversified steelmaker, said it wasn't able to make a $12.3 million payment to its pension trust fund as required last Saturday. CF&I said it has been talking for some time with the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corp. and the United Steelworkers of America about the company's unfunded pension liability. CF&I also said it is seeking investors to upgrade its production facilities.
