New UPS Center in California

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- United Parcel Service of America Inc. broke ground for construction of a new package-sorting and delivery center near Ontario Airport in Ontario, Calif. The new air hub, which will serve the West Coast, will employ more than 1,400 people on a 160-acre site. UPS expects the $53 million hub to begin operating in late 1992.
