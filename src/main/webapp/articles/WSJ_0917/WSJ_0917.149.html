Business Brief -- Westinghouse Electric Corp.: Company Will Be Partner In Baltimore Theme Park

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

Westinghouse Electric Corp. said it will become project manager, systems integrator and a limited partner in an Asian theme park and trade complex planned for the Baltimore area. A Westinghouse spokesman said the Pittsburgh company would invest "several million dollars" in the project.

The first phase of the project is estimated to cost about $1 billion, Westinghouse said.

Westinghouse said a unit of its Electronic Systems Group in Baltimore will serve as prime systems manager for the business and entertainment complex of the project, called Worldbridge Centre.

The project is aimed at increasing trade, communication and cultural exchange between the Eastern U.S. and the Asian Pacific region, Westinghouse said.

The project's general partner is Asia/USA Development Corp., of Baltimore.

Westinghouse is a broadcasting, financial services and a electrical parts distribution concern.
