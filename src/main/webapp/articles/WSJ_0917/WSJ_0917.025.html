Who's News: Philip Morris Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

PHILIP MORRIS Co. (New York) -- Effective Oct. 1, four people were named to new posts at the Miller Brewing Co. subsidiary of the tobacco, food and brewing company. Warren H. Dunn, previously senior vice president of administration, was named executive vice president. Mr. Dunn will assume responsibility for the marketing division while retaining his previous responsibilities for the legal, corporate affairs and strategic planning divisions. Allen A. Schumer, senior vice president of operations, was named senior vice president of administration. He is responsible for finance and accounting, information systems and purchasing. Mr. Schumer is succeeded by Billy R. Apple, who had been vice president of plant operations. Virgis W. Colbert, previously vice president of materials manufacturing, succeeds Mr. Apple.
