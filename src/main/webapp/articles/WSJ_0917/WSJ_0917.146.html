Business Brief -- Cubic Corp.: Up to 85 Layoffs Possible At Communications Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

Cubic Corp., a San Diego-based high-technology concern, said its Cubic Communications Inc. unit in nearby Oceanside, Calif., might lay off as many as 85 of its 120 employees in November and December. Cubic said business prospects for its Communications unit product lines "remain strong, but the competitive nature of the defense industry requires that management review every facet of the operations, including space and personnel requirements." Cubic Communications markets freeway call boxes and transmitters and receivers in the U.S. and overseas.

Cubic said it is studying various options, including closing the Communications unit and consolidating operations at company-owned facilities in San Diego. The company is leasing its Oceanside facility.

For the nine months ended June 30, Cubic's profit rose 30% to $19.1 million, or $2.74 a share, from $14.6 million, or $2.01 share, a year earlier. Sales rose 7.2% to $266.9 million from $249 million.
