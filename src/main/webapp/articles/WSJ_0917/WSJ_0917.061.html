Max & Erma's Stock Repurchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Max & Erma's Restaurants Inc. said its board authorized the repurchase from time to time through March 15, of as much as $1 million of its common shares. The stock will be repurchased on the open market or through privately negotiated transactions, the company said.

The company said it considers its stock undervalued. In over-the-counter trading Friday, Max & Erma's rose 12.5 cents to $4.25.

Max & Erma's said it has 3.2 million shares outstanding. Over the past year, the stock has traded as high as $8.50 and as low as $3.875.
