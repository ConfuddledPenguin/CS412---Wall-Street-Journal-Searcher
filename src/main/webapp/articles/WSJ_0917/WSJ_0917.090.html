Martin Marietta Gets $323.4 Million Pact From the Air Force

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Martin Marietta Corp. received a $323.4 million Air Force contract for space launch vehicles.

Hercules Inc. got a $225.8 million Air Force contract for development of the Midgetman missile.

System Control Technology received a $112 million Air Force contract for logistics support.

Lockheed Corp. got a $42.4 million Air Force contract for pilot training and space studies.

General Electric Co. was awarded a $40.1 million Navy contract for anti-submarine warfare systems.

Grumman Corp. received a $25 million Air Force contract for space studies.

Dyncorp Aerospace Operations got a $20.6 million Air Force pact for aircraft maintenance.

Thiokol Corp. got an $11.2 million Army contract for operating an ammunition plant.

Key Airlines received a $10.1 million Air Force contract for air transportation services.
