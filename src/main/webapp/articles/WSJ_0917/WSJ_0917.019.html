Who's News: Kroger Co. Names Pichler as Chairman

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

CINCINNATI -- Kroger Co. Chief Executive Officer Joseph A. Pichler, 49 years old, was elected to the additional post of chairman of the food-store operator. Mr. Pichler, who also had been president and chief operating officer, was named chief executive in June. He succeeds Lyle Everingham, 64, who has announced that he will retire next year. Mr. Everingham, who will continue as a board member, was elected chairman of the executive committee.

Richard L. Bere, 59, formerly a senior vice president, was named president, chief operating officer and a director, expanding the board to 15 members. He was succeeded by Patrick J. Kenney, formerly president of Kroger's Texas operating group of food stores.
