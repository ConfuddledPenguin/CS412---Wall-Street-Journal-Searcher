NS Unit Completes Slab Caster

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

NEWPORT, Ky. -- Newport Steel Corp., a unit of NS Group Inc., said it completed ahead of schedule and on budget a $45 million continuous-slab caster here. The caster and reheat furnace project is the largest capital investment made by Newport.

Newport said it expects to produce about 40% continuous-cast material by December and 100% by June 1991.

NS Group is a holding company that also operates units that make steel bars and tubular steel.
