Sasol Dismisses Strikers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa -- Sasol Ltd., South Africa's oil-from-coal producer, dismissed 2,000 striking workers from its Sigma colliery near Sasolburg. A company spokesman said the strikers had failed to respond to management's return-to-work deadline Thursday afternoon. The dismissed workers were striking over wage demands. The company said the dismissed workers were given one week to reapply for employment. Sasol plans to recruit new workers if this deadline isn't met.
