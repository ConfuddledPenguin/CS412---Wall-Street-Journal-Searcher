Business Brief -- Data General Corp.: Concern Settles Two Suits For a Total of $6 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

Data General Corp. said it settled two shareholder lawsuits, agreeing to pay the plaintiff classes a total of $6 million. The five-year-old suits, Malanka vs. Data General and Lewis vs. de Castro, had charged that Data General executives including Edson de Castro, then president and chief executive officer, had sold stock before announcing negative news about the company.

Neither Data General, a minicomputer maker in Westboro, Mass., nor the executives admitted any liability. Data General said insurance will cover a portion of the settlement.

Data General also said it was sued in U.S. District Court in Boston in another shareholder suit charging the company made misleading statements about its business earlier this year. The company said it believes the suit is without merit.
