Lee Enterprises Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

DAVENPORT, Iowa -- Lee Enterprises Inc. said it completed the acquisition of the 50% of Napp Systems Inc. it didn't own from Nippon Paint (USA) Inc., for $100 million. Lee and Nippon had jointly operated Napp, a San Marcos, Calif., maker of printing plates, for 17 years.
