PLM International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- PLM International Inc., extending a previously reported understanding, said that Transcisco Industries Inc. agreed not to launch a proxy contest at the PLM annual meeting Oct. 17, and that PLM would use its "best efforts" to help Transcisco sell some or all of its 36% PLM stake. The agreement essentially extends a year-old accord between the two companies through next Jan. 10. Transcisco is a railcar-maintenance company that was formerly known as PLM Cos., from which PLM International, a transportation-leasing company, was formed in 1988.
