Peace Chore

"Close military bases" is a thought

That congressmen say is great --

Until someone proposes

To close one in his own state.

-- George O. Ludcke.

Higher Education

Sending our children to college

Is educational, no doubt,

So far we've learned many things

We'll have to do without.

-- Gail Cooke.
