Marketing & Media: Dow Jones & Co. in Thailand

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

NEW YORK -- Dow Jones & Co. said it signed an agreement with Nation Publishing Group of Bangkok, Thailand, to print The Asian Wall Street Journal in Bangkok, its fourth printing location, beginning in mid-1991. The Asian Wall Street Journal, with a circulation of 38,357, also is printed at its headquarters in Hong Kong as well as in Singapore and Tokyo.

Dow Jones owns a minority interest in Nation Publishing Group, which owns The Nation, Thailand's leading English-language newspaper. Nation Publishing is The Asian Wall Street Journal's advertising representative and distributor in Thailand.

Dow Jones also publishes The Wall Street Journal and The Wall Street Journal Europe, Barron's magazine and the Ottaway group of community newspapers. In addition, the company operates electronic business information services and owns Telerate Inc., a provider of computerized financial information.
