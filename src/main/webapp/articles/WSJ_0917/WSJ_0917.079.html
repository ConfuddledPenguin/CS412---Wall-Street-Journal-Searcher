International: French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

PARIS -- French consumer prices rose 0.6% in August from July and were up 3.5% from a year earlier, according to preliminary estimates published Friday by the National Statistics Institute. The 0.6% increase represents a sharp acceleration from July's 0.3% gain. The upswing had been anticipated, however, as a result of the pressure on oil prices following Iraq's invasion of Kuwait.
