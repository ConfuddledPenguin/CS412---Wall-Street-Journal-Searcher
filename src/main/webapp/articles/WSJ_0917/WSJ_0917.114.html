Forum Group to Miss Payment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Forum Group Inc., citing "liquidity demands," said it wouldn't make an interest payment, totaling $2.75 million, on its 6 1/4% convertible subordinated debentures that had been payable Aug. 15. The developer and operator of retirement communities also said it was anticipating a corporate restructuring. For the fiscal first quarter ended June 30, Forum reported a loss of $7 million, or 22 cents a share, on revenue of $28.8 million.

In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Forum fell 12.5 cents to 25 cents.
