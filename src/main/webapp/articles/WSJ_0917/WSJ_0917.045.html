International: Chinese-Japanese Venture Set

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

TOKYO -- C. Itoh & Co., a leading Japanese trading house, said a new joint venture in China will develop computer software for business purposes, such as financial management for Japanese companies facing labor shortages. Itoh officials said the Century Intelligence Software Co. venture was set up in Beijing with an affiliate, Century Research Center Corp., which owns 39%, and China International Trust & Investment Corp., which holds 49%; Itoh owns 12%.

This is believed to be the first joint venture between Chinese and Japanese companies since the Tiananmen Square massacre in June 1989 that triggered protests from other countries, including Japan.
