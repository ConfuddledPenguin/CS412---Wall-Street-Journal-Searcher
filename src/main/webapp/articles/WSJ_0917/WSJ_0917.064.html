Letters to the Editor: Sharpton, Maddox Mason: Heroes All

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

Your Aug. 20 editorial "The Black Man's Burden" should more appropriately have been titled "A Thorn in the White Man's Side." The Rev. Al Sharpton, in my opinion, acts and speaks to the frustrations, injustices and double standards imposed on all African-Americans in their daily pursuit of liberty, equality and happiness.

What is puzzling is that the only black person you could find to "trash" Mr. Sharpton is a politician from Maryland who obviously is trying to ingratiate himself with the white community.

We African-Americans need more giants like attorneys Alton Maddox and C. Vernon Mason. Your journalistic garbage condemning them won't change that. A new day is finally on the horizon.

Charles L. Hairston

New Rochelle, N.Y.
