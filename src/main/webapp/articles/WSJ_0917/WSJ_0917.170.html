Technology: Upjohn Reaches Marketing Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/17/90

KALAMAZOO, Mich. -- Upjohn Co. said it agreed to co-promote its dermatology products with Owen/Galderma Laboratories Inc., a marketer of dermatological products. An Upjohn official said Owen/Galderma will contribute 57 sales representatives to expand the pharmaceutical concern's sales force in promoting Rogaine Topical Solution male-pattern baldness drug and Cleocin T acne product line to dermatologists in the U.S.

"We feel this focus on dermatologists will help with the continued growth of Celocin T and Rogaine," Upjohn said. The agreement is effective Jan. 1.
