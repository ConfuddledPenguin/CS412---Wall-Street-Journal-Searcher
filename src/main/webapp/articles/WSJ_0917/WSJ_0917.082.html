International: Japanese Wholesale Prices Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/17/90

TOKYO -- Japan's wholesale prices in August increased 1.1% from a year earlier and edged up 0.3% from the previous month, the Bank of Japan said. The wholesale price index stood at 90.7 (1985 equals 100).

Central bank officials said rising domestic and import prices pushed up overall price levels in the latest reporting month.
