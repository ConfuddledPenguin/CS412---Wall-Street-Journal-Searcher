Who's News: General Housewares Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

GENERAL HOUSEWARES Corp. (Stamford, Conn.) -- Robert L. Gray was named vice president-finance, treasurer and chief financial officer, succeeding Philip H. Dallow, who is leaving the maker of cookware and cutlery products. Mr. Gray had been treasurer of Helene Curtis, a Chicago-based cosmetics company.
