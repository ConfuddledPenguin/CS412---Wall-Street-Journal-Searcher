Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

MAXWELL: Media executive Robert Maxwell signed an agreement with West Germany's Gruner & Jahr AG to acquire Berliner Verlag, East Germany's largest newspaper and magazine publisher. Mr. Maxwell's Mirror Group Newspapers would operate the publisher in a joint venture with Gruner & Jahr, a unit of Bertlesmann AG. Terms weren't disclosed.

MAGAZINES: McGraw-Hill Inc. acquired Southwest Contractor magazine from Akers-Runbeck Publishing Co., Phoenix, Ariz., and Louisiana Contractor magazine from Rhodes Publishing Co., Baton Rouge, La. The prices weren't disclosed.

WHO'S NEWS: Advance Publications named Simon Master executive vice president, international, of its Random House Inc. subsidiary. He remains deputy chairman of Random Century Group in London while assuming responsibility for all international operations. . . . Sony Corp.'s Columbia Pictures Entertainment Co. subsidiary named Steven Mendelson senior vice president of comedy development at Columbia Pictures Television. He was vice president of comedy development.
