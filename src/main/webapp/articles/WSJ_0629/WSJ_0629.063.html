Japan's Steel Exports Decline

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

TOKYO -- Japan's May steel exports dropped 22% from a year earlier as producers continued to direct their efforts at the robust domestic market, the Japan Iron and Steel Federation said. Shipments in May slipped to 1,344,000 metric tons, with declines in most markets. Exports were down 1.6% from April. A metric ton is 2,204.6 pounds.
