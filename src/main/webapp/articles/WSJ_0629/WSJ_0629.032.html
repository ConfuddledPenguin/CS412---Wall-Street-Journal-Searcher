M/I Schottenstein Homes Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- M/I Schottenstein Homes Inc. said shareholders approved the previously announced buy-out by a family trust for $7.50 a share. The residential developer said Irving and Melvin Schottenstein and their family trusts, which own 80% of the company, will acquire the remaining 20%, or 1,055,000 shares.

The value of the share-purchase is about $7.9 million. The company, which has acted as a financial adviser to the Schottensteins, will provide about $2.3 million, and the Schottenstein families will fund the remaining $5.6 million, the company said.
