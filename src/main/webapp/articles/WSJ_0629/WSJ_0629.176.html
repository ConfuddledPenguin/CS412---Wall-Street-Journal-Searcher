Business Brief -- NS Group Inc.: Steelmaker Plans to Acquire Two Pennsylvania Properties

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

NS Group Inc. said it entered a into a letter of intent with Ambridge Tube Corp., Houston, to acquire the Koppel Steel Works and Ambridge Rolling Mills facilities. Terms weren't disclosed. The Newport, Ky., steelmaker said the facilities were formerly operated by the Tubular Products Division of Babcock & Wilcox, an operating unit of McDermott International Inc. In January, Babcock & Wilcox entered into a definitive agreement to sell the facilities to Ambridge Tube.

The Koppel facility, located in Koppel, Pa., contains a melt shop installed in 1985 and has the capacity to produce 400,000 tons of steel products annually. The Ambridge facility, located in nearby Ambridge, Pa., contains a tube-making facility installed in 1976 designed to make specialty tubing including bearing, mechanical, pressure and oil country tubing. Both facilities were idled in 1988.

The acquisition would allow NS to expand its tubular and bar markets.
