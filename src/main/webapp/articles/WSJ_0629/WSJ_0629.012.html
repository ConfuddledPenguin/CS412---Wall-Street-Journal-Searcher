Conseco Firm Completes Deal

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

CARMEL, Ind. -- Conseco Inc. said Conseco Capital Partners L.P., a company it created with institutional investors, has completed the purchase of Temple-Inland Inc.'s Great American Reserve Insurance Co. for $143.5 million. Conseco, an insurance holding company, said GE Capital, a unit of General Electric Co., provided $98 million in debt financing for the purchase. GE Capital also is a limited partner in Conseco Partners, which provided $7.5 million for the transaction.

Temple-Inland, a Diboll, Texas, forest products and financial services concern, said the sale allows it to concentrate its financial services business on mortgage-loan servicing operations and Texas thrifts. Temple-Inland said it won't record a significant gain on the sale, contrary to earlier expectations.
