Sudbury Inc. to Post Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

CLEVELAND -- Sudbury Inc., maker of industrial products, said it expects to report fiscal 1990 net income of about $2 million, including profit of $10.3 million from a joint venture. Sudbury, whose year ended May 31, posted a net loss of $15.6 million in 1989, including a loss of $4.7 million from continuing operations, on sales of $510.5 million.
