World Bank Clears Two Loans to India

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

WASHINGTON -- The World Bank approved two loans totaling $583 million to India to help finance electric power facilities. The bank also cleared $275 million in loans to Nigeria to upgrade the country's telecommunications network and help finance urban development projects.

The International Development Association, a bank affiliate, approved a $50 million credit to Mauritania to restructure some public enterprises and close down others.
