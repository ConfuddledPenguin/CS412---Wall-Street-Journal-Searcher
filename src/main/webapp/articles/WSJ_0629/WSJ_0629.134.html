Oshkosh Truck Corp. Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

WASHINGTON -- Oshkosh Truck Corp. was given a $70 million Army contract for 363 tactical trucks.

FMC Corp. won a $22.5 million Navy contract for missile-launching systems.

Varian Associates Inc. got a $15 million Army contract for aviator night-vision goggles.
