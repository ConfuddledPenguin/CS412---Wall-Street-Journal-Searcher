Money Fund Assets Fell $444.9 Million In the Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

NEW YORK -- Assets of the nation's 513 money market funds fell $444.9 million to $384.99 billion in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. While assets of 250 general purpose funds rose $27.7 million to $133.99 billion, assets of 102 broker-dealer funds declined $356.4 million to $159.17 billion. Assets of 161 institutional funds dropped $116.2 million to $91.83 billion.

The number of money market funds was revised from 512 the previous week to include one new general purpose fund with assets of $112.5 million. For the week ended June 20, money market fund assets were revised to $385.43 billion and the reported increase of $1.29 billion in the week was revised to $1.32 billion.
