Who's News: Met-Coil Systems Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

MET-COIL SYSTEMS Inc. (Cedar Rapids, Iowa) -- Martin J. Huebschman, 43-year-old president and chief executive officer of this maker of metal-working machinery and automated equipment, resigned to pursue other interests. Until the board names a successor, his responsibilities will be assumed by Chairman Raymond H. Blakeman, 66.
