Business Brief -- JSB Financial Inc.: New York Concern Becomes Federal Stock Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

JSB Financial Inc., the holding company for Jamaica Savings Bank, said it converted to a federally chartered stock savings bank from a federally chartered mutual savings bank. The New York bank raised $160 million, offering shares of common to employees, eligible account holders, the general public and others at $10 a share.

JSB Financial's common stock, which trades on the NASDAQ National Market System under the symbol JSBF, closed yesterday at $12.50, down 25 cents.

Jamaica Savings Bank had assets of $1.46 billion, deposits of $1.23 billion and retained earnings of $197.1 million at Dec. 31, 1989.
