Who's News: Fifth Third Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

FIFTH THIRD BANCORP (Cincinnati) -- P. Michael Brumm, senior vice president and trust officer, was named chief financial officer of this bank holding company, succeeding N. Beverley Tucker Jr., who quit to do agricultural consulting. Robert J. King, executive vice president, was named president and chief operating officer of the Fifth Third Bank of Toledo; as president, he succeeds Charles L. McKelvy Jr., who remains chief executive officer and was named to the added, new post of chairman of the Toledo bank.
