Business Brief -- Security Pacific Corp.: Seattle Unit Will Assume Certain Gibraltar Deposits

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

Security Pacific Bancorp. Northwest, Seattle, said it will assume the core deposits and certain assets of Gibraltar Savings Bank of Washington from the Resolution Trust Corp. The transaction gives the Security Pacific Corp. unit about $228 million in Gibraltar's core deposits plus Gibraltar's six branches in King County, Wash. For 15 months, Gibraltar of Washington has been held in receivership by the RTC, the federal agency that takes custody of ailing thrifts. Full details of the consolidation haven't been determined.

The action follows Wednesday's parallel announcement by the bank and thrift's larger affiliates. Security Pacific National Bank of Los Angeles, a unit of Los Angeles-based Security Pacific Corp., was the successful bidder for Gibraltar Savings of Beverly Hills, a unit of Gibraltar Financial Corp., assuming approximately $3.9 billion in core deposits of the thrift and $2.7 billion of its residential loans.
