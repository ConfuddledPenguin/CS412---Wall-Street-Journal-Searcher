Hamilton Oil Corp. Discovery

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

DENVER -- Hamilton Oil Corp. said it discovered oil in a horizontal well drilled in the U.K. sector of the North Sea. The company said this is the first horizontal well it has drilled in the Crawford field in the North Sea. The well flowed at a rate of 6,900 barrels of oil a day through a 72/64 inch choke.

Hamilton Oil Corp. is the operator, with a 36.33% interest. Its partners are BP Petroleum Development Ltd., with a 30.33% interest; Elf Oil & Gas Ltd., with a 23.33% interest; Ultramar Exploration Ltd., with an 8.34% interest, and Monument Resources Ltd., with a 1.67% interest.
