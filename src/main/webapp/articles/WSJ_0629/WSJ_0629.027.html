Who's News: Crown Central Petroleum Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

CROWN CENTRAL PETROLEUM Corp. (Baltimore) -- Donald R. Yearwood, 50 years old, senior vice president of American Trading & Production Corp., was elected a director of this refiner and marketer of petroleum products, succeeding J. Warren Weiss, 66, who retired as a senior officer of American Trading.
