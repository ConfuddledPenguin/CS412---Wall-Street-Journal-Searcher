Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 287,537 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, down 1% from 290,369 tons in the preceding week but up 11% from 259,687 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is 2,204.6 pounds. So far in 1990, ingot output is 6,986,435 metric tons, down 9.1% from 7,689,996 tons a year earlier.
