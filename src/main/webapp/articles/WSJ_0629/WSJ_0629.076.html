Who's News: Hollywood Park Realty Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

HOLLYWOOD PARK REALTY ENTERPRISES Inc. (Inglewood, Calif.) -- Vernon O. Underwood Jr., president and chief executive officer of Young's Market Co., was elected a director, increasing board membership to eight. Mr. Underwood is the son of the late Vernon O. Underwood Sr., former chairman and chief executive of Hollywood Park Operating Co. Hollywood Park Realty is a real estate investment trust that leases racetrack operations to Hollywood Park Operating. The stocks of both companies are paired for trading.
