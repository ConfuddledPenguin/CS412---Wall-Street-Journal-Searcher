Business Brief -- First Federal Bancorp: Michigan Bank Says Group Withdrew Offer of Funds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

First Federal Bancorp, Pontiac, Mich., said the investor group that recently offered to infuse the bank with $40 million has withdrawn its offer. According to First Federal, the investor group said its decision was based on conversations with its adviser regarding the ability to attain the group's objectives in a non-assisted transaction. First Federal announced the offer last month, when it identified the group only as current shareholders.

At that time First Federal also disclosed that the Office of Thrift Supervision had limited the operations of two of its subsidiaries, First Federal Savings Bank & Trust, and Citizens Federal Savings Bank. The federal regulators took that action because the subsidiaries failed to meet new federal capital requirements.

Leo D. Ottoni, First Federal chief executive officer, said the bank holding company will continue its attempts to comply with the tougher capital requirements.
