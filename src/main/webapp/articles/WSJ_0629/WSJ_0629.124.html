Who's News: International Consumer Brands Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

INTERNATIONAL CONSUMER BRANDS Inc. (Trumbull, Conn.) -- Robert P. Freeman, who held the posts of president and chief operating officer for seven months, has left the company. Bruce A. Hahn, chairman and chief executive officer, who was succeeded as president by Mr. Freeman, said Mr. Freeman's departure "results from a combination of streamlining and cost-cutting modes the company has adopted along with Mr. Freeman's desire to pursue other interests." The company said Mr. Freeman's duties will be assumed by Mr. Hahn and by the chief financial officer. Mr. Freeman was unavailable for comment.
