Citicorp Sets Expansion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

NEW YORK -- Citicorp said it will open offices in Czechoslovakia and Poland in response to the countries' movement toward market-based economies. The offices, which are expected to open next week in Prague and Warsaw, will help corporate clients of its Citibank unit identify local partners and finance Central European joint ventures. The offices also will help Czechoslovak and Polish companies participating in government-sponsored privatization programs find foreign partners.

Citibank entered the Central European market in 1986 in a joint venture with National Bank of Hungary in Budapest.
