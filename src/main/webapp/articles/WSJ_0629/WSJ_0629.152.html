Who's News: International Business Machines Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS MACHINES Corp. (Armonk, N.Y.) -- Lucie J. Fjeldstad and Ralph W. Clark were appointed presidents of two new divisions. Ms. Fjeldstad, a corporate vice president, will head the Multimedia and Education division, which will develop and market multimedia and educational products. Mr. Clark, also a corporate vice president, will run the General and Public Sector division, which will develop and market products and services for the transportation, public sector, health, utilities, communications, media and business services industries. Separately, William W.K. Rich and Nobuo Mii were elected vice presidents, and John E. Hickey was elected corporate secretary. Mr. Rich also was named president of the Services Sector division. He succeeds Marvin L. Mann, who will be on special assignment.
