Chariot Group Seeks Delisting

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

NEW YORK -- Chariot Group Inc. said it asked the Securities and Exchange Commission to delist its common shares from the American Stock Exchange because of limited activity. The company, a producer of aluminum windows and doors, said the exchange advised it that the last day the stock will be traded is July 13. Chariot is also the largest shareholder in Synalloy Corp., a South Carolina stainless-tube and specialty-chemical maker.

Chariot shares are listed at an asking price of $1.75.
