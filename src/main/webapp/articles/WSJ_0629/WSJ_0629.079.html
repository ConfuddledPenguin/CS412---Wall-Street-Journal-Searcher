Who's News: Minorco

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

MINORCO (USA) -- Robert L. Zerga was named chairman and chief executive officer of Independence Mining Co., a Reno, Nev., gold-mining unit. He was also named a director of Denver-based Minorco (USA). Mr. Zerga was executive vice president of Meridian Minerals Co., a unit of Burlington Resources Inc. He was previously with Newmont Gold Co., Magma Copper Co. and Kennecott Copper Corp.
