  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

U.S. TRUST Corp. (New York) -- Maureen Scannell Bateman, 46 years old, previously vice president and counsel at the Bankers Trust Co. unit of Bankers Trust New York Corp., was named senior vice president and general counsel at this private banking, asset-management and securities-services concern, succeeding Briscoe R. Smith, 52, who is returning to private law practice.
