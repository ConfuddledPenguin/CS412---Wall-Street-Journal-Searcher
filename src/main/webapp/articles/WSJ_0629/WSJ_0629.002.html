Who's News: Engelhard Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

ENGELHARD Corp. (Edison, N.J.) -- L. Donald LaTorre, 52 years old, previously president of the pigments and additives division of this specialty chemical maker, was elected senior vice president and chief operating officer, succeeding Cyrus H. Holley, who will remain an executive vice president and a director until his scheduled retirement next year at age 55.
