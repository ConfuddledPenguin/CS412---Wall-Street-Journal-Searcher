Who's News: Old Dominion Electric Cooperative

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

OLD DOMINION ELECTRIC Cooperative (Glen Allen, Va.) -- John P. Edwards, senior vice president for customer affairs at Public Service Co. of New Hampshire, was named president and chief executive officer of this electric cooperative, effective Aug. 20. He succeeds Guy C. Lewis Jr., who has been serving as acting president and chief executive since last May. Mr. Edwards was also named president and chief executive of the Virginia, Maryland and Delaware Association of Electric Cooperatives.
