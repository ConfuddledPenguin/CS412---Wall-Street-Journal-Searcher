Chemfix to Redeem Warrants

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

METAIRIE, La. -- Chemfix Technologies Inc. said it will redeem on Aug. 6 common stock purchase warrants for 10 cents a warrant. Until that date, holders may exercise the warrants at a price of $6.50 a share. There are 488,750 outstanding of these warrants, which were part of a unit offering in 1983. The environmental services concern said it has about 7.2 million common shares outstanding.
