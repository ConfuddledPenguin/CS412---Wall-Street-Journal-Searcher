Business Brief -- Morrison Knudsen Corp.: GM Gives Firm Contract For Renovation of Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

Morrison Knudsen Corp., Boise, Idaho, said it was chosen to manage construction of a $219 million plant renovation for General Motors Corp. The auto maker's truck and bus group awarded the contract to the construction and engineering company to manage a retrofitting and retooling of GM's Shreveport, La., assembly plant. A new model of light pickup truck is to be produced there for the 1993 model year.

Production will involve three assembly complexes and two metal fabricating plants. Preconstruction services are set to begin immediately in Shreveport, with the bulk of the construction and tooling installation taking place over the next two years, Morrison Knudsen said.
