Heck's Inc. Unit's Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

NITRO, W. Va. -- Heck's Inc. said shareholders approved the previously announced sale of its retail division to Retail Acquisition Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. The sale is expected to close in July, leaving the company with its Steel City Products division, a wholesale distributor of automotive parts and accessories based in Pittsburgh. Shareholders also approved resolutions to change the company's name to Hallwood Industries Inc. and its state of incorporation to Delaware from West Virginia.
