Justice Agency Asks Dennison and Avery For Data on Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- Dennison Manufacturing Co. said it and Avery International Corp. received requests from the Antitrust Division of the Justice Department for additional information on their merger. Pasadena, Calif.-based Avery and Dennison, based here, agreed last month to merge in a stock swap with an indicated value of $456 million. The merger would create one of the largest office-products concerns in the U.S. with combined annual sales of about $2.5 billion.

The companies said the requests from the department were anticipated and will extend the waiting period before the merger can close.

They said they will respond promptly to the requests and expect to complete the merger in September.
