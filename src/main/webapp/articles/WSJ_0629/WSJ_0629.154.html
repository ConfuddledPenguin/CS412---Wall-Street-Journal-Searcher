Business Brief -- Holderbank Financiere Glaris Ltd.: Holnam of U.S. to Purchase Unit of Texas Industries

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

Holnam Inc., a Dundee, Mich.-based unit of Holderbank Financiere Glaris Ltd. of Switzerland, said it preliminarily agreed to purchase the United Cement Co. subsidiary of Texas Industries Inc., Dallas, for $61 million. United Cement consists of one cement plant in Mississippi -- the only cement plant in the state -- and seven distribution centers. Holnam, which makes cement and other related products, said it expects to complete the purchase later this summer. Holnam is the former Dundee Cement Co., which merged with Ideal Basic Industries in March, boosting its annual sales to $1 billion.
