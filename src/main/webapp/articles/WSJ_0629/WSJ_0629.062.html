Who's News: Exxon Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

EXXON Corp. (New York) -- In a reorganization of its corporate and public affairs department, this energy company named three executives: Elliot R. Cattarulla, vice president of public affairs, effective Aug. 1; T. Peter Townsend, vice president for investor relations, effective July 1, and David L. Baird, corporate secretary, effective Aug. 1.
