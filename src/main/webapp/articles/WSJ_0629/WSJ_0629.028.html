Safety-Kleen Building Facility

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

ELGIN, Ill. -- Safety-Kleen Corp. said it is building a $40 million oil re-refinery in East Chicago, Ind., which will process 75 million gallons of used automotive and industrial oil annually. The maker of parts-cleaner services and solvent recycling said the plant, to be completed by May 1991, will produce 43 million gallons of high-quality base lubricating oil, as well as other petroleum products.

Saftely-Kleen said the re-refinery is twice the size of the largest facility now in operation, its Breslau, Ontario, plant.
