Who's News: Kemper Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

KEMPER Corp. (Long Grove, Ill.) -- Jay B. Walters, corporate finance director at the Blunt Ellis & Loewi Inc. unit of this insurance and investment services concern, was named chairman and chief executive officer of the unit, a Milwaukee-based regional brokerage firm, succeeding Thomas S. Franke, who resigned in May to pursue other interests.
