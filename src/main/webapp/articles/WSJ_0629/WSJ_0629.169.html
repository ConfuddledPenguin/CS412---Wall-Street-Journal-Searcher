Wichita River Oil Study

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

DENVER -- Wichita River Oil Corp. said it appointed a special committee of its board to consider ways to maximize shareholder value. The oil production company said the committee, which is composed of outside directors, hopes to finish the study "within a matter of weeks."

In composite trading on the American Stock Exchange yesterday the company's shares closed at $4.25, up 37.5 cents.
