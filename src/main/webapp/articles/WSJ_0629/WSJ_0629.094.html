Financing Business: Maclean Hunter Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

MACLEAN HUNTER Ltd., Toronto, said it agreed to sell 150 million Canadian dollars (US$128.3 million) of 12.2% debentures, maturing in 1995, to a group of underwriters comprising Wood Gundy Inc., Toronto-Dominion Securities Inc. and Burns Fry Ltd. Maclean Hunter, a publishing and broadcasting company, said proceeds will be used to repay short-term debt from last year's acquisition of Selkirk Communications Ltd., a television and radio broadcaster based in Toronto. Maclean Hunter said it expects to file a preliminary short-form prospectus for the offering with securities regulators shortly.
