Newmont Mining Holders Vote

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

DENVER -- Newmont Mining Corp. said holders approved the sale of the company's 55% stake in Peabody Holding Co. to Hanson PLC for $725.6 million. Newmont, a gold mining concern, said the sale was approved by holders of more than 80% of the common stock outstanding. Hanson, which has interests in tobacco, specialty chemicals, building products and mining, currently owns 49% of Newmont Mining and 45% of Peabody, a large U.S. coal producer.
