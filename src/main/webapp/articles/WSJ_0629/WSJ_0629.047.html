Federal Express Volume Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

MEMPHIS, Tenn. -- Federal Express Corp. said average daily volume of packages and documents increased 11.5% in May, before a one-time change in reporting procedures, to 1.3 million units. Total monthly volume, before the reporting change, increased 11.5% to 28.4 million units. With the reporting change, which includes the company's non-U.S. subsidiaries' volumes for April and May, instead of just April as in the year-ago volume report, Federal Express's average daily volume increased 11.9%.
