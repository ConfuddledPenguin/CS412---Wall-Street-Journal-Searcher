Thyssen Plant Modernization

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

DUESSELDORF, West Germany Thyssen AG said its steel divisions would invest up to $15 million to modernize its Thyssen Steel Detroit plant, which serves the automotive industry. Thyssen said the modernization, slated for a 1991 completion, will add slitting, blanking and cut-to-length capabilities.
