France's Inflation Rate Slows

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

PARIS -- France's consumer price index in May rose 0.2% from April and 3% from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute said, confirming preliminary results. The latest data put the index -- the year 1980 is equal to 100 -- at 182.8, up from 182.4 in April and 177.5 in May 1990. The May result represents a slowdown from April, when price growth was 0.4% on a monthly basis and 3.2% on a year-to-year basis.

Much of the May improvement was recorded in the service sector, where monthly price growth eased to 0.2% from April's 0.8%. The April pace reflected the regular inclusion of housing and rent costs on the index for the first month of every calendar quarter.
