Who's News: Varity Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/29/90

VARITY Corp. (Toronto) -- Neil Arnold was appointed chief financial officer of this industrial equipment manufacturer. Mr. Arnold, 41 years old, succeeds James Vance, 60, who is retiring, the company said. Mr. Arnold, senior vice president and chief administrative officer of Varity, was formerly the company's treasurer.
