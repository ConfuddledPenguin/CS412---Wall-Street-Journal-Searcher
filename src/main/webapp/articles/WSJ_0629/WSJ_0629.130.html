Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/29/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Failure Group Inc., initial offering of 2.3 million common shares, of which 1.5 million will be sold by the company and 800,000 by holders, via Prudential-Bache Capital Funding and Hambrecht & Quist Inc.

Gerrity Oil & Gas Corp., proposed offering of two million shares of common stock, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Boettcher & Co.

KfW International Inc., a subsidiary of Kreditanstalt fur Wiederaufbau, shelf offering of up to $600 million of debt securities.

The Latin America Investment Fund Inc., initial public offering of a minimum of five million shares (or $75 million), via Salomon Brothers Inc., Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Medical Imaging Centers of America Inc., offering of 2.45 million shares of common stock, of which 1,961,429 shares are being sold by the company and 488,571 by holders, via Smith Barney.

Mr. Coffee Inc., offering of 762,577 common shares.

Murex Clinical Technologies Corp., proposed offering of up to C$24 million, or about US$20,474,000, of units, each consisting of one common share and one common share purchase warrant, with two warrants entitling the holder to acquire one common share, via Deacon Barclays de Zoete Wedd Ltd.

Nichols Institute, offering of 1,767,032 common shares, of which 850,000 are being offered by the company and 917,032 shares by holders, via Kidder, Peabody & Co. and William Blair & Co.

Texas Utilities Electric Co., a unit of Texas Utilities Co., shelf offering of $400 million of first mortgage and collateral trust bonds.

T2 Medical Inc., proposed offering of 2.2 million common shares, of which 600,000 will be sold by the company and the balance by holders, via Kidder Peabody and Robinson-Humphrey Co.
