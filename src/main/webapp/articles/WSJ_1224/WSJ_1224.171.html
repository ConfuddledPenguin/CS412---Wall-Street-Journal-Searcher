ITT Gets Army Job Totaling $193.1 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

WASHINGTON -- ITT Corp. received a $193.1 million Army contract for radios.

Environmental & Safety Designs Inc. -- Allen & Hoshall, a joint venture, got a $100 million Navy contract for base pollution cleanup.

American Telephone & Telegraph Co. received an $83.5 million Navy contract for oceanographic services.

United Technologies Corp. got $63.8 million in contracts for Air Force jet engines and Army helicopters.

Sequa Corp. said its Atlantic Research unit won a roughly $60 million contract for work on the U.S. Army's environmental test facility at Fort Huachuca, Ariz.

Boeing Co. was awarded a $58.5 million Air Force contract for aircraft support.

General Dynamics Corp. received $51.9 million in contracts for Air Force aircraft software and Navy submarine services.

Unisys Corp. got a $49.5 million Navy contract for computer sets.

International Business Machines Corp. received a $42.5 million contract for Navy helicopter electronics and Air Force space system support.

General Electric Co. got a $37.5 million Air Force contract for aircraft engines.

Olin Corp. received a $27.2 million Army contract for 120-mm cartridges.

Oshkosh Truck Corp. got a $24.7 million Army contract for tactical vehicles.

Engineered Air Systems Inc. received a $23.6 million Air Force contract for aircraft air conditioners.

Raytheon Co. got a $21.9 million Navy contract for magnetic silencing equipment.

Contel Federal Systems, a unit of Contel Corp., received a $15.1 million Air Force contract for space communications equipment.

BDM International Corp. was awarded a $12.9 million Army contract for air defense equipment.
