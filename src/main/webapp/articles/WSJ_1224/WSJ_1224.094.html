Health Care REIT Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

TOLEDO, Ohio -- Health Care REIT Inc. said it leased its 120-bed nursing home in Camp Verde, Ariz., previously classified as other real estate owned, to Arbors Health Care Center Inc. for a 10-year period. Arbors is a subsidiary of Signature Health Care Corp.

The real estate concern said the carrying value of the property at Dec. 1 was approximately $3 million.
