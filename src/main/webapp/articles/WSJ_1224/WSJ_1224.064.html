GM to Open Office in Moscow

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

MOSCOW -- General Motors Corp. will open permanent offices in Moscow and the Russian auto manufacturing city of Togliatti, the Tass news agency reported. In June, General Motors signed a five-year, $1 billion deal with the Volga Auto Works to supply 100,000 to 150,000 engine control systems a year. The agreement with the auto works, located in Togliatti, is by far the largest deal ever signed by a U.S. auto maker with the Soviet Union.

The Volga Auto Works builds Zhiguli and Lada compact cars, as well as the Niva, a Soviet Jeep.
