Home & City Savings Bank Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

ALBANY, N.Y. -- Home & City Savings Bank said it agreed to be acquired by Rose Associates for cash and notes valued at $28 a share, or about $81.2 million. Under terms, the limited partnership will pay $24 in cash plus $4 of 9% debentures for each of Home & City's 2.9 million shares. The partnership currently holds 8.8% of Home & City.
