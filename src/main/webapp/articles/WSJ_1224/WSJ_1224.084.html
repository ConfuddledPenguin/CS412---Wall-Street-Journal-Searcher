Publicker Industries Inc. Is Sued

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

OLD GREENWICH, Conn. -- Publicker Industries Inc. said the U.S. government, at the request of the Environmental Protection Agency, filed suit against the company and two other undisclosed parties in federal court in Philadelphia. The suit relates to cleanup costs at a former Publicker storage facility in Philadelphia where the EPA has allegedly spent more than $22 million under the federal Superfund Act.

A spokesman for Publicker said the extent of the company's liability wasn't known. But the company has said that if it is determined to be liable for any "significant portion" of the amounts sought by the U.S., it could have a "material adverse effect" on the financial condition of the alcohol and chemical company.
