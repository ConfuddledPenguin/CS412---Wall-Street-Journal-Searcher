Who's News: Industrial Bank of Japan Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

INDUSTRIAL BANK OF JAPAN Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Daniel J. Callahan Jr. was named a senior vice president at Aubrey G. Lanston & Co., a New York-based U.S. government securities dealer, and a unit of this bank. Mr Callahan, 39 years old, was previously a senior vice president at Deutschebank's U.S. government securities unit.
