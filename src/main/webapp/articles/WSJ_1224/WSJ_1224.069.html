James River Plans Facility

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- James River Corp. said directors approved construction of a new headquarters and technology center near Cincinnati for its $1.5 billion-a-year food and consumer packaging business. The facility will consolidate in a single location facilities now in San Leandro, Calif.; Neenah, Wis.; Kalamazoo, Mich., and Cincinnati.

Cost of the project wasn't disclosed.
