Marketing & Media: K-III Holdings to Buy Two Field Corp. Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

NEW YORK -- K-III Holdings L.P. said it definitively agreed with closely held Field Corp. to buy two educational publishing subsidiaries, the children's book publishers Field Publications and the encyclopedia publisher Funk & Wagnalls. Terms of the transaction, which is expected to close in early 1991, weren't disclosed. But investment bankers previously estimated the purchase price will be less than the $300 million a year in sales for the two businesses combined.

K-III Holdings, a partnership of former Macmillan Inc. executives and Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co., a New York leveraged buy-out firm, specializes in publishing and information companies. Recently, it has acquired special-interest publishers and book clubs.
