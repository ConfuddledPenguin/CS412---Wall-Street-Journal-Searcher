Action Industries' Outlook

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

PITTSBURGH -- Action Industries Inc., a marketer of houseware and giftware promotional programs, said it expects a "material decline" in second-quarter sales, citing order cancellations reflecting the general economic downturn, but predicted it will be profitable for the period. A year earlier, Action earned $3.4 million, or 62 cents a share, on sales of $57.6 million.
