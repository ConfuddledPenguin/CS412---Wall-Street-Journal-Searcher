Hecla, Alta End Discussions

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

COEUR D'ALENE, Idaho -- Hecla Mining Co. said it halted discussions about a possible business combination with Alta Gold Co. of Salt Lake City. The mining concerns agreed in October to consider a combination, such as a merger or a joint venture, of their businesses, Hecla said, but decided not to proceed with such a deal.
