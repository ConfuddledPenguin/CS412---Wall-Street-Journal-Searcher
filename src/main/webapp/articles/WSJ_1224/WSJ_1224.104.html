Trans Financial to Merge Banks

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

BOWLING GREEN, Ky. -- Trans Financial Bancorp, a bank holding company, said its two commercial banks, Citizens National Bank in Bowling Green and Citizens Bank & Trust Co. in Glasgow and Cave City, will merge and change their names to Trans Financial Bank, effective next Monday. The company said the merger will provide greater efficiencies in automation, marketing, check processing and branch operations.
