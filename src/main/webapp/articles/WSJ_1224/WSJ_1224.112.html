Business Brief -- BancTexas Group Inc.: Firm Says Investor Group To Buy Newly Issued Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

BancTexas Group Inc. said an investor group headed by Billy B. Goldberg of Houston agreed to purchase between 16 million and 20 million newly issued shares for 25 cents each, or $4 million to $5 million. The Dallas bank holding company said the proposed transaction, which remains subject to due diligence and regulatory and shareholder approval, would leave the group with a 45% to 55% stake, allowing it to nominate a majority to the board.

BancTexas, which owns banks in Houston and McKinney, Texas, said the investor group also plans to ask the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. to permanently waive any claims against BancTexas arising from the recent failure of the company's Dallas bank. BancTexas said it and the investor group are also attempting to arrange the redemption of the FDIC's warrants to buy about 12% of the company's stock.
