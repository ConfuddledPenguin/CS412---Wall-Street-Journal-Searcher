Business Brief -- Ameritech: Company Reaches Accord To Acquire Knowledge Data

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

Ameritech said it reached a definitive agreement to acquire Knowledge Data Systems Inc. for $26 million, or 94 cents a share. Under the agreement, Chicago-based Ameritech will start a cash tender offer before next Monday. Knowledge Data and certain shareholders granted Ameritech an option to buy up to 40% of the shares outstanding on a fully diluted basis at the same price.

Knowledge Data, based in San Francisco, provides data processing systems and related services to health-care institutions and medical laboratories.

After the tender offer is completed, which is expected by the end of January, Knowledge Data will operate as a unit of Ameritech Information Systems.
