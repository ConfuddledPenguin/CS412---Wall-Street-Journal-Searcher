Who's News: Newmont Mining Corp. and Newmont Gold Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

NEWMONT MINING Corp. and NEWMONT GOLD Co. (both Denver) -- Pierre Haas, 70, chairman of R.E.L. Partners N.V., a Netherlands firm, was named a director of these mining companies, succeeding David H. Clarke, 48, who had represented Hanson Industries, a unit of Hanson PLC, on the board. Hanson previously held a 49% stake in Newmont.
