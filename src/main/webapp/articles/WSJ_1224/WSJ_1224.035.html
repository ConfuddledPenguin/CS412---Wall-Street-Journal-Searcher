Financing Business: Fag Kugelfischer Georg Schaefer KGaA

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

FAG KUGELFISCHER GEORG SCHAEFER KGaA's FAG Finance Corp. unit, Stamford, Conn., said it completed its $131 million tender offer for Barden Corp., Danbury, Conn., having purchased or obtained purchase rights to all of the company's common shares. Barden was the last independent U.S. maker of precision ball bearings, with 20% of the market. FAG Kugelfischer of West Germany makes bearings and industrial systems.
