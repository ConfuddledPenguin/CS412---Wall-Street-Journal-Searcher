Technology: Texas Instruments, Samsung

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

DALLAS -- Texas Instruments Inc. said it reached a five-year licensing agreement with Samsung Electronics Co. of South Korea, settling one of several patent issues with foreign chip makers. The company didn't disclose the amount of royalties it expects to receive from Samsung but said the total will "greatly exceed" royalties under the chip makers' previous agreement, which expires at year end. The new agreement is subject to approval by the South Korean government.

Several other licensing agreements between Texas Instruments and foreign chip makers expire at the end of this year, and the company said negotiations for new agreements were continuing.
