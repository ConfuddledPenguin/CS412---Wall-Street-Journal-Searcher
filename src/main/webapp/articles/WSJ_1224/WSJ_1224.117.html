Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

MEDIA: John W. Kluge and Stuart Subotnick, who together hold about a 40% stake in Image Entertainment Inc., said they retained Salomon Brothers Inc. "to render financial advisory and investment banking services" related to the investors' Image holding. A spokeswoman for the investors said she couldn't elaborate. Mr. Kluge is chairman and majority owner of Metromedia Co., based in Secaucus, N.J. Mr. Subotnick is Metromedia executive vice president and minority owner. Image Entertainment is a Chatsworth, Calif.-based supplier of laser disks.
