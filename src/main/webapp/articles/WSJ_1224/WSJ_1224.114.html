Business Brief -- Warehouse Club Inc.: Accord to Sell 51% Stake To A&P Chain Is Ended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

Warehouse Club Inc. said in Chicago that its earlier agreement to sell about a 51% stake to Great Atlantic & Pacific Tea Co. for $11 million has been terminated. Under the tentative accord announced last month, loss-plagued Warehouse Club was to issue 7.3 million new shares to A&P. "No other transaction between Warehouse Club and A&P is presently contemplated," the company said Friday.

Warehouse Club didn't say why the deal collapsed, or whether it intends to seek another investor. Warehouse operates 10 stores that sell merchandise at wholesale or near-wholesale prices; A&P is a supermarket chain.

Officials at A&P, of Montvale, N.J., couldn't be reached for comment. A&P is 52%-owned by the Tengelmann Group of Germany.
