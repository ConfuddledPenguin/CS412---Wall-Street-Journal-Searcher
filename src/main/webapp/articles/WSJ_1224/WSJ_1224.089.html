Canadian Retail Sales Decline

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian retail sales in October fell 0.3% to a seasonally adjusted 16.12 billion Canadian dollars (US$13.91 billion) from September, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. This marked the third consecutive monthly drop. The October decline was partly attributed to weak auto sales.

Separately, Statistics Canada reported that Canadian building permits in October rose 5.5% from September.
