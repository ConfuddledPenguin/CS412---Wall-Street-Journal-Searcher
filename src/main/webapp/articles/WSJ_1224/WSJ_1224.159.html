Japan Car Output Rises 2.2%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

TOKYO -- Japan's production of cars, buses and trucks climbed 2.2% in November from a year earlier, but was much lower than October's 12% year-to-year increase, the Japan Automobile Manufacturer's Association reported. Japan's auto makers produced 1,160,036 vehicles in November, down 12% from a record 1,263,321 units in October.

A spokesman for the manufacturers' group said auto makers likely had scaled back production following October's record level because of slowing growth in consumer spending.
