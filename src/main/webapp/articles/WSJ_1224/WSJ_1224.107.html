Business Brief -- Winnebago Industries Inc.: First-Period Loss Widened On 48% Drop in Revenue

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

Winnebago Industries Inc. said its loss for the first quarter, ended Dec. 1, widened to $8 million, or 32 cents a share, from $4.6 million, or 19 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue dropped 48% to $40.7 million from $78.9 million. The Forest City, Iowa, maker of motor homes blamed the industry slump on "Iraq's actions in the Middle East and concerns about a slowing economy." The Mideast crisis raises fears among motor home buyers about the availability and price of gasoline.
