The following were among Friday's offerings and pricings in U.S. and non-U.S. capital markets, with terms and syndicate manager, as compiled by Dow Jones Capital Markets Report: MUNICIPAL

Nassau County, N.Y. -- $64 million 3 1/2-month tax anticipation notes, due April 12, 1991, were apparently won by a Bear Stearns & Co. group. The notes are rated single-A-1 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and carry a coupon of 5.18%. The notes weren't reoffered to investors.

EUROBONDS

Nissan Motors Co. (Japan) -- 20 billion yen of 7.2% Eurobonds due April 23, 1999, priced at 101 3/8, via Daiwa Europe Ltd. There is a short first coupon and the payment date is Jan. 23.

Nissan Motors Co. (Japan) -- 30 billion yen of 7 1/4% Eurobonds due April 23, 1997, priced at 101 3/8, via Yamaichi International Europe Ltd. There is a short first coupon and the payment date is Jan. 23.

Cassa di Risparmio delle Provincie Lombarde, Cariplo (Italy) -- 10 billion yen of 7% depository receipts via the Law Debenture Trust Co., due Jan. 18, 1996, priced at 101 7/8, via IBJ International Ltd.
