Financing Business: Reliance Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

RELIANCE ELECTRIC CO. (Cleveland) -- Reliance said its board authorized the purchase of up to $70 million of its preferred stock and subordinated debt on the open market in 1991. The authorization is subject to the approval of senior bank creditors. The face value of the debentures outstanding is $590 million, while the preferred stock outstanding has a par value of $126 million. In March, the maker of industrial and telecommunications equipment was authorized to buy $50 million of debentures in 1990, of which $41 million of the debentures has been purchased.
