Who's News: Pacific Western Bancshares Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

PACIFIC WESTERN BANCSHARES Inc. (San Jose, Calif.) -- James R. Barranti, 44 years old, was appointed to the board of this banking concern. Mr. Barranti, who remains as the company's executive vice president/cashier, fills a vacancy on the 13-member board.
