Japanese Electronics Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

TOKYO -- Production by Japan's electronics industry in 1991 is expected to rise 6.7% to 25.49 trillion yen ($189 billion), the Electric Industries Association of Japan said. The asociation predicted growth will be led by industrial electronic equipment, production of which is expected to total 12.12 trillion yen, up 7.6%, thanks to expanded demand for computers, computer-related equipment and communications systems.

Meanwhile, pressure on electronics parts production is expected from the Middle East crisis, the slump in the U.S. economy and higher production costs, the report said. Production in the category is seen growing 4.1% in 1991 to 3.96 trillion yen.
