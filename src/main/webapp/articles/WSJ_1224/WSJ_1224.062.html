Digital Equipment to Close Site

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

MAYNARD, Mass. -- Digital Equipment Corp., a computer concern, said it will close a disk, tape and optical storage manufacturing and design facility in Enfield, Conn., moving workers and operations to Springfield, Mass. A spokeswoman said the move won't result in the loss of jobs but will cut costs by consolidating operations at the nearby Springfield site. She said Digital owns the 77,000-square-foot Enfield plant, situated on 88 acres. Its future hasn't been determined, she said.
