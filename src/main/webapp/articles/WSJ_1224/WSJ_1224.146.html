International Brief -- Groupe Cockerill Sambre: Belgian Firm, Arbed End Talks About Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

Steel makers Groupe Cockerill Sambre of Belgium and Groupe Arbed of Luxembourg have broken off talks started earlier this year to combine their flat-steel activities, Arbed said. Arbed said that "studies conducted during the past few months have shown that the constraints of the two groups aren't reconcilable on certain essential points."

Further details weren't provided in the statement.

The two steel groups announced last August they were discussing a 50-50 joint venture to combine their production and sales of flat steel. However, in November, executives at Arbed's Belgian unit, Sidmar SA, said the talks had become blocked over the question of control of the new joint venture.
