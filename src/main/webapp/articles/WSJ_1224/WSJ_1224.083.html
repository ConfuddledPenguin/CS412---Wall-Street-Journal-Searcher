Weekend Warrior

Some friends have tennis elbow

Others suffer with jogger's knee,

Which causes a left-out feeling

For couch potatoes, like me.

With gridiron season in swing,

I'm less apt to feel inferior

When complaining about my bad case

Of TV-football posterior.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
