Who's News: Seagram Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

SEAGRAM Co. (Montreal) -- Jack M. Cooper was named vice president and chief information officer, a new post, effective Jan. 1, at this maker of spirits, wines and soft drinks. Mr. Cooper, previously president of CSX technology, a unit of CSX Corp., Jacksonville, Fla., will be responsible for world-wide information systems and services.
