Mazda Boosts Price of Navajo

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

DETROIT -- Mazda Motor of America said it is raising the base price of its 1991 Navajo sport utility vehicle by 1.6% to $17,835. The auto maker said in September that the Navajo would cost $17,560. Mazda said the price increase is at least partly a result of the rising cost of doing business. The price increase is effective for vehicles that arrive at showrooms on or after Jan. 2, 1991.
