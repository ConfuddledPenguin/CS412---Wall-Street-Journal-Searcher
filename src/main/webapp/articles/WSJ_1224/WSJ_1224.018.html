Who's News: LSB Bancshares Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

LSB BANCSHARES Inc. (Lexington, N.C.) -- Robert F. Lowe, 48 years old, president and chief executive officer of this holding company for Lexington State Bank, takes the additional post of chairman, succeeding Haynes F. Sherron, 70, who is retiring.
