Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Paula J. Saint-Amour was named senior vice president, wholesale travel U.S.A., a new post at American Express Travel Related Services Inc., a subsidiary of this travel, financial and information services company. Ms. Saint-Amour was previously vice president treasury and budget planning and financial analysis.
