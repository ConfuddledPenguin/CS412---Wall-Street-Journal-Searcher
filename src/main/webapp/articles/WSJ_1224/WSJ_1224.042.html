Who's News: Calmat Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

CALMAT Co. (Los Angeles) -- A. Frederick Gerstell, president and chief executive officer of this maker of construction materials, was elected to the additional post of chairman. He succeeds William Jenkins, who will retire from that post Jan. 1. Mr. Jenkins, 70 years old, will remain a director. Mr. Gerstell, 52, was president and chief operating officer until 1988 when he was named to his current posts.
