Securities Markets to Close Early Today for Christmas

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

NEW YORK -- U.S. securities markets generally will close early today for Christmas Eve and will be closed tomorrow. The New York Stock Exchange and American Stock Exchange will close at 2 p.m. EST, and the Public Securities Association has recommended that the U.S. government securities market close at the same time.

The Pacific Stock Exchange will close trading in equities and options activities between 2:10 p.m. and 2:30 p.m. EST today.

In Canada, banks, exchanges, government offices and most businesses will be closed Tuesday and Wednesday for the Christmas and Boxing Day holidays. There won't be any mail delivery.
