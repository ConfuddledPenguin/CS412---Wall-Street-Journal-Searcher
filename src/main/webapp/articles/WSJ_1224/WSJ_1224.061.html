Who's News: Westinghouse Electric Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

WESTINGHOUSE ELECTRIC Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Warren H. Hollinshead, formerly finance deputy and a vice president, was named chief financial officer of this broadcasting, financial services and power generation equipment concern. He succeeds Harry F. Murray, who retired. Mr. Hollinshead joined the company in 1964 and has held a variety of positions in Westinghouse's law, treasury and corporate development departments.
