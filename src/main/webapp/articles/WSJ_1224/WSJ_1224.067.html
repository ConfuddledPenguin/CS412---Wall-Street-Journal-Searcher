Mutual of New York Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

NEW YORK -- Mutual of New York, a New York-based life insurance company, sold its midtown Manhattan headquarters building to Mendick Co. and three unidentified investment groups for approximately $108 million. The 28-story building, which is 40 years old, recently underwent a $40 million renovation.

MONY will continue to occupy about one-third of the building under a long-term lease.

The investment groups, which are advised by Chase Investors Management Corp., will hold a majority stake in the building. Chase Investors has acquired three other buildings with Mendic, which owns with its partners more than $2.5 billion in commercial real estate.
