International: Container Shippers Will Disband

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

HONG KONG -- Two container-line groups operating between Asia and the U.S. will disband in December 1991, with the members planning to pursue individual routes and schedules. The Asia-U.S. West Coast group is made up of Orient Overseas Container Line Ltd. of Hong Kong, Neptune Orient Lines Ltd. of Singapore and Nippon Liner Service Ltd. of Japan. The Asia-U.S. East Coast group is made up of Orient Overseas, Neptune Orient and Kawasaki Kisen Kaisha Ltd. of Japan.
