Business Brief -- Prentiss Properties Ltd.: Pension Funds Committed $90 Million for Real Estate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

Prentiss Properties Ltd., Dallas, said pension funds have committed about $90 million to a newly formed investment fund for the acquisition of office and industrial properties. The new fund, Prentiss Properties Acquisition Partners, expects to close its first purchase in January, a spokeswoman for Prentiss Properties said. The fund wouldn't provide details of property to be acquired.

The investment fund expects to raise a total of $100 million to $115 million by mid-January, the spokeswoman said.

Prentiss Properties Ltd., a closely held commercial real estate concern, will take an undisclosed partnership interest in the properties to be acquired, and will collect fees for managing the properties.

The investment fund is headed by Joshua M. Brown, who was a vice president of Fayez Saforim & Co. in Houston prior to joining Prentiss Properties.
