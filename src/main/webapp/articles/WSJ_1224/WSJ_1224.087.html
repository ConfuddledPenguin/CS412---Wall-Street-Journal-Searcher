Pittston Gets 10-Year Coal Pacts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Pittston Co. said it signed two 10-year agreements to supply low sulphur coal to subsidiaries of American Electric Power Co. A total value for the accords wasn't disclosed. The first agreement, with American Electric's Appalachian Power Co., calls for Pittston to supply a minimum of one million tons annually for 10 years starting in Oct. 1, 1991. In the second pact, Pittston would supply the Ohio Power Co. unit, starting with 240,000 tons in the first year and gradually increasing to one million tons by the sixth year of the contract.
