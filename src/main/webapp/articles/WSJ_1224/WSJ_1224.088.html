Storebrand America Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

TARRYTOWN, N.Y. -- Storebrand America Inc. said it plans to raise its stake in Belvedere Corp. to 54.9% by purchasing an additional 290,000 common shares. The company, a unit of Norwegian insurer Storebrand AS, declined to disclose terms. It said it is authorized to acquire the additional 5% stake under a 1989 agreement with Belvedere, a New York-based reinsurer. The purchases will be done in the open market or through private transactions, Storebrand said.

Belvedere last month ended talks aimed at the merger of Belvedere and Storebrand America's Christiania Holding Co. in exchange for Belvedere's common stock. The transaction would have increased Storebrand America's stake to 82%.
