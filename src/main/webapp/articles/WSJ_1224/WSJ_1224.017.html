Financing Business: Medusa Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

MEDUSA Corp., Cleveland, said it completed the private placement of $35 million in 10% senior notes, due Dec. 15, 1995. The construction concern said proceeds will be used to repay debt. Medusa also said it arranged with three banks a new $20 million revolving credit facility to be used for working capital.
