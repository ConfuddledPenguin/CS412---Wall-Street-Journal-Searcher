Who's News: State Board of Insurance

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

STATE BOARD OF INSURANCE (Texas) -- Philip W. Barnes, 48 years old, was named commissioner, succeeding A.W. Pogue, who is leaving for health reasons. Mr. Barnes, a principal in the Austin office of the accounting firm KPMG Peat Marwick, will take office Jan. 7. Mr. Pogue, who has been a commissioner since Jan. 4, 1989, will return to his previous position as associate commissioner for regulated lines and licensing. The state board sets rates and regulates insurance carriers in Texas.
