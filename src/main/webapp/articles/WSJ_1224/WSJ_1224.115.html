Business Brief -- Walt Disney Co.: Directors Approve Addition Of Shares to Incentive Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

Walt Disney Co. said its board approved the addition of 8.5 million shares to the company's stock incentive plan. The Burbank, Calif., entertainment company said the addition will be presented to shareholders at the company's next shareholder meeting in March. Under the plan, stock options are granted to executives over a five-year period and the executives are allowed to exercise 20% of the options a year.

Walt Disney said the board also approved increasing the number of common shares authorized for repurchase by 8.5 million shares. The company said those repurchases would offset any potential dilution from the stock incentive plan.

The company currently has about 135 million common shares outstanding.
