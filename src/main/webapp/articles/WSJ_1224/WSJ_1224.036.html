Financing Business: AMR Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

AMR Corp., Fort Worth, Texas, said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a proposed public offering of five million common shares. The company, parent of American Airlines, said the offering is expected to begin in January and has a par value of $1 a share. Proceeds are expected to be used to help fund AMR's planned acquisition of Trans World Airlines' London routes; AMR said it agreed to buy six routes last week for $445 million. AMR has 63 million shares outstanding. Goldman, Sachs & Co. will be the lead manager of the offering.
