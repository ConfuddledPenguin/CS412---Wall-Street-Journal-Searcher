Buffton Plans to Sell Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/24/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Buffton Corp. said it has signed a letter of intent to sell the assets of National Pipe Co. of Vestal, New York, to the Regency Group of Houston for approximately $14 million. The manufacturer of avionic, electronic and plastic products said the money will be used to reduce bank debt.

Closing of the transaction, which must be approved by Buffton's board, is scheduled for Jan. 31.
