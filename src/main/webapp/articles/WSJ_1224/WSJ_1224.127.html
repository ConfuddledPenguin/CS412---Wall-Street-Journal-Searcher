Business Brief -- PacifiCorp: Pacific Telecom Unit Bids For Mid-Plains Telephone

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

Pacific Telecom Inc., a unit of PacifiCorp, a Portland, Ore.-based utility holding company, said it proposed to acquire Mid-Plains Telephone Inc. by exchanging PacifiCorp common stock valued at about $51 million for all of the common shares of the Middleton, Wis., company. Mid-Plains provides local phone service in Middleton, Cross Plains and parts of Madison. It had total assets of $27.3 million on Sept. 30, Pacific Telecom said.

Under the bid, made to the Mid-Plains board, Mid-Plains holders would receive for each share about 1.19 PacifiCorp common shares. Pacific Telecom said MidPlains has 1,933,467 shares outstanding. PacifiCorp shares closed Friday at $22.375, up 37.5 cents, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading. Mid-Plains officials couldn't be reached for comment.

"This offer is consistent with our commitment to expand our business . . . in the rural and suburban areas of Wisconsin," C.E. Robinson, Pacific Telecom chairman and chief executive officer, said.
