Financing Business: Westwood One Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/24/90

WESTWOOD ONE Inc., a Los Angeles-based producer and distributor of radio programs, said it amended its offer to exchange $400 principal amount of its 9% convertible senior subordinated debentures, due 2002, for each $1,000 principal amount of its 6 3/4% convertible subordinated debentures, due 2011. The amendment reduces the conversion price of the 9% debentures to $3.50 a share from $5.25, and eliminates the company's option to redeem these debentures prior to April 23, 1993. A spokesman said Westwood amended the offer because there was "interest from a large number of holders who said they would accept an exchange under the revised terms." About $8.7 million principal amount of the 6 3/4% debentures had been tendered as of noon EST Friday.
