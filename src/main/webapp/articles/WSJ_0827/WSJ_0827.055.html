Wet Seal Net Nearly Doubles

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Wet Seal Inc., a specialty retailer that went public last month, said that second-quarter net income nearly doubled to $2.3 million, or 22 cents a share, on a 54% sales increase. In the comparable quarter ended July 29, 1989, the specialty retailer had net of $1.2 million, or 13 cents a share. Sales in the latest quarter rose to $27.7 million from $18.1 million.

Wet Seal said the results reflected strong sales in its mature stores as well as in seven new stores that opened in the retailer's fiscal first half in California, Hawaii and Arizona. In addition, the company has opened six stores this month, including three in Florida, and plans to open seven more this year.

Wet Seal said it had 80 stores at the end of the quarter, compared with 68 a year earlier. The recent openings bring the total number of stores to 86. In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Wet Seal's shares rose $1.50 to close at $13.75.
