McCormick to Buy Back Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

HUNT VALLEY, Md. -- McCormick & Co., a maker of spices, flavorings and food products, said it began a program to purchase as many as two million of its 40 million common shares outstanding, in open-market transactions from time to time. The company said its most recent buy-back program, which was announced last Sept. 18 and covered about 2.1 million shares, has been completed.
