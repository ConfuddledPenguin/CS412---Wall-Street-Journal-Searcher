Who's News: Ultramar PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

ULTRAMAR PLC (London) -- Nick Di Tomaso was named group senior vice president, refining and marketing, of American Ultramar Ltd., a Tarrytown, N.Y., unit of this energy holding company. Mr. Di Tomaso, who also joins American Ultramar's executive committee, is currently president of Ultramar's Canada unit and will be succeeded by Guy Archambault, currently executive vice president. Both appointments take effect Oct. 1. Frank Sisti, currently president of Ultramar Inc., a California refining unit, was named senior vice president, corporate development at American Ultramar. He is succeeded by John G. Drosdick, previously president and chief operating officer of Tosco Corp., Santa Monica, Calif. The appointments take effect Nov. 1.
