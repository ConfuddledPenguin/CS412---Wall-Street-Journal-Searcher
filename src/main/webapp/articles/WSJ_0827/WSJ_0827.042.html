Japan's Vehicle Output Soars

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

TOKYO -- Japan's production of cars, buses and trucks in July soared 10.1% from a year earlier to 1.2 million units from 1.1 million, marking the second-largest year-to-year percentage gain for a month and a record total for the month of July, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers' Association reported. The record year-to-year gain was a 10.8% jump in May. In June, by comparison, output slipped 1%.

Domestic sales, growing 8.3% to 766,174 units in July, fueled the rise, the association said. Export figures are expected to show a 3% gain.
