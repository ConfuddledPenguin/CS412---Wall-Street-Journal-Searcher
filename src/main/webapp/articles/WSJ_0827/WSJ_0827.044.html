Hasbro Inc. Stock Repurchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

PAWTUCKET, R.I. -- Hasbro Inc. said its board of directors approved the company's purchase of up to three million of its common shares "in the open market or otherwise." Hasbro said it would use some of the repurchased shares for funding its employee stock-option plans and outstanding warrants.

On July 31, Hasbro had 57.9 million shares outstanding. The shares closed Friday at $13 apiece, down from $17.325 on Aug. 1.
