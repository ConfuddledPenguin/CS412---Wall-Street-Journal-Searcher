Magma Repays Its Bank Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

SAN MANUEL, Ariz. -- Magma Copper Co. said it has repaid in full its bank debt outstanding, which was $32.2 million on June 30. The debt was part of a credit agreement with a group of banks led by Continental Bank, Chicago, and National Westminster Bank PLC as co-agents. The copper producer now has approximately $362 million in unsecured long-term debt, with no maturities until Dec. 31, 1992.
