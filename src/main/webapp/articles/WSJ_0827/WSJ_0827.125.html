Business Brief -- A.O. Smith Corp.: Auto-Equipment Company Resumes Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

A.O. Smith Corp. said it resumed a stock-repurchase program which had been suspended in June 1989. The remaining authorization allows the Milwaukee-based automotive-equipment company to buy back up to $7.5 million of its common and convertible exchangeable preferred shares.

A growing number of companies have opted to either dust off old share-repurchase plans, or authorize new buy-backs, in response to the stock market's recent sharp downturn.
