International: Australian Firm Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

SYDNEY, Australia -- OTC Ltd., Australia's government-owned international telecommunications company, said it received a 10-year contract valued at more than 250 million Australian dollars (US$208.4 million) to help Vietnam develop and manage its international telecommunications network. OTC already is involved in Vietnam through a contract signed in 1988 to build satellite earth stations. Under the new contract, OTC will construct two digital international telephone exchanges in Vietnam, invest in domestic telecommunications projects and train local staff.
