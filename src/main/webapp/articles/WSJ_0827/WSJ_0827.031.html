International: Stora Puts Finess Up for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

LONDON -- Stora Kopparbergs Bergslags AB of Sweden, a pulp and paper maker, said it plans to sell its Finess AB unit, a tissues and paper napkin maker with annual sales of about 2.2 billion kronor ($385.2 million). An asking price wasn't disclosed but an analyst estimated 1.6 billion kronor as likely. Stora said it will provide more detailed information on Sept. 6, following a board meeting.
