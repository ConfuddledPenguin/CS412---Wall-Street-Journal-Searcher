Price Board Clears Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

SAN DIEGO -- Price Co., an operator of members-only warehouse stores, said its board authorized management to buy back in transactions from time to time an additional $50 million of any of its securities outstanding. Under a previous authorization, Price since January purchased 1,447,500 of its 49.9 million common shares outstanding for $52 million.
