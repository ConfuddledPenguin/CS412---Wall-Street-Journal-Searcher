Who's News: Vikonics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

VIKONICS Inc. (Secaucus, N.J.) -- Richard N. Perle was named to the board, increasing membership to six, at this maker of computerized security systems, primarily for the armed forces and government agencies. Mr. Perle, formerly an assistant secretary of defense, is currently a consultant to the secretary of state and to several U.S. and multinational corporations.
