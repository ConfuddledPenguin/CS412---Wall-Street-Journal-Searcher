Who's News: Storage Technology Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

STORAGE TECHNOLOGY Corp. (Louisville, Colo.) -- Robert J. Luth, former executive vice president and chief financial officer of Savin Corp., was named corporate vice president of financial planning and treasurer of this maker of data storage equipment. Mr. Luth succeeds Carl R. Vertuca Jr., who was named to the new position of corporate vice president of development.
