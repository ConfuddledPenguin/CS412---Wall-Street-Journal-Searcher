Salomon Phibro Oil Trust Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

NEW YORK -- Salomon Brothers Inc.'s new Phibro Oil Trust units were the most active issue on the American Stock Exchange in their first day of trading Friday. Salomon said it priced 16 million units, four million more than proposed, at $4.65 a unit. Almost 2.7 million units traded, and the issue closed slightly higher at $4.75.

The Phibro units, which allows investors to bet on the price of crude oil five years from now, were brought to market by Salomon and Bear, Stearns & Co. after an earlier proposed version was pulled in May when oil markets plunged.
