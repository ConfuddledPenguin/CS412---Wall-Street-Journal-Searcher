Northrop Wins Air Force Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

WASHINGTON -- Northrop Corp. received a $35.4 million Air Force contract for support of undergraduate pilot training.

TRW Corp. received a $30.9 million Air Force contract for improvements to the Cheyenne Mountain complex.

California Microwave Inc. got a $17.8 million Army contract for a classified system.

Institute for Defense Analysis was awarded a $10 million Army contract for defense studies.
