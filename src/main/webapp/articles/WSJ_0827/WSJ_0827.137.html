Business Brief -- Penn Central Corp.: Concern Seeks More Shares In Amended Buy-Back Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

Penn Central Corp., amending its previously announced offer, said it will buy back 10 million shares of its 62.7 million outstanding at $23 a share. Penn Central, which has businesses in industrial manufacturing, defense services, insurance and energy, said it was modifying its offer because of a "deterioration in general market conditions, including the market price of the company's common stock."

Previously Penn Central had offered to purchase up to six million shares at a price of $26 a share.

The Cincinnati-based company said its repurchase offer expires midnight Sept. 10 and is not contingent upon a miniumum number of shares being tendered. American Financial Corp., which owns 34.3% of Penn Central's comon stock, said it does not plan to tender any of its shares.
