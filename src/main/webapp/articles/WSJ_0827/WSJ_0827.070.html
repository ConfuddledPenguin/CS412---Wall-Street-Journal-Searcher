Financing Business: Dexter Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

DEXTER Corp. (Windsor Locks, Conn.) said its board authorized the buy-back of as many as an additional one million shares of common stock outstanding. The specialty materials concern said the added shares covered under the authorization represent about 4% of its more than 24.1 million common shares outstanding. Under a previous authorization to buy back one million shares, Dexter has bought 929,600 shares. It said no further purchases will be made under that authorizaton.
