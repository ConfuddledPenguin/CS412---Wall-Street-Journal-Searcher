Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

AD REVENUE: Times Mirror Co. said ad revenue of its Newspaper Publishing Group for the five weeks ended Aug. 5 edged up 0.6% to $143.6 million from $142.8 million for the similar period a year earlier. For 31 weeks ended Aug. 5, ad revenue fell 0.9% to $985.7 million. The cumulative results for 1990 contain 31 Sundays, one less than a year earlier.
