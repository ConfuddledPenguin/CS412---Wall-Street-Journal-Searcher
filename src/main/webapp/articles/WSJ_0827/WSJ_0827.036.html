McDevitt & Street to Be Sold

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- McDevitt & Street Co., a large, closely held contracting company, said it agreed to be acquired by the Bovis Construction Group unit of London-based Peninsular & Oriental Steam Navigation Co., which also has interests in ferry lines and passenger shipping and owns Princess Cruises in the U.S. Terms weren't disclosed. With revenue last year of $880 million, McDevitt & Street is North Carolina's largest private business. The company builds office buildings, industrial facilities, stores and hotels, among other things.
