Toyota Exports Rose in July

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

TOKYO -- Toyota Motor Corp. said exports rose for the second month in a row, jumping 10% to 146,089 units in July from a year earlier. Japan's No. 1 auto maker said exports to the U.S. rose 10% to 63,330 units, but exports to the European Community dropped 12% to 22,264 units.

Separately, Nissan Motor Co., Japan's No. 2 auto maker, said exports fell 11% to 77,302 units in July, the 11th consecutive monthly decline. Exports to the U.S. fell 17% to 25,181 units, while those to the EC nations dropped 10% to 26,291 units.
