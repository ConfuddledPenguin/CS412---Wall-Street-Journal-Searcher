Golden Nugget Inc. Stock Is Sold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

WASHINGTON -- Stephen A. Wynn, chairman of Golden Nugget Inc., said he sold 150,000 shares in the Las Vegas, Nev., hotel and casino operator, reducing his stake to the equivalent 31.1% of common shares outstanding. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Mr. Wynn said he sold the shares from July 3 to July 11 for $34.375 to $35.625 a share.

Mr. Wynn said he continues to hold 5,460,831 Golden Nugget shares.
