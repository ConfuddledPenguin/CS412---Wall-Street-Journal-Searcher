Business Brief -- ASK Corp.: Pact Set With Union Fund For a $20 Million Infusion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

ASK Corp. of Waco, Texas, said it entered into a letter of understanding with the Sheet Metal Workers' National Pension Fund, regarding the previously announced plan of the fund and another institution to infuse $20 million of debt and equity into the maker of natural gas heating and airconditioning systems. ASK said the letter sets out the main elements of a proposed definitive agreement, and establishes projected time frames for completion of due diligience and closing of the transaction, subject to approval by the boards of the three parties involved.

The company said it plans to use the $20 million to acquire "certain technology and equipment," but ASK President Brian D. Pardo declined to discuss details of the proposed acquisitions or identify the third party involved in the negotiations.
