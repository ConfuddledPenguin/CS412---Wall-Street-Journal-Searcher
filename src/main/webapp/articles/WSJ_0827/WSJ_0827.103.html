Financing Business: Hall Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/27/90

HALL FINANCIAL Corp. (Dallas) said it was notified by one of its principal lenders that the company is in default on two working capital loans totaling $21.4 million. The real-estate concern said the default notice wasn't triggered by non-payment of the loans but by an alleged default on a $6.5 million guaranty of a separate loan to an affiliated partnership. The company said it is disputing the claims of default and is in negotiations with the lender, which the company didn't identify.
