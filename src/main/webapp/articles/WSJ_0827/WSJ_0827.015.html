  • Date: 08/27/90

CROP GENETICS INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Hanover, Md.) -- Joseph Kelly, chief operating officer of this agricultural biotechnology concern, was named to the newly created position of president and chief executive officer. Mr. Kelly, 45 years old, who also has been chief financial officer of the company since 1984, succeeds John B. Henry as chief executive. Mr. Henry will remain chairman.
