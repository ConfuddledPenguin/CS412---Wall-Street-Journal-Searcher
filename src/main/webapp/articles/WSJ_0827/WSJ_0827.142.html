Business Brief -- Tektronix Inc.: Board Adopts `Poison Pill' To Deter Hostile Takeovers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/27/90

Tektronix Inc. said its board adopted a shareholder rights plan aimed at discouraging unwanted takeovers. The "poison pill" plan would make any hostile takeover attempt much more expensive. Tektronix said it didn't adopt the plan in response to any specific threat.

The Beaverton, Ore., electronics testing and measurements concern has been plagued for years by stagnant sales and dwindling margins. In May, it announced plans to lay of 1,300 workers and close down some business lines. For the fiscal year ended May 26, it had a net loss of $92.6 million, or $3.19 a share, on revenue of $1.4 billion.
