Who's News: American President Appoints John Lillie To New Top Position

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

OAKLAND, Calif. -- American President Cos. named John M. Lillie, 53 years old, to the newly created post of president and chief operating officer, effective Aug. 6. American President is a transportation services and container distribution concern.

Mr. Lillie was elected a director on the company's board in January. Mr. Lillie, a general partner with the private investment company of Sequoia Associates, in Menlo Park, Calif., is a former chairman and chief executive officer of the Lucky Stores Inc. grocery chain.

American President also said it named Timothy J. Rhein, 49, as a director, increasing board membership to 13 from 12. Mr. Rhein is president and chief executive officer of American President Domestic, the company's domestic transportation subsidiary.
