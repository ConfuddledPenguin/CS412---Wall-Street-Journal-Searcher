Who's News: Roadway Services Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

ROADWAY SERVICES Inc. (Akron, Ohio) -- J.T. Topping, president of this freight carrier's Roadway Express unit, was named vice chairman, a new post. Michael W. Wickham, executive vice president of administration and finance of Roadway Express, succeeds Mr. Topping as president of that unit.
