LEONARD RUBIN of the law firm of Bracewell & Patterson in Washington, D.C., represented the Arkansas Independent Bankers Association in a suit against the Resolution Trust Corp. NOBLE DRILLING Corp. filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 10,350,000 common shares -- five million for itself and four million for shareholders. The remaining shares cover overallotment agreements with underwriters. Monday's edition misstated the size of the offering.

(See: "Recent SEC Filings" -- WSJ July 9, 1990)
