Business Brief -- Noxso Corp.: Patent Received for Process In Plant's Pollution Control

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

Noxso Corp., Pittsburgh, said it received a patent for a process to remove sulfur and other pollutants from coal-fired power plants. The company's sole business is the development and commercialization of this pollution-control technology. Noxso said the patent is the last of three covering its process to remove sulfur and nitrogen oxide from burning high-sulfur coal. The process involves the use of absorbent beads made from aluminum oxide and coated with sodium. The latest patent covers the regeneration stage of the process, which chemically treats the materials used so they can be reused, Noxso said.
