AirTran's Mesaba June Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- AirTran Corp. said June traffic at its Mesaba Aviation unit rose 18% to 18.3 million revenue passenger miles from 15.5 million. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The regional carrier said its load factor, or percentage of seats filled, dropped to 51.9% from the year-earlier 61.7%. The drop reflects an increase in capacity and an unusually high year-earlier load factor, Mesaba said.
