Financing Business: Orthopedic Services Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

ORTHOPEDIC SERVICES Inc., a Wayne, Pa.-based maker of orthopedic appliances, said it made an initial public offering of three million common shares at $16 each. Proceeds will be used to repay debts and redeem preferred stock, and for working capital. Lehman Brothers and Paribas Corp. are co-managers of the underwriting group.
