NCR Gets $10 Million Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- NCR Corp. said it received a $10 million contract to automate Fleet/Norstar Financial Group's network of branch offices with NCR's FTS II branch automation system. NCR, which makes computers and bank and retail automation systems, said the contract could exceed $20 million over the next three years.
