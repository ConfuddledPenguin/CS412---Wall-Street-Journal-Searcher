Pan American's June Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

NEW YORK -- Pan American World Airways said its June traffic rose slightly to 2.86 billion revenue passenger miles from 2.83 billion revenue miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. Load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, dipped slightly to 70.2% from 70.5%.

For the year to date, the Pan Am Corp. unit said it flew 14.7 billion revenue passenger miles, up 11.4% from 13.2 billion revenue passenger miles a year earlier. Load factor was 63% compared with 60.7%.
