Engelhard Ends Degussa Talks

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

EDISON, N.J. -- Engelhard Corp. said it ended negotiations to sell its world-wide gold and silver businesses to Degussa AG because "acceptable terms" couldn't be reached. Engelhard, a specialty chemical concern, said it won't try to find another buyer for the whole group of gold and silver businesses. Instead, it will keep the European segment and attempt to sell the less profitable North American operations.

Degussa is a West German chemical and metal producer.
