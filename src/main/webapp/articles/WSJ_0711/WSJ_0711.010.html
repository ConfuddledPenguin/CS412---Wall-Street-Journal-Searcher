Comair Passenger Boardings Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

CINCINNATI -- Comair Holdings Inc. said 156,597 passengers boarded its Comair Inc. regional airline during June, 13.9% more than the year-earlier 137,430 passenger boardings. Revenue passenger miles increased 24.8% to 32.6 million miles in June from 26.2 million miles a year earlier, the company said. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
