Hitachi's Maxell Corp., TDK Unit Face Suit Filed by Wholesaler

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

MIAMI -- Two electronics companies, Maxell Corp. of America and TDK Electronics Corp., were charged with antitrust violations in a suit brought by a local wholesaler. Closely held Micro Computer Supply Inc., which sells computer diskettes and other computer supplies and accessories, alleged that Maxell and TDK engaged in discriminatory pricing practices, including the granting of discounts to wholesalers of the firms' consumer products.

The suit, filed in U.S. District Court here, doesn't seek specific damages.

Neither Maxell, a unit of Hitachi Ltd. in Japan, nor TDK, a unit of TDK Corp., also based in Japan, could be reached for comment.
