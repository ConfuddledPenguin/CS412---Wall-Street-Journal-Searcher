China's Industrial Output Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

BEIJING -- China's industrial production, responding to an infusion of bank credit, shot up 5.9% in June from a year earlier, the newspaper China Daily reported. With June's strong showing, industrial output for the 1990 first half was 2.2% above the year-earlier period and totaled 925.4 billion yuan ($196.5 billion).

However, the state statistical bureau warned that industry will have to maintain an 8% growth rate in the second half of the year to fulfill the state plan, the official Xinhua news agency reported. The plan projected an annual growth rate of 6%.
