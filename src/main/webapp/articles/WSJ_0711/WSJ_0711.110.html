Business Brief -- Datapoint Corp.: Depressed Computer Sales Produce the Layoff of 91

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

Datapoint Corp., citing continued depressed sales of its computer systems, said it's laying off 91 employees effective immediately. The layoffs at the San Antonio, Texas, headquarters effectively close Datapoint's corporate sales efforts in the U.S. The company now will rely on third-party resellers for domestic sales, a company spokesman said.

Earlier this week, the troubled computer and communications concern lost an appeal in a patent-infringement case brought against it by Northern Telecom Inc. A company spokesman, however, said there wasn't any connection between the layoffs and the appeal decision.

For its third quarter ended April 28, Datapoint reported a loss of $8.4 million, or $1.07 a share, compared with a loss of $21.3 million, or $2.35 a share, the year earlier.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Datapoint shares closed yeserday at $2, down 12.5 cents.
