Kirschner Medical Disputes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

TIMONIUM, Md. -- Kirschner Medical Corp. said it settled all legal disputes with ALM Equipment Hospitaliers S.A., a former supplier of surgical lights to Kirschner's Chick division. Although terms of the settlement were not disclosed, Kirschner said the agreement ends the company's liabilities to ALM, and it will allow Kirschner to recover about $1.76 million from reserves previously established for those liabilities.

Kirschner manufactures orthopedic instruments and implants.
