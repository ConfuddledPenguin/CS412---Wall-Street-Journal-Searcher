Freeport-McMoRan Sells Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

NEW ORLEANS -- Freeport-McMoRan Resource Partners Ltd. said a unit completed the sale of its stake in a Korean chemical concern and will report a $72.5 million gain on the sale. The unit, Agrico Korea Enterprises S.A., sold its 25% interest in Namhae Chemical Corp. to Korea's National Agricultural Cooperative Federation for $80 million.

Freeport-McMoRan Resource Partners, an affiliate of Freeport-McMoRan Inc., didn't say in which period it plans to record the gain.
