Who's News: Castle & Cooke Inc.'s DeLorenzo Is Named To Executive Posts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

LOS ANGELES -- David A. DeLorenzo, 43 years old, was named executive vice president of Castle & Cooke Inc. and president of its Dole Food Co. subsidiary. Mr. DeLorenzo, formerly president of Dole Produce Co., also joins David Murdock, Raymond F. Henze III and Alan B. Sellers as a member of the office of the chairman at Castle & Cooke, a holding company with interests in food and real estate.

Dole Food's former president, Robert W. Fisher, 52, recently resigned to become president of PPI Del Monte Tropical Fruit Co., a unit of London-based Polly Peck International PLC.

Mr. DeLorenzo joined Castle & Cooke in 1970 and has held various management positions at the company. He is a graduate of Colgate University and holds a master's degree in business administration from the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School.

Also, Harry E. Stephens III, acting senior vice president of Castle & Cooke's Dole Packaged Foods Co., was named president of the unit. Mr. Stephens, 40, joined the parent company in 1986 as vice president, planning and business development.

Castle & Cooke recently delayed indefinitely its planned spinoff of Dole Food, because of financing concerns and uncertainty about the tax treatment of the transaction.
