Who's News: Paramount Pictures Names D. Kirkpatrick To Movie Group Post

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

HOLLYWOOD -- David Kirkpatrick was named executive vice president of the motion picture group of Paramount Pictures, a new post. Paramount Pictures is a unit of Paramount Communications Co.

Mr. Kirkpatrick resigned as president of production of the Walt Disney Pictures and Touchstone Pictures divisions of Walt Disney Co., effective July 20. While there he helped to oversee the production of "Pretty Woman" and "Dick Tracy."

David Hoberman, president of Touchstone Pictures and Walt Disney Pictures, said Mr. Kirkpatrick's departure was voluntary and amicable. But he said, "There was a little frustration. He wanted more personal control over films and mangement. We continue to be very close friends."

Prior to his tenure of more than a year at Disney, Mr. Kirkpatrick was president of the motion picture group of Weintraub Entertainment Group for two years. He also had an eight-year tenure at Paramount, where his last title was executive vice president of production.
