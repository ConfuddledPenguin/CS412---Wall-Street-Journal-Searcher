Treasury Plans to Sell Monday $18 Billion Of Short-Term Bills

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise about $2.1 billion with the sale Monday of about $18 billion in short-term bills. Maturing bills outstanding total $15.89 billion. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills, which will be dated July 19 and mature on Oct. 18, 1990, and Jan. 17, 1991, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
