Financing Business: Medical Care International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

MEDICAL CARE INTERNATIONAL Inc. said it registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer $50 million principal amount of convertible subordinated debentures, due 2015. The Dallas-based operator of outpatient surgical centers said it will use the proceeds to support its long-term expansion plans and for general corporate purposes. Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. is the lead underwriter.
