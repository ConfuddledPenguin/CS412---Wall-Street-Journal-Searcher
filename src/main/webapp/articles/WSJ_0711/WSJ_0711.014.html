Sale of Gerber Unit Complete

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

FREMONT, Mich. -- Gerber Products Co. said it completed the previously announced sale of its Gerber Children's Centers Inc. unit to an affiliate of KD Equities. Terms weren't disclosed. The baby-food concern said the sale includes 115 child-care centers in 12 states. Gerber Children's Centers also manages four other centers for other employers, with a total enrollment of more than 13,000 children.

Gerber said it won't record a significant gain on the sale. KD Equities is a New York private investment partnership.
