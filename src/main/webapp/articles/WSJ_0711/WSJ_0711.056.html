Who's News: Essex Cop.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

ESSEX Corp. (Alexandria, Va.) -- Harold P. Hanson was named a director of this systems manufacturing and design company. Mr. Hanson, 68 years old, succeeds Charles S. Smith, 44, who retired from the board to pursue other business interests. Mr. Hanson is a former executive director of the Committee on Science, Space and Technology of the U.S. House of Representatives.
