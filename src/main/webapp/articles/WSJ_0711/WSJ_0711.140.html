Business Brief -- UDC-Universal Development L.P.: Builder to Report Decline In Net for Second Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

UDC-Universal Development L.P., citing partly a weaker housing market in California, said it expects to report second-quarter net income "significantly" lower than the year-earlier $12 million, or $1.11 a share. The Tempe, Ariz., builder of family and adult residential communities said new-home closings in the latest quarter fell 3% to $87 million from the record $89 million a year earlier. UDC-Universal also attributed the decline in net to lower margins, fewer home closings in California, and the company's partial recapitalization last year. The company said it will report second-quarter earnings in early August.

The company noted that demand for housing in Arizona has improved from a year earlier, and said it expects increases in California sales as new subdivisions open in the second half. UDC-Universal builds in Arizona, California, Florida, Georgia, North Carolina and South Carolina.
