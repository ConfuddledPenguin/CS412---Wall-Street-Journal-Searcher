Hewlett-Packard Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Hewlett-Packard Co. said its Canadian unit agreed in principle to acquire Idacom Ltd., Edmonton, Alberta. Terms weren't disclosed. The agreement is subject to approval by Idacom shareholders.

Idacom, which is closely held, is a supplier of electronic instruments used in data communication. It employs 120 people.
