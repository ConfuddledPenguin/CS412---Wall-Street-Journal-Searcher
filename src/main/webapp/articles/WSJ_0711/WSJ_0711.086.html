Canada Auctions Bonds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government auctioned 850 million Canadian dollars (US$733.4 million) of 10 3/4% bonds maturing Dec. 15, 1995. The average accepted bid yielded 11.248% for a price equivalent of 98.002.

Proceeds of the sale will be used for general government purposes, the department said.
