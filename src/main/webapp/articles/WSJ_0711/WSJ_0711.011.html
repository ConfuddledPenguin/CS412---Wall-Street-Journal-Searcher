America West Traffic Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines Inc. said its June traffic rose 42% to 936,252,000 revenue passenger miles from 660,073,000 revenue passenger miles in the year-earlier month. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. Load factor for the month rose to 64% from 60%.

For the first six months of the year, America West said its traffic rose 43% to 5.3 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.7 billion. Load factor rose to 62% from 57%.
