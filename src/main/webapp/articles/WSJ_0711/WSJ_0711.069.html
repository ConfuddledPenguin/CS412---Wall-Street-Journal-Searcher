South Africa's Producer Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa -- South Africa's producer price index in May rose 11.3% from a year earlier, the government's Central Statistical Services said. The index was 196.3 points in May, compared with 176.4 a year earlier, and was up 1.1% from April's revised 194.1. April's year-to-year increase was 11.6%.

Producer prices for imported commodities rose 7.2% in May, compared with 10.1% in April, and prices of locally produced commodities rose 12.2% in May and 12% in April.
