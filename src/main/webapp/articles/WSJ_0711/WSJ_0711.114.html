Marketing Brief -- Paramount Communications Inc.: Movie Firm Plans to Boost Its Stake in TVX Broadcast

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

Paramount Communications Inc. said it will increase its holdings in TVX Broadcast Group Inc. and may seek to acquire the remaining minority interest in the Virginia Beach, Va., television station owner. Paramount paid about $110 million for a 79% stake in TVX late last year, when it also acquired $30 million in TVX debt obligations. Paramount said it agreed to purchase an additional 757,852 TVX shares from Citicorp Venture Capital Ltd. and First Capital Corp. of Chicago for $7.50 a share, increasing its holdings to 83.3%. Paramount, a New York-based movie and TV entertainment concern, said it would offer to acquire the remaining shares at the same $7.50 price.

Paramount last year exercised its option to purchase the TVX stake in a move seen as an expansion into broadcasting.

With about seven million TVX shares outstanding on a fully diluted basis, purchase of the remaining shares would cost about $52 million at the $7.50 a share price.
