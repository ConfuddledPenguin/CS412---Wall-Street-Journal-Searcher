International Brief -- Jacobs Suchard AG: Philip Morris Denies Rumor On Confectionary Unit Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

Philip Morris Cos. plans substantial investments in building up the confectionary and coffee businesses of Jacobs Suchard AG, according to a vice president of the New York company's Kraft foods subsidiary. "We will invest heavily (in Suchard) in the upcoming years," said Luc E. Vandevelde in defending as "fair and attractive" the takeover offer that officially was made yesterday to shareholders of the Swiss company.

He dismissed as an unfounded rumor that Philip Morris is considering selling off the Suchard confectionary division. "It would be totally against the spirit of the deal," the Kraft executive insisted. "We will sell to nobody."

The acquisition -- valued at nearly seven billion Swiss francs (about $5 billion) -- was announced last month and is part of Philip Morris's international expansion strategy.
