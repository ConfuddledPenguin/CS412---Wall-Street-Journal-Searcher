Letters to the Editor: You Forgot Something

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

The May 24 "Your Money Matters" column on insurance policies discusses commissionable life-insurance policies vs. noncommissionable policies, but fails to say where the agent who sells the non-commissionable policy is going to be compensated, unless missionaries will sell the policy. Obviously, the person who sells or recommends the policy with no commissions will have to charge a fee. You make no mention of these fees. Duncan E. Skogsberg

Manager

Life Insurance Department

Euclid Insurance Agencies Inc.

Elmhurst, Ill.
