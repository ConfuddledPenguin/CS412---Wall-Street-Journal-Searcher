Commercial Paper Rates Rise at Citicorp's Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.21% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.16% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $390 million were submitted. Accepted bids were at 8.21%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate rose to 8.16% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.092% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $380 million were submitted. Accepted bids were at 8.16%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
