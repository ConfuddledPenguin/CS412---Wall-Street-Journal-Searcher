Incompatible Programming

Romantically, we wed in June,

A gilt-edged bridal carriage,

But strife became apparent soon --

You see, 'twas a mixed marriage.

It's difficult, I will not lie,

Though my good wife's a gem,

She loves her Macintosh, while my

System's IBM.

-- Bob Sack.
