Fedders Corp. Plans Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

PEAPACK, N.J. -- Fedders Corp. plans to acquire the Emerson Quiet Kool room air conditioner division of Jepson Corp. Fedders, which makes room air conditioners, declined to disclose terms. Jepson is a subsidiary of Great American Management & Investment Inc. Great American Management, based in Chicago, has interests in real estate, financial services, manufacturing, agricultural chemicals and fertilizers.

The acquisition is subject to regulatory clearance, Fedders said, adding that in 1989, Emerson sold $120 million of dehumidifiers and air conditioners for rooms and recreational vehicles.
