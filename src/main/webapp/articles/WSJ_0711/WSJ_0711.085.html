Who's News: Waste Management Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/11/90

WASTE MANAGEMENT Inc. (Oak Brook, Ill.) -- D.P. Payne, currently a vice president and Midwest area general manager for International Business Machines Corp., was named a senior vice president at this waste-handling concern, effective Aug. 1. Mr. Payne, 47 years old, will become one of three senior vice presidents at the company; in the newly created post, he will be responsible for Waste Management's strategic planning, sales and marketing, information systems and quality control. Separately, Waste Management named Thomas C. Hau, 54, currently a partner at the accounting firm of Arthur Andersen & Co., as vice president, controller, effective Sept. 1. In that post, he succeeds Jerry Girsch, who remains a vice president and now will serve as controller for Waste Management's North American operation, in addition to his other operating duties.
