FCC Puts at Three Months Time to Review MCI Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

WASHINGTON -- The Federal Communications Commission clarified that the review of MCI Communications Corp.'s proposed acquisition of Telecom USA Inc. will take about three months, unless opponents raised "valid objections." The agency yesterday indicated that approval of the friendly acquisition was expected to take a minimum of six months. An FCC spokeswoman said MCI and Telecom USA have yet to file data on their plan with the commission.

In addition to FCC approval, the merger requires clearance from Telecom USA shareholders, the Justice Department and regulatory agencies in states where both companies operate.
