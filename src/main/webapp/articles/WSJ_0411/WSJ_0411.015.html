Mesaba Aviation Traffic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Mesaba Aviation Inc., a unit of AirTran Corp., said its March traffic rose 31% to 14.6 million revenue passenger miles from 11.2 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The carrier, which operates as Northwest Airlink, said its load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, fell to 50.5% from 55.8%.
