Who's News: W.R. Grace & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

W.R. GRACE & Co. (New York) -- Bernd A. Schulte was appointed group executive of General Development Group, the strategic planning arm of this international specialty chemical company. He succeeds Edward T. Harvey, 41 years old, who recently resigned to pursue other business interests. Mr. Schulte, 47, a corporate vice president, most recently was responsible for special projects, reporting to the office of the chairman. He will continue in that post.
