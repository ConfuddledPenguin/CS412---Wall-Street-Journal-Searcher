Who's News: Hawkeye Bancorp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

HAWKEYE BANCORP. (Des Moines, Iowa) -- Robert W. Murray was elected president and chief operating officer of this Iowa bank holding company. Mr. Murray, 55 years old, had been executive vice president. He succeeds Paul Dunlap, 59 years old, as president. Mr. Dunlap remains chairman and chief executive officer.
