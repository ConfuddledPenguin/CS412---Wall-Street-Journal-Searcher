German Construction Rising

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

MUNICH -- The West German construction industry will grow faster than gross national product and expand between 4% and 5% this year, the Ifo-Institut for Economic Research said. The research institute, which earlier this week forecast that GNP will grow 3.75% this year, said the number of new hires in the West German construction industry will jump to between 20,000 and 30,000 this year. It said that the construction industry is in the third year of a powerful expansion and that the growth can't be covered through more overtime and increases in productivity.

The construction industry is being propelled primarily by a surge in housing construction and construction related to the country's bouyant economy, the institute said.
