REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Non-Bank Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

You've heard about the budget crisis in New York State. This is what it has come to: The AP reports that a retired postal worker outbid four banks to loan Genesee County $110,000 for one year at 5.82%. There may be a financing innovation in there somewhere, but insofar as taxes are going up in many states, we suppose someone will see this as one way to make government pay part of the tax bill.
