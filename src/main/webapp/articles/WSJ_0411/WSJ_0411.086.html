Rhone Extends Bid for Rorer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

PARIS -- Rhone-Poulenc S.A. extended the expiration date of its tender offer for the shares of Rorer Group Inc. until noon EDT May 4. The French chemicals and pharmaceuticals concern said the offer was extended to coincide with a Rorer shareholders meeting, at which holders will consider amendments to Rorer's charter that are a condition of the offer. Rorer also is a pharmaceuticals concern.
