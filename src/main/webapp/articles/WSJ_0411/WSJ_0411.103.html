Canada Bond Yield Announced

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government announced an average yield of 12.54% at an auction of 900 million Canadian dollars (US$774.8 million) of 11.75% bonds due Oct. 15, 1993. Proceeds of the issue will be applied toward the redemption of C$2.13 billion of government bonds maturing May 1.
