Norway's Consumer Prices Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

OSLO -- Norway's consumer prices rose 1.1% in March from February and were up 4.5% from a year earlier, the Central Bureau of Statistics announced. The new index figure was 229.8 as of March 15, up from 227.2 Feb. 15 and up from 219.8 as of March 15, 1989 (1979 equals 100).

Contributing most to last month's increase was the index group for clothes and shoes, which rose 4.9% from the previous month, the bureau said.
