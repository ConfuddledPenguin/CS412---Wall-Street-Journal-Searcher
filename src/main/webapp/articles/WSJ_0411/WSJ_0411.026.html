Citicorp's Auction Rates On Commercial Paper Fall

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.213% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs. The rate was 8.244% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $451 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.208% to 8.218%.

Citicorp also said the average rate fell to 8.1595% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.246% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $425 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.159% to 8.16%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity Tuesday.
