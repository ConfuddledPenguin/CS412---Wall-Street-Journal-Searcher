French Steel Output Down

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

PARIS -- French companies produced 1,760,000 metric tons of crude steel in March, down 0.6% from a year earlier, according to industry data released yesterday. The National Steel Manufacturers Association said the March results bring French output for the first three months of the year to 4,972,000 tons, down 2.6% from the like 1989 period.
