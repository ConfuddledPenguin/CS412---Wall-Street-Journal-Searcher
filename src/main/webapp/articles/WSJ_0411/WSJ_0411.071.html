Who's News: BEI Holdings Ltd. Restructures Managers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

ATLANTA -- BEI Holdings Ltd. said it completed a top management restructuring following last year's acquisition of Golembe Associates Inc., and consolidated its East Coast and West Coast consulting operations. The financial institution consulting company named Nevins D. Baxter to the new position of vice chairman of BEI Holdings. Mr. Baxter, 48 years old, was formerly chairman of Golembe Associates.

Thomas M. McGrath has been named to the new post of chairman and chief executive officer of BEI Golembe Consulting, the company's primary consulting subsidiary. Mr. McGrath, 41, was previously president of the company's West Coast consulting operation.

Peter Merrill has been named president of BEI Golembe Consulting, also a new position. The 54-year-old Mr. Merrill will take on national responsibility for sales and implementation of consulting services, and business development.
