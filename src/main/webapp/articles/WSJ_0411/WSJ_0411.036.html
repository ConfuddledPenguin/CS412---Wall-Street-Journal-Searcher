Business Brief -- Martech USA: Czechoslovakia Cleanup Job For the Environment Is Set

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

Martech USA said it signed a letter of intent with Czechoslovakia's new government to conduct environmental cleanup of evacuated Soviet military bases and other environmentally sensitive areas. The company said it is still holding discussions with the government over terms of the transaction.

Martech, an environmental cleanup company based in Anchorage, Alaska, said it will form a joint venture with Geo Test, a Czechoslovakian engineering and hydrology company.

Martech said there are more than 165 Russian bases and other contaminated sites in Czechoslovakia. It said it will begin work April 25 in Frenstat, at the first base to be evacuated by Soviet troops.
