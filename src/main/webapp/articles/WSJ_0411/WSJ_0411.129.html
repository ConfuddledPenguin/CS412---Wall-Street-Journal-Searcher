Who's News: American Health Services Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

AMERICAN HEALTH SERVICES Corp. (Newport Beach, Calif.) -- Sandra E. Stiles, Joseph A. Boystak, and Roger Kimmel, all designees of OmniCorp Holdings Inc., were elected to the board of this operator of diagnostic-imaging centers. American Health agreed to the three designees as part of OmniCorp's $7 million infusion to American Health in exchange for 29% of American Health's stock. American Health says the OmniCorp designees don't plan any real changes in the way the company is operated or its goals. OmniCorp is a unit of Omni Holding AG, a Swiss financial and industrial-services concern. The designees fill three of four vacancies left on the seven-member board by the resignation of Gerald Shefsky, Herman Becker-Fluegel, Garry W. Bond and David H. Brown. The company said it will fill the fourth vacancy within the next few months. American Health also said that S. Lewis Meyer, president and chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of chairman, succeeding Mr. Shefsky, who resigned a month ago.
