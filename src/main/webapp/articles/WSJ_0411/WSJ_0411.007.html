Site Unseen?

Good advice in hunting homes

The bright suburban way

Is: Learn what you are looking for --

Ranchero? Cute Chalet?

Casita? Vivienda? One

At Dockside? Villa West?

Hilltop? Windsong? Great Tree Farms?

All different from the rest?

To buy, you must decide by name --

Don't waver, don't retreat;

No subdivision offers you

A house along a street.

-- Ralph Shaffer.
