Arkla Exploration Tests Well

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

SHREVEPORT, La. -- Arkla Exploration said it tested a new natural-gas and oil-discovery well, its third successful rank wildcat in the Mississippi-Louisiana region this year and its 10th there since 1987. The Neyland Heirs No.1 well, in Amite County, Miss., just north of the Louisiana line, flowed at the rate of 360 barrels of oil and 458,000 cubic feet of natural gas a day through a 10/64-inch opening. It was completed at depths between 12,041 and 12,051 feet in the Lower Tuscaloosa formation.
