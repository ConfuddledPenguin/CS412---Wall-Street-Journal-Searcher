Dresser Acquires British Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

DALLAS -- Dresser Industries Inc. said it acquired Mono Group Ltd., a British company that supplies pumps to the sewage, mining, paper, sugar and chemical industries. The purchase price wasn't disclosed. Mono, based in Manchester, England, has annual sales of about $75 million. It operates facilities in Manchester and Melbourne, Australia. Dresser, which supplies products and services to the energy industry, will merge Mono into its pump division.
