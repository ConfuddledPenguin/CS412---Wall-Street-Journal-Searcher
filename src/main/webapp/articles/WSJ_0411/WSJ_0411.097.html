Jerrico Inc. Holders Meeting

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

LEXINGTON, Ky. -- Jerrico Inc. said a shareholders meeting will be held May 11 to vote on its previously announced acquisition by New York investors. Jerrico, which operates a chain of seafood fast-food restaurants, set April 9 as the record date for those shareholders entitled to vote on the acquisition.

The investor group has purchased 19.1 million shares, or about 98% of the shares outstanding, for $24.25 a share.
