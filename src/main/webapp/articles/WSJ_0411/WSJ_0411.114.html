Business Brief -- Mellon Bank Corp.: Firm Will Buy AmeriTrust ATM Processing Operation

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

Mellon Bank Corp., Pittsburgh, said it agreed to acquire AmeriTrust Corp.'s automated teller machine processing business. Terms weren't disclosed. Under the agreement, Mellon will offer ATM processing services to 19 financial institutions previously serviced by Ameritrust through the InstaNet regional network.

Mellon will also provide the network line between AmeriTrust's ASSET network and the InstaNet shared regional network. AmeriTrust will continue to operate the 270 ATM ASSET network.

The InstaNet network has 441 ATMs in the Cleveland area, with about eight million transactions a year.

AmeriTrust, a bank holding company based in Cleveland, said the company decided to leave the ATM processing business to concentrate on its retail business.

Mellon, a bank holding company, has been expanding its ATM processing business. Last month, it acquired the New England ATM transaction processing business from State Street Bank & Trust Co.
