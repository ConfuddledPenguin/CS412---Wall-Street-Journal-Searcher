CB&T Financial to Buy Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

FAIRMONT, W.Va. -- CB&T Financial Corp. said it agreed to acquire Peoples National Bank of Rowlesburg, W.Va. Terms weren't disclosed. Peoples National Bank has assets of $12 million. CB&T has assets of about $500 million.

The agreement is subject to regulatory and shareholder approvals.
