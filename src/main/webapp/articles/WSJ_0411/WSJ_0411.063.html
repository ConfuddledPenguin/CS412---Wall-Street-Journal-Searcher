Treasury Plans to Sell $16.4 Billion of Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to pay down about $26.9 billion on the federal debt with the sale Monday of about $16.4 billion in short-term bills. Maturing bills outstanding total $43.3 billion. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills, which will be dated April 19 and will mature on July 19 and Oct. 18, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
