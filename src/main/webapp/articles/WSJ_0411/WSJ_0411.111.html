Japanese Machine Orders Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

TOKYO -- Machinery orders received by Japanese companies in February rose 29.4% from a year earlier to 1.659 trillion yen ($10.48 billion), the Economic Planning Agency said. The gain, following a 0.2% year-on-year dip in December and an 11.3% increase in January, resulted from substantial increases of orders in all categories, an agency official said. With the exception of December 1989, machinery orders have been rising since October 1988.
