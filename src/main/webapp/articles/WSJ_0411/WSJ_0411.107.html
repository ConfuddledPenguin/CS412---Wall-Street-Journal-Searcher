International Brief -- Dresdner Bank AG: Partial Operating Earnings Increased 17% Last Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

Citing "continuing favorable economic developments," Dresdner Bank AG said its earnings in 1989 grew strongly. The West German commercial banking group said its group partial operating profit rose 17% to 1.88 billion marks ($1.11 billion) from 1.60 billion marks ($944 million) a year earlier.

On a group basis, the company said that the full operating profit climbed at a higher rate than the partial operating profit because of higher earnings from trading for its own account. The bank didn't provide any data on full operating profit.

Partial operating profit excludes profit or losses from trading for its own account. Group results contain all of the bank's world-wide activities.
