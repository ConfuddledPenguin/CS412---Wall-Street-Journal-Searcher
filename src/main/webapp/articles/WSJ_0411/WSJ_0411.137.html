Business Brief -- Lomas Financial Corp.: Mortgage-Service Portfolio To Be Bought by Subsidiary

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

Lomas Financial Corp., Dallas, said its Lomas Mortgage USA subsidiary agreed to buy the $3.5 billion mortgage-servicing portfolio of Bright Mortgage Co. from Resolution Trust Corp. A spokeswoman said the government agency will receive $33 million in cash for the portfolio, leaving it with $100 million in Bright Mortgage assets still to liquidate. It said Lomas was one of three bidders for the portfolio.

Lomas said the purchase will increase its mortgage-servicing portfolio by 17% to $24.5 billion from $21 billion and marks its largest acquisition in recent years.

Lomas Financial, which is operating under protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, said it already has received court approval for the transaction. It said the move will allow its core mortgage-banking business to continue to grow.

Bright Mortgage previously was a unit of Bright Banc, a Dallas-based savings and loan that was sold by regulators in early February to Banc One Corp. of Columbus, Ohio.
