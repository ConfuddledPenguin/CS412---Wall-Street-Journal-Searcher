Who's News: Chock Full O'Nuts Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

CHOCK FULL O'NUTS Corp. (New York) -- David S. Weil was elected to the board of this coffee and food-services concern, increasing board membership to 11. Mr. Weil, 65 years old, is president and chief executive officer of Ampacet Corp., a plastics raw-material producer.
