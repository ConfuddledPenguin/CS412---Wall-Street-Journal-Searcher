China's Industrial Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

BEIJING -- China' slumping economy recorded zero growth in industrial output during the first quarter, although some signs of recovery were seen in March. State Statistical Bureau figures, reported in the Chinese official press, also showed that smaller township and private enterprises continued to expand, while state-run enterprises showed declining output and productivity.

Industrial output for March was up 1.4% from the like month in 1989. Output in state enterprises was the same as last year, while collectives showed 0.7% growth, township enterprises 5.4% and others, mainly small private firms, 32.3%.
