Technology Brief -- Lotus Development Corp.: Product for Apple Computer Will Create Marketing Lists

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

Lotus Development Corp. said it will make a new product for Apple Computer Inc.'s Macintosh personal computers that combines compact-disk, read-only memory data bases on U.S. businesses and households with software for creating marketing lists. Available in the third quarter of 1990, Lotus MarketPlace, priced at $695, will be distributed through computer software retailers.

Lotus, based in Cambridge, Mass., and Apple, in Cupertino, Calif., also announced an agreement under which the companies will market the product through a number of cooperative programs during the year.
