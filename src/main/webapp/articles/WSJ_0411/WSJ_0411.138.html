International Brief -- Hachette S.A.: Consolidated Net, Revenue For Last Year Show Gains

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

The publishing group Hachette S.A. said its consolidated profit after payments to minority interests rose 43% to 469 million French francs ($83.3 million) in 1989 from 328 million francs a year earlier. The Paris-based group noted, however, that nonrecurring items accounted for a big portion of the gain, and said its earnings excluding these elements would have been 331 million francs, up 2.5% from 323 million francs in 1988. Hachette's consolidated 1989 revenue was 29.06 billion francs, up 19% from 24.41 billion francs in 1988.

The adjusted figures are in line with the group's previous forecasts that financial costs associated with its acquisition of the U.S. publishing groups Grolier Inc. and Diamandis Communications Inc. would leave 1989 earnings little changed from a year earlier.

Following the earnings improvement, Hachette said it plans to boost its net annual dividend to 3.90 francs a share from 3.30 francs. Payment and record dates weren't specified.
