Dividend Brief -- Crompton & Knowles Corp.: Firm Boosts Payout by 35% For Third Year in a Row

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/11/90

Crompton & Knowles Corp. directors boosted the quarterly dividend 35%, to 21 cents a share from 15.5 cents, payable May 25 to stock of record May 4. The move marks the third consecutive year in which the board of the specialty chemicals and equipment producer has raised the payout 35%.

The board is confident that the company can "sustain the momentum of the last several years," Crompton & Knowles said. At March 31, the company had 11.9 million shares outstanding.
