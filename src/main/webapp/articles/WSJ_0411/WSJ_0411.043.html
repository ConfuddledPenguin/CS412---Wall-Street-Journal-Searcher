  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

MAGAZINES: Reader's Digest Association Inc. will launch Budgets Famille next October through its French subsidiary. The monthly magazine, designed to help families manage their finances, is the company's first magazine start-up in France since it began publishing a French-language edition of Reader's Digest in 1947.

WHO'S NEWS: Nancy Klopper was named senior vice president, production, for Act III Communications Inc.'s Act III Productions division. She was vice president, talent and artist relations, at Twentieth Century Fox. . . . MGM/UA Communications Co. named Leanna J. Heath to the new post of vice president, senior production counsel. She had been a partner with Bushkin, Gaims, Gaines & Jones.
