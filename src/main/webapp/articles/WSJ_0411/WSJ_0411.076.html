GMAC to Redeem Debentures

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Acceptance Corp., the financial arm of General Motors Corp., said it will exercise its option to redeem on June 1, 1990, all of its 12% debentures outstanding due to mature June 1, 2005. The debentures will be redeemed at 102% of their principal amount. The debentures were sold in June 1980 in the amount of $250,000,000, of which $119,144,000 are currently outstanding.
