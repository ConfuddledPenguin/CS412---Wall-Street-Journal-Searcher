Who's News: Rally's Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

RALLY'S Inc. (Louisville, Ky.) -- Wayne M. Albritton, Rally's executive vice president, operations, and Mary Hart, co-host of the television show Entertainment Tonight, were elected directors of this fast-food chain. They succeed William E. Trotter II and James M. Trotter III, two Rally's franchisees, who resigned from the board.
