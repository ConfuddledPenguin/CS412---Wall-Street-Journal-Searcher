Du Pont Agrees to Sell 3 Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/11/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Du Pont Co. said it reached agreements to sell three process and instruments units in its polymer products department as part of a strategy to focus on core businesses. Terms weren't disclosed. The company signed a letter of intent to sell its Glasgow, Del., thermal analyzer business to a new company led by management and Chemical Venture Partners, an affiliate of Chemical Banking Corp.

Two other businesses-process instruments and air and noise monitoring instruments-will be sold to Ametek Inc., a Paoli, Pa., industrial manufacturer.
