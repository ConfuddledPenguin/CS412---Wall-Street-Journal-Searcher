IN AN ARTICLE on the deregulation of the London stock market, published in October 1986, this newspaper reported on a telefax published by a London research firm, Global Analysis Systems Limited. The Journal did not intend to imply that the telefax was produced other than as a product of GAS's research and analysis, or that GAS had any improper motive for distributing it. (See: "British Jitters: London's Exchange Braces for Big Bang Set to Occur Monday --- Deregulation Will Increase Competition, but That Is About All That Is Certain --- Prospect of Slump, Scandal" -- WSJ Oct. 24, 1986)
