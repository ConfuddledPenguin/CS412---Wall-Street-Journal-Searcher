Who's News: Polly Peck International PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

POLLY PECK INTERNATIONAL PLC (London) -- Robert W. Fisher, 41 years old, resigned after five months as chief operating officer of PPI Del Monte Tropical Fruit Co., a Coral Gables, Fla.-based unit of this holding company. Mr. Fisher, in a statement, said Polly Peck has dropped the product line expansions and food-business reorganization for which he was hired. Mr. Fisher, who was previously president of the Dole Food Co. unit of Castle & Cooke Inc., didn't elaborate on his plans. PPI said it doesn't plan to replace him.
