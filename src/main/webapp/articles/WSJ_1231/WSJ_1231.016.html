Hadson Energy Acquires Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

OKLAHOMA CITY -- Hadson Energy Resources Corp. said it acquired Baruch-Foster Corp. for $16.9 million. The energy concern said Dallas-based Baruch-Foster adds an estimated 2.3 million barrels of oil and 9.5 billion cubic feet of gas to its reserves.
