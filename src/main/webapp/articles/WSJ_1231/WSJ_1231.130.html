Business Brief -- Ventura County National Bancorp: Fourth-Quarter Net Is Put Slightly Above 1989 Figure

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

Ventura County National Bancorp, Oxnard, Calif., expects to report net income for the fourth quarter of about $698,000, or 13 cents a share, on revenue of about $9.4 million, W.E. McAleer, president and chief executive officer, said. The company had net income in the year-ago quarter of $686,000, or 13 cents a share, on revenue of $8.5 million.

The company paid a 10% stock dividend in February 1990. At the end of this year, there were 4.8 million common shares outstanding, compared with 4.3 million at the end of last year.

Ventura operates Ventura County National Bank and Frontier Bank and both banks cater primarily to medium-size businesses. The banks have a total of eight branches in Ventura County and Orange County, Calif.

For the year, Mr. McAleer said Ventura expects to report net income of $3.4 million to $3.6 million, or 71 cents to 73 cents a share, on revenue of $40.8 million. A year earlier, the company reported net income of $2.8 million, or 71 cents a share, on revenue of $28.4 million.
