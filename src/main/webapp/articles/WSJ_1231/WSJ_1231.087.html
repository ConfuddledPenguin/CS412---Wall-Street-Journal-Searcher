Who's News: FMS Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

FMS FINANCIAL Corp. (Burlington, N.J.) -- Craig W. Yates, a board member and major shareholder, was named president and chief executive officer of FMS and Farmers and Mechanics' Savings Bank, a wholly owned subsidiary. Mr. Yates, 49 years old, succeeds Michael D. Bisceglia, 55, who will continue as a member of the board of directors. The naming of Mr. Yates is part of a reorganization at the savings bank, which reported a loss of $1.06 million, or 82 cents a share for the nine months ended Sept. 30. Mr. Yates, who holds a 9.9% stake in the bank, stressed that Farmers and Mechanics continues to meet all regulatory and capital requirements, despite a "difficult year for real estate values in New Jersey."
