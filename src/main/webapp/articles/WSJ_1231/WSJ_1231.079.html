Who's News: IE Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

IE INDUSTRIES Inc. (Cedar Rapids, Iowa) -- Blake O. Fisher Jr., formerly vice president, finance, and treasurer for Consumers Power Co., was named executive vice president and chief financial officer of this utility holding concern. As chief financial officer, Mr. Fisher, 46 years old, succeeds J.B. Rehnstrom, 60, who remains a director and was named to the newly created post of senior vice president, special projects, and corporate secretary.
