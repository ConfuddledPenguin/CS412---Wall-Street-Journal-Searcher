IBM Telecommunications Hub

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

CANBERRA, Australia -- International Business Machines Corp. plans to establish a major telecommunications networking hub for the Asia-Pacific region in Australia. The move would see the Armonk, N.Y., computer company double its existing Sydney-based networking capacity over the next two years. Regional hubs provide a secure and cost-effective way for international companies to channel their business data, computer communications and telephone traffic throughout a region and around the world.
