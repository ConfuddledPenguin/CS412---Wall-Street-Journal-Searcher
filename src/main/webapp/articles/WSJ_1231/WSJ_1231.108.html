Business Brief -- American Recreation Centers Inc.: Purchase of 400,000 Shares By ESOP Cleared by Board

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

American Recreation Centers Inc. said its board authorized the purchase of 400,000 shares by its Employee Stock Ownership Plan. The transaction was financed by a loan to the employee organization from the company and will be repaid over five years from annual contributions made to the organization by the company. The company said it used its credit line to secure the loan.

The Sacramento, Calif., company said it purchases stock for its members from time to time on the open market and added that it will continue to do so.

American Recreation Centers owns 22 bowling centers in California and five in Texas. It also owns Right Start Catalog Inc., a direct marketer of products for infants and children.
