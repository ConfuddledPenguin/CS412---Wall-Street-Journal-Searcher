Business Brief -- Numerica Financial Corp.: Thrift Unit Under Order To Tighten Lending Rules

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

Numerica Financial Corp. said its Numerica Savings Bank unit has received a cease-and-desist order obliging the bank to tighten lending policies and raise about $40 million in capital by June 1991. The Manchester, N.H., thrift is ordered to strengthen lending standards, especially in commerical real estate, where it has experienced most of its problems as the New England econony has continued to sag.

A cease-and-desist order, considered the most serious regulatory step short of seizure, obligates the bank's board to ensure changes are made by the institution's management.

In the third quarter, Numerica's loss widened to $4.1 million, or 93 cents a share, from $1 million, or 23 cents a share, a year earlier.
