Initial Jobless Claims Declined in Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment fell to a seasonally adjusted 441,000 the week ended Dec. 15 from 455,000 the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended Dec. 8 was a seasonally adjusted 3,006,000, or 2.9% of those covered by unemployment insurance, up from 2,953,000 the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate was 2.8%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended Dec. 8 rose to 3,065,600 from 2,991,900 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
