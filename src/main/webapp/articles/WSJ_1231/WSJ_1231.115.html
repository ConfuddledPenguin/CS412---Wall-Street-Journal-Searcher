Marketing Brief -- Tele-Communications Inc.: Heritage Unit, Parent Slate Consolidation of Cable Units

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

Heritage Communications Inc. of Des Moines, Iowa, and its parent, Tele-Communications Inc. of Denver, said they will consolidate their cable television operations in the 1991 first quarter. The consolidation, which will result in the loss of an unspecified number of jobs at Heritage headquarters, is taking place earlier than initially planned. When Tele-Communications acquired Heritage in 1987, the companies said they would manage their cable operations independently for a five-year transition period. But in announcing the consolidation, officials said planning had proceeded more rapidly than expected.

J.C. Sparkman, executive vice president and chief operating officer at Tele-Communications, said he hopes virtually all Heritage employees will remain with Tele-Communications after the consolidation. He also said the company will continue using the Heritage name. Heritage has about 1,800 employees nationally, 90 of them in the Iowa headquarters.
