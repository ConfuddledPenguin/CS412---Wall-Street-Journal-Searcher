Financing Business: Magma Power Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

MAGMA POWER Co., San Diego, said J.P. Morgan & Co., a New York-based banking company, exercised a warrant to buy one million shares of Magma common stock for $14 each, or $14 million. The operator of geothermal power plants issued the warrant to Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. of New York in 1987 in connection with a financing agreement. The warrant was to have expired today.
