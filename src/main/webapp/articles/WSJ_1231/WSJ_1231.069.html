REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Parting Is Sweet Sorrow

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

Governments aren't used to cutting back, so the reduction in legislative staffing that was part of California's Prop. 140 term-limit initiative is terra incognita. The solons are taking good care of the help scheduled to depart by July 1. The state Senate Rules Committee has authorized five months' severance pay for employees of two or more years, and three months' lump sum for those who've worked for a year. Generous indeed by private-sector standards, but we'd expect nothing less.
