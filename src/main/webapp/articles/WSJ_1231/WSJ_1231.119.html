Business Brief -- Occidental Petroleum Corp.: Oil Firm Moves 51% Stake In IBP to a Holding Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

Occidental Petroleum Corp. said it transferred its 51% stake in IBP Inc. to a holding company called Occidental Agribusiness Investments Inc. A spokesman declined to comment on whether Occidental transferred the 24 million IBP shares in preparation for a sale of its stake in the giant Dakota City, Neb., beef and pork packer.

Since Occidental's patriarch, Armand Hammer, died earlier this month, analysts have been speculating that the new regime, led by Ray R. Irani, will dispose of several sacred cows acquired under Dr. Hammer's reign -- chief among them IBP, where the operations contrast oddly with the Los Angeles company's primary interests in petroleum and chemicals.

In a statement, Occidental said the transfer of shares to Occidental Agribusiness from Occidental Petroleum Investment Co. "conforms the corporate structure of Occidental's interest in IBP to that of other operating divisions of Occidental and does not represent any change in the ownership."
