Financing Business: Homeplex Mortgage Investments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

HOMEPLEX MORTGAGE INVESTMENTS Corp., Phoenix, Ariz., said it obtained a $50 million, three-year term loan and will resume paying quarterly dividends in the first quarter of 1991. The real estate investment trust, which didn't disclose the amount of the next dividend, last paid a dividend of 50 cents on Jan. 30, 1990. The company said it used the first $20 million of proceeds, plus other short-term borrowings of $5.7 million, to repay $25.7 million of indebtedness outstanding under its existing loan agreement maturing on Feb. 1. The company plans to use additional proceeds from the loan to repay the remainder of its short-term indebtedness. The term loan will reduce the company's overall borrowing costs, Homeplex said.
