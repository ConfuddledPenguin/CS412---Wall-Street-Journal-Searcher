Energy Service Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

DALLAS -- Energy Service Co. said it agreed to acquire from Argosy Offshore Ltd., a Texas partnership, 20 supply and utility vessels serving the Gulf of Mexico oil industry. Energy Service will pay $26.3 million in cash and as much as $5 million in merchandise credits from its supply division. The acquisition would increase the contract drilling and oil service company's fleet to 38 vessels. The Argosy vessels are all chartered exclusively to Chevron Corp.
