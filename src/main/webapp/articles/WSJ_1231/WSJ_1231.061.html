Who's News: Home Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

HOME FINANCIAL Corp. (Johnson City, Tenn.) -- Jack L. Myers, 55 years old, an attorney who has been general counsel to this savings and loan holding company since 1966, was elected to the board. He fills one of the vacancies created last fall when William L. Jenkins resigned to become a judge and W. Kyle Huddle resigned after 30 years as a director.
