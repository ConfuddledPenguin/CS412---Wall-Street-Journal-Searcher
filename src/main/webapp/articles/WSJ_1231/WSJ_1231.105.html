Business Brief -- HQ Office Supplies Warehouse: Board Agrees to Complete Merger With Former Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

HQ Office Supplies Warehouse Inc. said its board reached "an agreement in principle" with its affiliate and former subsidiary, HQ Office International Inc., to complete a merger proposed on Sept. 4. The operator of office supply stores currently owns 25% of HQ Office International. The transaction is subject to approval of the shareholders of HQ Office International, regulatory approvals, and other closing conditions. HQ Office Supplies said it expects the merger will be completed in early 1991.

The company also said it entered into "a definitive agreement" for the sale of substantially all its HQ Office Supplies Warehouse retail stores in Southern California to Staples Inc., and "an agreement in principle" to sell its stores in Northern California and Las Vegas, Nev., to BizMart Inc.

HQ Office Supplies said its "repositioning" is due in part to its inability to close financing on a $40 million private preferred equity placement during the fall of 1990. In addition, the company said it was hurt earlier this year by delays on the part of its underwriter.
