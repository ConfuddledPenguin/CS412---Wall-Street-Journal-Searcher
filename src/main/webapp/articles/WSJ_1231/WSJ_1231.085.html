Business Brief -- IVAX Corp.: Miami Company Acquires Harris Pharmaceuticals Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

IVAX Corp. said it completed the acquisition of Harris Pharmaceuticals Ltd. for 6,250,000 common shares in a transaction valued at $73.4 million. Harris makes and markets generic pharmaceuticals and has facilities in London and Waterford, Ireland. Miami-based IVAX is a holding company with interests in pharmaceuticals and medical diagnostics. Three operating officers of Harris-Isaac Kaye, Cyril Beck and Nigel Nortonwere appointed to IVAX's board.
