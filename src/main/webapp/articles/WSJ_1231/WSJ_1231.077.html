New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange and the American Stock Exchange each announced one new listing.

OEA Inc., Denver, Col., a maker of explosive devices used in missiles, aircraft and space vehicles, previously traded on the Amex, begins trading on the Big Board Friday under ticker symbol OEA.

Tolland Bank, Tolland, Conn., a state-chartered stock savings bank, previously traded on the national market system of the over-the-counter market, began trading on the Amex under TBK.

Options began trading at the Amex on three listed stocks: Jefferson-Pilot Corp., a holding company for subsidiaries providing insurance, financial and communications products and services, under JP; Lincoln National Corp., an insurance holding company, under LNC; and National City Corp., a bank holding company with branches in Ohio, Kentucky and Southern Indiana, under NCC.
