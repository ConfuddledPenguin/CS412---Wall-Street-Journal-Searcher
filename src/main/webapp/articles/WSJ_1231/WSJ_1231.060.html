United Technologies Wins Navy Contract For $151.2 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

WASHINGTON -- United Technologies Corp. received a $151.2 million Navy contract for SH-60f helicopters.

ITT Corp. won a $63.7 million Navy contract for ship radars.

Hughes Communications Services Inc., a unit of General Motors Corp., got a $48 million Navy contract for satellite communications.

Northern Telecom Inc. was awarded a $40 million Air Force contract for telephone systems.

Vitro Corp., a unit of Penn Central Corp., received a $30.1 million Navy contract for Trident Missile engineering services.

General Motors Corp. got a $23.2 million Navy contract for aircraft engines.

Raytheon Co. won a $18.6 million Army contract for Patriot missiles.

Lockheed Corp. was awarded a $15.7 million Navy contract for Trident missile equipment.

Honeywell Inc. received a $10.9 million Air Force contract for aircraft navigation equipment.

Grumman Corp. got a $10.7 million Navy contract for aircraft ejection seats.
