Business Brief -- Flagship Financial Corp.: Company Adds $4.7 Million To S&L Loan-Loss Reserves

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

Flagship Financial Corp. said it added $4.7 million to loan-loss reserves, resulting in losses for the quarter of about $3 million, or 86 cents a share, and $13 million, or $3.75 a share, for the year. The Jenkintown, Pa., thrift company, which owns First American Savings and Brandywine Savings Bank, had net income of $1.4 million, or 41 cents a share, in the year-earlier fourth quarter. For all of 1989, Flagship had net of $6.1 million, or $1.75 a share. The results included pretax gains on the sales of loans and securities.

The company said about $2 million of the addition to reserves is related to a $6.4 million loan for the construction of a commercial office building. Flagship classified the loan as nonperforming as of June 30.
