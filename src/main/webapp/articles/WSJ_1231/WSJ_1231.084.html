Disclosure Inc. Agrees To Buy Bechtel Corp. Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

BETHESDA, Md. -- Disclosure Inc. said it reached an agreement in principle to purchase Bechtel Information Services Inc. The acquisition would make Disclosure, a U.S. unit of Dutch publisher VNU N.V., the contract provider of corporate filings with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

The companies refused to disclose the terms of the agreement, which won't be closed until next month. Bechtel Information Services is a unit of Bechtel Corp., the large San Francisco-based engineering concern. Bechtel Information helps run the SEC's Electronic Data Gathering Analysis and Retrieval (EDGAR) project, operates the SEC's public-reference facilities in Chicago, New York and Washington; and fulfills public requests for filings made with the SEC.

Disclosure already provides public information on 12,000 U.S. companies and operates a retrieval service for federal and state legislative, regulatory and judicial documents.
