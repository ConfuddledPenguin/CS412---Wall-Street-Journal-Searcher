International Brief -- Thai Airways International: Airline Seeks to List Shares On the Bangkok Exchange

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

Thai Airways International, Thailand's flag carrier, has applied to list its shares on the Bangkok stock exchange. The government had given the airline permission to raise $118.6 million of capital, Air Chief Marshal Kaset Rochananil told a news conference.

Mr. Kaset said Thai Air expected to raise domestic fares by 15% on main routes by the middle of January. International fares have already been raised 7% in November to cover higher fuel costs.
