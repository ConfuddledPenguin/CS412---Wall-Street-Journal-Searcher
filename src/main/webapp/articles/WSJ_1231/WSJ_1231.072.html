Who's News: Ameriana Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

AMERIANA BANCORP (New Castle, Ind.) -- Paul W. Prior, chairman and chief executive officer of this banking concern and its subsidiaries, will retire effective today. Mr. Prior, 69 years old, had been chairman since 1973. Ameriana's board appointed Harry J. Bailey, now president and chief operating officer of the company, to the additional post of chief executive.
