Who's News: Midlantic's McDonald Named Chief of Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

EDISON, N.J. -- Midlantic Corp. said Desmond P. McDonald, president of its Midlantic National Bank unit, will assume the additional post of chief executive officer, effective tomorrow. Mr. McDonald, 63 years old, takes over the post from Robert Van Buren, 65, who remains chairman and chief executive for the holding company.

"For almost 15 years, I have held these duel responsibilities and, in light of current economic conditions and looking to the future, it is appropriate that my emphasis and attention be dedicated to the overall performance of Midlantic Corporation," said Mr. Van Buren in a statement.

Mr. McDonald has been president of the banking unit since 1976, and in recent years has been assuming more day-to-day executive responsibilities of the bank.

Midlantic National Bank, with $9 billion in assets, is the lead bank of Midlantic Corp., a $24 billion bank holding company operating in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and New York.
