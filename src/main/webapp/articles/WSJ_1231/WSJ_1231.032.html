International: French Consumer Prices Fall

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

PARIS -- France's consumer-price index fell 0.2% in November from October, the National Statistics Institute reported. The decline follows a rise of 0.5% in October and leaves the year-to-year inflation rate at 3.6%, down from 3.9% in October.

The index, which is based on 1980 equaling 100, stood at 186.4, down from 186.7 in October and 180.0 a year earlier.
