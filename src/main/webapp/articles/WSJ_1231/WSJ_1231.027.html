Temperature Tantrum

We who dwell in the Northland

Know all that winter brings:

The white snowfall and rosy cheeks

And the bracing wind that sings.

Those who live in the Southland

Miss all this in their mellow milieu,

So do we envy our Southern friends?

You bet your life we do]

-- George O. Ludcke.
