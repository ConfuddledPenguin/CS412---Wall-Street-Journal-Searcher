FDP Expects to Post A Profit of $193,000 For Fourth Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

MIAMI -- FDP Corp. expects to report fourth-quarter net income of about $193,000, or six cents a share, compared with a year-earlier net loss of $53,835, or two cents a share, said Michael C. Goldberg, president and chief executive officer. The company, which makes and markets software systems for the pension and life-insurance industries, said revenue in the fourth quarter ended Nov. 30 rose 22% to about $4.5 million from $3.7 million.

"Now we're starting to see the results of development that we were putting into place over the last few years," Mr. Goldberg said, adding that he expects the trend of higher revenue and earnings to continue through 1991.

Mr. Goldberg said the company's profit margin widened to 20% from 9% a year earlier. He said selling, general and administrative expenses held steady at about $785,000 in the quarter.

For all of fiscal 1990, FDP expects to report earnings of about $483,000, or 14 cents a share, on revenue of $16.9 million, compared with 1989 loss of $100,000, or three cents a share, on revenue of $14.6 million.
