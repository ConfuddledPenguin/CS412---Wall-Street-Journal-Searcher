Potomac Electric Power Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

WASHINGTON -- Potomac Electric Power Co. said it filed a request for an electric rate increase of 9.6%, or $56.4 million, for its District of Columbia business. The requested rates would increase revenue in two steps: $41.2 million, effective in late 1991, and $15.2 million, effective June 1, 1992, to coincide with the start-up of a 138-megawatt combustion turbine and related facilities at the company's Dickerson Generating Station.

Potomac Electric said its last District of Columbia rate increase, 1.6% or $9.5 million, was granted in July 1990. The company, however, had requested an increase of 5.8% or $33.4 million, and subsequently filed for reconsideration, a request still pending with regulators.
