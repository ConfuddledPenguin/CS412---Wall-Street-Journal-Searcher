Highland Superstores Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

PLYMOUTH, Mich. -- Highland Superstores Inc. said it reached agreement with its bank lenders to defer reducing the consumer electronics concern's line of credit in January, as they normally do. Under the agreement, the company said, $11 million of the scheduled $22 million seasonal reduction has been deferred and will be payable in installments through March 15, when $7 million of the deferred $11 million will be due.
