Equifax, Telecredit Stock Swap Approved

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

ATLANTA -- Equifax Inc. and Telecredit Inc. said shareholders of both companies approved Equifax's acquisition of Telecredit in a stock swap valued at $513 million. The merger makes Telecredit a wholly owned subsidiary of Equifax through a tax-free exchange of 2.86 shares of Equifax for each share of Telecredit. That number was raised from 2.69 in November to compensate for a decline in Equifax stock value.

Equifax provides services such as insurance information reporting, credit verification and direct-mail marketing assistance. The company said Lee Ault will remain chief executive officer of Telecredit, which is based in Los Angeles. The company's main operations are in Tampa, Fla., and its primary services are check authorization and credit-card processing.
