First Western Financial Meeting

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- First Western Financial Corp., in talks with an unidentified potential buyer, said it set a record date of Jan. 11 for an annual shareholders meeting on Feb. 14. R.G. Taylor, chairman of the savings and loan holding company, said the meeting, originally scheduled for Nov. 29, had been deferred to allow management to concentrate on talks with the possible buyer.

Mr. Taylor said that any acquisition agreement reached in the near future isn't likely to be submitted to shareholders until after the February meeting.
