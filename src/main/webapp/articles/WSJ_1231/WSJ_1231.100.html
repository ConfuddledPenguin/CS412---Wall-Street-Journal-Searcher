Business Brief -- Great Lakes Bancorp: Write-Off of Good Will To Lead to Loss for 1990

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

Great Lakes Bancorp said it expects to write off between $25 million and $28 million in good will, which will result in a loss of as much as $23 million in 1990. Great Lakes Bancorp said future earnings will benefit from the write-off because the annual expense of amortizing good will and other intangibles is reduced.

The company also said it will add between $15 million and $18 million to its reserves to protect it from possible loan losses and improve future earnings. The increase will boost reserves to between $23 million and $26 million.
