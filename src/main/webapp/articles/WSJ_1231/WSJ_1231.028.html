Union Camp, Chase Packaging

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

WAYNE, N.J. -- Union Camp Corp., a paper, packaging and chemicals concern, said it acquired all of the common and preferred shares of Chase Packaging Corp., an Old Greenwich, Conn., paper and plastic bag maker, for about $86 million, after Chase shareholders approved the sale. Union Camp said the Chase acquisition will give the company total bag sales of more than $500 million a year. Union Camp had 1989 sales of $2.8 billion.
