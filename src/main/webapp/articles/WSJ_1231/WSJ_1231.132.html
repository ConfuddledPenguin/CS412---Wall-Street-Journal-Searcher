Who's News: Fibreboard Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/31/90

FIBREBOARD Corp. (Concord, Calif.) -- Harvie M. Merrill, 69 years old, will retire as a director of this building products company. He will also retire from several other boards for health reasons. The company will consider a successor for Mr. Merrill on the five-member board.
