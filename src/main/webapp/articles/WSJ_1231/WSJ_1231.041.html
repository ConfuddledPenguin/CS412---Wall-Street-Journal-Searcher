Charter One Financial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/31/90

CLEVELAND -- Charter One Financial Inc. said it expects to report a net loss for the year following a one-time, fourth-quarter charge of $28.2 million to write off the bank's good will. Charter One said that excluding the charge, it expects to report record earnings for the year. Last year's net was $16.9 million, or $2.44 a share.

Charter One said writing off the bank's good will is expected to eliminate annual operating expenses by about $1.3 million after tax.

Good will represents the difference between the purchase price of an asset and its fair market value.
