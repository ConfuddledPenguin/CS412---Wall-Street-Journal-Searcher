Benj. Franklin Federal S&L

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Benj. Franklin Federal Savings & Loan Associationd said write-offs associated with new federal capital requirements resulted in a loss of $377.1 million for 1989. The loss had been anticipated under a previously announced restructuring program. The ailing thrift was placed into conservatorship of the Resolution Trust Corp. in February but continues retail operations while the RTC negotiates a resolution of its problems. Benj. Franklin said its loss resulted largely from the write-off of $282.7 million of good will accumulated from previous acquisitions.
