Harnischfeger Unit Strike Ends

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

MILWAUKEE -- Harnischfeger Industries Inc. said employees at its Beloit Corp. unit ended a strike that had idled about 1,000 workers since it began April 9. The equipment maker said the machinists union at the Beloit, Wis., operation had voted to approve a new three-year contract, and return to work this morning.

An official said the company doesn't anticipate that the brief strike will significantly hurt earnings.
