Business Brief -- CNB Bancshares Inc.: Definitive Agreement Is Set On Buying Valley Federal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

CNB Bancshares Inc., Evansville, Ind., said it agreed definitely to acquire Valley Federal Savings Bank in a cash and stock transaction valued at about $11.4 million, or $10.50 a share. In September, CNB agreed to pay $11.50 a share for the Terre Haute, Ind.-based banking concern, but the company said the purchase price was reduced because of higher interest rates and other factors that have affected the market value of Valley Federal's loan portfolio since then.

Under the pact, CNB will pay $10.50 a share for each of Valley Federal's nearly 1.1 million shares outstanding. The company said 60% of the purchase price will be paid in the form of CNB common stock, at a conversion value of $21 a share.

In connection with this agreement, CNB said Valley Federal granted it an option to buy as much as 25% of its stock at $7.85 a share under certain circumstances. Valley Federal has assets of about $240 million and CNB has assets of about $1.36 billion.
