Business Brief -- Texas Utilities Co.: NRC Approves Full License For Comanche Peak Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

Texas Utilities Co. said the Nuclear Regulatory Commission approved the issuance of a license for full-power operation of Comanche Peak Unit 1, some 17 years after construction began. Texas Utilities, the plant's owner, said the unit is expected to be at full power during the peak season of electric use this summer.

The NRC issued a low-power operating license in February. Testing at low power began April 3 and was completed April 6, the Dallas utility said.

Comanche Peak, about 75 miles southwest of Dallas, had a series of setbacks that included cost overruns and delays. Its licensing process was suspended in early 1985 amid allegations of improper design and construction, but the NRC said concerns had been resolved.
