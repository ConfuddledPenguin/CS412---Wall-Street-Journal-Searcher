  • Date: 04/17/90

Zbigniew Brzezinski, national security adviser under Jimmy Carter, in the March 26 issue of Fortune.

The U.S. has been the global policeman, but I think increasingly we will be the global air traffic controller. You obey the cop because he will put you in jail. You obey the traffic controller because you don't want to crash. The world system still needs an arbitrator, and the U.S. will play that role.
