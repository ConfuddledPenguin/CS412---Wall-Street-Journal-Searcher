Canada to Sell Four Bond Issues

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government said it will offer 1.5 billion Canadian dollars (US$1.28 billion) of bonds in four issues this week. The sale will include 11.75% bonds due Oct. 15, 1993; 11.75% bonds due March 1, 1995; 11.50% bonds due Sept. 1, 2000; and 11.25% bonds due June 1, 2015. Offering terms are to be announced today.

The Bank of Canada has agreed to purchase at least C$100 million of the new bonds. The remainder will be available to public investors.

Proceeds will be applied toward redemption of C$2.13 billion of government bonds maturing May 1 and for general government purposes, the Finance Department said.
