EMC Corp. to Report Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

HOPKINTON, Mass. -- EMC Corp. said it expects to report a profit of about $735,000, or three cents to four cents a share, on record revenue of about $40 million for the first quarter. Last year, the maker of computer parts reported a net loss of $7.5 million, or 32 cents a share, on revenue of $24.1 million.

The company said the growth reflects improved sales of its disk systems and its so-called solid state disks, which use semiconductors that work in place of disk drives.
