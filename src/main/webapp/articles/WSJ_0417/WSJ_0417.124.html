Business Brief -- Eeco Inc.: Net Loss of $1.4 Million Estimated for Jan. 1 Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

Eeco Inc., Santa Ana, Calif., said it expects to report a $1.4 million net loss for the year ended Jan. 1, but that publication of its financial results would be delayed by the proposed sale of its Maxi-Switch computer keyboard unit. The company said the estimated loss, on $63 million of sales, includes a $6.7 million loss from continuing operations and a $2.2 million loss from the planned disposition of Maxi-Switch.

For 1988, the maker of high-technology electronics products had a loss of $13.2 million on $13.9 million in sales.

Eeco said a party it didn't identify has agreed in principle to buy the Maxi-Switch unit. Terms weren't disclosed. Eeco expects the sale to be final next month.

Eeco also said its $10 million credit agreement with Sanwa Bank of California expired March 31, but that Sanwa hadn't declared the loan in default.
