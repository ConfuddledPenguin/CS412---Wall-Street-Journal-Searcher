Who's News: Doroff May Get Bonwit Post

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

NEW YORK -- Frank Doroff is expected to be named president of the two-store Bonwit Teller chain owned by Pyramid Cos., according to people familiar with the situation. Mr. Doroff is currently chairman of Federated Allied Merchandising Services, the importing and manufacturing division of Federated Stores Inc.
