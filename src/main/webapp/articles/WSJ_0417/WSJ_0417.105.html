Business Brief -- Union Electric Co.: Retail Properties Purchase From Unit of Entergy Set

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

Union Electric Co., St. Louis, said it agreed to acquire retail electric properties in Missouri from the Arkansas Power & Light Co. unit of Entergy Corp., New Orleans, for about $70 million. The utility said that under the agreement, 28,000 Missouri retail electric customers served by Arkansas Power would become Union Electric customers. The company said it will purchase electrical power from Arkansas Power for 10 years initially, and has an option to extend the power purchases for at least an additional five years.

Union Electric said the agreement, which is subject to approval by the boards of both companies and certain regulatory authorities, is expected to be completed by the end of 1990.
