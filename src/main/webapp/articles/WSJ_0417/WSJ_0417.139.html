Total Energold Corp. Completes Placement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

VANCOUVER, British Columbia -- Total Energold Corp. said it completed a private placement of about 4.4 million common shares, and used the proceeds of $10 million to acquire 12.5% of a partnership that invests in small resource companies. The previously proposed placement, to a wholly owned unit of Total Cie. Francaise des Petroles, a Paris-based oil concern, increased the unit's interest in Total Energold's shares outstanding to about 69%, the company said.

Total Energold, which has interests in oil, natural gas and gold, said it subsequently acquired 12.5% of Ventures Trident II Limited Partnership, a professionally managed limited partnership. Details about the partnership and its holdings weren't disclosed, and company executives weren't available to elaborate.

A field geologist with Total Energold said he believes Ventures Trident's interests include a stake in an Alaskan gold deposit.
