General Instrument Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

WASHINGTON -- General Instrument Corp. was given a $233.2 million Air Force contract for aircraft radar-warning receivers.

Grumman Corp. won a $98.6 million Navy contract for F-14D aircraft.

Calcasieu Refining Co. got a $32.8 Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.
