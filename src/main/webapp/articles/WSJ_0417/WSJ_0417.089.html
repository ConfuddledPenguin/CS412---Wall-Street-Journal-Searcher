Giga-tronics to Post Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

PLEASANT HILL, Calif. -- Giga-tronics Inc. said slumping orders, production delays and write-downs on obsolete inventory would result in a loss of $250,000 for its fourth fiscal quarter, ended March 31. The maker of defense electronic equipment earned $2.7 million, or 90 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter.

Giga-tronics said that as a result of the downturn in orders, it was firing 14 employees, or about 10% of its work force. The company, however, called the staff reductions and write-downs "isolated events" and said it expects to "return to quarterly profitability in the near future."
