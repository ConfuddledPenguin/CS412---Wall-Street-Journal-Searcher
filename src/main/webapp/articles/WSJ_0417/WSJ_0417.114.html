Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

THE NATIONAL: The daily sports paper, the National, plans to begin editions for San Diego and San Francisco by July. The paper had its debut on Jan. 31, with editions in New York, Chicago and Los Angeles. The National said it has been publishing a daily average of about 350,000 copies and selling an average of 200,000.

WHO'S NEWS: New York Times Co. named five senior corporate executives as vice presidents of the company: Stephen Golden, director of operations for the forest products group; Leslie Mardenborough, director of corporate personnel; Gordon Medenica, planning director; Thomas H. Nied, tax director; and Solomon B. Watson IV, general counsel. They will continue in their current posts.
