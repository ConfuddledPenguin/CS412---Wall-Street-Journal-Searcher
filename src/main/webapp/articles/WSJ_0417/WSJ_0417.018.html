ZyMOS Corp. Restates Results

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- ZyMOS Corp. said it restated its 1988 and 1989 financial results to reflect a change in accounting for a gain on the sale of equipment and technology to an affiliate. The microchip maker said proceeds from the sale, originally booked as a gain, were reclassified as contributions of capital. For the full year ended Dec. 31 1989, ZyMOS said its net loss was restated to $2.9 million, or nine cents a share, from $1.9 million, or six cents a share. For 1988, the restatement resulted in a loss of $3.2 million, or 11 cents a share, from profit of $189,000, or one cent a share. Net before restatement included an extraordinary gain of $5.8 million from the early retirement of debt.
