Raw-Steel Production Rose 1.1% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 1.1% last week to 2,019,000 tons from 1,998,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 1.5% from the 2,050,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 89.8% of its capability last week, compared with 88.9% the previous week and 92.7% a year ago.
