Business Brief -- Sceptre Resources Ltd.: Some Oil, Gas Properties Will Be Offered for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

Sceptre Resources Ltd. said it will offer for sale a group of Canadian oil and gas properties, which in aggregate produce about 700 barrels of oil or gas liquids, and 21 million cubic feet of natural gas a day. Sceptre, in Calgary, Alberta, didn't estimate a value for the properties, but an analyst said they could fetch about 50 million Canadian dollars (US$43 million).

The estimate is in line with previous company figures, which called for the sale of about C$60 million of "non-strategic" oil and gas assets as part of a debt-reduction program. Sceptre has already agreed to sell a package of U.S. assets for US$11.5 million.

Sceptre's long-term bank debt, which totaled C$305.9 million on Dec. 31, shrank to about C$208.8 million after an issue of common shares was completed in February, said a company executive.

Sceptre's debt swelled with its C$273 million purchase of Oakwood Petroleums Ltd. early last year. Based on 1989 results, the Canadian assets Sceptre plans to sell equal about 13% of the company's gas output, and 4.3% of oil or gas liquids production.
