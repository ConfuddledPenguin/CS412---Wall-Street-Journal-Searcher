Business Brief -- Torchmark Corp.: First-Quarter Net Grew 13% For Insurance-Holding Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

Torchmark Corp., Birmingham, Ala., reported that net income, excluding realized investment gains, rose 13% in the first quarter due in part to improved margins in insurance operations and higher investment income. Net increased to $57.9 million from $51.1 million.

Earnings, excluding realized investment gains, grew 16% to $1.07 from 92 cents a share.

Torchmark, an insurance-holding company, is waging a proxy fight to install its own slate of five independent nominees to the board of American General Corp., a Houston-based insurance concern that it wants to acquire.

Torchmark said realized after-tax investment gains were $2.5 million, or four cents a share, compared with $492,000, or one cent a share.

The firm said revenue rose 9% to $437.4 million from $400.5 million.
