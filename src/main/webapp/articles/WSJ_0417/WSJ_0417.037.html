Who's News: Neolens Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

NEOLENS Inc. (Miami) -- Malcolm Walker, 52 years old, previously vice president, operations, at the Cole Vision Corp. optical department of Sears, Roebuck & Co., was named president of this polycarbonate ophthalmic lenses concern, succeeding Henry Clayman, who remains a director.
