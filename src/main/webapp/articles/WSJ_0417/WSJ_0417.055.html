Beazer's U.S. Units Renamed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

NEW YORK -- Beazer PLC of Bath, England, said it changed the names of its two principal U.S. operating companies. Beazer Materials & Services Inc., serving the eastern half of the U.S., has been renamed Beazer East Inc. Gifford-Hill & Co., serving the western part of the country, has been renamed Beazer West Inc. Both are units of Beazer U.S.A., the holding company for the two building materials and services companies. Beazer PLC generates 47% of its revenue in the U.S.
