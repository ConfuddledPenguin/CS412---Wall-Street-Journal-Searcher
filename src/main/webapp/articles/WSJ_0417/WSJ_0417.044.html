Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Ford Capital B.V., a unit of Ford Motor Co., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.

Image Data Corp., initial offering of 1.4 million common shares, of which one million are being sold by the company and 400,000 by holders, via a Ladenburg, Thalmann & Co. led group.

MARCOR Environmental Inc., initial offering of 1.5 million common shares, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Province of New Brunswick, Canada, shelf offering of up to $350 million of debt securities.

U S West Inc., proposed offering of 14 million common shares, via Goldman, Sachs & Co., Merrill Lynch Capital Markets, Salomon Brothers Inc., and Yamaichi Securities Co.
