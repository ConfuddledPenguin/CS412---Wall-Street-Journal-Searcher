Manhattan Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

NEW YORK -- Manhattan Savings Bank account holders approved a plan to convert to stock from mutual ownership and to merge into Williamsburgh Savings Bank, a unit of Republic New York Corp. About 86% of the votes were cast for conversion. Manhattan Savings Bank, at Dec. 31, had assets of $3.1 billion and deposits of $2.8 billion. Williamsburgh had year-end assets of $3.7 billion and deposits of $2.6 billion. The combined savings bank, to be named Manhattan Savings Bank, will have 29 offices in the New York metropolitan area.
