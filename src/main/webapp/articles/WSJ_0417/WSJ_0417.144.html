Business Brief -- Canonie Environmental Services: Deficit Will Be Reported For Its Fiscal 4th Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

Canonie Environmental Services Corp., Porter, Ind., said it expects to report a loss of about 10 to 12 cents a share for its fourth quarter ended Feb. 28. In the year-earlier period, the hazardous-waste management concern had net income of $731,000, or 13 cents a share, on revenue of $9 million.

Regarding the deficit for the latest period, the company cited additional expenses associated with a major project, "continued losses" in its laboratory operation, and employee severance costs.

The quarterly loss will trim Canonie's earnings for fiscal 1990 to about eight to 10 cents a share, the company said. In fiscal 1989, Canonie earned $5.4 million, or 96 cents a share, on revenue of $48 million.
