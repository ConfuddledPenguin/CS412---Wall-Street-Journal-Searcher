Fannie Mae Plans Financing

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Federal National Mortgage Association said it and Prudential Home Mortgage will provide financing for $2 billion of home mortgages over the next 12 months. Prudential Home Mortgage of St. Louis, an affiliate of Prudential Insurance Co. of America, will originate a variety of fixed- and adjustable-rate mortgages that will be sold to Fannie Mae for cash or exchanged for mortgage-backed securities issued by Fannie Mae.
