Business Brief -- Midlantic Corp.: Bank Holding Firm's Net Plunged in First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

Midlantic Corp.'s first-quarter profit, reflecting a previously disclosed $108 million addition to reserves, plunged from year-ago levels. Net income for the Edison, N.J., bank holding company totaled $1.4 million, or a penny a share, down from $59.5 million, or $1.56 a share.

Net interest income climbed 7.7% to $228.6 million from $212.3 million. Non-interest income jumped 33% to $65 million. The addition to reserves for possible loan losses increased the total at the end of March to $449 million from $388 million at the end of 1989.

At the end of the quarter, Midlantic's assets totaled $23.7 billion, compared with $19.7 billion a year earlier.
