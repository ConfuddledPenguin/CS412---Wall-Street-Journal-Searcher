Marriott Sells Roy Rogers Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Marriott Corp. said it completed the sale of its Roy Rogers restaurant business to Hardee's Food Systems Inc. for $365 million in cash, plus the assumption by Hardee's of certain liabilities. Hardees is a unit of Imasco Ltd. The Roy Rogers chain consists of more than 600 restaurants, of which more than 350 are company owned.

Marriott said the sale would have no impact on 1990 earnings because proceeds are being invested in its remaining family restaurant business.
