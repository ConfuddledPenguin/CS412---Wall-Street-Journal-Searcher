Business Brief -- Amdura Corp.: Hardware Distribution Line Put on the Auction Block

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

Amdura Corp., which is operating under federal bankruptcy-law protection, said it will solicit bids for its main hardware distribution business, meaning that all its operating businesses now are up for sale. The Denver-based company said it will solicit bids for CoastAmerica Corp., CoastAmerica's related subsidiaries, and Amdura National Distribution Co. Previously, the firm put its two manufacturing units up for sale in an effort to trim debt.

The firm, whose management changed after a proxy fight last year, sought protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code earlier this month when its bank group refused to extend additional credit.

Amdura said it has postponed release of year-end results while it tries to resolve significant accounting issues regarding the company's future. However, for the nine months ended Sept. 30, it had a net loss from continuing operations, reflecting the hardlines business, of $33.7 milion, or $5.14 a share, on revenue of $480.3 million. Including discontinued operations, it had net income for the nine months of $3 million, or 20 cents a share.
