Dividend News: Frederick's Of Hollywood Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

FREDERICK'S OF HOLLYWOOD Inc. said it declared a 3-for-2 stock split and a 5% increase in its semiannual cash dividend to 35 cents a share. The Los Angeles specialty retailer said the split, payable as a stock dividend June 15 to stock of record May 15, will increase its shares outstanding to about 5.6 million.
