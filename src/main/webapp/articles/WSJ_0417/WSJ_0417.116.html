Business Brief -- Golden Nugget Inc.: First-Quarter Net Rose 71%, Reflecting Mirage Business

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

Golden Nugget Inc., reflecting strong business at its new Mirage resort, said first-quarter net income jumped 71% to $17.3 million, or 92 cents a share, from $10.1 million, or 55 cents a share, a year earlier. Operating revenue rose to $208.1 million from $49.9 million.

Stephen Wynn, chairman, said the Mirage had operating profit of $44.3 million in the quarter. He said the resort's expenses, which were scaled back following a late November opening, total about $1.1 million a day. Non-casino revenues are running about $800,000 a day, he said.

Mr. Wynn said the company's downtown casino reported an 86% increase in earnings despite the publicity generated by the Mirage. The downtown casino reported $13.5 million in operating profit, which Mr. Wynn said was a record first-quarter performance for the property. The Laughlin casino, which is under expansion, reported $810,000 in operating profit.

The results were stronger than most analysts expected.
