Cedar Group to Buy Gardenbolt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/17/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Cedar Group Inc. said it entered an agreement in principle to acquire Gardenbolt International for $6 million in cash at closing, and an additional $1 million contingent upon Gardenbolt's future results over a four-year period. Cedar Group distributes nuts, bolts and related products. Gardenbolt, of Sayreville, N.J., imports fasteners. The acquisition is subject to definitive agreements and due diligence.
