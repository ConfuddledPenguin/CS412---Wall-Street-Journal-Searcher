Business Brief -- II-VI Inc.: Firm Expects to Post Drop In Fiscal 3rd-Period Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

II-VI Inc. expects to report fiscal third-quarter net income fell to between 7 cents and 10 cents a share, from 18 cents in the year-earlier period. The Pittsburgh-based optical components concern said revenue for the quarter ended March 31 would total about $3,850,000, compared with $3,759,000 last year.

The company attributed the lower-than-expected revenue and earnings to reduced domestic sales to manufacturers, reduced margins in its infrared materials business, and the slow start-up of its Singapore manufacturing subsidiary. Results will be reported within a week.
