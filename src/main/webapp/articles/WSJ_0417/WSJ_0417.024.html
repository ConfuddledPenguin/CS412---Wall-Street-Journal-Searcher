Egghead to Have $500,000 Gain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

ISSAQUAH, Wash. -- Egghead Discount Software said it will post a gain of $500,000 from the previously announced sale of its software training subsidiary to closely held 5750 Inc. The unit, known as Egghead University, was founded in 1988 and operates a network of software training centers for businesses and individuals. Egghead is a high-volume retailer of personal computer software with about 200 stores nationwide.
