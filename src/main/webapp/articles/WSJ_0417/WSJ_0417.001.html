Temco National Gain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Temco National Corp. said it expects to report a $1 million gain in its second quarter from the sale of a wholesale medical supply unit to Foster Medical Supply Inc. Terms of the sale weren't disclosed.

Foster, a closely held company based in Dedham, Mass., said the medical supply unit, owned by Temco's Wendt-Bristol Co. subsidiary, will be combined with its current operations.
