Metromedia Forms New Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/17/90

NEW YORK -- Metromedia Co., the closely held partnership controlled by billionaire John W. Kluge, said it formed the Kluge & Co. division to succeed Kluge Subotnick Perkowski, a now-dissolved leveraged buy-out firm. The new operation is a merger-and-acquisitions advisory group, Metromedia said. It will focus on Metromedia's investment portfolio and seek out acquisitions beyond Metromedia's core businesses, the company said.

Metromedia owns, among other things, telecommunications, robotic-painting and computer-software operations. It also holds a majority interest in Orion Pictures.
