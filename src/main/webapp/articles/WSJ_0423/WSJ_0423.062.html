Who's News: Grumman Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

GRUMMAN Corp. (Bethpage, N.Y.) -- John E. Harrison was appointed president of this defense contractor's Electronics Systems Division. He succeeds Robert A. Nafis, 61 years old, who is retiring. Mr. Harrison, 54, previously was president of Grumman's Space Systems Division.
