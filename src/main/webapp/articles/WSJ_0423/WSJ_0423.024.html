Who's News: Rospatch Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

ROSPATCH Corp. (Grand Rapids, Mich.) -- Joseph J. Miglore, 44 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this maker of defense products and furniture. He succeeds Joseph A. Parini, who resigned March 30. Mr. Miglore recently served as vice president, finance and administration, of Haworth Inc., an office-furniture maker.
