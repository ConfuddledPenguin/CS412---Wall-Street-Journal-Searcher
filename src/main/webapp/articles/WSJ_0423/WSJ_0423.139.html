Commodity Fund Values Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

CHICAGO -- The average value of investment units in 131 publicly offered commodity funds rose 2.5% in March, according to Norwood Securities, which tracks the monthly performance of the funds. The majority of the funds rose in value for the fifth consecutive month.

Public commodity funds are managed pools of money in which limited-partnership units are offered to investors.
