Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 272,819 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, up 7.1% from 254,665 tons in the previous week but down 14% from 317,551 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production fell 14% to 4,029,954 metric tons from 4,660,494 tons a year earlier.
