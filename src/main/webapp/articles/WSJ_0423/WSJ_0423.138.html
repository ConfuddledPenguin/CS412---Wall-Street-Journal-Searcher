Business Brief -- General Housewares Corp.: Blyth Industries Buys Unit; Bid Is Accepted for Another

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

General Housewares Corp., Stamford, Conn., said it completed the sale of one unit and accepted a management buy-out bid for another, with combined proceeds expected to be about $15 million. The Colonial Candle operations was sold to closely held Blyth Industries Inc., based in Greenwich, Conn. Also, General Housewares accepted a proposal from an employee management group to acquire the Kitchen Textile Coordinates business. Both units have been based in Hyannis, Mass.

Kitchen Textile makes kitchen accessories, such as aprons and place mats. It also markets canister sets and mugs. The sale is expected to be completed around the end of June.

General Housewares has said the proceeds would go to pay down long-term debt, as the company concentrates on its retail cookware business.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, General Housewares closed at $11.50 a share, down 25 cents.
