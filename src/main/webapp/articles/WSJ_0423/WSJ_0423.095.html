Who's News: Bergen Brunswig Unit Names Martini Chief During Interim Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

WEST DES MOINES, Iowa -- Bergen Brunswig Corp.'s Commtron Corp. subsidiary said Robert Martini, vice chairman, will take on the post of president until a new chief executive is named. Mr. Martini will assume the additional position May 1. He succeeds Gary Rockhold, 47 years old, who resigned to become president of PolyGram Group Distribution, a unit of N.V. Philips's PolyGram N.V. subsidiary. Mr. Martini, 57, is president of Bergen Brunswig, which owns 80% of Commtron.

Commtron is a distributor of pre-recorded videocassettes and consumer electronics. Bergen Brunswig, Orange, Calif., has interests in health services and consumer-electronics products.
