Japan Posts Trade Deficit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

TOKYO -- Japan posted a trade deficit of $182 million for the first 10 days of April, compared with a $2.52 billion surplus a year earlier. Imports rose 43% to $7.04 billion from $4.91 billion, while exports fell 7.8% to $6.86 billion from $7.44 billion.

In March, Japan's trade surplus widened to $7.09 billion from $6.61 billion a year earlier. For the fiscal year March 31, Japan's trade surplus narrowed to $59.7 billion from $78.9 billion.
