Who's News: Aon's Patrick Ryan Adds Post

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

CHICAGO -- Aon Corp. said Patrick G. Ryan, president and chief executive officer, was named to the additional post of chairman. As chairman, the 51-year-old Mr. Ryan succeeds W. Clement Stone, the 87-year-old founder of Aon's predecessor company. Mr. Stone announced in February that he would step down from his position with the insurance holding company.
