Who's News: Coca-Cola Appoints Senior Vice President For U.S. Marketing

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

ATLANTA -- Coca-Cola Co., as expected, named Theodore J. Host, president of Boyle-Midway Household Products Inc., as senior vice president, marketing, for Coke's domestic division. The appointment of the 44-year-old Mr. Host fills a two-year vacancy in the job. Mr. Host, who joins Coke May 1, will report to Ira C. Herbert, president of CocaCola USA, the soft-drink company's domestic marketing arm.

At Boyle-Midway, Mr. Host oversaw products including Woolite, Black Flag and Easy Off. Last month, American Home Products Corp. agreed to sell its BoyleMidway division for $1.25 billion to Reckitt & Colman PLC, a London-based consumer products maker. Mr. Host was also an executive with American Home's food division, whose products include Chef Boyardee.

Mr. Host declined to comment.
