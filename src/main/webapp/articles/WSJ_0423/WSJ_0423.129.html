International: British Coal Pension Funds To Seek Globe Investment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

LONDON -- British Coal Corp.'s pension funds are together to bid 191 pence ($3.14) a share for the 66.2% of Globe Investment Trust they don't own after the funds purchased a further 5% of Britain's largest investment trust. The bid by the British Coal Staff Superannuation Scheme and the Mineworkers' Pension Scheme would value the whole of Globe at #1.02 billion ($1.68 billion).

The bid was prompted by the funds' purchase of 26.8 million Globe shares at 191 pence, which raised the funds' combined holdings in Globe to 33.8%.

Under United Kingdom law, any company that acquires more than 29.9% of another must make an offer to buy the remaining shares of that company at an equivalent price.
