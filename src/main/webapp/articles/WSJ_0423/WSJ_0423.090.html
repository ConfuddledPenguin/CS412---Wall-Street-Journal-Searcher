New Stock Listings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange, the American Stock Exchange and the National Market System of the over-the-counter market each announced two new stock listings.

Allstate Municipal Income Opportunities Trust III joined the Big Board under the symbol AIO in an initial public offering. The New York closed-end investment company works primarily with a diversified portfolio of non-rated municipal obligations.

Blackstone Advantage Term Trust Inc. began an initial public offering under the symbol BAT. The New York closed-end investment company specializes in mortgage-backed securities and zero coupon securities.

On the American Stock Exchange, Pacific-European Growth Fund Inc. began an initial public offering under the symbol PEF. The Minneapolis closed-end investment company works with securities of companies based in the Pacific Basin and Europe.

Veronex Resources Ltd., Los Angeles, began trading under the symbol VX. The natural resource company has holdings in oil and gas, gold and forest products. The company previously traded over the counter.

In the over-the-counter market, Pool Energy Services Co., a Houston oil-well servicing company, began trading under the symbol PESC.

Sullivan Dental Products Inc., West Allis, Wis., started trading under SULL. The company markets and distributes dental supplies and equipment.
