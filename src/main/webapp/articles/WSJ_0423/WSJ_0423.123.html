Barnett Banks Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

JACKSONVILLE, Fla. -- Barnett Banks Inc. said it is completing the acquisition of First American Bank & Trust Co., North Palm Beach, Fla., effective Thursday. Barnett, a bank holding company with assets of more than $29 billion, said First American's 32 offices will become part of four Barnett Bank units.
