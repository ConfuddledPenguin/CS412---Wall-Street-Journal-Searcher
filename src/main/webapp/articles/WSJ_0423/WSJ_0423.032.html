MCA Sells Unit to Acclaim

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

UNIVERSAL CITY, Calif. -- MCA Inc., an entertainment concern, said it completed the sale of LJN Toys Ltd. to Acclaim Entertainment Inc., an Oyster Bay, N.Y., computer software company, for cash and 1.2 million shares of Acclaim stock. Terms weren't disclosed, but a person close to the transaction valued it at $20 million to $25 million. The deal was valued at $30 million when the sale agreement was announced last month. The value declined because some of LJN's receivables were liquidated before the sale closed, MCA said.
