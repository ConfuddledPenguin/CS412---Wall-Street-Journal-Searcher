Business Brief -- Phoenix Technologies Ltd.: Loss of $4.6 Million Posted For Fiscal Second Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

Phoenix Technologies Ltd., Norwood, Mass., reported a fiscal second-quarter net loss of $4.6 million, or 46 cents a share, compared with year-earlier net income of $2.3 million, or 21 cents a share. The loss for the quarter ended March 31 was slightly less than Phoenix forecast last month when it warned investors that the second quarter would show a deficit.

Revenue slumped to $8.7 million from $17.6 million a year earlier.

Phoenix late last year changed its revenue-recognition policies, booking business as it is paid for rather than when products are shipped. The company has said the new policy will build a "solid, predictable revenue stream" after a period of adjustment.

The second-quarter results include a $1 million charge for reducing its head count and streamlining operations, Phoenix said.

The company said bookings of new business, particularly in the software-publishing area, increased strongly.
