Who's News: Roberts Pharmaceutical Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

ROBERTS PHARMACEUTICAL Corp. (Eatontown, N.J.) -- Yale Brozen and A. James Sederis were elected directors of this drug maker, increasing board membership to seven. Mr. Brozen, a business and economics consultant, is an adjunct scholar at the American Enterprise Institute for Public Policy Research and a professor of business economics emeritus at the University of Chicago's Graduate School of Business. Mr. Sederis owns and operates a pharmaceuticals-consulting firm.
