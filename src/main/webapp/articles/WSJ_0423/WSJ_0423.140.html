U.K. Institutional Investments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

LONDON -- Investments by United Kingdom institutions in British ordinary shares during the fourth quarter rose to #1 billion ($1.65 billion) from a net disinvestment of #374 million in the fourth quarter, the Bank of England said. The figure is down from the #3.2 billion invested by institutions in U.K. shares in the year-earlier fourth quarter.

The data were disclosed in the British central bank's quarterly report on institutional investment, which is intended to show the scope of investment activity among a wide range of pension funds, insurance companies and investment trusts.
