International Brief -- British & Commonwealth PLC: Executive Says Firm Knew Of Atlantic Computer Woes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

British & Commonwealth PLC officials were aware of the full extent of the financial troubles at its computer leasing subsidiary more than a year before the London-based company made them public, the chief executive of the subsidiary said. The brief statement issued Friday by Atlantic Computer Ltd.'s David McCormick contradicts remarks by B&C officials Tuesday that they had only learned of the depths of Atlantic's problems a few days before announcing the write-off of the division's assets.

The write-off of B&C's #550 million ($912 million) investment in Atlantic led the company Tuesday to request that its shares be suspended indefinitely and to the indefinite postponement of its results for the year ended Dec. 31.
