Canada Seeks Nominations For Beaufort Sea Oil Rights

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government invited the oil industry to submit by Sept. 29 nominations for oil and gas rights in the western part of the Beaufort Sea. Nominations are expressions of interest in particular areas. The Beaufort Sea is part of the Arctic Ocean.

The Indian and Northern Affairs Department said bids for exploration rights in the western Beaufort Sea will be called after the nominations are assessed. Several oil and gas finds have been made in the Beaufort Sea, but none of the discoveries has been brought into production because of the high costs of transporting the oil and gas to markets.

The government last month awarded exploration licences in the Beaufort Sea's central region. They were the first new licences to be issued for the region in 20 years.
