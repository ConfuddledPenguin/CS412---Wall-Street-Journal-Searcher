Dividend Brief -- Hollywood Park Realty: Payout on Common Is Set, The First in Almost 3 Years

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

Hollywood Park Realty Enterprises Inc. and Hollywood Park Operating Co., an Inglewood, Calif.-based race track owner and operator whose stock is paired for trading, declared a dividend of 12.5 cents a share on common stock. It is the first dividend in nearly three years. The dividend is payable June 1 to holders of record April 20.

Hollywood Park Operating also elected Allen E. Paulson as a director. Mr. Paulson, who is chairman and chief executive officer of Gulfstream Aerospace Corp., is an owner and breeder of thoroughbred racehorses.
