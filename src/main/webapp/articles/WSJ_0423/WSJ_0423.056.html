FAA Certifies Boeing Jets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

SEATTLE -- Boeing Co. said the Federal Aviation Administration certified its 757 and 767 twin-jets for additional extended-range services. The approvals allow the Boeing jetliners to fly more direct routes on long overwater trips or over undeveloped land areas, saving on fuel and flight times. The certifications are for the 757-200 and 767-200 and -300 jetliners, with specific engine combinations.
