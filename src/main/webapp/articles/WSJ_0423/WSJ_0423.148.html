Who's News: Kellogg Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

KELLOGG CO. (Battle Creek, Mich.) -- Claudio X. Gonzalez, 55 years old, chairman and chief executive officer of Kimberly-Clark de Mexico S.A., was elected a director of this cereal maker, increasing board membership to 13. He succeeds Brian P. Burns, chairman of BF Enterprises Inc., who retired.
