American Business Computers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

AKRON, Ohio -- American Business Computers Corp. said it intends to file a shelf registration with the Securities Exchange Commission for a secondary offering of its common shares at one cent par value. The 7.5 million shares to be registered consist of those currently held by certain directors and purchasers from various private placements. The food service products concern, which is not placing any shares in the offering, said it expects to file the registration in June or July.
