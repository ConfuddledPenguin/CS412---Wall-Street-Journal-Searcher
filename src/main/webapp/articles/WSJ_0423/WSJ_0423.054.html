International: Taiwan Export Orders Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

TAIPEI -- Export orders for Taiwanese products increased to $5.93 billion in March, up 18% from February but down 5.2% from March 1989, the Economics Ministry said. The ministry said the higher number of working days in March contributed to the monthly gain. Furthermore, March usually is a big month for export orders, it said.

Among the biggest gains, orders for electronic goods jumped to $1.43 billion in March from $1.12 billion in February, while orders for shoes rose to $417 million from $333 million.

For the quarter ended March 31, export orders fell to $15.88 billion from $16.35 billion a year earlier.
