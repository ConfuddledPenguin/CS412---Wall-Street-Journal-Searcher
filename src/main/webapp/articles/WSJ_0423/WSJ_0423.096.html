Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

PEOPLE'S BANK (Bridgeport, Conn.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. lowered the rating on the bank's commercial paper to single-A-3 from single-A-2 and on its certificates of deposit. The ratings concern cited the continuing rise in the bank's nonperforming assets, primarily commercial real-estate loans.
