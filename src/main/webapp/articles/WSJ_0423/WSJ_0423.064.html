Who's News: Abbott Laboratories

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

ABBOTT LABORATORIES (North Chicago, Ill.) -- Gary P. Coughlan, formerly senior vice president, finance, at the Kraft General Foods unit of Philip Morris Cos., was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this maker of pharmaceuticals and hospital products. In the new posts, Mr. Coughlan, 46 years old, fills the vacancy created when Duane L. Burnham was named Abbott's chief executive officer in December. Mr. Burnham, 48, has since additionally been named chairman. Separately, John R. Walter, chairman, president and chief executive officer of R.R. Donnelley & Sons Co., was named to Abbott's board, filling a vacancy.
