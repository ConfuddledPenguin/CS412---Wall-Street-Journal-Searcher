Bogert Oil Agrees to Be Bought

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

OKLAHOMA CITY, Okla. -- Bogert Oil Co. said it agreed to be acquired by a unit of Louis Dreyfus Energy Corp. of Wilton, Conn., for about $29 million, or $13.20 a share. A shareholders' meeting to vote on the acquisition hasn't been scheduled.

Richard D. Bogert, who owns slightly more than half of the shares outstanding of this oil and gas concern, said he gave Louis Dreyfus Energy a proxy to vote in favor of the merger, which would be through LDEC Acquisition Corp., a unit.

In national over-the-counter trading Friday, Bogert Oil shares closed at $12.25, up 50 cents.
