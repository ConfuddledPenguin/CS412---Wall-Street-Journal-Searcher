Business Brief -- AAR Corp.: `Modest Gain' in Earnings Is Expected for Fiscal 1990

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/23/90

AAR Corp., Elk Grove Village, Ill., said that it expects a "modest gain" in earnings for the fiscal year ending May 31, but that it has a "cautionary view" of the fourth quarter. The company said the outlook was given by its chairman, Ira A. Eichner, during a presentation at a conference last week. On Friday, AAR issued a statement in response to recent inquiries about the trading activity in its common stock. The company said it believes that the activity resulted from comments during the presentation.

In fiscal 1989, the aircraft products and services concern earned $24.8 million, or $1.56 a share, on sales of $406.4 million. In the fourth quarter of fiscal 1989, AAR earned $7.4 million, or 46 a share, on sales of $113.4 million.

A spokesman for the company couldn't be immediately reached for comment.
