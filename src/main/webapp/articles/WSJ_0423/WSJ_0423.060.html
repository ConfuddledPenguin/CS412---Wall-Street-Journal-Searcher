Ex-Im Bank Approves Loan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

WASHINGTON -- The U.S. Export-Import Bank said it approved a $13.1 million direct loan to finance equipment for a pulp and paper mill project in Thailand. In 1987, the Ex-Im Bank approved a $62 million loan for the same project.

The new loan will cover 85% of the cost of paper-handling equipment to be supplied to Panjapol Paper Industry Co., by Black Clawson Co., Middletown, Ohio, and other U.S. companies. The $226 million paper mill project is being engineered by Beloit Corp., of Beloit, Wis. The plant is slated for completion in December 1992.
