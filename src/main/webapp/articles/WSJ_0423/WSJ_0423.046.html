Who's News: First Union Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/23/90

FIRST UNION Corp. (Charlotte, N.C.) -- John A. Mitchell and G. Kennedy Thompson, formerly executive vice presidents at the First Union National Bank of Florida unit of this bank-holding company, were named to the new posts of vice chairmen at the Jacksonville, Fla., unit. Larry J. Wertz, formerly a senior vice president at the Florida unit, was named an executive vice president there and remains its chief financial officer.
