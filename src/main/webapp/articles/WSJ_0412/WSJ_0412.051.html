Swedish Deficit Widens

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

STOCKHOLM -- The deficit on Sweden's current account balance widened to 3.1 billion kronor ($507.5 million) in February from a deficit of 0.3 billion kronor ($49 million) in the like month a year earlier, the Riksbank, Sweden's central bank, said. The deficit on the current account, which includes traded items, services and transfers, was higher than the 2.4 billion-krona deficit recorded in January, the Riksbank said.

It was also slightly higher than the average prediction of analysts of a 3 billion-krona deficit.
