Financing Business: Good Guys Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

GOOD GUYS Inc., a San Francisco-based stereo and electronics equipment retailer, announced a public offering of 1.2 million shares priced at $14.50 each. Of the total shares being offered, 600,000 are being sold by the company and the rest by certain holders. Proceeds will be used to pay debt. Montgomery Securities is underwriter of the offering. Good Guys has about 4.5 million shares outstanding.
