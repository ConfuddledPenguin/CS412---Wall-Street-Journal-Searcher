Texas-New Mexico Power Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Texas-New Mexico Power Co. filed with the Public Utility Commission of Texas for a $43.1 million, or 13.34%, rate increase, which it said is needed partly to cover the costs of a 150-megawatt power plant that is nearing completion in Robertson County, Texas. The utility said it expects its rate case to be heard in July.
