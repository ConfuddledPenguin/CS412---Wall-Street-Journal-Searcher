Amoco Unit Has Oil Discovery

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

LONDON -- An Amoco Corp. affiliate said it discovered oil in the Central Graben area of the British North Sea. The affiliate, Amoco (U.K.) Exploration Co., said the exploratory well 22/23a-3, located about 130 miles east of Aberdeen, Scotland, flowed 5,600 barrels of oil a day. The well was drilled to a depth of 10,936 feet in 310 feet of water, the affiliate said.

Amoco (U.K.) operates the field and holds a 30.77% working interest. Other owners and their stakes are Enterprise Oil PLC with 41.02%, Amerada Hess Ltd., 23.08%, and North Sea Inc., 5.13%.
