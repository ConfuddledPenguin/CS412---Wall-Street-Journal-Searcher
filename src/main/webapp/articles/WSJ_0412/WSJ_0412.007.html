Greyhound Restores Service to 286 Sites

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

DALLAS -- Greyhound Lines Inc. said it is restoring service to 286 communities in a move the strike-bound bus company said would increase its passenger service network by 28%. The company has been operating with replacement drivers and union crossovers since March 2, when 9,400 members of the Amalgamated Transit Union walked off the job in a contract dispute over wages and workplace issues.

The unit of GLI Holding Co. said the increase, announced just before the busy Easter travel weekend, boosts its daily scheduled service to about 72% of pre-strike levels.
