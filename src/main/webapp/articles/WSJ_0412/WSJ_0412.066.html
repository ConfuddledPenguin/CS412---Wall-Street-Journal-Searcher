Letters to the Editor: Freedom's Price Tag

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

In reply to your March 21 editorial "No Tears for New Jersey": Many women in New Jersey voted for Gov. Jim Florio, known as a liberal, but also as a candidate who would protect a woman's abortion rights. The other candidate promised no new taxes and no abortion rights. Freedom carries a price tag. Ellen L. Heuston

Newton, N.J.

---

I was struck by the timing of your editorial: March 21 coincided with the first day of spring, which to me has always meant the return of fowl, particularly ducks. I feel so abused by Gov. Florio's tax proposals that I want to declare open season on this particular duck. How can the governor (1) so callously ignore the will of the people and (2) really believe that those making between $50,000 and $100,000 are "rich" in this obscenely expensive state.

Richard Certo

Ridgewood, N.J.
