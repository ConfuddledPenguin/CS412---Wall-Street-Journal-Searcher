ARMSTRONG WORLD INDUSTRIES Inc. realized a $2.7 million gain in the 1989 first quarter as a result of certain litigation settlements. Sales in the 1989 first quarter were $619 million as recently restated by the company. In yesterday's edition, the gain was incorrectly attributed to the 1990 first quarter and the sales figure was misstated. (See: "Armstrong World to Post `Significant' Rise in 1st Period Net" -- WSJ April 11, 1990)
