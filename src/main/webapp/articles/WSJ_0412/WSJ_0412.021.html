  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

DALLAS -- American Medical International Inc. said that it signed a preliminary agreement valued at more than $100 million over five years calling for Perot Systems Corp. to handle its data-processing and telecommunications operations. American Medical said the agreement is expected to reduce its operating costs by at least $20 million a year, while giving it and its hospitals access to newer hospital data-processing products.

American Medical owns acute-care and psychiatric hospitals. Perot Systems, founded by Dallas computer magnate H. Ross Perot, is a contractor of information systems.
