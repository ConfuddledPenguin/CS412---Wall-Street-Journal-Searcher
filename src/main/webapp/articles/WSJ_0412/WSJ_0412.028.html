Merrill Lynch Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

NEW YORK -- Merrill Lynch & Co. said its Securities Options Corp. unit purchased the market-making clearing activities of Geldermann Securities Inc. of Chicago. Value of the transaction wasn't disclosed. The acquisition provides Merrill Lynch with 41 new accounts. The Securities Options operations clears transactions for market-makers on the Chicago Board Options Exchange and Pacific Options Exchange, a Merrill Lynch spokesman said.
