Spain's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

MADRID -- Spain's consumer price index rose a moderate 0.4% in March from February, mainly because of higher housing, household good and health costs, according to provisional figures released by the National Institute of Statistics. Last month's CPI data indicate that prices rose 7% from March last year and 2% from December.

Last year's annual rate of inflation was 6.9% and the Economy Ministry has forecast a 5.7% inflation rate for 1990.
