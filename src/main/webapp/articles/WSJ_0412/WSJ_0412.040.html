Who's News: Eastman Kodak

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

EASTMAN KODAK Co. (Rochester, N.Y.) -- Daniel A. Carp, previously vice president and general manager for this photographic, chemical and pharmaceutical company's Latin American region, was named vice president and general manager of European marketing companies, succeeding Manfred Muehleisen, who was named general manager of Kodak AG operations in West Germany, in turn succeeding Ernst Lieser, who is to retire in December.
