Stock, Commodity Markets Will Be Closed Tomorrow

  • Author: A Wall Street Journal News Roundup
  • Date: 04/12/90

Stock and commodity markets will be closed tomorrow, in observance of Good Friday. Many banks and most businesses will be open. Federal government offices will be open, and there will be mail deliveries. Some state and local government offices will be closed either all day or after noon.

In Canada, banks, exchanges, government offices and most businesses will be closed for the Good Friday holiday. Most government offices also will be closed on Easter Monday. There won't be any mail delivery on Friday or Monday.
