Campbell Soup Plant to Close

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

CAMDEN, N.J. -- Campbell Soup Co. said its frozen-food plant in Pocomoke City, Md., with more than 200 employees, will close today as part of the food company's world-wide restructuring program. Production of Campbell's Great Starts products will be shifted to its Salisbury, Md., plant.
