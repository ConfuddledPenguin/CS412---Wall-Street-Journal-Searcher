TIE Buys Back Preferred

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

SHELTON, Conn. -- TIE/communications Inc., a supplier of telephone systems, said it bought back preferred shares from a Pritzker family entity, HCR Partners, in exchange for a note valued at $46.5 million and warrants. Under the agreement, TIE said it will pay $15 million of the note to HCR, which is controlled by Pritzker family interests, after TIE completes the sale of certain operations; $4 million is scheduled to be paid on May 1, 1991, and the rest in monthly installments through 1996. The note carries a 10% annual interest rate and is secured by substantially all of TIE's assets.

HCR also received six-year warrants to buy one million TIE common shares for $1.25 to $3 each.
