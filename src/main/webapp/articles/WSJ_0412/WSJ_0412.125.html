Business Brief -- Kimberly-Clark Corp.: First-Period Net Rose 14% On 11% Increase in Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

Kimberly-Clark Corp. said its net income rose 14% for the first quarter, to $117.2 million, or $1.46 a share, from $103.1 million, or $1.28 a share, last year. The Dallas maker of consumer and health products and newsprint said its sales increased 11% to $1.57 billion from $1.42 billion last year.

Kimberly-Clark said revenue increases in its North American consumer product lines, coupled with decreases in manufacturing costs and marketing expenses, contributed to the improvement. The company said North American newsprint markets, however, continue to be weak and its two newprint facilities turned in small operating losses for the period.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Kimberly-Clark closed at $67.875 a share, up $1.375.
