Guy F. Atkinson Unit's Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

SOUTH SAN FRANCISCO, Calif. -- Guy F. Atkinson Co. said a unit received a contract valued at about $35 million Canadian (US$30.2 million) to modernize a paper mill in British Columbia, Canada. The company said the unit, Commonwealth Construction Co., will install a new paper machine, winders, grinders and other equipment for the Howe Sound Pulp & Paper Co. mill. The project is scheduled for completion in January.
