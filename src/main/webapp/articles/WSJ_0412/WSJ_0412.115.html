Business Brief -- Craig Corp.: Quarterly Profit Restated Downward to $1.5 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

Craig Corp., Los Angeles, said it was restating downward its earnings for the first quarter, ended Dec. 31. The move reflects Craig's share of nonrecurring losses in December by Reading Co. in which Craig then held a 20% stake. Craig, with interests in retail supermarkets and the importing of photographic accessories, said it restated its first-quarter earnings to $1.5 million, or 32 cents a share. Previously, the company reported earnings of $1.7 million, or 38 cents a share.

Edward Kane, president of Craig, said he expects results for Philadelphia-based Reading, which has interests in real estate and manufacturing, "would be positive in the future." Craig now holds 34.3% of Reading's Class A common stock.
