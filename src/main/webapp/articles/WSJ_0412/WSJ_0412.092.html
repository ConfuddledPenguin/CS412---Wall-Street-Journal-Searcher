Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Health Equity Properties Inc., proposed offering three million common shares, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

European Investment Bank, a shelf offering of as much as $1 billion principal amount of debt securities on terms to be set at the time of sale. When combined with securities already registered, the filing gives the company as much as $1.6 billion principal amount of securities available for sale.
