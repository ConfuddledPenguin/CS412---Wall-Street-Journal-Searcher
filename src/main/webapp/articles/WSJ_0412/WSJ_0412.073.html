From a letter to the Times of London by Antony Rouse, on March 16:

That's terrific news you report (March 13) -- the creation of a subsidiary of Oxford University in Japan -- but it needs development.

Give them a subsidiary of Eton, a branch of the Guards Officers' Mess at Wellington Barracks, a replica of White's Club and the Japanese economy will be on its knees within a generation.
