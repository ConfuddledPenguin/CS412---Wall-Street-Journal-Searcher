Dividend Brief -- Woolworth Corp.: Retailer Sets 2-for-1 Split And Boosts Dividend 11%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

Woolworth Corp. declared a 2-for-1 stock split, payable May 31 to stock of record May 3 and increased its quarterly dividend by 11%. The New York-based retailing concern raised its dividend to 52 cents a share from 47 cents a share, on a pre-split basis, payable June 1 to shares of record May 3.

After the split, Woolworth said it will have 129 million common shares outstanding and 158,000 shares of $2.20 Series A convertible preferred. Each share of preferred will be convertible into 5.68 shares of common, as compared with 2.84 shares before the split.

The company said the actions reflect its "increasingly strong financial performance." For the year ended Jan. 27, the company earned $329 million, or $5.11 a share, on revenue of $8.8 billion.
