Liberty National Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Liberty National Bancorp said shareholders approved increasing authorized common shares to 40 million from 30 million. The bank holding company said the increase was necessary to have shares available for any future acquisitions, stock splits or other actions.

Shareholders also approved an amendment to the company's stock option plan. The change includes a provision to increase the number of shares reserved for the plan as the company's shares outstanding are increased.
