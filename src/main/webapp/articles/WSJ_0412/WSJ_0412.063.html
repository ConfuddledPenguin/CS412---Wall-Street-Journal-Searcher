Malaysian Trade Surplus

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

KUALA LUMPUR -- Malaysia's merchandise trade surplus shrank 26% to 679.8 million ringgit ($249 million) in January from 923.7 million ringgit ($338 million) in the year-earlier month, the government statistics department said. However, the surplus continued to register moderate but steady improvement from October last year, when the merchandise trade account dropped into a small deficit.
