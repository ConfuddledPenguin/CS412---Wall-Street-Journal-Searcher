Marketing Brief -- Little Prince Productions Ltd.: Merger in Stock Swap Set With KB Communications

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

Little Prince Productions Ltd., a stage, film and television production company, and KB Communications Inc., an entertainment marketing and production company, agreed in principle to merge in a stock swap, valued at about $10 million, that would give KB holders 80% ownership in the new company. Under the agreement, KB holders will receive 6.5 Little Prince shares for each KB share. Both companies are based in New York.

Little Prince was one of the producers of the Broadway revival of the comedy "Born Yesterday," and is producing a television special in Italy based on the book "The Little Prince," by Antoine de Saint-Exupery. It had an animated television series based on the book that appeared on Nickelodeon, a cable TV channel. KB makes and distributes popular Western music in China.
