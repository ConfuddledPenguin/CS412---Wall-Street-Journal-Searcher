Boeing Gets Maersk Air Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

SEATTLE -- Boeing Co., an aircraft maker and defense contractor, said Maersk Air, an independent Danish airline, ordered a Boeing 737-500 plane and three 737-300s for about $127 million. It said Maersk has already taken delivery of 25 Boeing 737s. Boeing has received orders for 193 Boeing 737-500s and 951 of the 737-300s since it began production of the aircraft in 1968, a spokeswoman said.
