Who's News: Philadelphia Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

PHILADELPHIA ELECTRIC Co. (Philadelphia) -- Corbin A. McNeill Jr., 50-year-old executive vice president, nuclear operations, was elected president and chief operating officer, effective April 16, succeeding Joseph F. Paquette Jr., 55, who will remain chairman and chief executive officer. Dickinson M. Smith, 56, vice president of the company's Peach Bottom Atomic Power Station, will become senior vice president, nuclear operations.
