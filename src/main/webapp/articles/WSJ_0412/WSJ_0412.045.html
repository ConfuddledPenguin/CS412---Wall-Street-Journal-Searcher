Who's News: Software Toolworks Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

SOFTWARE TOOLWORKS Inc. (Chatsworth, Calif.) -- Elizabeth Barker, 34 years old, was appointed president and chief operating officer of this computer software distributor, effective June 1. Ms. Barker joins Software Toolworks from her previous post as general partner at Montgomery Securities in San Francisco. Last September at Montgomery, she managed a public offering for Software Toolworks. As president, she succeeds Leslie Crane, 56, who will remain as chairman of Software. The post of chief operating officer is new, the company said. Ms. Barker was elected a director of Software in February.
