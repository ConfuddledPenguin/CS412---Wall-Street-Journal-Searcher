Who's News: Newmark & Lewis Names Fleisher to Be President

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

HICKSVILLE, N.Y. -- Newmark & Lewis Inc., a New York-area discount retailer of home appliances and electronics, said it named Ron Fleisher, 43 years old, president and chief operating officer, effective May 1. The appointment of Mr. Fleisher, who was president of the Eastern region of Silo Inc., a national appliance and electronics retailer, marks the first time Newmark & Lewis hired an outsider as president. The operating chief's post is new; as president, Mr. Fleisher will succeed Richard Lewis, 66, who will become co-chairman with Warren Hyman, 68.

The company said Mr. Fleisher's hiring will permit Messrs. Lewis and Hyman, who are also to be co-chief executive officers, to spend more time on planning and strategic growth.

Mr. Fleisher's appointment is to coincide with Newmark & Lewis's anticipated addition of 17 stores it expects to acquire for $7.8 million and the assumption of some liabilities from Brick Church Appliance Inc., Edison, N.J. Following that, Newmark & Lewis will have a total of 67 stores.
