Who's News: Caterpillar Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

CATERPILLAR Inc. (Peoria, Ill.) -- James P. Gorter, 60-year-old chairman of Baker, Fentress & Co. and a limited partner of Goldman, Sachs & Co., was elected a director of this maker of heavy equipment, increasing board membership to 13.
