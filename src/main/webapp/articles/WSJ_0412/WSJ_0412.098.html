Who's News: Time Warner Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

TIME WARNER Inc. (New York) -- R. Mackereth Ruckman was elected vice president and treasurer of this entertainment concern. He succeeds Glenn Britt, 41 years old, former senior vice president and treasurer, who was recently named executive vice president of the Time Warner Cable unit. Mr. Ruckman, 40, previously was assistant treasurer of the parent.
