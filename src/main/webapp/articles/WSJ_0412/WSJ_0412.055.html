Burlington Resources Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/12/90

SEATTLE -- Burlington Resources Inc. said its board authorized the repurchase of as many as 10 million additional shares of its stock. The energy company has been buying back stock since 1988. Burlington said that during that time, it has repurchased nine million shares at an average price of $41.67. As of March 31, it had about 141.6 million shares outstanding.

In New York Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Burlington closed at $39.875 cents, down 50 cents.
