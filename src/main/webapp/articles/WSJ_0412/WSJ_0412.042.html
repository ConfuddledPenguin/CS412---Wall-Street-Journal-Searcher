Who's News: Liberty National Bancorp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

LIBERTY NATIONAL BANCORP (Louisville, Ky.) -- Owsley Brown Frazier, vice chairman of Brown-Forman Corp., and George E. Gans III, president and chief executive officer of Paul Semonin Co., were elected directors of this bank holding company, succeeding Chester Racke and Russell Badgett Jr., who retired.
