Technology Brief -- ON Technology Inc.: Apple Computer and Others Buy Stake in Software Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

ON Technology Inc. said it received financing from a number of investors, including Apple Computer Inc., and said it began volume shipments of its first software product. ON Technology, co-founded in 1987 by Mitchell Kapor, founder of Lotus Development Corp., said its backers included Kleiner, Perkins, Caufield & Byers, the Cole-Gilburne Fund, and Mr. Kapor. The company, based in Cambridge, Mass., didn't disclose terms of the financing or Apple's stake.

Its new product, ON Location, is a computer disk-searching program designed for use with Apple's Macintosh computer.
