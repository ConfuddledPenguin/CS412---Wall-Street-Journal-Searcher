Who's News: Valley Federal S&L Says Nelms Quits Top Positions

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

VAN NUYS, Calif. -- Valley Federal Savings & Loan said Dan E. Nelms resigned as president and chief executive officer of the severely troubled thrift. It said Mr. Nelms also resigned from the board.

A spokeswoman said Mr. Nelms stepped down for "personal reasons," but wouldn't elaborate. Mr. Nelms couldn't be reached to comment.

Valley Federal said Scott A. Braly, chief financial officer, was named interim president and chief executive.

Valley Federal is among the country's sickest thrifts. Regulators have ordered it to halt lending and investment activities, a move that generally is a precursor to government takeover. It had a loss of $73 million in the nine months ended Sept. 30 and has announced it will report a $66 million write-down for the fourth quarter, for losses at a mobile-home subsidiary. Its results for the fourth quarter and year haven't been released, but it has said it expects to report tangible capital after the write-down of negative $43 million.
