CONNECTICUT ENERGY Corp. (Bridgeport, Conn.) -- The holding company for utility, oil and gas production, and pipeline businesses said in the proxy statement for the April 24 annual meeting that it expects to name J.R. Crespo to the additional post of chairman. Mr. Crespo, 47 years old, continues as president and chief executive officer of the parent and of the Southern Connecticut Gas Co. utility unit.
