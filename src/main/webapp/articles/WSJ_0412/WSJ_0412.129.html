International Brief -- Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd.: Australian Firm Posts Loss Of $580.3 Million for Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/12/90

Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd. of Australia reported a loss of 758.2 million Australian dollars (US$580.3 million) for its fiscal first half, ended Dec. 31, 1989, compared with a year-earlier profit of A$152.8 million (US$116.9 million). In the latest full fiscal year ended June 30, Bond Corp. plunged into the red by A$980.2 million, including extraordinary losses, the largest corporate loss in Australian history.

Revenue for the debt-burdened brewing, resources, property and media company fell 23% to A$3.97 billion in the latest half from A$5.18 billion a year earlier.
