Eastman Kodak Price Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Eastman Kodak Co. said it will increase rental, service and supply prices for most of its copier and electronic publishing products. The maker of photographic, chemical and pharmaceutical products said rental and service rates would increase 5%, effective July 1, for its copier equipment, including its Ektaprint and ColorEdge copiers as well as IBM copiers serviced by Kodak. Prices of supply items, such as toner, will increase by 3% to 5%, Kodak said.

Effective June 1, service prices for Kodak's Ektaprint electronic products will increase 20%. Selected older equipment will be handled under separate pricing rates.

The company attributed the increases to rising costs for material and labor.
