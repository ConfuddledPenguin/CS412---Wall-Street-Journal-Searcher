Minnesota Mining Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- Minnesota Mining & Manufacturing said its board authorized the repurchase of as many as six million common shares between June 1, 1990, and May 31, 1991. The company said the authorization is an extension of a continuing program to reacquire shares for employee stock-purchase plans and for other corporate purposes.

Minnesota Mining, a diversified manufacturer, said it had 222,504,750 shares outstanding as of March 31.
