Who's News: Penta Systems International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

PENTA SYSTEMS INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Baltimore) -- Frederick A. Ayres was named president and chief operating officer of this software-development firm, filling a position vacated in January by C. Victor Meyer, who was president and chief executive officer. Mr. Ayres, 41 years old, is a member of Northcote Partners, an investment firm that helped Penta manage its 1989 restructuring. William A. Hohns, 38, an executive vice president of Waldman Graphics, was appointed a director, filling Mr. Meyer's seat.
