Who's News: Texaco Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

TEXACO Inc. (White Plains, N.Y.) -- Ralph S. Cunningham, formerly chief executive officer of Clark Oil & Refining Corp., was named president of the Texaco Chemical Co. subsidiary of this major oil company and a vice president of the parent. He succeeds Willis B. Reals, who is retiring.
