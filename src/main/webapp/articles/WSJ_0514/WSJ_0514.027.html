Encyclopaedia Britannica

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

CHICAGO -- Encyclopaedia Britannica Inc. said it purchased 65,000 shares of American Learning Corp. on the open market, boosting its stake in the unit to 83%. Closely held Britannica said it made the purchase under an earlier authorization to buy as many as 200,000 American Learning shares; the latest purchase completes the authorization.

American Learning offers specialized scholastic instruction.
