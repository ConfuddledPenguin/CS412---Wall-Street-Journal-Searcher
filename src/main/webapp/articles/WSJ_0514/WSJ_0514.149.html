Aztar Sets Stock Repurchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

PHOENIZ, Ariz. -- Aztar Corp. said its directors authorized the repurchase of as much as two million shares of the casino and hotel company's common stock, or about 5 % of the shares outstanding. In national over-the-counter trading on Friday, Aztar closed at $6.375, up 37.5 cents.
