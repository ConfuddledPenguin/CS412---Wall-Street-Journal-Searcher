TEKTRONIX Inc. (Beaverton, Ore.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it downgraded the senior debt rating on Tektronix to Baa-3 from Baa-2, and lowered the rating on its commercial paper to Prime-3 from Prime-2. About $150 million of securities are affected by the action. The rating concern said the downgrading reflects the likelihood of continued weak revenue growth, low margins and reduced cash flow protection for debt. A spokeswoman for the electronics concern had no comment on the action.
