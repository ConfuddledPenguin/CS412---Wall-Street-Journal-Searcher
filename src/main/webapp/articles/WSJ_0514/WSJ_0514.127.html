International Brief -- Metallgesellschaft AG: Group Pretax Net and Sales Climb by 10% in Half Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

Metallgesellschaft AG said group pretax profit and group sales both climbed 10% in the first six months of fiscal 1990 from a year earlier. Group pretax earnings at the West German metals concern climbed to 224 million marks ($136.9 million) in the first half of the year ending Sept. 30, while sales firmed to 9.8 billion marks, Chairman Heinz Schimmelbusch said at the annual shareholder meeting.

Mr. Schimmelbusch traced the better results to the group's restructuring efforts, which have helped to reduce its sensitivity to the fluctuations in metals prices. But he declined to make a profit forecast for the full year.
