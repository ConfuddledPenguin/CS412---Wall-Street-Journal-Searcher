General Motors Unit Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Hughes Georgia Inc., a unit of General Motors Corp., received a $194 million Air Force contract for Maverick missiles and parts.

General Dynamics Corp. was awarded a $63.2 million Air Force contract for support of F-16 aircraft and Atlas II space-launch vehicles.

Olin Corp. won a $51 million contract for Navy 20-millimeter ammunition and Army small-arms propellant.

Aerojet General Corp. got a $16.1 million Air Force contact for MX missile rockets.

United Technologies Corp. received a $15.1 million Army contract for UH-60l helicopters.
