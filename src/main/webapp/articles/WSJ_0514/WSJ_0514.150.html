Fidelity National Repurchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fidelity National Financial Inc., a title insurance concern, said it completed a Dutch auction by repurchasing 210,542 of its shares at $10 each. Also, under a previously announced agreement, Fidelity said it plans to repurchase 100,000 shares at $10 each from William P. Foley II, president and chief executive officer. Prior to the offer, Mr. Foley controlled about 37% of Fidelity stock.
