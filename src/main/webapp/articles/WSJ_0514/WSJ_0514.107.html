Who's News: Litton Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

LITTON INDUSTRIES Inc. (Beverly Hills, Calif.) -- John R. Russell was elected executive vice president and chief operating officer of the company's Western Atlas International Inc. subsidiary, succeeding Carroll M. Browning, who retired. Mr. Russell previously was senior vice president and chief financial officer of the unit. Thomas B. Hix Jr., controller of the Houston-based high-tech oil-services subsidiary, was elected a vice president and succeeds Mr. Russell as chief financial officer.
