Wisconsin University Grant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

MADISON, Wisc. -- The University of Wisconsin-Madison said David W. Grainger and the Grainger Foundation donated $8 million toward construction of a new School of Business building, and an additional $1 million to fund a new program in business ethics at the school. Mr. Grainger, chairman of electrical-products wholesaler W.W. Grainger Inc., is a 1950 graduate of UW-Madison.

University officials said construction of what will be known as the Grainger Hall of Business Administration will begin in 1991, with completion expected in 1993. The cost is expected to be $31 million.
