Business Brief -- Security Pacific Corp.: Unit Gets Certain Assets, Liabilities of Ailing Thrift

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

Security Pacific National Bank said it acquired certain assets and assumed certain liabilities of Washington Savings & Loan Association from the Resolution Trust Corp. Los Angeles-based Security Pacific said it will receive about $69 million in deposits from the RTC in the transaction.

Washington Savings & Loan, based in Stockton, Calif., has been in conservatorship with the RTC since April 1989. The 10-year-old thrift has four branches and about 7,000 customers in the Stockton area.

Security Pacific Bank is the principal operating unit of Security Pacific Corp., the nation's fifth-largest bank holding company with more than $86 billion in assets.
