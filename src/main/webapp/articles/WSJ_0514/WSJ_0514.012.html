Stake in Hotel Investors

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Leonard M. and Merri Jean Ross, a Beverly Hills, Calif., couple, said they acquired a 6.6% stake in Hotel Investors Trust and Hotel Investors Corp.'s common shares outstanding. Shares of Hotel Investors Trust, a Woodland Hills, Calif., real-estate investment trust, and Hotel Investors Corp., a San Diego hotel-management concern, are paired and trade together on the New York Stock Exchange. On Friday, the shares closed unchanged at $4 in composite trading.
