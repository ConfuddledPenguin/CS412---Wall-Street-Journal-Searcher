Cleveland-Cliffs Unit's Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

CLEVELAND -- Cleveland-Cliffs Inc. said its Pickands Mather subsidiary agreed to sell certain coal leasehold interests in eastern Kentucky to a unit of A.T. Massey Co. for $3.9 million. The transaction is expected to close by May 31 and will result in an after-tax gain of $2.6 million, Cleveland-Cliffs said.
