Bay View Capital Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Theodore H. Kruttschnitt, a Burlingame, Calif., investor, said he acquired a 7.5% stake in Bay View Capital Corp. for investment purposes. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Kruttschnitt said he holds 453,000 shares, all bought from April 24 to May 3 for $15.75 to $17.125 each.

Bay View, a savings and loan holding concern, is based in San Mateo, Calif.

Lawrence Adams, a Bay View vice president, said the company hadn't seen the SEC filing and had no comment.

Mr. Kruttschnitt said in an interview that his filing indicated he may or may not buy additional shares and added that his intentions toward Bay View were friendly.
