Sprouse-Reitz Stores Gets Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Sprouse-Reitz Stores Inc. said it received an unsolicited buy-out proposal for $17 a share, or about $24.4 million. The company said its board is considering the offer from Robert Bisno, chairman and chief executive officer of TransAction Financial Corp. of Oakland, Calif. The real estate property manager and investor, who owns 920 shares of voting common and 41,000 shares of nonvoting common, has indicated he would reduce the size of the company.

Sprouse-Reitz currently operates 287 general merchandise stores in 10 Western states.
