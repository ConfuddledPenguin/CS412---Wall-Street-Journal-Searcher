Business Brief -- Bank of New England Corp.: Unit Signs Consent Order With Comptroller's Office

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

Bank of New England-West, a unit of Bank of New England Corp., said it signed a consent cease and desist order with the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency. As reported, the parent company, Bank of New England Corp. signed a similar consent order with the Federal Reserve earlier this year and several of its other banking units have signed similar consent orders with the Comptroller.

The Springfield, Mass.-based unit said the order deals "comprehensively with the development and implementation of plans, policies and procedures at the bank and details improvements to be made in the operations of the bank."

In a statement, the unit said many of the requirements and standards called for in the order "are well under way or have already been completed."
