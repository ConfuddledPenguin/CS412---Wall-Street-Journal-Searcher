Canada Jobless Rate at 7.2%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's unemployment rate in April was 7.2%, unchanged from March but down from 7.7% a year earlier, Statistics Canada said. The agency said the seasonally adjusted level of employment rose by 56,000 people to 12,649,000 in April from March, while the level of unemployment rose by 12,000 to 987,000.

From April 1989, employment rose by 262,000 to 12,428,000, while the number of unemployed fell 62,000 to 1,043,000.
