Who's News: Hinderliter Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

HINDERLITER INDUSTRIES Inc. (Tulsa, Okla.) -- John L. Robertson, 70 years old, resigned as chairman, chief executive officer and director of this metals manufacturer. Mr. Robertson, who intends to remain as a consultant, quit his posts because "he came to the point that he didn't want to remain full-time, day-to-day," said Timothy Brasher, chief financial officer. Mr. Brasher said the board plans to meet "in a month or so" to discuss possible successors.
