Bob Evans to Buy Back Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Bob Evans Farms Inc. directors authorized the purchase of as many as one million shares, or 3% of the company's common stock, on the open market. The company, which operates 241 restaurants in 14 states, said the timing of purchases and the number of shares bought would depend on market conditions.

The stock will be held as treasury shares and will be available for issue on the exercise of stock options or for other general corporate purposes, the company said. Under a repurchase program that ended April 27, 430,000 shares of an authorized 500,000 were bought. As of that date, Bob Evans Farms had 32.2 million shares outstanding.
