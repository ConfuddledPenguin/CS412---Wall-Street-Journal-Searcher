Bliss & Laughlin Sells 39% Stake to Stelco

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

HARVEY, Ill. -- Bliss & Laughlin Industries Inc. said it sold a 39% stake in the company to Stelco Inc. for $18.7 million, after shareholders approved the plan. As part of the same transaction, Bliss paid $12.4 million to acquire the assets of Stelco's Canadian Drawn Steel Co. subsidiary. Bliss, a maker of steel bars, said it will use the $6.3 million of net proceeds realized to pay down debt.

Bliss sold Stelco 1,559,759 newly issued common shares for $12 apiece; the Bliss stake the Toronto concern received is "the same approximate percentage owned by the company's management and their families," Bliss said.
