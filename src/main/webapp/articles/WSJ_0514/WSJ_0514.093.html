Colorado National Bankshares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

DENVER -- Colorado National Bankshares Inc. said it received $1.1 million, after taxes, from its insurer in settlement of a fraud loss connected to its credit card operations. The bank holding company recorded a $2 million after-tax charge last year, when a Minneapolis company that processed merchant sales drafts for Colorado National's 50%-owned credit-card unit allegedly failed to forward certain payments to merchants.
