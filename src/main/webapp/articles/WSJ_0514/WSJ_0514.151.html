Mozambique Emergency Aid

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

UNITED NATIONS -- Thirteen nations have pledged $106.4 million in emergency food and relief aid for Mozambique, a country torn by civil war for more than a decade, U.N. officials said. They said the aid was pledged in response to a U.N. appeal to assist 1.4 million internally displaced Mozambicans and 154,000 returnees from neighboring states.

The pledges, made in late April and reported Friday, fell $30 million short of the 1990-1991 emergency requirements of $136 million requested by the government of Mozambique and the U.N.
