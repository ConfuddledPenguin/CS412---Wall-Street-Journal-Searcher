Who's News: Unum Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

UNUM Corp. (Portland, Maine) -- Robert E. Dillon, executive vice president of Sony Corp. of America, and Donald W. Harward, president of Bates College, were elected directors of this insurance concern, succeeding Edith Weiner and Gordon Donaldson, both of whom retired.
