Southern Mineral Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

HOUSTON -- Southern Mineral Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to sell its interest in the Trimble Field of Mississippi for about $11 million in cash. The oil and gas exploration company wouldn't identify the buyer, but said it expects to close the transaction by June 30. The Trimble Field, discovered in late 1988, currently produces 25 million cubic feet of natural gas a day.
