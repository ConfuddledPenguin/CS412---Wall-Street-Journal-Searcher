Business Brief -- Genetics Institute Inc.: Joint Venture to Develop Drugs to Aid Bone Growth

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

Genetics Institute Inc. said it expects to announce on May 21 the formation of a joint venture between it and a Japanese company to develop drugs that promote bone growth. The company said the venture will focus on bone morphogenetic proteins, which may someday help in treating orthopedic, dental and other bone disorders.

Last year, the biotechnology concern reported that it was awarded a U.S. patent for bone morphogenetic protein-1, or BMP-1, a substance that can induce formation of new cartilage. The company also has filed patent applications on other BMP proteins.

Genetics Institute, Cambridge, Mass., said terms of the Japanese joint venture will allow it to retain North American rights to the potential BMP products.
