Who's News: Waban Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

WABAN Inc. (Natick, Mass.) -- John R. Chase, 50 years old, resigned as president of the HomeClub unit of this warehouse club merchandiser for personal reasons. The company said James F. Halpin, Waban executive vice president and president of its BJ's Wholesale Club division, will succeed Mr. Chase as president of HomeClub. Herbert J. Zarkin, also a Waban executive vice president, will succeed Mr. Halpin as president of BJ's. Mr. Halpin, 39, and Mr. Zarkin, 51, will continue as executive vice presidents of Waban.
