Salant Posts 1st-Period Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

NEW YORK -- Salant Corp., citing a depressed retail environment, said its first-quarter net loss widened to $2.3 million, or 67 cents a share, from $427,000, or 12 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales for the apparel maker fell 7.8% to $101.9 million from $110.5 million.
