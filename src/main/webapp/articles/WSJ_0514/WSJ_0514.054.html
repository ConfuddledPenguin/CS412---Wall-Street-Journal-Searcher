Who's News: Varian Reshuffles Senior Management Under Restructuring

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Varian Associates Inc. announced several senior management appointments under its plan to restructure the electronics company. Allen J. Lauer, 52 years old, a senior vice president, was named executive vice president in charge of analytical instruments. Richard M. Levy, 51, also a senior vice president, was named executive vice president of semiconductor processing and medical equipment. A.D. Wilunowski, 42, a corporate vice president, was named executive vice president of electron devices.

In addition, the company said it named Al Ferreira, 45, to the newly created post of vice president of operations. Richard H. Thompson, 52, formerly director of business development for the Pacific Rim region, was named to the new post of vice president, international. Derrel De Passe, 42, formerly director of government affairs, was named vice president of government affairs.
