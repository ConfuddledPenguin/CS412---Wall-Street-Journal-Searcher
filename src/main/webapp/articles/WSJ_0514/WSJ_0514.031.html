Unit Corp. Drops Stock Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Unit Corp. said it withdrew its proposed offering of one million shares of convertible exchangeable preferred from the market because of "significant common stock price declines during the registration period." The oil and natural gas company said the offering terms were "excessively dilutive to our existing shareholders and not consistent with our objectives to manage long-term asset value."
