Business Brief -- Datamag Inc.: Shareholder Says the SEC Is Investigating the Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

Datamag Inc. is being investigated by the Securities and Exchange Commission, said DMM Trust, an Iowa-based trust holding a 29% Datamag stake. In an SEC filing, the trust said it is concerned about the viability of Datamag and about the current management's ability to lead the company to profitability.The filing did not disclose the nature of the investigation.

Datamag Comptroller Dan Lohmann said that he hadn't seen the filing and that the company had no comment. Datamag, Scottsdale, Ariz., is a maker of magnetic memory media products.
