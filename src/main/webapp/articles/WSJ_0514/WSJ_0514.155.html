Whittaker Sells Yardney Power

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/14/90

LOS ANGELES -- Whittaker Corp. said it sold the Yardney Power Systems division of its Whittaker Technical Products Inc. unit to a group led by the division management. Terms weren't disclosed. Yardney Power, based in Pawcatuck, Conn., makes batteries for military and industrial use. Whittaker Corp., a maker of high-technology equipment, said the sale is related to its continuing divestiture program.
