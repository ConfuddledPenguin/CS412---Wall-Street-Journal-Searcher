New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange added four new common-stock listings, while the American Stock Exchange added one, and the National Market System of the over-the-counter market five. On the Big Board, City National Corp., a Beverly Hills banking and data-processing firm, begins trading Thursday under the symbol CYN.

Dial REIT Inc., an Omaha real estate investment trust, begins trading Friday under the symbol DR.

France Growth Fund Inc., a New York management investment company, is an initial public offering that began trading Friday under the symbol FRF.

Pioneer Financial Services Inc., a Rockford, Ill., national insurance and marketing company, begins trading Wednesday under the symbol PFS.

On the Amex, Schult Homes Corp., a Middlebury, Ind., producer of manufactured homes previously traded over the counter, begins trading Thursday under the symbol SHC.

On the OTC market, Barton Industries Inc., a Shawnee, Okla., oil and gas valve manufacturer, began trading under BART.

Canyon Resources Corp., a Golden, Colo., gold and silver mining company, began trading under CYNR.

Mid Atlantic Medical Services Inc., a Rockville, Md., health care organization operator, began trading under MAMS.

Midwesco Filter Resources Inc., a Winchester, Va., manufacturer of filter bags for industrial pollution systems, began trading under MFRI.

RasterOps, a Santa Clara, Calif., color imaging products manufacturer, began trading under ROPS.
