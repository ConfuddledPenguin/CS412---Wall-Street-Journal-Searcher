Software Services of America

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

NORTH ANDOVER, Mass. -- Software Services of America Inc. said one of its shareholders filed a class action lawsuit against the company and its president, Bertram R. Hickman Jr., charging that the company "artificially inflated" its stock price by making misleading statements. The suit was filed in federal district court in Boston by Sherwin E. Ross, the company said. The suit alleges that the company made "materially false and misleading statements and material omissions" between October 1986 and March 1989, the company added.

Software Services, a maker of banking software, said it intends to defend itself against the suit vigorously.
