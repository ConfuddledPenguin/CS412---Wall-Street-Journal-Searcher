Poseidon Dismisses Chief; Investor Seeks Return of Investment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

NEW YORK -- Poseidon Pools of America Inc. said it fired its chief executive officer. It also said a party that invested $150,000 in the company wants its money back. A spokesman said the announcements are unrelated, but declined to elaborate further.

The manufacturer and retailer of above-ground swimming pools said it dismissed Joseph A. Cioni, its president and chief executive officer, who was hired in February. It said the company's chairman, George Tassone, was reinstated as Poseidon's chief executive.

Separately, Poseidon said the investor has alleged that the company failed to disclose at the time of the investment certain "improper conduct" by Mr. Tassone. The company said its board has established a committee of outside directors to investigate the allegations. Mr. Tassone could not be reached for comment.
