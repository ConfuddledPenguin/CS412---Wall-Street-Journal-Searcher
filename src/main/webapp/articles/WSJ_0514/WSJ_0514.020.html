Price Co. Repurchases Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

SAN DIEGO -- Price Co. said it has acquired 747,500 shares of its common stock since February, bringing the total number of shares it has repurchased to 1,247,500. The 747,500 shares were acquired by the members-only discount department-store chain in two phases, with 207,500 of them bought at an average price of $38.50 during the latter part of the second quarter ended March 18, 1990, and the remaining 540,000 shares acquired at an average price of $33.125 during the current third quarter.

All of the purchases were made pursuant to a previously announced board authorization to acquire up to an aggregate of $50 million of any of the company's outstanding securities.
