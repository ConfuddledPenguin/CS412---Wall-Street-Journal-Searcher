  • Date: 05/14/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Arvin Industries Inc., shelf offering of up to $150 million medium-term notes, half of which will be issued by its indirect unit, Arvin Overseas Finance B.V. via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Canada's Province of Newfoundland, shelf offering of up to $350 million of debentures, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Kelley Oil Corp., offering of 2.5 million shares of common, of which one million will be sold by the company, 1.5 million by holders, via Tucker Anthony Inc. and Eppler, Guerin & Turner Inc.

Patriot Select Dividend Trust, initial offering of seven million common shares, via Dean Witter Reynolds Inc. and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.
