American Stores May Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/14/90

SALT LAKE CITY -- American Stores Co. said it is considering the sale of Buttrey Food & Drug, a division of its wholly owned subsidiary, American Food & Drug Inc. American Stores said the move stems from a need to review "marginal performing assets" in the wake of litigation by the California attorney general blocking the merger of its Alpha Beta markets with Lucky Stores Inc.
