Who's News: Unisys Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

UNISYS Corp. (Blue Bell, Pa.) -- J. Carl Masi was named vice president of corporate marketing at this computer maker. Mr. Masi, 49 years old, succeeds Jack Holton, who was named vice president, strategic account marketing. He was previously chairman and chief executive officer for International Data Corp., a high-technology market research and consulting company, based in Framingham, Mass. Prior to IDC, Mr. Masi held several senior level posts during 12 years at Wang Laboratories Inc., in Lowell, Mass.
