Armco Modifies Notes Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

PARSIPPANY, N.J. -- Armco Inc. said it has modified its consent solicitation and offer to purchase its 13 1/2% senior notes due 1994 from consenting noteholders. The modification increases the consent fee to $17.50 from $10 for each $1,000 principal amount and extends the offer to 5:01 p.m., EST, Friday, Sept. 28.

Armco wants noteholders to approve an amendment that allows the company to continue paying common stock dividends. The steelmaker needs 50.1% of the outstanding $164 million in notes to approve the change and had about $53.5 million as of Sept. 17.
