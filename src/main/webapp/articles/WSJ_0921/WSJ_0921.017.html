Who's News: CPC International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

CPC INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Englewood Cliffs, N.J.) -- Gordon Granger was named president of the Latin America consumer foods division, effective Oct. 1, at this food company. Mr. Granger, 53 years old, succeeds Osvaldo Martinez, 65, who will retire Jan. 1. Mr. Granger was previously vice president, consumer operations, for CPC's Latin America division and concurrently general manager for its Brazilian subsidiary.
