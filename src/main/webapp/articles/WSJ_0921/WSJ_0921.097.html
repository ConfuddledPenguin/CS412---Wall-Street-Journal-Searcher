Acme Steel Purchases Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

RIVERDALE, Ill. -- Acme Steel Co., in an effort to expand its steel processing business, said it bought certain assets from Donovan Steel Inc. of Toledo, Ohio, for undisclosed terms. The steel maker said it bought slitters and other equipment from Donovan and will lease the former Donovan facility, which will do business as Alta Slitting Corp., a newly formed Acme subsidiary.
