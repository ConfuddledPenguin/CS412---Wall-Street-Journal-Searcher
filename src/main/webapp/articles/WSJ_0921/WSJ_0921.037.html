Who's News: National City Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

NATIONAL CITY Corp. (Cleveland) -- Roger M. Dalton, previously senior vice president and director of community banking at First Kentucky National Corp., has been named president and chief executive officer of National City's CommerceNational Bank of Lexington, Ky. Robert E. Showalter, previously president and chief executive officer of CommerceNational, has been elected president and chief executive officer of National City's Ohio Citizens Bank of Toledo. Robert G. Siefers, previously president and chief executive officer of Ohio Citizens Bank, has joined the executive management team at National City.
