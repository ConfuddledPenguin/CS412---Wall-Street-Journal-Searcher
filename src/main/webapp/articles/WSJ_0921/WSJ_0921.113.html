Logicon Gets Job Extension

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

LOS ANGELES -- Logicon Inc. said it received a four-year contract extension valued at $33.5 million to provide advanced technology services to the Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Pasadena, Calif. Logicon said its R&D Associates subsidiary has provided software engineering, systems development and operational support services to JPL since 1987. The extension brings the contract's total value to $67.7 million, Logicon said.
