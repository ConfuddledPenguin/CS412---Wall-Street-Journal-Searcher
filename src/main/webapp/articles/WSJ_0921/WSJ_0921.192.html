Jacobs Engineering Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc., an engineering and design company, said it was awarded two engineering projects by Eli Lilly & Co. at the pharmaceutical company's Clinton Laboratories in Indiana. The projects have a combined value of more than $150 million.

The first project involves a $90 million, 42,000-square-foot addition of chemical manufacturing capacity that is scheduled for completion in early 1992. The second involves removing all of the plant's underground chemical storage tanks and replacing them with above ground tanks at a cost of about $60 million.

Completion is scheduled for the end of 1992.
