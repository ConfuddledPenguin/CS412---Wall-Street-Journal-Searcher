Halliburton Increases Budget

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

DALLAS -- Halliburton Co. said it would increase its 1990 capital budget for property, plant and equipment by 11%, to $395 million from $355 million. The increase marks the second time this year that the company has raised its 1990 spending budget. The oil services concern said that more than 80% of the current budget of $395 million is allocated to its oil field services group. The group's capital needs have increased in response to growing demand for a variety of its services, Halliburton said.
