Business Brief -- American Credit Optical Inc.: Firm to Buy Some Assets Of Allied Marketing Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

American Credit Optical Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement to purchase $20 million of the operating assets of Dallas-based Allied Marketing Group Inc. The assets are related primarily to closely held Allied's direct mail marketing division. American Credit Optical, a Fort Worth, Texas, financial concern, said the purchase is subject to financing, which Dean Witter Reynolds Inc. has been engaged to arrange. The company said it expects to complete the transaction within the next several weeks.
