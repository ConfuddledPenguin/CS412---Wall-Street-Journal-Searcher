Business Brief -- Smit Internationale N.V.: Dutch Salvaging Firm Posts Narrower Loss for 1st Half

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

Smit Internationale N.V. reported a loss for the first half of 1990 of 7.2 million guilders ($4.1 million), a slight narrowing from the 8.6 million loss posted in the year-earlier period. However, the Dutch salvaging company projected that earnings for the year would at least equal its 1989 net profit of 4 million guilders.

Smit said operating profit improved to 18 million guilders from 1.0 million guilders, but earnings were affected by losses incurred by associated companies in which Smit has holdings.
