Who's News: Image Bank Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

IMAGE BANK Inc. (New York) -- Sheldon F. Peckar was named chief financial officer and treasurer at this communications company. Mr. Peckar, 46 years old, was previously executive vice president, finance. He succeeds Paul Eichler, 56, who will remain an executive vice president and a member of the board in addition to serving as investor relations officer.
