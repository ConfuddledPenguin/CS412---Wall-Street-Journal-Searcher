Ashland Oil Unit's Agreement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

ASHLAND, Ky. -- Ashland Oil Inc., a diversified energy concern, said its APAC Construction Group signed a letter of intent to acquire a North Carolina highway and utilities contractor. Terms of sale weren't disclosed. The Kitty Hawk, N.C., family-owned contractor, Outer Banks Contractors Inc., is expected to have revenue of more than $40 million this year, Ashland said. Outer Banks' senior managers have agreed to continue in their current positions after the acquisition, Ashland said.

APAC, a major highway contractor, is based in Atlanta. Outer Banks "provides a geographical strategic fit to our existing operations in North Carolina," APAC said.
