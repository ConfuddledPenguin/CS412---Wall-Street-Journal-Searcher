McDonnell Douglas Gets Air Force Contract Totaling $836.4 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. received an $836.4 million Air Force contract for F-15E aircraft. The F-15E, the company's most sophisticated jet fighter, has the ability to attack targets on the ground from long range. Work on the contract is to be completed in May 1992.

In other awards yesterday, Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., received a $17.4 million Navy contract for aircraft electronics.

International Business Machines Corp. got a $14.9 million Navy contract for aircraft electronics.

Honeywell Inc. was given a $13 million Air Force contract for aircraft navigation equipment.

Litton Systems Inc., a unit of Litton Industries Inc., won a $12.3 million Air Force contract for aircraft navigation equipment.
