Who's News: Sherritt Gordon Picks Delaney as Chairman

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

TORONTO -- Sherritt Gordon Ltd. said Ian Delaney was appointed chairman and chief executive officer of the chemical and metals manufacturer. Mr. Delaney, 47 years old, replaces Edward Donegan as chairman. The company said Bruce Walter was named president. Mr. Walter, 32, replaces Charles Heindrich, 49, formerly president and chief executive officer.

On Wednesday, a group including Messrs. Delaney and Walter succeeded in replacing the former Sherritt board with their own nominees at a special meeting of shareholders.

Mr. Delaney said previously there would be an executive position available for Mr. Heindrich if he decides to stay with Sherritt.
