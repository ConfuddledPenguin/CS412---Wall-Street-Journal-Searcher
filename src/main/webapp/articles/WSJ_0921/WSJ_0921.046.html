Who's News: Ameritrust Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

AMERITRUST Corp. (Cleveland, Ohio) -- Three executives have been appointed to the company's newly formed Credit Policy and Risk Management Group. Peter R. Butcher, from the San Francisco-based banking firm ICON Associates, has been named executive vice president and senior credit policy officer. He'll manage the corporation's loan portfolio and develop and implement lending strategies. Kevin M. Blakely, from the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency, has been named senior vice president. He'll review credit and credit administration risk throughout the corporation.

Karl G. Grunwalt, a vice president of a unit of AmeriTrust's Merchant Banking Division, has been promoted to senior vice president. His duties include identifying and overseeing management of potential problem loans.
