Healthco to Give Gemini Partners L.P. 3 Seats on New Board

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

BOSTON -- Healthco International Inc., ending a four-month battle with dissident shareholder Gemini Partners L.P., agreed to give Gemini three seats on a new seven member Healthco board. Gemini, a New York limited partnership with a 9.96% stake in Healthco, had been soliciting consents from shareholders to remove Healthco's current board. It said earlier this month that it had enough consents to do so, though Healthco disagreed with Gemini's count.

Healthco said it will convene its annual meeting on Sept. 25 and immediately adjourn the meeting to a later date to allow time to send out revised proxy materials outlining the terms of the settlement with Gemini.

Healthco, a dental equipment firm, also said it will form a special committee of major shareholders to review the progress of its proposed acquisition by Dallas buyout specialist Hicks, Muse & Co. As reported, Hicks Muse agreed earlier this month to acquire Healthco for $19.25 a share, or $250 million.

Prior to reaching the settlement with Healthco, Gemini had criticized the $19.25-a-share price as "ludicrously" low and said that if its slate was elected it would work to get a better price for Healthco.

In a joint statement with Healthco, Gemini executive general partner Emmanuel R. Pearlman said Gemini "was pleased to arrive at an amicable resolution with Healthco which we believe is in the best interests of all Healthco stockholders." He couldn't be reached for further comment.

A spokesman for Healthco said various suits filed by Healthco and Gemini in connection with the consent solicitation will be withdrawn or settled by the two parties.

Commenting on the settlement, Healthco Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Marvin M. Cyker said: "We can now put this behind us and devote ourselves to the real tasks -- running Healthco's business for the benefit of all of our various constituencies and completing as soon as possible our $19.25 cash merger with Hicks Muse."
