PPG to Close Flat-Glass Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

PITTSBURGH -- PPG Industries Inc. said it will shut down its Crystal City, Mo., flat-glass manufacturing plant by the end of the year because of excess capacity in that industry and high costs to modernize the plant. PPG will meet with officials of the Aluminum, Brick & Glass Workers International Union, which represents the plant's 231 production, maintenance and clerical employees, to negotiate benefits and other conditions of termination. The paints, chemical and glass manufacturer said the closing wouldn't result in a charge.
