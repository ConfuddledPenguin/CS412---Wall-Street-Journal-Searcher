Martin Marietta Sells Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

BETHESDA, Md. -- Martin Marietta Corp. said it completed the first sale of holdings of its Orlando Central Park Inc. unit in Orlando, Fla., under a divestiture program announced last year. The $42.5 million sale includes 150 acres for hotel and retail development and the Mercado Shopping Village in the Plaza international project in Orlando, the company said. The properties were sold to three groups of investors, who weren't identified.

The company had announced in August 1989 that it planned to shed its Orlando Central Park holdings, and said yesterday that efforts to complete the program are continuing. The company's 50% interest in Orlando Peabody Hotel isn't included in the program.
