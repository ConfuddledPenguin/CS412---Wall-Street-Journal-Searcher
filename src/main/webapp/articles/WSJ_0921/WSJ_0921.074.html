Drug Emporium Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Drug Emporium Inc. said it acquired its Charleston, S.C., franchise and agreed to acquire two others for a total of about $8.8 million in cash and common stock. The discount drugstore chain expects to complete the purchase of franchises in St. Louis and San Diego within 60 days. With the acquisitions, the chain would own and operate 96 stores and franchise another 103 nationwide.
