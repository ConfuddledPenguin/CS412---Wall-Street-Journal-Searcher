Business Brief -- Amdura Corp.: Court Approves Settlement For Unpaid Medical Claims

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

Amdura Corp. said the U.S. Bankruptcy Court had approved a settlement agreement providing for the payment of unpaid retiree medical claims incurred prior to July 31, 1990, and for the establishment of an ongoing medical and life insurance plan for retirees and their dependents. The settlement covers about 2,000 former employees of the independent hardware distributor and its subsidiaries, the company said. Funding for the plan will come from retiree premium payments and a retiree benefit fund in excess of $1 million to be established by Amdura and its subsidiaries, the company said.

On April 2, Amdura and distribution subsidiaries filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. Amdura is a manufacturer of lifting and construction equipment based in Denver.
