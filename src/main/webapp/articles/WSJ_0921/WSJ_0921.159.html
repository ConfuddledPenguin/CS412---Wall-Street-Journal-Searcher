Business Brief -- Bonneville Pacific Corp: Portland General Agrees To Buy Up to 49% Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

Portland General Corp. has signed a definitive agreement to acquire as much as 49% of the common stock of Bonneville Pacific Corp., an independent energy, food processing and waste management concern, for about $34 million. Portland General, an energy holding company in Portland, Ore., will buy a $10 million debenture convertible into 1.3 million Bonneville common shares at $7.50 a share; 3.3 million new common shares at $6 each; and warrants outstanding at $1 each to buy four million additional common shares at $6 each.

Also, Portland General has the option to acquire additional common stock to raise its stake to the 49% total. Richard G. Reiten, Portland General's president, said the agreement is part of the company's efforts to build up its independent power business. The transaction is expected to close in mid-October.
