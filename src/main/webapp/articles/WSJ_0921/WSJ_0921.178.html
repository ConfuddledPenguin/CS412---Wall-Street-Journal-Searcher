Business Brief -- National Semiconductor Corp.: For Its First Quarter, Firm Reports $165.5 Million Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

As expected, National Semiconductor Corp. reported a net loss of $165.5 million, or $1.63 a share, for the first quarter ending Aug. 26, compared with a net loss of $21.9 million, or 24 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter. The company's bigger losses largely reflect a previously reported pretax charge of $143.6 million, which it took to cover a host of reorganizing costs.

Last month, the Santa Clara, Calif., chip maker announced that it was eliminating several product groups, closing two assembly lines, and laying off 6.2% of its work force, or 2,000 employees.

National Semiconductor's sales for the first period rose to $442.7 million, from $397.6 million a year ago.

This is the sixth net loss the company has posted in the past eight quarters.
