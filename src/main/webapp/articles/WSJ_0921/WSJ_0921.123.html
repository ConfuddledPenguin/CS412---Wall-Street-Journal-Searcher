Financing Business: Litton Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

LITTON INDUSTRIES Inc. began an offering of $1 billion face value of zero-coupon bonds, called liquid yield option notes, due Sept. 26, 2010. The Beverly Hills, Calif., defense and marine systems company will make the offering via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets. The offering is expected to raise about $258 million in net proceeds which the diversified technology concern will use to pay debt. The bonds, which have an annual yield to maturity of 6.75%, are convertible at a rate of 3.063 shares of common stock per note, representing an initial conversion price of $86.54.
