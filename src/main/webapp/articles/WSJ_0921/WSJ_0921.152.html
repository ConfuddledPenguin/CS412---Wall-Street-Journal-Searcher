Business Brief -- C-Cor Electronics Inc.: Loss, Decline in Revenue Is Expected in First Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

C-Cor Electronics Inc. said it expects to report a loss and a decline in revenue for the first quarter ending Sept. 28. The State College, Pa., electronics equipment maker attributed the slowdown to a lack of orders caused by the uncertainty in the cable television industry, which Congress is considering reregulating. The company said it also was hurt by the lending practices of financial institutions.

C-Cor said it expects fiber-optic product shipments from its C-Cor/Comlux Inc. unit to be at a record high in the first quarter.

In the year-ago first quarter, the company had net income of $1.1 million, or 26 cents a share, on revenue of $13.2 million.
