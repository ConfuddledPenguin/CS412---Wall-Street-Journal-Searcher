Marketing Brief -- MGM/UA Communications Co.: Pathe Communications Adds Payment Toward Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

MGM/UA Communications Co. said it received checks totaling $50 million from Pathe Communications Corp., bringing to $353 million the amount Pathe has paid in cash toward its proposed $1.3 billion acquisition of MGM/UA. The payment is the last in a series of good-faith interim payments required under the companies' merger agreement. Pathe, based in Beverly Hills, Calif., has also given MGM/UA a $75 million security interest in three films slated by Pathe.

The merger pact calls for MGM/UA shareholders to receive a total of $21.50 a share, including a $4-a-share payment distributed on July 20. Payment of the remaining $17.50 a share and closing of the merger is set to occur by Oct. 23.

MGM/UA holders will meet to vote on the merger Oct. 2. The polling is a formality because Kirk Kerkorian, who controls about 80% of MGM/UA's shares, already agreed to vote in favor of the merger.

MGM/UA rose 25 cents to $14 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
