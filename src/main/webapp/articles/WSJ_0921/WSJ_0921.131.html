Marriott Signs Domino's To Bring Pizza to Schools

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

BETHESDA, Md. -- Marriott Corp. signed an agreement with Domino's Pizza Inc. to bring pizza to more than 200 school districts and 450 college campuses. Under the agreement, Domino's, a pizza delivery concern based in Ann Arbor, Mich., will deliver pizzas to the schools and colleges where Marriott provides dining services. In schools, the pizzas will be served in the cafeteria. At universities, the price of dorm-delivered pizzas will be deducted from students' meal accounts.

Marriott, a lodging and food-service concern, said the agreement offers a popular fast-food product to its student customers and allows Domino's to enter a new marketplace.
