Boeing Unit's Jetliner Orders

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

SEATTLE, Wash. -- Boeing Commercial Airplane Group said British Airways ordered five more 757 jetliners valued at about $270 million. Three previously held 757 options were converted to firm orders, and the additional two medium- to long-range jets had been ordered but not previously announced, Boeing said. Boeing Commercial Airplane, which has received 663 orders for the 757 aircraft, is a unit of Boeing Co., a commercial aircraft maker and defense contractor.
