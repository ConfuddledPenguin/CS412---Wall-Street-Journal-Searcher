Technology Brief -- 3Com Corp.: Company Says Net Income Quadrupled for 1st Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

3Com Corp. reported that net income for the first quarter ended Aug. 31 quadrupled, to $5 million, or 17 cents a share, from $1.2 million, or 4 cents a share, in the year-ago period. Sales rose 19% to $106.2 million from $89.1 million.

The Santa Clara, Calif., manufacturer of computer network systems said that new products, such as its transmissions and adapter devices, contributed to a third of sales, up from 10% a year earlier. 3Com said the new-product sales helped to offset weakening interest in the company's older workgroup systems products.
