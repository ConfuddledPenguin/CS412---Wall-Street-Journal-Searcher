International: Portuguese Bank's Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

LISBON -- The government announced Thursday it was revising the legal status of Portugal's largest commercial bank in preparation for its privatization. Banco Portugues do Atlantico will be transformed from a purely state company into a public limited company in which the state will remain the only shareholder. The eventual privatization of BPA is scheduled to be held in three stages. The first sale of 33% of shares expected before the end of this year.
