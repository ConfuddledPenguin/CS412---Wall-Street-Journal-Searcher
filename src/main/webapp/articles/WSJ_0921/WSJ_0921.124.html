Financing Business: First Seismic Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

FIRST SEISMIC Corp., a Denver-based marketer of seismic data, said it is offering one million common shares in its initial public offering at $9 each. All of the shares are being offered by the company. Selling stockholders have granted the underwriters an option to purchase as many as an additional 150,000 shares to cover over-allotments. Net proceeds from the offering will be used to repay debt, finance new seismic surveys and purchase seismic data, and for working capital and general corporate purposes. Rotan Mosle Inc. is the managing underwriter of the offering.
