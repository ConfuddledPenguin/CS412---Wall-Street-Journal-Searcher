Who's News: Occidental Petroleum Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

OCCIDENTAL PETROLEUM Corp. (Los Angeles) -- John F. Riordan was elected to the additional post of chief executive officer of the company's MidCon Corp. natural gas transmission subsidiary. He succeeds O.C. Davis, 70 years old, who continues as chairman. Mr. Riordan, 54, is also president and chief operating officer of the unit, based in Lombard, Ill.
