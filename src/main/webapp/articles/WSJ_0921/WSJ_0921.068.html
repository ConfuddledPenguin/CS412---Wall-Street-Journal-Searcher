Canadian Steel Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 159,248 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, up 0.4% from 158,653 tons in the preceding week but down 46% from 286,816 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The drop from the 1989 pace is partly attributable to an ongoing strike at Stelco Inc., a steel concern.

The cumulative total in 1990 was 9,421,764 tons, down 16% from 11,143,714 tons a year earlier.
