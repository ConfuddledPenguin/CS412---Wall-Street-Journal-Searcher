Pinnacle West Sells Malapai Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Pinnacle West Capital Corp. said it completed the sale of its uranium mining subsidiary, Malapai Resources Co., to a U.S. affiliate of Electricite de France for $38 million in cash. Pinnacle West, a utility holding company, agreed to sell the unit to the French electric utility in April. Proceeds from the sale will be used to reduce debt, Pinnacle West said.
