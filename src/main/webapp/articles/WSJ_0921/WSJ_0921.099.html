Technology: N.V. Philips to Sell Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

EINDHOVEN, the Netherlands -- As expected, N.V. Philips, the Dutch electronics giant, said that it would sell its 15% stake in AT&T Network Systems International BV back to American Telephone & Telegraph Co. A spokesman for Philips declined to provide financial information on how the transfer of the stake, which is effective immediately, will be done. The transfer of Philips's 15% stake in AT&T-NSI back to AT&T will boost AT&T's holdings in the joint venture to 74% from 59%.

Spain's Telefonica de Espana S.A. and Italy's Stet Group hold 6% and 20% stakes, respectively, in the joint venture.
