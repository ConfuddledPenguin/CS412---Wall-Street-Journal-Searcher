Dah Sing Financial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

HONG KONG -- Dah Sing Financial Holdings Ltd. said that Sumitomo Life Insurance Co. completed its purchase of a 9% stake in the financial services group for 113 million Hong Kong dollars (US$14.6 million). The Hong Kong group, which entered into a preliminary agreement with the Japanese insurance concern in August, said it will use the proceeds of the sale for working capital.

Dah Sing, already 18% owned by Mitsui Trust & Banking Co., said the Sumitomo link will help it build up its recently established life insurance unit.
