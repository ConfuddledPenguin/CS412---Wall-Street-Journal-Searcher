Who's News: Empire Blue Cross & Blue Shield

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

EMPIRE BLUE CROSS & BLUE SHIELD (New York) -- Donald L. Morchower was named executive vice president and chief operating officer, a new post at this private, not-for-profit health insurer. Mr. Morchower will be responsible for customer service and sales and will retain his current roles as executive vice president and chief information officer.
