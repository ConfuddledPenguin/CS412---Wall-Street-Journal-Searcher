Kobe Steel to Help Voest-Alpine Supply Auto Firms in Europe

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 09/21/90

TOKYO -- Kobe Steel Ltd. will provide Austria's Voest-Alpine Stahl Linz G.m.b.H. with technology to produce a corrosion-resistant steel used by the automobile industry, the Japanese company said. With this technology, Voest-Alpine could meet the steel requirements of Japanese auto makers that are expanding into Europe, a Kobe spokesman said.

The two companies signed a three-year agreement in Linz, Austria, for Kobe to supply the technology and to guide the Austrian company in making the so-called Hi-Super DN steel.

Voest-Alpine is the fourth European company to receive technical assistance from Japanese companies in making corrosion-resistant steel for auto makers, the Kobe spokesman said. The Austrian company is Europe's eighth-largest steel producer, with output of 4.5 million metric tons of crude steel last year. A metric ton is 2,204.6 pounds.
