Business Brief -- HealthVest: Regent Health Gets Lease To Operate Medical Center

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

HealthVest,an ailing real estate investment trust, said it had completed the previously announced transfer of the operating lease on Eastwood Medical Center in Memphis, Tenn., to Regent Health Group Inc., a closely held hospital-management company. HealthVest said the new 10-year lease provides for minimum annual rental payments to the trust of about $4.8 million and for additional rent on a formula basis. The agreement also provides for deferral of the first three months' rental, the company said. Healthvest, based in Austin, Texas, said Healthcare International Inc., the hospital management concern that leases many of its properties, will remain obligated to HealthVest for the difference between the annual base rent owned on Eastwood Medical Center through the termination of its original lease in February 1992 and the rent paid by Regent under the new lease. HealthVest estimated that amount at about $2.8 million a year.

For the six months, HealthVest reported a net loss of $6.8 million, or 64 cents a share, on revenue of $16 million, down from net income of $10.9 million, or 96 cents a share, on revenue of $29.4 million in the year-earlier period.
