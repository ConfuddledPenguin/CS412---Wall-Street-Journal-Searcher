Who's News: Medco Containment Services Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

MEDCO CONTAINMENT SERVICES Inc. (Fair Lawn, N.J.) -- Robert J. Levenson was named senior executive vice president and chief operating officer, a new post, at this provider of prescription drug programs to employers and insurers. Mr. Levenson was previously group president and a board member at Automatic Data Processing Inc., a Roseland, N.J.-based computing services concern. Per G.H. Lofberg, previously executive vice president, was named senior executive vice president, strategic planning and marketing.
