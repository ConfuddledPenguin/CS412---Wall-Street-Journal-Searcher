Who's News: Jet Aviation Management AG

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

JET AVIATION MANAGEMENT AG (Zurich) -- C.W. Hough was named an executive vice president at Jet Aviation Business Jets Inc., a Teterboro, N.J.-based subsidiary of this general aviation services company. Mr. Hough, 54 years old, was previously with Cobey Corp., an aircraft management company, based in Weston, Mass.
