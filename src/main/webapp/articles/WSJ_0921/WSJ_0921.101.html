Who's News: New York Life Insurance Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

NEW YORK LIFE INSURANCE Co. (New York) -- Michael J. Nocera, 44 years old, previously senior vice president, new product development, at the PaineWebber Properties Inc. unit of PaineWebber Group Inc., was named vice president and head of the partnership investments department at this insurer, succeeding Kurt A. Cerulli, who resigned.
