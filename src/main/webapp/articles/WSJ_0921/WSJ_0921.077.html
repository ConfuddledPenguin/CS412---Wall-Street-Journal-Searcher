J&L Specialty Products

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/21/90

PITTSBURGH -- J&L Specialty Products Corp. said it will redeem $200 million of its 14% senior subordinated notes in early November 1990. The redemption price will be 112.444% of the principal amount of the bonds plus accrued interest to the date of redemption. The stainless steelmaker was acquired in June by French stainless steelmaker, Ugine ACG.
