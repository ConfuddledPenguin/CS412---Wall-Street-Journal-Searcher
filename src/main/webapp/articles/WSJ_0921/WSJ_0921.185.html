Who's News: Medical Graphics Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

MEDICAL GRAPHICS Corp. (St. Paul, Minn.) -- Wayne G. Faris was named to an additional post on the board. Faris, 48, a partner in the law firm of Oppenheimer, Wolff & Donnelly, has acted as Medical Graphics' general counsel and secretary since 1988. Medical Graphics is a maker of computerized diagnostic systems.
