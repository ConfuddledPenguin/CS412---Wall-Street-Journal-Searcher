Who's News: Seafirst Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

SEAFIRST Corp. (Seattle) -- Luke S. Helms, 46 years old, was named chairman and chief executive officer, succeeding Richard P. Cooley, who will retire at the end of the year. Mr. Helms, currently president at the Seafirst Bank unit, will also take over as chairman and chief executive officer of Seafirst Bank; Mr. Cooley, 66, will step down from those posts Oct. 15. Mr. Helms joined Seafirst Bank in 1974. The company named Kevin B. Ferrell, 43, a senior vice president at BankAmerica Corp., to take over as president of Seafirst Bank. Seafirst Corp. is a unit of BankAmerica.
