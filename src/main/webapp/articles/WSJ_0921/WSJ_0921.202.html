Who's News: National Westminster Bank PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/13/90

NATIONAL WESTMINSTER BANK PLC (London) -- Roger Flemington, 58 years old, was named deputy group chief executive of this U.K. bank, effective Oct 1. Mr. Flemington is currently chief executive of U.K. financial services at the bank. He will be succeeded in that post by Derek Wanless, 42, currently general manager of U.K. branch business. Mr. Wanless, in turn, will be succeeded in that job by Martin Gray, 44, currently deputy general manager of group strategy and communications. That position will be filled by Bernard Horn, 44, previously general manager of the group chief executive's office. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

BERNARD HORN has been appointed general manager, group strategy and communications, at National Westminster Bank PLC. His title was misstated in the Sept. 13 edition.

(WSJ Sept. 21, 1990)
