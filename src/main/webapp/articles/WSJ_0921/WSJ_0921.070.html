Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/21/90

UNION PACIFIC Corp. (Bethlehem, Pa.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. affirmed its single-A rating on debt securities of this railroad, transportation, hazardous waste and real estate company. The ratings concern said the company's operating units show "continued favorable earnings prospects" while the proposed sale of its real estate business should help reduce debt leverage.
