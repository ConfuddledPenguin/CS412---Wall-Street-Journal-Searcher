Halsey Drug Co. Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

NEW YORK -- Halsey Drug Co. said its directors authorized the purchase of 250,000 of the generic drug company's 5.6 million shares outstanding. The company said management decided on the buy-back because it considered the stock undervalued. A spokesman said the purchase wasn't meant as an anti-takeover device.

The purchase is to be completed by year's end. Halsey shares closed yesterday at $3.0625, down 25 cents, in American Stock Exchange composite trading.
