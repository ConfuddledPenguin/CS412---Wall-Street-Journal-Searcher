Suntory's Stake in Allied-Lyons

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

LONDON -- Allied-Lyons PLC said Suntory Ltd. of Japan, a beverage concern, increased its shareholding in the British beverage company to just under 5% from 2.5%. Suntory subscribed for 19.1 million new Allied-Lyons shares at 446 pence each, paying a total of #85 million ($163.8 million). Suntory first acquired shares in Allied-Lyons in October 1988, forming the joint-venture company Suntory-Allied Lyons. A standstill agreement between the two companies prevents Suntory from increasing its stake beyond 5%.
