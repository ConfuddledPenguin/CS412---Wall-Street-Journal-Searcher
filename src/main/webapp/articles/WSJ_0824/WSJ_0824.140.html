Business Brief -- Sumitomo Metal Mining Co.: Estimates for Fiscal Half Sales, Pretax Profit Lifted

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

Sumitomo Metal Mining Co. of Japan revised its prediction of sales for its first half ending Sept. 30 to 274 billion yen ($1.88 billion) from 268 billion yen, encouraged by rising nonferrous metal prices and strong demand for construction materials in recent months. As a result, pretax profit in the first half is expected to reach 14 billion yen, up from the initially predicted 11 billion yen.

Sumitomo Metal Mining also said its pretax profit for the fiscal year ending March 31 is expected to rise to 27.5 billion yen from the previously estimated 22 billion yen. A year earlier, the company had pretax profit of 23.9 billion yen.
