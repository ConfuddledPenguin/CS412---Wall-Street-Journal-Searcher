Angeles to Expand Mall Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

LOS ANGELES -- Angeles Corp. said it reached an agreement to acquire its joint-venture partner's interest in four factory-direct shopping malls and a related property management and development business for an undisclosed price. Angeles said it would buy H&W Venture Co.'s interest in the malls, which have an estimated aggregate value of more than $70 million. The malls are located in Barstow, Calif., Wytheville, Va., Osage Beach, Mo., and Branson, Mo.

Angeles, an investment management concern, said the acquisition is expected to close in about two weeks.
