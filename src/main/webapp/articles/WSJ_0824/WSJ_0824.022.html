Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 151,255 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, down 8.5% from 165,257 tons in the preceding week and down 48% from 293,566 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production fell 13% to 8,773,699 metric tons from 10,030,085 tons a year earlier.
