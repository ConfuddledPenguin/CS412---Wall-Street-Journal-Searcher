Orbit Instrument Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

HAUPPAUGE, N.Y. -- Orbit Instrument Corp. announced plans to purchase an additional one million shares of its common, or 11% of total shares oustanding, in open-market trading or in privately negotiated block transactions. The company has bought nearly 1.2 million of its shares since Jan. 1. Orbit Instrument currently has 8.9 million shares outstanding.

President Max Reissman said the price of the company's stock continues to be an attractive investment, and does not detract from its program of business acquisitions.

Orbit Instrument is a manufacturer of customized electronic components and subsystems for military and non-military applications.
