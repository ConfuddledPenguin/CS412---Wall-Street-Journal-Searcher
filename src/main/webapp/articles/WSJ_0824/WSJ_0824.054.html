Who's News: Breakwater Resources Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

BREAKWATER RESOURCES Ltd. (Vancouver, British Columbia) -- Peter Steen, 59 years old, was named chairman of this gold and zinc mining company, succeeding Peter Brown, 48, who remains a director. Mr. Steen is president and chief executive officer of Corona Corp., a Toronto-based gold mining concern that owns 27.4% of Breakwater.
