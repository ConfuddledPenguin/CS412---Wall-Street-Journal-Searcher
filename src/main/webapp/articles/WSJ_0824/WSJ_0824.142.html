Business Brief -- B.F. Goodrich Co.: Credit Totaling $21.3 Million To Be Taken in 3rd Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

B.F. Goodrich Co. said it would post a third-quarter after-tax credit of $21.3 million, including a $30.8 million credit from a tax judgment and a $9.5 million charge from write-offs. The chemical, aerospace, and tire company said it reached an agreement with the Internal Revenue Service over a 1985 tax deduction for its Convent Chemical Corp. unit, which it sold in 1985. The deduction was originally disallowed.

The company, based in Akron, Ohio, said it would post the $9.5 million charge for write-offs of its Canadian Geon Vinyl division and other assets.
