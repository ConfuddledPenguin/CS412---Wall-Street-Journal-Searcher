Who's News: Heldor Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

HELDOR INDUSTRIES Inc. (Morristown, N.J.) -- Robert J. Wisniewski was named executive vice president, a new post, at this maker of swimming pool equipment. Mr. Wisniewski, 48, was previously president of Gattefosse Corp., Elmsford, N.Y., an affiliate of Gattefosse S.A., a French specialty chemicals firm.
