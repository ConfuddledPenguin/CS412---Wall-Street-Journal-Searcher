International Brief -- Lend Lease Corp.: Australian Firm Posts Rise In Annual Operating Profit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

Lend Lease Corp. of Australia, a property and financial services concern, reported that for its fiscal year ended June 30, operating profit after taxes rose 18% to 160.5 million Australian dollars (US$131.7 million) from A$135.6 million a year earlier. The latest results didn't include any special items. Thus, earnings rose 2.8% to A$160.5 million from A$156.2 million a year earlier, when results included a A$20.6 million extraordinary item. Per-share earnings rose to 95.8 Australian cents from 90.7 cents. The annual dividend, including special payments, is 70 cents a share, up from 69 cents.

Operating revenue rose 23% to A$1.82 billion from A$1.48 billion.

Stuart Hornery, Lend Lease chairman, said profit will increase further in the company's current fiscal year.
