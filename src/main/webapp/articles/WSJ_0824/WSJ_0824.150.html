Money-Fund Assets Rose in Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 520 money-market funds rose $6.22 billion to $408 billion in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 253 general-purpose funds rose $2.40 billion to $142.45 billion, and assets of 105 broker-dealer funds rose $2.67 billion to $166.07 billion. Assets of 162 institutional funds rose $1.15 billion to $99.48 billion.

"Massive asset inflows continued," said Jacob Dreyer, vice president and chief economist at the institute. Investors moved money out of equities and bonds and into short-term products.

Mutual fund assets for the week ended Aug. 15 were revised to $401.78 billion. The increase for the week was revised to $2.54 billion.
