Letters to the Editor: Open Secret

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

In "Dynasty Undone" on the family feud affecting U-Haul (page one, July 16), I am quoted as saying, in reference to Joe and Mark Shoen, "Their desire for absolute power is insatiable. The more they have, the more they want." These are accurate quotes from a confidential psychological report that I submitted to their father and then-chairman of the board, L.S. Shoen, 11 years ago. My concern is that the public will believe that I authorized the release of these quotes and therefore lose confidence in the professional psychologist's commitment to the principle of confidentiality. I did not authorize, nor did I know beforehand, that these statements would be released. When I completed my 10 months of work with the Shoen family, I wrote and gave to the principal officer of U-Haul my assessment of the working members of the family. It was clearly marked confidential, but once a report leaves my hands I no longer own nor control it. I am firmly committed to the principle of confidentiality as stated by the American Psychological Association and the Arizona Board of Psychological Examiners. Jerry R. Day

Certified Psychologist

Time Out Counseling Center

Tucson, Ariz.
