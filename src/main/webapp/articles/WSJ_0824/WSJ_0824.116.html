Business Brief -- Amdura Corp.: Coast to Coast Unit Assets' Sale to Servistar Completed

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

Amdura Corp., a Denver-based automotive and hardware distributor and specialty manufacturer, said it completed a previously announced sale of assets in its Coast to Coast Stores Inc. subsidiary to Servistar Corp., a dealer-owned hardware cooperative based in Butler, Pa. Amdura said cash received at closing totaled about $52.5 million, though Coast to Coast's estate might receive additional payments of as much as $6.5 million, depending on Servistar's success in signing up additional independently owned Coast to Coast Stores. The Coast to Coast unit sells hardware to individually owned retail stores operating under the Coast to Coast franchise.

The assets involved in the Coast to Coast transaction included inventory, two distribution centers and Coast to Coast's trademarks; Amdura said the deal excluded receivables and various other Coast to Coast assets. Amdura and its hard-lines distribution subsidiaries, including Coast to Coast Stores, filed for protection last April from creditors' lawsuits under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, pending formation of a reorganization plan.
