Who's News: Central Maine Power Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

CENTRAL MAINE POWER Co. (Augusta, Maine) -- Carlton D. Reed Jr., chairman and chief executive officer of Reed & Reed Builders of Woolwich, a construction firm in Woolwich, Maine, was elected vice chairman of this electric utility company. The post had been vacant since January, when John J. Russell, who previously held the position, was named chairman.
