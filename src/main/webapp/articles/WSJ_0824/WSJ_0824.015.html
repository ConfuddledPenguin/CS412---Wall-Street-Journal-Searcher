Imperial Oil to Sell Coal Mine

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

TORONTO -- Imperial Oil Ltd., 70%-owned by Exxon Corp., said it is seeking a buyer for a coal mine near Sparwood, British Columbia. The mine produces about 1.6 million metric tons of coal annually and has annual revenue of 50 million to 100 million Canadian dollars (US$44 million to US$88 million), a spokesman said. A metric ton is about 2,204.62 pounds.

An Imperial spokesman said selling the mine would help reduce debt from the oil and gas concern's C$5 billion acquisition of Texaco Canada Inc. this year.
