Who's News: Hasbro Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

HASBRO Inc. (Pawtucket, R.I.) -- Dan Owen was named president of this toymaker's Playskool division, succeeding Stephen Schwartz, who resigned earlier this week. Mr. Owen was previously senior vice president of Playskool. Hasbro also named Norman Walker president of its newly formed international division and E. David Wilson president of its Milton Bradley division. Walker was previously president of European operations for Hasbro and Wilson was senior vice president of sales at Milton Bradley. At Milton Bradley, Wilson succeeds George R. Ditomassi, who was named co-chief operating officer of Hasbro, responsible for games and international operations, earlier this week in a series of senior management changes.
