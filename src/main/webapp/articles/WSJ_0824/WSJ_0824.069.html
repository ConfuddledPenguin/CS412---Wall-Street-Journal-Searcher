Who's News: Imcera Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

IMCERA GROUP Inc. (Northbrook, Ill.) -- Boyd D. Wainscott, 46 years old, formerly executive vice president of the Pitman-Moore Inc. unit of this maker of animal health and nutrition products, was named president and chief executive officer of the subsidiary. He succeeds M. Blake Ingle, 48, who was elected president and chief operating officer of Imcera in July.
