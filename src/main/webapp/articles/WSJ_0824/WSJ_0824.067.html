Lukens Steel to Raise Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

COATESVILLE, Pa. -- Lukens Steel Co., a subsidiary of Lukens Inc., said it will raise prices of carbon plate steel and carbon steel floor plate by $20 a ton. The increase raises carbon and HSLA steel plate to $485 a ton and carbon floor plate to $525 a ton. Lukens Steel specializes in the production of carbon, alloy and clad steel plate. The price increases will apply to orders received Monday or later and scheduled for shipment beginning Oct. 15.
