Michael Baker Acquires Firms

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

BEAVER, Pa. -- Michael Baker Corp., which provides operation, maintenance and professional engineering services for facilities design, environmental management and transportation, said it acquired MO Inc. and MO Projects Inc., both Houston-based providers of operation and maintenance support for industry. Terms weren't disclosed. The company said the MO concerns, which were privately owned by Michael Gibbs, had sales of $25 million in the year ended June 30. In its second quarter ended July 1, Michael Baker earned $673,689, or 24 cents a share, on revenue of $28.8 million.
