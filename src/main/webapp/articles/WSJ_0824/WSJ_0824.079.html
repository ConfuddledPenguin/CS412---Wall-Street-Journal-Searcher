Oxford Energy Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

SANTA ROSA, Calif. -- Oxford Energy Co. said it has begun a one-year stock repurchase program under which it will buy back a "limited" number of its 9.9 million shares outstanding. The company didn't specify how many shares it would buy.

In trading on the American Stock Exchange, Oxford closed at $3, down 25 cents. Robert J. Cushman, chief financial officer of the owner and operator of alternative energy and recycling facilities, said the company believes its stock is "undervalued at present levels."
