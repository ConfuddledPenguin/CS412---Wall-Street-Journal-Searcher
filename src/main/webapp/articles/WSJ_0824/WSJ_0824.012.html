East German Electricity Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

PARIS -- French state-owned Electricite de France, or EDF, said the agency that oversees privatizations in East Germany, the Treuhandanstalt, approved a plan in which a group to be led by EDF will be able to participate in the restructuring of East Germany's electricity system. The agency signed an accord Tuesday with three West German electrical concerns and EDF, which allows the EDF-led group to acquire a participation of as much as 15% in the future electric company that will be in charge of power production and transportation -- but not distribution -- in East Germany.

EDF hasn't disclosed the make-up of the group, which will include other European electrical companies.
