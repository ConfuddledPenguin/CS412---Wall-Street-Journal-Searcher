Business Brief -- Goal Systems Inc.: Software Concern Settles Suit With Computer Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

Goal Systems Inc. said it settled a lawsuit by Computer Associates International Inc. of California. The settlement provides for an undisclosed series of fixed payments to Computer Associates, and resulted in a second-quarter charge of $530,000, or two cents a share.

The suit claimed that Goal's JOBTRAC and RUNTRAC products infringed on Computer Associates' copyrights, and used misappropriated trade secrets.

Separately, Goal, an Ohio-based software products concern, said net income from the second quarter, ended July 31, was $2.1 million, or 11 cents a share, on revenue of $26.4 million. In the year-ago period, Goal Systems reported a restated net income of $1.6 million, or nine cents a share, on revenue of $19.7 million.
