Who's News: Neco Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

NECO ENTERPRISES Inc. (Providence, R.I.) -- Paul E. Buff was named president of this holding company, succeeding David F. LaRoche, who remains chairman and chief executive officer. Mr. Buff also was named chief operating officer, a new post, and remains president of the Quechee Lakes Corp. unit. Mr. LaRoche assumed the additional post of secretary, succeeding Richard J. Hayes, who also resigned as a director to return to private accounting practice, but will remain treasurer and chief financial officer until a successor is found.
