Business Brief -- Primerica Corp.: Fingerhut Unit Will Hire 1,500 Workers in Minnesota

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

Fingerhut Corp., a Minnetonka, Minn., unit of New York-based Primerica Corp., said it expects to hire 1,500 employees to meet anticipated seasonal business needs. Fingerhut, a direct-mail marketer of consumer products, plans to hire 500 regular full-time and 1,000 seasonal business office, warehouse and telemarketing workers for its St. Cloud, Minn., order-fulfillment operation. Hiring will be completed by late October and employment will extend through the Christmas selling season. Fingerhut currently employs 6,000 workers in Minnesota and Wisconsin.
