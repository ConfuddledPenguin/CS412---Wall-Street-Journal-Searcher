AT&T Service at ITT Hotels

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

BASKING RIDGE, N.J. -- American Telephone & Telegraph Co. said it has agreed to provide long-distance operator service at 52 ITT Sheraton hotels in the U.S. AT&T said the contract, valued at approximately $15 million over three years, provides all guest rooms in the participating hotels with 24-hour service.

An AT&T spokesman said the telecommunications company is negotiating similar terms with the remaining 280 Sheraton hotel franchises of ITT Sheraton, a subsidiary of New York-based ITT Corp., which also has interests in financial services and industrial and defense products.
