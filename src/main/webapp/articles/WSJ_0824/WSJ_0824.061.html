Financing Business: CII Financial Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

CII FINANCIAL Inc., Burbank, Calif., said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer $40 million principal amount of its convertible subordinated debentures, due 2005. The provider of workers' compensation insurance said that Kidder, Peabody & Co., Allen & Co. and Bateman Eichler, Hill Richards Inc. will lead the underwriters. A CII spokesman said proceeds will be used to increase capital to write insurance policies and to fund a proposed insurance-premium finance subsidiary, and for general corporate purposes.
