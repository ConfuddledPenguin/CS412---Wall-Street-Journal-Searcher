R.R. Donnelley Plans Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

CHICAGO -- R.R. Donnelley & Sons Co., a printing company, said its board authorized the purchase of as many as 5.6 million, or 7.2%, of its common shares outstanding, in open-market buy-backs from time to time and in negotiated transactions. The company said it expects to finance the buy-backs with available cash and borrowings.
