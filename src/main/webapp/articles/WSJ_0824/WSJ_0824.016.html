Revco Sale Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

TWINSBURG, Ohio -- Revco D.S. Inc. said it reached an agreement to sell 146 stores in the Southwest, completing its plan to divest itself of 712 stores in 17 states. The retail drugstores, in Texas, New Mexico, Arizona, and Colorado, were sold to RDS Acquisition Corp. for an undisclosed amount. The company said RDS also agreed to lease the Revco distribution center in Glendale, Ariz.

The sales must be approved by the bankruptcy court in Akron, Ohio. Revco has been under bankruptcy-law protection from creditors since July 28, 1988.
