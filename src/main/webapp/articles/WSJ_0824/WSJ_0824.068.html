Daiwa, Nikko Seek Swiss Bank Licenses

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 08/24/90

TOKYO -- Daiwa Securities Co. and Nikko Securities Co. have applied to Swiss authorities for banking licenses, officials at the two major Japanese brokerage firms said. The licenses would allow the firms to expand the scope of their operations in Switzerland and compete on a more equal footing. "It's essential to our business," a Daiwa executive said.

While both companies operate subsidiaries in Switzerland, their chief Japanese rivals, Nomura Securities Co. and Yamaichi Securities Co., have had licenses to offer banking services since 1988.

Officials at Daiwa and Nikko said they didn't know when Swiss authorities would reply to the applications.
