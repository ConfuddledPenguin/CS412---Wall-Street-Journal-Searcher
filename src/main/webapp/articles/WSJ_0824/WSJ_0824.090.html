  • Date: 08/24/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Associates Corp. of North America, a unit of Associates First Capital Corp., which is an indirect subsidiary of Ford Motor Co., shelf offering of $3 billion in debt securities and warrants.

Fibermux Corp., initial offering of 3,125,000 common shares, of which 1,875,000 will be sold by the company, 1,250,000 by holders, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Wisconsin Health Organization Insurance Corp., a unit of Carenetwork Inc., initial offering of 2.5 million shares, of which 1,750,000 will be sold by the company, 750,000 by holders, via Dillon, Read & Co. and Robert W. Baird & Co.
