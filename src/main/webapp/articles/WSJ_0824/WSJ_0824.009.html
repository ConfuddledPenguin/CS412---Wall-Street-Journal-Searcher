Softsel Changes Name

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Softsel Computer Products Inc. said its shareholders approved a change in the company's name to Merisel Inc. The change, effective immediately, reflects the company's acquisition in April of Microamerica Inc. Merisel wants to convey through the name its goal "to combine the best aspects" of both companies, a spokeswoman said.

Merisel is a distributor of microcomputer hardware and software products to retailers and other sellers to computer users. About 30% of Merisel's sales are overseas. The company began over-the-counter trading yesterday under the new symbol MSEL.
