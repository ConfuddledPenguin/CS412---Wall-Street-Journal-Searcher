Dividend News: Curtiss-Wright Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/24/90

CURTISS-WRIGHT Corp. declared a third-quarter dividend of 25 cents a share, payable Oct. 31 to stock of record Oct. 17. The dividend, a reduction from first- and second-quarter dividends of 40 cents a share, follows a special cash dividend of $30 a share, or $150.5 million, paid Aug. 15. A spokesman said he assumes the company will continue to pay 25 cents quarterly. Based in Lyndhurst, N.J., Curtiss-Wright produces precision products and provides engineering services.
