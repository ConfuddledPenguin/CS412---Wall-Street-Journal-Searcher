Gelman Sciences Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/24/90

ANN ARBOR, Mich. -- Gelman Sciences Inc. said it anticipates that it will report a loss of approximately $1.5 million for its fiscal year ended July 31. The health-devices company had net income of $2.1 million, or 98 cents a share, on sales of $71.1 million, for its fiscal year ended in 1989. Net sales for 1990 were approximately $73 million.

Gelman said financial results for fiscal 1990 were hurt by lower-than-planned sales, poor performance of the company's Australian subsidiary, and higher-than-anticipated expenses in Gelman Sciences Sunbeam Process operations and year-end adjustments.

The company expects to release year-end results in late September.
