Letters to the Editor: Good as Gold

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

Robert L. Bartley's July 10 editorial-page article, "The Great International Growth Slowdown," is brilliant. As one option to establishing international monetary order, a return to the gold standard seems sensible. In a follow-up article, could you please expand upon the pros and cons of gold's ability to bring back an era of international economic growth -- which as a byproduct should contribute greatly to world peace, a higher standard of living and a higher quality of life for five billion people. Irving Lipsiner

Bethany, Conn.
