CCNB Acquisition Pact Ended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

MIDDLETOWN, Pa. -- CCNB Corp. and Homestead Holding Corp. said they terminated a previously announced proposal for CCNB to acquire Homestead Holding in an exchange of stock valued at $17.9 million. The proposed acquisition was terminated after CCNB requested on July 17 to renegotiate the $15.48 a share price it offered last June in a letter of intent to acquire Homestead Holding. Under terms of the proposal, each share of Homestead Holding would have been exchanged for 0.86 share of CCNB. Homestead Holding is the parent company of Middletown-based Homestead Savings Association. CCNB, New Cumberland, Pa., is the holding company for CCNB Bank, Gettysburg National Bank and Parent Federal Savings Bank.
