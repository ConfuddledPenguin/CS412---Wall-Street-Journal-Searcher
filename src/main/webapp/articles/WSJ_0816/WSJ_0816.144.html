REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Markets Defended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

New York City Comptroller Elizabeth Holtzman, a Democrat, on the proposed stock-transaction tax: "A bullet aimed at the heart of New York City. This is a tax on all Americans who save in the form of stocks and bonds, and in particular, it is a tax on all American workers' pension funds. This is the wrong tax at the wrong time." Next thing we know, Governor Cuomo will be coming out for a cut in the capital-gains tax.
