Cable Applications Inc. Says 2 Officials Quit And Auditors Resign

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

GARDEN CITY, N.Y. -- Cable Applications Inc. said that Richard Capaldo, chairman and president, and Vincent Cestaro, vice president, resigned their executive posts and directorships. The company also said its auditors, Margolin, Winer & Evens, had resigned. The auditor resigned, Cable Applications said, because the company failed to file certain tax returns and lacked supporting data for some expense reimbursements and other matters. The auditors thus found it difficult to confirm certain potential liabilities, Cable Applications said.

Cable Applications said it has taken steps including the appointment of a financial consultant to monitor operations and expenditures. Cable Applications supplies temporary technical personnel to aerospace and other firms and installs cable used in various telecommunications applications.

Messrs. Capaldo and Cestaro couldn't be reached for comment. Cable Applications said Mr. Capaldo will continue to assist the company in its telecommunications-installation segment.
