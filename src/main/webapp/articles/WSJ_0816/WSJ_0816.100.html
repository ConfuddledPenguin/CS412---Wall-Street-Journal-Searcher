Pechiney's Howmet Buys Site

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Howmet Corp., a maker of superalloy castings and metals, and a unit of French state-owned Pechiney Group, said it purchased a site in North Haven, Conn., for a new plant to refurbish parts for jet aircraft engines. The price wasn't disclosed. Howmet, which already operates two U.S. plants that refurbish aircraft parts, said the acquisition includes a building where the company plans to establish a facility for coating and repairing parts for advanced jet planes, such as blades and vanes.
