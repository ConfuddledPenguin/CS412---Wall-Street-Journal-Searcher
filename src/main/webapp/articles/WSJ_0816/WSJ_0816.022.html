DUBIN & SWIECA CAPITAL MANAGEMENT Inc. (New York) -- Glenn Dubin and Henry Swieca, both former senior vice presidents of Shearson Lehman Brothers Holdings, New York, formed their own investment management company. At Shearson they had headed an investment unit that concentrated in managing portfolios of futures and options. The new company, which will specialize in the formation and management of multi-adviser futures portfolios, has more than $450 million under management.
