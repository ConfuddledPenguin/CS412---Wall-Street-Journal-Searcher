Peter J. Solomon Co. Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

NEW YORK -- An investment group headed by Peter J. Solomon, former vice chairman at Shearson Lehman Hutton, has purchased 37 Wickes Lumber Co. retail outlets in the Southeast. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed. The lumber and building-supplies facilities, which generated sales of $160 million in 1989, represent the first acquisition by the Peter J. Solomon Co. The new venture, to be called Leeds Building Products, will be based in Atlanta and run by Kenneth L. Miron, a member of Mr. Solomon's investment group.

The purchase will leave Vernon Hills, Ill.-based Wickes Lumber with 152 retail outlets nationwide.

The purchase will "notably reduce" the debt incurred by the company in a $300 million 1988 management buy-out, according to Ric Morphet, Wickes senior vice president of administration.
