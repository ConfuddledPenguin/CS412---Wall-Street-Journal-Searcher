Who's News: Imperial Chemical Industries PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

IMPERIAL CHEMICAL INDUSTRIES PLC (London) -- Philip Daubeney, 51-year-old managing director of ICI India, was named regional executive of this chemical company's African and Eastern region, effective in October, succeeding Roy Allen, 55, who is to retire. George Ewart, 52, general manager of chemical products at ICI Chemicals & Polymers Ltd., was appointed managing director-designate of ICI India, to succeed Mr. Daubeney.
