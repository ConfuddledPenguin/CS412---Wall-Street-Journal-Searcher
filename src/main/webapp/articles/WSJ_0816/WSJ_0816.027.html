Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Gary P. Tobin was named vice president of communications and public affairs at the information services unit of this travel, financial and information services company. Mr. Tobin, 45 years old, was previously vice president of U.S. public affairs at the travel-related services unit, based in New York.
