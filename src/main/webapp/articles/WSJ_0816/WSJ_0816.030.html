Canada Schedules Bond Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government said it will auction next Tuesday 1.2 billion Canadian dollars (US$1.05 billion) of two-year government bonds maturing Sept. 5, 1992. Proceeds will be used to redeem C$650 million of government bonds maturing Sept. 5, 1990, and for general government purposes.
