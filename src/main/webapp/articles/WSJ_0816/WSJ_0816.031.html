General Instrument Buy-Out

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

NEW YORK -- General Instrument Corp. holders have tendered some 95.5% of the cable-TV equipment company under a $44.50-a-share offer from leveraged buy-out specialist Forstmann Little & Co. The offer, announced last month and set to expire last Monday, was extended through 5 p.m. EDT Tuesday because of a power outage in New York's financial district that prevented some holders from tendering.

General Instrument and Forstmann Little are based here.
