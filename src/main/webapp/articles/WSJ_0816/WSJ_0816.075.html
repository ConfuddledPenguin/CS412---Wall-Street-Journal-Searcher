Business Brief -- Atlantic Richfield Co.: Price Freeze Will Continue; Middle East Crisis Is Cited

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

Atlantic Richfield Co. said that in response to the Middle East crisis, it would continue to freeze wholesale gas, diesel and jet fuel prices. Los Angeles-based Atlantic Richfield initially froze gas prices last week for one week. The company said it was now extending the freeze for an unspecified period. "We want to comply with President Bush's request to hold the line on gas prices," said an Atlantic Richfield Co. spokesman. The company's sales have increased 10% since Iraq's invasion of Kuwait, he added.
