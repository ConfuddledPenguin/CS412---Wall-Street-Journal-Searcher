Tembec's Patriot to Buy Mill

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

MONTREAL -- Tembec Inc., a pulp and paper concern, said its 50%-owned Patriot Paper Corp. agreed to acquire an idle paper mill in Boston from James River Corp., a Richmond, Va., pulp and paper company. Terms weren't disclosed. Tembec said Patriot will invest about US$50 million over the next 18 months to modernize the mill's equipment and increase the capacity of its de-inking plant. Patriot plans to produce office and printing papers, using pulp made from recycled white, or office, papers collected in the Boston area. The Boston mill can produce about 200 tons of paper a day.

Tembec, which holds an option to increase its interest in Patriot to 73%, said the remaining 50% of Patriot is held by two U.S. businessmen.
