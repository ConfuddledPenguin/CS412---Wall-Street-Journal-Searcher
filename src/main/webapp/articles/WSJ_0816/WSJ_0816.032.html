Dividend News: First of America Stock Split

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

KALAMAZOO, Mich. -- First of America Bank Corp. declared a 2-for-1 stock split to be distributed Oct. 19 to shareholders of record Sept. 21. The bank holding company also declared an increase in the quarterly cash dividend to 30 cents a share, payable Oct. 19 to shareholders of record Sept. 21. The company said the new payout, after the effect of the stock split, represents a 9% rise in the quarterly dividend. The split will increase total shares outstanding to 34.8 million from 17.4 million.
