Who's News: Marsh & McLennan Realigns Reinsurance

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

NEW YORK -- Marsh & McLennan Cos., one of the world's largest insurance brokers, said it will place all of its world-wide reinsurance brokerage operations under its Guy Carpenter & Co. subsidiary. Marsh & McLennan said Richard H. Blum, president of Guy Carpenter, will become chairman of the unit, which will split reinsurance responsibilities between the New York and London offices. Reinsurance is the assumption by an insurance company of all or part of a risk initially undertaken by another insurer.

"This new structure will ensure that all of our global and local expertise and resources are available to existing and prospective clients everywhere in the world," Mr. Blum said.

A New York operating committee will handle reinsurance business in the U.S. and Canada, and a London-based operating committee will handle that elsewhere in the world.
