Provident Bancorp Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

CINCINNATI -- Provident Bancorp Inc. said it would acquire Suburban Federal Savings Bank of Cincinnati in a merger conversion. Suburban Federal is a mutual institution, which means it's owned by depositers and has no stock. Provident, a $2 billion bank holding company, said it would offer Suburban depositers the opportunity to subscribe to stock of Provident.
