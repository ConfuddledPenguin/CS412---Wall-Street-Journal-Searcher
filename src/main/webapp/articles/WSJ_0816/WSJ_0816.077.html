Image Bank Plans to Post Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

NEW YORK -- Image Bank Inc., which licenses reproduction rights to photos and film, expects to report a loss of about $584,000, or 16 cents a share, for the third quarter ended June 30. The loss stems from a $700,000 write-off of foreign tax credits the company hasn't used. The write-off is required for accounting reasons, but Image Bank still can use the tax credits over five years, the company said. For the year-earlier period, Image Bank, which went public in August 1989, reported pro-forma net income of $151,000, or seven cents a share.
