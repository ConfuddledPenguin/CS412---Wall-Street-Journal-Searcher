Logicon to Buy Hadson Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

LOS ANGELES -- Logicon Inc. said it agreed in principle to acquire the Ultrasystems Defense Inc. subsidiary of Oklahoma City-based Hadson Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. Ultrasystems Defense, an Irvine, Calif.-based professional services company specializing in systems engineering and software development, has annual revenue of about $27 million and employs 280 people. Logicon provides advanced technology systems and services to support national security, civil and industrial needs.
