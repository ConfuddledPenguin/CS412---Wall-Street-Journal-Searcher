Wheeling-Pittsburgh Creditors

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

WHEELING, W.Va. -- Wheeling-Pittsburgh Steel Corp. said certain creditors will receive less than expected under the company's reorganization plan as a result of a tentative labor settlement with the United Steelworkers of America. The steelmaker said the specific effect on distributions will be included in an amended plan to be filed soon. The amended plan will also include provisions to issue an additional 1.5 million common shares to participants in the company's existing employee investment plan.
