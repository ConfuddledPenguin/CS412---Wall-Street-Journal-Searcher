Pratt & Whitney Wins Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

WASHINGTON -- Pratt & Whitney, a division of United Technologies Corp., received a $156 million Air Force contract for F-16 aircraft engines.

Rockwell International Corp. won a $25.9 million Air Force contract for navigational satellites.

Dyncorp was awarded a $18.2 million Air Force contract for jet pilot training.
