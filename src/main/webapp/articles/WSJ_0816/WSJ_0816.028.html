Occidental Unit Redemption

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

LOS ANGELES -- Occidental Petroleum Corp. said its MidCon Corp. subsidiary will redeem on Sept. 17 all $175 million outstanding of two debenture issues at 100% of their principal amounts. The company said the redemptions, which will result in lower interest expense, are part of its continuing program to lengthen maturities of long-term debt. The debentures being called are the $100 million outstanding of 13% series due Sept. 15, 1993, and $75 million of 13 3/8% series due Oct. 15, 1994.

Separately, Occidental said that the Flotta crude-oil pipeline system in the North Sea will be closed for repairs for two weeks beginning Aug. 23.
