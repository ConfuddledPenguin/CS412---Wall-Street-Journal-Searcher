Treasury Alters Deadline For Some Auction Tenders

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury Department said it set a new noon Eastern time deadline for the submission of noncompetitive tenders for its securities auctions, effective Nov. 5 at the auction of short-term bills. The deadline for submissions of competitive tenders will remain at 1 p.m. Eastern time, the Treasury said in its news release.

Currently, all tenders are due at 1 p.m. on auction day.

The noncompetitive deadline is being changed to permit auction results to be released to the financial markets more quickly, the Treasury said.

The new deadline for noncompetitive tenders, in addition to increasing automation of the Treasury's process for determining auction results, should permit the department to meet its goal of regularly releasing results by about 2 p.m. Eastern time, the Treasury said.

"Earlier release of auction results is expected to facilitate secondary market trading of Treasury securities and reduce the Treasury's financing costs," the department said.
