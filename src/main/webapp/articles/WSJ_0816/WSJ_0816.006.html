Texas Meridian Reorganization

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

HOUSTON -- Texas Meridian Resources Ltd., a master limited partnership, said it plans to reorganize into a corporate entity and that in conjunction with the restructuring, Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. will increase the oil and gas concern's existing revolving credit line to $32.5 million plus a new subordinated note in the amount of $5 million.
