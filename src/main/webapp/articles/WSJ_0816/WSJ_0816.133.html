Business Brief -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. New Unit Formed to Track Firms Issuing Junk Bonds

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it formed a new unit to track U.S. companies that issue junk bonds, in response to heightened investor concerns about the increasingly risky business climate. The new speculative-grade unit, within this rating service's industrial-company group, includes six analysts who will monitor debt securities rated Ba-1 and lower.

M. Douglas Watson Jr., director of industrial ratings at Moody's, said the new team of analysts will focus on the ability of speculative-grade securities issuers to "generate cash and to tap external sources of liquidity."
