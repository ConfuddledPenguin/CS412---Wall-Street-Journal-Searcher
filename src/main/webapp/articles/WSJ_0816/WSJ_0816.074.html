National Education Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- National Education Corp. said that its Applied Learning International unit and John Wiley & Sons' Wilson Learning Corp. subsidiary formed a partnership for Applied Learning to distribute all of Wilson's videotape training courses. National Education said that the distribution rights are exclusive in the U.S., Canada, and the United Kingdom. National Education is a vocational and industrial-training and educational-publishing company. John Wiley & Sons is a New York-based publisher.
