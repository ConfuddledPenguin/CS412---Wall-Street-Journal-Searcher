Lowndes Ventures Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

LONDON -- Lowndes Queensway PLC, a U.K. retailing group, said it has moved to seek court-appointed administrative receivers. The move follows discussions with the company's bankers, Lowndes said.

Lowndes Queensway, which was acquired by investment group Lowndes Ventures in July 1989 through a leveraged buyout, moved in January to refinance its debts. Its troubles were exacerbated by rising U.K. interest rates and plunging consumer demand for its furniture and household goods.
