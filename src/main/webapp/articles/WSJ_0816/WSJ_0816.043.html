Technology: On-Line Software International

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

FORT LEE, N.J. -- On-Line Software International Inc.'s board approved a measure designed to make hostile takeovers prohibitively expensive. The computer software and services company will distribute rights to buy preferred stock to holders of common shares on Sept. 6. Under certain circumstances, the rights allow the holders to buy shares of On-Line or its buyer at half price.

A spokeswoman said the board, which announced the action Tuesday, knows of no attempts to take control of On-Line Software.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, On-Line shares closed at $7 a share, up 25 cents.
