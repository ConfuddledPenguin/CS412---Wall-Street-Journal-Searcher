FirstFed Michigan Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

DETROIT -- FirstFed Michigan Corp. said it is starting to repurchase 750,000 shares, or about 7%, of its common stock outstanding. The thrift holding company said it expects to complete the buy-back program within 12 months, subject to market conditions. FirstFed officials said the repurchase will be made through open-market negotiations, but officials didn't rule out the possibility of unsolicited negotiated transactions or other types of repurchases. The company said any repurchased shares would become authorized, but unissued. They will be used for general corporate purposes.
