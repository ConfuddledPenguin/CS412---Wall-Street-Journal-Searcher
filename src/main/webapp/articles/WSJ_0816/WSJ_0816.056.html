Who's News: Primerica Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/16/90

PRIMERICA Corp. (New York) -- Donald R. Cooper was named chairman and chief executive officer of Massachusetts Indemnity & Life Insurance Co., a unit of this financial services company. Mr. Cooper, 46 years old, succeeds J. Treacy Beyer, 50, who will continue as chairman and chief executive officer of A.L. Williams Corp., a sales and marketing unit that distributes term life insurance underwritten by Massachusetts Indemnity. Both units are based in Duluth, Ga.
