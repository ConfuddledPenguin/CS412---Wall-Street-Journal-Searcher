Business Brief -- American Stores Co.: Unit Agrees to Sell Buttrey In $184 Million Transaction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

American Stores Co. said its Skaggs Alpha Beta unit agreed to sell its Buttrey Food and Drug division to an investment group led by management of Buttrey and the merchant banking firm of Freeman Spogli & Co. American Stores said the transaction, expected to close in 60 days, is valued at $184 million -- $180 million in cash and $4 million in lease assumptions. American said it will retain $36 million in debt obligations related to the division.

The Buttrey division, based in Great Falls, Mont., operates 44 food outlets and drugstores in six states, and has annual sales of $476 million, American said.

Freeman Spogli, based in Los Angeles, specializes in management-led buy-outs.

American Stores, based in Salt Lake City, operates food stores and drugstores in 37 states.
