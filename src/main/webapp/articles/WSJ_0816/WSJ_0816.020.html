Chrysler Corp. Finance Rates

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

HIGHLAND PARK, Mich. -- Chrysler Corp. lowered finance rates on 10 models, and extended incentives on its other 1989 and 1990 cars and trucks. The No. 3 U.S. auto maker said it will reduce interest rates to 4.8%, from 7.9%, on 25-month to 48-month loans and to 7.9%, from 10.9%, on 49-month to 60-month loans on some vehicles. Models affected by the lower interest rate are: the Chrysler LeBaron coupe, New Yorker, Salon, Landau, Fifth Avenue and Imperial; Dodge Daytona and Dynasty and the Jeep Cherokee and Wagoneer Limited.

The company extended through Sept. 30 the other incentives, with rebates ranging from $500 to $2,500.
