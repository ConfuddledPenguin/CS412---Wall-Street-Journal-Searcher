Sunrise Preschools Warrants

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/16/90

SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. -- Sunrise Preschools Inc. said it extended for one year to Sept. 11, 1991, the expiration date for holders of its warrants to exercise them for one million of the company's common shares. The operator of preschools in Arizona and Hawaii said the warrants, which were issued as part of its initial public offering in 1987, have an exercise price of $5 a share.
