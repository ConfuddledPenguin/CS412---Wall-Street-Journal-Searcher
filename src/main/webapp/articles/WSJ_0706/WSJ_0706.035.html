Who's News: Imcera Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

IMCERA GROUP Inc. (Northbrook, Ill.) -- M. Blakeman Ingle, 48 years old, was named president and chief operating officer, new posts at this maker of medical, specialty chemicals, animal-health and nutrition products, which formerly was named International Minerals & Chemical Corp. Mr. Ingle previously was an executive vice president of Imcera and will remain president and chief executive officer of the Pitman-Moore Inc. unit until a successor is named.
