Business Brief -- Millipore Corp.: Company Says Sales Grew By 12% in Second Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

Millipore Corp. said sales from its continuing lines of business grew 12% in the second quarter compared with the year-ago period, while sales in the first half grew 11% from a year earlier. Millipore said the growth rates exclude results from its process water division, which was sold in November.

Including results from the process water division, Millipore said, sales growth was 4% over the year-ago quarter and 3% over the year-earlier six months. Had currency exchange rates remained constant in 1989 and 1990, Millipore said, its growth rate, excluding results for the sold division, would have been 10% for both the quarter and first half.

For the 1989 second quarter, Millipore had net income of $13.5 million, or 48 cents a share, on sales of $167 million. For the first half, Millipore had net income of $25.9 million, or 93 cents a share, on sales of $327.8 million.

Millipore, a Bedford, Mass., analytical and purification products concern, will release its second quarter results later this month.
