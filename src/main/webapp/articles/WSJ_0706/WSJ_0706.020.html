Italy's Jobless Rate Declines

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

ROME -- Italy's unemployment rate fell to 10.6% of the labor force in April from 11.5% in January and from 11.9% a year earlier, according to figures released yesterday by the state statistical agency, ISTAT. The unemployment rate was still by far the highest in the southern, so-called Mezzogiorno region, but even here the jobless rate showed a significant decline. The southern unemployment rate fell to 19.1% of the labor force in April from 20.1% in January and from 21.3% a year earlier.

In central Italy, 9.6% of the work force was unemployed, compared with 10.8% in January and 10.5% a year earlier. In the industrialized north, the unemployment rate fell to 4.9% in April from 5.5% in January and from 5.8% a year earlier.
