Penn Traffic Exemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

JOHNSTOWN, Pa. -- Penn Traffic Co. said its directors voted unanimously to exempt the corporation from Pennsylvania's recently enacted "anti-takeover" act. "We have decided that the Pennsylvania Business Corporation Law as in effect prior to the enactment of the anti-takeover amendments is more favorable to our shareholders," said Gary D. Hirsch, chairman of Penn Traffic, a retailer.

Pennsylvania recently enacted legislation making it easier for companies incorporated in the state to resist hostile takeovers.
