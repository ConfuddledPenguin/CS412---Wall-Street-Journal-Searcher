Stryker, Zimmer Settle Suit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

KALAMAZOO, Mich. -- Stryker Corp. and Warsaw, Ind.-based Zimmer Inc. said today that they had reached a settlement of patent litigation in which Zimmer agreed to pay an undisclosed amount of money. Zimmer also agreed to continue to refrain from selling a hip implant while Stryker can continue to market its own hip implant.

Both companies had earlier accused each other of patent infringement.
