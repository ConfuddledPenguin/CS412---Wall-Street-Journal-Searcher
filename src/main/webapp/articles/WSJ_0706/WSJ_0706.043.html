No Doze

("Decaffeinated coffee may raise cholesterol and increase chances of heart disease." -- News item)

So my decaf's bad?

That news gives fright --

It will make me lie

Awake all night.

-- Robert Gordon.

Rent Control

"Two can live as cheaply as one"

Is a maxim we've long enjoyed.

I'm glad to say it's still true today --

As long as the two are employed.

-- Gail Cooke.
