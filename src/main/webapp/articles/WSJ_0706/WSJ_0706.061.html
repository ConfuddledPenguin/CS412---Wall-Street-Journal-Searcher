Business Brief -- FileNet Corp.: Image-Processing Company To Post 31% Rise in Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

FileNet Corp. said it expects to report that second-quarter net income rose about 31% to $2.1 million, or 20 cents a share, from $1.6 million, or 15 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue is expected to be about $27.5 million, up from $20.4 million. For the six months, net income should increase to roughly $3.4 million, or 32 cents a share, compared with $2 million, or 20 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose to about $51.3 million from $37.9 million.

The Costa Mesa, Calif., maker of document image-processing equipment expects to report results during the July 23 week.
