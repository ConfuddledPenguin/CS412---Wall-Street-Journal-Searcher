State Jobless Claims Rose in June 23 Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits rose to a seasonally adjusted 365,000 in the week ended June 23 from 359,000 in the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended June 16 fell to a seasonally adjusted 2,439,000, or 2.3% of those covered by unemployment insurance, from 2,472,000 the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate was 2.4%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended June 16 fell to 2,218,200 from 2,253,200 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
