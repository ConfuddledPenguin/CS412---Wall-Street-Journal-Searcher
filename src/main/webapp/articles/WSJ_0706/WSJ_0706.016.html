Rose's Stores Inc.'s Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

HENDERSON, N.C. -- Rose's Stores Inc., a discount retailer, said its June sales rose 7.7% to $116.2 million from $107.9 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year increased 6.1%. For the first five months of its fiscal year, Rose's sales increased 6% to $610.9 million from $576.2 million a year earlier. Comparable-store sales rose 3.4%.

Rose's said it switched the day its period ends to Saturday from Thursday, affecting both June and five-months sales comparisons.
