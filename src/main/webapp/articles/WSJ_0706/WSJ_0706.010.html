United Gaming, USI Set Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- United Gaming Inc., which makes, sells and operates coin-operated gambling devices in Nevada and abroad, said it agreed with closely held USI International Inc. of New York to sell slot machines and coin-operated gambling devices for distribution in the Soviet Union. Under the agreement, United Gaming will ship an initial order of 250 machines, with additional shipments to be made if additional orders are placed. The agreement also stipulates that United Gaming will retain ownership of the machines until payment is received.
