Hong Kong's Tourist Arrivals

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

HONG KONG -- Tourist arrivals in Hong Kong fell 6.3% to 499,816 in May from 533,692 a year earlier, when the ill-fated pro-democracy movement in Beijing started to hurt local tourism, the Hong Kong Tourist Association said. In this year's first five months, visitor arrivals fell 2.2% to 2,359,000 from 2,412,000 a year earlier.

Visits by Taiwanese, the largest single group of local tourists, dropped 11.1% in May, and those by Japanese tourists, the second-largest group, slipped 0.3%. The number of U.S. visitors, the next-largest group, plunged 20.6%.
