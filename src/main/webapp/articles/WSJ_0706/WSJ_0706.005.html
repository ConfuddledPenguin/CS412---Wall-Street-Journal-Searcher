West Germany's Raw Steel

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West German production of raw steel in the first half fell 6.7% to 19.38 million metric tons, compared with a year earlier, the Federal Statistics Office said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. First-half output of raw iron declined 8.2% to 15.28 million metric tons.

In June, the country produced slightly more than three million metric tons of raw steel, down 12% from a year earlier, and smelters made 9.8% less raw iron at 2.4 million tons.

Steel and iron production in West Germany has been soft this year, in part because end users in some sectors stockpiled supplies last year.
