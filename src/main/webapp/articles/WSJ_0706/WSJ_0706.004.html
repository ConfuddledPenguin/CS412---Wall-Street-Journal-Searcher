Technology: Cyanamid Co-Marketing Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

WAYNE, N.J. -- The Lederle Laboratories division of American Cyanamid Co. said it reached an agreement with American Home Products Corp. to co-market Verelan, an antihypertensive drug. Lederle has licensed U.S. marketing rights to the drug, a calcium channel blocker known generically as verapamil, from Elan Corp. of Ireland. Lederle said its sales force will begin selling the drug this month along with two divisions of American Home, Wyeth-Ayerst Laboratories and A.H. Robins Co.
