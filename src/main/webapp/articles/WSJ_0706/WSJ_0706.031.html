Southwestern Energy Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

FAYETTEVILLE, Ark. -- Southwestern Energy Co. said it plans to redeem all 8.5% senior convertible debentures outstanding due Sept. 15, 2010. The debentures, which have a total face amount of $30 million, will be redeemed at a price of $105.10 plus accrued. Until that date, the oil and natural gas company said the debentures can be converted to the company's common stock at a rate of $32.375 a share.
