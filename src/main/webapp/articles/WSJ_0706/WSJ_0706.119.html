Who's News: Hopper Soliday Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

HOPPER SOLIDAY Corp. (Philadelphia) -- William E. Bierlin Jr. was named a director of this financial services holding company. He succeeds Dexter Newbold, who resigned. Mr. Bierlin is also vice chairman of the parent's major subsidiary, W.H. Newbold's Son & Co./Hopper Soliday & Co.
