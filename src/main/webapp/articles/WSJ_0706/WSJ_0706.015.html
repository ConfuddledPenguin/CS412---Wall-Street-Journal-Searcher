G&K Services Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- G&K Services Inc. said it has signed an agreement to acquire Work Wear Corp. of Canada Ltd. for about $68 million, plus the repayment of $12 million in long-term debt. G&K, a uniform leasing firm, said the agreement is subject to the completion of financing, Canadian government approval and certain other conditions.

Work Wear Canada, a uniform leasing company, is an indirect subsidiary of Work Wear Corp. Inc. in the U.S. G&K said the purchase doesn't include any U.S. manufacturing operations.

It said financing in the form of long-term debt is being arranged by a consortium of U.S. and Canadian banks.
