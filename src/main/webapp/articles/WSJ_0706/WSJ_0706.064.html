Midlantic National Buys Business Line From NatWest Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

EDISON, N.J. -- Midlantic National Bank, the main bank of Midlantic Corp., said it acquired the corporate trust business of National Westminster Bank NJ. Midlantic purchased the trusteeship, paying agent and registrar accounts -- primarily with New Jersey municipalities, school districts and corporations -- serviced by NatWest NJ, a subsidiary of Britain's National Westminster Bank PLC. Terms weren't disclosed.

With the acquisition, Midlantic National Bank's corporate trust services portfolio amounts to $10 billion, with a total of approximately 1,300 accounts. The transaction is expected to be completed during August, when NatWest's current accounts are moved to Midlantic.

Midlantic Corp. is a $24 billion interstate bank holding company with headquarters in Edison, N.J. The company operates 514 offices primarily in New Jersey, Pennsylvania and New York.
