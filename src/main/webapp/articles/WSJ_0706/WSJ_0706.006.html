Midway Air's June Traffic Rose

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

CHICAGO -- Midway Airlines said its June traffic increased 35% to 407.2 million revenue passenger miles from 302.6 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, slipped to 56.7% from 59.3%.

For the first six months of this year, Midway's traffic rose 48% to 2.5 billion revenue passenger miles from 1.69 billion miles a year earlier. The load factor increased to 59.5% from 56.5%.
