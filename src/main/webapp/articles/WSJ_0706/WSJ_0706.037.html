Who's News: Litton Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

LITTON INDUSTRIES Inc. (Beverly Hills, Calif.) -- John M. Leonis was elected senior vice president and Larry A. Frame was named vice president of this technology-based company. Mr. Leonis, who previously served as corporate vice president, will remain group executive of Litton's Guidance & Control Systems division. Mr. Frame will retain his position as president of that division, which makes and produces navigation and computer systems for military and commercial applications. Litton provides advanced electronic and defense systems, industrial automation systems and resource exploration services.
