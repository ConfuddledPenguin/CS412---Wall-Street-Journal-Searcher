Citicorp Paper Rate Falls To 8.16% at Latest Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.16% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.23% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $400 million were submitted. Accepted bids were at 8.16%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 8.092% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.18% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $360 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.08% to 8.10%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
