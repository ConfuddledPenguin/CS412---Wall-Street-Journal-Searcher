Shearson Schedules Meeting

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

NEW YORK -- Shearson Lehman Hutton Holdings Inc. has scheduled its annual meeting Aug. 9 for a shareholder vote on becoming a wholly owned subsidiary of American Express Co. Holders of record June 15 will be asked to vote on a proposal to approve the previously announced merger agreement with American Express. Under the terms of the agreement, the travel and financial services company would acquire the roughly 30% of the brokerage firm's common shares outstanding that it doesn't already own.
