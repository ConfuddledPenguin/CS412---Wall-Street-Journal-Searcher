Wolohan Lumber's Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

SAGINAW, Mich. -- Wolohan Lumber Co. said its June sales rose 11% to $30.3 million from $27.3 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year increased 3.1%. For the six months through June, sales increased 9.7% to $141.4 million from $128.9 million a year earlier. Same-store sales were up 4.3%.

Wolohan operates 51 lumber and building-materials retail stores in Illinois, Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio, Missouri, and Wisconsin.
