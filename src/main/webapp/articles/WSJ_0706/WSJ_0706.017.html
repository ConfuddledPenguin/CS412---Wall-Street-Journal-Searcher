Kentana Sets Hable Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

CARLSBAD, Calif. -- Kentana Development Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire R.E. Hable Co., a highway construction company based in Corsicana, Texas. Terms weren't disclosed, but Kentana retained Interco Funding Group Inc., a Miami investment banking firm, to finance the transaction. Hable has assets of $44 million, annual sales of about $50 million and a net worth, or assets minus liabilities, of $13.8 million. Kentana develops and sells property in and around the Richland Chambers Reservoir in Texas, about 60 miles south of Dallas.
