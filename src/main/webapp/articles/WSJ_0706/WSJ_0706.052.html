MarCor Resorts Extends Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- MarCor Resorts Inc. extended its tender offer for the limited partnership interests in MarCor Resorts L.P.V. to July 16, 1990, from June 29. So far, about 55.6% of the partnership interests have been tendered. The proposal includes the exchange of MarCor Resorts L.P.V. units for preferred stock of a newly created MarCor Resorts subsidiary, which will in turn be convertible into MarCor Resorts common stock. After the exchange, MarCor Resorts will own at least 90.5% of MarCor Resorts L.P.V.

The transaction requires the approval of Nevada gaming regulators and MarCor Resorts stockholders. MarCor Resorts is a real estate concern that operates a Las Vegas hotel-casino.
