Offer for Norton Is Extended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

PARIS -- Compagnie de Saint-Gobain S.A. extended the expiration date of its $90-a-share tender offer for Norton Co. to July 17 from today. In April, Saint-Gobain agreed to acquire Norton for about $1.9 billion, topping a $75-a-share acquisition proposal from hostile suitor BTR PLC.

As of July 3, approximately 9.5 million shares, or 45.42% of Norton's common stock, had been tendered to Saint-Gobain.
