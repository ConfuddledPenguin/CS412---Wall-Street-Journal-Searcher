Hamilton Oil Discovers Gas

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

DENVER -- Hamilton Oil Corp. said that it discovered natural gas in the Irish Sea off the west coast of England, and that the well tested natural gas from two separate intervals at a combined rate of 70 million cubic feet a day through a two-inch choke, or opening. Hamilton is the operator with a 45% interest. Its partners are Ultramar Exploration Ltd. with 30% and Monument Resources Ltd. with 25%.
