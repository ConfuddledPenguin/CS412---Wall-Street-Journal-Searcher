Business Brief -- Electro Scientific Industries Inc.: Company Expects to Post Fiscal Fourth-Quarter Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

Electro Scientific Industries Inc., Portland, Ore., said it anticipates posting a loss for its fourth quarter ended May 31, but it declined to estimate the deficit. The maker of electronic equipment cited deferred expenses from development projects and cleanup costs related to the sale in April of its Ballantine Laboratories Inc. unit to a New Jersey company.

In the fiscal 1989 fourth quarter, Electro Scientific earned $2.2 million, or 36 cents a share, on sales of $23.6 million.

The company throughout the latest fiscal year was plagued by slumping sales. For the fiscal nine months ended Feb. 28, Electro Scientific had a loss of $3.1 million, or 52 cents a share, on sales of $57.7 million. As part of a recent cost-cutting program, the company has reduced its work force 15% to about 680 employees.
