Business Brief -- Prime Motor Inns Inc.: Sale of Franchise System For Rodeway Inns Is Closed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/06/90

Prime Motor Inns Inc., Fairfield, N.J., closed the sale of the Rodeway Inns International franchise system for $14.9 million to Manor Care Inc., Silver Spring, Md. "As with the sale earlier this week of the Howard Johnson and Ramada franchise systems, we will apply the proceeds of this transaction to further reduce our bank debt," Prime's chairman, Peter E. Simon, said. Prime sold Ramada and Howard Johnson to Blackstone Group's Blackstone Capital Partners L.P. affiliate for $170 milllion.

"Our intent is to continue our program to pay down the balance of our bank debt by the end of the calendar year," Mr. Simon said.

Rodeway will be managed by Manor Care's franchise lodging subsidiary, Quality Inns International Inc. Quality Inns has hotels in 19 countries under the names Quality, Comfort, Clarion and Sleep Inns.
