Who's News: FPL Group Unit Taps Frank for President

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

MIAMI -- Florida Power & Light Co., the principal unit of FPL Group Inc., named Stephen E. Frank, 48 years old, as president and chief operating officer, succeeding Robert E. Tallon, who retired June 30. Mr. Frank, who is currently executive vice president and chief financial officer of TRW Inc., in Cleveland, was formerly a vice president and controller at GTE Corp., Stamford, Conn. He will assume his new post in mid-August.

Mr. Frank reported to James L. Broadhead, FPL's chairman and chief executive officer, for about three years when the two men worked at GTE, an FPL spokesman said. "I'm sure that was a factor in his getting the job," said an FPL spokesman. In a statement, Mr. Broadhead cited Mr. Frank's strong leadership skills and experience in both regulated and nonregulated businesses.
