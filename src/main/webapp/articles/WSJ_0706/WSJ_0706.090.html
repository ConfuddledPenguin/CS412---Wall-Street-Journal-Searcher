International Brief -- British & Commonwealth Holdings: Thompson Resigns as Head Of Financial Services Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/06/90

Peter Thompson, chairman of British & Commonwealth Holdings PLC, resigned yesterday, a company statement said. The one-line statement said only that the official had resigned as a director of the London financial services concern.

Company officials weren't immediately available for comment.

Philip Arkless, company secretary, later said the resignation was effective July 2. He said Mr. Thompson hadn't given a reason for his departure in his letter of resignation.

Mr. Arkless also said it wasn't clear at this stage whether Mr. Thompson would be replaced, given the company's "unusual circumstances."

British & Commonwealth is under the administration of Price Waterhouse given its inability to raise bank financing last month.
