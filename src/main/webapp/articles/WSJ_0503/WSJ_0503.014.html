Wolohan Lumber Co. Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

SAGINAW, Mich. -- Wolohan Lumber Co. said its sales for April rose 11% to $26.2 million from $23.6 million a year ago. Sales for stores open one year or longer climbed 6.1% Sales for the first four months of the year climbed 9.4% to $79.3 million from $72.5 million for the same period a year ago. Same-store sales for the period rose 5.4%.
