Who's News: Conagra Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

CONAGRA Inc. (Omaha, Neb.) -- Scott W. Rahn, formerly president of the ConAgra Frozen Foods division, was named to the new post of vice president, internal development, of this food and grain company. James R. Tindall will succeed Mr. Rahn and continues as president and chief operating officer of ConAgra Prepared Food Cos.
