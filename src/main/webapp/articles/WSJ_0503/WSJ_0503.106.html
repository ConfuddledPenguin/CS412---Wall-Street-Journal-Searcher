International Brief -- Hoechst AG: Group Pretax Earnings Fell By 8.5% in First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

Hoechst AG said its first-quarter group pretax profit fell 8.5% to 920 million marks ($547.6 million), while sales increased 5% to 11.03 billion marks. The West German chemical company said that even though pretax profit fell, it still expects to post profit for all of 1990 similar to that posted for 1989.

Hoechst had profit of 2.13 billion marks in 1989 on 45.9 billion marks in sales.

In the current year, the company financial performance has been "handicapped" by unfavorable foreign exchange rates and downward pressure on prices, the company said.

However it said that it managed to have "a good volume development" in Western Europe.
