Summagraphics Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

SEYMOUR, Conn. -- Summagraphics Corp. said it completed its previously announced acquisition of the Houston Instrument division of Ametek Inc. for $30.2 million. The price consists of $25.2 million in cash and a $5 million five-year convertible subordinated note that will entitle Ametek, a New York-based industrial manufacturing concern, to buy 8.8% of Summagraphics's shares outstanding.

Summagraphics makes computer-graphic tablets for use with high-resolution computers; Houston Instruments makes computer peripherals.
