Toys `R' Us Sales Rise 23%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

PARAMUS, N.J. -- Toys "R" Us Inc. said sales for its first quarter, ended April 29, rose 23% to $943.6 million from $767.7 million. The toy retailer said comparable-store sales in the U.S. rose 9.8%. Charles Lazarus, chairman and chief executive officer, said "our sales performance reflects strong sales in video game products as well as non-electronic toys."
