Indonesian Trade Surplus

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

JAKARTA, Indonesia -- Indonesia recorded a trade surplus of $557 million in February, with exports totaling $1.90 billion and imports at $1.34 billion during the month, the government said. Crude oil and gas exports accounted for $739 million of February's total export figure.

The country had a 15% decline in crude oil and gas export earnings because of an oil price decline on the world market in February, but export earnings from other commodities rose 26%.
