First Financial Management

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

ATLANTA -- First Financial Management Corp. said that its board of directors authorized the purchase of up to $90 million of the company's common stock. The financial services concern said it will buy shares in both open-market and private transactions.

The company said its shares are "greatly undervalued" at the current stock price. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, First Financial's shares closed at $20.50, up $1.75.
