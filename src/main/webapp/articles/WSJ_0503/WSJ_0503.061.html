GM Customer Rebate Program

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Corp.'s Oldsmobile division has increased the rebate on its 1990 Toronado to $2,500 from $1,500 and has announced a new rebate of $1,000 on its 1990 Cutlass Ciera effective through Aug. 2. The Chevrolet division also announced rebates. Both divisions are offering the option of discount financing terms ranging from 7.9% to 10.9%.
