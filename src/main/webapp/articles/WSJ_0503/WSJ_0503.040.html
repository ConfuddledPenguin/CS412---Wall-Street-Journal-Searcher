Michaels Stores' Sales Increase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

IRVING, Texas -- Michaels Stores Inc. said April sales rose 34% from a year earlier, with sales at stores open more than a year rising about 18%. The crafts retailer said April sales rose to $27.2 million from $20.3 million a year earlier. It attributed the showing to the fact that Easter fell in April this year, whereas last year it was in March.

For the first quarter ended April 29, Michaels said sales increased about 28% to $71.7 million from $56.2 million. Sales at stores open more than one year increased about 12%.
