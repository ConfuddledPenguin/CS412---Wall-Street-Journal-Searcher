MERCK & Co. (Rahway, N.J.) -- Dr. Eve Slater was named vice president of clinical and regulatory development, a newly created position, for Merck Sharp & Dohme Research Laboratories, a division of this world-wide health products firm. She previously was executive director of clinical and regulatory development.
