Who's News: Boeing Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

BOEING Co. (Seattle) -- Boyd E. Givan, 54 years old, was named chief financial officer of this aerospace concern. He succeeds Harold W. Haynes, 67, who retired last month. Mr. Givan had been vice president, financial operations and treasurer before being named senior vice president several weeks ago as part of a planned transition. He retains that title in addition to the chief financial officer designation.
