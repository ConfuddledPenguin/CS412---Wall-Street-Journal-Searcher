Who's News: M.A. Hanna Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

M.A. HANNA Co. (Cleveland) -- Douglas J. McGregor, Hanna's president and chief operating officer; Jack L. Cockwell, executive vice president and chief operating officer of Brascan Ltd.; and Paul M. Marshall, Brascan's vice chairman, were elected directors of this plastics and other polymers company. Four directors retired, reducing the board to 12 members.
