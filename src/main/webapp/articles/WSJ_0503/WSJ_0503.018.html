Who's News: Roche Holding Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

ROCHE HOLDING Ltd. -- Kathy Ordonez was named vice president of Hoffmann-La Roche Inc., a health-care company based in Nutley, N.J., and president of Roche Diagnostic Systems Inc., where Ms. Ordonez was previously vice president. The diagnostic products and instruments unit and its U.S. parent company are owned by Roche Holding, Basel, Switzerland.
