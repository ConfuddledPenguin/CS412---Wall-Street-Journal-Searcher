Australia Consumer Index Falls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

SYDNEY -- An index of Australian consumer sentiment plunged a seasonally adjusted 13.3% in April from March to its lowest level on record. This confirms the economy is slowing, according to Westpac Banking Corp., which compiles the monthly index with Melbourne University's Institute of Applied Economic and Social Research.

The index has dropped 35.5% since reaching a peak in September 1988. Rising Australian interest rates last year sent consumer confidence sliding.
