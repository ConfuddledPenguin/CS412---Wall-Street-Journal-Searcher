Japan's Net Foreign Bond Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

TOKYO -- Japanese investors sold $1.75 billion net of foreign bonds in March, the Japanese Finance Ministry said. This compares with a net purchase in February of $3.69 billion of foreign bonds. The Japanese last sold net in January.

Foreign investors in March bought $924 million net of Japanese bonds, down from a net purchase of $2.49 billion in February.

In stocks, Japanese were net sellers of foreign stock and foreigners were net sellers of Japanese stock.

Japanese investors sold $257 million net of foreign stocks, the first net sale since March of last year, and a sharp reversal from February when they bought $2.48 billion net. Foreign investors continued this year's trend of net selling of Japanese stock, selling $7.27 billion net in March, after selling $2.37 billion net in February.
