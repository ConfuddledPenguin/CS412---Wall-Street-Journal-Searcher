Who's News: National City Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

NATIONAL CITY Corp. (Cleveland) -- Richard E. Beeman, executive vice president of National City Bank, was elected a senior vice president of the parent holding company, with responsibility for the $40 billion-asset trust business. Gregory L. Tunis, a senior vice president of National City Bank, was elected executive vice president of corporate administration for the holding company.
