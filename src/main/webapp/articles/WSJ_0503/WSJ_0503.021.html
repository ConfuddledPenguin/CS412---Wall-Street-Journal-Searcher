Walgreen Sales Rose in April

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

DEERFIELD, Ill. -- Walgreen Co. said its April sales rose 17.2% to $500 million from $426.7 million a year earlier. The operator of drugstores said year-to-date sales were up 12.9% to $1.97 billion from $1.74 billion a year earlier.

Walgreen said its comparable store sales increased 13.4%.
