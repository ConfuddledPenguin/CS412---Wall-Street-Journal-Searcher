West German Machine Orders

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Orders to the West German machinery and plant equipment industry were flat in March from March 1989, the German Association of Machinery Makers said. While domestic orders slowed for the first time in several months, foreign orders reversed their recent decline and rose in March.

Domestic orders fell an inflation-adjusted 8% in March from a year earlier, while foreign orders rose an inflation-adjusted 8%.
