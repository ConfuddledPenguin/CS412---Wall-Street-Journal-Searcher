Financing Business: Dynasty Classics Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

DYNASTY CLASSICS Corp., a Carson, Calif., home decor maker, announced an intial public offering of 2,529,700 common shares at $11.25 a share, via Morgan Stanley & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc., with 2.5 million to be sold in a new financing by the company and 29,700 shares to be sold by a shareholder. Proceeds will be used to redeem $1.5 million in preferred stock outstanding to repay short-term debt and working capital requirements.
