Weatherford International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

HOUSTON -- Weatherford International Inc. said it has completed the sale of 3,450,000 shares of the company's common at $5.50 a share. Net proceeds, about $17.8 million, will be used to pay debt and for general working capital purposes. Weatherford is an international energy service and manufacturing company.
