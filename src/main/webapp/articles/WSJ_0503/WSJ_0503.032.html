Central Reserve Life Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

BEREA, Ohio -- Central Reserve Life Corp. said it will report lower earnings for the first quarter ended March 31. For the year-ago quarter, the insurance company reported earnings of $558,000, or 13 cents a share. The company said the lower earnings are from higher costs associated with the increase in premiums. Results will be available next week.
