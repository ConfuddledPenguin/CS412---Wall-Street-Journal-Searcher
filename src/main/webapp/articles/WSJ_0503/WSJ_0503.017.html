Parker Drilling Unit Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

HOUSTON -- Parker Gas Cos., a unit of Parker Drilling Co., said it agreed to sell its pipeline business to Western Gas Processors Ltd. of Denver. A sale price wasn't disclosed. Included in the sale are 450 miles of pipeline located primarily in the Giddings, Texas, area and Parker's natural gas-gathering and measuring segments. Parker's gas-treating portion of the business was not part of the sale.

The transaction is subject to approval of the Texas Railroad Commission and Federal Trade Commission pre-merger regulations. Closing is set for July 31.
