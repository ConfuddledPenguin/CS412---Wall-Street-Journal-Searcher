Wells American Forms Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

WEST COLUMBIA, S.C. -- Wells American Corp. said it formed a subsidiary to develop and market computer hardware products. The maker of microcomputers, which closed its manufacturing operations in January because of financial problems, said the new hardware will be compatible with Apple Computer Inc.'s MacIntosh computer systems.

Wells American said the unit, NewCo Technology Inc., has a research and development program and plans to begin manufacturing operations this year.
