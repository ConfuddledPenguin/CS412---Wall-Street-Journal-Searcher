Who's News: Unilever Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

UNILEVER GROUP (New York) -- Melinda M. Sweet was named to the new position of director of environmental affairs at the Lever Brothers Co. household products unit. She will report directly to Lever's president and chief executive officer, David F. Webb. Ms. Sweet had been assistant general counsel for Lever, in charge of environmental compliance, since 1986.
