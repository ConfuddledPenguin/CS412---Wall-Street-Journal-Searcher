Italy's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

MILAN -- Italy's consumer price index rose 0.4% in April from March, and was up 5.8% from a year earlier, the state statistical institute Istat reported yesterday. The inflation rate was the lowest registered in the past 14 months.

Istat said the index registered 104.8 in April, compared with 104.4 in March and 104.0 in February.

The index isn't seasonally adjusted.
