Dividend Brief -- Republic Gypsum Co.: Housing Conditions Cited In Omission of Dividend

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

Republic Gypsum Co. said its directors voted to omit the five-cent quarterly dividend, citing unfavorable conditions in the housing and construction markets. The Dallas-based manufacturer of gypsum wallboard and paperboard said the omission will save the company about $2 million.

The company said its board declared a 5% common stock dividend to be distributed June 15 to stock of record May 31. The stock dividend, which won't increase shareholders' proportionate interest in the company, isn't in lieu of the cash dividend, it said.
