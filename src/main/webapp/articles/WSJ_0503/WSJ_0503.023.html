Bearings Inc. Sets Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

CLEVELAND -- Bearings Inc. said it agreed in principle to acquire King Bearing Inc., a Corona, Calif.-based bearings distributor, from a unit of Emerson Electric Co. Bearings Inc. declined to state the purchase price or to disclose annual sales of King.

King operates 94 branches in nine Western and Southwestern states, Bearings said, distributing electrical, mechanical and fluid power transmission parts and industrial rubber products as well as bearings.

Bearings Inc. distributes bearings, power transmission parts and other industrial products through 269 branches located in 35 states and Puerto Rico.

If necessary approvals are received, the purchase is expected to be completed about June 30, the company said.
