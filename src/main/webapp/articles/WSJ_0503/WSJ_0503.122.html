Business Brief -- Unocal Corp.: Partnership Unit to Acquire Subsidiary of Placer Dome

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

Unocal Corp. said it agreed to acquire the Prairie Producing unit of Placer Dome Inc. for about $336 million and assumed debt. Placer is a Vancouver-based gold producer. The transaction, which includes $252 million in cash, adds to Los Angeles-based Unocal's already substantial natural gas reserves in East Texas and Louisiana and provides new exploratory lands in geographical areas close to Unocal operations. The buyer is a subsidiary of Union Exploration Partners, a Houston-based limited partnership 95%-owned by the oil giant.

The two companies calculate Prairie's holdings differently, but Prairie's proven reserves are estimated at between 10.4 million and 15.7 million barrels of oil and natural gas liquids, between 83 million and 105.5 billion cubic feet of natural gas and about 20,000 acres of exploratory land. Prairie also has interests in three gas-processing plants.
