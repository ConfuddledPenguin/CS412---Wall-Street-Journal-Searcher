Who's News: Hollywood Park Operating Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

HOLLYWOOD PARK OPERATING Co. (Inglewood, Calif.) -- Lodwrick M. Cook was named a director of this race-track operator, expanding the size of the board to 11 members. Mr. Cook is chairman and chief executive officer of Atlantic Richfield Co., the Los Angeles-based oil, gas and minerals concern.
