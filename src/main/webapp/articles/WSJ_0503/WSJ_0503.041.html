Japan's April Trade Deficit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/03/90

TOKYO -- Japan posted an $11.473 million trade deficit in the first 20 days of April, compared with a $3.125 billion surplus a year earlier, the Japanese Ministry of Finance said. Imports surged to $13.750 billion in the period, up 29.5% from $10.617 billion a year earlier. Exports slipped to $13.739 billion, down slightly from $13.742 billion a year earlier.

The ministry doesn't release a detailed report of intramonth trade statistics.

Japan's trade surplus totaled $7.085 billion in March, the latest full reporting period, expanding from $6.608 billion a year earlier.
