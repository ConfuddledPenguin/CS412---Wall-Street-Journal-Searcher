Who's News: Polly Peck International PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

POLLY PECK INTERNATIONAL PLC (London) -- Sir Michael Sandberg, 62 years old, has been named non-executive director of this global trading and manufacturing company, effective June 1. Sir Michael was chairman of Hongkong & Shanghai Banking Corp. from 1977 to 1986.
