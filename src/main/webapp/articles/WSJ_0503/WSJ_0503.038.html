HealthVest Standstill Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

AUSTIN, Texas -- HealthVest, a real estate investment trust, said it has complied with documentation requirements of a standstill agreement with lenders and expects the accord to remain in effect until Dec. 14. Under terms, HealthVest's lender groups have agreed to refrain from foreclosing on about $270 million of property that was pledged as collateral to secure about $252 million in debt.

During the standstill period, the trust said it will continue efforts to restructure the debt. The lenders involved include Chemical Banking Corp.'s Chemical Bank, the Bank of Tokyo, a group of European banks and several U.S. insurance companies.
