Business Brief -- B. F. Goodrich Co.: Sale of Warrant to Michelin Brings $20.5 Million Gain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/03/90

B.F. Goodrich Co., Akron, Ohio, said it will have a second-quarter gain of about $20.5 million from the sale of a warrant to Michelin Group of France. The warrant would have enabled B.F. Goodrich, a plastic, specialty chemical and aircraft-product concern, to purchase up to 7% of Uniroyal Goodrich Tire Co.

Michelin said it completed the acquisition of Uniroyal Goodrich, also based here.

In the 1989 second quarter, Goodrich earned $52.3 million, or $1.98 a share, on sales of $628.8 million.

Uniroyal Goodrich was formed in 1986 when Goodrich and Uniroyal combined their tire businesses into a joint-venture partnership. In 1988, Goodrich sold its 50% interest in Uniroyal Goodrich to a group of investors organized by Clayton & Dubilier of New York.

At that time, Goodrich received a warrant to purchase indirectly as much as 7% of Uniroyal Goodrich.
