J.C. Penney's Stinu Sets Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

DALLAS -- Stinu Corp. of America, a unit of J.C. Penney Co., said it signed an international franchise agreement for Units stores with Miki Corp. of Japan, an operator of retail specialty stores. The value of the pact wasn't disclosed, and officials couldn't be reached for comment. Under the agreement, Miki will open 20 Units stores in Japan in four years and 100 in all. Units stores, with more than 200 locations in the U.S., sell interlocking knit separates for women. Last year, the Penney subsidiary opened its first foreign stores in Britain, Canada and Mexico.
