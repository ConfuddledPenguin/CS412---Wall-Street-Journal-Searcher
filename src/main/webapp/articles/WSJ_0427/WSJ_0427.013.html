Nynex Agrees to Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

NEW YORK -- Nynex Corp. said it agreed in principle to acquire Stockholder Systems Inc., Norcross, Ga., for stock valued at about $65 million. Stockholder Systems, a marketer of financial-application software, has 200 employees and annual sales of about $26 million.

Nynex, a regional telecommunications concern, said terms call for Stockholder Systems holders to receive Nynex common shares valued at $15 to $16.20 for each of the 4,050,000 Stockholder Systems shares outstanding.

Nynex hopes the acquisition will help it become a leading provider of software products and services for the banking industry.
