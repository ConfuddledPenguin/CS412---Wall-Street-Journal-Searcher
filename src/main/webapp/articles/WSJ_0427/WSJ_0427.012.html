Sweden's Producer Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's producer prices in March fell 0.1% from February, but rose 4.5% from a year earlier, Statistics Sweden, the national statistics agency, said. Export prices in March dropped 0.3% from February, but were up 3.2% from the year-earlier month. Import prices in March slipped 1.2% from February, mainly because of falling oil prices, but rose 0.9% from a year earlier.
