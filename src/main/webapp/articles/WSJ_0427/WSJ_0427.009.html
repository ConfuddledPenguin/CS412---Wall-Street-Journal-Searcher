Beard Oil Co. Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

OKLAHOMA CITY -- Beard Oil Co. said its board authorized the repurchase of as many as 300,000 of the company's common stock outstanding. The company said the shares will be purchased periodically in the open market or private transactions, and will be held for general corporate purchases.

The authorization covers nearly 5% of the company's 5,869,651 common shares outstanding at year end.
