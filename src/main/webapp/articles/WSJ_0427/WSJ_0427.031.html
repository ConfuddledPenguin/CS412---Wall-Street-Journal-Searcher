Philip Morris Shareholders Snub Anti-Tobacco Request

  • Author: A Wall Street Journal News Roundup
  • Date: 04/27/90

Philip Morris Cos. said its shareholders defeated a resolution that would have required the company to cease its business in tobacco products after Dec. 31, 1999. The New York-based packaged goods maker said its shareholders also rejected a proposal to establish a review committee to determine, among other things, the impact of promotions and advertising on childrens' decision to smoke.

Philip Morris makes Marlboro, the world's best-selling cigarette.
