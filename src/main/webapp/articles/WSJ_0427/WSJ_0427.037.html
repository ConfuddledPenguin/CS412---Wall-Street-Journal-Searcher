Letters to the Editor: Grossed Out

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

Your report on the Art Buchwald-Paramount Pictures Corp. legal battle concerning the motion picture "Coming to America" (March 21) may mislead the reader by your saying Paramount was "taking in revenue of $275 million world-wide." Paramount did not take in this money. The motion-picture exhibitors did (i.e. "box-office gross"). Paramount took its share of that gross ("rentals" or "distributor's gross"), which is usually less than 50% of the box-office gross -- in this case $125 million. Jack Blankley,

CPA

Los Angeles
