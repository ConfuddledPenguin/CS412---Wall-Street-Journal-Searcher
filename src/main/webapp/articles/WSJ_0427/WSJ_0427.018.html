Letters to the Editor: Befriend a Film Crew

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

After reading "LA Residents Grow Ever More Negative Toward Film Crews" (page one, March 30), I had to wonder why production companies would continue to film where they are not wanted. So many towns and cities could use "an average of $32,450 a day" boost to their economies. If Los Angeles does not want the film crews, then location managers should choose other cities. Kelly Hoggan

Salt Lake City
