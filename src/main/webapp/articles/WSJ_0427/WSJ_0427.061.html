Offering by 50-OFF Stores Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- 50-OFF Stores Inc. said it began a public offering of 2.5 million shares of common stock, including one million shares from a holder, at $3.375 a share. The holder has also granted an overallotment option of 375,000 shares of the discount apparel retailer to the underwriting group, which is managed by Epplin, Guerin & Turner Inc. of Dallas.
