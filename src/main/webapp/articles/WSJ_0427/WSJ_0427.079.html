Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 286,668 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, up 5.1% from 272,819 tons in the preceding week but down 3% from 295,575 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production fell 13% to 4,316,622 metric tons from 4,956,069 tons a year earlier.
