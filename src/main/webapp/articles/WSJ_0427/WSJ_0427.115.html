  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

DEXTER Corp. (Hartford, Conn.) -- This specialty chemical company named as directors Bernard Fox, president and chief operating officer of Northeast Utilities; Robert Furek, president and chief executive officer of Heublein Inc., and Max Weber, president and chief operating officer of Owens-Corning Fiberglas Corp. Four board members retired: Walter Connolly Jr., former chairman of Bank of New England Corp.; Donald Davis, chairman of the executive committee, Stanley Works; Donald Roon, president of Roon Foundation, and Peter Scott, retired chairman of Emhart Corp.
