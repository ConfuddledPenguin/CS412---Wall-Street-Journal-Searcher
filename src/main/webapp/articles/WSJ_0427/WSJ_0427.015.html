Weatherford Completes Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

HOUSTON -- Weatherford International Inc., an energy service concern, said it completed its offering of three million common shares, with proceeds to be used for payment of debt and general corporate purposes. As of March 31, Weatherford had 22,930,467 common shares outstanding. Salomon Brothers Inc. and Howard, Weil, Labouisse, Friedrichs Inc. managed the underwriting.
