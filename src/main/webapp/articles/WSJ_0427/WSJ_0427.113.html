Business Brief -- Akzo N.V.: Company Posts 11% Drop In First-Quarter Earnings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

Hurt by narrower profit margins, Dutch chemical giant Akzo N.V. reported that first-quarter net income fell 11% from a year earlier. Akzo recorded profit of 201.7 million guilders ($106.8 million) compared with the year-earlier 227.4 million guilders even though sales rose 1.8%. Sales by the chemical group rose to 4.58 billion guilders from 4.5 billion guilders.

Akzo said pretax operating profit fell to 8.2% of sales compared with 9.1% a year earlier. The chemical group's pretax operating profit fell to 375.2 million guilders from 410.4 million guilders.

As with net profit, per-share earnings also declined 11% to 4.66 guilders from the 5.25 guilders.
