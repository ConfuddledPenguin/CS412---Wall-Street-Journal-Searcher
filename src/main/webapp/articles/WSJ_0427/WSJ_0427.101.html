Who's News: Sequa Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

SEQUA Corp. (New York) -- Antonio L. Savoca, president of the Atlantic Research Corp. subsidiary; Daniel L. Marquitz, president of the Valley Line unit; and John Cabrey, vice president of the Sequa Chemicals unit, were elected vice presidents of this company, which makes and repairs jet engines, and has interests in military electronics and electro-optics, marine transportation, and machinery used to make food and beverage cans.
