Benton Oil & Gas Co. Well

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

VENTURA, Calif. -- Benton Oil & Gas Co. said it completed the Newhall Resources 25-1 well, which tested four million cubic feet of gas a day with no water. Calculated absolute flow was determined to be 22.5 million cubic feet a day. The well was tested on a 24/64-inch restricted opening with flowing tubing pressure of 2,109 pounds per square inch. The well was drilled to 7,300 feet, and pay was found between 6,542 feet and 6,596 feet.
