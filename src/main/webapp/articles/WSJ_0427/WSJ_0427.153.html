Who's News: First Boston Creates Post on Fixed-Income

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

NEW YORK -- First Boston Corp. named Walid Chammah, a managing director and head of asset-backed finance, to the newly created post of head of fixed-income capital markets. Mr. Chammah, 36 years old, will coordinate First Boston's investment banking division with its sales and trading business, the firm said. He will report to Charles Murphy, head of the investment banking group, and Ronald Stuart, a senior manager in the fixed-income area.

The move reflects the increasing importance First Boston places on the fixed-income business, officials of the firm said. This business recently has been in the doldrums.

Reporting to Mr. Chammah in his new post will be Richard Dubusc, head of syndicate; Peter Milhaupt, head of the market officers group; and Dennis Melchior, head of new-issue marketing. Mr. Chammah also will remain head of First Boston's asset-backed finance group.

First Boston is a unit of CS First Boston Inc., which is 44.5%-owned by the parent of Credit Suisse.
