Fluor Unit Gets Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp., an engineering, construction and technical services company, said that its Fluor Daniel unit was awarded two engineering, procurement and construction-management contracts by the Pillsbury Co. unit of London-based Grand Metropolitan PLC, and that the Texas Utilities Electric Co. unit of Dallas-based Texas Utilities Co. renewed its maintenance contract for two more years. The jobs' value wasn't disclosed. Fluor said the Pillsbury contracts are for expansion of the Wellston, Ohio, plant and renovation of the idle Murfreesboro, Tenn., facility.
