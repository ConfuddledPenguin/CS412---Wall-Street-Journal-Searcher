Who's News: Toys "R" Us Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

TOYS "R" US Inc. (Paramus, N.J.) -- Biagio Gabardi, most recently a partner with Gran Sole Group, a closely held Italian hypermarket concern, was named president of Toys "R" Us-Italy. The toy retailer plans to begin retail operations in Italy in the fall of 1991.
