Swiss Money-Laundering Laws

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

BERN, Switzerland -- The government is formulating a package of measures to supplement new laws that make laundering money a crime. Lutz Krauskopf, vice director of the federal office of justice, said that the additional measures could be prepared as a draft proposal for the federal council, or executive branch of government, to consider in May. By year end, legislation could be submitted to parliament.

The anti-laundering laws already passed are expected to take effect July 1.
