  • Date: 04/27/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

AT&T Capital Corp., a unit of American Telephone & Telegraph Co., offering of up to $2.1 billion of notes and warrants to purchase notes.

Delta Air Lines Inc., shelf offering of up to $750 million of debt securities.

Digital Systems International Inc., initial offering of two million common shares, of which 1.41 million will be offered by the company and 590,000 by holders, via Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. and Alex. Brown & Sons Inc.

Seligman Quality Municipal Fund Inc., initial offering of 10 million common shares.
