Boeing Gets Aircraft Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

SEATTLE -- Boeing Co.'s Commercial Airplane Group said it received an order for seven 737-500 aircraft from Intercredit Corp., a leasing concern based in Atlanta. The order is valued at about $200 million. In the year to date, the Boeing unit has received orders for 113 aircraft valued at about $8.1 billion.
