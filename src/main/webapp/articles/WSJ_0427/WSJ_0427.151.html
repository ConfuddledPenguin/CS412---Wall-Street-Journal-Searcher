Business Brief -- Crown Resources Corp.: Option Obtained to Acquire Nevada Goldfields' Properties

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

Crown Resources Corp. said it obtained a 30-day option to acquire mining properties from Nevada Goldfields Corp. The acquisition, if completed, is valued by Denver-based Crown at $4 million. Under terms of the option agreement, Crown Resources would pay Nevada Goldfields' secured lenders $1.5 million, assume a $1.5 million note at the prime rate plus 1%, payable within two years, and issue 100,000 shares of Crown common stock. Nevada Goldfields' secured lenders also will receive 50% of the net profit in one of the mining operations, with a maximum payout of $1 million.

In what a Crown spokesman termed a separate transaction, Crown Resources also agreed to acquire, in a $1 million private placement, 1,153,500 shares of Nevada Goldfields common stock and warrants to purchase 500,000 more at $1 a share. If the private placement is completed, Crown would hold a 12.5% stake in Nevada Goldfields common stock.
