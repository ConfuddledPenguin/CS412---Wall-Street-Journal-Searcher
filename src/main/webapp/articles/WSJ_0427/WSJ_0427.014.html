Who's News: Rochester Community Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

ROCHESTER COMMUNITY SAVINGS BANK (Rochester, N.Y.) -- Royal E. Fliedner, president and chief executive officer of Shadow Lawn Savings Bank SLA of West Long Branch, N.J., was named a director of this savings bank, increasing the size of the board to 17 from 16.
