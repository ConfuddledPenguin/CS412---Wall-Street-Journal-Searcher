WHO'S NEWS: Peter S. Prichard, editor of USA Today, was named to the additional post of chief news executive of Gannett Co. He formerly was senior vice president, news. The new appointment resulted from the retirement of John C. Quinn, former editor of USA Today, as executive vice president, news. Mr. Quinn also resigned as a director. . . . Michael A. Pietrangelo, citing family considerations, resigned as president and chief operating officer of Western Publishing Group Inc. Frank DiPrima, who was chief operating officer of Thompson Medical Co., will succeed him. Mr. Pietrangelo will remain on Western's board. He will become president and chief executive officer of the Cleo Inc. division of Gibson Greetings Inc.
