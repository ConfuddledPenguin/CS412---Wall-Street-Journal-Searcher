Norway's Unemployment Falls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

OSLO -- Norway's unemployment rate fell to 4.2% in April from 4.4% in March, but was up from 3.7% a year earlier, said Arbeidsdirektoratet, the government's labor agency. Norway's jobless rate peaked at 4.7% in January, when 101,900 people were out of work.

The April total was 91,500, down 2,300 from March but up 11,700 from a year earlier. Of the total, 59,000 were men and 32,500 women.

Not included in the official jobless rate were 50,200 people employed in extraordinary state work programs, down 4,100 from March, but up 11,200 from 12 months earlier.
