ASK Corp. Marketing Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

WACO, Texas -- ASK Corp. said that it reached an agreement with Mitsui Engineering & Shipbuilding Co. covering the world-wide sales of ASK's products and technology. An ASK spokesman wouldn't disclose the value of the agreement, but said it was a significant one for the company. The agreement calls for an initial marketing program in the U.S. and Japan. If successful, ASK said, it would then be introduced in other world markets.

ASK makes heating, ventilating, air-conditioning and cogeneration products. Mitsui is a Tokyo-based company with interests in shipbuilding, engineering and power-generation products and services.
