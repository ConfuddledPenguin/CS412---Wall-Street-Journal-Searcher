Who's News: E-Systems Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

E-SYSTEMS Inc. (Dallas) -- Terry W. Heil, senior vice president of E-Systems' information systems group, was named senior vice president of the company. In his new position, Mr. Heil will share responsibility for all the operational units of this maker of defense electronic systems with E-Systems' president and chief operating officer, Lowell Lawson. Formerly, operations were supervised by two group vice presidents and the president, but in a reorganization the group vice president positions will be eliminated. Charles R. Farmer Jr., senior vice president of the company's electronic systems group, has announced he intends to retire June 8.
