ABB Group's Unit for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/27/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Asea Brown Boveri Inc., a unit of Zurich, Switzerland-based ABB Group, said it has put up for sale its Sprout-Bauer Inc. unit. The unit is a supplier of pulping and process equipment with annual sales of $125 million. The decision to sell the Muncy, Pa.-based division is in line with ABB Group's strategy to focus its resources on its core business of electrical engineering products and services, a spokeswoman said.
