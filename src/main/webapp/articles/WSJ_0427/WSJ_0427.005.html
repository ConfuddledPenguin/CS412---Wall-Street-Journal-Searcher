Who's News: Sara Lee Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

SARA LEE Corp. (Chicago) -- Allen F. Jacobson, chairman and chief executive officer of Minnesota Mining & Manufacturing Co., was named a director of this food and consumer products concern, increasing board membership to 18. Mark J. McCarville, formerly vice president, corporate development, was named senior vice president, corporate development, and David W. Bryan, formerly president of the Fuller Brush Co. unit, was named vice president, business planning, both new posts.
