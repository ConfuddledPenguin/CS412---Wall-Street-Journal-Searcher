Business Brief -- Cies. Europeennes Reunis: De Benedetti Holding Firm Says Net Profit Gained 52%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

Cies. Europeennes Reunis said net profit after payments to minority interests totaled an estimated one billion French francs ($177.6 million) in 1989, up 52% from 656 million francs the previous year. The holding company for the investments of Carlo De Benedetti noted, however, that the earnings figures weren't comparable because of a merger with Dumenil-Leble S.A. in December.

Cerus said consolidated revenue totaled 27.5 billion francs last year.

Management proposed a net dividend of 10 francs a share, up from 7.4 francs a share paid out last year, Cerus said. A shareholders meeting is set for June 20.

Cerus oversees Mr. De Benedetti's stakes in Valeo S.A., Groupe Yves Saint-Laurent S.A., Societe Generale de Belgique S.A., and other companies in Europe.
