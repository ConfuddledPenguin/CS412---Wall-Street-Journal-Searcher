Who's News: American Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/27/90

AMERICAN SAVINGS BANK (White Plains, N.Y.) -- Philip M. Blumenfeld, previously a consultant for this bank, was named executive vice president, nonperforming loans. Earlier this month, H.L. Van Varick resigned as executive vice president, real estate lending, a post American doesn't immediately plan to fill.
