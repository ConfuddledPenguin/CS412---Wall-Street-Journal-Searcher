Business Brief -- Bechtel Group Inc.: Work Booked During 1989 Rose 21% From Year Ago

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

Bechtel Group Inc. said in San Francisco that new work booked in 1989 rose 21% from 1988. The closely held engineering and construction concern said it signed up $5.4 billion of new projects in 1989, compared with $4.5 billion a year earlier.

The company also reported that its revenue work-off -- the amount of work performed and billed -- in 1989 was $5.1 billion, up 14% from $4.5 billion in 1988.

Bechtel said its non-manual work force increased 7% in 1989 to 19,200. Total work force for the company is 27,800.
