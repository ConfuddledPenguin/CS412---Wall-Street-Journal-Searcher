Seagull to Sell Reserves

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

HOUSTON -- Seagull Energy Corp. said it plans to offer for sale about 8.3 billion cubic feet of its proved domestic natural gas reserves and 1.5 million barrels of oil reserves. Seagull said the sale, expected to be concluded by year end, involves mature reserves acquired since late 1988 that aren't strategic to the company's operations. The sale will allow some operating economies and help fund continuing exploration, Seagull said.
