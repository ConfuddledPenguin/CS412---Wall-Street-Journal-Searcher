Trinity Industries Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

DALLAS -- Trinity Industries Inc., a metal products maker, said it completed the redemption of its $201,880,000 face amount of liquid-yield option notes, due 2001, with 98.8% of the holders converting their notes to stock and the remainder accepting cash. Trinity said that each note was convertible into 14.2 Trinity common shares, and that it issued about 2,832,000 new shares as part of the redemption. It also said it paid about $900,000 in cash to redeem the remaining notes for about $372.33 each.
