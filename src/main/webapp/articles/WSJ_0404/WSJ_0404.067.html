French Output Forecast

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

PARIS -- French government forecasters expect the country's real gross domestic product to rise by 3.2% in 1990 and 2.8% in 1991 after a 3.7% increase in 1989, the Economics Ministry said. The forecast, which was made by the National Accounts Commission and is used by the government in working up its annual draft budget, represents an upward revision from the 1990 growth estimate of 3% that was made by the body last September.
