Alaska Air's Horizon Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

Seattle-Horizon Air, a unit of Alaska Air Group Inc., said revenue passenger miles in March rose 13% to 29.7 million from 26.2 million flown a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline said load factor, or percentage of seats filled by paying passengers, was 48.5%, down from 52.4% a year ago.

For the three months, revenue passenger miles flown were 80.4 million, up 11% from 72.7 million flown a year ago. Load factor for the three months was 48.4%, down from 51.3% a year earlier.
