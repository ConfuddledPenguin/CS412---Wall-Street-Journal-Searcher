Midway Air Traffic Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

CHICAGO -- Midway Airlines said March traffic rose 57% to 493.2 million revenue passenger miles from 314.8 million in the year-ago period. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The carrier said its March load factor, or percentage of seats filled, increased to 67.3% from 61.1%.

For the year to date, traffic rose 53% to 1.26 billion revenue passenger miles from 821.3 million.

The carrier said its year-to-date load factor rose to 61.7% from 55.7%.
