Mobil Unit Gas Discovery

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

NEW YORK -- Mobil Corp. said its West German exploration and production affiliate made a "significant" natural gas discovery in its Ahrensheide permit area in northern Germany. The 100%-owned Walsrode West Z-1 wildcat well tested at a rate of 27 million cubic feet of gas a day at 15,000 feet. Mobil said its affiliate, Mobil Erdgas-Erdoel G.m.b.H., will drill an appraisal well on the site in the second half of the year.
