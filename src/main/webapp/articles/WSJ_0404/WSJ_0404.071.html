Inspiration Resources Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

NEW YORK -- Inspiration Resources Corp. said it purchased LTM Inc. and Rogue Aggregates Inc., two affiliated, closely held companies. Terms weren't disclosed. The companies, based in Medford, Ore., produce construction materials for residential, commercial and government projects in southern Oregon and Northern California. The companies' combined sales for 1989 were about $22 million.

Inspiration Resources is a natural resources company that provides products and services for the agriculture, mining and construction industries.
