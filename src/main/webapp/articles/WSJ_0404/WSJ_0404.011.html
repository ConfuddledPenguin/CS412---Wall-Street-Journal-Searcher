OPEC Oil Output for March

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

LONDON -- Oil production by the 13-member Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries in March was 23.7 million barrels a day, unchanged from February levels, according to the Paris-based International Energy Agency. Among OPEC countries, a 200,000 barrel-a-day rise in Iranian production in March, to 3.1 million barrels a day, was counterbalanced by a slight decline in Libyan and Venezuelan production.
