Dividend News: Angeles Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

ANGELES CORP. set its initial cash distribution on its 1.7 million newly issued units at 30.56 cents per unit, and said the amount would be paid April 30 to unitholders of record April 16. Issuance of the units was part of the company's recent acquisition of the assets of several affiliated entities for $36.6 million. Each unit consists of one-half share of common stock, one-fiftieth of a new 12 1/2% subordinated debenture due 2005, and a special cash payment of $1 over the four-year life of the unit. Angeles, a Los Angeles investment management concern, said that the initial cash payment covers the period between March 3 and April 15.
