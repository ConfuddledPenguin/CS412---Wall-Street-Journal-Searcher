Wolohan Lumber Sales Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

SAGINAW, Mich. -- Wolohan Lumber Co. said March sales rose 14% to $20.3 million from $17.8 million a year ago. Same-store sales increased 10%. Sales for the first three months of 1990 rose 8.4% to $53.1 million from $49 million, with same-store sales up 5%.

Wolohan operates 49 lumber and building material retail stores, mainly in the Midwest.
