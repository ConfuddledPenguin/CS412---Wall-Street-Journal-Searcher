  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

W.R. GRACE & Co. (New York) -- Jacques Theumann, 55, was appointed president of this specialty chemical firm's European Darex division, based in Lausanne, Switzerland, and was elected a Grace corporate vice president. He succeeds Jean-Louis Greze who was recently named an executive vice president of the Grace Specialty Chemicals Co. unit. Mr. Theumann previously was vice president of the European Darex division's Cryovac unit. He will report to Mr. Greze.
