INTER-REGIONAL FINANCIAL GROUP Inc. (Minneapolis) -- Irving Weiser, 42-year-old president and chief executive officer of this financial-services concern, was named to the additional posts of president and chief executive of its Dain Bosworth Inc. brokerage firm unit, succeeding Fred Friswold, who resigned earlier this year.
