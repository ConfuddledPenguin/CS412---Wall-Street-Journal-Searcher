Calgene in Pact With Sakata

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

DAVIS, Calif. -- Calgene Inc. said it signed an agreement with Japan's Sakata Seed Corp. to genetically engineer disease resistance in a Sakata vegetable line. Terms weren't disclosed. The project is designed to reduce or eliminate fungicides used on vegetable crops for certain diseases, Calgene said.

Calgene is a biotechnology concern. Yokohama-based Sakata Seed, which develops hybrid vegetable and flower seeds, markets much of the world's broccoli seed.
