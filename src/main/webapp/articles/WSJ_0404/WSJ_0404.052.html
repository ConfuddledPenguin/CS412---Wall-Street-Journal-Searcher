Who's News: Prudential-Bache Hires Brophy to Head Desk For Institution Trades

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

NEW YORK -- Prudential-Bache Securities Inc. hired the third person in a year to head its institutional stock-trading desk. Martin Brophy, 47 years old, joined the firm this week as head of equity capital commitment. He was hired by Edward Braniff, president of the firm's equity division. Mr. Brophy previously worked at UBS Securities Inc. as a block trader.

Prior to Mr. Brophy, the big securities firm's equity desk was run by Philip Puccio. Mr. Puccio resigned from the firm in early March to join another brokerage concern. He had succeeded Dan Marciano, who resigned from the firm last May after the firm said he and another trader violated the firm's trading policies.

Prudential-Bache Securities Inc. is a division of Prudential Insurance Co. of America.
