EC Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

LUXEMBOURG -- Consumer prices in the European Community rose 0.5% in February, leaving the EC's average annual inflation rate at 5.2%, the EC statistics agency Eurostat reported. Eurostat said February's rise in prices in the EC was due to substantial increases in West Germany and the Netherlands (both 0.4%), Spain and Britain (both 0.6%), Italy (0.8%) and Portugal (2.3%).

In the other EC nations, prices rose moderately in February, Eurostat said.
