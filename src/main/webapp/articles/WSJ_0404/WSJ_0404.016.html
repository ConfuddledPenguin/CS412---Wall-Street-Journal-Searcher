Technology: Hewlett-Packard Printer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Hewlett-Packard Co. said it introduced its HP PaintWriter Xl color-printer exclusively for users of Apple Computer Inc.'s MacIntosh. The computer maker said the printer retails for $2,995 and is designed to be shared by MacIntosh users whose computers are linked to an Appletalk network.
