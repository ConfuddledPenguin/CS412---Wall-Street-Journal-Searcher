US Sprint Launches a Service

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

RESTON, Va. -- US Sprint Communications Co., a unit of United Telecommunications Inc., Kansas City, Mo., launched a service that allows calling from the U.S. to most nations with assistance from a Japanese-speaking operator and to bill the call to a Japanese credit card. US Sprint began the service under an agreement with JCB International, a major credit-card company in Japan.
