GM Unit Signs Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

DALLAS -- General Motors Corp.'s Electronic Data Systems Corp. said it signed a five-year credit-card and merchant processing contract with First Tennessee Bank of Memphis. Terms weren't disclosed.

Under the agreement, EDS said it will provide credit-card processing and merchant accounting services for First Tennessee Bank, the principal subsidiary of First Tennessee National Corp. First Tennessee Bank has more than 600,000 cardholders and a base of 30,000 merchants.
