Mercom Inc. Stock Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

BLOOMFIELD HILLS, Mich. -- Mercom Inc. said it purchased 368,753, or 13.3%, of its shares for $4,240,659, or $11.50 a share, from the Michigan Gas Utilities Employees' Stock Savings and Purchase plan of Utilicorp United Inc. The Utilicorp plan received the shares in a May 1989 spinoff transaction. Mercom said a commercial bank loan is financing the purchase, which its board authorized.
