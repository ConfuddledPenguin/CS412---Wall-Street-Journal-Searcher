Copperweld Holder Vote

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

PITTSBURGH -- Copperweld Corp. said shareholders will vote at a special meeting June 6 on a previously reported plan by Imetal of France to acquire the 34.8% of Copperweld stock Imetal doesn't already own at $17 a share. Copperweld produces tubing, rods and other metal products.
