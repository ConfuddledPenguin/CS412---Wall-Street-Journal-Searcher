Archive Controls 95% of Cipher

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

COSTA MESA, Calif. -- Archive Corp., a maker of computer tape drives, said 95% of Cipher Data Products Inc.'s 14.9 million common shares had been tendered as part of its takeover of Cipher, which also makes computer tape drives. Tag Merrick, Archive general counsel, said the company intends to complete the acquisition of San Diego-based Cipher for about $120 million within two weeks.
