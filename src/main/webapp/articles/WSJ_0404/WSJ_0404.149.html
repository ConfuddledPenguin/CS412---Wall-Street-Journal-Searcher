Who's News: Baldor Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

BALDOR ELECTRIC Co. (Fort Smith, Ark.) -- Robert L. Qualls, 55 years old, was elected president, succeeding Quentin D. Ponder, 60, who resigned to return to his consulting business. Mr. Qualls was executive vice president of finance and planning of this electric motor maker.
