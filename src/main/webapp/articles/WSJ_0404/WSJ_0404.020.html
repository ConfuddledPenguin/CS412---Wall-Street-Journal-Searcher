Treasury Plans to Sell $16.4 Billion of Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.15 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $16.4 billion in short-term bills to redeem $15.26 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on July 12 and Oct. 11, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
