Commercial Paper Rates Fall at Citicorp's Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.244% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.274% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $445 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.24% to 8.247%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 8.246% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.262% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $425 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.24% to 8.247%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
