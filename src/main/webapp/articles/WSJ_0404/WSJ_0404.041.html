Intermec, Symbol Technologies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

LYNWOOD, Wash. -- Intermec Corp., a maker of bar-code data systems, said it agreed to pay Bohemia, N.Y.-based Symbol Technologies Inc., also a bar-code systems maker, at least $50 million over a six-year period, including royalties of $21.2 million, for a license to make certain products under Symbol's laser scanning patents, and purchases of $28.8 million of Symbol's scanning products.
