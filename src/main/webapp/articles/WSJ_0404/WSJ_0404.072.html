American Medical Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/04/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- American Medical International Inc. said that shareholders at its annual meeting approved the hospital company's acquisition by IMA Holdings Corp. The purchase is scheduled to be completed later this month. The move was expected as IMA Holdings, an investor group including CS First Boston Group's First Boston Corp. and the Pritzker family of Chicago, acquired 86% of American Medical's 71.5 million common shares outstanding in a tender offer late last year.
