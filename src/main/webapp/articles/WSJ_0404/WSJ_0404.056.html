Who's News: GE Names Executive For Environment Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/04/90

FAIRFIELD, Conn. -- General Electric Co. said it named Stephen D. Ramsey, a Washington, D.C., environmental lawyer and former head of the Justice Department's environmental-enforcement unit, as vice president, corporate environmental programs. Mr. Ramsey, 42 years old, succeeds W. Roger Strelow, 46, who left to join Bechtel Corp. as vice president of its environmental programs.

GE said Mr. Ramsey will be in charge of ensuring "consistent state-of-the-art" environmental practices across its 13 businesses in the U.S. and abroad. He will direct a staff of technical, management and legal personnel.

Mr. Ramsey currently is a senior partner at the law firm of Sidley & Austin.
