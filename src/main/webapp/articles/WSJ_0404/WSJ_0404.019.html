The Global View column on this page Monday, "Who Will Pay the Bill for Russia's Decline?" used some inaccurately transmitted statistics regarding man-days lost in the Soviet Union. The correct numbers are 9.1 million mandays lost in the first two months this year, compared with 7.3 million for the full year 1989. (See: "Who Will Pay the Price for Russia's Decline?" -- WSJ April 2, 1990).
