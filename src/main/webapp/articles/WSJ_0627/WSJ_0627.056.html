Browning-Ferris Will Sell Cans to Alcoa Subsidiary

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

HOUSTON -- Browning-Ferris Industries Inc., a waste-management concern, said it agreed to sell the used aluminum cans it collects to a unit of Aluminum Co. of America that recycles cans. Browning-Ferris said the pact is the first time that Pittsburgh-based Alcoa has arranged to be supplied by a waste-collection firm, and the first time that Browning-Ferris has agreed to supply an aluminum company.

A Browning-Ferris spokesman wouldn't describe the revenue that the company might generate by selling its cans to Alcoa.

Last year in the U.S., Browning-Ferris collected about one billion used aluminum beverage cans.
