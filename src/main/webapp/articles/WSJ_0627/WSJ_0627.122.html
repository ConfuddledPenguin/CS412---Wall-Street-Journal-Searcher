Business Brief -- Canada Packers Inc.: Holders Approve Transaction Involving a British Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

Canada Packers Inc. shareholders approved a complex transaction that would result in the acquisition of a 56% stake in the company by a subsidiary of Hillsdown Holdings PLC of Britain. A. Roger Porretti, Canada Packers president and chief executive officer, said the transaction was approved by 73% of shareholders who voted. He said the proposal still requires approval by Canadian regulatory authorities.

Under the previously reported proposal, Canada Packers, the largest food-processing company in Canada, would issue 29.5 million new shares to acquire Maple Leaf Mills Ltd., a Hillsdown unit. Hillsdown, also a food-processing company, would offer to acquire 7.2 million Canada Packers shares, or about 20% of the existing shares, at 16.50 Canadian dollars (US$14.02) each.

The combined company would have annual revenue of about C$4 billion.
