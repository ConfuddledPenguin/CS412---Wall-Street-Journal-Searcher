Turner Revolving-Credit Line

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

NEW YORK -- Turner Corp., a major general-construction company, reached agreement on a three-year $32.5 million revolving-credit facility with a group of banks led by Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. This is Turner's first committed, unsecured revolving-credit facility, and it replaces certain "overnight," or very short-term, lines of credit the company has had, a spokesman said. Turner will maintain some overnight credit, he added.
