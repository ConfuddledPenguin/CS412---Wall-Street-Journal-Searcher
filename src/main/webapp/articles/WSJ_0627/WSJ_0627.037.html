Ipalco Sets Rights Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Ipalco Enterprises Inc. said its board approved a shareholder rights plan designed to protect against "coercive" takeover tactics. The utility holding concern said the plan wasn't adopted in response to any specific effort to gain control of the company, "and we are not aware of any such attempt."

Under the plan, shareholders will on July 11 be issued rights that, in the event of an unwanted takeover attempt, under certain circumstances will allow them to buy stock in the company at half-price.
