Who's News: International Business Machines Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS MACHINES Corp. (Armonk, N.Y.) -- Judith Richards Hope and Frank A. Metz Jr. were elected to the board. Mrs. Hope is a senior partner at Paul, Hastings, Janofsky & Walker, a California law firm. Mr. Metz is IBM's senior vice president of finance and planning. Their election expands IBM's board to 20 directors.
