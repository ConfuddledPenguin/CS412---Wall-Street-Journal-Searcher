CONDE NAST PUBLICATIONS Inc. acquired the subscription list of Cook's magazine, and not the magazine as reported in yesterday's edition. Cook's publisher, Pennington Publishing Inc., had previously decided to close the magazine after efforts to find a buyer failed. (See: "Marketing & Media: Conde Nast Buys Cook's Magazine and Will Fold It" -- WSJ June 26, 1990)
