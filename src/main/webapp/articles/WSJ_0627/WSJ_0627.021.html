Oregon Steel Mills Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Oregon Steel Mills Inc. said it signed a letter of intent with Corporacion Venezolana de Guayana (CVG) to build a hot iron metal briquette plant in the state of Bolivar, Venezuela. The project is to be carried out by a joint venture of Oregon Steel Mills and CVG. Initially, Oregon would hold at least an 80% interest, with CVG retaining as much as 20%. The company said it expects to execute definitive contracts within 12 months.

Full financial details weren't disclosed, but the company said it expects the plant will require a total estimated investment of $165 million, and is expected to produce 750,000 metric tons of product a year.
