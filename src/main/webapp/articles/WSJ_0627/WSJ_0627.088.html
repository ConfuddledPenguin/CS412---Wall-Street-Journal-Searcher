REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Lip Reading

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

Washington is agog that President Bush handed out a statement on the budget that included the words, "tax revenue increases." The town's Democrats and their supporters haven't sounded so happy since 1960. Hmmm. What's that word "revenue" doing in there? Is it possible to have a tax revenue increase without a "tax increase"? We think so. It's called a capital-gains tax cut. Stay tuned.
