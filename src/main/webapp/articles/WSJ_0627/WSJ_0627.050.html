Morrison Knudsen Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it received a multimillion-dollar, long-term contract for operations and maintenance services for an International Business Machines Corp. distribution facility in Mechanicsburg, Pa. Terms weren't disclosed. The project, already under way, is for a three-year base period with two optional one-year extensions. The work will eventually involve 60 people performing work in areas of building maintenance, utility systems, roads and grounds.
