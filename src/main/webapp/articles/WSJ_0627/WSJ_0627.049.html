Financing Business: Loewen Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/27/90

LOEWEN GROUP Inc., a fast-growing funeral home chain based in Vancouver, British Columbia, said it agreed to sell 64.5 million Canadian dollars (US$54.8 million) of common shares to an underwriting group. Loewen said proceeds from the sale to three Toronto securities dealers of 2.7 million common shares, at C$23.875 each, will be used to help fund the company's acquisition program. The dealers plan a subsequent public offering of the shares in Canada, a spokesman said. Closing of the transaction is expected by mid-July. Loewen, which is North America's second-largest funeral home chain and which last week announced plans to buy a Houston funeral home chain, has about 11.5 million common shares outstanding.
