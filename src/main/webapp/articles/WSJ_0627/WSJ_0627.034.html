Saint-Gobain's Offer for Norton

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

PARIS -- Compagnie de Saint-Gobain S.A. extended its $90-a-share tender offer for Norton Co. The offer and withdrawal rights are set to expire July 6. Tender rights were scheduled to expire today. In April, Saint-Gobain agreed to acquire Worcester, Mass.-based Norton for $90 a share, or about $1.9 billion, outbidding a $75-a-share offer by BTR PLC.

As of Monday, about 8,893,005 shares, or 42.5% of Norton's common stock, had been tendered to Saint-Gobain.
