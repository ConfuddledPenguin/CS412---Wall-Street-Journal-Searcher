Business Brief -- Atari Games Corp.: Concern Sets Buy-Back Pact To Get Shares From Namco

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

Atari Games Corp. said it signed an agreement to buy back all of its shares held by one of its two largest outside shareholders, Namco Ltd. of Japan. Under the pact, whose terms weren't disclosed, Namco will give its shares back to Atari, with an undisclosed amount of cash and other considerations, in exchange for ownership of Atari Operations Inc., a subsidiary that operates 40 video game arcades in the U.S.

Atari Games said the transaction will broaden its opportunities to conduct business in Asia.

Atari Games, Milpitas, Calif., is a privately held developer of video games whose majority shareholder after this transaction will be Time Warner Inc.

Atari Games isn't affiliated with Atari Corp., the maker of computers and video games.
