Who's News: Cara Operations Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/27/90

CARA OPERATIONS Ltd. (Toronto) -- M. Bernard Syron, chief executive officer of this food-service concern, was named to the additional post of chairman, succeeding Paul Phelan, majority shareholder, who was named honorary chairman. Gunter Otto, chief operating officer, was named to the additional post of president, succeeding Mr. Syron.
