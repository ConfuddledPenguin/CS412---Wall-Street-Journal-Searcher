Amcore to Buy Back Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/27/90

ROCKFORD, Ill. -- Amcore Financial Inc. said its board approved the purchase of as many as $2 million of its common stock from time to time in the open market or through privately negotiated transactions. A spokesman for the bank holding company said the dollar value would allow Amcore to buy about 142,850 shares, or 2.7% of the common outstanding, at the current price of about $14.

The company, which has about 5.1 million shares outstanding, said the stock will become treasury shares and will be used for general corporate purposes.
