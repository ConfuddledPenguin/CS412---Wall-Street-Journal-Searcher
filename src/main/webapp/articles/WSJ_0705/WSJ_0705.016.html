Halliburton Unit Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

DALLAS -- Brown & Root Braun, a unit of Halliburton Co., said it was awarded a contract to rebuild two of the high-density polyethylene units destroyed last October in an explosion and fire at Phillips Petroleum Corp.'s Phillips 66 Co. plant in Pasadena, Texas. Terms weren't disclosed. Phillips is a Bartlesville, Okla., energy and chemical concern.
