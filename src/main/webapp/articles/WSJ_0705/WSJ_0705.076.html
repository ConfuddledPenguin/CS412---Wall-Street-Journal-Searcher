Business Brief -- Eastman Kodak Co.: Recyclable 35mm Camera To Be Offered by October

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

Eastman Kodak Co., Rochester, N.Y., said it plans to introduce a recyclable 35mm camera, the Fun Saver 35. The photographic, pharmaceutical and chemical concern said the camera, with a built-in electronic flash, will be priced at less than $16 and available to consumers by mid-October.

Designed for indoor and outdoor use, each camera will be sold loaded with a 24-exposure roll of Kodacolor Gold 400 print film, Kodak said. Upon completing all 24 exposures, a user may return the entire camera to a photofinisher, who will process the film and return the camera for recycling.
