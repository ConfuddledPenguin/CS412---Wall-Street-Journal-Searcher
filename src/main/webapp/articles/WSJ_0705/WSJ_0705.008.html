John Fluke's Net Orders

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

EVERETT, Wash. -- John Fluke Mfg. Co. said net orders for its third quarter ended June 29 were "lower than anticipated" at $53.7 million. The company declined to say what it had hoped orders would be. It cited reduced orders from defense contractors and "belt-tightening measures" by certain customers. John Fluke makes test and measurement instruments.
