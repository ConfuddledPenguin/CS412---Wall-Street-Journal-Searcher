Who's News: Columbia Gas System Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

COLUMBIA GAS SYSTEM Inc. (Wilmington, Del.) -- James P. Holland was elected chairman and chief executive officer of this natural gas pipeline company's Columbia Gas Transmission Corp. and Columbia Gulf Transmission Co. units, effective Sept. 1. Mr. Holland, 41 years old, is currently president of both subsidiaries. He will succeed John D. Daly, who will become an executive vice president and director of the parent company. R. Larry Robinson, senior vice president of planning for the two subsidiaries, will succeed Mr. Holland as president.
