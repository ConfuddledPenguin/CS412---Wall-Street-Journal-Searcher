Who's News: Cyprus Minerals Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

CYPRUS MINERALS Co. (Denver) -- Philip C. Wolf, 42 years old, was named senior vice president of this mining company's industrial minerals and specialty metals operations. He will continue as president of Cyprus Industrial Minerals Co. and will assume the title of president of Cyprus Foote Mineral Co. Mr. Wolf is succeeding Thomas A. Williams, 54, who resigned as executive vice president to pursue other interests.
