Who's News: Hadron Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

HADRON Inc. (Fairfax, Va.) -- James L. Arnold, vice president and general counsel of this integrated high technology company, was elected chairman. Mr. Arnold, 43 years old, succeeds Dominic A. Laiti, who left the post in June 1989 to become president of Globalink Language Services Inc., a spinoff of Hadron.
