Who's News: EDO Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

EDO Corp. (New York) -- Alfred Brittain III was elected to the board of this maker of electronic and specialized aerospace equipment, bringing its membership to 12. Mr. Brittain, 67 years old, is a director and former chairman of Bankers Trust New York Corp., and is a director of Philip Morris Cos.
