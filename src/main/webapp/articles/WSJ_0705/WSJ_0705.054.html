Bank of Montreal Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

MONTREAL -- Bank of Montreal said it sold its 20% stake in Paris-based Banque Transatlantique to Credit Industriel et Commercial de Paris for about 23 million Canadian dollars ($19.8 million). Bank of Montreal said Credit Industriel et Commercial now will hold about 60% of Banque Transatlantique. Bank of Montreal said it will realize a pretax capital gain of about C$17 million on the sale. Bank of Montreal said it will continue to maintain its own representative office in Paris.
