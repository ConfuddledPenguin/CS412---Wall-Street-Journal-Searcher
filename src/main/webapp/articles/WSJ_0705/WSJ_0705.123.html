SSI Services Receives $540 Million Contract From the Air Force

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

WASHINGTON -- SSI Services Inc. of Tullahoma, Tenn., received a $540 million Air Force contract for support of the Arnold Engineering Development Center.

Sverdrup Technology received a $300.1 million Air Force contract for operating propulsion test facilities at the Arnold Engineering Development Center.

Calspan Corp. received a $276.5 million Air Force contract for operating aerospace dynamics test facilities at the Arnold Engineering Development Center. The center is the Air Force's only facility for testing large rocket engines.

Lockheed Corp. received a $188.4 million Air Force contract for C-130 aircraft.

STC Submarine Systems Inc. received a $40 million Navy contract for fiber optic cables.

Rockwell International Corp. received a $39.8 million Air Force contract for communications equipment.

General Electric Co. received a $21.5 million Navy contract for main reduction gears.

Otomelara S.p.A, an Italian firm, received a $12.1 million Navy contract for 76mm gun mounts.
