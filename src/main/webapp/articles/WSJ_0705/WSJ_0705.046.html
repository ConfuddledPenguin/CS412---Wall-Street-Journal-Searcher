Health-Mor Inc. Acquires Bliss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

LOMBARD, Ill. -- Health-Mor Inc., a maker of vacuum cleaners and specialty machinery, said it completed its acquisition of closely held Bliss Manufacturing Co., a maker of metal stampings for the automotive industry, based in Youngstown, Ohio. Terms of the cash-and-stock transaction weren't disclosed. Bliss had $55.4 million in sales for the year ended Sept. 30.
