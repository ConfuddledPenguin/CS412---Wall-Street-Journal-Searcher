REUTER INC.'S National Market System short position in the month to midJune was 9,941, not the 512,341 shares listed in the June 27 edition. Reuters Holdings PLC's National Market System short position in the month to mid-June was 2,734,527, not 2,232,127 as reported in the same edition. The incorrect numbers were due to a reporting error by a Nasdaq member firm. ---

WILLIAMS-SONOMA Inc. sold ex-distribution Monday and closed in national overthe-counter trading at $25.125, after adjustment for a 3-for-2 stock split. Failure to make the stock split adjustment caused the company to be mistakenly listed as a Price Percentage Loser in Tuesday's edition.

---

JOHN GORDON of Deltec Securities Corp., who heads a steering committee of Southland Corp. security holders, said issues remain between security holders and Southland regarding a revised debt exchange offer by the company. In Tuesday's edition, Mr. Gordon was quoted as saying he was favorably impressed by the exchange offer's details. Mr. Gordon said his comments related only to the relative allocation of values among various security holder groups and not to the overall amount being offered by Southland.

(See: "Southland Unveils Debt-Offer Details, Stock-Split Plan" -- WSJ July 3, 1990)
