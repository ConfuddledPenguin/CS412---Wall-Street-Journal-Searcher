Marketing Brief -- Rogers Communications Inc.: Company Agrees to Acquire Skyline Cablevision Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

Rogers Communications Inc. said it agreed to purchase all the shares it doesn't already own of Skyline Cablevision Ltd., a closely held cable television concern. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed. Skyline Cablevision, Ottawa, has 115,000 subscribers.

A Rogers Communications official said the company already has a stake of "less than 50%" in Skyline Cablevision, and that the acquisition of the remaining shares from a group of individuals in Ottawa would bring its stake to 100%.

Toronto-based Rogers Communications, through its Rogers Cablesystems Ltd. unit, has 1.6 million cable subscribers. Rogers Communications also has interests in telecommunications, broadcasting and mobile communications.
