Alaska Air's June Traffic Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

SEATTLE -- Alaska Airlines, a unit of Alaska Air Group Inc., said its June traffic increased 7% to 425.1 million revenue passenger miles from 396.6 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, decreased to 57.8% from 59.8%.
