HOVNANIAN ENTERPRISES Inc. (Red Bank, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it affirmed its single-B-plus rating on $92 million of subordinated debt of this home builder. The rating concern said its move reflects Hovnanian's "strong competitive position . . . impressive earnings track record, solid capital base, and the expectation that the company will prudently manage its operations, outperform competitors, and maintain profitability during the current residential market downturn in the Northeast." ---

PHILIPS INDUSTRIES NV (Eindhoven, the Netherlands) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. placed the senior and short-term debt totaling $1.4 billion of the company's U.S. unit, North American Philips Corp., under review for a possible downgrade. The review was prompted by the parent company's expected $1.07 billion loss for the year, resulting from special charges for its unprofitable computer and semiconductor businesses.
