Stone & Webster 4-Year Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

NEW YORK -- Stone & Webster Engineering Corp., received a four-year contract to provide maintenance and other services to Illinois Power Co.'s nuclear power plant in Clinton, Ill. Terms weren't disclosed. The Boston unit of construction and engineering company Stone & Webster Inc., has been providing maintenance services at Clinton since 1985. Illinois Power is a Decatur, Ill., utility.
