Datron Acquires Unit of Vard

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

SIMI VALLEY, Calif. -- Datron Systems Inc., which designs and makes satellite communication systems and microwave products for military and commercial uses, said it completed the acquisition of Transco Products, a Camarillo, Calif., designer of microwave antennas and switches, from Vard Newport Co., a holding company based in Burbank, Calif., for about $10 million. Transco had sales of $22.9 million last year.
