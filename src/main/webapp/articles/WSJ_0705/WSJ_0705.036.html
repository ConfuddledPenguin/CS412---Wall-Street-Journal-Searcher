Bechtel Group Affiliate Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Bechtel Investments sold its controlling interest in Dual Drilling Co. to Mosvold Shipping A/S of Kristiansand, Norway, for $170 million. Dual, of Dallas, is among the nation's largest offshore oil drilling concerns. "Essentially, it was a portfolio decision that the investment company has taken to reduce our high exposure to energy investments," said a Bechtel spokesman. "We found a buyer who offered us a good price and we accepted their offer."

The affiliate of Bechtel Group Inc. acquired its stake in Dual about 10 years ago. Dual operates in the Gulf of Mexico and the U.S. Pacific Ocean, as well as off the coasts of Brazil, India and Indonesia. Mosvold is a publicly held company that, with this acquisition, will have about 1,800 employees.
