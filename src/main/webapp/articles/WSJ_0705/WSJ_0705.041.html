ADC Acquires an LPL Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- ADC Telecommunications Inc. said that it acquired American Lightwave Systems Inc., a maker of fiber-optic video transmission equipment and a 65%-owned unit of LPL Technologies Inc., Wallingford, Conn., for $10.7 million, and that it agreed to make "certain guaranteed and contingent payments over a 3 1/2-year period" totaling at least $4 million. ADC, which acquired LPL's 65% stake and certain American Lightwave employees' 35% holding, said the transaction "adds significantly" to its fiber-optic capabilities and "positions us to participate in the fast-growing video-services delivery market." ADC said it and American Lightwave are cooperating in providing field trials with major telephone companies of voice and video systems.
