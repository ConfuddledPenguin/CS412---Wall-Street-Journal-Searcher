Dutch Industrial Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

AMSTERDAM -- Dutch industry's first-quarter sales rose 2.5%, based on strong growth in exports. Including the first-quarter rise of 1% in Dutch industrial prices, sales as measured by prices increased a total of 3.5%.

Dutch industrial exports grew 4.5%, and industrial sales for use within the Netherlands rose a less robust 0.5%. Dutch industries also were able to add 1.5% to their export prices, bringing the to 6% the total increase in Dutch industrial sales for export as measured by prices. Dutch industries didn't increase prices in the domestic market.
