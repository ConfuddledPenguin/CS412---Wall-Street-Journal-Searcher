Treasury Plans to Raise $5.2 Billion Next Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise about $5.2 billion in cash next week through sales of bills and notes. On Monday, it plans to raise $1.9 billion with the sale of about $18 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.09 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Oct. 11 and Jan. 10, 1991, respectively. The bills will be available in minimum denominations of $10,000.

On Wednesday, the Treasury plans to raise $3.3 billion in cash with the sale of about $8 billion in seven-year notes to redeem $4.7 billion in maturing notes. The offering will mature July 15. The notes will be available in minimum $1,000 denominations.

Bids for the bills and notes must be received by 1 p.m. EDT on the day of the auction at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
