Cleveland-Cliffs to Post Gain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

CLEVELAND -- Cleveland-Cliffs Inc., a natural resources concern, said it will report a third-quarter gain of $2.6 million, or 22 cents a share, from the completion of its previously announced $3.9 million sale of a unit's interests in Kentucky coal reserves. A year earlier, the company earned $16.5 million, or $1.42 a share, on revenue of $95 million.
