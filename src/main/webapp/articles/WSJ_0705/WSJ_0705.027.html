Unocal Unit Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

LOS ANGELES -- Unocal Corp. said one of its units completed the acquisition of the Prairie Producing unit of Placer Dome Inc. for about $336 million. Placer is a Vancouver-based gold producer. The transaction, which includes about $252 million in cash, adds to Unocal's natural gas reserves and new exploratory lands in East Texas and Louisiana. The buyer is a unit of Union Exploration Partners, or UXP, a Houston-based limited partnership 95%-owned by the oil giant.
