UPS Acquires Seabourne Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- United Parcel Service of America Inc., a giant package-delivery company, acquired the Seabourne European Express Parcels division of Seabourne Express Ltd. of Antwerp, Belgium. Closely held UPS didn't disclose terms. The purchase of the division, which has annual revenue of more than $26 million, will help UPS develop a Europewide ground-delivery system. It now covers all of Europe through air-delivery routes.
