Who's News: Royal Bank of Scotland Group PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

ROYAL BANK OF SCOTLAND GROUP PLC (Edinburgh) -- George Younger, 58, was named chairman of the board of directors of this bank holding company's principal subsidiary, Royal Bank of Scotland PLC. Mr. Younger, the former secretary of state for defense, has been deputy chairman since January and succeeds Sir Michael Herris, 67, who will remain as director of the bank and also retain the chairmanship of the parent company.
