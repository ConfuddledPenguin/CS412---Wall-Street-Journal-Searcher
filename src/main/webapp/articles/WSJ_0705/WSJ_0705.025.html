Alitalia S.p.A. Bid

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

MILAN -- Alitalia S.p.A., Italy's flagship air carrier, said it has pulled out of the international bidding for Aerolineas Argentinas, Argentina's state airline. Alitalia said it decided not to participate in the bid after determining "the impossibility of developing a practicable management program under the restrictive terms of the Argentine government."

The privatization of Argentina's state airlines will involve 85% of the company's share capital, while the remaining portion will remain in the hands of the state.

Under terms stipulated by the Argentine government regarding the portion to be privatized, only 49% can end up in foreign hands, and only 30% can be sold to a foreign airline.
