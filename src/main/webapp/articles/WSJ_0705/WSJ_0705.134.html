Niagara Exchange Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Niagara Exchange Corp. said that the New York State Department of Insurance disallowed the purchase of $8.2 million in preferred stock by W.R. Berkley Corp. The proposed sale of the 970,000 shares of preferred stock had been designed, Niagara said, to help replace funds paid out last year by a Niagara subsidiary on claims arising from Hurricane Hugo.

Niagara Chairman Charles J. Clauss said "management is considering its options and responsibilities in light of the insurance department's decision." The insurer, which writes property and casualty insurance, did not elaborate on the reasons for that decision.
