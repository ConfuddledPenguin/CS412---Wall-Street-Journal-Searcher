GM's Olds Offers a Rebate

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Corp.'s Oldsmobile division said it is offering $1,500 consumer rebates on its 1990 Oldsmobile Eighty-Eight models in several sales zones covering most of the U.S. through Aug. 2. Oldsmobile already offered consumer rebates nationally on its Cutlasss Ciera, Toronado, Trofeo and Silhouette models, and it has applied factory-to-dealer incentives to most of its other models.

Separately, Ford Motor Co. said it extended through Aug. 8 from next Tuesday consumer incentives on its trucks. The program, which covers the Aerostar, Bronco II, Bronco, Ranger and Econoline/Club models, includes discount financing and cash rebates of as much as $1,500.
