Atkinson Sells Interest in Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

SOUTH SAN FRANCISCO, Calif. -- Guy F. Atkinson Co., a construction, manufacturing, and oil and gas exploration concern, said it sold its interest in AWD Technologies Inc., an affiliated hazardous-waste remediation company, to Dow Chemical Co., Midland, Mich. Terms of the sale, completed last month, weren't disclosed.
