ATLANTA -- Delta Air Lines said June traffic fell 1.8%, to 5.43 billion revenue passenger miles from 5.53 billion in the year-earlier period. Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, was 65.44%, compared with 70.98%. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger carried one mile.

For the year to date, the carrier said its traffic fell 1.2%, to 28.74 billion revenue passenger miles from 29.11 billion a year ago. Load factor fell to 59.64% from 64.24%.
