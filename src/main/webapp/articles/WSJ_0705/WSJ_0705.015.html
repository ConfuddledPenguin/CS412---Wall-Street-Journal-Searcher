Lufthansa's Volume Up 24%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

FRANKFURT -- Deutsche Lufthansa AG said it boosted its passenger traffic in May 24% from a year earlier to around two million people. The 51%-state-owned West German flag carrier added that in the first five months of the year, the volume of its passenger business rose 9.6% to 8.5 million people.

The airline said it carried 350,000 metric tons of freight in the first five months of the year, 2.4% more than a year earlier.
