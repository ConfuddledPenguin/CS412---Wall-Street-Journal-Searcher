Business Brief -- Beeba's Creations Inc.: Company Plans to Liquidate Interests in Two Operations

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

Beeba's Creations Inc. said it plans to liquidate its minority interests in its two remaining offshore manufacturing operations, resulting in a $4 million pre-tax charge against earnings. The San Diego-based company also reported a net loss of $5.3 million, or $1.19 a share, for the third quarter ended May 31, compared with net income of $1.6 million, or 35 cents a share, a year ago. The writeoff accounted for 53 cents a share of the loss.

The importer and distributor of women's clothing blamed its problems on decreasing gross margins caused by bankruptcy filings by major retailers, poor weather, weak economic conditions in several parts of the country and competitive pressures.

As a result, said Steven P. Wyandt, chief executive officer, "The company has been unable to liquidate its spring and summer inventory within season as in prior years and will be forced to carry a significant portion of this inventory into fiscal 1991."
