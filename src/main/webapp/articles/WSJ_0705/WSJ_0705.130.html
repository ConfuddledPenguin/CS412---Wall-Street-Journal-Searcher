Varlen Completes Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/05/90

NAPERVILLE, Ill. -- Varlen Corp., which makes precision industrial products, said it completed the acquisition of Walter Herzog G.m.b.H., a West German maker of automated laboratory-analysis equipment for the petroleum industry. Terms of the cash transaction weren't disclosed. Varlen said Herzog has annual sales of about $15 million. Herzog, based in Lauda-Konigshofen, is Varlen's first overseas acquisition, and will be operated as an independent subsidiary.
