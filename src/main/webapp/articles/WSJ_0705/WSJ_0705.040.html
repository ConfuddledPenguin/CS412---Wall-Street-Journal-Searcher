Who's News: Securities & Investments Board

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/05/90

SECURITIES & INVESTMENTS BOARD (London) -- John Craven, 49 years old, was named a director of this regulatory body for the British investment industry. Mr. Craven is chairman of Morgan Grenfell Group PLC and member of the board of managing directors of the Deutsche Bank AG, which owns Morgan Grenfell. Also, Lord Runciman, 56, is to be named a deputy chairman as of next month, succeeding Sir Mark Weinberg, 58. Lord Runciman is chairman of Walter Runciman PLC and currently an SIB board director.
