Fluor Unit Wins NASA Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp. said its FD Services facility-operations and maintenance unit received a $27 million contract from the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. The engineering and construction company said the contract is for construction services on as-needed, capital-improvement projects at the Johnson Space Center in Houston, Texas. The one-year contract includes four one-year renewal options, Fluor said.

Separately, the company's Fluor Daniel Inc. unit said it received construction contracts from two units of Danbury, Conn.-based Union Carbide Corp. The value of the contracts wasn't disclosed.
