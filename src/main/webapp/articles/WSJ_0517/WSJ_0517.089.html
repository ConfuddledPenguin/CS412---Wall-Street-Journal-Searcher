Who's News: Owens-Corning Fiberglas Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

OWENS-CORNING FIBERGLAS Corp. (Toledo, Ohio) -- Warren D. Knowlton was named vice president, sales, for this insulation and construction-products maker's industrial-materials group. He succeeds Robert J. DeGange, who recently was named vice president, textile-materials marketing for the industrial-materials group. Mr. Knowlton, 43, was vice president, finance and business planning for the company's roofing-products operating division of the construction-products group.
