Ark Restaurants Buys Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

NEW YORK -- Ark Restaurants Corp. said it agreed to purchase 380,000 shares, or about 11% of its common stock from Ernest Bogen, chairman, for $3.25 a share. The company said Mr. Bogen, who will retain 10,000 shares, will remain chairman. At the same time, it said he will also work on special projects as a consultant.

In composite trading on the American Stock Exchange yesterday, Ark's shares closed at $3.12, down 12 cents.

Ark owns or operates 20 restaurants and bars in New York, Boston and Washington, D.C.
