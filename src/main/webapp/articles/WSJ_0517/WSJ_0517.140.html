Marketing Brief -- Turner Broadcasting System Inc.: Accord Reached to Create Southeast Sports Network

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

Turner Broadcasting System Inc. said it has agreed with TCI Sports Inc. and Scripps Howard Productions Inc. to create SportSouth Network, a regional sports cable network for the Southeast. As an affiliate of Prime Network, a national sports-program service, SportSouth Network will begin serving Alabama, Georgia, Mississippi, Tennessee, South Carolina and parts of North Carolina on Aug. 29, Turner said. The cable-television programming concern said the new network will broadcast at least eight hours of programming a day on weekdays and 12 hours a day on weekends.

TCI is a unit of Denver-based Tele-Communications Inc., and Scripps is a unit of Scripps Howard Broadcasting, Cincinnati.

Atlanta-based Turner said SportSouth will carry Atlanta Braves baseball games not already telecast by the company's TBS Superstation, Atlanta Hawks basketball games, as well as college football, baseball and basketball games, auto racing and other events. The network will be based in Atlanta.
