Who's News: Dyson-Kissner-Moran Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

DYSON-KISSNER-MORAN Corp. (New York) -- Ernest H. Lorch, 58-year-old president of this investment firm, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer, succeeding John A. Moran, also 58, who remains chairman, focusing on major special projects and long-range planning. Robert R. Dyson, 43, executive vice president, was named to the additional post of chief operating officer, succeeding Mr. Lorch.
