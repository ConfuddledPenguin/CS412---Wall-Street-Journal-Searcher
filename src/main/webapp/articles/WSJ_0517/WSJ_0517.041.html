Who's News: MCI Names Akerson, Liebhaber to Positions As Group Executives

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

WASHINGTON -- MCI Communications Corp. named Daniel F. Akerson and Richard T. Liebhaber to new posts of executive vice president and group executive, effective immediately. Both men report to Bert C. Roberts, president and chief operating officer.

Mr. Akerson, who has been executive vice president and chief financial officer, now is responsible for MCI's seven domestic operating divisions and related activities. Previously, each of the division presidents reported directly to Mr. Roberts. Mr. Akerson, 41 years old, will continue as chief financial officer until a successor is named, a spokeswoman said. He was named chief financial officer in 1987. Before that he was president of the company's Atlanta-based Southeast division.

Mr. Liebhaber, 55, continues his responsibility for MCI's technical activities such as engineering, network operations and systems. Mr. Liebhaber worked for International Business Machines Corp. for 31 years in various management positions before joining MCI in 1985.
