Business Brief -- Ohio Edison Co.: Firm Lists Revenue Goals If Rate Increase Is Granted

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

Ohio Edison Co. said in Akron that its revenue would increase 4.7% this year, 4.4% in 1991 and 2.7% in 1992 if the Public Utilities Commission of Ohio approves a recommended rate boost. However, the electric utility said the three-step increase would become null and void if the commission doesn't grant an increase in revenue of at least $198.5 million.

The rate rise recommended by the commission's staff is part of a "conditional stipulation" that the commission is considering for adoption. Ohio Edison said formal hearings on the rate boost have ended and a decision is expected by midsummer.

Ohio Edison had requested a $218 million increase in revenue in August 1989. That request included a two-step increase in revenue of 7.5% this year and a 5.3% increase in 1991.
