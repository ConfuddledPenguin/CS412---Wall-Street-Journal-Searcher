Boeing Gets Order for Jets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

SEATTLE -- Boeing Co., an aerospace concern, said it received an order from Philippine Airlines for four Boeing 737-300 jetliners valued at $140 million and options for four more. The aircraft will be powered by CFM-563B1 engines made by CFM International, a joint company of General Electric Co. and France's Snecma.

Boeing said the Philippine Airlines job brings its total orders for 737s this year to 118 of the jetliners, valued at about $8.3 billion.
