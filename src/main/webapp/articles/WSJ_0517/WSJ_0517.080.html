REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Sitting Tight

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

A Soviet circus troupe has chosen to remain in a motel in Atlanta, rather than go back to the Soviet Union. Members of the Great Circus Bim Bom ran out of money shortly after starting their U.S. tour, but have turned down an offer of tickets home. Businessman Steven Leber says they "would rather be here making no money." We guess this means that even zero dollars are still worth more than perestroika's rubles.
