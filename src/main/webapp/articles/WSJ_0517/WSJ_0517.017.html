DSC Communications Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

DALLAS -- DSC Communications Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire Optilink Corp. for $54 million in either cash or stock. The purchase, which requires the approval of Optilink shareholders, is expected to be completed in the third quarter.

Both Optilink, based in Petaluma, Calif., and DSC are telecommunications-equipment makers.
