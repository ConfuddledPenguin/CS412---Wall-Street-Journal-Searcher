Dividend News: Tacoma Boatbuilding Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

TACOMA BOATBUILDING Co. -- Shareholders approved a 1-for-10 reverse split of the Tacoma, Wash., company's common stock. Tacoma Boatbuilding, which builds and repairs ships for the U.S. Navy and Coast Guard, said the split took effect at the close of business yesterday.
