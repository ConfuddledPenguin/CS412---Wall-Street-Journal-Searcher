Who's News: RJR Nabisco Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

RJR NABISCO Inc. (New York) -- Kenneth E. Glover, 38 years old, vice president and manager at the First Chicago Capital Markets Inc. affiliate of First National Bank of Chicago, was named senior vice president, corporate and government relations, a new post at this food and tobacco company, effective June 1.
