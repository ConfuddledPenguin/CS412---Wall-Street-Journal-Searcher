Who's News: Loral Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

LORAL Corp. -- Harry Emerzian, 51 years old, was named vice president, special operations, a new position, for Loral Systems Manufacturing Co., a Hauppauge, N.Y., unit. Mr. Emerzian, previously group vice president of Microwave, an operating group of this New York-based defense electronics maker, is succeeded by Bernard Leibowitz, 60, who retains his position as president of the Narda division, also based in Hauppauge.
