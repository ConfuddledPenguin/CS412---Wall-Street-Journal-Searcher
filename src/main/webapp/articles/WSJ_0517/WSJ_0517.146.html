Business Brief -- Talley Industries Inc.: Posner Group Boosts Stake To 7.4% of Common Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

A group led by Miami financier Victor Posner said it raised its stake in Talley Industries Inc. to 7.4% of the common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the group, which also includes Chesapeake Insurance Co., said it holds 653,476 Talley Industries shares, assuming the conversion of preferred stock and debentures. The stake includes purchases of 141,700 shares between May 4 and last Friday for $8.625 to $10.25 a share.

In Phoenix, Ariz., a Talley spokesman said that the company hasn't had any contact with the investment group. Talley is a maker of high-technology products.

In an earlier filing, the group said it acquired the shares for investment purposes.
