Boeing Gets Air Force Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Boeing Co. won a $50.9 million Air Force contract for modifying KC-135 aircraft engines.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. got a $21.5 million Navy contract for F/A-18 aircraft parts and for AV-8B aircraft.

Hercules Inc. was given a $12 million Army contract for operating an ammunition plant.
