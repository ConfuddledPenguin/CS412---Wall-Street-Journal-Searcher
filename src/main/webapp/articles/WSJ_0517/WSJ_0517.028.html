Pier 1 Imports Sales Increase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Pier 1 Imports Inc. said its sales in the month ended May 5 rose 24.2%, to $43.3 million from $34.8 million a year earlier. The specialty retailer said that in the month sales at stores open more than a year rose 8% from the year-earlier level.
