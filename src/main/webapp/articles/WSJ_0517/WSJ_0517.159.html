BEI Electronics Sets Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- BEI Electronics Inc. said it plans to purchase as many as 250,000 more of its common shares from time to time on the open market. Earlier this year, BEI, which makes electronics systems, said it would purchase 250,000 of its common shares. So far, it has bought 222,700 shares.
