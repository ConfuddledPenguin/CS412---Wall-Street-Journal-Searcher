Business Brief -- Reading & Bates Corp.: Firm Cuts Long-Term Debt But Fears Bankruptcy Filing

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

Reading & Bates Corp. said in Houston that it concluded an agreement that will significantly reduce its long-term obligations, but that a heavy debt load and other problems may still result in the company's seeking Chapter 11 federal Bankruptcy Code protection. The energy concern, which completed an extensive restructuring last year, said its cash flow forecasts indicate it may have trouble meeting loan obligations due later this year.

Reading & Bates said it used one such loan to help finance an agreement with Dow Chemical Co. under which it gave Dow cash, property and a 3.4% equity stake in exchange for relief from certain financial obligations related to a drilling rig it jointly owned with Dow. Reading & Bates will continue to provide management and operating services for the rig.

The company said it is talking with certain lenders about a possible recapitalization.
