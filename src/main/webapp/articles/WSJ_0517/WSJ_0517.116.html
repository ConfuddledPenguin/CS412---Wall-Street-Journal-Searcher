S&L in Plano, Texas, Shut by Bailout Agency

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

WASHINGTON -- The Resolution Trust Corp. closed down Hallmark Savings Association, Plano, Texas, and paid its depositors. The RTC, the savings-and-loan bailout agency, said it received no acceptable bids for a sale or transfer of the thrift, which has $143.5 million in assets and $196.3 million in deposits.

The payment of insured depositors will begin in a few days, the RTC said, at a place and time to be announced.

The RTC has targeted 141 institutions for sale or liquidation in the April-June quarter. Any of those thrifts that don't receive acceptable bids will be liquidated, agency officials have said.
