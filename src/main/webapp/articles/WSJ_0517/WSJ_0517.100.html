Canada Will Auction Bonds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government said it would auction next Wednesday 1 billion Canadian dollars (US$849.4 million) of two-year government bonds maturing June 6, 1992. Proceeds of the issue will be used to redeem C$550 million of government bonds maturing June 6 and for general purposes of the Canadian government.
