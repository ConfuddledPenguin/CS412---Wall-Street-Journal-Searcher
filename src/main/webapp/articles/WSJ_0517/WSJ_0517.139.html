Business Brief -- Northern Telecom Ltd.: Firm to Spend $100 Million To Build New R&D Site

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

Northern Telecom Ltd. as expected said it will spend $100 million to build a new research and development facility to house its R&D operations in the Ottawa area. The new site will be built next to Northern's main Bell Northern Research Center in Nepean, Ontario, bringing total R&D floor space to 1.3 million square feet. It will be used to house satellite operations that are currently leasing offices in the Ottawa area.

Northern currently employs 4,400 workers in its Ottawa R&D operations and is expected to add several thousand more to the operation over the next 10 years. Its other R&D sites include Raleigh, N.C.; Atlanta; Richardson, Texas; and Maidenhead, outside London.
