Sweden's Industrial Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's industrial production in March fell 0.7% from February and 2% from a year earlier, Statistics Sweden, the national statistics agency, said. The drop followed a 0.2% decline in February and a 1.3% rise in January.

Industrial production in the first quarter fell 1% from a year earlier, but the decline was from a record level. The pulp and steel sectors registered drops in production, while the paper, chemical and engineering industries reported continued high production levels.
