Who's News: Deere & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

DEERE & Co. (Moline, Ill.) -- Robert A. Hanson, 65-year-old chairman of this maker of farm and industrial equipment, will retire, effective June 1, but will remain a director. Deere said a successor will be elected at a regular board meeting scheduled for May 30. Hans W. Becherer, 55, who was president, succeeded Mr. Hanson as chief executive officer last August, as part of a succession plan.
