Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

PEOPLE'S BANK (Bridgeport, Conn.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it lowered its ratings on the commercial paper and short-term debt of this savings bank to not prime from prime-3. The rating concern cited weakening asset quality because of continued deterioration in the Connecticut real estate market. But Moody's noted that the company has a valuable retail franchise in Connecticut, a strong capital position, and low interest-rate sensitivity.
