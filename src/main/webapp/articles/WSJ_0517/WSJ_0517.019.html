Who's News: Franklin Electronic Publishers Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

FRANKLIN ELECTRONIC PUBLISHERS Inc., (Mount Holly, N.J.) -- Peter N. Yianilos was named to the new post of president of this maker of electronic spelling checkers, bibles and dictionaries. Mr. Yianilos, 33 years old, continues as Franklin's chief scientist and a director. He has held those posts since May 1988, when Franklin acquired Proximity Technology Inc., which he founded in 1979. Morton E. David, 53, continues as Franklin's chairman and chief executive officer.
