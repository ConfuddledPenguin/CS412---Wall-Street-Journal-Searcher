French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

PARIS -- French consumer prices in April rose a preliminary 0.4% from March and 3.2% from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute said. The April result follows a definitive monthly gain of 0.3% in March, while the latest year-to-year rate is down from 3.4% in the previous month. Both figures are in line with previous forecasts by economists, who had anticipated a slight acceleration in April as a result of technical pressure on service prices.

The decline in year-to-year price growth, despite the higher monthly result, reflects an unusually high 0.6% increase in consumer prices in April 1989.
