Who's News: Jefferson Smurfit Group PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

JEFFERSON SMURFIT GROUP PLC (Dublin) -- Ray Miezelis, 58 years old, chief executive officer of Smurfit Carton de Venezuela, was designated chief executive officer-elect of this paper and packaging concern's interests in Europe, effective in September, and will assume the post next January, succeeding John Coleman, 62, who will become chairman of Smurfit International, with special responsibilities for Eastern Europe.
