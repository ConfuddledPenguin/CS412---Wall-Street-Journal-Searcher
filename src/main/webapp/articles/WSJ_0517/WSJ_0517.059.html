Dividend News: American International Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

AMERICAN INTERNATIONAL GROUP Inc., New York, declared a 25% common stock dividend, payable July 23 to stock of record June 25. The company also raised the quarterly dividend rate on the new shares to 11 cents, an effective increase of 14.6% on the quarterly payout rate. The increased cash dividend is payable Sept. 21 to stock of record Sept. 7.
