Business Brief -- Inacomp Computer Centers Inc.: Company Completes Sale Of 9.81% Fixed-Rate Notes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

Inacomp Computer Centers Inc., Troy, Mich., said it completed a $30 million private placement of 9.81% of fixed-interest senior notes. Inacomp said proceeds from the debt placement will be used to refinance Inacomp's short-term borrowings under its existing revolving credit facility.

Inacomp's chairman and president, Rick Inatome, said the completion of the debt restructuring "is a very positive move for Inacomp because it will enhance our ability to further invest in Inacomp's strategic growth. We will continue our successful strategy to increase market share through the provision of value-added services, franchising and dealer programs."

The company said the placement was managed by the Capital Market division of NBD Bank and Shea, Paschall & Powell Inc.
