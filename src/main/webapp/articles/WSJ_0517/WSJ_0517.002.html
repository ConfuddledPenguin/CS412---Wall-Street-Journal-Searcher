International Paper Buys Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

PURCHASE, N.Y. -- International Paper Co., a paper and forest products company, said it completed the purchase of closely held Nevamar Corp., an Odenton, Md., producer of laminates for counter tops and other surfaces. Terms weren't disclosed. Nevamar had 1989 sales of $140 million.
