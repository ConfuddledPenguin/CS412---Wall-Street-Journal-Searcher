Business Brief -- Data General Corp.: Four Models Are Unveiled Of Aviion Workstation Line

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

Data General Corp. unveiled four new models of its Aviion workstation family that are based on Motorola Inc.'s 88000 RISC microprocessor. Data General, Westboro, Mass., said its new AV-200 workstation, with a base price of $5,950, runs at 17 million instructions per second, or MIPS, a common industry measure of performance. The company said its price of $350 per MIPS is lower than that of RISC workstations of other computer makers.

Data General, a maker of midrange systems, has been going through a difficult period as sales of its proprietary midrange computers slow down. It is counting on the Aviion series, introduced last year, to lead to a turnaround.

Data General also unveiled the $12,995 AV 3200 server system, which can work as the center of a network of workstations or personal computers. It also introduced two faster, more expensive servers.
