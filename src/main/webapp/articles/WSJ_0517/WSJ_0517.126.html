Business Brief -- Borden Inc.: Two Plants Are to Be Built For a Total of $70 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

Borden Inc., a New York-based producer of foods, nonfood consumer products, and packaging and industrial products, said it plans to build two plants for a total of $70 million to produce pasta and food-service products. The $50 million pasta plant in St. Louis will have the capacity to produce 250 million pounds a year. Production there is to begin in late 1991, with employment expected to exceed 200.

The $20 million food-service facililty in Jackson, Miss., will process or package individual-portion products, as well as bulk-sized oil-based items, with operations scheduled to begin in the 1990 fourth quarter. The plant will have about 400 employees when fully operational.

Borden had 1989 world-wide sales of $7.6 billion.
