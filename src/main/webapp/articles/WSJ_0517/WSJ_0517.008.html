French GDP Posts Growth

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

PARIS -- French gross domestic product grew 1% in the fourth quarter, following a 0.6% rate of expansion in the third quarter, the National Statistics Institute said. For all of 1989, GDP was up 4%, higher than the 3.7% growth rate previously estimated by Economy Minister Pierre Beregovoy. The latest figures revise the GDP numbers published in February, which showed a growth rate of 1.1% in the fourth quarter and 0.4% in the third quarter. The statistics agency said the latest figures take into account definitive results for fourth-quarter industrial production, which weren't available in February.

Industrial production grew 0.6% in the fourth quarter, down from the February estimate of 1.1%. Household consumption was revised to an increase of 0.7% from 0.4%.
