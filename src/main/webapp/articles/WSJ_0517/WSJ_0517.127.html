Who's News: Eeco Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/17/90

EECO Inc. (Santa Ana, Calif.) -- George B. DeHuff III was elected president and chief executive officer of this maker of electronic products that earlier this month filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. Mr. DeHuff, 36 years old and who was president of the company's switch division, succeeds Robert B. Bonney, 75, who will remain chairman.
