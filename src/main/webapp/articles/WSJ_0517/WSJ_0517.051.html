Lincoln National Redemptions

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

FORT WAYNE, Ind. -- Lincoln National Corp., an insurer, said it will redeem on June 21 the 500 shares of its short-term auction rate, or Star, Series C preferred stock and on June 28 the 500 shares of its Star preferred Series D preferred. The redemption price for each series is $100,000 a share, plus accumulated dividends. The dividends are $926.92 a share for Series C and $920.11 a share for Series D. All dividends will stop accruing on the redemption dates.
