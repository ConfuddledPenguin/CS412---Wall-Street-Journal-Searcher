Du Pont Sells Laserscope Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Laserscope said that Du Pont Co. has sold a major portion of its common stock ownership in the company. Laserscope, which makes laser equipment used in surgery, said Du Pont, the huge Wilmington, Del.-based chemical concern, acquired a minority equity position in the company in 1986. Currently two Du Pont executives serve on Laserscope's board and both will continue to do so, the company said.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Laserscope closed at $20.125, down 50 cents.
