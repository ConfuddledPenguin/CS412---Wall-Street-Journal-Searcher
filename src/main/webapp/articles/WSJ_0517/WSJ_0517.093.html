Smith Laboratories Inc. Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/17/90

SAN DIEGO -- Smith Laboratories Inc. said that following shareholder approval it completed its merger with Columbia Hospital Corp., a closely held acute-care hospital concern controlled by former Bass family financial adviser Richard Rainwater and Richard Scott, Columbia's chairman. The combination of Smith, a San Diego-based maker of medical equipment, and Columbia Hospital involves a tax-free swap of shares of Smith Laboratories, Columbia Hospital, and seven Columbia Hospital affiliates, for shares of Columbia Hospital Corp., a new firm.
