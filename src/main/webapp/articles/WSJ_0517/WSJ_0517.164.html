Old-Time Options

In this age of serial monogamy and

Parallel polygamy

And mutually consenting adult

Menage a trigamy,

I'd like to raise a gentle voice

For those bygone days of plainer choice,

Like a little simple bigamy.

-- Peter Friedman.

---

Budget Culture

In the arts, my taste runs to cheap thrills,

Of the kind critics long have deplored,

Not for reasons of taste, but simply because

They're all that I can afford.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

---

Time Study

We heard it when we were young,

Then say it at a later stage --

And that is the case history

Of "when I was your age . . ."

-- Mimi Kay.
