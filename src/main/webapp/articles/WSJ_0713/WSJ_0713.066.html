Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 224,171 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, down 20% from 280,137 tons in the preceeding week and down 27% from 306,731 tons a year earlier, said Statistics Canada, a government agency. The cumulative total in 1990 was 7,493,148 tons, down 9.5% from 8,279,327 tons a year ago.
