Who's News: Fortune Financial Elects Torell Chairman And Mason as Chief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

CLEARWATER, Fla. -- Fortune Financial Group Inc. elected John R. Torell III chairman of the thrift holding company. Mr. Torell, 50 years old, has been an associate with Private Capital Partners Inc., a merchant banking company in New York.

Rausey W. Mason, 52, was elected president and chief executive officer of Fortune Financial. He has served as executive vice president of the company. Both individuals succeed John A. Moran, 57, who resigned to obtain regulatory approval for his company to increase its ownership of Fortune Financial's stock. Mr. Moran is chairman of New York-based Dyson Kissner-Moran Corp., a closely held investment company that owns 9.97% of the holding company's stock.

The Office of Thrift Supervision has stipulated that Mr. Moran must relinquish his duties as chairman, president and chief executive officer in order to obtain permission to purchase up to 20% of Fortune Financial's 4,350,000 shares outstanding. Mr. Moran will remain as a director of both Fortune Financial Group and its subsidiary, Fortune Savings Bank.
