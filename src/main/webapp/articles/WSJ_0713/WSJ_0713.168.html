Management Co. Interest

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

LOS ANGELES -- Management Co. Entertainment Group said it will delay a semiannual interest payment of about $1.4 million due Sunday on convertible subordinated debentures due 2009. The entertainment concern is currently in discussions with G.E. Capital, a unit of General Electric Corp., about extending and restructuring $72.5 million of bridge and credit loans that mature tomorrow. G.E. Capital took steps to block Management Co.'s interest payment to bondholders.

On Tuesday, G.E. Capital advanced the company about $3 million to meet certain obligations of Management Co.'s United Kingdom subsidiary.

Management Co. is also negotiating with Virgin Group Ltd., a creditor and the largest shareholder of the company, about restructuring and extending its debt.
