Turner Corp. Unit Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

NEW YORK -- Turner Corp. said its Turner Construction Co. unit won seven construction contracts in the San Francisco Bay area totaling $110 million in June. It said the contracts were a result of the unit's expansion efforts in San Jose. For all of last year, Turner, the nation's largest general builder, completed $3.6 billion of work.
