Acme United's U.K. Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

FAIRFIELD, Conn. -- Acme United Corp., a producer of surgical equipment and household shears, scissors and rulers, said it paid $800,000 for the business and facilities of Homeric Ltd., a scissors manufacturer in Sheffield, England. Acme said it is strengthening its European operations. The company purchased Emil Schlemper G.m.b.H., a German scissors manufacturer, in December.
