Titan Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

SAN DIEGO -- Titan Corp., which makes computer products and provides services for defense programs, said it completed the acquisition of M/A-Com Government Systems Inc. from M/A-Com Inc. of Burlington, Mass. Terms weren't disclosed. M/A-Com Government Systems, a supplier of earth-based satellite communications equipment to the U.S. government, with about $40 million in annual revenue, was renamed Titan Linkabit Corp.
