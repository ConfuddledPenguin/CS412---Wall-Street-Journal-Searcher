Who's News: Diasonics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

DIASONICS Inc. (Milpitas, Calif.) -- Roderick A. Young, 46 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this maker of medical-diagnostics equipment. Mr. Young succeeds Ruediger Naumann-Etienne, 43, who left the company to form an international investment advisory firm. Mr. Naumann-Etienne will remain a member of the Diasonics board. Mr. Young was president of Toshiba America MRI Inc., the former Diasonics MRI division that Toshiba Corp.'s U.S. unit acquired last year. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

RODERICK A. YOUNG was named president and chief operating officer of Diasonics Inc. Mr. Young's title was misstated in Friday's edition.

(WSJ July 16, 1990)
