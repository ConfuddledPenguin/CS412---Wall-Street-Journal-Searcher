Business Brief -- Nicron Resources Ltd.: Aztec Mining Co. of Sydney Bids for All of Mining Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

Aztec Mining Co. Ltd. of Sydney, Australia, which is 49.4% owned by U.S. metals and energy company Amax Inc., yesterday said it is bidding for all of Nicron Resources Ltd., an Australian mining company. Aztec said in a statement it already owns 19.99% of Nicron Resources.

Aztec is offering shareholders four Aztec shares for every five shares in Nicron Resources. The offer, which isn't subject to a minimum acceptance condition. requires regulatory approval.

If the takeover offer is successful, Amax's shareholding in Aztec will be diluted to 33.2%, the company said.

Aztec said it holds naturalized status in terms of Australia's foreign investment policy.

Aztec has 62% of Bounty Gold Mine and 20% of the Scuddles Zinc Mine, both in Western Australia.

Aztec also has interests in a number of other base metal and gold exploration projects, including 24% of the Chessy Copper zinc project in France.
