SANTA MONICA, Calif. -- National Medical Enterprises Inc. said it plans to spend about $37.2 million to expand three psychiatric hospitals and to build two new hospitals and a new alcohol and substance-abuse treatment center. National Medical, a health-care services concern, said it is constructing new 80-bed psychiatric hospitals in Indianapolis and in Topeka, Kan. The new 44-bed alcohol and substance abuse hospital is in Seattle.
