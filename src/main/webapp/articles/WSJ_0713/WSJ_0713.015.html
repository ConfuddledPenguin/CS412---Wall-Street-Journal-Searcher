Ross Stores Inc.'s Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

NEWARK, Calif. -- Ross Stores Inc., which operates a national chain of retail apparel stores, said sales for the five weeks ended last Saturday rose 11% to $73 million from $66 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year were flat. The company had 161 stores in operation at the end of the latest period, up from 143 a year earlier. For the five months ended last Saturday, sales rose 7.4% to $304 million from $283 million a year earlier. Comparable-store sales fell 3%.
