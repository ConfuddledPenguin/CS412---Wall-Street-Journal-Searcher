Spain's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

MADRID -- Spain's consumer price index in June rose 0.3% from May, bringing the year-to-year inflation rate to 6.5% last month, down from 6.8% in May, the National Statistics Institute said. In May, consumer prices were unchanged from April. Prices excluding volatile food prices edged up 0.4% in June from the previous month.
