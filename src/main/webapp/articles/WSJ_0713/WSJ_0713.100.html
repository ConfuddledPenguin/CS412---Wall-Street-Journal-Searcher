Mitsubishi Mining Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

TOKYO -- Mitsubishi Mining & Cement Co. has reached an agreement to buy 100% of the shares of Dillingham Construction Guam Ltd. of the U.S., a Mitsubishi spokesman said Thursday. The purchase price wasn't disclosed. Mitsubishi's unit in the U.S., Mitsubishi Cement Development Corp., is expected to purchase all the shares of Dillingham Construction Guam from the parent company, Dillingham Construction Corp., Pleasanton, Calif., by the end of this month.

Mitsubishi expects future demand for construction materials in Guam and Saipan to grow as the growth of the tourism industry there boosts construction of resort facilities.

Dillingham Construction Guam has construction materials factories on the islands, including four concrete plants. The company had sales of $40 million in 1989.
