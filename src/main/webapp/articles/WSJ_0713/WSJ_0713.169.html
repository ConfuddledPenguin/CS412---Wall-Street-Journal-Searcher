Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Airborne Freight Corp., proposed offering of 2.5 million shares of common stock, via a group led by Goldman, Sachs & Co.

American Plastics Recycling Group Ltd., initial offering of 900,000 common shares, via Paragon Capital Corp.

Canada's Province of Quebec, shelf offering of up to $1,249,270,000 of debt securities and warrants to purchase debt securities.

Fleet/Norstar Financial Group Inc., shelf offering of up to $700 million of senior or subordinated debt securities and warrants to purchase senior or subordinarted debt securities, via Salomon Brothers Inc., Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and others.

Mountain States Telephone & Telegraph Co., a unit of U S West Inc., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.

Radius Inc., initial offering of three million common shares, of which two million will be sold by Radius and one million by holders, via Morgan Stanley & Co. Inc. and Hambrecht & Quist Inc.

Rocky Mountain Helicopters Inc., initial offering of 1.5 million common shares, of which 1.2 million will be sold by the company and 300,000 shares by holders, via Blunt Ellis & Loewi Inc.

Sauer Inc., initial offering of four million common shares, of which one million is to be sold in the U.S., with 700,000 shares sold by the company and 300,000 by holders, via Merrill Lynch and BV Capital Markets Inc.
