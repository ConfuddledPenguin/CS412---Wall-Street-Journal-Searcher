International Brief -- Rank Organisation PLC: Pretax Profit Climbs 24%; Firm Sees Problems Ahead

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

Rank Organisation PLC, a major U.K. communications and entertainment concern, said pretax profit for the half year to May 12 increased 24%, though the company forecast growing difficulties stemming from poor economic conditions in the U.K. and the U.S. Pretax profit increased to #137.6 million ($247.7 million) from #111 million. Market forecasts had ranged between #135 million and #138 million. Sales increased 17% to #522.2 million from #447.4 million.

Pretax profit from Rank Xerox, a joint venture between Rank and Xerox Corp. of the U.S., rose 2.7% to #80 million from #77.9 million. Fuji Xerox, located in Southeast Asia, had a decline in profit to Rank caused by higher investment in research and the development of products plus increases in operating costs, said Rank.

The company said its proposed acquisition of Mecca Leisure, a rival U.K. leisure concern, is "a major step forward in Rank's leisure activities in Europe and the U.S.," which should be beneficial to all shareholders.
