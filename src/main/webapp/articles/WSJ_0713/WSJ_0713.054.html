Who's News: Transamerica Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

TRANSAMERICA Corp. (San Francisco) -- Richard H. Finn, 55 years old, was named chief executive officer of this financial services concern's Transamerica Finance Group Inc. unit. Mr. Finn will also remain president of the unit. Mr. Finn succeeds Arthur E. Van Leuven, 64, who will remain Transamerica Finance's chairman until he retires Nov. 30.
