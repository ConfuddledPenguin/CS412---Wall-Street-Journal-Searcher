Who's News: Lockheed Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

LOCKHEED Corp. (Calabasas, Calif.) -- Gary Bowring was named president of a newly formed unit, Lockheed Commercial Aircraft Center Inc., which will modify and maintain Boeing 747 aircraft. The new subsidiary will be based in San Bernardino, Calif. Mr. Bowring joined Lockheed from the Dee Howard division of Aeritalia in San Antonio, Texas, where he was general manager and chief operating officer.
