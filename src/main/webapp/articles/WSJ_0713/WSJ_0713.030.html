Who's News: Kinark Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

KINARK Corp. (Tulsa, Okla.) -- Harry D. Jones, 53-year-old president, and Paul R. Chastain, 55, former executive vice president and deputy chief executive officer of this chemical and steel galvanizing concern, were named co-chairman and co-chief executive officer, succeeding Robert G. Walker, 65, who retired. Mr. Walker, the company's largest stockholder, will continue as an outside board member.
