Vulcan Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

CINCINNATI -- Vulcan International Corp. said it has formed a joint venture with Brunswick Bowling and Billiard Corp., a unit of Skokie, Ill.-based Brunswick Corp., to manufacture bowling pins. The joint venture will allow Vulcan to expand its Antigo, Wis., bowling pin plant to increase production to three times its current capacity, the company said.

Vulcan said Brunswick's bowling pin plant in Muskegon, Mich. will be closed once the Antigo plant is expanded.

Vulcan makes shoe supplies and bowling pins.
