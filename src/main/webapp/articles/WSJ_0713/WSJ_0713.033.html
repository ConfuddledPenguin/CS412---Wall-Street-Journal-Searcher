Who's News: Alberta Natural Gas Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

ALBERTA NATURAL GAS CO. (Calgary, Alberta) -- Norman E. Wagner, the 55-year-old chairman of this natural gas pipeline concern, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer. Mr. Wagner, formerly president of the University of Calgary, and a company director since 1986, succeeds Douglas R. Fenton. Mr. Fenton resigned as president and chief executive, effective immediately. A company spokesman wouldn't elaborate, and Mr. Fenton wasn't immediately available. Separately, Mr. Fenton also resigned as president and chief executive of a related concern, Alberta & Southern Gas Co., a gas marketing company. He is succeeded by Donald McMorland, formerly Alberta & Southern's executive vice-president and chief operating officer. Alberta Natural Gas and Alberta & Southern are both units of San Fransisco-based Pacific Gas & Electric Co.
