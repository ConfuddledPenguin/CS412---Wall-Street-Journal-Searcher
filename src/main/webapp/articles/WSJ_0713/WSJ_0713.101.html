Financing Business: Cincinnati Gas & Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

CINCINNATI GAS & ELECTRIC Co. said it has accepted the offer of Morgan Stanley & Co. and Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. for the sale of 500,000 shares of its cumulative preferred, 9.15% series, at $100 each. The Cincinnati utility said the stock is subject to mandatory redemption requirements of 25,000 shares a year beginning July 1, 1996. Proceeds will be used to repay debt incurred primarily from its construction program.
