Who's News: MBIA Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

MBIA Inc. (Armonk, N.Y.) -- Freda S. Johnson was named a director of this insurer of municipal securities, expanding its board to 12 members. Ms. Johnson, 43 years old, currently a public finance consultant, was former head of the public finance department for Moody's Investors Service Inc., the New York-based rating agency.
