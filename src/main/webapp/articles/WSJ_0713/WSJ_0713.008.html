Financing Business: Lazare Kaplan International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

LAZARE KAPLAN INTERNATIONAL Inc. said it plans a public offering of one million common shares, with proceeds to be used to pay down short-term debt. The New York-based distributor of diamonds and jewelry said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission and expects the offering to begin in mid-August. It currently has about 3.1 million shares outstanding and short-term debt of about $22.5 million. Lazare said the offering will be managed by Wertheim Schroder & Co.
