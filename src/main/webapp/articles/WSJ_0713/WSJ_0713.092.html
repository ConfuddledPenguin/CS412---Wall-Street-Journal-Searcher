Gantos's June Sales Rose 13%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc. said its June sales rose 13% to $22.8 million from $20.2 a year earlier. The company's comparable-store sales, covering stores open at least a year, were level with June 1989. For the 22-week period ending July 7, the women's apparel retailer's sales rose 18% to $105 million from $89.3 million. The comparable store sales in the 22-week period increased 3%.
