Who's News: Quebec Fund Agency Picks Savard for 2 Posts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

MONTREAL -- Caisse de Depot et Placement, the Quebec government's pension-fund agency, named Guy Savard executive vice president and chief operating officer, a new post. The appointment was expected. As reported, Mr. Savard was named a director May 30. Mr. Savard will be responsible for investment operations at Caisse de Depot, which manages close to 40 billion Canadian dollars ($34.52 billion) of provincial pension and insurance funds.

Mr. Savard reports to Jean-Claude Delorme, chairman and general manager since July 1. As reported, Mr. Delorme succeeded Jean Campeau, who becomes chairman of Domtar Inc., a Montreal forest products concern, this fall.

Mr. Savard was vice president of Raymond, Chabot, Martin, Pare, an accounting firm.
