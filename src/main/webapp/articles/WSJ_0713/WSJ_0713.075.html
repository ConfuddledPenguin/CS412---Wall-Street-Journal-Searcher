Business Brief -- MNC Financial Inc.: Quarterly Loss to Result From Loan Reserves Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

MNC Financial Inc., a Baltimore-based financial services company, said it expects to post a second-quarter loss of about $75 million, or 90 cents a fully diluted share, primarily because of a 71% increase in loan-loss reserves, and a write-off. In the year-earlier quarter, MNC earned $66.6 million, or 78 cents a fully diluted share.

The company said it completed a review of its loan portfolios and expects to increase second-quarter loan-loss reserves by about $230 million, bringing total reserves to about $550 million. MNC also said it plans to report a $94 million write-off for nonperforming loans in the quarter.
