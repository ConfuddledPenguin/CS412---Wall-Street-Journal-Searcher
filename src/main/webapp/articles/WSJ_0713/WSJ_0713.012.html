Initial State Jobless Claims Declined in Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits fell to a seasonally adjusted 350,000 the week ended June 30, from 365,000 the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended June 23 rose to a seasonally adjusted 2,440,000, or 2.3% of those covered by unemployment insurance, from 2,439,000 the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate was also 2.3%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended June 23 rose to 2,234,400, from 2,218,200 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
