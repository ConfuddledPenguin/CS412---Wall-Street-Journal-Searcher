Sidewalk Confession

I love to read the menus on

The windows from outside;

My friends indulge me every time

And take it in their stride.

I murmur, "Lovely `l'entre-cote',"

Point "Caviar's on ice,"

"Salad Nicoise]" But to myself,

I'm checking out the price.

-- Ralph Shaffer.

Human Percentages

Regarding interest, most of us

Are ambivalence prone;

We favor high rates for our savings,

But lower rates for a loan.

-- George O. Ludcke.
