Business Brief -- Kumagai Gumi (Hong Kong) Ltd.: Earnings for Fiscal Half Fell 41% to $6 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

Kumagai Gumi (Hong Kong) Ltd. said its profit for the half ended March 31 slumped 41% to 47 million Hong Kong dollars (US$6 million) from HK$79 million a year earlier. Earnings per share of the local arm of the Japanese construction giant dropped 41% to 15.9 Hong Kong cents from 27.1 cents. The company didn't provide a reason for the decline in profit, but predicted an improvement in the second half.

Revenue grew 3.1% to HK$1.48 billion from HK$1.43 billion.

Kumagai's board proposed an interim dividend of 10 Hong Kong cents a share, down 33% from 15 Hong Kong cents.
