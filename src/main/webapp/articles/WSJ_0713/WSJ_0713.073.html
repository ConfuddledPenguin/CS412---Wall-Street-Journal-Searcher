Letters to the Editor: Jack Murtha: He's Our Guy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

Your May 22 editorial "Besieged Incumbents" contained a misleading piece about Rep. Jack Murtha, Democrat of Pennsylvania. While technically correct that Mr. Murtha got only 51% of the votes cast in a three-man race, he handily defeated his nearest opponent 29,239 to 24,530 -- a comfortable margin. It was closer than it should have been, but it was not close. Robert Burkley got only 43%, and an 8% margin in a presidential election would be considered a landslide. The Johnstown, Pa., newspaper's new editor, while a friend and a man I admire, was wrong throughout the primary campaign in his perception of Mr. Murtha and what he has done for his district. The congressman's contributions to his district are and have been enormous.

While the newspaper in the end did endorse Mr. Murtha, that was too late to help much -- the damage had been done.

As a Republican, I suggest our district is indeed fortunate to have a man of Jack Murtha's quality to represent us. There are none in Congress better informed on the Defense Department; the nation is well served to have this winner of the Purple Heart -- with two volunteer combat tours as a Marine in Vietnam as well as his Korean War service -- as chairman of the Subcommittee on Defense Appropriations.

John E. Sheehan

Former Member

Board of Governors

Federal Reserve Board

Johnstown, Pa.
