French Crude Steel Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

PARIS -- French companies produced 1,713,000 metric tons of crude steel in June, up 1.7% from a year earlier, the National Steel Manufacturers' Association said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. The June result brings French output for the first half of this year to 9,947,000 metric tons, down 3.7% from a year earlier.
