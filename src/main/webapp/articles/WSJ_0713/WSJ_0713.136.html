Business Brief -- Occidental Petroleum Corp.: Interest in Acquiring Line Of Enimont, ICI Confirmed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

Occidental Petroleum Corp., Los Angeles, confirmed reports that it has "had an interest" in acquiring European Vinyls Corp., a jointly owned plastics business of Imperial Chemical Industries PLC of Britain and Enimont S.p.A. of Italy. Occidental Petroleum said that its Occidental Chemical Corp. unit has held discussions on the subject but that these talks haven't reached the level of negotiations. The company also said the values of any proposed transactions haven't been discussed. Enimont and Imperial Chemical denied a report in the Financial Times that they are discussing the sale of their jointly owned plastics business, one of the world's largest, for as much as $1.8 billion.

Enimont said it "denies having any discussion or negotiation to cede its interests in {European Vinyls}. Therefore, the reports of a possible cessation to Occidental Petroleum are without foundation." Separately, Imperial Chemical said it "is not involved in any discussions over a possible sale of its . . . interest in European Vinyls Corp."
