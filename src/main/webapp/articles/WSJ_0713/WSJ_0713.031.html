Who's News: Exxon Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

EXXON Corp. (New York) -- M.E. Gillis was named vice president, human resources, at this oil company, effective Sept. 1, succeeding T.H. Tiedemann Jr., who is to retire. Mr. Gillis was previously president of Exxon Chemical Americas in Houston, and senior vice president of Exxon Chemical Co. in Darien, Conn.
