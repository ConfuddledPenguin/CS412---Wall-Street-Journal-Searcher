Who's News: CareerCom's Rhodes Gets Executive Posts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

MIDDLETOWN, Pa. -- CareerCom Corp. said Gary W. Rhodes was elected executive vice president and chief operating officer. Mr. Rhodes, 42 years old, a former president and chief operating officer of the vocational training concern who left it in 1988 for personal reasons, rejoined it in February as president of the international division.

As chief operating officer, he fills a vacancy created in January, when Robert A. Smith was named chief executive officer. Mr. Rhodes, who remains head of the international division, also was elected a director.

CareerCom also said Darrell M. Trent, 52, and P. Richard Frieder, 56, were elected to the board. Mr. Trent is chairman and chief executive of Acton Development Co. and a senior fellow at Stanford University's Hoover Institute. Mr. Frieder is president and owner of KSD Industries Inc.

Mr. Frieder succeeds William R. Yocom, 64, who retired. The addition of the other two directors expands board membership to eight.
