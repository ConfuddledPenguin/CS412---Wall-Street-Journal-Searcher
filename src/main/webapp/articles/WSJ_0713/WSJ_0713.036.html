Lukens to Post Increase in Net

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/13/90

COATESVILLE, Pa. -- Lukens Inc., a maker of steel plates and other industrial products, said its net income for the second quarter will exceed the $1.06 a share a year earlier and the $1.26 a share in the 1990 first quarter. In the year-ago quarter, Lukens earned $8.9 million on sales of $163 million; the results included a pretax gain of $900,000 from the sale of a subsidiary. In the 1990 first quarter, the company earned $10.9 million on sales of $170.6 million.

Lukens expects to report its second-quarter results Tuesday.
