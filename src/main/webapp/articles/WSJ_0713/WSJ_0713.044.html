Cascades Acquires Rest of Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

KINGSEY FALLS, Quebec -- Cascades Inc., a pulp and paper concern, said it acquired the 50% of Cascades P.S.H. Inc. that it didn't already own from the Kaysersberg S.A. subsidiary of Beghin-Say of France. Terms weren't disclosed. Cascades said Cascades P.S.H. has annual sales of about 20 million Canadian dollars (US$17.3 million) and makes sanitary napkins and adult diapers, or disposable briefs, for the U.S. and Canadian markets.

A spokesman for Cascades said it intends to develop the unit's adult diaper business and may seek to acquire a maker of private-label adult diapers. He wouldn't elaborate.
