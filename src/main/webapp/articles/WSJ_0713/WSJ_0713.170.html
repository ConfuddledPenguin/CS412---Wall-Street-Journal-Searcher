Denning Mobile Robotics Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

WILMINGTON, Mass. -- Denning Mobile Robotics Inc. said it agreed with closely held Windsor Industries Inc., Englewood, Colo., to complete development of a self-navigating robot scrubber for cleaning commercial buildings. Denning also said Windsor will fund development of a vacuuming robot. It said Windsor could buy thousands of the vehicles over the next five years. An official of Windsor said it hopes to start marketing the robot scrubbers next year. The companies declined to disclose financial details.
