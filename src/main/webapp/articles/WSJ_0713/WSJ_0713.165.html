National Intergroup's Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/13/90

PITTSBURGH -- National Intergroup Inc. said it extended to July 31 its tender offer for the cumulative convertible preference units of Permian Partners Limited Partnership. The offer, for $3.85 a unit, was set to expire last Wednesday.

National Intergroup is a steel, retail and wholesale drug distribution concern. Permian Partners is a limited partnership involved in oil and gas gathering and marketing.
