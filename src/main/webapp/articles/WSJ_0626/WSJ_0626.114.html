Business Brief -- Hampton Utilities Trust: Two Ohio Investment Firms Hold Stake Totaling 17.9%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

First Enterprise Group Inc. and Investment Strategies Inc. said they have acquired a 17.9% stake in Hampton Utilities Trust and may consider seeking control of the San Mateo, Calif., trust. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the Pepper Pike, Ohio, investment firms, said they own on behalf of themselves and clients 185,000 shares, all bought from June 14 to June 20 for $9.375 to $9.75 each.

They said they will continue to evaluate their holdings with a view to maximizing their investment, the filing said.
