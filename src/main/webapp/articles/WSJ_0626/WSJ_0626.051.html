II-VI Inc. Fourth Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

SAXONBURG, Pa. -- II-VI Inc. said it expects fourth-quarter revenue and bookings to be close to last year's levels because of shortfalls in military and domestic commercial orders. In the year-ago fourth quarter, II-VI reported revenue of $3.6 million, bookings of $3.4 million and net income of $388,000.

The maker of precision optical and electro-optical devices also announced a 7% cut in its Saxonburg manufacturing facility's workforce of 230.
