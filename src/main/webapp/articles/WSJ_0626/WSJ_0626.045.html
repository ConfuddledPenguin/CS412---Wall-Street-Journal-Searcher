Credit Suisse Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

NEW YORK -- Credit Suisse said it acquired an 80% stake in BEA Associates Inc., an asset manager here handling a $10 billion portfolio. Terms weren't disclosed.

The move expands the asset-management operations at Credit Suisse, a banking unit of CS Holding, Zurich. William Wirth, a member of the executive board at Credit Suisse, becomes chairman of BEA Associates.
