Business Brief -- PPG Industries Inc.: Flat Glass Products Venture In Japan Set With C. Itoh

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

PPG Industries Inc., a Pittsburgh-based maker of glass, chemicals and coatings, said it will enter into a joint venture with C. Itoh & Co., a Japanese trading house, to sell and distribute PPG's flat glass products in Japan. PPG will own 51% of the new company, PPG-CI Co. of Tokyo. It wouldn't disclose the size of the project, which is intended to benefit from growing demand for glass by Japan's construction and auto industries.

PPG already has a joint venture with Asahi-Penn Chemical Co. of Chiba, Japan, to sell chemical products.
