Elcor Expects Deficit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

DALLAS -- Elcor Corp., citing the adverse effects of unusually heavy rains in the Southwest, said it expects to report a loss of about $700,000 for the fourth quarter, ending Saturday. The maker of roofing and industrial products, which reported a $6 million loss in the year-ago fourth quarter, said the rains temporarily reduced roofing installations in the area, which resulted in lower-than-expected shipments of the company's laminated glass fiber asphalt shingles.
