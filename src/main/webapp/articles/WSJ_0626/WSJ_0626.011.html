Convex Computer Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

RICHARDSON, Texas -- Convex Computer Corp. said it completed an offering of two million common shares at $18 each. In addition, the underwriters exercised an option to purchse 300,000 additional shares to cover overallotments. The supplier of supercomputers said proceeds will be used for general operating expenses. Goldman, Sachs & Co. was the lead underwriter.
