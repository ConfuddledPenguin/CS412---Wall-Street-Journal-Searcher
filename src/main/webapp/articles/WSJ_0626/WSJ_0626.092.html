Raw-Steel Production Increased 1% in Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 1% last week to 1,893,000 tons from 1,875,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 0.3% from the 1,887,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 84.2% of its capability last week, compared with 83.4% the previous week and 85.3% a year ago.
