Business Brief -- United Technologies Corp.: Unit Gets Orders, Options For Up to 27 Jet Engines

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

Pratt & Whitney, a unit of United Technologies Corp., said it received orders and options valued at $78 million for as many as 27 jet engines from two leasing companies. The JT8D-200 engines will power aircraft in McDonnell Douglas Corp.'s MD-80 series. Pratt & Whitney said Polaris Aircraft Leasing Corp., San Francisco, has three firm orders for MD-83 twin-jet aircraft, for delivery this year, and options for three more in 1993. The engine order is valued at $36 million if the options are exercised.

Charles F. Willis Co., Sausalito, Calif., ordered five engines and placed options on 10 more to support current operators of MD-80 planes. The value is $42 million if options are exercised. United Technologies, based in Hartford, Conn., is an aerospace and industrial products company.
