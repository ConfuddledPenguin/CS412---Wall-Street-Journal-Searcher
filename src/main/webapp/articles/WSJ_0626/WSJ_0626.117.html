International Brief -- Sankyo Co.: Consolidated Pretax Profit Rose 1.4% for Fiscal Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

Sankyo Co., a major Japanese pharmaceutical maker, said its consolidated pretax profit for the year ended March 31 rose 1.4%, to 36.16 billion yen ($233.4 million) from 35.67 billion yen a year earlier. Net income declined 7.9% to 13.02 billion yen from 14.13 billion yen, after paying a larger amount of taxes due to extraordinary profits caused by the disposition of reserves for retirement allowances. As a result, per-share net fell to 36.41 yen from 39.89 yen.

Sales were down slightly at 425.63 billion yen from 426.04 billion yen.

Sales of agrichemicals dropped 10.9% to 39.17 billion yen, affected by the government's continuing policy to cut the number of rice fields.

Sales of pharmaceuticals posted a 1.2% increase, to 282.22 billion yen, while those of foodstuffs inched up 1.6% to 48.79 billion yen.

Sankyo predicted consolidated pretax profit of 40 billion yen for next year, with expected net income of 18 billion yen. Sales were projected at 440 billion yen.
