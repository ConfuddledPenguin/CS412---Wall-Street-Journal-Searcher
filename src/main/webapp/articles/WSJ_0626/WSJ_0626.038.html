Who's News: Polifly Picks Paparatto For President and Chief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

NEW MILFORD, N.J. -- Polifly Financial Corp., holding company for Polifly Savings & Loan Association, named 42-year-old Joseph S. Paparatto president, chief executive officer and a director. Mr. Paparatto succeeds Gregg B. Van Dam, who resigned the positions in March under a consent agreement that the thrift signed with the federal Office of Thrift Supervision. The company was found out of compliance with the requirements of the thrift-reform law Congress passed in August.

Since Mr. Van Dam resigned, Polifly Chairman Frederick T. Muller, 74, had been acting chief executive. The board has seven members.

Mr. Paparatto had been president and chief executive of closely held Raritan Valley Savings/Hansen Savings Bank of East Brunswick, N.J.
