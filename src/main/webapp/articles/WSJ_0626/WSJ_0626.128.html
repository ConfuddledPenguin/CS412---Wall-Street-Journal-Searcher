Business Brief -- Material Sciences Corp.: Firm Hires William Blair To Study Sale of Company

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

Material Sciences Corp. said it hired William Blair & Co., a Chicago-based investment banking firm, to explore the possible sale of the company. The move follows an April announcement by the Elk Grove Village, Ill., maker of coatings and laminates that its board was reviewing "various strategic and financial alternatives" for the company. At the time, the company said it was "very concerned" about what it considered the market's under-valuation of the company.

In the American Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Material Sciences closed at $13.75, up $3.375. The company has 4.9 million shares outstanding.
