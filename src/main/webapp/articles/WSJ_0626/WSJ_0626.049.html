Tandy Shareholder Rights Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- Tandy Corp. said its board approved amendments to the company's shareholder rights plan that would allow individual holders to purchase additional shares at a reduced price if an individual investor accumulates 15% of the company's stock. Previously, the plan could be triggered if an investor acquired a 25% or greater stake without board approval. The electronics retailer and manufacturer said the changes weren't adopted in response to any specific effort to acquire control of the company.
