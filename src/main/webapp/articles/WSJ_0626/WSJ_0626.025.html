Axa Midi Expands Into Asia

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

PARIS -- Axa Midi Assurances S.A., the insurance unit of Cie. du Midi S.A., a French portfolio investment company, said its reinsurance arm, Axa Re S.A., opened a branch in Singapore to lead the company's drive into Southeast Asia. The Singapore unit, Axa Re Asia, is capitalized at US$27 million, Axa Midi said. Axa Re, which already has been operating branches in the U.S., Canada and Britain, had 1989 consolidated revenue of 2.1 billion francs ($374.3 million).
