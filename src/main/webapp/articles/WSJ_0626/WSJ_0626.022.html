Philips Industries Meeting Set

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Philips Industries Inc. set Aug. 6 for a special shareholder meeting to vote on the company's proposed acquisition by Tomkins PLC. London-based Tomkins signed a definitive agreement on June 15 to acquire Philips for $18.50 a share, or $550 million, after the collapse earlier this year of a leveraged buy-out of Philips by Merrill Lynch Capital Partners Inc.

Philips makes windows, bathtubs, airmoving equipment and other items for residential, commercial and industrial buildings, mobile homes and recreational vehicles.
