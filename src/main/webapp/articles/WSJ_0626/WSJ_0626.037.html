Dividend News: Hartford Steam Boiler Inspection & Insurance Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

HARTFORD STEAM BOILER INSPECTION & INSURANCE CO. voted to raise the quarterly dividend five cents a share, or 12.5%, to 45 cents. The Hartford, Conn.-based company insures boilers and other machinery. The increased dividend is payable July 26 to shares of record July 10. President Wilson Wilde noted it was the 25th consecutive year that shareholders have received a dividend increase.
