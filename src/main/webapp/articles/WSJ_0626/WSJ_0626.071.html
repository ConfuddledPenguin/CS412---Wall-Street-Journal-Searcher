Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- Edgar J. Roberts Jr. was named executive vice president of operations at the firm's Moody's Investors Service Inc. ratings agency unit, effective July 9. The post is a new one. Mr. Roberts currently is chief financial officer of the Inter-American Development Bank, which provides funds for Latin American development.
