Who's News: Blount Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

BLOUNT Inc. (Montgomery, Ala.) -- R. William Van Sant was elected chief executive officer of this manufacturing and construction company. Mr. Van Sant, 51 years old, has been president and chief operating officer of Blount since 1987. He will retain his position as president when he assumes the post of chief executive on Dec. 1. He succeeds Winton M. Blount, 69, who remains chairman.
