Business Brief -- One Bancorp: Maine Savings Bank Unit Plans to Sell Branch Offices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

Maine Savings Bank, a unit of One Bancorp, said it signed agreements in principle for the sale of its Bath and Camden banking offices to Casco Northern Bank, a unit of Bank of Boston Corp. Maine Savings, based in Portland, also agreed in principle to sell four other offices -- its Brewer, Bucksport and North Windham offices to Peoples Heritage Bank, and its South Paris office to Androscoggin Savings Bank.

Terms weren't disclosed and all sales require regulatory approval. The transactions are expected to be completed before year's end, Maine Savings said, and would reduce the bank's assets by $180 million to about $1.5 billion.

Maine Savings withdrew plans to sell five other offices offered in January that didn't sell, Bangor Mall, Bangor Main Street, Orono, Old Town and Waterville.
