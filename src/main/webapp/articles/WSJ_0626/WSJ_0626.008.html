Rexene Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

DALLAS -- Rexene Corp. said it completed its previously announced acquisition of PolyPac Inc. from a unit of Rauma Repola, a Finnish packaging company, for about $15 million. Rexene is a petrochemical company; PolyPac is a flexible-packaging facility located in Dalton, Ga.
