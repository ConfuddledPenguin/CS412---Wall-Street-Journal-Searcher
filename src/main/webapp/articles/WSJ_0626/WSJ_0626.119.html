Business Brief -- Willcox & Gibbs Inc.: Accord Reached to Acquire Filix Lastex, a French Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

Willcox & Gibbs Inc. expanded its covered-yarn operations by agreeing to pay $39 million for Filix Lastex S.A. of Troyes, France. Covered yarn is wrapped around the elastic product spandex; it is used mainly in manufacturing women's hosiery.

Willcox & Gibbs currently has covered-yarn operations in the U.S. and Canada, said President Richard Mackey. It also has interests in electrical parts. In 1989, Filix Lastex earned $3.2 million on sales of $51 million.The agreement is subject to conditions including approval by Willcox & Gibbs's board and clearance by the French government.

Willcox & Gibbs has reported that first-quarter net income sank 71% to $1.2 million, or nine cents a share, reflecting a $2 million charge for restructuring at certain divisions. Sales rose 3.4% to $139 million.

The company also has said it expects fully diluted earnings per share for 1990 of $1.15 to $1.20 before unusual charges, compared with $1.29 a share in 1989.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Willcox & Gibbs shares closed unchanged at $10.125.
