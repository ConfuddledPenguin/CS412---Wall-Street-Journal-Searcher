Who's News: Fuji Bank Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

FUJI BANK Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Tomohiro Kamio was named director and general manager of the New York branch, which has $43 billion of the parent's $390 billion of assets. Mr. Kamio, 50 years old, succeeds Toru Nonoyama, 54, who becomes a managing director for Fuji Bank, overseeing the Americas operations from headquarters in New York. Mr. Kamio formerly was based in Tokyo as general manager of the Americas division, which encompasses the U.S., Canada, the Caribbean and South America.
