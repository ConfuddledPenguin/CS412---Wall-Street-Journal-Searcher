Business Brief -- Dayton Hudson Corp.: Retailer Will Dismiss 850 At Marshall Field Operation

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

Dayton Hudson Corp., Minneapolis, said it plans to dismiss about 850 central-office employees of Chicago-based Marshall Field & Co. by the end of the year. Last week, Dayton Hudson completed its acquisition of the upscale retailing chain from B.A.T Industries PLC of Britain for $1.05 billion.

Dayton Hudson said the move will slash the central-office staff of Marshall Field by about half. The retailer said the terminations don't include store employees or support staff.

Employees of Marshall Field were warned in April by Dayton Hudson that it intended to consolidate much of the retailer's Chicago office operations in Minneapolis.

The Marshall Field staff that remains in Chicago will handle matters such as customer service and community relations.
