Lubrizol Stock Repurchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

CLEVELAND -- Lubrizol Corp. said directors authorized the specialty chemical concern to repurchase up to two million of its common shares. Lubrizol, which has 36 million shares outstanding, will make the repurchase on the open market. Lubrizol closed yesterday at $40.375, up 12.5 cents, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.

Separately, Lubrizol announced that Ray A. Andreas was elected vice president and chief financial officer, succeeding William T. Beargie, 60, who is retiring. Mr. Andreas, 45, had been corporate controller and vice president-finance of the Lubrizol Petroleum Chemicals Co. unit.
