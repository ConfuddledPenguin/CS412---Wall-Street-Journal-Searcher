Who's News: International Electronics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

INTERNATIONAL ELECTRONICS Inc. (Needham Heights, Mass.) -- Heath Paley, 42 years old, was named executive vice president. He is the founder of Ecco Industries, a maker of voice verifications systems. Mr. Paley and Giorgio Arra, 49, also were named directors. Mr. Arra is president of Fidinam Usa Inc., a management and investment firm.
