Marketing Brief -- Reader's Digest Association Inc. U.K. Subsidiary to Acquire Publishers David & Charles

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

Reader's Digest Association Inc.'s U.K. subsidiary agreed to acquire David & Charles PLC for $17 million. David & Charles publishes a variety of general interest and reference books; the company also runs a book club operation. Reader's Digest said that the board of David & Charles, based in Newton Abbot, England, will recommend that shareholders accept the proposal. Holders of some 64% of the ordinary shares of David & Charles have agreed to the acquisition.

Reader's Digest Association Ltd., London, is one of 16 international subsidiaries of the U.S. parent, based in Pleasantville, N.Y., which for several years has been expanding its global publishing holdings. The parent also directly markets books, magazines and other products by mail.
