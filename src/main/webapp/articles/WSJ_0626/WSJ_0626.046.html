Meridian Insurance Sets Charge

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Meridian Insurance Group Inc. said it will post a second-quarter charge of about $2 million, citing the effect of storms in early and mid-June. The insurer had a year-earlier loss of $1.4 million. The company said that despite a favorable trend in private passenger auto business, chances for a second-quarter profit are "problematic," because of numerous claims from tornado damage in parts of its marketing areas in Indiana, Ohio and Illinois.
