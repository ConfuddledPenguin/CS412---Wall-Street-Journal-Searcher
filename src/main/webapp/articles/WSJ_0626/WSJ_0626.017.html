Who's News: Quaker Oats Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

QUAKER OATS Co. (Chicago) -- John A. Boynton, assistant treasurer, was named vice president, corporate controller and planning, of this packaged food concern. He succeeds Terry G. Westbrook, who was named vice president, corporate finance. John G. Jartz, director, business development, was named vice president, business development, succeeding Ralph A. Harris, who took a post with another company.
