Business Brief -- MCI Communications Corp. Banc One and Household International Award Accords

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

MCI Communications Corp., Washington, D.C., said it received two multimillion-dollar contracts for voice and data communications services. Under a four-year, $42 million contract with Banc One Corp., MCI will provide the Columbus, Ohio-based financial institution with MCI Vnet service, a virtual private network, MCI 800 service, and a variety of customized data services linking the company's 700 subsidiaries and affiliate banks.

Under the second agreement, MCI will provide Household International Inc., a Chicago-based financial services company, with MCI 800 service and MCI Vnet service, a network linking more than 700 company locations. MCI wouldn't disclose the terms of the three-year agreement.
