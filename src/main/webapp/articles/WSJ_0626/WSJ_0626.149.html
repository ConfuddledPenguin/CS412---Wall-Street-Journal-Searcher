Business Brief -- Morrison Knudsen Corp.: Firm Expects Strong Profit For Quarter, Six Months

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it expects to report a strong second quarter, exceeding its year-ago profit of $7.2 million, or 63 cents a share. The company said its first half performance should top the year-ago period, when the construction and engineering concern posted profit of $13.6 million, or $1.19 a share.

Order backlog at the end of the second quarter ending June 30 should exceed $3 billion, up from $2.6 billion at the end of the first quarter, the company said.

The Boise, Idaho, company said its engineering and construction activities will be strong for the quarter, while its rail systems group will be adversely affected by start-up problems on a transit car contract with New York City.
