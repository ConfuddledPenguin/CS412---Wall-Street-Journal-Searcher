Who's News: International Mobile Machines Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

INTERNATIONAL MOBILE MACHINES Corp. (Philadelphia) -- William J. Hilsman, president and chief executive, was elected chairman of this digital cellular technology concern. Mr. Hilsman, 58, succeeds Sherwin I. Seligsohn, 54, who became chairman emeritus and who will now focus on patent capabilities for the company. Richard W. Pryor, 58, chief operating officer, was elected to the additional post of executive vice president.
