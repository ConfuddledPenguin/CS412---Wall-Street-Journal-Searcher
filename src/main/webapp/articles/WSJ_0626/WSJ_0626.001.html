Who's News: James Madison Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

JAMES MADISON Ltd. (Washington) -- Madison National Bank of Virginia, McClean, Va., a unit of this holding company, elected Donna W. Eacho as a director, bringing board membership to 14. She was a vice president at Crestar Bank, a unit of Crestar Financial Corp., both based in Richmond, Va.
