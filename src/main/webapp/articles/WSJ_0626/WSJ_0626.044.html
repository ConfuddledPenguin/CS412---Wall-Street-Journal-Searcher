Financing Business: California Energy Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

CALIFORNIA ENERGY Co. said it retained Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and First Boston Corp. as agents for a private placement of as much as $25 million of common stock. The San Francisco-based developer of geothermal energy plants said it is considering a public offering of common stock in the future to provide additional capital to finance its growth.
