Stratus Receives Lloyd's Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

MARLBORO, Mass. -- Stratus Computer Inc. said Lloyd's of London placed an initial $1.1 million order for its fault-tolerant continuous-processing systems. The order represents the first phase of an estimated $3.4 million Stratus-based project to link the computer systems of Lloyd's underwriters, brokers and third-party processing bureaus.
