International Brief -- Elders IXL Ltd.: Carter Holt Harvey to Buy Stake in Elders Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

Carter Holt Harvey Ltd., a New Zealand timber and building materials concern, said it is buying a 52.7% stake in Elders Resources NZFP Ltd. from Elders IXL Ltd. of Australia for 822.3 million New Zealand dollars (US$482.3 million), part of a major restructuring of Elders IXL announced in March. The sale is conditioned on Elders Resources' selling all except its forestry-based assets, which requires authorization by Elders Resources directors and shareholders, and approval by regulators in New Zealand and Australia, where Elders Resources has oil, gas and coal interests.

Carter Holt said the base price values the 411,159,696 shares it is buying at NZ$2 each. The final price is adjustable to between NZ$700 million and NZ$900 million, depending on the nonforestry assets' sale price. Once the stake is acquired, a Cart Holt-Elders Resources merger proposal will be put to holders of both companies.

Elders IXL's restructuring includes plans to sell resources and financial-services interests and spin off agribusiness operations to concentrate on its core brewing business.
