BALFOUR MACLAINE Corp. (New York) -- Bob Siebenmann was named managing director, a new post, at Balfour Maclaine International Ltd., a London-based unit of this international trading and financial services company. Prior to joining Balfour Maclaine International (UK) Ltd., a commodity futures brokerage firm, Mr. Siebenmann was head of the commercial and retail division of LIT America, a Chicago-based affiliate of LIT Futures Ltd., a futures brokerage firm in London.
