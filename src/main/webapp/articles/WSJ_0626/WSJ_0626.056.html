Letters to the Editor: Car-Rental Firms Unfairly Indicted

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

Your June 1 article "Car-Rental Firms Leave Drivers Dazed by Rip-Offs, Options, Misleading Ads" indicted the entire car-rental industry. I resent the unwarranted hatchet job done on the industry as a whole and National Car Rental in particular. You associate us with major abuses that I admit have occurred in this business. But they are abuses National has not been party to.

You berate us, for example, for charging for an extra day after a renter kept the car past the special promotional weekly rate time period. As most travelers know, promotions are just that. Ask an airline if you still get the promotional rate if you were supposed to return on a Sunday and you suddenly decided to come back Monday morning instead.

Michael J. Olsen

Vice President

Corporate Communications

National Car Rental System Inc.

Minneapolis
