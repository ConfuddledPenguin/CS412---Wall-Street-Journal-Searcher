Plum Creek Timber Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/26/90

SEATTLE -- Directors of Plum Creek Timber Co. L.P. authorized the company to buy back 7%, or about one million, of the company's units. The units may be repurchased in the open market or in negotiated transactions from time to time, the company said.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, the units closed at $26.25, up 37.5 cents.
