Who's News: Builders Transport Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

BUILDERS TRANSPORT Inc. (Camden, S.C.) -- John R. Morris was named chief executive officer of this trucking company. Mr. Morris, 46 years old, retains his positions as president and chief operating officer. He succeeds Charles C. Gay Jr., 42, who remains vice chairman. Separately, Builders Transport said Charles C. Gay, 69, resigned as chairman and as a director. No successor was named.
