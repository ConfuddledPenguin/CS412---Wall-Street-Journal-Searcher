AT&T Unit Gets Navy Work

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

WASHINGTON -- American Telephone & Telegraph Technologies Inc., a unit of American Telephone & Telegraph Co., won a $13.9 million Navy contract for oceanographic service.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., got a $10.4 million Army contract for aircraft infrared equipment.

Action Manufacturing Co. received a $10.2 million Army contract for mine parts.
