EC to Study Philip Morris Takeover of Swiss Suchard

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 06/26/90

BRUSSELS -- The European Community Commission Monday said it would review "with interest" the $3.8 billion takeover of Swiss chocolate and coffee concern Jacobs Suchard by Philip Morris Cos. of the U.S. The move will substantially enlarge the European position of Kraft General Foods, Philip Morris's food-processing arm, and could therefore affect competition within the EC.

A spokeswoman for the Commission said, however, that it was "very early days yet." She said that "nothing happens in the world these days without the EC being involved in some way or another."

Through the move Philip Morris will acquire such brand names as Tobler, Toblerone, Milka and Cote d'Or on the chocolate side and Night and Day, Jacques Vabre and Carte Noire in coffee.
