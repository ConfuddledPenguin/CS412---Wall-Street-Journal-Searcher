REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Food Alarms

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/26/90

Another federal official has warned against creating fears over the safety of the U.S. food supply. One consequence of driving the political system to enact extensive rules for food production techniques, said Agriculture Assistant Secretary Jack Parnell, is that it could raise the costs of food in the supermarket, hitting the poor the hardest. But food fear is largely a phenomenon among the upper middle class, for whom costs to poorer consumers isn't a primary concern.
