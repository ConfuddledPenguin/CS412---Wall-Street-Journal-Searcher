Raw-Steel Output Fell 4.1% in Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 4.1% last week to 1,781,000 tons from 1,858,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 1.5% from the 1,808,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 79.8% of its capability last week, compared with 82.7% the previous week and 81.4% a year ago.
