Ferruzzi Group Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

MILAN -- The Ferruzzi Group subsidiary Montedison S.p.A. said that it has acquired Hercules Inc.'s 50% interest in their equally owned venture, Moplefan SPA. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed. Moplefan, a European producer of polypropylene staple, yarn and film, currently has total production capacity of 120,000 tons per year.

Moplefan's 1989 revenue was about 320 billion lire ($264 million). The company employs 1,750.
