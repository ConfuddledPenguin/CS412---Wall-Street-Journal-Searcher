Mesa Airlines' Traffic Doubles

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

FARMINGTON, N.M. -- Mesa Airlines said its June traffic more than doubled to 14,122 revenue passenger miles from 6,285 a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's June load factor rose to 52.5% from 48.2%.

In the first half, traffic rose 89% to 62,265 revenue passenger miles from 32,867 a year earlier.
