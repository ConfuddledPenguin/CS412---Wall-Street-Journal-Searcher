Business Brief -- Good Guys Inc.: Firm Says Sales Rose 56% In the Fiscal Third Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

Good Guys Inc., citing new-store sales and strong sales in all product categories, said its third-quarter sales increased 56%. Sales in the quarter ended June 30 were $65.4 million, compared with $41.9 million a year earlier, Good Guys said. The consumer electronics retailer said it will release earnings figures later this month.

The San Francisco company said the increase came, in part, from six new stores added this year, which brought its total stores to 23 as of June 30 from 17 stores at the end of June 1989. Comparable store sales -- sales at stores in operation for more than a year -- were up 29% in the period, the company said.

Sales growth has been strong across the board in all products since last summer, said a Good Guys spokesman. For the first nine months of its 1990 fiscal year, Good Guys said, sales increased 51% to $215.1 million from $142.9 million. Comparable store sales for the nine months rose 26%.
