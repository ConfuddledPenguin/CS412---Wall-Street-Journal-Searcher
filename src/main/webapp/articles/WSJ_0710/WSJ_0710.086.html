Sprint Sets Soviet Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

RESTON, Va. -- US Sprint, a unit of United Telecommunications Inc., said it agreed to form a joint venture in the Soviet Union to operate a commercial video-conferencing service there. Sprint said its agreement is with the Main Trunk Lines Control Center of the Soviet Ministry of Communications.

The agreement is the second between Sprint and Soviet partners. Earlier this month Sprint agreed to participate in Sprint Networks USSR, a venture that will offer data communications and electronic messaging services in Moscow.
