Technology Brief -- Altos Computer Systems: Taiwan's Acer Group Begins Its Tender Offer for Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

Altos Computer Systems, San Jose, Calif., said that Taiwan's Acer Group began its previously announced tender offer for Altos's shares outstanding. Acer, a computer maker, is offering $8.35 a share as part of a merger agreement that Acer and Altos signed last Wednesday. The acquisition will be made by AAC Acquisition Corp., a subsidiary of Acer's U.S. unit. The tender offer will expire Aug. 3, the company said. Altos shares closed yesterday at $8.125, unchanged, in national over-the-counter trading.
