Northwest Air's Traffic Climbs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- NWA Inc. said June traffic at its Northwest Airlines unit climbed 7.2% to 4.82 billion revenue passenger miles from 4.49 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The closely held carrier's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, eased marginally to 72.2% from 72.4%.

In the first half, traffic increased 16% to 24.64 billion revenue passenger miles from 21.23 billion a year earlier. The load factor increased to 65.3% from 64.6%.
