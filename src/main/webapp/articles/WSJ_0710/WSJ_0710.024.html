BP Chemicals' Paper Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- BP Chemicals Inc. said it has formed a joint venture with a French firm to build a synthetic paper plant here. The unit of British Petroleum Co. said the partnership is with Mu Ro Inc., a subsidiary of Arjomari, a French specialty paper firm. Production should begin by mid-1991.

The synthetic paper is made from high-density polyethylene and is used for maps, charts and labels for recyclable packaging, among other things.
