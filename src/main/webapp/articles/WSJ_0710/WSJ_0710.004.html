Marketing & Media: Mead Swaps Stock for Dataline

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Mead Corp. said its Dataline Inc. unit has been acquired by Star Data Systems, a Markham, Ontario, financial-information firm. Under the agreement, Star Data acquired all shares of Dataline, a unit of Mead Data Central, in exchange for 15.1 million preferred shares of Star Data. That represents 36% of fully diluted equity.

The preferred shares aren't publicly traded, but they can be converted at any time into common shares on a one-for-one basis.

In Toronto over-the-counter trading yesterday, Star Data common shares closed at 85 Canadian cents (73 U.S. cents).

Dataline provides financial market information and access to various databases.
