Society Corp. Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

CLEVELAND -- Society Corp. said it increased the limit of its common share-purchase plan to 1.1 million shares from 750,000. The financial services company had 33.5 million common shares outstanding at the end of March. The buy-back offer expires Dec. 31.

As of Sunday, Society had bought back 733,300 shares.
