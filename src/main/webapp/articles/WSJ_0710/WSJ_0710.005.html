Metro Airlines Traffic Climbs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

DALLAS -- Metro Airlines said June traffic rose 38% to 59.5 million revenue passenger miles from 43 million a year earlier. Load factor was 61.3%, up from 58.8% a year earlier. For the-year-to-date, the carrier said traffic rose 43% to 302.8 million revenue passenger miles from 211.7 million in the 1989 first half.
