Highland Superstores Sales Fall

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

PLYMOUTH, Mich. -- Highland Superstores Inc. reported June sales of $68.3 million, a 2% drop from sales of $69.7 million a year earlier. Comparable-store sales were down 5%. The consumer electronic and appliance retailer said June sales don't include sales from the Shreveport, La., and Milwaukee stores, which the company closed recently.
