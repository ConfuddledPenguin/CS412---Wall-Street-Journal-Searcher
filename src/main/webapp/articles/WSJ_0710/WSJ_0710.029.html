British Airways' Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

NEW YORK -- British Airways said its June traffic rose 14% to 3.72 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.26 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, rose to 76% from 73.1%.

For the three months ended June 30, British Airways' traffic increased 13% to 10.59 billion revenue passenger miles from 9.35 billion a year earlier. The load factor rose to 72.2% from 70.4%.
