Barrister Information Systems

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Barrister Information Systems Corp., which supplies computer systems to the legal profession, said it completed an exchange of its 1989 13% subordinated convertible debentures, due Sept. 30, 1999, in the principal amount of $2,320,000. The company also said it exchanged $57,000 of its 1984 12% subordinated convertible debentures, due Sept. 30, 1991. Both issues were exchanged for a new 13% subordinated convertible debenture, due June 30, 2000.

As a result of the exchange, the company realized $794,410 in additional working capital.
