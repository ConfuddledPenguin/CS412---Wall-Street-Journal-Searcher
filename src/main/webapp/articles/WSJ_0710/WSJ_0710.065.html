Atlantic Southeast Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

ATLANTA -- Atlantic Southeast Airlines Inc. said June traffic rose 1.8% to 38.6 million revenue passenger miles from 37.9 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The regional carrier's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 54.8% from 56.5%.

In the first six months, traffic rose 10% to 213 million revenue passenger miles from 193.5 million. Load factor eased to 51.2% from 51.4%.
