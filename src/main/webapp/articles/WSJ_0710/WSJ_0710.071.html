WestAir Holding Inc.'s Traffic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- WestAir Holding Inc. said its June traffic increased 28% to 57 million revenue passenger miles from 44.7 million a year earlier, better than expected as the company's new Atlantic Coast Airlines and Dulles Airport hub in Washington, D.C., performed particularly well. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, eased to 47% from 48%.

In the first half, traffic rose 36% to 307.7 million revenue passenger miles from 226.6 million a year earlier. The load factor fell to 44% from 45%.
