Southwest Air's Traffic Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

DALLAS -- Southwest Airlines said its June traffic rose 1.4% to 883.3 million revenue passenger miles from 870.9 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline's June load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 63.9% from 68.4%.

In the first half, traffic rose 5.8% to 4.88 billion revenue passenger miles from 4.62 billion a year earlier.
