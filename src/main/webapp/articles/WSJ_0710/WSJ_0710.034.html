Hanson Sells Spice Business

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

LONDON -- Hanson PLC, a British industrial conglomerate, said it sold its Baltimore Spice business to an affiliate of Fuchs Gewuerze G.m.b.H., a West German seasonings and spice maker, for $23.9 million. The sale includes the manufacturing operations of the Old Bay Seasonings business, which Hanson bought in 1986 as part of its $930 million acquisition of SCM, a U.S. conglomerate, but Hanson will retain the retailing operations of the unit, the company said.
