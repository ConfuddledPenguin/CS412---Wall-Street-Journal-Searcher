EC's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

LUXEMBOURG -- The seasonally adjusted unemployment rate in the European Community for the first five months of this year remained steady at 8.6%, the EC statistical office said. Unemployment ranged from 1.7% in Luxembourg to 16.6% in Ireland, the report said.

The latest unseasonally adjusted total of officially registered jobless people in the EC was 13.9 million in April.
