Marketing & Media: American Business Computers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

AKRON, Ohio -- American Business Computers Corp., which makes products for the food-service industry, said it reached an agreement that gives the Pepsi-Cola unit of PepsiCo Inc., Purchase, N.Y., exclusive rights to its soft-drink dispensers and parts. Terms weren't disclosed. American Business Computers said the pact gives Pepsi rights to new products and allows it to supply parts to other dispenser makers.
