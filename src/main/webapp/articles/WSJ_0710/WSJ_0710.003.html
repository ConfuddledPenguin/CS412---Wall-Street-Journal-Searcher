Who's News: Textron Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

TEXTRON Inc. (Providence, R.I.) -- Richard A. Watson, 45 years old, was named group vice president of this aerospace, commercial products and financial services concern, succeeding Maurice G. Wilkins Jr., who had held the post temporarily and remains executive vice president, corporate development. Mr. Watson was succeeded as president of the Textron Investment Management Co. subsidiary by John D. Lemery, 40, who was the unit's senior vice president, investments.
