Who's News: Sears's Dean Witter Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

CHICAGO -- Sears Roebuck & Co. said it named Robert J. Dwyer, 46 years old, director of national sales at Dean Witter Reynolds Inc., the New York-based brokerage subsidiary of the retail and financial services company. Mr. Dwyer succeeds John O. Dix Jr., 40, who the company said wished to return to Dallas for family reasons and will be director of Dean Witter's Southwest region.

Mr. Dwyer remains an executive vice president, and was succeeded as director of the taxable fixed-income division by Mitchell M. Merlin, 36, who also was named an executive vice president. Mr. Merlin previously was managing director in corporate finance.
