Who's News: Sanwa Bank Picks Eda

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

OSAKA, Japan -- Sanwa Bank Ltd. named Minoru Eda managing director and general manager of its New York branch, succeeding Takeo Sakai, who will become deputy head of Sanwa's international division in Tokyo. As head of Sanwa's New York branch, Mr. Eda, 53 years old, who was previously based in Tokyo, is responsible for its largest branch outside of Japan. The New York branch has assets of over $18 billion. For fiscal year ended March 31, 1990, Sanwa reported assets of $387 billion world-wide.
