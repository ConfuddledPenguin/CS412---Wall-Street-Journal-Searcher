International Brief -- Mitsubishi Oil Co.: Merlin Petroleum Purchase By Firm and Unit Is Set

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd. said that a company jointly owned by Mitsubishi Oil Co. and a Mitsubishi unit will buy Merlin Petroleum Co. for $75 million. Bond, based in Australia, said it holds 79.5% of Merlin. Mitsubishi is a Japanese oil refiner and distributor. Merlin holds exploration interests in Papua New Guinea, in a concession operated by Chevron Corp. British Petroleum PLC also holds a stake in the concession. People familiar with the transaction said production in the concession area is expected to begin in 1992.

The sale, which is subject to regulatory and contractual procedures, is expected to be completed before July 31.
