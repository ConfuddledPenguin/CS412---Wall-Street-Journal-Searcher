Who's News: Cenvill Development Names Martin Taplin To Chairman's Post

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

LAKE WORTH, Fla. -- Martin W. Taplin, a real estate workout specialist and president of Martin W. Taplin Associates, was named chairman of Cenvill Development Corp., filling a vacancy. Cenvill, a real estate development company, is 96%-owned by First American Bank & Trust Co. in Lake Worth and came under control of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. when the bank was seized by regulators last year.

Additionally, Cenvill said Jeffrey Schottenstein, president of Schottenstein Realty Co. in Miami, and Seth Werner, president of Werner Capital Corp., a Miami real estate investment and management concern, were also named to the board and designated as vice chairmen, new posts.

The three new directors succeed Richard O'Brien, Kathryn Kelleher and Vincent Filippini, appointees of the FDIC who resigned. The company said the new directors were named primarily to bolster the company's continuing efforts to find a buyer with the help of Bear, Stearns & Co.
