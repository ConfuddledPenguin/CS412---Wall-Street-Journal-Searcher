Harsco Gets Korean Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

HARRISBURG, Pa. -- Harsco Corp. said it received a $260 million four-year contract to supply howitzers to the Republic of Korea. Harsco said it will supply 470 howitzer kits beginning next year. Samsung Shipbuilding & Heavy Industries will assemble the howitzers in Korea.

Harsco, a maker of products for defense, industrial, commercial and construction uses, has been co-producing the howitzers with Korea since 1984.
