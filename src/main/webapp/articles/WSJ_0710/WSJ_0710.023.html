Rochester Telephone's Deal

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Rochester Telephone Corp. said it completed the acquisition of Southland Telephone Co. from Telecom USA Inc. Rochester paid about $37.1 million for Southland, an Atmore, Ala.-based telephone company serving about 9,000 access lines in Alabama and 3,000 lines in Florida.
