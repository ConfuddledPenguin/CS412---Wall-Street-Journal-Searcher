The Netherlands' Trade Surplus

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

AMSTERDAM -- Because of 3% decline in imports, the Netherlands' trade surplus in April widened to 700 million guilders ($375.7 million) from the year-earlier surplus of 200 million guilders. But the trade surplus for the first four months of this year narrowed to three billion guilders from a surplus of 3.4 billion guilders a year earlier.
