REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Postal Perestroika

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

To save money, the U.S. Postal Service says it will contract out to private companies some of its letter-sorting work. The postal union workers' leader says this is a "disgrace" and vows to "fight it on the beaches, on the hill, in negotiations, wherever." Moscow may have a private postal system before it reaches the public backwaters of the U.S.
