Air Force Job to Weston

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/10/90

WEST CHESTER, Pa. -- Roy F. Weston Inc. said it received a five-year contract valued at $50 million to provide environmental services at hazardous-waste and toxic-waste sites on Air Force bases around the country. The environmental services concern said its work will include risk assessments, sample analyses, pilot studies and waste removal.

Weston has worked on more than 75 other Air Force projects.
