International Brief -- Rank Organisation PLC: Firm Gets Majority Stake In Mecca Leisure Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

Rank Organisation PLC claimed control of 50.7% of the ordinary shares of Mecca Leisure Group PLC, a rival British entertainment concern. Rank's financial advisers, J. Henry Schroder Wagg & Co., said a Rank takeover offer had received acceptances from Mecca shareholders representing 160 million Mecca shares.

Rank launched its bid June 1, offering 90.9 pence of its own shares for each Mecca ordinary share. Though Mecca initially rejected the offer, its board late last month decided to recommend acceptance when it became clear Mecca was unlikely to be able to carry out the asset sales needed to reduce its crippling debt load.

The Rank bid, which values Mecca at #512 million ($914 million), needs regulatory approval. Together, the two firms would hold large shares of the domestic bingo parlor and resort-village market.
