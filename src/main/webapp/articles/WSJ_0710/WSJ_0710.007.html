Ametek Inc. Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

PAOLI, Pa. -- Ametek Inc. said it agreed to buy Ciaramella-OEP Group, a closely held Italian manufacturer of electric motors. Terms weren't disclosed. Ametek, a manufacturer of industrial products, said Ciaramella-OEP has annual sales of about 82 billion lira, or $68 million.

Ametek said it expects the transaction to be completed later this month.
