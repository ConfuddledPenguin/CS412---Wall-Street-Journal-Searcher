Who's News: Weirton Steel Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

WEIRTON STEEL Corp. (Weirton, W.Va.) -- Dennis Mangino was named vice president of Weirton Technology Center, succeeding William T. Saunders. Mr. Saunders will continue with the company as a manager of technical projects while retaining his title of senior vice president of product development and research.
