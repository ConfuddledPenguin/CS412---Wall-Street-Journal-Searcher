Redemption Notices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended July 6, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

AURORA, COLO., will redeem $185,000 principal amount of Industrial Development Revenue Bonds, Series 1981A, LaQuinta Project, due Aug. 1, 2001, on Aug. 1, 1990. First Interstate Bank of Denver, Colo., is trustee.

CORPUS CHRISTI, TEXAS, HOUSING FINANCE CORP., will redeem $375,000 principal amount of its Single-Family Mortgage Revenue Bonds, Series 1980, on Aug. 1, 1990. Ameritrust Texas, N.A., Dallas, is trustee.

COUNTY COURT OF PRESTON COUNTY, W.Va., will redeem $60,000 of its Industrial Development Revenue Bonds, 1970 Kinney Shoe Project, on Aug. 1, 1990. Albright National Bank of Kingwood, W.Va., is trustee.

EASTON, MD., Health Care Facilities, on Aug. 1, 1990, will call $60,000 principal amount of its First Mortgage Revenue Bonds, Series 1980, William Hill Manor Project, due Aug. 1, 2010. Maryland National Bank, Baltimore, is trustee.

HAMILTON COUNTY, OHIO, will redeem $4.45 million Hospital Facilities Revenue Bonds, Series 1981, St. Francis St. George Hospital Project, on Aug. 15, 1990. Star Bank, Cincinnati, is trustee.

HARLAN COUNTY, KY., will call for redemption its First Mortgage Medical Facility Revenue Bonds, due Aug. 1, 2002, on Aug. 1, 1990. First National Bank of Henderson, Ky., is trustee.

MOORHEAD, MINN., will redeem $515,000 principal amount of its Residential Mortgage Revenue Bonds on Aug. 1, 1990. American National Bank & Trust Co., St. Paul, Minn., is trustee.

PUEBLO, COLO., will redeem $575,000 principal amount of its Single-Family Mortgage Revenue Bonds, 1978 Series A, due Aug. 1, 1991-1999, on Aug. 1, 1990. United Bank of Denver, Colo., is trustee.

SOUTH BEND, IND., will call $15,000 principal amount of Health Care Facilities FHA-insured mortgage revenue bonds, Series A, Fountainview Place of South Bend issue, due Aug. 1, 2012, on Aug. 1, 1990. Society Bank, South Bend, Ind., is paying agent.

VAIL, COLO., will call $120,000 principal amount of its Single-Family Mortgage Revenue Bonds, Series 1979, on Aug. 1, 1990. First Interstate Bank of Denver, Colo., is trustee.

VIRGINIA HOUSING DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY, will redeem on Aug. 1, 1990, its Home Mortgage Bonds, 1980 Series B. Signet Trust Co. is trustee.

CORPORATES

ATLANTIC ELECTRIC will call for redemption on Aug. 1, 1990, through a sinking fund, 8,000 shares of its 9.96% cumulative preferred stock on Aug. 1, 1990. Shares will be redeemed at the office of the company in Pleasantville, N.J.

TALMAN FINANCE CORP. will call for redemption 75 shares of Market Auction Preferred Stock on Aug. 20, 1990. Bankers Trust Co., New York, is trustee.
