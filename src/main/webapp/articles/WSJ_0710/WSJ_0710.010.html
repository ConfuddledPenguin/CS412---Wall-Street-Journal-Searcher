Foster Wheeler French Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

CLINTON, N.J. -- Foster Wheeler Corp. said its French subsidiary was awarded a contract to design and build a new plant for Indianapolis-based Eli Lilly & Co. that will produce synthetic human insulin, called humulin. The new facility, which is expected to cost most of the $115 million that Lilly has budgeted for expenditure in France, will be located near Lilly's existing plant in Fergersheim, near Strasbourg. The plant's start-up is slated for 1993.
