GM's Chevrolet Incentives

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/10/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Corp.'s Chevrolet division said it increased incentives on three models of its 1990 and 1991 cars and trucks. Chevrolet said buyers of the 1990 Chevy four-door S-10 Blazer will receive a $1,000 rebate. Buyers of the 1991 Camaro and 1990 Lumina APV will get $1,000 and $1,250, respectively.

The division said it will offer buyers the alternative of reduced financing rates ranging from 7.9% on 12-month to 48-month loans to 10.9% on 60-month loans.
