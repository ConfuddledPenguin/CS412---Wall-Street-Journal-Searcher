Technology Brief -- Cambridge BioScience Corp.: Investors Ratify Acquisition By Biotech Research Labs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Cambridge BioScience Corp. said its shareholders ratified the acquisition of the company by Biotech Research Laboratories Inc. The shareholders also approved the change of the company name to Cambridge Biotech Corp. Cambridge BioScience president Patrick J. Leonard will remain as president and chief executive officer of the new concern.

Anthony C. Nicholls, president and a director of Biotech Research of Rockville, Md., will become executive vice president of Cambridge Biotech.

The acquisition, already approved by Biotech Research shareholders, is expected to close by next week. Cambridge BioScience, Worcester, Mass., said the new company's board will consist of its current board members and four additional directors from Biotech Research: Thomas M. Li, W. Samuel Nisbet, Thomas T. Taylor and Douglas Yee.

Cambridge BioScience and Biotech Research are biotechnology concerns.
