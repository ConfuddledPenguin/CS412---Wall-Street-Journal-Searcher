Business Brief -- Ratners Group PLC: Technical Hurdle Removed In Kay Jewelers Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Ratners Group PLC waived a provision of its agreement to acquire Kay Jewelers Inc., removing a technical hurdle to the stock-swap deal. Ratners said it will no longer condition the acquisition on the company's receiving a majority of both classes of Kay's $150 million in junk bonds. Ratners has acquired more than 90% of one class of bonds but only a small fraction of the other class.

Ratners said it expects to complete its acquisition of Kay shortly after Kay's stockholders approve the deal in October.

Kay, a retail jewelry chain based in Alexandria, Va., has about 12.4 million shares outstanding. Under the agreement, each Kay share will be swapped for a convertible preferred share of Ratners. Ratners values the new stock at $17 a share, although some analysts put the stock's value at $14 to $15 a share.
