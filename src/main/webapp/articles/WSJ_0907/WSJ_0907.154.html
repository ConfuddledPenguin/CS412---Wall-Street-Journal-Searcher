International Brief -- American Telephone & Telegraph: Telefonica Nears Agreement For a Stake in Firm's Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

Telefonica de Espana S.A. said it is close to an agreement to acquire a stake in American Telephone & Telegraph Co.'s main European unit, as part of an effort to increase the Spanish telephone company's access to new technology. A Telefonica spokesman said the company is seeking to swap its 20% holding in AT&T Microelectronica Espana S.A. for a share in the Dutch-based AT&T Network Systems International B.V.

State-controlled Telefonica hopes to acquire about 6% of AT&T Network Systems and to win a seat on both the Dutch company's board and its management committee, the spokesman said. Negotiators are assessing the value of the share swap, a task hindered by exchange rate fluctuations in recent months, he said.
