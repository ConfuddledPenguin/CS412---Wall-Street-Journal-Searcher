WHO'S NEWS: Times Mirror Co. named Michael J. Davies publisher and chief executive of the Baltimore Sun. He succeeds Reg Murphy, who will become chairman of the paper. Mr. Davies is succeeded as publisher and chief executive of the Hartford Courant, another Times Mirror newspaper, by Raymond A. Jansen Jr., senior vice president and general manager of the Courant. He is succeeded as the Courant's editor by Michael B. Waller, who was the executive editor. . . . Susan Notarides was named senior vice president, licensing and merchandising, for MGM/UA Communications Co. She was vice president, licensing and merchandising.
