USAir Traffic Increases

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

ARLINGTON, Va. -- USAir, a unit of USAir Group Inc., said its August traffic rose 7.7% to 3.46 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.21 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The July load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, eased to 66.6% from 66.9%.

For the year-to-date, the airline said it flew 24 billion revenue passenger miles, up 5% from 22.8 billion in the year-earlier period. The load factor fell to 60.7% from 61.9%.

USAir said statistics for 1989 periods prior to its August 1989 merger with Piedmont Airlines have been restated to reflect the combined airline operations of both carriers on a pro forma basis.
