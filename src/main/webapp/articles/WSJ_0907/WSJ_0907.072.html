McDonnell Receives $200 Million Order From GATX Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

ST. LOUIS -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. said it received $200 million in aircraft orders from a leasing unit of Chicago-based GATX Corp. for one MD-11 long-range trijet, two MD-90-30s and options for two more MD-90-30s. Delivery is scheduled for late 1992 for the MD-11, and 1995 and 1996 for the MD-90-30s. Separately, McDonnell Douglas said Taiwan-based Great China Airlines signed a letter of intent to purchase six twin-jet transport planes valued at more than $200 million.

The order was for three MD-80s and three MD-90s. The first delivery of MD-80s is scheduled for next year, two more the following year and the three MD-90s in 1996.
