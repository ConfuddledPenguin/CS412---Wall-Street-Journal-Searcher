Initial Jobless Claims Fell in Aug. 25 Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits fell to a seasonally adjusted 370,000 in the week ended Aug. 25 from 382,000 in the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended Aug. 18 was a seasonally adjusted 2,507,000, or 2.4% of those covered by unemployment insurance, up from 2,473,000 in the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate also was 2.4%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended Aug. 18 fell to 2,321,700 from 2,367,200 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
