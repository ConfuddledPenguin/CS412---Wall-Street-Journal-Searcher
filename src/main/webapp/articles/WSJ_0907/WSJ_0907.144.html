Business Brief -- Engelhard Corp.: Unit That Checks Radiation Is Sold to French Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Engelhard Corp. sold for cash a business that supplies crystals and electronic systems that monitor levels of exposure to radiation. The buyer is a subsidiary of the Paris glass manufacturer Saint-Gobain S.A. Terms weren't disclosed; Engelhard had said in February that it wanted to sell the business as part of a restructuring.

Engelhard, of Edison, N.J., provides specialty chemical products, engineered materials and precious-metals management services.
