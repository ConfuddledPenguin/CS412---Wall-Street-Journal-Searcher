Letters to the Editor: Aid to the Enemy

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

Please tell me whose side you are on in your story "Many Americans Trapped in Kuwait Escape Detection" (Aug. 22). You did your best to "finger" all Americans who so far have managed to avoid capture. You gave every indication of aiding and abetting the enemy. Surely, nobody else stood to gain from the facts in this report. But the Americans have their lives to lose.

J.F. Parenteau

Overland Park, Kan.
