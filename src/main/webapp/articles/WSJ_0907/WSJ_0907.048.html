Millicom Unit in Bolivia

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

NEW YORK -- Millicom Inc. said a joint venture, in which one of its subsidiaries is a shareholder, was awarded a license to provide cellular-telephone service in Bolivia. The license, awarded in a bidding process, is the first that the Bolivian government has granted, Millicom said. The system initially will serve the country's major metropolitan areas. Officials declined to value the transaction.

Millicom, a communications company, said it initially would hold 46% of the operation. The subsidiary is Millicom International Holdings Ltd. Comvik International AB, a Swedish telecommunications concern, will hold 23%; the group's Bolivian partners, a group of local business people, will hold 31%.
