Business Brief -- Pic 'N' Save Corp.: Firm Posts Quarterly Loss; One-Time Charges a Factor

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Pic 'N' Save Corp. posted a fiscal second-quarter loss of $9.3 million, or 27 cents a share, after $19 million in one-time pretax charges related to its recent proxy contest and restructuring. In the quarter ended July 2, 1989, the Dominguez, Calif., company earned $6.4 million, or 18 cents a share. This year's second quarter ended July 29.

Sales rose 11%, to $112.7 million from $101.8 million.

Operating earnings, excluding the onetime charges, fell to $6.6 million from $12.1 million. Pic 'N' Save blamed the drop on shrinkage in its gross profit rate, higher store expenses as a percentage of sales and higher interest expenses.

Pic 'N' Save, a retail chain that specializes in close-out merchandise, recently cut short a threatened proxy fight with Girard Partners Limited Partnership and investor David Batchelder by agreeing to put Mr. Batchelder and two associates on the board, among other things.
