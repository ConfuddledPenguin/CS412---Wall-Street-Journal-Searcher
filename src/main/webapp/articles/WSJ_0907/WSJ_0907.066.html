Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- Howard W. Smith was named senior vice president for information marketing programs at the Business Information group, effective Oct. 1. Nick L. Mancini, 36 years old, will succeed Mr. Smith, 47, as president of Dun & Bradstreet Canada Ltd., effective Sept. 24. Mr. Mancini previously was vice president and general manager of the Financial Services group businesses at American Express Canada, a unit of the travel and financial services concern American Express Co. Dun & Bradstreet is a financial information and services company.
