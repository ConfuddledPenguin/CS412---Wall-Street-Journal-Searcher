REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Massachusetts Hostage

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

Massachusetts Lt. Gov. Evelyn Murphy threatened this week to seize control of the state's beleaguered finances and impose her own plan if Gov. Dukakis left on a trade mission. Considering all the water that's gone over this dam, we loved the incredible comment of Dukakis ally L. Edward Lashman, who said Ms. Murphy "is dangerously flirting with the economic health of the commonwealth of Massachusetts. That's irresponsible."
