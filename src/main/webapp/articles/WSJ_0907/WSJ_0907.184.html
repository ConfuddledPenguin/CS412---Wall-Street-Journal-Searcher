AmBase Sets Capital Infusion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

NEW YORK -- AmBase Corp. said it has been asked by the federal Office of Thrift Supervision to provide a $30 million capital infusion into its Carteret Savings Bank unit by Dec. 31. The amount is the company's remaining obligation under the terms of its acquisition of Carteret in 1988. The company, which contributed $20 million in December 1989, said it anticipated the request and foresaw no problems with fulfilling it.
