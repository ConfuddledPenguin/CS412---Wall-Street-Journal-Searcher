Who's News: Allou Health & Beauty Care Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

ALLOU HEALTH & BEAUTY CARE Inc. (Brentwood, N.Y.) -- Herman Jacobs, 30-year-old president of this distributor of health and beauty aids in the eastern U.S., was named to the added position of chief executive officer, succeeding his father, Victor Jacobs, 56, who remains chairman. David Shamilzadeh, 44, was named chief financial officer, a post he had held on an acting basis since June.
