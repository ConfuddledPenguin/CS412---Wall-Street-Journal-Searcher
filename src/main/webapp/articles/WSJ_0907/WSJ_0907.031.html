Who's News: Roosevelt Financial Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

ROOSEVELT FINANCIAL GROUP Inc. (Chesterfield, Mo.) -- Douglas T. Breeden, a finance professor at Duke University's Fuqua School of Business, was named a director of this thrift holding company. Mr. Breeden fills a vacancy created by the mandatory retirement of William E. Pettit, chairman of Neff-Petterson's Inc., Springfield, Mo.
