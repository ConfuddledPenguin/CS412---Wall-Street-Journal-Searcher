Gantos's August Sales Fell 2%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc., a women's clothing retailer, said its August sales fell 2% to $16.1 million from $16.4 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year dropped 18%. The year-earlier total included sales from Gantos's semiannual boutique clearance sale, held this year in July.

For the first eight months of this year, sales rose 15% to $138.4 million from $120.3 million a year earlier.
