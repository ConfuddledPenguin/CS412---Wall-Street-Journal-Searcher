Business Brief -- Barton Industries Inc.: Fiber-Optic Cable Contract Reached With Southern Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

Barton Industries Inc. said it signed a contract with Southern Co. giving Barton the right to build a fiber-optic transmission cable along selected routes on the Southern electric system. Barton, based in Shawnee, Okla., said the engineering and installation will be performed by Atlanta-based Southern electric system and that Barton, a maker of oil field equipment, will reimburse Southern for the costs. Barton wouldn't disclose the anticipated costs.

Barton said some of the fibers will be used by Southern's utilities network for internal communications, while the remainder will be used by Barton primarily to transmit information received from Barton's energy automation systems to its customers' corporate offices.
