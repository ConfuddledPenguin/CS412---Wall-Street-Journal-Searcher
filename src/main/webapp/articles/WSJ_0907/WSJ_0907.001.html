Who's News: New Hampshire Thrift Bank Shares Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

NEW HAMPSHIRE THRIFT BANC SHARES Inc. (New London, N.H.) -- Ralph B. Fifield Jr., most recently regional president of BankEast Corp., was named a director of this holding company, expanding board membership to 11, and was named executive vice president of its principal subsidiary, Lake Sunapee Savings Bank.
