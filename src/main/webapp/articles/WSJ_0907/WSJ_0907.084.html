Business Brief -- Permian Partners L.P.: Court Approves Settlement Of National Intergroup Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

National Intergroup Inc. said the Delaware Court of Chancery approved its settlement of litigation related to the company's tender offer for the outstanding cumulative convertible preference units of Permian Partners L.P. The tender offer is conditioned upon the issuance of a final court order approving terms of the previously announced settlement.

National Intergroup said it will mail unitholders a supplement to the offer with the revised terms of the contingent value rights, which are being offered pursuant to terms of the settlement. After the supplement is mailed, Pittsburgh-based National Intergroup plans to further extend the tender offer by a minimum of 10 days.

The drug wholesaler has offered to pay $3.85 for each preference unit of Permian Partners, a limited partnership involved in oil and gas gathering and marketing.
