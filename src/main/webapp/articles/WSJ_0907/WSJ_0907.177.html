Who's News: Supercomputer Systems Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

SUPERCOMPUTER SYSTEMS Inc. (Eau Claire, Wis.) -- Bruce N. Kasson, a former Cray Research Inc. sales executive, was named vice president, sales, at this supercomputer developer. William D. Macgeorge, a computer industry consultant formerly with International Business Machines Corp., was named director of manufacturing. Both positions are new.
