Business Brief -- Toro Co.: `Small Loss' to Be Posted For the Fiscal First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Toro Co., Minneapolis, said it expects to post a "small loss" for its first quarter ending Oct. 26, citing an anticipated decline in sales of snow throwers. In the year-earlier fiscal first quarter, the maker of outdoor maintenance and irrigation equipment earned $2.6 million, or 24 cents a share, on sales of $137 million.

The company also said it expects that per-share earnings for fiscal 1991 will be in the range of $2.20 to $2.40, compared with year-earlier profit of $16.6 million, or $1.55 a share. Toro said Lawn-Boy Inc., the lawn-mower maker it acquired last year from Outboard Marine Corp., should show a profit in fiscal 1991, reversing a substantial loss that reduced fiscal 1990 earnings.
