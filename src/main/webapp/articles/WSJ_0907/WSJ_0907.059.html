Consolidated Stores Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Consolidated Stores Corp. said August sales rose 31.6% to $52 million from $39.5 million a year earlier. Sales for the first seven months of fiscal 1990 were $342.3 million, up 15.6% from $296.2 million a year ago. Consolidated Stores sells close-out merchandise.
