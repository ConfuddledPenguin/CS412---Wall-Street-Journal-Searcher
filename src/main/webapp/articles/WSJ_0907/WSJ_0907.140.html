SCEcorp. Wins Lawsuit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

ROSEMEAD, Calif. -- SCEcorp. said it won summary judgments in an antitrust lawsuit filed against the company in 1983 by the city of Vernon. U.S. District Court Judge Mariana Pfaelzer dismissed the complaint, which alleged that the utility had failed to give the city greater access to its transmission lines and prevented the city from buying bulk power supplies from other sources. The suit also accused the company of failing to offer the city special rates.
