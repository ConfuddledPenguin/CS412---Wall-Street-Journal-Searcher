Santa Fe Unit Offers Issue of Debentures

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

CHICAGO -- Santa Fe Pacific Corp. said its SFP Pipeline Holdings Inc. unit is offering $219 million of variable-rate exchangeable debentures, due 2010, at a price of 92% of face amount. Santa Fe, a transportation and natural resources concern, said the unit will use about half of the net proceeds to pay a dividend to Santa Fe and will lend the remainder to Santa Fe.

In July, the Santa Fe unit filed a registration statement covering the offering. Santa Fe said it will use the funds to pay off part of its 16% redeemable senior subordinated debentures.

Goldman, Sachs & Co. is the managing underwriter of the offering, the company said.

Santa Fe said its newly formed unit will own all the capital stock of Santa Fe Pacific Pipelines Inc., which is the general partner in Santa Fe Pacific Pipeline Partners L.P. The partnership is one of the biggest independent U.S. pipeline carriers of refined petroleum products.
