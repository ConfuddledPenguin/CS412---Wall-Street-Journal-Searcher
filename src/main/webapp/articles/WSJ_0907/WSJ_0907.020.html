Business Brief -- Glaverbel S.A.: Belgian Glass Maker Posts 2% Rise in Profit for Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Glaverbel S.A. of Belgium, a glass making affiliate of Asahi Glass Co. of Japan, reported that first-half consolidated profit rose 2% to 1.51 billion Belgian francs ($47.2 million) from 1.48 billion francs a year earlier. Revenue rose 4.7% to 13.95 billion francs from 13.32 billion francs.

Per-share earnings increased 3.5% to 317 francs from 306 francs.
