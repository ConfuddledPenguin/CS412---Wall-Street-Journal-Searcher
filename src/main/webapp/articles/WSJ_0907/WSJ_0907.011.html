Fiat Unit's Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

MILAN -- Gilardini S.p.A., a diversified parts manufacturer controlled by auto maker Fiat S.p.A., announced Thursday it has purchased three manufacturers of car exhaust systems from ITT Industrie Riunite S.p.A. The terms of the transaction weren't disclosed.

The three companies -- Descam Nord SRL, IAO Sud SRL and ITT Industrie Riunite Sud SRL -- are units of ITT Corp. and have combined annual sales of 70 billion lire ($60 million) and a products catalog of some 1,400 items in the area of car exhaust systems.

The acquisition reinforces Gilardini's leadership position in Italy in the production of traditional and catalytic converters.
