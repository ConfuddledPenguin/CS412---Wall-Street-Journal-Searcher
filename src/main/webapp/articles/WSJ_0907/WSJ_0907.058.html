Hallwood Completes Stores Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

PITTSBURGH -- Hallwood Industries Inc., an automotive parts distributor formerly named Hecks Inc., said it completed the previously announced sale of its retail division to Retail Acquisition Corp., formed earlier this year by Jordache Enterprises Inc., a New York-based clothing maker, to purchase the stores. The retail division has 55 stores, which formerly operated as Heck's and Take 10 discount stores, and which have been renamed L.A. Joe Department Store.
