Vencor Files for Stock Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Vencor Inc., which operates a chain of hospitals, said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to the offering of two million common shares. Vencor said it will offer 1,850,000 shares, and the balance of 150,000 shares will be offered by certain holders. It said proceeds will be used to reduce debt and to buy and convert more hospital facilities.
