Exxon Corp. Receives $205.3 Million Order From Defense Agency

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

WASHINGTON -- Exxon Corp. received a $205.3 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

ABB Environmental Services Inc. was given a $100 million Navy contract for environmental restoration at Southern bases over 10 years.

Navajo Refining Co. won a $68 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

Honeywell Inc. received a $64.9 million Navy contract for torpedoes.

Sony Corp. of America was awarded a $34.4 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for disk-drive units.

Aircraft Braking System Corp. was issued a $50.1 million Air Force contract for F-16 aircraft brake parts.

Montana Refining Co. got an $11.2 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

ITT Corp. received an $11 million Navy contract for communications equipment.

Litton Systems Inc. was awarded a $10.1 million Navy contract for communications equipment.
