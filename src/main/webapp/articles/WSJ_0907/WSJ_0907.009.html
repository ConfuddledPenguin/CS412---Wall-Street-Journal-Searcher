Air Products to Build Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

ALLENTOWN, Pa. -- Air Products & Chemicals Inc. said it will build a hydrogen plant in Geismar, La., and expand its 30-mile hydrogen pipeline there to serve the Geismar chemicals complex operated by BASF Corp. Air Products said the new plant, which is expected to be operating by the fall of 1991, would initially produce more than 15 million standard cubic feet of hydrogen daily. A company spokesman declined to say how much the plant will cost to build.
