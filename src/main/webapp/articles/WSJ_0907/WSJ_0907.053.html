Who's News: Oracle Systems Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

ORACLE SYSTEMS Corp. (Redwood Shores, Calif.) -- Michael S. Fields was named president of the Oracle USA unit of this marketer of database management software. Mr. Fields, 45 years old, joined Oracle Systems in 1989 as vice president, Western operations, and most recently was vice president, U.S. field sales and consulting. He is a 20-year sales veteran of the software industry. Before Oracle, Mr. Fields was vice president, North American sales and service, at Applied Data Research, a database and software manufacturer.
