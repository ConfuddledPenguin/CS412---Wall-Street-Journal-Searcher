Who's News: Witco Chief Wishnick To Retire by Year-End; Toller to Succeed Him

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

NEW YORK -- William Wishnick, Witco Corp.'s 66-year-old chairman and chief executive officer, said he will retire by year's end. William R. Toller, vice chairman and chief financial officer, will succeed Mr. Wishnick Oct. 1 at the helm of the manufacturer of specialty chemical and petroleum products and engineered materials.

Mr. Toller, 50, had been president of Conoco Inc.'s Continental Carbon Co. unit when Witco acquired it in 1984. He became Witco's chief financial officer in 1986 and was named vice chairman in March 1990.

Denis Andreuzzi, 59, who was recently elected president and chief operating officer, was given the additional post of chairman of the executive committee.

Mr. Wishnick will remain a director and a member of the executive committee and the compensation and management committees. He joined Witco in 1949, becoming chairman in 1964 and chief executive in 1971. His father, Robert I. Wishnick, co-founded Witco in 1920.
