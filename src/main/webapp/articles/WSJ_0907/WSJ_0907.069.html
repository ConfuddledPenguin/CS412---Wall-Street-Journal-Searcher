Who's News: Terminal Data Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

TERMINAL DATA Corp. (Moorpark, Calif.) -- Jeffrey A. Wilson, 41 years old, president of the Adaptive Information Systems unit of Hitachi Ltd., was named president and chief executive officer of this designer and maker of document imaging systems, effective Sept. 17, succeeding David W. Krar Hutton, who resigned in July but remained temporarily as chief operating officer. Richard M. Drysdale, chairman since April, has been acting chief executive.
