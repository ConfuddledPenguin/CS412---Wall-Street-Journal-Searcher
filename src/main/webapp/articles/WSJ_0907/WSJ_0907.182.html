Swedish Unemployment Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's unemployment rate in August rose to 1.6% from 1.5% in July and in August 1989, Statistics Sweden said. The national statistics agency said 74,000 people were without work in August, up from July's 73,000 and the year-earlier 67,000.

The total size of Sweden's work force decreased to 4,650,000 in August from 4,674,000 in July.
