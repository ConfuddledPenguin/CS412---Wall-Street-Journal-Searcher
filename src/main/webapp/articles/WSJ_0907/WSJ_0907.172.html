Business Brief -- Worthen Banking Corp.: Appeals Court Bars Halt To Purchase From RTC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

Worthen Banking Corp., Little Rock, Ark., said a federal appeals court refused a last-ditch effort by Arkansas banking regulators to halt the bank-holding company's planned purchase of an insolvent thrift from the Resolution Trust Corp. The ruling by the U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals in St. Louis upheld an earlier decision by a three-member panel of the court that the RTC has the power to override state banking laws to sell insolvent thrifts and their branches to banks.

The Arkansas banking commissioner had requested the full court to stay the decision, which reversed an opinion by a district court judge. Worthen said it is working with the RTC to coordinate a closing of its purchase of certain assets and liabilities of Independence Federal Bank FSB of Batesville, Ark.

Arkansas banking officials couldn't be reached for comment.
