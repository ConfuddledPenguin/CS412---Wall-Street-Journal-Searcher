Price Waterhouse Cleared For a Solo Soviet Practice

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

NEW YORK -- Price Waterhouse said it received government approval to conduct its own accounting and auditing, tax and management consulting practice in the Soviet Union. The professional-services concern said it is the first such organization to be authorized to develop a wholly owned, multidisciplinary practice in the Soviet Union without entering into a joint venture.

Price Waterhouse has had an office in Moscow since September 1989. It is recruiting staff directly from Soviet universities. It has been staffing its office in Moscow with a Russian-speaking expatriate staff.

In June, the Soviet government granted permission to Arthur Andersen & Co., and Ernst & Young to perform audits on joint ventures of Soviet and Western companies. But those two firms established their practices in the Soviet Union through joint ventures with local entities.
