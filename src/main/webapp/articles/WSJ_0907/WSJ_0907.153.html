Who's News: Southmark's Galecke Named Operating Chief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

DALLAS -- Robert M. Galecke, executive vice president, treasurer and secretary of Southmark Corp., was named to the new position of chief operating officer of the real estate and thrift concern. Charles B. Brewer, executive vice president and general counsel, added the position of secretary; while David A. Stevenson, a vice president, succeeded Mr. Galecke as treasurer. Additionally, Richard G. Knoblock, president of the Electro Kinetics Division of Pacific Scientific Co., and Jack Lowe, chairman and chief executive of TD Industries, were appointed to the board. They succeeded James A. Colquitt and Arthur L. Crowe Jr., who served on an interim basis following Southmark's emergence from bankruptcy proceedings in mid-August.
