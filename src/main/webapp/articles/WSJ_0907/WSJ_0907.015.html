Armco Solicitation, Notes Offer Extended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

PARSIPPANY, N.J. -- Armco Inc. said it extended to next Wednesday from yesterday the expiration date of its consent solicitation and offer to purchase its 13.5% senior notes, due 1994. The steelmaker said that, as of last Wednesday, it had received consents representing about $29.3 million principal amount of the notes outstanding; those holders have agreed to Armco's amendment to pay a dividend of 10 cents a common share. Armco needs approval from a majority of the notes to pay the dividend. The company has $164 million in notes outstanding.

Armco said about $9.8 million principal amount of the $29.3 million of notes have been tendered and deposited. Armco previously said it would buy back as much as 30% of the notes for $1,070 plus interest for each $1,000 of notes.

Armco said it is considering amending the solicitation and offer. If the amendment is made, the solicitation and offer will be extended further.
