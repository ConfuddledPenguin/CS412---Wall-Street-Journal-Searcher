Computer Sciences Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Computer Sciences Corp. said it was awarded a four-year contract with a potential value of $70 million from the U.S. Army Communications & Electronics Command at Fort Monmouth, N.J. Computer Sciences said its Systems Engineering division with facilities in Shrewsbury, N.J., and Falls Church, Va., will provide a broad range of technical and management services to the command's center.
