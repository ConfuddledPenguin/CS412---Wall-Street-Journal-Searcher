Business Brief -- Polaroid Corp.: Venture Is Set With China For Instant-Camera Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Polaroid Corp., Cambridge, Mass., said it agreed with SMPIC, a Chinese photographic equipment maker and a unit of the Ministry of Light Industry, to create Polaroid of Shanghai Ltd., which will make and sell instant cameras in China. The new company, which also will package film, will initially have 130 employees.

Polaroid's venture into China is part of the photographic equipment concern's long-term growth strategy to expand instant photography beyond traditional geographic markets. In the past year, Polaroid opened facilities in the Soviet Union, India and Finland.

Initially, Polaroid of Shanghai will assemble Polaroid Supercolor 635CL instant cameras and package Polaroid 600 Plus instant film. Cameras and film will be marketed to business and industrial markets, as well as to China's more than 2,500 street photographers.
