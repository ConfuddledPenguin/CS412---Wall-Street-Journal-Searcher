El Paso Electric Rights Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

EL PASO, Texas -- El Paso Electric Co. said it adopted, subject to regulatory approval, a share-purchase rights plan under which it would declare a dividend distribution of one share-purchase right for each common share outstanding. The utility said the proposed plan can't be put into effect without prior approval from the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission and the New Mexico Public Service Commission, and wasn't adopted in response to any known takeover threats.

The rights would entitle shareholders to buy one newly issued common share at an exercise price of $25 and are exercisable only if a 15% or more stake in the company is acquired or a tender offer is made.
