Letters to the Editor: The Language Barrier

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

As Premier Robert Bourassa points out (Americas column, op-ed page, Aug. 17), currently high interest rates are not helping small business in Quebec. But another big factor not even mentioned is the province's continuing xenophobic intolerance of the English language. The welcome mat says "bienvenue" but something's getting lost in the translation. Gary A. Lyon

Westfield, N.J.
