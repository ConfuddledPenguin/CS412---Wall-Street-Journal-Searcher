West German Raw Steel Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Production of raw steel in West Germany in August fell 12% to 3.1 million metric tons from 3.6 million tons a year earlier, continuing a trend evident through the year, the Federal Statistics Office said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. The August output represented a daily production rate of 108,035 metric tons, down from a daily rate of 122,368 tons a year earlier. Both months had 29 production days.

In the first eight months of this year, West Germany's raw-steel production fell 7.7% to 25.7 million metric tons.

The volume of raw iron produced in August fell 14% from a year earlier to 2.4 million metric tons. In the first eight months, iron output declined 9.6% to 20.2 million tons.
