Autodie's Credit Extended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/07/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Autodie Corp. said it reached an agreement in principle to extend the forbearance agreement covering the Amended Revolving Credit Agreement with an undisclosed number of banks through Nov. 30, 1990. The maker of auto parts said the banks have agreed in principle to temporarily increase the amount of credit available to $58 million from $55 million during the forbearance period.

Autodie said it expects to sign definitive agreements with the banks in the next few days.
