Manor Care Buys Econo Lodges

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

SILVER SPRING, Md. -- Manor Care Inc. said it completed the purchase of Econo Lodges of America Inc. in a transaction valued at about $60 million. The Econo Lodges franchise system consists of 615 units in the U.S. and Canada as well as 85 Friendship Inns. Manor Care, a health-care services and lodging concern, said the acquisition gives its Choice Hotels International Inc. subsidiary a network of more than 2,300 hotels and motels with about 224,000 guest rooms in 19 countries.
