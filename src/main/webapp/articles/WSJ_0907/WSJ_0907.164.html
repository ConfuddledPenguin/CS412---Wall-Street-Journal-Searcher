Business Brief -- USX Corp.: Marathon Oil Unit to Install Platform in Gulf of Mexico

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/07/90

Marathon Oil Co., a unit of Pittsburgh-based USX Corp., said it will install a drilling and production platform in the Gulf of Mexico. The platform, located at a site called South Pass Block 86, will be used to develop more than 50 million equivalent barrels of hydrocarbon liquids discovered in 1988. The well tested at a rate of 9.6 million cubic feet of gas per day and 1,270 barrels of condensate per day.

The offshore Louisiana field was further delineated by a second well, which tested at a rate of 3,666 barrels of oil per day and 4.3 million cubic feet of gas per day.

The platform is being designed to handle production of 20,000 barrels of oil per day, with an initial gas handling capacity of 100 million cubic feet per day. Installation is set for early 1992.

Other partners in the development are Amerada Hess Corp., the Louisiana Land & Exploration Co. and OKC Limited Partnership. Each owns 25% of South Pass Block 86.
