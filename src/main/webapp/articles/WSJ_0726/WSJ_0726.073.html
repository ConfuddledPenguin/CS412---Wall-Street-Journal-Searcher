Who's News: Manufacturers Hanover Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

MANUFACTURERS HANOVER Corp. (New York) -- Carol Parry, 49 years old, was named a senior vice president of this bank holding company. Ms. Parry will be in charge of its regional banking group's business banking unit. She succeeds Jonathan Clayton, 53, senior vice president, who was named to head Manufacturers' professional credit unit. Ms. Parry had been president of C.J. Parry Associates, a New York management consulting company.
