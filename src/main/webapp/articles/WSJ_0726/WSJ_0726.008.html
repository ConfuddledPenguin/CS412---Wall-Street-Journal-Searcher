Healthcare Services Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

HUNTINGDON VALLEY, Pa. -- Healthcare Services Group Inc. said it began an offering of 875,000 common shares priced at $24.75 a share. The company said Morgan Stanley & Co. is the lead manager of the underwriting group.

Net proceeds from the sale will be used to fund the company's continued growth, to provide additional working capital and for general corporate purposes.

The company provides housekeeping, laundry and linen services to about 415 long-term care facilities, including nursing homes and retirement complexes, in 34 states.
