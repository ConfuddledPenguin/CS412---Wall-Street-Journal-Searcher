Microsoft Corp. Net In Fiscal 4th Period Increased by 76%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

REDMOND, Wash. -- Microsoft Corp. said net income for its fourth quarter rose 76% on a 53% increase in revenue. The company said net for the quarter ended June 30 was $79.9 million, or 65 cents a share, compared with $45.3 million, or 40 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue was $337 million, compared with $220.2 million.

For the year, net was $279.2 million, or $2.34 a share, a 64% rise from $170.5 million, or $1.52 a share. Revenue was $1.18 billion, a 47% increase from the previous year's $803.5 million.

"Microsoft is blessed with having a number of hot-selling new products," said Peter Rogers, an analyst with Robertson, Stevens & Co. "Microsoft Windows has really taken off. Their spreadsheets and word-processing package are in huge markets, and they are producing compelling products."
