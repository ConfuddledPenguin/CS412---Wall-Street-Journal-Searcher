Who's News: General Motors Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

GENERAL MOTORS Corp. (Detroit) -- Jeffrey E. Grant, a vice president of the Hughes Aircraft Co. unit, was named president of Hughes' Industrial Electronics Group, based in Torrance, Calif. He succeeds Arne Lavik who retired. Hughes Aircraft is an electronics company that specializes in high-technology products for military, scientific and commercial use.
