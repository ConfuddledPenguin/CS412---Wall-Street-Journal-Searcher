Marketing Brief -- American Financial Corp.: Two Retail Stores to Open, Featuring Cartoon Products

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

Hanna-Barbera Retail, a division of Hanna-Barbera Productions Inc. of Los Angeles, will open two retail stores featuring merchandise that will include toys, games, collectibles and licensed products from its cartoon series. Both stores will be located in Los Angeles-area shopping malls. The first, a 2,800-square-foot outlet, is scheduled to open at the Westside Pavilion next Wednesday. A 2,400-square-foot store will open in Del Amo Fashion Center Sept. 1, a company spokesman said.

Hanna-Barbera Productions is a unit of closely held American Financial Corp. of Cincinnati. The company produces the Flintstones and the Jetsons cartoon series.
