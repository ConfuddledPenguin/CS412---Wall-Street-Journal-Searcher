Business Brief -- Munich Re AG: Year's Group Profit Rose; Dividend to Remain Stable

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

Muenchener Rueckversicherungs-Gesellschaft (Munich Re) AG said that fiscal 1990 group net profit rose from year-earlier levels and that the company will leave its dividend unchanged at 10 Deutsche marks ($6.18) a share. The large, West German reinsurance group didn't provide detailed earnings data, but said premium income from reinsurance business rose 1.6% to 12.6 billion marks in the fiscal year that ended June 30 from 12.4 billion marks the year before

"Overall, the fiscal 1990 result will prove to be satisfactory," Munich Re said, adding that the strength of the Deutsche mark had a negative impact on premium income development. Without currency translation effects, total group premium income actually increased more than 4%.
