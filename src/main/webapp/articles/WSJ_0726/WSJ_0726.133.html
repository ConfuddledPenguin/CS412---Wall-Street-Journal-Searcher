Who's News: K-Tron International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

K-TRON INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Pitman, N.J.) -- Kevin C. Bowen, 38 years old, was named president of K-Tron Corp., the domestic feeder and blender manufacturing unit. He succeeds Robert Barnett, 41, who last September was named president of K-Tron Vertech Inc., a marketing unit for the blenders.
