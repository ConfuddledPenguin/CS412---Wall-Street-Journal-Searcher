Mentor Graphics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

BEAVERTON, Ore. -- Mentor Graphics Inc. said a shareholder filed a class-action suit alleging federal securities violations. The suit alleges that "the company withheld material and adverse information from the public during the period between April 20 and July 3," when the company announced that it didn't expect to meet previous earnings expectations. Chief Executive Officer and Chairman Thomas H. Bruggere called the suit frivolous.
