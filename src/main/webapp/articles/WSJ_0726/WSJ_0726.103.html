Business Brief -- Rohr Industries Inc.: Pact With Airbus Industrie For Housings Is Expanded

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

Rohr Industries Inc. said it reached a follow-on agreement with European manufacturer Airbus Industrie to supply engine housing systems for 800 airliners. The agreement is valued at more than $1 billion.

It calls for Rohr to furnish engine housing, or nacelle, systems for engines on two models of Airbus airliners, the A-320 and the A-321, initially in early 1992.

The A-321 is a stretched derivative of the current A-320-200 aircraft. Currently, Rohr is supplying nacelle systems for 200 Airbus A-320 airliners under a 1984 contract. The follow-on agreement expands the program to cover 1,000 airliners.

Airbus Industrie is an aircraft manufacturing consortium owned by Aerospatiale of France, Deutsche Airbus of West Germany, British Aerospace PLC of Britain and Construcciones Aeronauticas S.A. of Spain.

Chula Vista, Calif.-based Rohr designs, manufactures and supports aircraft engine nacelle systems and parts for commercial, military and business aircraft.
