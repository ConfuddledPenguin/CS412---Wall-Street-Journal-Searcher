Who's News: Mercedes-Benz of North America Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

MERCEDES-BENZ OF NORTH AMERICA Inc., Montvale, N.J., said Andrew Brimmer and Martin Hillenbrand were elected to the board. Mr. Brimmer is president of Brimmer & Co., an economic and financial consulting firm specializing in Federal Reserve monetary policy. Mr. Hillenbrand holds several positions at the University of Georgia, including professor of international relations and co-director for East-West trade policy studies. Mercedes-Benz of North America is the distribution arm of Daimler-Benz AG of West Germany.
