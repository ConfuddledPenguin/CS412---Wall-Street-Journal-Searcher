Who's News: JLG Picks L.D. Black To Be Its President

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

MCCONNELLSBURG, Pa. -- JLG Industries Inc. said directors elected L. David Black, formerly president and chief executive officer of Aro Corp., as JLG's president and chief operating officer. Mr. Black, 52 years old, succeeds Stephen R. Cecil, who resigned last month.

John L. Grove continues as chairman and chief executive officer of the manufacturer of specialized hydraulic machinery, including mobile aerial work platforms and cranes.

Charles H. Diller Jr., senior vice president and chief financial officer, was named executive vice president and chief financial officer.

Rao G. Bollimpalli, vice president, engineering; Michael Swartz, vice president, marketing, and Raymond F. Treml, vice president, manufacturing, were named senior vice presidents.
