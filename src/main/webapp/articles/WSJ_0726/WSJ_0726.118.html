Who's News: Ironstone Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

IRONSTONE GROUP Inc. (San Francisco) -- John Elting Treat, 43 years old, was elected a director of this wholesale plumbing and mechanical supplies distribution and energy exploration concern. He succeeds Steven N. Machtinger, 41, the company's chief legal counsel, who served as interim director.
