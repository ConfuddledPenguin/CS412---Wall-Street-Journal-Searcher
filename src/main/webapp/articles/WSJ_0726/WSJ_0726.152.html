International Brief -- British Aerospace PLC: Concern to Appeal Decision On Repayment of Subsidies

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

British Aerospace PLC said it will appeal the European Community Commission decision ordering the British company to repay subsidies that it received when it bought Rover Group in July 1988. Initially, British Aerospace said it would accept the commission's decision on repayment of #44.4 million ($80.6 million) of so-called sweeteners. But after what the company termed "a careful review" and independent professional advice, it said it must protect shareholders' interests and test its rights before the European Court of Justice.

The company said it has informed the government and shareholders of its decision.
