Allegheny International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

PITTSBURGH -- Allegheny International Inc. said directors voted to exempt the company from all provisions of the new Pennsylvania anti-takeover law. The company said the action isn't likely to have any impact on a current battle over a reorganization plan. Allegheny, a maker of consumer and industrial products that is operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code, has been attempting to fend off Japonica Partners, an investor group that is trying to gain control of the company.
