Who's News: Reynolds & Reynolds Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Reynolds & Reynolds Co. directors elected David R. Holmes, the company's president and chief executive officer, to the additional position of chairman. As chairman, Mr. Holmes, 49 years old, succeeds Terry D. Carder, who retired at age 55. Mr. Holmes succeeded Mr. Carder as chief executive in January 1989.

Reynolds & Reynolds produces business forms and computer systems, with a heavy concentration on auto dealerships.
