Seacoast Savings Bank Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

DOVER, N.H. -- Seacoast Savings Bank said it plans to create a bank holding company, Seacoast Capital Corp., to control the bank. Under the plan, which is subject to regulatory approval, shareholders of Seacoast Savings would receive one common share of Seacoast Capital for each share of the bank's common.

Seacoast Savings said the plan would provide "additional flexibility" and a "greater ability to attract potential investors."
