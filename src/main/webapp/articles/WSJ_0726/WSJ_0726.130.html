Nucor Corp. Steel Mill

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- Nucor Corp. said its new steel mill near Crawfordsville, Ind., operated profitably in June. The mill is operating at more than 60% of capacity, which is 800,000 tons of sheet steel a year. The mill is expected to reach capacity operation by the second quarter of 1991, Nucor said.

Nucor's shipments in the first half totaled 1,228,000 tons, up from 1,033,000 tons a year earlier. Production for the half totaled 1,668,000 tons, up from 1,285,000.
