Berisford International

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

LONDON -- Berisford International PLC, a food, financial services, property, commodities and industrial group, said its board decided not to proceed with a management buy-out of its property division. The company said it is "actively pursuing" other opportunities for the unit but didn't say what they might be.

Separately, the company said it completed the sale of NGI, its U.S. jewelry manufacturing business, to Michael Anthony Jewelers Inc. for $30 million.
