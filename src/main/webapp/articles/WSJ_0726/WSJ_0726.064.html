Norcen Energy Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

CALGARY, Alberta -- Norcen Energy Resources Ltd.'s propane unit said it agreed to acquire Skelgas Group Inc., a large U.S. retail marketer of propane, for $160 million. Norcen said the proposed acquisition by its Superior Propane Inc. unit, which is expected to be completed in September, would make Superior North America's third-largest propane marketer.

The purchase is subject to various regulatory filings and approvals, Norcen said. Norcen is a large oil and gas producer that is indirectly controlled by Toronto's Bronfman family.
