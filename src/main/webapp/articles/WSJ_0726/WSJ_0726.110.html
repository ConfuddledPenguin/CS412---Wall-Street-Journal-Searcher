International Brief -- Lasmo PLC: British Oil Concern Posts 17% Rise in After-Tax Net

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

Lasmo PLC, a major British independent oil group, reported a 17% rise in after-tax profit for the six months to June 30 on strong rises in production and sales. After-tax earnings rose to #32.4 million ($58.8 million) from #27.6 million.

Sales climbed 35% to #151 million from #112 million.
