Diasonics Stock Repurchases

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

MILPITAS, Calif. -- Diasonics Inc. said its board approved a plan to repurchase an additional five million shares of the company's stock, bringing to 17 million the number of shares authorized for repurchase. To date, the medical-equipment manufacturer has repurchased 8.5 million of its approximately 72.5 million common shares outstanding.

In composite trading yesterday on the American Stock Exchange, Diasonics shares closed at $3.25, up 25 cents.
