Business Brief -- Action Industries Inc.: Report of Operating Loss Is Expected for Fiscal Year

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

Action Industries Inc. said it will report an operating loss and additional restructuring charges for the fiscal year ended June 30. However, the company said it is unable to estimate the size of the losses. In fiscal 1989, Pittsburgh-based Action earned $1.8 million, or 33 cents a share, on sales of $134.2 million. The company sells housewares, gifts and other promotional merchandise to retailers, and provides advertising and other support services to help retailers build customer traffic.

The restructuring plan includes a sharp reduction in inventories and receivables, the sale of certain other nonproductive assets, curtailment of foreign operations and the elimination of about 25% of the nearly 250 office jobs at corporate headquarters, said R. Craig Kirsch, president and chief executive officer.

Action will focus on its core business, he said, pulling back from other activities.

Action stock rose 12.5 cents yesterday to close at $3 a share in American Stock Exchange composite trading.
