Banc One Unit to Add ATMs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Banc One Corp. said its Bank One, Texas unit has entered into an agreement to install automated teller machines (ATMs) in more than 350 Diamond Shamrock-owned gas station/convenience stores throughout Texas. The addition of the ATMs will bring Banc One's ATM network to over 1,000 machines nationally.

Banc One is a bank holding company with assets of $27.9 billion, exclusive of Bank One, Texas, which reported assets of $13.1 billion.
