Who's News: Russell Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

RUSSELL Corp. (Alexander City, Ala.) -- Dwight L. Carlisle Jr., 54 years old, has resigned as chief executive officer of this leisure and sportswear manufacturer, because of health problems. The company said Mr. Carlisle will remain as president, with responsibilities focused on manufacturing. E.C. Gwaltney, 72, Russell's chairman, will temporarily assume the chief executive officer's job until a successor can be found, the company said. Mr. Gwaltney served as Russell's chief executive officer from 1972 to 1988.
