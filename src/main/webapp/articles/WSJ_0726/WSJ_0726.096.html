Who's News: Astrocom Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

ASTROCOM Corp. (St. Paul, Minn.) -- Stephen C. O'Hara, the 40-year-old president and chief executive officer, added the title of chairman of this maker of digital communications equipment. As chairman, he succeeds Sidney N. Jerson, 60, who didn't stand for re-election when his term expired in May.
