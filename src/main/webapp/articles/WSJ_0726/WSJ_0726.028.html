REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Farm Facts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

If you don't know who really wants the farm bill the House is debating this week, economists at Purdue University have some answers from a survey of farmers in 21 states. Those with gross incomes of over $100,000 a year were most likely to support government farm programs. Nearly half of small farmers, for whom farm programs ostensibly are designed, want a gradual elimination of subsidies.
