Who's News: Laidlaw Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

LAIDLAW Inc. (Burlington, Ontario) -- Donald Jackson, 46-year-old president of this transportation and waste-management concern, was named to the additional position of chief executive officer, and Peter Widdrington, 60, non-executive chairman of John Labatt Ltd. and a Laidlaw director since 1986, was named chairman. They succeed Michael DeGroote, who said last month that he planned to resign from both posts. Canadian Pacific Ltd. has a 47.2% voting interest in Laidlaw.
