Dividend News: Farr Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

FARR Co., El Segundo, Calif., declared a 5-for-4 stock split and a quarterly dividend of six cents a share on post-split shares. Both are payable Sept. 7 to stock of record Aug. 17. The new rate is equal to the 7 1/2-cent dividend paid before the split. The producer of filtration systems currently has 2,836,277 common shares outstanding.
