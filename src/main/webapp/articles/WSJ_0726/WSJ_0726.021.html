Caesars World Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

LOS ANGELES -- Caesars World Inc. began its previously announced tender offer for the 13.4% stake it doesn't already own in Caesars New Jersey Inc. The $22-a-share offer for 2.2 million shares is scheduled to expire Aug. 21. Caesars World owns and operates hotels and casinos. Caesars New Jersey owns and operates Caesars Atlantic City, a hotel and casino.

Two shareholder suits seeking class-action status have been filed against the $48 million offer, charging that it is inadequate.
