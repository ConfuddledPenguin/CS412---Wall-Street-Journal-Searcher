Who's News: Mitsubishi Bank Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

MITSUBISHI BANK Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Yutaka Hasegawa was named senior executive officer of this commercial bank's North American headquarters and also director and general manager of its New York branch. Mr. Hasegawa, 50 years old, succeeds Takeshi Tange, who was named director and general manager of the international planning division at the bank's headquarters here. Previously, Mr. Hasegawa was general manager of the North American administration division.
