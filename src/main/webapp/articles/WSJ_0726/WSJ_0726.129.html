Business Brief -- Saskatchewan Oil & Gas Corp.: Regina Company to Acquire Certain Gulf Canada Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

Saskatchewan Oil & Gas Corp., or Saskoil, said it had agreed to acquire certain Saskatchewan oil and gas assets of Gulf Canada Resources Ltd. for about 38.2 million Canadian dollars (US$33 million). Saskoil of Regina, Saskatchewan, said the effective date of the purchase is April 1, though completion of the proposed transaction is still subject to various conditions. It is expected to close by Aug. 31.

Saskoil said the assets include Gulf Canada's working interests, ranging from 25% to 100%, in seven major heavy-oil producing areas, plus other minor producing areas, and a pipeline. The purchase would raise Saskoil's net daily oil output by about 4,700 barrels, to 31,000 barrels.

Gulf Canada, based in Calgary, Alberta, is a large oil and gas producer that is controlled by Toronto's Reichmann family.
