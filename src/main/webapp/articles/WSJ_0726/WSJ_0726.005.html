Who's News: Bowater Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

BOWATER Inc. (Darien, Conn.) -- John A. Rolls was named a director of this newsprint and paper producer. Mr. Rolls, 49 years old, fills the seat vacated by Robert C. Thompson, who died June 27 at age 66. Mr. Rolls is executive vice president and chief financial officer of United Technologies Corp., Hartford, Conn.
