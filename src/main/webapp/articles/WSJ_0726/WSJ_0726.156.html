International Brief -- Thai Airways International Ltd.: Thai National Carrier Posts Record Earnings in 1st Half

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

Thailand's national carrier, Thai Airways International Ltd., reported record earnings for the first six months of the fiscal year. The airline made 4.1415 billion baht ($161.5 million) in pretax profits for the period, the company said. That represented a 17% increase over the year-earlier period and the highest half-year profit in the airline's 30 years of operation.

During the six-month period, the airline carried 4.2 million passengers, 12.3% more than during the year-earlier period.
