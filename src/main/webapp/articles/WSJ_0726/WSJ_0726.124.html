Business Brief -- AST Research Inc.: Firm Reports Record Net Of $15.2 Million for Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

AST Research Inc. reported record fiscal fourth-quarter net income of $15.2 million, or $1.18 a share, compared with $2.5 million, or 21 cents a share for the year-ago quarter. The Irvine, Calif.-based company said revenue for the quarter reached $151.8 million, from $123.5 million last year, a 23% revenue increase for the period ended June 30. Net income for the year was $35.1 million, compared with a net loss of $7.5 million in 1989. Annual revenue increased 17%, to $533.8 million for fiscal 1990, from $456.5 million.

AST attributed the growth, in part, to its overseas expansion. International sales rose 30% in the year, the company said. AST develops and manufactures high-performance computing solutions with personal and industrial computer systems. The company's stock closed yesterday at $22.625, up $2.25 in over-the-counter trading.
