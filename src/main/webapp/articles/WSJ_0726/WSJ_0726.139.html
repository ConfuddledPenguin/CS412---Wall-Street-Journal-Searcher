Business Brief -- Adia Services Inc.: Second-Quarter Net Drops At Employment Concern

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

Adia Services Inc. said a sluggish economy caused second-quarter earnings to dip slightly, to $5 million, or 40 cents a share, compared with $5.4 million, or 43 cents a share in the year-earlier period. Revenue for the quarter rose 13% to $166.6 million from $148 million a year earlier. For the first six months, the Menlo Park, Calif., company's net income was $10 million, or 80 cents a share, compared with $10.3 million, or 83 cents a share in the year-earlier period. Revenue for the six months were $327.5 million compared with $289.9 million for the prior fiscal year.

Switzerland-based Adia S.A., one of the world's largest temporary employment agencies, owns a 66% stake in Adia Services.
