Dividend News: Chevron Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

CHEVRON Corp. boosted its quarterly dividend to 77.5 cents a share from 70 cents, payable Sept. 10 to stock of record Aug. 6. The San Francisco-based oil company said the increase reflected the company's strong earnings and cash flow for the first half of 1990. Earlier this week Chevron reported a 60% increase in second-quarter net income to $648 million, or $1.83 a share, after $58 million of gains. Revenue rose 8.5% to $8.9 billion from $8.2 billion.
