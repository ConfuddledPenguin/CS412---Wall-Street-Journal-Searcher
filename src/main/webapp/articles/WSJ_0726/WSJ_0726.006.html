Ikos Systems Stock Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/26/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- Ikos Systems Inc. said it began an initial public offering for up to 3.1 million shares of common stock at $6.50 per share. The company markets and develops systems for integrated circuits and electronic systems. Proceeds will be used for working capital and general corporate purposes, including research and development, the company said.

The company said that after the offering it will have 10.7 million common shares outstanding. The stock will trade under the symbol IKOS.
