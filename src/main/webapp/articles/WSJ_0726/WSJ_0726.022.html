Thiokol Buy-Back Approved

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

OGDEN, Utah -- Thiokol Corp. said its board approved a plan to repurchase up to 5% of its 19.2 million common shares outstanding. The shares will be bought periodically in the open market. Thiokol, which makes missiles and other defense and aerospace products, said it believes the stock is a good investment at current levels.

In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, Thiokol closed at $10.875, up 25 cents.
