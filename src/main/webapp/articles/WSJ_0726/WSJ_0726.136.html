McDonnell Gets Jobs From Air Force, Navy

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/26/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. received $298 million in contracts for Air Force F-15 aircraft and electronics equipment, and for Navy Harpoon missile parts.

General Dynamics Corp. got a $43.3 million Navy contract for the SSN-21 submarine program.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. received a $41.7 million Air Force contract for F-16 aircraft radar.

Verac Inc. was awarded a $24 million Air Force contract for electronics intelligence equipment.

Raytheon Co. got a $17.6 million Navy contract for Sidewinder missile parts.
