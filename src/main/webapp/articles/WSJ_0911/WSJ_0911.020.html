Hondo Oil & Gas Co

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

ROSWELL, N.M. -- Hondo Oil & Gas Co. said it discovered oil in Irion County, Texas. The well flowed at a rate of 503 barrels of 43 degree gravity oil per day through a 26/64 inch choke at a depth of 9,418 feet.

Hondo Oil owns 100% of the well.
