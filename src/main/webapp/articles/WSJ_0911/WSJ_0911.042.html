Equity Market Index Is Altered

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

NEW YORK -- Wheelabrator Technologies Inc. was dropped from the pollution-control and waste-management industry group of the Dow Jones Equity Market Index, because 55% of the concern now is owned by Waste Management Inc., already in the group. Chambers Development Co. Class A was added to the group. Both moves were effective yesterday.
