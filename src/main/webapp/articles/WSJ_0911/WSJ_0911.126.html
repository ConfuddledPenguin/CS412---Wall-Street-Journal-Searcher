Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

LATEST NEWS: United Press International, a unit of Infotechnology Inc., and its partners, States News Service and Financial World magazine, said they will distribute the Latest News newspaper on the Trump Shuttle. The paper, updated hourly from 2 to 7 p.m., will no longer be distributed on Pam Am's shuttle; negotiations on a long-term contract broke down.
