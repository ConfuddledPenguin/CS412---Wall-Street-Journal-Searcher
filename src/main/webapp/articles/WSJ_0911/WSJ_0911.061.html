Who's News: Sudbury Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

SUDBURY Inc. (Cleveland) -- William J. O'Neill Jr., president and chief executive officer of Clanco Management Corp. and Anthony G.V. Owen, director of Suter PLC, were named directors of this manufacturer of industrial products. Mr. O'Neill and Mr. Owen replace two members who resigned. There are eight members on the board.
