Raw-Steel Output Fell 0.7% in Latest Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 0.7% last week to 1,886,000 tons from 1,900,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 11.1% from the 1,697,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 84.5% of its capability last week, compared with 85.1% the previous week and 76.4% a year ago.
