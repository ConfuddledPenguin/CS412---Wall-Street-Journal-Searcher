Business Brief -- American Telephone & Telegraph: Financing Unit Purchases Subsidiary of Ford Motor

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

AT&T Capital Corp. said it purchased U.S. Instrument Rentals Inc., a subsidiary of Ford Motor Co.'s U.S. Leasing International unit. Terms weren't disclosed. The financing unit of American Telephone & Telegraph Co., the New York telecommunications giant, said U.S. Instrument's managers will remain in place.

U.S. Instrument, with assets of $120 million, finances general-purpose, chemical and analytical test equipment and data equipment. Based in San Mateo, Calif., it has 280 employees with distribution centers in San Carlos, Calif., and Dallas.

Thomas C. Wajnert, president and chief executive officer of AT&T Capital, said U.S. Instrument "will serve as the core of a new line of business for AT&T Capital."

Separately, AT&T said it began its Global Messaging service in Canada. Canada is the fourth country where AT&T has introduced the facsimile and electronic mail service. Although the portfolio of messaging services is a "relatively small piece of the total communications market, the segment promises dramatic growth in the coming years," AT&T said.
