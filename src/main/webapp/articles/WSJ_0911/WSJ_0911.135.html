Business Brief -- Rubbermaid Inc.: Regulators Approve Proposal To Acquire Eldon Industries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

Rubbermaid Inc. said it received regulatory clearance to proceed with its previously announced acquisition of Eldon Industries Inc., an office products maker based in Inglewood, Calif., in a stock transaction valued at about $233.5 million. Separately, Eldon said it will have a special shareholders meeting on Oct. 29 to consider its acquisition by Rubbermaid, a housewares maker based in Wooster, Ohio.

Rubbermaid said the exact number of shares it will issue to complete the transaction will be determined by the average price of Rubbermaid shares during the 10 trading days ending Oct. 24.
