Who's News: Paramount Communications Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

PARAMOUNT COMMUNICATIONS Inc. (New York) -- Nicholas J. Ashooh was named vice president-corporate communications of this entertainment concern. Mr. Ashooh, 35 years old, succeeds Jerry Sherman, who is retiring to establish his own consulting firm. Mr. Ashooh was formerly director of corporate communications for Public Service Co. of New Hampshire.
