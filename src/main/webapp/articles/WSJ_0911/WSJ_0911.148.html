Vornado Inc. REIT Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

SADDLE BROOK, N.J. -- Vornado Inc. said its board authorized management to convert the company into a real estate investment trust. The real estate leasing company said the conversion would involve, among other things, payout to shareholders either in cash or securities of all accumulated earnings of about $80 million.

Also under the conversion plan, the five largest stockholders, which have a 70% stake in the company, could hold no more than 50% of company shares, Vornado said.
