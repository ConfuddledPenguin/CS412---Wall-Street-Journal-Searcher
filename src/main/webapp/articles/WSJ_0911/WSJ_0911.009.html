Rhone-Poulenc to Sell Units

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

PARIS -- Rhone-Poulenc S.A. said it will sell more assets that aren't central to its core activities to help defray the cost of its $2 billion acquisition of Rorer Group Inc. of Fort Washington, Pa. Rhone-Poulenc Chairman Jean-Rene Fourtou said asset sales for all of 1990 could give the chemicals and pharmaceuticals group between one billion francs ($190 million) and 1.5 billion francs ($285 million) in fresh cash. He declined to identify which of the group's sectors would be affected by the new asset sales, however.
