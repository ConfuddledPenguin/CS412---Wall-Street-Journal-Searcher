Business Brief -- Amdura Corp.: United Hardware Submits Top Bid for Concern's Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

Amdura Corp. said that United Hardware Distributing Co. submitted the highest bid for assets of its Amdura National Distribution Co. unit during an auction in federal bankruptcy court. United Hardware, a hardware cooperative based in Plymouth, Minn., bid $21.5 million in cash for the Amdura unit's inventory and distribution center in Milbank, S.D. The assets were placed on the auction block after United Hardware topped an earlier offer from Our Own Hardware Co.

The United Hardware bid is subject to the approval of the bankruptcy judge overseeing Amdura's reorganization under Chapter 11 of the Bankruptcy Code. Amdura said a hearing on the sale is expected before Sept. 25.

Amdura, a Denver hardware distributor, said the bulk of the proceeds from the sale will be held in escrow for future payments to creditors. About $1 million will be placed in escrow to pay employee benefit claims and rebates due the unit's customers.
