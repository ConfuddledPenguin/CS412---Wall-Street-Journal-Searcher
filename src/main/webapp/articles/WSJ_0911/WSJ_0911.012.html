WestAir August Traffic Up 30%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- WestAir Holding Inc. said its August traffic rose 30% to 63.2 million revenue passenger miles from 48.6 million miles a year earlier. Load factor fell to 47.6% from 48.2%. For the eight months ended Aug. 31, traffic rose 35% to 427.5 million revenue pasenger miles from 316.4 million miles a year earlier. Load factor slipped to 44.7% from 45.8%.

A revenue passenger mile is one passenger flown one mile. Load factor is the percentage of seats occupied.
