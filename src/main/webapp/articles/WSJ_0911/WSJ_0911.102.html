Who's News: Republic New York Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

REPUBLIC NEW YORK Corp. (New York) -- John Heffer was named president and chief operating officer at Republic Factors Corp., a subsidiary of this bank holding company. Mr. Heffer, 44 years old, succeeds David Rubin, 64, who became chairman and chief executive officer last July. Mr. Heffer had been president and operating chief at CIT Group/Factoring, Livingston, N.J.
