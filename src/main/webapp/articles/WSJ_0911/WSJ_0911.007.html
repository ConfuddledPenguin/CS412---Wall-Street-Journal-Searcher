Who's News: Xytronyx Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

XYTRONYX Inc. (San Diego) -- Larry O. Bymaster, 48 years old, formerly vice president and a corporate officer of Cytotech Inc., was named president and chief operating officer of this biotechnology concern. As president, he succeeds Peter Baram, 63, who remains chairman and chief executive officer. The operating chief's post is new.
