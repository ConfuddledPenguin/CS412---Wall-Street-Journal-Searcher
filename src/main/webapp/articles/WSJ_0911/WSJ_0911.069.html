Who's News: Cowles Media Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

COWLES MEDIA Co. (Minneapolis) -- William D. Holiber was named vice president and group publisher at Hanson Publishing group, a Stamford, Conn., affiliate of this closely held newspaper and special-interest magazine and book company. Mr. Holiber, 35, will retain his post as publisher of Hanson's Inside Media, a national publication for media executives, and will assume additional responsibilities for marketing and editorial functions at Hanson's Catalog Age and Direct magazines.
