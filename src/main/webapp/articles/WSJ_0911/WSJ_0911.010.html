Southwest Airlines' Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

DALLAS -- Southwest Airlines said its August traffic rose 11% to 990 million revenue passenger miles from 894 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline's August load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, rose to 68.4% from 67.4%.

In the first eight months of this year, Southwest's traffic rose 6.7% to 6.79 billion revenue passenger miles from 6.37 billion a year earlier. The load factor declined to 62.7% from 65.2%.
