Mapco Unit Defense Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

WASHINGTON -- Mapco Alaska Petroleum Inc., a unit of Mapco Inc., won an $82.5 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

UNC Support Services, a unit of UNC Inc., got a $28.6 million Navy contract for helicopter maintenance.

Turbocombustor Technology Inc., a unit of Sequa Corp., received a $17.1 million Air Force contract for jet engine components.

Crysen Refining Co. got a $16 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., was awarded a $14.3 million Army contract for TOW missiles.
