Northwest Air August Traffic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- Northwest Airlines reported a monthly record of 5.45 billion revenue passenger miles for August, an 8.3% increase from 5.03 billion miles flown in August 1989. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The NWA Inc. unit said its load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, was unchanged at 76.8%.

For the first seven months of 1990, the airline said it flew 35.1 billion revenue passenger miles, a 14% increase from 30.8 billion miles a year earlier.
