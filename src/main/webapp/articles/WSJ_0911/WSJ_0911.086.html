Technology Brief -- Cetus Corp.: Firm Is Sued by Erbamont In Patent-Infringement Case

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

Cetus Corp. said Erbamont Inc., Stamford, Conn., sued Cetus, alleging patent infringement. Cetus, of Emeryville, Calif., said the suit was filed in federal court in Cleveland. Cetus, a biotechnology company, said Erbamont alleged that a Cetus joint venture's sales of doxorubicin, a widely prescribed chemotherapeutic agent, violates a patent held by a foreign subsidiary of Erbamont.

Cetus said it is continuing to market the product. Erbamont officials couldn't be reached for comment.
