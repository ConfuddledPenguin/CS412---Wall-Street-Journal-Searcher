Who's News: CalMat Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

CALMAT Co. (Los Angeles) -- Ronald C. Hadfield, senior vice president and chief financial officer, was elected to the newly created position of executive vice president, finance and administration. Norman A. Dangelo was elected to the new post of vice president, management information systems. He had been vice president, information systems for AST Research Inc. CalMat makes cement and rock products.
