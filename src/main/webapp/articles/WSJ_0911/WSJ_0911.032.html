Comair August Traffic Rose 35%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

CINCINNATI -- Comair Holdings Inc. said August traffic at its Comair unit rose 35% to 35.8 million revenue passenger miles from 26.5 million a year earlier. For the year to date, the regional carrier's traffic rose 36% to 250.5 million revenue passenger miles from 184.7 million. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
