Enterra to Buy Concerns' Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

HOUSTON -- Enterra Corp. said it agreed to buy substantially all the assets of Oilfield Rental Tools Ltd., Singapore, and Petrodrill Oilfield Services Ltd., Perth, Australia, for about $13 million in cash and stock. Oilfield Rental Tools and Petrodrill operate oil-field tool rental businesses in Southeast Asia and the South Pacific. Enterra is a tool-rental, service and pipeline equipment concern. The transaction is expected to close in late September.
