One Bancorp Exchange Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

PORTLAND, Maine -- One Bancorp said it will begin today a tender offer for its $36 million of 7 1/2% convertible subordinated debentures outstanding due 2001. The commercial banking concern is offering $100 in cash and three shares of a new issue of preferred for each $1,000 face amount of the debentures. Each share of preferred will have a liquidation value of $10 a share and can be exchanged for 26.67 One Bancorp common shares at any time. The preferred will carry a 7% non-cumulative dividend rate, but the financially troubled company said it doesn't plan to pay a dividend in the foreseeable future. The tender offer expires Oct. 10 and is subject to at least 75% of the debentures being tendered.
