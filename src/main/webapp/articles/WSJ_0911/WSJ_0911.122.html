Business Brief -- Corporate Data Sciences Inc.: Spinoff to Aril Shareholders Declared Effective by Board

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

Corporate Data Sciences Inc. said its board declared effective a spinoff to shareholders of Aril Group Inc. The transaction is part of a restructuring of Corporate Data into two separately traded companies. Corporate Data said its 22,119,256 Aril shares will be distributed pro rata to Corporate Data shareholders of record on Jan. 22, 1990, on the basis of 1.63 shares of Aril for each Corporate Data share held. Corporate Data said its agent would begin notifying shareholders immediately to make the distribution.

Corporate Data said it plans to continue its restructuring through a merger with the company's 90%-owned subsidiary, Consolidated Packaging Corp. After the merger, Corporate Data said its shares will become shares of Consolidated Packaging, a paper concern. Aril is a New York-based holding company with kitchen-cabinet operations.

The spinoff, originally set for January, was delayed because of Securities and Exchange Commission "paper work" and Aril's purchase from Corporate Data of the kitchen-cabinet operations, Corporate Data said. In exchange for those operations, Corporate Data received Aril shares that boosted to 72% Corporate Data's stake in Aril.
