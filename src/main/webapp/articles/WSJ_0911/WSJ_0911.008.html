Metro Air August Traffic Rose

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

DALLAS -- Metro Airlines said its August traffic rose 33% to 62.2 million revenue passenger miles from 46.9 million in August 1989. Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, increased to 60.1% from 55.6%. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. For the first eight months of 1990, Metro said it flew 425 million revenue passenger miles, a 41% increase from 302 million a year earlier. Load factor rose to 55.7% from 52.1%.
