  • Date: 09/11/90

LORAL Corp. (New York) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its ratings on about $410 million of this defense electronic company's debt and removed Loral's ratings from its CreditWatch surveillance list. The rating concern downgraded Loral's senior debt to triple-B from triple-B-plus, and subordinated debt to triple-B-minus from triple-B. S&P cited Loral's increased financial risk from its pending acquisition of the Ford Aerospace unit of Ford Motor Co. Shearson Lehman Brothers Inc., an American Express Co. unit, will hold a 49% stake in the unit, Loral has said. Initially, Loral will contribute about $150 million to the new entity, funded mainly by borrowings, S&P said. Loral also will have the option to increase its Ford Aerospace ownership, which could require significant additional funds over time.
