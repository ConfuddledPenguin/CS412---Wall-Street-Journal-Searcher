Western Union Offer Extended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

UPPER SADDLE RIVER, N.J. -- Western Union Corp., a communications and financial services company, said it extended to Sept. 24 from last Friday its offer to exchange new notes and common shares for two issues of notes. As of Friday, Western Union said, $327.5 million of the $500 million total of 19.25% notes and $1.4 million of the $30 million of 16% notes outstanding had been tendered in the offer, part of a recapitalization plan.
