DH Technology Inc. Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

SAN DIEGO -- DH Technology Inc., a maker of printers and printing supplies, said its board approved a plan to buy back as many as 150,000, or about 3%, of the approximately 4.8 million shares outstanding in transactions from time to time. The company said the shares bought back would eventually be distributed to certain managers and other employees as part of a stock option compensation plan. DH said its board might approve additional repurchases later, depending on stock prices. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, DH shares closed at $8.25, up 25 cents.
