Who's News: Citicorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

CITICORP (New York) -- Michael F. Chahine was named New York representative at Saudi American Bank, an affiliate of Citibank, this banking and financial services company's largest subsidiary. The 40-year-old Mr. Chahine succeeds Peter B. Eliot, 34, who will assume a new post at Citibank. Mr. Chahine, previously vice president of Citibank's private-banking division in the South Central region, will act as chief liaison for Saudi American Bank's clients in the U.S., Turkey and Saudi Arabia.
