Who's News El Paso Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/11/90

EL PASO ELECTRIC Co. (El Paso, Texas) -- John E. Droubay, 52 years old, formerly treasurer of the Utah Power & Light Co. unit of PacifiCorp, was named vice president and treasurer of this utility company, succeeding Gary Hedrick, 35, who was appointed vice president, financial planning and rate administration, a new post. James Mayhew, 35, formerly vice president, rate and regulatory affairs, was named vice president, rate and energy utilization, also a new position.
