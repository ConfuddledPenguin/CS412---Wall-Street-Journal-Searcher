Chinese Industrial Output Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

BEIJING -- China's industrial output rose 4.6% in August from a year earlier, but profits of industrial enterprises continued to fall, official reports said. Much of the increased production simply went into already large inventories, the English-language China Daily said.

The Economic Daily, meanwhile, said profits of industrial enterprises fell 56% in the first seven months of the year from the like period of 1989, while the amount owed by companies nearly doubled. It didn't disclose actual amounts of money.
