Westmoreland Coal Signs Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Westmoreland Coal Co. said it signed a six-year extension of its contract to provide low-sulfur coal to Appalachian Power Co., a unit of American Electric Power Co. The new agreement extends an existing contract for Westmoreland to supply about 600,000 tons of coal annually. The extension begins Dec. 1, 1995.

Although Westmoreland declined to place a value on the contract, a spokesman said the coal will be delivered at prices higher than current market base prices of $25 to $27 a ton.

American Electric is based in Columbus, Ohio.
