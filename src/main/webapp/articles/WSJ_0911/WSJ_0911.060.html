Ingersoll-Rand Co. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

WOODCLIFF LAKE, N.J. -- Ingersoll-Rand Co. said it agreed to acquire the paving and road-widening machinery businesses of Barber-Greene Co., a unit of Astec Industries Inc., Chattanooga, Tenn. Ingersoll-Rand didn't disclose the terms, but Astec said that the purchase price was based on a formula and that at June 30, the price would have been about $25 million.

Under the agreement, Ingersoll-Rand also would acquire Barber-Greene's manufacturing facility in DeKalb, Ill. Ingersoll-Rand makes construction equipment and mining machinery, among other things. Astec Industries manufactures road-making equipment.
