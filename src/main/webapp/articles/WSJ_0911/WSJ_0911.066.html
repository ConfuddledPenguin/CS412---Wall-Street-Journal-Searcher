Technology: Software Toolworks License

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

NOVATO, Calif. -- Software Toolworks Inc., a software publisher, said it received a license from Tokyo-based Nintendo Corp. to market Nintendo computer game cartridges in Japan. Terms weren't disclosed. "There are currently over 15 million Nintendo Entertainment System units in Japan, with relatively little game cartridge penetration by Nintendo licensees," said Les Crane, Software Toolworks chairman and chief executive officer.

Software Toolworks said it expects to release "Days of Thunder," a stock-car racing game based on the film of the same name, and a building game called "Loopz" in Japan by the end of March.
