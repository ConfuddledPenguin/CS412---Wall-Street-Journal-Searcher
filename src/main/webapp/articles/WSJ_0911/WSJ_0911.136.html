Who's News: Intermark Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/11/90

INTERMARK Inc. (La Jolla, Calif.) -- Frederick B. Chaney and Paul N. Schwartz, former directors of Triton Group Ltd., were elected directors of this holding company. The election follows the merger of Triton into a unit of Intermark last month. Mr. Chaney is president and chief executive officer of Vedax Sciences Corp. Mr. Schwartz is senior vice president-corporate development with Maxxam Inc. Their addition increases the board to eight members.
