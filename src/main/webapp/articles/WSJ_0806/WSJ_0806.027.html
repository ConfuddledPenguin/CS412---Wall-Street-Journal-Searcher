Mark IV Industries Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

AMHERST, N.Y. -- Mark IV Industries Inc. said its board approved the repurchase of up to 1.5 million shares, or about 11% of the company's 14 million common shares outstanding, in open-market or private transactions. Since April 1989, the maker of industrial products has purchased about 3.3 million common shares under previously authorized stock repurchase programs. The company said it will repurchase the stock over a period of time because it believes its stock to be "an appropriate investment."
