Crompton & Knowles Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Crompton & Knowles Corp. agreed to acquire the business and certain assets of Atlantic Industries Inc., a Nutley, N.J., producer of dyes for the textile and paper industries, from Great American Management & Investment Inc., Chicago. Terms weren't disclosed. Crompton & Knowles, a producer of dyes, specialty ingredients for foods and pharmaceuticals and specialized extrusion equipment, said the cash acquisition is expected to be completed in the third quarter.
