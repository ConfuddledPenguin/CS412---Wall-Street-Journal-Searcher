Bollinger Is Awarded $91.3 Million Contract For Navy Patrol Boats

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

WASHINGTON -- Bollinger Machine Shop & Shipyard Inc. received a $91.3 million Navy contract for eight coastal patrol boats.

Southwest Marine Inc. won a $42.1 million Navy contract for repair of dock landing ships.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. was awarded a $39 million contract for Navy T-45 trainers and Air Force F-15 aircraft parts.

TRW Inc. received a $34.1 million Air Force contract for space operations support work.

Lufkin Industries got a $14.5 million Navy contract for training ammunition.

Textron Inc. was granted a $14.1 million Navy contract for helicopter improvements.

Raytheon Co. got a $12.2 million Army contract for Stinger antitank missiles.
