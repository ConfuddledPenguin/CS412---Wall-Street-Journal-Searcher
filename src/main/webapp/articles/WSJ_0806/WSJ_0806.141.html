Baker Hughes Inc. to Sell Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

HOUSTON -- Baker Hughes Inc. said it agreed to sell the assets of its Totco division to Varco International Inc. for $20 million in cash and $20 million in stock. Baker Hughes, an oil-services concern, said Totco makes and markets equipment for oil drilling and other industrial uses. Totco had revenue of about $25.7 million for the year ended June 30.

Baker Hughes currently owns three million shares of Varco's about 25.7 million common shares outstanding, and a warrant to acquire an additional one million shares. Varco, Orange, Calif., is an energy industry tool and equipment maker.
