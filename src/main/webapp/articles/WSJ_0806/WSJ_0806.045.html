Letters to the Editor: Light on the Shadows

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

With regard to your July 13 page-one article "Shadow Life" about movie producer Dino Conte: I have been working in the entertainment industry for 18 years, as an executive and, as of late, as a screen writer. Mr. Conte has been an inspiration to me and to my work. It was Mr. Conte who purchased my first screenplay and personally assisted me with its rewrites. I thank Mr. Conte for the unselfish nurturing he gave me; he has helped me persevere.

I fail to recognize the article's purpose to enlighten me or your readers. The article read as though it were a fictional and malicious press release.

David Mirsky

Beverly Hills, Calif.
