Business Brief -- Loral Corp.: Definitive Pact Set to Buy Ford's Aerospace Subsidiary

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

Loral Corp., a New York-based defense contractor, said it definitively agreed to buy the Ford Aerospace Corp. unit of Ford Motor Co., Dearborn, Mich., for $715 million and the assumption of some debt. Loral and Ford said jointly that the transaction is subject to final regulatory approval and customary closing conditions, and that they expect to complete the deal within three months.

Loral earlier won a bidding contest for Ford Aerospace. On Friday, Loral Chairman Bernard Schwartz and a Ford spokesman said the terms of the transaction are basically unchanged from the bid made late last month. At the time, a person familiar with the bidding negotiations said the cash part of Loral's bid was about $811 million. But Mr. Schwartz said Loral and Ford hadn't disclosed any figures prior to Friday's announcement.

Loral said it doesn't plan now to shed any Ford Aerospace operations or make any major changes.
