Enimont, Atochem Stakes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

MILAN -- Enimont S.p.A. said it agreed with Atochem S.A. of France to buy an additional 20% stake in their jointly owned steamcracker unit at Dunkirk. Atochem and Enimont each also agreed to buy a 10% shareholding in Qapco, an ethylene and polyethylene producer owned by the Qatar General Petroleum Corp. No financial details of the two transactions were disclosed.

The transactions will further consolidate Enimont's leadership position in the European ethylene and polyethylene market.
