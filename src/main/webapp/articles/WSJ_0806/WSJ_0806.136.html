Who's News: Amerifirst Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

AMERIFIRST BANK (Miami) -- Laurence B. Brody, a Miami dentist and director of this thrift, was named chairman. Dr. Brody succeeds M. Lee Pearce, who resigned, he said, because he felt he could make no "further contribution" to the thrift's attempts to turn around. Since last spring when Dr. Pearce became chairman, the bank said, AmeriFirst had adopted several cost-cutting measures, which "were not enough to rescue AmeriFirst from its disastrous venture into commercial real estate."
