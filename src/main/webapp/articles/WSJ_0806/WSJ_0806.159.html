Business Brief -- Diasonics Inc.: Company Settles Dispute With Toshiba Unit on Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/11/90

Diasonics Inc. said it settled a dispute with a Toshiba Corp. unit over the final sale price to Toshiba of Diasonic's ultrasound business. Under the agreement, the Toshiba unit will pay Diasonics an additional $13.5 million, Diasonics said in Milipitas, Calif.

Diasonics completed the sale of its MRI division to Toshiba America Medical Systems Inc. last November for $167.5 million plus the assumption of certain debt. But in February Diasonics asked Toshiba for an additional $23 million to reflect certain equity changes in the division prior to the sale's closing date. The Toshiba unit balked, instead demanding a $19 million refund from Diasonics.

The accord resolves the disputes outstanding with Toshiba, a Diasonics official said. Diasonics' stock closed at $3.3125, down 18.75 cents, on the American Stock Exchange. The company disclosed the accord after the close of trading yesterday.

Corrections & Amplifications

DIASONICS Inc. sold its magnetic resonance imaging business to a Toshiba Corp. unit. In the July 11 edition, the business that Diasonics sold was misidentified.
