International Brief -- Bongrain S.A.: Food Firm's Revenue Rose By 7% in First Half of '90

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

Bongrain S.A. said its consolidated revenue rose 7% in the first half of 1990 to 4.36 billion French francs ($821 million) from 4.09 billion francs in the year-earlier period. The French food group said in a communique that after adjustments for changes in group structure and exchange rates, the sales gain would have been limited to 1.7%.

It didn't elaborate on the structural changes, but significant acquisitions made by Bongrain since the first half of 1989 include the Italian cheese group Ludovico S.p.a. Bongrain also bought the California dairy company Alta-Dena Inc. in February 1989.

For all of 1989, it reported consolidated net profit of 391 million francs after payments to minority interests, on revenue of 8.48 billion francs.
