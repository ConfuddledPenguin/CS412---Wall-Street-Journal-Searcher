GM Sets Oldsmobile Rebates

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

LANSING, Mich. -- General Motors Corp.'s Oldsmobile division said it will offer rebates ranging from $750 to $2,000 on several of its models through Sept. 26. Buyers have the option of choosing financing terms of 7.9% for a 48-month loan or 10.9% for 60 months.
