Mark Twain Bancshares Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

ST. LOUIS -- Mark Twain Bancshares Inc. said it increased the number of common shares authorized to be purchased under its stock buy-back program to 150,000 from 70,000. The bank holding company said it would use the shares for future employee stock-related benefit programs.

The company said that because of the recent decline in its share price the shares are an "exceptional value" at current levels. The stock closed at $13.25 Friday in over-the-counter trading.
