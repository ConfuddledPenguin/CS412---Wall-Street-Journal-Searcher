Great American Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

BATON ROUGE, La. -- Great American Corp. said banking regulators closed its last remaining operating unit, American Bank & Trust Co., and sold most of the assets and all the deposits to a unit of Hancock Holding Co., a Gulfport, Miss., bank holding concern. Great American said its assets now consist of a lawsuit against Union Planters Corp., Memphis, Tenn., which it accuses of breaching a contract to merge with Great American, and about $100,000 of cash and cash equivalents. Liabilities total about $3.5 million, the company added. A spokesman said Great American doesn't plan to file for federal bankruptcy-court protection.
