Belgium's Unemployment Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

BRUSSELS -- Belgium's seasonally unadjusted unemployment rate rose to 10.1% in July from 8.8% in June, but fell from 10.2% in July 1989, the Ministry of Employment said. The number of people out of work in Belgium totaled 352,255 in July, the ministry said. Unemployment historically tends to surge in July because of the number of people leaving school and entering the job market. In 1989, the July rate rose to 10.2% from 9.1% in June.

Unemployment among men was 6.8% in July, and among women, 14.8%, the ministry said.
