Petroleum Investment's Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

OKLAHOMA CITY -- Petroleum Investments Ltd. said it successfully completed an exchange offer in which a recently formed affiliate, Bradmar Petroleum Corp., swapped one-tenth of a share of common for each Petroleum Investments depositary unit outstanding. Petroleum Investments, an oil and gas master limited partnership, said 13.4 million depositary units, or about 79.9% of the total outstanding, were tendered to Bradmar. It added that Bradmar also received and will accept tenders of limited partner interests in nine affiliated limited partnerships, representing an aggregate of $1.9 million of exchange value.

The two companies expect to complete their consolidation Tuesday, Petroleum Investments said, adding that Bradmar common will begin trading on the American Stock Exchange Wednesday under the symbol BPC.
