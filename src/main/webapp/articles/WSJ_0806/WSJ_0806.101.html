Business Brief -- Sun Microsystems Inc.: Firm Plans Sale to AT&T Of Its Newly Issued Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

Sun Microsystems Inc., reflecting continuing cash needs and a previously reported arrangement, said it planned to sell stock valued at $191.8 million to American Telephone & Telegraph Co. Under a 1988 agreement, Sun has the right to sell to AT&T newly issued shares in an amount of up to 15% of Sun's common stock outstanding. The latest sale, covering 4,575,469 Sun shares, means that Sun has sold AT&T a total of 14.7% of its shares. Under the 1988 agreement, AT&T can buy an additional 5% stake through open market purchases. The latest sale covers an amount equal to nearly 5% of Sun's 92.7 million common shares outstanding.

Sun, a Mountain View, Calif., computer workstation maker, said it expects to complete the sale in 30 days. It said it expects to use proceeds for working capital needs and to increase capital.
