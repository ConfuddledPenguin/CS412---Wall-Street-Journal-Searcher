New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange announced two new listings, while the National Market System of the over-the-counter stock market added three issues. Joining the Big Board is Intellicall Inc., trading under the symbol ICL. It will begin trading Wednesday. The company, based in Carrollton, Texas, makes and markets pay telephones. It previously traded over the counter.

Vintage Petroleum Inc., a Tulsa independent oil and gas company, began trading last week under VPI as an initial public offering.

On the OTC national market, Allied Clinical Laboratories Inc., a Nashville owner of clinical testing labs, began trading under ACLB.

Bird Medical Technologies Inc., a Palm Springs, Calif., manufacturer of respiratory-care and infection-control products, began trading under BMTI.

Vanguard Real Estate Fund II, a real estate investment trust based in Valley Forge, Pa., will begin trading tomorrow under VRETS.

Separately, the American Stock Exchange opened trading in put and call options on the shares of International Game Technology, a Reno, Nev., manufacturer of gambling machines.

The Chicago Board Options Exchange began options trading on Chambers Development Co., a Pittsburgh waste-management company.

The Amex and the CBOE opened options trading on Safeway Inc., an Oakland, Calif., food retailer.
