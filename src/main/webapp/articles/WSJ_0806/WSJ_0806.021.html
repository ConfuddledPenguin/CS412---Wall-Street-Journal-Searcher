Group Boosts Whitehall Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

WASHINGTON -- Cambridge Capital Fund L.P. said it increased its stake in Whitehall Corp. to 12.2% even though it had disclosed in an earlier filing that it could not structure an acquisition of the Dallas-based defense contractor that was economically justifiable and still acceptable to Whitehall shareholders. In April, Cambridge proposed a tender offer at $20 a share, but withdrew it when it lost a proxy contest later that month. Cambridge said then it would explore alternatives. Cambridge bought 43,600 shares Wednesday and Thursday at $11.75 to $12 a share.
