Who's News: American Telephone & Telegraph Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

AMERICAN TELEPHONE & TELEGRAPH Co. has named James R. Walker, customer-service vice president of its small-business equipment unit, to the position of vice president-national sales and service, succeeding current national-sales chief Erik Jorgensen, who is retiring. The move is part of a consolidation in which AT&T has combined sales and service into 29 centers around the country, made each office into a profit-and-loss center and named four regional vice presidents to supervise them.
