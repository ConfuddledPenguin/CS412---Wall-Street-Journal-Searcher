Business Brief -- G.R.I. Corp.: Group of Investors Increases Common Holding to 13.75%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

A group led by a New York investor, Mark Boyar, said that it raised its stake in G.R.I. Corp., a Chicago-based mail-order marketing concern, to 13.75%, and that it plans to seek board representation. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the Boyar group said it is analyzing possible steps that G.R.I. could take to increase the value of its common stock. In connection with its G.R.I. investment, the group said it may take certain actions it considers appropriate, depending on various factors, including G.R.I.'s response to its request for board representation. G.R.I. officials weren't available to comment.

According to the filing, the group holds 309,267 G.R.I. common shares, including 104,500 bought in the open market from June 29 to last Tuesday for $5.07 to $6 a share. In American Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, G.R.I. shares closed at $5.625, unchanged.
