Dividend News: American Realty Trust Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

American Realty Trust Inc., which holds mortgages and real estate, said it is omitting its quarterly, to comply with restrictions in a loan agreement, and instead will determine dividends on an annual basis. The debt covenant limits the Dallas-based trust's payments to its annual income, which American Realty said it can't determine on a quarterly basis. American Realty paid a quarterly of 20 cents a share May 15. In 1989, it earned $5.2 million, or 60 cents a share. American Realty, which hasn't reported first-quarter results, said earnings continue to be hurt by mortgage defaults and investment losses.
