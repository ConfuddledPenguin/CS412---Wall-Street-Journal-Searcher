Rochester G&E Rates

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Rochester Gas & Electric Corp. said it filed a petition with the New York Public Service Commission asking for increases in electric and natural gas rates. The utility said it seeks to increase electric revenue by 6.3%, or $38.4 million, and gas revenue by 2.6%, or $5.9 million. It cited inflation and higher operating and maintenance costs.

The combined average annual rate increase would be 4% for consumers.
