JLG Fiscal 1990 Estimate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

McCONNELLSBURG, Pa. -- JLG Industries Inc. said that despite recent softening in orders, it expects to post record net income and sales for the year ended July 31. The company didn't disclose specific figures. For fiscal 1989, the hydraulic machinery concern had net income of $7.5 million on sales of $121.3 million.
