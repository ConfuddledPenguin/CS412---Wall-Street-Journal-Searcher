CABLE: The U.K.'s W.H. Smith Group PLC and ESPN of the U.S. are to get a #5 million ($9.3 million) cash injection from Canal Plus S.A., France's pay-TV venture, for their European Sports Network venture. Canal Plus will subscribe to new shares in the venture that will give it unspecified holdings in European Sports Network's TV Sport, Sportkanal and Sportnet affiliates. Canal Plus and General d'Image, which holds a 21.15% stake in Canal Plus, also gain the option to subscribe for a joint 15% holding in European Sports Network at an unspecified future time.
