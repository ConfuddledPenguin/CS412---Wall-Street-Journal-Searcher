Business Brief -- Cincinnati Bell Inc.: Unit Buys Majority Stake In 2 Telemarketing Firms

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

Cincinnati Bell Inc. said its marketing unit, Matrixx Marketing Inc., acquired majority ownership of two telemarketing agencies in Paris. The price was about $39 million. The two companies -- Phone Marketing Services S.A. (PMS) and Syscall S.A.R.L. -- were acquired from Sysmark Group S.A., also based in Paris.

Closely held Sysmark will retain a minority stake in the companies. Jean Paul Devai, Sysmark chairman, will become a director of Matrixx Marketing Europe and an executive director of Matrixx Marketing Inc.
