Italy's Foreign Trade Account

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

ROME -- Italy's foreign trade account swung to a surplus of 1.25 trillion lire ($1.07 billion) in June from a deficit of 728 billion lire a year earlier and from a 2.495 trillion-lira gap in May, Istat, the state statistical office, said. Istat also said the trade balance in the first six months of this year showed a deficit of 11.256 trillion lire, compared with a 14.27 trillion-lira deficit a year earlier. The trade statistics are provisional and aren't seasonally adjusted.
