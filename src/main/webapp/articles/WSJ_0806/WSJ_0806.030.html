Philips Industries Sets Meeting

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Philips Industries Inc. said shareholders will meet Aug. 22 to consider its agreement to be acquired by Tomkins PLC for $18.50 a share, or $550 million. The maker of building and industrial products said the British company may buy up to 10%, or 2,860,000, of Philips's shares outstanding before the acquisition.
