Seats on Stock Exchanges Sold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange said a seat was sold for $330,000, down $40,000 from the previous sale on July 17. Seats are currently quoted at $310,000, bid, and $330,000, asked. The American Stock Exchange said two seats were sold, for $117,500 and $115,000. The $117,500 sale was down $10,500 from the previous sale July 20. Seats are being quoted at $105,000, bid, and $133,000, asked.

The selling prices of the seats were the lowest since 1985, according to the exchanges.
