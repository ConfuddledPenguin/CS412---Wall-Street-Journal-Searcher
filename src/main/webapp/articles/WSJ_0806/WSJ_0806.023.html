Alltel Corp. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

HUDSON, Ohio -- Alltel Corp. said it bought a 19.8% interest in Chillicothe Telephone Co., Chillicothe, Ohio, for $5.3 million. Alltel, which provides telephone service in 25 states, said it purchased its stake from the Cleveland Foundation because it sees Chillicothe as a growth company.
