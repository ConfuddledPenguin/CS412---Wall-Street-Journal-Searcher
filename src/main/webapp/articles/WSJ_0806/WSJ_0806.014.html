Indonesian Oil Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

JAKARTA -- Indonesia's state-owned Pertamina oil company signed two production-sharing contracts with three foreign companies and a techical assistance contract with another Indonesian company. Conoco Indonesia Inc. of the U.S. signed for a 20-year extension of its production-sharing contract, which will terminate in October 1998.

Mobil Exploration Bomberai, also of the U.S., and BHP Petroleum Inc. of Australia together signed a 30-year production-sharing contract with Pertamina for oil and gas exploration in Babo, Irian Jaya.

Pertamina also signed a technical assistance contract with Humpuss Patragas Ltd., an Indonesian company, for 20-year exploration for oil and gas in an abandoned oil field in Cepu, a border area of central and east Java.
