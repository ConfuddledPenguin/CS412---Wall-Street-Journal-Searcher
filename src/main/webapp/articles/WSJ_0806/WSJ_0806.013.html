Banks May Clear Uruguay Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

MONTEVIDEO -- John Reed, the chairman of Citicorp, said foreign bankers next week will consider and may approve Uruguay's request to buy back $1.4 billion of its foreign debt at its market value. The proposal "has been accepted in general terms," Mr. Reed said. "Now we're discussing the numbers."

Citicorp coordinates the steering committee that represents the approximately 100 banks to which Uruguay owes money.
