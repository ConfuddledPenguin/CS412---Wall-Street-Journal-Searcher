Mutual Federal Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/06/90

ZANESVILLE, Ohio -- Mutual Federal Savings Bank said it received regulatory approval to be acquired in a stock transaction by Park National Corp., Newark, Ohio. Mutual Federal said it will receive 180,000 Park shares, or 0.2086 of a Park share for each Mutual Federal share. Mutual Federal valued Park shares at about $58.50 each.

The acquisition is subject to the approval of both Mutual Federal and Park National shareholders.
