Barrister Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/06/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Barrister Information Systems Corp. said it signed the first contract for its law firm management system software after four years of development. A spokesman for the integrated computer-based information systems concern said the deal with the Hartford, Conn.-based Halloran & Sage law firm is "not financially significant" but added that the company expects the software to be a multimillion-dollar business. Barrister had a loss of $6.5 million on revenue of $29.1 million in the year ended March 31.
