Ford Recalls Econoline Models

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

DETROIT -- Ford Motor Co. is recalling 11,000 1989 Econoline 150 and 250 vans with 5.8-liter engines to assure acceptable exhaust emissions. In some cases, certain emission control system nylon vacuum hoses may be damaged as a result of contact with the hot surfaces in the area, resulting in unacceptable oxides of nitrogen emission levels, Ford said. The auto maker will notify owners through the mail.
