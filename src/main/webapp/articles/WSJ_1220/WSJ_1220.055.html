Dividend News: Sierra Capital Cos.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

SIERRA CAPITAL Cos., San Francisco, said that one of the real estate investment trusts it manages, the Sierra Capital Realty Trust IV fund, won't pay a fourth-quarter dividend, citing the trust's expected loss of two major tenants next year, among other factors. It also said that because of "obvious uncertainties caused by the current economic climate," three of its trusts will evaluate their dividends in February meetings, "which would likely result in a reduction of dividends." These funds are Sierra Real Estate Equity Trust '83, Sierra Capital Realty Trust VII and Sierra Capital Realty Trust VIII. Sierra Capital Reality Trust VI may reduce or increase its dividend in February, the company said, depending on the outcome of a review.
