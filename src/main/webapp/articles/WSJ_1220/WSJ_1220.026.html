Fairfield Communities Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

LITTLE ROCK, Ark. -- Fairfield Communities Inc., a retirement and recreational communities developer that sought protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code on Oct. 3, said it received bankruptcy-court permission to borrow as much as $13.5 million through April 30 under a revolving credit agreement with Security Pacific Bank Arizona. The company said the borrowings will be secured by liens on certain contract receivables, real estate and other assets of Fairfield and its units.
