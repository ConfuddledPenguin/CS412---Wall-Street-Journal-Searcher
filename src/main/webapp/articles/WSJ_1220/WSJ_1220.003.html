Who's News: BI Names Randall Chairman

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/20/90

BOULDER, Colo. -- BI Inc., which makes electronic identification management systems for monitoring convicted criminals on home-release programs, said Frank L. Randall Jr., 72 years old and a director since 1983, was elected chairman, succeeding Gary T. Carroll, 47, a BI cofounder, who will remain a director. Mr. Randall is a retired vice chairman of North American Phillips Corp. Mr. Carroll had been chairman since 1982. A BI spokeswoman said Mr. Carroll suggested that he step aside and that Mr. Randall be named chairman.
