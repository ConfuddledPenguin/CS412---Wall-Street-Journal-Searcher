Bank of Montreal Cuts Prime

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

TORONTO -- Bank of Montreal cut its prime lending rate to 12.75% from 13%, effective today. It was the bank's seventh consecutive prime-rate drop since August. It follows the bank's reduction of one-quarter percentage point in its prime rate last week. The prime rate is the base for various lending charges.

Canada's other major banks are expected to lower their prime rates today to 12.75% from 13.25%. Bank of Montreal has recently led Canada's other banks in prime-rate reductions.
