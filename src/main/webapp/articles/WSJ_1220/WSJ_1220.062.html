It's About Time]

("CBS study shows the advantage of marketing to older viewers." -- WSJ news item)

It's me the network's paging,

I'm their viewing angel, aging.

It's on me that they're depending

For watching and then spending.

With my age group now they traffic,

I'm their favorite demographic.

-- Howard Kaufman.

Demosthenic

When a speaker is

Almost inaudible

I don't find it

Applaudable.

If he raises his pitch

To the nth decibel

I'm not apt to be Accessible.

I eschew the stentorian

And sidestep the faint,

Just give me clarity

With restraint.

-- Dow Richardson.
