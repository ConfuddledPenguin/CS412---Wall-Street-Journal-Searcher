Dividend News: Sovereign Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

SOVEREIGN BANCORP, Wyomissing, Pa., said it raised the quarterly cash dividend 20% to three cents a share from 2.5 cents, payable Feb. 15 to stock of record Feb. 1. The parent of Penn Savings Bank and Yardley Bank for Savings also declared a 6-for-5 stock split in the form of a 20% stock dividend, payable Feb. 15 to stock of record Jan. 31. The split will increase total shares outstanding to about 3.3 million. The cash dividend will be paid on the new number of shares held after the stock dividend is distributed. Sovereign said the dividend increases are intended to maximize shareholder value, based on the company's financial strength. Sovereign had third-quarter net income of $1.6 million, or 57 cents a share.
