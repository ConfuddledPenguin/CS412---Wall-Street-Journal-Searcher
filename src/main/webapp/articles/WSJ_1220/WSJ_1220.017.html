Who's News: Mobile Telecommunication Technologies Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

MOBILE TELECOMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGIES Corp. (Jackson, Miss.) -- David W. Garrison, 35 years old, was named president of SkyTel Corp., the principal subsidiary of this provider of nationwide paging services, succeeding John N. Palmer, 56, chairman and chief executive officer of Mobile, who had been SkyTel's interim president since August. Mr. Garrison previously was chairman of Dial Page, a Greenville, S.C., paging company.
