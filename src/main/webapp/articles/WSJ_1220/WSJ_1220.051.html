Who's News: Frank B. Hall & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

FRANK B. HALL & Co. (Briarcliff Manor, N.Y.) -- Joseph A. Califano Jr. was named a director, succeeding Peter L. Keane, who retired from the board of this insurance and reinsurance brokerage concern. Mr. Califano, former secretary of Health, Education and Welfare, is currently a senior partner at the law firm Dewey Ballantine Bushby Palmer & Wood, in its Washington, D.C., office.
