General Dynamics Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

WASHINGTON -- General Dynamics Corp. was awarded a $765 million Navy contract for a nuclear-powered ballistic-missile submarine.

McDonnell Douglas Helicopter Co., a unit of McDonnell Douglas Corp., got a $39.3 million Army contract for helicopter purchase and documentation.

Trimble Navigation Ltd. was given a $12.1 million Air Force contract for satellite communications equipment.
