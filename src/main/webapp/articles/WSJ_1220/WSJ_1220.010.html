Wilmington Trust Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/20/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Wilmington Trust Co. said it completed its acquisition of Peoples Bank of Harrington, Harrington, Del., in a transaction valued at $9.6 million. The purchase involved the exchange of 12.04 Wilmington Trust common shares for each Peoples Bank share. The deal was based on Wilmington Trust shares' closing price Dec. 18, valuing each Peoples Bank share at about $479. Peoples Bank has assets of $45 million.
