Who's News: Allied-Signal Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/20/90

ALLIED-SIGNAL Inc. (Morristown, N.J.) -- David C. Hill was named president of Allied Fibers, a nylon producing division of this advanced technology company with businesses in aerospace, automotive products and engineered materials. Mr. Hill, 44 years old, succeeds Daniel Burnham, effective Jan. 1. Mr. Burnham was named president of the air research group at the company's aerospace unit. Mr. Hill was previously senior vice president and general manager of Allied's industrial fibers unit.
