Marketing & Media: CBS Signs Production Deal With Husband-Wife Team

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/20/90

NEW YORK -- The CBS network said it signed a long-term agreement with the husband-wife team of Harry Thomason and Linda Bloodworth-Thomason, creators and executive producers of the CBS series "Designing Women" and "Evening Shade," to create five new prime-time series. The CBS Inc. unit said the deal with the couple's Mozark Productions Inc. is the "largest and most comprehensive production commitment" it has ever made with a single production company, but it didn't elaborate.

The eight-year deal calls for the duo to be exclusive to CBS for the first three years of the pact. After that, CBS gets a first look at any project. One of the five series commitments will be produced by CBS's in-house production unit.
