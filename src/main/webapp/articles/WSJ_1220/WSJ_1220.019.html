Financing Business: Masco Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/20/90

MASCO INDUSTRIES Inc. (Taylor, Mich.) said it is issuing $77.5 million of 12% exchangeable preferred stock to Masco Corp., in exchange for $30 million of cash and $10 million of Masco Industries common stock. The transaction resulted in part from an agreement between the two companies and reduces Masco Corp's. holdings of Masco Industries common to about 45%. Masco Industries manufactures custom engineering parts and specialty products. Masco Corp. manufactures building and home furnishing products. Separately, Masco Corp., also of Taylor, said it authorized the purchase of as many as five million of its common shares in open market purchases, privately negotiated transactions or otherwise.
