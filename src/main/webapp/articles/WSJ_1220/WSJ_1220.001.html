Medex Inc. Acquires Ivion Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/20/90

HILLIARD, Ohio -- Medex Inc., which designs, makes, assembles and markets disposable hospital products for life-support systems, said it acquired Ivion Corp. of Englewood, Colo., which develops and markets drug-delivery products. Terms weren't disclosed. Ivion, which will retain its name, location and management, has annual sales of $18 million. Medex has annual sales of $44.7 million.
