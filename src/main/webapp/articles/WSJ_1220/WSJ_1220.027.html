Publicker Industries Buys Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/20/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Publicker Industries Inc., an industrial chemicals and textile maker, said it purchased a group of nine companies -- five manufacturers, three service concerns and a distribution business -- from Hanson Industries Inc., the U.S. unit of Hanson PLC of Britain, for $31.7 million. Publicker had agreed to buy 10 companies from Hanson Industries for $36 million; the sale of the 10th concern is expected to be completed in January.

In the year ended Sept. 30, the nine-company group had revenue of $69.3 million and operating profit, before corporate charges and one-time items, of $5.1 million.
