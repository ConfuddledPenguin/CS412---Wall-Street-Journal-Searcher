Financing Business: United Medicorp Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

UNITED MEDICORP Inc. said it registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 2.5 million common shares. The Dallas-based medical insurance claims processing company said it expects the offer to take place after its planned 1-for-2 reverse stock split, which will be paid June 21 to stock of record on the same date. After the split, the company will have about 10.7 million common shares outstanding. Proceeds from the offering will be used to repay short-term debt, expand marketing efforts and increase the company's operations. Eppler, Guerin & Turner Inc. of Dallas is managing the underwriting.
