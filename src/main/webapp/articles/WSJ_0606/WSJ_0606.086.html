Weyerhaeuser Co. Division

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

TACOMA, Wash. -- Weyerhaeuser Co. said that as part of a year-old review of the company's businesses, it has decided to keep its Personal Care Products division. Securities analysts had speculated that Weyerhaeuser would sell the unit as part of the review, in which the company evaluated whether to keep or dispose of 25 of its businesses.
