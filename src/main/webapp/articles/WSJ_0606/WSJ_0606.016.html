Who's News: Painewebber Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

PAINEWEBBER GROUP Inc. (New York) -- Robert S. McKinney was named executive vice president and chief information officer at PaineWebber Inc., a unit of this securities firm. Mr. McKinney previously was managing director of systems at Kidder, Peabody & Co., the New York-based investment bank unit of General Electric Co.
