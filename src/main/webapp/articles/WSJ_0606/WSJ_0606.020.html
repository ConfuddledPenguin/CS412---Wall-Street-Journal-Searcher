Who's News: Boston Technology Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

BOSTON TECHNOLOGY Inc. (Cambridge, Mass.) -- Joseph E. Norberg, chief financial officer and treasurer of Hill, Holiday, Connors, Cosmopulos Inc., a Boston advertising agency, was named a director of this telephone equipment concern, succeeding Ronald J. Miller, former counsel to the company and a director for 3 1/2 years, whose resignation from the board the company said was mutually agreed upon.
