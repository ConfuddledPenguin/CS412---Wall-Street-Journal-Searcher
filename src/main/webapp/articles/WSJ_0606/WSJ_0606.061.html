Kansas City Southern Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- Kansas City Southern Industries Inc. said it raised its tender offer for the 12.9% of DST Systems Inc. that it doesn't already own to $15.85 a share, from $14. Kansas City Southern, a railroad and financial services data-processing concern, already owns 16.6 DST million shares.

The company said the offer is valued at about $39 million for the remaining roughly 2.5 million shares of DST.

DST is a financial services company based in Kansas City.
