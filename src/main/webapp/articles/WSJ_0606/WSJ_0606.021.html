Who's News: Toys "R" Us Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

TOYS "R" US Inc. (Paramus, N.J.) -- Debra Hyman was named to the new position of vice president, divisional merchandise manager of infants, sleepwear and accessories of the Kids "R" Us unit of this toy retailer. Ms. Hyman was previously girls buyer for the unit, which sells clothing for children.
