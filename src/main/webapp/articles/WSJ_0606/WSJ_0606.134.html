Belgian Unemployment Falls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

BRUSSELS -- Unemployment in Belgium fell to a nine-year low of 8.8% in May from 9.1% in April and 9.2% a year earlier, the Labor and Employment Ministry said. The latest rate was Belgium's lowest since November 1980, when it also registered 8.8%. The ministry said 335,369 people were unemployed in May, down from 342,254 in April and from 357,795 in May 1989. Unemployment among women was 203,107, or 12.9%; among men, it was 132,262, or 6%.
