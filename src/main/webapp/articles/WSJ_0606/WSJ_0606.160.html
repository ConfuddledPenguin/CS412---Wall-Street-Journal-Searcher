REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Setting Limits

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Well, it looks as if there may still be one or two things you still can't do. Even more amazing, you can't even get a court to legalize approval. This was established in a federal appeals court in Chicago yesterday, which ruled that two Illinois policemen couldn't wear diamond ear studs. The court decision said, "It is obvious from the record that the plaintiffs not only caused an adverse impact on police discipline, esprit de corps and uniformity" but also "caused great public dissatisfaction as well." It's nice to see esprit de corps win one.
