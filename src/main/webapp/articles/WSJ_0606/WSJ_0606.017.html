Midway Airlines' Traffic Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

CHICAGO -- Midway Airlines said its May traffic rose 36% to 400.2 million revenue passenger miles from 294.7 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's May load factor, or the number of available seats occupied, fell to 55.7% from 56.9%.

In the first five months of this year, Midway's traffic rose 51% to 2.09 billion revenue passenger miles from 1.39 billion miles a year earlier. The load factor rose to 60% from 55.9%.
