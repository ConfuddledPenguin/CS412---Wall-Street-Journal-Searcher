International Brief -- Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd.: Consolidated Pretax Profit For Fiscal '90 Surged 52%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd. of Japan said its consolidated pretax earnings for the year ended March 31 surged 52% to 146.49 billion yen ($962.5 million) from 96.27 billion yen a year earlier. Net income rose 8.9% to 67.51 billion yen, or 20.19 yen a share, from 62.01 billion yen, or 18.68 yen a share.

Sales increased 20% to 2.278 trillion yen from 1.89 trillion yen.

Despite a 9.83 billion-yen gain from the sales of corporate assets and a 14% drop in taxes to 50.41 billion yen, the pace of profit growth slowed sharply from the pretax level, as the company posted extraordinary losses of 51.24 billion yen, mostly representing a setback stemming from the currency conversion of the value of warrant bonds, as a result of sharp foreign exchange fluctuations, officials said. The fiscal 1989 results didn't have any such losses. Non-operating profit, including interest and dividend receipts, climbed 30% to 63.752 billion yen. Sales of power systems rose 37% to 646.25 billion yen; this line is Mitsubishi's largest, accounting for 28% of total sales.
