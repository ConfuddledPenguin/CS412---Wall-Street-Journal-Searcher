Munsingwear Retains Adviser

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Munsingwear Inc. said it hired Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc. as its investment banking adviser to assist in the previously announced restructuring of the apparel maker. Munsingwear, which said in May that it was seeking buyers for its units that sell clothing for infants and women, plans to focus on its men's division.
