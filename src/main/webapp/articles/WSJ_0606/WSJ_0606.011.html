Who's News: Natec Resources Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

NATEC RESOURCES Inc. (Houston) -- Ben R. Stuart was elected to the board of this acid rain pollution control concern, bringing the board total to seven. Mr. Stuart, vice president, operations, for Dresser Industries Inc., succeeds Arthur S. Bowes Jr., who didn't seek re-election. Dresser Industries is a Dallas energy services concern.
