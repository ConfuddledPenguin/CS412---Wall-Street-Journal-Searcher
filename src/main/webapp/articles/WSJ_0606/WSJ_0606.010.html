Financing Business: Mycogen Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

MYCOGEN Corp., San Diego, announced a public offering of 2.5 million common shares at $17.75 each, boosting its common shares outstanding to 11,228,655. Mycogen said Morgan Stanley & Co. will lead the underwriters. The maker of biological pesticides said proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes and to support research, development, manufacturing and marketing of its biopesticide products.
