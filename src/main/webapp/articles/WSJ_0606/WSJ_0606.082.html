Ex-Im Bank Loan to Indonesia

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

WASHINGTON -- The U.S. Export-Import bank approved a $15.1 million loan to Indonesia for the purchase of computer equipment primarily from Compaq Computer Corp., Houston. Indonesia's finance ministry is buying computers, printers, terminals and other equipment to expand its data-processing system. Total cost of the equipment is $17.8 million.
