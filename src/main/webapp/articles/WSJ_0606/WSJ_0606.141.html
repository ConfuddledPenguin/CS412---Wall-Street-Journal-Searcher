Canada Auctions 10.50% Bonds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government auctioned 850 million Canadian dollars (US$723.9 million) of 10.50% bonds maturing July 1, 2000. The average yield bid was 10.791% for a price equivalent of 98.236.

Proceeds of the sale will be used for general government purposes.
