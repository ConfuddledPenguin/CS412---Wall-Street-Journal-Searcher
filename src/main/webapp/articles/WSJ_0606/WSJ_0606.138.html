Commercial-Paper Rates Fall at Citicorp Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.1695% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.233% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $410 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.165% to 8.175%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 8.1047% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.262% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $400.3 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.064% to 8.12%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
