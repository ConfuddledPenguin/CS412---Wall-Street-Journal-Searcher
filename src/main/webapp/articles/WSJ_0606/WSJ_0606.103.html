Technology: Alden Gets UAL Unit Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

WESTBORO, Mass. -- Alden Electronics Inc., a maker of facsimile communications equipment, said it received a five-year, $430,000 contract from the United Airlines unit of UAL Corp., Chicago, for installation of its weather graphics dissemination system. The C5020 system will be used at United's corporate headquarters for graphically displaying areas of turbulence and significant weather developments throughout United's route structure.
