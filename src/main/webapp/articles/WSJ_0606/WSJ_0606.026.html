GM Unit to Exercise Option

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Acceptance Corp., the financing arm of General Motors Corp., said it will exercise its option to redeem on July 15 all of its 11.75% debentures due to mature July 15, 2000. The debentures will be redeemed at 102% of their face amount. The debentures were sold in July 1980 in the amount of $200 million, $150 million of which are currently outstanding.
