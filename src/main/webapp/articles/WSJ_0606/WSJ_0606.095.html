Technology: Sequoia Extends Liant Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

MARLBOROUGH, Mass. -- Sequoia Systems Inc., a maker of fault-tolerant systems, said it extended its marketing agreement with closely held Liant Software Corp., an integrated programming systems concern, adding Liant's Fortran programming language to Sequoia's product fold. Sequoia said Language Processors Inc., a Liant unit, will offer its Fortran language for the Sequoia Series 300, significantly enhancing the productivity of Sequoia's software development environment.
