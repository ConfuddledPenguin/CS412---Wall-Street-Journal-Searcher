Who's News: Nycor Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

NYCOR Inc. (Peapack, N.J.) -- Robert E. Logan Jr. was named vice president of finance and chief financial officer of this acquisition company. Mr. Logan, previously partner and vice president at Bard Associates Inc., a closely held venture capital and money management company in Chicago, succeeds Robert L. Laurent Jr. Mr. Laurent became chief financial officer of Fedders Corp., the Peapack, N.J., manufacturer of room air conditioners.
