Treasury to Sell Bills Totaling $17.2 Billion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.15 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $17.2 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.05 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Sept. 13 and Dec. 13, 1990, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
