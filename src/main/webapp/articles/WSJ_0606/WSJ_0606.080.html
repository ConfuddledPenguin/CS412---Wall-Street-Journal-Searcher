International Brief -- BASF AG: Chemical Group to Acquire Bulk of Tape Cassette Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

BASF AG said it agreed to buy the bulk of Agfa-Gaevert AG's consumer and professional audio/visual-tape cassette business for a sum it didn't disclose. The Frankfurt, West Germany, chemical group said that it expects to sign a letter of intent for the Agfa purchase today and that the companies will complete details as soon as possible.

Agfa is a unit of the West German chemical company Bayer AG.

BASF said it agreed to buy both the consumer and professional videotape cassette lines of Agfa but only the professional audiotape line. The consumer audiotape operations did not interest BASF.

BASF, among the world's largest producers of consumer and professional tapes, has 1.6 billion marks of annual sales, which represents about 13.5% of BASF's 11.89 billion marks of world-wide group sales last year.
