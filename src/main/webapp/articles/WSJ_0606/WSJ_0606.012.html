Financing Business: Boise Cascade Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

BOISE CASCADE Corp., Boise, Idaho, announced a public offering of $125 million of 9.85% notes due in 2002. The offering was largely sold at the time of pricing yesterday, the company said. The forest products company said the proceeds will go toward repayment of short-term debt and general corporate purposes. The underwriters are First Boston Corp., Goldman, Sachs & Co., Lazard Freres & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc.
