Royal International Meeting

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

DALLAS -- Royal International Optical Corp. said it scheduled a special meeting for June 28 to allow shareholders to vote on its proposed merger with U.S. Vision Inc. Under the offer, U.S. Vision will acquire 80% of Royal's stock for $12.50 a share in cash. The remaining shares will be exchanged for stock in the merged company, which will operate under Royal International's name.

In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, Royal closed at $10.25, up 12.5 cents.

U.S. Vision is closely held. Both Royal and U.S. Vision, based in Glendora, N.J., are optical retailers.
