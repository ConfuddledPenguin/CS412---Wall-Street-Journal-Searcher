Financing Business: Gensia Pharmaceuticals Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

GENSIA PHARMACEUTICALS Inc., San Diego, announced an initial public offering of 2.8 million common shares at $11 each. Alex. Brown & Sons Inc., Montgomery Securities and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. will manage the underwriters. Gensia said that if the underwriter's over-allotment option to purchase an additional 420,000 shares is exercised, Gensia will have about 13,557,000 shares outstanding after the offering. Gensia, which develops, makes and markets novel pharmaceutical products, said it plans to use net proceeds from the offering for funding research and development and for general corporate purposes.
