Who's News: Citicorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

CITICORP (New York) -- David E. Cripps was named head of offshore-investment management, a newly created post, at the asset-management arm of Citibank Private Bank, a unit of this banking holding company. Mr. Cripps was vice president and manager of the global-investment-management department at Manufacturer's Hanover Trust Co., New York.
