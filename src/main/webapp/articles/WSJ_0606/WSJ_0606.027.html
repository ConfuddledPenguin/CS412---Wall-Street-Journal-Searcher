Who's News: Conversion Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

CONVERSION INDUSTRIES Inc. (Pasadena, Calif.) -- William M. Hitchcock and D. Grant Macdonald were named to the board of this independent power-production company, expanding the size of the board to seven members. Conversion said Mr. Hitchcock is managing partner of Bedford Street Group, a Santa Monica, Calif., investment-management company and Mr. Macdonald is a director of Yorkton Continental Securities Inc., a Toronto-based securities brokerage firm.
