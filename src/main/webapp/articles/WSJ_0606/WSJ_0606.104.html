Financing Business: K-Swiss Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

K-SWISS Inc., a Pacoima, Calif.-based designer, developer and marketer of athletic footwear, announced an initial public offering of 1,450,000 shares of its Class A common stock at $17.50 each. Of the total, 1,115,000 shares are being sold by the company and 335,000 by stockholders. Proceeds will be used to reduce debt. Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets will lead the underwriters.
