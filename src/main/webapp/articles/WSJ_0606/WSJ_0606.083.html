Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

FILMS: The National Geographic Society reached an agreement with Imax Systems Corp. jointly to produce giant-screen films and educational materials. The first film is scheduled to be released in the fall of 1991. National Geographic for 25 years has produced shows for television.

RADIO: Shamrock Broadcasting Inc., a unit of Shamrock Holdings Inc., signed a letter of intent to sell its KMGC-FM radio station in Dallas to Alliance Broadcasting Inc. Terms weren't disclosed.

WHO'S NEWS: Nikki Rocco was named to the newly created post of senior vice president of distribution and marketing liaison for MCA Inc.'s Universal Pictures unit. She was vice president of Universal Pictures's distribution division.
