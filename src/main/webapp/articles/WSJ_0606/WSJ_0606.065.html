Financing Business: RPM Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

RPM Inc. said it will redeem on July 6 all $30 million outstanding of its 5 3/4% convertible subordinated debentures, due 2001. The Medina, Ohio, company, which makes products used in waterproofing, corrosion control and general maintenance, said it assumes that all of the debentures will be converted into common stock. As of March 31, RPM said, it had about 22.5 million common shares outstanding; conversion of all the bonds would increase that total to about 24.2 million.
