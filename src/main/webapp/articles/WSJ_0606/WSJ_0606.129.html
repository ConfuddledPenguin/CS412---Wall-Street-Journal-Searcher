Canada Order Backlog Declined

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian manufacturers recorded a 2.7% decline in their order backlog in March from February, according to Statistics Canada, a federal agency. March was the fifth consecutive month that manufacturers' unfilled orders decreased. Much of the March decline was attributed to a drop in the order backlogs of the transportation equipment and machinery industries.

New orders received by manufacturers in March declined 0.2% from February. New orders have declined in most months since October. Manufacturers' shipments rose 1.1% in March from February.
