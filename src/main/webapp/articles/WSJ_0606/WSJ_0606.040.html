FirsTier Buy-Back Suspended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

OMAHA, Neb. -- FirsTier Financial Inc., a bank holding company and financial services concern, said it suspended its previously announced plan to purchase 100,000 of its 5.8 million common shares outstanding. A spokesman declined to give a reason for the suspension, but he said the company has already bought back 25,000 shares.
