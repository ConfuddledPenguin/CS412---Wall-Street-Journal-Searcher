Business Brief -- Genus Inc.: Quarterly Loss Is Expected Of $4 Million-$5.1 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

Genus Inc., blaming increased competition and lower orders, said it expects a second-quarter loss on lower revenue. The loss will be between $4 million and $5.1 million, or 35 cents to 45 cents a share, the company said, compared with net income of $2.6 million, or 26 cents a share, a year earlier. The Mountain View, Calif., semiconductor-equipment company said it expects revenue of $9 million to $11 million, down from $21.3 million.

A Genus official said bookings of chipmaking equipment called CVD tungsten systems are lower than a year earlier. The company expects current business conditions to last through 1990, he said.

Genus's stock closed at $3.75 a share, down 50 cents, in national over-the-counter trading yesterday.
