Business Brief -- Manville Corp.: Firm's Forest Products Unit Buys Swedish Fiskeby Board

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Manville Corp., Denver, said its Manville Forest Products unit completed the acquisition of Fiskeby Board AB from Kooporativa Forbundet. Terms of the cash transaction weren't disclosed. However, Manville said the Swedish supplier of recycled and coated boxboard products had 1989 sales of about $65 million.

Separately, Standard & Poor's Corp. said it put Manville's senior debt and preferred stock ratings on CreditWatch with negative implications, following a joint court ruling ordering the company to return to federal bankruptcy court to reconsider the funding of a trust established to pay asbestos claims.

The rating concern said its move could affect about $220 million in senior unsecured debt, currently rated double-B; and preferred stock, currently rated single-B-plus.

Late last week, a U.S. district judge and a New York state judge gave the forest and building products company five weeks to consider revisions to the trust with the U.S. bankruptcy court judge.
