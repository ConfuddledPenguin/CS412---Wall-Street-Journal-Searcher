Who's News: Direct Connect International

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

DIRECT CONNECT INTERNATIONAL (Midland Park, N.J.) -- Robert E. Bewighouse was named senior vice president of sales at this toy maker with exclusive licenses for the Muppets products of the late Jim Henson. Mr. Bewighouse formerly was vice president of sales for Simplicity Pattern Co., a subsidiary of Simplicity Holdings Inc., New York.
