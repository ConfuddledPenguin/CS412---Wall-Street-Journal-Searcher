Who's News: Chattem Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

CHATTEM Inc. (Chattanooga, Tenn.) -- Zan Guerry, formerly executive vice president and chief operating officer of this chemicals and consumer products maker, was named chairman, president and chief executive officer. The 41-year-old Mr. Guerry succeeds his father, Alex Guerry Jr., who died last month.
