Litton Industries Unit Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- Litton Industries Inc. said that an oil-services unit awarded a $90 million contract to Ulstein Hatloe AS of Norway for construction of three seismic exploration ships. Litton, a technology company, said that the ships will be delivered in the second half of 1991 to Western Atlas International Inc., a majority-owned Litton subsidiary based in Houston.
