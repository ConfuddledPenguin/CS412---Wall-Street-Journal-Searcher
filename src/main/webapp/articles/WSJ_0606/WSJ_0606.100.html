International Brief -- Kloeckner-Werke AG: Firm to Buy Plastics Maker, Shed Mannstaedt Steel Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

Kloeckner-Werke AG, said it is buying a British-owned plastics company and is close to selling its Mannstaedt steel unit to British Steel PLC. The West German steel, plastics and machinery group said it has agreed to buy Eurotec Systemteile G.m.b.H., a maker of moulded parts for the auto industry, from B.A.T Industries PLC for 270 million marks ($160.2 million). Kloeckner-Werke also is taking over about 180 million marks of debt in the transaction.

Chairman Herbert Gienow said at a news conference that Kloeckner-Werke is close to concluding the sale of Mannstaedt to British Steel for about 300 million marks. That's in line with the company's continuing efforts to reduce dependence on steel, once its core business area.
