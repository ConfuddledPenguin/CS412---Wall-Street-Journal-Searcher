Business Brief -- Cobe Laboratories Inc.: Sweden's Gambro Extends Its Tender Offer for Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Gambro AB said it extended its $37-a-share tender offer for Cobe Laboratories Inc. until Monday while it complies with requests for information from the Federal Trade Commission. Cobe is a medical equipment company based in Lakewood, Colo.

Gambro, a Swedish medical equipment maker, said it provided the FTC with the information it requested yesterday, and said that it must wait at least 10 days after complying to complete the tender offer, unless the FTC terminates the waiting period.

As of late Monday, Gambro said that about six million Cobe shares, or about 91% of the outstanding stock, had been tendered under its offer.
