Business Brief -- Horizon Healthcare Corp.: Mortgages, Loan Refinanced With a New Lead Lender

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Horizon Healthcare Corp. said it refinanced about $20 million in mortgages and an $8 million working-capital loan with a new lead lender at lower interest rates. The Albuquerque, N.M.-based owner of nursing homes and retirement facilities said its new mortgage and credit agreement is led by HomeFed Bank of San Diego and carries an interest rate 1 1/2 percentage points above the prime rate.

Previously, the company said it had about $4.4 million in mortgages and a working-capital loan of about $8 million with banks led by First National Bank of Boston. The working-capital loan carried an interest rate of 3 1/2 points above prime.

The company said it will use additional cash from refinancing the mortgages to reduce its payables.
