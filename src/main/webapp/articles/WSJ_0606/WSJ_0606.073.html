IBM Cuts Prices of Some PCs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

ARMONK, N.Y. -- International Business Machines Corp. said it cut prices on several models of its Personal System/2 personal computers, including its popular Model 55 SX. The cuts, which were expected, range from 4% to 10%. The suggested price of a Model 55 SX with a 60-megabyte hard disk drive, for example, drops 9%, to $3,895 from $4,295.

In addition to two models of the 55 SX, IBM cut prices on two versions each of the Model 30 286 and Model 50Z. It also reduced the price of 30-megabyte hard disk drives 16%, to $695 from $824.
