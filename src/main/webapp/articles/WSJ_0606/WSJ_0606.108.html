Financing Business: Bizmart Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

BIZMART Inc. said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 2,750,000 common shares, including 750,000 to be sold by its venture-capital investors. The fast-growing office superstores chain, based in Arlington, Texas, said it will use the proceeds from the two million shares it is selling for expansion. BizMart has about 9.3 million shares outstanding. It said the venture-capital investors are selling about 15% of their BizMart stake. Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. is leading the underwriting.
