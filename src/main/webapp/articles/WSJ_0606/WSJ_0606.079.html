Bell Atlantic PBX Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Bell Atlantic Corp. said it completed the purchase of the mid-Atlantic regional PBX operations of Northern Telecom Ltd.'s Meridian Business Systems Group. Terms weren't disclosed. As part of the transaction, the company's Bell Atlanticom Systems, Inc. unit acquired a base of 500,000 private branch exchange ports serving 1,800 customers. The unit also became an authorized U.S. distributor of Meridian's SL-1 and SL-100 PBX systems.

In addition, about 560 Northern Telecom workers became employees of the Bell Atlantic unit. Northern Telecom is a unit of BCE Inc. of Canada.
