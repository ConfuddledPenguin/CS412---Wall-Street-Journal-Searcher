Quadrax Class B Warrants

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

PORTSMOUTH, R.I. -- Quadrax Corp. said it temporarily reduced the exercise price of its outstanding redeemable Class B common stock purchase warrants to $3 from $4. The reduced price is effective until July 3. The warrants expire July 7, 1992. Quadrax makes parts from advanced materials for aerospace and defense markets.
