H.F. Ahmanson Unit Debt Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

LOS ANGELES -- Home Savings of America, the principal subsidiary of H.F. Ahmanson & Co., said it sold $250 million in subordinated debt. The seven-year notes were priced at 99.7% of face value, yielding 10.56%, the company said. The proceeds from the sale bring Home Savings' total risk-based capital ratio, or the ratio of capital to a calculation of assets adjusted to reflect their relative risk, to 8.3%, well above the 6.4% required by regulators, the company said. The lead underwriter for the offering is Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Home Savings is the nation's biggest savings and loan institution.
