United Air's May Traffic Rose

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

CHICAGO -- United Airlines, a unit of UAL Corp., said its May traffic rose 7.9% to 6.05 billion revenue passenger miles from 5.6 billion miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passsenger flown one mile. The carrier's May load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, rose to 63.4% from 62.6%.
