Who's News: Mutual of New York

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/06/90

MUTUAL OF NEW YORK (New York) -- Robert Holland Jr. and Thomas C. Theobald were elected trustees at this insurance, pension and investment-management firm, increasing the number to 16. Mr. Holland is chairman and general partner of Gilreath Industries, a Detroit-based plastics manufacturer. Mr. Theobald is chairman of Chicago-based Continental Bank Corp.
