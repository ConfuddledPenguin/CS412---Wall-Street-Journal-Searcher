Business Brief -- Anac Holding Corp.: Revco Unit to Sell Stores To Jack Eckerd of Florida

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Revco D.S. Inc., a unit of Anac Holding Corp., said it agreed to sell 220 of its drugstores in five Southeastern states to Jack Eckerd Corp. of Clearwater, Fla. Terms weren't disclosed. For Revco, operating under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code, the sale represents a step in its plan, announced Jan. 26, to divest itself of 712 stores in 17 states. Under Chapter 11, Revco receives court protection from its creditors while it formulates a reorganization plan.

Revco, which along with its parent is based in Twinsburg, Ohio, has said it plans to pare down its drugstores to about 1,100 in 10 contiguous Eastern states that account for 64% of its $2.7 billion in yearly sales. Revco said the latest sale to closely held Eckerd is subject to approval by the U.S. Bankruptcy Court.
