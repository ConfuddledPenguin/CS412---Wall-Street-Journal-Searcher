Business Brief -- Transgenic Sciences Inc.: Laboratory Animal Services Bought in Stock Transaction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/06/90

Transgenic Sciences Inc. said it acquired Laboratory Animal Services Inc. of Springfield, Va., for 142,618 common shares. In over-the-counter trading yesterday, Transgenic shares ended at $3.625 bid, which would give the transaction an indicated value of about $517,000.

Transgenic, a Worcester, Mass., genetic engineering concern, said the acquisition will significantly improve its results for the year and increase annual revenue about 10%.

For the year ended June 30, 1989, Transgenic had a loss of $1.2 million, or 40 cents a share, on revenue of $105,104.

Laboratory Animal Services, a preclinical testing facility, has a number of government clients, including the National Cancer Institute.
