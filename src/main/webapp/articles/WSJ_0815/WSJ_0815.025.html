Who's News: McDermott International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

MCDERMOTT INTERNATIONAL Inc. (New Orleans) -- John P. Eckert was appointed to the newly created positions of president and chief operating officer of this energy services concern. Mr. Eckert, 56 years old, was formerly senior vice president and Group Executive with McDermott's defense and nuclear power division.
