Business Brief -- Air Products & Chemicals Inc.: Sale of Stake in Project Brings Firm $6 Million Gain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

Air Products & Chemicals Inc. said the sale of equity in an energy project will produce a one-time gain of $6 million, or 11 cents a share, in the fourth quarter ending Sept. 30. The company said this shouldn't affect analysts' consensus operating earnings estimates of about $55 million, or $1 a share, for the quarter and about $215 million, or $3.88 a share, for the year. Air Products, based in Allentown, Pa., said the gain will come from the sale of a 50% equity interest in a $190 million cogeneration plant under construction in Cambria County, Pa. The transaction to Energy Investors Fund LP of Boston, Mass., is valued at $30 million, which will produce a $6 million gain in the fourth quarter.

The Cambria facility will burn waste coal to generate electricity and steam that will be sold under 20-year supply contracts. Air Products and its equity partners have invested $1.2 billion in six environmental and energy projects. The sale of equity provides Air Products with additional financing, accelerated cash flows and improved tax benefits.
