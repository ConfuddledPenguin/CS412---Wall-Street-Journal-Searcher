Business Brief -- First Executive Corp.: Insurer Reports 40% Drop In Profit for 2nd Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

First Executive Corp. reported a 40% drop in second-quarter earnings and a sharp decline in assets that may in part reflect substantial numbers of insurance policy surrenders. The Los Angeles-based insurance holding company posted net income of $30.5 million, or 10 cents a share, down from $51 million, or 45 cents a share, a year earlier. Per-share amounts were computed on 84.7 million shares in the latest period, down from 98.3 million a year earlier.

Revenue fell 23% to $440.7 million from $570.1 million.

Policy holders in the first quarter redeemed a record $1.33 billion of insurance policies and annuities. And, while the amount of redemptions wasn't reported for the latest quarter, First Executive's assets tumbled $1.53 billion in the latest quarter, which may in part reflect policies being turned in. Assets totaled $16.47 billion at June 30, down from $18 billion at March 31 and $18.7 billion at June 30, 1989.
