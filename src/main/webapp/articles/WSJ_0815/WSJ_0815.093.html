  • Date: 08/15/90

LITTON INDUSTRIES Inc. (Beverly Hills, Calif.) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it lowered its ratings on the company's senior debt to Baa-2 from Baa-1, and on subordinated debt to Baa-3 from Baa-2. The Prime-2 rating on commercial paper was confirmed. Moody's said its action is based "on the expectation for lower operating margins because of the constraints in defense spending expected during the next several years, as well as the slowdown in demand for industrial automation equipment from the automotive industry."
