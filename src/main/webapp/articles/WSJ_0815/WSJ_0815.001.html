Redemption Notices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended Aug. 10, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

ADAMS COUNTY, COLO., will call for redemption $250,000 of single-family mortgage revenue bonds, 1980 series B, due Sept. 1, 1993, 1995 and 2011, on Sept. 1, 1990. First Interstate Bank of Denver is trustee.

BEAVER COUNTY INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY will call pollution control revenue bonds, 1975 series B, Duquesne Light Co. Mansfield project, on Oct. 1, 1990; and 1977 series E, Cleveland Electric Illuminating Co. Mansfield project, due Sept. 15, 1992, on Sept. 15, 1990. Ameritrust Co., Cleveland, is trustee.

BI-STATE DEVELOPMENT AGENCY, MISSOURI-ILLINOIS METROPOLITAN DISTRICT will call St. Louis Metropolitan Area Transit revenue bonds, series 1963, due March 1, 1993, on Sept. 1, 1990. Mercantile Bank of St. Louis is paying agent.

DADE COUNTY, FLA., HOUSING FINANCE AUTHORITY, on Sept. 1, 1990, will call for redemption $2.5 million principal amount of its Florida single-family mortgage revenue bonds, Series 1980, due Sept. 9, 1991-1996 and 2011. Chemical Bank, New York, N.Y., is paying agent.

FLAT ROCK, MICH., will recall on Sept. 1, 1990, $750,000 of industrial development revenue bonds, Ford Motor Co., series A, due Sept. 1, 1992 and $750,000 of series B bonds, due Sept. 1, 1991. Morgan Guaranty Trust Co., New York, and Manufacturers Bank of Detroit are paying agents.

ONEIDA COUNTY, N.Y., will redeem $1.15 million of industrial development revenue bonds, 1972 series A, Allied Mills project, due March 1, 1992, on Sept. 4, 1990. Marine Midland Bank, New York, is trustee.

PALM BEACH COUNTY, FLA., HOUSING FINANCE AUTHORITY will recall $80 million of single-family mortgage purchase revenue bonds, 1980 series, due Sept. 1, 19911994, 2000, 2005, and 2012. Redemption date is Sept. 1, 1990. Barnett Banks of South Florida, Jacksonville, is trustee.

PAYNE COUNTY, OKLA., will on Sept. 1, 1990, recall $1,395,000 of single-family mortgage revenue bonds, 1980 series A, due 1991-1995 and Sept. 1, 2010. Bank of Oklahoma, Tulsa, and Chase Manhattan Bank, New York, are paying agents.

PHOENIX MUTUAL MORTGAGE FUNDING CORP. will redeem $33,561,600 principal amount of its 10 1/4% sinking fund bonds, due Sept. 12, 1992, on Sept. 12, 1990. The Chase Manhattan Bank, Luxembourg-Grand; London, England, and Geneva, Switzerland, and Banque de Commerce, S.A., Brussels, Belgium, are paying agents.

SECOND COLUMBUS HOUSING DEVELOPMENT CORP. will recall on Sept. 1, 1990, $5,000 of 13.33% mortgage revenue bonds due Sept. 1, 2024, Bull Creek Apartments/ FHA-insured and section 8 assisted project. Shawmut Bank, Boston and Hartford, Conn., is paying agent.

SOMERSET COUNTY, MD., will call $400,000 of economic development development revenue bonds, Sysco Corp. series 1982, due Sept. 1, 1992, on Sept. 1, 1990. First City, Texas, Houston, is paying agent.

SOUTHWESTERN COUNTIES, COLO., will call for redemption $315,000 of single-family mortgage participation revenue bonds, series 1979, on Sept. 1, 1990. Bonds mature on Sept. 1, 1991-1999 and 2011. First Interstate Bank of Denver is trustee.

SUMMIT COUNTY, OHIO, on Sept. 1, 1990, will recall $165,000 principal amount of economic development revenue bonds, series 1982 R&A Properties project, due Sept. 1, 2012. Bankers Trust Co., New York, is paying agent.

URBANA, ILL., on Sept. 1, 1990, will redeem $265,000 of residential mortgage revenue bonds series 1979, due Sept. 1, 1991-1996 and 2006. Continental Bank, Chicago, is paying agent.

CORPORATES EUROPEAN INVESTMENT BANK will call for redemption on Oct. 1, 1990, $2,468,000 principal amount of its 8 3/8% bonds due Oct. 1, 1992, and $3,623,000 principal amount of its 9 1/8% bonds due Oct. 1, 1998. Citibank, New York, N.Y., is paying agent.

INDIANAPOLIS POWER & LIGHT CO. will call $160,000 principal amount of its 9 7/8% first mortgage bonds, due Sept. 15, 2005, on Sept. 15, 1990. American National Bank & Trust Co. of Chicago, Ill., is trustee.

STUDENT LOAN MARKETING ASSOCIATION will call its 9.25% notes due Sept. 10, 1992, on Sept. 10, 1990. The Federal Reserve Bank of New York is paying agent.
