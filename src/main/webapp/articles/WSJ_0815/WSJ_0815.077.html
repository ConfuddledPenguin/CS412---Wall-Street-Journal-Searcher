Tenneco Unit Wins $1.1 Billion Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

WASHINGTON -- Newport News Shipbuilding & Dry Dock Co., a unit of Tenneco Inc., won a $1.1 billion Navy contract for refueling and overhaul of the nuclear carrier USS Enterprise. The award is a contract modification, with work to be performed in Newport News, Va. The job is to be completed in May 1994.
