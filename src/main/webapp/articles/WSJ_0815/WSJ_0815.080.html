Business Brief -- Citizens Utilities Co.: Net Income for 2nd Quarter Reported at $23.3 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

Citizens Utilities Co. said it had second quarter net income of $23.3 million, or 56 cents a share on both its Series A and Series B stock. That was up 13% from a year-earlier $20.6 million, or 45 cents a share on its Series A stock and 46 cents a share on its Series B stock. The Stamford, Conn.-based company's common stock is in two series.

Second-quarter revenue increased 9.8% to $88.5 million from a year-ago $80.6 million.

The utility holding company said rate increases for its water and wastewater operations in Arizona and California increased revenues $1.4 million. The company added that it has applications pending to increase rates in Colorado, Pennsylvania and Vermont that could increase revenues by $6 million.
