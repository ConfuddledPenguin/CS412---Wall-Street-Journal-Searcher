ConAgra Inc. Completes Purchase of Beatrice Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

OMAHA, Neb. -- ConAgra Inc. said it completed the purchase of Beatrice Co. from Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co. for $1.34 billion in cash and stock. ConAgra said the acquisition, which solidifies its position as the nation's second largest food processor, wasn't challenged by the Federal Trade Commission after a two-month review. Antitrust regulators had requested additional information about the pact last month.

In trading yesterday at the New York Stock Exchange, ConAgra common shares rose $1.375 a share to close at $34.375. ConAgra announced the completion of the purchase after the close of trading.

As part of the purchase, ConAgra said it is issuing 11,076,151 shares of common stock and 141,955 shares of convertible preferred stock. The preferred stock has a dividend rate of 6.75% and is convertible on a fully diluted basis into 9,631,433 ConAgra common shares.

With the close of the Beatrice purchase, ConAgra has about 134.4 million common shares outstanding.
