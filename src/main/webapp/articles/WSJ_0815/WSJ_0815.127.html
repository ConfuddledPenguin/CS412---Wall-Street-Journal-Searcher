TransCanada Plans Debt Issue

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

TORONTO -- TransCanada PipeLines Ltd. said it plans to issue 150 million Canadian dollars (US$130.9 million) of 11.90%, 25-year debentures. The pipeline concern said proceeds from the issue, which matures Aug. 20, 2015, will be used to reduce short-term debt incurred to finance the company's 1990 utility construction program.

The underwriting group consists of Toronto investment dealers Nesbitt Thomson Deacon Inc., RBC Dominion Securities Inc., Scotia McLeod Inc., Wood Gundy Inc. and Burns Fry Ltd.
