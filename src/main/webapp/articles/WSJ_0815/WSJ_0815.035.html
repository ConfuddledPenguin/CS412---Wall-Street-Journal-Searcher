QUALITY SYSTEMS Inc. (Tustin, Calif.) -- Sy Rubenstein, 51 years old, the company's chief operating officer, was named president, succeeding Sheldon Razin, 52, who remains chairman and chief executive officer. The post of chief operating officer has been eliminated. Quality Systems sells computerized information processing systems to medical and dental group practices.
