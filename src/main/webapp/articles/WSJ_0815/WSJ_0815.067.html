Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Citibank (South Dakota) N.A. and Citibank (Nevada) N.A., units of Citicorp., offering of $1.41 billion of credit card participation certificates through a trust, via Citicorp Investment Bank Ltd. and Merrill Lynch International Ltd.

Crown Crafts Inc., proposed offering of 2,375,000 common shares, of which 1,445,000 shares will be offered by the company and 930,000 by holders.

Portland General Electric Co., offering of up to $200 million of first mortgage bonds.
