Rockwell Plans Shuttle Layoffs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Rockwell International Corp. said it will lay off as many as 300 employees from its space-shuttle production and assembly facilities in Downey and Palmdale, Calif., by Sept. 30. The layoffs come as production work on the Endeavour shuttle orbiter program is winding down, a Rockwell spokesman said.
