MCI, TelecomUSA Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

WASHINGTON -- MCI Communications Corp. and TelecomUSA Inc. completed a merger, announced April 9, which combines the nation's second and fourth largest long distance telecommunications companies. MCI acquires all shares outstanding of TelecomUSA's common stock for $42 a share, for a total purchase price of about $1.25 billion.
