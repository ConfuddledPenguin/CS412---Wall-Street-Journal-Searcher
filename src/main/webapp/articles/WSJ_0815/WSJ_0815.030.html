Mid-America Bancorp Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Mid-America Bancorp Inc. said it has submitted a proposal through the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp. to Resolution Trust Corp. to acquire the assets and assume the liabilities of Future Federal Savings Bank, a mutual savings association, of Louisville, Ky. According to the bank holding company's proposal, business operations would continue at all Future Federal branch locations. If consummated, Mid-America expects the acquisition to have a positive impact on the company's earnings. Mid-America said there is no assurance that its proposal will be accepted.
