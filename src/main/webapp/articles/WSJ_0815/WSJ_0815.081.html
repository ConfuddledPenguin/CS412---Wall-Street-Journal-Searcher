Commercial Paper Rates Rise at Citicorp's Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 7.861% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 7.793% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $415 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.847% to 7.87%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate rose to 7.764% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.7186% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $380 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.74% to 7.788%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
