Business Brief -- Sotheby's Holdings Inc.: Auction House Says Profit Rose 26% in Second Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

Sotheby's Holdings Inc. reported second-quarter net income climbed 26% on a 20% rise in revenue, led by auction sales of Impressionist paintings, books and manuscripts and other items. The New York-based auction house said net was $64.3 million, or $1.12 a share, up from $51.2 million, or 89 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue advanced to $196 million from $163 million.

Sotheby's cautioned that second-half total auction sales may drop from an "exceptionally strong" year-earlier figure of $1.6 billion. First-half auction sales climbed 20%, also to $1.6 billion.

The spring season saw "strong" demand and high prices for a number of artworks, said Michael L. Ainslie, president and chief executive officer.
