Boatmen's Adopts Poison Pill

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

ST. LOUIS, Mo. -- Boatmen's Bancshares Inc. said it adopted a shareholder rights plan aimed at protecting stockholders from "coercive and unfair takeover tactics by a hostile acquirer." The bank holding company said it isn't aware of any takeover attempt targeting Boatmen's, but considered adoption of the plan "prudent" in light of the "uncertainty of the recent takeover environment in the banking industry."

Under the plan, shareholders will on Aug. 31 be issued rights that, under certain hostile-takeover circumstances, permit the holder to buy stock in the company at half-price.
