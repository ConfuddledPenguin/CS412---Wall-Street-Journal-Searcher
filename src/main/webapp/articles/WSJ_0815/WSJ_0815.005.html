Who's News: Roy F. Weston Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

ROY F. WESTON Inc. (West Chester, Pa.) -- William J. Marrazzo, chief operating officer, was elected to the additional position of president of this environmental engineering, design and consulting company. Mr. Marrazzo, 41 years old, succeeds Roy F. Weston, 79, who remains chairman and chief executive officer.
