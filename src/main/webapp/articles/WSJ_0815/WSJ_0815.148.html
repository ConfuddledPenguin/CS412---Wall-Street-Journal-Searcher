Business Brief -- Health Care Property Investors: Real Estate Trust to Buy, Lease Back 12 Facilities

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

Health Care Property Investors Inc. said it agreed to acquire 12 medical facilities from Charter Medical Corp. of Macon, Ga., for $76.2 million, and lease back the facilities to Charter. The transaction is scheduled to be completed by Sept. 30. In addition, if certain conditions are met, Health Care Property would acquire and lease back five additional properties for about $36 million near the end of the first quarter of next year.

The initial 12 facilities to be acquired include nine psychiatric hospitals with 652 beds, one acute general hospital with 103 beds, and two adjoining medical office buildings. The facilities are located in six states in the Southeast and Midwest.

Health Care Property is a real estate investment trust that invests in health care facilities.
