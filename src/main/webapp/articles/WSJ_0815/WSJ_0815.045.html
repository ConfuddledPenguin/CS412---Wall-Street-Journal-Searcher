Who's News: Advanced Logic Research Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

ADVANCED LOGIC RESEARCH Inc. (Irvine, Calif.) -- Therese E. Myers and Kenneth W. Simonds were named directors, succeeding I.F. Tang and Frederick Tan, who resigned according to a plan made when the company went public in April. Mr. Myers is a founder and president of Quarterdeck Office Systems, a supplier of software to the computer industry. Mr. Simonds is chairman, president and chief executive officer of Teradata Corp., a Los Angeles-based maker of database-management computer systems.
