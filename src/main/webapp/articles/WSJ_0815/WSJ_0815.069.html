Liposome Technology Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

MENLO PARK, Calif. -- New York investor David Blech said in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that he increased his stake in Liposome Technology Inc. to 35.3% of the common shares outstanding. Mr. Blech said he holds 3,573,680 shares, including 123,500 shares purchased Aug. 1 at prices of $3.06 to $3.50 a share. No reason was given for the boost.

A Liposome spokesman said Mr. Blech already is the company's largest shareholder, but was unable to confirm the recent additions to his stake.
