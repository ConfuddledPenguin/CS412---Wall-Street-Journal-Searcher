Who's News: Long-Term Credit Bank of Japan Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

LONG-TERM CREDIT BANK OF JAPAN Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Takashi Uehara was named general manager of the New York branch, a subsidiary of this bank. Mr. Uehara succeeds Koji Hirao, who returns to Tokyo headquarters as director and general manager of its treasury and securities group. Mr. Uehara, previously general manager of the Americas division in Tokyo, was also named chairman of LTCB Trust Co. a New York-based subsidiary.
