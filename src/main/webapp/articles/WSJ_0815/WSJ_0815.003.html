U.K. Industrial Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

LONDON -- Industrial production in Britain rose 2.2% in June from May and was up 5.6% from June 1989, according to provisional data released by the Central Statistical Office. Market forecasts called for an 0.2% month-on-month rise in industrial output in June and a year-on-year advance of 3.4%.

The government attributed the sharp month-on-month rise largely to production of the U.K. energy sector, which is typically among the most erratic segments of its industrial output calculations.
