Technology: Franklin Electronic Dictionary

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

MT. HOLLY, N.J. -- Franklin Electronic Publishers Inc. said it will publish a Chinese/English dictionary in hand-held electronic form. Franklin said it has licensed electronic publishing rights to the Concise English/Chinese, Chinese/English dictionary from the Oxford University Press. The electronic dictionary, which will provide definitions, phonetic spelling and grammatical information, will be available in the fall of 1991.
