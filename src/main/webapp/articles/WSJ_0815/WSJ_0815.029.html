Lufthansa Slates Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Delaware North Cos. and LSG Lufthansa Service G.m.b.H., part of Lufthansa German Airlines, said in a joint announcement that LSG will acquire Dairy Farm Catering Group, the Hong Kong-based in-flight food-service and general catering subsidiaries of Delaware North.No sales price was disclosed. Officials of both companies said the announcement was made to end speculation that Dairy Farm, which has been hotly pursued by several companies, was still for sale. The sale is scheduled to close on Sept. 20, subject to two approvals.
