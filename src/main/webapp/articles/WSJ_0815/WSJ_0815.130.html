Westair Signs Option for Jets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- Westair Holding Inc. and its airline operating unit, United Express, signed a $700 million purchase option to buy as many as 60 Embraer EMB145 jets. The airline holding company said the preliminary agreement between it and the Brazilian aircraft manufacturer is subject to the airline signing a formal purchase agreement in 1992.

Under terms of the pact, Westair said the first delivery is scheduled for the fourth quarter of 1993.
