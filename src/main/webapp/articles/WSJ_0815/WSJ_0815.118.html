Business Brief -- Sequa Corp.: Earnings for 2nd Quarter Rose 8.1%; Units Improve

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

Sequa Corp. said second quarter net earnings rose 8.1%, reflecting improved earnings at its aerospace and specialty chemicals units. The company's net income for the quarter was $19.7 million, or $1.94 a share, compared to $18.2 million, or $1.67 a share, during the year-earlier period. Sequa posted revenue of $568.4 million, up from $482.8 million.

Sequa's results during the second quarter of 1989 included earnings from discontinued operations of $1.3 million, or 13 cents a share. Per share results for 1990 are based on approximately 9.6 million shares outstanding; 1989 results are based on 10.3 million shares outstanding. The company has been buying back shares since late 1987, including about 300,000 acquired shares in 1990.

For the six months, New York-based Sequa reported net income of $38 million, or, $3.72 a share, compared to $38.6 million, or $3.55 a share during 1989. Revenues during the six months increased to $1.1 billion from $930,000.
