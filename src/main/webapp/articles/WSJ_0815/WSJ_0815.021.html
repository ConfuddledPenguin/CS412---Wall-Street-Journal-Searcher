American Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

CINCINNATI -- American Financial Corp. said it is conducting negotiations to merge its Hunter Savings Association unit with Provident Bancorp Inc. If the merger is completed, American Financial will receive cash and stock for Hunter Savings. The new entity would be the third-largest commercial bank in Cincinnati, with about $3 billion in assets. American Financial is a private holding company owned by Carl H. Linder. Provident Bancorp, a Cincinnati-based bank holding company, was spun off from American Financial in 1980; it is also controlled by the Lindner family.
