Dividend News: MEM Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

MEM Co. -- This New York-based marketer of fragrances said it will omit its five-cent quarterly dividend in the third quarter. The company said it is experiencing a liquidity shortage. MEM has $16 million in bank debt, and is seeking to borrow an additional $10 million to finance its business during the Christmas selling season. Earlier this year, it reduced its quarterly dividend to five cents from 10 cents.
