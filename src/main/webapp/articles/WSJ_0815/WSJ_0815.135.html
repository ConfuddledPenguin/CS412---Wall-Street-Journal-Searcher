Ashton-Tate to Buy Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

TORRANCE, Calif. -- Ashton-Tate Corp. said its board authorized the repurchase of two million common shares. The maker of microcomputer software said it would buy the shares on the open market and use them for the company's employee stock option and purchase plans.

Ashton-Tate currently has about 27 million shares outstanding.
