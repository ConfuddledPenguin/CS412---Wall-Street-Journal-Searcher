Aviation's Villains

My flight's delayed, my luggage (new)

Is on its way to Kalamazoo,

The cocktail lounge is pouring belts

For folks who'd rather be somewhere else,

When asked when we'll get in the skies,

The counterpeople roll their eyes.

Whose fault is this familiar plight?

I'll tell you who -- the Brothers Wright.

-- Dick Emmons.
