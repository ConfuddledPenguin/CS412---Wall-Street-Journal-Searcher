Business Brief -- Durham Corp.: Investor Group Will Seek Sale of Firm or a Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

A group of investors that includes Financial Securities Advisers Inc. and Bailey Financial Group Inc. said it plans to propose that Durham Corp. solicit offers for the sale of the firm or a merger. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the group said it also asked Durham to consider increasing the number of its directors to 18 and elect B.W. Landstreet III to fill the spot that would be created. The group also proposed that J.C.D. Bailey be elected a director if holders vote to amend company bylaws to allow for more than 18 directors. The group said it holds a stake of about 5.8% in Durham.

In April, Raleigh, N.C.-based Durham, a life insurance holding company, rejected a proposal by the group to acquire all of its shares for $30 a share and subordinated debentures valued at $7.50 a share. Officials of Durham couldn't be reached for comment yesterday.
