Loral Unit's Israeli Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

NEW YORK -- Loral Corp. said the Israeli government selected a team the company is heading to provide sonar systems and associated electronics for Israeli Dolphin-class submarines. The contract, which is in final negotiations, is worth $92.5 million to the team, which includes the defense electronics firm's Loral Defense Systems-Akron unit, Krupp Atlas Elektronik G.m.b.H. of Bremen, West Germany, and selected Israeli subcontractors.

Under the program, Loral will supply systems for two submarines and one land-based test system. Deliveries are expected to begin in 1994.
