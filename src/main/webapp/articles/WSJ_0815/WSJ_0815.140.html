Manor Care Agrees on Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

SILVER SPRING, Md. -- Manor Care Inc. said it agreed to purchase the assets of Econo Lodges of America Inc. from New Image Realty Inc., in a transaction valued at about $60 million. The purchase includes 615 Econo Lodges franchise hotels in North America and 85 Friendship Inn franchises, which will be incorporated into Manor Care's Choice Hotels International Inc. operation. That operation will then number 2,301 hotels.
