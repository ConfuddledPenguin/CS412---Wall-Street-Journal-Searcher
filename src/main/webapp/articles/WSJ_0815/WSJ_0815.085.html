Business Brief -- Measurex Corp.: Suitor Files With SEC To Acquire 12.6% of Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

Harnischfeger Industries Inc. said in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that it raised its stake in Measurex Corp. to 12.6% of common shares outstanding. Harnischfeger, Milwaukee, which has a prior agreement with Measurex to acquire up to 20%, said it now holds 2.2 million shares, including 237,400 shares purchased between Aug. 6 and Aug. 13 at prices from $18 to $18.875.

Measurex, Cupertino, Calif., a maker of industrial process control systems, said it was "delighted" with the increased holding by Harnischfeger, a maker of paper-making machinery and mining equipment.
