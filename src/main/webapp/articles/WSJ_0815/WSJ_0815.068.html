Financing Business: Beverly Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

BEVERLY ENTERPRISES Inc. (Fort Smith, Ark.) -- said it is offering $40 million of 9% convertible subordinated debentures due Aug. 15, 2000. Each debenture is convertible into common stock at an initial conversion price of $6.45 a share. The offering was managed by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Stephens Inc. The company said it intends to use the net proceeds to repay debt and satisfy other obligations under a restructuring agreement. The company operates nursing facilities, pharmacies and retirement homes.
