International Brief -- Rheinmetall Berlin AG: Company Says Pretax Profit Grew 13% in the First Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/15/90

Rheinmetall Berlin AG said its group pretax profit rose 13% in the first half of 1990 to 117.4 million German marks ($74.4 million) from 104.4 million marks a year earlier. The big West German maker of weapons, machinery and automotive electronics said its financial results in the first half were better than expected.

Looking ahead for the remainder of 1990, the company said it expects "satisfactory developments for sales and earnings" for the world-wide group.
