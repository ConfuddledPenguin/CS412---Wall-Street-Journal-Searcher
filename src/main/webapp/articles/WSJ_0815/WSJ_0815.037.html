Letters to the Editor: Casting Wrong Mold On Plastics Recycling

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/15/90

Your July 5 page-one article on plastics recycling is inaccurate, poorly researched and in my opinion generally untrue. Millions of pounds a year of plastics are being recycled currently. A number of plants are on-stream, processing post-consumer plastics for recycling. New plants are under construction, including several facilities for National Polystyrene Recycling Co. to recycle post-consumer polystyrene. The editorialized article presented a sarcastic viewpoint of plastics recycling with generally incorrect information on recycling processes and certainly misinformation on recycled-plastics markets and uses. I cannot believe you have progressed from business reporting to business bashing for a significantly large industry.

James H. Browne

Acworth, Ga.
