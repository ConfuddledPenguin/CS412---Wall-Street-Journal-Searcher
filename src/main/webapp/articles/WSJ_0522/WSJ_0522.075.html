Who's News: Chemed Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

CHEMED Corp. (Cincinnati) -- Paul E. Barkman, president of Chemed's DuBois International Inc. unit; James A. Cunningham, director of First Boston Corp.; and Judi K. O'Steen, vice president/finance and treasurer of Omnicare Inc. were elected directors of this health-care chemicals company, filling three vacancies.
