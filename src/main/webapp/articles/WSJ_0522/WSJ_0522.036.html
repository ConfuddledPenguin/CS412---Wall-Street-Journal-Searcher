Clothes Call

Time was when jackets and ties were a must

To be properly attired;

Now even the better places have signs:

"Shoes and Shirts Required."

-- George O. Ludcke.

The Bard tied himself to a tether,

Because the world, in scene after scene,

Cannot get its act together.
