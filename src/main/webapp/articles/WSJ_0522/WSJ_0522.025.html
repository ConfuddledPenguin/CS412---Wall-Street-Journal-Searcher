International Rectifier, Siliconix Settle Suit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- International Rectifier Corp. and Siliconix Inc. said they reached agreement on patent-infringement litigation concerning International Rectifier's power-transistor technology. Under terms of the accord, Siloconix, of Santa Clara, Calif., will pay International Rectifier $12 million over a two-year period and will acquire a royalty-bearing license under the litigated patents.

Siliconix filed for protection under Chapter 11 of the Federal bankruptcy code April 10 after a U.S. district court held valid all five of International Rectifier's patents on the power-transistor technology. Implementation of the agreement is subject to approval of the bankruptcy court.

Siliconix supplies advanced semiconductor products. International Rectifier supplies power semiconductors and other products. The litigation had been pending since 1986.
