Who's News: Hudson General Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

HUDSON GENERAL Corp. (Great Neck, N.Y.) -- Paul R. Pollack, previously senior vice president of this aviation services company, was named executive vice president, operations, and chief operating officer. Raymond J. Rieder, previously vice president, marketing, was named senior vice president and chief marketing officer. Michael Rubin, previously vice president, finance, and chief financial officer, was named executive vice president, finance and administration, and chief financial officer.
