Dividend News: Huntway Partners L.P.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

HUNTWAY PARTNERS L.P. said, as expected, it will make a first-quarter distribution to its preference unit holders after obtaining a capital infusion and restructuring the partnership's bank loan. As previously reported, Huntway, a Valencia, Calif., refiner of asphalt and petroleum products, declared a distribution of 34.5 cents a unit, payable May 30 to preference units of record May 15. Huntway said it completed a $5 million private placement of 625,000 newly issued preference units at $8 each to an affiliate of its general partner. The sale increased Huntway's preference units outstanding to about 6.2 million.
