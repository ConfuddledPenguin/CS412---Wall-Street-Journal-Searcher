Business Brief -- BTR PLC: Firm Sets Purchase of Unit Of United Technologies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

BTR PLC, one of Britain's biggest industrial conglomerates, said its Schlegal Corp. subsidiary will buy Global Automotive Sealing Systems Group from United Technologies Corp. for $200 million. The purchase follows BTR's defeat last month in its quest for Norton Co., a Worcester, Mass.-based chemicals and abrasives maker that decided to be acquired by French glassmaker Cie. de Saint-Gobain S.A. in a friendly transaction.

In Hartford, Conn., United Technologies said proceeds of the sale will be used to reduce debt. The automotive sealing systems business develops, manufactures and markets auto body sealing products. It employs 4,100 people world-wide and had sales of about $275 million in 1989.
