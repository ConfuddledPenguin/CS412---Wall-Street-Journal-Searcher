South Korean Trade With U.S.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- South Korea's trade surplus with the U.S. shrank by 64% in 1989, while its trade deficit with Japan widened by 18%, according to a report by the Bank of Korea. The central bank said the country's trade surplus with the U.S. amounted to $6.1 billion in 1989, a sharp drop from $10.5 billion a year earlier.
