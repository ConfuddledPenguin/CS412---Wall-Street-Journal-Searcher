Sweden's Current Account Gap

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

STOCKHOLM -- The deficit on Sweden's current account, or balance of payments, widened to 3.3 billion kronor ($548.1 million) in March from a deficit of 600 million kronor a year earlier, the Riksbank, Sweden's central bank, said. The gap on the current account, which includes traded items, services and transfers, grew from the 3.1 billion-krona deficit recorded in February, the Riksbank said.

For the 12 months through March, the current account deficit widened to 40.3 billion kronor from a deficit of 13 billion kronor a year earlier.

March's trade surplus rose to 3.3 billion kronor from 1.7 billion kronor in February, and was up from a 2.6 billion-krona surplus in March 1989.
