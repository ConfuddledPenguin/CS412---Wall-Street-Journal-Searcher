Kirschner Medical Award

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

TIMONIUM, Md. -- Kirschner Medical Corp. said an American Arbitration Association panel awarded it $1.2 million in its dispute with a former supplier. The proceeding began with a complaint by the supplier, ALM Equipment Hospitaliers S.A. of France. The arbitration panel last summer decided that ALM had unfairly breached an agreement to supply Kirschner with surgical lights.
