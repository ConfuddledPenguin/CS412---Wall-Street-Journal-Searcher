Business Brief -- Moog Inc.: Company Plans Buy-Back Of Up to 300,000 Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

Moog Inc. said its board authorized the buy-back of as many as 200,000 shares of its Class A common and 100,000 shares of Class B common. The East Aurora, N.Y., maker of control parts and systems said the purchased shares will be used in connection with various employee benefit plans.

Moog has 6.3 million shares of Class A common and 1.7 million shares of Class B outstanding.
