Gerber Products to Sell Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

FREMONT, Mich. -- Gerber Products Co. said it agreed to sell its child-care subsidiary to a private investment partnership. The baby-food concern didn't disclose terms of the planned sale.

Gerber Children's Centers Inc. operates 115 centers in 12 states, and manages four others for other employers.
