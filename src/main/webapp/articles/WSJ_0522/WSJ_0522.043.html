Mitchell Energy Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

THE WOODLANDS, Texas -- Mitchell Energy & Development Corp. said it paid Oryx Energy Co., Dallas, $7.5 million for interests in 44 producing wells and approximately 7,000 acres of leases in northern Texas. Mitchell, an oil and natural gas and real estate concern, said the purchase gives it working interests ranging from 25% to 100%. It will operate all but three of the 44 wells.
