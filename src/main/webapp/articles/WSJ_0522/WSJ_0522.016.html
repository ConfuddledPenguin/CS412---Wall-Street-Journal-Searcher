Who's News: Federal Home Loan Bank of New York

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

FEDERAL HOME LOAN BANK OF NEW YORK (New York) -- Mark Broxmeyer, 41 years old, was named public-interest director of this financing and banking services concern, to complete an unexpired four-year term through Dec. 31. He is a general partner of Fairfield Properties, a Commack, N.Y., owner and manager of commercial and residential property.
