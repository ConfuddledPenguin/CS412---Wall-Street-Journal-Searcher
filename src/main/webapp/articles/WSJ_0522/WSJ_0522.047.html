Fluor Unit Receives Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp. said its Fluor Daniel Inc. unit was awarded a $19 million contract by Navajo Refining Co., a unit of Dallas-based Holly Corp., to provide engineering, procurement and construction services to expand Navajo's Artesia, N.M., refinery. The project, for which engineering is under way, will increase the refinery's gasoline production to 60,000 barrels a day from 40,000 barrels. Fluor is an engineering, construction and technical services company.
