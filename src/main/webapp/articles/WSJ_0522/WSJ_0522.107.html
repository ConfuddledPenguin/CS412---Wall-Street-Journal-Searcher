Business Brief -- Baltimore Gas & Electric Co.: Utility Requests Rate Hike Of 12.1%, or $198 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

Baltimore Gas & Electric Co., Baltimore, requested an annual rate increase of 12.1% or $198 million in an application filed with the Maryland Public Service Commission. The increase requests includes $45 million, proposed as the first part of a three-stage phase-in of revenues required for the commercial operation of a second generating unit at its Brandon Shores Power Plant. The plant is scheduled for completion in June 1991.

Two remaining phases are designed so customers won't pay any more during 1991 than they would have under a traditional single increase approach, the company said.
