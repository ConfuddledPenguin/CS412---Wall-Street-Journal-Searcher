Bank of New England Unit Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

BOSTON -- Bank of New England Corp. said it has reached final agreement with the Bank of Tokyo Ltd. on the sale of its BancNewEngland Leasing Group. Under terms of the accord, Bank of Tokyo will buy approximately $500 million of BancNewEngland's portfolio assets.

Bank of New England had previously stated that it would receive a premium of $92.5 million for the assets. But a BNE spokesman said the premium amount "has been downsized somewhat." The new figure wasn't disclosed.

The bank said the sale virtually completes its asset sales program, with the exception of affiliate banks in Maine and Rhode Island.
