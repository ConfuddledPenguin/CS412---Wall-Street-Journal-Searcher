Marketing Brief -- Carolco Pictures Inc.: French Firm Takes Stake Of 5% Via Production Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

Carolco Pictures Inc. said Canal Plus Productions, a wholly owned unit of Canal Plus S.A., a French pay-television concern, has taken a 5% stake in the Los Angeles-based entertainment concern as part of a production agreement between the two companies. Carolco said the Canal Plus unit will buy $30 million in new Carolco Series B convertible preferred shares. The preferred stock will be convertible into Carolco common at $18.50 a share and will pay a yearly dividend of 5%. Canal Plus also will receive a warrant to purchase 150,000 Carolco shares at $18.50, Carolco said. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Carolco common shares closed at $13, up 25 cents.

The companies also announced their intention to form a new joint venture to produce films. Canal Plus will appoint a representative to join Carolco's board. The agreement follows recent announcements by Canal Plus executives that the company wants to broaden its fledgling film-production business.
