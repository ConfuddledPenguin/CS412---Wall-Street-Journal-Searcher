  • Date: 05/22/90

Preston Townley, Conference Board president, to the Montreal Board of Trade on April 11:

When we plan meetings, we have waiting lists of executives ready to extol their successes. But good failures are hard to come by. . . . In fact, I can just about guarantee anyone in this audience a keynote speaking engagement if you are willing to tell how you fell on your face with your marketing plan last year.
