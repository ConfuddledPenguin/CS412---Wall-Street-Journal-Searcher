Who's News: Ashton-Tate Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

ASHTON-TATE Corp. (Torrance, Calif.) -- David R. Proctor, formerly Asia Pacific software products director at International Business Machines Corp., was named vice president and general manager, data base division, at this software company, succeeding Floyd Bradley, who held the post on an interim basis and remains vice president and general manager, Europe. Joseph F. Brilando, vice president, corporate marketing and strategy, resigned to become a consultant; the post is to be left vacant.
