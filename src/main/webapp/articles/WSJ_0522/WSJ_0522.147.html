International Brief -- Co op AG: Three Companies Mull Bid For Majority Stake in Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

Metro International AG, a Swiss-German retailing group, said it is considering making a joint bid with two partners for a majority stake in Co op AG -- a Frankfurt/Main, West Germany-based food retailer. Metro's partners are Lonrho PLC, a London-based industrial and trading conglomerate, and Asko Deutsche Kaufhaus AG, a Saarbrucken, West Germany-based retailing concern.

Metro didn't disclose any details on the offer for Co op. The three companies received financial information on Co op last Thursday and now have to study the data before making any further decision, Metro said.

Metro's announcement essentially confirmed reports by industry sources over the weekend that Metro and Lonrho are preparing a joint offer for Co op.

According to industry sources, Metro, Lonrho and Asko are willing to pay 400 million marks ($147.4 million) for Co op, including its debt of more than 500 million marks.
