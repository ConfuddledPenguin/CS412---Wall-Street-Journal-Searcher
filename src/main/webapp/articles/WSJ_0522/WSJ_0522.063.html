Who's News: Emerald Homes Limited Partnership

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

EMERALD HOMES Limited Partnership (Phoenix, Ariz.) -- Lewis J. Wright, 63 years old, who has run his own real estate company since he resigned last year as vice chairman of Western Savings, was elected president and chief executive officer of this home builder, succeeding Philip J. Polich, 41, who has been elected chairman of Emerald Homes Inc., its managing general partner. Mr. Polich last week was also named president of Verit Industries, a Phoenix-based real estate concern, which owns 23.5% of Emerald Homes L.P.
