Rohr May Buy Back Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

CHULA VISTA, Calif. -- Rohr Industries Inc. said it might buy back as many as 300,000 common shares. The maker of aircraft-engine parts said it was considering the purchases, to be made from time to time, because the company believes its stock to be a good investment at the current price.

Rohr's stock closed yesterday at $21.375, up 87.5 cents a share, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.

The company may use the repurchased stock for its employee-stock ownership plan or for other general purposes, it said. Rohr currently has about 17.8 million shares outstanding.
