Business Brief -- UAL Corp.: France Clears United Air For Chicago-Paris Flights

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

UAL Corp., Chicago, said the French government agreed to allow its United Airlines unit to operate nonstop Chicago-Paris flights, beginning Aug. 1. The company said the approval, coupled with an earlier authorization from the U.S. Department of Transportation, "clears the way for United to inaugurate service to its second European city this summer." United began its first flights to Europe last week, with service to Frankfurt, West Germany. The Paris flights represent "another important step" in the company's plan to expand its international service, UAL said.
