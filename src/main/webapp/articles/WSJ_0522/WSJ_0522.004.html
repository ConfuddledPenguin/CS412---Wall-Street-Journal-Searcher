DAVID N. MARTIN agreed in principle to become chairman of Fahlgren & Swink, a Parkersburg, W. Va.-based ad unit of Lintas:USA. He will continue as chairman of Hawley Martin Partners, which last week agreed to be acquired by Lintas. Yesterday's advertising column incorrectly reported that Hawley Martin and Fahlgren & Swink were merging and misstated the headquarters for Fahlgren & Swink. The story also incorrectly identified Cincinnati Bell as a client of Fahlgren & Swink. (See: "Marketing & Media -- Advertising: Outdoor Ad Sellers Seek Return of Packaged Goods to Billboards" -- WSJ May 21, 1990)
