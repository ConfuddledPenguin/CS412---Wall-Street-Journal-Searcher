Who's News: Pricor Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

PRICOR Inc. (Murfreesboro, Tenn.) -- Amy S. Harrison was named to the new position of vice chairman and Stephen H. Norris was named president and chief operating officer of this provider of correctional and detention services. Ms. Harrison had been executive vice president of the company's Advocate Schools subsidiary. Mr. Norris had been commissioner of the Tennessee Department of Corrections. He succeeds Gil R. Walker, who resigned. The company also named William J. Ballard to fill the vacant position of treasurer and chief financial officer. Mr. Ballard had been executive vice president of Rodgers Business Interests Ltd. in Nashville.
