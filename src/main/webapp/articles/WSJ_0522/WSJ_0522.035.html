Who's News: Organogenesis Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

ORGANOGENESIS Inc. (Cambridge, Mass.) -- William J. Hopke, vice president and treasurer of Dominion Capital Inc., and Neil P. Ramsey, a general partner of International Asset Management Fund, were named directors of this biotechnology concern. The appointments increase board membership to seven. As previously reported, Dominion Capital and International Asset Management were two primary investors in a $4.5 million privately placed common stock and warrant offering by Organogenesis.
