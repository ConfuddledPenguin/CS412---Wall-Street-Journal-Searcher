Who's News: BankAmerica Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

BANKAMERICA Corp. (San Francisco) -- Martin Stein, executive vice president and chief information officer at PaineWebber Inc., was named an executive vice president of this bank holding company and head of its BankAmerica Systems Engineering unit, succeeding Michael Simmons, who resigned last month. In addition, Eric Von Pingel, director of the real estate appraisal department, was named a senior vice president of the company's principal Bank of America subsidiary.
