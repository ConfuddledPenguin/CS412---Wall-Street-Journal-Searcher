Whittaker Credit Facility

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

LOS ANGELES -- Whittaker Corp. said it has commitments from several banks for a new, $140 million credit facility to replace debt remaining from a recapitalization it implemented last June. Whittaker, a maker of high-technology equipment, said the new facility gives the company the choice of borrowing at prime or other rates if they are more favorable. The facility should be in place within 30 days, Whittaker said. Security Pacific National Bank, based here, is the lead bank.

Whittaker borrowed $360 million for the recapitalization after rejecting a $47.50-a-share unsolicited takeover bid from a closely held investment concern in December 1988.
