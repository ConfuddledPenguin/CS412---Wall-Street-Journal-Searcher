Raw-Steel Output Fell 0.3% for Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 0.3% last week to 1,955,000 from 1,960,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's ouput rose 2.5% from the 1,908,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 87.0% of its capability last week, compared with 87.2% the previous week and 89.0% a year earlier.
