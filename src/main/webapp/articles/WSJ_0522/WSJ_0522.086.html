Who's News: Mobil Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

MOBIL CORP. (New York) -- Effective Sept. 1, Jack E. Crawford, 58-year-old vice president of Mobil Chemical Co. and general manager of the Petrochemicals division, becomes vice president of research, engineering and environmental affairs at Mobil Oil Corp. and president of Mobil Research & Development Corp. Mr. Crawford succeeds Joseph V. D'Ambrisi, 62, who retires Nov. 1 after 40 years. Succeeding Mr. Crawford is Jerry G. Griffith, 58, who currently is vice president of engineering at Mobil Research & Development. Edward F. Kondis, 50, who currently is vice president for natural gas at Mobil's Exploration and Producing division, succeeds Mr. Griffith.
