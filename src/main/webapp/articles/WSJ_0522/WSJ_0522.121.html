Business Brief -- K mart Corp.: Retailer Posts 6.4% Increase In Fiscal 1st-Quarter Profit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

K mart Corp., citing an emphasis on lower shelf prices, said net income for its first quarter ended May 2 increased 6.4% on a 16% rise in sales. The Troy, Mich., discount retailer said net income was $100.1 million, or 50 cents a share, compared with the year-earlier $94.1 million, or 47 cents a share. Sales rose to $7.04 billion from $6.07 billion.

The earnings also reflect the inclusion of K mart's recently acquired Pace Membership Warehouse stores, the company said.

K mart noted that net interest expense rose to $56.5 million from $33.2 million, primarily because of greater short-term borrowing for high inventory levels, but also partially related to the recent $322 million Pace acquisition.

The company said it is "encouraged by the improvement in sales in the first quarter and in early May," and said it anticipates "an improving trend in sales and earnings."
