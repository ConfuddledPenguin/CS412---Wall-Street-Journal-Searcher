Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Arctco Inc., proposed initial public offering of 3.2 million common shares, of which 1.6 million will be offered by the company and 1.6 million by holders, via PaineWebber Inc., Dain Bosworth Inc. and Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc.

Bank of San Francisco Holding Co., offering of 1,111,837 class A common shares, of which one million is to be sold by the company and the balance by a holder, via PaineWebber.

Columbia Gas System Inc., shelf offering of up to $100 million of debentures.

The Federated States of Micronesia, shelf offering of up to $300 million of medium-term notes, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

First Chicago Corp., shelf offering of $800 million in debt securities and warrants.

Orthopedic Services Inc., initial public offering of 2,250,000 common shares, via Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. and Paribas Corp.
