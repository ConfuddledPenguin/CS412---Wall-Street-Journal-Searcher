Financing Business: Detroit Edison Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

DETROIT EDISON Co. said it will redeem 200,000 shares of its $2.75 series preference stock pursuant to provisions of a sinking fund for this security. Certificates for the shares, to be selected through random drawing, will be redeemed July 15 at $25 a share, with payment to be made on or after that date. All shares redeemed will be canceled and will have the status of authorized but unissued shares, the utility said.
