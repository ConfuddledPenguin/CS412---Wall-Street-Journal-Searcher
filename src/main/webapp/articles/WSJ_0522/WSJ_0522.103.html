Latin American Fund Files For Initial Public Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

NEW YORK -- An investment group filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the initial public offering of a closed-end fund that will invest primarily in Mexico, Chile and Brazil. The offering is for a minimum of four million shares, or $60 million. A syndicate led by Salomon Inc. will underwrite the offering. The fund, called the Latin American Investment Fund Inc., will be jointly managed by Salomon Brothers Asset Management Inc. and BEA Associates Inc.

The fund will invest primarily in stocks listed on major Latin American exchanges, but its managers will also be able to invest in Latin American debt for trading or conversion into equity investments.
