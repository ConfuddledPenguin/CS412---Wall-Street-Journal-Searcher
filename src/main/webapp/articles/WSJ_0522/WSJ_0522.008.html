Valero Natural Gas Partners

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- Valero Natural Gas Partners Limited Partnership, a natural gas transmission concern, said it agreed to supply as much as 33 million cubic feet of natural gas a day, or about 12 billion cubic feet annually, at market prices during a 10-year period to Washington Gas Light Co., a utility based in the District of Columbia that serves customers in Washington, Maryland and Virginia.
