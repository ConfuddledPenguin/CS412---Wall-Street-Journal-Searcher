Lee Data Seeks Name Change

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/22/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Lee Data Corp. said it will ask shareholders in July to approve a change in the company's name to Apertus Technologies. Lee Data said Apertus Technologies was formed after the company sold its commercial and international business to IIS Inc. last March. IIS will continue using the Lee Data name. IIS is a subsidiary of I.I.S. Intelligent Information Systems of Israel.

The maker of computer hardware and software products and systems said it will continue to trade under the ticker symbol LEDA until further notice.
