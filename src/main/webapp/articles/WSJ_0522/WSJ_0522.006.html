Who's News: Student Loan Marketing Association

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/22/90

STUDENT LOAN MARKETING ASSOCIATION (Washington) -- Timothy G. Greene was named executive vice president and general counsel of this operator of a secondary market in educational loans. Mr. Greene, 51 years old, a Dallas lawyer who was Sallie Mae's first general counsel, will succeed Ronald F. Hunt, 47, who will begin a law practice in North Carolina in midsummer. Among the challenges Mr. Greene will face is a legal battle with commercial banks over Sallie Mae's right to originate student loans.
