Corrections & Amplifications: Browning-Ferris Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

CORNING Inc. has not filed a patent infringement suit against SpecTran Corp., a maker of optical fibers. An earlier edition incorrectly reported that Corning had filed such a suit to protect infringements against its optical fiber patents. (See: "Corning Boosts Fiber Output Capacity And Designs Tougher Fiber-Optic Cable" -- WSJ March 20, 1990)

---

BROWNING-FERRIS INDUSTRIES Inc. is pulling out of the hazardous waste treatment and disposal business and is closing or attempting to sell its hazardous-waste landfills. An earlier edition reported that the company was leaving the landfill business entirely. Browning-Ferris continues to operate solid-waste landfills.

(See: "Inside Track: Environmental Firm Executives Cut Holdings in Their Companies" -- WSJ April 11, 1990)
