Mellon Bank Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Mellon Bank Corp. said its Philadelphia bank subsidiary received approval from the comptroller of the currency to purchase 54 PSFS branch offices from Meritor Savings Bank. The acquisition, which was announced in December, is expected to be completed in mid-May.

Mellon, a Pittsburgh-based bank holding company, said the branch purchase will increase its deposit market share in the five-county Philadelphia market to about 14% from about 5%.
