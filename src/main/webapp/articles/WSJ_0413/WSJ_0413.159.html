Business Brief -- King World Productions Inc.: Company Posts 8% Increase In Fiscal 2nd-Quarter Profit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

King World Productions Inc. posted an 8% rise in net income on similar sales growth in the fiscal second quarter, aided by continuing strength in the TV ratings for its top shows -- "Wheel of Fortune," "The Oprah Winfrey Show" and "Jeopardy]" King World also said in New York that its newer nightly program, "Inside Edition," is faring well, rating among the top 10 highest-rated programs in the syndication market. Net in the fiscal second quarter, ended Feb. 28, rose to $17.9 million, or 69 cents a share, from $16.5 million, or 66 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose 8%, to $102.4 million from $94.5 million.

The company posted the higher earnings despite a reduction of $2.1 million, or eight cents a share, from costs associated with its recent acquisition of a Buffalo, N.Y., TV station.

Six-month net income rose 8% to $51.4 million, or $1.98 a share, as sales grew 17% to $270.7 million.
