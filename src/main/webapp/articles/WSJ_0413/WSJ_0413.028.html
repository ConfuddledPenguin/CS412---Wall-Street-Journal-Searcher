Harsco to Weigh Holders' Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

HARRISBURG -- Harsco Corp.'s president and chief executive officer, Malcolm W. Gambill, said the company's directors will consider an investor group's proposal at its next regularly scheduled board meeting April 24. Mr. Gambill said he has sent a letter to the investor group, the Carlyle Group of Washington, acknowledging receipt of their letter.

Carlyle's proposal calls for Harsco to sell its troubled defense group and pay a special $15 cash dividend to shareholders.
