Gottschalks Inc. Sales Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- Gottschalks Inc. said sales for the four weeks ended Saturday increased 18% to $21.1 million from $17.8 million a year ago. Sales at stores open more than one year increased about 10%. The department store chain said sales for the nine weeks ended Saturday rose 22% to $35.1 million from $28.8 million.
