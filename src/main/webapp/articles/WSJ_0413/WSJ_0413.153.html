Business Brief -- First Interstate Bancorp: Plan for Stock Buy-Back Is Amended by Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

First Interstate Bancorp, Los Angeles, said it is amending a dividend reinvestment and stock repurchase plan because arbitrage programs were causing First Interstate's stock to dip around the time the company declares its dividend. The plan provides discounts to holders who want to reinvest their dividends into purchasing stock or who want to repurchase stock under certain conditions. First Interstate said certain shareholders have been shorting the stock in the period when the programs are in effect, driving down the price so they can buy more shares and take greater advantage of the plan's discounts.

Essentially, the bank-holding company reduced those discounts and made certain other changes aimed at discouraging the practice.

First Interstate's stock is having enough trouble without the arbitrage problems. The bank has been hammered by a string of huge losses.
