Technitrol Cancels Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Technitrol Inc. said it terminated its previously announced letter of intent to acquire Mi-Tech Metals Inc. Roy E. Hock, chief executive officer of Technitrol, said that the proposed acquisition "did not withstand the due diligence process." Technitrol is a manufacturer of electronic, electrical and mechanical products.
