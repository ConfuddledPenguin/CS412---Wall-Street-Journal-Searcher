Ohio Art Labor Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

BRYAN, Ohio -- Ohio Art Co. said a new three-year labor accord was approved by Local 701, Allied Industrial Workers of America, AFL-CIO. The union represents production and maintenance employees at the Bryan plant. The company makes toys and plastic products.

The agreement is retroactive to March 16 and lasts until March 15, 1993.
