Who's News: Aritech Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

ARITECH Corp. (Framingham, Mass.) -- John R. Kountz was named a director of this electronic security equipment concern, expanding the board to seven members. Mr. Kountz is vice president of Kelso Management Co., an investment advisory concern that owns about 16.5% of Aritech. Aritech said Mr. Kountz was named to the board at Kelso's request.
