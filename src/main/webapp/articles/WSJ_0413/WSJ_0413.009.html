Bank of New England Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

BOSTON -- Bank of New England Corp. agreed to sell its mutual fund custodial business to Investors Bank & Trust Co., a 77.3% owned unit of Eaton Vance Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. The transaction is part of the troubled bank's efforts to reduce its asset-size and raise cash to meet federal capital regulatory standards.

The transaction would substantially boost the size of custodial operations at Investors Bank, which had $7 billion under custody prior to the agreement. The Bank of New England operations to be sold have $22 billion in mutual fund assets under custody.
