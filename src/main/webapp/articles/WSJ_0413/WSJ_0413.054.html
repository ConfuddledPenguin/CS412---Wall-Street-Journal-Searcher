Midland Co. Expects To Post Much Lower First-Quarter Earnings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

CINCINNATI -- Midland Co.'s chairman, J.P. Hayden Jr., said first-quarter earnings were "significantly less" than last year's quarter. In the year-earlier quarter, Midland had net income of $3,050,000, or 91 cents a share. Revenue was $37,374,000.

Mr. Hayden said the company's property and casualty operations experienced heavier than normal weather-related losses, due to severe weather in Alabama and Mississippi in February and March.

In addition, flooding along the Ohio and Mississippi rivers and their tributaries hurt the company's barge operations.

The company, a diversified insurance, finance and transportation concern, expects to report first-quarter results in about three weeks.
