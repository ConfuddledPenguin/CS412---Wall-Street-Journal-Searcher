CBOT Seat Is Sold for $325,000

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Board of Trade was sold for $325,000, up $1,000 from the previous sale last Monday. Seats currently are quoted at $305,000, bid, and $375,000, asked. The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set Aug. 31, 1987.
