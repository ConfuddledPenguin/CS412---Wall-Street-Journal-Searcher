Who's News: Alexander's Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

ALEXANDER'S Inc. (New York) -- Gerald N. Schrager was elected a director of this department store chain, which is 27%-owned by real estate developer Donald Trump. He succeeds Susan M. Heilbron, who resigned as a director and as an attorney for the Trump organization to become vice president and general counsel at Maxwell Communication Corp.'s Maxwell Macmillan group. Mr. Schrager, an attorney for the New York law firm Dreyer & Traub, represents Mr. Trump and the Trump organization in much of its real estate and related activities. Board membership stands at 17 members.
