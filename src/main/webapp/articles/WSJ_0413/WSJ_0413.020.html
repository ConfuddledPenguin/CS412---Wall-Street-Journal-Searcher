Marion Merrell Dow Inc. Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

MIDLAND, Mich. -- Dow Chemical Co. said it will purchase from time to time in open-market or private transactions an additional four million shares of Marion Merrell Dow Inc., raising its stake in the pharmaceutical concern to 68% from the current 67%, or 186.3 million of the 278.3 million shares outstanding. "Marion Merrell Dow is an outstanding value, and offers an excellent investment opportunity for Dow," said Enrique C. Falla, Dow Chemical's financial vice president and chief financial officer. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Marion Merrell shares closed at $25.375, up 75 cents.
