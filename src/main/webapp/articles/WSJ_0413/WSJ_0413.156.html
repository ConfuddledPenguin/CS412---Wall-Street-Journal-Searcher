Business Brief -- McCaw Cellular Communications: LIN Is Negotiating Change In Metromedia Transaction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

McCaw Cellular Communciations Inc., Kirkland, Wash., said LIN Broadcasting Communciations Inc. is negotiating with Metromedia Inc. on a proposed modification in terms of LIN's $1.9 billion proposed acquisition of certain Metromedia cellular-phone interests in Philadelphia and New York. McCaw has a controlling interest in LIN.

A McCaw spokesman declined to say why the talks started, or who made the proposal. But McCaw, a cellular-phone company, said the proposal calls for LIN to pay Metromedia $1.05 billion in cash and $850 million in stock of a LIN subsidiary. The proposal also calls for Metromedia to buy from LIN a 1.01% interest in a cellular system serving Philadelphia. LIN owns 51% of the system.

The original terms called for LIN to pay the full $1.9 billion in cash. McCaw said that if the new terms aren't accepted, the original deal will remain unchanged.

In over-the-counter trading yesterday, LIN closed at $68.50, up 50 cents.
