Care Enterprises Plan Cleared

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

TUSTIN, Calif. -- Care Enterprises Inc., a nursing home company that has been operating under bankruptcy-law protection for two years, said that its reorganization plan recently received court approval. Under terms of the plan, creditors holding about $75 million face amount of unsecured debt would receive 90% of Care's equity; the rest would be retained by existing shareholders. Care said its secured creditors, who are owed about $32 million, would soon receive a $3 million initial payment followed in about nine months by quarterly payments for five years. At that time, the balance would be paid in a lump sum.
