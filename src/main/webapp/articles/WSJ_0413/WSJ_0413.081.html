Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

STRIKE: A chapter of the International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees struck CBS Inc. in New York. About 200 members of the union's Local 1, representing stagehands on newscasts and other programs on WCBS-TV and the CBS-TV network, walked off their jobs yesterday. The previous contract expired Dec. 31.

RADIO: US Radio Limited Partnership completed the acquisition of four radio stations for $40.4 million in Reading, Pa.; Columbia, S.C.; and El Paso and Freeport, Texas. It now owns eight radio stations.

VIDEO: Great American Communications Co.'s Hanna-Barbera Home Video Inc. signed an exclusive home video distribution pact with Nippon Columbia Co. of Japan. Nippon will release 10 Hanna-Barbera titles in Japan this month, followed by 20 more within the next year.
