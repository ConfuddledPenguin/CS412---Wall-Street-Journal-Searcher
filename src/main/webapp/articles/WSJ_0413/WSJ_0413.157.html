Who's News: Daiwa Securities Trust Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

DAIWA SECURITIES TRUST Co. (Tokyo) -- David J. Askin, former managing director and head of fixed income research at Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc., joined this trust company as executive vice president and chief operating officer of its newly formed Global Portfolio Research department. The new department is responsible for developing and marketing global models for active and passive asset management and for the creation of mutual funds incorporating both equity and fixed income products.
