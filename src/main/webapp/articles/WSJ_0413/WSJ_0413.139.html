Business Brief -- ICN Pharmaceuticals Inc.: Net Deficit of $6.5 Million Posted for Fiscal 1st Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

Despite a 57% boost in fiscal first-quarter sales, ICN Pharmaceuticals Inc., Costa Mesa, Calif., reported a net loss of $6.5 million, or 54 cents a share. A year ago, the company reported net income of $2.9 million, or 19 cents a share. The company said it had an operating loss of $7.8 million, due to unfavorable currency adjustments stemming from a weak dollar, realized investment portfolio losses, increased expenses due to its 1989 acquisition of Flow Laboratories, and intensified marketing by pharmaceutical companies. A year earlier, earnings from operations totaled $722,000.

For the quarter, ended Feb. 28, the company reported extraordinary credits of $1.3 million, compared with year-earlier extraordinary credits of $2.2 million. Shares outstanding declined to 11,984,000 from 15,299,000.

In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, ICN Pharmaceuticals fell 12.5 cents, to $3.50.
