American Savings to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

WHITE PLAINS, N.Y. -- American Savings Bank said it is seeking a buyer for its AmSave Credit Corp. unit, which specializes in commercial and lease financing. William G. Lillis, president and chief executive of American Savings, said the move was in keeping with the company's back-to-basics strategy. "We are focusing on our core businesses, income-property lending and retail banking," said Mr. Lillis in a statement. "Our commitment to sell AmSave is a major step toward the implementation of this strategy."

AmSave Credit, established in 1983, had assets of $276 million at the end of 1989.
