Georgia S&L Is Placed In U.S. Receivership

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

WASHINGTON -- The Office of Thrift Supervision placed First Federal Savings & Loan Association, Warner Robins, Ga., in government receivership. First Federal, with $157.5 million in assets, was insolvent primarily because of delinquent commercial real estate loans and poor underwriting practices, the agency said. As of Dec. 31, the thrift held $28.9 million in delinquent loans, and was operating unsafely, the regulators said.

The Resolution Trust Corp., the federal government's S&L bailout agency, will act as receiver. First Federal will remain open for business, and deposits of as much as $100,000 remain insured.
