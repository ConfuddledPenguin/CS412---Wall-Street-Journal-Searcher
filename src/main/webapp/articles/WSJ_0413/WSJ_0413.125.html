Business Brief -- National Mine Service Co.: Tender Offer Is Completed By Unit of Anderson Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

National Mine Service Co. said its largest shareholder, an investment unit of Anderson Group PLC, has completed its tender offer for the remaining shares it doesn't already own. The offer was for $8.875 a share. Anderson owned 51.2%, or 2,487,000 shares, prior to the February offer.

Anderson accepted for purchase about 2.3 million additional shares, giving it nearly 98% of the shares outstanding. The total purchase price for the shares will be about $21 million.

Anderson intends to seek delisting of National Mine Services from the New York Stock Exchange.

Anderson is a Glasgow, Scotland, maker of longwall machinery used to mine coal. National Mine Service is an Indiana, Pa., maker of mine-safety equipment.
