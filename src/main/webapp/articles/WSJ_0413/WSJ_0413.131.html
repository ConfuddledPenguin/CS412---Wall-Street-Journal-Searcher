Business Brief -- Smiths Industries PLC: Pretax Earnings for Half Rose 6.3% to $82.4 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

Smiths Industries PLC, a British aerospace, medical systems and industrial products company, said that despite damaging disputes affecting two of its business units, pretax profit for the six months ended Feb. 3 rose 6.3% to #50.1 million ($82.4 million) from #47.1 million a year earlier. Profit after taxes increased 6.2% to #33.1 million from #31.1 million a year earlier. Per-share earnings rose 5.6% to 11.4 pence from 10.8 pence. Sales rose 3.5% to #317.8 million from #306.9 million.

Smiths declared an 11% rise in the interim dividend to 3.6 pence from 3.25 pence a year earlier.

"The underlying businesses of each of the three operating groups made good progress," Smiths said, "though the results of the aerospace and defense group have been damaged by two industrial disputes" at its Cheltenham plant and at Seattle-based Boeing Co., which is Smiths' main civil defense customer. Smiths said that without the industrial action, profit for the latest six months would have improved.
