International Brief -- Fokker N.V.: Aerospace Firm Reports Net For '89 More Than Tripled

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

Dutch aerospace group Fokker N.V., citing decreased financing charges in 1989, reported that 1989 net profit more than tripled. It said net rose to 42.4 million guilders ($22.5 million) from 13.3 million guilders in 1988. The aircraft builder said, "Fokker looks to the future with confidence, not least in view of the present order book of 10 billion guilders."

The company said that its net profit picture for 1989 was considerably improved by a reduction in financing charges to 55 million guilders from 64 million guilders in the year earlier. In addition, Fokker's participating interests in other companies contributed revenue of 15.9 million guilders in 1989 compared with 8.6 million guilders in 1988.
