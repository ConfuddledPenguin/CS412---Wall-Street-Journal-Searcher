Who's News: Sunwest Financial Services Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

SUNWEST FINANCIAL SERVICES Inc. (Albuquerque, N.M.) -- Donald R. Bonner, a Clovis, N.M., businessman, rancher and investor, and David W. Matthews, president and chief executive officer of Healy-Matthews Stationers of Santa Fe, N.M., were elected directors of this bank holding company, succeeding Hugh K. Frederick Jr. and Frank Potenziani, who retired. Board membership was reduced to 13 from 14, following the retirement of J. Leslie Davis.
