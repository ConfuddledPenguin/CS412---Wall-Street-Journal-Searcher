Brush Wellman Profit Falls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

CLEVELAND -- Brush Wellman Inc. said per-share earnings for the fiscal first-quarter declined between 42% and 44%. For the quarter ended April 2, the maker of engineered materials expects to post profit of between 33 cents and 34 cents a share on sales of $80 million, compared with 59 cents a share on sales of $80.9 million a year earlier.

Net income in the year-earlier quarter totaled $10.2 million.
