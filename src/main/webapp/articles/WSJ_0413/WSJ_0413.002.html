Who's News: Coastal Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

COASTAL Corp. (Houston) -- Larry R. Jouett, 42 years old, formerly vice president, operations, at this energy holding company, was named executive vice president and chief operating officer of the ANR Freight System Inc. unit, assuming the duties of Joseph F. Debella, who resigned as the unit's president and chief executive officer to pursue other business interests.
