Candela May Redeem 8% Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

WAYLAND, Mass. -- Candela Laser Corp. said it is considering redeeming its 8% convertible subordinated notes with a total principal amount of $3,625,000. The notes are convertible into 375,148 shares of common stock at prices of $9.64 to $9.75 a share. Holders would have the option of converting to common stock or cash for their position.

The maker of medical laser systems said the closing price of its stock has exceeded the required threshold, enabling it to call the notes at its option. Candela has 4.6 million shares outstanding. It filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission covering the stock issuable upon conversion of the notes.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Candela Laser shares rose $1.125 to $14.875.
