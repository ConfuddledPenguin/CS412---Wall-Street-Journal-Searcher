Rohm & Haas to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Rohm & Haas Co. said it will sell its Electro-Materials Corp. of America subsidiary to National Starch & Chemical Co. of Bridgewater, N.J. Terms weren't disclosed. The subsidiary, a maker of cermet pastes and polymer thick films for the hybrid electronics market, is based in Montgomeryville, Pa. The unit operates facilities in Montgomeryville and in Linton, England, and has 75 employees.
