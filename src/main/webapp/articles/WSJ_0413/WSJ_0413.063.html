Voplex to Sell Line to Sonoco

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

TROY, Mich. -- Sonoco Products Co. of Hartsville, S.C., signed a letter of intent to purchase Voplex Products Co.'s plastic cartridge business, the two companies said. Terms weren't disclosed.

Included in the sale would be Voplex's manufacturing and sales operation in Canandaigua, N.Y., which produces and markets cartridges for the packaging industry.

Voplex designs, engineers and manufactures decorative trim components for the automotive industry.
