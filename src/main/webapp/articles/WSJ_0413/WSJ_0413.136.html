Business Brief -- Automatic Data Processing Inc.: Quarterly Profit Rose 16% On Revenue Increase of 4%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

Automatic Data Processing Inc. posted a 16% rise in earnings for the fiscal third quarter on a 4% increase in revenue, reflecting a lower tax rate, among other factors. For the period ending March 31, the computing services concern earned $65 million, or 88 cents a share, compared with the year-earlier $56.2 million, or 76 cents a share. Revenue climbed to $470.8 million from $454 million.

The Roseland, N.J., company said the results also benefited from a change in the pricing and timing of billings for laser printing and processing of W-2 statements.

Nine-month net rose 13% to $152 million, or $2.07 a share, from the year-earlier $134.6 million, or $1.80 a share. Revenue rose 5.8% to $1.29 billion from $1.26 billion.
