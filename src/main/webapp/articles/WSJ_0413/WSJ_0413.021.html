Quantum Chemical Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

NEW YORK -- Quantum Chemical Corp. said it sold its 50% interest in RMI Titanium Co. for about $93.8 million through an initial public offering. Net gain on the sale is expected to be about $15 million. Quantum's 7.5 million shares in RMI were sold for $12.50 a share. In 1989, RMI Titanium contributed $8.6 million to Quantum's net income of $247.4 million. Quantum is a petrochemical concern.
