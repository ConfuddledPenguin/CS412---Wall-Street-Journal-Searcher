Mortgage & Realty Trust

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

ELKINS PARK, Pa. -- Mortgage & Realty Trust, which last month defaulted on its commercial paper and medium-term debt, filed for reorganization under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. The real estate investment trust said it decided not to go forward with a March 31 agreement with lenders for a $425 million revolving line of credit.
