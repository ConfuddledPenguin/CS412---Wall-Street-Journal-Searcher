The following were among yesterday's offerings and pricings in U.S. and non-U.S. capital markets, with terms and syndicate manager, as compiled by Dow Jones Capital Markets Report: MUNICIPALS

Milwaukee County, Wis. -- $53 million general obligation corporate purpose bonds, Series 1990 A, due Dec. 1, 1991-2009, apparently were won by a Prudential-Bache Capital Funding group. The bonds, rated single-A-1 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and double-A-minus by Standard & Poor's Corp., were priced for reoffering to yield from 6% in 1991 to 7.35% in 2008 and 2009. There are no term bonds.
