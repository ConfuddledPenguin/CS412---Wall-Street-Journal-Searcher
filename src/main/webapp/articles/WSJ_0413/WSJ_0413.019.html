Gantos Inc. Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc. said sales for the five weeks ended last Saturday rose 14% to $23 million from $20.1 million in the like period a year ago. Same-store sales dropped 1% in the five-week period. The women's clothing retailer said sales for the nine weeks ended Saturday rose 21% to $39.7 million from $32.8 million in the year-ago period. Same-store sales are up 3% for the year to date.
