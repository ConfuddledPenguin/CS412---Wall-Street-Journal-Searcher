Ford Motor Extends Shutdown of a Plant At Oakville, Ontario

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

DETROIT -- Ford Motor Co. said it extended for a second week the shutdown of its Tempo and Topaz car-assembly plant in Canada. The Oakville, Ontario, production facility, which was closed this week because of slow sales, is now scheduled to reopen April 23. About 2,200 hourly employees are affected by the temporary closing.

Virtually all U.S. auto workers are off today for Good Friday and are also off Monday.

Overall, the 10 major U.S. auto makers plan to build 103,219 cars this week, down 35.7% from 160,513 a year ago.
