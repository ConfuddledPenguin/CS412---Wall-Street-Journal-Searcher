Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 254,665 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, down 14% from 295,903 tons in the preceding week and down 21% from 322,188 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production totaled 3,757,135 tons, down 13% from 4,342,943 tons a year earlier.
