American Medical Is Acquired

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

BEVERLY HILLS, Calif. -- American Medical International Inc., a hospital company, said its acquisition has been completed by IMA Holdings Corp., an investor group including CS First Boston Group Inc.'s First Boston Corp. and the Pritzker family of Chicago. The group acquired 86% of American Medical's 71.5 million common shares outstanding in a tender offer late last year. The company said American Medical shares will no longer trade on the New York Stock Exchange.
