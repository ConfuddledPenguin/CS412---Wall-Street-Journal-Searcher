Ross Stores Says Sales Up 6%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

NEWARK, Calif. -- Ross Stores Inc. said its sales for the five weeks ended April 7 rose 6% to $70 million from $66 million a year ago. Sales at stores open more than a year fell 6%. For the two months ended April 7, the retail chain said sales rose 7% to $113 million from $106 million. Sales at stores open more than a year slipped 5% for the two months.

Ross said the decline in sales open more than a year was partly due to the timing of the Easter holiday, which was in March last year.
