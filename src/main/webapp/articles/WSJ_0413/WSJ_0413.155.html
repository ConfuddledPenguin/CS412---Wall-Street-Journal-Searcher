Business Brief -- Pic `N' Save Corp.: Partnership Asks Company To Yield Control of Board

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

A partnership controlled by David H. Batchelder, which Wednesday disclosed it holds a 6.6% stake in Pic `N' Save Corp. and plans to make a bid to acquire the company, said it formally asked the company to adopt a resolution giving it control of the board. A Pic `N' Save spokesman said that officials at the company declined to comment on the request or the potential bid for the company.

In the filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Girard Partners Limited Partnership said it seeks the adoption of a stockholder resolution to cut Pic `N' Save's board to five from seven members and that three Girard appointees be named to the board.

Pic `N' Save, Dominguez, Calif., is a retail chain that buys manufacturers' closeout merchandise and sells it at deep discounts to customers. Girard is based in La Jolla, Calif.
