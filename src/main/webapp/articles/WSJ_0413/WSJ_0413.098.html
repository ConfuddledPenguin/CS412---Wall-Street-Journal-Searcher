Who's News: Cycare Systems Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/13/90

CYCARE SYSTEMS Inc. (Phoenix, Ariz.) -- A. Theodore Engkvist and James L. Schamadan were elected directors. Mr. Engkvist, president of Nynex Information Solutions Group Inc., succeeds Stanley T. Hatch, who resigned because of other business interests. Dr. Schamadan, who is president and chief executive officer of Scottsdale Memorial Health Systems Inc., succeeds Samuel W. Owen. Mr. Owen was Hospital Corp. of America's designated representive under a stock-purchase agreement with CyCare. In October 1989 HCA sold the 1,020,000 shares it held, and its rights to a board seat under the agreement was terminated. CyCare provides computerized information processing, services and systems to the health care industry.
