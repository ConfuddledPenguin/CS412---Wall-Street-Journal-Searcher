JOHNSON & JOHNSON (East Brunswick, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it affirmed this health care company's triple-A senior debt and related ratings following announcement of a $125 million charge against its Latin American operations. S&P said the "largely noncash charge reflects costs mainly incurred in Argentina and Brazil," while the company's global diversification "limits the impact of any one economy."
