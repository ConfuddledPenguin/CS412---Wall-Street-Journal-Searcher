East German Oil Investment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/13/90

HAMBURG, West Germany -- The West German oil industry plans to invest between seven billion and 10 billion Deutsche marks ($4.19 billion to $5.98 billion) in East Germany, according to the West German Petroleum Association. The trade group said that the money will be primarily invested to expand the network of gasoline stations in East Germany and to promote the use of heating oil as a cleaner fuel source than soft coal, which is most frequently used.

The number of gas stations in the East German network should be increased to about 3,000 from the current 1,250, the association said.
