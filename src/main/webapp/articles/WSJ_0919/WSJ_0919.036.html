Who's News: Marsh & McLennan Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

MARSH & McLENNAN Co. (New York) -- Francis N. Bonsignore was named senior vice president for human resources and administration, at this insurance brokerage and investment consulting firm. Mr. Bonsignore, 44 years old, succeeds Edward O. Cole, 65, who retired. Mr. Bonsignore was previously partner and head of human resources at Price Waterhouse, the New York-based accounting firm.
