UNC Unit Gets Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

ANNAPOLIS, Md. -- UNC Inc. said its UNC Support Services unit received three military contracts valued at about $183 million over the next 15 years. The company won a U.S. Navy contract for on-site support and maintenance of 140 TH-57 helicopters and equipment. The contract is for $28.6 million in the first year, and about $28.2 million annually in the following three years.

UNC Support Services also won a five-year, $7 million simulator-instructor training contract from the U.S. Air Force. The award has four one-year options, at a value of about $1.3 million per year.

In addition, UNC Support Services will be part of a team to work on the Air Force's Special Operations Forces Aircrew Training System program in Akron, Ohio. The 15-year contract is worth about $65 million.

UNC services aircraft engines and components, manufactures jet engine parts and provides other services to the aviation industry.
