McGraw-Hill, Firm In France to Launch European Data Service

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

NEW YORK -- McGraw-Hill Inc.'s F.W. Dodge division and Publications du Moniteur, a subsidiary of CEP Communications S.A., Paris, agreed to launch a service to supply information on European construction. The new Pan European Construction Service will provide detailed printed information on all major new construction projects in Europe that construction and building-products companies bid on, a spokesman for Dodge said. The service will link seven major European providers of construction-project news.

Start-up costs will be minimal, the spokesman said, because the two services must only reroute their current flow of information to subscribers in different countries. The new service is expected to be available to an unspecified number of subscribers by the fall of 1991. Subscribers will pay $500 to $50,000 annually for the service, he said.

Through F.W. Dodge, McGraw-Hill is a major provider of construction-project news in the U.S. Its Dodge Europe division has similar activities in Europe.

Publications du Moniteur's weekly Le Moniteur is a leading European construction-project publication in France and Spain with a circulation of 75,000, according to McGraw-Hill.
