Integra-A Hotel Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

IRVING, Texas -- Integra-A Hotel & Restaurant Co.said it exercised its option to redeem its 13 1/2% bonds due 1993. The owner and operator of hotels said it has issued $8.5 million in principal amount of the bonds. Integra said redemption of the bonds is made possible by the agreement of ShowBiz Pizza Time Inc., a former Integra subsidiary, to prepay $3 million on certain debts it owes to Integra. The bonds originally were issued as units of certain Integra warrants that entitled the holders to buy 23.5 common shares at 30 cents a share. Those warrants aren't being redeemed and as of Sept. 30, they are transferable separate and apart from the bonds, the company said.

Bondholders other than Hallwood Group Inc., which holds about $6.9 million of the bonds, will be paid in full in cash, Integra said. For its holdings, Hallwood will receive about $1.4 million in cash and an assignment from Integra of certain remaining debts owed to the company by ShowBiz Pizza.
