JWP Inc. in S&P Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

NEW YORK -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said JWP Inc., a Purchase, N.Y., technical-services company, will join its composite index of 500 stocks effective at the close of trading today. JWP replaces Prime Motor Inns Inc., a Fairfield, N.J., hotel operator, which filed under the bankruptcy laws yesterday.
