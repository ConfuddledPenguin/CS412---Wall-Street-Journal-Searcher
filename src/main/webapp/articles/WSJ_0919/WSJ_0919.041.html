Who's News: Equitable Life Assurances Society of the U.S.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

EQUITABLE LIFE ASSURANCES SOCIETY OF THE U.S. (New York) -- Michael J. Campbell was named managing director in charge of the retail investment services group at Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp., an Equitable unit. Mr. Campbell, 45 years old, succeeds Richard Kaufman, who died in August. Mr. Campbell was previously senior vice president and manager of Donaldson Lufkin's New York regional sales office.
