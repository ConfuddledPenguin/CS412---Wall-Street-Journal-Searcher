Curragh's Yukon Mine Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

TORONTO -- Curragh Resources Inc. said it reached an agreement for financing that will allow it to proceed with developing a lead and zinc mine in the Yukon. The mining concern said Bank of Nova Scotia agreed to provide 55 million Canadian dollars (US$47.5 million) of the estimated C$70 million cost of the project in non-recourse financing. Exact terms weren't disclosed. Curragh is manager of the Mount Hundere lead and zinc property and has an 80% interest in the venture.
