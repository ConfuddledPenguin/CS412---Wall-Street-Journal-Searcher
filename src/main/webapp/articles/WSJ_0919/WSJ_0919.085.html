Business Brief -- Boeing Co.: Unit Gets Order for Planes From Dutch Firm's Division

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

Boeing Co. said its de Havilland unit received an order valued at about $22 million for two Dash 8 series 300 airplanes from Schreiner Airways B.V., a division of Schreiner Aviation Group of the Netherlands. The twin-engine turboprop planes will be delivered within the next few months, Boeing said. Schreiner Airways provides regional commuter service, charter service and other aviation services.

Boeing continues to discuss the possible sale of the de Havilland unit with Aeritalia of Italy and Aerospatiale of France. A team from the two potential buyers is expected to continue reviewing de Havilland's operations for another few weeks, said a de Havilland spokesman in Toronto. Boeing is based in Seattle.
