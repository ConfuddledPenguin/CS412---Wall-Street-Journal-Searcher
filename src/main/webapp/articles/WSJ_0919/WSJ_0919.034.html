McDonnell Douglas Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. was given a $45.7 million Air Force contract for the Israeli F-15 aircraft program.

General Instrument Corp. got a $45.4 million Army contract for radar equipment.

Power Conversion Inc. received a $45.2 million Army contract for batteries.

The BMY-Combat Systems division of Harsco Corp. won a $37 million Army contract for 155mm self-propelled howitzers.

Heintz Corp., a unit of Grabill Corp., was awarded a $12.7 million Air Force pact for jet-engine parts.
