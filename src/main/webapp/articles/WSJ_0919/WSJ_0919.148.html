Business Brief -- Lomas Financial Corp.: Net Loss of $44.1 Million Posted for Fiscal 4th Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

Lomas Financial Corp. reported profit from continuing operations of $4.9 million, or seven cents a share, for the fourth quarter ended June 30, compared with a loss from continuing operations of $282.9 million, or $7.69 a share, in the year-earlier period. The Dallas financial-services and real-estate concern said losses from discontinued operations of $49 million led to a net loss of $44.1 million, or $1.25 a share, on revenue of $69.9 million. In the year-earlier period, losses from discontinued operations of $20.3 million resulted in a net loss of $303 million, or $8.26 a share, on revenue of $77.2 million.

Lomas has been operating under protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code since last Sept. 24.

For the year, Lomas reported a net loss of $695.5 million, or $19.07 a share, including losses from discontinued operations of $243 million and losses from continuing operations of $452.4 million, on revenue of $290 million.

In the year-earlier period, the company reported a net loss of $298.2 million, or $8.24 a share, including losses from continuing operations of $311.7 million, a loss from discontinued operations of $2.2 million and an extraordinary gain from retirement of debt of $15.7 million, on revenue of $334 million.
