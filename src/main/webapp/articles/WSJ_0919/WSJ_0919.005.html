Penn Central Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

CINCINNATI -- Penn Central Corp. said it will purchase all 10,917,714 shares that were tendered in response to its offer to buy as many as 11 million shares at $23 each. The offer expired Sept. 10. The manufacturing, defense, insurance and energy concern originally estimated that 11.3 million shares had been tendered.
