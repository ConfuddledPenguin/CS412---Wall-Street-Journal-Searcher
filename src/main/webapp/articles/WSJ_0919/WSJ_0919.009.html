Calgon Completes Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

PITTSBURGH -- Calgon Carbon Corp. said it completed the purchase of the operating assets of TMPC Inc., Vero Beach, Fla. Terms weren't disclosed. Calgon makes and markets activated carbons and related services for filtration and purification systems. TMPC owns the operating assets of Vara International Inc., a design and construction company specializing in solvent recovery, pollution control and design work pertaining to chemical processes.
