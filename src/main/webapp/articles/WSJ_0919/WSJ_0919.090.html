Who's News: Pfizer Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

PFIZER Inc. (New York) -- Robert L. Unger was named senior vice president of the consumer health care division of this pharmaceuticals company's consumer products group. Mr. Unger, 50 years old, succeeds James F. Anderson, 56, who retired. Mr. Unger was previously vice president of marketing for the consumer health care division.
