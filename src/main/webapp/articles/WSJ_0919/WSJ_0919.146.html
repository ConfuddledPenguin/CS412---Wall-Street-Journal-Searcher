Business Brief -- L.F. Rothschild: Former Clerk Pleads Guilty To Conspiracy, Tax Evasion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

A former billing clerk for L.F. Rothschild, Unterberg, Towbin pleaded guilty to federal conspiracy and income tax evasion charges. Joseph Andrews of Parsippany, N.J., told U.S. District Judge Harold A. Ackerman that he stole more than $1 million from Rothschild, Unterberg, a New York brokerage firm, over a five-year period by issuing and negotiating fraudulent checks. Mr. Andrews also admitted that he failed to report more than $120,000 in income from the fraud.

Mr. Andrews, who will be sentenced Dec. 13, faces a maximum of 10 years in prison and fines of more than $2 million.
