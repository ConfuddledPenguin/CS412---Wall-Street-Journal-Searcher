Business Brief -- BSN Corp.: Firm Agrees to Provide List Of Shareholders to Pelham

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

BSN Corp. said it had agreed to provide a list of shareholders to Pelham Investment Associates L.P., a Houston investor group whose previous efforts to buy a controlling interest in the sporting-goods manufacturer were rebuffed by the company. In return for the BSN action, Pelham agreed to drop a civil suit it had filed against BSN in a Delaware Court of Chancery, according to a Securities and Exchange Commission filing.

Pelham, which says it holds a 7.99% stake in Dallas-based BSN, had previously said it was seeking a shareholders list in order to solicit consents or proxies, but a spokesman said the group hadn't decided whether to continue pursuing such action.

BSN President Michael Blumenfeld, who owns or controls about 15% of BSN's stock, said the company only agreed to provide the list because it was required to do so under Delaware law. Mr. Blumenfeld also indicated that he remained opposed to selling a controlling interest to Pelham. "We don't think they have the ability to acquire anything," he said.
