Who's News: Ames Department Stores Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

AMES DEPARTMENT STORES Inc. (Rocky Hill, Conn.) -- Walter F. Loeb, president of Loeb Associates, a closely held New York consulting firm, and publisher of Loeb Retail Letter, was named a director, expanding board membership to 11 at this discount retailer, which is operating under Chapter 11 protection of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code.
