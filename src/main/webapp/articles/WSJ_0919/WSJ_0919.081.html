Treasury Set to Raise A Net of $1.7 Billion At Bill Sale Monday

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.7 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $18.4 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.7 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Dec. 27, 1990, and March 28, 1991, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
