Syntex Unit Deworming Agent

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Syntex Corp. said that its Syntex Animal Health Inc. unit received approval from the U.S. Food and Drug Administration to market Synanthic Bovine Dewormer Suspensions, or oxfendazole, to treat common internal parasites in cattle. The deworming agent should be available from distributors and veterinarians in about three weeks, the company said.

The drug has been used for 10 years in many countries and was first introduced in Ireland in 1978.
