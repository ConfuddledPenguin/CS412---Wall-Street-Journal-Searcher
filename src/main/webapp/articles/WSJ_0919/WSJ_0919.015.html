Technology: Hewlett-Packard Gets Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Hewlett-Packard Co. said it received a $13 million contract from Nissan Motor Co. to supply the Japanese auto maker with workstations for use in mechanical computer-aided engineering. Nissan already has installed more than 300 Hewlett-Packard computers. The new contract provides two types of workstation models, including reduced instruction set computing, or RISC, workstations, the company said.
