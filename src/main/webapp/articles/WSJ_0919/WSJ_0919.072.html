Dividend News: Cedar Fair Limited Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

CEDAR FAIR LIMITED PARTNERSHIP boosted its quarterly cash distribution by 16%, to 36.25 cents from 31.25 cents, payable Nov. 15 to holders of record Oct. 5. The Sandusky, Ohio, amusement park concern said it raised the dividend because it expects 1990 net income to exceed last year's record $31.6 million, or $1.48 a unit.
