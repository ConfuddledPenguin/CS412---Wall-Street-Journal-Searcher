Business Brief -- Mission Resource Partners L.P.: Partnership Enters Talks For Acquisition by Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

Mission Resource Partners L.P. of Walnut Creek, Calif., said it entered discussions with an independent oil and natural gas company, which it declined to identify, to purchase substantially all of Mission's remaining assets. Mission, which has interests in about 475 oil and gas wells primarily in California, said the acquisition, if completed, would dissolve its master limited partnership. Unit-holders would probably receive $9 to $10 a unit, President Douglas K. Brown said.

In July 1989, Mission first announced that it was seeking the sale or merger of its partnership, and it has since sold several producing properties and property interests.
