Helm Resources' Plan for Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Helm Resources Inc. said it and a group of investors expect to bring a reorganized Scat Hovercraft Inc. out of bankruptcy court proceedings by next month, with a new marketing campaign for the maker of hovercraft vehicles. Helm said that with its investment in Scat, it will retain majority control of the Miami company after new stock is issued to the investors. Scat has been operating under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code to protect itself from creditor lawsuits. Helm said a plan of reorganization for Scat was recently approved in bankruptcy court, and a new board will be appointed when the reorganization is completed.

Helm is a diversified company, primarily involved in the distribution of thermoplastic resins.
