International Brief -- Cie. de Saint-Gobain: Firm Seeks Approval to Lift Its Stake in Oberland Glas

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

Cie. de Saint-Gobain said it applied for regulatory approval to raise its holdings in the West German glass group Oberland Glas AG above 50%, from the current level of 35%. A spokesman for the French glass and building-materials group said, however, that the move is preliminary at this stage and might not lead to an actual increase in its stake until 1991 or 1992.

Saint-Gobain acquired its stake in Oberland in 1988 through a share swap with the Wiegand family, which retains a 25% interest. The French company has first refusal rights on that 25% block, and would need prior regulatory approval before it could exercise them.

Oberland couldn't immediately be reached for comment.
