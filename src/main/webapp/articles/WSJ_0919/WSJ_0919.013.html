Rochester Telephone Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Rochester Telephone Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire DePue Telephone Co. of DePue, Ill. The price wasn't disclosed. Rochester will trade shares of its common for all 554 shares of DePue's outstanding stock. DePue's stock isn't listed.

DePue, which serves about 760 access lines in an area about 60 miles north of Bloomington, would become Rochester's eighth operating telephone company in Illinois and its 20th in the Midwest.

DePue had $475,000 of operating revenue for 1989. Rochester had $562 million.
