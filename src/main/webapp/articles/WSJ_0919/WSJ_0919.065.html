Business Brief -- National Intergroup Inc.: Drug Distributor Extends Purchase Offer for Permian

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

National Intergroup Inc. said it is considering extending its tender offer for all outstanding cumulative convertible preference units of Permian Partners L.P. If the offer is extended beyond Oct. 1, the wholesale and drug distributor, based in Pittsburgh, will reduce the cash portion of its purchase price to $3.5625 from $3.85 per preference unit of Permian, an oil concern.

The offer may be extended because certain terms of the offer have not been satisfied, National Intergroup said.
