Agency Rent-A-Car Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

CLEVELAND -- Agency Rent-A-Car Inc. said it will repurchase as many as one million shares of its common stock outstanding through a series of open-market transactions. The concern said the current market price of its stock doesn't reflect the company's inherent value. Agency had approximately 25.3 million shares outstanding as of Sept. 1.
