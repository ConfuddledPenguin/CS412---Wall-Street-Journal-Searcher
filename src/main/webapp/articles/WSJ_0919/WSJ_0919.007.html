Tab Products Earnings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Tab Products Co. reported earnings for its first quarter, ended Aug. 31, of $1.27 million, or 19 cents a share, little changed from $1.23 million, or 19 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue for the office-filing and furniture-systems company declined about 2%, to $32.8 million from $33.6 million.
