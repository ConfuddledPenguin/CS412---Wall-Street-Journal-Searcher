Hackneyed Fact

"Don't change horses in midstream"

Isn't always as true as they say,

But it's frequently noted and often quoted

As a wonderful one-horse cliche.

-- George O. Ludcke.

Deserve reviews that rave --

Except for those who do not

Let you forget they forgave.
