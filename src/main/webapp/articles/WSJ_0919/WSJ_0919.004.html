Chrysler Corp. Expansion Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

HIGHLAND PARK, Mich. -- Chrysler Corp.'s Acustar unit said it will spend $30 million to expand an auto-glass factory in Evart, Mich. The auto maker also said about 150 additional hourly and salaried employees will be taken on at the facility and 230,000 square feet will be added to the 50,000-square-foot building. The factory will make glass for Chrysler cars and for a Jeep off-road vehicle model that is due to be produced in the next two years.
