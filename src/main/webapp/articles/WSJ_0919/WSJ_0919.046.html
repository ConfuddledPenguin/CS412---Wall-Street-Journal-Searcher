Dividend News: San Juan Basin Royalty Trust

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

SAN JUAN BASIN ROYALTY TRUST, which is based in Fort Worth, Texas, said it won't make a cash contribution to unit holders on Sept. 28. Its Trustee Team Bank said production expenses exceeded revenue by $2.6 million from May through September. The oil and natural gas trust's portion of this excess is $1.9 million. Under the terms of the trust, losses on production are recovered from subsequent months' royalty income. The trust expects continued losses on production because of low demand and low prices for natural gas.
