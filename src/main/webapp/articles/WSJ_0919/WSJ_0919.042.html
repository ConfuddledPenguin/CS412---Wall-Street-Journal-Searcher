Who's News: Ford Motor Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

FORD MOTOR Co. (Dearborn, Mich.) -- Kenneth W. Harrigan, 63-year-old chief executive officer of the Ford Motor Co. of Canada unit, Oakville, Ontario, was appointed to the additional, newly created post of chairman, effective Oct. 1. As president, Mr. Harrigan will be succeeded by James O'Connor, 47, who also was named chief operating officer of the auto maker. Mr. O'Connor is general sales and marketing manager of the Parts & Service division of Ford Canada's parent company.
