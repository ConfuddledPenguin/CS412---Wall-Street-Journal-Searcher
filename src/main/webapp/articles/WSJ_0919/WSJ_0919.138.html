Business Brief -- Campeau Corp.: Allied and Federated Units Post Loss of $126.3 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

Campeau Corp.'s two retailing units, Federated Department Stores Inc. and Allied Stores Corp., reported a total loss of $126.3 million for the 13 weeks ended Aug. 4, a somewhat better performance than a year earlier. At the same time, the two companies -- both of which filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection on Jan. 15 -- reported that their total revenue for the period decreased slightly. Most retailers experienced sluggish sales during the summer months, leading many Wall Street analysts to speculate that the fall and Christmas selling periods will be more difficult than earlier anticipated.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Federated reported a loss of $80 million for the quarter ended Aug. 4 compared with a $78.9 million loss in the same period a year earlier. Allied suffered a $46.3 million loss compared with a $56.2 million loss in the same period a year earlier.

Revenue at Federated decreased 3.5% to $983.8 million for the quarter compared with $1.02 billion in the same quarter a year earlier. Revenue at Allied decreased 7.8% to $540.9 million compared with $586.8 million last year.

A spokesman for Federated and Allied also noted that the cash flow from continuing operations was stronger than expected for both companies.
