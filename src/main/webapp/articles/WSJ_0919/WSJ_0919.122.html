Business Brief -- GAF Corp.: Financing Accord Totaling $800 Million Is Concluded

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

GAF Corp. said it concluded a long-term financing agreement with Chase Manhattan Bank, Bank of New York, Bank of Nova Scotia and Banque Paribas for $800 million. The new credit includes an eight-year, $600 million term loan and a five-year revolving credit; with the funds, the company will repay debt incurred at the time of its March 1989 acquisition by a management group led by Samuel J. Heyman, chairman and chief executive officer.

As part of the agreement, GAF will also redeem the remaining $50 million of senior subordinated increasing-rate notes, due March 15, 1994, outstanding. GAF still has about $236 million of subordinated merger-related debentures due in 2005 outstanding.

GAF is a Wayne, N.J., manufacturer of specialty chemicals and building materials.
