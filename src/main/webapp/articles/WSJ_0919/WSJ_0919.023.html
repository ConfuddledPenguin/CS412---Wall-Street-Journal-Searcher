Penwest Ltd. Share Purchases

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

SEATTLE -- Penwest Ltd. said it may buy as many as 500,000 of its common shares on the open market from time to time under a previously announced plan to acquire as many as 900,000 shares. Penwest, which makes and markets specialty carbohydrate and synthetic polymer chemicals for paper-making, has bought 400,000 of its shares since its board authorized such purchases in April 1989 for use in stock option plans or acquisitions and for other corporate purposes. It has 7.1 million shares outstanding.

Separately, Penwest said analysts' estimates of earnings of 25 cents to 28 cents a share were in line with its own estimates for its fourth quarter, ended Aug. 31. It will disclose final results in October.
