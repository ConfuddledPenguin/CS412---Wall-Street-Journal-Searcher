Who's News: Alaska Airlines Names Vecci, 47, as President

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

SEATTLE -- Alaska Airlines, a unit of Alaska Air Group Inc., named Raymond J. Vecci president and chief executive officer. Mr. Vecci, 47, assumes these duties from Bruce R. Kennedy, who remains chairman of Alaska Airlines. Mr. Kennedy is also chairman, president and chief executive of Alaska Air Group. Mr. Vecci had been executive vice president and chief operating officer of Alaska Airlines since 1986. He also serves on the board of the airline and Alaska Air Group. He has worked for the company since 1975.

The board did not name a new chief operating officer; Mr. Vecci will continue to hold the post until the company appoints a replacement, the company said.
