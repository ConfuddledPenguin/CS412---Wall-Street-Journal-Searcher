Who's News: Leslie Fay Cos.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

LESLIE FAY Cos. (New York) -- Joel H. Newman was named president of the new Europe division of this maker of women's suits and sportswear. Mr. Newman, 54 years old, will continue as vice chairman and a member of the company's board. Alan Golub, 48, currently president of Leslie Fay, assumes the additional role of chief operating officer, previously held by Mr. Newman.
