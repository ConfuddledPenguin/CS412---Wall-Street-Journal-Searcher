Marketing & Media: TV Guide to Launch Manhattan Editions For Cable Subscribers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

NEW YORK -- Responding to stiff competition, TV Guide will publish two editions of its weekly magazine designed specifically for Manhattan cable subscribers. The special TV Guides, to begin publishing Nov. 3, will be sent to current subscribers who live in the Manhattan Cable and Paragon franchise areas. They will also be available on newsstands in the franchise areas of the two systems, along with the existing New York Metro edition.

The editions are part of a new effort by Rupert Murdoch's News Corp., which acquired TV Guide two years ago, to turn around the declining fortunes of the television-listings magazine. The two new editions, which include detailed program listings and channel locations of cable programs in each cable system, compete directly with currently produced local guides.

TV Guide launched its first special cable edition in 1986 for cable subscribers in the New York boroughs of Queens, Brooklyn and Staten Island. Since then, it has introduced customized editions in several other cities.
