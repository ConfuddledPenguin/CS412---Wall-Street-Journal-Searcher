Financing Business: Anadarko Petroleum

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

ANADARKO PETROLEUM (Houston) -- said it will redeem on Oct. 18, the entire $100 million principal amount outstanding of its 5 3/4% convertible subordinated debentures, due 2012. Under the exchange offer, holders will receive $1,042.97, which includes an early call premium and accrued interest, for each $1,000 principal amount of debentures held. Or, the company said, holders may opt to convert the debentures into Anadarko common at a conversion price of $32.625 a share. Anadarko, an oil and gas exploration and production concern, said a conversion into common of the debentures would reduce debt by about $100 million, from a level of about $474 million at June 30, and increase the number of shares outstanding by 5.8% or roughly 3.1 million shares. Anadarko has about 52.4 million shares currently outstanding.
