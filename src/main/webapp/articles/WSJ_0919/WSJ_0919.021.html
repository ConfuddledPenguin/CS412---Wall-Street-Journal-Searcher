Air Wis Traffic Up 18%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

APPLETON, Wis. -- Air Wis Services said its August traffic rose 18% to 48.7 million revenue passenger miles from 41.3 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

Air Wis Services' results include the company's Air Wisconsin and Aspen Airways units. The company, which operates as a commuter carrier for UAL Corp.'s United Airlines unit, said Aspen results aren't included in the year-ago period.

For the first eight months of the year, Air Wis traffic rose 9.8% to 319.2 million revenue passenger miles from 290.7 million miles a year ago.

August load factor, or percentage of seats filled, rose to 52.5% from 50.5% a year ago. For the year to date, load factor slipped to 45.7% from 47.7%.
