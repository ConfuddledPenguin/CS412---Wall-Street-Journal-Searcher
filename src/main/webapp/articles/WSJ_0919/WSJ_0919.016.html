L.M. Ericsson Unit's Orders

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

RICHARDSON, Texas -- Ericsson said it received orders totaling $125 million for cellular switches and radio base station equipment to expand Ericsson systems in six states. The telecommunications concern, a unit of L.M. Ericsson of Stockholm, Sweden, said the orders include the installation of switches and cell sites for Los Angeles Cellular Telephone Co. and for Chicago-based Cellular One, a Southwestern Bell Corp. company. In Florida, Ericsson will upgrade its APZ 212 processor and install cell sites for another company called Cellular One, which is owned and operated by McCaw Cellular Communications Inc. Ericsson also will expand McCaw systems in Sacramento, Calif., and Las Vegas, Nev., as well as Cellular Telephone Co. systems in the San Francisco Bay area.
