Ohio Edison Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

AKRON, Ohio -- Ohio Edison Co. said it will buy back 12 million of its 152 million outstanding shares of common stock on the open market for its new Employee Stock Ownership Plan. The ESOP, approved by the power company's board yesterday, will be incorporated into the company's existing employee savings plan.

Ohio Edison also lowered its quarterly dividend on outstanding adjustable rate Class A preferred stock, Series B, to 60.313 cents from 60.938 cents, payable Oct. 29 to stock of record Oct. 15.
