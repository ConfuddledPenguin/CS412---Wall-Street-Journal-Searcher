Business Brief -- ASK Computer Systems Inc.: Florida Investor Increases Stake to 9.67% From 6%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

James P. Lennane, a Naples, Fla., investor, said he raised his stake in ASK Computer Systems Inc. to 9.67% from about 6% of the common shares outstanding, according to a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission. Mr. Lennane said in the filing that he holds 1,283,100 shares, including 282,000 shares he purchased between Sept. 4 and Sept. 17 for $7.50 to $8.125 a share. The filing did not give a reason for the increase. Mr. Lennane couldn't be reached at his home, but an ASK Computer spokeswoman in Los Altos, Calif., said Mr. Lennane has been accumulating stock in the company for several months. "The last time he increased his stake to 6% {several months ago}, he said it was for investment purposes," she said. "We're inclined to take him at his word because following that, nothing happened."

Ask Computer is a Mountainview, Calif., manufacturer of computer management informations systems and software.
