Rates Increase at Citicorp's Sale of Commercial Paper

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 7.98% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 7.9397% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $405 million were submitted. Accepted bids were at 7.98%.

Citicorp also said the average rate rose to 7.928% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.924% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $375 million were submitted. Accepted bids were at 7.928%.

Earlier this month, Citicorp said this would be the last such auction of commercial paper. Growth in consumer deposits, the bank's balance-sheet management and its direct placement of commercial paper have eliminated the need for the auctions, Citicorp said.
