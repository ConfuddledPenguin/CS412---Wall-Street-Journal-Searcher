St. Paul Cos. to Buy Back Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- St. Paul Cos. said its board authorized the buy-back of as many as 2,120,000, or 5%, of its common shares outstanding. The property and liability insurance company said it has 42.4 million common shares outstanding, after buying back and retiring under an earlier program 6.9 million common shares in the first six months of this year.

A St. Paul spokeswoman said that the board authorized the latest buy-back in February, but that the clearance hadn't previously been disclosed.
