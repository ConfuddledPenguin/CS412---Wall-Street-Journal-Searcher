Who's News: General Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

GENERAL ELECTRIC Co. (Fairfield, Conn.) -- Lloyd G. Trotter was named a vice president at this broadcasting, financial services and aerospace company. The 45-year-old executive remains general manager of manufacturing operations at the electrical distribution and control business, Plainville, Conn. The appointment is effective Oct. 1.
