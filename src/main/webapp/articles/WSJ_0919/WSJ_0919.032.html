Provident Bancorp Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/19/90

CINCINNATI -- Provident Bancorp Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement to buy First Federal Savings & Loan Association of Lexington, Ky. Under terms of the agreement, depositors of First Federal, a $130 million savings and loan association, may buy Provident stock in a subscription offering. The transaction is subject to regulatory and First Federal member approval.
