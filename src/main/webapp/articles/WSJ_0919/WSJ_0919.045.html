Badger Meter Class B Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/19/90

MILWAUKEE -- Badger Meter Inc. said holders approved a plan that allows the company's officers and directors to purchase its Class B shares, which have enhanced voting rights. Previously, only eight holders could buy Class B shares as they became available. The change raises the total to 25. The maker of flow-measurement products said the Class B stock has 10 votes a share, while the company's common has one vote a share. The company said that as of Aug. 24, it had 562,785 Class B shares and 1,105,042 common shares outstanding. Badger said the move is consistent with its plans to boost employee ownership, raise cash and retain officers and directors.
