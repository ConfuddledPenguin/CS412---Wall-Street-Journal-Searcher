Hamilton Oil Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

DENVER -- Hamilton Oil Corp. said it discovered natural gas in the United Kingdom Sector of the North Sea. The well flowed at a rate of 37.6 million cubic feet of natural gas a day through a 3 1/2-inch tubing.

Hamilton Oil is the operator with a 35% interest. Its partners are Gas Council Ltd., with a 35% interest; Monument Resources Ltd., with 12.5%; Brasoil U.K. Ltd., with 10%, and Offshore Oil & Gas Development Ltd., with 7.5%.
