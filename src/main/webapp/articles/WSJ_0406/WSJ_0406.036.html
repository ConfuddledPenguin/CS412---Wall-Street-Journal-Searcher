Lockheed Awarded A Navy-Service Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

WASHINGTON -- Lockheed Corp. received a $259.7 million Navy contract for missile-engineering services for the U.S. and British navies.

Coopers & Lybrand was given a $14.8 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for accounting services.

H&R Co., a joint venture of Raytheon Co. and General Motors Corp.'s Hughes Aircraft Co., won an $11.8 million Navy contract for the advanced air-to-air missile program.

Raytheon also received a $10 million Navy contract for software.
