Who's News: Carter Hawley Hale Stores Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

CARTER HAWLEY HALE STORES Inc. (Los Angeles) -- H. Michael Hecht, chairman and chief executive officer of this retailer's Broadway-Southern California division, was additionally named an executive vice president at the parent. Mr. Hecht, 50 years old, will continue to be principally responsible for the Broadway-Southern California operations.
