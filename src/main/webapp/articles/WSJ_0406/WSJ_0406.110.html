Onex Corp. Unit Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

TORONTO -- Onex Corp. said its 80%-owned unit, Hidden Creek Industries, completed the acquisition of Tate & Lyle PLC's North American auto parts business for US$179.5 million. Onex, a holding company with interests in packaging, leasing and food products, said it invested about $30 million for a 90% interest in Automotive Industries Inc., of Strasburg, Va., and funded the rest of the transaction with preferred stock bought by Tate & Lyle, subordinate notes and senior bank debt. Automotive Industries makes plastic parts for major North American auto makers.
