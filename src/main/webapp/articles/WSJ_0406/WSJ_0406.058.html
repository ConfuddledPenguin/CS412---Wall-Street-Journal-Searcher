Money Fund Assets Fell in Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 507 money market funds fell $293.6 million, to $390.49 billion, in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a trade association. Assets of 246 general purpose funds rose $226 million, to $133.23 billion, while assets of 102 broker-dealer funds fell $570.1 million, to $165.79 billion.

Assets of 159 institutional funds rose $50.5 million, to $91.47 billion.
