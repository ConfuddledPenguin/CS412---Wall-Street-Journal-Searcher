Computer Sciences Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Computer Sciences Corp. said it won renewal of a National Aeronautics and Space Administration contract valued at as much as $65.7 million for engineering support services at the agency's Wallops Island, Va., experimental facility. The contract calls for payments of $23 million for an initial two years. Annual extensions after that can boost the contract to five years' duration and maximum value. The work includes support for NASA's rocket, balloon, aeronautical and launch-range programs at Wallops Island.

Computer Sciences has provided the services since 1970.
