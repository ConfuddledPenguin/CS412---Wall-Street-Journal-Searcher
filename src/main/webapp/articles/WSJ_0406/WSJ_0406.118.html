Business Brief -- Norcen Energy Resources Ltd.: Quebec Pension-Fund Panel Increases Its Stake to 8.99%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

Caisse de Depot et Placement, the Quebec government's pension-fund agency, said it increased its stake in Norcen Energy Resources Ltd. to 8.99% of the common shares outstanding. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Caisse said it holds 2,462,273 Norcen Energy Resources shares, including 143,500 shares bought between Feb. 20 and March 15 at $26.44 to $26.69 a share.

Caisse said it purchased the shares for investment purposes.

Norcen Energy, an oil and gas concern, is based in Calgary, Alberta.
