Canadian Steel Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 295,903 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, up 1.5% from 291,525 tons in the preceding week but down 6.6% from 316,716 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The cumulative total in 1990 was 3,502,877 tons, down 12% from 3,989,182 tons a year earlier.
