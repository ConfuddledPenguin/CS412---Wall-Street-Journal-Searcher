International: Import Car Sales Rose in Japan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

TOKYO -- Sales of imported cars and commercial vehicles in Japan soared 42% to 203,010 vehicles, topping the 200,000-unit level for the first time, the Japan Automobile Importers' Association said. The total, which compares with 142,495 units last year, marks the fourth year sales have set a record, the association said.

Import-vehicle sales jumped 60% to 25,943 units in March alone from 16,169 units a year earlier, the association said.

The record number of sales for one month marks the 75th consecutive month in which imported auto sales rose from year-earlier levels, the association said.

Although West German vehicle makers led the import sales, U.S. car makers sold 2,554 units in March, up 41% from 1,812 units in the year-earlier period.
