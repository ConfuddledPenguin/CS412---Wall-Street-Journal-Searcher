Financing Business: Enclean Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

ENCLEAN Inc. said it is offering to the public 1,351,932 shares of common at $9 each. Of the shares offered, 1,200,000 are being sold by the company, 79,412 are being sold by certain stockholders, and 72,520 are being sold upon exercise of warrants to be purchased from certain stockholders of the company, Enclean said in Alvin, Texas. Enclean said it won't receive any of the proceeds from the sale of common stock by selling stockholders, but will receive about $285,000 upon exercise of the warrants. Proceeds to the environmental company will be used to reduce debt and to complete several acquisitions. The underwriters have an option to purchase an additional 202,789 shares from Enclean to cover overallotments. Painewebber Inc. and Chicago Corp. are managing the offering.
