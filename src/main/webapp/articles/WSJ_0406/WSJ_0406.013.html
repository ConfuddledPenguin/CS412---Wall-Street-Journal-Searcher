Tesoro Petroleum Wins Verdict

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- Tesoro Petroleum Corp. said it won a $6.4 million verdict against a foreign subsidiary of Coastal Corp. The company said a New York Supreme Court jury found that Holborn Oil Co., a Bermuda-based unit of Coastal, had agreed to purchase 25,000 metric tons of gasoline from Tesoro in May 1979, before a steep decline in the world petroleum market. Holborn later denied entering into the contract and refused to accept delivery of the gasoline, Tesoro said, forcing Tesoro to sell the gasoline at a loss. The jury verdict represented Tesoro's loss and accrued interest.
