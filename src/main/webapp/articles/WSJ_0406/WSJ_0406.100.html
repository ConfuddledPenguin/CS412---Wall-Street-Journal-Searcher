Increased Offer for Qume

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

MILPITAS, Calif. -- Qume Corp. said the previously reported offer made by the company's chairman David Lee, and Wearnes Technology (Pte) Ltd. of Singapore has been increased to $6.75 a share, from $6.50 a share. The group making the offer holds about 22% of the company's stock. A Qume spokesman said the increased offer is valued at about $65 million. Qume, a maker of computer products, said the proposal is being reviewed by a special committee, which is also considering other alternatives.
