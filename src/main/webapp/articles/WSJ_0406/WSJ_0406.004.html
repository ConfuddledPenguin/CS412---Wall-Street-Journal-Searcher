Who's News: DeKalb Genetics Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

DEKALB GENETICS Corp. (DeKalb, Ill.) -- Thomas B. Rice, formerly executive vice president of the company's DeKalb Pfizer Genetics seed operation, was named president and chief operating officer of the unit. Mr. Rice, 43 years old, succeeds Kent Schulze, who resigned last month. Byron D. Ford, 46, formerly senior vice president at the unit, succeeds Mr. Rice as executive vice president.
