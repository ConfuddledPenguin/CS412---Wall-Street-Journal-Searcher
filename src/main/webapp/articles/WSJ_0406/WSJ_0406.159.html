Merchants National Earnings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Merchants National Corp. said earnings for the first quarter will be reduced $4 million, or 25 cents a share, as a result of an increase in nonperforming loans and a charge related to Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc. The banking concern said loans totaling $45 million in principal amount are being placed on nonaccrual status. The loans are all to Midwest borrowers and are mostly related to real-estate projects.

Merchants, which hasn't yet released its earnings, also said it will take a charge of about $3.3 million related to $5 million of commercial paper issued by Drexel, which filed for federal bankruptcy-law protection on Feb. 13. The charge won't affect the allowance for loan losses.

In the year-ago first quarter, Merchants earned $12.4 million, or 87 cents a share.
