Neco Enterprises Meeting

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

MIDDLETOWN, R.I. -- Neco Enterprises Inc. shareholders, at a special adjourned meeting, approved two proposals ratifying the purchases of certain assets of its chairman and principal stockholder, David F. LaRoche. The transactions required approval of holders of 80% of the company's stock. Mr. LaRoche, who holds an approximate stake of 75% of the Providence-based diversified company, was allowed to vote only 73% of the shares outstanding on the proposals.

The assets, including a Rhode Island mobile-home community, and resort properties in Quechee, Vt., were sold to Neco for a total of $24.4 million last June.
