Who's News: Chrysler Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

CHRYSLER Corp. (Highland Park, Mich.) -- Erick A. Reickert was named chairman of the Acustar Inc. parts subsidiary. Mr. Reickert, 54 years old, succeeds Thomas T. Stallkamp, 43, who was named a corporate vice president in charge of procurement and supply. The previous procurement and supply vice president, David R. Platt, 62, will retire May 1.
