Business Brief -- Mitsui Taiyo Kobe Bank Ltd.: J. Dickson Brown Agrees To Form a Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

Mitsui Taiyo Kobe Bank Ltd. and J. Dickson Brown Inc. of the U.S. agreed to form a joint venture in option and swap instruments, an official at the Japanese bank said. The new firm will make markets in interest-rate swaps and in a variety of options focusing on interest rates, according to the official. The new firm is expected to begin operation this autumn.

Mitsui Taiyo Kobe will hold 66.6% of the venture, with the rest held by J. Dickson Brown. The new firm, which will be be capitalized at $2.3 million, will be based in New York with representative offices in London and Tokyo, the bank official said.
