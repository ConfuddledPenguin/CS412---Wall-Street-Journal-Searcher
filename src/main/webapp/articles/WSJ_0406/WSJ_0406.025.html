Campbell Soup Facility Closing

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

CAMDEN, N.J. -- Campbell Soup Co. said it will close its Chicago warehouse Sept. 28 as part of a consolidation of distribution facilities in the Midwest. Following the facility's closing, the company will distribute its products to midwestern customers by using plants in Napoleon, Ohio, and Paris, Texas.

The company hasn't yet negotiated severance benefits for the Chicago facility's 70 union employees affected by the closing.
