LPL Agrees to Be Acquired

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

WILLINGFORD, Conn. -- LPL Technologies Inc. said it agreed to be acquired by a management group led by Chairman Lawrence J. DeGeorge and his son Lawrence F. DeGeorge. The group will pay $25 a share, $1 a share more than its previous bid, for the 20% of LPL's approximately 7.5 million voting shares that are publicly traded. That portion of the transaction will have a value of $37.5 million.

The company said certain affiliated and institutional holders of LPL shares, including certain members of management, have agreed to receive $17 in cash and a share of 12% cumulative redeemable preferred stock with a stated value of $8 for each LPL share.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, LPL shares jumped $1.375 to close at $24.125.
