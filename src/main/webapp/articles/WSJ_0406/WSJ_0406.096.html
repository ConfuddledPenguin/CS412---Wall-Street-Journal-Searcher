Roosevelt Financial Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

ST. LOUIS -- Roosevelt Financial Group Inc., holding company for Roosevelt Bank, said it expects to report a $4.6 million loss for the first quarter, equal to 80 cents a share, after liquidating most of its high-yield bond portfolio. The company said it reduced its portfolio to $3.5 million from $36.1 million, resulting in an after-tax charge of $7.7 million for the quarter ended March 31.
