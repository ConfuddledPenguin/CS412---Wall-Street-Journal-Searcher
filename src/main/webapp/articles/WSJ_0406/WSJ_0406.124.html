CBOT Seat Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Board of Trade was sold for $324,000, up $5,000 from the previous sale last Friday. Seats currently are quoted at $305,000 bid, $320,000 asked.

The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set Aug. 31, 1987.
