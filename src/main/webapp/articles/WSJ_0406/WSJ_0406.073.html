International Totalizator

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

CARLSBAD, Calif. -- International Totalizator Systems Inc. said it signed a preliminary agreement with Colony Farm Holdings Ltd. for a $7.6 million automated betting system for a proposed new race track in Vancouver, British Columbia. The agreement is subject to Canadian government approvals of the Colony Farm project, which would include a hotel, golf course and theme park in addition to the race track.

International Totalizator makes computerized ticketing and betting systems.
