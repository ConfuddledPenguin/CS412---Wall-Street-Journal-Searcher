Sweden's Jobless Rate Declines

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's unemployment rate in March fell to 1.3% from 1.4% in February and was unchanged from a year earlier, Statistics Sweden said. The national statistics agency said 60,000 persons were without work last month, lower than the preceding month's 63,000 persons and unchanged from March 1989.

The total size of Sweden's work force rose to 4,545,000 from 4,541,000 in February and was up from 4,456,000 a year earlier.

SCB said the 89,000 jobs created in the past year are mostly in the building sector.
