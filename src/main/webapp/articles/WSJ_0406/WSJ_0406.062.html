Belzberg's Armstrong Contact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

PHILADELPHIA -- A federal appeals court ruled that Canada's Belzberg family, which owns about 10.68% of Armstrong World Industries Inc., can directly contact shareholders regarding proposed amendments to the company's charter. The ruling modified a lower court order last month. A judge had ruled that the Belzberg family couldn't directly contact Armstrong shareholders regarding the amendments, which would revoke certain anti-takeover provisions in Armstrong's bylaws.

The Belzbergs are planning a proxy fight to elect four directors to Armstrong's board and to revoke the anti-takeover measures.

The Belzbergs have said they may seek to acquire the buildings-product manufacturer.
