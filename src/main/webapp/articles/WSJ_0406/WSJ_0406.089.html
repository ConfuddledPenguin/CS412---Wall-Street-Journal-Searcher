WestAir Holding Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- WestAir Holding Inc. said revenue passenger miles flown in March increased 42% to 56 million, from 39.5 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The company cited robust traffic on its California routes in addition to growth in its Washington-Dulles hub markets.

Load factor, or percentage of seats filled, was 46.2%, compared with 45.8% a year before.

For the first three months, WestAir said revenue passenger miles flown rose 41% to 146.1 million from 103.8 million. Load factor for the three months was 43.5%, compared with 44.3% a year earlier.
