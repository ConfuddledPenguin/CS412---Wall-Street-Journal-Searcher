Equitec Interest Payments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

OAKLAND, Calif. -- Equitec Financial Group Inc. said it suspended quarterly interest payments on its secured line of credit with PacifiCorp Credit Inc. The principal amount outstanding under the line of credit is $10.1 million. PacifiCorp Credit is unit of PacifiCorp, a Portland, Ore., holding company that also has utility and coal mining interests. PacifiCorp also controls 49.5% of Equitec.

Last month the asset-management services company delayed payment on its 10% convertible subordinated debentures due 2004, as it considers options relating to restructuring its debt. Equitec said it is working with its lenders.
