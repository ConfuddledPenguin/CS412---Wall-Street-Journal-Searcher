P. Jackson, R. Larson Approved by Senate For Oversight Board

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

WASHINGTON -- The Senate confirmed the nominations of Philip Jackson and Robert Larson to be members of the Oversight Board of the Resolution Trust Corp. The approvals, which came on voice votes, complete the five-member body. The board sets policy and supervises the work of the RTC, the agency created to sell or merge sick savings and loans.

Mr. Larson is chief executive officer of Taubman Co., a Bloomfield, Mich., real-estate development company. Mr. Jackson is an adjunct professor at Birmingham Southern College in Alabama and a former banking executive.

The other members of the board are Treasury Secretary Nicholas Brady, Housing and Urban Development Secretary Jack Kemp, and Federal Reserve Chairman Alan Greenspan.
