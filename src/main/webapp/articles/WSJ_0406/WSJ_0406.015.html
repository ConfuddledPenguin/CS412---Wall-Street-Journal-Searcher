Valhi to Buy Rest of Sybra Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

DALLAS -- Valhi Inc. said its board agreed to acquire the 3% of Sybra Inc. it doesn't already own for stock. Valhi, a holding company for Dallas investor Harold C. Simmons, said that it will issue 1.6 Valhi shares for each of the 79,292 Sybra shares outstanding. Holders of 87,820 Sybra stock options will receive 1.6 Valhi shares for each option when the options are exercised.

Sybra, based in Atlanta, owns and operates 152 Arby's restaurants.
