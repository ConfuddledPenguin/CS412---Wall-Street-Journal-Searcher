Thomas Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Thomas Industries Inc. said it formed a joint venture with Schreder Lighting, Brussels, to manufacture and market tunnel lighting products. Terms weren't released. Under the agreement, the products will be manufactured at Thomas's plant in Milan, Ill., utilizing design and technology developed by Schreder. Thomas will also market the products in the U.S., Canada and Mexico. The arrangement will "greatly accelerate our opportunities to market an expanded high-quality line of tunnel lighting products," Thomas said.
