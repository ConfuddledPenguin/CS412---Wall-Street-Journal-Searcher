International Brief -- AB L. M. Ericsson: Firm Gets Mexican Order For Telephone Equipment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

Swedish telecommunications group AB L.M. Ericsson said it signed a 1.1 billion-krona ($179 million) contract with the Mexican Telephone Administration for the delivery of AXE digital switching systems and power equipment in 1991. Ericsson said a further contract for the delivery of transmission equipment is expected to be signed shortly.

The group said the order is part of a five-year general agreement recently signed between Ericsson and Mexican Telephone with a total value of six billion kronor.

Further orders are also expected for power and transmission equipment as well as for other specific projects over the five-year period, Ericsson said.

It said the order was a result of the Mexican government's determination to expand the country's telecommunications system.
