Who's News: Bank of Boston Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

BANK OF BOSTON Corp. (Boston) -- Michael Simmons was named a group executive and executive vice president of technology and operations, a new position at this bank holding company, effective May 1. Mr. Simmons, 50 years old, is currently executive vice president of BankAmerica Systems Engineering, a division of BankAmerica Corp.'s Bank of America unit.
