Who's News: Optrotech Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

OPTROTECH Ltd. (Billerica, Mass.) -- The company added four directors to its board, increasing board membership to eight. New directors are: Jonathan Kolber, president of Claridge Israel Inc.; Mark Smith, vice president of Claridge Israel; Jacob Brin, chairman of SuperSol, a supermarket chain in Israel, and Dov Tadmor, managing director of Discount Investment Corp., of Israel. Optrotech is a computerized electro-optical inspection concern.
