West German Steel Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/06/90

WIESBADEN, West Germany -- The production of raw steel in West Germany slipped 5.2% to 9.8 million metric tons in the first quarter from the like period of 1989, the Federal Statistics Office said. Output of raw iron was down 6.9% to 7.8 million tons in the first three months of this year from a year earlier, the statistics office added.

In March alone, raw-steel output fell 0.9% to 3.5 million tons, while iron output was off 3.4% from a year earlier to 2.7 million tons, the agency said.
