International: West German New Orders

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/06/90

BONN -- New orders to the West German manufacturing industry rose a price-adjusted and seasonally adjusted 0.4% in February from January but dropped 2.4% from a year earlier, the Bonn Economics Ministry said in a preliminary report. The month-to-month rise reflects a 3% increase in foreign orders, while domestic order bookings fell 1%.

The February increase, which follows a 5.5% drop in January, was smaller than what most economists had expected.

In a two-month comparison, which is less subject to short-term fluctuations, January-February order bookings dropped 2.5% from the November-December period. Foreign orders fell 5.5%, while domestic orders were off 1%.
