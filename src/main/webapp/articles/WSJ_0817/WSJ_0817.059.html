S&P 500 Drops Philips Industries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

NEW YORK -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it is deleting Philips Industries Inc., Dayton, Ohio, from its index of 500 companies, on the close of trading Aug. 22. It will be replaced by Biomet Inc., a Warsaw, Ind., manufacturer of surgical implants, S&P said. London-based Tomkins PLC recently agreed to acquire Philips Industries, a maker of building and industrial products, for $550 million.
