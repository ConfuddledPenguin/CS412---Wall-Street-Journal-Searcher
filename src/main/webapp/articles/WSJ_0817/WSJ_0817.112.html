Business Brief -- Toshiba Corp.: West German Unit to Lift Laptop Computer Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

Toshiba Corp. of Japan will double production of laptop personal computers at its West German unit by October, half a year earlier than initially planned, to meet surging demand in Europe, a company official said. Toshiba Europa G.m.b.H., the West German unit, began producing 5,000 laptops a month at a new 25 million-mark ($15.9 million) factory in Regensburg, Bavaria, last April, with plans for monthly production of 10,000 units within a year, a target that was moved up six months.

The company is exporting from Japan that part of European demand that can't be supplied locally.

Toshiba holds a 38% share of the European laptop market. It is producing three laptop computer models of its T-series, compatible with International Business Corp. machines, with plans to begin producing an additional model. It also is considering producing book-type personal computers at the West German facility.
