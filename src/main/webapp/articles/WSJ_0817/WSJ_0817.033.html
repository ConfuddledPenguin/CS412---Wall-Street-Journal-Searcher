Dividend News: Quicksilver Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

QUICKSILVER ENTERPRISES INC. said its board approved a 3-for-1 split on its 2.4 million warrants to purchase common stock. After the split there will be 7.2 million warrants, which will be exercisable for one common share each at 15 cents a share. The Temecula, Calif.-based concern makes powered ultralight aircraft and recreational vehicles. In over-the-counter trading yesterday, Quicksilver shares closed at 43.8 cents, bid.
