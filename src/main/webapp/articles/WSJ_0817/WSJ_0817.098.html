American Health Properties Acquires 2 Florida Facilities

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

LOS ANGELES -- American Health Properties Inc. said it completed a $21.6 million purchase of two Florida properties, Retreat Psychiatric Hospital and Anclote Manor, from American Medical Holdings Inc. American Health Properties said the transaction marks the first stage of its purchase of American Medical's five-hospital psychiatric division for a total purchase price of about $72 million. "We expect to complete the acquisition of the remaining three properties, located in California and Massachusetts, during the third quarter of 1990," said Robert L. B. Diener, president and chief operating officer of the real estate investment trust.

The company said the two Florida facilities will be leased to operating companies comprised of each hospital's management and physicians. The initial term of each lease is 10 years, with three additional 10-year renewal options. The Retreat Psychiatric Hospital is a 100-bed facility in Sunrise, Fla.; Anclote Manor is a 130-bed facility in Tarpon Springs, Fla.
