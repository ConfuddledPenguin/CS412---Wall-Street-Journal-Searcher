Who's News: Advanced Logic Research Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

ADVANCED LOGIC RESEARCH Inc. (Irvine, Calif.) -- Therese E. Myers, president of Quarterdeck Office Systems, and Kenneth W. Simonds, chief executive officer of Teradata Corp., were named directors of this computer products concern. They succeed I.F. Tang and Frederick Tan, both of shareholder Wearnes Technology, who resigned as directors as part of an effort to broaden the board's composition.
