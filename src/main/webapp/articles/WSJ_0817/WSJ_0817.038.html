Who's News: Earl Scheib Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

EARL SCHEIB Inc. (Beverly Hills, Calif.) -- Robert Spencer, a mergers and acquisitions consultant, was elected to the board of this chain of automobile body shops. Mr. Spencer, 72 years old, succeeds Frederick Levy, 88, who resigned at the company's board meeting Wednesday. Mr. Levy gave no reason for his resignation, a company spokesman said.
