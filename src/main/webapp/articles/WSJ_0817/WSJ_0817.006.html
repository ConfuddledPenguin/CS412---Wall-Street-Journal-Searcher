  • Date: 08/17/90

STOUGHTON, Mass. -- Reebok International said its Rockport Co. Inc. unit has purchased all rights in Europe to the Rockport brand name from Rockport Lizenzund Handels Establishment, a Liechtenstein company. The transaction gives the U.S. firm Rockport Lizenz's registered rights to market in 20 European nations. A price wasn't given.

Rockport makes dress and casual shoes. With sales of about $192 million in 1989, the Marboro, Mass. based company is one of Reebok's largest subsidiaries. Reebok makes athletic shoes.
