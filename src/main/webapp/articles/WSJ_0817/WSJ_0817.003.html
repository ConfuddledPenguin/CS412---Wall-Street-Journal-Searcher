Who's News: American Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

AMERICAN SAVINGS BANK (White Plains, N.Y.) -- Edmond P. Hannigan was named senior vice president, heading the new loan-review department at this state-chartered savings bank. Mr. Hannigan, 56 years old, was previously vice president for loan review at the Uniondale, N.Y., branch of European American Bank, a commercial bank.
