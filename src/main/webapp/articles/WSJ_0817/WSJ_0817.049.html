Financing Business: Banc One Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

BANC ONE Corp. (Columbus, Ohio) -- This bank holding company said it completed the sale of $200 million in subordinated notes through an underwriting group headed by Salomon Brothers Inc. Banc One said it intends to use the proceeds of the sale to repay a portion of commercial paper borrowings incurred to fund its investment in Bank One Texas.
