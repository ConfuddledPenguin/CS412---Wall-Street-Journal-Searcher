Who's News: General Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

GENERAL ELECTRIC Co. (Fairfield, Conn.) -- David R. Nissen was named chairman of Monogram Bank USA, an Ohio-based credit card bank unit of GE Capital, GE's financial services subsidiary. Mr. Nissen succeeds Ray Neid, who became manager of European strategic development at GE Capital's retailer financial services unit. Mr. Nissen had been vice president and general manager of private label credit card financing.
