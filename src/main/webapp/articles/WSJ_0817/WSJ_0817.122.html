Marketing Brief -- Cineplex Odeon Corp.: Firm Extends Sale Deadline For 38 U.S. Movie Theaters

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

Cineplex Odeon Corp. said the deadline for the sale of 38 of its U.S. movie theater properties has been extended. As previously reported, Cineplex said this week that it may not be able to complete the $31.8 million sale of the theaters to Los Angeles-based Plitt Amusement Co. The closing date of the transaction, originally set for July 31, had already been extended to Aug. 15.

Toronto-based Cineplex said earlier it may not be able to complete the sale, one of many required under agreements with the bankers for the troubled movie theater chain. Cineplex declined to give a reason for the uncertainty surrounding a sale, but individuals in the industry said Plitt's bankers appear unwilling to finance the transaction at the current price.

Cineplex said it is continuing negotiations with Plitt, and didn't give a new deadline for closing the sale.
