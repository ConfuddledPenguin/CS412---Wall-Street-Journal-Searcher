Business Brief -- Automatic Data Processing Inc.: Firm Posts a 13% Increase In Fourth-Quarter Earnings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

Automatic Data Processing Inc. posted a 13% rise in earnings for the fiscal fourth quarter, and projected a 10% per-share earnings increase for fiscal 1991. In the quarter ended June 30, the computer services company earned $59.7 million, or 81 cents a share, up from $53 million, or 73 cents a share, a year ago. The growth was fueled in part by a lower effective tax rate. Fourth-quarter revenue was $425.8 million, up from $418.1 million last year.

For the fiscal year, earnings were up 13% to $211.7 million, or $2.88 a share. Revenue was up 2% to $1.71 billion.

The company predicted only "modest" fiscal 1991 revenue growth, in part because in the recent fiscal year it sold its manufacturing and banking information services and Canadian brokerage quotation services, as well as 81% of its Brazilian business.

The company said that capital spending would remain at about $100 million in fiscal 1991.
