TSL to Acquire Packaging Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

DENVER -- TSL Inc. said it has agreed to acquire Regent Enterprises, a closely held Carol Stream, Ill., packaging company, in a stock swap. The company didn't indicate a value for the deal. TSL will hold as much as 80% of Regent stock in return for 49% of TSL stock.

TSL is a development-stage company with patents for anti-tampering technology. A development-stage company is one that has just started up and has no significant revenue. Regent had 1989 revenue of about $20 million.

The deal is subject to approval of the boards of both companies.
