Franklin Bancorp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

WASHINGTON -- Franklin Bancorp. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire privately held Metropolitan Bancshares Inc. through an exchange of stock. The proposed transaction is expected to be completed by the end of 1990 or during the first quarter of 1991, pending due diligence and regulatory approval. Franklin is the holding company for Franklin National Bank, and Metropolitan is the holding company for Washington-based Metropolitan Bank. The combined commercial bank holding company would have assets of about $100 million.
