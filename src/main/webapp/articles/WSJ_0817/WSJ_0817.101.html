Dividend News: U.S. Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

U.S. BANCORP declared a 20% common stock dividend payable Sept. 14 to stock of record Aug. 27. After the stock dividend, the company will have about 60 million shares outstanding. Directors of the Portland, Ore.-based company also declared a post-split dividend at the regular rate of 25 cents a share, payable Oct. 5 to stock of record Sept. 17.
