Portuguese Bank Sets ADRs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

LISBON -- Banco Comercial Portugues, a private commercial bank, said it established a sponsored American depositary receipt program and expects the ADRs to begin actively trading next week. Each ADR will represent one underlying share and will trade in the over-the-counter market quoted in the so-called Pink Sheets. The program is through Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. Jardim Goncalves, chairman of the $3.4 billion bank, said the ADR program would diversify the bank's investor base and allow Americans access to the Portuguese market.
