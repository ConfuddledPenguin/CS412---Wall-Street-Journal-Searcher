  • Date: 08/17/90

MAGAZINES: Hearst Corp. has named Terry McDonell, 46 years old, as the new editor in chief of Esquire magazine. He succeeds Lee Eisenberg, 44, who will move to London to be founding editor of Hearst's new British edition of Esquire, scheduled to begin publishing in March 1991. Mr. McDonell has been the president and editor in chief of Smart magazine, which he jointly owned with New American Magazine Co. and its owner, Owen Lipstein.
