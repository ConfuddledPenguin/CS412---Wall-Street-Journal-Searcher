Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 165,257 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, down 5.8% from 175,432 tons in the preceding week and down 42% from 284,207 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production totaled 8,622,444 metric tons, down 11% from 9,736,519 tons a year earlier.
