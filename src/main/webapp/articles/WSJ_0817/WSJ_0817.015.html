International Controls

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

KALAMAZOO, Mich. -- International Controls Corp. said it has extended to Sept. 17 an offer to holders of its 12 3/4% senior subordinated debentures and its subordinated discount debentures to exchange those securities for a package of cash, new senior debentures and warrants to purchase the company's common stock. The offer was to have expired yesterday. International Controls also said it was advised that holders of the debentures have formed a committee to discuss the exchange offer with the truck, trailer and taxicab manufacturer.
