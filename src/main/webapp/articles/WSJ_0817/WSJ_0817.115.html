Parker Hannifin Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

CLEVELAND -- Parker Hannifin Corp. said its board has authorized the buy-back, from time to time, of as many as three million shares of common stock in the open market at prevailing prices. The buy-back will be funded from operating cash flow, and the shares initially will be held as treasury stock. Parker produces motion-control parts for industrial and aerospace markets.
