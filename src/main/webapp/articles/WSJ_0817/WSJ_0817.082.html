Business Brief -- Unocal Corp.: Price Freeze to Be Lifted On U.S. Wholesale Gasoline

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

Unocal Corp. said it will lift its one-week freeze on U.S. wholesale prices of gasoline; a spokesman said that prices would go up by one cent today in some Western states. The Los Angeles-based company implemented a one-week freeze Aug. 9 in response to a call by President Bush to act responsibly in the wake of world oil-price rises created by the Iraqi takeover of Kuwait.

Amoco Co. has also lifted its freeze on wholesale gasoline, jet fuel and distillate prices; but Atlantic Richfield Co. said it will continue its freeze indefinitely at both wholesale and retail levels.
