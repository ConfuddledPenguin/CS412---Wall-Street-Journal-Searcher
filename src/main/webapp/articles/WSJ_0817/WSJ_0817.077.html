Who's News: Met-Pro Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

MET-PRO Corp. (Harleysville, Pa.) -- Walter A. Everett, acting chief executive officer, was elected president, chief executive and a director of this maker of pollution-control systems, succeeding Glendon E. Rose, who resigned for personal reasons. Mr. Everett, who served in the same roles for many years, had retired in January of this year, but returned in June after Mr. Rose was hospitalized for surgery.
