Who's News: Airgas Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

AIRGAS Inc. (Radnor, Pa.) -- George de B. Bell, 66 years old, co-chairman of Janney Montgomery Scott Inc., and John A. H. Shober, 56, chief executive officier of Penn Virginia Corp., were appointed directors of this distributor of industrial, medical and specialty gases. They succeed two directors who retired: Richard Nalle, former chairman of Pennsylvania Hospital, and Frank Palamara, former chief financial officer of ARA Services Inc.
