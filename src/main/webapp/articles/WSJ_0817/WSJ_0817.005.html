Ohio Edison Gets Rate Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- The Public Utilities Commission of Ohio granted Ohio Edison Co. a rate increase of 8.5%, or about $142.3 million, less than the utility's request for a boost of about $216 million. The commission said the typical Ohio Edison customer using 500 kilowatts an hour of electricity now pays about $57.23 a month. Under the increase, this will rise to $62.38 a month, the commission estimated.
