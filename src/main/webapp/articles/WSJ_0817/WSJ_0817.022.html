Financing Business: Agnico-Eagle Mines Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

AGNICO-EAGLE MINES Ltd., Toronto, said it plans to issue 3.2 million units of warrants and common shares for total proceeds of 27.6 million Canadian dollars (US$24.1 million), to be used to finance the mining company's exploration and development projects. The units will consist of one common share and a warrant to buy one-half common share, and will be priced at C$8.63 each. For each two warrants, a holder may buy one Agnico-Eagle common share for C$9.13 for about two years from the date of issue. The issue will be underwritten by a group of Toronto-based investment dealers, led by RBC Dominion Securities Inc.
