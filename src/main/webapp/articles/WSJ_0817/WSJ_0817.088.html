REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: A-Team Hits Iraq

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

Not since D-Day has an invasion force so awesome been assembled. We're speaking here, of course, of the network-anchorman invasion of the Middle East. For days unconfirmed rumors of an anchorman buildup has fueled confusion about American television's goals in the Persian Gulf. Days also passed while the world waited breathlessly to learn whether Ted Koppel would be allowed into Iraq, and to figure out where the legions commanded by Dan Rather were stationed. These concerns have been resolved. Now, with ABC's Sam Donaldson and NBC's "Sunday Today" co-anchor Garrick Utley have also been deployed to the front, as has CBS's "This Morning" anchor Harry Smith. We of course look forward to their bulletins and interruptions, secure in the knowledge that Saddam Hussein, the Bully of Baghdad, has more than met his match.
