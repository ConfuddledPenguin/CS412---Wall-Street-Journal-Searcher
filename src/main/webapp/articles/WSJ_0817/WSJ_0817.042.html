Dividend News: Southern California Edison

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/17/90

SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA EDISON, a Rosemead, Calif.-based electric utility, declared a quarterly dividend of 27 cents a share on its 4.32% series of cumulative preferred stock. It also declared dividends on four series of $100 cumulative preferred stock: $1.95 on the 7.80% series, $2.135 on the 8.54% series, $2.175 on the 8.70% series and $2.175 on the 8.70% series-A. Each of the dividends is payable Sept. 30 to shareholders of record on Sept. 5. The utility is a unit of SCE Corp., also based in Rosemead.
