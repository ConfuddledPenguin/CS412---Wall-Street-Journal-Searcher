Forschner Group Earnings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/17/90

SHELTON, Conn. -- Forschner Group Inc. said if the dollar continues to remain weak against the Swiss franc, 1990 earnings may be substantially lower than a year earlier. Forschner is the U.S. marketer of Swiss Army Knives and other Swiss products; in 1989 it had net income of $3.1 million or 75 cents a share on sales of $43.8 million.

Prospect Group Inc., which holds a 58% interest in Forschner, plans next Tuesday to distribute its Forschner shares to its holders on the basis of one share for each 10.3 Prospect shares.
