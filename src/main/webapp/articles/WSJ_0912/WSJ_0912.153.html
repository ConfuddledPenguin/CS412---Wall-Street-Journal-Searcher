Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

American Telephone & Telegraph Co., shelf offering of $976 million of notes and warrants to purchase notes, via Salomon Bros. and First Boston Corp. Bank of New York Co. will act as trustee.

Exeter Bancorp., initial offering of 600,000 common shares, via Blunt Ellis & Loewi Inc.

Score Board Inc., offering of one million shares of common stock, 300,000 of which will be sold by certain holders.
