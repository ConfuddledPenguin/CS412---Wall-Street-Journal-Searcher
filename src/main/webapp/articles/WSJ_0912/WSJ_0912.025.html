Litton Industries Unit Wins Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

WASHINGTON -- Litton Systems, a unit of Litton Industries Inc., received a $202 million Navy contract for tactical air-operations control equipment.

Hess Oil Virgin Islands Corp. and Mobil Corp. each won a $47.7 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel. The agency also awarded a $17.9 million jet fuel contract to Chevron USA Inc., a unit of Chevron Corp.

Rockwell International Corp. was awarded a $28.9 million Air Force contract for B-1B aircraft modifications.

National Apparel Inc. got a $27 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for chemical protective suits.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. got an $11.6 million Air Force pact for aircraft fire-control radar spare parts.
