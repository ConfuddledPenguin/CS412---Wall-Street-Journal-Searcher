Who's News: Procter & Gamble Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

PROCTER & GAMBLE Co. (Cincinnati) -- Robert L. Wehling, 51 years old, vice president, marketing services, at this consumer products concern, was named vice president, public affairs, effective Oct. 1, succeeding Gerald S. Gendell, 61, senior public affairs director since 1981 who plans to retire in March.
