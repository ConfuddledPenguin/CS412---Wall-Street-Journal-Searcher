Rate Rises at Citicorp's Auction of 91-Day Paper

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 7.9397% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 7.895% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $401 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.925% to 7.94%.

Citicorp also said the average rate rose to 7.924% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.875% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $375 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.915% to 7.935%.

The bank holding company scheduled an auction of $50 million of each maturity next Tuesday.
