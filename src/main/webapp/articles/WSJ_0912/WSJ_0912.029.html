Storage Technology Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

DENVER -- Federal Judge Sherman Finesilver approved an $13.7 million settlement of a 1984 class action lawsuit against former directors and officers of Storage Technology Corp., said Nicholas E. Chimicles, lead attorney for the 70,000 investors who are members of the suit. Under the terms of the settlement, investors will receive $6.3 million in cash and 377,750 shares in Storage Technology valued at $7.4 million at recent prices. The suit charged the company's former management with making misleading statements about finances between June 1982 and October 1984.

Storage Technology emerged from bankruptcy court protection under new management in 1987.
