Business Brief -- Kentucky Medical Insurance Co.: BGC Raises Stake to 23% Of Class A Common Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

BGC Special Equity L.P. said it raised its stake in Kentucky Medical Insurance Co. to 23% of the Class A common stock outstanding for investment purposes. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, BGC, a Louisville, Ky., investment partnership, said it holds 329,061 Kentucky Medical shares, including 82,865 shares bought from Aug. 15 to Aug. 31 at $17.375 to $19.625 each. During the year, BGC has steadily been increasing its stake in the Louisville provider of medical liability insurance to doctors, hospitals and health-care facilities.

Officials at Kentucky Medical couldn't immediately be reached.
