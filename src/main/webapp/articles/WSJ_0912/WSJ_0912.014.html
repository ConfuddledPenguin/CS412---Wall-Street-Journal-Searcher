Who's News: Cortex Pharmaceuticals Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

CORTEX PHARMACEUTICALS Inc. (Irvine, Calif.) -- Vaughan H.J. Shalson, 44 years old, previously president and chief executive officer of Bio-Response Inc., was named president and chief executive officer of this operator of laboratories that focus on discovery and development of therapeutics for age-related neurodegenerative disorders, succeeding Harold Hutchings, who resigned last April. Raymond Bartus, senior vice president, research and development, had been interim president.
