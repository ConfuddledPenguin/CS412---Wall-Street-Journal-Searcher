Who's News: Van Ommeren Ceteco N.V.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

VAN OMMEREN CETECO N.V. (Rotterdam, Holland) -- Carel J. van den Driest was named chairman of the board of management, effective Jan. 1, of this transport tank storage and trading company. Mr. van den Driest, 42 years old, succeeds Wim H. Brouwer, 60, who retires at the end of the year. Mr. van den Driest had been a managing director of the company's Rotterdam and Singapore subsidiaries.
