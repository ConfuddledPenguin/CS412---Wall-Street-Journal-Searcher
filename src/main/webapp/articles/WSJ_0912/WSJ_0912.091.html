Business Brief -- Kaufman & Broad Home Corp.: Chief Sees '90 Pretax Profit At the Low End of Range

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

Kaufman & Broad Home Corp. President and Chief Executive Officer Bruce Karatz said he "feels more comfortable" with the lower end of analysts' estimates of fiscal 1990 earnings of $1.60 to $1.90 a share. Mr. Karatz, speaking at a Montgomery Securities investment conference in San Francisco, said revenue for the year ending Nov. 30 will exceed $1.3 billion and that pretax profit should plunge to around $90 million.

For 1989, the homebuilder, based in Los Angeles, reported what Mr. Karatz called "phenomenal" pretax earnings of $142.8 million, or $2.44 a share after taxes. Mr. Karatz said California's real estate market was at "near recessionary levels" but that the company's operations in France are strong, and will produce roughly half of the company's 1990 revenue and operating profit.
