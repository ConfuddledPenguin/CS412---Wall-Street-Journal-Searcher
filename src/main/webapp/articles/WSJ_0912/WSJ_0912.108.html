Letters to the Editor: Indecency Then, Indecency Now

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

The battle betwixt your editor, Robert L. Bartley, and special prosecutor Lawrence W. Walsh on L. Gordon Crovitz's remarks on the costs of the Iran-Contra prosecutions (editorial page, Aug. 9) would be hilarious if the dispute did not strike at the very foundation of our once-superb political system. After reading Mr. Walsh's letter one can only agree with attorney Joseph N. Welch's admonition to Sen. Joseph McCarthy, June 9, 1954: "Let us not assassinate this young lad further, Senator. You have done enough. Have you no sense of decency, sir, at long last]" Richard L. Barkley

Palo Alto, Calif.
