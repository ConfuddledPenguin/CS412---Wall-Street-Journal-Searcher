Penn Central Holders Of 11.3 Million Shares Act on Tender Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

CINCINNATI -- Penn Central Corp., an industrial, defense, insurance and energy concern, said 11,347,000 of its common shares were tendered in response to its offer to buy back 11 million shares at $23 each. The offer expired at midnight Monday. Under the offer for 11 million shares, Penn Central is to buy first from odd-lot holders, who tendered 26,000 shares, and then will buy 97% of the shares tendered by each other holder.

Of the 11,347,000 shares tendered, 3,047,000 were tendered subject to guarantees of delivery. If some of those shares aren't tendered and the total tendered falls to 11 million or below, Penn Central will buy back all the tendered shares.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Penn Central closed at $22.75 a share, down 25 cents.
