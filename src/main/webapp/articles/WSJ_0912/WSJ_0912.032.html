Dividend News: JSB Financial Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

JSB FINANCIAL Inc. said its board declared an initial dividend of 10 cents a share, payable Sept. 29 to stock of record Sept. 21. The Lynbrook, N.Y., holding company for Jamaica Savings Bank converted to stock ownership from a mutual savings bank on June 27, selling 16 million shares at $10 apiece. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, JSB shares rose 12.5 cents to close at $12.625.
