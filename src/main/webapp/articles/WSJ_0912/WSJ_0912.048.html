Feld Productions Launches Circus-Theme Retail Stores

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

VIENNA, Va. -- The Greatest Show on Earth is coming to shopping malls across the country. Ringling Bros. & Barnum & Bailey Circus, a unit of closely held Irvin Feld & Kenneth Feld Productions Inc., is launching a new national chain of circus-theme retail stores that sell everything from clown noses to handpainted carousel figures.

The new venture, Ringling Bros. & Barnum & Bailey Retail Inc., opens its first unit in late October at the Fair Oaks Mall in Fairfax, Va. Additional stores are scheduled for Farmington, Conn., and Short Hills, N.J. President Kenneth Feld said the company plans to open six stores a year.

The stores will sell children's clothing, stuffed toys, party favors and circus memorabilia. Items include custom-made suspenders with cutout leather animals as snaps; juggling kits and handpainted diaper sets. All merchandise is made specifically for the stores and isn't sold at Ringling Bros. & Barnum & Bailey Circus events.

The stores imitate a circus environment, featuring circus wagon doors and three separate rings centered around circus wagon reproduction showcases. Store personnel, trained by graduates of the company's clown college, wear official paint-splattered clown jumpsuits and entertain customers.

Feld Productions is a major live-entertainment company with annual sales exceeding $260 million.
