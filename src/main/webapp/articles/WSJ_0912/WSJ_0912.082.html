Who's News: D.F. King & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

D.F. KING & Co. (New York) -- This financial communications and proxy-solicitation firm named five people to new posts. James E. Long, 35, and John W. Cornwell Jr., 40, previously senior vice presidents, were named executive vice presidents. Named senior vice presidents were Walter A. Denby, 39, previously vice president and corporate secretary of USLife Corp., a New York holding company with interests in life insurance; Peter C. Harkins, 35, previously an executive at Lafer Management Corp., a New York investment firm; and Thomas G. Kies, 32, previously manager of the stock watch department at Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co., a New York bank.
