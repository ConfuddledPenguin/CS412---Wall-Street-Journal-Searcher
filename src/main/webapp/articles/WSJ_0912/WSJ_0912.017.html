Allwaste Sale of Guzzler Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

HOUSTON -- Allwaste Inc. said it completed the sale of its Guzzler Manufacturing Inc. unit to Powerscreen International PLC of the U.K., for about $18 million. The sale of Guzzler will result in a gain of $3.2 million to $3.5 million, or nine cents to 10 cents a share, in the fiscal first quarter ending Nov. 30.

In the year-earlier quarter, the company had net income of $4.5 million, or 13 cents a share, on revenue of $57.3 million. Proceeds of the sale will be used to expand the company's service businesses.
