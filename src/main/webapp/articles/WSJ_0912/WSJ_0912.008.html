Perot Systems Corp. Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

DALLAS -- Perot Systems Corp., the data processing company founded by billionaire H. Ross Perot, said it signed a $100 million, five-year contract to provide American Medical International Inc. with computer services. The agreement allows closely held Perot Systems to provide the hospital management company and its 50 acute care and psychiatric hospitals with data processing and communications services.
