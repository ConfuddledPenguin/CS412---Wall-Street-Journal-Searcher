Letters to the Editor: Reservists Can't Desert Their Debts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

In your Aug. 23 article "Call-Up Leaves Reservists, Bosses Asking How it Works" you said, "However, military law holds armed forces personnel responsible for their debts, and wages can be garnisheed to pay them." In fact, the only debts for which the Air Force is authorized to garnishee wages are child support and alimony. You are correct, however, that military law does hold its members responsible for a failure to pay just debts. Jay R. Hone

Major, USAFR

Albuquerque, N.M.
