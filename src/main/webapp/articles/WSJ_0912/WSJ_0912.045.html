Who's News: Midland Bank PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

MIDLAND BANK PLC (London) -- James K. Phelps was named managing director for private placements, a new post at Midland's international and investment banking arm based in New York. Mr. Phelps, 39 years old, was previously managing director for the private placement group at UBS Securities Inc., a New York subsidiary of United Bank of Switzerland, Zurich.
