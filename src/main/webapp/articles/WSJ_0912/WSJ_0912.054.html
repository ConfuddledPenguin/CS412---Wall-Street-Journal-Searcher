Business Brief -- Kinark Corp.: Steel Partners L.P. Raises Stake in Company to 8.8%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

A New York investment partnership said it raised its stake in Kinark Corp., based in Tulsa, Okla., to 8.8%, even as the group weighs a second offer for the owner of galvanizing plants and chemical-storage facilities. Although Kinark directors rejected an offer last May of $7 a share by Steel Partners L.P., it has continued to talk to the group. Last Friday, Kinark agreed to open its books to the partnership. Stuart Freedman, a spokesman for the investors, declined to discuss the possibility of a bid. "We're just beginning the process," he said.
