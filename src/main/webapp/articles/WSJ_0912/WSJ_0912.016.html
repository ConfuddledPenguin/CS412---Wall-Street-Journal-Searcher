AMR Unit Air Traffic Climbs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- American Airlines, a unit of AMR Corp., said August traffic rose 2.6% to 7.57 billion revenue passenger miles from 7.38 billion miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger carried one mile. Load factor, or the percentage of available seats that are occupied, for the month fell to 68.9% from 72.3% a year earlier.

For the year-to-date, American said it flew 51.73 billion revenue passenger miles, a 4.1% increase from 49.71 billion miles a year earlier. Load factor fell to 63.5% from 65.2%.
