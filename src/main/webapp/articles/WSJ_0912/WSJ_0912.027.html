Who's News: Geo. A. Hormel & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

GEO. A. HORMEL & Co. (Austin, Minn.) -- Alan M. Byers, formerly a meat products regional sales manager, was named president and chief operating officer of the FDL Marketing Inc. unit of this meat and food processing concern, succeeding James W. Cole, who was recently named group vice president of the parent company's new Foodservice Group.
