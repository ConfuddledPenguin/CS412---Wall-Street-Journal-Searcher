Kemper Corp. Reserve

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

LONG GROVE, Ill. -- Kemper Corp. said it established a $9.3 million reserve that will result in a charge of $6.1 million, or 13 cents a share, in the third quarter. The reserve will cover weather-related losses for its property-casualty and reinsurance segments. The company cited substantial claims created by a recent tornado that devastated several Chicago suburbs, as well as hail and windstorms in the Denver area.
