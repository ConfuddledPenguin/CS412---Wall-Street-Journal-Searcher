  • Date: 09/12/90

DALLAS -- Edisto Resources Corp. said its jointly owned Hall-Houston Offshore received $20 million in prepayments for natural gas production in the Gulf of Mexico. Hall-Houston Offshore, a venture of Edisto and Hall-Houston Oil Co., will receive an additional $4.1 million over the term of the five-year contract. Edisto also said it had closed its previously announced buy-back of Hall-Houston Offshore convertible debentures from Prudential-Bache investment funds for $17.2 million. Edisto said it sold at its cost half of the repurchased debt to Hall-Houston Oil.
