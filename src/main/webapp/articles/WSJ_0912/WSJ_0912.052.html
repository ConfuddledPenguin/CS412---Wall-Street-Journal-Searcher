Business Brief -- Avery International Corp.: Shareholder Votes Advance Planned Dennison Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

Avery International Corp. and Dennison Manufacturing Co. said their respective shareholders approved a proposed merger of the two companies at separate meetings yesterday. The affirmative vote represented about 71% of the shares outstanding of Avery International and 82% of Dennison's shares outstanding. Both companies make office products among other things.

Under terms of the merger, Dennison shareholders will receive 1.12 shares of Avery common stock for each share held of Dennison common. The merger will be completed shortly after the Justice Department finishes its antitrust review, which is scheduled to occur by Sept. 28, the companies said.

The successor company, Avery Dennison Corp., would be dominated by the board of Avery International and based at its corporate offices in Pasadena, Calif. Dennison is based in Framingham, Mass.
