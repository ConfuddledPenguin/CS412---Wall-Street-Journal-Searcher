American Capital & Research

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

FAIRFAX, Va. -- American Capital & Research Corp. agreed to buy Elder Resources NZFP Ltd.'s 50% interest in two engineering joint ventures, giving American Capital full ownership of the units. Terms weren't disclosed.

The sale is part of Carter Holt Harvey Ltd.'s plan to divest all of Elder Resources' non-forest assets. Last month, Carter Holt, a New Zealand concern, acquired 52.7% of Elder Resources from Elders IXL Ltd., an Australian company.

American Capital said the acquisition of the units, Kaiser Engineers Australia Pty. Ltd. and Kaiser Engineers International, will reunite all of the Kaiser Engineers companies under a single owner. American Capital already owns the Kaiser operations in the U.S.
