Canada's Housing Starts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian housing starts declined 7% in August to a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 163,000 units, from a July level of 175,000 units, Canadian Mortgage & Housing Corp., a government agency, said. The corporation attributed the August decline to high mortgage rates and a sharp deterioration in consumer confidence. It expects housing starts to decline further in coming months.
