Who's News: Cenvest Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

CENVEST Inc. (Meriden, Conn.) -- James H. Doak Jr., 57-year-old chairman and chief executive officer and a 31-year veteran of the bank holding company and its predecessors, plans to retire at year end. The board is searching for a successor to Mr. Doak, who has been chief executive for 13 years and chairman for nearly three. He began in 1960 as a teller trainee with what was then Meriden Savings Bank.
