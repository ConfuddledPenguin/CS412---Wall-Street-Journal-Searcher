Re Capital Set to Buy Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Re Capital Corp. directors approved a program to purchase an additional $2 million of the reinsurance company's common stock in the open market. Under previous programs, the company repurchased 1.2 million shares for $12.5 million; about $1.7 million remains available from previous programs.
