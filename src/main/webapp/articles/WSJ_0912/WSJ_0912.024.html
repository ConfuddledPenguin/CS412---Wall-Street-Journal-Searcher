Who's News: Eskenazi Resigns Positions From Groupe Bruxelles

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

BRUSSELS -- French Financier Gerard Eskenazi submitted his resignation from the posts of vice president and director of Groupe Bruxelles Lambert S.A., the Belgian holding company said. Mr. Eskenazi's resignation from Bruxelles Lambert follows his announced resignation as chairman of Pargesa Holding S.A., the Swiss holding company that controls a 38% share in Bruxelles Lambert.

Groupe Bruxelles Lambert said a board meeting would be called to accept Mr. Eskenazi's resignation.

Succeeding Mr. Eskenazi as chairman of Pargesa is Paul Desmarais. Mr. Desmarais, 63 years old, is chairman and controlling shareholder of Power Corp. of Canada, Montreal. Through companies they control, Mr. Desmarais and Belgian financier Albert Frere own 50% of Pargesa.
