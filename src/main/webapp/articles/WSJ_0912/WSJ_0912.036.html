International: Auto Sales in Spain Decrease

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

MADRID -- Spanish auto sales in the first eight months of this year fell 7.5% to 722,097 units from 780,461 a year earlier, Spain's association of car makers said. In August, car sales eased 0.9% to 73,472 units from 74,170 in July.
