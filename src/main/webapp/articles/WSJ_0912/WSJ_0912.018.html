ATC Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

BATON ROUGE, La. --Advanced Telecommunications Corp. said it was awarded a three-year contract by the state of Louisiana for all state-controlled pay telephone services. The contract is valued at more than $25 million. ATC, a regional long-distance telephone company, said it will provide operator services from more than 2,000 pay phones servicing correctional institutions, state universities, hospitals, the departments of Transportation and Public Safety and other locations.
