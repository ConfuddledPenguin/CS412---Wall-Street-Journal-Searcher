Business Brief -- Information International Inc.: Temporary Plan Adopted Regarding Holder Rights

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

Information International Inc. said it adopted a temporary shareholder rights plan to give the company's board "time to investigate the impact on the company's business plan" of continuing purchases of company shares by certain holders. A spokesman for the company, based in Culver City, Calif., said officials weren't available to elaborate on the plan. The spokesman said he couldn't identify the holders who have been acquiring more stock.

Information International, a maker of computer software for the publishing business, among others, said that terms of the plan are are contained in a summary that will be mailed to shareholders. The company said the plan will be in effect until Nov. 15, unless extended.
