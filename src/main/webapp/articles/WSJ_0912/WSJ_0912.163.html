GOODYEAR TIRE & RUBBER Co. (Akron, Ohio) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it placed the credit ratings of this tire maker under review for possible downgrade, affecting about $900 million of long-term debt. The ratings agency said it is examining Goodyear's profit outlook and cash-flow protection for fixed charges, and is also focusing on an appropriate valuation of its crude-oil pipeline investment. Under review are Goodyear's senior debt, rated Baa-3, revenue bonds, rated Ba-1, and commercial paper, rated Prime-3.
