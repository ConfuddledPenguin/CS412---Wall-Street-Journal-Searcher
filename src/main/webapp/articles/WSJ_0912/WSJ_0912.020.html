Orders for Japanese Machinery

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

TOKYO -- Japanese machinery makers received orders totaling 1.814 trillion yen ($13.01 billion) in July, up 24% from a year earlier, the Economic Planning Agency said. Orders showed broad and substantial gains. Those from overseas gained 40% from a year earlier to 494.2 billion yen, following a 71% jump in the previous month. Compared with June, however, orders were down a seasonally adjusted 5.1%.
