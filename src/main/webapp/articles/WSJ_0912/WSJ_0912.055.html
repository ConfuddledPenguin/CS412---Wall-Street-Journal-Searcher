Business Brief -- Abitibi-Price Inc.: Paperworkers Union Ends Talks, as Strike Continues

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

Abitibi-Price Inc., a Toronto-based newsprint concern, said the Canadian Paperworkers Union broke off contract talks, as a strike at seven of the company's paper mills continued. An Abitibi official said the union ended negotiations in Toronto and indicated it will ask its members to vote on the company's latest offer. Union officials were traveling and couldn't be reached for comment.

Workers at most of Abitibi's mills in eastern Canada went on strike Monday, shutting down about 7% of North American newsprint capacity.

Separately, the Quebec & Ontario Paper Co. unit of Tribune Co., Chicago, said workers at its newsprint mill in Baie Comeau, Quebec, ratified a new three-year contract. The unit said a strike at its Thorold, Ontario, mill continued.
