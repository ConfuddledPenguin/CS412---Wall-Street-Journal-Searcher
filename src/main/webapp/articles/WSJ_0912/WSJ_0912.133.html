Business Brief -- Helmerich & Payne Inc.: Pretax Gain of $19.7 Million Is Seen From Debt Swap

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

Helmerich & Payne Inc. said exchange of the remainder of its 7 1/2% subordinated debentures will result in a $19.7 million pretax gain to be recorded in the current and following quarter. There are about $34 million of the debentures outstanding. The Tulsa, Okla., contract drilling concern said each $1,000 debenture is exchangeable for 17.316 shares of Sun Co. and 17.316 shares of Oryx Energy Co. common owned by Helmerich & Payne.

Helmerich & Payne said that because holders may exchange the debentures at any time prior to the official Oct. 12 redemption date, it anticipates exchanging a portion of the debentures during both the current fourth quarter, ending Sept. 30, and the first quarter, starting Oct. 1.
