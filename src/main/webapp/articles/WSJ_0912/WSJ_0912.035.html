Who's News: Welbilt Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

WELBILT Corp. (New Hyde Park, N.Y.) -- Marion Antonini, chairman of this maker of commercial food-service equipment, was named to the additional posts of president and chief executive officer, succeeding Richard Hirsch, who remains executive committee chairman. Mr. Antonini was previously chairman of KD Equities, a New York investment firm.
