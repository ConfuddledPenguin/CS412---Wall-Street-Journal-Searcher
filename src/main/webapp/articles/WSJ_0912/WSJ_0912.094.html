International Brief -- Fuji Heavy Industries Ltd.: Subaru Maker Won't Pay First-Half Interim Dividend

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

Fuji Heavy Industries Ltd., maker of Subaru automobiles, said that it won't be paying its previously planned interim dividend for the half-year period ending Sept. 30. Although unconsolidated sales are expected to see a healthy 10% increase in the half year from the prior period, the Tokyo-based company said that an operating loss will be likely because of cost increases and the expense of a sales promotion for both domestic and overseas markets.

The company paid a dividend totaling 8 yen (six cents) a share during the prior full fiscal year.

Fuji Heavy said it is in the process of restructuring operations in order to boost performance.
