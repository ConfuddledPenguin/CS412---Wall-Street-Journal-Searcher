Continental Airlines' Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

HOUSTON -- Continental Airlines, a unit of Continental Airlines Holdings, said August traffic rose 0.3% to 4.11 billion revenue passenger miles, from 4.10 billion revenue passenger miles in the year-earlier period. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. Load factor, or the percentage of available seats that are occupied, was 69.2% for the month, compared with 71.5% last year. For the year-to-date, Continental said it flew 26.67 billion revenue passenger miles, a 0.4% increase from 26.58 billion a year ago. Load factor fell to 61.8% from 61.9%.
