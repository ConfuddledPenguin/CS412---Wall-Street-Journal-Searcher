Business Brief -- Geo. A. Hormel & Co.: Gourmet America Inc. Acquired From Doskocil

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

Geo. A. Hormel & Co., Austin, Minn., said it acquired Gourmet America Inc., a small importer of such specialty food items as hors d'oeuvres, vinegars, mustards and sparkling waters. Gourmet America, based in Hingham, Mass., had been part of Wilson Foods Corp., a unit of Doskocil Cos. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed.

Hormel said its entry into the specialty-foods market is intended, in part, to use Gourmet America's distribution network for Hormel's meat products and other prepared foods, as well as to add gourmet items to its mail-order catalog business.
