Walgreen to Buy Assets of Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

DEERFIELD, Ill. -- Walgreen Co. said it agreed in principle to acquire the assets of Lee Drug Inc., a nine-unit drugstore chain in New Hampshire and Massachusetts. Terms weren't disclosed. Walgreen, which already operates 1,564 stores in 28 states and Puerto Rico, said it plans to open more than 100 units this year.
