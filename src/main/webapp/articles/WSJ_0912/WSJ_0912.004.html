MarCor Holders Approve Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/12/90

LAS VEGAS, Nev. -- MarCor Resorts Inc. said its shareholders approved the $36 million acquisition of interests in a limited partnership that would increase MarCor's stake to more than 90% from 34.9%. The limited partnership, MarCor Resorts L.P.V., which owns and operates the Rio Suite Hotel & Casino in Las Vegas, will receive equity in MarCor in return for the interests.

MarCor, which owns real-estate and gaming properties, has said it is disposing of its non-gaming assets. At its annual meeting Monday, Chairman Anthony A. Marnell II said that if the disposal of these assets isn't completed in 1990, the company expects to report a net loss for 1990.
