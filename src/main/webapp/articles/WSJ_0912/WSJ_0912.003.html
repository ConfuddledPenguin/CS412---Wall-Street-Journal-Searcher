Dutch Business Bankruptcies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/12/90

AMSTERDAM -- The number of business bankruptcies in the Netherlands in the first eight months of this year declined 4.5% to 2,351 from 2,461 a year earlier, the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics said. In August alone, 268 businesses failed, down 7.3% from 289 in August 1989.
