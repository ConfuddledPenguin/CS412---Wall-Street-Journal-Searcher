Business Brief -- Commodore International Ltd.: Number of Stores That Sell Amiga Rises to About 1,200

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

Commodore International Ltd. said it signed up some large retail chains, increasing to approximately 1,200 from 800 the number of stores carrying its Amiga personal computer. Commodore, West Chester, Pa., said the retailers include Montgomery Ward, Macy's of California, VideoConcepts, McDuff, Highland Appliance, Sun TV and Famous-Barr. Commodore added that it hopes to add more retailers by Nov. 15, in time for the Christmas season.

Adding stores has been a major focus for Commodore in the U.S., because, while the Amiga has long been considered an impressive machine, it has suffered from lack of shelf space.
