Who's News: Medstone International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Medstone International Inc. said Freeman Rose, 42 years old, resigned as chief executive officer, but will remain chairman. He was succeeded as chief executive by Richard Ferrari, 36, president and chief operating officer. David Radlinski, Medstone's chief financial officer, said Mr. Rose resigned the chief executive post "to pursue some other interests." Mr. Rose couldn't be reached for comment.

Medstone makes and markets medical services and equipment, mostly for treatment of kidney stones.
