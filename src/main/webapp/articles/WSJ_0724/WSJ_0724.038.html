Oryx Energy Co. Well

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

DALLAS -- Oryx Energy Co. said it made another discovery in its horizontal drilling program in the Pearsall field in South Texas. The well, the Lloyd W. Booth No. 1, tested at a daily rate of 1,697 barrels of oil and 744,000 cubic feet of natural gas through a 20/64-inch opening. Oryx said the well has a horizontal displacement of 4,204 feet and produces through intervals between 6,165 feet and 10,126 feet, which was the total depth. The well is about five miles north of Big Wells, Texas.
