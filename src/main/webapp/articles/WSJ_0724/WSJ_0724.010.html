Dauphin Deposit Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

HARRISBURG, Pa. -- Dauphin Deposit Corp. authorized the purchase of as many as 300,000 of its 12,714,914 shares outstanding. The banking corporation said it expects to use available cash for the purchase of the shares, which it would reissue for its employee stock purchase plan and stock option plan.
