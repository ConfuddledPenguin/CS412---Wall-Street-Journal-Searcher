Edisto Acquires ESCO

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

DALLAS -- Edisto Resources Corp. said it completed the acquisition of ESCO Energy Inc. for $9.2 million in cash and 1.3 million Edisto shares. Edisto also said it sold 282,800 shares on July 16 to institutional investors in a private placement. Edisto, an energy exploration and production company, now has 15.6 million shares outstanding.
