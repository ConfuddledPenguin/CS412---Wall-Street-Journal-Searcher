U.S. Steel Production Increased Last Week 1.4% to 1,847,000 Tons

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 1.4% last week to 1,847,000 tons from 1,821,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 0.2% from the 1,850,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 82.8% of its capability last week, compared with 81.6% the previous week and 83.3% a year ago.
