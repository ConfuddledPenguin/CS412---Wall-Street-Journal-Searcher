Who's News: Champion Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

CHAMPION ENTERPRISES Inc. (Dryden, Mich.) -- Clare G. Wentworth, group vice president of this holding company of home manufacturers and president of the company's Champion Motor Coach Inc., has resigned. The company said it doesn't know why Mr. Wentworth left his positions. Walter R. Young Jr., president and chief executive officer, will temporarily assume Mr. Wentworth's responsibilities.
