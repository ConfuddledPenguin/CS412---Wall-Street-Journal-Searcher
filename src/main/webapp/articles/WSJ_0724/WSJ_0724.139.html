Business Brief -- Mack Trucks Inc.: Renault Moves Step Closer To Acquiring Rest of Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

Renault Vehicules Industriels S.A. said it reached an agreement with some lenders of Mack Trucks Inc. to extend debt maturity and waive certain violations of debt covenants, satisfying one condition of the French concern's $6-a-share tender offer for the 55% of Mack it doesn't own. But Renault, a unit of state-owned Regie Nationale des Usines Renault, said Mack is continuing to negotiate a debt extension and waivers with another group of its lenders, led by Chemical Bank. The Chemical Bank syndicate agreed to extend the maturity date of Mack's debt to tomorrow, but Renault is seeking an extension until Dec. 31.

Chemical Bank declined to comment on the talks. Mack referred all questions to Renault.

Separately, Renault said the Department of Justice has asked it for additional information regarding Renault's proposal to form a joint venture between Mack, based in Allentown, Pa., and the truck division of Volvo AB, based in Sweden.
