Who's News: Banc One Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

BANC ONE Corp. (Columbus, Ohio) -- Joseph D. Barnette Jr. was elected president and chief executive officer of Banc One Indiana Corp., a unit of this bank holding company. Mr. Barnette continues as president and chief executive officer of Bank One, Indianapolis, a unit of Banc One Indiana Corp. Mr. Barnette succeeds as chief executive Frank E. McKinney Jr., who retired in January. The president post is new.
