Who's News: Fleet/Norstar Group Picks Three Officers To Be Vice Chairmen

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

PROVIDENCE, R.I. -- Fleet/Norstar Financial Group said it elected three executive vice presidents to the newly created position of vice chairman. Terrence Murray, 50 years old, continues as Fleet's chairman and chief executive officer.

Named vice chairman were Charles W. Carey, John W. Flynn and Robert L. Mushkin. They will relinquish their titles of executive vice president, a spokesman said.

Mr. Carey, 48, is responsible for Fleet's commercial banking and real estate activities, investment banking unit and its asset-based lending operations.

Mr. Flynn, 51, who is also chief financial officer, has responsibility for Fleet's public policy, legal, controllers, corporate communications and treasury functions; and Mr. Mushkin, 51, is responsible for the nine banks that make up Fleet's New England and New York state banking groups.

Fleet also said it expanded the responsiblities of four other executive vice presidents by giving them additional management duties.
