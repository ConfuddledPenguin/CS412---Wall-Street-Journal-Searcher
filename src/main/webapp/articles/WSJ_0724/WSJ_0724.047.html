Who's News: Standard Federal Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

STANDARD FEDERAL BANK (Troy, Mich.) -- Joseph E. Krul, 45 years old, was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this bank holding company. Clayton J. Trible Jr., 40, was named senior vice president and treasurer and Frederick A. Bartel, 37, was appointed vice president and assistant treasurer. Mr. Trible succeeds Jack Wood, who is retiring. Mr. Krul and Mr. Bartel are assuming new positions.
