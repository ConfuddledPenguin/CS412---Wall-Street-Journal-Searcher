  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

LOS ANGELES -- Kaufman & Broad Home Corp. and Catellus Development Corp., a unit of Santa Fe Pacific Corp., said they plan to develop jointly a $300 million residential community in Stockton, Calif. The 400-acre community will include up to 2,000 housing units as well as parks, schools, light commercial development and open space.
