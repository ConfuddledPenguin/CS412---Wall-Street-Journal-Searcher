Who's News: IMO Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

IMO INDUSTRIES Inc. (Lawrenceville, N.J.) -- Arthur E. Van Leuven, who is to retire at the end of November as executive vice president and a director of Transamerica Corp. and as chairman and chief executive officer of two of that company's units, was named a director of this supplier of analytical and optical instruments and other products to industrial and defense customers, increasing to five the number of outside directors on the seven-member board.
