Who's News: Halliburton Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

HALLIBURTON Co. (Dallas) -- W. Bernard Pieper was named chief executive officer of the Brown & Root Inc. unit of this oil services concern, effective Aug. 1. He succeeds T. Louis Austin Jr., who is retiring. Additionally, Tommy E. Knight was named executive vice president, operations, of the unit.
