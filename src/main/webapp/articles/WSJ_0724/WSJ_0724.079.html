Dividend News: PharmaControl Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

PHARMACONTROL Corp. declared a 1-for-8 reverse stock split. The Englewood Cliffs, N.J., maker of generic drugs and testing devices said the reverse split will be for shares of record Aug. 6. It said it will make a cash payment for fractional shares held on the record date. PharmaControl has about 10 million shares outstanding.
