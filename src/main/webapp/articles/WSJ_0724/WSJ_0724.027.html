Kodak to Expand Line of Gear

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Eastman Kodak Co., a photography, chemical, drug and office equipment concern, said it plans to introduce late next year a smaller, lower-cost model of its chemical analyzer for the health care industry. Kodak now produces an analyzer, capable of performing 750 tests an hour, that sells for about $180,000, and a unit that can perform 500 tests an hour and is priced at $110,000. The coming smaller model, capable of 250 tests hourly, hasn't been priced, but it will be less expensive than the larger machines, Kodak said.
