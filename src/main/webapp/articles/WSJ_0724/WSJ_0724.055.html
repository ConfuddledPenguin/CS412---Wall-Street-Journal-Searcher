Equifax Pact to Buy Telecredit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

ATLANTA -- Equifax Inc. and Telecredit Inc. said they signed a definitive agreement providing for the acquisition of Telecredit by Equifax in a stock swap valued at about $580 million. Equifax, which provides credit reports and similar information to retailers, banks and insurers, said the acquisition will be completed by this fall. The company said the transaction will be handled as a tax-free merger and each share of Telecredit will be converted into the right to receive 2.69 shares of Equifax common stock, subject to certain adjustments.

Telecredit guarantees personal checks and provides credit card and debit card processing services.
