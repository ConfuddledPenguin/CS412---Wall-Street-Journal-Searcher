Business Brief -- Liz Claiborne Inc.: Apparel Maker's Earnings Rose 21.2% in 2nd Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

Liz Claiborne Inc. said that continued strong performance at wholesale and retail levels helped its second-quarter earnings surge 21.2%. The New York big apparel maker said its second-quarter net was $34.9 million, or 40 cents a share, from $28.8 million, or 33 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales grew 25.3% to $340.3 million from $271.6 million in the 1989 second quarter.

In the first half of 1990, net income rose 19.8% to $87.5 million, or $1 share, from $73.1 million, or 83 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales climbed 22% to $761.1 million from $624.5 million a year earlier.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Liz Claiborne shares closed at $30.50, down 75 cents.
