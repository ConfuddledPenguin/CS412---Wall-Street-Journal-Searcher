Loral Wins Air Force Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

WASHINGTON -- Loral Corp. was given a $71.5 million Air Force contract for development and operation of air crew-training systems for MC-130 aircraft.

McDonnell Douglas Missile Systems Co., a unit of McDonnell Douglas Corp., was issued an $18.5 million combined contract for engineering of the Tomahawk weapon system.
