Howard Savings Posts Net Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

LIVINGSTON, N.J. -- Howard Savings Bank reported a second-quarter net loss of $15.7 million, or $1.09 a share, after adding $25 million to its allowance for possible loan losses. Howard is one of many thrifts that have been hurt by weak real estate markets in the Northeast. Howard's nonperforming loans climbed 25% from March 31 to stand at $503 million as of June 30.

The company had net income of $4.6 million, or 32 cents a share, in 1989's second quarter.
