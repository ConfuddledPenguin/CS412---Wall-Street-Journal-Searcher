Who's News: Detroit Edison Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

DETROIT EDISON Co. (Detroit) -- Larry G. Garberding, 51 years old, was named executive vice president, chief financial officer and a board member of this utility company. Mr. Garberding formerly was president of Nicor Inc., a natural gas utility serving suburban Chicago. Mr. Garberding assumed the position in Detroit formerly held by Ernest L. Grove Jr., who retired in September 1989.
