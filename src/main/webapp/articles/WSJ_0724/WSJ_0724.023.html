Tokos Medical FTC Subpoena

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

SANTA ANA, Calif. -- Tokos Medical Corp. said the Federal Trade Commission plans to issue a subpoena for certain information regarding Tokos's pending acquisition of Physiologic Diagnostic Service Inc. Tokos said last month that it agreed to acquire rival Physiologic Diagnostic for 2.7 million shares of Tokos common stock. Both firms provide specialized home health-care for pregnant women.
