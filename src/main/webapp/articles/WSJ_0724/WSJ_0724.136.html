Who's News: Son of De Benedetti Receives Top Position At Key Family Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

MILAN -- Rodolfo De Benedetti, the eldest son of Italian financier Carlo De Benedetti, was named general manager of Cie. Industriali Riunite S.p.A. (CIR), the family's key industrial holding company. The younger Mr. De Benedetti, 29 years old, will succeed Corrado Passera in this chief operating position at CIR, the company said. Mr. Passera resigned from CIR two weeks after he was named general manager of Arnoldo Mondadori Editore S.p.A., a publishing company controlled by CIR.

Rodolfo De Benedetti, who was general manager of the family financial holding concern Cofide S.p.A. and already a CIR boardmember, was appointed to his new post by CIR's board of directors Monday, the statement said.

Rodolfo De Benedetti has a degree in political economy and law from the University of Geneva. He started his career in finance in 1985 with Lombard Odier in Geneva, and in 1987 he worked with Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. in New York.

In 1988 he became director of international affairs at Cofide before he was appointed general manager in April 1989. He will leave his post at Cofide as a result of the new appointment. Mr. De Benedetti is married and has two children.
