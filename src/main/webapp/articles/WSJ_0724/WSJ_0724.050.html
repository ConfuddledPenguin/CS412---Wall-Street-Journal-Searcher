British Trade Deficit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

LONDON -- Britain's merchandise trade balance was in deficit by #1.56 billion ($2.84 billion) in June on a seasonally adjusted basis, compared with a deficit of #1.53 billion in May, while the current account was in deficit by #1.36 billion compared with a month-earlier deficit of #1.33 billion, the U.K. Central Statistical Office said.
