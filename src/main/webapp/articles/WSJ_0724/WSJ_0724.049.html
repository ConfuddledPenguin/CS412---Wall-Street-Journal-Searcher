Nippondenso Studies Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

TOKYO -- Japan's largest producer of electronic automotive parts, Nippondenso Co., confirmed that it is discussing a joint-production venture with Fiat Group to build air conditioners in Europe. A company spokesman said the two companies are studying a project to operate a factory together but haven't reached any agreement to do so. He denied a local newspaper report that Nippondenso and Fiat are near a decision to establish a venture.

Nippondenso is the second largest company in the Toyota Group, and sells parts to most auto producers in Japan.
