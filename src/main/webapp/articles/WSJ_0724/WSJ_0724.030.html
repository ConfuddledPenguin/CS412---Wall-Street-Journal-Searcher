Who's News: Fidelity New York Savings & Banking

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

FIDELITY NEW YORK SAVINGS & BANKING (Floral Park, N.Y.) -- Thomas V. Powderly, 52 years old, was named president and chief operating officer at this federally chartered savings bank. Mr. Powderly, previously executive vice president, succeeds Bruno Greco, 63, who was named vice chairman.
