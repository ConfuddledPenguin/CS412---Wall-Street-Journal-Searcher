Business Brief -- EG&G Inc.: Technology Group Receives Offer from Tokyo Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

EG&G Inc., Wellesley, Mass., said it received an unsolicited offer from NOK Corp. of Tokyo to purchase its Fluid Components Technology Group. The scientific and technical products concern didn't disclose terms of the offer. John M. Kucharski, company chairman and chief executive officer, said he plans to request that EG&G's board of directors enter negotiations with NOK July 25.

If consummated, the transaction would result in a substantial gain to EG&G, the company said, adding that "we would like to have closing by year-end, but any number of things could happen."

EG&G's fluid components group of Warwick, R.I., contributed about $180 million in revenue in 1989, the company said, while EG&G had total sales of $1.65 billion.
