Business Brief -- Lyondell Petrochemical Co.: Firm Boosts Capital Budget On Strong Earnings Report

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

Lyondell Petrochemical Co. said it is boosting its 1990 capital-spending budget by $20 million, to $168 million, in line with strong year-to-date performance. The Houston petrochemicals manufacturer reported second-quarter net income of $155 million, or $1.93 a share, compared with $103 million, or $1.29 a share, in the year-earlier period. Revenue was $1.40 billion, compared with $1.49 billion a year earlier.

Net for the six months was $233 million, or $2.91 a share, compared with $237 million, or $2.96 a share, a year earlier. Revenue was $2.89 billion compared with $2.75 billion.

The company said the increase in its capital-spending budget will facilitate increased production capacity and expansions in certain areas.
