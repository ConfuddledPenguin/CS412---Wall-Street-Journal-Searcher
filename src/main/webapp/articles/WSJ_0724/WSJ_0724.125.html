Who's News: Shearson Will Name Two Top Executives Of Consulting Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

NEW YORK -- Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. plans to name Leonard Reinhart president and Donald Robinson executive vice president of a new consulting group, which will recommend investment managers to the brokerage firm's clients. Mr. Reinhart, 35 years old, and Mr. Robinson, 32, have been senior vice president and first vice president, respectively, at Shearson's consulting-services division. The business that the two executives will lead has been part of consulting services; it will get a higher profile as an independent entity, Mr. Reinhart said.

The group "evaluates money managers {who are} independent of Shearson and recommends them to our clients," Mr. Reinhart said. "We determine the risk you're willing to take and what your goals are, and we match you up with an investment manager."
