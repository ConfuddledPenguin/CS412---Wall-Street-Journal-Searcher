Who's News: Bear, Stearns & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/23/90

BEAR, STEARNS & CO. (New York) -- Lewis Sachs was named fixed-income syndicate desk manager at this banking and brokerage subsidiary of Bear Stearns Cos., a holding company. Mr. Sachs, 26 years old, succeeds Craig Sanger, who left the company. Previously a member of the syndicate capital markets desk, Mr. Sachs assumes responsibility for coordinating underwriters and issuing clients in the debt market.
