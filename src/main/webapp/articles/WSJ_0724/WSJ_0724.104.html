Business Brief -- Chicago Milwaukee Corp.: Board Authorizes Buy-Back Of Stock in Open Market

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

Chicago Milwaukee Corp. said its board authorized the purchase of up to $30 million of the company's outstanding common stock and up to $15 million of its outstanding preferred stock. The Chicago-based holding company said the purchases will be made from time to time in the open market, subject to availability, at prices it deems appropriate. A spokesman for the company said shares purchased will be held in its treasury and that it hasn't any specific plans for the stock.

Chicago Milwaukee currently has about 2.1 million common shares and 317,629 shares of preferred stock outstanding.
