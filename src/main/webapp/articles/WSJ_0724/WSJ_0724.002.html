Money Rates

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

Monday, July 23, 1990

The key U.S. and foreign annual interest rates below are a guide to general levels but don't always represent actual transactions.

PRIME RATE: 10%. The base rate on corporate loans at large U.S. money center commercial banks.

FEDERAL FUNDS: 8 1/8% high, 7 15/16% low, 8 1/16% near closing bid, 8 1/8% offered. Reserves traded among commercial banks for overnight use in amounts of $1 million or more. Source: Babcock Fulton Prebon (U.S.A.) Inc.

DISCOUNT RATE: 7%. The charge on loans to depository institutions by the New York Federal Reserve Bank.

CALL MONEY: 9 1/4%. The charge on loans to brokers on stock exchange collateral.

COMMERCIAL PAPER placed directly by General Motors Acceptance Corp.: 7.875% 30 to 59 days; 7.825% 60 to 89 days; 7.75% 90 to 119 days; 7.70% 120 to 149 days; 7.65% 150 to 179 days; 7.55% 180 to 270 days.

COMMERCIAL PAPER: High-grade unsecured notes sold through dealers by major corporations in multiples of $1,000: 7.95% 30 days; 7.90% 60 days; 7.88% 90 days.

CERTIFICATES OF DEPOSIT: 7.67% one month; 7.71% two months; 7.73% three months; 7.89% six months; 8.10% one year. Average of top rates paid by major New York banks on primary new issues of negotiable C.D.s, usually on amounts of $1 million and more. The minimum unit is $100,000. Typical rates in the secondary market: 8% one month; 8% three months; 8% six months.

BANKERS ACCEPTANCES: 7.87% 30 days; 7.80% 60 days; 7.75% 90 days; 7.70% 120 days; 7.66% 150 days; 7.62% 180 days. Negotiable, bank-backed business credit instruments typically financing an import order.

LONDON LATE EURODOLLARS: 8 1/8% - 8% one month; 8 1/8% - 8% two months; 8 1/8% - 8% three months; 8 1/8% - 8% four months; 8 1/8% - 8% five months; 8 3/16% - 8 1/16% six months.

LONDON INTERBANK OFFERED RATES (LIBOR): 8 1/8% one month; 8 1/8% three months; 8 3/16% six months; 8 5/16% one year. The average of interbank offered rates for dollar deposits in the London market based on quotations at five major banks.

FOREIGN PRIME RATES: Canada 14.75%; Germany 10.50%; Japan 7.13%; Switzerland 11.25%; Britain 15%. These rate indications aren't directly comparable; lending practices vary widely by location.

TREASURY BILLS: Results of the Monday, July 23, 1990, auction of short-term U.S. government bills, sold at a discount from face value in units of $10,000 to $1 million: 7.49%, 13 weeks; 7.40%, 26 weeks.

FEDERAL HOME LOAN MORTGAGE CORP. (Freddie Mac): Posted yields on 30-year mortgage commitments for delivery within 30 days. 9.90%, standard conventional fixed-rate mortgages; 8.375% rate capped one-year adjustable rate mortgages. Source: Telerate Systems Inc.

FEDERAL NATIONAL MORTGAGE ASSOCIATION (Fannie Mae): Posted yields on 30-year mortgage commitments for delivery within 30 days (priced at par). 9.85%, standard conventional fixed-rate mortgages; 8.50%, 6/2 rate capped one-year adjustable rate mortgages. Source: Telerate Systems Inc.

MERRILL LYNCH READY ASSETS TRUST: 7.76%. Annualized average rate of return after expenses for the past 30 days; not a forecast of future returns.
