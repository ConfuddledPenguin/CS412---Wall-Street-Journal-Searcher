Business Brief -- Syntech International Inc.: Group Raises Stake in Firm, Says It Plans No Takeover

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

An investor group said it raised its stake in Syntech International Inc. to 11.2% from 8.2% of the common outstanding but maintained the purchase wasn't a prelude to a takeover of the Reno, Nev., company. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the investor group, which includes John Simmet and his closely held company, Simcote Inc., St. Paul, Minn., said it holds 652,000 Syntech shares, including 265,000 shares purchased between May 24 and last Thursday.

"I have no intentions," Mr. Simmet said from his home in Mendota Heights, Minn. "It's basically a fun thing. It's an investment." Syntech makes transaction-processing systems used in government-sponsored lotteries.
