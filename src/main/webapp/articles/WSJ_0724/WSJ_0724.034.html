Fluor Unit Awarded Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp. said its Fluor Daniel subsidiary was awarded a contract valued at about $300 million by Doswell L.P., a subsidiary of Los Angeles-based Diamond Energy Inc., to build a power plant in Hanover County, Va. Fluor will provide engineering and construction services for the 663,000-kilowatt power plant. Engineering work has already begun at Fluor's Southern California offices. Fluor said the plant, scheduled to be completed in 1992, will be the biggest independent power producer in the nation. Fluor Daniel, Fluor Corp.'s principal subsidiary, is an international engineering construction, maintenance and technical services company. Diamond Energy is a subsidiary of Mitsubishi Corp. of Japan.
