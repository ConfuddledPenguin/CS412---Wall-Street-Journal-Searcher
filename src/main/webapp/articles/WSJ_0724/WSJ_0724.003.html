Who's News: Pentair Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

PENTAIR Inc. (St. Paul, Minn.) -- Winslow H. Buxton, 51-year-old former vice president of the company's paper group, was named president and to the vacant post of chief operating officer of this maker of paper and power tools. He succeeds D. Eugene Nugent, 62, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer of the company. Mr. Buxton was also named a director, increasing board membership to nine.
