Blue Circle Industries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

LONDON -- Blue Circle Industries PLC, a British building materials manufacturer, said it agreed to buy the Italian bathroom hardware manufacturer Ceramica Dolomite S.p.A. for #36.5 million ($66.3 million). Blue Circle said the move is in line with its bid to expand in Europe. About 75% of Ceramica Dolomite's sales are in Italy, with the balance made elsewhere on the continent.
