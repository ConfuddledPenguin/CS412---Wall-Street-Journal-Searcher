Japanese Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

TOKYO -- Two of Japan's major trading houses, C. Itoh & Co. and Mitsubishi Corp., and Industrial Bank of Japan Ltd. plan to join hands in developing an industrial park in Dalian, China, C. Itoh officials said. Preparing the land for the industrial park alone will cost between 20 billion yen ($134.4 million) and 30 billion yen, officials said.
