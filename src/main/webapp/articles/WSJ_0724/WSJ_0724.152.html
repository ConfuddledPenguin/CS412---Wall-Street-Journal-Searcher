LIN Gets Financing To Complete Purchase From Metromedia Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

NEW YORK -- LIN Broadcasting Corp. said it received sufficient financing to complete its $1.9 billion purchase of a 46% stake in Metro One, a cellular-phone system here, from Metromedia Co. McCaw Cellular Communications Inc., a Kirkland, Wash., cellular-phone operator, holds 52% of LIN. LIN, which operates cellular systems and has broadcasting and publishing interests, raised a total $2.1 billion from two groups of banks.

Terms of the purchase call for Metromedia to receive $1.05 billion cash and $850 million of preferred stock in a LIN unit. The transaction, which LIN expects to close in August, would give LIN 93% of Metro One; other investors hold 7%.

A spokesman said LIN would use the remaining financing for general purposes.

Metromedia, New York, has telecommunications and other interests.
