Figgie International Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

WILLOUGHBY, Ohio -- Figgie International Inc. said that its Waite Hill Holdings insurance unit has acquired Hamilton Insurance Co., a Washington, D.C.-based carrier specializing in nonstandard personal lines automobile insurance. Terms weren't disclosed. Figgie sells fire protection systems, sporting goods, industrial machinery, electronic products and other items. Its Waite Hill unit, based in Richmond, Va., already owns two specialty property/casualty insurance companies.
