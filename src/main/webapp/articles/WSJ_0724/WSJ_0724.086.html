Dividend News: National Convenience Stores Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

NATIONAL CONVENIENCE STORES Inc. said its board voted to suspend the quarterly dividend on its common stock. The company's most recent quarterly dividend was eight cents a share, paid May 4. The convenience-store operator said that preliminary, unaudited results for its fiscal year ended June 30 show that its pretax operating loss has narrowed from a year earlier, but the improved performance "is not sufficient for the company to retain its common stock dividend." In fiscal 1989, the company posted a pretax operating loss of about $14.5 million, said J.C. Brewster, senior vice president, finance. The net loss was $8.9 million. After a gain on the sale of assets and an extraordinary credit from a debt swap, Mr. Brewster said, National Convenience will report a net profit for fiscal 1990.
