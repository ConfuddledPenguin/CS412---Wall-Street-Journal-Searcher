Who's News: Electro Rent Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

ELECTRO RENT Corp. (Van Nuys, Calif.) -- Craig R. Jones, the 44-year-old treasurer, was promoted to vice president and chief financial officer. He succeeds William Weitzman, 51, who continues as president and chief operating officer. The position of treasurer remains vacant. The company is involved in short-term rental and leasing of general purpose and telecommunications electronic test equipment, personal computers and engineering workstations.
