Ross Stores Expansion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

NEWARK, Calif. -- Ross Stores Inc., the discount apparel retailer, said that it will enter its first new major market since 1986, in the Philadelphia and southern New Jersey region. In addition, the company will open eight Ross "Dress For Less" stores Sunday, part of an expansion plan to add 30 new stores in 1990. With the opening of the eight stores, Ross said it will operate 169 stores in 16 states.
