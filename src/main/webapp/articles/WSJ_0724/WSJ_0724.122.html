Business Brief -- Union Bank of Switzerland: Earnings in Second Quarter Topped Year-Earlier Figure

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/24/90

Union Bank of Switzerland (UBS), the country's largest commercial bank, said earnings in the second quarter improved from the year-ago period, but were "still insufficient to offset the shortfall in earnings carried over from the previous quarter." In keeping with the practice of Swiss companies, UBS didn't provide specific earnings figures for the second quarter.

UBS said assets in the second quarter climbed to 181.3 billion francs ($129 billion) from 178.8 billion francs at the end of the first quarter, and were up from 176.1 billion francs at the end of 1989.
