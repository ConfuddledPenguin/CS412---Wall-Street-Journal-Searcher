Idemitsu Kosan Unit Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/24/90

TOKYO -- Idemitsu Petrochemical Co. and Repsol Quimica S.A. have agreed to a joint venture to produce polystyrene in Spain, an Idemitsu official said Monday. The venture will be owned equally by the two parents. Idemitsu Petrochemical is a subsidiary of Idemitsu Kosan Co., a major privately held oil company. Repsol Quimica is a unit of Repsol S.A., the Spanish state oil company.

The venture is due to begin production in the second half of 1992 at an output of 50,000 tons a year, the official said.

Idemitsu expects to sell a large part of the production to Japanese household appliance makers operating in Europe, according to the official. Polystyrene is the basic material of the shells of most appliances.
