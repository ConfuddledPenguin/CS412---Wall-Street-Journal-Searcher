International Brief -- Buehrmann-Tetterode: Dutch Company Purchases 2 U.S. Office Supply Firms

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

Dutch paper and packaging group Buehrmann-Tetterode N.V. said it has acquired two U.S. office supply companies through its subsidiary BT-USA Inc. The two companies, Bushart Office Products Inc. and E.W. Curry Co., have combined annual sales of about $55 million, according to Buehrmann-Tetterode.

The acquisitions will be integrated with three other U.S. office supply companies that Buehrmann-Tetterode has purchased since 1987.

Total U.S. sales by the group should reach $300 million annually, Buehrmann-Tetterode said.
