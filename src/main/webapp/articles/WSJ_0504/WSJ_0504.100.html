International Brief -- Saint-Gobain: Concern to Buy Glass Unit From South Africa's PGSI

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

Cie. de Saint-Gobain said it agreed to buy the British glass group Solaglas Ltd. for #96.5 million ($158.5 million) from its South African parent PGSI. The French glass and building materials group said the agreement also calls for it to sell its European automotive glass repair businesses to PGSI's Autoglass group unit for #16 million.

Solaglas generated revenue of about #173 million for the fiscal year ended March 31, mainly from its operations in flat glass processing, glazing, tempered glass, laminated glass, mirrors and double glazing.

Saint-Gobain's auto glass repair businesses, based in France and Germany, had 1989 revenue equivalent to #17 million.

Both transactions are subject to regulatory approval.
