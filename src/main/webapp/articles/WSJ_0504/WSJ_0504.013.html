Who's News: Bemis Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

BEMIS Co. (Minneapolis) -- John H. Roe, 50-year-old president and chief operating officer, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer of this packaging and graphics products concern, succeeding Howard Curler, 65, who continues as chairman. Mr. Roe relinquishes the title of chief operating officer. Also, Lawrence E. Schwanke, formerly director of personnel and industrial relations, was named to the new post of vice president, human resources.
