Papua New Guinea Oil Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

SYDNEY, Australia -- A joint venture of international oil companies has applied for a license to develop oil fields in Papua New Guinea. The group plans to start producing in mid-1992 at the rate of about 90,000 barrels a day. The group includes Chevron Corp. of the U.S., operator of the project, and British Petroleum Co. Each has a 25% stake.
