Business Brief -- Copperweld Corp.: Procedural Reasons Delay Meeting to Vote on Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

Copperweld Corp. said the special meeting to vote on the proposed merger of Copperweld into Imetal, a French concern that already controls Copperweld, will be delayed for procedural reasons. The meeting was set for June 6 but will be postponed until early July, the Pittsburgh company said. Copperweld said it won't have required materials from the Securities and Exchange Commission until late this month, which wouldn't allow time to mail proxy materials for a June 6 meeting.

Copperweld is a producer of wire, tubing and other specialized metal products.
