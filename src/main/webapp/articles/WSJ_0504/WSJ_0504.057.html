Who's News: Summagraphics Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

SUMMAGRAPHICS Corp. (Seymour, Conn.) -- Two vice presidents were named senior vice presidents in connection with the acquisition of the Houston Instrument division of Ametek Inc. William J. Greene became senior vice president, chief financial officer and treasurer. Robert B. Sims became senior vice president, secretary and general counsel. In addition, Paul D. Smith, senior vice president, chief technical officer, will now supervise all technical aspects of four newly created divisions of the computer peripheral products maker.
