Who's News: Culbro Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

CULBRO Corp. -- M.J. "Skip" Beebe joined Culbro as president and chief executive officer of this New York-based wholesale-tobacco distributor's Culbro Land Resources operation, based in Windsor, Conn. Mr. Beebe previously was president of Wilma Southeast Inc., commercial real estate developers in Atlanta.
