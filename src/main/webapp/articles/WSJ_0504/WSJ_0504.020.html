Northwest Air's April Traffic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- Northwest Airlines said April traffic rose 16% to 3.89 billion revenue passenger miles from 3.37 billion revenue passenger miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The NWA Inc. unit said load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, dropped to 61.9% from 62.9%.

For the first four months of 1990, Northwest said it flew 15.65 billion revenue passenger miles, up 21% from 12.97 billion miles. Load factor was 63.7%, up from 61.8%.
