Steel Shipment Drop Forecast

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

PARIS -- Continued weakness in the U.S. steel market could lead to a drop of more than 7% in steel shipments in 1990 compared with 1989, the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development said. At the same time, weak prices in the U.S. and world markets generally, together with lower production levels and higher costs, "may present some threat to continued profitable operations in the U.S. industry in 1990."

The group said the downturn in U.S. steel demand at the end of 1989 has continued through the 1990 first quarter, although the OECD said the drop "has not yet become severe."
