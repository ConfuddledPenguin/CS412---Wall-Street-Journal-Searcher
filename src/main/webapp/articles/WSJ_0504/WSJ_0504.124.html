International Brief -- British & Commonwealth Holdings: Concern Is Seeking Buyers Of Assets to Reduce Debt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

U.K. financial services group British & Commonwealth Holdings PLC said it is seeking buyers for its core merchant banking and related financial services operations. The concern disclosed last month that massive losses at its Atlantic Computers unit would require it to begin disposing of assets in a bid to reduce group indebtedness and stay in operation. The company said S.G. Warburg & Co., the British merchant bank, has been hired to find buyers.

The company said a number of potential purchasers have already expressed interest.

Officials declined to say how much they expected to generate from the sales, or when deals would be completed.
