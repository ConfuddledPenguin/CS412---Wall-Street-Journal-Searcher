Arrow Automotive Industries

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- Arrow Automotive Industries Inc. said it obtained credit approval and commitments for replacement financing for its working capital and real-estate loans. The rebuilder of auto replacement parts had been seeking replacement financing for about $21 million in credit facilities because of problems at its lead lender, Bank of New England, Boston.

It declined to disclose details of the financing or name the lenders.
