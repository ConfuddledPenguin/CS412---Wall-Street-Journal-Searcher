Phoenix Re 1st-Quarter Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

NEW YORK -- Phoenix Re Corp. reported a first-quarter loss and declining revenue, due mostly to catastrophic events in Europe. The loss was $1.9 million, or 48 cents a share, compared with profit of $1.5 million, or 37 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue for the quarter declined 15% to $8.3 million from $9.8 million a year ago.

The loss stemmed primarily from three European storms and a major hailstorm in Sydney, Australia. Phoenix, which provides reinsurance products and services internationally, said it had set aside a $4 million loss reserve for claims arising from these events.
