Identity Crisis

The mail-delivery system

Should be altered at all cost;

"Occupant" letters reach me,

While those with my name get lost.

-- Mimi Kay.

Homemade Threat?

With endless meetings and paper work,

Keeping corporate profit margins thin,

Our business problems aren't found abroad,

But boring from within.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
