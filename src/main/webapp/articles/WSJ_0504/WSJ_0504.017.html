Colt Holdings Inc. Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

NEW YORK -- Colt Industries Inc. said it changed its name to Coltec Industries Inc., effective yesterday, reflecting the company's sale of its firearms business March 22, a spokesman said. Excluding the firearms business, which accounted for only 6% of the company's $1.61 billion in sales last year, Coltec now has businesses in aerospace, automotive and other industrial products.

Coltec currently is owned by Colt Holdings Inc., which took Colt Industries private in June 1988.
