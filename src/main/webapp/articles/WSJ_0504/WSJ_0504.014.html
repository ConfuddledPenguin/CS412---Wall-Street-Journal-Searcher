Integra Financial's New Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

PITTSBURGH -- Integra Financial Corp. said it formed a new commercial leasing subsidiary. The unit, Integra Business Credit Co., will offer businesses a way to finance acquisition of equipment ranging in value from $50,000 to $5 million, the company said.

Integra is a financial holding company with subsidiaries in banking, insurance, brokerage and other businesses.
