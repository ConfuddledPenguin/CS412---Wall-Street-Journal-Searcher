United Air Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

CHICAGO -- United Airlines, a unit of UAL Corp., said its April traffic rose 7.7% to 5.82 billion revenue passenger miles from 5.40 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, slipped to 63.8% from 64%.

For the first four months of 1990, the airline said it flew 22.89 billion revenue passenger miles, up 6.7% from 21.44 billion a year earlier. The load factor rose to 64.6% from 63.6%.
