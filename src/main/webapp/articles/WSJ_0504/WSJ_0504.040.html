Noland's April Sales Fell 4.3%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

NEWPORT NEWS, Va. -- Noland Co. said April sales were $35.3 million, down 4.3% from $36.9 million a year ago. The wholesale distribution company said sales for the first four months of 1990 were $140.9 million, down slightly from $141.8 million.
