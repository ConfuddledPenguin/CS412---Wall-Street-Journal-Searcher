Bindley Western Industries

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

INDIANAPOLIS -- Bindley Western Industries Inc. said it reached an agreement with Peyton Distributors, a unit of Kroger Co., to be the primary supplier of pharmaceuticals to Peyton. The drug wholesaler said the agreement, effective last Tuesday, is expected to add revenue of more than $150 million annually. In 1989, Bindley had revenue of $1.56 billion.

Bindley also said that as a result of its strategies to improve performance and the continued growth of pharmaceutical sales, it is "comfortable with {Wall} Street expectations" for the current year. The company said analysts are currently forecasting per-share earnings of 85 cents for 1990, compared with 41 cents in 1989.
