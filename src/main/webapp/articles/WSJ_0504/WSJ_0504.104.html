Genzyme Unit's Initial Offering

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- IG Laboratories Inc., a unit of Genzyme Corp., said it is offering 1,875,000 common shares in its initial public offering at $7.50 a share. All of the shares are being offered by the company. Upon completion of the offering, IG Laboratories will have about 5.1 million shares outstanding and will be 56% owned by Genzyme, a Boston-based maker of health-care products. IG Labs provides genetic testing services to hospitals and other health-care providers.

Oppenheimer & Co. is the managing underwriter of the offering.
