Venezuela's Inflation Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

CARACAS, Venezuela -- Prices rose 2.6% in April, bringing the year's accumulated inflation rate to 9%, the Venezuelan Central Bank announced. Inflation came to 1.8% in March. The government estimates 1990 inflation at slightly below 30%, compared with 80.7% last year, when stringent economic reforms were implemented.
