Business Brief -- Tucson Electric Power Co.: Pima County Authority Issues $20 Million in Bonds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

Tucson Electric Power Co., Tucson Ariz., said the Industrial Development Authority of Pima County issued $20 million in tax-exempt bonds on behalf of the utility. The variable-rate bonds, issued via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets, have an initial interest rate of 7%, and final maturity is 2025. Proceeds initially were used to acquire a guaranteed investment contract, which provides credit enhancement until it is replaced by a letter of credit the utility is negotiating. Tucson Electric said it expects to have a $350 million master credit agreement in place by the end of June. After that, the bond proceeds will be used to pay construction costs of distribution facilities.
