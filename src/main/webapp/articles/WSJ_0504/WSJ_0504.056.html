BizMart 4th-Quarter Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

ARLINGTON, Texas -- BizMart Inc. said its sales for the fourth quarter ended April 28 more than tripled to about $45.1 million, from $14.8 million a year ago. The office-products chain, which now has 32 stores, said its sales for the fiscal year were about $132.8 million, up from $33.5 million in fiscal 1989. BizMart opened its first store in May 1988.

The company said it expects to have been profitable in the fourth quarter, despite store-opening costs, poor weather conditions and increased competition. But it said it will post a net loss for the fiscal year when it reports its results in mid-June.
