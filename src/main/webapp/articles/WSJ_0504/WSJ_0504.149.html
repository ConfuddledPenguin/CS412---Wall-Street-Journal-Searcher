CMS Enhancements Stock Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

TUSTIN, Calif. -- CMS Enhancements Inc. said directors approved a plan under which the company would buy back as much as nearly 5% of its common stock outstanding. CMS, a maker and distributor of a broad line of enhancement products for personal computers, said it feels its stock is undervalued. In December, in an effort to boost the stock price and attract institutional investors, CMS holders approved a 1-for-4 stock split, reducing shares outstanding to about 3.2 million from about 12.6 million.

On Monday, CMS said third-quarter earnings fell 62% to 13 cents a share.
