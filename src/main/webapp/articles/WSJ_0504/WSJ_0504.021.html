Singapore's Trade Gap Narrows

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

SINGAPORE -- Singapore's trade deficit narrowed to $679.1 million Singapore (US$362.6 million) in March from $1.83 billion Singapore in February, and was narrower than the gap of $1.07 billion Singapore in March 1989. March imports rose to $9.27 billion Singapore from $8.41 billion Singapore in February, and were up from $8.49 billion Singapore in March 1989.

Exports rose to $8.59 billion Singapore from $6.58 billion Singapore in February and $7.42 billion in March 1989.
