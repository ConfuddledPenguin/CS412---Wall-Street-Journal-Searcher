Alaska Air Group Unit's Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

SEATTLE -- Alaska Airlines said April traffic increased 16% to 322 million revenue passenger miles from 278.5 million in the year-ago month. The passenger load factor, the percentage of available seats occupied by fare-paying passengers, declined slightly to 49.6% from 50.9%. For the first four months of 1990, the Alaska Air Group Inc. unit flew 1.28 billion revenue passenger miles, up 18% from 1.08 billion in the same period a year ago. The passenger load factor dropped to 49.1% from 49.8%.
