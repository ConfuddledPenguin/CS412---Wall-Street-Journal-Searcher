Who's News: Melville Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

MELVILLE Corp. (Rye, N.Y.) -- This specialty retail concern named Ronald S. Staffieri president of its Kay-Bee Toy & Hobby Shops subsidiary, succeeding Saul Rubenstein, who retired late last year. Mr. Staffieri was previously president of Things Remembered Inc., a unit of Cole National Corp.
