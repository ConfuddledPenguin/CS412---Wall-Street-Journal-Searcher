URS Corp. Is Closing Its Software Business, Mitchell Management

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- URS Corp. said it is closing its software unit, Mitchell Management Systems Inc. in Westborough, Mass., resulting in a $7.5 million loss from discontinued operations in its fiscal second quarter ended April 30. The closing puts URS in violation of certain covenants of its credit agreement with Wells Fargo Bank, but the bank has given URS a 90-day forbearance while discussions concerning the breach take place. The company said the closing won't affect its ability to pay back debt, because future cash flow will be strengthened. Mitchell contributed 8% of URS's fiscal 1989 revenue of $99.5 million, and reported pretax losses of $1.4 million. In the first half of the current fiscal year, the unit's losses accelerated to $3.3 million, according to URS, an engineering and architectural consulting concern.
