Who's News: Merrill Lynch & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

MERRILL LYNCH & Co. (New York) -- Eugene H. Rotberg will resign as an executive vice president of this brokerage firm, effective July 31. Mr. Rotberg, formerly vice president and treasurer of the World Bank for 19 years, said he will devote time to teaching, to international public policy and to identifying investment opportunities in developing countries. The position will be left vacant, the firm said.
