  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

CAPITOL BANCORP. (Boston) -- This bank holding company added four directors to its board, expanding it to 15 members: Phillip Ciolfi, president of Selecterm Inc., Watertown, Mass.; Harold Gold, president of Graphic Leasing Corp., Waltham, Mass.; Donato Pizzuti, a private real estate developer in Boston; and Paul Roiff, president of Heath Management Co., also of Boston.
