Who's News: American Water Works Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

AMERICAN WATER WORKS Co. (Voorhees, N.J.) -- William O. Albertini, president of Bell Atlantic Enterprises Corp., and Paul W. Ware, president of Penn Fuel Gas Inc., were elected directors of this utility holding company. They succeed Samuel H. Ballam Jr., who reached the mandatory retirement age of 70, and John H. Ware 4th, who resigned to pursue other interests.
