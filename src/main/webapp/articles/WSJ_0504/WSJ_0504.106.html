Who's News: Ionics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/04/90

IONICS Inc. (Watertown, Mass.) -- Arthur L. Goldstein, 54-year-old president and chief executive officer of this maker of water-purification equipment, was named to the additional post of chairman. He succeeds Averell E. Daniell, 75, who was named chairman emeritus.
