West German Industry Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/04/90

BONN -- West Germany's industrial production dropped a seasonally adjusted 0.7% in March from February but was up 3.7% from a year earlier, the Bonn Economics Ministry said. It added, however, that in a later update, an upward revision of the monthly data by as much as 1.5 percentage points can be expected.
