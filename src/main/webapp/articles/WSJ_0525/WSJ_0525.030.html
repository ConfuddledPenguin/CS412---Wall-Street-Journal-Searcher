Italian Balance of Payments

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

ROME -- Italy's balance of payments surplus rose marginally to 2.232 trillion lire ($1.81 billion) in April from the 2.202 trillion lira surplus recorded a year earlier, the Bank of Italy said. The April surplus was sharply lower, however, than the 4.190 trillion lira surplus reported in March.

The central bank also reported that the balance of payments showed a surplus of 8.184 trillion lire in the first four months of 1990, compared with a surplus of 9.188 trillion lire in the year-earlier period.

The central bank said the balance of payments surplus was the result of a net capital outflow through the banking system of 1.148 trillion lire as well as inflows totaling 6.899 trillion lire in the form of international investments and loans.

All figures are provisional and aren't seasonally adjusted.
