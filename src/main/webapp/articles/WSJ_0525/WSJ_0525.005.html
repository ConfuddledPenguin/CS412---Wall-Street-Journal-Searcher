The Cellular Sell

("More than two million mobile phones are now humming away on the nation's highways." -- WSJ Labor Letter)

Then at noon, over meals;

Later, out at the club, it was par for the course --

But now it's "Deals on Wheels."

Getting It Together

Please don't send

Any gifts to me

That need "Part A" Attached to "Part B."

Just say to the clerk,

When my birthday comes,

"Something simple, assembled,

For a man with ten thumbs."

-- Lionel M. Kaufman.

Paws for Thought

That polar bears will mate at all,

And what is more, repeat,

Is quite a thing, considering

Both parties have cold feet.

-- Dick Emmons.
