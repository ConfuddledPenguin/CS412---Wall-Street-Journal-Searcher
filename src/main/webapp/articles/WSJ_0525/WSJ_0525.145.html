Business Brief -- Chrysler Corp.: Canadian Unit Will Spend $253.2 Million on Facility

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

Chrysler Canada Ltd., said it plans to spend 300 million Canadian dollars ($253.2 million) at its Windsor, Ontario, minivan plant. The Toronto-based company said C$21 million will be spent in plant improvements at the facility, with the remaining C$280 million to be spent on product development. The plant will build the redesigned 1991 minivan.

The Canadian unit of Chrysler Corp. also said its Pillette Road assembly plant in Windsor has "a good future." There had been some talk of the plant's being shut down. The plant is Chrysler's only source of large, rear-wheel-drive commercial vans and wagons.

Chrylser Canada said it will invest more than C$100 million in new equipment and plant improvements at the Pillette plant over the next three years.
