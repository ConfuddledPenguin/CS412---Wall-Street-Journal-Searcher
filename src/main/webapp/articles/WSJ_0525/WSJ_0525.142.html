Business Brief -- Georgia-Pacific Corp.: Paper Mill in Reading, Pa., Will Be Closed on July 24

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

Georgia-Pacific Corp., an Atlanta-based forest products concern, said it will close its paper mill in Reading, Pa., on July 24, idling about 100 workers. The company said the mill has become unprofitable because of aging equipment and increased competition. Georgia-Pacific had closed the plant for two weeks earlier in the year to review the operation and to attempt to restore it to profitability. But it said these steps weren't sufficient.

Paper that is being made in Reading will continue to be produced in Taylorville, Ill., among other company locations.
