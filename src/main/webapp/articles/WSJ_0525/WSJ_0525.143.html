Texaco Europe, U.S.S.R. Join in Geological Research

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

NEW YORK -- A European subsidiary of Texaco Inc. has struck an agreement with the Soviet Union to cooperate on geological research. As part of the accord, Texaco will furnish and install sophisticated computer facilities at Vniizezhgeologia, the All-Union Research Institute of the Ministry of Geology of the Soviet Union in Moscow. The computer facilities will process geophysical and geological data for assessment of oil and gas potential in the Soviet Union.

Texaco employees will help operate the equipment in Moscow. In addition, Soviet personnel associated with the institute's facilities in Moscow will participate in joint studies at Texaco's Houston facilities beginning in June.

Texaco officials believe that this agreement lays the groundwork for other Soviet-Texaco joint ventures in the future. "Texaco sees many possible business opportunities in the Soviet Union," said Texaco Europe President Paul B. Hicks Jr.
