Viacom Class B Stock Cleared

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

NEW YORK -- Viacom Inc. shareholders approved the creation of 150 million shares of Class B nonvoting common stock to allow the entertainment company greater flexibility in acquisitions. A total of 53.4 million Class B shares -- one for each common share outstanding -- will be issued and distributed as a dividend on June 13 to stock of record June 6, and will trade on the American Stock Exchange under the symbol VIAB. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

VIACOM Inc. will distribute new nonvoting Class B common shares to stock of record June 5. The record date was misstated in Friday's edition.

(WSJ May 29, 1990)
