Northrop Wins Air Force Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

WASHINGTON -- Northrop Corp. won a $75.5 million Air Force contract for aircraft countermeasures systems.

The Institutes for Defense Analyses got a $17.2 million Army contract for studies.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. was issued a $10.3 million Air Force contract for MX missile parts.
