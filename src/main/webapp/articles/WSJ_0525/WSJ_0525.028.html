ABB's Asea Brown Boveri Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Asea Brown Boveri Inc. said its power generation unit received six contracts, with an overall value of about $80 million, to upgrade 29 hydroelectric generators at major power generating projects. Asea Brown is part of the ABB Group, an electrical engineering company based in Zurich, Switzerland.
