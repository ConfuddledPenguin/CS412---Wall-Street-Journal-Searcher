Business Brief -- Pine Point Mines Ltd.: Nunachiaq, Cominco Obtain 97.2% of Shares Under Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

Nunachiaq Inc. said about 2.18 million shares of Pine Point Mines Ltd. were tendered to Nunachiaq's offer of 50 Canadian dollars (US$42.20) a share, giving it and associate Cominco Ltd. 97.2% of Pine Point. Nunachiaq, a closely held concern that is 50%-owned by Teck Corp., said it plans to acquire the remaining Pine Point shares, as permitted under Canadian business laws. The share purchases have a total indicated value of about C$115.7 million.

Nunachiaq, whose shareholders include West Germany's Metallgesellschaft AG, and Australia's M.I.M. Holdings Ltd., also owns about 41.7% of Cominco.

Cominco, Teck, and Pine Point are all Vancouver, British Columbia-based mining concerns.
