Business Brief -- Rochester Community Savings: Plan for Protecting Holders In Takeover Bids Adopted

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

Rochester Community Savings Bank, Rochester, N.Y., said its board adopted a rights plan to protect shareholders from any unfair or coercive takeover attempts. The savings bank said the plan wasn't adopted in response to any specific offer.

The rights plan calls for the declaration of a dividend of one right for each common share held on June 4. Each right will entitle the holder to purchase 1/100th of a share of new preferred stock at an exercise price of $100, should a person or group acquire 20% or more of the company's shares outstanding, among other conditions. The rights are to expire June 3, 2000.
