Who's News: McDonnell Douglas Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

MCDONNELL DOUGLAS Corp. (St. Louis) -- William L. Stowers, previously director of procurement methods and technology at McDonnell Aircraft Co., was named the unit's vice president, procurement, succeeding Roger Witte, who had been named vice president, contracts and pricing. Michael M. Sears, previously program manager for F/A-18 Hornet new business and chief program engineer on the advanced Hornet program, was named vice president and general manager, new aircraft products, at the division, succeeding James Sinnett, who had been named vice president, engineering.
