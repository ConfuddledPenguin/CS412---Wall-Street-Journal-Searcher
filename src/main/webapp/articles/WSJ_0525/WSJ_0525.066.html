Pacific Stock Exchange Post

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- The Pacific Stock Exchange said its board gave the additional title of chairman to its president and chief executive officer, Leopold Korins. Mr. Korin joined the exchange as president on Jan. 22 and was named chief executive three days later.

Donald "Terry" Brookshire, vice chairman, said the decision "is a strong vote of confidence" in Mr. Korin's performance since he joined the exchange.

The post of chairman of the exchange has been vacant since Maurice Mann resigned May 1.
