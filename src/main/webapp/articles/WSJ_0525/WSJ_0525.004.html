Phillips Petroleum Discovery

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

BARTLESVILLE, Okla. -- Phillips Petroleum Co. said a natural-gas discovery in the Southern Gas Basin of the U.K. North Sea flowed at a rate of 31.5 million cubic feet a day from a depth of 12,113 feet. The company said the well is about 80 miles from shore in a water depth of 95 feet. Phillips is the operator with a 35% interest, and other partners include American Petrofina Inc., with a 34.26% interest.
