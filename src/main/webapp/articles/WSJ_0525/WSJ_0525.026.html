South Korea Invests Abroad

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- South Korean investment overseas in the first four months of this year soared 92% to $219 million from $114 million a year earlier, the finance ministry said, citing a worsening domestic manufacturing environment because of rising wages and growing labor unrest. The number of projects rose to 118 from 72. South Korea's total overseas investments to date amount to $2.1 billion in 1,282 projects. Investments outstanding, adjusted for withdrawn projects, reached $1.6 billion in 1,009 projects.

In this year's first four months, $87 million was invested in Southeast Asia, $65 million in North America and $25 million in Europe.
