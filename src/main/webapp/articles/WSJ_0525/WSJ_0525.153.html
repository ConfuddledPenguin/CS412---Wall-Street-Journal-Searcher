Dividend News: Zenith National Insurance Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

ZENITH NATIONAL INSURANCE Corp. said it increased its regular quarterly dividend, to 22 cents a share from 21 cents. The dividend is payable Aug. 15, to stock of record at the close of business July 31. Separately, the worker's compensation insurance concern, based in Woodland Hills, Calif., said its board authorized the purchase of as many as one million of its common shares in addition to the one million share purchase it announced in September.
