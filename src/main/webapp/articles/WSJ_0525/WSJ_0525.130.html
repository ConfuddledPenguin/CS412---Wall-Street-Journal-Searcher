Marketing & Media: Honda's Dealer Incentives Include More Than Civics

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

Honda Motor Co. said its dealer incentive program is broader than the company earlier indicated. Honda said it started a factory-to-dealer cash incentive program May 13 that covers its Prelude car, the CRX model and all Civic hatchbacks, except the base model hatchback. This program is in addition to one that started April 13, which covers Civic four-door sedans and station wagons. The first program was disclosed in The Wall Street Journal yesterday. Both programs run through June 30.

The newest incentive program gives dealers meeting sales goals $500 for each Prelude, CRX or Civic hatchback they sell through June 30. Dealers can use these cash incentives to lower the price to buyers, so it's helpful for consumers to be aware of the programs. Typically, auto makers don't publicly announce dealer incentives; they do advertise direct-to-consumer incentives.

From January through April, Honda had sold 10,874 Preludes, down 29% from the same period last year.
