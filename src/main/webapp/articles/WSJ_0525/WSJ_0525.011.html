National Intergroup Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

PITTSBURGH -- National Intergroup Inc. said it began its previously announced tender offer for all the cumulative convertible preference units outstanding of Permian Partners Limited Partnership for $3.75 a unit. The price is subject to a reduction, reflecting any distributions made after May 18, 1990, and prior to the completion of the offer. The steel, retail and wholesale drugdistribution concern said the offer is conditioned upon, among other things, the tender of at least a majority of the units. The offer expires June 21, at 12:01 a.m. EDT.
