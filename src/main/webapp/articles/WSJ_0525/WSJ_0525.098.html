Tesoro Petroleum Credit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- Tesoro Petroleum Corp. said it increased an existing credit facility with a consortium of banks led by Chase Manhattan Bank to $130 million from $80 million. The refining and marketing concern said the credit facility does not include direct borrowing capability. The company didn't disclose what plans, if any, it had for the additional funds.
