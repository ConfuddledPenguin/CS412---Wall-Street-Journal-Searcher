  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

AD REVENUE: Times Mirror Co. said ad revenue of its Newspaper Publishing Group for the five weeks ended May 6 fell 1.9% to $168.3 million from $171.5 million a year earlier. For the 18 weeks ended May 6, total ad revenue fell 2% to $582.2 million. The cumulative results for 1990 contain 18 Sundays, one less than a year earlier.

RADIO: Sage Broadcasting Corp., a radio station operator, formed a new subsidiary, Sage Alerting Systems Inc., to market emergency alerting products and systems to industrial and governmental users.
