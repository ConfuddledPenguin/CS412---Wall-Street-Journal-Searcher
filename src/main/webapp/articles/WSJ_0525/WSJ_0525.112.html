Who's News: Ecogen Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

ECOGEN Inc. (Langhorne, Pa.) -- Mark S. Germain, managing director of D. Blech & Co., was elected a director of this agricultural biotechnology company, expanding board membership to nine. Blech, a venture capital investment company, holds a $1.5 million investment stake in Ecogen.
