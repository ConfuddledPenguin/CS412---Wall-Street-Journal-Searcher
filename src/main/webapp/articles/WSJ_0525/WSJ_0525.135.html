International Brief -- Honda Motor Co.: Group Pretax Earnings Fell 12% in Latest Fiscal Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

Honda Motor Co. said group pretax earnings in the year ended March 31 dropped 12% to 152.13 billion yen ($1 billion) from 172.09 billion yen a year earlier. Profit fell 16% to 81.68 billion yen, or 83.37 yen a share, from 97.3 billion yen, or 98.48 yen a share.

Sales increased 10% to 3.852 trillion yen from 3.489 trillion yen.

Honda's pretax earnings for the parent company alone were better, inching up 1.4% to 90.512 billion yen from 89.273 billion yen in the prior fiscal year.

Honda officials said growing marketing costs and higher nonoperating costs, including foreign exchange losses and interest payments, hurt earnings.

Robust demand in North America as well as in Japan, now enjoying its second longest postwar economic expansion, combined with the yen's decline during the year raise sales, according to officials.
