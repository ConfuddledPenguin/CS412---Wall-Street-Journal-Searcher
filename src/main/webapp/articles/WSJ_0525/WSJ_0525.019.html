Who's News: Schering-Plough Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/25/90

SCHERING-PLOUGH Corp. (Madison, N.J.) -- Frederick J. Fritz, formerly senior vice president OTC/Seasonal Marketing for the healthcare-products subsidiary, was named to the newly created position of senior vice president, marketing and sales, footcare, in the healthcare-products unit. The footcare-product lines, which include Dr. Scholl's products and anti-fungals, accounted for approximately $200 million in sales in 1989.
