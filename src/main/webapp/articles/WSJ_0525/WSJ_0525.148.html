Business Brief -- Fujitsu Ltd.: Pretax Net Increased 8.3% In the Latest Fiscal Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

Consolidated pretax profit at Fujitsu Ltd. rose 8.3% in the year ended March 31 on the basis of strong growth in the Japanese company's main information-system division. Pretax profit rose to 150.06 billion yen ($990.5 million) from 138.53 billion yen in fiscal 1988, the company said.

Consolidated net income increased 24% to 86.76 billion yen from 69.95 billion yen.

Sales increased 6.8% to 2.55 trillion yen from 2.387 trillion yen. Fujitsu reported 7% growth in sales of information-management products, such as office-automation systems, to 1.690 trillion yen from 1.579 trillion yen. Sales of electronic devices, such as computer memories, rose 6.7% to 356.79 billion yen from 334.32 billion yen.

Sales of communications equipment, which fell sharply in recent years because of falling orders from Nippon Telegraph & Telephone Corp., recovered. Such sales rose 5% to 394.369 billion yen from 375.464 billion yen.
