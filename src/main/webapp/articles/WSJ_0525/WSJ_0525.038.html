Hong Kong Trade Gap Widens

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

HONG KONG -- Hong Kong's April trade deficit widened to 3.37 billion Hong Kong dollars (US$433 million) from HK$2.36 billion a year earlier due to a drop in domestic exports, the government said. The trade deficit narrowed, however, from the shortfall of HK$3.86 billion recorded in March.

Total exports increased 3.8% to HK$47.27 billion from HK$45.56 billion a year earlier, but was little changed from HK$47.26 billion the previous month.

April's imports were up 5.7% to HK$50.64 from HK$47.92 billion in April 1989, but off 0.9% from HK$51.117 billion in March.
