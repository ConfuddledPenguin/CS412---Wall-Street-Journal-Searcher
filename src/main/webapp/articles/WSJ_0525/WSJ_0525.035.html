American Express's Credit Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/25/90

NEW YORK -- Directors of the American Express Credit Corp. unit of American Express Co. authorized redemption on July 1 of its $50.9 million outstanding of 11.25% debentures, due July 1, 2000, at 102% of principal amount, plus accrued interest. American Express Credit Corp. purchases credit-card receivables.
