Business Brief -- TSR Inc.: Pretax Profit Is Expected In Quarter Due to Payment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

TSR Inc. said it expects to post pretax profit of $760,000 in its fiscal first quarter ending Aug. 31 as a result of a payment from American Express Bank Ltd., but a pretax loss for the year. TSR, a Hauppauge, N.Y., computer software and information-services concern, said American Express Bank, a unit of American Express Co., will exercise an option to end TSR's management contract for data processing services a year early. TSR will receive about $500,000 for the management contract through August 1991, its scheduled expiration.

It also will receive the fair market value for certain equipment of its data center facility, which it will turn over to American Express Bank. A spokesman for American Express said the move was related to cost savings.

James Hughes, TSR vice president, said the company paid about $3.5 million for the equipment several years ago. As a result of the transactions, TSR said it expects to receive $1.3 million, which will be recognized in the fiscal first quarter. Because of this and losses in TSR's continuing operations in consulting, it said it expects to post "significant losses" for the remaining three fiscal quarters. Those losses will more than offset the income recognized in the first quarter, TSR said.
