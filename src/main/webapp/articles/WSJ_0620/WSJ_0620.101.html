Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

PUBLISHING: Western Publishing Group Inc. agreed to buy Sight & Sound Inc., a producer of children's interactive electronic storybooks, as well as toys and games that talk. Terms weren't given.

ACQUISITION: New York-based Insight Communications Co. U.K. closed the acquisition of a 50%, controlling interest in Clyde Cablevision, a 4,800-subscriber cable-TV system serving Glasgow, Scotland, for #2 million ($3.4 million).

WHO'S NEWS: Carolco Pictures Inc., Los Angeles, named Rene Bonnell, chairman of Canal Plus Productions, a director, increasing the board to 13. . . . Walt Disney Co. said Harrison Ellenshaw was named to the new post of vice president, visual effects, for Walt Disney Pictures, a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios, a unit of the parent. He recently served as visual effects supervisor on Touchstone Pictures' "Dick Tracy."
