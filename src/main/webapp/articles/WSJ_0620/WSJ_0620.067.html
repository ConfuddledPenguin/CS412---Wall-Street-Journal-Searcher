GROW GROUP Inc. (New York) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it downgraded its rating on the subordinated debt of this maker of specialty coatings and chemical products to single-B-3 from single-B-2. The rating concern said about $80 million of debt is affected. Moody's didn't give further details on the action. ---

INGERSOLL-RAND Co. (Woodcliff Lake, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it upgraded its rating on about $575 million of debt of this supplier of non-electrical machinery and equipment. The rating concern raised its ratings on senior unsecured and shelf-registration debt to single-A from single-A-minus, and its commercial-paper rating to single-A-1 from single-A-2. It said the company is enjoying improved profitability after subpar performance in the past economic cycle. While demand for its products could weaken sharply, "improved financial controls and balance-sheet support should enable the company to weather any normal cyclical downturn," S&P said.
