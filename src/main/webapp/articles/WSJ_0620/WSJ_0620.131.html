Business Brief -- Gold King Consolidated Inc.: Concern Pours Gold, Silver From Adelaide Crown Mine

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

Gold King Consolidated Inc. said it poured its first gold and silver from the Adelaide Crown mine in Humboldt County, Nev. It said the mine is expected to produce about 20,000 ounces of gold a year. The Denver-based company said studies indicate that the Adelaide Crown property, which it is leasing from Icarus Exploration Co., has proven reserves of 585,000 tons of ore with a grade of 0.043 ounce per ton of gold and 1.313 ounces per ton of silver in one partially developed open pit mine on the property.

Gold King estimated that the pit has proven reserves of 165,000 tons of ore grading 0.015 ounce of gold per ton and 1.1 ounces of silver per ton. The company said it has identified other potential mines on the 2,100-acre property.
