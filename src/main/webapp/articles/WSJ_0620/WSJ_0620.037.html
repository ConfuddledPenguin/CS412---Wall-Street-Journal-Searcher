Who's News: Security Pacific Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

SECURITY PACIFIC Corp. (Los Angeles) -- Theodore E. Gildred was named a director of this bank holding concern, increasing board membership to 23. Mr. Gildred, 54 years old, is chairman of Lomas Santa Fe Cos., a San Diego real estate development and management concern. He also is a former U.S. ambassador to Argentina.
