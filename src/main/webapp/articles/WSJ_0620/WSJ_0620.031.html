Michael Baker's ESOP

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

BEAVER, Pa. -- Michael Baker Corp. said its Employee Stock Ownership Plan expects to buy $1.3 million in common stock between now and year end. The ESOP has already purchased $200,000 of stock in the design and engineering consulting company this year.

The additional purchases will be funded with employee contributions to 401 (k) savings plan. The Baker ESOP currently holds approximately 33% of the outstanding common shares.
