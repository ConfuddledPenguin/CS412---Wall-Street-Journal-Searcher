Japanese Ship Exports

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

TOKYO -- Japanese shipbuilders signed contracts to export 35 vessels weighing a total of 1,389,000 metric tons in May, up 132% by weight from a year earlier, the Japan Ship Exporters' Association reported. The total gross tonnage was pushed up widely because of increases in contracts for mid-sized or larger vessels, an official of the association said.
