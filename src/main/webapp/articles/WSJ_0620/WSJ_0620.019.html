Swedish Industrial Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's industrial production fell 1.8% in April from March and was down 3.3% from the corresponding month a year earlier, Statistics Sweden said. Industrial production fell 3.3% in the first four months this year from the year-earlier period, the report said.

The agency said all sectors, except paper, pulp and food, lowered their production levels in April.
