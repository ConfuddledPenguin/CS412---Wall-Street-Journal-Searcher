Who's News: General Re Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

GENERAL RE Corp. (Stamford, Conn.) -- The parent of General Reinsurance Group, an insurance and related services company, named two additional directors, increasing board membership to 14. They are Lucy Wilson Benson, a former U.S. undersecretary of state, currently president of the consulting firm Benson & Associates; and Kay Koplovitz, former vice president and executive director of UA-Columbia Satellite Services Inc. and currently president and chief executive officer of USA Network, a cable-television programming producer.
