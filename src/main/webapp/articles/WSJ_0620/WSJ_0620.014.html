Metropolitan Financial

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Metropolitan Financial Corp. said it agreed to sell its First Realty Ltd. unit to Hubbell Realty Co. of Des Moines, Iowa. Terms weren't disclosed. The bank holding company said the sale would result in a "modest" gain in the current quarter. In the year-earlier second quarter, the company earned $6.2 million, or 94 cents a share.

Metropolitan said the sale won't lessen its commitment to residential real estate sales as a means to enhance its mortgage lending operations or to its Des Moines savings bank operation.
