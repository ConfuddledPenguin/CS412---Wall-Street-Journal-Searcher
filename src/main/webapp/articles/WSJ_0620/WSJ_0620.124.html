Financing Business: Foothill Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

FOOTHILL GROUP Inc., Los Angeles, said its Foothill Capital Corp. unit completed a $50 million private placement of senior notes, together with a related $50 million in interest rate swap agreements designed to make the company's interest obligations vary with market rates. Foothill, a commercial lending and leasing concern, said the notes consist of $15 million of 10.18% senior notes, due June 1993; $15 million of 10.23% senior notes, due December 1993; $5 million of 10.27% senior notes, due June 1994; and $15 million of 10.35% senior notes, due June 1995. As a result of the interest rate swap agreements, the company will pay a variable interest rate tied to commercial paper rates. Proceeds from the placement will be used to repay short-term debt.
