Business Brief -- Humana Inc.: Conversion Program Attracts 99% of Subordinated Debt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

Humana Inc., Louisville, Ky., said 99% of its nearly $200 million face amount of convertible subordinated debentures were converted into 5,249,000 of its common shares. The debentures, due Aug. 15, 2009, were converted at a price of $37.80 a share, which equals 26.455 common shares per $1,000 principal amount of debentures.

Remaining debentures will be redeemed July 2 at $1,042.50 plus accrued interest of $32.35 from Feb. 15 through July 2.

Humana owns and operates 85 hospital and health benefit plans for groups and Medicare beneficiaries. A company spokesman said Humana has 104,087,000 common shares after the conversion.
