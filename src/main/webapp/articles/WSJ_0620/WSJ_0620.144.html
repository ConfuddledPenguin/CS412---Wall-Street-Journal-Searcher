PacifiCorp Unit Properties

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

PORTLAND, Ore. -- Nerco Inc. said it acquired oil and natural gas properties in the Trimble Field in Smith and Simpson counties, Miss., for $67 million. The energy and mining concern said the properties, bought from an investors group, include 14 producing wells along with undeveloped Trimble Field acreage. Nerco is a unit of PacifiCorp of Portland.
