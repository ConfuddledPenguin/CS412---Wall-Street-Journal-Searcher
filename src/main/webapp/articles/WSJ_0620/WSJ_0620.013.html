Swiss Trade Deficit Narrows

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

BERN, Switzerland -- Switzerland's trade deficit narrowed in May to 915.5 million Swiss francs ($646.3 million) from a revised gap of 1.15 billion francs in April, the government said. In May 1989, the revised trade deficit was 1.19 billion francs. Imports in May expanded to 8.61 billion francs from a revised 8.16 billion francs in April, and exports climbed to 7.69 billion francs from a revised 7.01 billion francs.
