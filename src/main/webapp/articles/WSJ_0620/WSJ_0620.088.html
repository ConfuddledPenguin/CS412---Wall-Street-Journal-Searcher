Commercial-Paper Rate Rises at Citicorp Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.1973% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.177% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $410 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.1818% to 8.20%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate rose to 8.1919% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.175% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $385 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.179% to 8.20%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
