Conner, Seagate Settle Litigation

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Conner Peripherals Inc. and Seagate Technology Inc. said they settled a lawsuit filed by Conner alleging Seagate had unlawfully deprived the company of its supply of thin-film heads, a component in hard-disk drives. Under the settlement, Seagate will furnish Conner with the heads "until 1991 on typical industry terms and conditions." Conner will drop its lawsuit and all other claims related to the dispute.
