Who's News: Clarcor Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

CLARCOR Inc. (Rockford, Ill.) -- Norman E. Johnson, formerly vice president and general manager of Donaldson Co.'s High Purity division, was named president of Clarcor's Baldwin Filters unit of Kearney, Neb. Mr. Johnson succeeds Donald R. Galinat, who is retiring. Clarcor is a diversified manufacturer.
