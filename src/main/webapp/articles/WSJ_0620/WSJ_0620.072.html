Technology: Sun Microsystems-FPS Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif. -- Sun Microsystems Inc. said it signed an agreement with FPS Computing to market the companies' workstations and computers jointly. Under terms of the accord, Sun could, for example, bundle an array of its scientific workstations and file servers, or terminals linked on a computer network, with a recently introduced FPS supercomputer, Sun said. The companies didn't disclose projected sales. Sun said combined Sun-FPS systems would have applications in the oil exploration, engineering design, computational chemistry and signal processing industries. FPS is based in Portland, Ore.
