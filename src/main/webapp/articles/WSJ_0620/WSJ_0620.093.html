Business Brief -- ICN Biomedicals Inc.: Net Income Increased 78% In Fiscal Second Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

ICN Biomedicals Inc., Costa Mesa, Calif., said fiscal second-quarter net income rose 78% to $2.8 million from $1.5 million a year ago, reflecting higher international sales and other benefits of a recent acquisition. Sales more than doubled to $35.3 million from $14.2 million in the quarter ended May 31. Per-share net was 25 cents compared with 15 cents, restated to reflect a 20% stock dividend paid in August.

ICN Biomedicals attributed its increases to the strength of the company's development and marketing of biomedical research tests and to the sales volume of Flow Laboratories, acquired in November.

The company said that international sales jumped to $20.8 million from $1.7 million, mainly reflecting Flow sales, which are primarily in Europe, Japan and Australia. Flow produces laboratory equipment, live cells and related products for life-science research.

In national over-the-counter trading, ICN Biomedicals fell 12.5 cents to $9.625.
