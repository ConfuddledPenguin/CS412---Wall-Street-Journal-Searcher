Who's News: JLG Industries President, Stephen R. Cecil, Resigns

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

MCCONNELLSBURG, Pa. -- JLG Industries Inc. said its board accepted the immediate resignation of Stephen R. Cecil as president and chief operating officer of the hydraulic machinery producer. Mr. Cecil, 47 years old, who also resigned from the board, is pursuing other business interests, the company said. His duties will be assumed by John L. Grove, chairman and chief executive officer, until the board names a new president. JLG said the board's search for a successor is expected to be completed shortly.

Mr. Grove, 69, said in an interview that he was saddened by Mr. Cecil's departure, which he described as friendly.
