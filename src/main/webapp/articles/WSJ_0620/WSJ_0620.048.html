Treasury Set to Raise $1.33 Billion Monday

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.33 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $17.2 billion in short-term bills to redeem $15.88 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Sept. 27 and Dec. 27, respectively.

The bills will be available in minimum $10,000 denominations. Tenders must be received by 1 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
