Who's News: Ford Motor Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

FORD MOTOR Co. (Dearborn, Mich.) -- John L. O'Connor will retire as vice president-controller of this U.S. auto maker, effective Oct. 1. Mr. O'Connor, 63 years old, joined Ford in 1952 and has held a variety of finance positions, including controller for North American Automotive Operations. Ford said it does not know when Mr. O'Connor will be suceeded.
