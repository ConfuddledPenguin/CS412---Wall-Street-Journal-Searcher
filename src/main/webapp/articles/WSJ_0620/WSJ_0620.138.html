Canada Government Bond Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government auctioned 650 million Canadian dollars (US$553.4 million) of 10.25% bonds maturing March 15, 2014. The average accepted yield bid was 10.706% for a price equivalent of 96.068.

Proceeds of the sale will be used to redeem C$600 million of maturing bonds and for general government purposes.
