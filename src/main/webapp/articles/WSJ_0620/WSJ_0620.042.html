Business Brief -- TW Holdings Inc.: Pact Allows Firm to Add 145 Hardee's Restaurants

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

TW Holdings Inc., Spartanburg, S.C., said it has reached an agreement with Hardee's Food Systems Inc. that will allow TW Holdings to add 145 Hardee's restaurants to its operations during the next five years. TW Holdings, through its TW Services subsidiary, currently owns and operates 470 Hardee's restaurants. For the 12 months ended March 31, average unit volume was $1,062,000.

Hardee's Food Systems, based in Rocky Mount, N.C., is a unit of Imasco Ltd. in Montreal.
