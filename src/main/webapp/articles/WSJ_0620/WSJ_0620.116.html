Who's News: First Union Corp. Says Georgius Is Named President of Parent

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

CHARLOTTE, N.C. -- First Union Corp. said its board elected John R. Georgius president of the bank holding company. Mr. Georgius, 45 years old, is chairman and chief executive officer of First Union National Bank of North Carolina, positions he has held since 1988. He will retain those posts.

In his new position as president of the parent, Mr. Georgius will manage First Union's banking operations and related staff in North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Florida and Tennessee.

The position of president had been held by First Union's chairman and chief executive, Edward E. Crutchfield. Mr. Crutchfield, 48, assumed those duties in 1988 when the company's president, Ben T. Craig, died.
