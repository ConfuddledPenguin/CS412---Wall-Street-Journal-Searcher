Who's News: Boston Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

BOSTON Co. named George W. Carmany III senior executive vice president in charge of international, administrative and corporate planning and development functions, effective July 1. Mr. Carmany has been senior executive vice president and chief administrative officer of American Express Co.'s American Express Bank Ltd. Boston Co. is a Boston-based unit of Shearson Lehman Hutton, a subsidiary of American Express.
