FlightSafety Purchase Scrapped

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

NEW YORK -- An agreement under which FlightSafety International Inc. was to acquire certain assets of Bicoastal Corp.'s SimuFlite division was terminated, the companies said. In April, FlightSafety agreed to pay about $64 million for the assets and to assume about $19 million in long-term debt. Both FlightSafety and SimuFlite have interests in pilot training.

FlightSafety Chairman Albert L. Ueltschi declined to say why the plan was ended. Bicoastal, Tampa, Fla., couldn't be reached for comment.

Bicoastal, formerly Singer Co., has been shedding various assets of what had been a defense-electronics company.
