International Brief -- Bank Leu: Majority of Voting Shares Back a CS Holding Alliance

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

Bank Leu, Switzerland's fifth-largest commercial bank, said holders of a majority of the bank's voting shares are willing to accept a proposed alliance with CS Holding AG through a new holding company that CS Holding will control. Terms haven't been disclosed. As a result, Bank Leu scheduled a special meeting of holders for Tuesday to vote on approving the transaction.

The new holding company, to be called Leu Holding, would be composed of Bank Leu and two small investment houses already in the CS Holding group, Bank Hofmann and Clariden Bank. Among CS Holding's other units is Credit Suisse, the country's third-largest commercial bank. Bank Leu's board on May 11 recommended that shareholders accept the CS Holding offer to swap Bank Leu shares for shares in the new holding company.
