Business Brief -- Susquehanna Bancshares Inc.: Bank Holding Firm Agrees To Buy Elmwood Federal

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/20/90

Susquehanna Bancshares Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire Elmwood Federal Savings Bank, Media, Pa., in an all-stock transaction valued at $24 million. Susquehanna, based in Lititz, Pa., said it will acquire all 1,459,125 Elmwood common shares outstanding in exchange for the 1,280,000 Susquehanna common shares. The exchange rate of about 0.88 of a Susquehanna share for each share of Elmwood equals $16.73 for each Elmwood share at Susquehanna's closing price yesterday of $19 a share in national over-the-counter trading.

Terms will be described in a joint proxy prospectus and are subject to shareholder approval.

Elmwood is a federally chartered savings bank with total assets of $236.6 million and serves southeastern Pennsylvania. Susquehanna is a multibank holding company and parent of six community banks in Pennsylvania. It reported assets of $1.55 billion as of March 31.
