Business Brief -- Advantage Life Products Inc.: Letter of Intent Is Signed For Settlement of Litigation

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

Advantage Life Products Inc. said it signed a letter of intent agreeing to settle litigation with More Direct Health Products Inc. More Direct, based in Carlsbad, Calif., is Advantage's sole supplier of a stop-smoking product whose retail distribution is Advantage's main business. The proposed settlement would bring More Direct's top officer onto Advantage's board and give More Direct warrants to buy 400,000 shares of Advantage stock, equivalent to 8.7% of the about 4.6 million shares currently outstanding.

Advantage, based in Laguna Hills, Calif., said More Direct's warrants would be exercisable at the June 12 closing price for its stock, which finished the day at $3 bid, $3.1875 asked, in over-the-counter trading. Yesterday, Advantage stock jumped $1 a share to close at $4.3125 bid.

The proposed settlement continues indefinitely a sales agreement between the two companies that otherwise would have expired in 1995. The arrangement includes 15% annual increases in Advantage's minimum purchases after 1995.
