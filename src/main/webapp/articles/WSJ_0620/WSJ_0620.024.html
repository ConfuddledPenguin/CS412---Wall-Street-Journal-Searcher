Who's News: Campeau Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

CAMPEAU Corp. (Toronto) -- Federated Department Stores Inc. named Michael Moore to the new post of senior vice president and chief financial officer at its Bloomingdale's subsidiary in New York. Mr. Moore had been vice president and controller at Bloomingdale's. Federated itself is a Cincinnati department-store unit of Campeau.
