International Brief -- Bayer AG: Earnings for 5 Months Fell On 2.2% Decline in Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

Bayer AG of West Germany, a chemical concern, said that its group earnings declined in the first five months of this year, though it didn't disclose profit figures, and that group sales fell 2.2% to 18.25 billion marks ($10.89 billion) from 18.66 billion marks a year earlier. The announcement at Bayer's annual meeting confirmed expectations of falling profits in the West German chemical industry, following several years of record results. Bayer said foreign-exchange fluctuations alone reduced its sales by about one billion marks and also depressed earnings.
