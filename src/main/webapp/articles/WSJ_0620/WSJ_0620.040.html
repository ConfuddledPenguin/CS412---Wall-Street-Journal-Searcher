Who's News: Micron Products Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/20/90

MICRON PRODUCTS Inc. (New York) -- Anthony A. Cetrone was named president, succeeding Kuno Laren. Mr. Cetrone, 58 years old, will function as the top executive, although the title of chief executive officer won't be used, said Mr. Laren, 66, who remains chairman. Mr. Cetrone was president of the Micron Medical Products Inc. subsidiary, which makes sensors used in medical electrodes. Separately, Micron said its Numedco unit, which distributes medical and surgical supplies, decided to scale down its business. Numedco has had losses for the past seven quarters since it took on acquisition-related debt.
