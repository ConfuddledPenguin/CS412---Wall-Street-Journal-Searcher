Lexington Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

LEXINGTON, Mass. -- Lexington Savings Bank said its shareholders approved the company's acquisition by Waltham Savings Bank, a unit of Waltham Corp., Waltham, Mass. Waltham has agreed to buy Lexington Savings for $10.75 a share, or about $25.5 million. Completion of the acquisition remains subject to regulatory approval.
