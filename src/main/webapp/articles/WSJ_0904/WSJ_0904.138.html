Breakdown Line

News reports are constant

On all sorts of diseases;

The steady flow of symptoms

Never really eases.

But among these is the question

With which we can dispense:

It's the article that asks

Why are we all so tense.

-- Joshua Adams.

Straggler's Theory

Self-starters have my deep respect --

They've earned their lofty rank.

We laggards follow, I suspect,

Because we need a crank.

-- Bert H. Kruse.
