Business Brief -- MedChem Products Inc.: Talks With Pharmacia AB Could Lead to `Agreement'

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

MedChem Products Inc., Woburn, Mass., said a federal appeals court hearing scheduled for Friday in its patent battle with Pharmacia AB was postponed because the companies "are close to an agreement in principle" to settle the litigation. Pharmacia, a Swedish concern, won an injunction in December in federal district court in Boston. The injunction limits MedChem's production of hyaluronic acid-based products used in eye surgery. The court earlier ruled that MedChem's Amvisc product line infringes on a product to which Pharmacia owns the license. MedChem has appealed the injunction.

MedChem, a maker of biomedical products, said, "There are still certain details to be worked out" before its dispute with Pharmacia can be settled. "Discussions are expected to continue in order to resolve these differences," the company added.
