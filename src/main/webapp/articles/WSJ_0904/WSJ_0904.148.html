In Friday's Americas column it was incorrectly stated that AT&T was negotiating with a Spanish company to buy Argentina's phone system. The U.S. company that has been involved in those negotiations is Bell Atlantic. (See: "The Americas: Argentina Tries Cosmetic Surgery on a Sagging Economy" -- WSJ Aug. 31, 1990)
