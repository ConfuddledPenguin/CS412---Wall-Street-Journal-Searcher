NovaCare Inc. Stock Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

VALLEY FORGE, Pa. -- NovaCare Inc. said it intends to offer 4.5 million shares at a currently undetermined price. Of the shares to be sold, 2 million will be offered by the company, and 2.5 million will be offered by holders of certain securities.

Robertson, Stephens & Co. is lead manager of the offering.

Proceeds will be used to repay debt and fund acquisitions, the company said.
