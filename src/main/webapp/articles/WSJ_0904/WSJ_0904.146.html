Who's News: May Department Stores Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

MAY DEPARTMENT STORES Co. (St. Louis) -- Joseph M. Melvin, formerly senior vice president, finance, for the retailer's May Co., California, department store division, was named chairman of its Filene's department store division, succeeding Carl Tooker, who took a post with another company.
