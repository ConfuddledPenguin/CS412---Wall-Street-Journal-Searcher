Business Brief -- Gradco Systems Inc.: Meeting Reset as Plenum, Tash Propose Rival Slate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

Gradco Systems Inc. adjourned its annual meeting last Friday after 8.9%-holder Plenum Publishing Corp. and its chairman Martin Tash said they would seek to elect their own slate of directors. Plenum disclosed its plans in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission earlier last week. Gradco, an Irving, Calif., maker of paper-handling equipment, responded by adjourning the meeting until Oct. 12 to give the company time to mail additional proxy materials regarding Plenum's recent actions to shareholders.
