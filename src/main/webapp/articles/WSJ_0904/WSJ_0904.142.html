Who's News: Scientific Software-Intercomp Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

SCIENTIFIC SOFTWARE-INTERCOMP Inc. (Denver) -- E.A. Breitenbach, chairman and chief executive officer of this software and consulting services concern, was named to the added posts of president and chief operating officer, succeeding R.C. McFarlane, who resigned. Mr. McFarlane will remain a consultant to the company.
