Inco Ltd. Indonesian Explosion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

TORONTO -- Inco Ltd. said an explosion killed two workers at one of the three electric furnaces at the PT International Nickel Indonesia nickel smelter. The nickel producer, which owns about 58% of the Indonesian company, said it didn't yet know the extent of damage from the explosion, which it said was apparently caused by a water leak.

Inco said production has been halted at the smelter since Aug. 25 for five weeks of repairs and other work related to an expansion program.
