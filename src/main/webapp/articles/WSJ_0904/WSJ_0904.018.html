Ivaco Sets Pact on Units' Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

MONTREAL -- Ivaco Inc., a steel products concern, said it definitively agreed to sell its two Niagara Lockport Industries units, which make specialty fabrics for the paper-making industry at five plants in the U.S. and Canada, to BTR PLC of Britain for 85 million Canadian dollars (US$74 million). BTR also will assume Niagara Lockport debt of about C$45 million. Ivaco said closing of the sale is conditioned on obtaining the approval of Canadian competition authorities by Nov. 30. Ivaco said the sale has already been approved by U.S. antitrust authorities.
