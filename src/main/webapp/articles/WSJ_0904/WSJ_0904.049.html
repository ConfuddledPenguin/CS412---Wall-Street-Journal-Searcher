  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

LONDON -- BBA Group PLC, a British auto and aviation parts manufacturer, said it agreed to buy a Dallas-based aerospace parts subsidiary of Hillman Co. in exchange for a 4.99% stake in BBA. BBA is to acquire Texstar Inc., which makes high technology plastic parts and composite materials for the aerospace industry.

Hillman will receive 11,960,000 BBA ordinary shares, which at 138.5 pence a share price values the deal at #16.6 million.

Textar had an operating profit of $4.3 million last year on sales of $32 million, with commercial and military aerospace markets accounting for 60% of the sales.
