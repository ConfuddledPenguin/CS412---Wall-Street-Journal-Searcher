Canada's Quarterly GDP Fell

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's inflation-adjusted gross domestic product declined at a compound annual rate of 1.6% in the second quarter, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The downturn affected all major areas of domestic demand, as well as corporate profits and personal disposable income. In terms of 1986 prices, GDP was at a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 571.33 billion Canadian dollars (US$498.09 billion) in the second quarter, off 0.4% from a first-quarter rate of C$573.69 billion.

The GDP implicit price index rose 0.9% from the first quarter and 3.1% from a year earlier.
