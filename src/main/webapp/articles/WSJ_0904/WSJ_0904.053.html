International: Royale Belge S.A.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

BRUSSELS -- Royale Belge S.A., a Belgian insurer, said it acquired the Belgian insurance operations of Groupe Le Foyer of Luxembourg. Terms weren't disclosed. The operations have annual premium income of more than 2 billion Belgian francs ($63 million), mostly in automotive, property and, to a lesser extent, life insurance, Royale Belge said.
