Italy's Consumer Prices Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

MILAN, Italy -- Italy's consumer price index rose 6.3% in August from a year earlier and was up 0.7% from July, the state statistical institute reported. Istat said the index registered 106.6 in August, compared with 105.9 in July.

The index (1989 equals 100) isn't seasonally adjusted.

The August increase followed a 0.4% monthly rise in July, when the year-to-year increase was 5.7%.
