Financing Business: American Business Computers Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

AMERICAN BUSINESS COMPUTERS Corp., Akron, Ohio, filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission, covering 7.5 million common shares previously sold through private placements. The company said that the registration permits holders to sell shares, but that it doesn't expect holders of major blocks of stock to sell their shares. The company said it has about 13.5 million shares outstanding, with six million of these relatively widely held. American Business Computers develops and markets products, such as soft-drink dispensers, for the food service industry. Major customers are among the holders of the newly registered shares.
