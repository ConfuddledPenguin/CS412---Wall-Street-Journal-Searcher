Law Brief -- Borland International Inc.: Court's Dismissal of Suit To Bring Venue Decision

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

Borland International Inc. said an action it filed in federal court in San Francisco against Lotus Development Corp. was dismissed, thereby allowing the federal court in Boston to decide where the case should be heard. In late June and early July, Borland, based in Scotts Valley, Calif., and Lotus, based in Cambridge, Mass., filed lawsuits in San Francisco and Boston both seeking to determine whether Borland's spreadsheet software would infringe on any Lotus copyrights covering its 1-2-3 spreadsheets.

One analyst, who asked not to be named, said the ruling was a blow to Borland, because the federal judge in Boston is presumed to take an expansive view of Lotus's rights in the case. Earlier this year, Judge Robert Keeton sided with Lotus in a copyright infringement case involving another software maker.
