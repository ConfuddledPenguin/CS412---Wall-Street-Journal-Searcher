Business Brief -- Meadowdale Foods Inc.: Kroger Co. Buys 25 Stores, Plus 4 Under Construction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

Kroger Co., Cincinnati, said it bought 25 Detroit area supermarkets, plus four others under construction, from Meadowdale Foods Inc., Detroit. Kroger said Foodland Distributors, Livonia, Mich., which currently supplies Kroger's Michigan supermarkets, purchased Meadowdale's Abner Wolf Distributors wholesale operations, and will supply the newly acquired Kroger stores. Those stores, previously operated under the Great Scott] name, will adopt the Kroger name.

Sources close to the transaction said Meadowdale received $74 million for the operations. Kroger didn't disclose how much of that was for the stores it purchased.

The purchase will double the number of Kroger stores in the Detroit area. The supermarket and drugstore chain previously operated 54 stores in Michigan, 29 of them in the Detroit area.
