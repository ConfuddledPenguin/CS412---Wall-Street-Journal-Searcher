Letters to the Editor: Why We're Over There

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

In regard to "The Stakes in the Gulf" (editorial, Aug. 15), I think the stupidity of President Bush's decision to send troops to the Mideast is beyond description. Your editorial conveniently fails to mention that the main reason our troops have been sent is that Congress has persistently and repeatedly refused to allow the Arab countries to arm themselves adequately. D. Thomas Urban, M.D.

San Jose, California
