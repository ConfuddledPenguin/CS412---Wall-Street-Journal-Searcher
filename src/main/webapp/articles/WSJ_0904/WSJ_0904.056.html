Corporate Data Sciences Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

LOS ANGELES -- Corporate Data Sciences Inc. said it completed the sale of its subsidiaries, Medallion Kitchens of Minnesota Inc. and Medallion Kitchens Inc., to Aril Group Inc. In exchange for the units, Corporate Data received 16.9 million Aril common shares, boosting Corporate Data's stake in the holding company to 72%.

Corporate Data has said it plans to spin off its Aril stake to its own shareholders. Following the spinoff, Corporate Data said it intends to continue a restructuring of the company through the merger of its 90%-owned Consolidated Packaging Corp. into Corporate Data.
