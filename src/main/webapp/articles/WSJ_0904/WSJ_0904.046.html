B.F. Goodrich Sells a Business

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

AKRON, Ohio -- B.F. Goodrich Co., a producer of polyvinyl chloride, specialty chemicals and aircraft parts and services, said it completed the sale of its off-highway braking systems business to Carlisle Cos., Syracuse, N.Y., which produces single-ply roofing, computer tape, specialty wire and cable, and other products, including brake parts. Terms weren't disclosed. The Goodrich unit produces brakes for construction, mining and other off-highway vehicles, and had 1989 sales of $47 million. Goodrich said the sale won't have a significant effect on its 1990 profit. Goodrich's sales last year were $2.4 billion.
