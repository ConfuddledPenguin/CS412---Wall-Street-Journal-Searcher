Atlantic Richfield Unit Wins Defense Contract Totaling $390.4 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

WASHINGTON -- Arco Products Co., a unit of Atlantic Richfield Co., received a $390.4 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. was awarded $156.2 million in contracts for Army helicopters and Air Force aircraft missiles and logistic support.

Oshkosh Truck Corp. won a $57 million Army contract for heavy vehicles.

General Electric Co. was issued a $44.7 million Air Force contract for missile parts and Army vehicle trainers.

Boeing Co. got a $22.3 million Air Force contract for airborne command post improvements.

ITT Corp. received a $21.7 million Army contract for communications equipment.

Martin Marietta Corp. won $19.3 million in Air Force contracts for navigation equipment and missile rocket motor storage.

United Telecontrol Electronics Inc. was awarded a $19.2 million Air Force contract for missile rail launchers.

ESL Inc. received an $18.2 million Army contract for aircraft electronics.

ECC International Corp. received a $14.2 million Air Force contract for maintenance training devices.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., received a $13.9 million Air Force contract for missile rail launchers.
