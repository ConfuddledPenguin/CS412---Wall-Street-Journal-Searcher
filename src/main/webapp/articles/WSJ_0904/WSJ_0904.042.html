United Bankshares Inc. Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

CHARLESTON, W.Va. -- United Bankshares Inc. said it completed the merger of First National Bank of Weirton, W. Va., into a unit of the bank holding company. First National of Weirton has assets of $43 million. United Bankshares has more than $1.2 billion of assets.
