Letters to the Editor: So What Else is New?

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

Michael Gartner (Viewpoint, op-ed page, Aug. 9) made two points in his article on the cost of the S&L bailout: (1) There is injustice in the world, and (2) the S&L bailout is projected to cost a whole lot of money. I anxiously await his sequel column where he outlines his just and inexpensive solution. David Kreutzer

Director

International Business Program

James Madison University

Harrisonburg, Va.
