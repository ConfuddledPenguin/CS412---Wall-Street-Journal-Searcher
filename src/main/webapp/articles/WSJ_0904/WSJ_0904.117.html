Business Brief -- Standard Products Co.: Plastic/Magnetic Products Unit of Pantasote Acquired

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

Standard Products Co., Cleveland, said it bought the Plastic/Magnetic Products division of Pantasote Inc., Greenwich, Conn. Terms weren't disclosed. The unit makes extruded and molded plastic and magnetic parts for home appliances and other uses. Sales last year were $24 million.

Standard Products, which is mainly a producer of rubber and plastic parts for cars and trucks, in 1989 bought Holm Industries, a maker of plastic and magnetic door seals for home appliances and residential doors. The newly acquired unit will complement the Holm operations, Standard Products said.
