Business Brief -- Nestor Inc.: Sligos of France Acquires 5% Stake, Signs Agreement

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

Nestor Inc. said in Providence, R.I., that Sligos S.A., a French financial services concern, bought "just under" a 5% stake in Nestor and signed a letter of intent to finance the development and licensing of certain Nestor financial products and services in Europe. Nestor makes software based on neural networks, computer systems that roughly mimic operation of the brain's neurons.

Sligos purchased 352,064 shares of Nestor's Series A preferred, convertible into an equal number of shares of the company's common, for $704,128, Nestor said.

If Sligos doesn't reach a "satisfactory" agreement to fund development and licensing of the Nestor products in Europe, it may require Nestor to buy back the preferred stock, at cost, Nestor said.
