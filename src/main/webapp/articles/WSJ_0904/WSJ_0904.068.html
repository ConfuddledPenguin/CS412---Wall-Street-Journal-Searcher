STOCK MUTUAL FUNDS lost an estimated $1.8 billion in assets in August, mainly because of investors' switching to other funds. If the estimate proves correct, the loss would be the worst since February 1989. The historical comparison was misstated in the headline of an article in Friday's edition. (See: "Mutual Funds: Stock Fund Sales Collapse, Marking Most Dismal Month Since 1987 Crash" -- WSJ Aug. 31, 1990)

HONDA MOTOR Co. planned to build 8,710 cars in the U.S. last week. An incorrect production estimate appeared in Friday's edition.
