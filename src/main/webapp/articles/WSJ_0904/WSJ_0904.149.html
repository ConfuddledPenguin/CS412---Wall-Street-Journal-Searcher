Who's News: Weatherford International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

WEATHERFORD INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Houston) -- Robert E. Hendrix was named chief operating officer and executive vice president of this energy services concern. The 44-year-old Mr. Hendrix succeeds Eugene L. Butler, who retains the titles of president and chief executive officer. The post of executive vice president had been vacant. Additionally, Charles J. Hooper, an investor and former president of Dresser Industries Inc.'s Dresser Minerals division, was elected a director, expanding the board to 10 from nine. Dresser Industries, Dallas, is an energy services concern.
