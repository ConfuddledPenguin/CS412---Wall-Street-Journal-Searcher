Who's News: New England's Riley Returns to Prudential

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

BOSTON -- New England Mutual Life Insurance Co. said Robert Riley, who was hired three months ago to head the Loomis, Sayles & Co. unit, is returning to Prudential Insurance Co. of America, based in Newark, N.J. Donald R. Shepherd, a Loomis Sayles executive vice president, will succeed Mr. Riley as the unit's president and chief executive officer. Mr. Shepherd, 53 years old, has headed the unit's Pasadena, Calif., office. Loomis Sayles manages about $25 billion in institutional and individual portfolios.

New England Mutual said Mr. Riley is returning to his previous post of executive vice president of the Prudential Investment Corp. unit. New England Mutual said Mr. Riley is rejoining Prudential under an arrangement that makes Boston his center of operations, "near his home in Cohasset, Mass." Mr. Riley said, "my interest was in finding the best balance of family and professional considerations."
