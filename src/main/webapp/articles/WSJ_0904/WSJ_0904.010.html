Who's News: SEI Appoints Greer To Post of President

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

WAYNE, Pa. -- SEI Corp. said Henry H. Greer was elected president and chief operating officer. Mr. Greer, 52 years old, has been a director of the company for 12 years, and most recently was executive vice president in charge of SEI's benefit-services unit and its information-services division, the company's largest operation.

He is a former International Business Machines Corp. branch manager and a former GTE Corp. marketing executive.

As SEI's president, he succeeds Alfred P. West Jr., who remains chairman and chief executive. A company spokesman said the position of chief operating officer had been vacant for several years.
