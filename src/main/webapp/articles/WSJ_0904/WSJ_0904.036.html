Raet N.V. Acquires Computer Service Unit Of Switzerland's Adia

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

ARNHEM, the Netherlands -- Raet NV, a computer software and services company based here, said it acquired Automation Center International, known as ACI, from Adia SA of Switzerland. Terms weren't disclosed. Raet said ACI's revenue this year is expected to total the equivalent of about $70 million, compared with projected revenue of more than $225 million at Raet.

Huibert Matthes, chief executive officer of Raet, declined to disclose profit figures for ACI but said they were better than the 5.5% return on sales recorded by Raet last year.

ACI, which has about 500 employees, operates offices in West Germany, Switzerland, Austria and Belgium. The company's specialties include setting up computer systems for car dealers and insurance brokers, as well as operation of data bases.

Raet has 1,850 employees. For 1989, the company reported net profit of 20 million guilders ($11.3 million) on revenue of 370 million guilders ($209.6 million).
