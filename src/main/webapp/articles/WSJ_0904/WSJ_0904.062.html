New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

NEW YORK -- The American Stock Exchange and the National Market System of the over-the-counter market each added two new stock listings.

Joining the Amex on Thursday is Henley International Inc., a Sugar Land, Texas, provider of products for physical therapy and other uses, joins the Amex Friday under the symbol HEN.

H.W. Kaufman Financial Group Inc., a Southfield, Mich., property and casualty insurer, under the symbol HWK.

Kaufman and Henley both previously traded over the counter.

On the OTC market, Park National Corp., a Newark, Ohio, bank holding company, began trading under PARK.

Vital Signs Inc., a Totowa, N.J., maker of medical products, is added to the listings under the symbol VITL.
