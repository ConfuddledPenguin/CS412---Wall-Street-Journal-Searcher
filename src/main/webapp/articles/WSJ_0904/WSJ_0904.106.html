Business Brief -- Royal Trustco Ltd.: Credit Lyonnais Buys Some Asset-Management Segments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

Royal Trustco Ltd. said it sold the Hong Kong and Singapore-based asset-management operations of its Royal Trust Asset Management Asia Ltd. unit to Credit Lyonnais. Terms of the sale weren't disclosed. Royal Trust said it expects to report a gain on the sale, but that it will have "no material effect" on Royal Trustco's profits.

Royal Trust said the operations being sold don't fit into its strategic plans to concentrate internationally on areas such as private banking or client portfolio management and specialized corporate finance, areas in which the company says it has competitive strength.

Royal Trust is a Toronto-based trust company. Credit Lyonnais is a major French financial services institution.
