Canadian Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 170,609 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, up 13% from 151,255 tons in the preceding week but down 40% from 286,110 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For this year through last Saturday, production fell 13% to 8,944,308 metric tons from 10,316,195 tons a year earlier.
