Who's News: Italy's Chief Market Regulator

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

MILAN, Italy -- Bruno Pazzi, 78 years old, was appointed president of Consob, Italy's stock-market regulatory agency, filling the vacancy left when Franco Piga was appointed minister of state industry in July. Mr. Pazzi, who was appointed a Consob commissioner in 1978, was already serving as acting president following Mr. Piga's departure. Before joining Consob, Mr. Pazzi held several positions in Italy's beverage and distilling industry.
