Wheeling-Pittsburgh Steel

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

WHEELING, W.VA. -- Wheeling-Pittsburgh Steel Corp. said it will raise prices for some tin mill products 5.5%, effective Oct. 1. The company said it is following a price increase announced by several other producers. The price for electrolytic tinplate, tin free steel and black plate will be increased 5.5%, as will basis weight extras and tin coating extras, the company said.

The price schedule for tin coated sheet won't change, but the company said it will reduce competitive allowances $2 a hundred pounds.
