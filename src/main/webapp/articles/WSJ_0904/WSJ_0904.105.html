Technology Brief -- General Dynamics Corp.: Unit Gets Job to Launch An Italian-Dutch Satellite

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

General Dynamics Commercial Launch Services Inc., a San Diego-based unit of General Dynamics Corp., St. Louis, said it signed a contract with an agency of the Italian government to launch a scientific satellite. Terms weren't disclosed, but it is known that General Dynamics usually charges between $50 million and $60 million for its Atlas I launch service.

The satellite, a joint project of Italy and the Netherlands, will study X-ray emissions in space, General Dynamics said. The 2,868-pound satellite is to be launched from the Air Force station at Cape Canaveral, Fla., in 1994.
