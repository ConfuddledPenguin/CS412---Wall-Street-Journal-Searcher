Singapore June Trade Deficit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

KUALA LUMPUR, Malaysia -- Singapore's trade deficit widened 32% in June to 1.46 billion Singapore dollars (US$829.9 million) from S$1.08 billion (US$610 million) in May and grew 92% from S$761 million in June 1989. The June data, released later than expected, confirmed economists' projections of weakening exports. Exports fell 1.6% in the month to S$7.62 billion from S$7.74 billion in May, while imports rose 3% to S$9.08 billion from S$8.82 billion.

Compared with their levels in June 1989, export values grew 3.9%, while imports surged 12.2%. The figures are all reported in nominal terms.
