  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/04/90

CHICAGO -- Great American Management & Investment Inc. said a partnership that controls the company completed the previously announced sale of an 18% Great American stake to Hellman & Friedman. The company said Equity Holdings Ltd. sold two million shares of its Great American stock for $25 each. The sale trims its stake to 74% from 92%. Great American has interests in manufacturing, real estate, financial services, agricultural chemicals and fertilizers.
