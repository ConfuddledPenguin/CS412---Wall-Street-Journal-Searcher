Gantos's 5 New Stores

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc. said it plans to open five stores next year, well below the 30 stores it usually opens each year. The women's clothing retailer said it is opening fewer outlets because it needs to "pause and fine tune" its existing operations. Gantos said it plans to remodel five of its stores next year.
