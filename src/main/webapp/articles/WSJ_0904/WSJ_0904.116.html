International Lease's Purchase by American International Closed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/04/90

NEW YORK -- American International Group Inc., a multinational insurer, said it completed its acquisition of International Lease Finance Corp. on Friday, shortly after shareholders of the Beverly Hills, Calif.-based jet-leasing company approved the transaction, valued at $1.3 billion. International Lease holders accepted American International's cash-and-stock offer, even though American International shares had traded for several days below $66.40, a level in the acquisition agreement that would have allowed either company to walk away from the deal. Officials at both companies had said they were eager to complete the transaction.

American International shares moved above the $66.40 mark last week and closed Friday at $67.50, up 50 cents, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
