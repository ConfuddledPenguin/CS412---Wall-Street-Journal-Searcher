Chambers Development Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

PITTSBURGH -- Chambers Development Co. said it was awarded a five-year recycling contract that will pay between $8 million and $38 million, depending on how it is implemented. The contract, with Hunterdon County, N.J., requires the environmental services company to operate a recycling and transfer station and to transport and dispose of the county's solid waste, as of Nov. 1.
