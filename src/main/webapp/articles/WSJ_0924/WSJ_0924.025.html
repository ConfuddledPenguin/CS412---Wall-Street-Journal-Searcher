Kaiser-Francis Raises Stake in Farm & Home

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

WASHINGTON -- Kaiser-Francis Oil Co., Tulsa, Okla., said it raised its stake in Farm & Home Financial Corp., Nevada, Mich., to the equivalent of 9.97%. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Kaiser said it holds 439,088 shares, assuming the conversion of cumulative voting convertible preferred stock, including Series A preferred stock bought from Feb. 12 to Aug. 20 and 92,834 common shares bought from Aug. 24 to Aug. 31 at prices ranging from $8.625 to $9.125 a share.

The shares were bought for investment reasons.
