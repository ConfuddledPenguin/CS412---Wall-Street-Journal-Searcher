Commercial Metals Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

DALLAS -- Commercial Metals Co. said its board approved projected capital expenditures of $55 million for fiscal 1991, which began Sept. 1. The projected expenditures include $21 million for capital projects approved but not spent in fiscal 1990 plus $34 million of new projects. This is an increase of $11 million from capital expenditures of $44 million in fiscal 1990, said CMC, which manufactures, recycles and markets metal products.
