Tyco Toys Bid for Nasta

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

MOUNT LAUREL, N.J. -- Tyco Toys Inc. said Nasta International Inc. found Tyco's revised bid to be fair and recommended that stockholders tender their shares. As previously reported, the companies signed a definitive agreement for Tyco to acquire Nasta for a sweetened price of $1.25 a share, up from $1. Nasta is a New York maker of lower-priced toys.

As of last Monday 607,965 shares were tendered under the original offer, which would increase Tyco's stake in Nasta to 86% from its current 74% interest.
