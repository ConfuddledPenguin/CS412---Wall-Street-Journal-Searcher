Ohio Casualty Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

HAMILTON, Ohio -- Ohio Casualty Corp.'s board authorized the purchase of 1.5 million shares of the insurance holding company's common stock. The stock will be purchased from time to time on the open market or through privately negotiated transactions. The stock will be retired or held as treasury shares for stock options or other general corporate purposes.

Ohio Casualty currently has about 17.8 million common shares outstanding.
