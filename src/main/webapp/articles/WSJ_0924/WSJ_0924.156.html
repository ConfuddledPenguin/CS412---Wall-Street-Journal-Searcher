Who's News: McCaw Cellular Communications Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

MCCAW CELLULAR COMMUNICATIONS Inc. (Kirkland, Wash.) -- Peter L.S. Currie was named chief financial officer of this cellular phone company. Mr. Currie, 34 years old, had been the company's senior vice president for finance. He succeeds Rufus W. Lumry, 43, who is leaving to become vice chairman of Pace Communications. Mr. Currie has been working at McCaw since 1989.
