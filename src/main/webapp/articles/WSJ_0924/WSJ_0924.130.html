Business Brief -- Helian Health Group Inc.: Medical-Services Firm Posts 40% Gain in 3rd-Period Net

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

MONTEREY, Calif. -- Helian Health Group Inc. reported a 40% increase in earnings for the third quarter ended Aug. 31, to $579,827, or 14 cents a share, from $413,557, or 15 cents a share, a year before, when fewer shares were outstanding. Sales at the medical-services company rose 31% to $4.6 million from $3.5 million.
