International Brief -- Coca-Cola Amatil Ltd.: Coke Affiliate to Sell Assets Of Its Leigh Mardon Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

Coca-Cola Amatil Ltd. of Australia said it agreed to sell the packaging division and communications and systems division of its unit, Leigh Mardon Pty., to DBSM Capital Partners Ltd. and Prudential Asset Management Asia Ltd. for 260 million Australian dollars ($US215 million). Coca-Cola Amatil makes almost all the Coca-Cola consumed in Australia and is 59.5% owned by Coca-Cola Co., Atlanta.

DBSM Capital Partners is a unit of Dominguez Barry Samuel Montagu Ltd., an Australian investment bank that is a unit of Midland Bank PLC.

Coca-Cola Amatil said it expects the sale of the Leigh Mardon units to be completed by Nov. 30, although the agreement to sell is subject to shareholder approval.
