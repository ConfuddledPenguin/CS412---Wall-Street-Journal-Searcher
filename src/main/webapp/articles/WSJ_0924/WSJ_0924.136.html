International Brief -- Aeritalia-Societa Aerospaziale: Aerospace Group Says Sales To Advance 17% in Year

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

Aeritalia-Societa Aerospaziale Italiana said consolidated group sales will rise 17% to 2.8 trillion lire ($2.38 billion) this year from 2.4 trillion lire in 1989. The Italian aerospace group also said consolidated sales grew 30% in the first half to 1.225 trillion lire from 940 billion lire in the 1989 first half.

Although it didn't provide earnings figures for the first half, Aeritalia said gross consolidated profit was above 35 billion lire, but down from 1989. The company blamed the downturn in the defense industry and the dollar's drop over the past year.

Aeritalia said new orders totaled 860 billion lire as of June 30, while total orders were valued at 5.5 trillion lire. Investments stood at 98 billion lire, up from 77 billion lire a year ago.
