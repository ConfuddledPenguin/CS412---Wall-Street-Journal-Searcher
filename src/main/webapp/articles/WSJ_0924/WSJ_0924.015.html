West Germany's Auto Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Auto production in West Germany climbed 16% in August to 309,000 from 266,784 a year earlier, boosted by a higher number of work days, but fell 2% when adjusted for the extra days, the West German Automobile Association said. Because holiday-related plant closings last year fell earlier in the summer than this year, there were 16.5 work days in August, up from 14 days in August 1989.
