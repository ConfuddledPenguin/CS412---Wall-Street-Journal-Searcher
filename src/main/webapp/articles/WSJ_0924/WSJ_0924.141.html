Business Brief -- Dentsu Inc.: Japan's Largest Ad Agency To Have Branch in Moscow

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

Dentsu Inc., Japan's largest advertising concern, said it will set up a branch office in Moscow. With the new office, which is scheduled to be established next Monday, Dentsu will become the first Japanese advertising company to create an office in the Soviet Union.

Although the advertising market in the Soviet Union is still in the developmental stage, Dentsu officials said, it has potential to allow advertising businesses to grow as the nation's economy shifts gears to a market base.

The new office will map out and collect information in the local advertising market and will provide Soviet enterprises with advertising know-how. The new office will also assist Japanese companies trying to penetrate the Soviet market.
