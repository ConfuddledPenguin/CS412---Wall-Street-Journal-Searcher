International Brief -- Bayer AG: Firm Is Cleared to Acquire Nova Corp.'s Polysar Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

West Germany's Bayer AG said it received permission from both Investment Canada and the West German antitrust authority, the Bundeskartellamt, to acquire the Polysar Rubber division of Nova Corp. of Alberta. Nova said in May it was selling the division to Bayer for 1.25 billion Canadian dollars (US$1.08 billion). All necessary approvals for the acquisition have now been received and closing is expected to take place in a few weeks. The U.S. Federal Trade Commission and Canada's Bureau of Competition Policy previously approved the acquisition.

After the acquisition, Polysar will assume responsibility for Bayer's rubber activities in North America under the name Polysar Rubber Corp., to be based in Toronto. Bayer said it intends to invest about C$120 million in Polysar's Canadian operations within the next three years. Research activities based at Sarnia, Ontario, are to be intensified.
