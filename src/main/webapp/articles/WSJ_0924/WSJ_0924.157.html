Kasler Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

SAN BERNARDINO, Calif. -- Kasler Corp. said it received a $43.4 million contract from the California Department of Transportation for another part of the Century Freeway in Los Angeles. The contract is the 10th Kasler has received on the freeway, which will stretch 18 miles from Los Angeles International Airport to Norwalk, Calif., when it is finished. Kasler is a contractor that specializes in heavy-construction work.
