PEPSICO Inc. short interest on the New York Stock Exchange in the month ended Sept. 14 was 4,183,239 shares, following a 3-for-1 stock split on Sept. 4. Friday's edition failed to note that the total for the month to mid-August, of 898,979 shares, was based on the old stock outstanding prior to the split. ---

UNDER AN AGREEMENT by which CalFed Inc. will sell its subsidiary, Beneficial Standard Life Insurance Co., to Conseco Inc., CalFed may purchase back from Beneficial $35 million of real estate assets acquired through foreclosure, as well as some nonperforming and other mortgage loans within three years. The period of time was misstated in Friday's edition.

(See: "CalFed Set to Sell Unit to Conseco Inc. for $140.2 Million" -- WSJ Sept. 21, 1990)
