International Brief -- Ciments Francais S.A.: Paris Cement Group Profit Rose 7% in the First Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

Ciments Francais S.A. said consolidated net profit after payments to minority interests rose 7% in the first half, despite a marked slowdown in activity in North America. The Paris cement group said profit was 402 million French francs ($76.2 million) in the first half, up from 377 million francs a year earlier.

Ciments Francais previously said consolidated revenue gained 30% to 7.29 billion francs from 5.63 billion francs, buoyed by strong gains from southern European activities.

Consolidated operating profit was up 27% to 1.18 billion francs from 928 million francs.

The company said that full-year net profit should rise from 1989's 1.1 billion francs on revenue of 12.5 billion francs, but didn't elaborate.

In the latest half, 57% of sales were in France. Cement products accounted for 49% of overall sales, while 51% came from construction materials.
