New Securities Issues

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

The following were among Friday's offerings and pricings in U.S. and non-U.S. capital markets, with terms and syndicate manager, as compiled by Dow Jones Capital Markets Report. CORPORATE

Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. of New York -- $100 million of 8 1/2% notes due Oct. 1, 1993, priced at 99.90 to yield 8.538%. The notes, which were priced at a spread of 32 basis points above the Treasury's three-year note, will be reoffered at variable prices. Rated triple-A by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and by Standard & Poor's Corp., the issue will be sold through Lehman Brothers.

MORTGAGE-BACKED

Merrill Lynch Trust 47 -- $300 million of Remic mortgage securities offered in nine classes by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets Inc. The offering is backed by Government National Mortgage Association 9% securities with a weighted average remaining term to maturity of 26.67 years. Among classes for which details were available, yields ranged from 8.87%, or 80 basis points above two-year Treasury securities, to 10.15%, or 112 basis points above 30-year Treasurys.

OTHER U.S.

Federal Farm Credit Banks -- $100 million of 8.87% notes due Oct. 10, 1995, priced at par. The notes, which are noncallable for one year, were priced at 30 basis points above the Treasury's five-year note through underwriters led by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

EUROBONDS

Score 1 Ltd. (special-purpose vehicle) -- 10 billion yen of 7.15% Eurobonds due May 11, 1993, at issue price of 101 3/8 via lead underwriter J.P. Morgan Securities Asia Ltd. Redemption linked to Japanese government bond futures. Fees 1 3/8.

Nippon Kasei Chemical Co. (Japan) -- 60 million Swiss francs of privately placed notes due Oct. 11, 1995, with equity-purchase warrants. Fixed 5 1/4% coupon at par via Swiss Bank Corp. Guarantee by Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank. Warrant exercise period Oct. 22, 1990, to Oct. 4, 1995. Terms fixed Oct. 4. Fees 1 1/2.
