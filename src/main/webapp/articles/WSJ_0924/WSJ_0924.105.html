Who's News: Members Service Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

MEMBERS SERVICE Corp. (Houston) -- William Lynton, 47 years old, was named chairman and chief executive of this mergers and acquisitions concern. Mr. Lynton was formerly a managing partner in a number of limited and general partnerships in Houston and Dallas. He succeeds Leon R. Hooten III, who resigned earlier this month after the company filed suit claiming he improperly appropriated corporate property. The two sides reached an agreement under which the company agreed to dismiss the lawsuit and repurchase two million shares held by Mr. Hooten. Members Service also said it elected Arthur S. Feher Jr., a New York consultant, to its board, succeeding James R. Beazle, who resigned from the board but who remains a vice president at the company.
