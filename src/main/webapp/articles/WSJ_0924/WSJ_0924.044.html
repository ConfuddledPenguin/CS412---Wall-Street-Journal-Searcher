Netherlands Posts Trade Surplus

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

AMSTERDAM -- The Dutch trade surplus in June amounted to 1.2 billion guilders ($676.1 million), as the Netherlands imported 19.3 billion guilders of goods and its exports totaled 20.5 billion guilders, the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics said. The June trade data represent a strong improvement from May, when the Netherlands posted a trade deficit of 900 million guilders, on imports of 20.5 billion guilders and exports of 19.6 billion guilders.

The June trade surplus also was an improvement from a year earlier, when the Netherlands had a deficit of 200 million guilders, with imports amounting to 20 billion guilders and exports of 19.8 billion guilders.
