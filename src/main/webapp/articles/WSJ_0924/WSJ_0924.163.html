Aztar Corp. Unit's Redemption

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Aztar Corp. said its Ramada New Jersey Holdings Corp. unit intends to redeem its 459,000 outstanding shares of convertible Class A preferred stock at $9 a share, the maximum allowed under the company's certificate of incorporation. The redemption will be effective as of Jan. 4, 1991, the gambling concern said.
