Business Brief -- Sundstrand Corp.: Accord Signed to Purchase Remainder of Milton Roy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

Sundstrand Corp. has entered into a definitive agreement to purchase the 85% of Milton Roy Co. that it doesn't already own, for $28 a share, or $128 million. The company, based in Rockford, Ill., said the purchase, which is subject to regulatory and shareholder approvals, is expected to close in December or in "early" January 1991. Sundstrand said Milton Roy will become a subsidiary after the aquisition is completed.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Sundstrand shares closed at $26.75, up $3.75. Milton Roy's shares fell 65.5 cents, closing at $23.25.

Milton Roy is a St. Petersburg, Fla.-based maker of instrumentation and control equipment. Sundstrand makes aerospace and industrial equipment.
