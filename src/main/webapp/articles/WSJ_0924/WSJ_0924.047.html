Marketing & Media: Foote, Cone & Belding

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

CHICAGO -- Foote, Cone & Belding said it named Mike Koelker to the new position of corporate director of creative development and elected him as an addition to the company's board of directors. Mr. Koelker, 52 years old, who joined the advertising agency in 1968, will continue in his current post as executive creative director of San Francisco-based operations, the company said.
