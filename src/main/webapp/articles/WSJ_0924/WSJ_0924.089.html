Alexander & Alexander Names Two to Posts Of Deputy Chairmen

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

NEW YORK -- Alexander & Alexander Services Inc. named William M. Wilson and Michael K. White deputy chairmen, as part of the newly created office of the chairman. Mr. Wilson, 53 years old, will still be responsible for the European operations of the insurance brokerage concern, from his office in London. Mr. White, 51, will continue to head the company's North American and South American operations.

Chairman T.H. Irvin said he decided to create the new management team to better coordinate the concern's world-wide operations, which now account for 50% of its revenue and employee base.
