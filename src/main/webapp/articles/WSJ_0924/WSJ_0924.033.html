CSC Appoints Director

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

WARREN, Ohio -- CSC Industries Inc. said Kanji Tomita, executive vice president of Daido Steel Co., was appointed a director. He succeeds Takanori Yamamoto, also a Daido executive vice president, who resigned because of a realignment of his duties at Daido. CSC Industries is the parent company of Copperweld Steel Co., which produces carbon and alloy steel bars.
