Everybody Anonymous?

Support groups are helping dieters

And those now troubled by drink;

And some involved in marriages

That teeter on the brink.

As these groups seem to multiply quickly,

A new one's needed, no doubt,

To support those not belonging

And feeling sadly left out.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
