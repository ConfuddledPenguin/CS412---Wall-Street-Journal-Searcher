Who's News: CSX Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

CSX Corp. (Richmond, Va.) -- John L. Sweeney was named executive vice president of CSX Transportation Inc., a Jacksonville, Fla., subsidiary of this freight transportation company. CSX Transportation provides rail transport and distribution services. Mr. Sweeney, 62 years old, will be responsible primarily for rail labor negotiations. He had been senior vice president of corporate services.
