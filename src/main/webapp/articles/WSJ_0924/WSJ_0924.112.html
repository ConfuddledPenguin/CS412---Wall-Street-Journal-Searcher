Letters to the Editor: Billie Jean and Bobby

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

Regarding Frederick C. Klein's Sept. 4 Leisure & Arts article on the U.S. Tennis Open, he had one date wrong. The famous match between Billie Jean King and Bobby Riggs was on Sept. 20, 1973, not Sept. 30. Charles A. Schmidt

San Antonio, Texas
