Canada Slates New Issue Of 12-Year Savings Bonds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government said it will start selling on Oct. 18 a new issue of 12-year Canada Savings Bonds. The interest rate to be paid on the new bonds and the purchase limit will be announced in mid-October. Sales may be terminated at any time at the government's discretion.

Only Canadian residents can buy the bonds, which can be redeemed at any time at face value. The government currently has 34.68 billion Canadian dollars (US$30.1 billion) of Canada Savings Bonds outstanding.
