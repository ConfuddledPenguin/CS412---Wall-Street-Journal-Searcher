Dividend News: Enserch Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

ENSERCH Corp., a Dallas-based energy concern, said it declared a special dividend of one share of its Pool Energy Service Co. unit's common stock for each 32.4 shares of Enserch common held. The dividend is payable Nov. 15 to stock of record Oct. 10. Holders entitled to fewer than 100 shares of Pool common may opt for cash, in lieu of the stock, through a selling arrangement.
