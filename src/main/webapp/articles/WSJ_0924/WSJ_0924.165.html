Cintas Sets Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

CINCINNATI -- Cintas Corp. said it agreed to form a joint venture with Toronto's Cadet Uniform Rental Inc. The agreement marks the Cincinnati uniform concern's first venture outside the U.S. Closely held Cadet, which rents uniforms, has annual sales of about $24 million. Cintas has annual sales of about $285 million.
