Southern Union Hires Adviser

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

AUSTIN, Texas -- Southern Union Co. said it hired Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. to advise it on the possible sale of its Southern Union Exploration Co. unit as a means of enhancing shareholder value. The natural gas concern said the advisers will consider various alternatives for the unit, including a sale of existing production and reserves.
