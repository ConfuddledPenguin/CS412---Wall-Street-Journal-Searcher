Belgian Consumer Price Index

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

BRUSSELS -- Fueled by surging energy prices, Belgium's preliminary unadjusted consumer price index rose by 1.32 points, to 145.93 points, in September, the Ministry of Economy said. The government said inflation in the latest 12 months was 3.69%.

Rising petroleum product prices accounted for 0.96 point of the total rise, the ministry said.
