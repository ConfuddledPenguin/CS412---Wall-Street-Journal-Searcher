Angeion to Sell Stock to Hanrow

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Angeion Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement to sell 500,000 shares of its unregistered stock at $3.35 a share to Hanrow Financial Group Ltd. Angeion, a maker of medical products, said the 500,000 shares represent about 6% of its stock. The company said it expects the transaction to be completed by "the end" of September.

As reported earlier, Hanrow, a merchant banking investment partnership, signed a letter of interest July 31 for the purchase.
