REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: The ACLU Zoo

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

School days are upon us, as well as the threat of lawsuits by the American Civil Liberties Union to undermine school order and discipline. A student showed up at East Islip High School on Long Island wearing a T-shirt inscribed with the legend, "Adolf Hitler European Tour 1939-1945." He removed it under threat of suspension, and went to the ACLU. Attorney David Jampol threatened suit, declaring, "This is definitely a free speech issue." We think school officials should be allowed to run the schools. To preserve and display every species of free speech, the ACLU could always open a zoo.
