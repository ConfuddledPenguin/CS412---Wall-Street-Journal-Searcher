Caesars World Tender Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

LOS ANGELES -- Caesars World Inc. extended its $22-a-share tender offer for the 2,175,945 shares of Caesars New Jersey Inc. it doesn't already own to Oct. 17 from last Friday. The offer was initially set to expire in August. Caesar's World, a hotel and casino operator, has agreed in principle to make an additional payment to Caesars New Jersey shareholders of 25 cents a share as part of a legal settlement brought by some holders of the latter. Final court approval isn't expected for at least two months.
