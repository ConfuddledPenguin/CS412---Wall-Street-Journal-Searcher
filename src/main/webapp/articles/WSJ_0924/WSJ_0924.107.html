United Technologies Gets $167.7 Million Of Air Force Work

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

WASHINGTON -- United Technologies Corp. received $167.7 million in Air Force contracts for Minuteman missile rocket motors and jet-engine parts.

American Telephone & Telegraph Co. was awarded a $96 million Navy contract for telecommunications services.

Teledyne Inc. got a $53.1 million Air Force contract for target drones.

General Electric Co. received $47.5 million in contracts for Navy aircraft electronics and Army turbine engines.

Lockheed Corp. got a $42.5 million Air Force contract for support of space operations.

BP Oil Co., a unit of BP America Inc., received an $18.7 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. got an $18.4 million Air Force contract for aircraft radar.

ITT Corp. was awarded a $14.6 million Army contract for countermeasures sets.

Cable & Computer Technology Inc. received a $13.9 million Navy contract for aircraft computers.

Gunver Manufacturing Co. got a $12.2 million Air Force contract for jet-engine parts.

Motorola Inc. received an $11.1 million Navy contract for communications equipment.

Mason & Hanger-Silas Mason Co. got a $10.1 million Army contract for operating an ammunition plant.
