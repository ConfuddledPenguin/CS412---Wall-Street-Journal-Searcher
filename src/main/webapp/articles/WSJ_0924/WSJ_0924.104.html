Computer Task Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

WASHINGTON -- World Software Group B.V. of the Netherlands said it boosted its stake in Computer Task Group Inc., Buffalo, N.Y., to 14.66% of the common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, World Software said it holds 1,184,300 Computer Task common shares, including 270,000 shares bought on Sept. 12 for $9.09 each.

No reason was given for the boost in the stake.
