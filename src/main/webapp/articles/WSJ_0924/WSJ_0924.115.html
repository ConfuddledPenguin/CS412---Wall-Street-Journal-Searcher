Business Brief -- Computer Factory Inc.: Board Gets Takeover Bid From Bay Street Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

A spokesman for Computer Factory Inc. confirmed that it received a $4-a-share takeover proposal from a San Francisco merchant banking firm and said the board is reviewing the plan. Directors of the Elmsford, N.Y., personal-computer retailer "undoubtedly will take up" the proposal from Bay Street Group "before any regularly scheduled meeting," the spokesman said.

"There may be an informal {directors} meeting early next week, but it won't include the full board," the spokesman added. The takeover proposal, contained in a letter to Computer Factory's board, is valued at more than $34 million.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, Computer Factory closed at $3.375, up 25 cents.
