Business Brief -- Nichols Institute: Company Indicates Earnings Will Be Up This Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

Nichols Institute said earnings for its third quarter, ending Sunday, could be 10% lower than the highest projection published by analysts. The medical diagnostic testing company said it was referring to an estimate of $2.4 million, or 16 cents a share. In the year-ago quarter, the San Juan Capistrano, Calif., company earned $1.3 million, or 11 cents a share, on revenue of $32.8 million.

The company said it had unexpected difficulties in consolidating the operations of Physicians Medical Laboratories, a company it acquired in January of this year, with its existing Medlab Inc. operation in Portland, Ore.

Dr. Albert L. Nichols, president, chief executive officer and chairman, said the company has terminated several managers from Physicians Medical Laboratories and installed new managers from within Nichols.
