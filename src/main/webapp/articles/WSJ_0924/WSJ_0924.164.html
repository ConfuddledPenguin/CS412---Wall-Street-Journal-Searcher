Matsushita Electric Grill

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/24/90

TOKYO -- Matsushita Electric Industrial Co. is introducing a microwave grill oven equipped with "fuzzy logic" technology next month, the company said. The new oven lets consumers cook without the tedious process of setting the temperature and estimating food volume and roasting conditions, it said.

Matsushita intends to produce the NEF600 grill oven at a rate of 15,000 units a month.
