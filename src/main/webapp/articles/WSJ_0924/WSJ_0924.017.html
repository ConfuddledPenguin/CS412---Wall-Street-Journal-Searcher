Fleishman-Hillard Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

ST. LOUIS -- Fleishman-Hillard, a public relations firm, said it has formed a joint venture in Belgium with Information & Enterprise, a consulting firm in Paris. The new company, F-H & Associates S.A., opened its doors this month. Fleishman-Hillard owns 51% of the joint venture and Information & Entreprise 49%.

Fleishman-Hillard, in addition to offices in several U.S. cities, has offices in London and Paris and operates throughout Asia. It took in $38 million in fees in 1989.
