National Intergroup Reduces Cash Price In Its Tender Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

PITTSBURGH -- National Intergroup Inc. said it reduced its cash purchase price in its tender offer for its sole general partner, Permian Partners Limited Partnership, to $3.5625 from $3.85 per outstanding cumulative convertible preference unit, subject to adjustment. The reduction reflects the recently declared cash distribution of 28.75 cents a preference unit for the third quarter. The $3.5625 cash portion of the purchase price will be subject to further reduction to reflect any distributions as of a record date subsequent to Oct. 1 and before completion of the offer.

National Intergroup, an oil and drug distribution and metals concern, also extended the tender offer from midnight EDT tomorrow to midnight EDT Oct. 5.

As of the close of business on Thursday, approximately 7.4 million preference units had been tendered.

The offer is conditioned upon, among other things, the tender of at least 7,875,001 of the publicly held preference units representing a majority of the publicly held preference units, the company said.
