International Brief -- Electrolux AB: Commercial Services Unit Is Put on Auction Block

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/24/90

Electrolux AB of Sweden, an appliance concern, put up for sale its commercial services division, in an effort to concentrate on its core operations of making and selling consumer and industrial equipment. It didn't disclose an asking price. The commercial services unit, which has 30,000 employees world-wide in environmental cleaning and janitorial services, had 1989 revenue of 3.9 billion kronor ($675.1 million), and profit after depreciation of 260 million kronor, the company said. It accounts for only about 5% of Electrolux's business. Electrolux said the division's profit and revenue for this year are expected to increase.

An official, explaining the decision to sell, said the business "differs markedly in character from other areas" of the company. The unit's sale will mean that "the capital which will be released will increase our freedom of action in future developments in our main areas," the official said.
