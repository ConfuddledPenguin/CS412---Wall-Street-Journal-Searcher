Colombia Inflation Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

BOGOTA, Colombia -- Colombia's inflation index increased 2.89% in March and climbed a total of 10.7% for the first quarter of the year, according to the National Department of Statistics. The price increases were above those anticipated by the government, which hopes to obtain an inflation index below the 26% registered in 1989.

The largest price increases in March were posted in health services, including medication.
