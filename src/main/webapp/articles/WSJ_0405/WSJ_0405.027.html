James River Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- James River Corp. said it completed the sale of its nonwoven products division to Holzstoff Holding AG of Switzerland for about $175 million. The nonwoven products division, which makes materials for diapers and hospital clothing, was sold as part of James River's refocusing on its core paper businesses.

The unit had sales of about $90 million for the first nine months of fiscal 1990, which began May 1.
