Who's News: National Mercantile Bancorp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

NATIONAL MERCANTILE BANCORP. (Los Angeles) -- Terry T. Tomich resigned as president, chief executive officer and a director because of "philosophical disagreements" with the board, the bank holding company said. Mr. Tomich, 49 years old, had been president and chief executive officer since December 1982. Howard P. Ladd, the company's 68-year-old chairman, will serve as interim chief executive until a permanent successor is named.
