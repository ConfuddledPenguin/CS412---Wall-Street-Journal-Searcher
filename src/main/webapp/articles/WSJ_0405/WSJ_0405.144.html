Business Brief -- Pinnacle West Capital Corp.: Utility Company Sells Hotel To Unit of Kabuto Decom

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

Pinnacle West Capital Corp., Phoenix, Ariz., in a long-awaited transaction, said it sold its Wigwam resort hotel for $70 million to a unit of Kabuto Decom Inc., a Japanese construction and real estate concern. Pinnacle West Capital, an electric utility holding company whose ill-starred diversification efforts led it to the edge of bankruptcy last year, has had the resort on the auction block for about a year.

The transaction, at a price somewhat lower than suggested by analysts, is also apparently subject to substantial performance guarantees by Pinnacle West that the resort will live up to expectations.

Under the sales agreement, Pinnacle West's SunCor Development Co. real estate unit will continue to manage the property. SunCor has guaranteed certain financial performance at the resort and must make payments back to Kabuto if those goals aren't met, John Ogden, president and chief executive officer of SunCor said. He declined, however, to elaborate on the guarantees.

Mr. Ogden said the property has a book value of just over $50 million, indicating a gain on the sale.
