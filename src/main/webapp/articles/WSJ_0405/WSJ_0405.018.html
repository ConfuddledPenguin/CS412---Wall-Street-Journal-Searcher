Canadian Retail Sales Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian retail sales rose 2% in January, led by an upswing in motor vehicle sales, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The January gain from December followed three months of sluggish retail business.

Sales by motor vehicle dealers rose 6.8% in January following a 0.5% increase in December. Sales by auto parts and service stores increased 5.7%.
