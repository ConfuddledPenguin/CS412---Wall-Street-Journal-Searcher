Sevenson Gets Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

NIAGARA FALLS, N.Y. -- Sevenson Environmental Services Inc. said it received two contracts totaling $15.5 million for the cleanup of industrial waste sites in New Jersey. One contract valued at $13.4 million is for decontamination and partial demolition of a facility at Manville, N.J., previously operated by a unit of Manville Corp., Denver.

The second contract, for $2.1 million, is for excavation and off-site disposal of contaminated soil from a former industrial site operated by Youngs Rubber Co. at Trenton, N.J.
