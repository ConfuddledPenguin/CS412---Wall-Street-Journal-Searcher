Hewlett-Packard Tokyo Lab

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Hewlett-Packard Co. said it established a research laboratory in Tokyo. The Japanese laboratory will focus on applied research in integrated circuit testing, photonics and low-temperature electronic devices. Most of the research staff will be hired locally, Hewlett-Packard said. The staff will work with other company scientists and engineers, as well as visiting scientists and professors, to develop technology for Hewlett-Packard.

The Tokyo center is part of an expansion of Hewlett-Packard's research activities. Last year, the company established a new science center in the U.S., and one in Italy.
