Knowledge Data Signs Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

SALT LAKE CITY -- Knowledge Data Systems Inc. said it signed an agreement to sell as much as $35 million in hardware and software to Kaiser Permanente's Northern California region. The region contains 14 hospitals and 27 outpatient medical offices that serve about 2.2 million people. Knowledge Data said it will install its patient care management system and other systems in Kaiser facilities over a five-year period.
