UAL's United Airlines Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

CHICAGO -- UAL Corp. said March traffic at its United Airlines unit climbed 4.5% to 6.31 billion revenue passenger miles from 6.04 billion miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The carrier's load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, dropped in March to 67.7% from the year-earlier 68.8%.

International traffic increased 27% in the latest month, while traffic at its much-larger domestic segment slipped 1.4%.

For the year to date, United's traffic was 17.07 billion revenue passenger miles, up 6.4% from 16.04 billion miles a year earlier. Load factor for the year to date was 64.9%, up from 63.5%.

United's international traffic increased 32% in the latest year-to-date, while its domestic traffic was essentially flat.
