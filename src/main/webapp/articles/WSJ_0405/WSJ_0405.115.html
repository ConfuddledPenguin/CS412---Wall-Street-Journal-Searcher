Technology Brief -- Victor Co. of Japan: Small, Light Video System Using Super VHS Unveiled

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

Victor Co. of Japan Ltd. has designed what it says is the world's smallest and lightest super VHS video system. The new system, called the VHS Movie Compo, is priced at 288,000 yen ($1,806.34) a set. It will be released on Japan's domestic market May 16, JVC said Wednesday.

A company official said the new system is composed of five major parts: an S-VHS compact video deck, a video camera, liquid crystal display monitor, TV tuner, and rechargeable battery.

Because it uses a newly developed lightweight thin mechanism, the S-VHS video recorder is 20% lighter and 40% smaller than conventional models, yet offers high-fidelity sound, the company said.

JVC plans to offer the new system in the U.S. this summer and in Europe in the near future.
