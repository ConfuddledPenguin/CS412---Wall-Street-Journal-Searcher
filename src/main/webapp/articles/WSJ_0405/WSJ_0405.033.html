Qume Corp. Offer Extended

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

MILPITAS, Calif. -- Qume Corp. said a previously reported offer for the company has been extended. The group making the offer, Qume Chairman David Lee and Wearnes Technology (Pte.) Ltd., a Singapore investment firm, hasn't set an expiration date. The offer originally was made earlier this year.

Qume, a maker of computer products, is considering the $6.50-a-share offer, which values the company at roughly $50 million.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Qume shares rose 12.5 cents to close at $6.125.
