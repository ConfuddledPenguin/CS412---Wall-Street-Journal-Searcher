Who's News: British Aerospace Commercial Aircraft Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

BRITISH AEROSPACE (COMMERCIAL AIRCRAFT) Ltd. (Hertfordshire, England) -- Robert McKinlay was named managing director of this British aerospace company. Mr. McKinlay, who is currently managing director, Airbus division of British Aerospace (Commercial Aircraft), succeeds Maurice Dixon, who is leaving the company to take up a senior appointment outside British Aerospace.
