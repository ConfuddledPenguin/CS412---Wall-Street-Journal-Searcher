O'Brien Energy Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

PHILADELPHIA -- O'Brien Energy Systems said its subsidiary, O'Brien Fuels Inc., acquired 76% of Enercol Energy Ltd. of the U.K. Terms weren't disclosed.

Lancashire Enterprise Ltd., a British government venture capital fund, owns the balance of Enercol, which is involved in landfill gas project development and compressed natural gas for vehicle fuel.
