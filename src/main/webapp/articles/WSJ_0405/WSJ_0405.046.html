Resolution Funding Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

WASHINGTON -- The Resolution Funding Corp. said it plans to sell $3.5 billion of 40-year bonds next Tuesday. Proceeds will be used to finance the overhaul of the nation's savings-and-loan industry. The new bonds will be dated April 17 and mature April 15, 2030.

Tenders for the bonds, available in minimum denominations of $1,000, must be received by 1 p.m. Tuesday, April 10, at Federal Reserve Banks.
