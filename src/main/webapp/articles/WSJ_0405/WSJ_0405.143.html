Business Brief -- United Technologies Inc.: Unit Gets Engine Orders Of $59 Million From China

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

United Technologies Inc.'s Pratt & Whitney unit said it received orders valued at $59 million to supply 20 JT8D-200 aircraft engines to the Chinese government. It also received an option for 20 more, bringing the potential value of the order to $118 million. The engines will power McDonnell Douglas MD-80 twinjets that the Chinese will produce jointly with St. Louis-based McDonnell Douglas Corp.

The engine order resulted from Shanghai Aviation Industrial Corp. of Shanghai and China National Aero-Technology Import & Export Corp.'s agreement with McDonnell Douglas to jointly build 10 MD-80 twinjets, with an option for 10 more. Pratt & Whitney's JT8D-200 engine is the exclusive power plant for McDonnell Douglas MD-80 series jets.

United Technologies, based in Hartford, Conn., is an aerospace and industrial products concern.
