West German Unemployment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West Germany's unemployment rate fell to 7.7% of the labor force in March from 8.2% in February, the Federal Labor Office said. The decline in unemployment to 2,013,057 in March was slightly steeper than expected. An AP-Dow Jones survey of West German economists had called for March unemployment to be 7.8%.
