Chicago Exchange Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

CHICAGO -- Futures contracts on the one-month London Interbank Offer Rate (Libor) begin trading today on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange. The contract is designed to help banks and other institutions better manage short-term interest rate risks. Like the Merc's three-month Eurodollar futures contract, the Libor contract will be cash settled. The last trading day will be two London business days preceding the third Wednesday of the contract month.
