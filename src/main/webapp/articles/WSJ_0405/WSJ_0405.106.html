Bank of America Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- BankAmerica Corp.'s Bank of America subsidiary said it was awarded a contract from Severn River Crossing Ltd. to arrange $865 million for a bridge project in the U.K. Bank of America said it is lead underwriter for the financing, which involves bank debt jointly underwritten by Lloyds Bank PLC of Britain and Caisse Nationale de Credit Agricole of France. Proceeds will be used to construct a second bridge across the Severn river, and to refinance current debt on the existing bridge. The new bridge will link Avon in the west of England to Gwent in Wales, and will become part of Britain's M4 motorway.

Severn River Crossing was established to hold a 30-year concession to design and build the new bridge, and operate it along with the new bridge.
