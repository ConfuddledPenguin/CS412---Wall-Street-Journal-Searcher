McDermott Unit Gets Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

NEW ORLEANS -- McDermott Marine Construction, a unit of McDermott International Inc., received a $250 million contract to design and build 15 offshore sulfur-mining platforms for Freeport-McMoRan Resource Partners. Freeport-McMoRan Resource Partners is a limited partnership and an affiliate of Freeport-McMoRan Inc.

The platforms are to be installed in the Gulf of Mexico, off the coast of Louisiana. The contract also calls for construction of 13 bridges, a power-plant module and related facilities. Fabrication will be at McDermott's fabrication yards in Bayou Boeuf and New Iberia, La., as well as at the company's Gulfport, Miss., shipyard.
