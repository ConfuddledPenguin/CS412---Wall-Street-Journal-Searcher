Business Brief -- Amre Inc.: Management Group to Bid For Shares It Doesn't Own

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

Amre Inc., Dallas, said a management group expects to propose an acquisition of the shares of the company it doesn't already own for $8.75 a share, subject to financing and certain other conditions. The company, which markets home-improvement products under the Sears, Roebuck & Co. name, said a special committee of its board had met with the group, which consists of Steven D. Bedowitze, president; Robert Levin, executive vice president; and Ronald I. Wagner, senior vice president.

Collectively, the management group already owns about 56% of Amre's common stock outstanding, the company said.

Amre said a special committee of outside directors would evaluate any offer that is made, but a company spokesman declined to discuss details or say when an offer might be expected.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Amre closed at $7.875, down 37.5 cents.
