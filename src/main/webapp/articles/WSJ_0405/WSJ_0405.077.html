Financing Business: Canadian Tire Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

CANADIAN TIRE Corp. said it plans to issue 150 million Canadian dollars (US$128.7 million) of 12.1% debentures due 2010. The Toronto-based retailer said Wood Gundy Inc., Gordon Capital Corp. and Burns Fry Ltd. have agreed to purchase the issue around May 10. The company said it would file a preliminary prospectus with all the Canadian provinces in the next few days. The company said net proceeds will be used to repay short-term debt and to fund capital expenditures.
