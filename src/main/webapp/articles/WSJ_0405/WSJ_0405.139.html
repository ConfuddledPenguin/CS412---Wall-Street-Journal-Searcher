Business Brief -- Air Products & Chemicals Inc.: Unit Gets Options on Stake Of 5.9% in Spanish Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

Air Products & Chemicals Inc. said a subsidiary acquired options on a 5.9% stake of Carburos Metalicos S.A., which, if exercised, would boost Air Products' share of the Spanish industrial gas and metal concern to about 30%. The Allentown, Pa., company said that the cost and exercise price of the options, which are exercisable over the next four years, would involve a transaction of about $75 million at current exchange rates. Air Products received immediate voting rights for the optioned shares.

An Air Products spokesman said the options agreement is part of the company's strategic expansion in Europe.

In connection with the agreement, Ron Sullam, a vice president with Air Products Europe, was named senior vice chairman of Carburos Metalicos. Two more Air Products representatives are expected to be added to the Spanish concern's board, bringing Air Products' representation to four members.
