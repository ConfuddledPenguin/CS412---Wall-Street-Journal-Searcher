Treasury Sets the Sale Of Seven-Year Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $2.68 billion in new cash with the sale Wednesday of about $7.5 billion in seven-year notes to redeem $4.83 billion in maturing notes. The new notes will mature April 15, 1997.

Tenders for the notes, available in minimum $1,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Wednesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
