Arkla Exploration Tests Well

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

SHREVEPORT, La. -- Arkla Exploration Co. said it tested an oil and gas well in southwestern Mississippi. The independent oil and natural gas producer said its Neyland Heirs No. 1 well flowed at a daily rate of 360 barrels of oil and 458,000 cubic feet of natural gas from depths of between 12,041 and 12,051 feet.
