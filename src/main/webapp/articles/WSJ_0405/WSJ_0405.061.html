Who's News: Braintree Savings Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

BRAINTREE SAVINGS BANK (Braintree, Mass.) -- Donald C. Olson, chief operating officer of this savings bank, was named to the additional post of president. Mr. Olson, 46 years old, succeeds Winthrop Sargent IV, 55, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer.
