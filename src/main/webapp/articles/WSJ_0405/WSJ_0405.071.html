Laidlaw Agrees to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

BURLINGTON, Ontario -- Laidlaw Inc. said it has agreed to sell its Byers Transport Ltd. unit to Herb Assman, president of Northern Interior FastFrate Ltd. of Prince George, British Columbia, for $19 million. A Laidlaw spokeswoman said Byers no longer fits in with the company's core businesses of waste management and passenger transport.
