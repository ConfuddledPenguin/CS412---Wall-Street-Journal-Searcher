Who's News: Angio-Medical Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

ANGIO-MEDICAL Corp. (New York) -- Robert S. Sinn, founder of this pharmaceuticals and cosmetics company, resigned as chairman and chief executive officer to start up a private company. Mr. Sinn, 59 years old, also resigned as a director. W. Frederick Hymes, 53, will succeed Mr. Sinn and will continue as president and chief operating officer. John D. Douros Jr., 60, a pharmaceuticals consultant, was elected to succeed Mr. Sinn as one of the company's five directors.
