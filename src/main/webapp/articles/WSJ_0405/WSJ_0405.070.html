Who's News: Petro-Canada Names Stanford to Two Posts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

CALGARY, Alberta -- James M. Stanford, 52 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of Petro-Canada Inc., succeeding Edward Lakusta, 60, who is retiring. Mr. Stanford, an engineer who joined the state-owned oil and natural gas concern in 1978 after 19 years at Mobil Corp.'s Canadian unit, is currently president of Petro-Canada Resources, a company division. He will report to Wilbert Hopper, PetroCanada's chairman and chief executive officer.

The Canadian government recently announced plans to proceed next year with an initial public offering of 15% of Petro-Canada, with assets of more than 8.5 billion Canadian dollars (US$7.3 billion).
