Canada to Hold Bond Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government announced plans to auction 900 million Canadian dollars (US$770.85 million) of 11.75% bonds due Oct. 15, 1993, on April 10. Proceeds of the issue will be applied toward the redemption of C$2.13 billion of government bonds maturing May 1.
