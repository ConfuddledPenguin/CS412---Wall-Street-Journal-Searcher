Bombardier Unit Gets Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

MONTREAL -- Bombardier Inc. said its Belgian subsidiary, BN Constructions Ferroviaires et Metaliques S.A., and Holec N.V. of the Netherlands were awarded a contract of 44 million Canadian dollars (US$38 million) by HTM, which operates a public transit system in The Hague, Netherlands. The contract is for 34 double-articulated light rail vehicles, which will be manufactured by BN in Belgium and use Holec's direct-current traction equipment. Deliveries are to begin in 1992. Bombardier is a Montreal-based transportation, defense and aerospace concern.
