Avondale Industries Wins Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

WASHINGTON -- Avondale Industries Inc. was issued a $104.4 million Navy contract for an oceanographic survey ship.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. received awards of $18.2 million for Army helicopter parts and Air Force jet engine flight tests.

General Electric Co. got a $17 million Navy pact for submarine communications.

Israel Aircraft Industries Ltd. was awarded a $10.1 million Navy contract for a helicopter night targeting system.
