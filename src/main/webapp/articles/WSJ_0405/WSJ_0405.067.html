Carolco Pictures Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

LOS ANGELES -- Carolco Pictures Inc., an entertainment concern, said that 94% of the units of Vista Organization Partnership Limited Partnership were tendered under Carolco's exchange offer. Carolco offered $2.45 in Carolco units for each Vista partnership unit. Each Carolco unit consists of a $1,000 face amount 14% senior note due 1993 and 30 Carolco warrants for common stock. Each warrant entitles its holder to purchase one Carolco common share at $11.25 until June 1, 1993.

Vista is an inactive movie financing concern.
