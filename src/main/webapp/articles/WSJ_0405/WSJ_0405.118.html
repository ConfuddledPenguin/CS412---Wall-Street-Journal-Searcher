Who's News: TSB Group PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

TSB GROUP PLC (London) -- The following are named as executive directors of this U.K. banking and financial services group: Hamish Donaldson, chief executive, corporate banking, TSB Bank PLC; Peter Ellwood, chief executive, retail banking, and Sir Richard Lloyd, chairman, Hill Samuel Bank Ltd.
