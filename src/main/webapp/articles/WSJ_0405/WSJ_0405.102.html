Business Brief -- Upjohn Co.: Clearance Is Set to Market Regaine to Women in U.K.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/05/90

Upjohn Co. said it received approval from the United Kingdom department of health to market Regaine Topical Solution in the U.K. to women with thinning hair. Regaine is called Rogaine in the U.S. and has been sold in the U.S. as a prescription-only anti-baldness drug for men since October 1988. Upjohn, of Kalamazoo, Mich., last September asked the U.S. Food and Drug Administration for approval to market Rogaine to women in the U.S. and is still awaiting a decision.

Rogaine, a 2% solution of minoxidil, is currently available for men in 63 countries and for women in 20.
