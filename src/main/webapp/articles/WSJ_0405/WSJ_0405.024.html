American Magnetics Name Change

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/05/90

LOS ANGELES -- American Magnetics Corp. stockholders approved the company's proposed name change to Damon Group Inc. Last year, through Nomad Partners L.P., American Magnetics acquired Damon Corp., a Needham Heights, Mass., biotechnology and medical laboratory concern, which is now its largest principal business.

American Magnetics is a biotechnology and medical laboratory and computer products concern.
