Letters to the Editor: Citicorp Pension Fund

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Your Nov. 19 Heard on the Street column said USF&G Corp., in its efforts to expand during the 1980s, ". . . bought mutual funds and Citicorp's pension fund business." While USF&G did buy Citicorp's domestic institutional investment management business, it did not buy our pension-fund business. Citibank remains one of the largest trustees and custodians for private and public pension funds. Christina Loddo Epstein

Director of Marketing

Citicorp

Long Island City, N.Y.
