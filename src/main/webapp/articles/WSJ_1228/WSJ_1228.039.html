Marketing & Media: Walt Disney Hires Magazine Executive For Publishing Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

BURBANK, Calif. -- Walt Disney Co. said it hired a top New York-based magazine executive as part of a larger push into publishing magazines and comics. John Skipper, who had been president and publishing director of Spin magazine, has been named vice president of magazine publishing at Disney Publishing, a unit of Disney. Mr. Skipper, before joining Spin last year, was a top executive for 10 years at Straight Arrow Publishers Inc.'s US and Rolling Stone magazines.

At Disney, Mr. Skipper is expected to help develop products for a growing line of Disney publications. Disney became a publisher only in recent years, and has developed or licensed publications based largely on its cartoon characters.

Mr. Skipper will oversee the recently launched Disney Adventures, a digest-sized monthly magazine for children seven to 14 years old. He also will oversee Disney's domestic magazine licensing activity, including the Mickey Mouse magazine, which is published by Welsh Publishing Group Inc.
