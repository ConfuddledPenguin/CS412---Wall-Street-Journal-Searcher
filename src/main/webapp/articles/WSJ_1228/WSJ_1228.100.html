International Brief -- Albert Fisher Group PLC: Some of Campbell Soup's Lines in Europe Acquired

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Albert Fisher Group PLC of Britain, a food distribution company, said it bought the European frozen-vegetable and seafood operations of Campbell Soup Co., Camden, N.J., for #15 million ($28.2 million). Albert Fisher said the purchase covers Campbell's Freshbake frozen-vegetable operations in Britain and its seafood operations in Britain and Belgium.

Campbell's chief executive officer, David Johnson, said, "This sale results from our strategic decision to focus our European efforts on branded value-added prepared foods." Campbell said the sale also is a major step in the restructuring and divestiture program the company announced earlier this year.

Albert Fisher said the acquisition is an important step in developing the company's European food-processing and distribution operations. It said Campbell's frozen-vegetable assets, when combined with Albert Fisher's operations, will form the largest frozen-vegetable business in Britain. Money for the purchase came from cash reserves, Albert Fisher said.
