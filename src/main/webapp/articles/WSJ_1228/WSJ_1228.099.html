INTEREST RATES on corporate underpayments of taxes under the new tax act will increase in 1991 to about 13% from 11%. The current year's rate was misstated in Wednesday's Tax Report column, which also misstated the excise tax proportion, 2%, of total taxes collected by the Internal Revenue Service in the 12 months ended June 30. (See: "Tax Report: A Special Summary and Forecast Of Federal and State Tax Developments" -- WSJ Dec. 26, 1990)

ITOMAN & Co.'s agreement to sell its 88.22% stake in Itoman Total Housing Corp. will reduce Itoman's debt on a consolidated basis by 196.2 billion yen ($1.4 billion). The size of the debt reduction was misstated in yesterday's edition.

(See: "International Brief -- Itoman & Co.: Stake in Housing Concern To Be Sold for $66 Million" -- WSJ Dec. 27, 1990)
