Business Brief -- Tokio Marine & Fire Insurance: Stake of 9.9% Is Acquired In U S West Insurer Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Financial Security Assurance Holdings Ltd. said Tokio Marine & Fire Insurance Co. of Japan purchased 9.9% of its shares in a transaction valued at nearly $49 million. The financial guaranty insurance company had been wholly owned by U S West Capital Corp., a business unit of U S West Inc., a telecommunications company based in Englewood, Colo. As part of the agreement, Tokio Marine and Financial Security Assurance will market financial guaranty insurance in Japan. Tokio Marine, the largest and oldest property-casualty insurance company in Japan, also will have a seat on the Financial Security Assurance board.

The transaction marks an attempt by both companies to take advantage of Japan's expanding asset-backed securities market. The deal also represents the first time that a Japanese insurer, other than a life insurance company, has invested in a financial guarantor, a Financial Security Assurance spokeswoman said.
