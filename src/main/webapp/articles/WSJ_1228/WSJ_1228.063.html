Who's News: Lam Research Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

LAM RESEARCH Corp. (Fremont, Calif.) -- Roger Emerick, the chief executive officer of this semiconductor equipment supply concern, will temporarily assume the position of president following the death of Carl A. Kountz, the president and chief operating officer. Mr. Kountz, 45 years old, died on Dec. 24 after a brief bout with cancer. The company said it would seek an executive outside the company to fill Mr. Kountz's position as a director.
