Clinton Gas Sets Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Clinton Gas Systems Inc. said it will repurchase in the open market as much as 5%, or 308,000 shares, of the company's common shares outstanding. The company, which has interests in natural gas, oil and energy, also said that it purchased a 42-mile natural gas pipeline system in Ashtabula County, Ohio, which will be attached to a 16-mile system already owned by Clinton Gas Systems. Terms weren't disclosed.
