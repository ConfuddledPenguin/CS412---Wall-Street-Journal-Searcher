ExecuFirst Retains Accountants

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

PHILADELPHIA -- ExecuFirst Bancorp Inc. said it retained Coopers & Lybrand as independent accountants for the company and its wholly owned subsidiary, First Executive Bank. ExecuFirst said it turned to Coopers & Lybrand after Laventhol & Horwath, its original accountants, filed for bankruptcy protection last month.
