Business Brief -- Dana Corp.: Vehicle Parts Maker Plans To Sell Diamond S&L Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Dana Corp., a maker of vehicle parts, said it will seek to sell its Diamond Savings & Loan subsidiary in Findlay, Ohio, and that it retained Goldman, Sachs & Co. to advise Dana with the proposed sale. Diamond has more than $900 million in assets and 27 branches in northern and central Ohio.

Dana, of Toledo, Ohio, plans to expand its distribution and international activities in the next 10 years, said Southwood J. Morcott, Dana's chairman and chief executive officer. "We feel that the interests of Diamond's customers, depositors and employees would best be served by affiliation with a strong parent which is committed to growth and expansion in the thrift or banking industries."
