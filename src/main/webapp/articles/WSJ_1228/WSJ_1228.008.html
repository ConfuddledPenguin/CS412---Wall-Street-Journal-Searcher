Amstar to Sell Milford Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

NEW HAVEN, Conn. -- Amstar Corp. is negotiating to sell its Milford Products Corp. saw products unit to the U.S. unit of Swedish toolmaker Sandvik AB. Amstar said the two companies have signed a nonbinding letter of intent to negotiate a final purchase agreement that could result in the sale of Milford's stock to Sandvik for about $20 million.

Milford, a manufacturer of saw blade products, employs 325 people in plants and distribution centers in Branford, Conn., and Banbury, Oxon, England. The transaction is expected to close in January 1991.
