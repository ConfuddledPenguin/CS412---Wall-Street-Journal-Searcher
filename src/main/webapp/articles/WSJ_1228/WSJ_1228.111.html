Business Brief -- Matsushita Electric Industrial Co.: Tender Offer for MCA Inc. Expected to Be on Schedule

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Matsushita Electric Industrial Co. said it expects to complete its $66-a-share tender offer for MCA Inc. on schedule, by 12:01 a.m. EST Saturday. The Japanese electronics giant said it doesn't expect to extend the offer for the Universal City, Calif., entertainment concern beyond that date. Barring any last-minute surprises, the $6.59 billion acquisition therefore should be completed this year.

MCA also filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission related to the planned spinoff of New York-area television station WWOR-TV to its shareholders as part of the Matsushita acquisition.

The newly spun-off company, which will be called Pinelands Inc., rather than Red Oak as originally contemplated, is expected to have about 16.9 million common shares outstanding, or one share for every five MCA shares. MCA said the station had operating cash flow of about $28 million for the first nine months of 1990, up about 55% from $18 million for the same period in 1989. Revenue of $141 million was up 18% from $119 million a year ago.
