Hollinger Inc. Slates Move

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

TORONTO -- Shareholders of Hollinger Inc. voted to move the company's registered head office to Vancouver, British Columbia, from Toronto. Hollinger, a newspaper publishing concern, said its most significant Canadian operations are in western Canada, including the Sterling newspaper chain. The company's president and chief operating officer, David Radler, lives in Vancouver.

Other major Hollinger executives will remain in Toronto, and officials said the move isn't expected to affect the company's operations.
