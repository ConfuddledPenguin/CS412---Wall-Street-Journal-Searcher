Kaiser Steel Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

RANCHO CUCAMONGA, Calif. -- Kaiser Steel Resources Inc. said that Browning Ferris Industries Inc. purchased a 50% interest in a company developing a municipal landfill at Kaiser's closed iron ore pits east of Los Angeles. Kaiser said BFI paid $10 million for the equity position in the company, called Mine Reclamation Corp. BFI also committed back-stop financing for the eventual development and operation of the landfill, Kaiser said.

Kaiser, a waste management and land development company, also said that it formed a joint venture with EDCO Disposal to develop a municipal solid-waste transfer station and a materials recycling facility in Fontana, Calif.
