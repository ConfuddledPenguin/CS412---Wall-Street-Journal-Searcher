Business Brief -- Caesars World Inc.: Concern Completes Purchase Of Caesars New Jersey Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

Caesars World Inc. said it completed the acquisition of the 146,718 publicly held common shares of Caesars New Jersey Inc. that it didn't already own, for about $3.3 million. As a result, Caesars New Jersey became a wholly owned subsidiary of Los Angeles-based Caesars World. Under the terms of the merger, former public shareholders of Caesars New Jersey, which owns and operates Caesars Atlantic City, are entitled to receive $22.25 a share in cash, without interest.

The merger follows a recently completed tender offer by Caesars World for the common shares outstanding of Caesars New Jersey that it did not already own. Under the tender offer, which expired Nov. 30, some 2,029,227 shares were tendered, and Caesars World increased its holdings to 99% of shares outstanding in Caesars New Jersey.

Caesars New Jersey's shares on the American Stock Exchange will be discontinued and registration of the shares under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 will be terminated. Caesars World owns and operates hotels and casinos.
