Business Brief -- Vencor Inc.: Firm Seeks to End Accord To Run Louisiana Hospital

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Vencor Inc. said it's negotiating to terminate its contract to manage Vencor Hospital South Louisiana, a 49-bed hospital in Gonzales, La. The hospital contributes less than 3% of the company's annual revenue. Vencor, which originally leased the hospital in 1988 and entered into a contract to manage it in April 1990, said termination is expected to occur within four months. The Louisville, Ky., company is negotiating with hospital board members.

Vencor, which owns or leases 12 other hospitals, said the Louisiana hospital didn't fit into the company's strategic planning, and that Vencor is primarily interested in operating hospitals in major metropolitan areas.
