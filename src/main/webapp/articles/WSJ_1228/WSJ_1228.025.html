Martin Marietta Corp. And Westinghouse Win Army Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

WASHINGTON -- Martin Marietta Corp., in partnership with Westinghouse Electric Corp., received $374.9 million in contracts for Army Apache helicopter improvements and for Air Force aircraft-navigation systems.

General Electric Co. received $89.4 million in Air Force and Army contracts for aircraft engines.

Hughes Electro-Optical Operations Inc., a unit of General Motors Corp., was given a $34.1 million Army contract for armored-vehicle missiles.

Stanford Telecommunications Inc. received a $23 million Navy contract for satellite communications equipment.

FN Manufacturing Inc. won a $13.6 million Army contract for M-16 rifles.

LTD Corp. was awarded an $11.1 million Army contract for missile systems support.
