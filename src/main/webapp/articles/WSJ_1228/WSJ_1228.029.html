Deprenyl Research Ltd. Drug

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

TORONTO -- Deprenyl Research Ltd. said it is seeking permission for doctors to prescribe its Eldepryl (R) medication for patients newly diagnosed as suffering from Parkinson's disease. In Canada, Eldepryl (R) is approved only for treatment of patients with advanced-stage Parkinson's disease. Deprenyl said permission to treat new patients with the drug was received in the United Kingdom in May.

The request for approval to use Eldepryl (R) as initial therapy in new patients is based on results of clinical studies in Canada and the U.S., Deprenyl said.

Eldepryl (R) is being investigated as a possible treatment for other neurodegenerative diseases, particularly Alzheimer's.
