Letters to the Editor: Selfless Dedication

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

Congratulations for printing the page-one articles about Dr. Homes Morton and the Amish/Mennonites in Lancaster County, Pa., on Sept. 20 and Nov. 18. In a world of selfishness, Dr. Morton's work is truly the epitome of the Hippocratic Oath. Your first article so moved me that I wanted to donate funds for his work. Unfortunately, I lost the article but now I have been redeemed. Please tell your readers that his Clinic for Special Children Inc. is at P.O. Box 128 in Strasburg, Pa. And thank you again for this wonderful article on human dedication.

Barry J. Branagan

Thousand Oaks, Calif.
