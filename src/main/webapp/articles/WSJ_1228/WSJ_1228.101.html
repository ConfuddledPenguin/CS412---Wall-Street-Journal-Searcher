Business Brief -- Beverly Enterprises Inc.: Refinancing Is Completed For Restructuring Debt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Beverly Enterprises Inc. said it completed a $413 million refinancing of the remaining debt covered by its 1988 restructuring agreement. The Fort Smith, Ark., health-care concern said the refinancing consists of a bank credit facility led by J.P. Morgan & Co.'s Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. of New York unit, a senior secured note offering placed by Merrill Lynch & Co. and Stephens Inc. and a commercial paper program placed by J.P. Morgan Securities Inc., also a J.P. Morgan unit. The various debt instruments have maturities ranging from three to seven years. Terms of the debt instruments weren't disclosed and officials of Beverly couldn't be reached for further amplification.

This was the last of about $700 million of debt refinancing completed by the company since the spring of 1989, when new management took over.
