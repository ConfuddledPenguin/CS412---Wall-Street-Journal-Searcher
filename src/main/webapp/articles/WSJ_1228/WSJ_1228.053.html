Who's News: Barclays Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

BARCLAYS GROUP -- Kent A. Logan was named chief executive officer for U.S. equities, a new post at Barclays de Zoete Wedd, a New York investment banking and securities subsidiary of Barclays Group, based in London. Mr. Logan, 46 years old, was previously a managing director in charge of institutional customers at PaineWebber Inc., New York. Mr. Logan is recognized in the industry for his role in developing recent changes in the institutional equity business, including revision of fees charged to institutional investors.
