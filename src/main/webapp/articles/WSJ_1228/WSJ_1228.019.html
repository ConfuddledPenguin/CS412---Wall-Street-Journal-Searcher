Big O Tires Retains Gruntal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

ENGLEWOOD, Colo. -- Big O Tires Inc. said it retained Gruntal Capital Markets to serve as its exclusive investment banker and financial advisor. Stanley Hollander, managing director of Gruntal Capital Markets, said the firm will be "looking at a lot of alternatives to maximize shareholder value and grow Big O's business." He wouldn't be more specific.

Gruntal Capital Markets, a unit of Gruntal & Co., a brokerage firm, replaces NCB Business Credit Corp. as the investment banker for Big O, which owns and franchises tire stores. Big O said that Gruntal provides it with financial services and access to capital markets for emerging, midsize companies.
