International: Western Germany's Costs Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

FRANKFURT, Germany -- The cost of living in western Germany in December was unchanged from November, the Wiesbaden-based Federal Statistics Office said in a preliminary report, which was mostly in line with economists' expectations. Living costs in the 11 states that make up what was formerly West Germany, however, rose 2.7% from December 1989. The preliminary index is based on data supplied last week by four western German states: Hesse, Bavaria, Baden-Wuerttemburg and North Rhine-Westphalia. Final results will be published in the middle of January, after all 11 of these states report their price data.
