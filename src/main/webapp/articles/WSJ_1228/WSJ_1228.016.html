Pawling Savings Bank Reserves

  • Author: null
  • Date: 12/28/90

PAWLING, N.Y. -- Pawling Savings Bank said it added an additional $7.5 million to its loan loss reserves in the fourth quarter, due to continued deteriorating real estate loan values. The addition boosted reserves at year end to approximately $14.5 million. Reserves at the end of the third quarter totaled $12.9 million. The bank, the primary subsidiary of Progressive Bank Inc., said it wrote off about $7 million in loans in the fourth quarter.

Pawling Savings has assets of about $600 million.
