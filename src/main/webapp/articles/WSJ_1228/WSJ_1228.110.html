Business Brief -- Base Ten Systems Inc.: Concern Reports 39% Drop In Earnings for Fiscal Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Base Ten Systems Inc. said net income for the fiscal year ended Oct. 31 fell 39% to $1.8 million, or 50 cents a share, on revenue of $47.6 million. In the year-earlier period, the Trenton, N.J., maker of electronics systems for defense and commercial applications reported net income of $2.9 million, or 82 cents a share. Results included income from discontinued operations of $1.3 million, or 37 cents a share. Revenues were $48.9 million. Fiscal fourth-quarter figures weren't immediately available.

Looking forward, Myles M. Kranzler, chairman and president, said the company expects that "after a slow start, 1991 will be profitable but with revenue in the $30 million to $35 million range."

For 1992, Mr. Kranzler said the company sees sales of "over $40 million with better than 80 cents a share net income, and a continuation of growth in both sales and profits in 1993."
