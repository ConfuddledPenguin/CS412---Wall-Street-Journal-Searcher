Norwest Corp. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Norwest Corp. said the Federal Reserve Board approved its planned $19-a-share, or $41 million, acquisition of Wyoming National Bancorp. Based in Casper, the Wyoming bank holding concern, which has assets of about $450 million, owns six banks in the state.

Norwest, which has assets of about $29.6 billion, said it expects to close the transaction in January.
