Business Brief -- Westcott Communications Inc.: Firm Holds Sandy Stake, May Seek to Buy Company

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 12/28/90

Westcott Communications Inc. said it holds a 6.38% stake in Sandy Corp.'s common shares outstanding and may seek to acquire the company. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the Carrollton, Texas, specialized broadcasting concern said it has met on several occasions with Sandy to express its interest in a combination of the two companys.

According to the filing, on Sept. 13 Westcott proposed the merger of itself and Sandy where each Sandy share would be exchanged for $4.125, subject to certain terms. Subsequent conversations with representatives of Sandy led Westcott to believe that certain conditions set forth in its Sept. 13 letters could not be readily satisfied by the company.

On Dec. 7, Westcott received an unsolicited memorandum from John Sheehy, a director of Sandy, which Westcott believes was intended as a basis for new negotiations between the company and Westcott. Westcott has not responded to such memorandum and no discussions between Westcott and the company are currently taking place.

Westcott purchased 41,400 shares from Dec. 17 to Dec. 24 bringing its total holdings in Sandy to 142,900 shares.

Sandy, Troy, Mich., is a training company for major corporations. The company had no comment on the filing.
