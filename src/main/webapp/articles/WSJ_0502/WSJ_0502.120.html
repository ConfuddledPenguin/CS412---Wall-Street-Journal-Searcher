Who's News: Lone Star Technologies Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

LONE STAR TECHNOLOGIES Inc. -- Allan D. Nichols was named chairman, president and chief executive officer of American Federal Bank FSB, Dallas, a thrift unit. Mr. Nichols, 52 years old, was previously president and chief executive of Sentry Bancshares and Sentry Bank & Trust in Roswell, Ga. He succeeds William E. Gibson, who resigned in January as chairman, president and chief executive of the $3.2 billion thrift.
