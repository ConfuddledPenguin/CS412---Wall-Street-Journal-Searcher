Business Brief -- MagneTek Inc.: Firm Enters Joint Venture With Hungarian Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

MagneTek Inc., Los Angeles, said it entered a joint venture agreement with a Hungarian concern to manufacture electric motors in Budapest and distribute them throughout Europe. MagneTek, an electrical equipment manufacturer with plants in West Germany and the U.K., says it will form a 50-50 limited liability corporation with a unit of the major Hungarian motor manufacturer Evig to make integral motors.

This segment of Evig's business has revenue of $12 million a year. MagneTek says the joint venture will raise this to $16 million in the first year through distribution of the products in new markets. Most of Evig's motors have been sold domestically.

A MagneTek spokesman wouldn't specify the company's initial investment in the joint venture.
