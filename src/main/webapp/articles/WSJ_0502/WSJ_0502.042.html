  • Date: 05/02/90

BAUSCH & LOMB Inc. (Rochester, N.Y.) -- Perry Rosenthal, M.D., founder of Polymer Technology Corp., which this vision-care products and instruments maker acquired in 1983, resigned as a director to pursue personal business interests related to the development of contact lenses. He will continue as a consultant to Polymer Technology.
