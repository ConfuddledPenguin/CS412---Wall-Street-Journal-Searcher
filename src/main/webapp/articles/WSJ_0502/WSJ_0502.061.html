Business Brief -- British & Commonwealth Holdings: Bondholders Won't Declare Troubled Firm in Default

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

British & Commonwealth Holdings PLC (B&C), a troubled U.K. financial-services group, said holders of its bonds won't declare the company in default. Law Debentures Trust Corp. PLC, trustee for holders of several classes of the company's bonds, has decided against forcing the company to repay the bonds at this time.

"Law Debentures recognizes that B&C and its advisers are working on proposals" that would restructure the company in a way that would ensure the company would meet its credit obligations, B&C said.

It will resist calling the company in default so long as it "remains otherwise satisfied that the interests of its beneficiaries will not be further prejudiced," B&C said.
