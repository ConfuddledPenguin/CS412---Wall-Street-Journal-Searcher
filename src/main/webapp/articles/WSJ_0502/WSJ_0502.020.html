Norwest Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Norwest Corp. said it completed the previously reported acquisition of First Interstate Corp. of Wisconsin in a transaction valued at about $215 million. The bank holding company said the transaction adds $2 billion in assets and 51 banking offices in three states to its operations. Norwest now has assets of $26.7 billion.
