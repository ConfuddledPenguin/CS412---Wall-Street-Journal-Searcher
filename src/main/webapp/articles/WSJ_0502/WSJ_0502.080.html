Business Brief -- Jerrico Inc.: Restaurant Company Agrees To Sale of 2 of Its Chains

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

Jerrico Inc., Lexington, Ky., said it agreed to sell two restaurant chains and plans to sell a third to enable Jerrico to concentrate on its Long John Silver's Seafood Shoppes. The company will sell its 23 company-owned Jerry's family-style restaurants and franchise rights to 23 franchised Jerry's units to Great American Restaurants Inc., Atlanta. Jerrico declined to disclose the sale price.

In addition, Jerrico said it agreed to sell Fazoli's, a five-restaurant Italian-food unit, and plans to sell the two-restaurant Florenz unit, which also sells Italian food.

The Long John Silver's unit has about 1,500 restaurants, two-thirds of them owned by Jerrico. The company said it wants to add 200 franchised units within five years.
