Who's News: American International Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

AMERICAN INTERNATIONAL GROUP Inc. (New York) -- Joseph C. Smetana, 52 years old, was designated chairman and chief executive officer of this international insurer's AIG Risk Management Inc. unit, and president of American International Global. He will be succeeded as AIG Risk president and chief operating officer by John G. Gantz, 41. Vincent J. Masucci, 42, president of American International Global, will become director of AIG regional operations and assume Mr. Gantz's duties in regional and branch management for the North American system. All the appointments take effect June 1.
