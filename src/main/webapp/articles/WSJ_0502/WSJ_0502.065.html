Financing Business: Mountaineer Bankshares of W.Va. Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

MOUNTAINEER BANKSHARES OF W.VA. Inc. said directors approved purchasing as much as 2% of the Martinsburg, W.Va., bank holding company's approximately 2.6 million shares outstanding. Buy-backs may be made in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions. Shares bought back will be used for the employee benefit program and other corporate purposes.
