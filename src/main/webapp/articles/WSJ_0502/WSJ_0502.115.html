CalMat Sells Buildings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

LOS ANGELES -- CalMat Co. said it completed the sale of 11 industrial buildings in San Diego for about $15.7 million. CalMat has been selling real-estate assets since 1988 as part of a restructuring effort. The company has been focusing on its asphalt, concrete and cement business and using some of the proceeds from the real-estate sales to buy back stock.
