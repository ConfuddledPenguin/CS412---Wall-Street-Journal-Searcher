Ford Extends Sales Incentives

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

DETROIT -- Ford Motor Co. extended sales incentives for most of its 1990 cars and trucks, offering customers the choice of cash rebates of as much as $1,500 or financing rates as low as 6.9%. The latest program, which began yesterday and expires May 31, replaces one that ended Monday. The new 1991 Ford Escort, which went on sale at Ford dealerships last week, isn't included in the new program. The cash incentive of $1,000 on the leftover 1990 Escort was unchanged from last month's program, a Ford spokesman said.

There were some minor changes in the program. Ford, for example, increased rebates on the Ford Probe by $500 to $1,500 but it decreased incentives on the Crown Victoria by $200 to $750.
