Vitalink Is Lowering Its Prices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

FREMONT, Calif. -- Vitalink Communications Corp. said it reduced the prices of three hardware products that connect local area computer networks -- its TransLan III and TransLan 350 remote Ethernet bridges and its TransPATH 350 remote Ethernet bridge-router. The company said the price cuts of about 15% were part of a strategy to boost Vitalink's world-wide market share. Vitalink said it also will offer increased discounts to international distributors and resellers. Sales reached $70 million for the 12-month period ended March 31.
