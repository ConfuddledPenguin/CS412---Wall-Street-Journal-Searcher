Who's News: American President Appoints New Chiefs Of Two Subsidiaries

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

OAKLAND, Calif. -- American President Cos. named Timothy J. Rhein president and chief executive officer of American President Domestic, its domestic transportation subsidiary, and J. (George) Hayashi president and chief executive officer of its Pacific Basin container shipping unit, American President Lines. Mr. Rhein succeeds Donald C. Orris, who resigned. Mr. Hayashi, formerly vice chairman of American President Cos., succeeds Mr. Rhein as head of the shipping unit.

In related moves, Michael Diaz was named to the new position of executive vice president and chief operating officer of American President Domestic, and John G. Burgess to the new position of executive vice president and chief operating officer of American President Lines. Mr. Diaz was formerly president of American President Intermodal, a subsidiary that operates the company's double-stack container train system in North America. Mr. Burgess had been senior vice president, North America for American President Lines.
