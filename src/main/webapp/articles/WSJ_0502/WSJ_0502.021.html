Letters to the Editor: Unfair Portrayal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

Concerning your March 23 page-one article on Orbital Sciences Corp: It would seem the press is unable to portray those who seek to develop space as anything but "Space Nuts." In fact, Orbital Sciences is a small but robust company that has conservatively paced its growth and ambitions. Reducing the efforts and success of more than 500 employees, over many years, to an almost cartoonlike depiction is a gross injustice to both OSC and all those entrepreneurial companies that strive to create value in the use of space. Alden M. Richards

New York
