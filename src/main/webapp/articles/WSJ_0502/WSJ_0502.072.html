International Brief -- Ferranti International PLC: Officer Says Firm to Take Major Write-Offs in 1990

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

Ferranti International PLC will take "very substantial" write-offs this year, said Chairman Eugene Anderson. He said at the company's extraordinary general meeting in London that the majority of the write-offs will have "no immediate cash-flow implications but will, when combined with the extraordinary gains which have been achieved in recent sales of assets, more accurately reflect company assets and liabilities."

After allegations of contract fraud, the U.K. electronics and defense equipment maker last year wrote off about #185 million ($303.5 million) and arranged special, short-term credit facilities with its bankers.
