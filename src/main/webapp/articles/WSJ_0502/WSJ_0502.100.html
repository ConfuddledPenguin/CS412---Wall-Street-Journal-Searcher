DSC Gets Contract in Japan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

DALLAS -- DSC Communications Corp. said it received a contract from DDI Corp. of Tokyo to provide tandem switching equipment and intelligent network system products. Terms weren't disclosed. DSC said the products will be used for DDI's countrywide telecommunications network, which was formed to provide long-distance service as an alternative to Nippon Telegraph & Telephone Corp. DSC said it expects to deliver most of the products by the end of 1991.
