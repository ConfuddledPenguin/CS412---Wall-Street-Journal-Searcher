Who's News: Wang Laboratories Names Youngest Son Of Founder to Board

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

LOWELL, Mass. -- Courtney Wang, youngest son of the late An Wang, was named a director of Wang Laboratories Inc., taking his father's seat on the board. Courtney Wang, 33 years old, is a Wang Laboratories vice president and an area sales director in Dallas. Mr. Wang's mother, Lorraine Wang, 70, and older brother Frederick A. Wang, 39, are already Wang directors.

The Wang family controls the computer maker through holdings of Class C stock that elect a majority of the board. They have said they plan to retain their ownership.

An Wang was chairman and chief executive officer before he died of cancer in March. Frederick Wang was president of Wang for two years, but he resigned last summer in the wake of huge losses, management turmoil and a debt crisis.

Richard Miller, 49, a former General Electric Co. executive, succeeded Frederick Wang and after An Wang's death was named chairman and chief executive as well.
