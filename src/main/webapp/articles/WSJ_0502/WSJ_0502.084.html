Business Brief -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc.: Mutual Cooperation Accord Set With Maeda of Japan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

Jacobs Engineering Group Inc., Pasadena, Calif., said it signed a mutual cooperation agreement with Maeda Corp. of Tokyo, under which the companies will pursue engineering and construction projects internationally. Initially, Jacobs and Maeda will target projects in the U.S. A Jacobs spokesman said that neither company could project the value of the association, but that in several years, the agreement could produce a "substantial portion" of both firms' business.

In addition to construction and engineering services, Jacobs provides environmental and hazardous-waste services. Maeda, one of Japan's largest engineering and construction firms, is known for its work on civil engineering projects, factories and commercial buildings.
