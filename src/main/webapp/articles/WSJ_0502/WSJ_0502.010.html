Who's News: International Multifoods Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

INTERNATIONAL MULTIFOODS Corp. (Minneapolis) -- Duncan H. Cocroft, formerly a vice president at SmithKline Beecham PLC, was named vice president, finance, and chief financial officer of the food concern. He succeeds K. Marvin Eberts Jr., who resigned in February to pursue other interests. Since Mr. Eberts's resignation, Gerhardt P. Kirchmann has served as acting chief financial officer.
