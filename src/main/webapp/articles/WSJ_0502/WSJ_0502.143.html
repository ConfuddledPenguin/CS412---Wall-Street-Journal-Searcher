Who's News: Marsh & McLennan Cos.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

MARSH & MCLENNAN Cos. (New York) -- This insurance broker named three top executives for its insurance program management services unit. The unit, renamed Seabury & Smith Inc., previously was Marsh & McLennan Group Associates Inc. Bernard F. Rogers was named chairman; succeeding him as president is Joel Hirsch, former executive vice president. Claude Y. Mercier, previously president and chief executive officer of Encon Group, a Seabury & Smith subsidiary, was named executive vice president.
