Business Brief -- General Computer Corp.: Proposed Accord Is Reached In Fight With Revco D.S.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

General Computer Corp., Twinsburg, Ohio, said it reached a proposed settlement of all claims between GCC and Revco D.S. Inc., a drug-store chain operating under Chapter 11 provisions of the federal Bankruptcy Code. General Computer said the settlement could have a favorable impact on earnings for its May 31 fourth quarter, but declined to estimate the amount.

Under terms of the proposed settlement, General Computer would pay $130,000 to Revco, but would receive about $450,000 from a disputed tax refund and accumulated interest on the refund.

The agreement also would eliminate a $575,000 promissory note due Revco and receivables from Revco of $290,000.

The settlement is subject to approval by the bankruptcy court. General Computer provides computer systems and services for pharmacies and medical offices.
