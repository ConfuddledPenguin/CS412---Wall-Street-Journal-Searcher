Financing Business: Ramsay Health Care Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

RAMSAY HEALTH CARE Inc. (New Orleans) said it completed refinancing of $129.5 million in senior debt. The company said it has entered into new credit agreements for $54.5 million in senior notes, $34 million in bank term debt, $31 million in letters of credit, $3 million in senior subordinated notes and $5 million in working capital. The balance was applied to transactions costs. The company said $111 million will be used to repay senior bank debt, $12.5 million to buy a psychiatric hospital in DeSoto, Texas, and $3 million to reduce the company's 13% subordinated promissory note. The agreements are with a group of insurance companies led by Aetna Insurance Co. and a group of banks led by HomeFed Bank and First Union National Bank of North Carolina.
