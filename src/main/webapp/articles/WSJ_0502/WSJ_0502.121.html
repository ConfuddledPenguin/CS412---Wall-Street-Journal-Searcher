GM Unit Wins Navy Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

WASHINGTON -- Hughes Aircraft Co., a subsidiary of General Motors Corp., received a $56.6 million Navy contract for Trident missile-guidance parts.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. won an $18.5 million Army contract for helicopter repair.

GTE Government Systems Corp., a unit of GTE Corp., was given a $15.7 million Navy contract for electronics parts.

Boeing Co. got an $11.8 million Air Force contract for space booster-rocket parts.

Raytheon Co. was issued a $10 million Navy contract for Trident missile-guidance parts.
