Dutch Trade Is Balanced

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

AMSTERDAM -- Dutch imports equaled exports in February, with exports growing 3% and imports rising 8%, both to 18.4 billion guilders ($9.75 billion), from 17.9 billion guilders and 17.1 billion guilders, respectively, a year earlier, the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics said. For the first two months of this year, the Netherlands posted a trade surplus of 500 million guilders, with exports amounting to 37.9 billion guilders and imports 37.4 billion guilders. Exports rose 5.5% from the year-earlier 35.9 billion guilders, and imports rose 9.4% from 34.2 billion guilders.
