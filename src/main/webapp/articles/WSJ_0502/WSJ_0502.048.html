Who's News: Signal Apparel Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

SIGNAL APPAREL Co. (Chattanooga, Tenn.) -- Roger Holland was named president and chief executive officer of this apparel maker. The 49-year-old Mr. Holland had been chief executive of Champion Products Inc. He succeeds Robert B. Colbert Jr., 68, who remains a director.
