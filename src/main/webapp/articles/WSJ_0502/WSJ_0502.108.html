Business Brief -- Gap Inc.: Firm's President, Drexler, Sold Shares for $23 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

Millard Drexler, president of the Gap Inc., credited with rejuvenating the apparel chain, sold 368,000 shares in January and March for $23 million, according to the company's proxy statement. Since most of the shares were granted him under his compensation agreement, most of the $23 million is a net gain to him. Mr. Drexler also received salary and cash bonuses last year totaling $1,745,981. He still owns 1.2 million restricted shares, obtained through grants, currently worth nearly $78 million. Mr. Drexler transformed the Gap from a jeans reseller to a more successful casual-wear chain with more diversified merchandise, in the process driving up the San Bruno, Calif.-based company's earnings and stock price over the past three years.
