Who's News: Dennison Manufacturing Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

DENNISON MANUFACTURING Co. (Framingham, Mass.) -- This office supplies and fastener products concern named four senior vice presidents: Jacquie D. Arthur, previously vice president, strategic planning; Anthony F. Bova, previously group vice president, systems and packaging; Gerald P. Hendrick, previously vice president, who remains general counsel; and John C. McCurrach, previously group vice president, stationery products.
