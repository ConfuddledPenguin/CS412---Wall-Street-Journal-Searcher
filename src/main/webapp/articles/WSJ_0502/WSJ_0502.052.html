Herman Miller Acquires Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

ZEELAND, Mich. -- Herman Miller Inc., an office furniture maker, said it completed the acquisition of closely held Meridian Inc. of Spring Lake, Mich., a metal-case goods maker, through a stock swap valued at about $28.6 million. Herman Miller said it exchanged 1,380,000 of its common shares for all of Meridian's shares outstanding.
