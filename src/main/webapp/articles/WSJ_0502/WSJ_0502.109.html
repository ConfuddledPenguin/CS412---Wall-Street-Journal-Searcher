Aspect Telecommunications

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

SAN JOSE, Calif. -- Aspect Telecommunications Corp. completed an initial public offering of two million shares at $14.50 a share. Of the total, 1.9 million shares were offered by the company, and the rest by selling shareholders. Proceeds will be used by the maker of transaction processing systems to bolster working capital, to purchase capital equipment and to finance leasehold improvements.
