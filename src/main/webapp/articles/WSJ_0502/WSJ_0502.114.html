Business Brief -- Pic `N' Save Corp.: Partnership Is Seeking Data As Part of Its Planned Bid

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

A partnership controlled by David H. Batchelder said it is seeking access to nonpublic financial and other information about Pic `N' Save Corp., Dominguez, Calif., in connection with the partnership's planned bid to acquire the company. Girard Partners Limited Partnership disclosed in a filing that its representatives met with those of the retail chain Saturday and that Pic 'N' Save indicated it is evaluating "potential courses of action to enhance stockholder value."

Pic `N' Save officials declined to comment on the meeting or their plans for the company. Mr. Batchelder didn't return calls to his office in La Jolla, Calif.

At the meeting, Girard said it was willing to enter into "appropriate confidentiality arrangements" to gain access to the data, according to the filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Girard previously said it is soliciting proxies to gain a majority of seats on Pic 'N' Save's board. In the latest filing, Girard said it began its solicitation to remove five directors. If elected, the Girard nominees plan to seek acquisition proposals.
