Technology: Cambridge BioScience Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

WORCESTER, Mass. -- Cambridge BioScience Corp. said that the Food and Drug Administration gave it a license for its Recombigen AIDS antibody detection test. Cambridge BioScience said the two-hour test, designed for high-volume screening at blood banks and laboratories, gives few false positive readings compared with other tests.
