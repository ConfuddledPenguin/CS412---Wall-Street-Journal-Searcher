Who's News: International Minerals & Chemical Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

INTERNATIONAL MINERALS & CHEMICAL Corp. (Northbrook, Ill.) -- Dr. Ronald G. Evens, director of the Mallinckrodt Institute of Radiology at Washington University's School of Medicine, was named a director of this maker of animal health and nutrition products, increasing board membership to 12.
