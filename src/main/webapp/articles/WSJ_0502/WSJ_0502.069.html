Niagara Exchange Sells $8.2 Million of Stock To W.R. Berkley Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Niagara Exchange Corp. said W.R. Berkley Corp., Greenwich, Conn., purchased 970,000 shares of Niagara preferred stock at $8.50 a share. The proceeds of $8.2 million will provide additional capital to strengthen Niagara's balance sheet following heavy losses from Hurricane Hugo.

Niagara is the parent of Exchange Insurance Co. Berkley also is involved in property casualty insurance.

Under terms of the agreement, Berkley won't buy additional Niagara Exchange shares during the next three years without prior approval of the Niagara board, the company said.

The preferred stock will have an 8 1/4% dividend rate, payable quarterly in cash, or at the option of the Niagara board, in additional shares, the company said. If regulatory approvals are received for the voting rights, Berkley will control 35% of the voting power of all Niagara Exchange shares.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Niagara common closed at $7, unchanged.
