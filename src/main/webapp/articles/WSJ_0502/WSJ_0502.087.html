Business Brief -- First Western Bancorp Inc.: Bank Holding Firm to Buy Stock Savings Association

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/02/90

First Western Bancorp Inc. reached a definitive agreement to acquire First Federal of Western Pennsylvania for a combination of cash and stock with a total value of nearly $24 million. Under terms of the agreement, holders of the 1.3 million shares outstanding of First Federal common stock will receive either $19 a share in cash or 0.76 share of First Western common for each First Federal share. Holders may elect to receive a combination of cash and stock, the companies said.

The companies said 40% of the First Federal shares will be purchased for cash and the remaining 60% exchanged for First Western stock.

First Western, based in New Castle, Pa., is a bank holding company with total assets of $569 million. First Federal, of Sharon, Pa., is a federally chartered stock savings association with assets of $352 million.
