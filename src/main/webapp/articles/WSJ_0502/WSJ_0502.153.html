Quaker State Completes Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

OIL CITY, Pa. -- Quaker State Corp., which primarily produces lubricants, said it completed the sale of a small refinery and a wax plant to the Petrowax PA Inc. unit of New York-based U.S. Petroleum Corp. for $20 million in cash and a $10 million note. Quaker State said that it recognized a $3.5 million pretax profit on the sale of Petrowax, which specializes in making and selling waxes, and that additional gains will be recognized as the note is collected.
