Who's News: International Business Machines Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/02/90

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS MACHINES Corp. (Armonk, N.Y.) -- Stephen B. Schwartz was elected senior vice president of this computer concern. Mr. Schwartz recently was appointed a senior executive assigned to try to cut through IBM's formidable bureaucracy and achieve better quality control. Most recently, as an IBM vice president and general manager, Mr. Schwartz ran IBM's AS/400 minicomputer line.
