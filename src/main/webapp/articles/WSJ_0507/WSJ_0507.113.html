Business Brief -- Petro-Canada Inc.: Firm Is Offering Packages Of Oil and Gas Properties

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

Petro-Canada Inc. said it is offering for sale 10 packages of Canadian oil and gas properties, representing about 9% of its oil and gas liquids output and 4% of its natural gas production. The state-owned integrated oil and gas concern, which is preparing to be privatized, said the sale is part of a plan to rationalize assets. It didn't disclose a value for the properties.

The assets last year generated net operating revenue of about 38.5 million Canadian dollars (US$32.6 million), on average gross daily production of 8,900 barrels of oil or gas liquids and 24 million cubic feet of natural gas.

Waterous Securities Inc., Calgary, Alberta, has been retained as Petro-Canada's financial adviser and agent for the offering, which will take place in two phases, the first of which is scheduled for this month, the company said.
