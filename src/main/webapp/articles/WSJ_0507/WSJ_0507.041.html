Who's News: Tosco Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

TOSCO Corp. (Santa Monica, Calif.) -- James M. Cleary, senior vice president of refining, was named president of Tosco Refining Co., a division. He succeeds John G. Drosdick., who is resigning. The appointment is effective June 30. Peter A. Sutton, a vice president, was appointed senior vice president, succeeding William Floyd, who resigned. Wilkes McClave, assistant general counsel and secretary, was named senior vice president and general counsel, succeeding Eric Schwartz, who resigned. Mr. McClave continues as corporate secretary. The resignations coincide with the company's announcement that it plans to move its corporate offices to the East Bay area of San Francisco during the fourth quarter. Tosco is an independent oil refiner engaged in refining and marketing wholesale petroleum products. It also makes and distributes phosphate-based fertilizer products.
