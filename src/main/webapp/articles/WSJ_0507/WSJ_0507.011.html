Canada Steel Ingot Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel ingot production totaled 288,032 metric tons in the week ended April 28, up 0.5% from 286,668 tons in the preceding week but down 12% from 328,754 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The cumulative total in 1990 was 4.6 million tons, down 13% from 5.3 million tons a year earlier. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds.
