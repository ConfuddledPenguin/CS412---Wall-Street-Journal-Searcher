Who's News: Richfood Holdings Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

RICHFOOD HOLDINGS Inc. (Richmond, Va.) -- Donald D. Bennett, executive vice president of Wetterau Inc. and president of its food-distribution group, was named president and chief executive officer of this wholesale food distributor, effective in mid-May. Mr. Bennett, 53 years old, succeeds W.C. Taliaferro, who resigned in December.
