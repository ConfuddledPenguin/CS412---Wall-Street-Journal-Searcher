Turning Over an Old Leaf

In salads I'm

Not picky, no;

But I eschew

Radicchio,

And arugula

Is depressing

Even under

Loads of dressing.

So, if exotic

Goodies fret us,

We should stick

To plain old lettuce.

-- Robert Gordon.

The New Elite

Not titles, status, fame nor chic,

Nor plaques upon the wall;

We win our place in the elect

With low cholesterol.

-- Richard F. Barrett.
