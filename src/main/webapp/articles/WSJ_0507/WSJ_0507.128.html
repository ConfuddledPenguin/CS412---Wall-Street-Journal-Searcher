International Brief -- Arab Banking Corp.: Firm Plans Stock Offering To International Investors

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

Arab Banking Corp. said it plans to offer 25 million shares to international investors at about $15 apiece. Abdulla A. Saudi, president and chief executive officer of the Arab world's largest bank, said that at $15 each, a price close to the bank's estimated net asset, the offering would raise $375 million before fees and expenses.

Arab Banking is the first Gulf-based institution to offer its shares to non-Arab investors.

Arab Banking said it has invited international financial institutions to underwrite the offering, which the bank will use to finance expansion plans in Europe and the Arab world.

Underwriting commitments totaling $235 million have been received from Arab Banking holders and other financial institutions, including the banks that will organize the offering, Mr. Saudi said.
