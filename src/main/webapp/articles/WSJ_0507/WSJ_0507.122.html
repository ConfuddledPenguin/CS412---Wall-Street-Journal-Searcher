Ex-Im Bank to Back Colombia Firm Loan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

WASHINGTON -- The U.S. Export-Import Bank said it will guarantee a $24.2 million private credit to an oil pipeline company in Colombia for the purchase of equipment from U.S. suppliers. The loan will be provided by New York-based Private Export Funding Corp. to Oleoducto de Colombia S.A., of Bogota, which is building a $321.5 million crude-oil pipeline from Vasconia, Colombia, to Covenas on the Caribbean coast.

Pipeline equipment and services will be supplied by Ramseyer & Miller, a unit of Techint Inc., of New York. The Ex-Im Bank said R&M will arrange for the purchase of pumps, tanks, switches, control values and other equipment from various U.S. suppliers.
