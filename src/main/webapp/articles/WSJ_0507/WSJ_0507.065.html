American Brands Unit Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

OLD GREENWICH, Conn. -- American Brands Inc. said its Acco World Corp. office supply unit acquired Hetzel & Co., a maker of plastic folders, binders and other office accessories. Terms weren't disclosed. Based in Stuttgart, West Germany, Hetzel also has operations in Switzerland and Belgium. Its 1989 revenue was $21 million.

American Brands also has tobacco, distilled spirits and life insurance businesses.
