Hughes Aircraft Says Suit Over Charges Was Dropped

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

LOS ANGELES -- Hughes Aircraft Co. said a former Defense Department official dropped a $360 million whistleblower suit against the company after the government declined to participate in the case. Patrick M. Kelley, who was in charge of overseeing contracts at the aerospace company's Ground Systems Group, had alleged that between 1983 and 1989 Hughes mischarged certain labor costs and submitted false claims against the government totaling $70 million. Hughes said the Justice Department filed papers dismissing the suit in federal court here.

An attorney for Mr. Kelley said the case was under seal and referred calls to the Justice Department, where a spokesman declined comment. Hughes said in a statement that all its costs were proper.
