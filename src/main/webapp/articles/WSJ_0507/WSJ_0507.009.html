Who's News: Mark Twain Bancshares Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

MARK TWAIN BANCSHARES Inc. (St. Louis) -- Peter F. Benoist and Jack Deutsch were elected directors of this bank holding company. Mr. Benoist, executive vice president, succeeds former chairman Adam Aronson, who retired from the board. The election of Mr. Deutsch, executive vice president of closely held Standard Machine & Equipment Co., expands board membership to 12.
