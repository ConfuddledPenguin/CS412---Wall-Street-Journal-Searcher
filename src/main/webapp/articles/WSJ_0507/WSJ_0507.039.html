Who's News: ServiceMaster Limited Partnership

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

SERVICEMASTER Limited Partnership (Downers Grove, Ill.) -- Chief Executive Officer and President C. William Pollard was named to to the additional post of chairman of this management- and consumer-services concern. Mr. Pollard, 51 years old, succeeds Kenneth T. Wessner, 68, who is retiring but will remain with the company as vice chairman. Carlos H. Cantu, 56, formerly executive vice president and chief operating officer of ServiceMaster's consumer- and commercial-services division, was named president and chief operating officer of the business group. Charles W. Stair, 49, formerly executive vice president and chief operating officer of the management-services division, was named president and chief operating officer of the division.
