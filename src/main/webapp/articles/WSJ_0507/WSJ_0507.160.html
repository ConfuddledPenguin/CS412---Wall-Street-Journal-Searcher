New Stock Listings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange announced three new stock listings, the American Stock Exchange two and the National Market System of the over-the counter market four.

On the Big Board, Hartford Steam Boiler Inspection & Insurance Co., a Hartford, Conn., engineering service and insurance firm, will begin trading Wednesday under HSB.

Northwestern Public Service Co., a Huron, S.D., electricity and natural gas utility, joins the New York exchange tomorrow under NPS.

Union Dominion Realty Trust Inc., a Richmond, Va., real estate investment trust, begins trading today under the symbol UDR. Joining the Amex is Interchange Financial Services Corp., a Saddle Brook, N.J., bank holding company, under ISB.

United-Guardian Inc., a Hauppauge, N.Y., pharmaceuticals company, began trading under UG. The five new Big Board and Amex companies previously traded over the counter. On the OTC market, Aspect Telecommunications Corp., a San Jose, Calif., telephone transaction-systems maker, began trading under ASPT.

Dynasty Classics Corp., a Carson, Calif., home decorating products firm, started trading under DNST.

IG Laboratories Inc., a Framingham, Mass., genetic testing services firm, was listed under IGLI.

Immune Response Corp., a San Diego biopharmaceutical company, began trading under the symbol IMNR. The Amex also opened trading on a second offering of put warrants by Salomon Inc., based on the Financial Times Stock Exchange 100-share index, under the symbol FTP.WS. And the American exchange began trading put and call options on the shares of Tonka Corp. Options give the holder the right but not the obligation to buy or sell shares of a company at a set price within a certain period.
