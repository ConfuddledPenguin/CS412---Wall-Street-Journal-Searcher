Taiwan Offshore Banking

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

TAIPEI -- The total assets of Taiwan's 25 offshore banking units increased 32% to $16.9 billion by the end of March from $12.8 billion in March 1989, the Central Bank said. The report said 82% of the assets were from Asia, 12% from North and South America, 5% from Europe and 1% from other areas. Out of all assets, 88% were either deposited or loaned in Asia, according to the Central Bank.
