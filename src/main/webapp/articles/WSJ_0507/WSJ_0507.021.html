Detrex Rights Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

SOUTHFIELD, Mich. -- Detrex Corp. adopted a shareholders rights plan to protect the company against "hostile acquirers." Preferred-stock purchase rights will be distributed as a dividend at the rate of one right for each share of common stock held May 15, the company said. Each right will entitle shareholders to buy 1/100th of a newly issued share of Series A preferred stock of Detrex at an exercise price of $80, if someone acquires more than a 15% stake in the company.
