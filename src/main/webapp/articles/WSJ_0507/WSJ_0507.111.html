Business Brief -- PS Group Inc.: Buffett Firms Hold 11.04% Of Company as Investment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

Warren E. Buffett, chairman of Berkshire Hathaway Inc., said he controls an 11.04% stake in PS Group Inc.'s common stock for investment purposes. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Buffett said that three companies he controls, National Indemnity Co., National Liability & Fire Insurance Co. and Columbia Insurance Co., together hold 603,275 PS Group common shares, including 373,575 shares bought March 16 to April 25 for $32.125 to $32.50 each.

Mr. Buffett said the PS Group shares were bought for "investment purposes only."

George Shortley, PS Group's president and chief executive, said the San Diego, Calif., aircraft leasing and energy company was pleased by Mr. Buffett's investment.
