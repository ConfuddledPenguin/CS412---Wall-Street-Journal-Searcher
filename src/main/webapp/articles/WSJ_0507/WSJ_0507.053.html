Perkin-Elmer Corp. Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

NORWALK, Conn. -- Perkin-Elmer Corp. said it sold a controlling stake of about 80% in its MRJ Inc. unit to a group of managers and employees. Terms weren't disclosed. The sale of Oakton, Va.-based MRJ, which was part of Perkin-Elmer's Government Systems business segment, is part of the company's restructuring program.

Perkin-Elmer makes analytical instruments.
