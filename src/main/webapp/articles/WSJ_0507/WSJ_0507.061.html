Thermo Electron Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

WALTHAM, Mass. -- Thermo Electron Corp. said it agreed to acquire E.M. Lamort S.A., a closely held, French maker of equipment for recycling paper. Thermo declined to disclose terms.

The agreement is subject to approval by regulators in France and the U.S. and by Thermo's directors.

Under the agreement, Lamort, which had sales of more than $35 million last year, would become part of Thermo's Web Systems subsidiary, which makes equipment for the paper industry. The two already have some joint-venture activities.

Thermo Electron makes cogeneration systems, industrial-process equipment, analytical instruments and biomedical products.
