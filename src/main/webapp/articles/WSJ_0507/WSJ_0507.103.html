Business Brief -- AmeriFirst Bank: Kidder Hired to Help Raise Capital, Add to Stock Value

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

AmeriFirst Bank said it hired Kidder, Peabody & Co. to help raise capital for the bank and to develop plans to enhance the value of its stock. Miami-based AmeriFirst said it will work with Kidder's Financial Services Group, which specializes in transactions for financial institutions, including restructurings and recapitalizations.

In April, AmeriFirst settled four shareholder lawsuits, which had alleged that the bank had misled shareholders about its financial condition in public filings. AmeriFirst said the settlement was an essential step toward its objective of raising new capital to meet regulatory requirements.

Kidder is a unit of General Electric Co.
