Daihatsu's U.S. Unit Is Sued by Distributor

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

ADDISON, Ill. -- A defunct U.S. distributor of Daihatsu Motor Co.'s Hi-Jet off-road vehicles has filed a $11.9 million suit against the Japanese auto maker's U.S. subsidiary, Daihatsu America Inc. and two of its officers. Terrain Vehicles Inc. said it filed the suit in December in a California state court. The suit alleges that Los Angeles-based Daihatsu conspired to interfere with Terrain's contract with Daihatsu in an effort to acquire distribution rights to Hi-Jet.

The two Daihatsu officers named in the suit are Sota Fukunaka and Cecil R. Brown.

Daihatsu America declined to comment on the suit.
