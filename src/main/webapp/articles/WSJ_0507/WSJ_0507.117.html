Marketing Brief -- Pathe Communications Corp.: Concern Reports 1st-Quarter Net Income of $46.4 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

Pathe Communications Corp. reported first-quarter net income of $46.4 million, or 77 cents a share, compared with $10.3 million, or 91 cents, a year earlier. The number of outstanding common shares rose to 59.7 million, from 11.4 million. The results include a $50.1 million gain from the sale of certain European movie theaters that the Los Angeles-based company now operates. An additional $30 million gain will be recorded later this year, the company said. The 1990 results also list extraordinary gains of $18 million from an extinguishment of debt and utilization of tax loss carryforwards. The year-ago quarter included an extraordinary gain of $23.9 million for repurchase of subordinated debt.

Revenue was $76.6 million, compared with $91.8 million a year ago. Pathe President Giancarlo Parretti said, "The upcoming introduction of our first slate of films will have an extremely positive financial impact on our company." Pathe's first film is scheduled to be released this summer.

Pathe is currently engaged in a $1.26 billion cash tender offer for MGM/UA Communications Co., which could, with extensions, continue until June 23. A third $50 million down payment is due May 17.
