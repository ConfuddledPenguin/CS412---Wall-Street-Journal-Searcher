California Energy Sets Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- California Energy Co. said it reached an agreement to acquire certain geothermal operations from a unit of Chevron Corp. for $68.7 million. The purchase price could be reduced to $51.2 million in the event Chevron's partner in a Beowawe, Nev., operation exercises a right of first refusal to buy Chevron's 50% interest in a partnership that operates a 15.6-megawatt facility.

Chevron's unit, Chevron Resources Co., said the sale includes a 9,000-kilowatt generating plant in Desert Peak, Nev., its 69% interest in a Roosevelt, Utah, field that sells steam to a utility plant, and certain leasehold interests.
