Marketing & Media: Broadcast Union Rejects `Final' Offer From NBC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

NEW YORK -- The National Association of Broadcast Employees and Technicians voted overwhelmingly to reject the "final" contract proposal from National Broadcasting Co., a unit of General Electric Co. An NBC spokeswoman said the next step will be to listen to what NABET wants changed in the final contract. However, one official said, "we're not changing our position that much because this was our final offer."

NABET, which represents about 2,500 technicians, writers and producers, has been working without a contract since March 31. In 1987, NABET went on strike against NBC for 17 weeks.
