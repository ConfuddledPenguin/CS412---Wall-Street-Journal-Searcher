USX Unit's Exploration Well

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

HOUSTON -- Marathon Oil Co., a USX Corp. subsidiary, said an exploration well in the Celtic Sea produced 1,600 barrels of oil a day on a one-half inch choke, plus 5.4 million cubic feet of natural gas. The well, which is located on block 48/24 about 16 miles southwest of the Kinsale Head Gas Field, was drilled in 331 feet of water to a depth of 8,475 feet, Marathon said. The well has been temporarily abandoned while results are being further evaluated, the company said.

The semi-submersible drilling unit has been moved to a location in the northern part of the same block, Marathon said.
