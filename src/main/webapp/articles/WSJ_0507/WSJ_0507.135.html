Hunt Manufacturing Acquires Bunzl Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Hunt Manufacturing Co. said it completed its acquisition of the Graphic Arts Group of Bunzl PLC for #22 million ($36.1 million) plus a single contingent cash payment. Hunt, a maker of office and art supplies, said the contingent payment is for up to #4.75 million, based on the next three years of cumulative net sales of the acquired businesses and certain related products.

The acquired businesses include Seal Products Inc., a Connecticut-based marketer of mounting and laminating equipment and supplies; Ademco Ltd., which markets a similar line of products in the U.K.; and Coated Specialities Ltd., a maker of adhesive-based films and tapes for the graphic arts industry.

Besides adding brands and distribution capabilities, the acquisition will give Hunt a manufacturing base in Europe through Coated Specialities, which has operations in West Germany.
