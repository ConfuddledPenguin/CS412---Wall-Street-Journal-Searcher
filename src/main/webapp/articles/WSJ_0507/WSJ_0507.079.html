French Industrial Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

PARIS -- France's seasonally adjusted index of industrial production fell 0.1% in the fourth quarter of 1989 from the previous quarter but was up 3.2% from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute reported Friday. The state agency said the latest quarterly decline followed a revised 0.6% advance in the third quarter. Prior to Friday's revision, growth for the third quarter had been reported as 0.4%.
