Raytheon Gets Navy Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

WASHINGTON -- Raytheon Co. received a $41.3 million Navy contract for North Atlantic Treaty Organization Seasparrow missiles.

Lockheed Corp. was awarded a $31.4 million Air Force contract for C-130H aircraft.

Hughes Aircraft Co., a unit of General Motors Corp., received a $14 million Navy contract for Phoenix missile support work.
