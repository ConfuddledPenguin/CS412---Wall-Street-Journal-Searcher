Business Brief -- Sotheby's Holdings Inc.: Profit Rose 15%, Sales 26% During the First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

Sotheby's Holdings Inc. reported that first-quarter net income rose 15% on strong buying in all collecting areas. The New York auction concern's net rose to $6 million, or 10 cents a share, from $5.2 million, or nine cents a share, a year earlier. Sales grew 26% to $61.4 million from $48.9 million.

Michael L. Ainslie, Sotheby's president, said that in the first quarter, auction sales of $282.3 million were up 39% from a year earlier, with strong buying in Old Master paintings, jewelry, furniture and rare books.

Sotheby's shares rose 50 cents Friday to close at $19.75 a share in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange.
