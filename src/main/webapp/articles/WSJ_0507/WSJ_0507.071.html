Heilig-Meyers Co. April Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- Heilig-Meyers Co. reported that April sales rose 7.6%, to $32.2 million, from $29.9 million a year ago. The home furnishings retailer said same-store sales rose 1%. The company currently operates 306 stores in 12 states.
