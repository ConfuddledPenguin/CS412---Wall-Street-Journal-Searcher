Business Brief -- Raychem Corp.: Sun Microsystems Agrees To Buy Silicon Valley Site

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

Raychem Corp., Menlo Park, Calif., said it agreed to sell an undeveloped 57.3-acre industrial site in Silicon Valley to Sun Microsystems Inc. for $39 million. The transaction is expected is be completed next year. Sun Microsystems, of Mountain View, Calif., makes computer workstations. Raychem, which recently reported a third-quarter loss of $108 million, previously announced it would sell its land in Silicon Valley and acquire manufacturing sites in other, less-expensive parts of the U.S. The company, a maker of aerospace, automotive, electronics and telecommunications products, said a larger-than-expected drop in its defense business was partly responsible for its most recent loss.
