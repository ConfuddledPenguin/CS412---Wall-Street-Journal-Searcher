Who's News: Xylogics Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

XYLOGICS INC. (Burlington, Mass.) -- This maker of computer disks and tape controllers said that Peter Savage, president and chief operating officer, resigned. Bruce Bergman, chairman and chief executive officer, will become president. Mr. Bergman said that Mr. Savage's resignation reflects streamlining in the face of a shrinking revenue base. Mr. Savage will remain a consultant to Xylogics.
