Who's News: Gibson Greetings Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

GIBSON GREETINGS Inc. (Cincinnati) -- Albert R. Pezzillo, senior vice president of American Home Products Corp., and Burton B. Staniar, chairman and chief executive officer of Westinghouse Broadcasting Co., a unit of Westinghouse Electric Corp., were elected directors of this producer of greeting cards, gift wrap and related products. They fill vacancies created by retirements from the board.
