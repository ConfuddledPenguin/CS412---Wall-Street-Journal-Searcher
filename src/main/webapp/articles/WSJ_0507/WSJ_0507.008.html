Who's News: Mbia Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/07/90

MBIA Inc. (Armonk, N.Y.) -- Joseph W. Brown Jr., was elected a director at the company's annual meeting. As president and chief operating officer of Fireman's Fund Insurance Co., Mr. Brown succeeds Robert W. Bruce III as one of the Fireman's Fund representatives on the board of this insurer of municipal bonds. Mr. Brown was previously an MBIA director from December 1986 through May 1989.
