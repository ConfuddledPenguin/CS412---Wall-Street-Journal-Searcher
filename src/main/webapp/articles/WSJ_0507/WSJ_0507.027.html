Bowne to Buy Some Assets Of Sorg Inc. for $6 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/07/90

NEW YORK -- Bowne & Co. said it reached a definitive agreement to purchase certain assets of Sorg Inc. for $6 million, including the Sorg name and trademark and printing equipment. Sorg, a New York-based financial printer, has been operating with protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code since August. The agreement is subject to approval of the bankruptcy court.

Bowne, another financial printer, said it will employ a dozen members of Sorg's New York management and sales staff. The company said press equipment included in the purchase will give Bowne extra capacity to handle work in process from Sorg's New York facility, and it will allow Bowne to retire some of its older equipment. The agreement doesn't include Sorg's real estate, Chicago office and various other assets.
