Business Brief -- NCNB Corp.: Mortgage-Service Acquisition From San Jacinto Unit Set

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

NCNB Corp., in a move to bolster its mortgage-service operations, said it agreed to acquire a large portfolio from Dallas-based Fundamental Mortgage Corp., a unit of San Jacinto Savings Association, Houston. Terms weren't disclosed. The agreement calls for NCNB, a banking company based in Charlotte, N.C., to purchase Fundamental's mortgage-servicing agreements with a principal balance outstanding of as much as $10.4 billion, as well as Fundamental's loan-servicing facility in Louisville, Ky., with about 300 employees.

NCNB said the proposed purchase would triple the size of its NCNB Mortgage Corp., which now manages $4.8 billion in mortgage-servicing agreements. The agreement, which is subject to due diligence by both companies and approvals by directors of the companies and regulators, is expected to be completed by the end of the third quarter.
