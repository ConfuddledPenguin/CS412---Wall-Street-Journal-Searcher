Who's News: Arctic Alaska Fisheries Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

ARCTIC ALASKA FISHERIES Corp. (Seattle) -- Ronald R. Jensen was named president and chief executive officer of this fishing and seafood-processing concern. He succeeds Terry J. Baker, who resigned to devote more time to family and outside interests, the company said. Mr. Baker will remain a director and assume a new position as vice chairman. Mr. Jensen is a former president of Sea-Alaska Products, a ConAgra Inc. unit.
