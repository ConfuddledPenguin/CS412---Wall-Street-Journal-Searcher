Who's News: Tenneco Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

TENNECO Inc. (Houston) -- Robert C. Thomas, formerly president of the Tenneco Gas Co. unit of this conglomerate, was named to the new positions of chairman and chief executive officer of the unit. Stephen D. Chesebro', formerly executive vice president, marketing, for the unit, succeeds Mr. Thomas as president and was additionally appointed to the new position of chief operating officer of the unit.
