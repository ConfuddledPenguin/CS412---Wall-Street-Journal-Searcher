Business Brief -- Healthco International Inc.: Company Considers Merger To Boost Shareholder Value

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

Healthco International Inc. said it is considering a merger or other actions to improve shareholder value, and has had "recent expressions of interest in a transaction involving the company." They didn't elaborate further. Shares of Healthco, a Boston dental equipment maker that is facing a fight for control with Gemini Partners L.P., rose $2.50 to $18 yesterday in heavy over-the-counter trading.

Healthco set June 25 as the record date for a shareholder consent solicitation that Gemini is making in an effort to gain control of Healthco's board of directors. New York-based Gemini owns just under 10% of Healthco's shares.

Healthco said it hoped shareholders wouldn't make a decision on the consent solicitation until it completes its value-improvement moves. In order to elect its slate of five candidates for Healthco's board, Gemini must obtain consents from a majority of shareholders within 60 days from the solicitation record date.

Healthco said shareholders would have an opportunity to review its value-improvement program at the annual meeting, scheduled for Sept. 6.
