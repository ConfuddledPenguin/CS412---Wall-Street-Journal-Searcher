Cardinal Distribution Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

DUBLIN, Ohio -- Cardinal Distribution Inc. said it completed its purchase of the stock of Ohio-Valley Clarksburg Inc., a wholesale drug distributor, for about $27.1 million. Ohio-Valley, based in Wheeling, W.Va., had revenue for the year ended March 31 of about $165 million. It serves West Virginia, Pennsylvania, Ohio, Maryland and Virginia.

Cardinal is a wholesale drug distributor.
