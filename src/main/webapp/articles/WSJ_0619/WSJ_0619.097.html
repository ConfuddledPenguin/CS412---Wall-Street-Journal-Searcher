Business Brief -- Key Centurion Bancshares Inc.: Stock Buy-Backs Postponed; Merger Proposals Are Cited

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

Key Centurion Bancshares Inc., Charleston, W.Va., said it plans to postpone a stock-repurchase program as a result of three recent merger proposals. In addition, the bank said its monthly purchases, when they resume, will be smaller than announced in February. Under a $12 million stock-repurchase plan, the bank has bought back about 237,000 of its common shares. It has about 20.7 million common shares outstanding, a spokesman said.

Now the bank said it doesn't plan any stock buy-backs until the fourth quarter. At that point it will begin purchasing $300,000 of its common stock on a monthly basis rather than $1 million of common stock on a monthly basis.

However, Key Centurion said it still anticipates eventually purchasing a total of $12 million of its common stock.
