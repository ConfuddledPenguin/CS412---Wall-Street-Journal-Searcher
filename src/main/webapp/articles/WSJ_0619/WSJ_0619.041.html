Who's News: Eastern Utilities Associates

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

EASTERN UTILITIES ASSOCIATES (Boston) -- Donald Pardus was elected chairman, succeeding John F.G. Eichorn, effective July 1. The company said John R. Stevens was elected president by the trustees at the same time. Mr. Stevens has been senior executive vice president and chief operating officer. He retains the latter title. Mr. Eichorn will continue to hold his trustee's seat.
