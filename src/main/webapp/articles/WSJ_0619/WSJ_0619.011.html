Dividend News: JWP Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

JWP Inc. declared a 3-for-2 stock split, payable July 16 to stock of record June 29. Andrew T. Dwyer, chairman and president, said the move reflects "continued excellent performance" by the Purchase, N.Y., company, which has interests in environmental systems and services, and operates a water utility. JWP has about 25.4 million shares outstanding. Mr. Dwyer said the split also will make the stock attractive to a broader group of investors. JWP's first-quarter net income rose 41% to $11 million, or 50 cents a share, on a 49% climb in revenue to $513 million.
