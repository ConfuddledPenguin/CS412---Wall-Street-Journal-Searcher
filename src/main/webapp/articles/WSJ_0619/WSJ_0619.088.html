Furon to Redeem Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

LAGUNA NIGUEL, Calif. -- Furon Co. said it plans to redeem all of its outstanding $23.4 million face amount of 8% convertible subordinated debentures due 2011. The maker of polymer products said it will pay $1,048.88 on July 19 for each $1,000 face amount of debentures. The debentures may be converted prior to the close of business July 18 into shares of Furon common at a rate of 74.42 shares for each $1,000 face amount of debentures.

Any debenture holder who converted the debentures would receive shares and cash for fractions of shares with a total value of $1,302.35 based on yesterday's closing price of $17.50. The actual value will vary with the stock price at the time of conversion.

A regular interest payment of $40 for each $1,000 face amount of debentures will be paid July 16 to bonds of record June 30. Any debentures converted before July 16 won't be entitled to any interest.
