Sweden's Current Account Gap

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

STOCKHOLM -- The deficit on Sweden's current account narrowed to 1.5 billion kronor ($246.45 million) in April from a deficit of 2.7 billion kronor a year earlier, the Riksbank, Sweden's central bank, said. The gap on the current account, which includes traded items, services and transfers, was markedly lower than the 3.3 billion-krona deficit in March, the bank said, citing chiefly better trade figures.

The 12-month period through April showed a current account deficit of 39.2 billion kronor, compared with a deficit of 16.3 billion kronor a year earlier.
