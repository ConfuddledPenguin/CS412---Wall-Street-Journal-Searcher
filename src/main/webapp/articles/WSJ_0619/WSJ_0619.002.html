Philippine Trade Deficit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

MANILA -- The Philippine trade deficit widened 75% to $1.29 billion during the first four months of the year from $733 million in the year-earlier period, the government National Statistics Office said. Exports in the four-month period reached $2.57 billion, while imports amounted to $3.85 billion, the statistics office said in its preliminary international trade report.

These figures compare with exports of $2.43 billion and imports of $3.16 billion a year earlier.
