Who's News: Bank of Tokyo Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

BANK OF TOKYO Ltd. -- The bank named Keishi Fujii, 51 years old, president of Bank of Tokyo Trust Co. N.Y., a U.S. subsidiary. The Tokyo-based bank also said it expects to name Mr. Fujii a director of the parent. Mr. Fujii, previously general manager of the corporate advisory division at the parent, succeeds Hiroshi Hayashi, 53, who assumes a new post at Tokyo headquarters.
