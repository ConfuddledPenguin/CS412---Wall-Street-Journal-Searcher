Who's News: Estee Lauder Cos.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

ESTEE LAUDER Cos. (New York) -- The closely held cosmetics company's Estee Lauder U.S.A. unit appointed Robert J. Luzzi, 37 years old, who owns a design company here, to the new position of senior vice president of creative design. Mr. Luzzi will report to Robin Burns, president and chief executive officer of the Lauder subsidiary.
