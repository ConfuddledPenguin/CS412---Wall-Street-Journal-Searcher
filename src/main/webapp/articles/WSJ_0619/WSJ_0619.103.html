Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

PUBLISHING: The senior executives of Chicago magazine formed a new company with Landmark Communications Inc. that will acquire the stake in the monthly held by Adams Communications Inc. Terms, including the specific stake Adams holds, weren't disclosed.

WHO'S NEWS: Andrew Susskind was named president of Imagine Films Entertainment Inc.'s Imagine Television unit. He was president of the television division of Weintraub Entertainment Group Inc. He assumes some duties of Robert A. Harris, who left to become an independent producer. Brian Grazer and Ron Howard, co-chairmen of Imagine Films Entertainment, have assumed Mr. Harris's former duties for motion pictures.
