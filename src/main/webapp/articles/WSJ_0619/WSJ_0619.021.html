Litton Industries Gets Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

Litton Industries Inc., Beverly Hills, Calif., said it received a contract it valued at about $13 million from Limited Inc. to provide apparel handling and control systems for two new Limited distribution centers in Reynoldsburg, Ohio. Litton said its material handling systems division, based in Hebron, Ky., will install the systems by June 1991. Limited, based in Columbus, Ohio, is a women's clothing retailer.
