Business Brief -- Seagull Energy Corp.: Oil, Natural Gas Discovered In Texas, Gulf of Mexico

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

Seagull Energy Corp. said it made oil and natural gas discoveries at two wells it operates offshore in the Gulf of Mexico and onshore in South Texas. The Houston energy concern said its Brazos 399G-3 well -- offshore about 15 miles south of Freeport, Texas, in 60 feet of water -- flowed at a rate of 4.6 million cubic feet of gas a day through a 19/64-inch choke. Seagull holds a 37.5% working interest in the discovery, and other partners are Ashland Oil Inc. and Southwestern Energy Co.

Seagull also said the Steen-Mixon No. 1 in Karnes County in South Texas tested at a rate of 1.6 million cubic feet of gas and 89 barrels of oil through a 10/64-inch choke. The company holds a 30.15% stake in the well, and partners include Resource Reserves Co. and Bellwether Exploration Co.
