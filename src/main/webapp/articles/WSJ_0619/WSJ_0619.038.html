Who's News: National Medical Enterprises Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

NATIONAL MEDICAL ENTERPRISES Inc. (Santa Monica, Calif.) -- Ronald T. Bernstein was named executive vice president of this health care company's Specialty Hospital Group and chief operating officer of the group's Psychiatric Institutes of America unit. Mr. Bernstein, 39 years old, succeeds Bill R. Vickers, 53 years old, who was named a regional senior vice president of Psychiatric Institutes of America.
