Raw-Steel Production Declined 2.8% in Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 2.8% last week to 1,875,000 tons from 1,930,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 2.5% from the 1,923,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 83.4% of its capability last week, compared with 85.9% the previous week and 86.9% a year ago.
