Rio Algom Ltd. Pays $3.1 Million for Stake In Uranium Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

TORONTO -- Rio Algom Ltd. said it acquired an 8% stake in Uranium Resources Inc. "with a view to the possibility" of acquiring all of the company. Rio Algom, which last year broke off talks to acquire Dallas-based Uranium Resources for $2.55 a share, said it bought 2.1 million Uranium Resources shares for $1.50 each, or a total of about $3.1 million, from Elders Resources Finance Inc., a New York-based unit of Elders Resources Ltd. of New Zealand.

Rio Algom said it also paid Elders Resources $2.5 million, or 35 cents each, for warrants to acquire an additional 7.1 million Uranium Resources shares. The warrants, if exercised, would give Rio Algom a fully diluted stake of about 25% in Uranium Resources. The warrants are exercisable at $1.25 each until March 1992.

A Rio Algom spokesman said the company held further acquisition talks with Uranium Resources management last month but that these also were unsuccessful. He said Rio Algom offered a lower price this time of between $1.50 and $2.55 for each Uranium Resources share.

Uranium Resources said it considers the value of the company to be "substantially greater" than what Rio Algom was offering. "We have elected not to pursue further discussions with Rio at this time because of the apparently wide discrepancy in values placed on the company," a company statement said.

Rio Algom, which is 52%-owned by RTZ Corp. of Britain, said it may seek further meetings with Uranium Resources management to discuss acquiring the company.
