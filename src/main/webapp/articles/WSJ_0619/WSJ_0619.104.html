Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Allied Clinical Laboratories Inc., proposed initial offering of 1.7 million common shares, via Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Central Telephone Co., a unit of Centel Corp., shelf offering of up to $150 million of first mortgage bonds.

Crescent Jewelers Inc., proposed initial offering of 2.2 million common shares, via Alex. Brown and Kidder Peabody & Co.

FCC National Bank, a unit of First Chicago Corp., offering of $1 billion of certificates backed by credit card receivables, via First Chicago Capital Markets Inc., First Boston Corp., Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc.

Handex Environmental Recovery Inc., offering of 750,000 common shares, of which 250,000 are being offered by the company and 500,000 shares by holders, via Alex. Brown.

Jones Medical Industries Inc., offering of 900,000 common shares, of which 750,000 shares are to be offered by the company and 150,000 shares by holders, via Raymond James & Associates Inc.

Merrill Lynch & Co., shelf offering of up to $1.5 billion of debt securities and warrants.

Vector Aeromotive Corp., proposed offering of 1.5 million units, each consisting of one common share and one common stock purchase warrant, via Commonwealth Associates.
