Southern Mineral to Post Gain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

HOUSTON -- Southern Mineral Corp. said it expects to record a $5.7 million second-quarter gain from the sale of its interests in an oil and natural gas field in Mississippi. In the year-earlier second quarter, the energy exploration concern had net income of $337,000, or eight cents a share, on revenue of $850,000.

Southern Mineral said it sold its interests in the Trimble Field, which is in the Smith and Simpson counties of Mississippi, to Nerco Oil & Gas Co. for $10.8 million. Nerco is a unit of PacifiCorp of Portland, Ore.
