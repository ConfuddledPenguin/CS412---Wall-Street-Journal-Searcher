Met-Coil Sells Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

CEDAR RAPIDS, Iowa -- Met-Coil Systems Corp. said it sold certain assets and the rights to its Mark One Corp. unit to a group including management of the subsidiary. Terms weren't disclosed. The maker of metal forming and fabricating machinery said Mark One, a maker of industrial parts washers and integrated handling systems, had annual sales of less than 5% of its total revenue for the year ended May 31. The company hasn't announced those results yet, but for the previous year, Met-Coil had sales of $69.6 million.
