Business Brief -- Flextronics Inc.: Disk-Drive Maker Expects To Post 4th-Quarter Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

Flextronics Inc. said it expects to report a fiscal fourth-quarter loss of $22 million on sales of about $41 million. Results for the quarter ended March 31 include costs associated with the Fremont, Calif., company's recently completed sale of a 55% stake in its Asian operations to an investment group.

In the year-earlier quarter, the disk-drive maker reported a loss of $8.6 million on sales of $50.1 million.

Flextronics said the asset sale will result in "significant net profit" for the current quarter after accounting for a onetime after-tax gain of about $13 million. It didn't estimate the profit.

---

Corrections & Amplifications

FLEXTRONICS Inc., an electronics manufacturer, said a fiscal fourth-quarter loss that it expects to report stemmed from costs associated with the sale or closure of certain U.S. operations. In Tuesday's edition, the cause of the loss was misstated.

(WSJ June 21, 1990)
