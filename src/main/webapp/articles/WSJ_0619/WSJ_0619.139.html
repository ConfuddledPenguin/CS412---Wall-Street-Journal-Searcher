International Brief -- BET PLC: Pretax Profit for Fiscal '90 Rose 19% to $550.9 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

BET PLC of Britain, a support-services concern, said pretax profit for the year ended March 31 rose 19% to #322.3 million ($550.9 million) from #270.6 million a year earlier. Profit after taxes and minority interests and before extraordinary items rose 23% to #222.2 million from #181 million. Per-share earnings increased 12% to 28.5 pence from 25.5 pence. Profit attributable to shareholders, after taxes and special items, fell 2.3% to #224.3 million from #231.8 million; the latest year included a #2.1 million special gain, and year-earlier results a #50.8 million gain from a unit's sale. Operating profit rose 24% to #362 million from #291 million.

Sales increased 21% to #2.7 billion from #2.2 billion.

BET, noting the results show its stability, said that support services in the construction industry focus on the heavy and highly specialized end of that business and helped BET combat the overall downturn in British building, and that its other services, including waste removal, office cleaning and distribution, depend mostly on long-term contracts, providing resilience.
