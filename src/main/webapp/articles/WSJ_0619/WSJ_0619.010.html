Multi-Color Corp. to Post Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

CINCINNATI -- Multi-Color Corp., citing greater than expected costs in starting up two new facilities and reduced orders, said it expects to post a loss for the first quarter ending July 2. The label producer didn't specify the size of the loss. A year earlier, Multi-Color earned $1.7 million, or 80 cents a share, on sales of $61.9 million.

The company said present orders have resumed a more normal pattern, production has increased and backlogs have returned to comfortable levels. As a result, earnings "will get back on track in the second quarter," it predicted.
