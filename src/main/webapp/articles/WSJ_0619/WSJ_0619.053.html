Who's News: Philadelphia Stock Exchange

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

PHILADELPHIA STOCK EXCHANGE (Philadelphia) -- The securities exchange named William A. Cedar chief financial officer and senior vice president of finance and administration. Mr. Cedar, who was chief financial officer of Securities Industry Automation Corp., a New York data-processing systems planning company, succeeds Thomas Foley, who joined Print Northwest Inc., a printing company in Tacoma, Wash.
