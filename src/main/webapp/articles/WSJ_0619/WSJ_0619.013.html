Arrow Automotive Industries

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- Arrow Automotive Industries Inc. said its board voted to end discussions on a possible sale of the company and elected Jim Osment to succeed Harry Holzwasser as chief executive officer. Mr. Osment also is president of Arrow, and formerly served as its chief operating officer. Mr. Holzwasser will continue in the post of chairman.

The company said it was terminating its engagement of Tucker, Anthony Inc., which was hired in March to pursue strategic and financing alternatives, including a possible sale. The hiring of Tucker was linked to the expiration of secured financing arrangements at that time. Arrow announced that it obtained replacement financing earlier this month.
