Who's News: Pennsylvania Power & Light Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

PENNSYLVANIA POWER & LIGHT Co. (Allentown, Pa.) -- John T. Kauffman was elected chairman, president and chief executive officer of this electric utility, succeeding Robert K. Campbell, who died Saturday after a short illness. Mr. Kauffman, 63, formerly executive vice president and chief operating officer, and Charles E. Russoli, executive vice president and chief financial officer, have been in charge of the company's day-to-day operations since June 11 at Mr. Campbell's request. No one has been named to succeed Mr. Kauffman as executive vice president and chief operating officer, a company spokesman said.
