Borden Buys Unit of Richdale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

NEW YORK -- Borden Inc., a food and consumer-products company, said it acquired Quinlan Pretzel Co., a Denver, Pa., unit of closely held Richdale Ltd., Chicago. Terms of the cash transaction weren't disclosed. Quinlan, with 1990 sales forecast at $13 million, joins Borden's Wise Food snack operation. Quinlan's products are sold largely in the eastern third of the U.S. Quinlan has 85 employees.
