Who's News: Primerica Appoints McAtee Vice Chairman Of Smith Barney Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Primerica Corp. named John J. McAtee Jr., a lawyer in private practice, one of three vice chairmen at its Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. unit. Mr. McAtee, who will be in charge of investment banking, will report to Smith Barney's chairman and chief executive officer, Frank G. Zarb. The 54-year-old Mr. McAtee also will become a member of the Smith Barney executive committee and board, effective July 5. Primerica, a financial services company, reported record results for Smith Barney in 1989.

Mr. McAtee is a partner at the New York firm of Davis Polk & Wardwell, and has advised corporate clients in various merger-and-acquisition activities.

He has been a frequent lecturer and author on corporate finance activities.
