Business Brief -- HQ Office Supplies Warehouse: Chain Completes Public Sale For an Initial $8.6 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

HQ Office Supplies Warehouse Inc., Long Beach, Calif., said it completed a public offering of 10,000 units of securities, providing initial net proceeds of $8.6 million. The operator of office supply stores said it will use proceeds to open three new stores this summer, to pay debt and for working capital.

Each unit consists of $1,000 principal amount of convertible subordinated debentures due June 1, 1995, and 200 Class C warrants to purchase 200 Class A common shares. The units were priced at $1,000 each.
