Sock of Ages

If drama's based on conflict,

Must life become a bore,

Now that peace has broken out

And we're at odds no more?

Something tells me deep inside,

Around the solar plexus,

We should give thanks we'll always have

The battle of the sexes.

-- Richard F. Barrett.

Warning Signal

"Don't take this personally"

Is a statement that galls,

Since it's usually said

Before the ax falls.

-- Joshua Adams.

Contemplation Ration

Meditation soothes me,

Which is surprising when

It's something I only

Practice now and zen.

-- Gail Cooke.
