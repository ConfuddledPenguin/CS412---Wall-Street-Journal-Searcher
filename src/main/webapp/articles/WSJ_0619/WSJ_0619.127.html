International Brief -- Lloyd's of London: Exchange and Sun Alliance Set European Insurance Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/19/90

Lloyd's of London, the insurance exchange, and Sun Alliance PLC joined to market big-risk insurance in Europe, the two sides said. The joint venture between Sun Alliance, Britain's biggest general insurance company, and Lloyd's is aimed at drawing European-based business to London in the wake of the European Community's forthcoming market unification.

Called Eurosure, the insurance will be marketed through Lloyd's four biggest brokers, CT Bowring & Co., Alexander Howden Ltd. and the insurance-broking units of Sedgwick Group PLC and Willis Faber PLC.

Sun Alliance and 35 Lloyd's underwriting syndicates will give the brokers a ready facility to arrange policies on large risks that they might otherwise find difficult to handle, a Lloyd's spokesman said.

The areas of risk to be covered are property, business interruption, public and product liability and marine cargo cover.
