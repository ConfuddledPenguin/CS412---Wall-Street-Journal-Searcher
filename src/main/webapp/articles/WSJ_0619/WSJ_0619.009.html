U.K. Industrial Output Up

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/19/90

LONDON -- U.K. industrial production rose 0.5% in April from March and was up 0.6% from April 1989, according to provisional data released by the U.K. Central Statistical Office. Output in the energy sector, which can vary greatly with swings in the oil market, rose 2.3% in April from March and was up 0.7% from a year earlier.
