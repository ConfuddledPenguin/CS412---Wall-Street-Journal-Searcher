International Brief -- GPA Group PLC: Irish Aircraft Lessor Signs 10-Year Pact With China

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

GPA Group PLC of Ireland, an aircraft lessor, said it signed a 10-year lease agreement with the Canton region of the Civil Aviation Administration of China for five 737-300 planes and five 737-500s made by Boeing Co., Seattle. The new aircraft, which will be delivered between December 1990 and December 1991, will enter service with China Southern, the new airline that will replace the Canton CAAC regional administration in September. As part of the agreement, GPA said, it will buy 10 older-version 737s from CAAC and lease them to airlines in China or elsewhere.

GPA said the agreement represents the first time a Chinese airline has signed for a so-called operating lease -- a relatively short-term, off-balance-sheet rental -- of passenger jetliners. The company estimated that China will need to lease or buy 400 more jetliners in the next 15 years.
