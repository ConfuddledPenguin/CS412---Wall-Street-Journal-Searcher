USX Bidding Is Concluded

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

PITTSBURGH -- USX Corp. said its program to solicit bids and sell the exploration and production assets of its Texas Oil & Gas Corp. subsidiary was concluded. Bids were solicited on six geographic packages of properties and USX expects to conclude transactions resulting in the sale of three packages. These transactions are expected to be closed by the end of the third quarter. No further bids are being solicited for the remaining properties.

USX is a steel and energy concern.
