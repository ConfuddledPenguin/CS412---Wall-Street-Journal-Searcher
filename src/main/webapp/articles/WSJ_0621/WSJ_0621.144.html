International Brief -- British Coal Corp.: Pension Funds Increase Bid For Globe Investment Trust

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

British Coal Corp.'s pension funds increased their hostile takeover offer for Globe Investment Trust PLC's ordinary shares to 205 pence ($3.53) from 191 pence. The new offer, which the pension funds declared final unless a rival bidder appears, values Globe at #1.11 billion ($1.91 billion).

The coal pension funds launched their bid in April and had succeeded in gaining acceptances for only 1.42% of Globe shares. Prior to launching the bid, the pension funds owned slightly less than 34% of Globe.
