HOME DEPOT Inc. (Atlanta) -- Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it raised its rating on the 6 3/4% convertible subordinated debentures of this building materials and home improvement company to single-A-2 from single-A-3. The rating concern said the change affects about $260 million in long-term debt. Moody's also said it assigned a prospective single-A-2 rating to Home Depot's proposed $200 million convertible subordinated debenture issue. Moody's said its actions are based on its expectation that Home Depot will continue to show superior cash flow and returns.
