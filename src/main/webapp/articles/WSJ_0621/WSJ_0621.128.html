Investment in U.K. Unit Trusts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

LONDON -- Net new investment in U.K. unit trusts totaled #191.9 million ($330.3 million) in May, up more than 12 times from #15.6 million in April, the Unit Trust Association said. The latest month's figure for the trusts, similar to open-ended mutual funds, is down 12% from the #219.4 million in net new investment recorded a year earlier.

The value of funds under management by association members rose 7.1% to #57.1 billion from #53.3 billion in April and 10% from #51.7 billion a year earlier.

The association attributed the performance to the upturn in the value of both the U.K. and other major world stock markets during the month.

Sales of unit trust shares to investors were up 13% to #754 million in May from #666 million in April but were off 2.2% from #771 million in May 1989.

Repurchases of the shares dropped 13% to #562 million in May from #650 million in April but rose 1.8% from #552 million a year earlier.
