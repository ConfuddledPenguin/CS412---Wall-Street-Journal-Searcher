Who's News: Hills Department Stores Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

HILLS DEPARTMENT STORES Inc. (Canton, Mass.) -- John C. Brouillard, 42 years old, was named to the new position of executive vice president-chief financial officer of this discount store chain. He was previously senior vice president-finance and administration. Two others also were named executive vice presidents: Eugene J. O'Donnell, previously senior vice president-general merchandise manager; and Andrew J. Samuto, previously senior vice president-real estate development and human resources.
