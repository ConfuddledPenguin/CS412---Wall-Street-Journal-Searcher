Treasury Plans Sale Of Notes Next Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $2.18 billion in new cash with the sale Tuesday of about $11.25 billion in two-year notes and the sale Wednesday of about $8.25 billion in four-year notes to redeem $17.32 billion in maturing securities. The offerings mature on June 30, 1992, and June 30, 1994, respectively.

Tenders for the two-year notes, available in minimum $5,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Tuesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.

Tenders for the four-year notes, available in minimum $1,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Wednesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
