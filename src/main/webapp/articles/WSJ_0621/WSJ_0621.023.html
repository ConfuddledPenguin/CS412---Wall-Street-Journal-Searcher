Financing Business: Mark IV Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

MARK IV INDUSTRIES Inc. said it obtained a $300 million credit facility with a bank group led by Continental Bank and Security Pacific National Bank. The Amherst, N.Y., maker of technical products and systems said the new financing arrangement replaces an existing $125 million revolving credit facility. It added that the financing would not only meet internal needs, but could be used to make acquisitions or to purchase its stock or debt. The new credit facility consists of a three-year revolving loan that can be converted into a four-year term loan, the company said.
