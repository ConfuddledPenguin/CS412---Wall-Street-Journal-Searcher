Who's News: Meridian Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

MERIDIAN BANCORP (Reading, Pa.) -- Steven P. Kurtz, 35 years old, formerly executive vice president, corporate and commercial lending, in Meridian Bank's Delaware Valley division, was named chairman, president and chief executive officer of the American Title Insurance unit, Miami, succeeding John W. Tagge, who resigned for personal reasons but remains a consultant during the transition.
