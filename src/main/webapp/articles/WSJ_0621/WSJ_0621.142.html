International Brief -- Hambros PLC: Pretax Profit Increased 10%, Boosted by Banking Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

Hambros PLC, a British merchant bank and financial services concern, said pretax profit rose 10% in the year ended March 31. Pretax profit was #75.3 million ($129.8 million), up from a restated #68.7 million a year earlier. Hambros had reported its pretax profit as #70.2 million the year earlier. Hambros said it restated its 1989 accounts to reflect changes in accounting methods applied to deferred costs.

Net profit attributable to shareholders rose 42% to #59.2 million from a restated #41.6 million a year earlier. Net profit for the year-earlier period was initially reported as #41.9 million.

Per-share earnings rose 15% to 28.9 pence from a restated 25.1 pence. Per-share earnings were first reported at 25.3 pence.

The dividend for the year is 11.5 pence a share, up 15% from 10 pence.

Hambros linked its profit growth to a 74% jump in profit from its Hambros Bank PLC operation. Hambros Bank's pretax profit rose to #64.1 million from #36.8 million a year earlier.
