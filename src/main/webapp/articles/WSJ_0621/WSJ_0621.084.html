Japanese Deficit Bonds

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

TOKYO -- Because of a drop in tax revenue from financial concerns, the Finance Ministry issued 208.5 billion yen ($1.35 billion) of deficit-financing bonds to its trust fund bureau for the past fiscal year, ministry officials said. An official familiar with the ministry's debt issuing plans said these were the only deficit financing bonds issued for fiscal 1989, which ended March 31. The ministry was originally budgeted to issue 1.331 trillion yen of deficit-financing bonds, but that was reduced to 681 billion yen in the revised budget.

The Finance Ministry has been reducing the amount of deficit-financing bonds that it issues with the aim of eliminating them.
