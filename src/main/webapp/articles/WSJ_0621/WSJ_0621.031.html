Financing Business: Valhi Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

VALHI Inc., Dallas, has withdrawn its proposed public offering of $100 million in convertible subordinated debentures due 2015, citing unfavorable market conditions. The preliminary prospectus was offered May 31, but a subsequent 10-day road show "generated significant interest, but not at terms acceptable to us," a company executive said. Valhi, a holding company controlled by Dallas billionaire Harold Simmons, planned to use the proceeds to accelerate repayment of debt maturing in 1994. But last week Moody's Investors Service Inc. assigned a single-B-3 credit rating to the proposed debentures, saying its assessment of the proposal included its junior subordinated position and the superior position of hefty debt at the operating subsidiary level.
