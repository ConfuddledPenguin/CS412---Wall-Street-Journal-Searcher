Who's News: Columbia Gas System Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

COLUMBIA GAS SYSTEM Inc. (Wilmington, Del.) -- John D. Daly, 53 years old, was named executive vice president and a director of this natural gas utility holding company, effective Sept. 1. As a director, he will succeed J. Robert Fletcher, who is to retire. Mr. Daly is chairman and chief executive officer of the Columbia Gas Transmission Cos. unit; his successor hasn't been named.
