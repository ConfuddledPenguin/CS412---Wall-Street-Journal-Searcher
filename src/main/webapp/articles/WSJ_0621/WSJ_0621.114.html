Business Brief -- Coca-Cola Co.: Australia's Coca-Cola Amatil Pursues Indonesian Franchise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

Coca-Cola Amatil Ltd., a Sydney, Australia, company that makes 99% of the Coca-Cola consumed in Australia and is 59.5%-owned by Coca-Cola Co. of the U.S., is negotiating to buy a stake in an Indonesian Coke franchise. A spokesman for Coca-Cola Amatil said the company is "actively pursuing" Coca-Cola interests in Southeast Asia.

He said the company's chairman and managing director Dean Wills has been to Jakarta for discussion about buying into a Coca-Cola franchise there, but said it would be "months" until negotiations are finalized.

Coca-Cola Amatil currently has no beverage interests in Southeast Asia. It owns 99% of the Coca-Cola franchises in Australia, all of the New Zealand franchises and around 70% of the Austrian franchises.
