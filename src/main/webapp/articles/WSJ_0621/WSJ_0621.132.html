Merge Dirge

I'm somewhat in awe

Of the corporate raider

Who can be either overt

Or an enfilader.

I wonder if the taker-over

Feels elated or shaken

Or even imagines himself

As the overtaken.

I'm edgy enough

When I am followed,

But that's nothing compared

With being swallowed.

-- Dow Richardson.
