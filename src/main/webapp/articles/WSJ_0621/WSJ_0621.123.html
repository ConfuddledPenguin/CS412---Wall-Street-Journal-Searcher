West German Auto Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West German personal car production rose 16% in May to 419,600 units from 361,186 a year earlier, the German Automobile Industry Association said. Some of the increase came from an additional work day in May, compared with the year-earlier month, but even on a calendar-adjusted basis, output climbed 11%.

In the first five months of this year, car production increased 6% to 2,071,000 from 1,969,000 a year earlier.
