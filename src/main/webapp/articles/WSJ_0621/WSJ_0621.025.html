Dividend News: Smith International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

SMITH INTERNATIONAL Inc., a Houston-based energy services concern, said that under a newly adopted stockholder rights plan, it will distribute as a dividend one preferred-stock purchase right for each common share held. The rights expire June 19, 2000. The company said each right, when exercised, entitles the holder to purchase 1/100th of a newly issued share of Series A junior participating preferred at an exercise price of $50. The rights become exercisable if a 20% ownership stake in the company is acquired, among other conditions. A spokeswoman said adoption of the plan wasn't a response to any known takeover threats. Rights plans can be used as a defense against a hostile bid by making an offer more expensive.
