Exabyte Corp. Signs Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

BOULDER, Colo. -- Exabyte Corp. said it signed a three-year, $50 million agreement to buy 8mm mechanical tape decks from Sony Corp. The contract, valued at $25 million in the first year, replaces an existing agreement that was effective through next May. Exabyte makes cartridge tape storage systems for mainframe computers.
