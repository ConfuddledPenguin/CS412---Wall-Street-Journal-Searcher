B.F. Goodrich Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

AKRON, Ohio -- B.F. Goodrich Co. said its aerospace division agreed to form a joint venture in Britain with an individual, Denis Bishop, to repair, overhaul and exchange aircraft landing gear and other parts. Terms weren't disclosed. Mr. Bishop, a majority shareholder of GB Aerospace Ltd., of Windsor, England, was named managing director of the newly created company, B.F. Goodrich Component Services Ltd. It will be housed in a facility under construction outside of London, Goodrich said.

Goodrich, a specialty chemical concern, said the venture marks the second business launched in Britain this year by its aerospace division. An aircraft tire, wheel and brake service center began operation outside London in May.
