Who's News: Tidewater Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

TIDEWATER Inc. (New Orleans) -- Donald T. Bollinger, 40 years old, chairman of Bollinger Machine Shop & Shipyard Inc., Lockport., La., and Hugh J. Kelly, 65, formerly president of Ocean Drilling & Exploration Co., were elected directors of this operator of oil-service boats, increasing board membership to seven.
