Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Seat

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

NEW YORK -- A membership on the Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange was sold for $92,000. That was down from the previous sale Friday at $95,000 and one a year ago for $108,000. Currently, a seat is bid at $80,000 and offered at $99,000, according to an exchange spokeswoman.
