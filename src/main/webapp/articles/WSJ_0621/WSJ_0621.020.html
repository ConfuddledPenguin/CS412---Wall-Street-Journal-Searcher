Rockwell International

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

WASHINGTON -- Rockwell International Corp. was issued a $59.6 million Air Force contract for research on the Maui Optical Spacetracking Station.

Computer Sciences Raytheon, a joint venture of Computer Sciences Corp. and Raytheon Co., won a $20 million Air Force contract for support of the Eastern Test Range.

Texas Instruments Inc. was awarded a $16.8 million Navy contract for aircraft radar equipment.

Loral Randtron Systems, a unit of Loral Corp., got a $14.2 million Navy contract for aircraft antenna assemblies.
