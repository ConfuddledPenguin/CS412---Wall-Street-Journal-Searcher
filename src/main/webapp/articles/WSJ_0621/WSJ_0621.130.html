Business Brief -- Ethyl Corp.: Research Facility in Belgium Planned for European Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

Ethyl S.A., the European unit of Ethyl Corp., said it plans to locate a research and development facility at the Catholic University of Leuven, Belgium, about 19 miles southeast of Brussels. Ethyl S.A. said its initial investment in the facility will be about $8 million for 20 acres of land -- with an option to buy five more acres -- initial construction and equipment. The company said construction will begin in mid-1991 and the first phase is expected to be completed in early 1993.

In addition to research and development, the facility will market and sell Ethyl S.A.'s industrial chemicals, petroleum additives, and other specialty chemicals, many of which are manufactured in its Feluy, Belgium, complex.

Ethyl Corp., Richmond, Va., also has an R&D facility in England, which serves Europe and the Middle East.
