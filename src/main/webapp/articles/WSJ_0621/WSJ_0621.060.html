Keystone International Rights

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

HOUSTON -- Keystone International Inc. said that under its new rights plan it will give holders of its common stock one depositary preferred share purchase right for each common share held. The manufacturer of flow-control products said the rights take effect upon accumulation of 20% or more of the company's common and weren't adopted in response to "any specific effort to acquire control of the company." The rights expire in March 2000.
