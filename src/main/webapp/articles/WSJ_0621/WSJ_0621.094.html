Marketing & Media: Straight Arrow to Launch Men's Magazine in Fall '91

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

NEW YORK -- Straight Arrow Publishers Inc. said it will launch a new general interest men's magazine, joining an increasingly crowded field. Straight Arrow, which publishes Rolling Stone and US magazines, said it will launch the as-yet-unnamed magazine in fall 1991. The closely held company had recently been rumored as a buyer for Smart, a year-old men's magazine owned by New American Magazines Co.

The Straight Arrow publication will join a horde of new U.S. men's magazines that focus on issues beyond typical male magazine topics. News Corp.'s Murdoch Magazines plans to launch Men's Life this fall. Christopher Kimball, the founder of Cook's magazine, is planning Men, aimed at the 25-to-40-year-old set. And Conde Nast Publications Inc. has revamped Details as a style magazine for young men.

John Rasmus, 36-years-old, who for the past six years has been the editor of Outside, a Chicago-based monthly, will be the editor of Straight Arrow's new publication. Under Mr. Rasmus, Outside magazine won two National Magazine Awards.
