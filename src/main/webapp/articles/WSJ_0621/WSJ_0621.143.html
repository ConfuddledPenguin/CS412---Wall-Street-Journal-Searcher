Business Brief -- Moto Photo Inc.: Firm Expects to Post Drop In Second-Quarter Earnings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

Moto Photo Inc., Dayton, Ohio, said that it expects to report profit for the second quarter, but that the net income will be lower than the year-earlier $201,018, or one cent a share. The franchiser and owner of one-hour photo processing stores said earnings from those operations improved in the current quarter, compared with a year earlier. But it said the year-ago results included a substantial profit from store refranchising.

Moto Photo said it expects to report "considerably improved" first-half results. In the 1989 first half, the company had a net loss of $1.2 million. The company also said full-year earnings "will be substantially better." It had a 1989 net loss of $1.9 million, or 44 cents a share.
