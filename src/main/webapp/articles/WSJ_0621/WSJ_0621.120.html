U.K. Current Account Deficit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

LONDON -- Britain's current account deficit is expected to have narrowed to #1.4 billion ($2.4 billion) in May from #1.78 billion in April, based on an average of forecasts by 10 economists. Similarly, the U.K. merchandise trade deficit in May is expected to have narrowed to #1.6 billion from #1.78 billion in April.

The May trade data are due tomorrow.
