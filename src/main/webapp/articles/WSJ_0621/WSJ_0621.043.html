Who's News: Manufacturers Hanover Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

MANUFACTURERS HANOVER Corp. (New York) -- Three senior managers were promoted: Prescott H. Blatterman, III, to executive vice president and credit policy officer for the regional banking group, a new position; Salvatore J. Bommarito, to senior vice president in charge of mergers and acquisitions and high-yield placements, also a new position; and Michael M. Cassell, to executive vice president in charge of the private banking and investment division. Mr. Cassell succeeds Lindsay Macarthur, who is retiring at the end of the month.
