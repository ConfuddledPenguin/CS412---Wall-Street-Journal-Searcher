Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

WHO'S NEWS: Harrison Ellenshaw was named to the new post of vice president, visual effects, for Walt Disney Pictures, a subsidiary of Walt Disney Co.'s Walt Disney Studios. He recently served as visual effects supervisor on Touchstone Pictures' "Dick Tracy." . . . Rene Bonnell, chairman of Canal Plus Productions, was appointed a director of Carolco Pictures Inc., increasing the board to 13.
