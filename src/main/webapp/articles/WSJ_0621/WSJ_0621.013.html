Who's News: Two Pesos Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

TWO PESOS Inc. (Houston, Texas) -- Ghulam M. Bombaywala, having exercised an option to buy an additional 1.4 million shares of the company's common stock from chairman Marno M. McDermott, Jr., increasing his stake to 2 million shares, or 24.7%, will become chief executive of the fast-food company July 1. In addition, Mr. Bombaywala has been granted voting rights to Mr. McDermott's remaining 2 million shares of common stock, giving him control of 49.2% of the shares outstanding.
