Business Brief -- Du Pont Co.: Chemical Concern Will Buy A Unit of JGL Investments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

Du Pont Co. said it will acquire Fibremakers, a supplier of nylon and polyester to Australian fibers markets, from JGL Investments of Melbourne, for $150 million. Du Pont, based in Wilmington, Del., said the acquisition is expected to be completed by July 1.

The transaction is part of Du Pont's $1 billion expansion of manufacturing operations in the Asia Pacific region during the next decade, a Du Pont official said.

Fibremakers, of Bayswater, Victoria, has a plant there and one in Penrith, New South Wales. Du Pont said it will manufacture carpet fibers and industrial polyester and nylon at the sites for Asia Pacific markets.
