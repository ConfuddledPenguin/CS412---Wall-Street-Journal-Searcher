Who's News: Royal Bank of Scotland Group PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

ROYAL BANK OF SCOTLAND GROUP PLC -- George Mathewson, 50 years old, was named deputy group chief executive of this bank-holding company. Mr. Mathewson was previously director of strategy and planning for the group. Rob E. Farley, 59, deputy group chief executive since 1986, will retire in September.
