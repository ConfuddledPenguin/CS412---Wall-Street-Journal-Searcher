First National Corp. Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- First National Corp. said shareholders at the annual meeting approved increasing the company's authorized common shares to 7.3 million from 5.3 million. Shareholders also approved changing the company's name to C.A. Short International Inc., effective tomorrow.

The company is a supplier of employee motivational and incentive awards.
