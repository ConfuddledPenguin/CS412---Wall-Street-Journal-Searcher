Times Mirror Talks End

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

LOS ANGELES -- Times Mirror Co.'s Southern Connecticut Newspapers Inc. said that negotiations to acquire Acorn Press Inc. ended by mutual agreement. A reason wasn't given. As previously reported, the unit of the Los Angeles-based media company agreed in principle last March to acquire Acorn, publisher of five weekly newspapers in Fairfield County, Conn. Terms weren't disclosed.
