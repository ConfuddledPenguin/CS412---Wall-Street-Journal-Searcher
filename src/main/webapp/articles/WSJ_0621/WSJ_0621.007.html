Seat on Chicago Merc Is Sold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange was sold for $485,000, down $10,000 from the previous sale of $495,000. Seats currently are quoted at $395,000, bid, and $505,000, asked. The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set March 9, 1989.
