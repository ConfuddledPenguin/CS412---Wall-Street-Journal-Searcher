Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Atlantic City Electric Co., a unit of Atlantic Energy Inc., shelf offering of 1.2 million shares of preferred stock.

Biomedical Waste Systems Inc., initial offering of 1.5 million units, each consisting of four common shares and four redeemable class A warrants, which entitle the holder to purchase one common share and one redeemable Class B warrant, via D.H. Blair & Co.

Cardinal Distribution Inc., offering of $60 million of convertible subordinated debentures, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Contel Corp., proposed offering of 14.5 million common shares, via Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc.

Magic Restaurants Inc., offering of 550,000 common shares and 550,000 redeemable warrants, via Rosenkrantz Lyon & Ross.

United Illuminating Co., offering of $220 million of secured lease obligation bonds, via Morgan Stanley & Co.

USAir Inc., a unit of USAir Group Inc., shelf offering of up to $500 million of debt securities.
