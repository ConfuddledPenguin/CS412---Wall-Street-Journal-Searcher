Who's News: Riser Foods Realigns Management Positions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/21/90

BEDFORD HEIGHTS, Ohio -- Riser Foods Inc. said Anthony Rego, who had been a co-chairman of the food store chain, was named chairman and chief executive officer. Mr. Rego, 48 years old, who will be responsible for corporate administration, direction and planning, said the move is aimed at clarifying the company's chain of command and improving operating efficiency.

Michael Borstein, 51, who had shared the chairman's position with Mr. Rego, was named vice chairman. He will be responsible for the company's Seaway Foods wholesale operations.

As part of the changes, the company said Anthony Petrillo stepped down as executive vice president, but now is a consultant to Riser management.

Charles Rini continues as president and chief operating officer at Riser, the company said.
