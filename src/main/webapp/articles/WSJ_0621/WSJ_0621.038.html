Swiss Jobless Rate Unchanged

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

BERN, Switzerland -- Switzerland's jobless rate of 0.5% in May was unchanged from April, but was down from 0.6% a year earlier, the government said. Despite the flat unemployment rate in May, the number of unemployed people fell to 15,979 from 16,634 in April. In May 1989, the number of jobless stood at 17,225. The number of unfilled jobs declined to 18,376 in May from 18,701 in April, but rose from 18,031 a year earlier.

The May jobless rate among men was 0.4%, and among women, 0.7%.
