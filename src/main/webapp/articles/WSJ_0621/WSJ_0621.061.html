Esselte Business Merger Clears

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/21/90

GARDEN CITY, N.Y. -- Esselte Business Systems Inc. said shareholders approved its merger agreement with parent Esselte AB of Sweden, making it a wholly owned subsidiary. Esselte AB already owned 78% of the shares outstanding of the U.S. holding company for the Swedish concern's office, graphic arts and retail supplies business, and agreed last November to acquire the remaining 4.9 million shares for at least $227.9 million. The company said that following the merger vote yesterday, each remaining share will be converted to $48.06.

A spokesman said trading in the shares was discontinued after the close of the New York Stock Exchange yesterday, where they finished at $48.125, up 25 cents.
