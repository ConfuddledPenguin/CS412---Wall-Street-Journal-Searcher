NKK to Link With Raycom

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

TOKYO -- NKK Corp. will form a linkup with Raycom Systems Inc., based in Boulder, Colo., to manufacture and sell Raycom's high-speed, fiber-optic computer networking systems in Japan. NKK will begin selling one of Raycom's Local Area Networks in August under the name Transway, NKK said. The system links smaller computer networks, allowing large volumes of data to be exchanged at high speeds.
