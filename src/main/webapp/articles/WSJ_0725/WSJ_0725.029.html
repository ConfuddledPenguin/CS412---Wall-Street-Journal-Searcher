Hong Kong Prices Advance

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

HONG KONG -- Hong Kong's main measure of consumer prices advanced 9.1% in June from a year earlier, the government reported. For the 50% of urban households that spent between 2,000 and 6,499 Hong Kong dollars ($258 and $837) a month, consumer prices rose 1% from May.

For the 30% of households spending between 6,500 and 9,999 Hong Kong dollars, prices climbed 9.3% from a year ago and 0.9% from the preceding month.
