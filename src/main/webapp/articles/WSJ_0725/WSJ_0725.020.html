Bancorp Hawaii Buys FirstFed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

HONOLULU -- Bancorp Hawaii Inc. said it completed the acquisition of FirstFed America Inc., parent of a 26-branch thrift association, for $140 million. The Office of Thrift Supervision and the Federal Reserve Board this month approved the sale, in which FirstFed shareholders will receive $38.50 a share. Bancorp Hawaii, with $9.8 billion in assets after the acquisition, required that FirstFed's board and three bank officers resign as part of the deal.
