Niagara Exchange Halts Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Niagara Exchange Corp. said it has rescinded an agreement under which W.R. Berkley Corp., Greenwich, Conn., had purchased 970,000 shares of Niagara preferred stock for $8.2 million. The agreement was disallowed by the New York Department of Insurance. Niagara sought the investment to replace funds paid out last year by a Niagara subsidiary on claims arising from Hurricane Hugo.

Niagara and W.R. Berkley are both property and casualty insurance holding companies. The companies said they are continuing to discuss a possible investment in Niagara by Berkley.
