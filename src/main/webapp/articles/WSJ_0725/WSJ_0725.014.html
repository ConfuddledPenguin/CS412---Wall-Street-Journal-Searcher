Pinnacle West Capital Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Pinnacle West Capital Corp. said it will sell its uranium mining subsidiary, Malapai Resources Co., for $38 million to an affiliate of Electricite de France. The utility holding company agreed to sell the unit to the French electric utility in April, but a price wasn't disclosed. Pinnacle West said completion of the sale is subject to certain closing conditions, including approval by agencies that regulate mining activities in Texas and Wyoming.

Proceeds from the sale will be used to reduce debt.
