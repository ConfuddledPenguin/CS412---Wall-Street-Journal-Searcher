Abitibi-Price to Idle Three Mills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

TORONTO -- Abitibi-Price Inc., a forest products concern 78.9%-owned by Olympia & York Developments Ltd., said it is shutting temporarily three of its printing-paper mills, two in Quebec and one in Ontario, for eight to nine days each this month and next month, to scale down inventories. The company said idling the mills will remove 10,000 tons of uncoated groundwood grades and 4,000 tons of coated papers from the market.
