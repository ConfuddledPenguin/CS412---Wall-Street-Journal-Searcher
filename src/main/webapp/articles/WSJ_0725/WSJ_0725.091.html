Letters to the Editor: In Illinois, We Follow The Law of the Land

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

Your June 25 article "Thompson Issues Order on Patronage in Illinois" (which appeared in some editions) correctly reported that I issued an executive order directing all agencies under my control to follow the U.S. Supreme Court's ruling in Rutan v. Republican Party in Illinois. However, the article goes on to say that the "spoils system" may not be dead in Illinois and attempts to support that allegation with my explanation that when job candidates are equally qualified, "We'll hire the Republican."

The quote is correct, but it was made in reference to hiring practices before the Rutan decision.

My executive order makes it clear to everyone in Illinois government that the U.S. Supreme Court decision is the law of the land and must be followed.

James R. Thompson

Governor

State of Illinois

Springfield, Ill.
