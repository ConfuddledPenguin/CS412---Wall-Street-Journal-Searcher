Mitsubishi Power Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

TOKYO -- Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd., jointly with Mitsubishi Corp. and Siemens AG of West Germany, received an order for a power plant fired by coal and natural gas from Indonesia's electricity authority, Perusahaan Unum Listrik Negara, the Japanese company said. The plant's cost was not disclosed.
