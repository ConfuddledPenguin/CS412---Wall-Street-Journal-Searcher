Technology Brief -- BellSouth Corp.: Unit Seeks License to Study Wireless Telephone Market

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

BellSouth Enterprises, a unit of BellSouth Corp., said it has applied to the Federal Communications Commission for an experimental radio license to conduct a personal communications network trial in Atlanta, Ga. The trial, which is to take place in two phases, is intended to test the market for wireless telecommunications, such as radio telephones. BellSouth said it wants to look at how such services meet consumer needs and how they change the consumer patterns.

BellSouth is already conducting a nationwide study of broad-based marketing opportunities for personal communications networks. This study is intended to build on the results of the first study, particularly in the way potential business customers would use the networks.

Results are expected in two years.
