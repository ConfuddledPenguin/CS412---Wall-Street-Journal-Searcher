Commercial-Paper Rates Fall at Citicorp Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 7.944% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.01% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $420 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.94% to 7.948%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 7.8495% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.9647% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $380 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.849% to 7.85%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
