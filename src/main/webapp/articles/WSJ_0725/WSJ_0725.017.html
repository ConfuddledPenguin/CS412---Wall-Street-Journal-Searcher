Who's News: Borden Chemicals & Plastics L.P.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

BORDEN CHEMICALS & PLASTICS L.P. (Columbus, Ohio) -- Joseph M. Saggese, an executive vice president of Borden Inc., was elected chairman, president, chief executive officer and a director of this partnership, succeeding Anthony S. D'Amato, who was recently named president and chief operating officer of Borden Inc. The partnership was established in 1987 to acquire and operate Borden Inc.'s basic chemicals and polyvinyl chloride resins business in Geismar, La., and Illiopolis, Ill. Borden Inc. owns a 2% interest and serves as general partner.
