Who's News: Chase Manhattan Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

CHASE MANHATTAN Corp. (New York) -- Robert D. Hunter, 46 years old, was named executive vice president of the Chase Manhattan Bank subsidiary of this bank holding company. Previously senior vice president, Mr. Hunter continues as national financial-services executive in charge of private banking, upscale consumer lending, luxury auto financing, investment services, home mortgage servicing and origination and education finance.
