Warm Reminder

In summer bliss,

At beach or hills,

You forget

The heating bills.

These bits of paper,

Please remember,

Come again

In late September.

-- Robert Gordon.

High Style

Though I'm not ready for thousand-dollar suits,

I might give one a chance --

But my question to the haberdasher is:

Will I get two pairs of pants?

-- Bud Stiller.
