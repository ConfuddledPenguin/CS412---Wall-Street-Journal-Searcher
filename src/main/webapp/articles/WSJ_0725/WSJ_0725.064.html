Who's News: Bell Atlantic Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

BELL ATLANTIC Corp. (Arlington, Va.) -- Thomas C. Burns, president and chief executive of Bell Atlantic's Chesapeake & Potomac Telephone Co. of West Virginia, was named senior executive and chief representative for Bell Atlantic and Ameritech Corp. in New Zealand. Both companies joined two New Zealand concerns last month to buy the state-owned telecommunications company. Mr. Burns will be succeeeded as president and chief executive of Chesapeake & Potomac in West Virginia by David E. Berry, currently vice president of human resources for Bell Atlantic. Mr. Berry, in turn, will be succeeded by Charles W. Christ Jr., currently vice president in charge of operations for Chesapeake & Potomac Telephone Co. of Virginia.
