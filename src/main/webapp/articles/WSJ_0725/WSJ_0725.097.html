Business Brief -- Continental AG: General Tire Unit Extends Price Jump to Wholesalers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

General Tire, an Akron, Ohio-based unit of Continental AG of West Germany, said it is raising prices on private-brand tires by 2% to 4%, effective Sept. 4. Most U.S. tire makers, including General, previously announced similar price increases on replacement tires that they sell under their own brand names. General's increase becomes effective Aug. 1.

The new move by General extends that increase to tires sold to major wholesalers, mass merchandisers and other concerns that contract with tire manufacturers to produce tires for them under private brands.

Replacement tire prices have been discounted more than usual this year because the auto production slowdown created a tire surplus.
