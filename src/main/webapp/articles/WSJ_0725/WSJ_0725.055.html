Spiegel Earnings Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- Spiegel Inc. said second-quarter profit rose 15% on a 23% increase in revenue. The catalog retailer reported earnings of $7.7 million, or 15 cents a share, compared with $6.7 million, or 13 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue rose to $403.7 million from $327.6 million.

John J. Shea, chief executive officer, said he sees "significant opportunities for growth" despite "an extremely competitive" retail environment that he expects to continue through the end of the year.
