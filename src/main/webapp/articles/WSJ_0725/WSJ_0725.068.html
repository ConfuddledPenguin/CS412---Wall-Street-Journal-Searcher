Hawaiian Air Gets U.S. Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

WASHINGTON -- Hawaiian Airlines, a unit of HAL Inc., received a $56.8 million Air Force contract for long-range airlifting.

Bell Helicopter Textron Inc., a unit of Textron Inc., got a $12.9 million Navy contract for attack helicopters.

Lockheed Corp. was awarded an $11 million Navy contract for aircraft electronics.
