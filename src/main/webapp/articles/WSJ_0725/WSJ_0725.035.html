Morrison Knudsen Wins Accord

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it received a $36 million contract to build a container terminal facility for the Port of Los Angeles. The largest of four structures planned for the site will be a four-level 61,000 square-foot office building. The company said it expects to complete construction in early 1992.
