Treasury Will Raise $2.3 Billion in Cash

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $2.3 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of a record $18.4 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.1 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Nov. 1 and Jan. 31, 1991, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
