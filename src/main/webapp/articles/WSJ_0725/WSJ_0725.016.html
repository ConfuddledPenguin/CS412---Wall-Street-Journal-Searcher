Who's News: SRI International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

SRI INTERNATIONAL Inc. -- James E. Carnes, 50 years old, was named president-elect of David Sarnoff Research Center, Princeton, N.J., a unit of SRI, effective Dec. 1. Mr. Carnes succeeds James J. Tietjen, who on Monday was named president and chief executive officer of SRI, also effective Dec. 1. Mr. Carnes was previously vice president of consumer electronics and information sciences at the research center.
