GATX Purchase From Exxon

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

CHICAGO -- A GATX Corp. unit said it completed the purchase of a 1.6 million-barrel petroleum terminal in Paulsboro, N.J., from Exxon Corp. Terms weren't disclosed. GATX Terminals Corp., which entered into the agreement late last year, said it has been leasing and operating a portion of the facility since the beginning of this year.

GATX has interests in rail car leasing, maintenance and management as well as capital goods leasing, financial services, real estate, bulk liquid terminal storage and pipelines and shipping.
