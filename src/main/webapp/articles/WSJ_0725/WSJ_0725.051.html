Hewlett-Packard Resale Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

PALO ALTO, Calif. -- Hewlett-Packard Co. said it signed a three-year, $40 million agreement, with Visionary Design Systems to resell its HP workstations and computer-aided design software. Visionary Design Systems, a closely held custom software firm based in Mountain View, Calif., will also provide consulting, training and custom software services to Hewlett-Packard, a maker of computers and testing and measurement equipment.
