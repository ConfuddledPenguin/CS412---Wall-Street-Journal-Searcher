Crop Genetics Offers Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

HANOVER, Md. -- Crop Genetics International Corp. said it began an offering of 2.5 million in 9 1/2% convertible exchangeable preferred stock priced at $10 a share. The company said it granted managing underwriters Lehman Brothers, a unit of Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. and Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc. an option to purchase an additional 375,000 shares to cover over-allotments.

Crop Genetics, an agricultural biotechnology firm, said it will use proceeds to finance research and development of various commercial products.

The company currently has 4.6 million shares outstanding.
