Who's News: GNW Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

GNW FINANCIAL Corp. (Bremerton, Wash.) -- Ralph Lintz, 50 years old, was named president and chief operating officer for this thrift holding corporation. He succeeds Raymond L. Soule, 55, who will continue as chairman and chief executive officer. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

GNW FINANCIAL Corp. named Ralph Lintz chief operating officer of Great Northwest Federal Savings & Loan Association, a unit of this holding company. Wednesday's edition misstated his position.

(WSJ July 27, 1990)
