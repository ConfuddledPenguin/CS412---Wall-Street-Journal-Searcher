Business Brief -- Tambrands Inc.: Net Rose 21% in Quarter At Personal-Products Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

Tambrands Inc. said second-quarter net income rose 21%, reflecting improved margins, work-force reductions and the divestitures of its home diagnostics and cosmetics businesses this year. The maker of personal-care products said second-quarter results were records; net rose to $31.8 million, or $1.49 a share, from $26.2 million, or $1.17 a share; sales rose 1.9% to $169.3 million from $166.1 million. There were about one million fewer average shares outstanding in the latest quarter.

Tambrands, of Lake Success, N.Y., said the fact that sales rose despite the divestitures reflected a product introduction in the U.S. and increased shipments in Europe.

For the six months, net rose 22% to $53.8 million, or $2.50 a share, from $44.2 million, or $1.98 a share. Revenue rose to $328.5 million from $296 million.
