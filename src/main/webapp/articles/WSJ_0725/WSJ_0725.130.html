Business Brief -- Melville Corp.: Retailer's 2nd-Quarter Net Rose 27% on Higher Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

Specialty retailer Melville Corp., Rye, N.Y., reported second-quarter earnings rose 27% on a 16% sales increase, noting that the strong, Easter-week selling period occurred in the quarter. Net income rose to $78.3 million, or 72 cents a share, from $61.3 million, or 56 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales rose to $1.95 billion from $1.68 billion. The 1989 Easter week was in the first quarter.

In the first six months, earnings were flat at $86 million, or 76 cents a share, compared with $85.9 million, or 78 cents share, a year ago. The company cited softness in the apparel and footwear businesses in New England and larger-than-expected markdowns in leather apparel.
