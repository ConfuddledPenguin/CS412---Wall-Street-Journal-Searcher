Dividend News: Robert Half International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

ROBERT HALF INTERNATIONAL Inc., Menlo Park, Calif., instituted an anti-takeover "poison pill," declaring a dividend of one preferred share purchase right for each share of common stock. The dividend will be payable Aug. 8 to stock of record on that date. The rights will expire Aug. 8, 2000. The terms of the rights are activated only if 30% or more of the company's stock is acquired by a person or group. In the event of a sale or merger, the holder is entitled to purchase stock in the acquiring company at half the market price. Robert Half International specializes in the placement of tax, banking and financial personnel services.
