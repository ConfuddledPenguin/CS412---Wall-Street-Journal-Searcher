Letters to the Editor: Portney's Complaint

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

In your July 13 lead editorial, "Environmental Balance," you correctly summarize some of my conclusions about the likely benefits and costs of the clean-air legislation now before Congress. In doing so, however, you refer to me as "a leading environmentalist." While I do care deeply about the environment, the term "environmentalist" is almost always reserved for members of environmental-advocacy organizations. I believe your editorial creates the impression that I speak for those groups, and that is quite unfair to them since they may disagree strongly with my conclusions.

Paul R. Portney

Vice President

Resources for the Future

Washington
