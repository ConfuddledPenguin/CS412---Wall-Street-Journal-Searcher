Who's News: Amgen's Chief, Binder, Adds Chairman's Post

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

THOUSAND OAKS, Calif. -- Gordon M. Binder, 54-year-old chief executive officer of Amgen Inc., a biotechnology company, was elected to the additional post of chairman, succeeding George B. Rathmann. Mr. Rathmann, 62, was re-elected to the board and will serve as chairman emeritus. A chemist, Mr. Rathmann joined Amgen as president and chief executive in 1980, and was elected chairman in 1986. In October 1988, he resigned as president and chief executive but remained chairman.

He said the election of Mr. Binder "completes the transition in management that began two years ago."
