Who's News: BCE Mobile Communications Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

BCE MOBILE COMMUNICATIONS Inc. (Montreal) -- John McLennan, 45-year-old chief executive officer of this cellular telephone company, was named to the additional post of president, succeeding Alan Walter, 47. Leonce Montambault and Robert Kearney, respectively chairman and executive vice president of Bell Canada, were named to the BCE Mobile board, succeeding Mr. Walter and C. Wesley Scott, who accepted positions with Bell Canada. Bell Canada, a telephone utility, is owned by BCE Inc., a Montreal-based holding company that also owns 73% of BCE Mobile.
