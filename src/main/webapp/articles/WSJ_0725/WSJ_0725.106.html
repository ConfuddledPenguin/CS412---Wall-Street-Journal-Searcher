Business Brief -- CalMat Co.: Company Posts Lower Net For Quarter and 1st Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

CalMat Co. reported lower second-quarter and first-half earnings and said it expects 1990 earnings to be lower than last year's $78.1 million, or $2.52 a share. The Los Angeles-based producer of construction materials cited a decline in construction activity in many of its market areas and a significant reduction for cement and ready-mixed concrete in Southern California.

In the quarter, net income fell 31% to $11.8 million, or 40 cents a share, from $17.1 million, or 55 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue declined 8% to $162.4 million from $176.6 million. First-half net decreased 27% to $22.2 million, or 74 cents a share, from $30.3 million, or 98 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue slipped 5.6% to $310.6 million from $329.2 million.

CalMat also declared a one-time dividend of 10 cents a share payable Sept. 6 to stock of record on Aug. 10. It said it declared the payout to fulfill a commitment.
