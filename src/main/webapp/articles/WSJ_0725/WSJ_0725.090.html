Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

American Airlines Inc., a unit of AMR Corp., offering of $288 million of equipment trust certificates, via Merrill Lynch Capital Markets and Salomon Brothers Inc.

Community Health Systems Inc., offering of 1.8 million common shares, via J.C. Bradford & Co. and Bateman Eicher, Hill Richards Inc.

Eaton ETN Offshore Ltd., a unit of Eaton Corp., shelf offering of $100 million of debt securities and warrants to purchase debt securities.

Leslie's Poolmart, offering of 2.4 million common shares, 1.9 million will be sold by the company and 500,000 shares by holders, via Montgomery Securities and Robertson, Stephens & Co.

Mexico Capital Growth Fund Inc., initial offering of five million common shares, via First Boston Corp., Bear, Stearns & Co. and Prudential-Bache Capital Funding.

Unicare Financial Corp., secondary offering of 1.5 million common shares, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. and Dean Witter Reynolds Inc.
