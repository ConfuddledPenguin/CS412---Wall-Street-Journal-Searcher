Who's News: Corroon & Black Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

CORROON & BLACK Corp. (New York) -- Michael P. Sabanos was named vice president and controller of this insurance brokerage company, succeeding Robert P. Cuthbert, who was named first vice president and chief financial officer late last year. Mr. Sabanos was senior manager at Deloitte & Touche, a New York-based accounting firm.
