Financing Business: Glenmore Distilleries Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

GLENMORE DISTILLERIES CO., Louisville, Ky., said it borrowed $35 million through a private placement of senior notes and has arranged a new $70 million revolving credit agreement with banks. The 10.63% notes are due June 30, 2000. They were placed with a group of insurance companies, and proceeds were used to pay debt, the company said. First Boston Corp. acted as Glenmore's agent. The revolving credit replaces a $75 million facility with the same group of banks. It is split into two portions -- $30 million for working capital and $40 million for possible acquisitions. Citizens Fidelity Bank & Trust Co. of Louisville serves as agent bank. Glenmore is a maker of alcoholic beverages.
