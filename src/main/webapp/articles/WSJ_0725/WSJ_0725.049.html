Daimler-Benz Unit's Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

BONN -- AEG AG said it obtained a 30 million mark contract ($18.5 million) from Stahlwerke Peine-Salzgitter AG, a unit of Salzgitter AG, to modernize part of its steel works. AEG, the electrical unit of West German auto maker Daimler-Benz AG, said the contract involves modernization of the electrical system of a hot-rolled steel mill including the fundamental automation of the unit. Work is scheduled to be completed by the end of 1991.

The Salzgitter steel and engineering group is a subsidiary of Preussag AG, a West German energy, metals, trading and engineering concern.
