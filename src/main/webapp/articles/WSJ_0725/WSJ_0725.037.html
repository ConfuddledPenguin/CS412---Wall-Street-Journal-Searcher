Revco Wins Court Extension

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

TWINSBURG, Ohio -- Revco D.S. Inc. said the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Akron extended until Aug. 31 the exclusive period that the drugstore chain has to file a reorganization plan. The previous extension was to expire July 31. The court gave Revco until Nov. 30 to obtain acceptance of its plan.

Revco has been operating under Chapter 11 protection from creditors since July 28, 1988.
