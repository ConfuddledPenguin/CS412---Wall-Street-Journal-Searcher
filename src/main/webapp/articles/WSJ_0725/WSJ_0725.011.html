Armco Inc. Reports Loss of $85.1 Million In Second Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

PARSIPPANY, N.J. -- Armco Inc. reported an expected second-quarter loss as a result of a series of unusual charges, combined with poor results from steel operations. The loss totaled $85.1 million, or 98 cents a share, which contrasted with net income of $75.6 million, or 84 cents a share, a year earlier. In the 1989 quarter, Armco had a large gain on the sale of most of its carbon steel operations to a joint venture with Kawasaki Steel Corp. of Japan.

Armco said it expects to be profitable for the second half, but didn't make a specific forecast.

Armco said that excluding unusual items in both years, the company had a second-quarter loss of $5.7 million, compared with profit of $33.7 million in the year-earlier period.

Armco said its board will decide Friday whether to continue paying the 10 cent quarterly dividend. As previously reported, provisions of the 13.5% senior notes due in 1994 could prevent Armco from paying a dividend on common stock after the loss.

Sales for the quarter were $457.2 million, down from $665.6 million a year earlier. The 1989 quarter's sales included $204.3 million from Armco's former Eastern Steel division.

The latest quarter included unusual charges of $30 million for Armco's finance and insurance runoff companies and charges of $15 million for federal tax assessments for prior years. Other charges totaling $56 million resulted from a breach-of-contract suit against Armco in Houston, a judgment against Armco in Minnesota and other litigation and claims.

Partly offsetting those charges, the company had gains of $3.5 million on the sale of a tubing plant in Wildwood, Fla., and $18.1 million on the sale of an additional 5% ownership of Armco Steel Co. L.P. to Kawasaki Steel.

For the first half, Armco had a net loss of $72.2 million, or 86 cents a share, in contrast to profit of $116.5 million, or $1.28 a share, a year earlier. Six-month sales were $903.1 million, down from $1.57 billion a year earlier.
