Canadian Firms' New Orders

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/25/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian manufacturers registered a 1.2% rise in new orders in May from April and a 0.6% decline in inventories, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. May was the second consecutive month in which new orders increased. They went through a sharp drop earlier in the year.

Manufacturers' shipments rose 0.1% in May as a result of an 8.8% increase in shipments by transportation equipment industries. For January through May, manufacturers' shipments were down 2.9% from a year earlier.
