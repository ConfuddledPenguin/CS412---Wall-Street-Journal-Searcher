Dividend News: Turbo Resources Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

TURBO RESOURCES Ltd., a petroleum refining and marketing concern based in Calgary, Alberta, said it proposed a 1-for-10 consolidation of its common shares, subject to regulatory and shareholder approvals. A special shareholders meeting will be held in Calgary, at a date that has yet to be specified, to vote on the proposal, Turbo said. The number of shares outstanding wasn't immediately available.
