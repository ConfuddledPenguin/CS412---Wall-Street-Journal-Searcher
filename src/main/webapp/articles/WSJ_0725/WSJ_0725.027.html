UNC Sells Two Units

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

ANNAPOLIS, Md. -- UNC Inc. said it sold two environmental-service units to Chem-Nuclear Environmental Services Inc. based in Oakbrook, Ill., for $24 million. The units, UNC Geotech and UNC Remediation, both based in Grand Junction, Colo., provide environmental restoration and waste-management services to the federal government.

UNC said the gain from the sale will be offset by an expected second-quarter write-down of its investment in its third environmental services unit UNC Reclamation, which the company is closing. The aviation products and services company said it does not expect to record a loss in the second quarter.
