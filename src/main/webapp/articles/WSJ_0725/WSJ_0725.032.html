Dividend News: Merck & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/25/90

MERCK & Co. increased its quarterly dividend 24% to 56 cents a share from 45 cents, payable Oct. 1 to stock of record Sept. 4. The Rahway, N.J., pharmaceutical company said the rise is intended to keep dividends in line with earnings growth. Merck last boosted its payout in July 1989.
