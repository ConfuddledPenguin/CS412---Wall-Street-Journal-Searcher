Business Brief -- Jacor Communications Inc.: Noteholders Agree Again To Deferral of Payments

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

Jacor Communications Inc., Cincinnati, said holders of certain notes have agreed to a further deferral of payments, while the radio and cable television concern continues negotiations with its creditors to restructure its debts. Payments on the notes that were initially due Aug. 23 were first deferred until yesterday and now have been deferred until Sept. 13.

The company previously hired Salomon Brothers Inc. to help negotiate a refinancing of its debt.
