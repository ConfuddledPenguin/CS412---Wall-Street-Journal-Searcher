Genzyme's IG Labs to Buy Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

FRAMINGHAM, Mass. -- IG Laboratories Inc., which provides genetic testing services and is 56%-owned by Boston-based Genzyme Corp., said it definitively agreed to purchase Genetic Associates of Miami Inc., which provides prenatal testing services to hospitals and clinical laboratories in Florida and Georgia. Terms weren't disclosed.
