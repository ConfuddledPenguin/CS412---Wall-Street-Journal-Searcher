Netherlands Consumer Credit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

THE HAGUE -- Consumer credit in the Netherlands increased 2% to 4.14 billion guilders ($2.35 billion) in the first half from 4.06 billion guilders a year earlier, but the underlying rate of growth was slackening, government figures show. In the 1989 first half, consumer credit rose 4% from a year earlier.
