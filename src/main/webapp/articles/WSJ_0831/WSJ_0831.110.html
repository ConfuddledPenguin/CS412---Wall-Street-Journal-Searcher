Business Brief -- Genovese Drug Stores Inc.: Fiscal Second-Quarter Profit Doubled From Year Earlier

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

Genovese Drug Stores Inc., Melville, N.Y., said fiscal second-quarter profit doubled from the year-earlier period on a 6% sales gain. Profit for the 12-week quarter ended Aug. 17 was $795,000, or 12 cents a share, up from $397,000, or six cents a share, in the year-earlier period.

Sales at the 93-store chain rose to $96.9 million from $91.1 million. For the fiscal first half of 28 weeks, net income jumped 22%, to $1.8 million, from $1.5 million; while sales increased 10%, to $225.4 million, from $204.5 million.

Genovese sold two stores and closed one in the first half. It said it plans two new stores and renovations in 24 more before the end of the fiscal year.
