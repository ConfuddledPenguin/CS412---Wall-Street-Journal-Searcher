Who's News: Northrop Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

NORTHROP Corp. (Los Angeles) -- Togo D. West Jr., a partner in the Washington office of Patterson, Belknap, Webb & Tyler, was elected senior vice president of government relations, effective in mid-September. Mr. West served as special assistant to the secretary of defense in 1979 and was general counsel of the Defense Department from January 1980 to January 1981. In his new post, he succeeds Stanley Ebner, who retired from this aerospace concern.
