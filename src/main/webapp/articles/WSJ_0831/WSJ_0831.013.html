Calgon Carbon to Buy Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

PITTSBURGH -- Calgon Carbon Corp., which produces activated carbon and supplies related equipment and services, said it agreed to purchase the operating assets of TMPC Inc., Vero Beach, Fla., principally a design and construction company specializing in solvent recovery, pollution control and the design of chemical processes. Terms weren't disclosed. Calgon said that sales of the unit last year were about $10 million, and that it expects to complete the purchase by mid-September.
