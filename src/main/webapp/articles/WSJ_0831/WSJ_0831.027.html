Who's News: Harcourt Brace Jovanovich Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

HARCOURT BRACE JOVANOVICH Inc. (Orlando, Fla.) -- Joseph R. Wright Jr. and Robert E. Evanson were elected to the board of this publishing and insurance concern. Mr. Wright is a vice chairman and a director of W.R. Grace & Co. in New York. He was also director of the Office of Management and Budget in President Ronald Reagan's cabinet from 1988 to 1989. Mr. Evanson is an executive vice president and the chief financial officer of Harcourt. He was also elected treasurer of the company.
