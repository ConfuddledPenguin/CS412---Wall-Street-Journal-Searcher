Business Brief -- EECO Inc.: Firm Files Plan at Court To Reorganize by Year-End

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

EECO Inc., Santa Ana, Calif., said it filed a plan of reorganization with the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Santa Ana that calls for satisfying creditors by continuing to sell off non-core businesses. EECO, which sought bankruptcy-law protection in May, said it hopes to sell its hotel reservations systems business in the next 60 to 90 days. The company sold its Maxi-Switch computer keyboard division this month to Silitek, a Taiwan-based electronics concern.

George B. DeHuff, EECO president and chief executive officer, said the company hopes the court will approve the reorganization plan by year's end. The restructured EECO will continue to manufacture electromechanical switches and electronic keypads in the U.S. and electromechanical switches and keyboards in Scotland.
