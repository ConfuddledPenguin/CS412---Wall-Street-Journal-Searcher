Business Brief -- Portland General Electric Co.: Public Agency Recommends $917,000 '91 Revenue Hike

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

Portland General Electric Co.. Portland, Ore., said the Oregon Public Utility Commission's staff recommended increasing its 1991 revenue by $917,000, a fraction of the $81 million the company requested in the latest round of rate-increase negotiations. In March, the utility filed a request for an $89 million increase in revenue. The utility commission came back last month with a recommendation that the Portland General Corp. unit be allowed a $6 million increase.

Portland General Electric said it will have an opportunity to rebut the commission's most recent recommendation and to hold settlement discussions with it before public hearings on the rate request open Nov. 26.
