Japan's Wholesale Prices Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

TOKYO -- Japan's overall wholesale prices from Aug. 11-20 rose 0.1% from the previous 10-day period and were up 1% from a year earlier, the Bank of Japan, the central bank, said. The wholesale price index, in which the year 1985 equals 100, stood at 90.6 at the end of the latest 10-day period.

Domestic prices rose 0.1% from the previous 10 days and increased 0.5% from a year earlier. Central bank officials said this was fueled by higher prices for processed food, especially pork and eggs.

In July, the latest full reporting month, wholesale prices rose 0.4% from the previous month, and increased 0.5% from a year earlier.
