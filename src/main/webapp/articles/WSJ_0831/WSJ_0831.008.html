Who's News: Ashland Oil Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

ASHLAND OIL Inc. (Ashland, Ky.) -- G. Thomas Wilkinson was named president of Ashland Exploration Inc., the company's oil and gas production unit, and a vice president of the parent company. Mr. Wilkinson, formerly executive vice president of Ashland Exploration, succeeds James R. Boyd, who continues as a senior vice president and group operating officer of Ashland Oil. Ashland is mainly a refiner and marketer of petroleum products, but it also is involved in chemicals, engineering and construction.
