GE Environmental Research

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

FAIRFIELD, Conn. -- General Electric Co. said it named Samuel A. Schulhof, a former associate administrator of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, as director of its newly created Environmental Research Center. GE said the research center, based at the company's research and development center in Schenectady, N.Y., will focus environmental research and development on practical applications and market opportunities for newly developed technologies. Mr. Schulhof comes to GE from the University of Pittsburgh, where he was associate vice president for research and chief operating officer for the University of Pittsburgh Trust.
