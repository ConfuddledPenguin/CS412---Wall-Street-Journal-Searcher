Air Force Awards Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

WASHINGTON -- Computer Sciences Raytheon, a joint venture of Computer Sciences Corp. and Raytheon Co., won a $68.2 million Air Force contract for operation of the Eastern Space and Missile Center.

ITT Corp. got a $58.9 million Air Force contract for operating the Western Space and Missile Center.

Union Oil Co. of California, a Unocal Corp. subsidiary, was awarded a $26 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

BMY Corp. was issued a $12.3 million Army contract for five-ton trucks.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. received an $11.3 million Air Force contract for tanker-transport-trainer design.
