Who's News: Jaguar Cars Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

JAGUAR CARS Inc. (Mahwah, N.J.) -- Michael H. Dale, 55 years old, was named president of the U.S. sales arm of Jaguar Ltd., the British luxury car maker and a unit of Ford Motor Co. He succeeds Graham W. Whitehead, 61, who retires Oct. 1. Mr. Dale was previously senior vice president of Jaguar Cars, responsible for the company's U.S. sales operations and marketing activities.
