Equitec Seeks Chapter 11 Relief

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

OAKLAND, Calif. -- Equitec Financial Group Inc., a real estate syndication firm, said it and certain of its subsidiaries sought relief under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code. The company said it needed to seek protection from creditors because of "difficult times in the real estate industry." The move will give it time to sell some of its real estate partnerships to Hallwood Realty L.P. of Dallas, the company said. Equitec also is seeking court approval to sell some of its assets to Wells Fargo Bank and Stephens Inc.

According to its Chapter 11 filing, the company's assets total $80.7 million and its liabilities total $91.7 million, including $40 million of bonds outstanding. Because of its eroding financial condition, Equitec missed bond payments in March and June.
