U S West Inc. Venture Plans Cable-TV Stakes In Norway, Sweden

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

DENVER -- U S West Cable Communications, a division of U S West Inc., and United International Holdings said they formed a $100 million joint venture to acquire overseas cable-television systems and related businesses. To be named United Communications International, the joint venture will buy an 86% interest in Norkabel, the second largest cable-TV operator in Norway, and acquire a 25% stake in Swedish Cable & Dish, the second largest cable operator in Sweden.

Norkabel serves 118,000 basic subscribers in southern Norway. Swedish Cable & Dish serves 96,000 throughout Sweden.

U S West, based in Denver, said it has a 90% interest in the joint venture. "A partnership has different sharing ratios as money is being returned," said William Elsner, president of United International. He said his company will eventually have about a one-third interest in the deal.

The venture's board will consist of an equal number of representatives from each partner, with United International acting as the managing partner.

U S West already participates in cable operations in the U.K., France and Hong Kong. Denver-based United International, formed last year by former officials of United Cable Television Corp., manages domestic media investments and international cable television systems.
