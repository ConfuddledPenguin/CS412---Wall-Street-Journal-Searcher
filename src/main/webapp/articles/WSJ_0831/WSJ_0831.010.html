Humana to Sell Two Hospitals

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Humana Inc., which owns and operates 83 hospitals and has 1.2 million members in its health benefit plans, said it agreed to sell its hospitals in Pasadena, Texas, and Shreveport, La. Terms weren't disclosed. Pasadena Health Care Management Inc. agreed to buy the 208-bed, acute-care hospital in the Texas city. Community Psy chiatric Centers of Laguna Hills, Calif., will buy the 174-bed psychiatric facility in Shreveport, Humana said. The transactions are expected to be completed within 30 days.
