Money Fund Assets Fell $542.2 Million In the Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the 520 U.S. money market mutual funds dropped $542.2 million, to $407.45 billion, in the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said. The trade group also revised the week-earlier total-asset figure to $407.99 billion, up $6.21 billion. The institute had previously reported the total assets for the week ended Aug. 22 as $408 billion, up $6.22 billion.

For the latest week, 162 institutional funds posted a $772.5 million decline in assets, to $98.7 billion; 253 general-purpose funds advanced $931.9 million to $143.38 billion; and 105 broker-dealer funds sank $701.6 million to $165.37 billion.
