Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

WHO'S NEWS: Tribune Co.'s New York Daily News named Howard Greenberg, 41, vice president and director of circulation. He previously held those posts at Tribune's News & Sun-Sentinel Co. subsidiary. He succeeds Thomas G. Iaquinta, who was named assistant to the publisher. . . . Viacom Inc.'s Viacom International Inc. named Henry J. Leingang, 43, to the new post of vice president and chief information officer. He was vice president of information services at Trian Group, formerly Triangle Industries Inc. Viacom Inc. is 84%-owned by National Amusements Inc.
