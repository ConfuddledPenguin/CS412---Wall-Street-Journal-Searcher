Chambers Development Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

PITTSBURGH -- Chambers Development Co., which operates waste-collection, hauling, recycling and disposal businesses and provides industrial security services, said it agreed to buy a sanitary landfill site and a refuse trucking operation near Allentown, Pa. The company declined to disclose the purchase price. Chambers said the acquisition is conditional on the state's issuance of required permits. The businesses, part of Chrin Brothers Landfill and Chrin Hauling Inc., together will generate more than $12 million of annual sales, Chambers said.
