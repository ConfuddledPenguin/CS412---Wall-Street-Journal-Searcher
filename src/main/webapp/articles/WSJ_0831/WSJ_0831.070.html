CBS Inc. litigation counsel Heddy Gold said CBS commentator Andy Rooney's statements in a broadcast about a product he sampled weren't deemed fact but were "inherently unverifiable comments, because they reflect Mr. Rooney's personal experience with the product." Ms. Gold was incorrectly quoted in an item in yesterday's Legal Beat column about the dismissal of a libel suit against Mr. Rooney. (See: "Law -- Legal Beat: Dismissal of Suit Against Rooney Upheld" -- WSJ Aug. 30, 1990)
