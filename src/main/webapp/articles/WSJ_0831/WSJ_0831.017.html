Community Psychiatric Centers

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

LAGUNA HILLS, Calif. -- Community Psychiatric Centers said it agreed in principle to purchase Humana Hospital-Brentwood, a 174-bed psychiatric facility in Shreveport, La., from Humana Inc., a Louisville, Ky., hospital operator. Financial terms weren't disclosed. Community Psychiatric owns and operates 48 psychiatric hospitals in the U.S., Puerto Rico and the U.K.
