French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

PARIS -- France's consumer price index in July rose 0.3% from June and was up 3% from a year earlier, confirming previously reported preliminary results, the National Statistics Institute said. The latest data put the index -- in which 1980 is equal to 100 -- at 183.6, up from 183.1 in June and 178.2 in July 1989. The final result represents a slight acceleration from June, when monthly price growth came in at 0.2%.
