Triton Energy Finds Oil

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

DALLAS -- Triton Energy Corp. said it discovered oil in the Paris Basin of France. While the Sancy Les Provins No. 1 exploratory well tested only at a rate of 160 barrels of oil a day, the No. 3 confirmation well erupted at a rate of 1,200 barrels of oil a day from a depth of 6,300 feet. Neither discovery had been previously reported by the company. Total Exploration of Paris is the operator, with a 50% interest. Its partner is Triton France, a unit of Triton Energy Corp., with the other 50% interest.
