Business Brief -- Allou Health & Beauty Care Inc.: First-Period Profit Rose 14% On Record Quarterly Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

Allou Health & Beauty Care Inc. reported fiscal first-quarter earnings rose 14% as quarterly sales reached a record. The distributor of health and beauty aids posted first-quarter earnings of $268,738, or 10 cents a share, compared with $235,437, or nine cents a share, the year before.

Sales rose 11% to $19.9 million from $17.9 million.

At an annual stockholders meeting in Brentwood, N.Y., Allou executives said the company is "expanding rapidly into the distribution of selected food products, and expects sharp increases in sales and earnings in the current fiscal year."
