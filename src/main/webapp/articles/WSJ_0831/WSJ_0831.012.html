Cedar Fair Amusement Park

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

SANDUSKY, Ohio -- Cedar Fair L.P. will spend more than $9 million to upgrade its Cedar Point amusement park for the 1991 season, about the same amount as the partnership invested at the Sandusky park for 1990, according to Cedar Fair Management Co., the managing general partner. The major 1991 project is the addition of a $7.5 million wooden roller coaster, the company said. The 160-foot-tall addition will be the world's tallest wooden roller coaster and will make Cedar Point the only amusement park with 10 roller coasters, the company said.
