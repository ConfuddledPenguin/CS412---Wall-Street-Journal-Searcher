International Brief -- Oakbridge Ltd.: Australian Firm Posts Surge In Operating Profit for Year

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

Australian coal miner Oakbridge Ltd. reported that operating profit for the fiscal year ended June 30 soared to 42.7 million Australian dollars (US$35.5 million) from A$3.4 million a year earlier. Oakbridge attributed the sharp increase to a restructuring of the company and rationalization of its assets, including the acquisition of the Australian coal-mining assets of Elders Resources NZFP Ltd. at June 30, 1989.

Firmer coal prices and a favorable exchange rate also boosted profit.

Total operating revenue jumped to A$428.6 million from A$108.9 million, as sales revenue quadrupled to A$397.9 million from A$100 million.

Oakbridge posted an operating profit before tax and abnormal items of A$25.8 million, up 659% from A$3.4 million.

It said an abnormal profit of A$25.2 million came from the sale of 20% of the assets of the Clarence Colliery and from a pension-fund surplus. Last year Oakbridge reported no abnormal profits.
