MEI Diversified Inc. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- MEI Diversified Inc. said it completed the acquisition of three companies that operate professional beauty salons in department stores for $50 million, plus the assumption of certain liabilities. MEI said it now owns 80% of MEI-Regis Salon Corp., a new company formed to acquire Glemby Co. of New York, Essanelle Salon Co. and Maxim's, both of Minneapolis. The remaining 20% of the new company will be owned by Minneapolis-based Regis Corp., the current owners of both Essanelle and Maxim's.

MEI said the combined companies, which have annual sales of about $400 million, operate over 2,000 salons in the U.S. and Canada.
