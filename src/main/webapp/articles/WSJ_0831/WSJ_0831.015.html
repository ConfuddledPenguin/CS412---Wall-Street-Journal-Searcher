Osaka Gas Sets Up Unit in U.S.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

TOKYO -- Osaka Gas Co., Japan's second-largest gas supplier, said it established a subsidiary, Osaka Gas America Inc., in Pasadena, Calif., as the base for its activities in North America. The new company is capitalized at $3 million. It is owned 50% by Osaka Gas and 50% by its wholly owned subsidiaries. It will export products and materials to its parent and affiliates in Japan, and market Osaka Gas products in North America.

The U.S. unit is Osaka Gas's second base of business overseas. Last August, it created Osaka Gas Europe PLC in London.
