GTS Forms Venture With Moscow Lab For Seismic Research

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

HOUSTON -- GTS Corp. said it has formed a seismic-data reprocessing and marketing joint venture with the Laboratory of Regional Geodynamics of Moscow. GTS, which provides seismic data and data-processing services for oil and gas exploration, said the venture will be self-supporting through its contracts.

As part of the equal-partnership venture, the two concerns will process existing onshore and offshore seismic and geophysical data for areas in the Soviet Union that have recently been opened to international energy exploration companies.

In addition, GTS will assist in upgrading the seismic-data equipment of a Soviet research vessel, which is owned by the Moscow laboratory. GTS will also augment the vessel's staff. The vessel will be used in the near future to obtain new seismic data under contracts with international oil concerns.
