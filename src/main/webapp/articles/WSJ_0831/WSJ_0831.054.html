Major U.S. Makers Planned to Assemble 2% More Cars in Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

DETROIT -- The 10 major domestic auto makers planned to build 124,038 cars this week, up 2% from 121,653 cars made a year earlier. All U.S. assembly plants will be closed Monday because of the Labor Day holiday.
