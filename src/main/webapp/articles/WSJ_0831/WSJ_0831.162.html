New Securities Issues

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

The following were among yesterday's offerings and pricings in U.S. and non-U.S. capital markets, with terms and syndicate manager, as compiled by Dow Jones Capital Markets Report: MORTGAGES

Collateralized Mortgage Securities Corp. -- $300 million of real estate mortgage investment conduit, or Remic, mortgage securities offered by First Boston Corp. The offering, through a special trust called CMSC 1990-5, is backed by Ginnie Mae's 9 1/2% securities.

Residential Funding Corp. -- $200 million of Remic mortgage securities offered in eight classes by UBS Securities Inc. The offering, Residential Funding Mortgage Securities I Inc. 1990-10, is backed by 30-year whole loans. Coupons were set at 8 1/2% on classes for which details were available.

EUROBONDS

Republic of Italy (Sovereign) -- $1 billion of Eurobonds due March 1, 1999, coupon 9 5/8%, issue price 99.85 to yield 9.63%, via J.P. Morgan Securities Ltd. Bonds fungible with outstanding $1 billion issue of same terms. Fees 0.325%.

Toshiba America Inc. (Japanese parent) -- 10 billion yen of Eurobonds due Sept. 14, 1995, 7% coupon, 100.58 issue price, via lead underwriter Nomura International PLC. Backed by "keepwell" agreement from parent Toshiba Corp. Commissions 1.875.

Baproven (Venezuela) -- 200 million deutsche marks of 11 1/8% Eurobonds due Oct. 5, 1995, issue priced par 11.11%, via Bankers Trust G.m.b.H. Guaranteed by Petroleos de Venezuela. Noncallable. Commissions total 2 1/2 points.

Misawa Homes (Japan) -- 150 million marks of bonds with equity-purchase warrants, coupon fixed at 5 1/4% due Sept. 27, 1994, priced at par, via Nomura Bank. Guaranteed by Tokai Bank. Final terms fixed Sept. 5. Commissions 2 1/4.
