Marketing & Media: Reebok Hitches Its Wagon To `Flattened' Tennis Star

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

STOUGHTON, Mass. -- Reebok International Ltd. yesterday said it was "proud" to announce that it had landed a contract with Soviet "tennis sensation" Alexander Volkov to promote its shoes and clothes. On Wednesday, Reebok crowed, Mr. Volkov had scored a "major upset" at the U.S. Open by beating top seed Stefan Edberg. Mr. Volkov will "help Reebok become a household name in the Soviet Union," the company added.

Reebok, however, was running one step slow: Mr. Volkov's upset victory was on Tuesday. On Wednesday, he was flattened at the U.S. Open by Todd Witsken, 6-2, 6-2, 6-3.

Company officials couldn't be reached for comment.
