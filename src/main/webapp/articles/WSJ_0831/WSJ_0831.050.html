Efficacy Rarity

Our government has not lost

Its sound, proficient ways

In these most adverse, trying

Census-taking days.

And I'm impelled to praise it

With silly prose and rhymes,

For I have now been counted

No less than five times.

-- Dalton L. Nevels.
