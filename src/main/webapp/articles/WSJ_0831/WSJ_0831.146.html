Scott Paper's Brazilian Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

PHILADELPHIA -- Scott Paper Co. said its Scott Worldwide unit sold its 49% stake in Brazil-based Campanhia de Papeis, a sanitary-tissue operation, to a subsidiary of Industries Klabin de Papel e Cellulosa S.A. The transaction is valued at about $49 million.

Proceeds from the sale will be used for debt reduction and other world-wide growth opportunities.

The sale, which is subject to the approval of Scott's board, should be completed in September.
