  • Date: 08/31/90

BOISE, Idaho -- TJ International said it is trying to buy a division of Alpine Engineered Products Inc., Pompano Fla. TJ International said the division, Alpine Structures, Oxford, N.C., is a manufacturer of lumber with annual sales of about $15 million.

Terms of the purchase, expected to close in mid-October, weren't disclosed. TJ International sells building materials.
