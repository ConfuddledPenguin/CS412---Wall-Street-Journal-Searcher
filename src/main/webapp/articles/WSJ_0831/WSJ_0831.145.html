Phillips Petroleum Gas Find

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/31/90

BARTLESVILLE, Okla. -- Phillips Petroleum Co. said a natural gas discovery about 50 miles east of Houston in Chambers County, Texas, flowed at a rate of about 3.1 million cubic feet of gas and 217 barrels of condensate a day through a 10/64-inch choke. Phillips is the operator of the project and other partners include Hyundai Corp. of South Korea and Opicoil America Inc. of Taiwan.
