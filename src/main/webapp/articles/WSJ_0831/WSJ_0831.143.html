T&N PLC Completes Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/31/90

ANN ARBOR, Mich. -- T&N PLC, of Britain, said it completed the merger of T&N Automotive Components Inc. with J.P. Industries Inc., Ann Arbor, Mich. The merger came after T&N, an equipment manufacturer, completed its tender offer for all the shares outstanding of J.P. Industries for $17.30 a share.

J.P. Industries makes engine and transmission parts.
