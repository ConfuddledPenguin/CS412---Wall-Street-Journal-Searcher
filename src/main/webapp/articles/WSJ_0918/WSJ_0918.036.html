Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- William J. Blomquist was named head of the Europe, Middle East, and Africa region and also was named executive vice president at American Express Bank Ltd., a subsidiary of the travel, financial and information services company. Mr. Blomquist, 46 years old, succeeds James P. Flaherty, 43, who will head marketing and development at the bank. Mr. Blomquist had been senior vice president and head of the bank's Swiss subsidiary.
