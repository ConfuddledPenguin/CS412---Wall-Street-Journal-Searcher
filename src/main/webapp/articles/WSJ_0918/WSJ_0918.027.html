Melville Corp. Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

RYE, N.Y. -- Specialty retailer Melville Corp. said it completed the acquisition of Peoples Drug Stores Inc., a unit of Montreal-based Imasco Ltd., for $325 million cash. Peoples Drug Stores, which operates 490 stores in the mid-Atlantic region, reported sales of $1.02 billion last year. Melville operates 6,925 stores across the country, including Marshalls, CVS, Chess King and Kay-Bee Toy.
