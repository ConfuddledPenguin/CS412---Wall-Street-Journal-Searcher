Cimflex Teknowledge Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

PITTSBURGH -- Cimflex Teknowledge Corp. said it has completed the conversion of its diagnostics business into a new high-technology company called Applied Diagnostics Inc. Applied Diagnostics is involved with troubleshooting electronic circuit assemblies and certain types of manufacturing equipment. Those products were developed by Cimflex over the last three years through a research and development financing agreement with PaineWebber R & D Partners L.P. The initial shareholders of Applied Diagnostics are PaineWebber and Cimflex. Cimflex provides software and integrated systems for factory management, automation and control.
