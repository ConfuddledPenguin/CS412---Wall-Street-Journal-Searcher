Maxwell Communications Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

NEW YORK -- Maxwell AD/SAT Inc. said it became a wholly owned subsidiary of Maxwell Communication Corp. and named Richard G. Atkins as president, effective today. Maxwell previously owned a controlling interest in AD/SAT. Further details of the acquisition weren't disclosed.

The company said that AD/SAT will become part of the Maxwell Multi-Media Publishing Division.

Maxwell AD/SAT Inc. operates a satellite network to transmit advertising to 125 daily newspapers in 33 states, representing more than half the daily and 65% of the Sunday circulation in this country.
