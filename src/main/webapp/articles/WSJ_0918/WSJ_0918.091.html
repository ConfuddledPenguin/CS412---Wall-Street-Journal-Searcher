Texscan Unit Gets Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

EL PASO, Texas -- Texscan Corp. said its Op-Tech division was awarded two contracts, totaling $2.3 million, to supply fiber optic systems to the United Kingdom Department of Transport. The Op-Tech division had revenue of $4 million in the fiscal year ended April 30, Texscan said.
