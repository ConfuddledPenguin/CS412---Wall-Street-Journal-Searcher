International Brief -- C. Itoh & Co. Ltd.: Tokyo Firm Forms Venture With Westinghouse Credit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

C. Itoh & Co. Ltd., a major Japanese trading house, said it will establish a finance joint venture in London with Westinghouse Credit Corp. of the U.S. to engage in merchant banking activities in Europe. The joint venture, European Capital Co. Ltd., will be capitalized at #1 million ($1.9 million) and will formally begin operations on Sept. 20. The joint venture will engage in merchant banking activities such as project finance, trade finance and merger and acquisition consulting.

European Capital Co. Ltd. will be a member of The Securities Association (TSA) of the U.K.

Tokyo-based C. Itoh will hold a 25% share in the venture while Westinghouse Credit, a unit of Westinghouse Electric Corp. of Pittsburgh, will have a 20% share.

Century Leasing System Co. Ltd., a C. Itoh group member, will hold a 5% share of the joint venture, while the remaining 50% will be divided among roughly five European executives.

A C. Itoh spokesman said the parent companies are planning to lend a total of seven billion yen ($51.1 million) to the joint venture for trade finance.
