Westcoast Energy Inc. Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

VANCOUVER, British Columbia -- Westcoast Energy Inc. said it sold its head office building and adjacent land here for about 53 million Canadian dollars (US$45.5 million). A spokesman for Westcoast, whose interests include pipelines and oil and gas production, said the sale to a group of investors will generate a gain during the current quarter. He didn't disclose the amount.

Westcoast said it will remain at its current quarters under a 10-year lease with the new owners.
