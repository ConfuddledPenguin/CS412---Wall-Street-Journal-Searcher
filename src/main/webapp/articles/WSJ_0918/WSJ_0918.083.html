Who's News: Bank of Tokyo Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

BANK OF TOKYO Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Koretsugu Kodama was named resident managing director for the Americas and chairman of Bank of Tokyo Trust Co., a New York subsidiary of the international bank. Mr. Kodama, 55 years old, succeeds Kenji Yoshizawa, 58, who is returning to Tokyo headquarters as deputy president. Mr. Kodama had been managing director in charge of corporate banking in Tokyo.
