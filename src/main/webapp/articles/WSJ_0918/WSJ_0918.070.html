Classified Financial Corp. Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Classified Financial Corp. said shareholders approved a previously announced plan to merge the company into its majority-owned subsidiary, Rathbone, King & Seeley Inc. The company said that under the merger, which it expects to complete by Sept. 30, Classified Financial shareholders will receive pro rata shares of Classified's net cash and common stock of Rathbone held by Classified. The merger will not, however, affect Rathbone's stock.

Rathbone operates property casualty insurance and related businesses through its subsidiaries, American Star Insurance Co. and Rathbone, King & Seeley Insurance Services Inc.
