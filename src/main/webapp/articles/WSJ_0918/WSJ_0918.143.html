Business Brief -- Newmont Mining Corp.: Adviser Is Hired for Stake In Newmont Australia Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

Newmont Mining Corp., Denver, said it retained Potter Warburg & Co., of Melbourne, Australia, to advise the company concerning its 24% stake in Newmont Australia Ltd. Newmont Mining sold a 19.99% stake in Newmont Australia to Poseidon Gold Ltd. last month for $137.7 million, or $1.04 a share. Newmont said at the time it would either sell its remaining shares -- the 24% stake -- under a Poseidon Gold offer or on the open market. Poseidon is offering to exchange one of its shares for every two shares of Newmont Australia it doesn't already own.

Newmont said it will wait to sell its shares until Newmont Australia has evaluated the Poseidon offer.
