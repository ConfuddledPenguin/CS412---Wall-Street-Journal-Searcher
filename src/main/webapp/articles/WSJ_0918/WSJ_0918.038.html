Alltel Corp. Gas Operations

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

LITTLE ROCK, Ark. -- Alltel Corp. said it has put its gas operations up for sale as part of its strategy to focus on its main business of telephone communications and data processing. Stephens Inc. has been retained to assist in the sale. Alltel obtained the gas operations, which serve more than 67,900 customers in California, Oregon and Nevada, as part of its 1988 acquisition of CP National Corp. of San Francisco. The gas operations have annual operating revenue of $40 million.
