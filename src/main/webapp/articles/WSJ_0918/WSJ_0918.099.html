Redemption Notices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended Sept. 14, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

BEAVER COUNTY, PA., INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY will call for redemption $50,000 principal amount of 8 3/8% series B pollution control revenue bonds, Pennsylvania Power Co. project, dated Nov. 1, 1974, on Nov. 1, 1990. Ameritrust Co. Natl Association is trustee

FINLAND will redeem $3,260,000 principal amount of 8 3/8% external loan bonds dated Oct. 15, 1977, due Oct. 15, 1992, on Oct. 15, 1990. Bankers Trust Co. is paying agent.

MISSOURI HOUSING DEVELOPMENT COMMISION will on Oct. 15, 1990 call $1,200,000 principal amount of series April 15, 1982 qualified mortgage purchase notes due April 15, 1991-1997 and 2002, and $300,000 principal amount of series April 15, 1980 mortgage purchase bonds due Oct. 15, 1991-1997 and 2011. Mercantile Bank of St. Louis is trustee.

NIAGARA FRONTIER TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY will call $85,000 principal amount of 1983 series A general obligation bonds, Tonawanda turn-out project, on Nov. 1, 1990. Bank of New York is trustee.

OHIO AIR QUALITY DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY will on Oct. 15, 1990 call for redemption its 10% and 10 1/8 1980 series B pollution control revenue bonds, Ohio Edison Co. project, due Aug. 1, 2000 and 2010 respectively. Ameritrust Co. Natl Association is trustee

PENNSYLVANIA will redeem on Oct. 15, 1990 $32,490,000 principal amount of its 1980 second series S and fifth series D general obligation bonds due April 15, 1991-2000. Philadelphia Natl Bank is paying agent.

PORT AUTHORITY OF NEW YORK & NEW JERSEY will call $494,000 principal amount of its 3 1/4% twentieth series consolidated bonds, due April 1, 1993, on Oct. 1, 1990. Mellon Securities Transfer Services is paying agent.

CORPORATES

EUROPEAN INVESTMENT BANK will on Oct. 15, 1990 redeem $20,000,000 principal amount for 12 5/8% mandatory sinking fund notes and $20,000,000 principal amount for 12 5/8% optional sinking fund notes, dated Oct. 15, 1984, due Oct. 15, 1999. Chase Manhattan Bank is paying agent.

HELMERICH & PAYNE FINANCE, N.V. will call $34,044,000 of 7 1/2% subordinated debentures due 1995 on Oct. 12, 1990. Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. is trustee.

KEYS HOSPITAL FOUNDATION, INC. will call for redemption $2,530,000 of series 1980 first mortgage hospital revenue bonds, Mariners Hospital project, on Oct. 15, 1990. Due dates are June 1, 1991, 1992, 1999 and 2010. Barnett Banks Trust Co., N.A. is trustee.
