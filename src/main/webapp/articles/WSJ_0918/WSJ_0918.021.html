Canada Will Offer $1.4 Billion in Bonds In Four Maturities

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government said it will offer 1.6 billion Canadian dollars (US$1.4 billion) of government bonds in four maturities this week. The sale will involve 10.25% bonds due Feb. 1, 1994; 10.25% bonds due March 1, 1996; 10.5% bonds due March 1, 2001; 11.25% bonds due June 1, 2015. Offering terms are to be announced today. The Bank of Canada agreed to purchase at least C$100 million of the new bonds. The remainder will be available to public investors.

Proceeds of the issue will be used to redeem C$1.1 billion of government bonds maturing Oct. 1 and for general government purposes.
