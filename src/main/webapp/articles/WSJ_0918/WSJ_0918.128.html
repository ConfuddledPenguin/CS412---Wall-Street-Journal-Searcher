Business Brief -- Hallwood Group Inc.: Conditions Met to Combine Equitec-Backed Partnerships

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

Hallwood Group Inc., Dallas, said the minimum participation conditions have been met for the consolidation of 11 real estate partnerships initially sponsored by Equitec Financial Group Inc. The consolidation will take place under Hallwood Realty Partners L.P., an affiliate of Hallwood Group, which engages in merchant-banking services and specializes in corporate rescue financing, and invests in real estate, oil and gas, restaurants, retailing and textiles. Hallwood Realty Partners has received consents representing about 82% of the aggregate exchange values of the 11 Equitec partnerships.

In a related development, Equitec and its affiliate, Equitec Properties Co., filed a joint motion in the U.S. Bankruptcy Court for Northern District of California last Friday, seeking authorization to complete the consolidation. A hearing on the motion was scheduled for Oct. 19; thus, the deadline yesterday for consolidation was extended to Oct. 1.
