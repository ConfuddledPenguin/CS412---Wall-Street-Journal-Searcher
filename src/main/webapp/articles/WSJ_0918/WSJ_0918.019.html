Who's News: Home Shopping Network Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

HOME SHOPPING NETWORK Inc. (St. Petersburg, Fla.) -- Les R. Wandler was named an executive vice president and director of this video retailer, effective Sept. 25. Mr. Wandler, 44 years old, will retain his position as chief financial officer and treasurer. He succeeds Nando DiFilippo Jr., 42, who resigned Friday as executive vice president and general counsel. A company spokeswoman said Mr. DiFilippo resigned for personal reasons.
