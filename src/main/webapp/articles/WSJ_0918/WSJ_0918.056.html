East Germany's Cost of Living

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- The cost of living in East Germany rose by 7.5% in July from June, but was down 5.5% from July 1989, a German research institute said. The Leipziger Instituts fuer Marktforschung also said the average monthly income of a family of four in East Germany fell more than 5% in July, from a year earlier, due to the restructuring of East Germany's social welfare and income-tax regimes following the July 1 monetary and economic union with West Germany.

Leipziger Instituts, which specializes in tracking economic trends in East Germany, produces data considered fairly reliable.
