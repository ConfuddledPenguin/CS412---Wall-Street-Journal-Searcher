Computer Sciences Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Computer Sciences Corp. said the Army awarded it a contract to provide scientific support-services to the Texcom Experimentation Center located at Fort Hunter-Liggett, Calif., and Fort Ord, Calif. The contract will cover a one-year base period and four one-year renewable options with a total estimated value of $65 million.

Computer Sciences said it will provide a broad range of services that include the planning, execution, data collection, data analysis and reporting of experiments. These are designed to help the Army evaluate the effectiveness of various mixes of manpower and military equipment in combat.
