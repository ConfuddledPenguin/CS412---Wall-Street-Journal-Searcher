Business Brief -- PS Group Inc.: Rights Plan Altered to Let Buffett Firms Boost Stakes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

PS Group Inc.'s board amended the company's shareholder rights plan to allow ownership of up to 22.5% of the company's common stock by companies controlled by Warren E. Buffett, chairman of Berkshire Hathaway Inc. PS Group said the amendment was in response to a request by Mr. Buffett that Berkshire Hathaway or its affiliates be permitted to acquire more than 20% of PS Group stock. As of May, three companies controlled by Mr. Buffett held about an 11% stake.

A Berkshire Hathaway official in Omaha, Neb., wouldn't say how many PS Group shares the company or its affiliates currently own.

PS Group, an aircraft leasing and holding company based in San Diego, said the board considers Mr. Buffett to be "a very constructive long-term investor."
