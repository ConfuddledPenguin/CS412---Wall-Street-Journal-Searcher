Corporate-Front Quiet

("Our bosses aren't very responsive, most workers believe." -- WSJ news item)

This is a true statement,

And here's how I know why:

I asked my boss about it

And he would not reply.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

Second Choice

When a thing must be done

I do it myself;

I don't put a problem

Up on a shelf.

I dig in at once

And go right to it --

If I cannot find

Someone else to do it.

-- Joshua Adams.
