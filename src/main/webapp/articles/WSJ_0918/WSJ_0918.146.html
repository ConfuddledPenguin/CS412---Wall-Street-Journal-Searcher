Business Brief -- VeloBind Inc.: Firm Talking With Suitor Over Possible Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

Following a flurry of trading of its stock Friday, VeloBind Inc. disclosed yesterday that it is in negotiations to be acquired by a company it declined to identify. No agreement has been reached, according to VeloBind's president, Carl J. Swenson. VeloBind, Fremont, Calif., makes and markets professional office binding systems.

"They specifically asked not to be identified," Grant E. Rollin, VeloBind's chief financial officer, said of its suitor, adding that VeloBind has been "chatting for a while" with the company on the possible accord to purchase all of VeloBind's shares outstanding.

On Friday, VeloBind's stock rose $1.125, to $7.50, on 409,000 shares traded in national over-the-counter trading.
