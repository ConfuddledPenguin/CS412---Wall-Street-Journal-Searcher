Raw-Steel Production Fell 0.2% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 0.2% last week to 1,882,000 tons from 1,886,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 8.4% from the 1,736,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 84.3% of its capability last week, compared with 84.5% the previous week and 78.1% a year ago.
