Boeing Air Force Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

WASHINGTON -- Boeing Co. was given a $140.5 million Air Force contract for engine modification kits for KC-135R aircraft.

Northrop Corp. received a $67 million increase to an Air Force contract for risk-reduction activities for the Advanced Tactical Fighter.

Calcasieu Refining Co. got a $29.7 million Defense Logistics Agency contract for jet fuel.

Flameco Engineering Inc. won a $15.6 million Air Force pact for engine-nozzle segments used on F-15 and F-16 aircraft.

Tennessee Apparel Corp. was awarded a $14.9 million modification to a Defense Logistics Agency contract for cold-weather trousers.
