Business Brief -- Emerson Radio Corp.: Company to Form Venture With Fidenas Investment

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

Emerson Radio Corp., North Bergen, N.J., agreed to form a joint venture to sell its products in Europe with Fidenas Investment Ltd., a Zurich, Switzerland, investment firm and holder of 12% of Emerson. Emerson said it will hold 80% of the venture, Emerson Radio & Technology International, and provide $3 million of initial capital. Fidenas will hold 20% and contribute $2 million.

Emerson indefinitely postponed its annual meeting, originally scheduled for Sept. 24, to revise the proxy materials and allow shareholders to vote on the transaction.

Under terms of the venture, Fidenas Investment would add two members to Emerson Radio's board, bringing the board to a total of eight directors.

Emerson, which sells personal computers and consumer electronics in what has been a competitive U.S. market, will compete in Europe with such companies as N.V. Philips and Thomson S.A.

Fidenas Investment, incorporated in the Bahamas, is one of a number of firms under the umbrella of Fidenas Group. The group has interests in banking and management consulting.
