Business Brief -- Bank of Boston Corp.: Group Lifts Stake to 9.9%, May Seek a Further Boost

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

A group including CBS Inc.'s chief executive officer, Laurence A. Tisch, raised its stake in Bank of Boston Corp. to 9.9% and may seek Federal Reserve Board clearance to boost the holding to as much as 14.9%. The purchase is the third within the last year. In December, the group took a 6.6% stake in the bank holding company, and said at the time that it might boost or cut the holding. In August it lifted the stake to 8.9%.

In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the Tisch group said it now holds 7,255,220 Bank of Boston shares. Its most recent transactions include 765,200 shares bought Aug. 13 and Sept. 11 at $8 to $10 apiece. When the group announced its initial purchase last December, the stock was trading at about $18.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Bank of Boston shares rose 25 cents to $7.875.
