Letters to the Editor: Attacking Abortion In a Positive Way

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

Kudos to Mary Cunningham Agee and her Nurturing Network ("Business World" by Tim W. Ferguson, Sept. 4). It's inspiring to know that, amid all the negative rhetoric surrounding the abortion issue, there are those who are attacking the tragedy of abortion in a positive way. Her work also helps dramatize the fact that many abortions might be included in the unfortunate "convenience" category, not the poverty or teen-mother category.

Also deserving of praise are those companies throwing support behind such endeavors. Our society is on an irreversible course that has more and more women in the work force. The challenge is to seek ways to resolve the conflict between career and family.

Maybe when women come to learn more about what Mrs. Agee calls the "ultimate violence" that victimizes them, those companies that have made the effort to accommodate the family needs of their women employees will reap their rewards on the bottom line.

Joseph G. Maty

St. Louis
