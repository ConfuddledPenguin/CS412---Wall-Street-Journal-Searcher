CommerceBancorp Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

NEWPORT BEACH, Calif. -- CommerceBancorp, a bank holding company, said its board authorized spending as much as $1 million to repurchase the company's stock. CommerceBancorp said that at current prices $1 million would buy about 97,561 shares, or about 4.6% of the company's common shares outstanding.
