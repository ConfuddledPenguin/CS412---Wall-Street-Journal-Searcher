Who's News: PaineWebber Group Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

PAINEWEBBER GROUP Inc. (New York) -- James W. Ziglar was named a managing director in the municipal investment banking division at PaineWebber Inc., a subsidiary of this investment and securities firm. Mr. Ziglar, 44 years old, was previously a managing director at Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc. Prior to that, Mr. Ziglar was assistant secretary of the Interior Department for water and science during the Reagan administration.
