Chicago Mercantile Seat Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange was sold for $434,000, down $32,000 from the previous sale. Seats currently are quoted at $380,000, bid, and $435,000, asked. The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set March 9, 1989.
