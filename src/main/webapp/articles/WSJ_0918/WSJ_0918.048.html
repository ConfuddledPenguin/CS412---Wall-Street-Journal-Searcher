Who's News: Mutual Life Insurance Co. of New York

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

MUTUAL LIFE INSURANCE CO. OF NEW YORK -- Richard C. Scott was named to the new post of president of the real estate investment management unit of this insurance, pension and investment company. Mr. Scott, 52 years old, was previously vice president and part owner of Aldrich, Eastman & Waltch, a real estate advisory firm in Boston.
