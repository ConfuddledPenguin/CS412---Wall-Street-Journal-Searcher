Bond Unit Extends Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

PERTH, Western Australia -- Bond Brewing Holdings Ltd. said its subsidiary, Dimola Pty., has extended its offer to purchase as much as 90% of Bond's principal amount outstanding of 12 5/8% extendable subordinated debentures to midnight EDT Friday. The depositary has advised Bond and Dimola that, as of noon EDT last Friday, about 8.5% of the outstanding principal amount of debentures had been tendered to the depositary.
