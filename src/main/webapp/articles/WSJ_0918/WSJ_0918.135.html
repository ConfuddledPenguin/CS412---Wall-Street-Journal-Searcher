Business Brief -- Caesars World Inc.: Accord Met for Withdrawal Of Shareholder's Petition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

Caesars World Inc. said an agreement has been reached for the withdrawal of a petition by a shareholder of Caesars New Jersey Inc. with the New Jersey Casino Control Commission in connection with Caesars World's pending tender offer for Caesars New Jersey shares. The holder had alleged, among other things, that the $22 a share Caesars World is offering for the 2.2 million shares of Caesars New Jersey it doesn't already own was inadequate.

Caesars World said the withdrawal is provided for in an amendment to its previously announced agreement in principle connected with the settlement of certain shareholder lawsuits related to the tender offer. Caesars World holds about 86.6% of Caesars New Jersey's 16.3 million outstanding shares.

As a result of the petition withdrawal, a New Jersey Casino Control Commission hearing on the issue, previously scheduled for Wednesday, has been adjourned.
