Capital Holding Buy-Back Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Capital Holding Corp. said it may acquire as many as 1,450,000 common shares under a buy-back program as well as 550,000 shares through its employee pension fund. The life insurance company said it believes its stock is undervalued. At the end of 1989, Capital Holding had 46.1 million shares outstanding.
