Who's News: Howard Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

HOWARD SAVINGS BANK (Newark, N.J.) -- Thomas E. Albright was named executive vice president and chief financial officer, succeeding Joseph G. Wojak, who resigned the post. Mr. Albright, 39 years old, was previously director of financial systems integration at First Fidelity Bancorp., a bank holding company based in Newark.
