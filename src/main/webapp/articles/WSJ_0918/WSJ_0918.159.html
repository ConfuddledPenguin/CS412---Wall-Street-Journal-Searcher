Business Brief -- Kansas City Power & Light Co.: Firm Extends Tender Offer For Kansas Gas & Electric

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

Kansas City Power & Light Co. said it is extending its $27-a-share, or $857 million, tender offer for Kansas Gas & Electric Co. to Oct. 19. Kansas City P&L also said about 44% of Kansas G&E's common shares have been tendered to it. As of Aug. 20, about 43% of Kansas G&E's common shares had been tendered.

The offer, which previously had been extended, was to have expired last Friday.

Kansas City P&L said more than 13.5 million shares of Kansas G&E, along with 40,595 shares of its 4.5% preferred, 32,577 shares of 4.32% preferred and 24,406 shares of 4.28% preferred have been tendered.

"Kansas Gas & Electric stockholders continue to send a clear and positive signal concerning our offer," said Kansas City P&L President Drue Jennings in a statement.

Last week, Kansas City G&E disclosed in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that it was negotiating to be acquired by a company other than Kansas City P&L. It refused to identify the company.
