United Companies Financial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

DALLAS -- United Companies Financial Corp. said it had signed a definitive agreement with Foster Mortgage Corp. to acquire substantially all of its assets. United Companies, based in Baton Rouge, La., is a financial services holding company. Foster is a unit of Benjamin Franklin Federal Savings Association, a Houston-based thrift that is under the conservatorship of the Resolution Trust Corp. The RTC has already approved the transaction, United Companies said.

Foster currently services about $8 billion in mortgage loans. United Companies said the acquisition closing date is projected for Nov. 1, with the purchase price being based primarily on the portfolio balances as of Oct. 31.
