Business Brief -- Standard Register Co.: Ohio Firm Projects Drop In Net for Quarter, Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

Standard Register Co., hurting from a weak general economy, higher paper costs and tough competition in the business-forms industry, said it expects lower third-quarter and full-year earnings. The Dayton, Ohio, company also said it will begin a major cost reduction program over the next six months, slashing employment and closing several production facilities. The company didn't elaborate. However, it said the program will result in a one-time fourth-quarter charge. It didn't disclose the amount.

Last year, the company earned $40.4 million, or $1.35 a share, on revenue of $708.9 million. In last year's third quarter, the company earned $7.5 million, or 25 cents a share, on revenue of $174.1 million. In last year's fourth quarter, the company earned $14.8 million, or 49 cents a share, on revenue of $187.1 million.
