Applebee's to Buy Back Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/18/90

KANSAS CITY, Mo. -- Applebee's International Inc. said its board authorized the purchase of its common because the board believes the current market price doesn't reflect its true value. The restaurant developer said it will make "aggressive purchases" of as many as 100,000 shares in the open market.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Applebee closed at $9.75 a share, up $1.
