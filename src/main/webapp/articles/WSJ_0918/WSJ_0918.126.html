Business Brief -- Ford Motor Co.: ABB Flakt Unit Receives Contract for Firm's Facility

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/18/90

A division of ABB Flakt Inc. said Ford Motor Co. awarded it a $200 million contract to complete a paint finishing facility at Ford's Oakville, Ontario, assembly plant. The 641,000-square-foot facility will be located near the assembly plant that builds the Ford Tempo and Mercury Topaz models, said ABB Flakt Alpha, a division of ABB Flakt. The building is expected to be completed by January 1992.

ABB Flakt, an Atlanta provider of paint finishing systems for the automotive industry, said it will administer the contract, in part, through a joint venture with Fluor Daniel Inc., a Greenville, S.C., engineering, construction and technical services subsidiary of Fluor Corp.
