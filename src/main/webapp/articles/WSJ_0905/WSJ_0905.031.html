Who's News: Vernitron Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

VERNITRON Corp. (Deer Park, N.Y.) -- Mitchell L. Dutton, 41 years old, previously general manager of the AST Bearings division of this closely held maker of motors and controls, was named to the new post of president of the division, a Montville, N.J., importer and wholesaler of bearings for aerospace, instrumentation and automotive markets.
