Who's News: Schering, SmithKline Name U.S. Executives

  • Author: A Wall Street Journal News Roundup
  • Date: 09/05/90

Two big pharmaceutical companies named executives to head their respective U.S. businesses. Schering-Plough Corp., Madison, N.J., said Rodolfo C. Bryce was appointed president of Schering Laboratories, the company's U.S. prescription-products unit. Mr. Bryce, 44 years old, previously was senior vice president, prescription marketing and sales, at Schering Labs.

Mr. Bryce succeeds Jean-Pierre Garnier, 43, who was named president of the U.S. pharmaceuticals business of SmithKline Beecham PLC, London, succeeding Frederick Kyle, who was named president, commercial operations, of the Anglo-American drug concern's world-wide operations earlier this year.
