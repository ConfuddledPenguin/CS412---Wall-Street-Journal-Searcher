Dividend News: Sunwest Financial Services Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

SUNWEST FINANCIAL SERVICES Inc. slashed its quarterly to 12 cents a share from 28 cents but said it expects to report a profit for the year. Sunwest, based in Albuquerque, N.M., which had a $7.6 million loss in the first half, including $3.3 million in expenses associated with its voluntary retirement program and $32.6 million in loan-loss provisions, said earnings improved in the first two months of the third quarter. In July and August, the bank holding company had net income of over $3.1 million, due to a reduction in the loan-loss provision and better controls on non-interest expenses. The rise in non-performing assets has "stabilized," a spokesman said, even though the state's real estate woes haven't improved. The July and August earnings trend is expected to continue and result in positive earnings for the year an official said. The dividend is payable Oct. 1 to stock of record Oct. 1.
