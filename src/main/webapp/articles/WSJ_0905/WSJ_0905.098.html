Who's News: HQ Office Supplies Warehouse Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

HQ OFFICE SUPPLIES WAREHOUSE Inc. (Long Beach, Calif.) -- George J. Handgis was elected president and chief executive officer of the office-supply retailer. He succeeds Brad R. Tukey, 43 years old, who resigned to pursue other interests but will remain on the company's board and serve as a consultant. Mr. Handgis, 38, is president and chief executive of HQ Office International Inc., a former subsidiary of HQ Office Supplies Warehouse. The companies also announced they are discussing a possible business combination, but that no agreement has been reached.
