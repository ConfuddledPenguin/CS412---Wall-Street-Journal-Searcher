Rate Declines at Citicorp's Auction of 91-Day Paper

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 7.895% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.018% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $335 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.892% to 7.90%.

Citicorp also said the average rate fell to 7.875% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.011% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $330 million were submitted. Accepted bids were all at 7.875%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
