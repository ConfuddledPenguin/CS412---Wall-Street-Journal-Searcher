American Capital & Research

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

FAIRFAX, Va. -- American Capital & Research Corp. said Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory awarded a subsidiary a one-year contract for $8 million, with two possible one-year extensions of $8 million each. The subsidiary, ICF Kaiser Engineers, will hire 50 to 65 people to complete the contract, under which ICF will provide scientific, management and technical expertise to all Livermore departments. ICF assists several Livermore departments under previous contracts. Livermore works for the federal government on projects such as fusion, lasers and nuclear testing.

American Capital is an environmental, engineering and professional services consulting concern.
