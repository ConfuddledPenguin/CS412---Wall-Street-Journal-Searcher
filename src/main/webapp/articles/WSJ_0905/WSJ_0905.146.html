Business Brief -- J.P. Morgan & Co.: Sumitomo Life to Refinance Company's London Offices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

J.P. Morgan & Co. said it arranged for a $416 million refinancing of its London office complex through Sumitomo Life Insurance Co. of Japan. The New York-based bank holding company said it had entered into an agreement with Sumitomo Life that would give the Japanese insurance company a 52.5% stake on a structured basis of Morgan Place, the office complex it is building at 60 Victoria Embankment.

Under terms of the agreement, J.P. Morgan would receive a 25-year lease with renewal options for 50 years. The company said no immediate gain would be realized from the transaction and that the benefits would be realized over the 25-year life of the lease.
