Technology Brief -- Millicom Inc.: Joint Venture Is Formed With SCS Mobilecom Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

Millicom Inc., New York, said its subsidiary, Millicom Technologies Corp., formed a joint venture with SCS Mobilecom Inc., Port Washington, N.Y., to develop a design to test its personal communications network. Millicom, an international telecommunications company, has been working on developing PCNs -- the latest generation of wallet-sized wireless telephones -- to compete with existing cellular technology.

In May, the Federal Communications Commission granted Millicom a license to test PCNs in Texas and Florida. But in order to proceed, Millicom needs a frequency to test the network on, as well as handsets that can incorporate the new technology.

In teaming up with SCS Mobilecom, Millicom gains access to SCS's expertise in spread spectrum technology, a signaling technique that allows a radio communication system to operate over several frequencies without interfering with other signals. Millicom says it is talking with various manufacturers but has yet to select one to produce the handsets.
