Who's News: Lauer Gets Goodrich Presidency

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

AKRON, Ohio -- B.F. Goodrich Co., a plastics, specialty chemicals and aerospace concern, said John N. Lauer, 51 years old, was elected president, succeeding Leigh Carter, 65, who retired last Saturday. Mr. Lauer joined Goodrich last year as an executive vice president and was named chief operating officer in June. Previously, he was president of the engineering plastics group at Hoechst Celanese, the U.S. unit of Hoechst AG of West Germany.
