Technology: Tokos Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

SANTA ANA, Calif. -- Tokos Medical Corp. said it completed the acquisition of Physiologic Diagnostic Service Inc., Atlanta, for 2.7 million shares of Tokos common stock. Tokos, which went public earlier this year, said the transaction would be accounted for as a pooling of interests. Tokos provides specialized home health care for pregnant women, and said it would integrate its sales, marketing, research and regulatory functions with Physiologic Diagnostic.

Tokos's stock closed at $11.675, up $1.675 in national over-the-counter trading, giving the transaction an indicated value of $31.5 million.
