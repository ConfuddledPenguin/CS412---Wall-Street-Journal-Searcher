Letters to the Editor: A Scary Scenario

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

On Aug. 10, your page-one Washington Wire column reported that the Washington Institute for Near East Policy had predicted that the 1990s oil market would be characterized by "stable or decreasing prices." I hope that this Washington Institute for Near East Policy has no influence on Washington's Near East Policy.

David K. Hoffman

Partner

The Simulation Group

Sacramento, Calif.
