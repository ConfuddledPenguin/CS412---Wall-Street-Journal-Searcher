L.E. Myers Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- L.E. Myers Co. said it plans to purchase as many as 250,000 shares of its common stock, or about 10.3% of its total shares outstanding. The electrical construction concern said the repurchased shares are for possible use in employee stock option plans. Myers said it may repurchase the shares from time to time on the open market or in negotiated transactions.

In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, the company's stock closed at $14, up $1.25.
