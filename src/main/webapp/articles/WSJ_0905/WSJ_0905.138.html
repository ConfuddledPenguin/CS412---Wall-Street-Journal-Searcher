Who's News: PLM International's Megeath Quits Board

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- S.A. Megeath III announced his resignation from the board of PLM International Inc. after being stripped of the chairmanship and all other positions except his directorship. In tendering his resignation, Mr. Megeath, 44 years old, cited "intense" disagreement with the board over the company's operations, policies and practices. In an interview, he also complained that he had been kept in the dark on company activity by management. But he declined to elaborate on the substance of the dispute. PLM officials didn't return phone calls.

PLM announced that its board had elected J. Alec Merriam as the new chairman. Mr. Merriam is an investor and formerly executive vice president of Crowley Maritime Corp. Robert Tidball, PLM's chief executive officer and president, whom Mr. Megeath singled out for criticism, wasn't immediately available for comment.

PLM is a transportation equipment leasing company.
