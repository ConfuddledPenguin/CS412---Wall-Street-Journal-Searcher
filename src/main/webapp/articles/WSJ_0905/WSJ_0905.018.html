Letters to the Editor: Affront to Eurasians?

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

Regarding your comment on Actors' Equity's initial decision to bar Jonathan Pryce from playing the role of a Eurasian in "Miss Saigon" because it would be an affront to the Asian community ("Banned on Broadway" editorial, Aug. 9), I cannot help but think that somebody has gotten something mixed up. A Eurasian is a person of both European and Asian ancestry. Hence, if it is wrong for Mr. Pryce, a European, to play the role, it is equally wrong for an Asian actor to do so. By Actors' Equity's definition, the role should have been open only to someone of mixed European and Asian ancestry.

Charles L. Horn

Bloomington, Minn.
