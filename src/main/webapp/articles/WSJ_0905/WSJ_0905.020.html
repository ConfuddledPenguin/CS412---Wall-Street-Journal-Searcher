Who's News: PNC Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

PNC FINANCIAL Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Wilson W. Wyatt Jr., formerly vice president of corporate affairs and government relations of Batus Inc., Louisville, Ky., was named senior vice president, corporate and public affairs, of this bank holding company. Mr. Wyatt, 46 years old, succeeds Robert J. O'Gara, who resigned last year.
