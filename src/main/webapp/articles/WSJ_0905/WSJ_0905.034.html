Who's News: Analytical Surveys Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

ANALYTICAL SURVEYS Inc. (Colorado Springs, Colo.) -- Sid Corder was named president and chief operating officer, positions previously held by chief executive officer John Thorpe. Before coming to this geographic services concern, Mr. Corder was president of Cubic Corp.'s Cubic Western Data unit, a San Diego-based electronics company.
