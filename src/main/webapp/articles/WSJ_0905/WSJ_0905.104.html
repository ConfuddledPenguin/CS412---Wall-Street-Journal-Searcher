Business Brief -- Amdura Corp.: Company Gets Higher Offer For Hardware Distributor

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

Amdura Corp. said it received a new bid for its Amdura National Distribution Co. unit from United Hardware Distributing Co. that topped an earlier bid from Our Own Hardware, setting up a possible auction in federal bankruptcy court. Amdura, a Denver automotive and hardware distributor and specialty manufacturer, declined to release terms of the bids from the two companies, both of which are dealer-owned hardware cooperatives based in Minneapolis.

But an Amdura spokesman said the latest bid topped the earlier one by at least $1 million, as specified by the federal bankruptcy-court judge overseeing Amdura's reorganization under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code, which gives the company protection from creditors. He added that a hearing on the sale is scheduled for Friday, at which time interested parties can submit higher bids.

Amdura National, a wholesale hardware distributor that serves Trustworthy hardware stores in the upper Midwest, also is operating under bankruptcy-court protection.
