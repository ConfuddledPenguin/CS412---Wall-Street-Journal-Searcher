Portsmouth Bank Shares Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

PORTSMOUTH, N.H. -- Portsmouth Bank Shares Inc., a bank holding company, said that it received regulatory approval to purchase an additional 5%, or 189,638, of its 3.8 million common shares outstanding, and that it will make the buybacks in the open market over the next six months. During the past 12 months, Portsmouth Bank bought back 161,400 shares.
