Philippines Logs a Trade Surplus

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

MANILA, the Philippines -- The Philippines' trade surplus with the U.S. for the first half narrowed 16% to $271.2 million from a $324.1 million surplus a year earlier, the National Statistics Office said. Exports to the U.S. rose 17% in the half, and imports gained at a much faster rate of 29%, the agency said.
