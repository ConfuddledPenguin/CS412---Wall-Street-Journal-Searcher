Dana Corp. Buys Certain Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

TOLEDO, Ohio -- Dana Corp., a maker of drive-train, chassis and engine parts, said it acquired the machining and assembly assets of Columbus Auto Parts Co. of Rivers Junction and Ann Arbor, Mich. The price wasn't disclosed. Columbus Auto Parts has annual sales of about $50 million. Dana had 1989 sales of $4.9 billion.
