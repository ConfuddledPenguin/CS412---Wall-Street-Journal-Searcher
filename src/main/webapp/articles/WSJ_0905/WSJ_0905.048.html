Technology: Symantec Completes Merger

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

CUPERTINO, Calif. -- Symantec Corp., a maker of software for personal computers, disclosed the completion of its previously announced merger with Peter Norton Computing Inc., a closely held software concern. Under the merger, which was completed Friday, shareholders of Peter Norton Computing received approximately 2.9 million Symantec common shares and option holders received options to purchase about 400,000 common stock equivalents. The total value of the transaction is about $70 million. Symantec's stock closed at $24.25 a share, up $2.25, in national over-the-counter trading yesterday.
