Portec Judgment Is Reduced

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- Portec Inc. said a federal appeals court in Denver reduced a judgment in a lawsuit against it to $1.2 million from $2.2 million. Portec, which makes engineered products for various industries, including railroads, said the judgment resulted from a jury trial in 1988, in a suit by the British Post Office. Portec said the suit claimed that certain of its manufacturing operations weren't properly licensed.

Portec said that if the British Post Office refuses the judgment reduction, the case would have to be retried.
