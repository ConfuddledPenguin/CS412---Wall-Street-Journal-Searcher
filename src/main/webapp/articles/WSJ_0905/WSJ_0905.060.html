Kinark Accord Is Ended

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Kinark Corp. said its previously announced accord to sell its Camelot Hotels to Wilmae Inc. for $12 million was terminated. Paul Chastain, Kinark's co-chief executive officer, said closely held Wilmae, of Santa Rosa, Calif., "had delays in obtaining financing" and failed to make a required $50,000 deposit. He added that Wilmae will forfeit an earlier $50,000 deposit.

Kinark, which owns galvanizing plants and chemical-storage facilities, said it plans to continue efforts to sell the hotels.
