Westinghouse Electric Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

PITTSBURGH -- Westinghouse Electric Corp. received a $93 million contract to build a 312-megawatt natural gas-fired power generating plant for Commonwealth Atlantic Limited Partnership, a partnership between a subsidiary of Mission Energy Co., Fairfax, Va., and a division of Long Lake Energy Co., New York, N.Y. Westinghouse said the power plant will be built in Chesapeake, Va.
