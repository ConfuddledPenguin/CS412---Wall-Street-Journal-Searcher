Entergy Expands Repurchases

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

NEW ORLEANS -- Entergy Corp. said it will spend as much as an additional $135 million on its stock repurchase program. The utility holding company said it has purchased about 13.8 million shares, or roughly $275 million of its common, since initiating the program in November 1989. Entergy has about 190.8 million shares outstanding.
