Raytheon $17.5 Million Charge

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

LEXINGTON, Mass. -- Raytheon Co. said it expects to report a $17.5 million one-time charge against third-quarter earnings related to the closure of a unit's Topton, Pa., manufacturing plant. Raytheon, an electronics, aircraft and missile concern, had net income of $137.4 million, or $2.08 a share, on sales of $2.2 billion for the 1989 third quarter.

The unit, Caloric Corp., an appliance maker, said it plans to cease manufacturing by the end of the year, terminating 400 of its 600 employees. It cited slow sales.
