R.R. Donnelley & Sons Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

CHICAGO -- R.R. Donnelley & Sons Co. said it completed its $487.5 million acquisition of Meredith/Burda Cos., a joint venture owned by Meredith Corp. and members of West Germany's Burda family. Donnelley is the country's biggest commercial printer. Meredith is a publisher based in Des Moines, Iowa.

Last week the transaction got clearance from a federal judge, who brushed aside Federal Trade Commission arguments that the combination would violate antitrust law.
