Banks in Michigan Plan Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

HOLLAND, Mich. -- First Michigan Bank Corp. and Northwestern Bank Corp. of East Jordan, Mich., said they signed a letter of intent to merge in a transaction valued at $16 million to $17 million. First Michigan Bank said it will exchange debentures, cash and common stock for all of the Northwestern Bank shares outstanding. The acquisition is subject to approval by various regulatory agencies and Northwestern shareholders.
