International Brief -- Hoechst AG: German Chemical Concern's Pretax Profit for Half Fell

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

Hoechst AG of West Germany, a chemical concern, said its group pretax profit for the first half fell 14% to 1.82 billion marks ($1.15 billion) from 2.11 billion marks a year earlier. World-wide group sales fell 2.2% to 22.43 billion marks from 22.94 billion marks.

The company said that economic and market developments in several key geographic areas were weak in the first half, and that its sales and earnings were hurt by the mark's strength against other currencies, especially the dollar and the yen.
