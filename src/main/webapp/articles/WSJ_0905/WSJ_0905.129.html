Marketing Brief -- Times Mirror Co.: Vice President Is Appointed For the Newspaper Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

Richard T. Schlosberg III, 46 years old, was elected group vice president-newspapers of Times Mirror Co., Los Angeles. Duties related to that post had been split among several executives since 1988. Mr. Schlosberg will become the media company's third group vice president when he assumes his new post later this month. He also will be chairman of the newspaper management committee with responsibility for six company newspapers, and for group advertising sales, newsprint purchases and other newspaper matters.

Mr. Schlosberg has been president of the Los Angeles Times newspaper since 1988. A successor hasn't been named.

Donald F. Wright, 56, senior vice president, assumed added responsibility for various financial and administrative functions of Times Mirror, including budget and planning. He relinquishes responsibility to Mr. Schlosberg for four newspapers. He continues to oversee the company's broadcast and cable television groups.
