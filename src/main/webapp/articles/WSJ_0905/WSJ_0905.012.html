Technology: Kodak's Stake in Vortech Data

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Eastman Kodak Co. has taken an equity position in Vortech Data Inc., a Reston, Va., supplier of medical-image management systems. Terms weren't disclosed. The two companies are establishing an alliance that will include a joint product development and marketing program.

Eastman Kodak is a leading supplier of medical diagnostic imaging systems, including electronic products and X-ray film.
