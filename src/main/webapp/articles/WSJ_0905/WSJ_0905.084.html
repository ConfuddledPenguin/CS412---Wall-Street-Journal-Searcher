Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

American Pacific Corp., offering 2,062,000 shares of common stock, of which 1.9 million will be sold by the company, 162,000 shares by selling holders, via Paine-Webber Inc. and Kidder, Peabody & Co.

Central Maine Power Co., offering of 2.5 million shares of common stock.

Energy Ventures Inc., offering of 1,372,000 shares of common stock, of which 1.3 million shares will be sold by the company, 72,000 shares by a selling holder, via Lehman Brothers and Howard, Weil, Labouisse, Friedrichs Inc.

NovaCare Inc., offering of 4.5 million common shares, of which two million will be sold by the company, 2.5 million by selling holders, via Robertson, Stephens & Co. and Lehman Brothers International.

Safeco Corp., shelf offering of $200 million in debt securities.

CENTRAL MAINE POWER Co. filed a shelf offering with the Securities and Exchange Commission to issue as many as 2.5 million common shares. Yesterday's edition incorrectly described the offering.
