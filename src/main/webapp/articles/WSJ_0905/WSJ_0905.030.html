First Federal Financial Services

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

WOOSTER, Ohio -- First Federal Financial Services Corp. said it ended its offer to acquire First Federal Savings & Loan Association of Mount Vernon, Ohio. In July, First Federal Financial announced a definitive agreement to buy the thrift, subject to various contingencies. At the time, the company reported that First Federal of Mount Vernon had $77.4 million in assets and a negative net worth.

First Federal Financial officials said their due diligence of First Federal of Mount Vernon disclosed possible loan losses in the thrift's portfolio.
