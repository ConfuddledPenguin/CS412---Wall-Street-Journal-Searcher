International Brief -- Pancontinental Mining Ltd.: Fiscal '90 Operating Profit Rose 9% at Australian Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

Pancontinental Mining Ltd. of Australia reported that for the year ended June 30, after-tax operating profit rose 9% to 27.3 million Australian dollars (US$22.2 million) from A$25.1 million a year earlier. Per-share earnings rose to 16.5 Australian cents from 15.2 cents. The dividend was kept at 3.5 cents a share.

Sales rose 22% to A$169.3 million from A$138.3 million.

Tony Grey, Pancontinental's chairman, said that earnings from gold were affected by lower world bullion prices, but that this was offset by greater earnings from coal.
