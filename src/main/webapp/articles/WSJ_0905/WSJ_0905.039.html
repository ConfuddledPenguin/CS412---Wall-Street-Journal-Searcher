Mid-American Waste Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

CANAL WINCHESTER, Ohio -- Mid-American Waste Systems Inc. said it has reached an agreement with the Campo Band of Mission Indians to build a landfill on the tribe's land near San Diego. Terms weren't disclosed. The 600-acre site will have capacity for 30 million tons of waste gathered from San Diego county. Mid-American manages landfills in six states.
