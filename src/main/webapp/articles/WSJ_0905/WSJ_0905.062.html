Stake in Aeronautical Support

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

NEW YORK -- American International Group Inc., a multinational insurer, said its International Lease Finance Corp. unit, a Beverly Hills, Calif., jet-leasing concern that American International acquired last week, agreed to acquire a 19.5% stake in Aeronautical Support Inc., a Deerfield Beach, Fla., supplier of engines, equipment and spare parts for commercial aircraft. Terms weren't disclosed. International Lease recently agreed to sell more than $10 million of spare parts for Boeing Co. 757 planes to Aeronautical Support, which has annual sales of about $9 million.
