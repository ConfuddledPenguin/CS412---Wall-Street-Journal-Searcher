Who's News: Eaton Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

EATON Corp. (Cleveland) -- Stanley V. Jaskolski, executive manager of technology development, has been elected vice president of technical management. Mr. Jaskolski, 51 years old, succeeds Warren B. Offutt, who will retire Oct. 1. Eaton makes engineered products for the automotive, industrial, commercial and defense markets.
