Raw-Steel Production Fell 1.9% Last Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 1.9% last week to 1,900,000 tons from 1,937,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output rose 12% from the 1,703,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 85.1% of its capability last week, compared with 86.8% the previous week and 76.6% a year ago.
