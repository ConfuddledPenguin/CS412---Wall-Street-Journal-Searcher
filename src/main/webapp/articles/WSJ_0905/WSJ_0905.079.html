REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Mass. Exodus

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/05/90

States such as New York and New Jersey, whose politicians are currently playing Russian roulette with their economies, might consider what it would be like to have the AP run a headline like this about them: "Poll Finds Many Would Consider Leaving Massachusetts." Just out in Massachusetts, the poll found an incredible 47% saying they'd consider getting out if things got worse. One-third of those polled say they're cutting back on groceries. Meanwhile, with Washington budget summiteers reconvening soon, an entire nation waits to see if it'll be trapped by a tax increase.
