Who's News: Diagnostek Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

DIAGNOSTEK Inc. (Albuquerque, N.M.) -- E. Gerald Riesenbach was named to the board of this health-services and pharmacy cost-containment company, succeeding Richard Hatchett, who resigned. Mr. Riesenbach is a senior partner in the Philadelphia law firm Wolf, Block, Schorr and Solis-Cohen.
