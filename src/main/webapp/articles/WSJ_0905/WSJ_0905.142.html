Business Brief -- Search Exploration Inc.: Dallas Concern Amends Suit Against Sonat Inc.'s Marshall

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

Search Exploration Inc. said it filed an amendment to its multimillion-dollar lawsuit against Sonat Inc.'s Marshall Exploration Inc. subsidiary to include charges under the Racketeer Influenced and Corrupt Organizations statute. The amended complaint, filed in state court in Dallas, charges that Marshall and related defendants "have engaged in a systematic pattern of mail and wire fraud for more than six years." According to the complaint, Marshall, an oil and gas exploration and production company, overcharged investors for its services and issued "often misleading, deceptive and ultimately fraudulent" assessments of wells it managed. Search, a Dallas-based exploration and production company, added that "this wrongful conduct extended to numerous wells and injured tens if not hundreds of investors."

A spokesman for Sonat, a Birmingham, Ala.-based diversified energy company, called the Search complaint "totally without merit."
