Morgan's Foods Rights Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

CLEVELAND, Ohio -- Morgan's Foods Inc. said it will sell an additional 600,000 of its common shares under a rights offering. The operator of Kentucky Fried Chicken restaurants said Whale Securities Co. will act as placement agent for the shares and any shares not purchased through its previously announced rights offering.

The offering provides that each shareholder can purchase, for $1 a share, one common share for every two shares owned. Morgan's Foods has 11,848,000 shares outstanding.

In addition, the company said Citicorp North America Inc., a unit of Citicorp, has agreed to refinance about $19 million of its debt. The refinancing agreement is contingent upon the accumulation by the company of at least $3 million in the rights offering. The commitment also provides a $3 million line of credit for remodeling or buying more restaurants.
