CMS Energy Unit Adjustments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

JACKSON, Mich. -- CMS Energy Corp. said its Consumers Power Co. utility unit will refund $29.5 million to its gas customers from 1987 because of refunds received from natural gas pipeline suppliers and will recover $11.7 million from gas customers because of a 1988 underrecovery of gas costs. The utility's typical residential gas customer who has received service since 1987 will receive a net credit of approximately $11.60 as a result of the adjustments, CMS said.
