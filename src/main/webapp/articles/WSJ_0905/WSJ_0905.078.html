Treasury Plans to Raise Total of $1.6 Billion With Auction of Bills

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/05/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.6 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $18.4 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.8 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Dec. 13, 1990, and March 14, 1991, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
