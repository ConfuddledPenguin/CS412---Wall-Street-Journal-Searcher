  • Date: 05/01/90

LOTUS DEVELOPMENT Corp. (Cambridge, Mass.) -- Alexander V. d'Arbeloff, 62 years old, resigned as a director of this personal computer software maker to devote more time to Teradyne Corp., where he is chairman and chief executive officer. His departure, leaving Lotus with five directors, makes way for combining the boards of Lotus and Novell Inc., a Provo, Utah, network software maker, in a merger announced last month. Carol R. Goldberg, 59, president of Avcar Group Ltd., was re-elected a Lotus director but will resign after the merger is completed this summer. The two actions will result in a combined board of seven members, four from Lotus and three from Novell.
