Canada's Output

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's gross domestic product fell 0.1% in February as a result of declines in the output of both goods and services, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. Gross domestic product is the total value of a country's output of goods and services.

The February decline followed a 0.3% decrease in January and a 0.2% gain in December. In February, production of both goods and service industries fell 0.1%. The service-industry drop was the first monthly decline since March 1989.
