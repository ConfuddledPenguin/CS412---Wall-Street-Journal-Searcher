Schneider Bid for Canada Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

TORONTO -- The Canadian unit of Groupe Schneider S.A. of France said 19.6 million, or 99.7%, of the common shares outstanding of Federal Pioneer Ltd., an electrical equipment maker, were tendered to Schneider Canada's takeover offer of 15 Canadian dollars (US$12.88) each, or an indicated C$300 million (US$257.8 million). Schneider, which has extensive holdings in the electrical equipment industry, said that under Canadian law, it will acquire the remaining Federal Pioneer shares that weren't tendered.
