Who's News: Primebank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

PRIMEBANK (Grand Rapids, Mich.) -- Edward F. Havlik and Terrence M. O'Rourke were elected to the board of the bank. Mr. Havlik, 46 years old, has served on the board as an advisory director since 1986. Mr. O'Rourke, 51, succeeds retiring Director Harold Schumacher. Mr. Havlik is chairman of United Development Management Co. Mr. O'Rourke is president of Blodgett Memorial Medical Center and serves on several local boards, including the board of the Grand Rapids Area Medical Education Center.
