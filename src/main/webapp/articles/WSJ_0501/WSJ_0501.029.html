Pacific G&E to Redeem Bonds

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Pacific Gas & Electric Co. said it will redeem on June 1 two mortgage-bond issues with combined face amounts of $174 million. Holders of the first and refunding mortgage bond Series 85A will receive $1,098 for every $1,000 of bonds. Holders of the Series 85B bonds will receive $1,081.20 for every $1,000 of bonds.

The utility said the redemptions will reduce its annual financing costs by about $1.7 million, resulting in a total savings over the life of the bonds of about $18.2 million.
