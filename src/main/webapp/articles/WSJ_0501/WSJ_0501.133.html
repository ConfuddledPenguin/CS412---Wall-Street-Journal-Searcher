Boise Cascade Mill for Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Boise Cascade Corp. said it plans to seek a buyer for its Vancouver, Wash., paper mill. The forest products concern said its intent to sell reflects a decision not to focus on carbonless paper, which is produced at the mill. The mill is the smallest of the company's 10 paper-manufacturing plants.
