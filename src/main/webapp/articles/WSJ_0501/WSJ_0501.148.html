Business Brief -- Aca Joe Inc.: Licensees Accuse Retailer Of Fraud, Contract Breach

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

Aca Joe Inc. said it was sued by licensees, who charged the clothing retailer with fraud and breach of contract, among other things. The plaintiffs, who filed the suit in federal district court in San Francisco, said they are seeking $10 million in compensatory damages and unspecified punitive damages. The San Francisco company said it was reviewing the complaint. It declined to comment further.

In the suit, the plaintiffs, which include four current licensees and two former licensees, alleged that the company deceived them by publicly stating its intent to continue operating a licensed outlet system when, the suit alleges, the company had decided to switch to wholesale systems. The suit also alleged that the company failed to ship merchandise the licensees had ordered and paid for.

Aca Joe, which filed for protection from creditors under the federal Bankruptcy Code in May 1988, emerged from Chapter 11 last year.
