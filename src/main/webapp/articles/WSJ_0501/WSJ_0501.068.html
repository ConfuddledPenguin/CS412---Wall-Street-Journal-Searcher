Aircoa Hospitality Services Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

DENVER -- Aircoa Hospitality Services Inc., a hotel management concern, said it asked the Securities and Exchange Commission to deregister its common stock, which is traded over the counter, and to suspend its financial reporting obligations within 90 days, because the number of its common stockholders and senior unsecured note holders has fallen below 300 each.
