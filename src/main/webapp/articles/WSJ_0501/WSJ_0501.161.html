International Brief -- Tootal Group PLC: Annual Pretax Net Slipped 16%, First Fall in 9 Years

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

Tootal Group PLC, a British clothing, textile and thread maker, said pretax profit for the year ended Jan. 31 fell 16% to #35.7 million ($58.4 million) from #42.3 million a year earlier, the first such decline in nine years. Profit after taxes and minority interests, or profit attributable to shareholders, dropped 18% to #24.4 million from #29.7 million. Per-share earnings slumped 22% to 8.53 pence from 11 pence.

Sales rose 7.8% to #529.8 million from #491.5 million.

Tootal is to recommend a final dividend of 3.05 pence a share, raising the total payout for the year 2.1% to 4.95 pence from 4.85 pence.

Despite the earnings decline, Tootal said its streamlining strategy, begun in 1986, showed a "major advance" in the past year, shifting the balance of earnings away from a heavy dependence on Africa and the commodity end of British textiles into more growth markets, especially Asia.
