Bankruptcies in Canada Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian bankruptcies rose to 4,104 in March, up 34.9% from 3,043 a year earlier, the federal Consumer and Corporate Affairs Department said. Individual bankruptcies totaled 3,235 and business bankruptcies totaled 869 in March.

In the first three months of the year, bankruptcies rose to 11,421, up 29.1% from 8,846 from a year earlier.
