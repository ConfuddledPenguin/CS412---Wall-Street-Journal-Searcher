Who's News: Amity Bancorp Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

AMITY BANCORP Inc. (New Haven, Conn.) -- Robert J. Hausman, chairman of Loews Corp.'s Loews Hotels, New York, and Marcial Cuevas, executive director of Community Action Agency Inc., New Haven, were named directors of this bank holding company, increasing the board to 15 members.
