Federal-Mogul Corp. Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

SOUTHFIELD, Mich. -- Federal-Mogul Corp. said it purchased select assets of Australian Automatic Transmission Spares (AATS), a division of Brebo Pty. Ltd. The purchase was made through Federal-Mogul's Australian subsidiary, Federal-Mogul Pty. Ltd. Terms weren't disclosed.

AATS is an assembler and distributor of automatic transmission service repair kits.
