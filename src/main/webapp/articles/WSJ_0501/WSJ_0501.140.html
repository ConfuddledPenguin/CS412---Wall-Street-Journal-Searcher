BC Gas to Sell Preferred

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

VANCOUVER, British Columbia -- BC Gas Inc., a natural-gas utility, said it agreed to sell to Scotia-McLeod Inc., a Toronto securities dealer, 20 million Canadian dollars (US$17.2 million) of preferred shares. BC Gas said proceeds will be used to reduce debt. The dividend rate on the shares, which will be cumulative, redeemable and non-convertible, will be 9% annually for the first five years, and set by negotiation subsequently, it said.
