Transgenic Sciences To Redeem Warrants

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

WORCESTER, Mass. -- Transgenic Sciences Inc. said it will redeem the Class A warrants issued in connection with its initial public offering last May. The warrants are redeemable at five cents each on May 30. Prior to 5 p.m. the day before the redemption date, each Class A warrant is exercisable to purchase for $2 one share of common stock and two redeemable Class B warrants. D.H. Blair & Co. is serving as the solicitation agent.

If all the Class A warrants are exercised, Transgenic Sciences will realize $3.5 million. To date, warrants totaling approximately $710,000, or 18.5% of the total, have been exercised. The Class B warrants may be exercised from May 2, 1990, to May 1, 1994, to purchase one share of common stock. In certain circumstances, the Class B warrants are subject to earlier redemption by the company.

TSI is a biotechnology company.
