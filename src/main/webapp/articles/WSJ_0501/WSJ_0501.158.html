International Brief -- British Steel PLC: Spain's Aristrain Is Expected To Accept Takeover Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

British Steel PLC could soon be about to realize its ambition of acquiring one of Spain's most profitable steel companies, Jose Maria Aristrain S.A. Industry sources said Aristrain's board is expected to decide later this week in favor of selling the Olaberria-based company to British Steel.

A British Steel spokesman in London refused to comment, but the company is understood to have offered around $410 million for the family-owned company, which has annual revenue of about 50 billion pesetas ($471.5 million).

The planned purchase, if it materializes, is part of British Steel's strategy for expansion in continental Europe, where it has servicing and stocking centers but no manufacturing subsidiaries.

Aristrain is Spain's largest producer of steel sections and has an extensive marketing network in Spain. Its acquisition would provide British Steel with a valuable base in Southern Europe.
