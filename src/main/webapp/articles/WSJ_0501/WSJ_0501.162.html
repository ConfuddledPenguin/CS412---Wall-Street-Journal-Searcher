CleveTrust Realty Investors

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

WESTLAKE, Ohio -- CleveTrust Realty Investors said the $1.5 million sale of a shopping center in Endinboro, Pa., will result in a gain of about $340,000 in the fiscal third quarter, ending June 30. Proceeds will be used to pay debt, the real-estate investment trust said.
