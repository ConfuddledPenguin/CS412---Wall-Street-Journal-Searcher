West German Wholesale Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

WIESBADEN, West Germany -- West German wholesale sales were up a real, or price-adjusted, 3.0% in March from a year earlier, based on preliminary data, the Federal Statistics Office said. The office also reported that wholesale sales also rose 3.0% in nominal terms from a year earlier. Wholesale sales in March were 78 billion marks ($46.4 billion).

In the first quarter, wholesale sales totaled 213 billion marks, also a 3.0% increase in nominal and real terms, the statistics office said.
