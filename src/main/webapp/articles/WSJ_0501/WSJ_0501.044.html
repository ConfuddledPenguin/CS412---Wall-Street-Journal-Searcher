WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Crest Industries Inc., initial offering of 900,000 common shares, via Raymond James & Associates Inc.

East-West Europe Fund Inc., initial offering of 8.5 million common shares, via First Boston Corp.

The Kingdom of Denmark, offering of $100 million of S&P 500 Index Protected Equity Notes, via a PaineWebber Inc. group.

Sara Lee Corp., offering of up to 1,500 shares, or $150 million, of auction preferred stock, via Goldman, Sachs & Co.

Xilinx Inc., proposed initial offering of 2.5 million common shares, of which 750,000 shares will be newly issued common stock and 1,750,000 shares will be offered by holders, via a Morgan Stanley & Co. and Cowen & Co. group.
