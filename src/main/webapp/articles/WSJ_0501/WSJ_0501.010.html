Who's News: Showboat Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

SHOWBOAT Inc. (Las Vegas, Nev.) -- William J. Downey resigned as a director and as executive vice president of the company's subsidiary, Atlantic City Showboat Inc. He continues as a full-time consultant to the hotel/casino operator. The company said it doesn't currently plan to replace Mr. Downey as a director, leaving the board with 11 members.
