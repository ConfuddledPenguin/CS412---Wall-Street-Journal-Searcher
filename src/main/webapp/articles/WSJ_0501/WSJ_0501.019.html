B.F. Goodrich Co. Expansion

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

AKRON, Ohio -- B.F. Goodrich Co. said it will invest about $14 million to expand capacity to produce hydrophilic resins at Calvert City, Ky., and Pohang, South Korea. The expansion, which is scheduled to be completed in mid-1991, will increase the company's world-wide capacity for the materials by 35%, Goodrich said.

Hydrophilic resins are additives used as thickening and suspension agents in cosmetics and personal-care products, and a number of other uses, the company said.

Goodrich is a plastics, specialty chemicals and aerospace products concern.
