Who's News: Sears Canada Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

SEARS CANADA Inc. (Toronto) -- Larry Ginther and William Turner were elected directors of this 60%-owned subsidiary of Sears, Roebuck & Co., succeeding Laurence Cudmore and Arthur Mingay. Mr. Ginther is vice president and chief financial officer of Sears Canada and Mr. Turner is vice president, merchandising and catalogue. Mr. Mingay, 70 years old, had reached the company's mandatory retirement age. Mr. Cudmore, as previously reported, was appointed president of retail with the parent company in Chicago late last year.
