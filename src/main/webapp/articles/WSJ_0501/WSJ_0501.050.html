Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

KAUFMAN & BROAD HOME Corp. -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its rating on the subordinated debt of this Los Angeles home builder to double-B-minus from double-B-plus. About $405 million of face value of zero-coupon debt is affected. The implied senior debt rating is double-B-plus, S&P said. The ratings agency attributed the move to weakness in the U.S. housing market, as well as its expectation that Kaufman & Broad's leverage will exceed earlier projections.
