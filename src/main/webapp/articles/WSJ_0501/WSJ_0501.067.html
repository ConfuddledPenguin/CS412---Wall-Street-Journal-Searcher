Presidio Oil Sets Redemption

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

DENVER -- Presidio Oil Co., an oil and gas concern, said it will redeem on June 1 the remaining 9.5% cumulative convertible preferred shares outstanding for $5 each. Holders have until May 24 to convert each preferred share into 1.25 Class A common shares. An earlier exchange offer was accepted by holders of 96% of the preferred stock, the company said.

The redemption, financed by a specially created sinking fund, is aimed at improving earnings on Presidio's common stock, a spokeswoman said.
