Fish and Scraps?

Recycle plastic, save the world,

The nature lovers urge;

Now scientists in England have

Prepared a plastic purge.

Yes, little bits of plastic

Have the same effect as bran.

So, throw that plastic on your food,

Not in the garbage can]

-- Harriet Cooper.
