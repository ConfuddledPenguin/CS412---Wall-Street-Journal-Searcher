Omni Exploration Plans Hiring

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Omni Exploration Inc. directors authorized hiring investment bankers to explore the possible sale of the company or other transactions that would maximize shareholder value. The market price of the common stock doesn't fairly reflect the value of Omni's oil and natural gas reserves, said Gary Novinskie, president.

Omni Exploration is an oil and gas production company with reserves mainly in Texas, Oklahoma, Louisiana and Kansas. For the fiscal year ended last Sept. 30, the company had a net loss of $235,000, or one cent a share, on revenue of $2.1 million.
