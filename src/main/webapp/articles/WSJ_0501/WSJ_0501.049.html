Jacobs Engineering Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said it received a contract it valued at $13 million from Orient Chemical Corp. for design and construction of a chemical manufacturing plant in Seaford, Del. Jacobs said that the plant will produce dyestuffs used in the manufacture of ink, shoe polish and plastic products. Orient Chemical, based in Port Newark, N.J., is an affiliate of Orient Chemical Industries of Japan.
