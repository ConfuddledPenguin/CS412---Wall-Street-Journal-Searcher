Business Brief -- United Gaming Inc.: Wilms to Pay $10 Million To Lift His Stake to 59%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

United Gaming Inc. said it reached an agreement in principle under which Alfred H. Wilms, chairman, will buy an additional $10 million of the company's common stock. Mr. Wilms now owns 3,732,091 United Gaming common shares, or about 47% of its stock outstanding. The additional investment, which is subject to several conditions including board approval, would boost his stake approximately 12%, to about 59% of the company's shares.

Based in Las Vegas, Nev., United Gaming operates gambling machine routes and sells gambling equipment.

David Scheinman, president, said the investment would give the company capital to fund its entry into the casino hotel business and is Mr. Wilms's vote of confidence in the company's future.
