Who's News: Ametek Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

AMETEK Inc. (Paoli, Pa.) -- Roger K. Derr was named executive vice president and chief operating officer of this manufacturing concern. Mr. Derr, 58 years old, succeeds John Dandalides, who retired. Allan Kornfeld, chief financial officer, was given the additional title of executive vice president; he will continue to serve as chief financial officer.
