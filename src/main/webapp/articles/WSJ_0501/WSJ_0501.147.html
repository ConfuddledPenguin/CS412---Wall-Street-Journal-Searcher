Business Brief -- Material Sciences Corp.: Board Reviewing Strategic, Financial Options for Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

Material Sciences Corp., which recently reported a big loss for fiscal 1990, said its board is reviewing "various strategic and financial alternatives" for the company. Last month, the maker of coatings and laminates said charges from the sale of a unit and a write-down of some assets led to a loss of $30.4 million, or $6.10 a share, for the year ended Feb. 28. Sales were $152.7 million.

A spokesman said the board is "very concerned" about the value put on the Elk Grove Village, Ill., company by the stock market. In American Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, the stock was unchanged at $9.25, its 52-week low. The company's announcement came after the close of trading. The spokesman said the board believes the company is "worth much more than $45 million," the indicated market value based on its nearly five million shares outstanding. The company wouldn't say what alternatives it's exploring.
