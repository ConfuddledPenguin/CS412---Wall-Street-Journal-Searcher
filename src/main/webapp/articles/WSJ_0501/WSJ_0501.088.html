Carter-Wallace Buys Hygeia Sciences Unit From Tambrands Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

NEW YORK -- Carter-Wallace Inc. said it acquired Hygeia Sciences Inc., which markets First Response at-home pregnancy and ovulation test kits, from Tambrands Inc. Terms weren't disclosed. Carter-Wallace, a marketer of toiletries and pharmaceutical products, said that the First Response will complement its existing line of Answer at-home pregnancy and ovulation test kits and will make it the leading marketer of such kits. Tambrands said that First Response, with annual sales of about $28 million, has about a 25% market share of the in-home test kit market, while Answer has about a 10% market share.

Tambrands, based in Lake Success, N.Y., is a maker of personal-care products. In selling its Hygeia Science subsidiary, Tambrands said that it has completed its plan to divest noncore businesses to concentrate on marketing Tampax tampons and Maxithins sanitary pads. Last February, Tambrands sold its cosmetics subsidiary, Physicians Formula Cosmetics Inc., to Pierre Farbre, a French-based cosmetics company.
