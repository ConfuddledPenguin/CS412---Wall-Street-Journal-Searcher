Business Brief -- Financial Corp. of Santa Barbara: Big Board Says It Reviews Financial Status for Listing

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

The New York Stock Exchange said it is reviewing the financial eligibility of Financial Corp. of Santa Barbara for continued listing on the exchange, following last week's seizure by federal regulators of the company's thrift unit. The Santa Barbara, Calif., concern, whose Santa Barbara Savings & Loan Association is its main unit, didn't return phone calls yesterday seeking comment. The parent expressed surprise last Friday when regulators seized the weak thrift and placed it in conservatorship.

Santa Barbara Savings is one of several of California's biggest S&Ls that have failed recently, or are in danger of failing, because of the latest wave of problems brought on by ill-fated diversification, either outside the state or outside traditional home-lending.
