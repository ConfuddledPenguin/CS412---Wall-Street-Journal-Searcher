Who's News: Armstrong World Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

ARMSTRONG WORLD INDUSTRIES Inc. (Lancaster, Pa.) -- Robert H. Caldwell, 59 years old, who retired in February as senior executive vice president of this floor and building products maker, retired as a director. Shareholders at the annual meeting voted on four new directors, one of whom will succeed Mr. Caldwell; election results won't be available until at least May 11.
