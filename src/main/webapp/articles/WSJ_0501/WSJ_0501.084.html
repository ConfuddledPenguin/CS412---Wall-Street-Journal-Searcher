Who's News: Colgate-Palmolive Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

COLGATE-PALMOLIVE Co. -- Stephen K. Zrike was named president and chief executive officer of Princess House, a North Dighton, Mass., unit of this consumer-products concern. Mr. Zrike succeeds Richard C. Brown, who is retiring. Mr. Zrike joined Princess House in 1985 and was executive vice president until recently. Princess House makes and markets crystal and other gift and decorative products.
