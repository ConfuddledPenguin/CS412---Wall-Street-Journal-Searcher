First National's Fiscal Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- First National Corp. said it changed from a calendar year to a fiscal year ending Feb. 28 as a result of the acquisition of Clyde A. Short Co. on that date. The fiscal year more closely reflects First National's business cycle since the acquisition, the company said. Clyde A. Short, now First National's major business, supplies employee-motivation and incentive-award programs to industry.

For the two months ended Feb. 28, profit was $68,974, or three cents a share, on revenue of $208,973, First National said. Comparable figures for a year earlier aren't available, the company said.
