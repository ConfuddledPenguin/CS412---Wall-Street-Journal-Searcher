Pittston Unit's Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

GREENWICH, Conn. -- Pittston Co. said its Brink's Inc. unit purchased all the shares it didn't already own in an air-courier and armored-car company based in the United Kingdom. Terms weren't disclosed. Brink's, which previously owned 35% of Brink's-MAT Ltd., London, purchased the remaining 65% from its partner, MAT Group Ltd. Brink's-MAT, which operates in several European countries as well as in Bahrain and Hong Kong, had revenue of about $65 million last year.

Pittston is a coal, security and transportation concern.
