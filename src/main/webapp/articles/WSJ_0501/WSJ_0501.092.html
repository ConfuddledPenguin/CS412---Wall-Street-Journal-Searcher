ICH Sets Transaction for Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- I.C.H. Corp. said it agreed to sell a unit, Marquette National Life Insurance Co., to Consolidated National Successor Corp. and I.C.H. Chairman Robert T. Shaw, who owns 55% of Consolidated, which in turn owns 22% of the common and all the Class B shares of I.C.H. The price wasn't disclosed. I.C.H. said it doesn't expect the sale to result in significant gains or losses in the current year.
