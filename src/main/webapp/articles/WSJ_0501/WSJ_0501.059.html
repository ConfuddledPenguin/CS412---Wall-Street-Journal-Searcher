Dividend News: Foster Wheeler Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

FOSTER WHEELER Corp. increased its quarterly dividend 14% to 12.5 cents a share from 11 cents, payable June 15 to stock of record May 15. The Clinton, N.J., engineering, construction and real estate development company reported first-quarter net income rose 20% to $8.4 million, or 24 cents a share, from $6.9 million, or 20 cents a share, a year earlier, when results included a $3 million gain from a pension plan settlement. Revenue increased 18% to $311.2 million from $263.1 million.
