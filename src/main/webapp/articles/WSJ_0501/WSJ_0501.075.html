Lincoln National Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/01/90

FORT WAYNE, Ind. -- Lincoln National Corp. said it completed the previously announced sale of National Reinsurance Corp. to a holding company organized by Robert M. Bass Group Inc. and Acadia Partners Limited Partnership. Lincoln National said it received $320.1 million in cash, plus $75 million face amount in preferred stock of the holding company formed to acquire National Reinsurance.

The insurance-holding company said it will use proceeds to redeem its auction rate preferred stock and to reduce short-term debt.
