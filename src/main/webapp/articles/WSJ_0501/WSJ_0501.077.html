Triton Energy's Europe Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

DALLAS -- Triton Energy Corp., an oil and gas concern with extensive overseas holdings, said that it dropped plans to sell its 59.47% interest in its Triton Europe PLC unit, but that Triton Europe is continuing discussions to sell all or part of its oil and gas assets in the British and Netherlands part of the North Sea. The company previously estimated the value of its stake in the European unit at about $180 million, based on Triton Europe's stock price on the London Stock Exchange. Triton Energy, which has operations in France, Britain and the North Sea, said it ended talks about a possible sale of its stake, because it doesn't believe it would receive an appropriate price for the assets.
