U.S. Raw-Steel Output Fell 1.3% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by U.S. mills declined 1.3% last week to 1,929,000 tons from 1,954,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 5.6% from the 2,044,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 85.8% of its capability last week, compared with 86.9% the previous week and 92.4% a year earlier.
