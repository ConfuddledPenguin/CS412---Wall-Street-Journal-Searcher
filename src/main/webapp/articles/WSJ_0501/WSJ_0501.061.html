Oak Industries to Sell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/01/90

SAN DIEGO, Calif. -- Oak Industries Inc. said it reached an agreement in principle to sell its Oak Communications Inc. subsidiary to the Lorain Group, a San Diego-based investment firm. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed, and the sale is expected to close by late June if all conditions are met.

William S. Antle III, Oak's president and chief executive officer, said the sale would help the parent company improve return of its operations and fund acquisitions. Oak Communications is a maker of cable and setellite communications equipment whose principal manufacturing plant is in Taipei, Taiwan. Oak Industries is an electronic parts maker.
