  • Date: 05/01/90

MOUNT AIRY, N.C. -- Insteel Industries Inc. said Stelwire, a Hamilton, Ontario-based unit of Stelco Inc., will become the Canadian distributor for a new construction panel produced by Insteel. The new system uses a welded wire frame integrated with an expanded polystyrene insulating core. Concrete is poured on each side of the unit after it is in place, producing a steel-reinforced concrete sandwich wall.
