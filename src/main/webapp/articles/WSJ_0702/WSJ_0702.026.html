BTR Completes Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

LONDON -- BTR PLC, an industrial conglomerate, said it completed the acquisition of the automotive-sealing unit of Hartford, Conn.-based United Technologies Corp. for $191 million. The unit, which has 4,100 employees and six manufacturing operations in the U.S. and Europe, had $275 million in sales in 1989, BTR said.
