WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Marquette Electronics, proposed initial offering of 2,140,000 common shares, of which 725,000 are being sold by the company and 1,415,000 by holders, via Robert W. Baird & Co. and Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co.

Seagull Energy Corp., offering of 2.5 million common shares, via Lehman Brothers, The First Boston Corp., Smith Barney, Lehman Brothers International, and Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd.

Vishay Intertechnology Inc., offering of two million common shares, via Bear Stearns & Co. Inc., Salomon Brothers Inc. and Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc.

Williams-Sonoma Inc., proposed offering of 1.4 million common shares, of which 887,000 will be sold by the company and 513,000 shares by holders, via Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Montgomery Securities.
