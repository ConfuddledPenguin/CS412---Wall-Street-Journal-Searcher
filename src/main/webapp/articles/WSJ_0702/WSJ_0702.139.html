Who's News: Defiance Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

DEFIANCE Inc. (Defiance, Ohio) -- Thomas H. Roulston Sr., president and chief executive officer, was named chairman of this supplier of technology and products to manufacturers in the transportation industry. Mr. Roulston succeeds Allen J. Portnoy, who remains a director. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

THOMAS H. ROULSTON SR. was named chairman of Defiance Inc. In yesterday's edition, it was incorrectly stated that Mr. Roulston was Defiance's president and chief executive officer.

(WSJ July 3, 1990)
