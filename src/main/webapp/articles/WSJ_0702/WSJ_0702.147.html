Who's News: Continental Air Holdings Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

CONTINENTAL AIR HOLDINGS Inc. (Houston) -- Neal F. Meehan, formerly president of Continental Express, was named to the new position of senior vice president for customer service for Continental Airlines. Separately, Barry P. Simon, formerly chief counsel of Eastern Airlines, was named senior vice president for legal affairs, general counsel and secretary for Continental. He succeeds Richard D. Hirst, who took a similar position at Northwest Airlines.
