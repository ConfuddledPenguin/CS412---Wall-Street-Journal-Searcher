Letters to the Editor: No Bankruptcy Bias

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

Concerning your May 30 article "More Employers Check Credit Histories of Job Seekers to Judge Their Character": Employers who check credit histories of job seekers should be warned that the 1984 amendments to the Bankruptcy Code prohibit them from discriminating against those who have been bankrupt or have failed to pay debts that are dischargeable in bankruptcy: 11 U.S.C. 525(b).

Roger A. Weber

Cincinnati
