American Home Products Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

NEW YORK -- American Home Products Corp. said it completed most of the sale of its household products and depilatory businesses to Reckitt & Colman PLC. The pharmaceutical manufacturer said the sale of the businesses in the U.S. and 17 other countries had closed. It said Reckitt & Colman, a London-based marketer of consumer brands, expected to complete its purchase of the remaining American Home operations in six other countries before the end of 1990.

The total purchase price is $1.25 billion.
