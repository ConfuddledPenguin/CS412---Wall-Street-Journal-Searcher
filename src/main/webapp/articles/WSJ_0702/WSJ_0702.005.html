New Stock Listings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

NEW YORK -- The New York Stock Exchange announced one new listing and the American Stock Exchange announced an initial public offering, while the Pacific Stock Exchange added one listing and the National Market System of the over-the-counter market announced nine initial public offerings.

Kent Electronics Corp., a Houston producer of custom-made electronics will begin trading on the Big Board on Friday under the symbol KNT. It previously traded on the Amex.

Benchmark Electronics Inc., a Clute, Texas, electronics manufacturing services company, began trading last week on the Amex as an initial public offering under BEI.

Go-Video Inc., a Scottsdale, Ariz. maker of dual-deck videocassette recorders, joined the Pacific Stock Exchange in May and began trading under GVI. Go-Video also trades on the over-the-counter market.

Joining the OTC market, AICorp Inc., a Waltham, Mass., maker of computer-aided software engineering products, began trading under AICP. Arctco Inc., a Thief River Falls, Minn., maker of snowmobiles and related accessories, began trading under ACAT. CFSB Bancorp Inc., a Lansing, Mich., bank holding company for Capitol Federal, began trading under CFSB. Crest Industries Inc, a Miami Fla., distributor of home improvement products, began trading under CRII. Horsehead Resource Development Co., a Palmerton, Pa., hazardous waste recovery company, began trading under HHRD. JSB Financial Inc., a Lynbrook, N.Y., holding company for Jamaica Savings Bank, FSB, began trading under JSBF. Sundowner Offshore Services Inc., a Houston oil and gas well services company, began trading under SOSI. Swift Transportation Co., a Phoenix holding company for Swift Transportation and Cooper Motor Lines, truckload carriers, began trading under SWFT. Vidmark Inc., a Santa Monica, Calif., distributor of motion pictures, began trading under VDMK.
