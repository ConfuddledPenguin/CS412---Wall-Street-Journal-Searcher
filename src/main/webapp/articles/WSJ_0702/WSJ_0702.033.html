Siebe Commences Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

BOSTON -- Siebe PLC said it commenced a previously announced tender offer for all outstanding shares of Foxboro Co.'s common stock at $52 a share. The offer, scheduled to expire July 27, is subject to the tender of at least two-thirds of the outstanding shares, on a fully diluted basis.

Siebe PLC is a British company that manufactures industrial and environmental controls, specialized engineerings, safety and life support products.

On June 25, Siebe agreed to buy Foxboro Co., the Massachusetts-based company that produces process controls for manufacturing.
