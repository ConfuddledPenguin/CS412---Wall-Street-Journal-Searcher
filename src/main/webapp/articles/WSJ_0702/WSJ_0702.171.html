Alta Suspends Mine Operations

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

SALT LAKE CITY -- Alta Gold Co. said it laid off 66 employees as it suspended mining operations at its Easy Junior gold mine near Ely, Nev. The mining concern cited slumping gold prices and increased operational costs. It said it would resume production at the mine by year end "if the price of gold permits." Alta, which operates four gold mines, has about 450 employees, reflecting a reduction of about 230 workers since gold prices began slipping earlier this year.
