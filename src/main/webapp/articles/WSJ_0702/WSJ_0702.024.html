CMS Energy Unit's Rate Move

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

JACKSON, Mich. -- CMS Energy Corp's. Consumers Power Co. unit asked regulators to approve an average 1.7% increase in its electric rates to pay for the eventual decommissioning of the utility's two nuclear power plants, Big Rock Point and Palisades. The request, if granted, would produce approximately $36.2 million in additional revenue annually, to be placed in external trusts for investment, CMS said.
