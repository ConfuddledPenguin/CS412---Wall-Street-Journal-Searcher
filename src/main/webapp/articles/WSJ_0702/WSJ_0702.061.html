Precision Timing

Where does the small investor go

When the market sharply declines?

Once the stocks reach their bottom,

He moves to the sidelines.

And there he prudently stays

Through each daily groan and creak,

And then boldly rushes in

As the market reaches peak.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

Recovery Rooms

Our hale-and-hearty weekend athletes,

In their tough workouts never shirk,

Knowing after the sweating and straining,

There'll be five days to relax at work.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.
