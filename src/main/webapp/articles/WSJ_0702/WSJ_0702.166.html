Wainoco Acquires Properties

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

HOUSTON -- Wainoco Oil Corp., an oil and gas exploration and production company, said it completed its acquisition of the U.S. oil and gas properties of Tredegar Industries Inc., a Richmond, Va., plastics and aluminum maker, for $15.3 million. The properties were acquired for less than the $16.5 million initially expected, because Amerada Hess Corp., New York, bought some of the properties under a preferential purchase right, Wainoco said.
