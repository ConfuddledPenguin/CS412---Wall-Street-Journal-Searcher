Cadillac Extends Incentives

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

DETROIT -- General Motors Corp.'s Cadillac division said it is extending incentives on certain 1990 models through Sept. 26. The program was to end June 30. Cadillac said the rebate on its De Ville, Fleetwood and Brougham models is $1,500, and $2,000 on Eldorado and Seville.
