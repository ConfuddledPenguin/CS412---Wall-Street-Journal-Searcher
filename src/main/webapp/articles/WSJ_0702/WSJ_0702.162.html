Integra-A Pacts Terminated

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

DALLAS -- Integra-A Hotel & Restaurant Co. said previously announced agreements that would have provided new capital for the company have been terminated. The agreements also would have allowed Hallwood Group Inc. to sell its 13% share in Integra. The agreements had called for a $10 million loan from affiliates of Burrus Investment Group Inc. and Westbrooke Hospitality Corp. and the purchase, by those affiliates, of Hallwood's stake in Integra.

Integra, which owns and operates hotels, said it will seek negotiations with Hallwood for an extension of current financial accommodations.
