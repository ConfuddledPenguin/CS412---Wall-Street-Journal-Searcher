Multimedia Redeems Debt in a Refinancing

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

GREENVILLE, S.C. -- Multimedia Inc. said it is redeeming its subordinated redeemable discount debentures, due 2005, at their $480 million face value, as part of a $1.1 billion refinancing package. The newspaper, broadcasting and cable-television concern, which issued the discount debentures as part of a recapitalization in 1985, said its latest refinancing package is composed of a $700 million bank facility and a $400 million private placement of unsecured senior notes.

In addition to redeeming the debt, Multimedia said it will use the proceeds from the refinancing to service its bank debt and to create a pool of about $375 million for acquisitions.

Multimedia said its new credit agreements allow it to buy back stock within certain limitations; it may do so based on market conditions. The refinancing facilities provide an overall interest rate of about 11%, the company said.
