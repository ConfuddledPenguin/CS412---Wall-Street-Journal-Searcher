Japanese Unemployment

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

TOKYO -- Japan's seasonally adjusted jobless rate stood at 2.1% of the labor force in May, unchanged from April, the Prime Minister's Office said. The unemployment rate has stayed at a low 2% to 2.2% since June 1989, reflecting Japan's continued economic expansion.

In May, the total number of jobholders was 63.4 million, rising by 1.3 million people, or 2.1%, from the previous year.

Non-farm businesses reported a year-on-year increase in jobholders of 3.8%, to a total of 48.5 million people. The number of jobholders in the construction industry jumped 7% to 6.1 million.
