Business Brief -- Banknorth Group Inc.: Loan-Loss Reserve Is Raised $5.2 Million for 2nd Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

Banknorth Group Inc., Burlington, Vt., said it plans to add $5.2 million to its loan-loss reserve for the second quarter. The company said it expects second-quarter earnings to be in the range of 33 cents to 38 cents a share. In the year-ago quarter, the company had net income of 69 cents a share -- restated in December after the concern merged with Howard Bancorp, of Burlington, Vt.

Banknorth said it expects to take charge-offs of about $2.5 million in the second quarter.

Banknorth said its nonperforming assets are about $28.6 million, or about 1.7% of total assets. On March 31, nonperforming assets were $21.9 million, or 1.4% of total assets.
