Business Brief -- J.P. Industries Inc.: T&N Extends Expiration Date of Its Tender Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

T&N PLC said it extended the expiration date of its tender offer to buy all the common stock of J.P. Industries Inc., an Ann Arbor, Mich., automotive engine-parts maker, after regulators requested additional time. T&N, a U.K. equipment manufacturer, began its tender offer in April, after J.P. Industries agreed to be acquired by T&N for $190 million, or $17.30 a share, plus assumption of $185 million of debt.

The companies said the Federal Trade Commission requested more time to complete its analysis of information submitted by the companies under the Hart-Scott-Rodino antitrust law. The FTC also requested an earlier extension in May, after requesting additional information.

T&N said it will extend the offer until July 13. The offer had been scheduled to expire on Tuesday. As of this past Wednesday, shareholders had tendered 6,274,359 shares, or nearly 60% of the shares outstanding.
