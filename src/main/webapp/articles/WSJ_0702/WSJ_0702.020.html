Dividend News: Hotel Investors Trust Nearly Halves Payout

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

LOS ANGELES -- Hotel Investors Trust cut its quarterly dividend to 13 cents from 25 cents a share, payable Aug. 1 to shareholders of record July 10. The shares of Hotel Investors Trust, a real estate investment trust, and Hotel Investors Corp., a hotel management company, are paired for trading on the New York Stock Exchange. The stock rose 12.5 cents Friday to close at $4.

The companies are in the process of selling most of their assets, and have retained Salomon Bros. Inc. to review a possible sale of two Las Vegas, Nev., casino hotels. As previously announced, the companies have agreed to sell 31 hotels to Park Plaza Inc., an affiliate of EIE International Corp. of Japan.

Hotel Investors Trust and Hotel Investors Corp. reported a combined loss of $60.4 million, or $4.97 a share, for 1989. The loss included a $55.8 million reserve for the planned sale of certain properties.

The two companies said their boards have adopted amendments to the trustees' regulations and corporation bylaws concerning the conduct of shareholder meetings. A company spokeswoman said the changes include the clarification of rules on shareholder proposals being presented to the boards.
