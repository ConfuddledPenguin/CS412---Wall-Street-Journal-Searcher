Business Brief -- Hillsdown Holdings PLC: Unit Is Tendered 96.4% Of Canada Packers Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

Hillsdown Holdings (Canada) Ltd. of Toronto, a wholly owned unit of Hillsdown Holdings PLC of Britain, said 34.8 million shares of Toronto-based Canada Packers Inc., or about 96.4% of the total outstanding, were tendered to its offer. Hillsdown, a food-processing company, said it plans to take up 7.2 million, or about 20%, of Canada Packers shares, on a pro rata basis at 16.50 Canadian dollars (US$14.12) each. The tender offer is a part of Hillsdown's agreement to acquire a 56% stake in Canada Packers, Canada's largest food-processing concern.

Also under the agreement, Hillsdown's Maple Leaf Mills Ltd. unit will be transferred to Canada Packers, in exchange for 29.5 million new Canada Packers shares. The arrangement is subject to regulatory approval.
