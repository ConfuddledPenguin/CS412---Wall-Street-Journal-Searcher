Who's News: WPP Group PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

WPP GROUP PLC (London) -- This advertising and marketing services company named David Wynn-Morgan deputy chief executive officer, a new post, at Hill & Knowlton International S.A., Brussels, its management unit for Europe, Africa and the Middle East. Mr. Wynn-Morgan also is a director of the international subsidiary's immediate parent, Hill & Knowlton Inc. He will continue as chairman of Hill & Knowlton (U.K.) Ltd., London, and as executive vice president for European financial relations.
