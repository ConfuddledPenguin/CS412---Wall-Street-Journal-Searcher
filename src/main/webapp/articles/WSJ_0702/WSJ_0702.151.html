Who's News: Reader's Digest Association Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

READER'S DIGEST ASSOCIATION Inc.(Pleasantville, N.Y.) -- Anthony W. Ruggiero was elected vice president and chief financial officer and Melvin R. Laird was elected to the board of this publisher and direct marketer. Mr. Ruggiero, 49 years old, succeeds Vernon Thomas, who stepped down earlier for health reasons. Mr. Laird, 67, fills a vacancy created when Richard F. McLoughlin retired as president. Mr. Laird is Reader's Digest's senior vice president and counselor for national and international affairs.
