Wetterau Pact to Buy Korman

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

ST. LOUIS -- Wetterau Inc. said it reached a preliminary agreement to acquire closely held Korman Group Cos., a New Jersey grocery store chain. Terms of the pact weren't disclosed.

Wetterau, a food distributor, said the acquisition is subject to a definitive agreement and compliance with regulatory requirements.

Korman, which operates 14 stores, has annual sales of about $120 million.
