CIS Technologies Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

TULSA, Okla. -- C.I.S. Technologies Inc. said its majority shareholder, Swiss Reinsurance Co. of Zurich, Switzerland, plans to sell its 53% stake in the company. C.I.S., which manages insurance claims for the hospital industry, said Swiss Reinsurance's decision wasn't related to C.I.S.'s performance, but was a "strategic decision." C.I.S. said it will support the holder's efforts, but added that it intends to remain independent.
