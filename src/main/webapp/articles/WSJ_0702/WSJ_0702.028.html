Fisons PLC Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

LONDON -- Fisons PLC, a British pharmaceuticals and laboratory-equipment maker, said it will take a controlling interest in a Japanese-based joint venture with Fujisawa Pharmaceutical Co. Fujisawa has agreed to sell Fisons a 15% stake in Fujisawa-Fisons KK, Fisons said. The purchase will lift Fisons's holding to 65%.

The joint venture, begun in 1980, markets Fisons drugs in Japan.

Fisons said it plans to develop the Osaka-based joint venture's access to the Japanese hospital market.
