Who's News: Datamarine's Hill Resigns

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

POCASSET, Mass. -- Datamarine International Inc., a marine instruments maker, said Howard H. Hill, 58 years old, resigned as chairman, president, and chief executive officer "to pursue other interests." Mr. Hill, one of the company's founders, said the resignation was voluntary. David Thompson, chief operating officer, will be acting chief executive, while the board searches for a new president and chief executive. The company said it hadn't named a new chairman and isn't looking for one.
