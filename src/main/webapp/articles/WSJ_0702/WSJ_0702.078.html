Letters to the Editor: Frugal Litigation

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

Your Law column commentary (June 4) about the joint trial of state and federal cases relating to a common nucleus of fact in the asbestos litigation highlights the imagination of the federal and state judges involved and their determination to resolve complex and expensive-to-litigate cases in as economic a method as possible. Concurrent litigation of related issues is not uncommon, and you deserve credit for publicizing the method; it is hoped, other judges will follow suit.

Robert Steinbock-Sinclair

Oak Brook, Ill.
