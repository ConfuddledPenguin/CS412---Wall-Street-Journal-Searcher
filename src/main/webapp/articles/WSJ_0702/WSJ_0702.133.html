Business Brief -- Amrep Corp.: Loss of $1.9 Million Posted For Fiscal Fourth Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

Amrep Corp., a New York-based real estate developer and magazine distributor, reported a loss of $1.9 million, or 28 cents a share, for its fourth quarter ended April 30. The company had year-earlier net income of $174,000, or three cents a share. Revenue fell 15% to $20.4 million from $24.1 million.

Amrep officials were unavailable to elaborate on the results.

In early June, Kane-Miller Corp., a Tarrytown, N.Y., food processor, disclosed to the Securities and Exchange Commission that it owned 8.1% of Amrep and that it might seek control of the company. Later in the month, Peter B. Bedford, a Lafayette, Calif., real estate developer, about doubled his Amrep holding to 16.16% and said he and the company had discussed a possible board seat for him or a representative.
