Control Resource Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

MICHIGAN CITY, Ind. -- Control Resource Industries Inc., which makes equipment and supplies used in asbestos abatement and industrial safety products, said its board authorized the purchase of an additional 500,000 common shares under its July 1988 program that initially allowed buying back as many as 200,000 shares. The company has about 5.4 million shares outstanding. Shares bought back will be used for general corporate purposes, Control Resource said.
