Who's News: Unilever Group

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

UNILEVER GROUP (New York) -- Unilever U.S., the U.S. arm of the British and Dutch consumer-goods company named two senior vice presidents at the Thomas J. Lipton Inc. food and beverage unit, based in Englewood Cliffs, N.J.: David W. St. Clair, law and administration, and David E. Grein, finance. Mr. St. Clair, currently a director, had been vice president, general counsel and secretary. Mr. Grein, previously vice president and treasurer, also was named chief financial officer and a director.
