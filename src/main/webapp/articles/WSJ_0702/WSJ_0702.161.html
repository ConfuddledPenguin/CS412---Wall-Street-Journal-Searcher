Arvin Industries Buys Business

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

COLUMBUS, Ind. -- Arvin Industries Inc., an auto parts maker, said it purchased the engine-manifold and exhaust-tube business of Tubauto S.A., a unit of Vallourec Group of Paris. Terms weren't disclosed. Arvin said Tubauto has annual sales of about $20 million and makes exhaust parts for several auto makers.
