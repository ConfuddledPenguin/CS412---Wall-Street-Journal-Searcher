Australia's Current Account

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/02/90

CANBERRA, Australia -- Australia's current account deficit widened to an unadjusted 1.55 billion Australian dollars (US$1.22 billion) in May from a revised balance-of-payments gap of A$952 million in April, but narrowed from a deficit of A$1.97 billion in May 1989, the Bureau of Statistics said. In seasonally adjusted terms, the current account deficit narrowed to A$1.34 billion in May from a A$1.35 billion gap in April and from a deficit of A$1.87 billion in May 1989.
