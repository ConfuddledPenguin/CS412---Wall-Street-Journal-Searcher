Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

CALTON Inc. (Freehold, N.J.) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its rating on the $62 million of subordinated debt of this home builder to triple-C-plus from single-B-minus. The rating concern said its action reflects Calton's diminished liquidity and financial flexibility. S&P noted the recent weak New Jersey-Pennsylvania real estate market and "excess supply" in the Florida market. Calton probably won't return to 1986 and 1988 profitability levels in the next two years, S&P said.
