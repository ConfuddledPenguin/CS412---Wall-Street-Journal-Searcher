Financing Business: Great Lakes Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/02/90

GREAT LAKES BANCORP, Ann Arbor, Mich., said it began a public offering of its preferred units last Friday. The offering will raise $24 million of tangible capital for the bank, or as much as $27.6 million if the underwriters exercise their option for additional units. The company is offering 2.4 million preferred units. The underwriters, Roney & Co. and Ohio Co., have a 30-day option to purchase as many as 360,000 additional preferred units. Quarterly dividends will be paid, if declared, at an annual rate of $10 a preferred share through June 30, 1995.
