Who's News: Dresser Industries Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

DRESSER INDUSTRIES Inc. (Dallas) -- James L. Bryan was elected to the new post of vice president, operations, of this energy services concern. Mr. Bryan was formerly president of M-1 Drilling Fluids Co., a Houston-based joint venture owned 64% by Dresser and 36% by Halliburton Co. of Dallas. William A. Lakey, former president of Dresser's Swaco Geolograph division, which is now part of M-1 Drilling, succeeds Mr. Bryan as president of M-1 Drilling.
