Who's News: Glenfed Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

GLENFED Inc. (Glendale, Calif.) -- Norman M. Coulson, 57 years old, currently vice chairman, president and chief executive officer of this thrift holding company, will become chairman effective July 1, succeeding Raymond D. Edwards, 71, who is retiring. Keith P. Russell Jr., 44, currently senior executive vice president and chief operating officer, will become president, succeeding Mr. Coulson.
