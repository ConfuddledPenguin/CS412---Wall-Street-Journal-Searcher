Who's News: International Business Machines Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS MACHINES CORP. (Armonk, N.Y.) -- Arthur J. Hedge Jr., formerly vice president, real estate and construction, was named vice president, environmental affairs, for this computer company. In the new position, he will oversee IBM's environmental, energy and safety programs. His previous post will be filled by Lee A. Dayton, formerly senior managing director, management services, IBM Asia Pacific.
