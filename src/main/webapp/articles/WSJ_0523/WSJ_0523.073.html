Foster Wheeler Agreement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

CLINTON, N.J. -- Foster Wheeler Corp. signed a cooperation agreement to design pharmaceutical projects with a major Japanese engineering and construction company. The U.S. design, engineering and construction company will train and advise engineers at Tokyo-based Ishikawajima-Harima Heavy Industries.

The companies will design pharmaceutical projects for Japanese companies world-wide and non-Japanese pharmaceutical companies in Japan.
