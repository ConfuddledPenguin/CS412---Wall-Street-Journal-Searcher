Jacobs Engineering Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said its Irish subsidiary, Jacobs International Inc., was awarded a $66 million contract to increase production at the Arab Potash Co.'s fertilizer plant in Jordan. Jacobs Engineering, an international concern, said the contract aims to increase production at the Dead Sea plant by 33%. Potash is extracted from Dead Sea brine, using a system of solar evaporation pans.
