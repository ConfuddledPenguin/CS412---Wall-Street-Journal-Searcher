Treasury Set to Raise $750 Million at Sale Of Short-Term Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $750 million in new cash with the sale Tuesday of about $16.8 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.05 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing Aug. 30 and Nov. 29, 1990, respectively. They will be available in minimum denominations of $10,000.

Tenders must be received by 1 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time Tuesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
