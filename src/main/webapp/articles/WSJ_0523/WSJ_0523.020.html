  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

QUESTECH Inc. (Falls Church, Va.) -- Bruce Sundlun, a director of this professional-services firm since 1971 and a former chairman of Outlet Communications Inc., was elected chairman, succeeding John L. McLucas, who plans to pursue other interests in space and aviation. Mr. Sundlun has been in private investment banking since he retired from Outlet Communications in 1988.
