Business Brief -- Agnico Eagle Mines Ltd.: Shareholder Plans to Form Group to Seek a Takeover

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

A shareholder of Agnico Eagle Mines Ltd., Toronto, said he plans to establish a group of holders to pursue a takeover bid for the widely held gold mining company. The shareholder, Jacques Forget, who owns about 3% of Agnico's shares, said he will seek the change in control as a result of what he called "numerous errors in management." Mr. Forget said he also will attempt to delay or prevent Agnico's proposed offering of 40 million Canadian dollars (US$34 million) of shares and warrants. Agnico Eagle officials weren't available to comment.

On the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday, Agnico Eagle shares gained 87.5 Canadian cents to close at C$9.625, giving the company a current indicated market value of about C$264.1 million.
