GTI to Buy Valor Electronics

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

SAN DIEGO -- GTI Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire about 90% of the stock outstanding of closely held Valor Electronics Co. for about $18.3 million in cash, stock and debt. GTI, which makes and distributes electronic circuit boards for the auto and computer industries, is 52.9% owned by Telemetrix PLC, a London electronics concern. Valor, based in San Diego, makes electronic parts based on magnetics technology.

Completion of the acquisition is subject to execution of a definitive agreement, approval of the transaction by directors and shareholders of both companies and regulatory approvals.
