Who's News: Golden Valley Microwave Foods Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

GOLDEN VALLEY MICROWAVE FOODS Inc. (Minneapolis) -- Glen E. Morin was named president and chief operating officer, filling a post that has remained vacant since March, when former President Robert A. Sander died of a heart attack. For the past two years Mr. Morin, 63 years old, served as chairman of the food company's audit committee. Prior to assuming that position in 1988 he was Golden Valley's executive vice president, finance.
