Business Brief -- Mercom Inc.: C-TEC Increases Its Stake To 19.9% of Common Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

C-TEC Corp. said it raised its stake in Mercom Inc. to 19.9% from 11.68% of the common stock outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, C-TEC said it holds 475,665 shares, including 152,920 shares bought from May 3 to May 8 for $10.46 to $11.29 each.

A spokesman for C-TEC, Wilkes-Barre, Pa., declined to comment on whether discussions have begun with Bloomfield Hills, Mich.-based Mercom management and whether C-TEC plans to increase its stake. Mercom declined to comment.

In its previous filing, C-TEC said it planned to seek discussions with Mercom's management regarding its business and policies.
