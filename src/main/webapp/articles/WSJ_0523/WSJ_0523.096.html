Business Brief -- Pacific Telesis Group: Phone Firm, Masada Units Get British Cable Franchises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

Units of Pacific Telesis Group, San Francisco, and Masada Corp., Birmingham, Ala., said they were awarded British cable television franchises for Manchester, Salford and Trafford. The boroughs contain 363,000 homes. The companies said that the Manchester system is expected to be in operation in about a year, and that service in parts of the other franchise territories will begin by the end of 1991.

Pacific Telesis, a regional telephone company, is an investor in 12 British cable franchises covering more than 1.5 million homes. Masada has joined Pacific Telesis on six British franchises.
