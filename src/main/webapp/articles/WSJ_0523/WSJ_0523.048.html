Thai Cement Plant Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

TOKYO -- Sumitomo Corp., a major Japanese trading house, and Ube Industries Ltd., a maker of chemicals, cement and industrial machinery, said they received an order for a cement-making plant from Thailand. They said the plant ordered by Siam Cement Co. of Bangkok is capable of making three million tons of cement a year.

The plant for Siam Cement, valued at 25 billion yen ($163 million), will be installed in Khao Wong north of Bangkok by March 1993.
