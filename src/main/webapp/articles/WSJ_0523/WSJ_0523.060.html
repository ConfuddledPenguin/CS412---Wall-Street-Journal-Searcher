Huffington Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

HOUSTON -- Roy M. Huffington Inc. said it completed the sale of its Huffington Corp. unit to Chinese Petroleum Corp. of Taiwan. Terms weren't disclosed. Chinese Petroleum is the national oil company of Taiwan.

Closely held Huffington has assets estimated at between $700 million and $900 million, including a 20% interest in an Indonesian joint venture responsible for supplying natural gas to one of the world's largest liquefied natural gas facilities.
