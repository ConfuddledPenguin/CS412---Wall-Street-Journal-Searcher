International Brief -- Rhone-Poulenc S.A.: Quarterly Profit Fell 9.6%; Exchange Fluctuations Cited

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

Rhone-Poulenc S.A. said foreign exchange fluctuations contributed to a 9.6% drop in first-quarter net profit after payments to minority interests, to 1.37 billion francs ($245 million) from 1.52 billion francs a year earlier. The French pharmaceutical and chemical company said the decline of the dollar and yen against European currencies was particularly debilitating for its overseas income when translated into francs.

Consolidated revenue grew 7.4% to 19.93 billion francs, but the state-controlled concern noted that first-quarter revenue was down 2.9% when recent acquisitions were excluded from the balance sheet.
