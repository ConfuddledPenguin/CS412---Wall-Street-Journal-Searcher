Security Pacific Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

LOS ANGELES -- Security Pacific Corp. said it definitively agreed to acquire the 11-branch Santa Clarita National Bank, Valencia, Calif., for a previously reported $52.5 million in stock. Santa Clarita had assets of $258.8 million at March 31; Security Pacific, one of the largest U.S. bank holding companies, has assets of about $86 billion.
