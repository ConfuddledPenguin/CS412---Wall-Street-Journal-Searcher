Who's News: Cabot Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

CABOT Corp. (Waltham, Mass.) -- Robert P. Henderson, chairman of Greylock Management Corp., a venture capital company in Boston, was named a director of this specialty chemicals and energy concern, expanding the board to 16 members. The company also named Charles A. Gray, vice president of technology and general manager of its carbon black technology division, both new positions. Previously, Mr. Gray was director of technology and chief technologist, chemical products group of FMC Corp., a Chicago machinery and chemicals concern.
