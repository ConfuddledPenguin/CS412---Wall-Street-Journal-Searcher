Who's News: Bank of New England Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

BANK OF NEW ENGLAND Corp. (Boston) -- Larry W. Reed, 45 years old, a financial consultant and formerly senior vice president at Columbia Savings & Loan Association, Irvine, Calif., was named president and chief executive officer of BancNewEngland Mortgage Co., a Rhode Island-based subsidiary of the company's affiliate bank unit, BNE-Old Colony. He succeeds Joseph Whiteside, 48, who the company said "wants to seek new challenges and opportunities," possibly within Bank of New England.
