Carbide Unit Buys Company

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

DANBURY, Conn. -- Union Carbide Corp.'s Union Carbide Chemicals & Plastics Co. unit said it acquired Vitaphore Corp., a closely held maker of biomedical products. The price wasn't disclosed.

Union Carbide said that Vitaphore, based in Menlo Park, Calif., will operate as a subsidiary of the chemical and plastic unit, and will be headed by John Walker, Vitaphore's president.

Vitaphore's products are used to control infections in ophthalmology and to control bleeding in surgery. It was founded in 1985 and currently employs 60.
