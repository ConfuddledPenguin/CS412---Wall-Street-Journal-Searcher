Who's News: Travelers Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

TRAVELERS Corp. (Hartford, Conn.) -- Dr. David J. Ottensmeyer, formerly executive vice president and chief medical officer at Equicor Inc., Nashville, Tenn., was named senior vice president and chief medical officer, new posts at the managed-care and employee-benefits operations division of this insurance, financial-services and investment company.
