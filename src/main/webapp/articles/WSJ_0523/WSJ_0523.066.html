CAE Unit Gets Simulator Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

TORONTO -- CAE Industries Ltd. said its CAE Electronics Ltd. unit was awarded a 23.5 million Canadian dollar (US$20 million) contract by America West Airlines, of Phoenix, Ariz., to design and make flight simulators. The simulators will be for de Havilland Dash 8 and Boeing 737-300/400 aircraft. The project, which includes simulators, visual systems and facilities, is scheduled for 1992. CAE is a manufacturer of military and commercial flight simulators and aviation equipment.
