Who's News: CS First Boston Picks Toll for Post in Japan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

TOKYO -- Maynard Toll, a managing director of CS First Boston Inc. in New York, will become president and branch manager of the investment bank's Japan operation, the company said. Mr. Toll, head of divestitures and corporate sales in New York, will succeed two senior executives who recently resigned. The former chairman of the Japan operation, James M. Walsh, and the former branch manager, Keisuke Inaba, both resigned after the group appointed a new head of Pacific operations, Allen D. Wheat.

Mr. Wheat, chairman of CS First Boston Pacific Inc., said he is "thrilled" Mr. Toll will be working in Tokyo again. Mr. Toll was First Boston's Tokyo representative from 1978 to 1981.
