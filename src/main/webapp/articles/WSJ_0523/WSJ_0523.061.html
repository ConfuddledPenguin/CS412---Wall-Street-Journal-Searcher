Soviets to Repay Finnish Debt

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

HELSINKI -- Finland's Prime Minister Harri Holkeri said the Soviet Union will repay its 300 million markka ($76.6 million) debt to Finland within a few days, press reports said. Soviet Deputy Prime Minister Stepan Sitaryan told Mr. Holkeri that the debt would be handled "in the first instance," the Helsingin Sanomat newspaper reported.

Messrs. Sitaryan and Holkeri head the joint Soviet-Finnish economic commission, which met in Leningrad to discuss joint trade questions.

Mr. Sitaryan told Mr. Holkeri that Finland retained its special position in trade relations with its superpower neighbor. He didn't mention Soviet repayment of debts to other Western countries, Mr. Holkeri said.
