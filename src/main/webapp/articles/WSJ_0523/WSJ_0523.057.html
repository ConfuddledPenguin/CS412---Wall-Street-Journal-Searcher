Dividend News: Rainbow Technologies Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

Rainbow Technologies Inc., Irvine, Calif., said its board approved a 2-for-1 stock split. Shareholders will vote on the proposed split at the company's annual meeting June 28. If they approve it, holders of record will receive one new share for every share held, payable in mid-July. Rainbow, a maker of computer-security devices, currently has about 1.8 million shares outstanding. Its stock closed yesterday in national over-the-counter trading at $20.00, up $1.50 cents.
