Who's News: M/A-Com Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

M/A-COM Inc. (Burlington, Mass.) -- Raymond F. Pettit, senior vice president of Rockefeller Group, was elected a director of this electronics parts maker. Mr. Pettit succeeds John Lynch, who resigned from the board and his posts as president and chief operating officer of the company in March.
