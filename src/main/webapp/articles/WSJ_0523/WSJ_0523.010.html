Who's News: J. & W. Seligman & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

J. & W. SELIGMAN & Co. (New York) -- Arturo E. Brillembourg, 43 years old, previously president and chief executive officer of Farmington Asset Management Ltd., a Chevy Chase, Md., money management firm, was named senior vice president, global fixed-income markets, a new position at this financial services and money management concern.
