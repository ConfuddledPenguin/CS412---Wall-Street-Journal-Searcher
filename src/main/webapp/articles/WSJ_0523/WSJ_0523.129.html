Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

NEWSPAPERS: Hearst Corp. is joining with Izvestia, a Soviet newspaper, to publish an "uncensored" weekly paper. A prototype issue, to be published July 4, will be edited by Soviet and U.S. journalists. Hearst said 25,000 copies will be distributed to "key" government, business and education leaders in Moscow, Leningrad, Kiev, Washington, D.C., and other U.S. cities.
