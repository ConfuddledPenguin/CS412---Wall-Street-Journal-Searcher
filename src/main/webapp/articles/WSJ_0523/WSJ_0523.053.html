Dividend News: UGI Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

UGI Corp. shareholders approved a 2-for-1 split in the company's common stock to be distributed July 1 to holders of record May 31. The Valley Forge, Pa.-based company currently has about 10 million shares outstanding. Shareholders also authorized a doubling in UGI's authorized common shares to 40 million from 20 million. The company, which previously announced a 4.9% dividend increase effective July 1, said the new rate on the post-split shares is 29.5 cents a share.
