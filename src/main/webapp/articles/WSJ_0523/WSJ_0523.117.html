Financing Business: Great Western Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

GREAT WESTERN FINANCIAL Corp. -- The Great Western Bank unit said it completed an offering of $130 million of 10-year fixed-rate subordinated debt through an underwriting group led by Merrill Lynch Capital Markets. The unit, based in Beverly Hills, Calif., said the offering increases further its risk-adjusted capital above the regulatory minimum.
