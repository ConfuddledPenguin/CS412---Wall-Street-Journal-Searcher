Rockwell Gets Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

ANAHEIM, Calif. -- Rockwell International Corp. said it received two additional contracts totaling $100 million from the Air Force for work on the Peacekeeper missile program. The aerospace concern said $81 million for guidance and control systems includes $4.9 million in funding already received. The remaining $24 million is for engineering services.
