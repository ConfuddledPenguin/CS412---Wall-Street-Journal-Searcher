Varco International Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

ORANGE, Calif. -- Varco International Inc. said it completed the acquisition of all of the assets of the Martin-Decker unit of Cooper Industries Inc. for $30 million. Varco is a petroleum industry tools and equipment maker. Martin-Decker makes instruments and equipment for oil-related and other industrial applications. Houston-based Cooper Industries is a maker of electrical, automotive, petroleum and industrial products.
