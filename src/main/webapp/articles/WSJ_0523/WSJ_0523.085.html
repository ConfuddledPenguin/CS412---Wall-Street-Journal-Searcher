Technology: Merck Opens a Lab in Italy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

RAHWAY, N.J. -- Merck & Co., a health products company, said it opened a research laboratory in Pomezia, Italy, near Rome, to undertake research in human diseases such as cancer and viral infections. The Instituto di Ricerche di Biologia Molecolare, a joint venture for research in Italy, was formed by an Italian subsidiary of Merck and Sigma Tau Industrie Farmaceutiche Riunite S.p.A., an Italian pharmaceutical company. The $50 million facility will employ approximately 100 researchers and nonscientific personnel.
