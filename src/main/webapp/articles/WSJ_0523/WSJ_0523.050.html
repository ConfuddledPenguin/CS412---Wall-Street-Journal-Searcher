United Technologies Order

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

EAST HARTFORD, Conn. -- Pratt & Whitney said it received a $290 million aircraft engine order from Japan Air System to power Boeing 747-400 aircraft. Pratt & Whitney, a unit of United Technologies Corp., said it will supply 19 engines, including spares, for four aircraft on firm order. If optioned aircraft are acquired, another 19 engines would be ordered. Deliveries will begin in 1995.

Japan Air System is Japan's third largest airline.
