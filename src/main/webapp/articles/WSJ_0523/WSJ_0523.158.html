Amoco Gas Discovery

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

DENVER -- Amoco Corp. said it made a "significant" natural gas discovery in the Ouachita Thrust area of the Arkoma Basin in southeastern Oklahoma. The well, the Amoco Retherford No. 1A, tested at a natural flow rate of 49.3 million cubic feet of natural gas a day, Amoco said.

The company said perforations in the Pennsylvanian Spiro sandstone were between 12,142 and 12,170 feet. Amoco is the operator, with about a 42% working interest, and Exxon Corp. holds the remaining 58% working interest.
