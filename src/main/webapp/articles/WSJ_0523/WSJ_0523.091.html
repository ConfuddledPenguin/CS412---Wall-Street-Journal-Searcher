Average Interest Rate Rises At Citicorp Weekly Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.1545% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.1478% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $415 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.15% to 8.159%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate rose to 8.1801% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.1466% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $386 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.15% to 8.19%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
