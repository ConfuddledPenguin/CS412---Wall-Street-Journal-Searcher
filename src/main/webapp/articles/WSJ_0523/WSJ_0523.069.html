Imco Recycling Gets Patent

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/23/90

IRVING, Texas -- Imco Recycling Inc. said it received a patent from the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office for a process that treats spent aluminum reduction cell linings, a smelter waste product. Imco, an aluminum-can recycler, said its process lowers the raw material and energy costs associated with aluminum recycling and reduces the unsafe elements in the linings.
