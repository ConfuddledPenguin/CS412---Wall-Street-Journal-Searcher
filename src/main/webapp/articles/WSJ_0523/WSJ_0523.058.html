Delta Dental Wins Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/23/90

WASHINGTON -- Delta Dental Plan of California won a $140.2 million defense contract for dental services.

Martin Marietta Corp. was given an $89.9 million Air Force contract for a solar-power satellite.

Avondale Industries Inc. got a $24.4 million Navy contract for destroyer overhaul.

Westinghouse Electric Corp. received a $14.3 million Navy contract for nuclear propulsion parts.
