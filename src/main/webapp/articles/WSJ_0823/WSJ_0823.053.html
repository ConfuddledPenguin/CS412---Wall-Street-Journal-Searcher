Who's News: Silk Greenhouse Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

SILK GREENHOUSE Inc. (Tampa, Fla.) -- B.B. Tuley, formerly president and chief executive officer of Michaels Stores Inc., was named president and chief executive officer of this artificial-plant retailer. Mr. Tuley, 54 years old, succeeds Jere Bradwell, 38, who remains chairman.
