Dutch Industrial Output Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

AMSTERDAM -- Dutch industrial production in the first half rose 4% from a year earlier, the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics said. In June, industrial output rose 1% from May and 5% from the year-earlier month. The first-half rise was particularly strong in the metal, paper and graphics industries, but the housing and construction industries posted lower than average growth rates of 1% each.
