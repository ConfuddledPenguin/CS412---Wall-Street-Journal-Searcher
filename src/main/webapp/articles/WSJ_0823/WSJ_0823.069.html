Scott & Stringfellow's Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

RICHMOND, Va. -- Scott & Stringfellow Financial Inc. received authorization from its board to repurchase an additional 100,000 common shares, increasing the company's stock buy-back program to 400,000 shares. The shares will be bought in the open market from time to time. Currently, the brokerage firm has 1,955,792 common shares outstanding.
