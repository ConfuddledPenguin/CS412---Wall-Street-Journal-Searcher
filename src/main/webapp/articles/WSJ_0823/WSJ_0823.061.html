Business Brief -- Dofasco Inc.: Algoma Unit, in Bid to Sell Huron Group, Hires Adviser

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

Algoma Steel Corp. of Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, said it hired Lancaster Financial Inc. to find a buyer for its Huron Steel group of companies, a steel auto parts maker based in Windsor, Ontario. Algoma, a unit of Dofasco Inc. of Hamilton, Ontario, Canada's largest steelmaker, said Huron has about 325 employees and had sales last year of 55 million Canadian dollars (US$48.3 million).

Algoma, which didn't disclose an asking price for Huron, said the planned sale is consistent with the company's strategy of focusing on its core steelmaking business. It said proceeds would be used for general corporate purposes and for reducing debt.
