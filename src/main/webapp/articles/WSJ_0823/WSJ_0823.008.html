Who's News: Image Bank Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

IMAGE BANK Inc. (New York) -- John J. Quinn was named to the board of this communications company, bringing membership to nine. Mr. Quinn, 69 years old, and currently involved in private business ventures, succeeds William A. Lupien, 48, who resigned because of other commitments. Currently, Mr. Lupien is chairman and chief executive officer of Mitchum, Jones & Templeton, a private investment firm, based in Los Angeles.
