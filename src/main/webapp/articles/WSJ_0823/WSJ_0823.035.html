Who's News: Rogers Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

ROGERS Corp. (Rogers, Conn.) -- Harry H. Birkenruth, senior vice president, polymer products group, was named to the new position of executive vice president of this maker of components and materials for the electronics and automotive markets. In his new post, Mr. Birkenruth, 59 years old, will assume responsibility for the company's electronic materials group in Chandler, Ariz., in addition to his current responsibilities with the polymer products group.
