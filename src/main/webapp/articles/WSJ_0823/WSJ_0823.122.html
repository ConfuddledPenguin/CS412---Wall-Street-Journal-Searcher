Business Brief -- Worthen Banking Corp.: Plan Is Reached to Acquire TC Bankshares and a Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

Worthen Banking Corp. said it signed a non-binding letter of intent to acquire all preferred and common stock outstanding of TC Bankshares Inc. and all preferred and about 98% of the common stock of Twin City Bank, a TC Bankshares unit. The bank holding company said completion of the transaction is subject to the parties reaching a definitive agreement, approval by the Federal Reserve Board and Worthen shareholders. Worthen also must obtain the necessary financing. Worthen said it is considering a capital offering in connection with the proposed transaction.

If all necessary approvals are met, Worthen, based in Little Rock, Ark., said it expects closing on or before Dec. 31. Worthen said the acquisition price would depend upon the earnings of Twin City and TC Bankshares, both Arkansas-based banking concerns, but will not exceed $75 million if closing occurs by Dec. 31. If the closing occurs after that date, the purchase price could exceed $75 million, depending on the two firms' earnings, Worthen said.
