Who's News: PNC Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

PNC FINANCIAL Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Bruce E. Robbins, 46 years old, formerly vice chairman of this bank holding company's Central Trust unit, was named chairman and chief executive officer of Central Trust, Cincinnati, succeeding Gary N. Kocher, who resigned to pursue other business opportunities. J. Clay Stinnett, formerly senior executive vice president of Central Trust, was named the unit's president and chief operating officer.
