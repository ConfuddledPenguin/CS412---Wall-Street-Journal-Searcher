  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

WHO'S NEWS: Live Entertainment Inc. named Theodore E. Bean executive vice president and chief financial officer. He is currently executive vice president and chief financial officer of K mart Corp.'s Pace Membership Warehouse Inc. He will succeed Roger R. Smith, who becomes executive vice president of Carolco Pictures Inc., which holds a 48% stake in Live.
