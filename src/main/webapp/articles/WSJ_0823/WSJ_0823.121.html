International Brief -- Veba AG: Group Net Income for Half Fell 1.5% to $250.2 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

Veba AG of West Germany, a diversified energy and trading company, reported that first-half group net income slipped 1.5% to 391 million marks ($250.2 million) from 397 million marks a year earlier. The company said the results were dragged down by the petroleum and chemical sectors, where earnings declined from "exceptionally good" year-earlier levels. But Veba said its power generation, trading, transport and services businesses had "stable profit levels" in the latest period. Pretax earnings fell 5.2% to 981 million marks from 1.04 billion marks. After taxes, profit eased 2.3% to 433 million marks from 443 million marks.

World-wide group sales rose 6.7% to 26.08 billion marks from 24.43 billion marks.
