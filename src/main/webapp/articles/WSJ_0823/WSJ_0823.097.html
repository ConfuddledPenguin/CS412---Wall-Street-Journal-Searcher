Financing Business: PWA Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

PWA Corp.'s Canadian Airlines International unit said it obtained a US$300 million credit facility to help finance the purchase of Boeing Co. 747-400 and 767-300ER aircraft that are to be delivered in 1991 and 1992. The Calgary, Alberta, company said the credit facility was provided by a syndicate of domestic and international banks led by Citibank Canada and Mitsui Taiyo Kobe Bank (London).
