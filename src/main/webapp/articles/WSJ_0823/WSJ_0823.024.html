Who's News: Harsco Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

HARSCO Corp. (Harrisburg, Pa.) -- Nick H. Prater, 61 years old, was elected a director of this defense and industrial products company. Mr. Prater is president and chief executive officer of Mobay Corp., a division of Bayer U.S.A. Inc. He succeeds Robert K. Campbell, former chairman and chief executive officer of Pennsylvania Power & Light Co., who died in June. Harsco's board membership will remain at 10.
