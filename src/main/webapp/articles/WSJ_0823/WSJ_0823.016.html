Bethlehem Steel Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

BETHLEHEM, Pa. -- Bethlehem Steel Corp. said it created a joint venture with Paris-based Chavanne-Ketin, a subsidiary of Usinor-Sacilor, the French steel company. Terms weren't disclosed. The venture, named Centec, will produce and sell cast iron rolls to North American metalworking customers. Bethlehem is the nation's second largest steel concern.

A company source said it expects the venture will produce annual revenue of $25 million.
