Amoco Chemical Co. Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

CHICAGO -- Amoco Corp. said its Amoco Chemical Co. unit retained Goldman, Sachs & Co. to assist it in the sale of the assets of its petroleum additives operation. The value of the assets wasn't disclosed. The company previously said it intended to sell the Clayton, Mo.-based operation, which has manufacturing facilities in Illinois and Mississippi and employs more than 570 people in the U.S. and abroad.
