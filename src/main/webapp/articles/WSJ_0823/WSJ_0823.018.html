Who's News: Xsirius Superconductivity Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

XSIRIUS SUPERCONDUCTIVITY Inc. (Scottsdale, Ariz.) -- Alan E. "Lanny" Ross, vice president and general manager of Rockwell International Corp., was elected a director, increasing the board to seven. The company is engaged in the commercialization of high temperature superconductors.
