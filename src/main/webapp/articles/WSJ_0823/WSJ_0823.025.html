TOYS "R" US Inc. (Paramus, N.J.) -- Larry Bouts, previously chief financial officer of the international snack food division of the PepsiCo Foods International unit of PepsiCo Inc., was named president of the Toys "R" Us International unit of this toy retailer, effective Sept. 2, succeeding Joseph Baczko, who resigned to pursue other personal and business interests, but will continue in an advisory capacity until year end.
