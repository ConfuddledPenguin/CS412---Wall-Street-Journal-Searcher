Goal Systems to Post Net Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

COLUMBUS, Ohio -- Goal Systems International Inc., a software products concern, said that for its second quarter ended July 31, it expects to post increases of "at least 30%" in net income and revenue. The company had restated year-earlier profit of $2 million, or 11 cents a share, on revenue of $15.3 million.
