Who's News: Hecla Mining Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

HECLA MINING Co. (Couer D'Alene, Idaho) -- Joseph Coors Jr., president and cheif executive officer of the Coors Ceramics Co. unit of Adolph Coors Co., was named a director of this mining concern, succeeding Norman Visnes, who resigned for health reasons.
