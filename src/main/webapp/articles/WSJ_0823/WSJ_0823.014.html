West German Auto Output

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

FRANKFURT -- West Germany's auto production fell 1% in July from a year earlier because of fewer workdays, according to the West German Automobile Association. But adjusted for the calendar, production actually climbed 6% in July from the previous year, the association added.

A total of 295,400 cars were produced in July compared with 297,165 the previous year. But there were only 15 working days in July this year, down from 16 in 1989. Most West German auto factories close for a number of weeks in the summer for vacations and plant maintenance.
