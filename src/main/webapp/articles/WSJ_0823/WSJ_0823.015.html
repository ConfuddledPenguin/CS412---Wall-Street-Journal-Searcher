Reuters Chief Sells Stake

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

LONDON -- Reuters Holdings PLC said Glen Renfrew, managing director and chief executive officer, sold 210,000 of the company's shares, totaling 20% of his personal holding, for #1.72 million ($3.29 million). The disposal represents a 0.05% stake in the international news agency and leaves Mr. Renfrew with 849,378 Reuters shares in his name.

A brief statement said Mr. Renfrew sold the stake in the market at 820 pence ($15.67) a share to finance loan payments and settle tax liabilities. Reuters declined to elaborate on the sale.
