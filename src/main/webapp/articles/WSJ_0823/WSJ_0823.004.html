Maynard Oil to Buy Interests

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

DALLAS -- Maynard Oil Co. said it agreed to pay $13.7 million for Sun Operating Limited Partnership's interest in five leases covering 860 surface acres of the Levelland Field in Hockley County, Texas. Maynard estimated the acquisition would add 525 net barrels to its daily production. Sun Operating is managed by Oryx Energy Co. of Dallas, which, as managing general partner, has a 98% ownership of the limited partnership.
