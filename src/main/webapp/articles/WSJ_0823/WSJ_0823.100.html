Contel Wins Navy Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

WASHINGTON -- Contel Corp. was given a $30 million Navy contract for telecommunications services.

The Defense Logistics Agency awarded jet-fuel contracts to Amoco Oil Co. for $20.4 million; B.F. Oil Co. for $17.3 million; and Laketon Refining Corp. for $16.4 million.

Crane Defense Systems, a subsidiary of Crane Co., was awarded a $17.2 million Navy contract for ship winch assemblies.

Rockwell International Corp. got a $10 million Air Force contract for B-1B aircraft computers.
