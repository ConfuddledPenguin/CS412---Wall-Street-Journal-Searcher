Business Brief -- Mission-Valley Bancorp: Merger Agreement Is Signed With Alameda, Calif., Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

Mission-Valley Bancorp, Pleasanton, Calif., said it and Northern California Community Bancorp. signed a letter of intent to merge. Terms call for shareholders of each bank to swap their holdings for new shares of the combined entity, which would be called California Bancshares Inc. As of now, Mission-Valley holders would receive one new share of California Bancshares for each share held; Northern California Community holders would receive 1.23 California Bancshares shares for each share held.

Northern California Community is based in Alameda, Calif.

The new bank would have about $775 million in assets. It would be made up of nine community banks in mostly smaller communities near San Francisco and San Jose.
