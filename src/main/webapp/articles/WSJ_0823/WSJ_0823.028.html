Who's News: K mart Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

K MART Corp. (Troy, Mich.) -- Lilyan H. Affinito, vice chairman of Maxxam Group Inc., and Joseph A. Califano Jr., a senior partner at the law firm of Dewey Ballantine and a former secretary of Health, Education and Welfare, were named directors of this retailing concern, increasing board membership to 14.
