International Brief -- BICC PLC: First-Half Pretax Earnings Rose 7.5% to $191.2 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

BICC PLC of Britain reported a 7.5% rise in first-half pretax profit to #100 million ($191.2 million) -- the top of analysts' estimates -- from #93 million a year earlier, as strong growth in the company's core cable business offset sluggishness at its home building unit. Profit after taxes and minority interests, or profit attributable to shareholders, increased 16% to #59 million from #51 million. Per-share earnings rose 1.9% to 21.8 pence from 21.4 pence. The year-earlier results included an extraordinary loss of #1 million; the latest period didn't have any special items.

Sales increased 9.7% to #1.94 billion from #1.77 billion.
