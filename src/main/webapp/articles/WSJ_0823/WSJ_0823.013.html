Acquisition of Philips Industries

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

DAYTON, Ohio -- Philips Industries Inc. said shareholders approved the previously announced acquisition of the company by Tomkins PLC. In June, London-based Tomkins, which makes lawnmowers, bicycles, handguns and other metal products, agreed to acquire Philips for $550 million, or $18.50 a share. Philips makes windows, bathtubs, air-moving equipment and other residential, commercial and industrial items. Philips said the acquisition was to be completed yesterday. Philips expects payment for its surrendered shares to begin Sept. 6.
