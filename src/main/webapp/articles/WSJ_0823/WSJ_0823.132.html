Forest Oil Finds Natural Gas

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

DENVER -- Forest Oil Corp. said it discovered natural gas offshore Louisiana on West Delta Block 20. The well flowed at a rate of 7.4 million cubic feet of gas per day through a 17/64 inch choke at a depth of 16,849 feet.

Forest Oil, with a 50% interest, drilled the well, but Wainoco Oil & Gas Co., with a 33.33% interest will operate the well. Newfield Exploration Co. holds a 16.67% interest.
