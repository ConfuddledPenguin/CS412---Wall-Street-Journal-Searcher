Integrated Waste Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Integrated Waste Services Inc. said it acquired Beco Consolidated Services Inc., a Niagara Falls, N.Y., industrial cleaning outfit. Terms weren't disclosed. The non-hazardous waste management company announced earlier this week its acquisition of Wizard Method Inc. and Southwestern Environmental Inc. Integrated expects revenue of $6 million for its first full year, ending Dec. 31.
