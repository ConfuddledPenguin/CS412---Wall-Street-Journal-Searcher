Who's News: General Motors Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

GENERAL MOTORS Corp. (Detroit) -- Robert J. Dankanyin, vice president for diversification with Hughes Aircraft Co., was elected a senior vice president of the El Segundo, Calif., unit. He remains head of the company's diversification program and continues as a member of the Hughes policy board. He also was named to the executive management committee. David R. Barclay, staff vice president for human resources development with Hughes, was elected to the new post of vice president for work force diversity. The Hughes unit is an electronics firm specializing in high-technology products for military, scientific and commercial use.
