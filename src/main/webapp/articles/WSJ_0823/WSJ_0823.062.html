Canada Wheat Stocks At July 31 Rose 30%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian wheat stocks totaled 6,517,000 metric tons at July 31, up 30% from 5,032,000 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton of wheat is equal to about 36.74 bushels. The stocks included 700,000 metric tons on farms, down from 787,000 tons a year earlier, and 5,817,000 tons at terminals and other commercial locations, up from 4,245,000 tons a year earlier.

Statistics Canada said stocks of wheat held on farms were down from a year earlier, because of an increase in wheat exports.

Stocks of barley totaled 2,049,000 metric tons, down from 2,800,000 tons a year earlier, and canola, or rapeseed, stocks totaled 739,000 tons, down from the year-earlier 1,149,000 tons.
