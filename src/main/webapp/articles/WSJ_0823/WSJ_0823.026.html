Contracts on Soviet Ruble Planned by Chicago Merc

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

CHICAGO -- The Chicago Mercantile Exchange said it intends at some point to trade futures and futures options contracts on the Soviet ruble. The futures exchange said such contracts couldn't begin trading until the Soviet Union's currency was freely convertible on world currency markets. The Merc didn't say when it believes convertibility would be achieved and a sufficient cash market developed to support futures trading.

The new futures contracts also would require approval by the Commodity Futures Trading Commission.
