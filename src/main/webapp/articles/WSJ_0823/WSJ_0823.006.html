Fuji Heavy Industries Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

TOKYO -- Japan's Fuji Heavy Industries Ltd., the maker of Subaru automobiles, said it received approval from the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission to seek a 100% stake in its U.S. sales subsidiary, Subaru of America. A special shareholders meeting will be held Aug. 27 to consider terms for the buy-out, a spokesman said. He added that no final decision has been made on what price Fuji Heavy Industries will offer for the shares outstanding.

Fuji Heavy Industries, which currently owns 49.6% of Subaru of America, is seeking to raise its share to 100% in an effort to reorganize and upgrade the company's sales effort in the U.S.
