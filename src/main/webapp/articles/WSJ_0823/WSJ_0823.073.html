Business Brief -- Overseas Shipholding Group Inc.: One Million Shares, 2.9% Of Total, Are Bought Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

Overseas Shipholding Group Inc., a New York-based operator of shipping fleets, said it bought one million of its common shares outstanding, or an indicated 2.9% of the total. Terms and sellers weren't disclosed and the company said it couldn't comment on whether the privately negotiated purchase was made in a single block or several smaller transactions. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Overseas shares closed at $15.875, down 37.5 cents.

The company had 34.9 million shares outstanding before it said on Monday that it would buy back the one million shares in the open market. It completed the private purchase Tuesday. Though Overseas was intent on purchasing the shares in the open market, "other opportunities came up," which necessitated the privately negotiated approach, a spokesman said.
