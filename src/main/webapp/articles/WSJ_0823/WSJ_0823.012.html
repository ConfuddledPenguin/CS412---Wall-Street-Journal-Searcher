CAE Industries Unit's Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/23/90

TORONTO -- CAE Industries Ltd. said its CAE Electronics Ltd. subsidiary received a contract valued at about 31 million Canadian dollars (US$27.2 million) to supply flight simulators for Boeing 737-300 and 757-200 aircraft to China's Civil Aviation Administration. The simulators will be delivered in the summer of 1992. CAE Industries manufactures aircraft simulator systems and other electronic equipment.
