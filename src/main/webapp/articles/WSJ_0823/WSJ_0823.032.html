Rough Riders

A firm hand with scrappy vacationing kids

Is a policy I always endorse;

So our annual family trip, too often,

Ends up as a Tour de Force.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

Character Flaw

There is one thing I say

That is really a sham:

"I don't mean to be critical" --

But I usually am.

-- Joshua Adams.
