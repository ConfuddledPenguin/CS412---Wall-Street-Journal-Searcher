IPCO Vote Set on Renaming

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/23/90

HACKENSACK, N.J. -- IPCO Corp. said shareholders are to vote in November on a board-approved proposal to change the company's name to Sterling Optical Corp., reflecting the completion of a divestiture program that stripped IPCO of all but its primary business, a chain of optical stores. IPCO's last non-optical business, Whaledent International, a dental products unit, was sold June 1 to Gurit-Heberlein AG, a Swiss chemical and textile finishing company, for $32 million.
