Business Brief -- PacifiCorp: Purchase of Phone Concern In Wisconsin Is Completed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

PacifiCorp, Portland, Ore., said it completed the previously announced acquisition of North-West Telecommunications Inc., a La Crosse, Wis.-based telephone company, for stock and cash. PacifiCorp is a diversified electric and telecommunications utility.

As a result of the acquisition, NorthWest shareholders will receive 1.97 shares of PacifiCorp common and $4.41 in cash for each share of North-West common stock and Class B common stock held. The PacifiCorp shares used in the merger were purchased on the open market. Based on the closing price of $20.15 a share for PacifiCorp common stock, determined during the July 5 through July 18 pricing period, the cost of the acquisition will be about $272 million, PacifiCorp said.

North-West Telecommunications will become a unit of Pacific Telcom Inc., a PacifiCorp company.
