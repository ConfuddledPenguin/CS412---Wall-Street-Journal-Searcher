Business Brief -- T.H.E. Corp.: Tons of Toys to Be Bought By Closely Held Retailer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

Tons of Toys Inc., Wareham, Mass., said the U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Boston approved its reorganization plan under which it will be acquired by T.H.E. Corp., a closely held Los Angeles toy retailer. Under the plan, a venture capital investor and bank that held convertible subordinated debt of Tons of Toys will exchange this debt for shares of convertible preferred stock of Tons of Toys. The plan also requires a reverse stock split in which public holders of Tons of Toys common stock will convert every 34.85192 shares of common stock for one share of common stock. T.H.E. shareholders will hold about 65% of the combined companies. Unsecured creditors are expected to receive about $2.8 million, or 20 cents on the dollar, of their claims.

Bank Niaga, Indonesia, which is T.H.E. Corp.'s principal lender, will increase its credit line to provide credit to Tons of Toys to finance inventory and working capital requirements, Tons of Toys said.

Bruno R. Ferretti, president, said that the completion of the bankruptcy proceedings will allow Tons of Toys to resume normal operations in its stores. Tons of Toys operates 20 toy supermarkets in the Northeast. T.H.E. operates 32 stores in California, Nevada and Washington.
