National Heritage Gets Notices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

DALLAS -- National Heritage Inc. said it received from LPIMC Inc. notices to terminate contracts under which it manages 29 nursing homes and retirement centers owned by LPIMC-controlled limited partnerships, and notices of default on leases covering 14 LPIMC properties. The Dallas-based manager of long care facilities said it is contesting the notices and is negotiating with LPIMC to resolve the dispute. The contracts and leases continue in force, National Heritage said.

LPIMC, based in Southbury, Conn., won control of 33 private syndicates after mounting a proxy contest to remove Southmark Corp. affiliates as general partner. Southmark owns 22% of National Heritage stock.
