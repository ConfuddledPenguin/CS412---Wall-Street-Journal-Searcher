Business Brief -- General Electric Co.: Bloomington, Ill., Operation Plans to Eliminate 60 Jobs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

General Electric Co., Fairfield, Conn., said its Electrical Distribution & Control unit would eliminate 60 jobs next year at its Bloomington, Ill., plant. The unit will move distribution activities now located in Bloomington to a national distribution center in Knoxville, Tenn. About 20 of the affected positions are expected to be reduced through attrition and early retirement, the company said. The remainder of the workers will be offered a variety of severance benefits.

The company also said it would spend $2 million over the next two years to boost manufacturing capability in the Bloomington plant. About 450 people now work there, making parts for products that regulate the flow of electricity.
