Letters to the Editor: Beyond Tail Fins

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

While I'm sure it matters not a whit to anyone other than his family or those who knew him well, your reference to the great automotive designer Harley Earl, in your story on Giorgetto Giugiaro, misspelled his name (page one, July 12). Also, his contributions to the legacy of automotive design went far beyond tail fins and two-tone paint to include curved windshields, power windows, the Corvette and a number of other innovations too numerous to list. Harley J. Earl II

Phoenix, Ariz.
