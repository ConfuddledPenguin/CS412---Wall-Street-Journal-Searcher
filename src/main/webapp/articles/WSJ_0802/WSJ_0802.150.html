Business Brief -- BSN Corp.: Adviser Is Soliciting Offers For All or Parts of Assets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

BSN Corp. said that PaineWebber Inc. is soliciting offers to buy all or parts of the assets of the Dallas sporting goods manufacturer. BSN expects the offers to exceed the company's book value of $9.20 a share. BSN's board already has rejected unsolicited offers that it considered inadequate. Houston-based Pelham Investment Associates Limited Partnership has acquired 7.3% of the company's shares and has sought to buy a controlling interest.

The BSN board commissioned an appraisal of the company in response to the purchase inquiries. "The study showed us that the value of the company is in excess of book value," said Michael Blumenfeld, president. In trading on the American Stock Exchange, BSN shares closed yesterday at $5.625, up 37.5 cents.

BSN also said it postponed its plans to sell its Sports Supply Group division in a public offering until it has evaluated the response to PaineWebber's solicitation. The board also expanded the company's share-repurchase program to two million shares from one million subject to asset sales.
