Who's News: Panhandle Eastern Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

PANHANDLE EASTERN Corp. (Houston) -- James B. Hipple was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this natural gas pipeline concern, succeeding Charles E. Lasseter, 57 years old, who exercised an early retirement option. Mr. Hipple, 55, formerly senior vice president, finance, for Panhandle Eastern, also succeeds Mr. Lasseter as a director of the company's Texas Eastern Products Pipeline Co. unit.
