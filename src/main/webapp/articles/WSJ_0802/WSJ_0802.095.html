Who's News: Jack Henry Chief Resigns

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

MONETT, Mo. -- Jack Henry & Associates Inc. said that Colin McAllister resigned as chief executive officer, executive vice president and a director. The company didn't explain the resignation, and officials couldn't be reached for comment. The company, which markets and installs data-processing software systems at financial institutions, said Jerry Hall, president, has assumed Mr. McAllister's duties, effective immediately.
