Business Brief -- Ranchmen's Resources Ltd.: Bralorne's Oil, Gas Assets To Be Purchased by Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

Ranchmen's Resources Ltd. said it agreed to purchase the oil and natural gas assets of Bralorne Resources Ltd. for 33 million Canadian dollars (US$28.6 million). The Calgary, Alberta, concern said the acquisition will help it achieve its goal of balancing its production between oil and natural gas. The Bralorne assets include estimated proven gas reserves of 53 billion cubic feet and 1.4 million barrels of proven oil and natural gas liquids reserves.

Ranchmen's also will acquire 50,000 acres of undeveloped land and nine gas plants. The properties are concentrated in east-central Alberta and farther north near Peace River, Alberta.

Bralorne's oil and gas division accounted for about 40% of the company's assets but only about 15% of its operating revenue, the Calgary concern said. Its other two operating divisions, the Canadian and U.S. industrial groups, provide products and services for the drilling and exploration sectors of the energy industry in North America.
