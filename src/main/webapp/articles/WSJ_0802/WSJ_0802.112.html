Michaels Stores July Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

IRVING, Texas -- Michaels Stores Inc. said its July sales rose 19.1%, to $27.8 million, from $23.4 million in the year-ago period. The specialty retail chain said that sales at stores open in July of both years increased 3.8%, to $23.8 million, from $23 million.
