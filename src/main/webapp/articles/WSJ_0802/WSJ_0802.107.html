Who's News: Society Corp. Names Vice Chairman Wert Chief Financial Officer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

CLEVELAND -- Society Corp. said James W. Wert, a vice chairman of this bank holding company, was named its chief financial officer. Mr. Wert, 43 years old, succeeds Robert M. Patrick, 56, senior vice president, who now is responsible for strategic financial analysis.

As vice chairman, Mr. Wert is responsible for Society's asset and liability management, investment banking, strategic financial planning and analysis, and commercial lending activities. As chief financial officer, he will also oversee accounting, treasury and financial systems functions.

As of June 30 Society had assets of $15.6 billion.
