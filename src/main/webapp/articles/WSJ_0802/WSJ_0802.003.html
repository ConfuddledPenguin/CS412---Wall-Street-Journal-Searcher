Who's News: USX Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

USX Corp. (Pittsburgh) -- Marathon Oil U.K., London, named John V. Parziale, 50 years old, president of the U.K. arm of Marathon Oil Co. Mr. Parziale succeeds William Madison, 47, who was named vice president of Marathon Oil Co., responsible for supply and transportation. Mr. Parziale previously held this position. Marathon Oil Co. is a unit of USX.
