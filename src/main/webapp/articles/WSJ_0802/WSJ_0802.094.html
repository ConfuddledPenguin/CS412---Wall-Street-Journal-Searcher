WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

American Adjustable Rate Term Trust Inc., initial offering of six million common shares, via Piper, Jaffray & Hopwood Inc.

Chemical Banking Corp., shelf offering of $750 million amount of debt securities and warrants.

Dillard Department Stores Inc., offering of four million shares of Class A common stock, one million to be sold by the company, three million to be sold by a subsidiary of Vendex International N.V., via Morgan Stanley & Co. in the United States and Morgan Stanley International.

Electronic Clearing House Inc., offering of 3.6 million common shares, via J.W. Gant & Associates Inc.

Kentucky Railworks Inc., initial offering of 3.5 million common shares, via Kidder, Peabody & Co.
