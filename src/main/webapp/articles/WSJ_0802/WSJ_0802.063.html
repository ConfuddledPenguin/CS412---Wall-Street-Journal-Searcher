Split Personality

To study an issue

Like a true sage,

I turn at once

To the Op-Ed page.

The opposing letters

Really light a fuse,

And it's not long before

I agree with all views.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

---

Unnatural Phenomenon

Our chopped-down forest

We now call

The North Woods Central

Shopping Mall,

Where if you sniff

('Mid traffic's din),

We're having the scent

Of pine piped in.

-- Robert Gordon.
