REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: The Streep Formula

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

Actress Meryl Streep, last seen on TV doing toxicological analysis to drive the apple-preserving chemical Alar from the market, is now analyzing her own profession. Citing a study that says men had twice as many roles as women in 1989's movies, Ms. Streep says that if this trend persists, "by the year 2000, we will have 13% of roles . . . and in 20 years we will be eliminated from the movies."
