Boeing Gets Order for Two Jets

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

SEATTLE -- Boeing Co. said it received a $117 million order for two 767-200 twin-engine jets from Mozambique's national airline, Linhas Aereas de Mozambique. The airline also obtained an option for one more of the extended-range jets. Boeing said each plane will be configured to carry 192 passengers non-stop between Paris and Maputo, Mozambique. It said the planes will be powered by PW4000 engines built by Pratt & Whitney, a unit of United Technologies Corp., Hartford, Conn.
