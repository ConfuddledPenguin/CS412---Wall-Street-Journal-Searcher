Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

NEW YORK -- A membership on the Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange was sold yesterday for $87,000, down from $92,000 in the previous sale on June 20 and from $125,000 on Aug. 1, 1989. Seats are currently quoted at $85,000, bid, and $90,000, offered, according to an exchange spokeswoman.
