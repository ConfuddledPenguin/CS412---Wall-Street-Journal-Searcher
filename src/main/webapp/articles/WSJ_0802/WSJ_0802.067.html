Pikeville National Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

PIKEVILLE, Ky. -- Pikeville National Corp. said it completed the previously announced acquisitions of Farmers Deposit Bank, Flemingsburg, Ky., and First American Bank, Ashland, Ky. Pikeville National said the bank holding company paid $19.3 million for First American Bank. Terms of the other acquisition weren't disclosed.

Pikeville National now has assets of $968.7 million. en
