Who's News: Macdermid Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

MACDERMID Inc. (Waterbury, Conn.) -- Daniel H. Leever was named president and chief operating officer, and Arthur J. LoVetere was named vice chairman. The company, which makes and markets specialty chemicals, said Mr. Leever most recently was senior vice president and chief operating officer. Mr. LoVetere, who expects to be ordained a permanent deacon in the Roman Catholic Church next year, intends to retire in 1995 after 35 years of service, MacDermid said.
