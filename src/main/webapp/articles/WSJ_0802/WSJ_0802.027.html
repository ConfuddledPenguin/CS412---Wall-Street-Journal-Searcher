First City Bancorp Forms Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

HOUSTON -- First City Bancorp of Texas said it formed a merchant bank unit to pursue management buy-outs, recapitalizations, expansion opportunities and divestitures for medium-sized companies based in the Southwest. The bank holding company said the merchant bank has already secured two agreements to place about $150 million of debt on the market, and added that "current business levels" will ensure the unit's profitability in its first year.
