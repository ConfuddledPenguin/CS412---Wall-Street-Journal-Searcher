Who's News: Cadmus Communications Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

CADMUS COMMUNICATIONS Corp. (Richmond, Va.) -- C. Stephenson Gillispie Jr. was named president and chief operating officer of this graphics communications holding company. Mr. Gillispie, 48 years old, will continue as president and chief executive officer of William Byrd Press, the largest Cadmus unit. He joined the press in 1978 and worked in various management positions before he became president and chief executive in 1987.
