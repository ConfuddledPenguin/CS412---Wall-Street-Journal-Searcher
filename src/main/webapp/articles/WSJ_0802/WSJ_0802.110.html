Who's News: Union Carbide Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

UNION CARBIDE Corp. (Danbury, Conn.) -- Edgar G. Hotard, 46 years old, was elected president of Union Carbide Industrial Gases Inc., a unit, and a vice president of this chemicals and plastics concern. He succeeds John R. MacLean, 59, who remains a Union Carbide vice president until his retirement later this year.
