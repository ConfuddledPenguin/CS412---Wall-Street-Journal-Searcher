Clorox's $465 Million Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

OAKLAND, Calif. -- Clorox Co. said it completed the previously reported purchase of American Cyanamid Co.'s household products business for $465 million. The purchase covers Cyanamid's well-known Pine Sol cleaner and Combat insecticide labels and is expected to boost Clorox's annual revenue by about $235 million. Clorox, with about $1.5 billion in annual sales, sells a number of household grocery products in addition to its popular bleach.
