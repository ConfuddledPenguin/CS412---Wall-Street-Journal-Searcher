Business Brief -- UNUM Corp.: Buying Insurer's Interests Completes Deal in Britain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

UNUM Corp., an insurer based in Portland, Maine, said it completed the final step of a British transaction by buying certain policyholder interests of N.E.L. Permanent Health Insurances Ltd., which is called Nelphi, for about $52 million. UNUM announced on July 2 the acquisition of N.E.L.'s parent, National Employers' Life Assurance Co. of Dorking, Surrey. UNUM then said certain policyholder interests of Nelphi also would be purchased, following an independent appraisal. Completion of the final part of the acquisition brings the total purchase price of National Employers' Life, its subsidiaries, related assets and policyholder interests, to about $120 million.

Nelphi is the British market leader in permanent health insurance, which is comparable to long-term disability insurance in the U.S. UNUM is the leading provider of group long-term disability insurance in the U.S.
