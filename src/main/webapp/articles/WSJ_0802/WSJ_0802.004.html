Who's News: Schierl Quits Fort Howard

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

GREEN BAY, Wis. -- Fort Howard Corp. said Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Paul J. Schierl, who is "recovering from complications following surgery," resigned from both positions. The closely held tissue-paper concern named President Donald DeMeuse to the additional post of chief executive; the chairman's post wasn't immediately filled.

Mr. Schierl led the management group that, along with Morgan Stanley Group Inc., took the company private in 1988 for $3.9 billion. Mr. DeMeuse was also a member of the management group.

Fort Howard said Mr. Schierl will continue as a consultant to Morgan Stanley working on special projects unrelated to Fort Howard.
