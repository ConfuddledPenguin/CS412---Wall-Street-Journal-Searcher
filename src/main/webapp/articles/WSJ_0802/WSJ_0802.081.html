Who's News: Flextronics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

FLEXTRONICS Inc. (Fremont, Calif.) -- David D. Craft, 51 years old, formerly executive vice president of this supplier of contract manufacturing services to the electronics industry, was named president, succeeding William L. Morgan, who had held the post about a year and left the company, which declined to comment on his departure.
