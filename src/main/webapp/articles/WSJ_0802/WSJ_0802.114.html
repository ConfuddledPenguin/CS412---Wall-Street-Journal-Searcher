Who's News: New York Life Insurance Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

NEW YORK LIFE INSURANCE Co. (New York) -- Harry Hohn, 58 years old, took office yesterday as chairman and chief executive officer of this mutual life insurance company, as announced in January. He succeeds Donald Ross, 65, who retired as chairman, chief executive officer and president. George Bundschuh, 56, assumed responsibilities yesterday as president; he had been vice chairman of the board.
