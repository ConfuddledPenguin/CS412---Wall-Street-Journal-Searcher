Bizmart Sales Rose in Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

ARLINGTON, Texas -- Bizmart Inc. said its sales for the first quarter ended July 28 more than doubled to $48.3 million from $19.7 million a year earlier. Sales increased 22% at stores in existence for more than a year. Bizmart, a chain of 41 office product superstores, also said it expected to report a loss for the quarter because of seasonal declines in sales and the expenses related to opening nine stores.
