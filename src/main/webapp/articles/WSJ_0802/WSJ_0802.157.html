Business Brief -- Borden Inc.: Food-Service Unit to Build `Hyperplant' in Pennsylvania

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

Borden Inc., New York, said it plans to build a $25 million "hyperplant" in Chambersburg, Pa., for its food-service group to produce individual serving products and bulk-size oil-based items. Production at the plant is scheduled for the second quarter of 1991 and full operation for the next quarter. About 400 jobs will be created at the new plant.

Borden is upgrading many of its production facilities; food-service projects account for about $90 million of planned capital spending of $650 million for 1990-1991. The company makes food and nonfood consumer proucts, packaging and industrial products.
