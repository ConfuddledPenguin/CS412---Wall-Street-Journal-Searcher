RTC's List of Properties for Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

WASHINGTON -- Here is the list of 71 properties that the Resolution Trust Corp., the government agency cleaning up the thrift mess, plans to auction over a nationwide television hookup on Nov. 15. However, properties could be sold prior to the televised auction, an RTC official said. The agency said that later this month it will be able to provide complete information about each property.
