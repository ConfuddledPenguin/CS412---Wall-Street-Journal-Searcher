Fluor Corp. Subsidiary Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Fluor Corp. said its Fluor Daniel Inc. subsidiary has been awarded contracts to build a natural gas plant in Venezuela and a $40 million newsprint recycling plant in Tennessee. Fluor said it will design and build a new natural gas liquids extraction plant and pipeline for Corpoven S.A., a Venezuelan oil and natural gas company. Terms of the contract weren't disclosed. Fluor said completion of the project is scheduled for early 1991.

Separately, Fluor said it will build a paper recycling plant addition to an existing newsprint mill in Calhoun, Tenn. The plant, owned by Bowater Inc. of Darien, Conn., will recycle 380 tons of newspapers and magazines a day. It is scheduled to open next year.
