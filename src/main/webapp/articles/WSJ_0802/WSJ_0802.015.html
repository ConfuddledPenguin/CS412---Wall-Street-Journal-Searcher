Foremost Corp. Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Foremost Corp. of America said that since July 31, 1990, it has purchased 997,000 shares of its common stock at an average price of $22.25 per share, which substantially completes the common stock repurchase plan announced July 30, 1990. Under that program, Foremost planned to repurchase up to one million shares of its common stock. Since November of 1987, Foremost has purchased approximately 2,222,000 of its own shares. Foremost currently has 10,715,898 shares of common stock outstanding.

Foremost Corporation of America and its subsidiaries primarily sell insurance for manufactured homes and recreational vehicles.
