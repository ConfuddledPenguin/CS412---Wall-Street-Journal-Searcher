Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

CITICORP (New York) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it raised the ratings on Citibank N.A.'s Conduit Series 1986-1 and 1986-2 mortgage-backed securities to double-A from double-A-minus. The rating affects $77.9 million of certificates of the bank holding company's unit. S&P said the ratings were upgraded because appreciation of mortgaged properties in California, combined with excellent performance of the mortgage pool, resulted in strong credit quality for the assets. ---

PRIMERICA HOLDINGS INC. -- Standard & Poor's Corp. upgraded the New York financial-services company's senior debt to triple-B-plus from triple-B and subordinated debt to triple-B from triple-B-minus. The rating concern noted Primerica's moves to boost the earnings capacity of its units, improve the quality of its capital, and reduce leverage.
