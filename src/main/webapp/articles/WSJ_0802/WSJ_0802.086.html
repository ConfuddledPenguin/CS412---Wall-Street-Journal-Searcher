  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Liberty National Bancorp. said it expects third-quarter net income to be reduced between 14 and 17 cents a share on a fully diluted basis, as a result of its increasing loan-loss provisions. The bank holding company, which had 1989 third-quarter profit of $8 million, or 71 cents a fully diluted share, said it expects to increase its provisions for loan losses to between $6.5 million and $7.1 million from $3.9 million in the second quarter.
