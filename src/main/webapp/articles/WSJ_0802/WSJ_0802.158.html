Pantera's Corp. Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

DALLAS -- Pantera's Corp. said a U.S. bankruptcy court judge confirmed its plan of reorganization to merge the company into its Pizza Inn Inc. subsidiary and reconstitute itself primarily as a franchise-service company. The franchiser of Pizza Inn restaurants, which has been renamed Pizza Inn Inc., said a new board has been elected with the approval of creditors.
