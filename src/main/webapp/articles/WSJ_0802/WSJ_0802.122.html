Business Brief -- CMS Enhancements Inc.: Firm Expects to Post Loss For Fiscal Fourth Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

CMS Enhancements Inc. said it expects to post a net loss for its fourth quarter ended June 30, reflecting more than $4.5 million in nonrecurring charges. CMS said most of the charges are related to a planned write-down of its computer data storage inventory. In the yearago quarter, CMS had net income of $1.2 million, or 10 cents a share, on revenue of $42.8 million. The company said it expects to report that total revenue in fiscal 1990 was about 7% lower than the $200.4 million it had the year before.

The Tustin, Calif., company has been battered in the past year by tough competition from other producers of computer mass data storage systems such as hard disks. For the nine months ended March 31, net income dropped 40% to $2.2 million from $3.6 million a year earlier.
