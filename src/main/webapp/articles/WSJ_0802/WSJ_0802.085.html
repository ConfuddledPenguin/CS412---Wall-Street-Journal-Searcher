Dividend News: Uranium Resources Inc. Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

DALLAS -- Uranium Resources Inc., a uranium mining company, said it made a 1-for-4 reverse stock split, effective yesterday, reducing the number of shares outstanding to 6.6 million. The company also said that, to reflect the reverse split, it will reduce to 0.25 from one the number of common shares that can be purchased with Uranium Resources warrants.
