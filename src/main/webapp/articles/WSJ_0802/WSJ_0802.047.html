Who's News: Campeau Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

CAMPEAU Corp. (Toronto) -- George Hitchman and James Raymond were appointed directors of this real estate and retailing concern. Mr. Hitchman, a director of several Canadian corporations, fills the vacancy created by the resignation of Pierre Peladeau, and Mr. Raymond was a nominee of the National Bank of Canada, of which he is a director. Their appointments increase board membership to 11 from 10.
