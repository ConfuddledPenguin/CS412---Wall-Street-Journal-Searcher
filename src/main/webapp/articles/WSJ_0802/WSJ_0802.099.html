Letters to the Editor: I Wouldn't, Would You?

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

In your July 3 article "They Were Cleaning Up Nicely by Recycling Piles of Old Paper," Detective Glenn Woods of Kent, Wash., said, "If you could take a bank for $1,200 a day without using a gun, wouldn't you do it?" To which I answer with a resounding, "No, I would not," and I would like to believe that most people in this country would not. Jeffrey Fulton

Diamond & Fulton, CPAs

Ramsey, N.J.
