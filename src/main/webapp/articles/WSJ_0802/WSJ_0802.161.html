Business Brief -- LSI Industries Inc.: Concern Expects to Report Fiscal 4th-Quarter Net Loss

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

LSI Industries Inc., Cincinnati, citing softness in its commercial-lighting business and other factors, said it expects to report a fiscal fourth-quarter loss of about $1.3 million, or 28 cents a share. That compares with year-earlier net income of $1.4 million, or 27 cents a share.

Sales in the quarter, which ended June 30, the company said, were $20.2 million, down from $22.1 million a year earlier.

For the full fiscal year, LSI said it expects to report net income of $1.4 million, or 29 cents a share, compared with net of $4 million, or 85 cents a share, a year earlier.

Sales were $83.7 million, compared with $57.4 million last year. Final audited figures are expected by mid-August.

LSI said sales of its graphics group, which provides commercial lighting, were below prior-year levels for the June quarter principally due to graphics programs of certain petroleum customers being deferred or reduced in scope. Further, Duramark Ltd., LSI's graphics subsidiary, had significant unexpected costs relating to entering the U.K. and European architectural graphics markets, the company said.
