Wharf (Holdings) Profit Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

HONG KONG -- Wharf (Holdings) Ltd., the investment holding company in the shipping magnate Y.K. Pao's corporate empire, said its profit for the year ended March 31 grew 12% to 1.25 billion Hong Kong dollars (US$160.8 million), or 65.7 Hong Kong cents a share, from HK$1.11 billion, or 59 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue increased 11% to HK$3.12 billion from HK$2.82 billion. Directors proposed a final dividend of 33 cents a share, increasing the full-year payout 17% to 43.5 cents from 37.3 cents a year earlier.
