EECO Inc. Sells Certain Assets

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

SANTA ANA, Calif. -- EECO Inc., which sought bankruptcy-law protection in May, said it sold for about $1.4 million certain assets of its Maxi-Switch computer keyboard division to Silitek, a Taiwan-based electronics concern. George B. Dehuff, EECO president and chief executive officer, said some sale proceeds will be used to reduce EECO's $10.3 million debt to Sanwa Bank of California, with the rest going to support EECO's restructuring plan.
