Allied Capital Corp. Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

WASHINGTON -- Allied Capital Corp. proposed taking public its Allied Advisory Inc. unit, subject to approval by shareholders of the parent company. The plan calls for shareholders to receive one share of Allied Advisory for each Allied Capital share held. The parent company currently has 5.9 million shares outstanding.

Allied Advisory manages two venture capital limited partnerships and one public company, Allied Capital Corp. II. If approved, Allied Advisory would also manage Allied Capital Corp.

Allied Capital Corp. said it is preparing documents in preparation for filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
