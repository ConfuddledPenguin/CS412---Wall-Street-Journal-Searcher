Business Brief -- APL Corp.: Posner-Controlled Company Misses Payments on Debt

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

APL Corp. said it missed a total of $4.1 million in debt payments due yesterday, permitting its creditors to declare about $27.1 million of principal outstanding "immediately due and payable" in 30 days. APL, controlled by Miami financier Victor Posner, said such a declaration would also trigger the acceleration of payment obligations on $8 million of other debt. It said it is negotiating with debt holders "concerning the cure or other satisfaction" of the payments it missed yesterday.
