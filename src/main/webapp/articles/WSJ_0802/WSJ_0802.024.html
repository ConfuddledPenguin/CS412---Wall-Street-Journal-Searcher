Fibreboard Corp. Resort

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

CONCORD, Calif. -- Fibreboard Corp. said it decided against selling its Northstar-at-Tahoe resort after an 18-month search failed to turn up a buyer at a satisfactory price. The company announced plans in December 1988 to sell the resort, located near Lake Tahoe in Northern Calif., as part of a restructuring aimed at focusing itself on core wood products and insulation businesses. The company said it entered into "serious" talks with two buyers, both foreign-based, but wasn't able to close the transaction. Fibreboard carries the resort on its books at about $19 million; some analysts thought the company was hoping to fetch double that price in a sale.
