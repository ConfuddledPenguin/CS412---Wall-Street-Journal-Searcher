CBI Industries Sets Redemption

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- CBI Industries Inc. said it is calling for the redemption of its $85.4 million outstanding of 7% convertible subordinated debentures due Oct. 30, 2011. The construction concern said the debentures will be redeemed at $52.45 for each $50 principal amount plus accrued and unpaid interest, for a total redemption price of $53.62.

CBI said the debentures are convertible into its common at $34 a share, or 1.471 shares for each $50 principal amount.

In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange yesterday, CBI closed at $41, down $1.125.

If the debentures are fully converted into common, CBI said, it will issue about 2.5 million shares. As of July 31, CBI had 19.5 million common shares outstanding. CBI said conversion rights expire at 3 p.m. EDT Aug. 31.
