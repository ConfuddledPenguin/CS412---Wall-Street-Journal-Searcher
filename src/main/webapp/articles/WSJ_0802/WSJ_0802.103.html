Who's News: Digital Products Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

DIGITAL PRODUCTS Corp. (Fort Lauderdale, Fla.) -- Digital said Theodore Sabarese, founder and former chairman and president of Ultimate Corp., was named president, chief executive officer and a director, succeeding Leopold Cohen, who will remain a director. The company also said John Dell, a consultant, was named a director, expanding the board's size to four members from two.
