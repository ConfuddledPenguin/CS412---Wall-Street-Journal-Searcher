Financing Business: Mackenzie Financial Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

MACKENZIE FINANCIAL Corp. said it agreed to issue three million special warrants to a group of Toronto investment dealers for total proceeds of 23.3 million Canadian dollars (US$20.2 million). The warrants will be distributed by private placement, Mackenzie Financial said, and will entitle the holder to acquire one common share of the company for no additional payment. The warrant issue is subject to regulatory approval. Mackenzie Financial, a Toronto-based mutual funds concern, said the issue is expected to close around the end of August. The group of investment dealers includes Gordon Capital Corp., Midland Walwyn Inc. and Wood Gundy Inc. In trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday, Mackenzie Financial shares closed at C$7.88, down 50 Canadian cents.
