International Brief -- Abbey National PLC: British Bank's Pretax Profit Climbed 38% in First Half

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

Abbey National PLC posted a 38% rise in first-half pretax profit to #280 million ($522 million) from #203 million a year earlier. Profit at Abbey National, which converted in early 1989 to a commercial banking concern from a building society, generally was in line with analysts' forecasts.

The bank said in a statement, "The first half has been satisfactory, but we continue to be cautious about the second half." It said that because there is no sign yet of U.K. interest rates starting to fall, Britain's housing market probably will remain under pressure. "Continuing competitive pressure on margins" will also mean that "caution is the order of the day," the company said.
