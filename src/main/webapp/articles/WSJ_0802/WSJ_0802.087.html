Who's News: Dun & Bradstreet Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/02/90

DUN & BRADSTREET Corp. (New York) -- William T. Whitenack, 49 years old, was named executive vice president of the Business Credit Services division of this business information company. He will run the division's Data Resources unit, where he previously served as senior vice president for operations. Succeeding Mr. Whitenack are Talmadge P. Phillips, 40, who will run Data Resources' Eastern operations; and Doug Nay, 46, who will oversee Western operations. Mr. Phillips was previously vice president, planning and administration, while Mr. Nay was vice president, business credit policy. Mr. Whitenack succeeds Patrick Sommers, who left the company to pursue other interests.
