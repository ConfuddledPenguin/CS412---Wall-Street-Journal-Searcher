Letters to the Editor: Overreaching

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

Your July 16 editorial Asides "Sweet Revenge," charging that "Democrats" froze the office expenses for former presidents in order to "reach" back to "whack four American Presidents," is slightly bizarre in view of the fact that the author of the amendment to freeze the freebies was the Hon. Joseph Skeen of New Mexico -- a Republican. Talk about "reaching."

Rep. Andy Jacobs Jr. (D., Ind.)

Washington
