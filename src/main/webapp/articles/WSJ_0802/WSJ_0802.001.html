Cincinnati Bell's NTT Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/02/90

CINCINNATI -- Cincinnati Bell Inc. said it agreed to provide technical and information management services for Nippon Telegraph & Telephone Corp. in an expanded contract valued at about $32.6 million. Cincinnati Bell said the agreement builds on an October 1988 software contract for NTT valued at about $88 million.

The services will be provided by Cincinnati Bell Information Systems, a software systems unit, through its distributor in Japan, Eckert Research International Corp.
