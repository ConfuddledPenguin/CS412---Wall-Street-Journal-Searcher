CBOT Seat Is Sold

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

CHICAGO -- A seat on the Chicago Board of Trade was sold for $324,000, up $4,000 from the previous sale last Friday. Seats currently are quoted at $315,000 bid, $325,000 asked.

The record price for a full membership on the exchange is $550,000, set Aug. 31, 1987.
