Business Brief -- Freeport-McMoRan Inc.: Distribution Period Extended For Affiliated Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

Freeport-McMoRan Inc. said it is extending the period for assured minimum 60-cent quarterly cash distributions on publicly held units of Freeport-McMoRan Resource Partners L.P. through the second quarter of 1993. The minimum-distribution period initially was scheduled to conclude with the second quarter of 1991. James R. Moffett, Freeport-McMoRan Inc. chairman and chief executive, said the New Orleans exploration and mining company feels confident in extending the minimum distribution for two years because the partnership's previously announced asset sales are expected to generate about $575 million in 1990 that will help reduce debt and fund development of its major sulfur discovery.

Separately, Freeport-McMoRan Oil & Gas Co., an affiliate of Freeport-McMoRan Inc., said directors approved a policy of paying a 10-cent annual dividend. It said the initial dividend will be declared in December, payable in January 1991.
