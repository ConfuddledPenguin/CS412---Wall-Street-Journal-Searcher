Business Brief -- Abbott Laboratories: Net Rose 14% on Increase In Sales in First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

Abbott Laboratories reported a 14% rise in net income for the first quarter on an 11% sales rise, continuing the recent solid growth by the North Chicago, Ill., maker of hospital products and pharmaceuticals. Net income was $225 million, or $1.02 a share, up from the year-earlier period's $198 million, or 88 cents a share. Sales rose to $1.44 billion from $1.3 billion.

Abbott said the introduction of products with higher profit margins, as well as productivity increases contributed to the upturn.

The company's international sales increased more than domestic sales, climbing about 12% compared with 10% growth in the U.S.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Abbott rose 50 cents to $68.125.
