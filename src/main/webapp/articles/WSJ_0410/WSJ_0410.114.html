Business Brief -- Kinburn Corp.: Firm Is Forced to Give Up Control of Its SHL Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

Kinburn Corp., an Ottawa industrial holding company, said it was forced to give up its control of SHL Systemhouse Inc., a major Canadian computer-systems integration company. Kinburn previously had announced it wouldn't be able to repay 831 million Canadian dollars (US$713.1 million) of loans outstanding, which became payable yesterday. The loans included about C$415 million due to BCE Inc., a Montreal-based holding company.

BCE, which owns 12.4% of Systemhouse shares, had an option to acquire 49% of Kinburn between 1993 and 1996. Kinburn said discussions between Kinburn and BCE broke down Friday.

Kinburn owns 50.1% of Systemhouse through its subsidiary Kinburn Technology Corp. Kinburn Technology had guaranteed the loans that were made to its parent, pledging its controlling interest in Systemhouse as well as other assets.
