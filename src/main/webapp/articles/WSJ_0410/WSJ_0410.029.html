FlightSafety International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

NEW YORK -- FlightSafety International Inc. said it agreed to buy certain assets of Bicoastal Corp.'s SimuFlite division for $64 million. FlightSafety, which provides aircraft and other high-technology training, said it also will assume about $19 million in liabilities of Bicoastal, formerly Singer Co. In addition, FlightSafety has agreed to assume certain training contracts from Bicoastal.

The transaction must be approved by the federal bankruptcy court that is supervising Bicoastal's reorganization.
