West German Deficit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West Germany's total capital account deficit narrowed to 5.68 billion marks ($3.35 billion) in February from 9.80 billion marks ($5.78 billion) in January, the Deutsche Bundesbank said. The long-term capital account deficit widened to 19.10 billion marks from 545 million a month earlier, the West German central bank said.

The short-term capital account swung to a surplus of 13.42 billion marks from a deficit of 9.26 billion in January, the Bundesbank said.
