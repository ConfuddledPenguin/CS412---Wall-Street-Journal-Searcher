Southwest Airlines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

DALLAS -- Southwest Airlines said it flew 861.6 million revenue passenger miles, up 6% from March last year. A revenue passenger mile is the equivalent of flying one paying passenger one mile. The carrier's March load factor, or the percentage of seats filled by paying passengers, decreased to 64.4%, from 68.7% a year ago. For the first quarter, Southwest flew 2.3 billion revenue passenger miles, up 5.1% from the same 1989 period. Its first-quarter load factor was 60.4%, off from 65.3% a year earlier.
