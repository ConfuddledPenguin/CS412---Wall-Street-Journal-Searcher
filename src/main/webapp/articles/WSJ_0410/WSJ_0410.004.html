Who's News: Paramount Communications Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

PARAMOUNT COMMUNICATIONS Inc. (New York) -- Peter D. Nalle was appointed president of the Professional Information Group, a unit of Simon & Schuster, the publishing arm of this communications concern. He assumes responsibilities previously held by Jonathan Newcomb, 43 years old, who continues as an executive vice president of the parent. Mr. Nalle, 42, previously was president and chief executive officer of J.B. Lippincott, the health-care publishing unit of Harper & Row.
