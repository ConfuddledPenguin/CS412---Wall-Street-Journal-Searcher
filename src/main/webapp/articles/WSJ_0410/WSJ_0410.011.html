Alamco Expects to Record Gain

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

CLARKSBURG, W.Va. -- Alamco Inc. said it expects to record a second-quarter after-tax gain of about $1.4 million, or 56 cents a share. The extraordinary gain is from the prepayment of a note to a trade vendor.

The producer of oil and natural gas reported net income of $436,000, or two cents a share, for the year-earlier second quarter. That figure includes a tax credit of $194,000, or one cent a share.
