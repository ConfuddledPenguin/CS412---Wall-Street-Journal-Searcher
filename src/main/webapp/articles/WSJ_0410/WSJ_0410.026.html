Tridex Corp. Plans Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

WESTPORT, Conn. -- Tridex Corp. definitively agreed to pay more than $5.5 million in cash and notes for Ithaca Peripherals Inc., a Groton, N.Y., maker of point-of-sale printers for retail and financial institutions and other markets. Tridex manufactures mark-sensing scanners and dot-matrix printers used in lotteries, automatic-teller machines and videotex systems.
