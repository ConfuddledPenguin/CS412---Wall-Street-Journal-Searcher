Transco Unit Tests Gas Wells

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

HOUSTON -- Transco Exploration & Production Co., a unit of Transco Energy Co., said it tested five natural-gas wells in the Gulf of Mexico that it expects to have onstream in the third quarter. Four development wells, scheduled for platform installation this summer, are located 162 miles southeast of Houston at East Cameron Block 109, Transco said. Transco is the operator and has a 76% interest in the wells. Zilkha Energy Co. owns the balance.

The company also said it completed a well at Brazos Block 491. Transco has 38% of that well. Zilkha has 16%; a subsidiary of Noble Affiliates Inc. has 23%; and a subsidiary of New England Electric System has 23%.
