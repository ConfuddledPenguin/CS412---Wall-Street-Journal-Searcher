Financing Business: Wolverine Exploration Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

WOLVERINE EXPLORATION Co. said it completed an offering of 1.8 million new convertible exchangeable preferred shares at $25 each, raising $44.2 million. The Fort Worth, Texas, oil and natural gas concern said proceeds will be used to repurchase $25 million in subordinated notes, for oil and gas exploration and development and to purchase 405,688 of its common shares from Investment Limited Partnership, an investment group controlled by Dort A. Cameron III of Greenwich, Conn. Alex. Brown & Sons led the underwriting.
