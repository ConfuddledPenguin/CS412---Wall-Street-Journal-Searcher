Who's News: International Multifoods Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

INTERNATIONAL MULTIFOODS Corp. (Minneapolis) -- Robert F. Maddocks, formerly senior vice president, human resources, for the U.S. food division of Beatrice Cos., was named vice president, human resources. He succeeds Jerauld N. Mattson, who was named vice president, human resources, for the company's frozen specialty foods division.
