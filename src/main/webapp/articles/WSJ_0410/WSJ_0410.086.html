Highland Superstores Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

PLYMOUTH, Mich. -- Highland Superstores Inc. reported March sales of $68.2 million, down 2% from sales of $69.6 million a year earlier. Comparable-store sales declined 9%. The consumer electronics retailer said its sales for the first two months of the year were $127.5 million, down 4% from $133.2 million in the year-earlier period. Comparable-store sales dropped 11%.
