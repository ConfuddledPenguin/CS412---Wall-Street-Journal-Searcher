One-on-One

("Survey Finds 25% of Work Force Has Anxiety Disorder, Stress-Linked Illness." -- WSJ headline)

Sometimes it's fellow workers

Who can have one annoyed;

Sometimes it's the surroundings

That one cannot avoid;

Sometimes it is the boss

In whom faith is destroyed --

None of which explains my case,

Since I am self-employed.

-- Arnold J. Zarett.

Shorter Is Better

Clergy should know that their sermons,

Which guide to heaven's portal,

Need not be eternal

In order to be immortal.

-- George O. Ludcke.
