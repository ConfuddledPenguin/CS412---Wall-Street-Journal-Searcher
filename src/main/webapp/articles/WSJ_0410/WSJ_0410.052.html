Southland Agrees to Sell Stores

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

DALLAS -- Southland Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement to sell its 56 7-Eleven stores and related properties in Memphis, Tenn., to Mapco Petroleum Inc., a unit of Mapco Inc. Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed.

Troubled Southland, a convenience store retailer that went private in a 1987 leveraged buy-out, has been selling assets to repay debt and said it would use proceeds from the transaction to reduce its long-term bank debt.

Mapco is a coal, refining and natural gas concern based in Tulsa, Okla.
