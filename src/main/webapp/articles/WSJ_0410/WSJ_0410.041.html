America West March Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines said its March traffic increased 35.5%, to 941.8 million revenue passenger miles. A revenue passenger mile is the equivalent of flying one paying passenger one mile. America West said its load factor rose to 65.2%, from 62.4% a year earlier. The airline said first-quarter traffic increased 35.6% over the 1989 period, to 2.5 billion revenue passenger miles, and its first-quarter load factor rose to 61.9%, from 59.8%.
