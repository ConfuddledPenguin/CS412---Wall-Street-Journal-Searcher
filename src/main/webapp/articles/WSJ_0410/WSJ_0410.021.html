West German Living Costs

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- The cost of living in West Germany rose 0.1% in the month to mid-March and 2.7% from March 1989, the Federal Statistics Office in Wiesbaden said. The final statistics for the month compare with a 0.2% increase reported in the preliminary data, released 11 days ago. The year-to-year rate of 2.7% was unchanged from the preliminary report.
