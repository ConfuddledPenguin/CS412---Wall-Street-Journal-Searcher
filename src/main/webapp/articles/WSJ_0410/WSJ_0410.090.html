Business Brief -- Western Co. of North America: Norwegian Firm Buys Stake Of 5.9% in Texas Concern

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

Arcade Shipping AS, a Norwegian offshore drilling investment company, said it acquired a 5.9% stake in Western Co. of North America, Fort Worth, Texas, and may seek board representation. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Arcade said it holds 740,000 Western common shares, all bought between Jan. 4 to March 15 for $13.425 to $17.70 a share.

A Western official declined to comment.

In the filing, Arcade said it may seek to buy more shares, dispose of them or seek to elect one or more of its designees to Western's board.

Another Norwegian company, Viking Supply Ships AS, has acquired a 10.68% stake in Western and is considering seeking support from other shareholders for a special meeting to replace Western's board.
