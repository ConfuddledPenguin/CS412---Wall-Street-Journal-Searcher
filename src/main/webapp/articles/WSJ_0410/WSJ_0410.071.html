Financing Business: AMR Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

AMR Corp. (Fort Worth, Texas) -- American Airlines, a unit, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission an offering of about $220 million of equipment trust certificates relating to the lease of McDonnell Douglas MD-80 and Boeing 757 aircraft. American is in the midst of a multiyear, multibillion-dollar expansion of its aircraft fleet.
