Letters to the Editor: Songs We Never Sang

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/10/90

I was caught off-guard by Dave Shiflett's March 20 Leisure & Arts review, "Flame-Broiled Marxist." Near the end of his article Mr. Shiflett mentions a chorale from the SS Hymnal. As one partial to supply-side economics, I would hate to see the SS acronym catch on. However, there is a fair degree of black humor in the phrase "SS Hymnal." Maybe you could nominate it for "Oxymoron of the Year."

Arthur J. Keegan

Vice President

HBO & Co.

Atlanta
