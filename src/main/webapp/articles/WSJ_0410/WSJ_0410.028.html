Metro Airlines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

DALLAS -- Metro Airlines said March traffic was 50.9 million revenue passenger miles, up 68.4% from March 1989. A revenue passenger mile is the equivalent of flying one paying passenger one mile. Metro's load factor increased to 53.1%, from 46.8%. For the quarter, Metro flew 135.8 million revenue passenger miles, up 48.6% from a year ago. The airline's load factor in the first three months rose to 50.4%, from 47.6% last year.
