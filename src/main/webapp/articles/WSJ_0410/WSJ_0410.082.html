Raw-Steel Production Rose 4.9% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 4.9% last week to 1,998,000 tons from 1,904,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 1.5% from the 2,029,000 tons produced a year earlier.

The industry used 88.9% of its capability last week, compared with 85.0% the previous week and 91.7% a year ago.

The capability utilization rate is a calculation designed to indicate at what percent of its production capability the industry is operating in a given week.
