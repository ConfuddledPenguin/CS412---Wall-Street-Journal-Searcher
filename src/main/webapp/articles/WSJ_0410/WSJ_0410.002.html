Who's News: AM International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/10/90

AM INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Chicago) -- The company said it merged its AM Multigraphics and international divisions under the AM Multigraphics title and named George H. Gilmore Jr. president of the new unit. Mr. Gilmore, 40 years old, headed the international division and is a former president of Mitel Inc. The graphics-equipment concern said Gerald J. McConnell, 47, vice president and head of the former AM Multigraphics unit, resigned from the company to pursue other interests.
