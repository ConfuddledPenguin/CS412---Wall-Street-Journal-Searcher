USAIR GROUP Inc.'s fleet includes 193 aircraft classified as the quieter Stage 3 jets, or 43.8% of the airline's total. In yesterday's edition, a chart provided by Avmark Inc. incorrectly placed USAir's Stage 3 share at 175 planes and 39.4% of its fleet. (See: "Environment: Public Outcries Move Airports to Cut Noise" -- WSJ April 9, 1990)

---

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS MACHINES Corp. clarified a growth estimate it made in yesterday's edition. The company expects revenue from its Personal System/2 business, not its personal-systems business, to grow by a percentage in the midteens or better, a spokesman said. The personal-systems business includes both PS/2 personal computers and workstations.

(See: "Technology: IBM Expects Growth in Personal-Systems Business to Be Strong" -- WSJ April 9, 1990)
