First Financial Bancorp Charge

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

MONROE, Ohio -- First Financial Bancorp said it will take a charge of $4.5 million against 1990 earnings to cover loan losses at its First National Bank of Southwestern Ohio. For 1989, First Financial reported net income of $15.2 million, or $3.01 a share.

The bank holding company said the onetime charge, estimated to be $3 million, or 60 cents a share, is linked to the default on three commercial real estate loans.

First Financial has assets of $1.2 billion and operates banks in Ohio and Indiana.
