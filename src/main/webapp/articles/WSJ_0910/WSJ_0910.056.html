PHM Board to Buy Back Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

BLOOMFIELD HILLS, Mich. -- PHM Corp. said its board authorized the company to buy back as many as one million shares of its stock from time to time over the next year. The home-building company said the shares may be purchased through transactions in the open market, private negotiations or a combination of both. The shares will be purchased with cash, and become authorized but unissued shares available for general corporate purposes.
