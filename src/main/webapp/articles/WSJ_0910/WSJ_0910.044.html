Noranda, Trelleborg Reach Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

TORONTO -- Noranda Inc. and Trelleborg A.B. said they signed a $1.73 billion refinancing of their joint purchase last year of Falconbridge Ltd. The refinancing consists of two floating-rate, seven-year term credit facilities: a $950 million syndicated bank facility arranged in Europe and a 900 million Canadian dollar ($779 million) syndicated bank facility arranged in Canada.

The facilities replace C$2.25 billion in bridge loans arranged with six Canadian banks at the time of the companies' C$2.85 billion acquisition of Falconbridge. The original loans would have been due Jan. 31, 1991. A Noranda spokesman said terms of the new financing are "slightly better" than the original facilities but couldn't say if it would result in any savings to the mining company. Trelleborg is an industrial group based in Sweden.
