Financing Business: Imasco Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

IMASCO FINANCIAL Corp., Montreal, an indirectly held unit of Imasco Ltd., said it will redeem at a cost of 300 million Canadian dollars (US$260 million) all of its issued and Series A C$2.25 First preferred shares outstanding at C$25 per share. An Imasco Financial spokesman said the company will likely refinance the C$300 million of debt using commercial paper.
