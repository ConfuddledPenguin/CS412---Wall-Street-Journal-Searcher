Business Brief -- Mitsubishi Electric Corp.: Firm's U.S. Plant to Double Production of Car Phones

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

Mitsubishi Electric Corp. plans to double production capacity of car phones at its Atlanta, Ga., plant to 400,000 units in fiscal 1991 to meet growing U.S. demand, a company spokesman said. The Tokyo company aims to maintain its share of roughly 10% of the U.S. market by expanding the line at the plant. The products are part of the newest car phone systems, which transmit voices digitally.
