Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

WHO'S NEWS: Whittle Communications L.P. named William Connell as its new president and chief operating officer. He most recently operated his own venture-capital business, Peregrine Capital Partners of Cincinnati. Before that, he was a 25-year veteran of Procter & Gamble Co. He succeeds Nick Glover, who resigned in January 1989. Time Warner Inc. bought a 50% interest in Whittle in late 1988.
