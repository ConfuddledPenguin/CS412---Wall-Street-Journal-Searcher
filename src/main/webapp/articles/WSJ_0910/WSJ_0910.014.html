Societe Generale Iran Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

PARIS -- Societe Generale said it signed a cooperation agreement with the Central Bank of Iran and the Iranian national petrochemical company, under which the French bank will arrange financing for $1.8 billion of petrochemical projects in Iran. Among the projects are the rebuilding of the petrochemical facility at Bandar Khomeini, damaged during the Iran-Iraq war, a chemical fertilizer factory in eastern Iran and the second phase of a petrochemicals complex at Arak.
