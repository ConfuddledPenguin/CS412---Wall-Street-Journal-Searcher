General Host's Loss Narrows

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- General Host Corp. posted a narrowed loss of $528,000 in the fiscal second quarter ended Aug. 12, compared with a year-earlier loss of $2.9 million. The year-ago quarter included a charge to earnings of $3.4 million stemming from an agreement to settle lawsuits brought against it concerning discontinued operations. Sales rose slightly in the quarter to $115.1 million from $112.4 a year ago.

For the six months, the company had net of $5 million, or 26 cents a share, compared with a year-ago loss of $1.3 million, which included the charge. Sales rose to $279.9 million from $275.2 million.

General Host operates a chain of lawn and garden products specialty retail stores.
