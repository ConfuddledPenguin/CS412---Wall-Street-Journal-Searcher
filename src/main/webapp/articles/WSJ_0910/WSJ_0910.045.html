Canada's Jobless Rate Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

OTTAWA -- Canada's unemployment rate rose to 8.3% of the work force in August from 7.8% in July as a result of the country's current economic slowdown, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. The August figure was the highest jobless rate since October 1987. Employment declined 0.5% in August to 12.6 million, its lowest level since last December.
