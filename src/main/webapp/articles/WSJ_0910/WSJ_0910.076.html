DNA Plant Technology Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

CINNAMINSON, N.J. -- DNA Plant Technology Corp. said it extended for one year the expiration date of its 2,111,006 public warrants outstanding. The warrants had been scheduled to expire on Jan. 17, 1991.

The agricultural biotechnology company said the warrants were originally sold as part of a public offering that began in 1984. Under terms of the offering, two warrants plus $10.50 in cash entitle the holder to buy 1.32 shares of DNA Plant Technology's common stock.
