Letters to the Editor: King Hussein's Phobias

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

Your Aug. 15 news story regarding King Hussein's conference with President Bush contains the statement that "King Hussein has come to rely on Iraq more and more for protection from Israel." This notion that King Hussein somehow has a legitimate fear of Israel has been repeated several times in articles over the past several weeks. It is, however, patently absurd. Israel has been the subject of repeated unprovoked military attacks by Jordan. Jordan's military and diplomatic hostility has been unceasing. If you reported that King Hussein stated that he believes that he has something to fear from Israel, the reader could then judge for himself. To report as a fact, however, that King Hussein has something to fear from Israel is without basis and constitutes irresponsible reporting.

Jonathan L. Flaxer

New York
