Value Received

With long years of work at meager pay,

My value to the firm is deceptive;

For though not innovative or brilliant,

I've certainly been cost-effective.

-- Edward F. Dempsey.

Materialistic

With all due respect to best sellers,

Which I acknowledge in haste,

All I acquired in kindergarten

Was a taste for library paste.

-- George O. Ludcke.
