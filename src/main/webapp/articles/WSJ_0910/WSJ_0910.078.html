Who's News: Hanson Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

HANSON INDUSTRIES Inc. (Iselin, N.J.) -- David T. DiPasquale was named president of the Endicott Johnson Footwear unit, based in Endicott, N.Y. He succeeds Charles Murray, who left the post. Mr. DiPasquale, 36 years old, was previously president of Stride-Rite Corp.'s Sperry Topsider division. Hanson is a unit of Britain's Hanson PLC.
