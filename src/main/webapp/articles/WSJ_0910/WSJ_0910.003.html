Who's News: Duro-Test Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

DURO-TEST Corp. (Fairfield N.J.) -- Dan E. Picini was named president and chief operating officer, new posts at this maker of lighting products. Mr. Picini, 57 years old, had been vice president and general manager for GTE Sylvania's U.S. lighting division.
