Business Brief -- ITT Corp.: Insurance Unit Signs Letter To Buy Surety Bond Line

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

ITT Hartford Insurance Group said it signed a letter of intent to acquire the surety bond business of Commercial Union Insurance Co. Terms were not disclosed. ITT Hartford said the acquisition is expected to increase its written premiums from surety bonds by about 10%. In 1989, ITT Hartford wrote about $67 million in such premiums.

ITT Hartford is a unit of New York-based ITT Corp. Commercial Union Insurance is a U.S. subsidiary of London-based Commercial Union PLC.
