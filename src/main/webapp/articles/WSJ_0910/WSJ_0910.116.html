Business Brief -- NWA Inc.: Northwest Air Lowers Fares To Europe for Fall, Winter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

Northwest Airlines lowered fares to its five European destinations for fall and winter travel, but tickets must be purchased by Sept. 21. Northwest, whose parent company is St. Paul, Minn., NWA Inc., flies to London, Paris, Frankfurt, Glasgow and Amsterdam. As examples of the lower fares, Boston passengers can travel roundtrip to London for $358, a fare $100 lower than the previous lowest fare; Chicago travelers can fly to Amsterdam for $378, a $98 reduction from the normal fare; and Los Angeles passengers can go to Glasgow, Scotland, for $518, a savings of $120.

To qualify for the discounts, travel must begin from Nov. 1 to Feb. 28 with all travel completed by March 8. Northwest passengers must also plan trips of six to 21 days. Seats are limited and the lower fares don't apply during the Christmas holiday period.
