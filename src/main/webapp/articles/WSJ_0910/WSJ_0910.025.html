LEGAL SOFTWARE SOLUTIONS Inc. (Melville, N.Y.) -- Frank Murphy, 42 years old, previously executive vice president of the Baxter Systems division of Baxter International Inc., was named chief executive officer of this maker of computer software systems for the debt-collection industry, succeeding Peter Fleischman, who resigned.
