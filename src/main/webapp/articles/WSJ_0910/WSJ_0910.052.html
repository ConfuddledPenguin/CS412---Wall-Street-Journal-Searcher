Who's News: Qantel Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

QANTEL Corp. (Hayward, Calif.) -- Karl H. Niemuller will resign as president and chief executive officer and Alan H. Friedman will resign as chief financial officer, both effective Dec. 6. Last December, Matthew E. Tutino resigned as head of the minicomputer maker, citing a desire to move on to other projects. In a news release, Qantel said Niemuller resigned "for personal reasons," but would continue serving on the board. Successors have not yet been named.
