Technology Brief -- Landmark Graphics Corp.: Firm Plans to Buy Makers Of Software for $23 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

Landmark Graphics Corp. said it signed letters of intent to buy two software concerns in stock transactions valued at a total of $23 million. The Houston supplier of computer-aided oil exploration technology said it proposes to acquire Inverse Theory & Application Inc., a Calgary Canada developer of software used in processing geophysical data; and Zycor Inc., an Austin, Texas, vendor of software used for interactive mapping and modeling of subsurface geology.

Landmark said it believes the transactions would boost revenue for fiscal 1991 about 20%. In the year ended June 30, the company had net income of $5.8 million, or 68 cents a share, on revenue of $56.7 million.
