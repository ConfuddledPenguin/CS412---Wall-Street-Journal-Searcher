Amcore Financial Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

ROCKFORD, Ill. -- Amcore Financial Inc. said its board has authorized the purchase of as much as $6 million of its common stock from time to time through the open market or privately negotiated purchases. The bank holding company said the program is in addition to a $2 million buyback announced last June. Amcore said about $1.6 million of common stock has been purchased under the prior authorization.

Amcore, which currently has 6.5 million common shares outstanding, said any shares purchased will become treasury stock and will be used for general corporate purposes.
