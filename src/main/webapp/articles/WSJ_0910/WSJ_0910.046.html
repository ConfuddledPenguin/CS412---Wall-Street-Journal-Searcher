Italy Orders Fell 3.4% in May

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

MILAN, Italy -- Italian industrial orders fell 3.4% in May from a year earlier, the State Statistical Institute announced. Domestic orders fell 1.9% in May from a year earlier, while foreign orders declined 6.5%.

For the first five months, orders to Italian industry rose 2.5% from the year-earlier period. Domestic orders rose 3.5% in the January-May period, while foreign orders gained by 0.4%.
