Who's News: Symbol Technologies Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

SYMBOL TECHNOLOGIES Inc. (Bohemia, N.Y.) -- Leonard Goldner was named senior vice president and general counsel at this maker of bar code data-capture systems. He had been a partner in the New York law firm Shereff, Friedman, Hoffman & Goodman, where he functioned as Symbol Technologies' outside corporate counsel.
