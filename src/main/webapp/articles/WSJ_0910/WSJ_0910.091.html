REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial): Ultimate Environmentalism

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

In rating the performances of Congressmen, the group Environmental Action included a population-control vote, which would have sent millions of dollars to a U.N. fund that has programs in China, whose population policy of one child per family includes forced abortions and sterilizations. When criticized for including this volatile issue as a litmus test in an environmental rating, Environmental Action replied that it was important because, "the pressure of population growth on the environment can undo all the progress we have made."
