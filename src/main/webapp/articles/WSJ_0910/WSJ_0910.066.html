Shipping Concerns' Buy-Backs

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

NEW YORK - B&H Ocean Carriers Ltd. and B&H Maritime Carriers Ltd., separately traded companies that are operated by the same management, said their boards approved proposals to purchase as much as 20% of their shares outstanding. B&H Ocean has about 4.1 million shares and B&H Maritime about 3.1 million shares outstanding. Michael S. Hudner, president of both shipping concerns, said the stocks were significantly undervalued. Through the buy-back programs, Mr. Hudner said, the companies intend "to both draw the attention of investors to this situation and to directly increase shareholder value."

In American Stock Exchange composite trading Friday, B&H Ocean shares closed at $11.625, up 75 cents, and B&H Maritime shares closed at $7.375, up 25 cents.
