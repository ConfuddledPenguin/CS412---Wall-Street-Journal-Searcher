Uranium Resources Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

DALLAS -- Uranium Resources Inc. said a subsidiary signed a long-term contract with a major U.S. electric utility to provide about 1.2 million pounds of uranium oxide in the 1992-1997 period. The uranium mining company estimated the value of the contract at $20 million, based on projections of deliveries and price escalations. The company declined to name the utility.
