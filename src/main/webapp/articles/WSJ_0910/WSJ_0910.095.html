International Brief -- Gevaert N.V.: Belgian Firm Says Profit Increased 31% in First Half

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

Gevaert N.V. said group net profit rose 31% to 1.1 billion Belgian francs ($34.4 million) in the first half, up from 842 million francs a year earlier. The Belgian company said operating profit grew 25% to 1.04 billion francs. Gevaert also said it expects consolidated earnings for the year to exceed 1.7 billion francs. However, the company warned that "the evolution of stock exchange prices since June 30, 1990, does not allow . . . to put forward a final figure."

Net profit for the parent company was 1.04 billion francs, up from 942 million francs a year earlier.
