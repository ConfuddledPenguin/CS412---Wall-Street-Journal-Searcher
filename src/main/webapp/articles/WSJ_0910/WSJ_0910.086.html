REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Ontario Lists Leftward

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

The word went out in November 1989 that socialism is dead, but the message doesn't seem to have arrived in Ontario yet. Voters in Canada's biggest and richest province gave the socialist New Democratic Party a huge majority in the provincial legislature on Thursday -- a majority that the NDP intends to use to raise taxes, impose centralized control of wages (via "comparable worth") and pull Canada's health care ever closer to a Soviet-like model. It makes us wonder if some Canadians are still getting their European news by dog sled?
