Canada Steel Output Falls 3.3%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 165,060 metric tons in the week ended Sept. 1, down 3.3% from 170,609 tons in the preceeding week but down 43% from 287,669 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. The cumulative total in 1990 was 9,109,368 tons, down 14.1% from 10,603,864 tons a year earlier.
