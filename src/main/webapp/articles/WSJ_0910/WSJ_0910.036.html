Timken to Upgrade Production

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

CANTON, Ohio -- Timken Co. said it will spend $13 million to upgrade production of large bearings. The project, which is part of the company's previously announced $1 billion capital investment program, will combine all U.S. large bearing production at Timken's Gambrinus bearing plant, located near Canton.

Timken produces bearings and specialty steel.
