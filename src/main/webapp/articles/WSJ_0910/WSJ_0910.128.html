Business Brief -- Uniroyal Goodrich Tire Co.: Michelin Unit to Increase Prices on Some Tires by 5%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

Uniroyal Goodrich Tire Co. said it will increase prices for its original-equipment commercial tires by 5%, effective Oct. 1. The Akron, Ohio, company, a unit of Groupe Michelin of France, attributed the move to "the escalating cost of raw materials." The increase follows a 2% to 6% increase on tire prices, also effective Oct. 1, announced by Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.

The price increases had been expected because of the rise in crude-oil prices stemming from the Mideast crisis.
