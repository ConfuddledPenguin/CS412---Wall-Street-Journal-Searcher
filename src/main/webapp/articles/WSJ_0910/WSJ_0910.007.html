Who's News: Paramount Communications Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

PARAMOUNT COMMUNICATIONS Inc. (New York) -- Ronald L. Nelson, 38-year-old chief financial officer of this entertainment and publishing concern, was named to the additional post of executive vice president, filling the strategic planning post vacated last month when Michael Hope resigned to pursue other interests. Mr. Nelson will continue to oversee investor relations.
