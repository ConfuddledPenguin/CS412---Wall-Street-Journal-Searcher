Caesars World Inc. Again Extends Its Bid For New Jersey Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/10/90

LOS ANGELES -- Caesars World Inc. again extended its $22-a-share tender offer for the 2,175,945 shares of Caesars New Jersey Inc. it doesn't already own to midnight Sept. 21 from last Friday. The hotel and casino operator holds about 86.6% of Caesars New Jersey's 16,261,531 common shares outstanding. As of Thursday, about 1,783,567 shares had been tendered.

Caesars World has agreed in principle to make an additional payment of 25 cents a share as part of a settlement of lawsuits brought by some Caesars New Jersey shareholders. Final court approval isn't expected for at least two months.

Caesars also said the New Jersey Casino Control Commission has scheduled a hearing Sept. 19 to consider a petition filed by a Caesars New Jersey holder alleging, among other things, that the $22-a-share price is inadequate. The hearing is to determine whether the petitioner has the standing to bring the issue before the regulators.
