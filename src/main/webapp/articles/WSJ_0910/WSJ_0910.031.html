Waste Management Says Wheelabrator Holders Clear Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

OAK BROOK, Ill. -- Waste Management Inc. said Wheelabrator Technologies Inc.'s shareholders approved a previously announced merger agreement between the two companies. Waste Management said the agreement increases its ownership of Wheelabrator's common stock to 55% from 22%. Wheelabrator, a Hampton, N.H., developer and operator of waste-to-energy facilities, will remain a separate public company.

The waste disposal concern said that in exchange for each share of Wheelabrator Technologies held, shareholders of record will receive 0.469 Waste Management share and 0.574 Wheelabrator share. Under the agreement, four Waste Management officers were named directors of Wheelabrator, in addition to the eleven current directors. They are Jerry E. Dempsey, Jerome D. Girsch, James E. Koenig, and Edwin G. Falkman.
