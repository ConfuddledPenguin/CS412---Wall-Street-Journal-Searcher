Anthony Industries Debentures

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

LOS ANGELES -- Anthony Industries said its board authorized the redemption on Oct. 15 of its 11 1/4% convertible subordinated debentures due Jan. 31, 2000, of which about $9 million principal amount are outstanding. For each $100 principal amount of debentures, holders may receive $100 plus accrued interest; convert them by Oct. 15 into Anthony common stock at a price of $7.90 a share; or sell them in the open market. About 1.1 million shares are subject to issuance on conversion of the debentures. The diversified maker of recreational and industrial products also said its board approved a stock repurchase of up to the number of shares issued on conversion of the debentures.
