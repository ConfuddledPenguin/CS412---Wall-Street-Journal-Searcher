Who's News: General Public Utilities Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/10/90

GENERAL PUBLIC UTILITIES Corp. (Parsippany, N.J.) -- Adm. Carlisle A.H. Trost, who retired as U.S. chief of naval operations in June, was named a director of this provider of electricity, increasing board membership to 12. He also will serve on the board of the GPU Nuclear Corp. unit.
