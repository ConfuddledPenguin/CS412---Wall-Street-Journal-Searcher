Who's News: USG Names Connolly To Posts of Chairman And Chief Executive

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

CHICAGO -- The board of USG Corp. elected Eugene B. Connolly, who has been president and chief executive officer, to the posts of chairman and chief executive officer. Anthony J. Falvo Jr., currently executive vice president and chief operating officer, was elected president and chief operating officer. Both changes take effect June 1.

Robert J. Day, currently chairman of the board, will continue in that post until his retirement May 31.

USG manufactures and distributes building materials.
