ILC Is Authorized on Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

SUNNYVALE, Calif. -- ILC Technology Inc. said its directors authorized the purchase of as many as 300,000 shares of its 2.2 milion common shares outstanding. Purchases, which are to be made on the open market from time to time, are a logical part of the company's restructuring program, ILC said.
