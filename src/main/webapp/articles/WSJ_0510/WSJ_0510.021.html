Who's News: Intel Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

INTEL Corp. (Santa Clara, Calif.) -- Frank Gill, 46-year-old vice president and director of sales, was named president of the Intel Systems Group, the computer systems operation of this microcomputer products company. Mr. Gill succeeds Leslie Vadasz, 53, who has indicated he plans to step down from that post prior to a summer sabbatical. The company said Mr. Vadasz, who helped the systems group double its revenue in the four years of his leadership, plans to return to Intel, in a role yet to be determined, after his sabbatical.
