Philippine Investments

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

MANILA, the Philippines -- Investments in the Philippines will slow down to a growth rate of 11.5% this year due to political and economic factors, Economic Planning Secretary Cayetano Paderanga Jr. said. The possibilities for growth have narrowed because of December's coup attempt, the ongoing drought and an electricity crisis, according to the economic planning department.

The government earlier had estimated that investments would grow between 12.8% and 18.4% this year.
