Who's News: Ameritech

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

AMERITECH (Chicago) -- Andres B. Bande was named president of Ameritech International Inc., a new subsidiary of Ameritech. Mr. Bande, 45 years old, is an international telecommunications adviser. He will begin his new position June 1. Ameritech is a regional telecommunications company.
