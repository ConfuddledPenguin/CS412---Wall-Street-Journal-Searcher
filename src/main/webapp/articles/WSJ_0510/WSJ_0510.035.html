Who's News: N.V. Philips

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

N.V. PHILIPS -- Helen Murphy was named to the new post of vice president, investor relations, North America, for PolyGram N.V., the Amsterdam recorded-music unit of this Dutch electronics concern. Before joining PolyGram in March, Ms. Murphy was a vice president, research, for Richardson Greenshields.
