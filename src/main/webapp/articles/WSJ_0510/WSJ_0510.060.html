Coeur d'Alene Net Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

COEUR D'ALENE, Idaho -- Coeur d'Alene Mines Corp. said first-quarter gold production and higher prices helped its first-quarter profit quadruple to $2.4 million or 24 cents a share, from $628,000, or 6 cents a share. Total revenue edged up 2%, to $14,363,000, from $14,055,000 in last year's first quarter, the mining concern said.

Silver operations continue to be profitable, despite lower prices. Coeur d'Alene also reduced its cost of mine operations from year-ago levels, the company added.
