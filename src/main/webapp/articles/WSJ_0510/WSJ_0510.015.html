Who's News: USMX Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

USMX Inc. (Denver) -- Keith R. Hulley, former senior vice president of Atlas Corp., a Denver gold mining company, was named president and chief executive officer of this gold mining company. Mr. Hulley, 49 years old, succeeds Michael Mehrtens, 53, as president. USMX said Mr. Mehrtens resigned in February "to pursue his own interests." The chief executive's position had been vacant.
