California Energy Gets Loan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- California Energy Co. said it completed financing for its agreement to buy certain geothermal operations of Chevron Resource Co. The company said it received a $35 million, six-year unsecured term loan from Credit Suisse Bank and National Westminster Bank.

California Energy agreed to acquire the properties of the Chevron Corp. unit in Oregon, Utah and Nevada for $68.7 million. The geothermal company said it is arranging an additional loan for a subsidiary to operate the acquired properties.
