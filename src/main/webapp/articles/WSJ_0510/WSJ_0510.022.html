Bayship Management Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

WASHINGTON -- Bayship Management won a $73.9 million Navy contract for operating and maintaining eight sealift ships for five years.

General Electric Co. got a $42.4 million Navy contract for helicopter engines.

Johns Hopkins University's Applied Physics Laboratory was awarded a $15.1 million Navy contract for research and development.

Lockheed Corp. was given a $10 million Army contract for a flight-experiment launch vehicle.
