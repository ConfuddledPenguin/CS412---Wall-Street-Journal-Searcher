Who's News: WR Lazard & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

WR LAZARD & Co. (New York) -- Frankie D. Hughes, 37 years old, was named a partner and director of the asset-management division of this investment management firm. Ms. Hughes, a former investment officer with Citicorp, has been a portfolio manager since she joined WR Lazard in 1987.
