Flow International Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

KENT, Wash. -- Flow International Corp. said it retained the firm of Wertheim Schroder & Co. as its investment banker to explore alternatives to "maximize shareholder value." Y.H. Pao, chairman of Flow, said the producer of high-pressure water jets may be a candidate for merger or acquisition, or other actions to improve value to the shareholders.

"Larger companies may be willing to pay a premium," he said, adding that Flow has received overtures from potential suitors, foreign and domestic. "It is the job of Wertheim to examine {these offers}."
