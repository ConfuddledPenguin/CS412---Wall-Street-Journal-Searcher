Who's News: Hipotronics Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

HIPOTRONICS Inc. (Brewster, N.Y.) -- Stephen S. Peschel, 34 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this maker of high-voltage test equipment and power supplies. As president, he succeeds Stanley G. Peschel, 59, who remains chairman and chief executive officer. Stephen Peschel had been executive vice president and secretary.
