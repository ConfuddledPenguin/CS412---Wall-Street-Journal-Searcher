Spain's Jobless Rate Falls

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

MADRID -- Spain's unemployment total fell to 2,380,000 in April from 2,410,000 the previous month, according to the Spanish Labor Ministry. Spain's unemployment rate stood at 15.9% of the work force in April, according to the ministry, down from 16.1% in March and 16.3% in April 1989.
