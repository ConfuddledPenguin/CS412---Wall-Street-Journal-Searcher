Who's News: Midland Bank PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

MIDLAND BANK PLC (London) -- Gene Lockhart, 40 years old, has been named chief executive officer, U.K. banking and group operations, of this British banking concern. Rodney Baker-Bates, 46, has been appointed managing director, U.K. banking. Ronald Price, 51, has been named managing director, group operations.
