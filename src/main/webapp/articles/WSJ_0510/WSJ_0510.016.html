Who's News: Microsoft Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

MICROSOFT Corp. (Redmond, Wash.) -- Paul G. Allen, a co-founder of this computer-software company, was named a director. Mr. Allen currently serves as chairman and president of Asymetrix, a Bellevue, Wash., software firm. Michael Hallman, who is slated to succeed Jon A. Shirley as Microsoft president and chief operating officer upon Mr. Shirley's retirement June 30, also was named a director. The appointment of the two increases Microsoft's board to seven members.
