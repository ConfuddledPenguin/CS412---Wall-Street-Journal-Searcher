First Midwest Buys Deposits

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

NAPERVILLE, Ill. -- First Midwest Bancorp Inc. said it acquired the deposits of the Streator and Marseilles, Illinois, offices of the failed, Streator-based Peoples Savings and Loan Association. The company's First Midwest Bank, headquartered in Joliet, said it paid $344,000 for the deposits to the Resolution Trust Corp., which is the federal agency overseeing the liquidation of failed thrifts.

The banking company said it assumed $22 million in deposits.
