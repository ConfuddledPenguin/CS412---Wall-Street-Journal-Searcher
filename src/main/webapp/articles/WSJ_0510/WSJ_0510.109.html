International Brief -- SmithKline Beecham PLC: Pretax Profit in 1st Quarter Fell 6.8% to $365.8 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

SmithKline Beecham PLC, a health, household products and pharmaceuticals concern, said first-quarter pretax profit dropped 6.8% to #219 million ($365.8 million) compared with #235 million in the year-earlier period. The first-quarter results are the first reported on a quarterly basis since the group was formed through a July 1989 merger. Year-earlier figures were presented as if the merger had taken place at the beginning of 1989. The concern was formed from Beecham PLC and SmithKline Beckman Corp.

Sales rose 15% to #1.3 billion from #1.1 billion.

Per-share earnings dropped 12% to 10.8 pence from 12.3 pence.

Analysts said the pretax figures were broadly in line with market expectations.

In the quarter, the company had nearly #1.5 billion of debt compared with #2.4 billion a year ago.

The company predicts a first-quarter dividend of 3.4 pence. The dividend, to be announced next week at the concern's annual general meeting, will be paid to shareholders in July.
