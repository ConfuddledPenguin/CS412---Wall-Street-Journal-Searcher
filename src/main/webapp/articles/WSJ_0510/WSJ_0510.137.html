East German Joblessness Soars

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Official unemployment in East Germany surged in April to 64,948 job seekers from 38,313 people the previous month, the East German state news agency reported. Citing statistics from East Germany's Ministry for Labor and Social Affairs, the agency said the number of job vacancies in the country receded substantially to 73,558 places by the end of the month. It didn't provide month-earlier data.

East Germany only recently began releasing unemployment data and its current data are considered to be incomplete. As unproductive state-owned companies are taken public as part of East Germany's economic integration with West Germany, many existing jobs are expected to be lost, swelling unemployment rolls.
