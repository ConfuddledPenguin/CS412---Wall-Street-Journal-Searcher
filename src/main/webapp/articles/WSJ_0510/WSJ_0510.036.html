Who's News: Barclays Bank PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

BARCLAYS BANK PLC (London) -- Alan Brown, 44 years old, was named country manager and director, Japan, of this British banking concern, succeeding Michael Tomalin, who is returning to London to run the new financial-institution group created by Barclays in London. Barclays de Zoete Wedd, the investment and securities unit of this British bank, has appointed Ben Grigsby, 40, as managing director of its operations in Japan, succeeding Michael Connors, 40, who is returning to London in September as Barclays de Zoete Wedd head of research.
