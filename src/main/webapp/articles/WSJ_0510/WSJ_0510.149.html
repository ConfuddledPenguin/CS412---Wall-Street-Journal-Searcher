Marketing Brief: NOTED....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

MOVIES: New Line Cinema Corp. plans to sell 600,000 newly issued common shares to clients of Capital Research & Management Co. and Capital Guardian Trust Co., both units of Capital Group Inc. The estimated $6 million investment would give the investors a 7.9% stake in New Line and boost New Line's shares outstanding to about 7.6 million. New Line will use the money to fund film production.

PUBLISHING: Maxwell Communication Corp.'s Macmillan Inc. unit acquired Thorndike, a Unity, Maine, publisher of large-print editions of current fiction and nonfiction books, for $5.2 million.
