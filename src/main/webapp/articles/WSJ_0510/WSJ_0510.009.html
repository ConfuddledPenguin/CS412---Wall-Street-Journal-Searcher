REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: State Comic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

May Day was tough on communists in Red Square this year, but for some in the U.S. the dream still lives. At the State Department, the Secretary's April 30 Open Forum "to commemorate May Day" featured Tim Wheeler, the Washington correspondent for the Communist Party U.S.A. newspaper, the People's Daily World. His speech topic: "New Potential for the Left: Communism After the Cold War Ends." It's nice to know someone at State has a sense of humor.
