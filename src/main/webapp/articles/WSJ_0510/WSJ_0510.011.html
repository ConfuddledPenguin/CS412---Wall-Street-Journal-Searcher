Dresher Plan Advances

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

BEDFORD PARK, Ill. -- Dresher Inc. said the Justice Department's Antitrust Division won't oppose the acquisition of Dresher by Leggett & Platt Inc. Leggett, a maker of furniture and bed parts, launched the tender offer for the maker of brass beds early last month and had extended its $2.50-a-share offer to yesterday pending the agency's decision.
