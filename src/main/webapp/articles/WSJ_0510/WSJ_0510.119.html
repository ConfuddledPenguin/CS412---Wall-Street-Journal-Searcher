Aca Joe Franchisees Sue Company, Alleging Deceptive Practice

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Aca Joe Inc. said a group of current and former franchisees of the apparel company filed a lawsuit against it in federal court here. The suit charges the company deceived the plaintiffs by indicating that the company wished to continue operating franchise outlets, when the company allegedly had decided to switch to wholesaling operations. Aca Joe labeled the suit as "without merit," and said it intends to conduct a vigorous defense.

The suit alleges breach of contract, fraudulent inducement, and violations of the Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations act.
