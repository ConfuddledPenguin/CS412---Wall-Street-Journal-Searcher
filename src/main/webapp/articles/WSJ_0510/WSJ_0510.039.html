Who's News: Schering-Plough Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

SCHERING-PLOUGH Corp. (Madison, N.J.) -- Richard E. Whittaker was named to the new position of senior vice president, marketing and sales, for over-the-counter and seasonal operations of this pharmaceutical and consumer products company's Schering-Plough Health-Care Products unit. Mr. Whittaker was a vice president of marketing at Johnson & Johnson Consumer Products Inc.
