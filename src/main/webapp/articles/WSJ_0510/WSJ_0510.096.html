British Prices Seen Rising

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

LONDON -- Britain's retail price index for April is expected to have risen 3.3% from March and 9.7% from a year ago, according to a survey of 10 economists. That would compare with March's monthly rise of 1% and March's year-on-year rise of 8.1%.

The official April figures are scheduled to be released Friday.

If the economists' projections are accurate, Britain's inflation rate in April reached its highest year-on-year level since March 1982, when it touched 10.4%, according to Yamaichi International (Europe) Ltd.
