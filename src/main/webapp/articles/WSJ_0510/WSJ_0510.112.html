Kroger Co. Near Labor Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

CINCINNATI -- Kroger Co. said it reached a tentative agreement on a new three-year labor contract for 8,500 clerks and meat cutters in the company's King Soopers division in Denver. Employees are voting on the contract. The supermarket chain said 6,000 retail clerks and meat cutters in Dallas-Fort Worth completed ratification of a new three-year contract last week.

Both contracts call for incentive compensation. No other details were released.
