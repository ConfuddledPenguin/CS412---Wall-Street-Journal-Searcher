Kustom Electronics Accord

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

OVERLAND PARK, Kan. -- Kustom Electronics Inc. said its board approved a letter of intent from Triax International Inc. to purchase Kustom's traffic safety unit. The offer from Triax and William E. Ruppert, an official at that company, to acquire substantially all assets and liabilities of the Kustom unit is for $4.3 million cash and a $700,000 promissory note to be paid over three years.

The proposed sale is subject to contingencies, including the buyers' financing and regulatory approval.
