Metro Air Traffic Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

DALLAS -- Metro Airlines said its April traffic rose 42%, to 52.4 million revenue passenger miles from 37 million in the year-earlier period. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, increased to 54.8% from 48.9%.

For the year to date, Metro flew 188.3 million revenue passenger miles, a 47% increase from 128.4 million a year ago. Load factor increased to 51.5% from 48% last year.
