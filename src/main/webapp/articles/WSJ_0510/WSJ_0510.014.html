Who's News: Owens-Corning Fiberglas Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

OWENS-CORNING FIBERGLAS Corp. (Toledo, Ohio) -- Sharell L. Mikesell, 46 years old, was named vice president, technology, for this maker of glass fiber products. Mr. Mikesell will oversee research, development and corporate engineering, and join the company's policy-setting management council. He succeeds Michael G. Griffith, who joined another company.
