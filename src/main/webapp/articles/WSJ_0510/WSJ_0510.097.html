Who's News: Hollywood Park Operating Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

HOLLYWOOD PARK OPERATING Co. (Inglewood, Calif.) -- Marvin Davis, citing time constraints, resigned from the board of this race-track operator said. Mr. Davis, 64 years old, is a private investor in energy and real-estate businesses, and is former owner of Twentieth Century Fox Film Corp. His resignation reduces the size of Hollywood Park's board to 10 members.
