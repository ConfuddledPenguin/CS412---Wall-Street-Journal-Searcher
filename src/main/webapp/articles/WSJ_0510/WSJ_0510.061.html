Sigma Designs Profit Forecast

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

FREMONT, Calif. -- Sigma Designs Inc. said profit for the fiscal-first quarter ended April 30 was "significantly below" the $1.6 million, or 27 cents a share, reported for the fiscal-fourth quarter ended Jan. 31. Sales of approximately $10 million fell below the $12 million posted in the fourth quarter, the computer-graphics company said. In last year's first quarter, Sigma posted profit of $3.1 million, or 49 cents a share, on sales of about $26 million.

The company blamed the unfavorable forecast on a reduction in revenue from Zenith Data Systems and weak demand from other customers.
