LawnAmerica Seeks Protection

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

TULSA, Okla. -- LawnAmerica Inc., a lawn care company, said it filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. The filing, made in federal bankruptcy court for the Northern District of Oklahoma, allows the company to continue operating while it tries to work out a plan to pay its debts.
