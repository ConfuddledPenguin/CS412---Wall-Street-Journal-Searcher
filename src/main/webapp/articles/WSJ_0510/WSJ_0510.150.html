Business Brief -- BCE Inc.: Montreal Trustco Seeking Commercial Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

Montreal Trustco Inc., a unit of BCE Inc., said it will offer approximately 37 million Canadian dollars (US$31.8 million) for all the shares outstanding of Commercial Financial Corp. Montreal Trustco said Commercial Financial holders with a combined 59% stake in the Toronto financial-services concern agreed to tender to Montreal Trustco's C$5.20-a-share bid, which is to be made before June 15. BCE is a Montreal telecommunications, manufacturing, energy and financial-services concern.
