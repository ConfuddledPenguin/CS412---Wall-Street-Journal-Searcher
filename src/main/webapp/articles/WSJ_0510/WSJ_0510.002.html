Greiner Gets Airport Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

DALLAS -- Greiner Engineering Inc. said it signed a contract with the government of Hong Kong to serve as planning and engineering consultant for the development of a $4.1 billion airport in Hong Kong. The planning and design of the project will proceed on an accelerated schedule so the facility can open in early 1997, the engineering and architectural services firm said. Terms of the contract weren't disclosed.
