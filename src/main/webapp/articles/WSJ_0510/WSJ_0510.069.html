Who's News: Savin Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

SAVIN Corp. (Stamford, Conn.) -- Robert J. Luth was named executive vice president and chief financial officer of this marketer of copiers and facsimile machines. Mr. Luth, 46, most recently was an independent consultant serving clients in the office-products industry. He also has been vice president of finance and chief financial officer of Telesphere International Inc. He succeeds Norman L. Harritt, 49, who resigned in October as executive vice president and chief financial and administrative officer. The administrative chief's responsibilities have been reassigned. Savin is 55%-owned by HCS Technology N.V., a Dutch office-machines marketer.
