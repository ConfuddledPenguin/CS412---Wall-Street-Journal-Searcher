Business Brief -- Storage Technology Corp.: Concern to File Statement With SEC for an Offering

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

Storage Technology Corp. said it intends to file a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a planned offering of $125 million of convertible subordinated debentures due 2015. The Louisville, Colo., maker of data-storage products said it expects to use a substantial amount of the proceeds from the planned offering to make additional redemptions or purchases of its 13.5% senior debentures due 1996.
