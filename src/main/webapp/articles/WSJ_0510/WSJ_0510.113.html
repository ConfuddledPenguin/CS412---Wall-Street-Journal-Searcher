International Brief -- Jefferson Smurfit Group PLC: Pretax Profit Increased 4.2% During Latest Fiscal Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

Jefferson Smurfit Group PLC said pretax profit for the fiscal year ended Jan. 31, edged up a meager 4.2% amid difficult trading conditions in Latin America and a lack of substantial price increases in its core businesses. Pretax profit at the Dublin, Ireland, paper and packaging concern rose to 246 million punts ($398.3 million) from 236 million punts in the year-ago period, while sales increased 21% to 1.66 billion punts from 1.37 billion punts.

A soft paper industry in the U.S. helped trim profit growth for the company's biggest-earning region, said Smurfit's secretary, Michael Pettigrew. In Latin America, pretax profit dipped 1.8% to 58.6 million punts from 59.7 million punts a year ago. Pressures on certain Latin American economies, particularly Venezuela's, curbed profit growth, Mr. Pettigrew said.
