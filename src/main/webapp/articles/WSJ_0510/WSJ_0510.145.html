China's Industrial Output Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

BEIJING -- Chinese industrial production picked up in April after a stagnant first quarter, with a 6.8% increase in output over March, the State Statistical Bureau announced yesterday. Compared with April 1989, however, this April's output of 164.6 billion renmimbi ($34.87 billion) was up only 2%, the official Xinhua News Agency quoted bureau officials as saying.

Chinese industry is reeling from the impact of a government austerity program that sharply cut official spending, new construction and loans. More than 3 million small rural factories shut down and numerous urban factories either closed or sharply cut production.
