Business Brief -- BSN Corp.: Investor Group May Seek To Acquire Managers' Stock

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

BSN Corp. said a Houston investor group including Pelham Investment Partners L.P. expressed interest in acquiring all BSN shares owned by management "at prices significantly above the current market price." But Michael Blumenfeld, president of the Dallas sporting-goods maker, said managers "are unwilling to pursue any proposal for our shares or control of BSN when a definitive operating plan by the potential acquirers has not been received or discussed."

In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the Pelham group said it is in "preliminary discussions" with Mr. Blumenthal, who owns or controls about 15%, of BSN. The group, which said it holds 6.63% of BSN, is interested in possibly buying "a substantial block" of BSN common, but less than a majority of the total outstanding. If it buys a block of shares, the group said it will seek representation on and may seek control of BSN's board.
