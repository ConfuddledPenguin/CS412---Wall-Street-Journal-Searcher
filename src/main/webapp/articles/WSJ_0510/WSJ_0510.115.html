Business Brief -- Grumman Corp.: Debt Ratings Are Lowered Due to Industry Conditions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

Moody's Investors Service Inc. said it downgraded its ratings on $525 million of Grumman Corp.'s senior and subordinated debt and preferred stock. The rating of Grumman's senior and subordinated debt fell to Baa-3 from Baa-2 and to Ba-2 from Baa-3, respectively. The rating of its preferred stock dropped to Ba-2 from Baa-3.

Moody's said it lowered the ratings because it expected unfavorable conditions in the defense industry to continue to pressure the Bethpage, N.Y., military contractor's cash flow for servicing debt.

Grumman declined to comment.
