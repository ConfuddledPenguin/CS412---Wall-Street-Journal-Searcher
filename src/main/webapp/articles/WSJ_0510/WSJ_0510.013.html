Who's News: First Bank System Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

FIRST BANK SYSTEM Inc. (Minneapolis) -- The banking concern said Daniel C. Rohr will join the company as an executive vice president and manager of the commercial banking group, effective May 21. Mr. Rohr will become part of the management team of the new chairman, president and chief executive officer, John F. Grundhofer. Mr. Rohr had been executive vice president and chief credit officer since 1988 at Columbia Savings & Loan Association in Beverly Hills, Calif.
