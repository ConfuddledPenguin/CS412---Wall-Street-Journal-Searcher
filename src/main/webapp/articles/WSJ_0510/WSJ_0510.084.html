Firan to Buy Alltel Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

HUDSON, Ohio -- Alltel Corp. said Toronto-based Firan Corp. signed a definitive agreement to acquire its Denro Inc. unit with members of Denro's current management. Terms weren't released, but Alltel said it won't record a material gain or loss on the sale. Denro, based in Gaithersburg, Md., manufactures communications switching equipment and systems for air traffic control.

Alltel, which provides local telephone service to more than 1.1 million customers in 25 states, had previously announced plans to sell its manufacturing operations.

Firan is a diversified manufacturing company. The acquisition is subject to regulatory approvals. It is expected to be completed on or near June 30.
