Rexene Joint Venture

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

DALLAS -- Rexene Corp. said it agreed to form a joint venture with Tokyo-based UBE Industries Ltd. to make and market a Rexene polymer in the Far East and Pacific Rim. Rexene, a chemicals maker, said the venture will initially produce and market 30,000 tons per year of amorphous polyalphaolefin, a patented polymer used as a component in adhesives and sealants.

UBE Industries is a diversified company with interests in plastics, rubber, chemicals, cement, machinery, plant engineering and coal.
