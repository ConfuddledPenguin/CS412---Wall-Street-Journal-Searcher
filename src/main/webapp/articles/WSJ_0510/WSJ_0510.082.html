America West Airlines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines said April traffic climbed 55.6%, to 899.5 million revenue passenger miles, from 578.1 million revenue passenger miles in the year-earlier period. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

Load factor, or percentage of seats filled, rose to 63%, from 53.6%.

For the year to date, America West said it flew 3.45 billion revenue passenger miles, up 40.3% from 2.46 billion revenue passenger miles a year ago. Load factor was 62.2%, compared with 57.7%.
