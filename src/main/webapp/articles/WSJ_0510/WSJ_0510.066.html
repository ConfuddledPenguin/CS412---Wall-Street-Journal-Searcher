Who's News: Infotechnology Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

INFOTECHNOLOGY Inc. (New York) -- The United Press International news-wire-service subsidiary appointed Pieter VanBennekom to the new post of executive vice president of editorial operations. Mr. VanBennekom, 44 years old, had been senior vice president of international operations for the unit. Some of the responsibilities in Mr. VanBennekom's new position were handled by James L. Whyte, 62, who remains executive vice president for news. Mr. Whyte will focus on building Washington-based UPI's project called electronic-news exchanges, in which the wire service and participating newspapers will exchange news reports. Infotechnology has information and communications interests, including 47% control of Financial News Network Inc.
