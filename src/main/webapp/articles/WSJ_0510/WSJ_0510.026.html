Dividend News: Mission-Valley Bancorp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

MISSION-VALLEY BANCORP., Pleasanton, Calif., said its directors approved an increase in the annual cash dividend to 20 cents a share from 16 cents, with the first semiannual dividend of 10 cents a share to be paid July 3 to shares of record June 13.
