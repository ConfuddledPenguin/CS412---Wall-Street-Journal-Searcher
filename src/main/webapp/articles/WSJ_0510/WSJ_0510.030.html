Who's News: Turbo Resources Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

TURBO RESOURCES Ltd. (Calgary, Alberta) -- V. Glenn Sundstrom was appointed president and chief executive officer of this financially ailing petroleum refining and marketing concern, succeeding Robert G. Brodie, who resigned yesterday. Mr. Sundstrom, a Turbo director since 1986, was previously a marketing and refining executive with Calgary-based Petro Canada Inc., a major integrated oil and gas concern. Mr. Brodie, who also resigned from his post as chairman, is returning to Vancouver to pursue certain business interests, the company said; those interests weren't specified. Mr. Brodie wasn't available to elaborate. Turbo said Andrew Janisch, president and chief executive of Quintana Exploration Canada Ltd., a small Calgary-based oil and gas concern, succeeds Mr. Brodie as chairman.
