  • Date: 05/10/90

TIME WARNER Inc. (New York) -- The publishing and entertainment company's Home Box Office Inc. cable-television subsidiary named Sheila Shayon to the new post of vice president of special projects, reporting directly to Michael Fuchs, the unit's chairman and chief executive officer. Most recently, she was director of special projects for HBO.
