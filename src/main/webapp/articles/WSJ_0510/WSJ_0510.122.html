Kinetic Concepts Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

SAN ANTONIO, Texas -- Kinetic Concepts Inc. said it agreed to purchase Medirec, a closely held Salt Lake City concern, for $26 million in cash and $10 million in securities. Medirec is a medical equipment rental concern that had revenue of about $42.5 million last year. Kinetic Concepts designs, manufactures and rents specialized beds.
