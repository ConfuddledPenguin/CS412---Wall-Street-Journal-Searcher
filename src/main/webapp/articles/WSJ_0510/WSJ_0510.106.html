International Brief -- Trafalgar House PLC: Pretax Profit Rose by 2.7% In the Latest Six Months

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

Trafalgar House PLC, a British construction and contracting concern, Wednesday reported a 2.7% rise in pretax profit for the six months ended March 31, with property and investments the only division showing a decline in earnings. Pretax profit for the half year to March 31 rose to #116.9 million ($195.2 million) from #113.8 million in the corresponding year-earlier period, hitting the midrange of market expectations.

Profit after tax but before extraordinary items was #93.4 million, compared with #90 million a year earlier. In the current interim period, Trafalgar House took a #15.2 million extraordinary loss, compared with a #2 million extraordinary loss in the year-earlier period.

Profit attributable to shareholders stood at #77.7 million, down from #87.5 million.

The company said its commercial and residential property interests are in "the doldrums for the time being" because of harsh U.K. economic conditions.
