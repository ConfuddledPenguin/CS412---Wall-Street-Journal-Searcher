Who's News: Computer Sciences Taps Crowley to Head CSC Consulting Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/10/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Computer Sciences Corp. said Paul J. Crowley was named president of CSC Consulting, the commercial-consulting unit of the computer-services company. Mr. Crowley, 59 years old, who was also elected a corporate vice president, succeeds Thomas P. Gerrity, who last month was named dean of the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School.

In an organizational change, CSC Consulting's European operations will report to company Chairman William R. Hoover rather than to the consulting group.

Mr. Crowley was chairman of CSC Partners Inc., a Waltham, Mass., consulting concern, which he co-founded in 1976 and was acquired by CSC Consulting in 1986.

The company also named James A. Champy senior vice president of CSC Consulting, a newly created position. Mr. Champy, 48, will continue to serve as chairman of the Index Group, the Cambridge, Mass., consulting concern founded by Mr. Gerrity and acquired in 1988 by CSC Consulting.
