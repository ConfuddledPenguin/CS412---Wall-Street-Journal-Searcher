Business Brief -- Lexington Savings Bank: Waltham Unit to Acquire Concern for $25.5 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

Waltham Corp. said its Waltham Savings Bank unit signed a definitive agreement to acquire Lexington Savings Bank for $10.75 a share, or about $25.5 million. The completion of the purchase is subject to regulatory approval, approval of Lexington's holders and certain conditions based on Lexington's net worth and nonperforming assets, Waltham, Mass.-based Waltham said.

Under the agreement, Lexington will pay its previously declared dividend of 10 cents a share on May 9, but will not pay further dividends, Waltham said.

As of March 31, Waltham Savings Bank had total assets of $535 million; Lexington, based in Lexington, Mass., had total assets of $222 million.
