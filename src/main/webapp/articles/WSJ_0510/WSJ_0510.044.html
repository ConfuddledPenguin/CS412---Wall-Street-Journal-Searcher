Who's News: Merrill Lynch & Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/10/90

MERRILL LYNCH & Co. (New York) -- Michael G. Philipp joins the securities firm's Merrill Lynch Capital Markets unit as managing director for the Institutional Financial Futures and Options business, effective June 4. Mr. Philipp, 37 years old, was vice president of international fixed income and derivative sales at securities firm Goldman, Sachs & Co. The Merrill post has been vacant for some time. Mr. Philipp's responsibilities were created out of a reorganization, a spokesman said.
