Computer Sciences Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

EL SEGUNDO, Calif. -- Computer Sciences Corp. said it received a $48 million contract from the General Services Administration to provide computer system development and software support services. The contract, awarded to the company's Applied Technology division in Falls Church, Va., covers an initial one-year period with three one-year options. Computer Sciences said the systems would be used for scientific and engineering applications by various U.S. government agencies.
