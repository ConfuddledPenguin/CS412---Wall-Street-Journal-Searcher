Steel Production Rose By 0.7% Last Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 0.7% last week to 1,858,000 tons from 1,845,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output fell 0.2% from 1,862,000 tons a year earlier. The industry used 83.3% of its capability last week, compared with 82.7% the previous week and 83.8% a year ago.
