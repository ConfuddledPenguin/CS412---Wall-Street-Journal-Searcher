REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Iraq's Benefit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

There's at least one silver lining to the tumult in the Middle East over Saddam Hussein's takeover of Kuwait. For awhile, we don't have to endure yet more press accounts and official pronouncements about how the West Bank is the source of all the problems that attend this region. After the sands fold over the Iraq problem, it'll be interesting to see if this small strip of land re-emerges to dominate thinking about the area.
