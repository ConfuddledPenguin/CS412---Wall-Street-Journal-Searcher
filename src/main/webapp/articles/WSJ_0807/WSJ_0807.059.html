North Coast Energy's Reserves

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

CLEVELAND -- North Coast Energy Inc. reported a significant increase in total oil and gas reserves for its year ended March 31. The energy concern said total reserves increased 72% to 30.4 billion cubic feet, of which only 34% are developed. All of North Coast reserves are domestic.

North Coast had a loss of $583,634, or 23 cents a share, in its first year as a public company, compared with a loss of $3 million, or $1.17 a share, a year earlier. Revenue fell to $5.4 million from $6 million.
