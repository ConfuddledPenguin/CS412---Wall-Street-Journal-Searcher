Letters to the Editor: Coming to Grips With Lying Lips

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

Those evil Democrats] With their "sonic boom of elation" at the president's change of lips, they gave your editorial writer an apoplectic attack ("Lipping It Up," June 28). I am sure that the resulting high blood pressure blurred his vision and he didn't notice that, in his written statement, the president never bothered to admit that he duped the American people and is now hiding behind the "bipartisan leadership." To write that "the Democratic Party exists now for the purpose of raising taxes" is sheer demagoguery. Anyway, if the Democrats are not going to raise taxes and generate revenue, how are you going to pay for all the B-2s that George Bush wants to build, the new fighter planes, Star Wars deployment, etc., etc.? How are you going to take care of all those S&L payments that will come due over the next 30 years? Cut Medicare you say? Well, I don't know. There are thousands of doctors out there, hundreds of businesses running hospitals; they make a good living off Medicare and they probably vote Republican.

I think you have a problem. And you can't let the deficit grow indefinitely or the Japanese will stop lending us money. What to do? How about joining the Democrats in writing a pragmatic, responsible budget and ensuring that the money is spent wisely and honestly. No more S&Ls, no more HUDs, no more defense scandals. Simple, isn't it?

Mack Suliteanu

Portola Valley, Calif.
