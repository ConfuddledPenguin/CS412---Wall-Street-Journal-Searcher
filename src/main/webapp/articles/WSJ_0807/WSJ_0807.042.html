Rexene Corp. to Buy Back Notes

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

DALLAS -- Rexene Corp. said its board authorized the maker of plastics products to use cash on hand to purchase its Series A senior guaranteed increasing-rate notes, due 1992, of which about $264 million remain outstanding after previous buy-backs. In its most recent quarterly earnings report, the company said it had available cash of about $80 million. Rexene also said it is continuing to evaluate alternatives for refinancing the Series A notes.
