Wyndham Foods Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Wyndham Foods Inc. said it completed the sale of the company, including its large baking company unit, to President Enterprises Corp. of Taiwan in a transaction valued at $335 million. Closely held Wyndham is considered one of the largest cookie makers in the U.S. President Enterprises is Taiwan's largest integrated food company with more than $1 billion in annual revenue.
