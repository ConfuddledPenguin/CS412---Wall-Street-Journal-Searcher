MAZDA MOTOR Corp. sold 120,030 imported cars in the Jan. 1 to July 30 period. For the period, Mazda's total car sales were 163,170 vehicles and its combined car and truck sales were 214,079 vehicles. These statistics were misstated in the car sales table in yesterday's edition because of incorrect information provided by Mazda. A corrected market share table appears {in this issue}. (See: "Economy: Domestic Auto Sales in July Increased by 1% --- General Motors Corp. Posts 2.3% Rise During Period; Still Uncertainty Looms" -- WSJ Aug. 6, 1990)
