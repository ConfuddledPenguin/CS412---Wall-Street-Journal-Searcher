Who's News: Microdyne Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

MICRODYNE Corp. (Ocala, Fla.) -- Robert K. Michaels, formerly chief financial officer of Generex Corp. in Cape Canaveral, Fla., was named vice president and chief financial officer of this supplier of telemetry and other communications equipment. He succeeds Eugene Day, who resigned.
