Who's News: Enzon Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

ENZON Inc. (South Plainfield, N.J.) -- This biopharmaceutical company named three directors, expanding board membership to seven: A.M. MacKinnon, 66 years old, former president and chief operating officer of Ciba-Geigy Corp.; Richard Cooper, M.D., 53, a hematologist, professor, dean and executive vice president of the Medical College of Wisconsin; and Norman Gross, 62, president of Norman Gross Associates management consultants.
