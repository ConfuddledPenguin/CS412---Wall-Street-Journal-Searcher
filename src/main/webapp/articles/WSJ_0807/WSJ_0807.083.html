The following were among yesterday's offerings and pricings in U.S. and non-U.S. capital markets, with terms and syndicate manager, as compiled by Dow Jones Capital Markets Report. MUNICIPAL

Maricopa County, Ariz. -- $75 million of Series 1990C general obligation bonds, via a Marine Midland Capital Markets Corp. group. The bonds, rated double-A-1/double-A, were priced for reoffering to yield from 6.65% in 1999 to 6.90% in 2003.

MORTGAGE

Federal National Mortgage Association -- $750 million issue of Remic mortgage securities offered in 21 classes by Bear Stearns & Co. The offering, Series 1990-103, uses Fannie Mae 9 1/2% securities backed by 30-year fixed-rate mortgages as collateral. Price and yield data weren't immediately available.

EUROBONDS

There were no major European debt issues priced yesterday.
