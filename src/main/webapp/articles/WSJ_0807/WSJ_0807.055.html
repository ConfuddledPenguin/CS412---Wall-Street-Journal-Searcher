Bank Hires Adviser for Unit Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Empire of America has retained the investment banking firm of Cohane Rafferty Securities Inc. to sell its Empire of America Realty Credit Corp. unit. The unit, which employs 475 people, has a $7.1 billion portfolio. Empire of America, a bank with $10 billion in assets, has been placed in receivership and is under the management of the federal government's bailout agency, the Resolution Trust Corp.
