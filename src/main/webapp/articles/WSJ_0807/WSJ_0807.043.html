Who's News: Chubb Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

CHUBB Corp. (Warren, N.J.) -- Robert Rusis was named senior vice president and general counsel at this insurance holding company, effective Aug. 1. Mr. Rusis, 56 years old, previously was senior vice president and general counsel at Chubb & Son Inc., the insurance underwriting division. He is succeeded by John J. Degnan, 45, previously a partner at Shanley & Fisher, a law firm in Morristown, N.J.
