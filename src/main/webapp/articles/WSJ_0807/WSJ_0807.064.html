UNSL Financial Plans Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

LEBANON, Mo. -- UNSL Financial Corp. said it approved the buy-back of as many as 150,000 shares of common stock. UNSL has 1.6 million shares outstanding. The buy-back will be made in the open market and in privately negotiated transactions. The shares will be added to the treasury and held for general corporate uses.
