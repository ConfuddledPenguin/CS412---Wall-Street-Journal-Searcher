Who's News: Prudential Insurance Co. of America

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

PRUDENTIAL INSURANCE CO. OF AMERICA (Newark, N.J.) -- At the Prudential-Bache Securities Inc. unit of this insurer, Erica Jo Mann, previously vice president at Lowe & Partners, a New York advertising agency, was named first vice president and director of advertising and marketing research, filling a vacancy at the New York-based investment firm.
