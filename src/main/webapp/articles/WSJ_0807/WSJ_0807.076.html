Union Carbide Buys Norkool

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

DANBURY, Conn. -- Union Carbide Corp. said it acquired Quantum Chemical Corp.'s Norkool Industrial Products, primarily a maker of industrial coolants. Terms weren't disclosed. The chemical and plastics maker said the acquisition will broaden its customer base in heat transfer fluids and provide new technology for corrosion inhibitors and specialty cleaners.
