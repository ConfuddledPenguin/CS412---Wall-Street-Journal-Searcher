Who's News: Unitrode Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

UNITRODE Corp. (Billerica, Mass.) -- Cosmo S. Trapani, 51 years old, was named chief financial officer and vice president of this maker of electronic parts, effective Monday. He succeeded Walter B. Gates, 59 years old, who resigned as senior vice president of finance, chief financial officer and a director "to pursue new interests," Unitrode said. The company said Mr. Gates will be staying with the company "over the next couple of months" during a transition period. Mr. Gates wasn't available for comment. Mr. Trapani was previously vice president of finance and treasurer of Instron Corp., a Canton, Mass., maker of instruments for advanced materials testing.
