Cleveland-Cliffs Projects Profit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

CLEVELAND -- Cleveland-Cliffs Inc. said it expects to post a third-quarter gain of $15 million, or $1.28 a share, from the sale of assets. The iron ore concern said it entered into an agreement to sell its forest lands and related assets in Michigan's Upper Pennisula to Ben Benson, a businessman in Accomac, Va., for about $25 million. Cleveland-Cliffs, which will retain mineral interest on the land, said the sale completes its exit from the forest product business.

A year earlier, Cleveland-Cliffs earned $16.5 million, or $1.42 a share, including a $1 million loss from discontinued operations. Revenue was $95 million.
