GE Unit Buys Financing Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- The GE Capital financial services arm of General Electric Co. said it completed the purchase of LeaseAmerica, a Cedar Rapids, Iowa, lease financing firm for dealers of office equipment and health-care products, from Aegon USA Inc., a financial services concern owned by Aegon N.V. of the Netherlands. Terms weren't disclosed. LeaseAmerica will be part of GE Capital Vendor Equipment Financing.
