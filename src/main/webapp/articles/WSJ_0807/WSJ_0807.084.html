Burmah Castrol PLC

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

LONDON -- Burmah Castrol PLC, a British petroleum producing and refining concern, said it sold its 29.7% stake in Premier Consolidated Oilfields PLC for 138 million pounds ($256.2 million). The 139 million shares were sold through British stock brokers Kleinwort Benson Securities for 99 pence a share, Burmah said.

Kleinwort Benson refused to comment on whether it has since sold the shares to investors.

Burmah said it sold the stake to raise cash for investment in its international marketing of specialized oil and chemical products. The decision didn't reflect a lack of confidence in Premier's "long-term prospects," Burmah said.
