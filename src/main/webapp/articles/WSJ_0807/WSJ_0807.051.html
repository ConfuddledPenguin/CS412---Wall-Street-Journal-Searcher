Who's News: Investcorp International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

INVESTCORP INTERNATIONAL Inc. -- Arthur Martinez, formerly senior vice president of the Batus Inc. unit of B.A.T Industries PLC of Britain, and Philip Miller, previously chairman and chief executive officer of B.A.T's Marshall Field retailing unit, were named vice chairmen, new posts, at Saks Fifth Avenue, a New York-based retailer owned by this partnership of U.S. and Arabian investors. Mr. Martinez will be responsible for finance and administration, Mr. Miller for marketing, new ventures and future growth; both will sit on the board.
