Kollmorgen to Close Division

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

SIMSBURY, Conn. -- Kollmorgen Corp. said it will close down its multiwire division after an agreement to sell the division to Winthrop Investment Corp. fell through. Kollmorgen, a maker of motors and electro-optical systems, didn't elaborate on why it failed to reach a definitive agreement, after signing a letter of intent for the sale last month. The company said previously that a closing of the division wouldn't significantly affect its results.
