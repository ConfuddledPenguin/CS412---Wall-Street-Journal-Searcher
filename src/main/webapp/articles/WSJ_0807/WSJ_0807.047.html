Who's News: National Gypsum Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

NATIONAL GYPSUM Co. (Dallas) -- Peter C. Browning will become president and chief executive officer of this construction concern and its privately held parent, Aancor Holdings Inc. on Oct. 1. Mr. Browning, 48 years old, succeeds John P. Hayes, who is retiring as chief executive officer of the companies. The president's position is a new post at the companies.
