Who's News: Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

DAI-ICHI KANGYO BANK Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Lawrence A. Marsiello was named president of CIT Group/Factoring, a unit of CIT Group, a subsidiary of this bank. CIT is a provider of financial advisory services based in Livingston, N.J. Mr. Marsiello, 40 years old, was previously executive vice president of CIT's business credit unit. He succeeds John Heffer, who resigned.
