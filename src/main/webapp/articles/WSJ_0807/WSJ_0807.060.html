Unisys Unit Gets Finland Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

WOODCLIFF LAKE, N.J. -- Timplex Inc., a communications networking unit of Unisys Corp., Blue Bell, Pa., said it received a $10 million contract from Helsinki Telephone Co. of Finland for a high-speed telecommunications network, to be used primarily by banks, insurance companies, airlines and computer companies.
