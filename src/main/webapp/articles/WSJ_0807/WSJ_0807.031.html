American Air's Traffic Is Flat

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- American Airlines, a unit of AMR Corp., said its July traffic of 7.24 billion revenue passenger miles was flat with year-earlier traffic. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline's July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 66.8% from 71.6%.

For the first seven months of this year, American's traffic rose 4.3% to 44.16 billion revenue passenger miles from 42.32 billion miles a year earlier. The load factor declined to 62.6% from 64.1%.
