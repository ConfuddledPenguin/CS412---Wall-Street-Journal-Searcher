Taiwan's Trade Surplus

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

TAIPEI, Taiwan -- Taiwan's trade surplus in July totaled $1.47 billion, a decline of 16.4% from a year earlier, the government said. The Directorate General of Budget, Accounting and Statistics said the surplus between January and July rose to $6.41 billion, down 19.4% from a year earlier. The July surplus, however, increased 33.6% from June's surplus level.
