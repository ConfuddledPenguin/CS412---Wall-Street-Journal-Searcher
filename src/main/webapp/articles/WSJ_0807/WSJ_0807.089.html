Redemption Notices

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended Aug. 3, 1990. The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive. Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer. MUNICIPALS

BAY GENERAL COMMUNITY HOSPITAL, will call for redemption its first mortgage hospital revenue bonds, Series 1975, on Sept. 1, 1990. Bankers Trust Co., New York, is paying agent.

EAST PEORIA, ILL., will redeem $30,000 principal amount of its collateralized industrial development revenue bonds, Series 1982, Construction Equipment Credit Union Project, on Sept. 1, 1990. Continental Bank, Chicago, is trustee.

GAITHERSBURG, MD., will call $445,000 of hospital facilities revenue bonds, Series 1979, due Sept. 1, 2009, on Sept. 1, 1990. Maryland National Bank, Baltimore, and First National Bank of St. Paul, Minn., are paying agents.

GRAND TERRACE, CALIF., COMMUNITY REDEVELOPMENT AGENCY will redeem $995,000 principal amount of residential mortgage revenue bonds, 1981 issue, on Sept. 1, 1990. Issues being called are 10.20% bonds due March 1, 1991 and 11.50% bonds due March 1, 2013. Security Pacific National Bank, Los Angeles and Glendale, Calif.; Bankers Trust, New York, and Harris Trust & Savings, Chicago, are paying agents.

MASSACHUSETTS will redeem $44,295,000 principal amount of general obligation bonds, Consolidated Loan of 1980, Series B, due Sept. 1, 1993-2000, on Sept. 1, 1990. Bonds may be redeemed at the State Treasurer's Office, Boston, or through State Street Bank & Trust Co., New York, and First National Bank of Chicago.

SAN DIEGO ARENA LEASE CO. will redeem $3,465,000 of its lease-hold mortgage bonds on Sept. 1, 1990. Bankers Trust Co., New York, is the paying agent.

SANTA FE, N.M., will call 6 3/4% refunding revenue bonds, Series 1978, due Oct. 1, 2001, St. Vincent Hospital Project, on Oct. 1, 1990. Harris Trust & Savings Bank, Chicago, is trustee.

TEXARKANA MEDICAL FACILITIES CORP. will call on Sept. 1, 1990, $205,000 principal amount of its 8.25% first mortgage hospital revenue bonds, Series 1977, due Sept. 1, 1996. State First National Bank, Texarkana, Ark., is trustee.

WAYNE COUNTY, MICH., ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT CORP. will redeem $720,000 of secured project revenue bonds, Grosse Pointe Cable Inc. project, Series 1981, on Sept. 1, 1990. Comerica Bank, Detroit, is trustee.

WINCHESTER, KY., will redeem on Sept. 1, 1990, $2,585,000 principal amount of its 5.50% industrial building revenue bonds, due Sept. 1, 1991. Continental Bank, Chicago, is trustee.

CORPORATES

EUROPEAN INVESTMENT BANK will call for partial redemption on Sept. 15, 1990, $10 million principal amount of its 10.15% bonds, due Sept. 15, 1999. Citibank, New York, is paying agent.

LONG ISLAND LIGHTING CO will call 120,000 shares of $3.31 Series T preferred stock on Sept. 15, 1990. First National Bank of Boston is redemption agent.

TALMAN FINANCE CORP. C, on Sept. 17, 1990, will call for partial redemption 100 shares of market auction preferred stock. Bankers Trust Co., New York, is paying agent.
