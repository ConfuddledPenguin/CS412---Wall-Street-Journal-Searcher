Japan's Imported Vehicle Sales

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

TOKYO -- Sales of imported cars, trucks and buses in Japan climbed 17.1% in July to 20,304 units from 17,339 units a year earlier, the Japan Automobile Importers' Association said. The results mark a record for the month and the 79th consecutive month of year-to-year increases. Import sales have been rising since January 1984. Though 17% growth is high, an association official said, year-to-year increases have been below 20% so far this year, which could make it impossible to reach the association's target of selling 240,000 units in 1990. Japanese dealers sold 180,000 imported vehicles in 1989.
