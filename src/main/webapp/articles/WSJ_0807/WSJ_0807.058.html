Order for Airbus Jets Completed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

PARIS -- Airbus Industrie said Mexicana de Aviacion signed a contract to buy 22 of the European aircraft consortium's A-320 commercial jets and take options on an additional 14 planes, firming up a commitment that was announced in May. Financial details of the order weren't disclosed, but people in the industry estimate the price of one Airbus A-320 jet, without spare parts or training, at $40 million. Delivery of the jets is scheduled to begin during the second half of 1991.
