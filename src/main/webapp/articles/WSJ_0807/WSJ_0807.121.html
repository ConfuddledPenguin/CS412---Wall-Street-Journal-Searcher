Business Brief -- La-Z-Boy Chair Co.: Profit Drop to Be Posted For the Fiscal First Quarter

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

La-Z-Boy Chair Co., Monroe, Mich., said it expects to post a decline in earnings for the first quarter ended July 29, because of weaker industry sales and poor performance by some of the company's operations. Though fiscal first-quarter financial results won't be available until the week of Aug. 20, preliminary indications are for earnings "well below" the year-earlier 13 cents a share, Charles T. Knabusch, chairman and president, said. In the fiscal 1990 first quarter, La-Z-Boy earned $2.3 million on sales of $117.7 million.

La-Z-Boy, a maker of reclining chairs and upholstered furniture, said that unless a "marked improvement in the economy and industry sales" occurs, its fiscal 1991 earnings also will decline from the year-earlier net income of $28.3 million, or $1.58 a share, on sales of $592.3 million.
