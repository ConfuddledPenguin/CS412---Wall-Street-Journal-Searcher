France's Industrial Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

PARIS -- France's index of industrial production in the first quarter rose 1.63% from a year earlier but declined 0.8% from the 1989 fourth quarter on a seasonally adjusted basis, the National Statistics Institute said. The governmental statistics body attributed the decline from the previous quarter to a 6.3% drop in energy-sector activity.
