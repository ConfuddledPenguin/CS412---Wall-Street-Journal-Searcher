Who's News: Memorex Telex N.V.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

MEMOREX TELEX N.V. (Amsterdam) -- David Faulkner, currently chief financial officer, will assume additional responsibility as executive vice president and head of U.S. operations, a new post, at this supplier of computer equipment and accessories. Mr. Faulkner, 50 years old, joined the company in August 1989. Memorex Telex also said Jean-Claude Zanolli, 46, currently an executive vice president, will assume additional responsibility for the business groups, logistics and manufacturing.
