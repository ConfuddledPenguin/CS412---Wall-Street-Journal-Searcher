Amdura to Sell Distribution Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

DENVER -- Amdura Corp. said it reached a definitive agreement to sell a unit, Amdura National Distribution Co., to Our Own Hardware, a privately held hardware cooperative based in Minneapolis. The all-cash transaction is subject to various closing conditions and the approval of the U.S. bankruptcy judge overseeing Amdura's reorganization under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. Amdura and Amdura National filed for reorganization under Chapter 11 on April 2.

A spokesman for Amdura said the purchase price hasn't been disclosed in court documents. Under the terms of the agreement, Our Own would acquire the inventory and South Dakota distribution center of Amdura National, a wholesale hardware distributor that serves Trustworthy hardware stores in the upper Midwest.
