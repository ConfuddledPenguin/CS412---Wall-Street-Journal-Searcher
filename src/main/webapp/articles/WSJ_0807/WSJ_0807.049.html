Who's News: Tokai Bank Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

TOKAI BANK Ltd. (Nagoya, Japan) -- Takashi Motoi was named general manager of regional headquarters for the Americas, and president of Tokai Trust Co. of New York, a subsidiary. Mr. Motoi, 55 years old, will oversee the bank's operations in North and South America. He previously was general manager of Tokai's international banking headquarters in Tokyo, and succeeds Kaneo Takeda, 53, who is returning to Japan as general manager of the Osaka branch.
