Financing Business: MNC Financial Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

MNC FINANCIAL Inc., a Baltimore-based holding company for commercial banks in the Washington-Baltimore area, said it agreed to raise about $180 million through a private placement of convertible preferred stock to Alfred Lerner, a director of MNC who owns 8.9% of its common stock. Under the agreement, other directors may subscribe to as much as 50% of the private placement. MNC said the pact is part of the company's efforts to strengthen its capital position. MNC's debt has been classified as below investment grade, reflecting a downturn in the region's economy and real estate market. According to the agreement, the $180 million of preferred shares will bear 12% quarterly cumulative cash dividends. Each preferred share will be convertible into common stock at a conversion price of $10.75 a common share. The transaction is expected to close this fall, pending a definitive agreement and regulatory approval.
