Who's News: Biocraft Laboratories Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

BIOCRAFT LABORATORIES Inc. (Fair Lawn, N.J.) -- Steven J. Sklar, who is currently vice president and treasurer, was named to the additional post of chief financial officer of this maker of generic pharmaceutical products. Biocraft also named three new vice presidents: Beryl Snyder, 33, currently general counselor; Brian Snyder, 32, currently controller; and Jay Snyder, 32, currently director of research and product development.
