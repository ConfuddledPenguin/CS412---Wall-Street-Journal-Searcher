Business Brief -- Fedders Corp.: Firm Says It Won't Reach Sales Target of $415 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

Fedders Corp. said it won't reach its sales target of $415 million this year because cool spring and summer weather hurt the market for room air conditioners. The Peapack, N.J., company, whose sales totaled $216 million in the first half, didn't elaborate on the statement. Last year, sales were $367.6 million, up 66% from 1988, as net income rose 7.8% to $23.7 million, or $1.28 a share.

Fedders shares fell $1.375 to $9.75 yesterday in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange.
