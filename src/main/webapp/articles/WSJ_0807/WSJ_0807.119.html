Business Brief -- J.C. Penney Co.: Business Services Unit Signs New Contract With Amoco

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

J.C. Penney Co. said its business services unit signed a "major" five-year contract to continue providing point-of-sale authorization of credit cards to Amoco Oil Co. Terms of the contract weren't disclosed. But J.C. Penney Business Services Inc. is the largest provider of point-of-sale authorization services to the petroleum industry, and Amoco accounts for 13% of all retail outlets that use the Penney system.

The original contract had a term of three years, with an option to renew for two years. But with the first three years expired, Amoco chose to renew for five.

In a related agreement, Dallas-based Penney said its J.C. Penney National Bank will continue to provide settlement for all Visa and MasterCard bank card purchases made at 3,000 Amoco service stations.
