US Sprint Wins Four Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/07/90

RESTON, Va. -- US Sprint Communications Co. said it won four contracts totaling $15.5 million to install international data communications networks and equipment. The contracts include an $8 million agreement with Citibank N.A., a unit of Citicorp, to provide a private international data network that would link 149 offices in 79 countries.

US Sprint, a unit of United Telecommunications Inc., Kansas City, Mo., also announced data network agreements for corporate customers in Mexico, South Korea and Taiwan.
