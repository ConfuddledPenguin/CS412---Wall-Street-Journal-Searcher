Loral Corp. Receives Air Force Contract Totaling $22.5 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/07/90

WASHINGTON -- Loral Corp. received a $22.5 million Air Force contract for material used in the manufacture of radar warning receivers for F-15 aircraft.

Magnavox Electronics, a subsidiary of North American Philips Corp., got a $17.7 million Navy contract for sonobuoys. North American Philips is a unit of N.V. Philips.

Lockheed Corp. was awarded a $13.3 million increase to an Air Force contract for tanker-rescue aircraft for the Air National Guard.

McDonnell Douglas Corp. won an $11.9 million Navy contract for spare parts for the F-A-18 fighter jet.

Norden Systems Inc., a unit of United Technologies Corp., got an $11.7 million Navy contract for 19 naval detection and tracking systems for various ships.

Turbocombuster Technology, a unit of Sequa Corp., was awarded an $11.4 million Air Force pact for external nozzles for engines used on F-15 aircraft.
