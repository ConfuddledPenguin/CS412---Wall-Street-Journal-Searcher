Average Interest Rates Fell At Citicorp Weekly Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 7.793% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 7.8192% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $410 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.789% to 7.797%.

Citicorp said that the average rate fell to 7.7186% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 7.728% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $370 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 7.698% to 7.73%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
