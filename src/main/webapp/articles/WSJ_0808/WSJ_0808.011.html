Du Pont Opens Plant in Turkey

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

WILMINGTON, Del. -- Du Pont Co. said it started up an industrial nylon fiber plant in Izmit, Turkey, under a 50-50 joint venture with Sabanci Group, a Turkish industrial company. The facility is part of Du Pont's plan to double its business in Europe by the mid-1990s. With a capacity of 50 million pounds a year, the plant will produce nylon 6,6 for tire reinforcement and other industrial applications. Commercial shipments to Europe and the Middle East are scheduled to begin in mid-August.
