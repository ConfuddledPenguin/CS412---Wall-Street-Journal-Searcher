Vintage Petroleum Stock Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Vintage Petroleum Inc. said it completed its initial public offering of four million common shares at $10.50 each. Existing holders continue to own about 12 million shares. The energy company said net proceeds of about $32.8 million will be used to acquire oil and gas properties and to reduce debt.
