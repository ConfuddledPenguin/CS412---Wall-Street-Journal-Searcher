Who's News: St. Paul Cos.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

ST. PAUL Cos. (St. Paul, Minn.) -- Ian A. Martin, a director of Grand Metropolitan PLC and head of its U.S. operations, and Lawrence E. Honig, vice chairman of May Department Stores Co., were named directors of this insurance company, filling vacancies on the 15-member board.
