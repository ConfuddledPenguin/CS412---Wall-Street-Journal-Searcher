Reliance Plans Share Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- Reliance Group Holdings Inc. said it plans to buy 700,000 of its 74.6 million shares outstanding from time to time in the open market. The insurance, real estate and investment company, controlled by Saul Steinberg, said the shares will be contributed to employee benefit plans.

Reliance shares closed yesterday at $5.25 a share in composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, up 12.5 cents a share.
