Who's News: Waste Management Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

WASTE MANAGEMENT Inc. (Oak Brook, Ill.) -- Joan Z. Bernstein, vice president and general counsel of Chemical Waste Management Inc., a subsidiary of Waste Managment, was named to the additional post of vice president of environmental policy and ethical standards at the parent company. She will be reponsible for the company's compliance with environmental standards and for implementation of its ethical training program.
