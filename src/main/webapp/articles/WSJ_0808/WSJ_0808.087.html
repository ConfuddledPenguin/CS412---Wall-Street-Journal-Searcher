Who's News: Sundstrand Corp. Names Three Officers To Oversee Operations

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

ROCKFORD, Ill. -- Sundstrand Corp. named three officers executive vice presidents, with most of the aerospace and industrial-product concern's operations reporting to one of the three. Paul Donovan, formerly a vice president and chief financial officer, was given the added duties of overseeing administration, personnel and public relations.

Robert Smuland, formerly a group vice president in charge of a portion of the aerospace group, was placed in charge of all of the group.

Berger Wallin, formerly a group vice president in charge of the industrial products group, was given the added duty of overseeing all environmental matters.

The three report to Harry Stonecipher, president and chief executive officer.

Also, Gary Hedges, formerly a vice president in the advanced technology group, was named a vice president of Sundstrand in charge of personnel and public relations, succeeding Phil Polgreen, who is retiring in January.
