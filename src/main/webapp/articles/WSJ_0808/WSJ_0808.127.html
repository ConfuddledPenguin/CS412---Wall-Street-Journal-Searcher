International Brief -- British Airways PLC: Pretax Profit for Quarter Rose 62% to $291.9 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

British Airways PLC reported that pretax profit for the first quarter ended June 30 rose 62% to #156 million ($291.9 million) from #96 million a year earlier, despite a slower rise in sales and the strong pound's adverse effect on the carrier's international earnings. The results were well above market expectations for pretax profit of #135 million. Profit after taxes, or that attributable to shareholders, increased 79% to #111 million from #62 million. Per-share earnings on a fully diluted basis rose 54% to 12.9 pence from 8.4 pence. Operating profit eased 0.8% to #123 million from #124 million. Pretax earnings were enhanced by net of #50 million in income from interest and asset disposals, compared with a year-earlier net loss of #1 million.

Sales increased 14% to #1.33 billion from #1.17 billion.
