Heritage Media Gets Financing

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

DALLAS -- Heritage Media Corp. said it obtained financing of $220 million and used a portion of the loans to refinance existing debts of its broadcast and Act Media units. The owner of television and radio stations said it expects to use the remainder of the financing to fund its pending acquisition of the 43% of POP Radio Corp. it doesn't already own. Heritage's current acquisition offer expires Oct. 31.
