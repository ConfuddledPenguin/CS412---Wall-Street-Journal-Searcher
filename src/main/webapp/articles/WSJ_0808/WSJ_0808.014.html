Bayly Corp. Retains Bear Stearns

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

DENVER -- Bayly Corp. said it retained Bear, Stearns & Co. as a financial adviser to help the company review options including a merger or recapitalization. "We're not ruling anything out," said George K. Hansen, chairman and chief executive officer of the apparel manufacturer. "This has been a troubled company for two years now."
