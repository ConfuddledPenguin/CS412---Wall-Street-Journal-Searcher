Charles Schwab Plans Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Charles Schwab Corp. said it authorized a stock buy-back of as many as an additional 750,000 shares of common stock based on prevailing market conditions. The company said it has just successfully completed a stock buy-back of one million shares of stock under previous authorization by its board. Currently, the discount brokerage and financial services concern, through its main subsidiary, Charles Schwab Co., has 25.2 million shares of stock outstanding, as of July 31.
