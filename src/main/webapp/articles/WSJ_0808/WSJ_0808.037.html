New Jersey Natural Gas Filing

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

WALL, N.J. -- New Jersey Natural Gas Co. said it filed with the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities for a $14.8 million, or 4.5%, rate increase to cover higher operating costs, financing and capital construction expenses. At the same time, the utility said it expects to issue a $14.5 million refund to customers toward the end of the year, because of a refund it expects to receive from a natural gas supplier.
