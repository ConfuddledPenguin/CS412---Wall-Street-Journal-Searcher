Business Brief -- CalFed Inc.: Unit to Acquire Branches Of Failed Empire Federal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

California Federal Bank, Los Angeles, said it agreed to acquire 13 Southern California branches and $265 million in deposits from failed Empire Federal Savings Bank of America. Terms weren't disclosed. The CalFed Inc. subsidiary said it would continue operating three of the branches and consolidate the rest within its existing network. The thrift said it would have 141 branches in California after the acquisition, which it hopes to complete on Sept. 7, subject to regulatory approval.

California Federal is the nation's fourth largest thrift with $23 billion in assets. Empire Federal, based in Buffalo, N.Y., with roughly $10 billion in assets, was seized by federal regulators earlier this year, and currently is managed by the Resolution Trust Corp.
