Who's News: Pancanadian Petroleum Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

PANCANADIAN PETROLEUM Ltd. (Calgary, Alberta) -- David P. O'Brien, 48-year-old president of this oil and gas production company, was appointed to the additional post of chief executive officer, effective Sept. 1, succeeding Bartlett B. Rombough, 65, who will remain chairman.
