Technology Brief -- Epitope Inc.: FDA Clears Clinical Trials For Company's AIDS Test

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

Epitope Inc. said the Food and Drug Administration approved its application to begin human clinical trials on a saliva-based AIDS diagnostic test. Epitope said preclinical trials show the test to be as accurate as blood tests taken to detect the AIDS virus. The clinical trials, expected to last for nine months, will be conducted in hospitals, laboratories, medical centers and dental schools in San Francisco; Tampa, Fla.; Portland, Ore.; New York; and Chicago.

The Beaverton, Ore., biotechnology concern said its system uses a saliva collection kit. The results are placed in a sealed tube and sent to company labs for testing.
