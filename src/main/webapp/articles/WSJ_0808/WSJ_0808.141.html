Business Brief -- La Quinta Motor Inns Inc.: Brierley's Industrial Equity Increases Holding to 14.4%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

Industrial Equity (Pacific) Ltd., a Hong Kong investment firm, said it raised its stake in La Quinta Motor Inns Inc., San Antonio, Texas, to 14.4% of the common shares outstanding. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Industrial Equity, a publicly traded company controlled by Brierley Investments Ltd. of New Zealand, said it holds 1,883,600 La Quinta shares, including 174,700 bought between May 9 and Friday for $13.95 to $15.17 a share. In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, La Quinta shares closed at $14.875, down 12.5 cents.

Industrial Equity has been steadily increasing its stake in La Quinta in the past 10 months. But the investment firm hasn't given any indication of its intentions in its SEC filings, and La Quinta Senior Vice President Fran Bissaillon said a representative of Industrial Equity shed no light on the firm's plans during a recent visit to La Quinta's corporate offices.
