Advanta Authorizes Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

HORSHAM, Pa. -- Advanta Corp. said its board authorized the purchase of 200,000 of its common shares, bringing its total 1990 stock buy-back authorization to 600,000 shares. Advanta also increased its quarterly dividend to three cents a share from 2.5 cents, payable Sept. 18 to stock of record Aug. 31.

The financial services holding company said stock purchases may be made in the open market or through negotiated transactions. The board recently completed the buy-back of 400,000 shares, an action it approved in February. As of June 30, the company had 9,426,000 shares outstanding.
