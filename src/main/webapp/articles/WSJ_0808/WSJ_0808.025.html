Consolidated Natural Gas Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

PITTSBURGH -- Consolidated Natural Gas Co. said it received approval from the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission to provide new services to utilities and other wholesale natural gas customers in Virginia. Under plans approved by the commission, the natural gas concern's CNG Transmission Corp. unit would provide long-term natural gas sales service to Virginia Natural Gas Inc. and Richmond's municipal gas utility. To provide the new services, CNG Transmission will construct facilities, including a 27-mile pipeline extension.
