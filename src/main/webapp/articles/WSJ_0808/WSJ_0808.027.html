Pratt & Lambert Inc. Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

BUFFALO, N.Y. -- Pratt & Lambert Inc. said its board authorized the purchase of as many as 300,000, or 5%, of the paint, chemical coatings and adhesives company's shares outstanding, through open-market or private transactions. The company said an additional 125,000 shares may be bought back under a September 1989 board resolution. Pratt & Lambert said it believes the buy-backs are an "advantageous use of corporate funds" and won't interfere with business plans.
