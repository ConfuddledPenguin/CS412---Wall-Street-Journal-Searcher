Northwest Air's Traffic Rises

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

ST. PAUL, Minn. -- Northwest Airlines, a unit of NWA Inc., said its July traffic rose 10% to 5.02 billion revenue passenger miles from 4.55 billion miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, increased to 72.7% from 70.3%.

For the first seven months of this year, Northwest's traffic climbed 15% to 29.66 billion revenue passenger miles from 25.78 billion miles a year earlier. The load factor rose to 66.5% from 65.5%.
