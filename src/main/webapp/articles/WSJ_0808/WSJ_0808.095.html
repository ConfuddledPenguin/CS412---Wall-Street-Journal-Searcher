RUBBERMAID Inc. signed a definitive agreement to buy Eldon Industries Inc. in a stock transaction valued at about $233.5 million. In yesterday's edition, the amount of the transaction was misstated. (See: "Rubbermaid Signs Accord to Acquire Eldon Industries" -- WSJ Aug. 7, 1990)

---

MATTHEW KULKIN is a broker at Advest Group Inc.'s Advest Inc. subsidiary. Yesterday's edition incorrectly said he no longer is employed at the brokerage company.

(See: "Four Settle Charges of Stock Exchange" -- WSJ Aug. 7, 1990)

---

PETER DAVIS is chairman of Reed International PLC, a British publishing concern. His name was misspelled in last Friday's edition.

(See: "Marketing Brief -- Reed International PLC: British Publisher to Acquire Estates Gazette Magazine" -- WSJ Aug. 3, 1990)
