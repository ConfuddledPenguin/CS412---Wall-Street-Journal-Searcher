Business Brief -- A.L. Laboratories Inc.: NMC Labs Is Purchased; Quarterly Profit Rose 12%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

A.L. Laboratories Inc., Fort Lee, N.J., said it acquired NMC Laboratories Inc., a Glendale, N.Y., maker of prescription creams and ointments for topical use. A.L. Labs, which has interests in pharmaceuticals and animal-health products, didn't disclose terms. But it said NMC Labs is expected to post 1990 sales of about $14 million and will contribute immediately to earnings. The unit complements A.L. Labs's Barre-National liquid pharmaceuticals business.

Separately, A.L. Labs reported that its second-quarter earnings climbed 12% to $2.6 million, or 23 cents a share, from $2.3 million, or 21 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue slipped 3.1% to $64.8 million from $66.9 million.

A.L. Labs said its second period, normally a weaker quarter, benefited from concentration on products carrying fatter profit margins and careful control of manufacturing costs.
