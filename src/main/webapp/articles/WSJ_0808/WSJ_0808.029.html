Who's News: Iowa Southern Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

IOWA SOUTHERN Inc. (Centerville, Iowa) -- Robert K. Harbour, 49 years old, formerly senior vice president of this utility holding company's Iowa Southern Utilities Co., was named president and chief operating officer of the unit, and vice president and a director of the parent, expanding board membership to nine. The operating chief and vice president posts are new. As the unit's president, Mr. Harbour succeeds C.R.S. Anderson, who remains chairman and chief executive officer of both the unit and the parent.
