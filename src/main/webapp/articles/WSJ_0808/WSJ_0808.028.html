Who's News: ACC Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

ACC Corp. (Rochester, N.Y.) -- Richard T. Aab, chairman and chief executive officer, assumed the additional responsibilities of president and chief operating officer. Mr. Aab succeeds Tansukh V. Ganatra, who resigned those positions, as well as president of the company's Canadian longdistance unit. Mr. Ganatra will remain president of the telecommunication company's Danbury Cellular Telephone Co. unit.
