S&P to Add Pep Boys to Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it will add Pep Boys Manny, Moe & Jack, a Philadelphia-based retailer of automotive parts and accessories, to its stock index of 500 companies. S&P said General Instrument Corp., a New York communications equipment and electronic components manufacturer, will be deleted from the list because it is being acquired by Forstmann Little & Co. The change will occur at the close of trading Monday, S&P said.
