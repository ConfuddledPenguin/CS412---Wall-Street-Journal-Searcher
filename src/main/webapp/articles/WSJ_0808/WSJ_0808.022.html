Metro Airlines July Traffic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

DALLAS -- Metro Airlines said July traffic rose 37.3%, to 59.9 million revenue passenger miles from 43.6 million a year earlier. Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, increased to 60.3% from 55.7%.

For the year to date, the carrier flew 362.7 million revenue passenger miles, a 42.0% rise, from 255.4 million a year earlier. Load factor increased to 55% from 51.5%.
