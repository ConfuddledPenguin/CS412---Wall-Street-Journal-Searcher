GTE, Contel Reach Definitive Agreement On $6.2 Billion Merger

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- GTE Corp. and Contel Corp. definitively agreed to a $6.2 billion merger through a swap of shares. Under terms of a preliminary agreement announced July 12, GTE will issue 1.27 of its shares for each of Contel's shares outstanding. The merger would create the largest provider of local-service telephone services in the U.S.

This fall, the companies will hold meetings at which shareholders will vote on the transaction. GTE and Contel hope to close the transaction by year end.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, GTE shares fell 37.5 cents to $27.125, while Contel's rose 62.5 cents to $31.25.
