WHO'S NEWS: Richard Lambert was named editor of the Financial Times. He will be succeeded as deputy editor of the Pearson PLC paper by Ian Hargreaves, who is director of news and current affairs at the British Broadcasting Corp. Sir Geoffrey Owen is stepping down Dec. 31 after a decade as editor. In April, he will become director of the business-policy program at the London School of Economics' Centre for Economic Performance.
