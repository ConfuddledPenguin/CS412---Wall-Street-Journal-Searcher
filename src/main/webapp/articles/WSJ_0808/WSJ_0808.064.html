America West Air's Traffic Rises

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines said July traffic climbed 44% to 979.2 million revenue passenger miles from 679.1 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The carrier's July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, rose to 61.2% from 58.3%.

In the first seven months, traffic increased 43% to 6.27 billion revenue passenger miles from 4.39 billion a year earlier. The load factor rose to 62% from 57.4%.
