Westinghouse Provides Funds

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

PITTSBURGH -- Westinghouse Electric Corp. said its Westinghouse Credit Corp. unit has provided $52.5 million as part of the recapitalization of United States Can Co., a Chicago-based container maker. The financing includes $30 million in senior debt and $22.5 million in junior subordinated debt. Westinghouse is a financial services, broadcast and electronics concern.
