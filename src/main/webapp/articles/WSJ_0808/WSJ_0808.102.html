Business Brief -- American Telephone & Telegraph: American Airlines Orders More Personal Computers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

American Telephone & Telegraph Co.'s computer unit received an extension of an estimated $100 million order for personal computers from American Airlines' Sabre Travel Information Network. American Airlines is a unit of AMR Corp. No value was placed on the extension, which follows a June 1989 contract for personal computers to be used in the Sabre network in the U.S.

An American Airlines spokeswoman said AT&T Computer Systems will need to convince some 4,500 overseas travel offices that currently use older Sabre equipment to change to newer AT&T personal computers.
