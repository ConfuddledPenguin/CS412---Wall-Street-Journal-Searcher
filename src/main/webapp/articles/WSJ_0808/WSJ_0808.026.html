Cooper to Acquire Swedish Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

HOUSTON -- Cooper Industries Inc. said it agreed to acquire AB Sani Maskiner, a Malmo, Sweden, maker of machinery and components for manufacturing window blinds and shades. Terms weren't disclosed. Sani Maskiner has annual sales of about $20 million and will become part of the Kirsch drapery hardware and window products division of Cooper, a diversified manufacturing concern.
