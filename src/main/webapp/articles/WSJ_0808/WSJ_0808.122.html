Business Brief -- Sbarro Inc.: Fast Food Chain Reports Net Rose 43% for Period

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

Sbarro Inc., the Commack, N.Y., Italian fast food restaurant chain, reported a 43% rise in net income for the 12 weeks ended July 15. Net income for the period was $3.5 million, or 26 cents a share, up from $2.4 million, or 18 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Sales rose 32% to $40.3 million from $30.6 million.

Sbarro said that for the twenty-eight weeks, net income rose 42% to $7.2 million, or 54 cents a share, from $5.1 million, or 38 cents a share. Sales rose 32% to $88.8 million from $67.4 million.

Of the 419 restaurants operating under the Sbarro name during the recent period, 100 were franchised. A year ago there were 343 units in operation, and 86 were franchised.

Sbarro shares closed yesterday at $28.375, up $1.25 in composite trading on the American Stock Exchange.
