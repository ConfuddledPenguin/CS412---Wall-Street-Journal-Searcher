Acquisition of Norton Clears White House

  • Author: Special to The Wall Street Journal
  • Date: 08/08/90

WASHINGTON -- President Bush has decided against intervening in the proposed acquisition of Norton Co. by Compagnie de Saint-Gobain of France. Worcester, Mass.-based Norton makes abrasive products and enginering materials and has engaged in extensive research and development of advanced ceramics and diamond films deemed to have national security significance.

White House press secretary Marlin Fitzwater said the Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States found that the acquisition wouldn't impair national security.

Last month, Saint-Gobain's subsidiary, SG Acquisition Corp., extended its $90-a-share tender offer for all of Norton's common shares outstanding to 5 p.m. EDT today.
