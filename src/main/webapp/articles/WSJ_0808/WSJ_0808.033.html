Eastern Airlines July Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

MIAMI -- Eastern Airlines said it flew 1.49 billion revenue passenger miles in July, compared with 698.8 million miles a year earlier. Results for July 1989 were hurt by a strike against the carrier.

Load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, was 64% last month compared with 74.8% a year earlier.

A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
