United Air's July Traffic Rose

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

CHICAGO -- United Airlines, a unit of UAL Corp., said July traffic rose 11% to 7.33 billion revenue passenger miles from 6.59 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline's July load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, fell to 71% from 72.9%.

In the first seven months, traffic rose 7.9% to 43.17 billion revenue passenger miles from 40 billion a year earlier. The load factor increased to 66.4% from 66.1%.
