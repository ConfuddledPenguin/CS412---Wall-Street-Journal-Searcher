Business Brief -- Capital Associates Inc.: Concern Expects to Report Losses for Quarter and Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

Capital Associates Inc., a Lakewood, Colo., leasing concern with financially strapped customers, said it expects to report losses for the fourth quarter and year ended May 31. The amount of the fourth-quarter loss hasn't been determined. But it will be substantial enough to create a loss for the year, the company said. In the first nine months, net income was $1.2 million, or 13 cents a share, on revenue of $120 million. In fiscal 1989, profit was $8.6 million, or 91 cents a share, on revenue of $140.4 million.

Capital Associates said the loss will result from reserves to be established for assets leased to companies experiencing "severe financial difficulties," among them Integrated Resources Inc., the real estate and financial-services firm that sought bankruptcy-law protection in February.

The company would not say whether it expects continued losses.
