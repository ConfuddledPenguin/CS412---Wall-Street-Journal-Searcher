Marketing & Media: Osborn Firm Buys TV Station

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- Northstar Television Group Inc., which is owned by an investor group led by Osborn Communications Corp., said it agreed to acquire WUHQ-TV, the ABC affiliate in Battle Creek, Mich., from Channel 41 Inc. of Battle Creek for a minimum of $10 million in cash and securities. The transaction requires federal regulatory approval. Northstar also owns stations in Grand Rapids, Mich.; Erie, Pa.; Providence, R.I.; and Jackson, Miss.
