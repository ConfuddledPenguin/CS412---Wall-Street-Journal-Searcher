Who's News: General Motors Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

GENERAL MOTORS Corp. (Detroit) -- Michael H. Erdman has been named vice president-marketing and elected to the board of General Motors of Canada Ltd., GM's Canadian operating unit. Mr. Erdman, formerly general marketing manager for GM's Chevrolet division, will succeed William J. Atkinson. Mr. Atkinson, 52 years old, is leaving GM of Canada to "pursue a General Motors retail franchising opportunity," the company said. GM didn't immediately name a successor to Mr. Erdman at Chevrolet.
