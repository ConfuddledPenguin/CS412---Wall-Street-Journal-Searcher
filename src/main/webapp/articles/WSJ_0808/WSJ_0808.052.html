WOOLWORTH Corp. (New York) -- Moody's Investors Service raised its rating on the senior debt of this retailing concern to single-A-1 from single-A-2, citing above-average profits and an improved balance sheet. About $182 million of debt outstanding was affected. Moody's boosted its rating on F.W. Woolworth Co. sinking fund debentures issues due 1996 and 1999 to single-A-1 from single-A-2.
