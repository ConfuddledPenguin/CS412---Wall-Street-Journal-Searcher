Treasury Plans to Sell About $18.4 Billion Of Short-Term Bills

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $2.25 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $18.4 billion in short-term bills to redeem $16.14 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on Nov. 15, 1990, and Feb. 14, 1991, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
