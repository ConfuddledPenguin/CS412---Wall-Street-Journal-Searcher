Horn & Hardart's Sale to Arby's

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- Horn & Hardart Co. said it will sell to Arby's Inc. for $5 million 22 franchised Arby's restaurants in southern Florida. Earlier this year, Horn & Hardart announced plans to sell all of its East Coast restaurant holdings, to focus on Hanover Cos., a direct marketing subsidiary. The company's remaining East Coast restaurant operations include 129 franchised and 22 company-owned Bojangles' Fried Chicken & Biscuits outlets and five Tony Roma's units.

Horn & Hardart plans to keep its International King's Table Inc. unit, a West Coast chain of 83 restaurants.
