Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

NEW YORK -- A membership on the Coffee, Sugar & Cocoa Exchange was sold for $81,000, up from $80,000 paid Friday, but down from $125,000 on Aug. 1, 1989. Seats are currently quoted at $81,000, bid, and $85,000, asked, an exchange spokeswoman said.
