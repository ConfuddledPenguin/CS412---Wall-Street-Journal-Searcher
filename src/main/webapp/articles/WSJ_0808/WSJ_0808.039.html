Who's News: Fuji Bank Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

FUJI BANK Ltd. (Tokyo) -- Steven A. Schwimmer was named managing director of the new derivative products group, responsible for interest-rate and currency swaps and options, at Fuji Capital Markets Corp., a New York-based subsidiary of this bank. Mr. Schwimmer, 36 years old, was previously a director in the interest-rate swaps department at CS First Boston, a New York-based investment bank.
