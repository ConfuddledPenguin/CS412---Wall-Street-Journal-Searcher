CAE Unit Gets Lufthansa Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

TORONTO -- CAE Industries Ltd., a maker of aircraft simulator systems and other electronic equipment, said its CAE Electronics Ltd. unit received a contract, valued at about 44 million Canadian dollars (US$38.3 million), to supply two flight simulators for the A-340-200 aircraft to Lufthansa German Airlines. The simulators, to be delivered to the West German carrier in September 1992, bring to 12 the number of orders placed by Lufthansa, CAE said. The A-340 planes are made by Airbus Industrie, a European aircraft consortium based in France.
