Jacobs Unit Wins Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said a unit received a $15.6 million contract from Glendale Memorial Hospital Health Center to build an addition to the Glendale, Calif., medical center. The 130,000 square-foot addition will include surgical suites, an emergency treatment center and X-ray facilities, Jacobs said. The project, awarded to Jacobs's Robert E. McKee Inc. unit, is set for completion in early 1992.
