Who's News: United Financial Corp. of South Carolina

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

UNITED FINANCIAL CORP. OF SOUTH CAROLINA (Greenwood, S.C.) -- James M. Doswell Jr., William M. Hull Jr., Don P. Ferguson Sr. and Jerry H. Padgett, all board members of a newly acquired unit, Home Federal Savings Bank of Rock Hill, S.C., were named directors of this thrift holding company, expanding board membership to 15.
