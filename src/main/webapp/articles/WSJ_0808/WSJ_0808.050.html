Southam to Buy Printing Plant

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/08/90

TORONTO -- Southam Inc. said it agreed to acquire Litho Prestige, a Quebec printing plant, from a unit of Hollinger Inc., a Toronto printing and publishing concern that is controlled by Conrad Black. The value of the transaction wasn't disclosed, but Southam said Litho Prestige has annual revenue of about 30 million Canadian dollars (US$26.1 million).

Southam said the printing plant will be integrated with its Imprimerie Canadienne Gazette printing plant, which has annual sales of about C$70 million.

Southam is a Toronto printing, publishing and retailing concern.
