Deutsche Babcock-Fiat Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/08/90

FRANKFURT -- Deutsche Babcock AG of West Germany, a machinery and engineering concern, said it and Fiat S.p.A. of Italy, an auto maker, agreed to form a joint-venture company that will specialize in pollution-control engineering in Italy. Babcock said its Deutsche Babcock Anlagen AG unit will own 25% of the venture, and Fiat's Fiatimpresit Sistemi Ambientali environmental engineering unit 75%.

The venture will be based in Milan and specialize in engineering services for gas emission, as well as waste-control systems for factories. Babcock primarily will supply know-how and an unspecified amount of cash to the venture, which will be managed by the Fiat unit.
