Amoco Unit's Natural Gas Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

CHICAGO -- An Amoco Corp. unit signed a 10-year natural gas transportation agreement with Natural Gas Pipeline Co. of America, a unit of Occidental Petroleum Corp.'s Midcon Corp. subsidiary. Under the agreement, Amoco Energy Trading Corp. can ship as much as 250 million cubic feet a day of natural gas from various sources in the middle of the continent to the Texas and Louisiana Gulf Coast, where it would have access to almost all major interstate pipelines to markets in the Gulf Coast, Southeast and Northeast.

Other terms weren't disclosed.
