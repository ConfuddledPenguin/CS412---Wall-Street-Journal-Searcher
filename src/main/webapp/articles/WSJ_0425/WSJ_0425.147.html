Business Brief -- Liz Claiborne Inc.: First-Period Net Rose 19%; Dividend Is Increased 25%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

Continuing to post strong sales in women's sportswear, Liz Claiborne Inc., New York, said first-quarter net income jumped 19%, and directors boosted the quarterly dividend 25% to 6.25 cents from five cents. The apparel maker said net rose to $52.6 million, or 60 cents a share, from $44.3 million, or 50 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales rose 19% to $420.9 million from $352.9 million.

Liz Claiborne stock closed yesterday in national-over-the-counter trading at $26.25, up 75 cents.

The new dividend is payable June 1 to stock of record May 11.

The company said that since December it has purchased about 1,050,000 of its common shares for $25 million, as part of a buy-back program.
