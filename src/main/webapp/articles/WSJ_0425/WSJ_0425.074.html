Technology: Digital Equipment, Apple To Unveil Joint Products

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Digital Equipment Corp. and Apple Computer Inc. will announce long-awaited products next week that tie their computers together. The expected products are the first fruits of a widely heralded technology alliance disclosed two years ago. Kenneth H. Olsen, Digital's president, and Apple's chief executive officer, John Sculley, will show off the products next Tuesday in New York.

The software products permit Apple computers to operate easily in Digital networks, sharing data files, printers and other devices.

The companies view the alliance as a strategic one, though they concede it isn't likely to set off a flood of new sales. Analysts say it should help Apple's prospects in commercial and industrial accounts, where Apple has been slow to penetrate, while shoring up Digital's appeal to desktop computer users, where it historically has been weak. Digital, based in Maynard, Mass., will market the products.
