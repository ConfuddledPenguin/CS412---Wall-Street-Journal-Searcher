First Interstate Bancorp Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

LOS ANGELES -- First Interstate Bancorp said it reached an agreement with National Convenience Stores that will allow First Interstate customers to use their automated teller machine cards to purchase merchandise and gasoline at certain of National's Stop N Go Stores outlets. The bank holding company said it expects about 100 California Stop N Go locations to participate in the program, using electronic terminals in those stores. It also expects to expand the program to Texas, and potentially Washington and Arizona, First Interstate said.
