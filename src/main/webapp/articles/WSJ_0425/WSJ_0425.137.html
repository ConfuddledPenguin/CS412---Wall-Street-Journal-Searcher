Business Brief -- Petroleum Helicopters Inc.: Industrial Equity (Pacific) Files to Raise Stake to 25%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Industrial Equity (Pacific) Ltd., a Hong Kong investment firm, said it filed with federal antitrust regulators to raise its stake in Petroleum Helicopters Inc. to as much as 25%. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Industrial Equity (Pacific) also said it sent a letter to Petroleum Helicopters Thursday saying it intended to seek board representation. According to the filing, that request was denied.

In New Orleans, John La Plante, secretary and treasurer of Petroleum Helicopters, had no comment "at the present time," but he confirmed the company received the letter seeking representation.

In December, Industrial Equity (Pacific) said it raised its stake in Petroleum Helicopters to the equivalent of 17%, including an option to buy 12%.

In the filing, Industrial Equity (Pacific) said it had discussions with Petroleum Helicopters' management concerning ways of maximizing long-term shareholder value and expects to continue such talks.

Petroleum Helicopters was quoted at $25.50 bid in over-the-counter trading.
