Who's News: Leeco Diagnostics Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

LEECO DIAGNOSTICS Inc. (Southfield, Mich.) -- Paul A. Rosinack, 42 years old, resigned as this diagnostics maker's chief executive officer, effective May 31, to pursue personal interests. Until a successor is named, Giovanni Recordati, chairman, will be interim chief executive of Leeco, which is 57%-owned by Recordati S.p.A., an international health-care company based in Milan, Italy.
