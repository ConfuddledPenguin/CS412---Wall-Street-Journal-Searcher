Who's News: GF Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

GF Corp. (Youngstown, Ohio) -- Ronald W. Myers, previously vice president of finance, was named president and chief executive officer of the maker of office furniture. Mr. Myers succeeds Ronald R. Anderson, who remains chairman. Last week, GF and its GF Furniture Systems Inc. unit filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. The company said the appointment of Mr. Myers was necessary to obtain $5 million in new borrowing to resume full production of office furniture in several manufacturing facilities.
