CBOT to Launch Oat Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

CHICAGO -- The Chicago Board of Trade said it plans to begin trading an oat futures-options contract Tuesday. The launch of the oat contract will mean that every CBOT agricultural futures contract "will be complemented by an options market," the exchange said. Months listed for the contract will be the same as the underlying oat futures, beginning with July 1990.
