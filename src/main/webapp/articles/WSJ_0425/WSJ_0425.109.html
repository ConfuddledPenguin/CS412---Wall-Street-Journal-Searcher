Who's News: Mead Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

MEAD Corp. (Dayton, Ohio) -- Charles W. Joiner, president of Mead Imaging, was appointed vice president and assistant to the president, new posts at this forest products and electronic publishing concern. Mr. Joiner is succeeded by Jack C. Goldfrank, director of strategic planning, printer products division, at Eastman Kodak Co.
