Business Brief -- MCI Communications Corp.: American Express Awards Network Services Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

MCI Communications Corp. said it received a non-exclusive, three-year contract from American Express Co., in which the Washington-based telecommunications company will provide a full range of network services to the New York-based travel and financial services concern's business units world-wide. The contract, valued at $60 million and guaranteed for three years, is the largest that American Express has awarded MCI since the two companies began working together in the early 1970s. The contract has an option for an additional two years, increasing its value to a potential $100 million, said Bert C. Roberts Jr., MCI president and chief operating officer.

MCI will provide services to all five of American Express's operating units: American Express Travel Related Services Co., American Express Information Services Co., American Express Bank, Shearson Lehman Hutton Inc. and IDS Financial Corp.
