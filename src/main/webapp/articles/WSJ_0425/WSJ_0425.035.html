Swiss Wholesale Prices Increase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

BERN -- Switzerland's wholesale prices rose 0.6% in March from February, and were up 2.0% from March a year earlier, the federal government reported. In February, Swiss wholesale prices increased 0.2% on a month-to-month basis, but climbed 2.3% from the year-earlier period.

The government's wholesale price index stood at 183.1 in March.

Consumer goods led the increase in wholesale prices in March, climbing 1.1% from February, and increasing 3.7% on a year-to-year basis. Raw materials and semi-finished goods rose 0.6% from February, but were up 1.1% from March a year earlier. Energy prices, however, fell 0.6% from February, but climbed 2.0% on a year-to-year basis.
