Dutch Industrial Output Falls

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

AMSTERDAM -- Dutch industrial production declined a seasonally adjusted 1% in February from the month earlier, according to the Dutch Central Bureau of Statistics, or CBS. In addition, the CBS reported that January's industrial production was also down 1% from December.

For the first two months of the year, industrial production was 3% lower than a year earlier, the CBS said.

The decline was led by the chemical, the housing, and the construction industries. At the same time, the metals, paper and graphics industries all posted gains.
