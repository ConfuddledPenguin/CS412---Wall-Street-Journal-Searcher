Who's News: Meridian National Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

MERIDIAN NATIONAL Corp. (Toledo, Ohio) -- Patrick J. Hylant, 41-year-old president of Hylant-MacLean Inc., an insurance brokerage firm here, was elected a director of this steel products distribution and processing concern, filling a vacancy created by the resignation of Lawrence Bell, whose law firm had represented Meridian National.
