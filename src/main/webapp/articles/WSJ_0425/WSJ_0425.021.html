International Minerals' Name

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

NORTHBROOK, Ill. -- International Minerals & Chemical Corp. said it will ask shareholders at a special meeting June 14 to approve a change in the company's name to Imcera. The company said its current name "no longer describes what we do. We've changed from a commodity chemical producer to a builder of technology businesses in human and animal health care and specialty chemicals."
