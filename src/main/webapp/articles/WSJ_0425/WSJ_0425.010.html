Kodak-Recycled Paperboard

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Eastman Kodak Corp. said it will begin converting its consumer film packaging this year to recycled paperboard, which is produced from newspapers, corrugated paperboard, carton stock and computer paper. The photographic, chemicals and pharmaceuticals concern said improved technology enables it to reprint, with high quality, its logo and other graphics on recycled stock. Kodak said the new packaging wouldn't increase its costs or film price.
