Who's News: Comerica Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

COMERICA Inc. (Detroit) -- John D. Lewis, 41 years old, was elected a director of this bank holding company. Mr. Lewis, a vice chairman of Comerica, succeeds Walter B. Ford II, who had been a board member since 1966. Mr. Ford, 69, was ineligible to stand for re-election under the company's retirement policy for directors.
