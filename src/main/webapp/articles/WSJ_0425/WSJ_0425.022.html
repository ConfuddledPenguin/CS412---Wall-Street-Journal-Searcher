Dividend News: Weyenberg Shoe Manufacturing Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

WEYENBERG SHOE MANUFACTURING Co. declared a 2-for-1 stock split and raised its quarterly 20%. The Milwaukee shoe concern increased its dividend to 15 cents a share from 12.5 cents, on a postsplit basis. Both the split and dividend are payable July 2 to stock of record June 1. The company has about 822,158 common shares outstanding. Separately, shareholders at the annual meeting approved an amendment that changes the name of the company to Weyco Group Inc. A spokesman said the old name doesn't reflect its current business, which involves "less manufacturing and more importing" of shoes.
