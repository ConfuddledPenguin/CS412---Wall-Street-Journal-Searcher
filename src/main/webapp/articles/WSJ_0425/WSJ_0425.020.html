GE Unit Gets Jet Engine Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

EVENDALE, Ohio -- The aircraft engines unit of General Electric Co., Fairfield, Conn., said Emirates Airlines, the state airline of Dubai, plans to buy $60 million of GE engines to power three new Airbus Industrie A-310-300 airplanes. The planes made by the European aircraft consortium will be delivered in 1992 and 1993.
