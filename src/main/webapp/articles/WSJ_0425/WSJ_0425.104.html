Who's News: Federal Pioneer Says 4 Schneider Nominees Are Named Directors

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

TORONTO -- Federal Pioneer Ltd. said four nominees of Groupe Schneider S.A. of France were appointed to Federal's eight-member board following Schneider's acquisition of a controlling stake in the Canadian electrical equipment maker. Schneider is offering 15 Canadian dollars (US$12.92) a share for all the common shares of Federal Pioneer. Last week it completed the purchase of a 63% stake in Federal Pioneer from Enfield Corp.

The French concern hasn't acquired the remainder of those shares as yet, Federal Pioneer said. The C$300 million bid is set to expire Monday.

The Schneider nominees are Lorenzo Folio, Michel Robin, Denis Lagrange and E. Jacques Courtois. They succeed Enfield representatives Willard L'Heureux, Robert Harding and James Meekison. There was one vacancy on the board.

Federal also said Mr. Folio was named chairman, succeeding Mr. L'Heureux, who resigned.

Enfield, an industrial holding concern in which the Bronfman family's merchant bank, Hees International Bancorp Inc., holds a 40% stake, tendered its 53.3% fully diluted stake in Federal Pioneer to the offer.

Schneider has extensive holdings in the electrical equipment industry. It plans to integrate Federal Pioneer with its MerlinGerin S.A. subsidiary.
