Slattery Group Tender Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

NEW YORK -- Slattery Group Inc. said 865,064 common shares had been tendered under its offer to purchase shares outstanding at $30 each; the offer expired Monday. Getty Petroleum Corp., Jericho, N.Y., which prior to the offer owned about 40% of the common shares of Slattery, agreed not to tender any shares under the offer. Excluding Getty's stake, about 99% of the shares outstanding have been tendered.

Slattery last year sold its construction subsidiary. Its remaining assets consist of investments and contingent assets and liabilities that mightn't be settled for several years.
