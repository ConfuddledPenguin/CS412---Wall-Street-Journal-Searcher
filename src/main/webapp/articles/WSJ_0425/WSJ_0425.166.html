Business Brief -- Tucson Electric Power Co.: Quarterly Loss Is Posted; Interest Costs Are Blamed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Tucson Electric Power Co. said it had a loss of $14.8 million, or 68 cents a share, in the first quarter, compared with net income of $20.4 million, or 75 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue fell to $120.9 million from $153.4 million. The Tucson, Ariz., utility attributed much of the setback to an increase in interest expense, which totaled $30.6 million for the quarter. The company is negotiating a new $350 million master credit agreement, which it now says it hopes to have in place by mid-summer.

The credit agreement, which the company earlier said it hoped to arrange by April 30, would provide an additional $90 million in borrowing capacity.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Tucson's stock closed at $11.25, unchanged.
