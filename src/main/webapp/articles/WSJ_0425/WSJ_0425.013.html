Allergan Quarterly Net

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

IRVINE, Calif. -- Allergan Inc., spun off as a public company by SmithKline Beckman Corp. in July, said first-quarter net fell 21%, to $13.6 million, or 20 cents a share. The maker of eye and skin-care products cited competitive pricing and the depressed contact-lens market.

In 1989's first quarter, Allergan had net of $17.3 million, or 26 cents a share. Sales rose 9% in the latest three months to $195.1 million from $178.6 million.
