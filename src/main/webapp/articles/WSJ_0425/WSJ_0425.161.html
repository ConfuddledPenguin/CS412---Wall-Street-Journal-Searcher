Business Brief -- Tambrands Inc.: First-Period Net Rose 22%; Strong Shipments Are Cited

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Tambrands Inc., Lake Success, N.Y., reported that net income rose 22% for the first quarter, fueled by strong shipments of tampons in the U.S. The company earned $22.1 million, or $1.02 a share, compared with $18 million, or 81 cents a share, in the year-earlier quarter. Revenue increased 23% to $159.3 million from $129.9 million.

Analysts said the unexpected steep increase in revenue was magnified by weak revenue last year. A 9% decline in research and development expenditures, coupled with a 13% drop in general and administrative costs, helped fuel earnings growth.

The percentage increase in per-share earnings exceeded the percentage increase in net income because Tambrands has repurchased its own shares since the first quarter of last year.

In New York Stock exchange composite trading, Tambrands closed at $76.125 a share, down 12.5 cents.
