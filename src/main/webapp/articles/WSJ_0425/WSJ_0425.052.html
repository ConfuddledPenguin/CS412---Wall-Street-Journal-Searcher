America West Airlines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- America West Airlines said it established $50 million of revolving credit with a group of banks led by Bank of America. The credit, secured by certain America West assets and extending for three years, will be used for general corporate purposes, the carrier said.

Other lenders in the group include the Industrial Bank of Japan, First Interstate Bank of Arizona, Valley National Bank and First Hawaiian Bank.
