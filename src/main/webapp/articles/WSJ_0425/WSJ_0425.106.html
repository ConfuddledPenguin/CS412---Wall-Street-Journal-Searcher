EG&G Inc. (Wellesley, Mass.) -- This maker of scientific instruments and parts added two directors, expanding its board to 13 members: Gail Deegan, senior vice president and chief adminstrative officer of Eastern Enterprises, an energy concern; and Greta E. Marshall, principal and founder of Marshall Plan, an investment management and counseling firm.
