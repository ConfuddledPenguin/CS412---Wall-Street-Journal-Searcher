Business Brief -- CMS Energy Corp.: An Infusion of $17 Million Into Four Projects Planned

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

CMS Energy Corp. said it has agreed to inject an additional $17 million into four projects owned by its partnership with Oxford Energy Co., which builds power plants that burn waste tires. In return for its investment, CMS will receive 40% of Oxford Energy's interest in the four energy projects. One of the plants is completed, another is under construction, while two others have not yet begun construction. CMS will make its contribution over about 20 months.

CMS already owns 49% of Oxford's stock. A CMS spokesman said the investment will not affect the level of CMS's Oxford stock holdings.

CMS, based in Dearborn, Mich., also said it will provide as much as $7.5 million as an equity contribution to one of the tires-to-energy projects in 1991, when that project is scheduled to be completed. Oxford stock closed at $4.25 a share, unchanged, in American Stock Exchange composite trading. CMS stock closed yesterday at $29, down 12.5 cents, in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
