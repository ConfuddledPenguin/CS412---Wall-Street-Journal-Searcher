Business Brief -- International Lease Finance: Pacts Totaling $530 Million Reached With 11 Airlines

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

International Lease Finance Corp., Beverly Hills, Calif., said it reached leasing agreements, valued at about $530 million, with 11 airlines. The time period of the leases, which cover 15 planes, ranges from five to 10 years. The owner and lessor of commercial jet aircraft also said it sold four aircraft and acted as agent in transferring leases of two aircraft owned by two financial institutions. The company said the $25 million it received in the transactions will be reflected in the fiscal second quarter ending May 31.

International Lease Finance said it expects second-quarter revenue to exceed the $87.1 million posted in the year-earlier quarter.
