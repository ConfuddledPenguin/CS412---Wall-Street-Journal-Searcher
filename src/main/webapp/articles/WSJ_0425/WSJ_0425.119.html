Tidewater Adopts Rights Plan

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

NEW ORLEANS -- Tidewater Inc. said its board adopted a shareholder rights plan to "deter coercive or unfair takeover tactics." The operator of marine operations for the oil and natural gas industry said the plan calls for one preferred stock purchase right to be distributed for each common share held May 7. The rights, which expire May 1, 2000, are exercisable after the accumulation of 15% or more of the company's common shares by a "hostile" buyer, Tidewater said.
