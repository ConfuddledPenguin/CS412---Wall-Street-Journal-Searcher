Treasury Plans to Sell $16.8 Billion of Bills

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.28 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $16.8 billion in short-term bills to redeem $15.52 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on Aug. 2 and Nov. 1, 1990, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
