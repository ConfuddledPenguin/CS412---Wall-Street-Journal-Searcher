Who's News: Whirlpool Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

WHIRLPOOL Corp. (Benton Harbor, Mich.) -- Paul G. Stern, vice chairman, president and chief executive officer of the Northern Telecom Ltd. unit of BCE Inc., and Miles L. Marsh, president and chief operating officer of Whitman Corp., were named directors of this maker of major home appliances, succeeding board members who didn't stand for re-election.
