Business Brief -- Image Bank Inc.: Shares Plunge $2.75, Losing One-Third of Their Value

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

Image Bank Inc.'s shares lost a third of their value as the New York stock-photography company restated fiscal first-quarter per-share earnings downward to seven cents from 12 cents. In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Image Bank fell $2.75 to $5.25.

Image Bank, which licenses reproductive rights for photographs, illustrations and cinematography, said the restatement reflects the addition of $365,069 to the cost of sales at a recently acquired U.K. subsidiary, Book People Ltd. The costs stemmed from a failure to account for certain purchase invoices for books that were part of the unit's inventory, Image Bank said.

Revised net income for the first quarter ended Dec. 31 is $261,079 compared with the $430,641 originally reported. For the year-earlier fiscal first quarter, Image Bank reported net income of $205,896, or nine cents a share. Shares outstanding in the periods were 3.7 million and 2.2 million, respectively.
