Interest Rates Rise Sharply At Citicorp Paper Auction

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate rose to 8.3725% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.227% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $410 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.37% to 8.375%.

Citicorp also said the average rate rose to 8.395% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper from 8.19% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $400 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.385% to 8.40%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
