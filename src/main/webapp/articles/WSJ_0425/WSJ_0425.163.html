Business Brief -- Enserch Corp.: Energy Concern to Record $9 Million Gain From Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

Enserch Corp. said it will record a gain of $9 million, or 14 cents a share, on the public offering of the company's discontinued oil-fields services unit. Enserch, a Dallas energy concern, said Pool Energy Services completed its offering of 11.5 million common shares, including one million from the underwriters overallotment option, at $10.25 a share. Enserch bought an additional two million shares in a private sale at the same price. The company didn't specify when it would take the gain.

Enserch will distribute the two million Pool Energy shares on a pro-rata basis to its shareholders as a special dividend later this year. Enserch said net proceeds from the sale, received in exchange for oil-field assets, are being used to reduce certain obligations, primarily leases and commerical paper.

Separately, Enserch reported first-quarter net income rose to $44.7 million, or 64 cents a share, from $35.8 million, or 55 cents a share, a year earlier. The latest period included a $22 million gain from a stock sale. Revenue increased to $771 million from a restated $752.1 million.
