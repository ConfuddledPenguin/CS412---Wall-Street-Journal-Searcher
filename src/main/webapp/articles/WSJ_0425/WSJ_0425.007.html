Unfilled Orders in Canada

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian manufacturers' unfilled orders in February fell 0.7% from January, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said, citing in part lower order backlogs at machinery and primary metal industries. Unfilled orders rose 0.7% in January and fell 1.8% in December. In February, manufacturers' shipments rose 1.9%, because of the reopening of some auto plants that were temporarily closed in January.
