Ranks of Southern Baptists Appear to Be Overstated

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

The Southern Baptist Convention officially claims 14.9 million members, making it the largest of America's Protestant denominations. By contrast, the Roman Catholic Church claims 54 million adherents in the U.S., although sociologists say that number includes many "cultural Catholics" who are no longer practicing church members, and the American Baptist Churches, formerly the Northern Baptists, claim 1.6 million members.

Southern Baptist statisticians and even some top denomination officials acknowledge that the 14.9 million figure for the Southern Baptists is overstated, and that as many as half that number no longer set foot in a Southern Baptist church.

The figure includes as many as 4.4 million so-called nonresident members, a technical term that actually allows the counting as members those the church has simply lost touch with, Southern Baptist pastors say.

From church records, the convention's Sunday School Board also calculates that another three million people haven't attended their church or donated to one in the past year.
