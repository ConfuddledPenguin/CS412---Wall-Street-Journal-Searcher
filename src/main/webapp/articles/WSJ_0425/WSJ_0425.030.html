French Trade Gap Narrows

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

PARIS -- France's seasonally adjusted foreign trade deficit narrowed to a provisional 858 million francs ($150 million) in March from a revised February deficit of 1.08 billion francs, the national customs office said. The February deficit previously had been reported by the customs office as 961 million francs.

The March deficit figure was sharply narrower than the two billion francs predicted by analysts.
