Dividend News: Amca International Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

AMCA INTERNATIONAL Ltd. shareholders at the annual meeting approved a 1-for-5 consolidation of common shares and changing the name of the diversified industrial company to United Dominion Industries Ltd., both effective June 4. Following the consolidation, the Charlotte, N.C., company will have about 27 million common shares outstanding. AMCA is 55.4%-held by Canadian Pacific Ltd. a Montreal-based resource and transportation company.
