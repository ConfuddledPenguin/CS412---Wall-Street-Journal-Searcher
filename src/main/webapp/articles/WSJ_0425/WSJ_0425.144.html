Uniroyal Says U.S. Backs Acquisition by Michelin

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

AKRON, Ohio (AP) -- The federal government has decided to allow Michelin Group to proceed with its planned $1.5 billion acquisition of Uniroyal Goodrich Tire Co., according to Uniroyal. The tire maker said it was informed of the decision by the Justice Department's antitrust division.

Michelin, based in France, last September said it would pay $690 million in cash and assume $810 million in debt to acquire Uniroyal Goodrich, creating the world's largest tire maker.
