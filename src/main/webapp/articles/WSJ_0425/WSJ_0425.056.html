Dividend News: ADAC Laboratories

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

ADAC LABORATORIES said it suspended its quarterly common stock dividend because of a slowdown in orders from customers. The Milpitas, Calif., medical imaging equipment company last paid a four cent dividend in its fiscal second quarter ended April 1. A spokesman for the company said Adac has been affected by slower orders from hospitals, which the company said are "taking a harder look at their capital budgets." In addition, the company said it has been hurt by increased competition. For the second quarter, the company had net income of $1.2 million, or three cents a share, down nearly 71% from the $4.1 million, or nine cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue declined nearly 17% to $20 million from $24 million.
