New Mercury Capri Is Priced

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

DETROIT -- Ford Motor Co. said the base price of its new Mercury Capri convertible, which is to go on sale this summer, will be $12,588, and that of the high-performance Capri XR2, with a 132-horsepower, turbocharged engine, will be $15,522. The Capri is expected to compete with cars such as the highly successful Mazda Miata, which was introduced last year. Market research indicates that Capri buyers will be 25 to 35 years old, and that many will be first-time buyers, a Ford official said, predicting the car will sell particularly well on the West Coast and East Coast and in metropolitan areas.
