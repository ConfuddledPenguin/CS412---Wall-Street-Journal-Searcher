Business Brief -- Hallmark Cards Inc.: Buy-Back Offer Completed For Univision Junk Bonds

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

Hallmark Cards Inc. said it completed an offer to buy back at a discount junk bonds issued by its Univision Inc. unit. Hallmark, a closely held Kansas City, Mo., greeting-card concern, said it accepted for payment $161.8 million, or 98%, of the unit's senior subordinated discount notes due 1998. Also, it accepted for payment $89.9 million, or 86%, of the unit's subordinated debentures due 1999. The purchase totaled $251.7 million of junk bonds.

As reported earlier this month, holders of a majority of $270 million of Univision junk bonds had voted to accept a sweetened tender offer from Hallmark.

Hallmark launched the tender offer in February after Univision, the nation's largest Spanish-language broadcast concern, missed interest payments.
