Business Brief -- Kaufman & Broad Home Corp.: California Builder Expects To Post Lower Net in Year

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Kaufman & Broad Home Corp. expects to report that net income for the fiscal year ending Nov. 30 was lower than the previous year's $81.4 million, or $2.44 a share. Bruce Karatz, president and chief executive officer of the Los Angeles-based single-family home builder, said after a meeting in New York that he agreed with analysts' predictions that earnings this year would be lower than those of a year ago, but he declined to be more specific.

Mr. Karatz said there has been softening in the California real estate market, which accounts for 60% of the firm's real estate revenue. The remainder comes from France, where the company also is involved in commercial real estate. Mr. Karatz said the company is spending more on aggressive marketing and on sales incentives.
