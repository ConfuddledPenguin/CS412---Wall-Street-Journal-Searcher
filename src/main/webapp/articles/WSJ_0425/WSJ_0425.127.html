International Brief -- Dresdner Bank Group: Stake of 7.5% Is Acquired In Taiwan's Investment Arm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

Dresdner Bank Group of West Germany said it acquired a 7.5% stake in Grand Cathay Securities Corp. of Taipei, an integrated securities house that is the investment arm of the ruling Kuomintang Party. This marks the first time a European bank has penetrated Taiwan's securities business. A Dresdner official cited the liberalization of Taiwan's financial market in choosing to make the investment. Grand Cathay was established in December 1988, capitalized at 1.5 billion New Taiwan dollars (US$57.3 million). Dresdner Bank set up a representative office in Taiwan in 1985.

The bank also announced the formation of Dresdner Thornton Securities Investment Consulting Co. in Taipei to provide local investors with Deutscher InvestmentTrust mutual funds and Thornton Group funds in Asia and Europe. It will be a joint venture by Dresdner Bank Group and one of its members, the Thornton Group.
