Dividend News: Cooper Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

COOPER INDUSTRIES Inc. shareholders approved increasing the company's authorized $8 convertible exchangeable preferred stock, making possible a 5-for-1 split of the shares. Cooper, a Houston-based maker of electrical products, tools, hardware, automotive products, and petroleum and industrial equipment, said the holders approved increasing the authorized preferred stock to 36,197,499 shares from 9,495,143. The split is effective May 4 to stock of record May 25. The shareholders also approved creating a new class of 10 million shares of $2 preferred stock. ---

Corrections & Amplifications

COOPER INDUSTRIES Inc.'s 5-for-1 stock split on preferred shares is effective May 25 to stock of record May 4. In yesterday's edition the dates were transposed.

(WSJ April 26, 1990)
