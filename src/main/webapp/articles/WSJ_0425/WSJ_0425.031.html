Dividend News: BSD Bancorp

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

BSD Bancorp increased its semiannual dividend by 60%, to eight cents a share from five cents. The dividend is payable May 18 to stock of record May 4. The San Diego bank holding company also said first-quarter net rose 34%, to $1 million, or 27 cents a share, from $750,000, or 21 cents a share, a year ago.
