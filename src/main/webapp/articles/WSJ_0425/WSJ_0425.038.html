REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Socialism Takes Flight

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/25/90

Outside of a few redoubts such as North Korea and the Chinese party headquarters, state-run industries are a concept in retreat these days. But in retreat to where? To the high councils of California] Air fares among major cities in the state, including the capital of Sacramento, have risen dramatically in recent months. Naturally, legislators are upset. But instead of privatizing airports and piercing the lock that existing carriers have on gates, State Senator Art Torres has proposed formation of a state airline to -- believe it or not -- compete and hold prices down. In 1990, some folks are still up in the clouds.
