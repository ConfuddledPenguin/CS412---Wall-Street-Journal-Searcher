Business Brief -- Fluor Corp.: Engineering Unit Receives Contracts From 3 Concerns

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Fluor Daniel Inc., the principal engineering and construction unit of Fluor Corp., Irvine, Calif., said it was awarded three contracts. The unit received a $23 million contract from Augusta Newsprint Co. in Augusta, Ga., for procurement and construction services for a paper recycling plant. Completion of the 250-ton-a-day facility is scheduled for September 1990.

Fluor Daniel also said it got a $24 million contract from Cape Industries of Wilmington, N.C., to provide procurement and construction management services for a dimethyl manufacturing facility. Cape Industries is a joint venture owned by a unit of Hoechst Celanese Corp. and American Petrofina Inc.

Fluor Daniel said it also got a contract from Sun Refining & Marketing, a unit of Sun Co. of Philadelphia, for engineering services to develop a 300-gallon-a-minute sour water stripper. Terms of the contract weren't disclosed.
