Business Brief -- Presidio Oil Co.: Exchange Bid for Preferred Attracts 1,450,278 Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

Presidio Oil Co., Denver, said that as of Monday, 1,450,278 shares of its 9 1/2% cumulative convertible preferred stock had been tendered under terms of its previously announced exchange offer. The independent oil and gas company said enough shareholders had tendered their stock to trigger the mandatory redemption of the remaining preferred shares outstanding.

Presidio is offering to exchange 1.35 shares of its Class A common stock for each of the 2,262,279 shares of preferred stock outstanding. The offer expires tomorrow at 5 p.m. EST. Those tendering their shares after that deadline will receive 1.25 shares of common for each share of preferred until May 24 or $5 a share in cash plus accrued dividends thereafter.
