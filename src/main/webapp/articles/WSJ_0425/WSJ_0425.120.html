Who's News: Emerson Electric Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

EMERSON ELECTRIC Co. (St. Louis) -- Jean-Paul L. Montupet, formerly vice president of Leroy-Somer S.A., was named to the new post of executive vice president for this maker of electrical and electronic equipment's world-wide industrial motors and drives business. Emerson recently acquired Leroy-Somer S.A., a French electric motor manufacturer.
