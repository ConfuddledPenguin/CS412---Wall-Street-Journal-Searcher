Who's News: Kaman Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/25/90

KAMAN CORP. (Bloomfield, Conn.) -- Harvey S. Levenson, 49 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this aerospace defense contractor. As president he succeeds Charles H. Kaman, 70, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer. The title of chief operating officer is new. Mr. Levenson was most recently senior vice president, chief financial officer and chief administrative officer. The company didn't name a new chief financial officer.
