Business Brief -- Angeles Corp.: Real Estate Firm Expects To Post 3rd-Quarter Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

Angeles Corp. said it expects to report a $3.9 million net loss for its fiscal third quarter ended March 31, mainly because of the company's withdrawal from certain real estate investment programs. In the year-earlier quarter, the Los Angeles investment management concern earned 723,000, or 16 cents a share, on revenue of $9.9 million.

Angeles said it will no longer offer real estate limited partnerships or real estate investment trusts to individual investors because the vehicles have fallen out of public favor. The decision will result in the elimination of roughly 50 jobs "at every level," possibly including some corporate officers, a spokesman said. The staff reductions and related moves will result in annual savings of about $2.5 million, he said.
