Who's News: Kerr-Mcgee Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

KERR-MCGEE Corp.(Oklahoma City) -- John J. Murphy, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Dresser Industries Inc., and John J. Nevin, former chairman and chief executive of Firestone Tire & Rubber Co. (now Bridgestone/Firestone Inc.), were elected directors of this energy concern, filling vacancies.
