AMR Corp.'s American Airlines

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

FORT WORTH, Texas -- American Airlines, a unit of AMR Corp., said April traffic rose 5.9%, to 6.17 billion revenue passenger miles, from 5.82 billion revenue passenger miles in the earlier period. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

Load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, fell to 62.1%, from 62.3% a year ago.

For the year to date, American said it flew 23.6 billion revenue passenger miles, up 7.4%, from 22.0 billion revenue passenger miles in the year-ago period. Load factor was 60.4%, down from 60.5%.
