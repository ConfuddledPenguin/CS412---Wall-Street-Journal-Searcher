Mesaba Aviation Traffic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Mesaba Aviation Inc., a unit of AirTran Corp., said its April traffic rose 29% to 14.3 million revenue passenger miles from 11.1 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The carrier, which operates as Northwest Airlink, said its load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, fell to 46.6% from 52.2%. The regional carrier operates under a marketing agreement with Northwest Airlines, a unit of NWA Inc., and provides service to 35 airports.
