Who's News: American Isuzu Motors Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

AMERICAN ISUZU MOTORS Inc. (Los Angeles) -- E.F. "Fritz" Kern, 50 years old, was named senior vice president and general manager for the U.S. sales arm of Isuzu Motors Ltd. of Japan. Mr. Kern, who will report to American Isuzu president Kozo Sakaino, succeeds John E. Reilly, 64, who was named to a new position as chairman of American Isuzu Motors. In that capacity, Mr. Reilly will act as a "senior adviser" to executives at Isuzu's American operations and a "senior consultant" to the parent company in Japan.
