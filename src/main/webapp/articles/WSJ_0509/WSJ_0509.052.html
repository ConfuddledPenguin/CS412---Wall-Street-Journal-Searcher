Who's News: L.B. Foster Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

L.B. FOSTER Co. (Pittsburgh) -- Lee B. Foster, formerly executive vice president of this maker of steel and tubular products, was named president and chief executive officer. He succeeds Joseph H. Dugan, 64 years old, who resigned. Mr. Foster, 43, is the grandson of L.B. Foster, who founded the company in 1902.
