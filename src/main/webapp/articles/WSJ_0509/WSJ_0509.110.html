Business Brief -- Philip Morris Cos.: Firm to Relocate Employees To Westchester, New York

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

Philip Morris Cos. plans to relocate about 500 of its 1,700 Manhattan employees to Rye Brook in Westchester County. The New York-based tobacco, food and brewing company said its main offices at 120 Park Avenue near 41st Street have become overcrowded. And its lease on additional space at 100 Park expires in September 1991.

The headquarters of two subsidiaries will be moving; they are the Philip Morris International tobacco operations and Philip Morris Capital Corp. financial-services unit.

The move, which will begin later this year, is expected to take a year to complete, Philip Morris said. The company, which employs 150,000 world-wide, owns a building in Rye Brook; the Kraft General Foods International unit already is headquartered there.
