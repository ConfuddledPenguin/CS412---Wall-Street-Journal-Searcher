International Brief -- AB L. M. Ericsson: Pretax Profit Soared 94%, Sales 30% in First Period

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

Swedish telecommunications group AB L.M. Ericsson said its pretax profit in the first three months soared 94% to 1.18 billion kronor ($195 million) from 609 million kronor ($100.8 million) in the corresponding period a year earlier. Ericsson said consolidated sales increased 30% to 10.41 billion kronor from 7.99 billion.

Ericsson's interim report exceeded market expectations and the pretax profit was almost 280 million kronor above the average prediction of analysts.

The group said income per share after taxes and full conversion leaped 88% to 17.25 kronor from 9.19 in the first threemonth period last year.

Ericsson said the improvement was attributable mainly to the public telecommunications and radio communications business areas, both of which posted a strong increase in volume and favorable margin development.
