Comerica Headquarters Site

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

DETROIT -- Comerica Inc. said it will build a $200 million headquarters office in downtown Detroit on a site now occupied by a concert hall named for Henry and Edsel Ford. The banking company said it is still working out financing for the project, but expects to receive about $18 million in loans for the project from the City of Detroit Downtown Development Authority.

Comerica will maintain an equity interest in the completed development. The agreement also contains provisions that will allow the development authority to receive a portion of the building's cash-flow and sales proceeds revenue.
