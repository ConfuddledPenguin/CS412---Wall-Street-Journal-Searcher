U.K. Retail Sales

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

LONDON -- Retail sales volume fell a revised 1.8% in March from February and was up 0.2% from March 1989, the U.K. Central Statistical Office said. In the three months ended in March, retail sales volume was up 0.75% from the previous three months and up 1.5% from a year earlier, unchanged from the provisional estimate.
