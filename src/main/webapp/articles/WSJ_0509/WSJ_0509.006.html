Century Telephone Gain

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

MONROE, La. -- Century Telephone Enterprises Inc. said it expects to report a $2.2 million second-quarter gain from the sale of the remainder of its investment in Telecom USA. The company said the gain is equal to about seven cents a share. In the year-ago quarter, Century had net income of $5.3 million, or 18 cents a share, on revenue of $49.2 million.

The telecommunications concern reported first-quarter net of $6.8 million, or 22 cents a share, including a $354,000, or one-cent-a-share, gain from the sale of a portion of its Telecom stock.
