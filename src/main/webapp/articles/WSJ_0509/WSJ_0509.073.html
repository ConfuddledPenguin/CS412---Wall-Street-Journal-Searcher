Treasury Will Raise $1.1 Billion in Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.1 billion in new cash with the sale Monday of about $16.8 billion in short-term bills to redeem $15.71 billion in maturing bills. The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on Aug. 16 and Nov. 15, respectively.

Tenders for the bills, available in minimum denominations of $10,000, must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
