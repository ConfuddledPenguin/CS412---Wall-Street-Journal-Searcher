Montana Power Doubles Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

BUTTE, Montana -- Montana Power Co. said it amended its articles of incorporation to double the number of shares of common stock authorized to 120 million. The company said the change reflects a 2-for-1 stock split that took effect Feb. 1, and the possible need for additional common shares should rights become exercisable under a shareholder protection rights plan established in June 1989.
