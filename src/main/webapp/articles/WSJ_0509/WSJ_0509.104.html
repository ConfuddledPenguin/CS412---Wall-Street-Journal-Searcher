Who's News: American Express Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

AMERICAN EXPRESS Co. (New York) -- Patricia Patterson was named senior vice president of consumer lending for the American Express Travel Related Services Inc. unit. Ms. Patterson was assistant general counsel for Travel Related Services and general counsel for Centurion Bank, another unit of the travel and financial-services company. In her new position, she succeeds Craig Hoenshell, who recently was named president of the Travelers Cheque group.
