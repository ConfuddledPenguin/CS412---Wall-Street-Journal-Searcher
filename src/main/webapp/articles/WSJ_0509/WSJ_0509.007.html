Ship Orders for South Korea

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

SEOUL, South Korea -- South Korean shipyards for the first four months of this year received orders for 60 new ships totaling 3.8 million gross tons, more than triple a year ago, the Trade and Industry Ministry said. All but one ship was for export.

Officials said the orders, already close to an annual record tonnage of four million tons in 1983, were valued at $3.13 billion, more than double a year ago.

They said South Korean shipyards completed 761,000 tons of ships in the past four months and are expected to complete about 3.6 million tons of ships this year if workers end their strike soon at the Hyundai Heavy Industries Co. shipyard in Ulsan, the world's largest.
