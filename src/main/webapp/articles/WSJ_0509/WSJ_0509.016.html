OPEC Wellhead Crude Oil

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

LONDON -- Wellhead crude-oil production by the 13 member countries of the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries in April totaled 23.5 million barrels a day, down from a revised 24 million barrels a day in March, the International Energy Agency reported. The Paris-based agency said in its monthly "Oil Market Report" that OPEC countries also produced 1.9 million barrels a day of natural-gas liquids and condensates in April, unchanged from March.

The agency said its preliminary figures put world oil supply in April at 54.2 million barrels a day, down from 54.6 million barrels a day in March, but in line with an average 54.2 million barrels a day for the first quarter. April's decline was "due almost entirely to a decrease in OPEC production," the IEA said.
