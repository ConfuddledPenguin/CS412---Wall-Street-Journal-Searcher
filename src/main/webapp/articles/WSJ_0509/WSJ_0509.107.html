Business Brief -- Hadson Corp.: Firm Slates Joint Venture For Coal-Fired Power Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

Hadson Corp., Oklahoma City, said it entered a joint venture agreement with a Westmoreland Coal Co. unit to build a $275 million, coal-fired power plant in North Carolina. The energy and defense concern said it expects to complete the project in late 1993. The plant will sell power to Virginia Electric & Power Co.'s North Carolina Power & Light affiliate, the company said.

Hadson said it also signed an amended agreement to build an $85 million gas-fired power plant near Albany, N.Y. Hadson had planned to run the plant with coal, but changed the plans for environmental reasons, the company added.
