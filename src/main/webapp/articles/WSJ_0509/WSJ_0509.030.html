Commercial-Paper Rates Decline in Citicorp Auction

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

NEW YORK -- The average interest rate fell to 8.27% at Citicorp's $50 million weekly auction of 91-day commercial paper, or corporate IOUs, from 8.451% at last week's sale. Bids totaling $415 million were submitted. All accepted bids were at 8.27%.

Citicorp also said that the average rate fell to 8.2968% at its $50 million auction of 182-day commercial paper, down from 8.49% at last week's sale.

Bids totaling $390 million were submitted. Accepted bids ranged from 8.28% to 8.30%.

The bank holding company will auction another $50 million in each maturity next Tuesday.
