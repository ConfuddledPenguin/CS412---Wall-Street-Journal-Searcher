Highland Superstores Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

PLYMOUTH, Mich. -- Highland Superstores Inc. reported April sales of $61.7 million, down 2% from sales of $63.3 million a year ago. Comparable-store sales dropped 6%. Highland, a consumer electronics retailer, said its sales for the first three months of the year were $185.3 million, down 6% from $196.5 million in the year-earlier period. Comparable-store sales declined 10%.
