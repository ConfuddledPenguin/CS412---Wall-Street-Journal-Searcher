Who's News: Ingersoll-Rand Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

INGERSOLL-RAND CO. (Woodcliff Lake, N.J.) -- Daniel E. Kletter, 52 years old, Larry H. Pitsch, 49, J. Frank Travis, 54, and R. Barry Uber, 45, were elected vice presidents of the parent industrial-machinery manufacturer. Messrs. Kletter, Pitsch, Travis and Uber retain their titles as president of the Construction Equipment, Industrial Process Machinery, Air Compressor, and Production Equipment groups, respectively.
