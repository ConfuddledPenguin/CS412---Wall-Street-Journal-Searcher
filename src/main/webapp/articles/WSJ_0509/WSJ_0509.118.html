Business Brief -- General Electric Co. PLC: Ferranti Unit to Lead Team On Eurofighter Radar Work

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

A unit of General Electric Co. PLC of the U.K. has been selected to head development of the radar system for the four-nation Eurofighter aircraft project, U.K. Defense Minister Tom King said. Mr. King said that Ferranti Defence Systems Ltd. has been formally selected to head a consortium to develop the radar system for the fighter aircraft. General Electric, which isn't related to the U.S. company of the same name, estimates the value of the contract at #300 million ($500 million), although it could escalate substantially if Eurofighter goes into full production by the late 1990s.

General Electric acquired Ferranti International PLC's defense electronics unit for #270 million in February. The other countries involved in the Eurofighter project are Germany, Italy and Spain.
