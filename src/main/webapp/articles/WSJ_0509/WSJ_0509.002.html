Swiss Cost-of-Living Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

BERN -- Switzerland's cost-of-living index rose 0.2% in April from March, and increased 4.6% from a year earlier, the government said. The April figures represented a slowdown from the March rises of 0.3% month to month and 5.0% year to year. In the year-earlier April, the month-to-month increase was 0.5%, while the year-to-year increase was 2.6%.
