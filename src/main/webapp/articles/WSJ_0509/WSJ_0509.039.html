Stone Container Paper Bag

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

CHICAGO -- Stone Container Corp. said it successfully tested a paper bag product containing 20% old newsprint. The company, a supplier of recycled newsprint, said it completed full-scale production testing in the past 10 days at a Jacksonville, Fla., plant. Production plans call for recycling 60 million pounds of old newspapers for use in the bag.

Stone Container, with $5.3 billion in 1989 sales, said it expects to be able to make the bag at several of its mills within a few months.
