Who's News: American Republic Names Two to Board

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

TORRANCE, Calif. -- American Republic Bancorp said it named two shareholders, who are also directors of its Republic Bank unit, to the board of the parent company, expanding it to nine members. "The action was taken to avoid the possibility of a contest over the election of directors" at the annual shareholders meeting, originally set for tomorrow but postponed until revised proxy materials are ready, the company said.

The new directors are Edmond J. Russ, an attorney and former mayor of Gardena, Calif., and Henry Zdonek, a certified public accountant. Mr. Russ said in an interview that he nominated Mr. Zdonek in a letter sent to American Republic's president last month.

"It wasn't a proxy fight; any shareholder can nominate someone to the board," Mr. Russ said.

The two men hope to generate more local business for the bank in their new positions. "There might {also} have been some differences with the board," but "they weren't major enough to be publicly announced," Mr. Russ said.
