Who's News: CPC International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

CPC INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Englewood Cliffs, N.J.) -- The responsibilities of two of this food company's executives were expanded. Konrad Schlatter, 54 years old, vice president for financial planning, reporting and control, was named senior vice president of finance. Angelo Abdela, 48, corporate treasurer, adds the title of vice president.
