Business Brief -- Alpha Capital Co.: Investment Company Holds 5.1% Pacific Western Stake

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

Alpha Capital Co., an Oakland, Calif., investment firm, said it holds for investment purposes a 5.1% stake in Pacific Western Bancshares Inc., San Jose, Calif. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Alpha said it holds for itself and clients 623,828 Pacific Western common shares, including 171,200 shares bought in the past 60 days for $11.50 to $12.25 each.

Alpha said it plans to work with Pacific Western's present management and board to maximize shareholder value. While it has no plans at present, such activities may involve a sale of the company to a third party, the filing said.

A spokesman for Alpha Capital said the company doesn't have current plans to expand its holding but may do so in the future.

Phillip R. Boyce, chairman and chief executive officer of Pacific Western Bancshares, said the move simply increases a friendly and longstanding investment in the company by Alpha.
