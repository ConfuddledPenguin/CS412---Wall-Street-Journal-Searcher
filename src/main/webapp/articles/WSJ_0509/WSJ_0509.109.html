Who's News: Tate & Lyle PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

TATE & LYLE PLC -- This London-based producer of sweeteners and starches named William C. Shanley, 65 years old, president of Tate & Lyle Inc., its U.S. subsidiary, based in Wilmington, Del., effective Oct. 1, after his retirement from Amstar Sugar Corp., its cane sugar refining unit. Jack F. Lay, 61, senior vice president, succeeds Mr. Shanley as president and chief executive officer at Amstar.
