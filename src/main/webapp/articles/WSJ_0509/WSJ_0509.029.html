Who's News: Polaroid Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

POLAROID Corp. (Cambridge, Mass.) -- Sheldon A. Buckler, a member of Polaroid's office of the chief executive, was named to the additional post of vice chairman, a new position at this instant-photography concern. Mr. Buckler, 58 years old, was also named to the company's executive committee.
