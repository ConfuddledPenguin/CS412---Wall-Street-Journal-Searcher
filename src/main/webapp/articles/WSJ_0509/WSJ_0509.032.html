REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Calling Ed Norton

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

Too bad Jackie Gleason didn't live long enough to work this one into "The Honeymooners." The Sacramento, Calif., Public Works Department has held a contest to rename manhole covers. It was supposed to be just for fun and entries have poured in from everywhere. The winner will be announced tonight. Sacramento's mayor says the sewer department is "keeping up with contemporary standards." Fine. We always thought "manhole" was demeaning to men.
