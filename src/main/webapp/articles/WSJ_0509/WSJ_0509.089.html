Marine Midland Banks Capital

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

NEW YORK -- Marine Midland Banks Inc. said that its parent, Hongkong & Shanghai Banking Corp. Ltd., invested $200 million in new capital in Marine. The investment is split evenly between equity and subordinated debt. The investment brings Marine's Tier 1 capital (chiefly common equity) to 4.7%, up from 4.1% March 30. Hongkong bank has said that it plans to boost Marine's Tier 1 capital to 5% by the end of this year.

Ann L. Robinson, a bond analyst with Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette, called the increased investment "a nice statement of support" by the parent, especially in light of Marine's rising levels of troubled loans.
