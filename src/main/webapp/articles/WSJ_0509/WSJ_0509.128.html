Technology Brief -- Cambridge BioScience Corp.: Agreement Reached to Buy An Interest in Dublin Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

Cambridge BioScience Corp. said it agreed in principle to acquire a stake in Noctech Ltd., a maker of infectious disease products based in Dublin, Ireland. A Cambridge BioScience spokesman said the company expects to invest $750,000 to $800,000 in Noctech and to own "a little less" than 20% of Noctech when the transaction is completed.

Noctech will serve as Cambridge BioScience's marketing and distribution base in Europe, the biotechnology concern said. It added that initially its diagnostic tests for AIDS and other viruses will be manufactured at Noctech, and that later other of its products are expected to be made at the Irish company.

Cambridge BioScience is headquartered in Worcester, Mass.
