Financing Business: In Home Health Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

IN HOME HEALTH Inc. said it plans a $2.5 million stock offering. In Home Health, which provides home health care services, said at current prices the offering would amount to the sale of about 1.7 million of its 3.7 million shares outstanding. The Plymouth, Minn., company said Lew Lieberbaum & Co. of New York is underwriting the offering.
