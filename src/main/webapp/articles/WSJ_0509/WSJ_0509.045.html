  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

CPT Corp. (Eden Prairie, Minn.) -- President and chief executive officer of 12 months, David Clinton, resigned but will work as a part-time consultant as the office-automation-equipment concern continues a restructuring aimed at restoring profitability, the company said. It also said its board appointed an executive committee of four directors to oversee turnaround efforts.
