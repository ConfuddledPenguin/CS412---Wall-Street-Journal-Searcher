Who's News: Seven Oaks International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

SEVEN OAKS INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Memphis, Tenn.) -- Sam A. Sarno Sr. and Sam A. Sarno Jr. both resigned as directors of the retail-coupon clearinghouse, citing "other commitments which prevented them from continuing in service to Seven Oaks." The elder Mr. Sarno had been a director of the company since its founding in 1971, and had served as chairman and chief executive officer until his retirement from the posts in 1987. The younger Mr. Sarno had been a director since 1980 and had served as president and chief operating officer until he resigned both posts last year. Following the resignations, the company's board voted to reduce its number to seven from nine.
