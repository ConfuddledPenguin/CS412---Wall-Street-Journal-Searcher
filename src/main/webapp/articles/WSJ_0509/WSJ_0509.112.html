Amphitheaters: Sony Corp.'s CBS Records Inc. set a joint venture with Pace Group for the development of outdoor amphitheaters and the creation of live-entertainment tours to fill them. Closely held Pace operates amphitheaters and produces such live events as tractor-pulls and rock concerts. CBS Records said the venture also aims to supply music recordings and videos of the performances it produces.
