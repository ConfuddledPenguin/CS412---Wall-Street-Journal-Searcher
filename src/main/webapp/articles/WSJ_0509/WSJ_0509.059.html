Care Enterprises Posts '89 Loss

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

TUSTIN, Calif. -- Care Enterprises Inc., a nursing home company that has been operating under bankruptcy-law protection for two years, reported a 1989 net loss of $46.7 million, or $4.14 a share. In 1988, the company had a net loss of $29 million, or $2.57 a share. Revenue rose 4% to $257 million from $247.8 million.

Care said its 1988 and 1989 losses include "substantial charges" related to the establishment of reserves for discontinued operations and to a reorganization plan, which recently received court approval.
