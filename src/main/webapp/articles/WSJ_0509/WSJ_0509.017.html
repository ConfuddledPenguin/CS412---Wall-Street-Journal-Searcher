Italy's 1st-Period Debt Narrows

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

MILAN -- Italy's public-sector deficit narrowed to a provisional 25.318 trillion lire ($20.85 billion) in the first quarter of the year from a revised deficit of 30.307 trillion lire in the first three months of 1989, the Treasury announced yesterday. The government deficit for the first quarter of 1989 was revised upward from an original estimate of 29.746 trillion lire.

The Treasury said that tax revenue amounted to 70.955 trillion lire. Public sector spending totaled 79.037 trillion lire, while Treasury operations resulted in a deficit of 17.236 trillion lire.

The deficit was covered with the issuing of medium-term and long-term government securities totaling 9.833 trillion lire, debt issues placed abroad resulting in net revenue of 397 billion lire and other kinds of government debt totaling 15.088 trillion lire.
