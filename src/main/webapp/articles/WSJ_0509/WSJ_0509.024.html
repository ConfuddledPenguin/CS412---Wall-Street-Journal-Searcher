Who's News: Several Executives Resign Posts at Tosco As Firm Plans to Move

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

SANTA MONICA, Calif. -- Tosco Corp. said James M. Cleary, senior vice president of refining, will become president of the Tosco Refining Co. division effective June 30. He succeeds John G. Drosdick, who is resigning. Peter A. Sutton, a vice president, was promoted to senior vice president, succeeding William Floyd, who resigned. Wilkes McClave, assistant general counsel and secretary, was appointed senior vice president and general counsel, succeeding Eric Schwartz, who resigned. Mr. McClave continues as corporate secretary.

The resignations coincide with the company's announcement that it plans to move its corporate offices to the East Bay area of San Francisco during the fourth quarter.

Tosco, an independent oil refiner engaged in refining and marketing wholesale petroleum products, makes and distributes phosphate-based fertilizer products.
