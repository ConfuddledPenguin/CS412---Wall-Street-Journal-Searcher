Who's News: Graco Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 05/09/90

GRACO Inc. (Minneapolis) -- Lee R. Mitau, a partner in the law firm of Dorsey & Whitney, was elected to the board of this fluids technology company. He was elected to a three-year term, succeeding Maynard B. Hasselquist, a longtime director who didn't stand for re-election.
