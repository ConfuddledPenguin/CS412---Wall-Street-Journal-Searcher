Letters to the Editor: Levee, Not Levy

  • Author: null
  • Date: 05/09/90

In the first line of the March 28 Leisure & Arts piece "Dark Days for Duck," author Michael Pearce says, "I had stood alone on the levy . . ." May I offer the following correction. Levy: the imposition or collection of an assessment. Levee: an embankment for preventing flooding.

When in doubt, remember the lines from that old song, "Waitin' on the levee, Waitin' for the Robert E. Lee."

R.R. Regnier

Phoenix, Ariz.
