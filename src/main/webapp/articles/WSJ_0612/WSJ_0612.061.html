U.K. Producer Output Prices

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

LONDON -- Producer output prices, or prices of manufactured goods, rose 0.5% in May from April, while producer input prices, or prices of raw materials and fuels purchased by manufacturing industry, fell 1.1%, Britain's Central Statistical Office said. The agency said the year-on-year rise in output prices was 6.2%, which was the highest since 6.3% in December 1982. It attributed the drop in input prices largely to lower fuel, metal and electricity prices. Input prices were down 1.3% from May 1989.
