Who's News: Information Resources Forms Office to Hold Two Top Positions

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

CHICAGO -- Information Resources Inc. said it has formed an office of the chief executive, which will include both the position of chief executive officer and chief operating officer. The marketing-information concern said the office will be shared by 42-year-old Gian Fulgoni, who has been the chief executive and who continues as vice chairman, and James Andress, 51, who has been the chief operating officer and continues as president.

Information Resources' chairman, John Malec, 45, said the action "formalizes" the structure that has been in place at the company for the last six months. The new office, he said, "isn't very different from our past structure. We've operated with an executive committee since our inception."
