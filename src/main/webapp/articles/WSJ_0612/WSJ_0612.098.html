Who's News: CF Income Partners Limited Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

CF INCOME PARTNERS LIMITED PARTNERSHIP (Los Angeles) -- Jeffrey H. Lynford, 42 years old, was elected vice chairman and chief financial officer of CF Management Corp., the general partner of CF Income. As chief financial officer, he succeeds Raymond H. Olinger, 61, who retires July 1. The post of vice chairman is newly created. Mr. Lynford is chairman of Wellsford Group Inc., an affiliate of Wellsford Capital Partners Limited Partnership, which acquired 20% of CF Management late last year. CF Partners invests in income-producing multifamily residential, commercial and industrial properties.
