Who's News: BICC PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

BICC PLC (London) -- The cable and construction company named two vice presidents to new posts at its North American subsidiary based in New York. John Hollinghurst becomes vice president of research and development, retaining his responsibilities as vice president of technology for Phillips Cables Ltd., BICC's Ontario subsidiary. Laurence Cardiff becomes vice president of procurement, retaining his responsibilities as director of purchasing at both Phillips Cables and Cablec Corp. BICC's New City, N.Y., subsidiary.
