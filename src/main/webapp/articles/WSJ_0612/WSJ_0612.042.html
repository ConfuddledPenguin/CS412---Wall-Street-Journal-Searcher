Who's News: Dayton Hudson Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

DAYTON HUDSON Corp. (Minneapolis) -- Gary Witkin, 41-year-old executive vice president, stores, at Marshall Field & Co., will be named Field president when this retailer closes its pending $1.04 billion purchase of the retailing chain from B.A.T Industries PLC of Britain. Field's two top executives, Philip B. Miller, chairman and chief executive officer, and Burnett W. Donoho, president and chief operating officer, both said earlier they will resign when Dayton Hudson completes the acquisition. Under the management structure Dayton Hudson plans, Mr. Witkin will be the top official at Marshall Field.
