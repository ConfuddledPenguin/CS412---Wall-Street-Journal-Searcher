Recent SEC Filings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Banco Central S.A., offering of four million American depositary shares, each representing the right to receive one half of one share of stock, to be sold by subsidiary Productora de Seguros-Correduria de Suguros S.A., via Prudential-Bache Capital Funding and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Cherokee Inc., initial offering of four million class B common shares, via PaineWebber Inc.

Maytag Corp., shelf offering of up to $250 million of debt securities and warrants to purchase debt securities.

Norwest Corp., offering of four million depositary shares, each representing one quarter share of cumulative preferred stock, via Merrill Lynch.

Pamida Holdings Corp., initial offering of 2.8 million common shares, of which two million will be sold by Pamida and 800,000 by holders, via Merrill Lynch.

Trimble Navigation Ltd., proposed initial offering of three million shares of common stock, of which 2.5 million are being sold by the company and 500,000 by holders, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. and Needham & Co.
