Business Brief -- Earth Technology Corp.: Third-Quarter Net Income Declined on Special Items

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

Earth Technology Corp. said net income for the third quarter ended May 25 was reduced about $100,000, or three cents a share, because of non-operating items. The items include a percentage of a restated operating loss at Waste Processor Industries Inc., in which Earth Technology holds a 24% stake, and a valuation adjustment from a deferred compensation arrangement in Earth Technology's acquisition of Alternative Ways Inc.

A year earlier, the Long Beach, Calif., company had profit of $32,952, or one cent a share, on revenue of $6 million. The consulting firm, specializing in environmental and engineering issues, said it will release full results in about 10 days.
