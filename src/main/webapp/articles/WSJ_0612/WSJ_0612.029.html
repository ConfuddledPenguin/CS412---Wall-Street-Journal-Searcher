Who's News: Public Service Co. of New Mexico

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

PUBLIC SERVICE CO. OF NEW MEXICO (Albuquerque, N.M.) -- Ashton B. Collins, president of Reddy Communications Inc. and a director of this utility, is expected to be elected P.S. New Mexico's chairman at its annual meeting, scheduled for next Tuesday, to succeed Jerry D. Geist, who is to retire that day as chairman, president and chief executive officer. John T. Ackerman, president and chief operating officer of P.S. New Mexico's Gas Co. of New Mexico unit, is expected to be named to succeed Mr. Geist as president and chief executive.
