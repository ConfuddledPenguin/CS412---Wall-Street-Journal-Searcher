Edisto Plans to Buy Tulsa Firm

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

DALLAS -- Edisto Resources Corp. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire Tulsa-based Esco Energy Inc. for about $9.2 million in cash and about 1,324,000 shares of Edisto common stock. Esco is a closely held energy company that operates three subsidiaries, Vesta Energy Co., Omega Pipeline Co. and Esco Exploration Inc. James R. McNab Jr., chairman of Edisto, said the acquisition opens major Midwest markets for Edisto.

Edisto closed at $17, up 50 cents, in American Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday.
