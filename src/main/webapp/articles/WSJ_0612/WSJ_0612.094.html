Norway's Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

OSLO -- Norway's consumer prices rose 0.2% for the month ending May 15 and were up 3.9% from the year-earlier period, the state Central Bureau of Statistics said. Travel and transportation showed the sharpest increase, with that May index up 1.1% from April and 2.1% from May 1989, the bureau said. Food prices declined 0.1% in May from April.
