International Brief -- Mitsubishi Metal Corp.: Consolidated Pretax Profit Increased 27% in Fiscal 1990

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

Mitsubishi Metal Corp. said it posted a 27% rise in consolidated pretax profit in the year ended March 31 to 34.48 billion yen ($224.5 million) from 27.26 billion yen in the previous fiscal year. The Japanese company's net income jumped 32% to 15.15 billion yen, or 22.55 yen a share, from 11.48 billion yen, or 18.68 yen a share.

Mitsubishi Metal said revenue dropped 7.3% to 789.18 billion yen from 851.29 billion yen because of a change in accounting policy. The change removed proceeds from gold and silver trading from the overall revenues category.

Mitsubishi Metal forecasts sharply higher consolidated sales and earnings levels for the current fiscal year because they include predictions for Mitsubishi Mining & Cement Co.'s performance in the last four months of the year. The two companies will merge Dec. 1.

Sales are seen at 914 billion yen, pretax profit at 43 billion yen, and net income at 18.5 billion yen.
