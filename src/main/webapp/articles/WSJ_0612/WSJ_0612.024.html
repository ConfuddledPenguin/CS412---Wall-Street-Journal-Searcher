McDonnell Douglas Gets Job

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

WASHINGTON -- McDonnell Douglas Corp. received an $89.2 million Navy contract for production of F/A-18C and F/A-18D aircraft. McDonnell Douglas also announced it received a $65.7 million contract to develop upgraded hardware and software for the F/A-18's radar.

Bath Iron Works Corp. won a $23.4 million Navy contract for the DDG-51 destroyer program.
