Coke Bottler in Bolivia Reportedly Kidnapped

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

ATLANTA -- Coca-Cola Co. said the chairman of its independent bottler in La Paz, Bolivia, was reported missing and may have been kidnapped. But a company spokesman couldn't confirm wire service reports that Bolivian industrialist Jorge Lonsdale was nabbed by hooded gunmen while driving to work yesterday.

Witnesses said three men intercepted Mr. Lonsdale's car three blocks from his office and shot and seriously wounded his chauffeur, according to wire reports. The bottling executive was bundled into the trunk of a waiting car which sped off toward the northern sector of La Paz, the witnesses told a local radio station.

Police said they didn't know whether the industrialist was wounded in the shooting, according to the reports.
