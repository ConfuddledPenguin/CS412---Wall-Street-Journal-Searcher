Financing Business: Pacific Gas & Electric Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

PACIFIC GAS & ELECTRIC Co., San Francisco, is offering $150 million of first and refunding mortgage bonds, 9.30% Series 90A, due July 1, 2023, at 95.75% of their principal amount, plus accrued interest from June 1, to yield 9.73%, through First Boston Corp. and associates.
