REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Forward Motion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

The prospects for the world economy took a step forward Sunday as President Bush and President Salinas of Mexico announced their intention to seek a free-trade agreement. "The speed of the change requires decisive answers on all fronts, in all markets," Mr. Salinas said of the trade agreement. True enough, but it will be interesting to see what opposition creeps out of the woodwork.
