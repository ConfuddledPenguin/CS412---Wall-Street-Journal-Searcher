Business Brief -- Immunex Corp.: Investor Richard C. Hedreen Increases Holding to 7.91%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

Richard C. Hedreen, president of R.C. Hedreen Co., Seattle, said he raised his stake in Immunex Corp. to the equivalent of 7.91% of the common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Hedreen said that as of June 6 he controlled 633,789 Immunex common shares, assuming the conversion of debentures and the exercise of warrants. His Immunex holdings include securities held by immediate family members, the filing said.

Last December, Mr. Hedreen said he controlled the equivalent of a 6.39% stake in Immunex.

In the filing, Mr. Hedreen said he acquired his Immunex holdings solely for investment purposes.

Seattle-based Immunex, a biotechnology concern, declined to comment.
