Business Brief -- Ultimate Corp.: Midlantic Corp. Unit Sells Company a Block of Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

Ultimate Corp., East Hanover, N.J., bought from Midlantic National Bank, 575,000 Ultimate shares, which the bank had received as part of a personal bankruptcy-law settlement made by former Ultimate Chairman Theodore Sabarese. Ultimate paid $6.50 a share, or $3.7 million, for the block, leaving the computer-systems concern with 8.9 million shares outstanding.

Last June, Mr. Sabarese resigned as Ultimate's chairman and made a personal filing in U.S. Bankruptcy Court in Newark, N.J. In a recent Securities and Exchange Commission filing, he disclosed the settlement with the bank and said that he might bid to take over Ultimate. He couldn't be reached for comment.

Midlantic is a subsidiary of Midlantic Corp., Edison, N.J.
