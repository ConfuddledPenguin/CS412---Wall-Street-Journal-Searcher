Porsche Unit Recalls Cars

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

RENO, Nev. -- The U.S. sales arm of Porsche AG said it is recalling 19 of its 928 S4 and 928 GT models because of a defect that could cause fuel leaks and, potentially, fires. Porsche said the fuel tanks of the affected models are attached to the car with a mounting nut that could crack. As a result, fuel could leak from the tank. If the gasoline comes in contact with a hot exhaust system, a fire might occur. The auto maker said it isn't aware of any fires, accidents or injuries caused by the defect.
