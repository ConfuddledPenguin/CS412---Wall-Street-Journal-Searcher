AirTran's Mesaba Unit Traffic

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Mesaba Aviation Inc., a unit of AirTran Corp., said its May traffic rose 19% to 15.4 million revenue passenger miles from 12.9 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The carrier, which operates as Northwest Airlink, said its load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, fell to 48% from 52.4%.
