Business Brief -- Grossman's Inc.: Retailer Expects Earnings `Below' Year-Ago Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

Grossman's Inc. said second-quarter net income will be "well below" the year-ago $10.6 million, or 40 cents a share. The Braintree, Mass., building-supplies retailer blamed the decline on poor business conditions in the Northeast and the expense of launching a new merchandising plan with name brands, broader selection and lower prices. It said that "customer reaction to the program has been heartening."

The company also said its May sales on continuing operations rose 4.1% to $78.5 million from $75.5 million in May 1989. For the June quarter last year, Grossman's reported sales of $343.9 million. Sales for the quarter from continuing operations were $237.9 million.

Grossman's sold its operations in the Northwest in the year-ago third quarter.
