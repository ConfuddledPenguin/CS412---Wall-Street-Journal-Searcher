Who's News: Fairfield Communities Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

FAIRFIELD COMMUNITIES Inc. (Little Rock, Ark.) -- Leo C. McKenna, a self-employed financial consultant, was elected to the board, succeeding Malcolm R. Myers, who resigned. Additionally, J. Steven Wilson, chairman and president of Wilson Financial Corp., was elected to the board, succeeding Augustus Adams III, who also resigned. Fairfield develops and builds retirement and recreational communities.
