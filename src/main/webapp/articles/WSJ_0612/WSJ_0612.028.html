  • Date: 06/12/90

BANKERS TRUST Co. (New York) -- Paul Worthen Higgins becomes head of international private banking and senior vice president at the Private Bank, succeeding Barbara Thomas, who left the subsidiary of the banking company for health reasons. Mr. Higgins returns to Bankers Trust after 2 1/2 years as senior managing director for the investment and securities services division at Marine Midland Bank, in New York, and chairman of Marinvest, its institutional asset-management subsidiary.
