Business Brief -- Sara Lee Corp.: Bakery Operation to Close Plant, Dismiss 500 Workers

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

Sara Lee Corp., Chicago, said its bakery operation plans to close a plant in Deerfield, Ill., and dismiss the facility's 500 workers by March 1. The food concern's bakery unit, which has about 4,500 employees, also said it will trim its administrative staff by about 100 employees.

The Deerfield plant's production of frozen baked goods will be shifted to other facilities, the company said. The closure "is necessary for us to remain competitive," said an official. A spokeswoman noted that the plant, built in 1964, lacks certain production capabilities the newer plants have.

Sara Lee has a total world-wide work force of about 100,000.
