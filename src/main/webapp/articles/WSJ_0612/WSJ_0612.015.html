Metro Airlines Traffic Rises 37%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

DALLAS -- Metro Airlines Inc. said May traffic climbed 37% to 55.1 million revenue passenger miles from 40.3 million miles in the year-earlier period. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, rose to 56.2% from 52.8%.

For the year-to-date, Metro said it flew 243.4 million revenue passenger miles, a 44% increase from 168.7 million miles a year ago. Load factor advanced to 52.5% from 49%.
