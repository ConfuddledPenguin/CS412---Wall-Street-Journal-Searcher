Financing Business: Canadian Pacific Forest Products Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

CANADIAN PACIFIC FOREST PRODUCTS Ltd. (Toronto) -- This forest products concern, which is 79.7%-owned by Montreal-based Canadian Pacific Ltd., said it closed a private placement of US$200 million of 20-year senior notes with U.S. institutional investors. The company said the notes were issued in two series: $98 million of Series A notes due June 15, 2010 and bearing interest at 10.63%; and $102 million of Series B notes at 10.5% interest, also due June 15, 2010, but with a sinking fund starting in 2001, producing an average life of 15.5 years. The firm said proceeds will be used to repay debt incurred to finance its capital expenditure program. Canadian Pacific Ltd. has interests in resources, real estate, telecommunication, energy, transportation and hotels.
