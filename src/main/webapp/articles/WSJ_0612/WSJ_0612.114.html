Financing Business: Trimble Navigation Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

TRIMBLE NAVIGATION Ltd., a Sunnyvale, Calif., maker of navigational instruments, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of three million common shares, of which 2.5 million are being offered by the company and 500,000 by certain holders. It said the shares are expected to be priced between $8 and $10 each. Proceeds will be used for retiring debt and for working capital. Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co. is underwriting the offering.
