CPT Settles Two Lawsuits

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

EDEN PRAIRIE, Minn. -- CPT Corp. said it reached settlements of two lawsuits pending against the company in federal court. The office-automation-equipment concern had been sued by participants in its employee stock ownership plan seeking to diversify their stock portfolio. CPT also faced a contract claim from the Justice Department. The combined settlements are within CPT's reserves established in prior quarters, the company said. The ESOP settlement of $2 million, which awaits court approval, will be paid from insurance proceeds. In the contract case, insurance will cover $200,000, and CPT itself will pay $200,000 in annual installments of $50,000 each.
