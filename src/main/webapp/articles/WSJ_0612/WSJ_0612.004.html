John Hancock Unit Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

BOSTON -- John Hancock Financial Services said it acquired Cost Care Inc., a closely held Huntington Beach, Calif., concern. Terms weren't disclosed. Cost Care, which had revenue of about $23 million last year, provides health cost and treatment monitoring services for corporations. Hancock, a financial services unit of John Hancock Mutual Life Insurance Co., said Cost Care will be combined with its own cost-containment operations in a group that it said will have $40 million in combined revenue this year.
