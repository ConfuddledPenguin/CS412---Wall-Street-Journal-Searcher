Credit Ratings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

VALHI Inc. (Dallas) -- Moody's Investor Service Inc. said it assigned a single-B-3 credit rating to the proposed convertible subordinated debentures, due 2015, of this holding company. It also confirmed its single-B-2 rating for Valhi's 12.5% senior subordinated notes, due 1998. In a related action, it raised the rating on NL Industries Inc.'s sinking fund debentures to single-B-1 from single-B-2. Valhi owns 66% of NL Industries. About $467 million of long-term debt is affected. The rating concern said its assessment of Valhi's proposed debentures included the issue's junior subordinated position and the superior position of hefty debt at the operating subsidiary level. In upgrading NL Industries' sinking fund debentures, it cited the company's good operating performance and strong market position, especially in its production of titanium oxide.
