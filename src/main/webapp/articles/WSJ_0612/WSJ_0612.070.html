W.W. Grainger Accords

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

CHICAGO -- W.W. Grainger Inc. said it agreed in principle to acquire two closely held regional distributing concerns. Grainger, a national distributor of commercial and industrial products, didn't disclose terms of the planned transactions.

It said it reached a tentative accord to acquire Bossert Co., a Kansas City, Mo., distributor of general industrial products in Missouri and Kansas, which had 1989 sales of $22 million. Separately, it agreed to acquire W.M. Pattison Supply Co. of Cleveland, which had sales last year of $23 million.
