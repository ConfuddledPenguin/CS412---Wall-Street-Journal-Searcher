Qume Extends Deadline

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

MILPITAS, Calif. -- Qume Corp. said it extended the deadline for arranging financing for the proposed acquisition of Qume by a management-led investor group for $7.25 a share, or $86 million. The group is headed by David Lee, chairman and chief executive officer of the computer-products maker. Mr. Lee said he expects to have the financing in hand by June 25.

In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Qume closed at $6.625, unchanged.
