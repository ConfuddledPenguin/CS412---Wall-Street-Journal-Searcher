Phelps Dodge Labor Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Phelps Dodge Corp. said it reached tentative agreement on a new three-year labor contract covering about 900 workers at its mine, mill and smelter near Silver City, N.M. The agreement, which is subject to ratification by union members, increases wage rates about 21% over the life of the contract, the company said. The existing contract expires June 30, the company said.

The agreement calls for an increase of $2 an hour on July 1, another 25 cents an hour a year later and a $400 lump sum payment to each worker in the third year, Phelps Dodge said.

Phelp Dodge produces copper and other metals.
