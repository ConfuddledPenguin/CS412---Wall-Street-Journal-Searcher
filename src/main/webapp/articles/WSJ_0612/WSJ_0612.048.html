Dynascan Buy-Back Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/12/90

CHICAGO -- Dynascan Corp. said it is offering to buy the stock of shareholders who own less than 100 shares of its common stock as of June 5. The maker of consumer electronic products said shareholders accepting the offer will receive $6 a share. Dynascan said the offer allows shareholders to sell their shares without paying brokerage commissions or other selling fees.

Dynascan, which has more than six million shares outstanding, said about 18,000 shares are odd-lot holdings. The offer is scheduled to expire on July 6.
