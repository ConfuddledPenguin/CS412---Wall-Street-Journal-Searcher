Business Brief -- Union Carbide Corp.: Sale of Operation by Unit Will Boost 2nd-Period Net

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/12/90

Union Carbide Chemicals & Plastics Inc. said it expects a second-quarter earnings increase of 12 cents a share to its parent, Danbury, Conn.-based Union Carbide Corp., from the sale of its primary alcohol ethoxylates (PAE) business. The sale, to Hoechst Celanese Corp., was announced today, although the price wasn't disclosed. Hoechst Celanese is a unit of Hoechst AG, of West Germany.

Hoechst Celanese's acquisition of UCC&P's PAE business includes inventories and, under a licensing agreement, associated business technologies. No plants, real property or personnel are included in the deal, the company said. Union Carbide Chemicals will continue to manufacture PAEs for Hoechst Celanese under a multiyear agreement.
