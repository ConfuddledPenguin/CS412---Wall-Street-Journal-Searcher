  • Date: 06/14/90

BOSTON -- The Massachusetts Water Resources Authority awarded a $119.4 million construction management contract to ICF Kaiser Engineers, a subsidiary of American Capital & Research Corp., Fairfax, Va. The contract, which is part of the $6.1 billion Boston harbor cleanup plan, covers 5 1/2 years. Stone & Webster will receive $28 million of the award as the major sub-consultant on the project, the state agency added.
