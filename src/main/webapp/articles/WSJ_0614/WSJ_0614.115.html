Business Brief -- United Technologies Inc.: Pratt & Whitney Unit Gets $312 Million Engine Order

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Pratt & Whitney, a unit of United Technologies Inc., said it received a $312 million order from International Lease Finance Corp. for 48 of its engines, to be delivered beginning in 1994. International Lease Finance, a Beverly Hills, Calif.-based owner and lessor of jet aircraft, placed firm orders for 28 of Pratt & Whitney's PW2000 engines for its growing fleet of Boeing 757 mid-range aircraft. It also will take 20 high-thrust PW4000 engines for its fleet of wide-body aircraft, Pratt & Whitney said.

The company, based in East Hartford, Conn., said that the order was International Lease's third significant order in two years.
