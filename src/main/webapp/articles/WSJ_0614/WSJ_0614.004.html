Who's News: Central Illinois Public Service Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

CENTRAL ILLINOIS PUBLIC SERVICE Co. (Springfield, Ill.) -- Lowell A. Dodd, formerly vice president for division operations at this electric and natural gas utility concern, was named senior vice president for operations. Mr. Dodd, 51 years old, succeeds Robert G. Lane, 63, who is retiring.
