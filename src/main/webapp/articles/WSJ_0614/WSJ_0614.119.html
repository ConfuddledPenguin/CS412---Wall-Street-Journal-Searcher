Business Brief -- Pic 'N' Save Corp.: Retail Chain, Investors File Confidentiality Agreement

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

Girard Partners L.P., controlled by investor David H. Batchelder, said it entered into a confidentiality agreement with Pic 'N' Save Corp. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Girard said it signed the pact in order to receive nonpublic information needed to form an acquisition proposal for Pic 'N' Save.

Pic 'N' Save and Girard, which have been at loggerheads over efforts by Girard to oust Pic 'N' Save's board, recently agreed to a truce while Pic 'N' Save management conducts an auction to sell the company.

Based in Dominguez, Calif., Pic 'N' Save operates a chain of retail stores that specialize in close-out merchandise.
