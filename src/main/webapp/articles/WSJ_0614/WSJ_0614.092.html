Hadco Stock Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

SALEM, N.H. -- Hadco Corp. said it bought back from its chairman Horace H. Irvine II 300,000 shares of common stock; the company also bought 100,000 shares of common from trusts he established, all at $4.875 a share, or a total of $1.9 million. Following the stock sales, Mr. Irvine and trusts he created hold 3,884,216 shares of Hadco common, or about 37% of Hadco's shares outstanding. The company said Mr. Irvine agreed he won't trade additional shares in the open market this year.

Hadco, a maker of printed circuit boards, also said its board authorized the purchase of up to $2 million of additional shares in the open market this year. And Hadco directors said they won't sell their stock holdings this year.
