Wiser Oil Opens Texas Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

SISTERSVILLE, W.Va. -- Wiser Oil Co. said it began operating a natural gas liquids plant in Concho County, Texas, to process gas from the company's Aqaritta oilfield. The plant is removing 7,500 gallons a day of natural gas liquids from 1.2 million cubic feet a day of natural gas, Wiser said. The gas is being reinjected into the field to maintain pressure and enhance oil recovery.

Wiser said that oil production from the field is 700 barrels a day, and that additional drilling will be required to delineate the limits of the field. Wiser has a 62.5% working interest and a 50% net revenue interest in the field.
