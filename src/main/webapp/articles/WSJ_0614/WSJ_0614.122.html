GenCorp Finishes Station Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

FAIRLAWN, Ohio -- GenCorp Inc. said the final appeal concerning its 1988 sale of station KHJ-TV in Los Angeles to Burbank, Calif.-based Walt Disney Co. has been dismissed. The transfer of the station, currently KCAL-TV, is now complete, GenCorp said. GenCorp, which has aerospace, automotive and plastics businesses, has been disposing of its radio and television stations after a lengthy controversy over the company's fitness to own broadcasting licenses.
