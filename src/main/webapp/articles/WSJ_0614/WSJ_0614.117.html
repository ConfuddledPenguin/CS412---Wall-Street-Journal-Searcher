Business Brief -- H.H. Robertson Co.: Pittsburgh Firm Progresses Toward Funding Ceco Deal

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

H.H. Robertson Co. said it is making progress in arranging financing for the acquisition of Ceco Industries Inc., Oakbrook Terrace, Ill. Robertson, a building products and contracting concern in Pittsburgh, said it received a written commitment from Wells Fargo Bank for $150 million of financing. In addition, Frontera S.A., a Swiss investment company, definitively agreed to buy $40 million of Robertson stock after the merger.

Robertson had agreed earlier to pay $15 million cash plus 3.4 million to 4.1 million shares of Robertson common stock for the closely held Ceco, and to assume $85 million of Ceco debt. Following the transaction, Frontera and the current owners of Ceco each would own about 25% of Robertson's outstanding shares.

Ceco manufactures doors, metal buildings and roof systems, and is a provider of concrete forming services.
