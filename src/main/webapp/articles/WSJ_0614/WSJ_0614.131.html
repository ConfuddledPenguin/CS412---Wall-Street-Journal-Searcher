Lifetime Corp. Buys Markka

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

BOSTON -- Lifetime Corp. bought Markka Healthcare Inc. of Atlanta for about $12 million, with $1.1 million of the purchase price contingent upon Markka's revenue for the following 12 months. Markka, a provider of infusion therapy and respiratory devices, had annual revenue of about $6 million in 1989.

Lifetime, a provider of home health-care personnel, said about $4 million of the total purchase price may be paid in company stock.
