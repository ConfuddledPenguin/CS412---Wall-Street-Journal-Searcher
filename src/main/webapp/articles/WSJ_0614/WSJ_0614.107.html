WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Kimmins Environmental Services Corp., proposed offering of 800,000 common shares, via Whale Securities Co.

Lunar Corp., proposed initial offering of 1,475,000 shares of common stock, of which one million will be sold by the company and 475,000 by holders, via Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co., Volpe, Welty & Co. and Robert W. Baird & Co.

West Penn Power Co., a unit of Allegheny Power System Inc., shelf offering of up to $130 million of first mortgage bonds.
