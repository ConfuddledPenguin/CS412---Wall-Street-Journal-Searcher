Who's News: Sifco Picks Gotschall For Additional Post Of Chief Executive

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

CLEVELAND -- Sifco Industries Inc. directors elected Jeffrey P. Gotschall, president, to the additional position of chief executive officer of this producer of aircraft-engine parts and other metal products. As chief executive, Mr. Gotschall, who is 42 years old, succeeds Kevin O'Donnell, who is 65 and will retire June 30. Mr. O'Donnell will continue as a director.

Mr. Gotschall was president of the company's turbine-parts group until his election as Sifco's president in 1989. He has been a director since 1986. He is a nephew of the company's chairman, Charles H. Smith Jr., and his grandfather, Charles H. Smith Sr., served as president of the company from 1925 to 1942.
