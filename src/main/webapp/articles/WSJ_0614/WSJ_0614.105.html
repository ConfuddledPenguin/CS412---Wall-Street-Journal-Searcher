Who's News: Fannie Mae Chooses Riordan for Position In Operations, Systems

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

WASHINGTON -- The Federal National Mortgage Association named Dale P. Riordan as executive vice president for operations and systems, succeeding Samuel A. Alward, who will become a technical adviser on information technology to Fannie Mae's president. Robert J. Levin, whose most recent post at Fannie Mae has been senior vice president for marketing and mortgage-backed securities, was chosen to succeed Mr. Riordan as Fannie Mae's executive vice president for marketing.

Larry H. Dale will take Mr. Levin's former post. He will retain his current responsibilities as senior vice president for multifamily finance and housing initiatives until a successor is named.

Fannie Mae's chairman, David Maxwell, said that with the completion of the huge job of computerizing details on six million mortgages, "Fannie Mae needs to integrate fully its business side with the company's information technology capacities."
