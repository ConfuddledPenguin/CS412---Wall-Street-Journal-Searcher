Business Brief -- Getty Petroleum Corp.: Gasoline Marketer Reports 1st-Quarter Net Sank 97%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Getty Petroleum Corp., the independent gasoline marketer, reported fiscal first-quarter net income sank 97%. The company cited "extreme volatility affecting our cost of product" and a "very competitive reselling market." In the quarter ended April 30, earnings dropped to $196,000, or two cents a share, from $7.7 million, or 58 cents a share, in the year-earlier period. Revenue fell 8.2% to $251 million from $273.4 million.

The Jericho, N.Y.-based company also said that results reflected $1.1 million of additional expense from compliance with environmental rules. Getty also reported a $2.1 million gain from an accounting adjustment.

Getty, formerly Power Test Corp., was formed by the purchase of the Getty name, marketing assets and terminals from Texaco Inc., when the White Plains, N.Y., oil giant bought Getty Oil Co.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Getty Petroleum shares closed unchanged at $19.75.
