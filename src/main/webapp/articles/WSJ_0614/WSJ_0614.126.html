Canada Will Auction Bonds

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

OTTAWA -- The Canadian government next Tuesday will auction 650 million Canadian dollars (US$554.6 million) of 10.25% bonds, due March 15, 2014, the Finance Department said. Proceeds from the sale will be used to redeem C$600 million of maturing government bonds and for general government purposes.
