International Brief -- Barclays Bank PLC: Company Intends to Raise $100 Million in U.S. Issue

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Barclays Bank PLC said yesterday it plans to raise $100 million through an issue of preference shares to U.S. investors. The British bank said the fixed-rate, noncumulative dollar-denominated preference shares will be redeemable at the option of the bank in 10 years.

Brian Worsley, assistant treasurer of Barclays, said the bank may raise more than the level announced depending upon market reaction.

He noted that Barclays's entry into the U.S. preference-share market last year began with a planned $200 million issue, but eventually it blossomed to $500 million after strong interest was shown among investors.

And he added that Barclays has the ability to raise additional dollar capital under a shelf registration filed a number of months ago with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, notifying the SEC that it planned to raise up to $500 million through additional preference shares.
