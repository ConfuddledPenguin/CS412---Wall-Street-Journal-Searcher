Business Brief -- SCEcorp: San Diego Gas & Electric Merger Is Upheld by U.S.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

SCEcorp, Rosemead, Calif., said that the Justice Department won't oppose the proposed merger of the holding company and its Southern California Edison Co. utility unit with San Diego Gas & Electric Co. The merger was approved by SCEcorp and San Diego Gas & Electric shareholders in April 1989; it is awaiting approvals by the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission and the California Public Utilities Commission.

The Justice Department, a government agency that reviews antitrust matters, has agreed on conditions that it says "adequately resolve any concerns" about the impact of the proposed merger on competition, SCEcorp said.

Under the agreement, the combined company would extend commitments to make transmission capacity in certain areas available to other utilities. SCEcorp and San Diego Gas & Electric also agreed they wouldn't enter into any power purchase contracts with affiliated energy producers without prior approval of the California commission.
