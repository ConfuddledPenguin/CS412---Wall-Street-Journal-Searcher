Who's News: Wellcome PLC

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

WELLCOME PLC (London) -- James Cochrane, 46, and Keith Merrifield, 48, have been appointed to the boards of both this international pharmaceutical group and Wellcome Foundation Ltd., the group's operating arm, effective in August. Mr. Cochrane is currently president, Northern Europe, of Bristol-Myers Squibb Co. On the retirement of Claude Matons, he will be responsible for Wellcome's operations in Europe and for Calmic International Ltd, a wholly owned subsidiary of the group. Mr. Merrifield is currently managing director of another unit, Wellcome International Trading Ltd., and will become responsible for international operations and Wellcome Environmental Health, a third unit.
