Spanish Car Sales Fell in May

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

MADRID -- Spanish car sales dropped 4.8% in May to 93,573 units from 98,339 a year earlier, the Spanish Association of Car Makers said. In the first five months of this year, sales fell 5.8% to 450,334 cars from 478,183 in the year-earlier period.

In May, imported car sales fell 2.4% to 12,258 units from 12,553 a year earlier, and domestic sales slipped 5.2% to 81,315 cars from 85,786.
