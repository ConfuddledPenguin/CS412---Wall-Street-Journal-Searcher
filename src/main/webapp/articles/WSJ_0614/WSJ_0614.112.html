French Crude Steel Production

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

PARIS -- French companies produced 1,701,000 metric tons of crude steel in May, down 5.2% from a year earlier, the National Steel Manufacturers' Association said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds. For the first five months of this year, output totaled 8,285,000 metric tons, down 4.1% from the year-earlier period.
