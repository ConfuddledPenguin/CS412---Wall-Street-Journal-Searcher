  • Date: 06/14/90

MAGAZINES: Bob Guccione's General Media International Inc. acquired three automotive magazines -- Stock Car Racing, SuperStock and Drag, and Open Wheel -- from Lopez Publications. The price wasn't disclosed. General Media, which recently acquired Compute] magazine from Capital Cities/ABC Inc., said it is "actively" seeking other automotive magazines to buy.

NEWSPAPERS: British publisher Robert Maxwell is still pursuing a proposed purchase of 49% of the West Australian newspaper, a Maxwell spokesman said, despite a rebuff from the Australian government, which opposes foreign control of a major paper. Agreement to buy the stake from Bell Group Ltd., a Bond Corp. Holdings Ltd. unit, was announced Monday.

WHO'S NEWS: Robert B. Bogart was named vice president of human resources administration at Macmillan Inc., the New York-based unit of Maxwell Communication Corp. He succeeds Cam Starrett, who left the company. Mr. Bogart was senior vice president, corporate human resources and administration, at Primerica Corp.
