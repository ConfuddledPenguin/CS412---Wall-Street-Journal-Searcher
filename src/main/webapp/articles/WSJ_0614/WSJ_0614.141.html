Business Brief -- TCS Enterprises Inc.: Firm Posts Quarterly Loss, Sees Lower Earnings Ahead

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

TCS Enterprises Inc., San Diego, posted a net loss of $61,000, or two cents a share, for the second quarter, ended April 30, and said it expects lower earnings for the rest of the fiscal year. In the year-earlier second quarter, the financial and business services company earned $538,000, or 17 cents a share.

Tom C. Stickel, chairman and chief executive officer, said that the second-quarter loss resulted from "a reduction in income from real estate-related projects from last year's second quarter versus this year." He added that the lower earnings for the year will result from "a substantial increase in capital expenditures as we further develop our publishing efforts."
