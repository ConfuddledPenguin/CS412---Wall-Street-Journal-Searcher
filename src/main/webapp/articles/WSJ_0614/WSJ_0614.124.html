International Brief -- Racal Electronics PLC: Pretax Earnings Rose 13% For Year Ended March 31

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

Racal Electronics PLC said pretax profit for the year ended March 31 rose 13% to #201 million ($342.4 million) from #178 million a year earlier largely because of substantial growth in its telecommunications sector. The company said profit after tax and minority interests was little changed at #117 million.

Racal's 80%-owned Racal Telecom PLC, which was created out of its telecommunications division and listed for trading on London's Stock Exchange in late 1988, separately reported a 95% rise in pretax profit on a 69% increase in sales.
