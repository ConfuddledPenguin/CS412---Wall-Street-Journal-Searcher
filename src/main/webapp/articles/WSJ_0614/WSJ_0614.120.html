Phelps Dodge Labor Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

PHOENIX, Ariz. -- Phelps Dodge Corp. said unionized employees at it Silver City, N.M., copper mine ratified a new three-year labor contract that provides wage increases of about 21% over the life of the contract. The contract, which covers about 900 workers, becomes effective July 1. Phelps Dodge is a major producer of copper and other metals.
