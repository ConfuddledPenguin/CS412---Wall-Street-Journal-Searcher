International Brief -- KLM Royal Dutch Airlines: Carrier Reports Net Income Dropped 8% in Fiscal 1990

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

Hurt by costs that rose faster than its income, KLM Royal Dutch Airlines reported final figures that showed that its profit in the year ended March 30, dropped 8% to 340 million guilders ($178 million) from 370 million guilders. In terms of its per-share profit, KLM reported that this figure declined 9.1% to 6.43 guilders from 7.08 guilders.

Although KLM reported that its revenue in the most recent fiscal year rose by 8.2% to 6.46 billion guilders from the year-earlier level of 5.97 billion guilders, this advance was outstripped by a rise in costs of 10% to 6.150 billion guilders from 5.581 billion guilders.
