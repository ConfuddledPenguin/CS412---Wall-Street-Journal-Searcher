Business Brief -- Olympia Broadcasting Corp.: Seattle Concern in Filing For Chapter 11 Protection

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Olympia Broadcasting Corp. said it filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. Seattle-based Olympia, which had a 1989 loss of $11 million on revenue of $15.8 million, had been deteriorating for months and had met little success in attempts to sell radio stations to raise money.

The company listed assets of $21.9 million and liabilities of $38 million. It said the filing also covers one subsidiary but does not include Olympia Broadcasting Networks unit, its radio networks unit, or any of the units that own the radio stations.
