Liberty Corp. Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

GREENEVILLE, S.C. -- Liberty Corp. said it agreed to buy back 760,591, or 8.9%, of its common shares outstanding from Industrial Equity (Pacific) Ltd. for $50 a share, or $38 million. The insurance holding company said Hong Kong-based Industrial Equity agreed to limit its future holding in Liberty to up to 4.9% of Liberty's 8.5 million common shares.
