Who's News: Russ Togs Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

RUSS TOGS Inc. (New York) -- John W. Burden III, retired chairman of Federated/Allied Department Stores, the U.S. unit of Campeau Corp., was named a director of this apparel maker, filling a vacancy left by Robert S. Rubin's resignation, and will act as a consultant to Russ Togs.
