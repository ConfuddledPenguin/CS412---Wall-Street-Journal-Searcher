REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Saint Madonna

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Rock reviewers in New York City are doing some nimble writing about Madonna's "Blond Ambition" concert, in which she performed in a "dominatrix" outfit, is attended on a red velvet bed by male dancers and kicks on the ground her two female backup singers, among other things. One local critic wrote: "She pushed the limits of morality but never once went over the edge." Maybe theologians should be reviewing this show.
