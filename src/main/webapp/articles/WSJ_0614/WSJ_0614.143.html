Business Brief -- Armstrong World Industries Inc.: Thomasville Furniture Unit To Buy Plant From Ladd

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Thomasville Furniture Industries Inc., a unit of Armstrong World Industries, Inc., said it signed a letter of intent to buy a furniture manufacturing plant in High Point, N.C., from Ladd Furniture Inc. The amount wasn't disclosed. The purchase, expected to be completed by late July, includes manufacturing equipment, a 205,000-square-foot building and about 22 acres of land, the companies announced.

Thomasville Furniture's President Frederick B. Starr said he expects production to begin in 1991. He had no estimate on eventual employment at the site.

Thomasville Furniture, based in Thomasville, N.C., and its affiliates operate plants in North Carolina, Virginia, Mississippi and Tennessee.

The proposed purchase is subject to approval by Thomasville and Armstrong.
