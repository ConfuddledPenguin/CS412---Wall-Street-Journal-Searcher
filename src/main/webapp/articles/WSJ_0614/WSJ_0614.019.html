Who's News: Belmoral Mines Ltd.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

BELMORAL MINES Ltd. (Toronto) -- J. Gordon Strasser, acting president and chief executive officer of this gold mining company, was confirmed in the posts, succeeding Kenneth Dalton, who retired as chairman and chief executive, and Clive Brown, who retired as president. Messrs. Dalton and Brown remain directors. A new chairman wasn't named.
