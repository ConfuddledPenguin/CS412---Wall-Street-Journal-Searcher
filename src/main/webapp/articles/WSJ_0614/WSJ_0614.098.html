Who's News: Santa Fe Energy Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

SANTA FE ENERGY RESOURCES Inc. (Houston) -- James L. Payne, president and chief executive officer of this oil and gas exploration and production company, was elected to the additional post of chairman. The company also expanded the board to nine members from six and elected as new directors Rod Dammeyer, president of Itel Corp.; Lionel G. Dodd, chief operating officer of O&Y Enterprises Inc.; and Allan V. Martini, a retired senior vice president of Chevron Corp. Separately, J.L. Bridwell, senior vice president for exploration and land, resigned as a director, creating a vacancy.
