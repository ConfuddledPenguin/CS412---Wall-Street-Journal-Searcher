Business Brief -- Sussex Trust Co.: Wilmington Trust Will Buy Bank in $68 Million Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 06/14/90

Wilmington Trust Co. said it agreed to acquire Sussex Trust Co. for $68 million, or $42.50 a share, in a tax-free stock swap. Sussex Trust stockholders will receive between 1.037 and 1.149 shares of Wilmington Trust stock for each share of Sussex stock, depending on the average market price of Wilmington stock during the 20 days before the transaction receives regulatory approval.

Under the plan, a holding company for Wilmington Trust will operate the two banks. They will remain Wilmington Trust Co. in northern Delaware and Sussex Trust in Southern Delaware.

Sussex has $420 million in assets and 20 locations in the state.
