Business Brief -- Petroleum Investments Ltd.: Firm to Merge Partnerships In Oil, Gas With Bradmar

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

Petroleum Investments Ltd., Oklahoma City, said it plans to consolidate its oil and gas partnerships with a recently formed affiliate, Bradmar Petroleum Corp. The company said Bradmar will offer to exchange one share of its common stock for every 10 units of the partnerships and for every five dollars of exchange value of the partnerships' interests in the programs. Bradmar is also offering 16,935 shares for the general partner's interest in the limited partnerships. The exchange offer, which expires July 13, is subject to the receipt of about 8.4 million of the 16.8 million outstanding units.

Petroleum Investments said it has decided not to make a quarterly distribution during the offering period; therefore, no distributions will be made in the second quarter. The partnership last paid a distribution of two cents on September 29, 1989.
