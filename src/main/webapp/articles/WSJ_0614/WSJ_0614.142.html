Hughes Aircraft Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 06/14/90

LOS ANGELES -- Hughes Aircraft Co. said it acquired the Avicom International unit of Lockheed Corp., Calabasas, Calif. Terms weren't disclosed. Avicom, Pasadena, Calif., is a supplier of audio and video entertainment and communication systems to the commercial airline industry. It has about 250 employees.

Hughes, a unit of General Motors Corp., said the acquisition will allow it to offer aircraft makers and airlines "complete cabin communications capability, including data handling, and a total service support network." Hughes already supplies audio signal distribution systems to the commercial airline industry.
