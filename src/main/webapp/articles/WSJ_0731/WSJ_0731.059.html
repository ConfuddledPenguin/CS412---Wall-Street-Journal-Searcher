Letters to the Editor: Trefoiled Again

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

I was interested to note that you added a new word to the French language in your June 22 article on Shamrock Holdings. You say trefoil is French for shamrock. Until now, trefoil was not French for shamrock or anything else. {The French word for shamrock, actually, is "trefle."}

Howard Brandon

Madison, Ga.
