IMF Approves Honduras Loan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

WASHINGTON -- The International Monetary Fund approved a $41 million loan to Honduras to support economic adjustments in the Central American country during the next 12 months. Honduras qualified for the IMF financial assistance after paying overdue loans.

The IMF noted that Honduras also cleared up its loan arrears to the World Bank and the Inter-American Development Bank in late June and early July and thus has regained eligibility for new credits from all three international agencies.
