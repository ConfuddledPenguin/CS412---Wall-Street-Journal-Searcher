International Brief -- Asahi Breweries Ltd.: Unconsolidated Pretax Profit For First Half Rose 3.2%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

Asahi Breweries Ltd. of Japan reported that its first-half unconsolidated pretax earnings rose 3.2% to 11.76 billion yen ($78.9 million) from 11.39 billion yen a year earlier. Net income climbed 14% to 3.74 billion yen from 3.29 billion yen. Per-share net fell to 9.35 yen from 10.66 yen, because of an increase in shares outstanding.

Sales increased 13% to 324.32 billion yen from 287.11 billion yen, because of the popularity in the domestic market of Asahi's dry beer, a sharp-tasting beer with a slightly higher alcohol content than standard beers have. Asahi has about an 86% share of the domestic market for dry beer.
