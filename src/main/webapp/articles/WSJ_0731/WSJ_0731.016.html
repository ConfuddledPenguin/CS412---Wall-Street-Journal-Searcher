Raw-Steel Production Fell 0.1% Last Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

WASHINGTON -- Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 0.1% last week to 1,845,000 tons from 1,847,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said. Last week's output was unchanged from the same period a year earlier.

The industry operated at 82.7% of its capacity last week, compared with 82.8% the previous week and 83% a year ago.
