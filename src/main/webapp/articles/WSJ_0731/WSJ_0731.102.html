Business Brief -- New Plan Realty Trust: Focus Will Be on Property That Is Foreclosed by Banks

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

New Plan Realty Trust, the nation's largest equity real estate investment trust, announced yesterday that it intends to step up its acquisition of shopping centers and apartment complexes by focusing on foreclosed bank property. "We feel that this marketplace is a good one," said William Newman, chairman, adding that New Plan has accumulated $140 million with which to buy properties over the next 12 to 18 months.

New Plan, based in New York, invests primarily in strip centers in the East, not large enclosed malls. Mr. Newman said that the trust aims to pick up the distressed properties at 25% to 50% of replacement value. In fiscal 1990, the trust purchased 14 shopping centers.
