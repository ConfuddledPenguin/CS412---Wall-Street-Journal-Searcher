Business Brief -- Giant Group Ltd.: Board Authorizes Purchase Of Rally's Common Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

Giant Group Ltd., Beverly Hills, Calif., said its board authorized the purchase of common stock in Rally's Inc., a hamburger chain in which Giant is the leader of a group that holds about a 44% stake. Giant Group didn't say how much stock it might acquire in public or private transactions, and cautioned that its prospective purchases depended on the price of Rally's stock, interest rates, market conditions and other factors.

Rally's stock appeared little-affected by Giant's cautious announcement, closing unchanged at $16 a share in national over-the-counter trading.

Giant, a cement, waste processing and fast-food concern controlled by Beverly Hills financier Burt Sugarman, said in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing last October that it had sought approval under federal antitrust laws to acquire more than a 50% stake in Rally's, based in Louisville, Ky.

In Louisville, Richard Sherman, chairman and chief executive officer of Rally's, welcomed Giant's prospective purchases. "They've been an excellent shareholder," he said.

Rally's currently has about 6.8 million shares outstanding.
