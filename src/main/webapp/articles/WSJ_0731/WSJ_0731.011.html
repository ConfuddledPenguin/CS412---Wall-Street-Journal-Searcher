Guardian Bancorp Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

LOS ANGELES -- Guardian Bancorp said it will periodically purchase as many as 75,000 of its common shares in the open market or in unsolicited private transactions at market prices. Paul M. Harris, chairman, said the holding company for Guardian Bank believes that at current levels, the stock is undervalued.

In American Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Guardian Bancorp fell 25 cents to $12.875.
