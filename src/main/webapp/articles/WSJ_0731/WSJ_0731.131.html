Parker Drilling Completes Deal

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

TULSA, Okla. -- Parker Drilling Co., an energy-industry drilling contractor, said it completed the sale of the pipeline assets of its Parker Gas Cos. unit to Western Gas Processors Ltd., Denver. Terms weren't disclosed. The assets included 450 miles of pipeline primarily in Giddings, Texas, Parker said.
