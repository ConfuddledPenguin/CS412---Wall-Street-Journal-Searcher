Loan to Turkey Guaranteed

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

WASHINGTON -- The U.S. Export-Import Bank said it will guarantee a $37.5 million loan by Citibank N.A., New York, to the Turkish government for the purchase of five Blackhawk helicopters from the Sikorsky Aircraft division of United Technologies Corp. The helicopters, which will be used by Turkey's national police for law enforcement, medical emergencies and rescue operations, will cost $44.4 million, including spare parts and related ground-support equipment.
