Who's News: First Constitution Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

FIRST CONSTITUTION FINANCIAL Corp. (New Haven, Conn.) -- John J. Crawford, 45 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this thrift institution, after holding the posts on a temporary basis since April, following the resignation of Walter R. Miller Jr. Mr. Crawford was previously a director of the thrift and a financial consultant.
