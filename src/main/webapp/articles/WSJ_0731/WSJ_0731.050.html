REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: The Blue Pencil Ax

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

Gov. George Deukmejian and a sufficiency of legislators have agreed to hit up Californians for $800 million in miscellany taxes and fees and to install a Gramm-Rudman type spending fix. This is not exactly a precedent for any budget deal in Washington. The governor can make this deal stick by line-item vetoing $400 million from the budget, but President Bush still has no blue pencil in his kit bag.
