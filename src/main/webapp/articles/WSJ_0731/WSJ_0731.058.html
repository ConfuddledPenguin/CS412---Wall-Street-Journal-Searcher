Chevron Sells Bahamas Facilities

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

SAN FRANCISCO -- Chevron Corp. said it completed the sale of its Freeport, Bahamas, storage and marine facilities to Petroleos de Venezuela S.A., the state-owned oil company of Venezuela. Chevron said it will receive about $120 million in cash, plus a long-term contractual right to storage services in Freeport. The company previously hadn't indicated the payment. The sale includes storage and marine facilities, and a refinery that has been inoperative since 1985.
