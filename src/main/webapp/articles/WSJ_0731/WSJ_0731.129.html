OEA Settles Colorado Dispute

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

DENVER -- OEA Inc. said it reached an out-of-court settlement with the Colorado Department of Health that allowed the maker of explosive and propellant devices to resume production last Sunday. OEA had suspended operations last week, after the state agency sued the company, alleging violations of the Colorado Hazardous Waste Act. The settlement requires OEA to change its handling of waste water, but the company said the changes won't have a material effect on its finances.
