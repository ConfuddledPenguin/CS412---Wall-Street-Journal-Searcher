Who's News: Pfizer Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

PFIZER Inc. (New York) -- This pharmaceutical and health care company named Barry M. Bloom, 61 years old, to the new post of Pfizer senior vice president, research and development, effective Sept. 1. Succeeding Mr. Bloom as president of the Central Research division is John F. Niblack, 51. Mr. Bloom also had been a vice president of the parent; Mr. Niblack, currently executive vice president of Central Research, will be nominated as a vice president of Pfizer. His successor as executive vice president at Central Research hasn't been named.
