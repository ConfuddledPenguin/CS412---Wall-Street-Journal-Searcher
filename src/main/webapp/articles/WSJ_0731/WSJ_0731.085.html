Bank Declines State Protection

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

PITTSBURGH -- Community Bancorp, a bank holding company, said it declined protection, as allowed, under Pennsylvania's new anti-takeover law, the nation's toughest, which places voting restrictions on large shareholders and discourages so-called greenmail, or a company's buying back its stock from an unwanted suitor at higher-than-market prices.
