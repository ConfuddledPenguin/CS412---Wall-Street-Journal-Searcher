Who's News: Connecticut Bancorp Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

CONNECTICUT BANCORP Inc. (Norwalk, Conn.) -- Kevin G. Woelflein was named chairman of the holding company and its Norwalk Bank subsidiary. Mr. Woelflein, 57 years old, succeeds Frank J. Cooke, 69, who is retiring. Mr. Woelflein remains president of U.S. Capital Investments Co., a Greenwich, Conn., bank-advisory firm.
