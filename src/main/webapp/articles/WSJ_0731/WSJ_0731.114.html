Business Brief -- Canadian Pacific Ltd: Pulp-Wood Producer Plans To Close Mill for a Month

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

Canadian Pacific Forest Products Ltd., citing soft demand, said it plans to shut its pulp mill in Gold River, British Columbia, for a month beginning Aug. 4. The 80%-owned subsidiary of Canadian Pacific Ltd. said the mill has annual capacity of 240,000 metric tons of bleached kraft softwood pulp. Wood pulp is used to make paper and other products. A metric ton equals about 2,204.6 pounds.

The Canadian pulp industry has been plagued this year by weak demand. The Canadian Pulp and Paper Association said Canadian pulp mills operated at about 80% of capacity in June, down from 96% a year earlier.
