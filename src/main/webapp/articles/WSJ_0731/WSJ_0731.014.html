Magna to Sell Unit to Siemens

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

MARKHAM, Ontario -- Magna International Inc. said it agreed to sell its automotive electric-motors division to a unit of Siemens AG of West Germany. Terms weren't disclosed. Magna, an auto-parts maker, said earlier this month that it was in the final stages of negotiating the sale of the division, which has annual sales of about 120 million Canadian dollars ($104 million), to the Siemens unit.
