Trimac to Buy Transit Business

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

CALGARY, Alberta -- Trimac Ltd. said its Trimac Transportation System subsidiary agreed to acquire CP Bulk Systems, a unit of Canadian Pacific Ltd. Terms weren't disclosed. Trimac said CP Bulk is a transportation concern specializing in bulk commodities and generated revenue last year of about 40 million Canadian dollars (US$34.7 million). The proposed transaction is subject to regulatory approval.
