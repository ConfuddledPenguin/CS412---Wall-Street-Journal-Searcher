Who's News: Daiwa Securities Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

DAIWA SECURITIES Co. (Tokyo) -- Russell J. Greenberg, previously a director of mergers at the Prudential-Bache Securities Inc. of Prudential Insurance Co. of America, was named executive vice president and head of mergers and acquisitions at Daiwa Securities America Inc., the U.S. subsidiary of this securities firm, succeeding Sidney Koch, who will become a special adviser.
