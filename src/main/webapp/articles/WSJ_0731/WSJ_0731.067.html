Inter-Regional Financial

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

MINNEAPOLIS -- Inter-Regional Financial Group Inc. said it reached a settlement of a lawsuit with Touche Ross & Co. providing for a cash payment of $19.75 million to Inter-Regional by September of this year. The suit resolves a 1986 lawsuit by IFG, a financial services holding company, relating to certain audit services by Touche.

The settlement will increase IFG's net income by about $12 million, or a $1.25 a share, for the third quarter ending Sept. 30. In the quarter ended last Sept. 30, the company reported net income of $1.4 million, or 15 cents a share, and revenue of $81.8 million.
