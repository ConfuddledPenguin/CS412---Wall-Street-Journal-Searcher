Business Brief -- Baldwin Technology Co.: Printing Equipment Concern Acquires Misomex Units

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

Baldwin Technology Co. of Rowayton, Conn., completed the acquisition of Misomex AB of Sweden and Misomex of North America Inc. from Dyson-Kissner-Moran Corp. for $44 million. The acquisition, which includes Misomex subsidiaries in England, Sweden, Germany and the U.S., expands Baldwin's current product lines in printing-control equipment and moves the company into prepress equipment, which is used to transfer images to printing plates.

Gerald A. Nathe, president of Baldwin Americas Corp., a unit of Baldwin Technology, will serve as chairman of Misomex International, which will oversee the Misomex subsidiaries. Howard LeVine remains president of Misomex of North America, and Gustav Talts and Gordon Kirsopp will continue as managing directors of Misomex AB and Misomex UK, respectively.
