Business Brief -- Alpine Group Inc.: General Plasma Unit Sold To Ontario Corp. of Indiana

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

Alpine Group Inc. said it reached a definitive agreement to sell its General Plasma Inc. unit for $19 million to Ontario Corp., Muncie, Ind. General Plasma operates thermal spray coating systems. Alpine, based in Hackensack, N.J., said it expects to complete the transaction by Aug. 31, subject to completion of financing by Ontario. It said it would continue its operations in the electronic information display business.

Separately, Alpine reported earnings of $3.8 million, or 74 cents a share, for the fiscal year ended April 30, compared with a loss of $11.7 million a year earlier. The profit was attributable to a $20 million extraordinary gain from "extinguishment of debt," the company said. The company posted losses of $4.3 million on continuing operations and $11.8 million on discontinued operations. In fiscal 1989, those losses were $5.4 million and $6.3 million respectively. Sales from continuing operations were $7.9 million, compared with $6.3 million the previous year.
