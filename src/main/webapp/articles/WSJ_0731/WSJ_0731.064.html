Foremost Corp. of America

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Foremost Corp. of America said it plans to buy back up to one million of its common shares. The insurance company said the new authorization comes in addition to a prior stock repurchase plan, under which Foremost purchased about 1.2 million of its shares since November 1987. No purchases under the newly authorized plan have been made, Foremost said.

The company said it will purchase the shares from time to time, depending on market conditions and availability. Foremost currently has 11.7 shares of common stock outstanding.
