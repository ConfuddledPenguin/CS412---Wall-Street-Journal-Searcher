Family Bancorp Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

HAVERHILL, Mass. -- Family Bancorp said that in an agreement with the Federal Deposit Insurance Corp., it acquired the assets and certain deposits of the insolvent U.S. Savings Bank of America, Seabrook, N.H., which was closed Friday. Family, which has assets of about $480 million, said it infused $1.5 million of capital into U.S. Savings and renamed it Family Savings Bank of New Hampshire, with assets of about $14 million, including the infusion.
