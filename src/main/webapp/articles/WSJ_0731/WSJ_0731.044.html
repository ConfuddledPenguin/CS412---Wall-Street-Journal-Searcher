Diagnostek Unit Acquires Epic

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

ALBUQUERQUE, N.M. -- Diagnostek Inc. said its Saga Acquisition Corp. unit acquired Epic Health Group in a tender offer valued at $13.5 million. Saga purchased 10.5 million, or 96%, of Epic's common shares and 200,000, or 100%, of Epic's Series A convertible preferred shares. Both Diagnostek and Epic are mail-order providers of prescription drugs. Diagnostek also manages pharmacies for health care institutions.
