AT&T Gets Amtrak Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

MORRISTOWN, N.J. -- AT&T Computer Systems received a $14 million contract to improve Amtrak's reservation and ticketing systems. The company is a subsidiary of American Telephone & Telegraph Co., the New York telecommunications giant.

AT&T Computer Systems will install and maintain more than 2,100 networked computers, software and peripheral equipment at more than 300 Amtrak facilities.
