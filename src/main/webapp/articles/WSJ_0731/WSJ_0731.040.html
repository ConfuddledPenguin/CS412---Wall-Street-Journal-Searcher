International Technology Pact

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

TORRANCE, Calif. -- International Technology Corp. said it signed a definitive agreement with U.S. Testing Co. to acquire for cash the assets of U.S. Testing's analytical laboratory located in Richland, Wash. Terms were not disclosed.

The acquisition, which is subject to certain conditions, including the completion of an environmental audit and survey, is expected to close in mid-August, International Technology said.

U.S. Testing, a Hoboken, N.J.-based environmental-testing concern, is a unit of SGS North America, a subsidiary of SGS S.A., Geneva, Switzerland.

The Richland laboratory performs analytical testing of hazardous nuclear, chemical, and mixed-waste substances.

International Technology is an environmental management company providing services to government and industry.
