MCI Gets Job From Insurer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

WASHINGTON -- MCI Communications Corp. said it agreed to serve as the main telecommunications carrier for Continental Corp., a New York-based property and casualty insurer, in a three-year deal valued at about $13 million. MCI said it will provide the full range of voice and data services to Continental, which has about $13 billion in assets.
