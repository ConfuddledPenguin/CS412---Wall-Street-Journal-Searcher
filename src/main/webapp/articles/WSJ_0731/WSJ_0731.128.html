PITTSBURGH -- Security Bureau Inc., a subsidiary of Chambers Development Co., an environmental company, said it acquired Dynamic Protection Services Inc., a security services concern in Orange, Calif. Terms weren't disclosed. The acquisition is expected to generate annual revenue of $4.1 million, Chambers said. Security Bureau, the 14th-largest security services company in the U.S., provides uniformed guard services, crisis management, security surveys, loss prevention and investigative services. Chambers offers services in collection, hauling, disposal, recycling and landfill development.
