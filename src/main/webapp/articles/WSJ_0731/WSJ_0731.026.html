Who's News: WSGP Partners Limited Partnership

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

WSGP PARTNERS Limited Partnership (Los Angeles) -- Richard K. Roeder, 43 years old, previously a partner in the law firm Paul, Hastings, Janofsky & Walker, was named a managing director at this investment firm founded by William E. Simon, a former Treasury secretary, and Gerald L. Parsky.
