International Brief -- Bayerische Vereinsbank AG: Partial Operating Earnings For First Half Grew 3.7%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 07/31/90

Bayerische Vereinsbank AG of West Germany, citing higher mortgage lending and a general rise in overall loan business, said its partial group operating profit for the first half increased 3.7%. The big mortgage and commercial banking concern said partial operating profit, which excludes income from the bank's trading in its own account, rose to 421 million marks ($261.2 million) from 406 million marks a year earlier.

First-half full operating profit exceeded that of a year earlier, the bank said, but didn't give details.

At the parent bank, partial operating profit rose 11% to 284 million marks from 256 million marks. The bank's full operating profit rose sharply, the bank said, citing higher profit from trading in its own account in stocks, bonds and foreign exchange.
