Rustenburg Posts Lower Profit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 07/31/90

JOHANNESBURG, South Africa -- Rustenburg Platinum Holdings Ltd. said its earnings fell 7.5% in the year ended June 30. Net profit amounted to 551.3 million rand ($211.3 million) from the previous financial year's figure of 595.7 million rand. Earnings amounted to 440.0 cents ($1.68) a share compared with 475.4 cents the previous year.

An unchanged final dividend of 185 cents a share has been declared, bringing total dividend distribution for the year to 310 cents, up 3.3% from 300 cents in the previous year.
