Swedish Trade Surplus

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's March foreign trade surplus rose to 3.4 billion kronor ($558 million) from 1.8 billion kronor in February and from 2.6 billion kronor in the corresponding month last year, Statistics Sweden said. The March surplus exceeded by far the 1.8 billion krona average prediction of analysts.

The national statistics agency said preliminary March figures showed that exports totaled 32.3 billion kronor, up 9% from March 1989, while imports amounted to 28.9 billion kronor, up 7% from the corresponding month last year.
