Rohr Gets Boeing Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

CHULA VISTA, Calif. -- Rohr Industries Inc., which makes aircraft engine housings and parts, and solid rocket motor casings and nozzles, said it received a contract from Seattle-based Boeing Co. valued at more than $280 million, including options, to provide jet engine struts for Boeing 757 aircraft. The new contract begins in January; Rohr has delivered struts for more than 300 Boeing 757s since 1981 under a 500-airplane contract. A strut is the structure that attaches the propulsion system to the aircraft.
