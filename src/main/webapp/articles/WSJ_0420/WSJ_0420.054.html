REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Dukakis Holds Bunker Hill

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

Michael Dukakis has said that foreign investment will help Massachusetts out of its economic slump, but this week he signed an emergency anti-takeover law to protect Norton Co. from BTR. This is a new "war for independence," he said, likening the British firm's bid to "another attempt by a foreign power to interfere with our ability to share our own destiny." Hmm. We imagine Norton shareholders, denied a vote on the anti-takeover rules, will count themselves as conscientious objectors.
