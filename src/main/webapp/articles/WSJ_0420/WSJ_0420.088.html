Marketing Brief: Noted....

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

HEPBURN: Publisher Alfred A. Knopf, part of Random House Inc., acquired the world rights to Katharine Hepburn's memoirs; the price wasn't disclosed. The book, tentatively titled "Me," is expected out in hardcover next year. The actress in 1987 wrote a best seller on making "The African Queen," also published by Knopf. Random House's Ballantine Books will publish a paperback version a year later.
