West German Power Use

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- Consumption of electricity in West Germany by companies, the government and private households increased 2% in the first quarter of 1990 from the first quarter of 1989, the Association of German Electric Utilities said. The association said that 98 billion kilowatt hours were were used in the period from January to March. It traced the increase to the strong growth in the nation's economy.
