Who's News: Revlon Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

REVLON Inc. (New York) -- Peter S. Zegras was named president of Fashion & Designer Fragrance Group, a new unit within the Fashion & Designer Group at this cosmetic and beauty products company. Mr. Zegras, 49 years old, previously was senior vice president, special projects, of the Fashion & Designer Group.
