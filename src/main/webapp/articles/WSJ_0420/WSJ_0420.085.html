Weyerhaeuser Profit Falls

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

TACOMA, Wash. -- Weyerhaeuser Co. said first-quarter profit slumped 12% due to depressed prices for most pulp and paper products. The forest products concern reported net income of $130.1 million, or 61 cents a share, compared with a profit of $148.1 million, or 70 cents a share, a year earlier. Revenue for the quarter ended April 1 declined 2% to $2.28 billion from $2.32 billion.
