Business Brief -- Chambers Development Co.: Permit Granted for Landfill In Somerset County, Pa.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

Chambers Development Co., a Pittsburgh-based environmental services concern, said it received a solid-waste permit from the Pennsylvania Department of Environmental Resources for a 34-acre landfill in Somerset County, Pa. With the permit, the landfill can accept an average of 2,200 tons a day of solid waste, Chambers said.

The company said it has permit applications pending for 37 acres at the Monroeville Landfill in Allegheny County, Pa., and for 82 acres at the Arden Landfill in Washington County, Pa. It also has a pending application to reopen the Fulkroad Landfill in Dauphin County, Pa.
