Who's News: Nynex Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

NYNEX Corp. (White Plains, N.Y.) -- Ivan G. Seidenberg, previously senior vice president of this telecommunications concern, was named president of its new Worldwide Information & Cellular Services Group, and Matthew J. Stover, previously senior vice president, communications, at American Express Co., was named Nynex vice president, external affairs; both appointments are effective May 1.
