Australian Retail Sales Up

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

CANBERRA, Australia -- Retail sales in Australia totaled a seasonally adjusted 7.01 billion Australian dollars (US$5.40 billion) in February, up 0.7% from A$6.97 billion (US$5.37 billion) in January and up 8% from A$6.45 billion in February 1989, the Bureau of Statistics said.
