Business Brief -- Federal Pioneer Ltd.: Canadian Regulators Clear French Firm's Tender Offer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

Group Schneider S.A. of France said it received Canadian regulatory approval to proceed with its previously reported bid for all the shares of Toronto-based Federal Pioneer Ltd., an electrical equipment maker, for a total of about 300 million Canadian dollars (US$258 million). The French industrial concern said it immediately will take up and pay for all the shares that had been tendered as of Wednesday to its offer of C$15 (US$12.91) a share. The offer remains open until noon EDT April 30.

In trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday, Federal Pioneer shares closed at at C$15, up 25 Canadian cents.

Enfield Corp., a Toronto holding company, previously agreed to tender its 53% stake in Federal Pioneer.
