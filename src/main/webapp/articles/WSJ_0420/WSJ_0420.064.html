Who's News: Medstone International Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

MEDSTONE INTERNATIONAL Inc. (Irvine, Calif.) -- Richard Ferrari, formerly executive vice president for ADAC Laboratories, was named president and chief operating officer. He succeeds Jack Olshansky, a director who served as interim president and chief operating officer since November 1989. Medstone makes and markets medical services and equipment, mostly for the treatment of kidney stones.
