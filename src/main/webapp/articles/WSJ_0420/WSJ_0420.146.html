Business Brief -- North Fork Bancorp.: Holding Firm Will Acquire Eastchester Financial Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

North Fork Bancorp. said in Mattituck, N.Y., that it agreed definitively to acquire Eastchester Financial Corp. for $20 a share, or $79.5 million. As part of the agreement, Eastchester agreed to suspend the payment of its quarterly dividend of 10 cents a share. Eastchester Savings Bank, a unit of Eastchester Financial, will operate as a division of North Fork's Southold Savings Bank unit. The accord requires shareholder and regulatory approvals.

Malcolm J. Delaney, president and chief executive officer of Eastchester Financial, will serve as chief executive of the Eastchester division and as a vice chairman of Southold Savings.

North Fork Bancorp. is the holding company for North Fork Bank & Trust Co. as well as for Southold Savings. Assets totaled $1.8 billion at Dec. 31, 1989, when Eastchester's assets totaled $524.9 million.
