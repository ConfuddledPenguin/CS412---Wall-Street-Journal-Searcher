Shell Oil Mulls Selling a Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

HOUSTON -- Shell Oil Co., a unit of Royal Dutch/Shell Group, said that it is considering selling Triton Biosciences Inc., its wholly-owned health-care subsidiary, which develops pharmaceuticals and diagnostics for serious illnesses, and that it retained Morgan Stanley & Co. of New York as financial adviser for the project. Shell said it views Triton as "a successful health-care operation," but decided "after extensive review . . . that Triton is not sufficiently related to {Shell's} principal petrochemical business to retain it for the long term." Triton has about 300 employees in Alameda, Calif.
