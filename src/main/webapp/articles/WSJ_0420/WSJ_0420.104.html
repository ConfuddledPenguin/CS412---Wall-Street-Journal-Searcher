Who's News: Bank South Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

BANK SOUTH Corp. (Atlanta) -- Robert P. Guyton was elected chairman of this banking company. Mr. Guyton, 53 years old, succeeds Rawson Haverty, who has been chairman since 1977. Mr. Haverty, 69, will continue as a director. Mr. Guyton retains his positions as president and chief executive officer of Bank South.
