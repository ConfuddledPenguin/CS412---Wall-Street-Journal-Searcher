Business Brief -- Dresher Inc.: Leggett Says U.S. Requests More Facts About Its Bid

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

Leggett & Platt Inc. said that the Antitrust Division of the U.S. Department of Justice has issued a request for additional information in connection with Leggett's planned purchase of Dresher Inc. Based in Carthage, Mo., Leggett & Platt makes furniture and bed parts. Dresher manufactures brass beds.

Earlier this month, Leggett began a $2.50-a-share tender offer for all of the common stock outstanding valued at about $11.5 million. Leggett said that depending on the timing of the response to the Justice Department, the tender offer may be extended beyond the May 4 expiration date.

Leggett & Platt said that it intends to respond promptly to the request and that it believes the purchase doesn't violate antitrust laws.
