Halliburton Co. Unit Ends Sale

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

DALLAS -- Halliburton Co. said its Brown & Root International Inc. unit terminated a letter of intent to sell certain marine construction assets to Offshore Pipelines Inc. The assets that were to be sold included 23 derrick, pipelay and other marine construction vessels, and interests in certain land facilities in the Far East, Middle East and the U.S.

Halliburton, an oil service and construction firm, said Brown & Root will continue to use these assets while considering other options.
