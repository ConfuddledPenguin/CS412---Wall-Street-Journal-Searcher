Pitt-Des Moines Stock Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

PITTSBURGH -- Pitt-Des Moines Inc. said it will establish an employee stock ownership plan. The company will acquire at fair market value and contribute to the ESOP about 57,000 shares of its common stock from certain Jackson family charitable trusts. The family of chairman emeritus William R. Jackson holds a total of 1,173,815 shares out of 2,400,000 shares outstanding.

The company said it has entered into agreements with certain members of the Jackson family providing for the future purchase from their estates of about 255,000 shares. Those shares represent a portion of their current share ownership.

The steel fabricator said the steps were taken to address what will happen to shares owned by the Jacksons upon their death.
