Financing Business: Barrett Resources Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

BARRETT RESOURCES Corp., a Denver-based oil and gas exploration company, said it sold 3.1 million common shares for $18.4 million. Proceeds will be used for funding additional developmental drilling and for repayment of $3.1 million in bank debt. Salomon Brothers Inc. was the underwriter.
