Carlisle Plan Is Dropped

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

SYRACUSE, N.Y. -- Carlisle Cos. said its agreement to sell its Bracmar division to Hicks Data Ltd. has been terminated. Carlisle, a maker of construction materials and computer tapes, said Hicks Data failed to satisfy the financing contingency in its Oct. 27, 1989, agreement.
