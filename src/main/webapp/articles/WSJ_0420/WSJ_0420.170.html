Business Brief -- BASF AG: German Chemical Concern To Raise Dividend Payout

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

BASF AG said it will raise its 1989 dividend 8% to 13 Deutsche marks ($7.78) a share from 12 marks and will pay an additional one-mark bonus to celebrate the company's 125th anniversary. The dividend announcement by the West German chemical group had been expected by analysts after BASF last month reported an 18% jump in 1989 group pretax earnings to a record 4.38 billion marks.

The total amount distributed to shareholders will climb 17% to 798 million marks from 684 million marks in 1988, and the addition to reserves will be boosted to 600 million marks from 500 million marks, BASF said.

The dividend payment still has to be approved by the company's annual shareholder meeting June 28.
