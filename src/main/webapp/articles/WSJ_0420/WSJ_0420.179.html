Business Brief -- PacifiCare Health Systems Inc.: Offer Planned to Acquire Maxicare Health Plans Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

PacifiCare Health Systems Inc., a health maintenance organization, said it plans to make an offer to acquire Maxicare Health Plans Inc., a rival HMO that has been operating under bankruptcy-law protection for 13 months. Peter J. Ratican, Maxicare chairman and chief executive officer, responded, "We have made it clear for more than a year that Maxicare is not for sale, so we are, quite frankly, puzzled by the timing of this announcement, which comes just two business days before a crucial public hearing on our reorganization efforts."

PacifiCare, based in Cypress, Calif., said it plans to deliver a proposal to Maxicare and its creditors' committees prior to a Santa Ana, Calif., bankruptcy-court hearing scheduled for Monday to consider Maxicare's previously filed reorganization plan.

PacifiCare declined to discuss any terms.

Roughly 40% of Los Angeles-based Maxicare's 320,000 members are in California. PacifiCare said 70% of its 636,000 members are in California.
