Johns Hopkins Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

WASHINGTON -- Johns Hopkins University's applied research laboratory received a $16.7 million Navy contract for military and space studies.

General Dynamics Corp. was given a $10 million Air Force contract for research and development for a radar target-identification system.

ICF Kaiser Engineers Inc. said it was awarded a 10-year, $50 million contract by the U.S. Air Force to assess hazardous-waste disposal problems at the nation's Air Force bases. The contract calls for the engineering-services concern to evaluate and design cleanup remedies for "potentially hazardous and toxic waste sites."
