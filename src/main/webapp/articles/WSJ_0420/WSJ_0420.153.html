Business Brief -- Hathaway Corp.: Ruling Asked on Tax Status Of a Spinoff Under Study

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

Hathaway Corp. said it asked the Internal Revenue Service to rule on whether the spinoff of its computer software operation would be tax-free. The Broomfield, Colo., maker of electronic systems said it is considering spinning off the shares of the software operation, to be known as Global Software Inc., to its shareholders. The business includes Carolina Global Inc. and its Global Software Inc. unit, which together account for just under half of Hathaway's revenue.

Hathaway said it will make a final decision about the spinoff after it receives the IRS ruling and wins approval from its lenders.
