Dividend News: Bergen Brunswig Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

BERGEN BRUNSWIG Corp., an Orange, Calif., health-care products concern, said it raised its quarterly cash dividends 25% each to 10 cents a Class A common share from eight cents, and to 95.285 cents a Class B common share from 76.228 cents, both payable June 1 to stock of record May 1.
