Business Brief -- Cara Operations Ltd.: Canadian Food Firm Sets Pact to Buy Grand & Toy

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

Cara Operations Ltd., a Toronto-based food services company, said it agreed in principle to acquire Grand & Toy Ltd., a wholesale and retail distributor of stationery and office supplies. Terms weren't disclosed, but Cara said the purchase price would be funded by internal resources and borrowings. It said part of the price may be raised through the issuance of as many as one million Class A nonvoting shares at the sellers' option. Cara's Class A shares were unchanged at 15.75 Canadian dollars (US$13.56) on the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday. Cara has about 19.8 million Class A shares outstanding.

Grand & Toy, which is controlled by the Grand family of Toronto, has annual sales of about C$285 million, Cara said. Officials at both companies couldn't be reached for comment. The transaction is subject to negotiation of a final definitive agreement.
