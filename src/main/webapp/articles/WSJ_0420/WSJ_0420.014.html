Sweden's Industrial Production

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

STOCKHOLM -- Sweden's industrial production in February fell 0.2% from January and 0.5% from a year earlier, Statistics Sweden, the national statistics agency, said. The drop followed increases of 1.3% in January and 1.7% in December. In the first two months of this year, industrial production fell 0.7% from a record year-earlier level.
