Who's News: Owens-Corning Fiberglas

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

OWENS-CORNING FIBERGLAS (Toledo) -- Max O. Weber, 60 years old, was named chief executive officer of this manufacturer of glass-fiber products, succeeding William W. Boeschenstein. Mr. Boeschenstein, 64, will retire in September. He will remain a director. Mr. Weber, who joined Owens-Corning in 1955, was named president and chief operating officer in 1988. He will continue to serve in that capacity after succeeding Mr. Boeschenstein Oct. 1.
