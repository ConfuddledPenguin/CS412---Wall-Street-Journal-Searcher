U.S. Auto Firms Say All Assembly Plants Are Open Next Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 04/20/90

DETROIT -- U.S. auto makers said all their vehicle-assembly plants are scheduled to operate next week, with several plants working overtime. General Motors Corp. scheduled daily and weekend overtime at its Wilmington, Del., Tarrytown, N.Y., Janesville, Wis., and Lansing, Mich., assembly plants.

Ford Motor Co. said it scheduled overtime at its productions facilities in Wixom and Wayne, Mich.; St. Louis and Kansas City, Mo.; Twin Cities, Minn.; Lorain, Ohio; and Louisville, Ky.
