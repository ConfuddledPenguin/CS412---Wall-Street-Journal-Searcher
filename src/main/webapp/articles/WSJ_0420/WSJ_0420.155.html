Business Brief -- Allied-Lyons PLC: Embassy Hotels to Be Sold By Firm to Jarvis Hotels

  • Author: null
  • Date: 04/20/90

Allied-Lyons PLC, a British beverage concern, said it will sell its Embassy Hotels chain to Jarvis Hotels Ltd. for #202 million ($331 million). The purchase, to be paid for with a mixture of cash and debt, is pending the completion of financing by Jarvis, Allied-Lyons said.

Jarvis is a newly formed hotel group headed by John Jarvis, former Hilton International Co. chairman and chief executive officer.

Allied-Lyons said proceeds from the sale, made in line with its plans to develop its core operations, will help reduce the group's borrowings.

Embassy operates 41 hotels with 3,150 bedrooms at major business centers of the U.K.
