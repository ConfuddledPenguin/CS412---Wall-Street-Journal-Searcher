Jacobs Engineering Group Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said it received a contract from Titanium Metals Corp., Henderson, Nev., to provide engineering services for a vacuum-distilled titanium sponge manufacturing facility. Terms weren't discosed. The project is scheduled to be completed in December 1992. Advanced technology and financing for the 22-million-pound annual capacity plant will be provided by a group of Japanese companies including Toho Titanium Co. and its primary shareholders: Nippon Mining Co. and Mitsui & Co. Titanium Metals is a unit of Houston-based Baroid Corp.
