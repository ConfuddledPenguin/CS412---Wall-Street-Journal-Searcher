Business Brief -- Cambridge BioScience Corp.: Firm Recalls 140 5-Minute Test Kits for AIDS Virus

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

Cambridge BioScience Corp. said it is recalling 140 kits of its 5-minute AIDS test because they no longer meet manufacturer specifications. Worcester, Mass.-based Cambridge BioScience, which makes the HIV-1 latex screening test, said the problem is consigned only to kits belonging to Lot A6535 and is requesting customers to discontinue use of them. A company spokesman said that the company plans to ask these customers to return the kits so they can be re-examined.

The defective lot, he said, "represents a very small percentage of the total number of lots made available by the company," since it began selling them last year. He noted that the company's quality control department has tested numerous other lots before and after the bad batch and found the kits to be functioning properly.
