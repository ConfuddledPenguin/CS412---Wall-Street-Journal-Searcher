Sverdrup Gets Air Force Pact

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

WASHINGTON -- Sverdrup Corp. received a $24 million Air Force contract for electronic-warfare simulator studies and analysis.

Associated Aerospace Activity Inc. got a $12.2 million Air Force contract for thermal barriers for jet engines used in F-15 and F-16 fighter aircraft.

General Electric Co. was awarded Air Force contracts totaling $14.95 million for radar communications and support services.
