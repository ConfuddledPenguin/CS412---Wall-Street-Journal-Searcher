Financing Business: Failure Group

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

FAILURE GROUP, a Menlo Park, Calif., consulting group devoted to analysis and prevention of engineering failures, on Friday commenced its initial public offering of 2.8 million shares at $13 each. Prudential-Bache Capital Funding and Hambrecht & Quist Inc. are managing the underwriting. Proceeds are to be used to reduce debt and for general corporate purposes, the company said.
