Taiwan Trade With China

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

TAIPEI -- Taiwan's indirect trade with China via Hong Kong gained 4.6% to $1.83 billion during the first half of the year from $1.75 billion a year earlier, the Board of Foreign Trade reported. Taiwan's exports to China totaled $1.48 billion between January and June, against $1.45 billion a year earlier. Taiwan's imports from China climbed 18% to $349 million from $295.7 million a year earlier.
