Treasury Will Raise $1.2 Billion New Cash

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

WASHINGTON -- The Treasury plans to raise $1.2 billion in new cash Thursday by selling about $10.5 billion in 52-week bills and redeeming $9.29 billion in maturing bills. The bills, which will be available in minimum denominations of $10,000, will be dated Aug. 30 and will mature Aug. 29, 1991.

Tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EDT Thursday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
