International Brief -- Elders IXL Ltd: Carter Holt Harvey Buys Stake in Elders Resources

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

Elders IXL Ltd. of Australia said it completed the sale of its 52.7% stake in Elders Resources Ltd. of New Zealand to Carter Holt Harvey Ltd. of New Zealand. John Elliott, chairman and chief executive officer, and other Elders IXL officials resigned from the board of Elders Resources, the company said.

"The conclusion of this sale represents a further significant step in the company's progress toward its transformation into a single-purpose international brewer," Elders IXL said. The brewing, resources, agribusiness and financial-services concern is selling assets to focus on brewing interests in Australia, Canada and Britain.

Carter Holt, a forestry concern, agreed in June to acquire Elders Resources for a base price of 623 million Australian dollars (US$502 million). That price can be adjusted up or down, depending on prices received for a proposed sale of mining assets held by Elders Resources.

Elders IXL was to receive A$414 million (US$333.6 million) on settlement of the sale, with the balance payable as the mining assets are sold. The sale represents a loss of about A$500 million (US$402 million) to Elders IXL on book value.
