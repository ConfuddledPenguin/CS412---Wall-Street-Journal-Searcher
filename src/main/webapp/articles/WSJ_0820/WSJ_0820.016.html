French Consumer Prices Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

PARIS -- French consumer prices rose 0.3% in July from June and were up 3.0% from a year earlier, according to preliminary estimates from the National Statistics Institute. The monthly increase was at the low end of market expectations and follows a monthly rise of 0.2% in June. The latest result left annual price growth unchanged at 3%.
