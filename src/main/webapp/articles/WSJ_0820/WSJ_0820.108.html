Business Brief -- Airbus Industrie: Consortium Received Orders For 51 Aircraft During July

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

Airbus Industrie said it received firm orders for 51 aircraft in July, bringing total unit sales to date to more than 1,500. The company said the 51 aircraft were ordered by eight customers, including two new ones.

Airbus is a consortium among Aerospatiale of France; Messerschmitt-BoelkowBlohm G.m.b.H., a unit of Daimler-Benz AG of West Germany; British Aerospace PLC; and Construcciones Aeronauticas S.A. of Spain.

As reported earlier, Airbus sales in the first half of 1990 dropped to 118 from 252 in the year-earlier half, caused in part by a four-month strike at British Aerospace, which makes wings for the planes.
