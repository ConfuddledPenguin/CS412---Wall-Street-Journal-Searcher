First Financial's Purchase

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

STEVENS POINT, Wis. -- First Financial Corp. said a unit acquired the deposits and part of the assets of a Peoria, Ill., thrift from the federal Resolution Trust Corp. for about $2.6 million. First Financial, a bank holding company, said Security Federal Savings and Loan Association has deposits of about $180 million.
