Ponce Federal Bank

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

PONCE, Puerto Rico -- Ponce Federal Bank said it has engaged Alex. Brown & Sons Inc. as its financial adviser to explore various options to maximize shareholder value, including the possible sale of the company. The troubled thrift also said it has recently been contacted by another institution, which it did not name, concerning the possible acquisition of Ponce Federal.
