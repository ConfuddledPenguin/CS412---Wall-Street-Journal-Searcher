NHI Nelson Holdings

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

TORONTO -- NHI Nelson Holdings International Ltd. said it expects to report a gain of about 2.5 million Canadian dollars (U.S.$2.2 million) in the current quarter from the repurchase of its 6% convertible subordinated debentures. The company said it paid about C$2.5 million to repurchase C$5 million of the debentures, due Oct. 31. A spokesman said that the company has about C$8 million of the debentures still outstanding and that it would be prepared to consider further discounted repurchases.

NHI is the holding company for Nelson Entertainment Group. NHI had net income last year of $4.5 million on revenue of $108.6 million.
