Who's News: Koala Technologies Taps Stephen Russo To Be Chief Executive

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

FREMONT, Calif. -- Stephen J. Russo, 34 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of Koala Technologies Corp., succeeding Douglas W. Kenny, who is 45. Mr. Kenny, according to a company press release, attributed his resignation to a dispute with the board over the future direction of the company. Mr. Kenny couldn't be reached for comment.

Mr. Russo, formerly chief financial officer of the plastics and electronics company, said the dispute focused on future acquisitions strategy. He said the board favored a focus on Koala's core plastics business, while its former president favored acquisitions in the field of electronics, Mr. Russo said.

The company posted third-quarter net income of $179,200, or one cent a share, compared with a year-earlier loss of $137,800, or one cent a share. Sales rose to $2.6 million from $2.5 million.
