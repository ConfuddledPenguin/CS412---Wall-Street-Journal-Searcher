Business Brief -- General Electric Co.: Aerospace Unit to Cut Jobs And Consolidate Department

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

General Electric Co.'s aerospace division, in its continuing effort to adjust to defense spending cuts, said it will consolidate its Re-Entry Systems Department into one facility. About 100 jobs will be eliminated. The department, which provides systems support for space programs, will be moved from the facility in Philadelphia to facilities in Valley Forge, Pa. GE Aerospace said that about 800 employees will relocate. In April, it announced the elimination of 4,200 jobs as part of a major consolidation.
