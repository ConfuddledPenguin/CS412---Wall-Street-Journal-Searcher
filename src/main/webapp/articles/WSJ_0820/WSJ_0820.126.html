May Department Stores Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

ST. LOUIS, Mo. -- May Department Stores Co. said its board authorized the repurchase of up to $250 million of May common stock. In composite New York Stock Exchange trading Friday, May shares closed at $46.625, down 25 cents. At that price, full exercise of the repurchase authorization would represent the purchase of about 5.36 million common shares, or about 4% of May's common and common equivalent shares outstanding.
