Newport to Repurchase Shares

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

FOUNTAIN VALLEY, Calif. -- Newport Corp. said it authorized the repurchase of one million of its common shares, or about 14% of the total outstanding, from time to time in the open market. The repurchase program is in addition to a one-million-share buy-back program authorized in 1987. So far, the maker of laser instruments and parts and accessories for the optics market has repurchased 774,000 shares under the earlier buy-back program.
