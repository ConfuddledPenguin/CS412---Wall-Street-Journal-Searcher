Ford Plant Is Idled

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

DEARBORN, Mich. -- Ford Motor Co. said it will extend the shutdown of its Dearborn, Mich., assembly plant through this week. The auto maker said the move was "necessitated by a parts shortage." Ford idled the plant last week because of weak demand for the Ford Mustang cars built there.

The extra week of no production will result in 3,700 fewer Mustangs for the company and affect about 2,100 hourly workers.
