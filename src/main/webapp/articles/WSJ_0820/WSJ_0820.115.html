International Brief -- Pioneer Electronic Corp.: Pretax Profit Rose 32% In Fiscal First Quarter

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

Pioneer Electronic Corp. said strong sales lifted its consolidated pretax profit 32% in the first quarter ended June 30. Pretax profit rose to 19.884 billion yen from 15.089 billion yen a year earlier.

Net income rose 67% to 9.922 billion ($673 million) yen from 5.955 billion yen ($404 million). Per-share net rose to 55.25 yen from 35.79 yen.

Sales rose 31% to 141.672 billion yen ($9.6 billion) from 107.859 billion yen ($7.3 billion). Sales of laser and compact disk players and their related software were particularly strong, according to Pioneer.

The value of sales abroad was also increased by the weakness of the yen in the first quarter, compared with a year earlier, the company said. Pioneer marked 82.044 billion yen of sales overseas, 58% of the total.

Pioneer didn't release a forecast for full-year results, but said it expects sales to rise further, aided by introduction of new products.
