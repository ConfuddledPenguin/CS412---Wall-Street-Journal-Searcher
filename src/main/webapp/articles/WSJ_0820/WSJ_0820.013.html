CMS Energy Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

DEARBORN, Mich. -- CMS Energy Corp. said the Michigan Supreme Court has delayed the company from using its interests in the Midland Cogeneration Venture. The utility holding company said interests include $1.2 billion of the cogeneration venture's notes and about $315 million of its equity interests.

The Michigan Court of Appeals on Wednesday reaffirmed its earlier denial to restrict use of these interests. In today's action, the Michigan Supreme Court said it will consider the need to review the actions of the Court of Appeals.
