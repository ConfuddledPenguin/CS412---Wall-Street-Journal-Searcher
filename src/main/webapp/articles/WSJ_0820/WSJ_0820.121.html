Manufacturers National

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

DETROIT -- Manufacturers National Corp.'s board approved a plan to repurchase as many as 200,000 shares of the banking company's common stock. The company said it will buy the stock in open market transactions for use in funding employee benefit plans or other corporate purposes. The plan is in addition to buy-backs announced in May 1989 and February 1990.

In over-the-counter trading Friday, Manufacturers National closed at $39.75, up 50 cents.
