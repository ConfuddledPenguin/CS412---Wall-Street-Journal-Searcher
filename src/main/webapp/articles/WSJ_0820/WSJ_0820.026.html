Interactive Media Technologies

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/20/90

SCOTTSDALE, Ariz. -- Interactive Media Technologies Inc. said it switched the investment firm on its $4 million private placement of 12% convertible subordinated debentures. The communications concern said it replaced the investment firm R.G. Dickinson, a unit of Stotler Group Inc., a futures concern which was forced into bankruptcy earlier this month. Interactive Media Technologies said it signed a letter of intent for two other investment banking firms, A.T. Brod Investment Banking and Wainwright Austin Stone & Co. to manage the offering. Proceeds will be used to increase manufacturing.
