Business Brief -- BellSouth Corp.: Cellular Telephone System Is Planned for New Zealand

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

BellSouth Corp., Atlanta, said it plans to develop a digital cellular mobile telephone system in New Zealand by mid-1992. The system will use the group speciale mobile, or GSM, standard being introduced throughout Europe, the company said in Wellington. BellSouth has management rights to a radio frequency band in New Zealand.

Terry Winters, managing director of BellSouth New Zealand Ltd., said a GSM network "will allow us to offer our customers cellular telephone services in the short term and various types of personal communications services by the turn of the century."
