Business Brief -- Foster Grant Corp.: Maker of Eyeglasses Files For Chapter 11 Protection

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

Foster Grant Corp. said it filed for protection from creditors under Chapter 11 of the federal bankruptcy code in Tucson, Ariz. The closely held maker of reading glasses and sunglasses said it had been hurt by competition from Asia, which has flooded the U.S. market with cheaper models. Based in Leominster, Mass., Foster Grant is the second-largest U.S. manufacturer of sunglasses behind Bausch & Lomb. Last year Foster Grant sold about $50 million of glasses, 75% of which were sunglasses.

As a result of the filing, the company said it would trim its staff, but it declined to say how large the layoff would be.
