International Brief -- Ciba-Geigy AG: Swiss Firm's Profit Fell 12% In First Half; Sales Off 2%

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/20/90

Ciba-Geigy AG, a major Swiss chemicals and pharmaceuticals group, said its group profit after taxes fell 12% in the first half to 1.22 billion francs ($946 million) from 1.38 billion francs in the like period in 1989. Cash flow fell 9% to 1.75 billion francs from 1.92 billion.

The company also restated its first-half sales to 10.91 billion francs, down 2% from 11.15 billion a year ago. Last month, the company had reported first-half 1990 sales of 10.89 billion francs.

The company blamed currency influences for the decline in results.

For the year as a whole, the company expects profit and sales to come in somewhat lower than last year's levels. The company posted 1989 group sales of 20.6 billion francs and group net profit of 1.56 billion francs.
