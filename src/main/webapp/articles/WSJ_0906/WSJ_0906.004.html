Zapata Sets Offshore Rig Sale

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

HOUSTON -- Zapata Corp. said it agreed to sell its offshore drilling rig fleet and related assets to Arethusa (Offshore) Ltd., a newly formed Bermuda company, for $298 million. Zapata previously had said it would sell the 12-rig fleet as part of a restructuring plan.

Zapata will hold an 8.75% stake in the new company and will continue to manage the rigs under a management contract. The sale is expected to close by Oct. 31.
