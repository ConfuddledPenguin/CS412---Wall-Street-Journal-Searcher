Who's News: One Bancorp

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

ONE BANCORP (Portland, Maine) -- Domenic Restuccia, 40 years old, was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this holding company, effective immediately. He succeeds Francis E. McFarland, 42, who will leave the company at the end of the month. The company said Mr. McFarland "has not announced his future plans at this time," and declined to comment further. Mr. McFarland couldn't immediately be reached for comment. Mr. Restuccia was most recently vice president and controller for the concern and senior vice president and chief financial officer of Maine Savings Bank, a unit.
