First National Bank of Cameron

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

MARTINSBURG, W.Va. -- Mountaineer Bankshares of W.Va. Inc. said it will acquire First National Bank of Cameron. The agreement is subject to regulatory approval. The parties plan to complete the transaction during the first quarter of 1991. First National Bank of Cameron has total assets of $17 million, and the bank holding company has total assets of $528 million.
