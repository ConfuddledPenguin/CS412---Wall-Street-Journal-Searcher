National Intergroup Extension

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

PITTSBURGH -- National Intergroup Inc., a drug wholesaler, said it again extended, to next Tuesday from last Tuesday, its tender offer of $3.85 for each cumulative convertible preference unit outstanding representing limited partnership interests in Permian Partners L.P. National Intergroup said 8,019,639 preference units had been tendered as of last Tuesday. The company has said the tender offer requires 7,875,001 units as a majority of the publicly held units.
