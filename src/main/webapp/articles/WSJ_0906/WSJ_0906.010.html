Wolohan Lumber's Sales Rise

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

SAGINAW, Mich. -- Wolohan Lumber Co., a lumber and building materials retailer, said its September sales rose 10% to $30 million from $27.2 million a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year decreased 3%. Sales for the first eight months of this year rose 11% to $201 million from $181.9 million a year earlier. Comparable-store sales increased 4.5%.
