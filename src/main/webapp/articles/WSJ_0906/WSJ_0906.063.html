Who's News: Hubco Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

HUBCO Inc. (Union City, N.J.) -- The holding company for Hudson United Bank named James E. Schierloh chairman, succeeding James C. McClave, who retired at age 65. Mr. Schierloh, 60, formerly was vice chairman. Mr. McClave, who has served as chairman for 14 years, will remain a director. The vice chairman's post will be vacant.
