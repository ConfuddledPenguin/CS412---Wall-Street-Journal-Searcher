General Electric Gets Air Force Contract Totaling $31.8 Million

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

WASHINGTON -- General Electric Co. was given a $31.8 million Air Force contract for F-16 fighter engines.

Sparta Inc. received a $14.8 million Air Force contract for software systems.

General Dynamics Corp. got a $12.6 million contract for Air Force training and Army tanks.

TRW Inc. was given a $12.8 million Navy contract for laser operations.

Johns Hopkins University was awarded an $11.4 million Navy contract for research and development.

Fansteel Inc. won an $11.1 million Air Force pact for jet-fighter engine components.

Loral Defense Systems, a unit of Loral Corp., received a $10 million Navy contract for development of an advanced anti-submarine target.
