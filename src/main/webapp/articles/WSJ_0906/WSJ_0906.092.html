Business Brief -- United Telecommunications Inc.: US Sprint Subsidiary Gets $127 Million in Contracts

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

US Sprint Communications Co., a unit of United Telecommunications Inc., said it reached contracts with several companies for telecommunications services with a total value of $127 million. National Carriers' Buyers Network, a San Jose, Calif.-based group of small-to-medium size regional carriers, agreed to a two-year, $78 million contract for a package of telecommunications services that supplement its personal networks and provide expanded service to its customers. The regional carriers buy telecommunications services and then repackage them for smaller customers.

San Diego-based American Healthcare Systems signed an agreement with Sprint estimated at more than $36 million over three years. Sprint said it will design and implement a communications network that links American Healthcare's 1,100 facilities. Sprint will also provide public pay telephone, patient calling and employee benefit-related services for the health-care alliance.

ITT Corp. signed a three-year agreement with Sprint valued at $10.8 million to provide a variety of telecommunications services. Based in New York, ITT is a diversified multinational corporation.

Fireman's Fund Mortgage Corp. awarded U.S. Sprint a two-year, $2.2 million contract for a multiple-location WATS system on a national basis. Based in Farmington Hills, Mich., Fireman's Fund Mortgage is a subsidiary of Fireman's Fund Insurance Co. of Novato, Calif.
