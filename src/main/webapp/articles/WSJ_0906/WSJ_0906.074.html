Sanford Approves Buy-Back

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

BELLWOOD, Ill. -- Sanford Corp. said its board approved the buy-back of as many as 1.5 million of its 14.8 million common shares. The maker of pens and other office and school supplies said the stock purchases would be made in the open market or in privately negotiated transactions over the next 18 months.

Henry Pearsall, chairman and chief executive officer, said the decision was made because "it looks like the best place to put our money." In national over-the-counter trading yesterday, Sanford's stock rose 50 cents a share to $18.50.
