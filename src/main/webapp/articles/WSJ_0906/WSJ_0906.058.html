Who's News: Phoenix Network Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

PHOENIX NETWORK Inc. (Sausalito, Calif.) -- Marc L. Goyette, 35 years old, chief operating officer and executive vice president, was promoted to the post of president. He will remain chief operating officer. Mr. Goyette succeeds Thomas H. Bell, the company's founder, who will continue as chief executive officer and chairman. Phoenix Network is a long distance telecommunications network manager.
