Bicoastal Corp. Sells Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

TAMPA, Fla. -- Bicoastal Corp., formerly Singer Co., said it completed the sale of its Link-Miles Ltd. subsidiary to Thomson (U.K.) Holding Ltd., a subsidiary of the French state-controlled defense and electronics group Thomson-CSF S.A. The transaction was announced in May.

Under terms, Thomson (U.K.) Holding paid some $70 million of the $100 million purchase price at closing, with $20 million deposited in escrow and $10 million retained by Thomson for contingencies.

Based in Lancing, England, Link-Miles develops and produces simulators for world-wide military and commercial applications.

Bicoastal is operating with protection against creditor lawsuits under Chapter 11 of federal bankruptcy law.
