Walgreen's August Sales Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

DEERFIELD, Ill. -- Walgreen Co., a drugstore operator, said its August sales rose 11% to $506.8 million from $456.8 million a year earlier. Sales in stores open at least a year rose 7.6%. For this year through August, sales rose 12% to $3.98 billion from $3.54 billion a year earlier. As of Aug. 31, Walgreen operated 1,564 drugstores, up from 1,484 a year earlier.
