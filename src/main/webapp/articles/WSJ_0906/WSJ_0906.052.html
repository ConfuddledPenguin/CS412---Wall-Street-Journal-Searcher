Timken to Build Plant in Brazil

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

CANTON, Ohio -- Timken Co., which produces bearings and specialty steel, said it will spend about $5 million to build a machining facility in Santa Barbara d'Oeste, Brazil, about 95 miles north of Sao Paulo, as part of the company's previously announced $1 billion capital investment program. The plant will turn forgings into bearing cups and cones.
