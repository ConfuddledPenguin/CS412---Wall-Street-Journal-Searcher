GE Unit Gets Contract in Korea

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

SCHENECTADY, N.Y. -- GE Power Generation, a unit of General Electric Co. of Fairfield, Conn., said it and Seoul-based Korea Heavy Industries & Construction Co. agreed to supply two coal-fired, 550-megawatt steam turbine generators to Korea Electric Power Corp. of Changwon, in a contract valued at more than $30 million to the GE unit. Last week, GE Power agreed to provide gas and steam turbine generators to Korea Electric in an order valued at more than $200 million to GE, which also has aerospace, broadcasting and financial-services businesses.
