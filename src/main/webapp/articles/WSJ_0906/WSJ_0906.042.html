Horn & Hardart Sells Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

NEW YORK -- Horn & Hardart Co. closed the sale of its Bojangles chicken-restaurant chain to a group including Sienna Holdings, a closely held Los Angeles investment firm. In return for Bojangles, Horn & Hardart received $20 million of its 14% senior subordinated debentures that the group held. The group also assumed $4 million of liabilities and capital leases.
