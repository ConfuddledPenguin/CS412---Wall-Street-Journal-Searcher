Business Brief -- Ogden Corp.: Allied Division to Acquire ARA's Ground Services Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

An Ogden Corp. unit agreed to acquire the Ground Services Inc. subsidiary of ARA Group Inc. Terms weren't disclosed.

Ogden's Allied Aviation Services division, which has been expanding, provides more than $400 million annually of services -- baggage, ticketing, food service, fueling and others -- to a number of airports and airlines. Ground Services, with annual revenue of $35 million, cleans aircraft, warehouses cargo and handles baggage.

Closely held ARA, based in Philadelphia, has businesses in food service, uniform rental, magazine and book distribution, child care and nursing homes. The company also manages hospital emergency rooms.

New York-based Ogden provides services to entertainment, aviation, government and building markets. Ogden also provides hazardous-waste and municipal solid-waste incineration services.
