International: Taiwan's Consumer Price Index

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

TAIPEI, Taiwan -- Taiwan's consumer price index in August climbed 5.63% from a year earlier, marking the biggest year-to-year growth since last October, when the index soared 5.94%, the government said. The August index, in which 1986 prices equal 100, stood at 112.4, compared with 106.41 a year earlier, and was up 1.65% from 110.58 in July. The latter was the biggest monthly gain since September 1989, the agency said.
