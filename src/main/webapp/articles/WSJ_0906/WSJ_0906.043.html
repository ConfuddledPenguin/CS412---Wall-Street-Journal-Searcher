International: West Germany's Jobless Rate

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

FRANKFURT, West Germany -- West Germany's unemployment rate fell to 6.9% in August from 7.1% in July, the Federal Labor Office in Nuremberg said. The number of unemployed West Germans declined to 1,812,812 in August from 1,863,670 a month earlier, a decline of 50,858. The figures are better than expectations of analysts and economists, who had predicted the jobless rate would remain flat at 7.1%.

Separately, East Germany reported that its unemployment level rose to 4.1% of the working population in August from a 3.1% level in July.
