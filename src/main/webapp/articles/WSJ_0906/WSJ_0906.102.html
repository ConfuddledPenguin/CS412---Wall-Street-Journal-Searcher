HEALTHCO INTERNATIONAL Inc., a dental equipment company, had net income of $5.5 million, or 70 cents a share, on revenue of $223.1 million, for the six months ended June 30. For the year-earlier first half, the company had net income of $5.3 million, or 66 cents a share, on revenue of $211.7 million. The figures were reported incorrectly in yesterday's edition. (See: "Healthco Agrees to Be Acquired By Hicks Muse" -- WSJ Sept. 5, 1990)

---

PAN AMERICAN WORLD AIRWAYS expects to receive $150 million from Lufthansa German Airlines for certain services and facilities, including the transfer of gate space. Tuesday's edition incorrectly described what Pan American was selling.

(See: "Business Brief -- Pan Am Corp.: Carrier Will Cut Flights, Lease Planes to Lufthansa" -- WSJ Sept. 4, 1990)

---

CENTRAL MAINE POWER Co. filed a shelf offering with the Securities and Exchange Commission to issue as many as 2.5 million common shares. Yesterday's edition incorrectly described the offering.

(See: "Recent SEC Filings" -- WSJ Sept. 5, 1990)
