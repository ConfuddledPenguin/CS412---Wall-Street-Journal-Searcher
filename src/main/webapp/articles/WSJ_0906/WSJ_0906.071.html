Financing Business: Royal Bank of Canada

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

ROYAL BANK OF CANADA, Montreal, said it will issue 200 million Canadian dollars (US$172.5 million) of 11.75% fixed-rate debentures, due Jan. 31, 2001. Proceeds of the issue will be used for general banking purposes and will enlarge the bank's capital base. RBC Dominion Securities Inc., a subsidiary of the bank, is lead underwriter.
