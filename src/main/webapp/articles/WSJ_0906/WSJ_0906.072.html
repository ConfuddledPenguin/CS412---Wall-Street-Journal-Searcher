Business Brief -- National Bank of Canada: Holders of Record Sept. 17 Will Receive Rights Issue

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

National Bank of Canada said it will issue one right for each share held to shareholders of record Sept. 17. Each 10 rights entitle the holder to purchase one common share for 8 Canadian dollars (US$6.90) until Oct. 9. National Bank, based in Montreal, said it has about 119.5 million shares outstanding. If all rights are exercised proceeds will be about C$95.6 million.

In trading on the Montreal Exchange yesterday National Bank closed at C$8.625, up 12.5 Canadian cents. National Bank said Levesque Beaubien Geoffrion Inc. and RBC Dominion Securities Inc. will act as advisers for the rights issue.
