Who's News: Norcen Energy Resources Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

NORCEN ENERGY RESOURCES Ltd. (Calgary, Alberta) -- Barry D. Cochrane was appointed president and chief executive officer of this oil and gas concern, effective Jan. 1, succeeding Edward G. Battle, who will assume the post of chairman. Mr. Cochrane, 55 years old, has served as Norcen's executive vice president and chief operating officer since last year. Mr. Battle, 59, will succeed Paul M. Marshall as chairman; Mr. Marshall will become deputy chairman.
