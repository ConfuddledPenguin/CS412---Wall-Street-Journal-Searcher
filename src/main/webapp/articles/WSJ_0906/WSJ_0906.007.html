General Dynamics Launch Unit

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

SAN DIEGO -- General Dynamics Commercial Launch Services Inc., a unit of St Louis-based General Dynamics Corp. said it signed a contract with a unit of General Electric Co. to launch a Japanese communications satellite. Terms weren't disclosed, but General Dynamics usually charges between $50 million and $60 million for its Atlas I launch service. The satellite will be built by GE's Astro-Space division in East Windsor, N.J., and launched in April from Cape Canaveral, Fla.
