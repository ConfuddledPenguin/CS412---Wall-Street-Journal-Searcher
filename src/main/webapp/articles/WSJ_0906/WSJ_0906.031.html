Who's News: Great American Management & Investment Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

GREAT AMERICAN MANAGEMENT & INVESTMENT Inc. (Chicago) -- F. Warren Hellman and David J. Johnson were elected directors of this diversified holding company, increasing board membership to 10. They are general partners of Hellman & Friedman, an investment banking partnership that recently acquired an 18% stake in Great American.
