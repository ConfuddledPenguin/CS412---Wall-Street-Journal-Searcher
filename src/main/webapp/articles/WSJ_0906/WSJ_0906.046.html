Marketing & Media: MCA Completes Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

UNIVERSAL CITY, Calif. -- MCA Inc. said it completed the acquisition of Universal City Cinemas, an 18-screen movie complex, from Cineplex Odeon Corp. of Canada for $45 million. MCA, which is a major shareholder in Cineplex, said a unit of the Toronto-based entertainment concern will continue to manage the theater.
