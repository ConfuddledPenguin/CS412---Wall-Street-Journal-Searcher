Business Brief -- Xerox Corp.: Small Copiers Introduced; Guarantee Program Begun

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

Xerox Corp. said it introduced two small convenience copiers, increasing to nine the number of small copiers it sells. The Stamford, Conn., company said the 5034 model is the first 50 Series small copier equipped with an automatic document handler that can invert originals to make copies on both sides of a piece of paper. The copier, with a list price of $9,100, will be sold through Xerox's own sales force.

The other new copier, the 5011 R/E, can process 12 copies a minute, accept originals as big as 11 inches by 17 inches, and reduce or enlarge in ratios of 65%, 75% and 130% of the original, Xerox said. It will retail for $2,895.

Xerox also said it is initiating a "total satisfaction guarantee" program for its products. Under the program, customers who maintain their Xerox equipment under Xerox authorized service or warranty can get a replacement if they are dissatisfied with the equipment. The program covers all Xerox products for three years.
