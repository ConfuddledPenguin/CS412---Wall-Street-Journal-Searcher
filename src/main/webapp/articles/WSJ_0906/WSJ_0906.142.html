International Brief -- TNT Ltd.: Operating Profit for Year Fell 37% to $114.9 Million

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

TNT Ltd. of Australia, a transportation company, reported that operating profit after taxes for the year ended June 30 fell 37% to 140.7 million Australian dollars (US$114.9 million) from A$222.6 million a year earlier. Extraordinary losses narrowed to A$864,000 from A$45.3 million. After accounting for extraordinary losses related to the closure of certain businesses, overall profit fell 27% to A$127.4 million from A$173.8 million. Per-share earnings fell 19% to 24.9 Australian cents from 30.6 cents, but the dividend was maintained at 15 cents a share.

Revenue rose 15% to A$4.48 billion from A$3.91 billion.
