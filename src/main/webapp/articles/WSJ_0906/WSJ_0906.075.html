Santa Fe Energy Resources

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

HOUSTON -- Santa Fe Energy Resources Inc. said it entered into crude oil and natural gas exploration and development agreements with the governments of Argentina and Bolivia, for an area totaling about 5.9 million acres. The independent exploration and production concern said the agreements represent its first operations in Bolivia and its fourth production sharing venture with the Argentine government. Santa Fe Energy said it expects to begin seismic activity on both projects in 1991.
