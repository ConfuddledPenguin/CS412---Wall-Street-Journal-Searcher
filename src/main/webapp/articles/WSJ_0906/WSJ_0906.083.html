Business Brief -- Lukens Inc.: `Record Results' for 1990 Expected, Chairman Says

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

Lukens Inc. said it should have "record results" for fiscal 1990, compared with last year's net income of $41.5 million, or $4.73 a share. Speaking to security analysts, W.R. Wilson, Lukens chairman and chief executive officer, said the company intends to find more export markets for its specialty plate steels.

Mr. Wilson suggested that acquisitions are part of the growth plan. The "ideal candidate" for acquisition is a company that would generate $225 million in sales and operating margins of 11% or more, he said.

Lukens officials said that representatives of the United Steelworkers of America union have agreed to meet next week to discuss a new contract. The union's contract doesn't expire for another year, but the Coatesville, Pa.-based company has asked the workers to open talks early.
