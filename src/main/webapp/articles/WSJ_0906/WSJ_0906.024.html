Gottschalks' August Sales Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

FRESNO, Calif. -- Gottschalks Inc., a regional department store chain, said sales for the four weeks ended Aug. 26 totaled $20.2 million, up 26% from a year earlier. Sales at stores open at least one year increased 11%. For the 30 weeks through Aug. 26, sales rose 25% to $134.3 million.
