Who's News: Data Switch Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

DATA SWITCH Corp. (Shelton, Conn.) -- Michael Stashower, a director, was named chief financial officer, succeeding Rudolph Morin. Mr. Stashower was most recently executive vice president of Softstrip Inc. Mr. Morin will remain executive vice president and a director of Data Switch, a data processing and communications systems concern, and will become general manager of the T-Bar division and chief development officer of the parent, a spokeswoman said. He succeeds Gerrit Rohner as general manager of the T-Bar division, a maker of small communications switching systems and parts. Mr. Rohner died in July.
