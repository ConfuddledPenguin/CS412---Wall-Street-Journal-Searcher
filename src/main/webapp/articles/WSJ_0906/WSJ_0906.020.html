Who's News: Johnson & Higgins

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

JOHNSON & HIGGINS (New York) -- John Howe and Stephen Ambidge have been named co-heads of a newly formed professional and financial risk division of Johnson & Higgins Ltd., the London arm of this insurance brokerage firm. Messrs. Howe and Ambidge were both affiliated with HJL Insurance Brokers, a division of Alexander Howden, a London-based insurance brokerage firm.
