REVIEW & OUTLOOK (Editorial) -- Asides: Saddam Noriega?

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

Yesterday Iraqi President Saddam Hussein called for an Islamic holy war against U.S. forces in the Persian Gulf, proclaiming: "Victory is very near." It occurs to us that the last person who decided to issue a declaration of war against the United States -- holy or otherwise -- was General Manuel Noriega. Shortly thereafter, U.S. troops were landing in Panama and Noriega was on the run.
