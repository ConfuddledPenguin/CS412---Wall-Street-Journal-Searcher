Inco Unit's Smelter Explosion

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

TORONTO -- Inco Ltd., a nickel producer, said that the death toll from an explosion last week at its Indonesian subsidiary's nickel smelter rose to six from a previously reported two, and that the company was still seeking to evaluate damage and the effect on production. The explosion took place in one of the smelter's three furnaces, after the first week of a five-week shutdown for maintenance and expansion. The smelter is operated by Inco's 58%-owned subsidiary, PT International Nickel Indonesia.
