Business Brief -- Barclays PLC: Pact Set to Acquire 60% Of Canadian Fund Manager

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

Barclays Bank of Canada, Toronto, said its parent, Barclays PLC of Britain, agreed in principle to buy 60% of E.J. McConnell & Associates Ltd., a Canadian independent fund manager. Terms weren't disclosed. The purchase will be made through the BZW Investment Management Ltd. unit of Barclays, one of Britain's leading fund managers, with more than 38 billion Canadian dollars (US$32.78 billion) in institutional investments under its management world-wide. E.J. McConnell has more than C$3 billion under management, Barclays said, adding that Ted McConnell, the founder of E.J. McConnell, will remain president of the firm.

BZW and E.J. McConnell have worked together since launching a joint fund, Barclays McConnell International Fund, in early 1989. The fund has attracted more than C$100 million, Barclays said.

The transaction is subject to completion of a definitive agreement and regulatory approvals, Barclays said.
