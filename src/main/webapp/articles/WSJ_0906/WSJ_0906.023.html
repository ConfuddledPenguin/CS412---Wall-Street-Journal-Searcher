Casey's General Stores Buy-Back

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

ANKENY, Iowa -- Casey's General Stores Inc., a convenience store chain, said its board approved the purchase of as many as one million, or 8.8%, of its approximately 11.4 million common shares outstanding, in open-market or privately negotiated transactions from time to time, depending on market conditions. Casey's said it will finance the buy-backs with cash from its working capital.
