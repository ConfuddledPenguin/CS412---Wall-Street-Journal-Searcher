Alaska Air's August Traffic Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

SEATTLE -- Alaska Air Group Inc. said its Alaska Airlines unit's August traffic rose 10% to 515.1 million revenue passenger miles from 468 million miles a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. The airline's August load factor, or the percentage of available seats occupied, decreased to 63.6% from 66.3%.

For this year through August, Alaska Airlines' traffic increased 13% to 3.05 billion revenue passenger miles from 2.69 billion miles a year earlier. The load factor fell to 54% from 55.7%.
