Communications Transmission

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/06/90

AUSTIN, Texas -- Communications Transmission Inc. said it received a $15.2 million payment from Metromedia/ITT, a unit of Metromedia Co. of Secaucus, N.J., for early termination of an analog lease agreement. CTI said it had been receiving monthly revenue from the agreement of about $871,000. Under its original terms, the agreement would have lasted as long as 1994.

The operator of microwave and fiberoptic communications networks said this was its last analog contract. It remains a provider of digital transmission services, including to Metromedia.

CTI said the payment will be used for working capital and to repay long-term debt.
