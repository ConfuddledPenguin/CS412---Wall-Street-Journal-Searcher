Midway Air's Traffic Rises 30%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/06/90

CHICAGO -- Midway Airlines said its August traffic rose 30% to 471 million revenue passenger miles from 361.9 million a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.

The carrier said its August load factor, or percentage of seats filled, slipped to 63% from 66%.

Year-to-date revenue passenger miles increased to about 3.4 billion from about 2.4 billion a year earlier. The load factor increased to 60% from 58%.
