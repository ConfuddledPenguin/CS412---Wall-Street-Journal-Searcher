Nodding Doing

About one-thirty, maybe two,

A nap is what I wish to do;

I feel like I've been hit, post-luncheon,

By someone with an ax or truncheon.

The world can go downhill on skids

As long as I can close my lids.

Revile me as a lout and loafa --

But go away. I want my sofa]
