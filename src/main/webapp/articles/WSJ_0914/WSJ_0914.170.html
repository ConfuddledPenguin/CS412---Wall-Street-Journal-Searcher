Business Brief -- Go-Video Inc.: Court Clears Way for Trial On Suit Against Japanese

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

Go-Video Inc., Scottsdale, Ariz., said a federal court decision cleared the way for a trial to begin on its anti-trust suit against five Japanese makers of videocassette recorders. Go-Video, which is marketing a dual-deck VCR, said a U.S. district court judge in Phoenix, Ariz., denied a motion by defendants to delay the trial and refused to reconsider his denial of a previous motion to dismiss the case.

The defendants, which Go-Video accuses of conspiring to block its efforts to market the VCR, are Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Victor Co. of Japan Ltd., Sony Corp., Sanyo Electric Co. and NEC Corp.

The new VCR, which can record two programs at once, plays one while recording another and duplicate tapes, hit the market last June.
