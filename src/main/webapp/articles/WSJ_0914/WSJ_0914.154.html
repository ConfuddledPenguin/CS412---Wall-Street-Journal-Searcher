Business Brief -- Keithley Instruments Inc.: Company to Market a Line Made by Japan's NF Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

Keithley Instruments Inc. said it agreed to market in North America a line of measurement instruments produced by NF Corp. of Japan that will complement its own devices. Keithley said the move not only represents a major addition of product technology and potential sales volume, but also is expected to increase its own sales of instruments as well.

Keithley, based in Cleveland, produces measurement instruments that are used in direct current, or DC, applications. NF's line of instruments measure in alternating current, or AC.

The agreement is effective immediately, subject to additional negotiations to be completed within 60 days, Keithley said.
