Divi Hotels Talks End

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

ARUBA, Dutch Caribbean -- Divi Hotels N.V. said talks ended with Accor S.A., a French hotel company, for the sale of all of Divi's assets and properties related to its Caribbean resort hotel business. Divi said Accor withdrew, citing uncertainty about what impact the Middle East crisis may have on the tourist trade from North America. The talks were over 11 hotel properties.
