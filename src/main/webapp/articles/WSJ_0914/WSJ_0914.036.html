Who's News: Transtechnology Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

TRANSTECHNOLOGY Corp. (Los Angeles) -- Michael A.F. Roberts, a managing director of Smith Barney, Harris Upham & Co., a unit of Primerica Corp., was elected a director of this maker of aerospace, advanced technology and industrial products, filling one of two vacancies created by the retirements of Frank Bryant and Jeff Asher. The company doesn't plan now to fill the remaining vacancy.
