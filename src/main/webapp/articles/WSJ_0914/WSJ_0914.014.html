Brown-Forman Share Purchase

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- Brown-Forman Corp. said its board authorized the repurchase of 333,333 Class B common shares for $20 million from an estate that it didn't identify. The price paid was $60 a share, the company said, which was the closing price of Brown-Forman Class B common in American Stock Exchange composite trading Wednesday.

As of April 30, the company had 17.4 million Class B common shares outstanding and 10.6 million Class A common shares outstanding, a spokesman said.

Brown-Forman makes alcoholic beverages such as Jack Daniels and Southern Comfort whiskeys, Lenox china and crystal, and Hartmann luggage.
