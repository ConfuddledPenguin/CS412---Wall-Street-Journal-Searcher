Money Fund Assets Rose $474.2 Million In the Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 522 money market funds rose $474.2 million to $407.01 billion in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 255 general-purpose funds rose $118 million to $143.8 billion, and assets of 105 broker-dealer funds rose $608.2 million to $165.57 billion. Assets of 162 institutional funds fell $252 million to $97.64 billion.

Mutual fund assets were revised for the Sept. 5 week to $406.54 billion. The decrease reported on that week was revised to $605.5 million.
