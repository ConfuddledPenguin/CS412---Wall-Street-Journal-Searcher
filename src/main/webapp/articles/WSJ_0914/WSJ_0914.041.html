Who's News: J.C. Penney Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

J.C. PENNEY Co. (Dallas) -- James L. Hailey has been elected president of the women's division of this department store retailer, succeeding John A. McConville, who is retiring. Additionally, Henry H. Scott was elected president of the children's division, succeeding Mr. Hailey.
