International Brief -- United Biscuits (Holdings) PLC: Pretax Profit Climbed 9.6% In First Half Ended July 14

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

United Biscuits (Holdings) PLC, a U.K. food manufacturer, said pretax profit for the first half ended July 14 rose 9.6%, with gains in the company's key operations offsetting a decline in earnings at the frozen and chilled food unit, Ross Youngs. United Biscuits' pretax profit of #83 million ($154.4 million), up from #75.7 million in the year-earlier period, was near the top end of market forecasts, which ranged between #81 million and #84 million.

The company said profit after tax, less minorities, or profit attributable to shareholders, firmed 11% to #53.9 million from #48.4 million. Per-share earnings rose 7.4% to 11.6 pence from 10.8 pence a year earlier.

United Biscuits didn't report any extraordinary items in either period.

Sales rose 7.8% to #1.38 billion from #1.28 billion.

Robert Clarke, chairman and chief executive officer, said Eric Nicoli, currently chief executive of European operations, will take over as group chief executive Jan. 1. Mr. Clarke will remain chairman.
