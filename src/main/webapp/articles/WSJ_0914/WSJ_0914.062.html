Canadian Steel Production Falls

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

OTTAWA -- Canadian steel production totaled 158,653 metric tons in the week ended last Saturday, down 3.9% from 165,060 tons in the preceding week and down 47% from 300,809 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a federal agency, said. A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.6 pounds. For the year through last Saturday, steel production fell 15% to 9.3 million metric tons from 10.9 million metric tons a year earlier.
