Rhone-Poulenc Rorer

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

PARIS -- Newly formed Rhone-Poulenc Rorer Inc. plans to cut 400 workers in France through a combination of early retirement and payroll cutbacks, a French union claimed. The company declined to comment, but said its governing board is scheduled to meet today in Paris and that a news conference will be called next week.

The Confederation Francaise Democratique du Travail (CFDT) said the company, created in July through the merger of Rhone-Poulenc's human health division and Rorer Group Inc. of the U.S., said the cuts would affect a variety of sites in France, including plants in Hauts-de-Seine and Seine-Maritime, as well as six factories of the Propharm S.A. unit. The CFDT said Rhone-Poulenc Rorer also intends to sell its factory in Monts, close to Tours.
