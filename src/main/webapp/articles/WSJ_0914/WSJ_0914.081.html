Who's News: Prudential Insurance Co. of America

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

PRUDENTIAL INSURANCE CO. OF AMERICA (Newark, N.J.) -- John R. Mueller was named senior vice president and head of human resources at Prudential-Bache Securities Inc., a New York-based subsidiary of this insurance company. Mr. Mueller, 43 years old, succeeds Paul Rumely, who joined Moody's Investors Service Inc., a New York-based credit rating agency. Mr. Mueller was previously senior vice president of human resources for the financial services division of McGraw-Hill Inc., the New York-based communications company.
