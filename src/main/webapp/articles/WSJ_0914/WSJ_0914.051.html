Recent SEC Filings

WASHINGTON -- The following issues were recently filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission:

Bando McGlocklin Capital Corp., offering of 1.2 million common shares, via Dain Bosworth Inc. and Robert W. Baird & Co. Inc.

Catalina Lighting Inc., offering of 1,010,477 common shares by selling holders.

Coastal Corp., offering of $200 million of senior notes and $200 million of senior debentures, via Lehman Brothers and Merrill Lynch Capital Markets.

Connecticut Water Service Inc., offering of 400,000 common shares, via Edward D. Jones & Co. and the Ohio Co.

Oryx Energy Co., offering of 7,259,394 shares of common stock, of which 5,809,394 will be sold in the United States and Canada, and 1,450,000 shares will be sold internationally, via Morgan Stanley & Co. and Morgan Stanley International.
