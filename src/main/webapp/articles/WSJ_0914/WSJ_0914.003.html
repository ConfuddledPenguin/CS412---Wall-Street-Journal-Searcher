First Mutual Savings Bank

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

BELLEVUE, Wash. -- First Mutual Savings Bank said it would incur a roughly $1.2 million after-tax charge in the third quarter ending Sept. 30, reflecting a more conservative accounting treatment of certain deferred loan-servicing rights. The thrift reported net income of $375,645, or 30 cents a share, in the year-earlier third quarter. It added that, despite the charge, it would remain in compliance with federal regulatory capital standards.
