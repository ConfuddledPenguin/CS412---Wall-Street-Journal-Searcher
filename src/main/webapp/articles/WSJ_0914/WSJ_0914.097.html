Who's News: Pitney Bowes Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

PITNEY BOWES Inc. (Stamford, Conn.) -- Michael J. Critelli was named chief personnel officer in addition to his post as vice president, secretary and general counsel at this business equipment and financial services company. Mr. Critelli, 41 years old, succeeds Anne Pole, 43, who was named vice president of manufacturing operations.
