Technology: Kodak Unveils a Fast Printer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

ROCHESTER, N.Y. -- Eastman Kodak Co. unveiled a centralized digital printing system, which it said will improve productivity for personal computer users. The new "Lionheart" printer is capable of operating at speeds to process as many as 92 pages a minute. Kodak said documents now are printed at the rate of about eight to 10 pages a minute.

The system's software also enables personal computers and workstations to be networked to the printer either directly or through a print server, Kodak said.
