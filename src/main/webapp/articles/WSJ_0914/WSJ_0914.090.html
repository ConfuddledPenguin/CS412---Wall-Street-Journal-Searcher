Allegheny & Western Wells

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

NEW YORK -- Allegheny & Western Energy Corp. said its TEX-HEX Corp. unit completed two horizontal discoveries, the Lane No. 4 and the Dickson No. 5, in the Austin Chalk field in south Texas. The energy concern said the Lane No. 4 well tested at a rate of 2,050 barrels of oil a day and 920,000 cubic feet of natural gas a day, through a 33/64th-inch choke. The Dickson No. 5 tested at a rate of 385 barrels of oil a day and 240,000 cubic feet of natural gas a day. The company wouldn't disclose the choke size or tubing pressure on the Dickson well, saying through a spokesman that "it doesn't have a significance."

TEX-HEX is the operator of all of Allegheny & Western's Austin Chalk acreage and holds a 25% working interest in the Lane well and a 50% working interest in the Dickson well. The company didn't disclose the names of working interest partners in either well.
