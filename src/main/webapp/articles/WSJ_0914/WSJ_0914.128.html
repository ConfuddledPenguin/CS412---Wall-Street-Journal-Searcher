Business Brief -- Renault S.A.: Holder Suits Opposing Bid For Mack Trucks Settled

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

French car manufacturer Renault S.A. said that class-action lawsuits that had been brought by shareholders challenging its offer to acquire Mack Trucks Inc. have been settled. The settlement of the suits, which had been consolidated in the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, is subject to completion of the offer and final court approvals.

The offer is being made by ITM Acquisition Corp., a subsidiary of Renault. On Sept. 6, Renault said that it had agreed to acquire all of Mack's common stock not owned directly or indirectly by it at an offer price of $6.25 a share.
