Humana Unit Acquisition

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

LOUISVILLE, Ky. -- A subsidiary of Humana Inc. has acquired and assumed the operation of Prime Health HMO, the health maintenance organization owned by Prime Health Management Services Inc. Prime Health HMO has approximately 80,000 members in Missouri and Kansas. Humana owns and operates 85 hospitals and has approximately 1.2 million members in health benefit plans for groups and Medicare beneficiaries.
