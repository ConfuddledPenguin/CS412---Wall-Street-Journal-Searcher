Business Brief -- Turner Corp.: Switzerland's Karl Steiner And Unit Form New Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

Turner Corp. said its subsidiary, Turner International Industries Inc., formed a new company with Karl Steiner Ltd. of Switzerland to pursue construction-related opportunities in Europe. Based in Brussels, Turner Steiner Europe Inc. will offer consulting and management services to clients with building plans in Europe.

Based in New York, Turner, a holding company that operates several construction subsidiaries, is one of the largest general builders in the country. Karl Steiner is a general contracting, design and construction company with subsidiaries in France, Germany and Italy.
