Jacor Communications Notes

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

CINCINNATI -- Jacor Communications Inc. said holders of certain notes have agreed to a further deferral of payments, while the radio and cable television concern continues negotiations with its creditors to restructure its debts. Payments on the notes that initially were due Aug. 23 were first deferred until yesterday and now have been deferred until Sept. 28, Jacor said.

The company previously hired Salomon Brothers Inc. to help negotiate a refinancing of its debt.
