Who's News: Prism Entertainment Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

PRISM ENTERTAINMENT Corp. (Los Angeles) -- Barry Collier, 47-year-old president and chief executive officer of this home video concern, was elected to the additional position of chairman, succeeding Paul Levinson, 48, who didn't stand for reelection as a director. The company markets videocassettes of theatrical and television films and other programs in the U.S. and Canada.
