Gencorp Inc. Unit Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Aerojet General Corp., a unit of GenCorp Inc., received a $212.9 million Air Force contract for full-scale development of the Stage II motor for the Midgetman missile.

CTA Inc. received a $36.5 million Air Force contract for training simulators.

Raytheon Co. was issued a $24 million Navy contract for satellite communications equipment.
