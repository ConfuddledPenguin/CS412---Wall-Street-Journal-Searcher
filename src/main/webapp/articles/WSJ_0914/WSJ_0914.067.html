Foster Wheeler Unit Gets Job

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

CLINTON, N.J. -- Foster Wheeler Corp., an engineering, construction, manufacturing, real estate development and environmental services company, said its Foster Wheeler Ltd. subsidiary was awarded a $17 million contract from Ontario Hydro, a Canadian electric utility, to design and make eight heat exchangers, which will be constructed at the Bruce Nuclear Generating Stations in Kincardine, Ontario.
