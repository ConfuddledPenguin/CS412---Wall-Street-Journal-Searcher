Business Brief -- K mart: Retailer Agrees to Acquire 22% Stake in OfficeMax Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

K mart Corp. said it agreed to acquire a 22% stake in OfficeMax Inc., an office supply chain that will purchase K mart's own smaller office supply operation. Terms weren't disclosed. The Troy, Mich., retailer said that OfficeMax will run its five Office Square stores, continuing the company's planned expansion. OfficeMax, a privately held Cleveland company with projected sales of $250 million for fiscal 1991, operates 34 stores in seven states. By the end of the current fiscal year, OfficeMax hopes to have about 65 stores.

K mart said that, under the agreement, it has the right to acquire additional equity in OfficeMax within "specified time frames," which it wouldn't disclose. The deal is subject to definitive agreements, as well as government approval and the approval of OfficeMax shareholders and the companies' boards.
