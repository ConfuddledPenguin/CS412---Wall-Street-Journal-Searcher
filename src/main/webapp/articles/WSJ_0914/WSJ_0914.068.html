Toyota Unit to Build U.S. Plant

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

ST. LOUIS -- Toyota Motor Corp.'s Bodine Aluminum said it will build a $50 million parts plant in Troy, Mo., that eventually will sell engine parts to the Japan-based auto maker, with production scheduled to begin in 1993. The plant will produce intake manifolds and cylinder heads that Bodine will sell to Toyota for use in its Georgetown, Ky., engine plant, said Toyota Motor Corp. Services of North America Inc., the New York subsidiary that bought Bodine earlier this year. Though Toyota has the Georgetown engine plant, it imports many parts from Japan, a Toyota spokesman in New York said.
