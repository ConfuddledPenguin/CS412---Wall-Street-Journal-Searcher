WHO'S NEWS: News Corp. named Richard Sarazen senior executive vice president, a new post, and a member of the office of the chief executive. David DeVoe, deputy chief financial officer, succeeds Mr. Sarazen as chief financial officer. . . . Reader's Digest Association Inc. named Gregory G. Coleman, 36, as the new group publisher of its Special Interest Magazine Group. He was publisher of Memories, the news-nostalgia magazine recently folded by Diamandis Communications Inc.
