International Brief -- Allianz AG: Insurer Expects 16% Boost In Gross Premium Income

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

The chairman of Allianz AG said he expects gross premium income to climb 16% to about 37 billion marks ($23.2 billion) in 1990 from 31.8 billion marks in 1989. Wolfgang Schieren also said in Frankfurt, West Germany, that in 1991, the insurer should see another burst of growth to 40 billion marks, with a contribution from U.S.-based Fireman's Fund Insurance Co., which it acquired in August.

By 1991, the results of Fireman's Fund will cause the foreign share of the concern's business to climb to 50% from 42% in 1989, it is estimated.
