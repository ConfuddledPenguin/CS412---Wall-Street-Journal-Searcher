Diebold, IBM Joint Venture

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

CANTON, Ohio -- Diebold Inc. and International Business Machines Corp. said they completed a previously announced agreement to form a joint venture to make and sell automatic teller machines worldwide. Terms weren't disclosed. The new company will begin operations later this year. The majority of the IBM-Diebold joint venture will be owned by Diebold.

The product line will include application systems and hardware for ATMs, as well as information stations that provide customers with financial information on their accounts, the company said.

---

Corrections & Amplifications

DIEBOLD Inc. and International Business Machines Corp. said their joint venture to make and sell automatic teller machines will begin operations immediately. The starting date of the new company was incorrectly stated in Friday's edition.
