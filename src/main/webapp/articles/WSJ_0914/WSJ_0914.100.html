Dividend News: Optical Radiation Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

OPTICAL RADIATION Corp., Azusa, Calif., declared a special cash dividend of $2 a share on its common stock, payable Nov. 1 to stock of record Oct. 12. The designer and maker of precision optical parts and systems for medical, industrial and scientific applications currently has about 6.4 million shares outstanding.
