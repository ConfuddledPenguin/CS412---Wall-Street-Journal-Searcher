Who's News: Dow Chemical Co.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

DOW CHEMICAL Co. (Midland, Mi.) -- Keith R. McKennon, executive vice president and president of Dow Chemical U.S.A., was appointed to the new position of executive vice president for technology. Mr. McKennon is in charge of world-wide research and development and manufacturing operations, reporting to Frank Popoff, president and chief executive officer. William S. Stavropoulos, group vice president for U.S. plastics and hydrocarbons, was elected a corporate vice president. He succeeds Mr. McKennon as president and retains authority over hydrocarbons and will report to Mr. Popoff. Anthony J. Carbone, a vice president of the plastics group, succeeds Mr. Stavropoulos as group vice president. Enrique J. Sosa, group vice president for U.S. chemicals and performance products, was elected a corporate vice president in charge of the Corporate Product Department. He succeeds Bernard B. Butcher, who will retire Nov. 1. Mr. Butcher will remain a corporate director and senior consultant.
