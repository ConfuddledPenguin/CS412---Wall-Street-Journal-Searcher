August Steel Output in Japan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

TOKYO -- High demand pushed August steel production in Japan up 4.3% to 9.1 million metric tons, compared with year-earlier levels, but the output was down 0.5% from the previous month, the Japan Iron and Steel Federation said. A metric ton is about 2,204.6 pounds. While seasonal energy conservation measures brought August output down from the previous month's total, a spokesman said the federation doesn't expect the rise in crude-oil prices that followed imposition of United Nations sanctions on Iraq to affect production.
