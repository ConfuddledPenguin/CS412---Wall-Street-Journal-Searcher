Chambers Development Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

PITTSBURGH -- Chambers Development Co. said it agreed to acquire the stock of Remote Landfill Services Inc. in Oliver Springs, Tenn. Terms weren't disclosed. Remote Landfill has a permit to construct and operate a 135-acre sanitary landfill in Anderson County, Chambers said.

Chambers said it expects to complete the transaction after resolution of certain litigation pending against Remote Landfill.
