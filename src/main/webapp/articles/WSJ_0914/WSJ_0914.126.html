GULFSTREAM AEROSPACE Corp. has been sold by Chrysler Corp. and is no longer a unit of the auto maker. The ownership was misstated in yesterday's edition. (See: "International: U.S.-Soviet Plan for Plane Venture Faces Obstacles" -- WSJ Sept. 13, 1990)

PIER 1 IMPORTS Inc.'s sales for the second quarter ended Sept. 2 at stores open at least one year declined 4.1%. The figure was given as an increase in yesterday's edition.

(See: "Pier 1 Sales Increase" -- WSJ Sept. 13, 1990)
