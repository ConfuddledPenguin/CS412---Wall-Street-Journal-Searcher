Financing Business: Shell Canada Ltd.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

SHELL CANADA Ltd., a Calgary, Alberta, oil and gas concern, said it plans to offer 150 million Canadian dollars (US$128.9 million) of 11.625% Eurobonds, due Oct. 11, 1995. The unit of Royal Dutch/Shell Group, an Anglo-Dutch concern, said proceeds from the bonds, which will be priced at 101.60 and denominated in units of C$1,000 and C$10,000, will be used for general purposes. Shell Canada said Toronto-based Wood Gundy Inc. will lead the issue's underwriting group.
