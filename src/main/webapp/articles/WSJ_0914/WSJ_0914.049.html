Morrison Knudsen Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

BOISE, Idaho -- Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it acquired CF Systems Corp., a closely held environmental technology concern. Morrison said the acquisition -- the terms of which it didn't disclose -- was part of an effort to bolster its environmental cleanup business. It said CF Systems, based in Boston, has developed several technologies for reduction and treatment of hazardous wastes, including a solvent-extraction system for petroleum sludges.

Morrison also has interests in engineering, design, construction, rail systems and mining.
