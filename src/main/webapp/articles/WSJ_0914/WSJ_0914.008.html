Allegheny Ludlum Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

PITTSBURGH -- Allegheny Ludlum Corp. said a prototype casting machine to produce stainless and carbon steel flat products will be built at its Lockport, N.Y., facility. The machine will be designed and installed by joint venture partner, Voest-Alpine Industrieanlagenbau Ges. G.m.b.H., of Linz, Austria.

Allegheny Ludlum said the melting and refining equipment at Lockport is suited to match the hot metal needs of the machine.

The joint venture partners haven't disclosed financial details about the project.
