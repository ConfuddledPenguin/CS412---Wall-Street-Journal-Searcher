Initial State Jobless Claims Increased in Sept. 1 Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

WASHINGTON -- Initial claims for regular state unemployment benefits rose to a seasonally adjusted 382,000 in the week ended Sept. 1 from 370,000 in the previous week, the Labor Department said. The number of people receiving regular state benefits in the week ended Aug. 25 totaled a seasonally adjusted 2,546,000, or 2.4% of those covered by unemployment insurance, up from 2,507,000 in the previous week, when the insured unemployment rate also was 2.4%.

Counting all state and federal benefit programs, the number of people receiving unemployment benefits in the week ended Aug. 25 fell to 2,319,800 from 2,321,700 a week earlier. These figures aren't seasonally adjusted.
