Savin Gets Contracts

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

STAMFORD, Conn. -- Savin Corp. said it received contracts valued at between $5 million and $10 million annually to supply photocopy machines to the Navy. Under the contracts, renewable for as long as five years, the distributor of office copiers and facsimile machines will supply photocopiers to Navy offices around the country. The contracts are on a cost-per-copy basis, which means Savin's revenue depends on how many copies Navy officials make.
