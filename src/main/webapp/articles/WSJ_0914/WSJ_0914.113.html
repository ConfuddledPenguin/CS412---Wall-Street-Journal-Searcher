Who's News: CSX Corp.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

CSX Corp. (Richmond, Va.) -- This transportation, energy, property and technologies company named three people to new posts at Sea-Land Service Inc., its transportation and distribution unit based in Edison, N.J. Robert J. Grassi, 44 years old, previously vice president, finance, and chief financial officer, was named senior vice president, finance. John O. Parker, 46, previously vice president, was named senior vice president, information resources. Michael D. Tebay, 44, previously vice president, was named senior vice president, marketing and development.
