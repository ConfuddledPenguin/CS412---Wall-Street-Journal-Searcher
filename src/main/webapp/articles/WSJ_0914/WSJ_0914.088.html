Allegheny International Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 09/14/90

PITTSBURGH -- Allegheny International Inc. said the Nuclear Regulatory Commission approved its plan to emerge from Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection. The company had expected to emerge last month, but the NRC raised questions. Any transfer of ownership, or control, of a nuclear license is subject to approval by the NRC. Allegheny's Chemetron unit has a such a nuclear license.

The maker of Sunbeam and Oster products said it now expects to emerge from bankruptcy protection the week of Sept. 24, at which time it will be effectively controlled by a New York investor group, Japonica Partners.
