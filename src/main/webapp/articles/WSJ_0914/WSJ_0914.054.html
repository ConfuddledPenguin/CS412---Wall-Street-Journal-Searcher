The Golden Years?

Life begins at forty,

So does faulty eyesight,

Twinges of lumbago,

Diets to stay light,

Napping after supper,

A yen for warmer climes,

And telling the same joke

Two or three times.

-- Gail Cooke.

---

Seminar Problem

Pie charts have the wrong effect,

Evoke my gourmet dream;

I think of cherry, pecan crunch,

Also banana cream.

-- Ralph Shaffer.

It's not whether you win or lose, but where you place the blame.
