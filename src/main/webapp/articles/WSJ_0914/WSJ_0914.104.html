Who's News: J.P. Morgan & Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 09/14/90

J.P. MORGAN & Co. (New York) -- Richard D. Simmons was named a director on the boards of both this bank and its wholly owned subsidiary Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. of New York, increasing board membership to 19 from 18. Mr. Simmons, 55 years old, is president of the Washington Post Co., a diversified media company, based in Washington, D.C.
