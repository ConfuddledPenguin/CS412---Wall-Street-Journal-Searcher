Who's News: InterTAN Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

INTERTAN Inc. (Barrie, Ontario) -- Robert E. Keto, president and chief operating officer, was named chief executive officer of this consumer electronics retailing chain. Mr. Keto, 46 years old, succeeds John Roach, 51, who resigned recently as chief executive officer but remains chairman of InterTAN.
