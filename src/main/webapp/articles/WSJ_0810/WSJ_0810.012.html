Sumitomo Chemical's Paris Plan

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

TOKYO -- Sumitomo Chemical Co. said it will set up a subsidiary in Paris in October to strengthen its agricultural chemical businesses for the European market unification planned for 1992. Officials of the Japanese chemical manufacturer said Sumitomo Chemical France S.A. will sell finished products in France, the largest agricultural chemical market in Europe, and in other European nations as well as promote sales in Africa. The unit is expected to have annual sales of four billion yen ($26.7 million) three years after it starts operating.
