Business Brief -- Porta Systems Corp.: Concern to Pay $6.7 Million To Acquire Electronics Firm

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

Porta Systems Corp. agreed to pay $2.50 a share, or about $6.7 million, for North Hills Electronics Inc., a Glen Cove, N.Y., manufacturer of connection products used in data-communications networks. The acquisition would broaden Syosset, N.Y.-based Porta Systems' lines of connection, protection and testing devices for telecommunications use, said Vincent Santulli, Porta Systems' chairman and chief executive officer.

For the first quarter ended April 30, North Hills earnings more than tripled to $77,886, or three cents a share, as sales climbed 22% to $3.5 million.

The acquisition is subject to execution of a definitive agreement, approval by North Hills stockholders, and other conditions.
