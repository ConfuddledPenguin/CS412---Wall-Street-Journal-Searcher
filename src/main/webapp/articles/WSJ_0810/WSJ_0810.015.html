Ameritech's Michigan Bell Unit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

CHICAGO -- Ameritech's Michigan Bell Co. unit proposed a new pricing structure in reaction to increased competition for interstate long-distance service, but said the move wouldn't boost revenue. In a filing with the Michigan Public Service Commission, the company said it would cut interstate long-distance rates 15% and would discount calls between cities and suburbs. The cuts, amounting to about $140 million, would be offset by raising pay-phone charges to 25 cents from 20 cents and by increasing charges for operator-assisted calls and information services.
