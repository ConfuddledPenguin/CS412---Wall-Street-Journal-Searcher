Dividend News: CenCor Inc.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

CENCOR Inc., a Kansas City, Mo.-based consumer finance and temporary services concern, said it plans a reverse 1-for-5 stock split in hopes of making its shares more attractive to investors. The company said it has about 5.7 million pre-reverse-split shares outstanding and said that the move will be effective Aug. 31. CenCor closed yesterday at $1.43, a level that deters many investors, the company said.
