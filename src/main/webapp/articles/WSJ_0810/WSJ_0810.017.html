Gantos Four-Week Sales Rise

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

GRAND RAPIDS, Mich. -- Gantos Inc. said it had sales of $17.3 million for the four weeks ended Aug. 4, up 15% from year-earlier sales of $15.1 million. Comparable-store sales rose 4%. The operator of women's apparel stores said sales in the July 1990 period included a semiannual clearance sale that last year was held in August.
