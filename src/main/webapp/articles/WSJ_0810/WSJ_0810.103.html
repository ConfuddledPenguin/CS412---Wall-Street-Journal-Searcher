Business Brief -- GTI Corp.: Telemetrix PLC Will Boost Its Stake in Firm to 65.4%

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

GTI Corp. said it plans to sell 2.5 million new shares of GTI common stock for $7.5 million to London-based Telemetrix PLC, its largest holder. The proposed sale would boost Telemetrix's holdings in the San Diego-based maker of electronic circuit boards to 65.4%, from a current 52.8%.

Proceeds from the sale would be used to finance part of GTI's proposed purchase of about 90% of Valor Electronics Co., a San Diego-based maker of electronic components, for about $18.5 million in cash and debt.

GTI said shareholders will be asked to approve the sale of stock to Telemetrix at an Aug. 28 special meeting.
