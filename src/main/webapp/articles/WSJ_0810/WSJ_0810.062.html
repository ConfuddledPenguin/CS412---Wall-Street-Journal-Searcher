LDI Plans to Buy Back Shares

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

CLEVELAND -- LDI Corp. said its board agreed to buy up to 300,000 shares of its common stock on the open market or in privately negotiated transactions. LDI has 6,453,000 common shares outstanding. LDI, which leases and sells data-processing, telecommunications and other high-tech equipment, said the move is a positive investment that will benefit shareholders.
