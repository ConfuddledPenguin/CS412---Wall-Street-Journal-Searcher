Thomson CSF Contract

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

PARIS -- Thomson CSF S.A. said it won a contract to supply 14 mobile air surveillance radars to the defense ministry of Turkey. The amount wasn't disclosed. The state-controlled French defense and electronics group said that the long-range, NATO Class One equipment will be produced locally under a joint venture called Tekfen-Thomson Tekfen Radars.

The radar will be used as part of Turkey's air defense network, the group said.
