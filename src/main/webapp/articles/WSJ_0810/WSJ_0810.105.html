Italy's Public Deficit Widens

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

ROME -- Italy's public sector deficit widened to a provisional 48.818 trillion lire ($41.9 billion) in the first half of the year from a revised 43.454 trillion lire deficit in the first six months of 1989, the Treasury announced. The government deficit for the first half of 1989 was revised downward from an original estimate of 43.909 trillion lire.

The Treasury said that tax revenue amounted to 178.978 trillion lire, up 18% from 151.257 trillion lire a year earlier. Public sector spending totaled 238.071 trillion lire, up 24% from 192.599 trillion lire, while Treasury operations resulted in a net gain of 10.275 trillion lire, compared with a net loss of 2.567 trillion lire a year earlier.
