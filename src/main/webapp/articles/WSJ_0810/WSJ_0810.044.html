Who's News: Carpenter Technology Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

CARPENTER TECHNOLOGY Corp. (Reading, Pa.) -- C. McCollister Evarts, senior vice president for health affairs and dean of the college of medicine at Pennsylvania State University, and James A. Flick Jr., executive vice president and chief financial officer of USF&G Corp., were named directors of this specialty steelmaker. These appointments fill the vacancy left by the June retirement of H. Robert Sharbaugh, an outside director, and increase Carpenter Technology's board to 15.
