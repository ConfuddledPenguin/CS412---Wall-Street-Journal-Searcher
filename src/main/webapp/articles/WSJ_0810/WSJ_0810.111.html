Jacobs Engineering Contract

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

PASADENA, Calif. -- Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said it received a $25 million contract from a division of Borden Inc. to build two production facilities in Jackson, Miss., and Chatsworth, Calif. Jacobs said it will transform an existing structure in Jackson into a 440,000-square-foot facility for the manufacture and distribution of products for Borden's grocery and specialty products division.

The Chatsworth project involves expansion of an existing Borden plant to increase production capacity for margarine, ketchup, sauces and other food products. Both projects are scheduled for completion early next year.
