Who's News: Bankers Trust Says Hill, Other Members Of Leasing Group Quit

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

NEW YORK -- Bankers Trust New York Corp. said Charles Hill, managing director of the bank's leasing group, and "an undetermined number" of other leasing group members resigned. A spokeswoman said Allen Levinson, a managing director in the corporate finance department, and James Clayton, head of the bank's European, Middle East and African department, were named co-heads of the leasing group.

Mr. Hill couldn't be reached for comment. Bankers Trust had a portfolio of about $300 million in leases at the end of 1989.
