British Airways July Traffic Rose

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

LONDON -- British Airways flew 4.09 billion revenue passenger miles in July, up 13.7% from 3.6 billion a year earlier. A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile. Passenger load factor, or the percentage of available seats filled, was 78.5%, compared with 76.3%.

In the year to date, the company flew 14.68 billion revenue passenger miles, up 13.3% from 12.96 billion. Passenger load factor was 73.8%, compared with 72%.
