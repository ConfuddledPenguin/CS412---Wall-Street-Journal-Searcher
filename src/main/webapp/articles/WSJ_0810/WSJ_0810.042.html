Who's News: Arrow Electronics Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

ARROW ELECTRONICS Inc. (Melville, N.Y.) -- Carlo Giersch was named to the board of this electronics distributor, expanding its membership to eight. Mr. Giersch, 53 years old, is chief executive officer and a majority shareholder of Spoerle Electronic, an electronics distributor in Frankfurt, West Germany, that is 40%-owned by Arrow.
