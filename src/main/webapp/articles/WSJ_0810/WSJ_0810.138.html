Business Brief -- Argonaut Group Inc.: Insurance Holding Company Offers to Buy Back Stock

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

Argonaut Group Inc. offered to purchase shares of its common stock from holders with 50 or fewer Argonaut shares as of yesterday. The Los Angeles insurance holding company said that under terms of the offer, participating shareholders must sell all their eligible shares. Argonaut said that for each share owned it will pay holders the highest closing price of Argonaut common between Aug. 21 and Sept. 25.

Argonaut said it is making the offer mainly to allow smaller shareholders to sell their shares without being charged any brokerage commission. A reduction in the number of small shareholder accounts also would reduce the company's administrative costs.

Many shareholders received their shares in 1986 when Teledyne Inc. spun off Argonaut to Teledyne shareholders. Argonaut said that Shareholder Communications Corp. will manage the offer, which is set to expire Sept. 25.
