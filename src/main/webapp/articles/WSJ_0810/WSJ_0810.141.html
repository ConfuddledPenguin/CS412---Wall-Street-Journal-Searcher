Business Brief -- Rochester Telephone Corp.: New York Firm to Acquire 3 Units of Houston Concern

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

Rochester Telephone Corp., Rochester, N.Y., said it has signed a letter of intent to acquire three telephone companies from Mid-South Telecommunications, Houston. The telephone holding company said the purchase of Fairmount Telephone, Fairmount, Ga.; Lamar County Telephone, Millport, Ala.; and Mid-South Telephone, Rienzi, Miss., represents an additional 7,900 access lines and two new states to the company's existing service area. Rochester Telephone's principal operating territory currently is a six-county area in Western-Central New York state.

Rochester Telephone said it will exchange shares of its common stock for all three telephone companies' outstanding stock, but specific terms weren't disclosed.
