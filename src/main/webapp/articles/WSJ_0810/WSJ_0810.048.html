Credit Ratings

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

INVESTCORP INTERNATIONAL Inc. (New York) -- Standard & Poor's Corp. said it lowered its ratings on the company's Color Tile Inc. senior debt to single-B from single-B-plus, subordinated debt to triple-C-plus from single-B-minus and preferred stock to triple-C from single-B-minus. About $280 million in securities is affected by the move. S&P said the ratings downgrades reflect Color Tile's highly leveraged capital structure and low cash flow.
