  • Date: 08/10/90

ARTISTIC GREETINGS Inc. (Elmira, N.Y.) -- Irving Stone, chairman of American Greetings Corp., and Morty Wyman, executive vice president of American Greetings, were elected directors of this manufacturer of greeting cards. This addition expands the company's board to seven. American Greetings owns 40% of Artistic Greetings' common stock.
