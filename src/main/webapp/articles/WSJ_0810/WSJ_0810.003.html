Dividend News: Johnston Industries Inc.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

JOHNSTON INDUSTRIES Inc. declared its first quarterly dividend of 12.5 cents a share, payable Sept. 28 to stock of record Aug. 24. The New York maker of industrial textiles, which has about 4.7 million shares outstanding, had paid a special dividend of 50 cents a share last September.
