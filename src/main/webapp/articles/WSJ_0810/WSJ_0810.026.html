First Bancorp. of Ohio

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

AKRON, Ohio -- First Bancorp. of Ohio said it expects a third quarter charge of $1,485,000, or 12 cents a share, from credit card operations. The loss is due to return of sales drafts deposited by a bank customer engaged in telemarketing. The year-ago figure was $8,991,000, or 74 cents a share.
