Business Brief -- Central Bancshares of the South: River Oaks Bancshares Inc. Agrees to Acquisition Offer

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

Central Bancshares of the South Inc. said it definitively agreed to acquire Houston-based River Oaks Bancshares Inc. for $44 million in cash and preferred stock. Birmingham, Ala.-based Central Bancshares said the boards of both companies approved the transaction, which is subject to regulatory approval.

Central Bancshares said the move fits with its strategy of strengthening its Texas presence. It said after completion of the acquisition, which is expected to occur in early 1991, River Oaks will become part of its Houston-based Compass Bancshares Inc. unit.

Terms of the agreement call for Compass to buy all of the outstanding shares of River Oaks for $17.6 million in cash and $26.4 million in Central Bancshares preferred stock with a coupon rate of 8%. River Oaks is 62%-owned by James E. Lyon, its chairman and chief executive, who plans to vote his shares in favor of the merger, Central Bancshares said.

River Oaks includes River Oaks Bank, with assets of $403 million, and River Oaks Trust Co., with $686 million in trust assets.
