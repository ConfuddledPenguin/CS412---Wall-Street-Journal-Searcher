Credit Ratings: S&P Boosts Ratings Of Unit of Primerica, Commercial Credit Co.

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

NEW YORK -- Ratings on some $4 billion of debt of Primerica Corp.'s Commercial Credit Co. subsidiary were boosted by Standard & Poor's Corp. The March acquisition of the consumer-finance receivables and branch offices of BarclaysAmerican/Financial, a unit of Barclays Group, "has brought significant opportunities for cost savings" and improved Commercial Credit's geographic diversity, the credit-rating company said.

Commercial Credit's senior debt rises to single-A from single-A-minus; the subordinated debt to single-A-minus from triple-B-plus; and the commercial paper to single-A-1 from single-A-2.

Earlier this month, S&P boosted ratings on Primerica Corp.'s Primerica Holdings subsidiary, and in June, Moody's Investors Service Inc. upgraded about $3 billion of debt of the two subsidiaries as well as Primerica Holdings' A.L. Williams unit.

In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Primerica's shares rose $1.125 to $31.125.
