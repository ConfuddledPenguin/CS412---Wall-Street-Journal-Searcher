U.S. Auto Firms Plan 14.8% Rise in Output In Vehicles for Week

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

DETROIT -- The 10 major U.S. auto makers planned to build 122,968 cars and trucks this week, up 14.8% from 107,067 manufactured a year ago. During the month of July, the 10 auto makers built 613,922 cars and trucks, up 24.3% from 493,955 the same month a year ago.

U.S. car output increased 28.8% in July and truck and bus production was up 18.1% from the year-earlier month.
