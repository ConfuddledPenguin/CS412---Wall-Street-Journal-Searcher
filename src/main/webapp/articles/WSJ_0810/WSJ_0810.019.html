Cadbury Schweppes Acquisition

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

LONDON -- The Department of Trade and Industry said it won't refer Cadbury Schweppes PLC's #125 million ($233.7 million) acquisition of Source Perrier's non-cola soft drinks business to the government's Mergers and Monopolies Commission for review. The decision effectively clears the way for the food and drinks concern's agreed bid for those operations of France's Perrier, announced in late May. The purchase includes two plants in the Paris area and one in Marseilles for various brands of the bottled water and beverage concern's subsidiary Compagnie d'Exploitation de Boissons Refraichissantes.
