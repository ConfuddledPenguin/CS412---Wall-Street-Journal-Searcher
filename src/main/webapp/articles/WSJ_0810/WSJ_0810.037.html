Money Funds' Assets Soared $6.03 Billion In the Latest Week

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

WASHINGTON -- Assets of the nation's 522 money-market funds rose $6.03 billion to $401.94 billion in the week ended Wednesday, according to the Investment Company Institute, a Washington-based trade association. Assets of 249 general-purpose funds rose $3.7 billion to $139.42 billion and assets of 104 broker-dealer funds rose $946.9 million to $162.24 billion. Assets of 169 institutional funds rose $1.39 billion to $100.29 billion.

Unsettled markets prompted investors to move out of stocks and bonds and place their money in money-market funds. "This is the largest single week's increase since Jan. 10," said Jacob Dreyer, vice president and chief economist at the institute.

Mutual funds' assets were revised for the previous week, to $395.91 billion. The increase reported for the Aug. 1 week was revised to $1.3 billion.
