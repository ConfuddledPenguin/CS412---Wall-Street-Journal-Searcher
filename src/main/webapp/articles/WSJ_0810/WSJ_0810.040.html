Who's News: Engelhard Corp.

  • Author: null
  • Date: 08/10/90

ENGELHARD Corp. (Edison, N.J.) -- L. Donald LaTorre, chief operating officer, was named a director, succeeding Fredric M. Guist, who resigned from the board. Mr. LaTorre, 52 years old, will fill a term that expires in 1993. Engelhard makes specialty chemical products and provides precious-metal management services.
