Saint-Gobain Offer for Norton

  • Author: Author Unknown
  • Date: 08/10/90

PARIS -- French glass and construction materials group Cie. de Saint-Gobain said it completed its public offer for Norton Co., with 95% of Norton shares tendered. Saint-Gobain said it will pay $90 for each of the 19.5 million shares tendered, bringing the cost of the transaction to $1.76 billion.

The company said it will proceed with the planned merger of the U.S. unit SG Acquisition Corp. and Norton. The merger must be approved by a shareholders' meeting, which is expected to take place the beginning of next month, the company said.

Saint-Gobain said shares that weren't tendered will be eligible for the $90 purchase price after the merger.
